# Couponalbum's Privacy Policy

Coupon Album respects your privacy. The site collects limited personal information like your name and email address when you register for our newsletter. 

Information provided by you will not be passed on to a third party and will solely be used to provide you information through our newsletter. You will have an option provided in each mail sent, to unsubscribe.

Coupon Album will Inform you of any change in Privacy policy before implementing it.
