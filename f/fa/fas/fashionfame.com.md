# FashionFame Privacy Policy | Fashion Fame

This privacy statement (“Privacy Policy”) covers the website Fashion Fame. Fashion Fame respects the privacy of its valued readers and knows that you care about how your personal information is used and shared. Please read this statement carefully before accessing Fashion Fame.

**Information Collection, Management & Sharing**

Fashion Fame is the sole owner of the information collected on Fashion Fame. Fashion Fame may collect information from its users at several different points on its website. No information collected will ever be transferred, sold or given away by Fashion Fame.

Fashion Fame may automatically receive and record certain “traffic data” on our server logs, collect certain non-personally identifying information, such as your computer’s IP address, cookie information, the type of browser in use, pages viewed, when you visit our website.

We do not link this information to any personally identifiable information, instead we use it to analyze trends and enhance your experience while using our site.

**Comments**

Users can choose to leave comments on their articles of interest and for that users must submit information including name and a valid email address. Fashion Fame uses this information to screen out users who leave comments prohibited by our terms and conditions of use, and will not pass this information to any other organization, unless you give us explicit permission to do so.

**Legal Disclaimer**

We make every effort to preserve user privacy but FashionFame may need to disclose personal information if we are required to do so by law, or if we have a good-faith belief that doing so necessary in order to conform to the law, cooperate with law enforcement agencies, comply with legal process.

**Links to Other Web Sites**

Fashion Fame contains links to other websites whose privacy policies may differ from ours. Fashion Fame is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other websites. We encourage our users to carefully read the privacy policy of any other web site you visit through FashionFame.com, which collects personally identifiable information.

**Notification of Changes**

Fashion Fame may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time, at its sole discretion. If we make changes to the Privacy Policy, it will be posted in this space.

**Questions and Suggestions**

For any queries or suggestions regarding our privacy policy, or about our practices with respect to Personal Information, please feel free to contact us at FashionFame.com.
