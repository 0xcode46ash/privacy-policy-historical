# Privacy Policy

| 

**Falcon Safety Products Inc. has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.**

The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: [http://www.falconsafety.com](http://www.falconsafety.com/).

Your IP address is used to help identify you and to gather broad demographic information. On certain pages we give you the opportunity to provide us with names, addresses, phone numbers, e-mail addresses, as well as information on how you use our products. This information is not gathered without your active involvement and knowledge. We use this information to improve our users Web experience, generate anonymous statistics or in some cases, to contact users via e-mail or postal mail about products that may be of interest to them. We will not provide your information to any third parties for use in any way.

**Corrections/Update**  
You may request that we change and modify information previously provided by sending a written e-mail request to [knewman@falconsafety.com](mailto:knewman@falconsafety.com).
