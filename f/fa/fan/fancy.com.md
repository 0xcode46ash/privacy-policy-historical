# Portland Real Estate :: Lyudmila Leissler :: Windermere Real Estate :: Oregon

We value your privacy. Your information will be kept confidential. We will NOT share or rent this information with any other company. We will use your information only to respond to your request or inquiry (ie. to email or send you information, or follow-up on requested information). Unless you request otherwise, we may contact you via email in the future to inform you about new offerings or services of interest relating to your initial request.

Registration

Although you are free to access most features and tools on our website, in order to perform "Save Searches", "Save Favorites", "Save Reports", "Opt-in For Email Notification Of New Listings", etc., you will be prompted to create your own individual account. During registration you will need to give certain information (such as name and email address). This information is used to contact you about any listings, reports, and services on our site in which you have expressed interest. At your option, you may also provide additional information, but it is not required.

Your Access to and Control Over Information

You may "opt out" of future contacts from us at any time. Contact us via the email address or phone number provided on our website to request "no future contact".

Cookies

A "cookie" is a small data text file that is stored on your computer during your visit and used only to help us improve your access to our site. Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and we do not use cookies to collect personal information. They are only used to maintain the integrity of an ongoing search string. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personal, identifiable information on our site. Our cookies are "sessionless" cookies and upon leaving our site, our cookies are self-cleansing. 

Remember The Risks Whenever You Use The Internet

This website may contain links to other websites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of other sites. Please contact those vendors directly if you have any questions about their privacy policies. We include (local)links to external web sites for your convenience only.

For any other information, please feel free to contact us.
