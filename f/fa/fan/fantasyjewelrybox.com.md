# Privacy Policy — Fantasy Jewelry Box

Your privacy is very important to us. That is why we will NEVER rent, sell or share any of your personal information. This includes, name, address, telephone or email information. Your information may be used internally by our company for contacting you regarding an order placed or to obtain additional information.If you have an account with us your information is stored securely online using the latest security methods from Yahoo!

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click [here](http://www.fantasyjewelrybox.com/www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp).

All images on FantasyJewelryBox.com are readily available in various places on the internet and believed to be in public domain. Images posted are believed to be posted within our rights according to the US Copyright Fair Use Act.

If you believe that any content appearing on FantasyJewelryBox.com infringes on your copyright, please let us know … and the infringing material will be removed as soon as possible.
