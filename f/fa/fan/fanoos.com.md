#### Fanoos Privacy Policy

Fanoos.com takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy. Also, please read our Children''s Privacy Policy to learn more about how we treat personally identifiable information collected from children and Privacy Information to learn how we treat such information collected in connection with Fanoos financial products and services. 

This Privacy Policy covers Fanoos.com treatment of personally identifiable information that Fanoos.com collects when you are on Fanoos.com, and when you use our services.  
This policy also covers Fanoos.com treatment of any personally identifiable information that Fanoos.com business partners share with Fanoos.com that may collect on a partner''s site. 

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Fanoos.com does not own or control, or to people that Fanoos.com does not employ or manage. 

**INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE**  
Fanoos.com collects personally identifiable information when you register for any of our accounts, when you use certain Fanoos products or services, when you visit Fanoos pages, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. Fanoos.com may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners. 

When you register with Fanoos.com , we ask for your name, email address, birth date, gender, occupation, industry, and personal interests. Once you register with Fanoos.com and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.

Fanoos.com also automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, Fanoos.com cookie information and the page you requested.  
Fanoos.com uses such information for three general purposes mainly: to customize the advertising and content you see, to fulfill your requests for certain products and services, and to contact you about specials and new products. 

**INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE**  
Fanoos.com will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.  
Fanoos.com may send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when:   
We have your consent to share the information;   
We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested;   
We need to send the information to companies who work on behalf of Fanoos.com to provide a product or service to you. (Unless we tell you differently, these companies do not have any right to use the personally identifiable information we provide to them beyond what is necessary to assist us.);  
We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; or   
We find that your actions on our web sites violate Fanoos.com Terms of Service or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services. 

**COOKIES**  
Fanoos.com may set and access Fanoos.com cookies on your computer.   
Fanoos.com allows other companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies'' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to Fanoos.com cookies.   
Fanoos.com uses web beacons to access our cookies within and outside our network of web sites and in connection with Fanoos.com products and services.

 **ACCOUNT MODIFICATIONS AND PREFERENCES**  
Fanoos.com gives you the ability to edit your Fanoos.com Account Information and preferences at any time, including whether you want Fanoos.com to contact you about specials and new products.  
You may request deletion of your Fanoos.com account by visiting our Account Deletion page, verifying your password once more and confirming your choice to terminate the account. 

**SECURITY**  
Your Fanoos.com Account Information is password-protected for your privacy and security.   
In certain areas Fanoos.com uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.

 **CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY**  
Fanoos.com may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.

 **QUESTIONS AND SUGGESTIONS**  
If you have questions or suggestions complete our [feedback form](http://www.fanoos.com/contact.asp)
