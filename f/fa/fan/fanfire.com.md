Privacy Assurance | Security Statement

## Fanfire Privacy Assurance

As the entertainment industry's premier developer and distributor of licensed celebrity merchandise, we are firmly committed to ensuring your privacy. Information that you provide while visiting our web site is treated as restricted, sensitive information and we protect it using advanced technology.

Below is a full overview of our policies regarding your privacy.

### Personal Information

Fanfire uses your personal information to: 

  * Create a user-friendly and satisfying online shopping experience for you
  * Quickly and expertly service your account
  * Provide you with products you have requested
  * Inform you about additional products or services that may be of interest to you



We want our customers to have complete authority over their personal information. In fact, you can visit our site without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself.

Our information gathering is initialized during the sales transaction process and when customers respond to promotional offers and direct communications. If you choose to give us personal information via the Internet that we or our business partners may need — either to correspond with you, process an order, or provide you with timely information — we will let you know how we plan to use such information. If you tell us that you do not wish to have this information used as a basis for further contact with you, we will honor that request. We do not spam or engage in intrusive campaigns.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties except under the following limited conditions: 

  * As part of normal business operations, we share select information for legal/regulatory purposes required by the Internal Revenue Service, Securities and Exchange Commission, and other regulatory and government entities.
  * We occasionally invite selected third parties to participate in offers we feel would be attractive to Fanfire customers. If you wish to permanently opt out of these offers, you may do so at any time.



Fanfire analyzes and reports on customer data in the aggregate?never on an individual basis.

### Information Security and Quality

We protect your account information by placing it on the secure portion of our Web site. Our network employs server authentication and data encryption technologies for added security.

  


### Online Surveys & Contests

Surveys and contests are regularly featured on our site. We ask visitors for their contact information, and in some cases, demographic information, so that we can provide you with more targeted promotional offers. You always have the choice to opt out of receiving future mailings.

### Cookies

A cookie is an element of data that a Web site can send to your browser for storage. The purpose: to provide more tailored communication from the Web site. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it. Fanfire's site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart. We also use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site.

### Links

Our Web site contains links to other sites. Fanfire is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

For more information about our privacy policy, please e-mail [info@fanfire.com](mailto:info@fanfire.com).

### Fanfire Security Statement

Credit-card security is top of mind for consumers who shop on the Internet. That's why we use the latest server authentication and data encryption technology to ensure every transaction you make on our site is 100% secure. Plus, we safeguard your account information on the secure portion of our Web site, behind the firewall.

Your private information remains just that private. This allows you to shop for your favorite entertainment merchandise with complete confidence and peace of mind.

If you prefer to make purchases by phone, please call us at 1-800-767-7160. Any of our customer-service representatives will be happy to help you with your order.
