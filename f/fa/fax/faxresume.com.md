# FaxResume - Fax Distribution Service

FaxResume.com Privacy Policy.

FaxResume.com Privacy Program ensures that all information provided to FaxResume.com will not be disclosed to any source without the approval of the client. This privacy statement discloses the privacy practices for FaxResume.com.

FaxResume.com is committed to safekeeping your privacy. This Privacy Statement outlines our privacy policy. This statement may change from time to time so please check back periodically. We will notify you directly if there is a material change in our privacy practices. We will take commercially reasonable measures to obtain written or active email consent from the user if FaxResume.com is going to be using the information collected from the user in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection. We will also post the changes in our privacy statement 30 days prior to a change. FaxResume.com explicitly asks when it needs information that personally identifies you. To register, FaxResume.com asks only for information required to provide FaxResume.com services. When you submit personal information, you are sharing it with FaxResume.com alone. Personal information is stored in secure operating environments not available to the public.  
FaxResume.com keeps your personal information private and does not disclose it to anyone. Your account information is password-protected so only you have access to it. Do not submit your password to anyone. FaxResume.com will never ask you for your password in an unsolicited phone call or email.  
Sign out of your account and close your browser when you finish so others cannot access your personal information. You may edit your account at this site at any time using your FaxResume.com user name and password. Your FaxResume.com account can be canceled, but this will stop your FaxResume.com services. Once you used fax resume to successful fax your resume to at least 1 location you can not be refunded the service fee.   
This site contains links to other sites. FaxResume.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of such sites. While we strive for security, FaxResume.com cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to or receive from our online services, and you do so at your own risk. Once we receive your transmission, we make our best effort to ensure its security.

Questions should be directed to faxresume@corp.FaxResume.com If you believe that FaxResume.com has not adhered to this policy, notify us by email and we will promptly address the problem. FaxResume.com also provides email links located on the "About Us" page to allow you to contact us directly with any questions or comments you may have. We read every message sent in and try to reply promptly to every one. This information is used to respond directly to your questions or comments. We may also file your comments to improve the site and service, or review and discard the information.  
Your personal information is only shared with third parties with your explicit permission. If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact FaxResume.com by emailing faxresume@corp.internetpcs.com .
