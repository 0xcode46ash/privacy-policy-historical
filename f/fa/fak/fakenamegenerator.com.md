# Fake Name Generator

## Privacy Policy

### What information do you collect?

We store normal statistical information, such as referring URL, IP address, and date/time of visit. Generated identities are not associated with any specific IP address or visitor.

Bulk identity orders are stored for one month to ensure that the recipient received them. After one month the bulk order is deleted. For tax purposes, we retain sales information for at least one year.

### Do you use cookies?

Our web analytics service and advertisers may set cookies.

### Do you sell my information?

No. The only information we will ever sell or release to third-parties is randomly generated.
