# Fakespot

  


#### **Privacy Policy**

 **EMAIL, MESSAGING, APPS**

Fakespot uses electronic mail, other communications applications and social media extensively. Your user experience with the System is entirely dependent on our ability to send and receive electronic mail and to otherwise communicate with you. You hereby expressly consent to our recording, storing and using your email address(es), your telephone, social media records and other communication devices for any purposes that may be relevant to the System and the efficient provision of the System, including, but not limited to, system messaging, product updates, service announcements and other marketing messages. You hereby expressly agree that under no circumstances shall we have any liability for sending or receiving email or notifications to any System user. It is not the Fakespot policy to send or facilitate Spam or other unsolicited marketing messages. You are expressly prohibited from using the System in any manner that might be considered as engaging in such activities and we reserve the right to immediately terminate access to the System under such circumstances and under such other circumstances as may be related to any such activities.

 **INFORMATION COLLECTION STORAGE AND USE**

Fakespot collects personal information when you use the System. Fakespot may combine collected information with information obtained from business partners or other companies. Fakespot stores information with cloud based services and exercises commercially reasonable security measures to protect your Information but Fakespot cannot be responsible for the integrity or loss of any data stored on third party facilities.When you register, we may ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, ZIP code, occupation, industry, family information including limited information related to minors and other personal information which may be required or reasonably related to real estate transactions. Such Information from users of the System is required to use the System.

 **INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE**

Fakespot does not rent, sell, or share identifying personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated business partners or companies except as may be necessary to provide products or services you've requested or under the following circumstances:

  * We provide the information to partners who work on behalf of or with Fakespot under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help Fakespot communicate with you and our marketing partners.
  * Fakespot will respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.
  * We transfer information about you if Fakespot is acquired by or merged with another company.
  * Fakespot does not provide any personal information to advertisers.
