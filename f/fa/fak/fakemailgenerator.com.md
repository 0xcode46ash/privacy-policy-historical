# FAQ - Fake Mail Generator

## FAQ

### What is the Fake Mail Generator?

The Fake Mail Generator is a website that allows you to circumvent email confirmations by giving you a one time email address to use. Next time a site asks for your email address, give them a Fake Mail Generator address and avoid the spam that is sure to follow. 

###  Why is it called the Fake Mail Generator?

This site is an extension of the [Fake Name Generator](http://www.fakenamegenerator.com/ "Fake Name Generator"), which generates fake names, addresses, and other information. The name of this site was chosen to match its parent site. 

### Isn't this a rip-off of Mailinator, 2Prong, and other sites?

This concept of this website isn't a unique idea, and the original code was written by another person and open sourced. So yes, its a rip-off. 

### How does this site make money?

This site is entirely advertiser supported.

Like many other Web sites, Fake Mail Generator makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

#### Cookies and Web Beacons

Fake Mail Generator does use cookies to store information about visitors' preferences, record user-specific information on which pages users access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors' browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
