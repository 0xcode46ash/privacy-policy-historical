# Invoice and Manage Your Clients with Confidence | Function Point

more secure

User information

Your personal information is secure. It’s safeguarded within our system and only used to provide you with service and assist with your login. We never access your account without your consent.

Full disclosure

We do track IP addresses and use cookies to administer Function Point software and our website. Behaviour patterns, including frequency of visits, popularity of applications, and estimating the population size of our users, along with customer-submitted requests, are gathered and analyzed to enhance user experience. We don’t analyze demographics and no information is ever used for any other purpose, nor sold to third-parties.

Your clients’ information

We understand how important your clients’ data security is to you. If you or your clients have any questions or concerns please e-mail [support@functionpoint.com](http://support@functionpoint.com/), or call 1-877-731-2522. 

Preventative measures

To ensure the safety of your and your company’s data, please follow these measures:

##### Don’t share your password.

##### Always sign off before leaving a shared or public computer.

##### Always use the SSL login option when signing on to Function Point.

Please note: the transmission of information over the internet is never fail-safe. (Duh.) However, we use the most up-to-date and relied-upon data transmission technology available to safeguard your data.
