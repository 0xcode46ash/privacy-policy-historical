# Privacy Policy - Function Loops

**PRIVACY POLICY**

Function Loops (We) is committed to protect your privacy online. 

 **Which  information do we collect?**

We only collect your email address and name for internal use. We also give you an option to join our mailing list to receive our newsletter and you can unsubscribe anytime you like, by pressing "unsubscribe" button. If you download free products from our website, you will be automatically subscribed to our newsletter and you can unsubscribe at anytime.

 If you make a purchase you will be redirected to a third party service called Payloadz, you can read their privacy policy here: https://www.payloadz.com/privacy.asp

 **Cookies**

Cookies are small files which many web sites transfer to your hard disk. They can inform the web site what pages you visit, and your preferences, which enable web sites to provide you with more personalized service. You can disable cookies on your browser.

 **How do we use your information?**

We do not sell or share your personal information, all the information is kept for us only in private, to send you additional information about us and our products, which maybe interesting for you. You can unsubscribe at any time.
