# Fugly.com Privacy Statement

**Privacy and You at Fugly**  
  
   
  
> The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for **Fugly,** located at [http://www.fugly.com](http://www.fugly.com/)  
  
   
  
**Information Automatically Logged**  
  
> Certain information is automatically logged every time you visit **Fugly** or any other Web site. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and for administration purposes. Your IP address is also used to gather broad demographic information that we use only for statistical purposes to measure traffic flow and bandwidht requirements.  
  
   
  
**Cookies **  
  
> Additonally, **Fugly** uses an outside ad company to display ads. These ads may also contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information. 
> 
> **Fugly** uses cookies to make sure you don't see the same ad repeatedly. **Fugly** also uses cookies to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our Forum section. Cookies placed on your system from **Fugly** are never used to gather any information. They are only used to make your visit to **Fugly** faster and easier. Choosing to disable cookies in your browser will have no affect on your ability to visit **Fugly**.   
  
   
  
**Registration Forms / Information Collected**  
  
> **Fugly** 's registration forms (in our Chat, Forum and other sections) only require users to give us a username and a valid email address. Contact information from the registration forms is used to get in touch with the customer when necessary and to verify the identity of the viewer. 
> 
> **Fugly** makes chat rooms, forums and message boards available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. **Fugly** collects all email addresses that are entered when viewers use these feature of our site. **Fugly** also collects the email addresses of viewers that send feedback to **Fugly** at any of the addresses provided throughout the Web Site. 
> 
> As a courtesy, and to keep viewers informaed of updates, changes and news regarding **Fugly** , the email addresses we collect are automatically added to our Mailing Lists. At any time, users may remove themselves from these lists by following the removal instructions that are provided with each email we send to our Mailing Lists. Fugly may, under certian circumstances, and at it's sole discression, share these email addresses with third parties.  
  
   
  
**External Links**  
  
> **Fugly** contains links to other sites. **Fugly** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.  
  
   
  
**Contacting us.**  
  
> For answers to any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please contact us through our [contact page.](https://web.archive.org/contact)
