# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

To enable you to benefit fully from this website we may request information from you, and by using our website you consent to the collection and use of such information. This may include, but is not limited to, details such as your name, address, areas of interest or credit card details. When you provide such information you can opt not to receive additional e-mail, post or telephone contact from us by selecting the appropriate options on the web page. When you link to our site via another web page, we may store the name of that web page for internal administration purposes.

**Use of information about you**

We will use the information you give us within Future Medicine Ltd and Future Science Ltd. We may use your contact information to provide notification of important changes to our site and new services, and about publications that may be of interest to you. If you would rather not receive this information, please contact us at [e-access@futuremedicine.com](mailto:e-access@futuremedicine.com). We use return e-mail addresses to answer the e-mail we receive. We use such addresses for this purpose only. We do not sell, trade or rent any information we receive from users via the website to third parties.

Although we share statistical information with other organizations to inform them about our services and products (e.g. information on site traffic), such statistics do not include any personal identifying information.

Where there are links on our site to other sites, the content of such linked sites is not covered by the Future Medicine privacy policy and we cannot be held responsible or liable for such sites or their corresponding privacy policies.

We may disclose information in special cases when we have reason to believe that disclosing this information is reasonably necessary to comply with the law. We may also disclose information to enforce or apply our terms of business, or protect our rights and those of our users.

**Use of 'cookies'**

'Cookies' - small amounts of information sent from a web server to your browser and stored on your computer's hard drive - contain information about the internet sites you have visited. We may use cookies to store and sometimes track information about you and to facilitate personalization of the service we provide. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies. However, you can usually change your browser to prevent this, but you may not be able to access some parts of our site without them.
