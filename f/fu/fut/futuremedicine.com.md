# 

Privacy Policy

  
This Privacy Policy covers the Website **[www.futuremedicine.com](http://www.futuremedicine.com/)** (the “Website”). We respect the privacy of every individual who visits the Website. This Policy sets out how we may use or disclose personal information about visitors to the Website and their rights in relation to that information. By entering and using the Website you are deemed to accept all terms and conditions outlined in this Policy.

**1\. Collection of Personal Information from Website Visitors**

Future Medicine Ltd may collect and/or track:

1.The home server domain names, e-mail addresses, operating system, and type of web browser of visitors to the Website;

2.The e-mail addresses of visitors who communicate with Future Medicine Ltd via e-mail;

3.Information provided by the visitor in online forms, registration forms surveys, and contest entries (including demographic, financial and personal profile data);

4.Aggregate and user-specific information on which pages visitors access; and

5.Payment information and financial details about our customers held on the e-commerce system.

6.Information collected from other sources: we may obtain information about you from other sources including contact and demographic information from pre-existing databases and other publicly available data

We may combine information you give to us with information we collect about you. We will use this information for the purposes set out below.

'Cookies' - small amounts of information sent from a web server to your browser and stored on your computer's hard drive - contain information about the internet sites you have visited. We may use cookies to store and sometimes track information about you and to facilitate personalization of the service we provide. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies. However, you can usually change your browser to prevent this, but you may not be able to access some parts of our site without them.

**2\. Use of Personal Data Collected**

Personal data collected by Future Medicine Ltd may be used by Future Medicine Ltd for editorial feedback purposes, marketing and promotional purposes, statistical analysis of visitors’ behaviour, product development, content improvement and to customize the content and layout of the Website, solve technical problems with the Website service, or calculate overall usage statistics.

Aggregate data (which does not identify individuals) on home server domain names, operating system, and type of web browser may be used for internal purposes.

Individually identifying information, such as names, postal and e-mail addresses, phone numbers, and other personal information, which visitors voluntarily provide to Future Medicine Ltd, may be added to the Future Medicine Ltd databases and used for future calls and mailings regarding website updates, new products and services and upcoming events.

**3\. Disclosure of Personal Data to Third Parties**

Future Medicine Ltd does not sell, license, share or intentionally make publicly available any individually identifying information to external third parties other than its sister companies, agents or contractors, such as its software developers, distributors or professional advisers, for the purposes of operating the Website and developing, manufacturing and distributing Future Medicine Ltd products.

Information may be passed to Future Medicine Ltd’s sister company Future Science Ltd. for the purpose of informing individuals about related products and services.

**4\. External Links**

The Website may contain links to other sites. Future Medicine Ltd is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites unless they are owned and operated by Future Medicine Ltd. We encourage our visitors to be aware when they leave the Website and to read the privacy policies of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected through or in connection with the Website.

**5\. Security Measures**

Future Medicine Ltd has implemented security features to prevent the unauthorised release of or access to visitors’ personal information. Please be advised, however, that while Future Medicine Ltd has endeavoured to create a completely secure Website, the Internet is not a 100% secure medium for the communication of information and Future Medicine Ltd is not responsible for the security of information transmitted via the Internet. Accordingly, the confidentiality of any communication or material transmitted to or from Future Medicine Ltd via Website or e-mail cannot be guaranteed.

All of our visitors’ personal information is made available only to those employees or contractors who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, our finance department and sales or customer service representatives, distributors or software developers).

**6\. Opt-Out Right**

Visitors may opt out of having their personal information used by Future Medicine Ltd for marketing and promotional purposes, by contacting either the sales or customer services departments of Future Medicine Ltd via post, e-mail or telephone at the addresses posted in the footer of this Website.

**7\. Access to and Ability to Correct Personal Data**

Upon request via post or e-mail Future Medicine Ltd will provide to visitors any personal information stored by Future Medicine Ltd regarding the visitor (Future Medicine Ltd reserves the right to charge a reasonable fee for fulfilling such requests). Visitors may correct or update their personal record or cause their personal record to be removed from the Future Medicine Ltd database. Please note that Future Medicine Ltd will only send personal records to the e-mail address on file for the visitor name associated with it.

**8\. Our Right to Contact the Visitor**

Future Medicine Ltd reserves the right to contact Website visitors regarding changes to the subscriber agreement, this Privacy Policy, or any other policies or agreements relevant to Website visitors.

**9\. Our Right to Change this Privacy Policy**

This Policy is effective as of 1 January 2007. Future Medicine Ltd reserves the right to change this Policy at any time by publishing on the Website notice of the existence and location of the new or revised Policy.

**10\. How to Contact Us**

Contact us at **[info@futuremedicine.com](mailto:info@futuremedicine.com).**
