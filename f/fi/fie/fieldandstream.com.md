# Field & Stream / OutdoorLife - Hunting

  
**Field & Stream and Outdoor Life Privacy Policy**

[](https://web.archive.org/olmagazine/toc.html) [](https://web.archive.org/fsmagazine/toc.html)

The site you are about to view follows the Field & Stream Privacy Policy. The Field & Stream Website is maintained by Times Mirror Interzines, 2 Park Avenue, New York, NY, 10016-5695. 

This site collects no personally identifying information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals. In addition, Field & Stream may place a "cookie" in the browser files of a Field & Stream user's computer. The cookie itself does not contain any personally identifying information although it will allow Field & Stream to relate a user's use of Field & Stream to information that the user has specifically and knowingly provided to Field & Stream. 

A user's personally identifying information may be used by Field & Stream for editorial purposes. Field & Stream may also use such information for marketing and promotional purposes and may share the information with companies that it has pre-screened. 

A user's personally identifying information will not be transferred to any other party unless otherwise stated at the time of collection. While Field & Stream will seek to require third parties with whom it has agreements to follow appropriate privacy policies, Field & Stream does not bear any responsibility for any actions or policies of such third parties. 

Field & Stream users should be aware that when they voluntarily disclose personal information (e.g., user name, e-mail address) on the bulletin boards that information can be collected and used by others and may result in unsolicited messages from other posters or parties. 

KIDS: Be sure to ask your parents for permission before you send any information about yourself to us or anyone else over the Internet. 

PARENTS: Any such information collected by Field & Stream from children is used for editorial participation and feedback purposes, together with any other purposes that will be described where the information is collected, and will not be transferred to any third party.
