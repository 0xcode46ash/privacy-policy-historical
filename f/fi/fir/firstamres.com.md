# First American Real Estate Solutions

**Privacy Policy for First American Websites**

**Privacy Statement**

This privacy statement discloses the privacy practices of First American Real Estate Solutions, L.P. (First American) operating through its websites.

**Information Collection and Use**

First American collects information at various points on this site, including personally identifiable information. First American may obtain personally identifiable information by companies that refer users to our web site. We may also require credit card account information to secure purchases made by users.

We collect this information in order to conduct business, improve our site’s performance and functionality, service and support our users, and notify users about changes in our business. 

If you choose not to provide us information, you may not be granted access to some or all of the pages on this web site.

**Registration**

In order to use this website you may be required to complete a registration form or contract and forward it to First American. You may be asked to complete this registration form on-line, or by fax or postal mail. Applications may be subject to a business credit evaluation. 

The registration form includes name and contact information, such as telephone number and e-mail address. We may use this information to contact you about your use of the site and any orders, submissions or inquiries you have made.

If we accept your registration you will be provided a user ID and password that will give you access to the services on our site. You are responsible for maintaining password secrecy. Do not share your password with any other person. Change your password regularly to minimize the possibility of unauthorized access to your account.

**Order Fulfillment**

Clients may choose to order products electronically from our site. Completed orders are accessible only to the client placing the order, and require a valid user ID and password.

**Cookies**

A cookie is a file stored on your hard drive containing information about you. We may use cookies to identify you and provide you a more customized browsing experience while you are on our site. For example, if you let us put a cookie on your hard drive, we may be able to give you access to pages without requiring you to log in. If you refuse to allow us to place a cookie on your hard drive, you may not be granted access to this site, or you may be restricted from some pages and services

Our site may contain links to other sites that may use cookie technology. We do not have access to or control over these cookies.

**Log Files**

We may track IP addresses and navigation patterns when we monitor site traffic. We use this information to improve the structure, content and general usability of our web site. Information from log files may be linked to personally identifiable information for billing purposes, or to provide service and support to our users. 

**Data Sharing**

We may share information about our clients with First American affiliates for the purposes of making available products and services we think may be of interest. We may share information about our clients with intermediaries who facilitate payment for products and services purchased on this web site.

**Links**

This web site contains links to other sites, but First American assumes no responsibility for the privacy practices of these sites. Please note than when you click on one of these links you are moving to a new web site. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of these sites as their privacy policies may differ from ours.

**Security**

First American takes reasonable precautions to protect users’ information, whether it is collected off-line or through the web site. 

All of our users’ information is maintained in a secure computer environment. Employees are granted access to this information only on a need-to-know basis to service users’ accounts. Access by employees is password protected, and employees are advised that the information to which they have access is sensitive and must be protected.
