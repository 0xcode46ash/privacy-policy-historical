**Privacy Policy**

Firstfoo.com takes privacy of an individual seriously. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.

Information we gather from our members are kept in confidence and are not shared with third parties. 

Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information through aggregating tracking information derived mainly by tallying page views throughout our sites. Such information enables us to better tailor our content to members' needs and to help our advertisers better understand the demographics of our audience.

This site contains links to other sites. FirstFoo.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

This site tracks user traffic patterns throughout all of our but this tracking is never associated with individual users.

This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided. To change any information previously provided, login with your registered username and password and change your personal profile.

Please contact us if you have any other questions regarding this site's usage.
