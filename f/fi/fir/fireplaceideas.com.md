# Privacy Policy - IdeaSmart

This Privacy Policy is between you and IdeaSmart.net (“IdeaSmart.net”, “we”, or “our”) and applies to your use of IdeaSmart.net’s website at www.ideasmart.net (the “Site”). 

## Our Commitment To Privacy

IdeaSmart.net understands that your privacy is important to you, and we are committed to protecting your privacy. To better protect your privacy, we provide this Privacy Policy to explain our online information practices for the Site. By using our Site, you consent to our collection and use of your data as described in this Privacy Policy. In addition, when you sign up for an account with IdeaSmart.net, we also collect personal information from you. We may combine the information you submit under your account with information we collect from your use of our services in order to provide you with a better experience and to improve the quality of our services. 

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our privacy practices evolve. Changes in our Privacy Policy will be posted on this page and supersede any prior posted Privacy Policy. You should periodically review this Privacy Policy from time to time for any changes. Unless stated otherwise, any changes to this Privacy Policy will take effect when they are posted. 

## Information We Collect

You are able to view and post responses to ideas without registering. In order to receive points and obtain access to more features of the Site, you will need to register by providing us with your email address. In order to improve our services we may also collect additional personally-identifiable information, including, but not limited to, first name, last name, and location. From all visitors to the Site, we may collect non-personally identifiable information, including IP address or mobile device ID numbers and browser type. 

## The Way We Use Information

We will not sell, exchange, transfer, or otherwise provide your personally identifiable information to any third party for marketing purposes without your consent, except as a result of a merger or acquisition. We may use your personal information and any non-personally identifiable information, however, in one or more of the following ways: 

  * To personalize your experience. 
  * To improve our Site. We continually strive to improve our Site offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you. 
  * To send periodic emails. The email address you provide may be used to send you information and updates pertaining to the Site, including occasional company news, updates, related product or service information, etc. 



## Children’s Privacy

IdeaSmart.net does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 and does not target its website to children under 13. We encourage parents and guardians to take an active role in their children’s online activities and interests. 

## Our Commitment to Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access and secure our data we have put in place reasonable physical, technical, and administrative procedures to safeguard and secure your personal information. We restrict access to personal information to those who need that information in order to provide services. 

## Third-Party Links

Occasionally, at our discretion, we may include or offer third-party products or services on our Site by providing links to third-party websites. These third-party websites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked websites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our Site and welcome any feedback about these websites. 

## How to Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy please email us at legal -at- ideasmart.net.
