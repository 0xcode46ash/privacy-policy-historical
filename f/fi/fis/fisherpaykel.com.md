# Fisher & Paykel Appliances

Fisher & Paykel Appliances is committed to protecting your privacy and to giving effect to the relevant privacy legislation. Most of our site can be accessed without the need to give us any information about yourself. The information below is designed to explain how we collect, use and protect any personal information you provide to us through the website.

**The information we collect**

Fisher & Paykel Appliances collect general and statistical information from your computer when you browse through our website. This information does not identify you personally but is simply used to enable us to evaluate the performance of the website and allow us to assess statistical information on the overall experience of our visitors within our websites.

At certain times we will request personal information from you in order for you to complete online registration forms, product or service information or when giving comments or feedback on the site. Our online forms will ask for limited personal information to ensure we are able to complete your request. Any information collected from these forms will only be used for the purposes specified. Fisher & Paykel Appliances does not sell or disclose your personal information to others

**The protection of your personal information**

Fisher & Paykel Appliances is committed to protecting your privacy. We use a variety of technologies, systems and procedures to help ensure your personal information is protected from unauthorised use. We store your personal information in computer servers in controlled facilities and allow only authorised employees to access this information. Although we make every effort to protect your personal information there is always some risk in transmitting information electronically. We do not ask for payment or credit card information through our websites.   


**Your acceptance**

By using our websites and submitting our online forms you are deemed to have accepted our privacy statement. Fisher & Paykel Appliances may make amendments to its privacy policy which from time to time. You have the right to correct any personal information about you which we may hold or to have this information removed from our records.
