# Find A Death: Celebrity Deaths - Privact Statement

| **NOTE:** Find A Death uses SSL encryption. We are a secure site: [https://findadeath.com](https://findadeath.com/)  
---  
Find A Death has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: [**findadeath.com**](https://findadeath.com.com/)  
   
  
Information Automatically Logged

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is also used to gather broad demographic information and for statistic purposes.  
  
   
  
Cookies 

Our Site uses cookies to make sure you don't see the same ad repeatedly. We also use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests.  
  
   
  
**Advertisers**

We use outside ad companies to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads and such are collected by our ad companies, and we do not have access to this information.   
  
   
  
Registration/Forms 

Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name and email address), and unique identifiers (like their IP address and domain name). Contact information from the registration forms are used to ship purchases, edit user information, and provide information about our company. The customer's contact information is also used to get in touch with the customer when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. 

Unique identifiers (like their username and password) are collected from Web site visitors to verify the user's identity, and for use as account registration in our record system.   
  
   
  
External Links 

This site contains links to other sites. findadeath.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.  
  
   
  
Security 

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. Only the administration is allowed to view the statistics (including IP addresses) coming from the Find A Death web site, using password protection and hidden files.
