# FIJI Water

Personal information such as name, address, email, telephone and especially credit card numbers that are given to us during ordering and all other contacts are not available to anyone except those directly involved in fulfilling your order and doing technical site maintenance. All information you send to us stays with us. FIJI Water has no third party agreements to share customer information.

When you place orders or access your account information, we offer the use of a secure server that encrypts all your personal information before it is sent to us to prevent unauthorized access to your information. We also offer the option of placing a credit card order over the phone.

FIJI Water uses a feature of your Internet browser called a "cookie" to assign a unique identification to your computer. Cookies are files that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive. These files allow you to easily access your customer data on our site. They also save you time by tracking your order information so that you do not have to re-enter this information upon every visit. FIJI Water does not and cannot use cookies to retrieve personal information about you from your computer.

We also do not use banner advertisements, which are often based on selling information about your purchasing history.

Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions about your privacy on this site.
