# Fileinz - The best way to share your files

This Fileinz Privacy&Policy describes the information we collect and how we process with it. 

  * Information we collect :  
We collect information from you when you register on our site, subscribe to our newsletter. When registering on our site, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your: name, e-mail address, mailing address, however you can also enter site anonymously.
  * IP Address :  
Our service log and use IP address for measuring usage only, we don't track or save it for recognize it compare with your other information.
  * Email Address :  
We collect the e-mail addresses of FilesIn Delivery recipients and senders for the purpose of logging and measuring usage. Fileinz does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with nonaffiliated companies.
  * Use of Cookies :  
Fileinz do use cookies to store the preferences of users.
  * Third Party Advertising :  
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, e-mail address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you.



Fileinz is commited to cooperate with any and all legal authorities if an investigation should arise.
