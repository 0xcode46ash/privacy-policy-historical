# The Film Society of The Film Society of Lincoln Center Privacy Policy

The Film Society of The Film Society of Lincoln Center
Privacy Policy
The Film Society of Lincoln Center, Inc. ("The Film Society of Lincoln Center")
knows that users of its web site care about the personal information they provide
to us. This privacy policy, which is part of our overall Terms and Conditions of
Use, is designed to provide you with information about how we collect, store and
use personally identifiable information obtained through our web site. We refer to
this information as "personal information." The Film Society of Lincoln Center
reserves the right to revise this privacy policy from time to time, and by using the
The Film Society of Lincoln Center web site, you are agreeing to the terms of the
then current privacy policy.
How Information Is Collected.
When you register at The Film Society of Lincoln Center to utilize certain of our
web site services, we will ask you for information such as your name, address, e-
mail address, telephone number, certain demographic information and (if
needed) credit card information. We need certain information in order to register
you as a unique user and let you access personalized services. However, other
requested information is optional and can be given at your discretion.
Like most web sites, The Film Society of Lincoln Center uses "cookies." A
"cookie" is a text file, invoked by standard browser software, that we place on
your hard drive to enable our systems to recognize your browser, track
transactions, and allow you to navigate our web site without having to re-enter
information. The information gathered by cookies is not made available to any
unaffiliated organizations. We may also automatically receive and store certain
other types of information, such as the type of web browser you use or the pages
you link to from the The Film Society of Lincoln Center web site.
Use of Information We Collect.
Personal information collected through this web site may be used by The Film
Society of Lincoln Center to communicate with you, register you as a user, allow
purchasing and shipping of tickets, subscriptions, memberships and/or
merchandise, enable you to participate in special promotions and to navigate
areas of our the web site without having to re-enter information. Such information
may also be used to administer our web site, in compliance with applicable law,
and as otherwise stated in this privacy policy. We may occasionally make some
personal information available to select other organizations, including but not
limited to, non-profit arts organizations, affiliated organizations, agents working
on our behalf, companies or organizations with whom we have a contractual or
sponsor relationship, or in connection with an asset transfer. Such information


would be limited to names, and email addresses. We may also provide
aggregated data to these and other organizations.
Certain of our programs use personal information to send updates and notices to
registered users via e-mail. If you provide your mailing address or telephone
number, The Film Society of Lincoln Center may also use your personal
information to contact you by mail or by telephone regarding special offers from
The Film Society of Lincoln Center, updated information about The Film Society
of Lincoln Center or the The Film Society of Lincoln Center web site, and new
services offered by The Film Society of Lincoln Center. If you wish to be removed
from this contact list, you may do so by contacting The Film Society of Lincoln
Center directly. (See the "Whom To Contact If You Have Questions" section
below).
Administration Of Our Web Site / Compliance With Law.
We may disclose your personal information when we have reason to believe that
disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact, or bring legal action
against someone who may be violating our Terms and Conditions of Use or to
protect the safety, property or other rights of our users or the public. In addition,
we may disclose or access account information for administrative and other
purposes that we believe are necessary to develop, maintain, and improve our
products and services. We also may disclose your personal information if we
believe in good faith that we are required to do so in order to comply with
applicable law, a subpoena, or other legal process.
You Can Review And Change Your Personal Information.
We encourage users to update and edit their personal information and keep it
current. You can easily correct or update your personal information via the web
site.
Security Used To Protect Your Information.
We store all user information in secure databases protected via a variety of
access controls, including password protection, electronic firewalls, and data
encryption.
Other Organizations Featured On Our Web Site.
We may provide links to other web sites, but The Film Society of Lincoln Center
is not responsible for the privacy policies, terms and conditions of use, or content
of any such web sites. We encourage you to familiarize yourself with the privacy
policies and terms and conditions of use of linked web sites. You may agree to
provide personal information to other companies who provide content for various


parts of our web site. If you agree to provide information to such companies, that
information will be subject to their privacy policies.
Use Of Our Web Site By Children.
We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under age
13. Any section of the web site directed at children under age 13 will not request
personal information.
Whom To Contact If You Have Questions.
If you have any questions, please contact ticketing@filmlinc.com.
