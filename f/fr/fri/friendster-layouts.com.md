# Privacy Policy | Friendster-Layouts.com

Friendster-Layouts.com does not distribute any of your personal information to third parties. Any information you provide us stays with us.

We may place cookies on your computer to improve your user experience and the functionality of this site. These cookies will not store personal information about you and will not be used in a malicious way.

Advertisements that appear on our web site may set and access cookies on your computer and is subject to the privacy policy of those parties providing the advertisement. However, the companies serving the advertising do not have access to any information stored with us.
