## Scope

This Privacy Notice applies to information, including personal information, collected about you by _Tale Chaser Publishing, Inc._.

## Information We Collect

_Tale Chaser Publishing, Inc._ collects information about you from a variety of sources, including:

Information we collect from you directly.

Information we collect about you when you visit _JackHumphrey.com_ , use or purchase our services, or view our online advertisements.

Information we collect about you from other sources, such as commercially available sources.

All the information we collect about you may be combined to help us customize our communications to you and to develop quality products and services.

## Information Uses / Sharing

Click here for more information [to Tier 2]

Tale Chaser Publishing, Inc. uses the information we collect to provide the products andservices you request, to tell you about other products and services we feel may be of value to you, and to manage our sites and services.

In general, we do not share your personal information with third-party marketers,unless we have asked for and obtained your express consent.

We may share your information:

With our third-party service suppliers, including those who work on behalf of _Tale Chaser Publishing, Inc._ and our business partners to send you joint communications that we hope you find of interest.

If _Tale Chaser Publishing, Inc._ is sold to another company.

To protect and defend the right and property of _Tale Chaser Publishing, Inc._ (including enforcing our Terms and Conditions).

When required by law, and/or public authorities.

## Your Choices

Click here for more information [to Tier 2]

You can choose not to provide information to _Tale Chaser Publishing, Inc._ or to limit the type of information provided.

You can choose to stop receiving communications from _Tale Chaser Publishing, Inc._.

You can choose to block third party advertising. This site protects user privacy through these written privacy policy terms, and so does not respond to Do Not Track signals.

You can view, correct or update the personal contact information you provide _Tale Chaser Publishing, Inc._.

You can request deletion of personal information provided by a minor to JackHumphrey.com without your permission or consent as parent or guardian.
