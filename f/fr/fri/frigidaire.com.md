# Frigidaire≈Ω Privacy Policy

You do not have to provide personal information to gain access to or browse our web site. Personal information may be collected in various forms including contest entry forms, product registraitons, purchasing extended service agreements, e-mail messages, and requests for information from Frigidaire. 

For your peace of mind, we do not sell or rent our customer list to another entity. However, Frigidaire or one of our affiliate companies may use personal information collected for internal marketing purposes such as market research. It may also be used to contact you about the latest product information or special promotions. 

If you have provided personal information, but do not wish to receive further information from Frigidaire, you can simply respond to our e-mail and tell us that you do not want to receive future communications. 

If you receive solicitations for products or services of no interest to you, promptly notify the solicitor that you do not want to receive any further unwanted solicitations. Remove your name from many national mailing and telephone solicitation lists by contacting the [Direct Marketing Association (DMA)](http://www.the-dma.org/consumers/offmailinglist.html) and asking them to add your name to their "Do Not Solicit" files, or by writing to Direct Marketing Association, Mail Preference Service, P. O. Box 9008, Farmington, NY 11735-9008.
