# Friskies.com

> **Friskies PetCare Company Privacy Policy**
> 
> It is our goal to respect your online privacy. We use tracking technology (cookies) only to gather information about the number of visitors to our Web site and to assist in tailoring our Web site to our visitors. Personal information cannot be collected via cookies. We only collect personally identifiable information, like names, addresses, e-mail addresses, etc., when voluntarily submitted by our online visitors. Information is not sold or transferred to third parties; it is used exclusively by Friskies and its parent company, Nestl√© USA. This information is kept secure and you may have it revised or deleted as directed at the point of collection. Friskies has no intention of collecting personal information from children under the age of 18. Any changes to this policy will be posted.
