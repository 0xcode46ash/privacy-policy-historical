# Franchise Nation Network - The Industry's Finest Franchise and Businesses for Sale!

  


##### PRIVACY POLICY 

Franchise Nation Network uses your information solely to serve you. We allow you to select whether or not you want to receive information to help you learn more about useful sales and marketing services. We occasionally provide notifications about special offers, or newsletters about events case studies, products and services, and success stories. 

### Information Collection

Franchise Nation Network is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. Franchise Nation Network collects information from our web visitors in email correspondence, by phone and in person. Your information such as name, address, phone number, and e-mail address are never sold, shared, or given to anyone not affiliated with and controlled by Franchise Nation Network. No one will ever contact you via phone, e-mail, mail, fax, etc. as a result of your registration with Franchise Nation Network or as the result of your providing us with your information. We do collect aggregate data that depicts trends, preferences, and testimonial results from customers. Aggregate information is used for sales and marketing strategies and never contains any particular or specific names, addresses, phone numbers, e-mail addresses, or any type of data associated with any person or business. 

### Confidentiality

Your information is a valuable asset to your business and protecting it is one of the core principles. We pledge to keep all of your information secure and confidential according to the procedures outlined in this privacy policy and to safeguard your data according to established industry security standards and procedures and we continually assess new technology for protecting your information. The information about your brand, marketing, advertising, discussions, plans, etc. are never sold, shared, or given to anyone not affiliated with and controlled by Franchise Nation Network. No one at Franchise Nation Network will give this information to anyone for any reason without your express written permission. When processing your orders, we may contract work with qualified professional suppliers, such as payment processors,printers, and other companies affiliated with Franchise Nation Network. These suppliers may see information about you and your company and distributors, but only in a production capacity. These companies are contractually bound by strict non-disclosure agreements. They are restricted to use your information only for the purpose of assisting us in processing your order. 

### Opt-In / Opt-Out

Franchise Nation Network may contact you via e-mail, snail mail or by phone. We may call you to verify your satisfaction with our service, discuss the details of a specific order, or notify you about new products or services. If you choose to receive these e-mails and calls, but wish to stop them at a later date, you can do so by changing your account profile online. For more information about how to receive or stop e-mails containing special offers, you may contact us via the contact page on this site or simply phone or fax us at the numbers provided. 

### Site Links

This web site contains links to other sites. They are provided for your convenience and general reference only. Be aware that Franchise Nation Network does not control nor is responsible in any way, directly or indirectly; for the practices, transactions, security, privacy and confidentiality of any reference link web site, company, employees, affiliates or vendors of Franchise Nation Network. Your use of this web site constitutes your full agreement to all terms, conditions, and notices contained herein. 

### Change Notifications

We attempt to update web content on a continuous basis including our privacy policies. Please refer to this page on a regular basis for any change in our policies.
