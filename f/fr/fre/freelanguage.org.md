# Privacy | Free Language

## Shhhhhhhh!

FreeLanguage.org will never share your information with any third parties, not even for big bucks! Cha ching.

In the event that we are required by law to disclose information about our users, we will contact you to let you know the specific details. No worries: we don't think this will happen.

You can feel safe, warm and fuzzy while browsing the site, [contacting us](https://web.archive.org/contact), [requesting a BETA user account](https://web.archive.org/request-a-beta-user-account) and [signing up for our mailing list](https://web.archive.org/lists) because we are never going to sell your information to any third party.
