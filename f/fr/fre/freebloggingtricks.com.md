# Privacy Policy

As professional group of bloggers at  **[FreeBloggingTricks.com](http://freebloggingtricks.com/)** we have not planed or intention for trying to capture or collect any of personal information of our visitors or readers and also we not using cookies to track user’s activity at this site. Our visitors have no reason to be concerned about the use of their personal information because we will never ask them for it at anytime.

### For RSS Feed and Email Subscription:

If a user wants or decide to subscribe us at through our RSS Feed or Email Subscription, we may ask them to provide some contact information such as Name and Email Address. Readers / Visitors / Subscribers may opt-out of these communications at any time. We fully confident to ask that user’s personal information will never be sold / advertised or given to a third party. (We will never spam our users ever)

### For Log Files and Stats:

Like mostly in some blogging platforms we use log files, in this case Awstats and StatPress. This can store information such as internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring, exit and visited pages, platform used, date or time stamp, to track user’s movement completely, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. These IP addresses and other information are not linked to personally identifiable information of the users.

### Cookies Usage:

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s computer to secure the information about user. [FreeBloggingTricks.com](http://freebloggingtricks.com/) does not use cookies. However, some of our business partners may use cookies on their sites (for example advertisers) just to protect them selves by some cheaters or scammers. We can not access or control these cookies once the advertisers have set them.

### External / Internal Links:

[**FreeBloggingTricks.com**](http://freebloggingtricks.com/) may have some links to other sites just for get linked to new users and to facilitate them. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites. We suggest users to be aware of this when they leave our site [FreeBloggingTricks.com](http://freebloggingtricks.com/) and to read the privacy statements of each and every site that collects personally identifiable information which may link to [FreeBloggingTricks.com](http://freebloggingtricks.com/). This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by us.

### Third Party OR Advertisers:

We can use some outside or third party advertising companies to display ads on [FreeBloggingTricks.com](http://freebloggingtricks.com/). These ads may contain cookies and are collected by the advertising companies, so it is clearly stated that we do not have access to this information. In our associated publisher networks you can find some known or some unknown advertising companies as like [Google Adsense](http://freebloggingtricks.com/category/blogging/google-adsense-blogging/ "Google Adsense"), Shareasale, Amazon, Buysellads, etc. Please check the websites of advertising companies for respective privacy policies.

Also Google as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on sites. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based sites on their visit to sites on the Internet. Users may opt-out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
