# FreeRice: Privacy

## Privacy

### Providing Visitors with Anonymous Access

You can access our Web site home page and browse our site without disclosing your personal data. You do not need to tell us who you are or reveal any personal information about yourself.

### The services and links of our Web site

Our Web site does not enable our visitors to communicate with other visitors or to post information to be accessed by others.

The ads appearing on this Web site are delivered to you by third party Web service providers such as LinkShare. In the course of delivering an ad to you they may collect certain non-personally identifiable information about you using cookies. If you would like to know more about the information gathering practices and "opt-out" procedures of our advertisers, please see their respective privacy policies.

### Automatic Collection of Information

We do not automatically log personal data nor do we link information automatically logged by other means with personal data about specific individuals.

We use temporary cookies to help track rice donations accurately. We do not use cookies to store personal data nor do we link non-personal information stored in cookies with personal data about specific individuals.

### Data Collection and Purpose Specification

We do not collect any personal data from our visitors when they use our services.

We do not collect information about our visitors from other sources, such as public records or bodies, or private organizations.

### Links to Other Sites

This Web site contains links to other sites that are not owned or controlled by us. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites.
