# FreedomWorks Privacy Policy -- FreedomWorks.org

**Policy Statement**  
FreedomWorks is dedicated to protecting consumer privacy on the Internet. We've developed our Privacy Practices from guidelines established by several Internet-based privacy organizations, including eTrust.com and Privacy.com. 

**Gathering and Usage of Information**  
Personal - FreedomWorks collects personal information from you on a voluntary basis and we don't require it to obtain access to our sites. Personal information may include name, mailing address, e-mail address, and phone number. 

**Opt-Out**  
You will only receive e-mail from us if you request to be added to our e-mail list. At any time, you may "opt-out" and remove your e-mail address from our files. 

**Cookies**  
FreedomWorks uses "cookie" technology to obtain non-personal information from our online visitors, such as browser/computer type, number of visitors, and site usage. We don't use cookies to extract personal information. 

**Links**  
Although our Web site and state sites contain links to other sites, FreedomWorks does not necessarily advocate, support or condone the privacy practices or content of these various Web sites. 

**Public Forums**  
Please remember that any information that you disclose in our chat rooms, forums, message boards, or new groups becomes public information. Exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. 

**Security**  
FreedomWorks has taken steps to make all information received from our online visitors as secure as possible against unauthorized access and use. All information is protected by our security measures, which are periodically reviewed. 

**Ongoing Privacy Efforts**  
FreedomWorks approaches our Internet communications with respect for and commitment to individual privacy rights and concerns. We support active self-regulation of this environment to ensure that responsible organizations maintain the right to use all communications media to interact with the public. As the Internet evolves, our policy will continue to develop. Please check back periodically to see changes and additions. 

**Contact Us**  
If you have any questions about our privacy practices or the FreedomWorks Web Site, please contact us by calling 202-783-3870 or by [email](http://www.freedomworks.org/contact/).
