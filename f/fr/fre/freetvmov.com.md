If you have any legal issues please contact the appropriate media file owners / host sites.

If a referenced movie is violating copyright and you want us to remove that content please us our contact us form.

**Collected Information**

Any information you provide for publication on the Site will be available to the public. It is your responsibility to exercise caution whenever you supply Personally Identifiable Information (PII) on the Site. Do not post your phone number and /or address within the information you post on the Site. Like most other Internet sites, we use cookies to personalize and enhance your experience on our sites. We will not gather any PII via the cookies. To remove cookies from your hard drives, simply follow the directions provided in the help file on your internet browser. You can still be able to access and use our sites even if you reject cookies. However, doing so may limit your ability to use some areas of our site.

You acknowledge and understand that the Google reCAPTCHA that installed at this site, works by collecting hardware and software information, such as device and application data and the results of integrity checks, and sending that data to Google for analysis.

**Links to Third Party Websites and Advertisements**

The [freetvmov.com](http://freetvmov.com/) site is linked to other website whose data policies and practices may be different from our own. Since we cannot control the information practices or policies of these third parties, it is your responsibility to check the privacy policy of any link you may decide to follow once you leave our site. Be sure to always check how third parties or advertisers will handle any information they may collect from your either directly or otherwise. Third party advertising companies may serve ads on the Site from time to time. Such companies may independently collect non-PII from you and use the same to provide advertisements about goods and services to you. [freetvmov.com](http://freetvmov.com/) does not control the collection and use of such information. You can, however, opt-out of this kind of advertising. Visit [networkadvertising.org](http://www.networkadvertising.org/) to find out how.

**Changes to this Privacy Policy**

We reserve the right to make changes to our privacy policy from time to time without prior notice. If and when we change our security policy, we will immediately post those changes to our homepage, this privacy document, or any other place we may deem appropriate.
