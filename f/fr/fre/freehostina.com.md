# FreeHostina - Free file hosting. UNLIMITED UPLOADS AND DOWNLOADS!

**Your Privacy**

The FreeHostina takes your personal privacy seriously. We will not disclose any personal information about you without your prior approval. In general, any information you provide to the FreeHostina team while contacting us will only be used within FreeHostina.

However, we may look through any private information about you that we possess and any files you uploaded to FreeHostina when we believe the information you posted or uploaded is illegal or does not comply with our 

[ _Terms of Use_](https://web.archive.org/tos.html)

 or Privacy Policy.

**Information You Volunteer**

FreeHostina is not responsible for the information you volunteer about yourself while uploading any file to FreeHostina or information that you choose to make public in your uploaded files. As a FreeHostina user you can view, correct or change your publicly available information while you are uploading your files.

**Users 16 and under**

If you are aged 16 or under, please get your parent/guardian's consent before you download or upload any files using the FreeHostina website.

**Contact Us**

If you have any questions on our Privacy Policy, you can contact us at 

[ _support@freehostina.com_](mailto:support@freehostina.com)
