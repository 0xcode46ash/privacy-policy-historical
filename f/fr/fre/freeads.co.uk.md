# Freeads.net - Classified ads, free ads

We will collect personal data as provided to us during the registration process. 

We will also collect personal data from other parts of the website service as required. We may hold personal data relating to the transactions the user enters into with Freeads or our suppliers. Please do not supply your personal data to us if you do not wish us to collect it. We may also ask for other data so that we can obtain a better understanding of our users' requirements and will share this information with selected partners and third parties. Cookie technology is used to monitor the users' use of Freeads which is used to help us provide a better service. We may use personal information about the user to build up a profile of the users' interests and preferences. By entering this site or responding to an advertisment the user will consent to Freeads processing personal data and is deemed to have accepted all of the terms above and acknowledge that we will be processing their personal data.
