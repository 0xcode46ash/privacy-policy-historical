# Privacy Policy - Promotional Products, Promotional Items, Corporate Gifts - Fresh Promotions Australia

**Fresh Promotions and Fresh Promotional Network Privacy Statement**

Fresh Promotions has created this privacy statement to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. This policy describes our practices regarding personal information collected through our website or through any other means.

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy from time to time; the effective date noted below will indicate the date the policy was last updated. Please visit our website periodically to review any changes.

**What Information do We Collect from You?**  
All information you provide will be kept confidential and is used to support our business relationship and the service we provide. This website uses an order inquiry form to allow you to request information, products, and services. We collect your contact information (such as your e-mail address or your company information) to allow us to send samples, orders, information about our products, and promotional materials. Your contact information is also used to get in touch with you when necessary. You may opt-out of receiving future communication. Just send an email to info@freshpromotions.com.au to make this request.

**Personal Information Disclosure**  
Fresh Promotions will not give or sell any information you provided to any third parties under any circumstances for its use in marketing or solicitation without your consent. The information protected includes any information about you, your order, and your purchase history. Your information may be shared with contractors of Fresh Promotions for the purpose of performing services for Fresh Promotions related only to your order or sample request. Fresh Promotions may release personal identifiable information when required to by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal process.

**Choice/Opt-Out**  
When you opt-in to receive communications from Fresh Promotions, you will receive information we believe will be of use and interest to you. For example:

We keep you informed about the latest deals, new service, sell-out specials, etc. that we think you will find valuable.

We may update you on any important changes to our website.
