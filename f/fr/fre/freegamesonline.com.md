 **Cookies**

A "cookie" is a small data text file that is placed in your browser and allows Freegamesonline.com to recognize you each time you visit this site(customisation etc). Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and Freegamesonline.com does not use cookies to collect personal information. Cookies may also be used by 3rd party content providers such as newsfeeds.
