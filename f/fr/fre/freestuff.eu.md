# Privacy Policy | Free Stuff

**Who is[Freestuff.eu](https://web.archive.org/index.php)?**

This Privacy Policy describes how FreeStuff.eu treats your personal data. FreeStuff.eu is a website operated by All Groove Limited. For the purposes of the Data Protection Act 1998 (the "Act"), the data processer is Lolagrove Limited and the data controller is All Groove Limited.

All Groove Limited deals with so-called opt-in marketing. By participating in activities via FreeStuff.eu you can win prizes. The rules for participation and prize draw can be found in our conditions of [prize policy](https://web.archive.org/prize-policy.php).

In this privacy policy a reference to "prize draws", "draws", "competitions" or "contests" will include a reference to any promotion where a user may or does qualify for a prize, gift, reward or other promotional item. A reference to a "prize" or "reward" will include a reference to any gift, reward or other promotional item earned. A reference to a "winner" will include a reference to a participant who has earned or qualified for a gift, reward or other promotional item.

* **Information provided by you through the Website**

When you participate in activities on the Website, you are asked to provide information about yourself. This includes your name, address, date of birth, gender, e-mail address and telephone number. We may also ask further questions regarding your marital status, education and for example about the price you want to receive, your interests, preferences and the receipt of products or information. Allgroove Limited and our [Sponsors](https://web.archive.org/sponsors.php) can also use your telephone number to send you a text message. These messages are for the verification of an order made.

  

* **Information provided by you via surveys**

Besides issuing competitions through FreeStuff.eu, AllGroove Limited or a client of AllGroove Limited can contact you through telephone, email or Internet for a survey. When you participate in such a survey, AllGroove Limited or the client which sends the survey shall process the personal data you provided in the survey. In such surveys you are asked to provide personal data such as your name, email address, address, postal code, telephone numbers, date of birth, gender, salary, education, and marital status, occupation, hobbies, interests, and (online) surfing habits.

  

* **Information provided by you to third parties**

AllGroove Limited can obtain information about you through third parties or 'list rental'. AllGroove Limited acquires for example the following personal data through third parties: name, email address, address, postal code, telephone numbers, date of birth, gender, salary, education and marital status, occupation, the industry sector in which you workin addition to information about your hobbies, interests and online habits. AllGroove Limited ensures that you have agreed that your data can be transferred to third parties such AllGroove Limited. If you do not want your data to be further processed by AllGroove Limited, you can send an e-mail to info@freestuff.eu

  

* **Information collected through cookies and third party pixels**

Besides the data which is collected by AllGroove Limited via FreeStuff.eu, via surveys and via third parties, AllGroove Limited uses cookies and other technology such as so-called third-party pixels to collect data about you.

  
A cookie is a small text file placed on your hard disk by the server of the Website. This allows the server to save your preferences and other data including passwords. Furthermore, Allgroove Limited uses cookies to track the visits to the Website and to track and aggregate statistical data regarding site visits to gather information, for example for reporting purposes for its internal organization or for third parties. You can decide whether to accept or refuse cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies. However, if you use your browser settings to block all cookies (including essential cookies) you may not be able to access all or parts of the Website.

Allgroove Limited uses the following cookies:

1\. Strictly necessary cookies. These are cookies that are required for the operation of the Website. They include, for example, cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of our Website, use a shopping cart or make use of other services.

2\. Analytical/performance cookies. They allow Allgroove Limited to recognizes and count the number of visitors and to see how visitors move around the Website when they are using it. This helps Allgroove Limited to improve the way the Website works, for example, by ensuring that users are finding what they are looking for easily.

3\. Functionality cookies. These are used to recognise you when you return to the Website. This enables Allgroove Limited to personalize content for you and remember your preferences (for example, your choice of language or region).

4\. Targeting cookies. These cookies record your visit to the Website, the pages you have visited and the links you have followed. Allgroove Limited will use this information to make the Website and the advertising displayed on it more relevant to your interests. Allgroove Limited may also share this information with third parties for this purpose.
