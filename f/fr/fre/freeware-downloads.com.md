## Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

As with most other websites, we collect and use the data contained in log files. For each visitor to our website, our web server automatically recognizes only the user's domain name, but not the e-mail address. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information such as the number of pages you view while at the website, downloads that have been made, and other general visiting patterns in order to ensure that our site is as useful and enjoyable as possible. We do not sell, disclose, lease, rent, or share any personal information, and we do not use cookies on this website.

If we are utilizing an Advertising Network that sets cookies, we would not receive or collect any of the information. Some outside parties (advertisers) may use cookies, web beacons to understand the way a user responds to the ads they deliver. You can chose to disable or selectively turn off our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings, or by managing preferences in programs such as Norton Internet Security. However, this can affect how you are able to interact with our site as well as other websites. This could include the inability to login to services or programs, such as logging into forums or accounts.

This site contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Websites.  
We reserve the right to change this policy statement at any time without prior notice.  
Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please [contact us.](https://web.archive.org/contact.php)
