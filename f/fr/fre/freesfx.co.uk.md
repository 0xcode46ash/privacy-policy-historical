# freeSFX - Privacy policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and to providing a secure and safe online environment. You can access, browse and download Sounds from our website without disclosing your personal data. We only request personal information for registration as a Content Publisher which is used solely for administration purposes and communication between ourselves and you. We will never trade, sell or distribute your personal details for any purpose or to any other party. Technical measures are in place to protect the personal information under our control against unauthorised access and modification. 

## Control of your Personal Information 

The information you provide to us for means of registration can be modified by yourself at anytime, with immediate effect. This includes passwords that were created for access to secure areas of our website. Such modifications can take place from within your members account area.

## Cookies 

Cookies are small, temporary text files that are downloaded to your computer's hard drive automatically by a website server and hold information about your online preferences. A website can only access the cookies it previously sent to your computer. Cookies are not harmful and cannot read data from your computer's hard drive. 

We use cookies only as a means of tracking user preferences and making your next visit more personal. They are not used for any other purpose. It is possible to turn off cookies in your internet browser's preferences and still use our website but it is recommended that you keep them enabled for a better online experience.

Freesfx.co.uk is not responsible in any way for the privacy policies or practices of any websites linking to or from this website. 

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us.
