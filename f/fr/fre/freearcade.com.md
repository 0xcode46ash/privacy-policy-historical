# FreeArcade-Privacy Statement

FreeArcade.com is published by Camadro, Inc.

We can be reached via e-mail at [**webmaster@FreeArcade.com**](mailto:webmaster@FreeArcade.com)

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes **no information** regarding the visitor's domain or e-mail address.

We collect aggregate information on what pages users **access or visit** and information **volunteered by the user** , such as specific requests by users to be added to our mailing list.

The information we collect is used for **internal review** and is then discarded. It is used to **improve the content** of our Web page, used to notify users about updates to our Web site **(but only if specificially requested to do so by the user)** and not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

FreeArcade.com works with a third party that serves ads to this site. To find out more about how Flycast manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to [Flycast privacy statement](http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html).
