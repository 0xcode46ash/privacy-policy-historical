# Privacy Policy - FreeTellaFriend.com

Privacy Policy  
 

We are serious about guarding and protecting the security of your personal information. We are not responsible or liable for the privacy issues of any outside websites we may be linking directly to, so we urge you to be careful when you enter any personal information outside our site. We welcome users of all ages to our content and services. If you are under the age of 12, we specifically request that you have the permission of your parents or legal guardian to view any content. We will not be held responsible for any information received or disclosed by our users and third parties. **

What data do we collect

**

  * We collect email addresses that were entered into mailing list forms. This data is collected with permission by the person submitting the email address. This data will be shared to the owner of the mailing list who placed the mailing list form on his/her website.
  * We collect a user's website, button clicks, friends told and buttons clicked for the purpose of giving statistic to the website owner.
  * We save all settings a user sets for his/her account.
  * We do not collect email addresses submitted by our users. This includes gathering e-mails from address books and tell a friend forms.
  * Like most web servers, our server automatically records activity on our site. Among other things, this allows us to find and fix web site problems. Your IP address is automatically logged by our servers for systems administration purposes. Your IP address does not hold any personal identifiable information.
  * We use cookies and we will attempt to limit the use of cookies as much as possible. We do not use cookies to see what other information is stored on your computer or attempt to access personal information about you.
  * We do not collect any data that is not relevant for us to improve our services. 
  * We do not collect any personal data of any user unless that user chooses to share such information with us such as their e-mail address.



 **What data is shared?**  
  
We do not collect any personal data. Our system mainly revolves around e-mail addresses. We do not share absolutely any data to any 3rd party companies. 

**Questions or Comments?**  
  
If you have any questions or comments please to [contact us](https://web.archive.org/contact_us.html).
