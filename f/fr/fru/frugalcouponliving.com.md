# Privacy Policy — Frugal Coupon Living

Your privacy is very important to Frugal Coupon Living. You may visit FCL’s private policy at any time via the bottom of FCL’s home page.

**COOKIES**

Frugal Coupon Living uses cookies to gather information on one’s user activity to measure the best advertisement effectiveness. FCL has no control or access to these cookies which are used by other third party advertisers. Third party advertisers on FCL include but are not limited to Glam, [Savings.com](http://www.savings.com/), Google Adsense and [Coupons.com](http://www.frugalcouponliving.com/Coupons1).  You may read more about [Google’s Privacy Policy ](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)and option out if you desire.

**PERSONAL INFORMATION**

Personal information gathered on Frugal Coupon Living is private and will not be given or sold to any third party disrupters.  Any information you voluntary provide (name, email address, mailing address, phone number) will be used for correspondence from FCL to you only.  In the circumstance of a giveaway, the information will be shared with the company supplying you with the winning item. When visiting FCL, an IP address is collected to gather use, times of access and dates. In addition, tracking of pages, downloads, etc will be noted so that Frugal Coupon Living can better serve you.  IP addresses do not share personal identifiable information. This information is used to for analytical purposes only.

**LINKS**

Frugal Coupon Living website does contain affiliate links. In the case of directing you to other sites, my opinion will be shared. This is not to used or substituted as your own. Links are for your reference. Once you are on a third parties site, you are responsible for the privacy policies of this website.

Frugal Coupon Living reserves the right of owner discretion to modify this privacy policy at any time.
