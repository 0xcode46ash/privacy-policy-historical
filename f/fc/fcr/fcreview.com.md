# Privacy policy - Flying Car Review

## Our Commitment To Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

## The Information We Collect

While you can access many features of the Flying Car Review site anonymously, you are required to provide personal information to request us to contact you.

## What we provide to third parties

Flying Car Review does not provide any of the information you provide to any third parties. The information you provide is used only to contact you, and then purged. No personal data is stored for any purpose.
