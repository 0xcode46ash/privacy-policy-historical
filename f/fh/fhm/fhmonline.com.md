# FHM Online - The Premiere Online Mens Magazine in the U.S.

FHM and FHMus.com are committed to protecting the privacy of personally identifiable information collected about you. We have established this Privacy Policy to help you understand how we intend to treat the information you provide to FHMus.com 

  We do not control the privacy policies of our advertisers or other sites to which we provide hyperlinks. You should also be aware that if you choose to voluntarily disclose personal information in chat, bulletin board or forum facilities, such personal information may be collected by others and may result in your receipt of unsolicited messages. 

  FHMus.com collects information in several ways. FHMus.comís registration and personalization forms may ask users to provide contact information such as their name, email address and zip code. We may ask users to voluntarily provide certain demographic information such as gender and lifestyle interests. We also track the Internet address of the domains from which users access FHMus.com. 

  We may use customer contact information from the registration form to send the user information about our company or promotional materials from some of our partners. FHMus.com may also contact you regarding your account status, to confirm registration and for other administrative purposes. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings. The email address you supply to us will not be used for any other purpose without your consent. 

  Demographic and interests profile data is used to tailor your experience at our site by showing you content that we think you may be interested in, and by displaying the content according to your preferences. 

  FHMus.com shares the information it gathers, in aggregate form, with advertisers and other partners. We do not release personal information about you as an individual to third parties without your consent. 

  Children under the age of thirteen should not submit personal information about themselves to this web site or to any of its associated services. www.FHMus.com is not directed to children under the age of thirteen nor do we knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of thirteen. If we learn that we have collected any such information without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information from our database as quickly as possible. 

  FHMus.com uses the services of Mediaplex.com for the serving and/or targeting of ads, promotions and other marketing messages. To do this, Mediaplex.com collects anonymous data through the use of cookies. To learn more about Mediaplex.com, including your ability to opt out of the Mediaplex.com system, go to: <http://www.mediaplex.com/mplx_privacy.shtml>.
