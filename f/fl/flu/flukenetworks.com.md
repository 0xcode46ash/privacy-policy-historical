# Privacy Policy

Fluke Networks’ Privacy Statement Overview  
  


**Scope**

This statement applies to the Fluke Networks and the <http://www.flukenetworks.com/> website.

**Personal Information**

  * We collect information you choose to submit during your registration.
  * We use common internet technologies such as cookies on our websites and emails. 
  * We sometimes obtain additional information about you, such as your demographic information, from other sources.



View our [detailed privacy policy ](http://www.flukenetworks.com/fnet/en-us/privacyPolicy/Privacy+Policy+Detail.htm)to learn more about Fluke Networks’ information collection practices.

**Uses**

  * We do not share, trade, or sell information about you with other marketers without your permission. 
  * If you request information about a product sold through distribution partners, we may pass your information to a member of our trusted distributor network in order to supply you with the products, offers and services for which you have expressed an interest.
  * We use the information you submit to provide you with the products and services you have expressed interest in.



Information you provide also helps us deliver content and information targeted to your interests over the web and if you choose to subscribe to our email subscription program.

**Your Choices**

  * You may unsubscribe at any time by clicking  on the Unsubscribe link in any communication sent to you.  You will then be unsubscribed from all future email communications in our program. 
  * You may access and edit your personal information you have submitted to Fluke Networks in your MyAccount/My Vision account.



**Important Information**

  * We take steps to protect the information you provide against unauthorized access and use.



**Email Privacy Policy**  

Fluke Networks offers email delivery of timely and relevant information about our products, promotions, events and technical resources to those who request it. We do not rent, sell or exchange email addresses. [View more details of this program and privacy measures](http://www.flukenetworks.com/fnet/en-us/privacyPolicy/Email+Privacy+Policy.htm). [Update your Email profile](http://www.flukenetworks.com/fnet/en-us/myAccount/EmailSubscriptions.htm).

**How to Contact Us**

Take a look at our detailed privacy policy to learn the ways and means we tailor your web experience while providing you with the utmost security and privacy at the same time.
