# Privacy on Flatmates.com.au

Information is collected for the purpose of providing a relevant and useful service. 

Collection of data is transparent and controlled by you. 

Privacy Guarantee:   


  * Your private details will never be provided to third parties.


  * You can review and edit all your information collected at any time at the [My Account ](https://web.archive.org/content.php/11.html)page. 
  * Some information is displayed publicaly when you choose to use the site. You will know which information is seen publicaly and which is not.
  * You can [unsubscribe](https://web.archive.org/content.php/12.html) from the entire site at any time.



Flatmates.com.au abides by the Privacy Act and Spam Act. Our privacy officer is Andrew Maloney.
