# FlamingText.com

### FlamingText.com: Privacy Policy

We will not sell or give away individual information to anyone. If you choose to give us your email address, we may use your email to distribute information about this site or other sites in the [bloke.com](http://www.bloke.com/) network. 

Aggregated information may be provided to advertisers. 

### Third Party Advertisers

This site uses the services of Engage and DoubleClick/Sonar for the serving of ads, promotions and other marketing messages when you visit this site. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other Web sites (not including your name, adress, email address or telephone number) in order to target these ads to those about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice, including your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please visit [www.sonar.com/naiprivacy](https://web.archive.org/cgi-bin/click.cgi?http://www.sonar.com/naiprivacy/) or [www.engage.com/privacy](https://web.archive.org/cgi-bin/click.cgi?http://www.engage.com/privacy). 

### Third Party Cookies

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertisers may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.
