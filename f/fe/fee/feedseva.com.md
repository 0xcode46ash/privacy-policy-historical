# Privacy Policy - Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia and the Eastern Shore

## 

##  Privacy Policy

The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia shares your concerns about privacy on 

the Internet. In order to better protect your privacy, The Foodbank of 

Southeastern Virginia has provided this notice explaining its online 

information practices and the choices you can make about the way your 

information is collected and used on the Site. Furthermore, this _Privacy_

_Policy_ , being part of the Terms, works in conjunction with the Terms and 

Conditions of Use, but does not modify or supercede it. On occasion, The 

Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia may run promotions or conduct other 

activities with affiliate partners from this Site. In such event, we may 

post additional privacy terms in conjunction with such promotion or 

activities that modify and/or supplement this _Privacy Policy_ and govern the 

use of information related to or received through such promotions or 

activities.

  
**The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia's Collection and Use of Your** **Information  
**    
In the course of using the Site, The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia may 

ask you for your name, e-mail address, postal address, telephone number, 

fax number as well as other relevant information. The Foodbank of 

Southeastern Virginia also may collect certain non-personally identifiable 

information when you visit many of our web pages such as the type of 

browser you are using (e.g., Netscape, Internet Explorer), the type of 

operating system you are using, (e.g., Windows 2000 or Mac OS), the domain 

name of your Internet service provider (e.g., America Online, Earthlink), 

aggregate data about the number of visits to the Site and/or aggregate data 

about the pages visited.

  
**How The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia Uses the Information** The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia uses the non-personally identifiable 

information ("Aggregate Data") to improve the design and content of the 

Site and to enable The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia to personalize 

your Internet experience. The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia may use the 

Aggregate Data to analyze Site usage as well as create services and/or 

products to fit your needs. Also, The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia 

shares this Aggregate Data with The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia's 

related entities, advertisers, partners and/or other third party vendors. 

The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia shall own such Aggregate Data and 

maintain copies of such as part of its records.

  
**_Except as otherwise indicated_ **, The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia uses 

the personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") you 

provide about yourself: (a) to respond to any of your inquiries or 

questions about our services; (b) for internal marketing purposes and as 

part of The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia's donor database; (c) to 

provide you additional and updated information, materials and other 

advertisements regarding the The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia 

services; (d) to offer you other materials and/or information that The 

Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia believes may be of interest to you; (e) 

to cooperate with government officials or parties in litigation under 

process of law, or as otherwise required by law; (f) to protect the 

security or integrity of the Site; (g) to protect against a threat of 

safety or destruction of property; and/or (h) to protect against legal 

liability.

  
**_In all cases_ **, however, The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia agents, 

employees and contractors who have access to Personal Information are 

required to protect this information in a manner that is consistent with 

this _Privacy Policy_. Furthermore, The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia 

encourages all its business Partners to adopt and adhere to standards of 

protection consistent with this _Privacy Policy_. The Foodbank of 

Southeastern Virginia will not use the Personal Information, except as set 

forth herein, without your permission; provided, however, The Foodbank of 

Southeastern Virginia shall own all such Personal Information and maintain 

copies of such as part of its records. If you do not wish to receive any 

such information or to allow The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia the 

right to use your personally identifiable information as described, please 

contact The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia in the manner identified in 

the "Opt-Out" section below. The Aggregate Data and Personal Information 

may be transferred, distributed or sold as part of The Foodbank of 

Southeastern Virginia or its assets.

  
**Opt Out** You may choose not to receive such information and/or to allow The Foodbank 

of Southeastern Virginia to use your information in the manner described 

above by simply checking the box provided on the contribution form itself, 

or by contacting us directly at [digitalmedia@foodbankonline.org](mailto:digitalmedia@foodbankonline.org). Upon receipt and 

processing of an "opt out" request, The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia 

will, within its commercially reasonable amount of time, remove your 

information from any future marketing communications.

  
**Security; No Liability  
**   
The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia has implemented security measures to 

help protect against the risk of loss, misuse and alteration of any 

information under The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia's control. The 

Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia protects your online order information by 

using Secure Sockets Layer ("SSL") technology. SSL encrypts your order 

information to avoid the decoding of that information by anyone other than 

The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia. Nevertheless, such security measures 

may not prevent all loss, misuse or alteration of information on the Site, 

and The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia is not responsible for any 

damages or liabilities relating to any such security failures. You 

understand that data and communications, including e-mail and other 

electronic communications, may be accessed by unauthorized third parties 

when communicated over the Internet. Furthermore, the Site contains links 

to other web sites. The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia is not 

responsible for nor can The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia ensure the 

privacy practices or content of such other sites.

  
**IP Addresses and Cookies  
**    
Like other web sites, The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia uses your IP 

address to help diagnose problems with The Foodbank of Southeastern 

Virginia's servers, to administer the Site and for security purposes.

 "Cookies" are pieces of data sent from a Web server to a Web browser that 

enable a Web server to identify users that visit a site hosted by that 

server. The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia may use Cookies in order to 

identify you to our site so that The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia can 

provide more customized information and services to you. In addition, our 

site may contain advertisements that may contain Cookies. In general, if 

you choose, you can disable the use of Cookies by reviewing your browser's 

preferences and options.

**A Special Note about Children**. 

 The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia does not knowingly gather any 

personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.  You 

must be 13 years of age or older to access the Site.  The Foodbank of 

Southeastern Virginia has no interest in collecting information from 

children under the age of 13.

  
**Modification of Privacy Policy  
**    
The Foodbank of Southeastern Virginia may change this Privacy Policy at any 

time, consistent with the terms set forth in the Terms and Conditions of 

Use , by posting the changed or amended Privacy Policy on the Site. Your 

continued viewing of or use of this Site following the posting of any 

changes to this Privacy Policy will signify your assent to any such changes 

or amendments.
