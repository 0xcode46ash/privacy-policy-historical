# FemaleNetwork.com | The Internet for Filipino Women | About

**OUR PRIVACY POLICY**

At FemaleNetwork, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect on the site to enhance your FemaleNetwork experience. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We share your personal information only to trustworthy sponsors if you indicate that you wish to receive information from them. We release demographics and aggregate statistics only to trustworthy third parties.

By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of this information by FemaleNetwork. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.

This document will shed light on all your questions about your activities on FemaleNetwork. Please read on for more details about our privacy policy.

FemaleNetwork welcomes your questions and comments about privacy. Please send e-mail to [info@femalenetwork.com](mailto:info@femalenetwork.com).
