# Fedena - Privacy Policy

We collect the following information automatically and it is only archived in system log files unless specified:

  * email address of people who communicate with us through email or register in our community
  * IP addresses and page views of all users of fedena, fedena community and fedena website visitors



And we will

  * never sell or disclose e-mail addresses to any third party
  * make every effort to maintain the security of all information stored within your Fedena account
  * comply with any legitimate requests from law enforcement agencies to disclose certain information to them to assist with their investigations
