# Fedena Pro Services - Privacy Policy

[Foradian](http://beta.foradian.org/) respects your preferences concerning the collection and use of your personal information. The following privacy policies is applicable for the usage of this website and its contents only. Please note that we have separate privacy policy for accessing our Fedena Pro Services, which is governed by specific agreements as applicable. 

We collect the following information automatically and it is only archived in system log files unless specified: 

  * email address of people who communicate with us through email or register in our community
  * IP addresses and page views of all visitors
  * Information about your use of the website gathered though cookies or other technologies



And we will

  * never sell or disclose e-mail addresses to any third party
  * make every effort to maintain the security of all information stored in our database
  * comply with any legitimate requests from law enforcement agencies to disclose certain information to them to assist with their investigations



If you have any questions about this policy, please first contact us at [info@foradian.com](mailto:info@foradian.com)
