# Fertility Friend On-Line Privacy Policy

**Privacy Statement**

Dawn Software is committed to respect your privacy. 

Dawn Software will not sell or transmit to others any personally identifiable data entered by Fertility Friend On-Line users. This personally identifiable data includes your name, e-mail address, charting data or any piece of information which could be associated with your name. 

Dawn Software will not collect name or e-mail address without your consent. Dawn Software will monitor your web site usage for administration and support purposes. 

Dawn Software strives to keep your personally identifiable information accurate and enable you, if necessary, to update or correct it. 

Dawn Software uses your personal information to bring you the technical and charting support that you expect. 

Dawn Software expects that you will keep your password secret to help us ensure protection of your private data. 

If you have any concern about our privacy policy, please make sure to send your comments/questions to [privacy@FertilityFriend.com](mailto:privacy@FertilityFriend.com)
