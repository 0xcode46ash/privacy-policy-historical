# Privacy Statement - Fertility Friend OnLine

**  
Privacy Statement**

Dawn Software is committed to respecting your privacy. Since we started offering Trying To Conceive tools, we have been extremely sensitive to the private nature of this type of information. An immediate consequence of this awareness is the total absence of advertising on our site. 

Dawn Software will not sell or transmit to others any personally identifiable data entered by Fertility Friend OnLine users. This personally identifiable data includes your name, e-mail address, charting data or any piece of information which could be associated with your name or email address. 

Dawn Software will not collect names or e-mail addresses without your consent. Dawn Software will monitor your web site usage only for administration and support purposes. 

Dawn Software strives to keep your personally identifiable information accurate and enable you, if necessary, to update or correct it. 

Dawn Software uses your personal information only to bring you the technical and charting support that you expect. 

Dawn Software uses data collected in an aggregate manner (no identification left) to improve its conception tools and provide its users with the most accurate calculations. 

Dawn Software expects that you will keep your login information (and particularly your password) secret to help us ensure protection of your private data. 

If you have any concerns about our privacy policy, please send your comments/questions using our [support form](https://web.archive.org/supportemail.html).
