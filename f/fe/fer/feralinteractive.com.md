# Privacy Policy | Feral Interactive

**Younger Feralites - Please check with your parents before entering any information on this or any other website. Ask them to read this privacy policy and discuss it with you.**

This is a general statement of principle, intended to give you a quick and clear understanding of our approach to your privacy. If you want more information on how we handle privacy in specific areas, we also provide detailed policies for:

  * Our [games](https://web.archive.org/en/about/privacy/games/).
  * Our [website](https://web.archive.org/en/about/privacy/web/).
  * Our use of [cookies](https://web.archive.org/en/about/privacy/cookies/).



Feral Interactive respects your privacy, and treats all communications from customers in confidence.

In some circumstances, such as when when you register a game, update your software or send a system report to us, we receive information about your computer. For the most part, this information cannot be used to identify you. However, it may include your e-mail address, which can be.

If identifying information is ever retained by Feral Interactive, we will not disclose it in any way to other individuals, companies or organisations, unless we are required to do so by law.

If you have a question or comment regarding any of our Privacy Policies, please [contact us](mailto:webzeit@feralinteractive.com) and we will do our best to address your concerns.
