# Fest300 - World's Best Festivals

You do not have to register to enjoy Fest300, but if you choose to [subscribe](https://web.archive.org/forms/newsletter), please know we will never provide personal information of any kind (name, email address, or other contact information) to any third party...ever. We do collect demographic information to help us create a better experience for you, and we do run surveys occasionally for this express purpose.

Pledge takers and subscribers should expect to hear from us from time to time via email to inform you about festival stories and reviews we've published. It's possible we might also email our community on behalf of strategic third party partners with information we consider relevant, but we will never share our email lists with third parties.

We comply with the [California Online Privacy Protection Act](http://oag.ca.gov/privacy/COPPA).

_A note to site visitors 13 or younger and parents or guardians:_

Fest300 is committed to complying fully with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 ([COPPA](http://www.coppa.org/))**. If you are a parent or guardian and would like to access, change or delete any personally identifiable information that we have collected from your child, or if you have any other questions about your child's use of this website, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/contact). Please include your child's name and e-mail address, as well as your own name and e-mail address, in any communication to assist us in responding to your inquiry or request.

###  Disclaimer

Information on Fest300 is intended to inform, educate, and encourage travel to festivals around the world. While we make every effort to publish accurate information, details can change without notice to us, so we recommend double checking important details like dates and contact information. We would be remiss if we didn't point out that while we want you to see the world, we also want you to do it safely. Remember, that you alone are responsible for your safety when traveling.
