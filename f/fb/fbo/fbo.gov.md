# privay Statement

****

**How we handle any information that we gather when you visit our web site**

We collect and store _only_ the following information about you: 

The date and time that you access our site; the IP address from which you are connecting; and the parts of our site that you access. We do not use cookies on our site. 

You identify yourself by sending an E-mail: 

When you send us personally-identifying information, i.e., in an electronic mail message containing a question or comment, or by filling out a form that E-mails us this information, we use this information to respond to your requests. We may forward your E-mail to other Government employees who are better able to answer your questions. 

**We want to make it clear that we will not obtain personally-identifying information about you when you visit our site, unless you choose to provide such information to us. Except for authorized law enforcement investigations, we do not share any information we receive with any outside parties.**

If you have any questions about these policies, please feel free to contact us at fbo.support@gsa.gov or toll-free at 877-472-3779.
