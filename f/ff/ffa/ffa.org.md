# ffa.org Privacy Policy

The National FFA Organization has created this privacy policy statement to demonstrate our organization’s commitment to your privacy. The type of information we receive about you depends entirely on how you use the site. Outlined below are the various methods of information collection and how this information is utilized within our organization.

**Information Collection**

Information collected by the National FFA Organization’s website is utilized solely for the purpose of conducting business and is never shared outside our organization, or with parts of the organization that utilize a different privacy policy. Personal information including name, address, e-mail, phone, etc., is provided on a voluntary basis only. We use this information to respond to submitted requests. Non-Identifying, Non-Aggregate information (IP address) is collected in order to measure site usage and make the site more useful and beneficial to our visitors.

National FFA Organization does not utilize ‘cookies’ to collect information given involuntarily by visitors to our site, nor does the organization sell, rent or otherwise transfer any information received.

National FFA Organization does not collect or maintain any information from individuals we actually know are under 13 in order to protect their privacy. Our website is of general content in nature and is not directed specifically to children in this age group.  We encourage all parents to assist their children with accessing information via the web. We continuously monitor our website for content that is inappropriate for children. 

Our site does contain links to other Internet websites and the National FFA Organization is not responsible for or privacy practices of any linked sites. A warning is issued when a visitor has accessed a link to a website outside the realm of the National FFA Organization website.

National FFA Organization reserves the right to change its privacy policy at any time in the future without prior notice.

Any questions regarding this statement should be directed to [webmaster@ffa.org](mailto:webmaster@ffa.org?SUBJECT=Privacy).

**Disclaimer**

All of the information on this site is presented in good faith and believed to be correct.  However, National FFA Organization makes no representations or warranties as to the completeness or accuracy of this information.
