# Royalty Free Stock Photos at Fotolia.com

Fotolia holds your privacy important to us. To demonstrate our dedication to privacy we have created this statement to outline our information collection and dissemination practices. To make this policy easy to find we have added a link to the homepage of the Fotolia.com website and in logical places where privacy policy information might be needed. 

Fotolia collects the following information: 

**User Information**

  * IP address



**Member Contact Information**

  * User Name and Password
  * Name (First Name and Last Name) and/or Organization Name
  * Address (Street Address, City, State, Zip Code)
  * Phone number (Optional)
  * Email Address



**Member Financial Information**

  * Paypal account (Email address only)
  * US Tax ID # (if applicable) (only for those who want to be paid)



**Use and Dissemination of Information**

The above information is collected using a variety of methods including web forms, and server logs. 

User information helps to provide diagnostic feedback to our servers. Member Contact Information helps us to facilitate members who request information, products, and services from Fotolia. Member Contact and Financial information collected is used to bill and send orders to customers, make payments to artists, and report 1099 information to the US government as required by law. Some financial information (credit card numbers and Paypal account information) may be collected by our US payment processors. Please see our payment processors privacy policy concerning collected information. At any time Members may request to opt out of receiving future mailings and even cancel their account by contacting Fotolia in writing. 

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. 

**How to Access or Correct Your Information**

You can access all your personally identifiable information that we collect online and maintain by logging into your account with a user name and password and clicking on member profile. We use this procedure to better safeguard your information. 

You can correct factual errors in your personally identifiable information by sending us a request that credibly shows error. 

To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections. 

**How to Contact Us**
