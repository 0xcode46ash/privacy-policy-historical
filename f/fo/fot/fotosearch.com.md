# Publitek, Inc. Privacy Policy

### Publitek, Inc. Privacy Policy

We collect certain information about you to enable us to do business with you, and to improve service to you on our website. We reserve the right to change our policy in the future, and the most current version will be posted at [www.fotosearch.com/privacypolicy/](https://web.archive.org/privacypolicy/)

 **What information we collect and why we collect it:**

  * Your name, phone number, address, and email address ("Personal Information") are collected when you register with us. Upon registering, you are given the opportunity to view unwatermarked comps and create lightboxes, which will allow you to gather images you like and organize them by category or client. By registering with us, you consent to the use of your Personal Information as indicated in this policy.
  * You may update your Personal Information at any time by going to "My Account".
  * In addition to your Personal Information, your credit card information is collected at the time of purchase, which is necessary to complete payment to us. Your credit card information is transmitted through a secure connection, and stored in a secure location. Only authorized personnel have access to your credit card information.
  * We collect information about the use of our website, including IP addresses, cookie identifiers, and website activity (“Website Data”). We analyze this data to determine what is of interest to our customers, and thereby improve our service, and for targeted advertising.
  * We keep a record of your orders, your lightboxes, and other miscellaneous information. It is not accessible to unauthorized personnel. 



**Emails from Publitek, Inc.:**

  * At the time of registration, we ask you for your email address so we can notify you of discounts and special offers. Some of these discounts can only be taken advantage of through the email you receive.
  * If at any time you find that you are receiving any email from us that you do not want, please send an email to [unsubscribe@fotosearch.com](mailto:unsubscribe@fotosearch.com) from the email address you wish to have removed, and you should no longer receive any emails of that type from us.



 **Sharing of Information:**

  * Publitek, Inc. may disclose the Personal Information described above to companies in our corporate group; service providers such as retailers, online and offline advertisers, and companies who help market our products; carefully selected business partners; and others, such as non-profit organizations, and third parties. We reserve the right to change this policy in the future.
  * The Website Data described above may be used by third parties to target advertising on other websites, and third parties may place cookies on users’ browsers for targeted advertising purposes as a result of visiting our website.
  * Publitek, Inc. may disclose that Licensee is a client of Publitek, Inc.’s and may use Licensee’s name and logo in doing so.
  * You may opt out of receiving targeted advertising by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out website at [www.networkadvertising.org/choices/](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/).



 **Security of our Data:**

  * When you submit an order to Publitek, Inc., you may do so over a secure connection. Additionally, we store your credit card information in a secure location, which can be accessed only by authorized personnel.
  * We take reasonable precautions to keep the information disclosed to us secure, however, we are not responsible for any breach of security or for any actions of any third party that received the information.
