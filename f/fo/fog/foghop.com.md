# Privacy Policy - Games at Foghop.com - Play Free Online Games

Foghop respects the privacy rights of its customers and protects the personal information collected, stored and used in connection with Foghop products, services and websites (the "Services"). To further this commitment, we have adopted this Privacy Policy to guide how we collect, store, and use the information you provide us.  


This Privacy Policy applies only to personal information submitted and collected through the Services. By installing, using, registering to or otherwise accessing the Services, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please do not install, use, register to or otherwise access the Services. Foghop reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at reasonable times, so please review it frequently. Your continued used of the Services will signify your acceptance to the changes to this Privacy Policy.  


To access the Services, Foghop may collect information about you, but only if you voluntarily provide it to us. The types of personal information collected in conjunction with the activities listed above will vary depending on the activity. The requested Personal Information may include, but are not limited to: (i) your name, (ii) mailing address, (iii) telephone number, (iv) email address, (v) photo or other image, (vi) credit card information, and (vii) location. Parents or legal guardians providing parental consent (via our Parental Consent Form) may be asked to provide their child's name and email address in addition to information about themselves. You may choose not to provide certain information, but in doing so you may not be able to take advantage of many of our features.

  
Foghop might use Cookies in order to improve the usage of the websites. Apart from this, the cookies are not used to store personal information. Foghop may use the cookies to display advertisements related to Foghop while you are surfing third party websites. Similarly we might be collecting your IP addresses for security and safety purposes.

  
Non-personal information such as log files that would report a website activity to record the hits would be collected by Foghop. In this, the type of device, type of browser, Operating System etc would be collected.

  
We provide the access to other websites from our website and we ask our user to read and understand the third party sites policies before providing any information to them. Foghop takes no responsibility in any form for the content presented on third party sites.

  
Foghop allows the usage of third party advertisements on Foghop.com. Every Advertiser is educated that no Ads that has sexual, drug, violent, offensive related material should be displayed. In case you come across any such material, notify us to foghop@gmail.com
