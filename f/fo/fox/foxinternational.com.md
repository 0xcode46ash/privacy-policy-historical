# Fox Home Entertainment Privacy Policy

Fox Home Entertainment recognizes the privacy concerns of visitors to its site on the Internet. Specifically, we recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of personally identifiable information collected about our visitors. As a general policy, no personally identifiable information, such as your name, address or email address, is automatically collected from visitors to our site. We may collect certain non-personal information used only to optimize our web pages for your computer (i.e., the identity of your Internet browser, the type of operating system you use, and the domain name of your Internet service provider). We may also use non-personal information to determine the total number of visitors to our website.

Personally-identifiable information about visitors to the Fox Home Entertainment site is collected only when knowingly and voluntarily submitted. In those instances when we do collect such information, the following applies: 

**Policies for Individuals Under the Age of 18**  
Fox Home Entertainment encourages parents and guardians to spend time online with their children and to participate in the interactive activities offered on this site. No information should be submitted to web sites by visitors under 18 years of age without the consent of their parent or guardian.

**Notice**  
We will notify you when we are collecting personally identifiable information about you, as well as the uses we intend to make of that information. For example, we may need to collect such information in connection with contest entry forms and registration for participation in online games and other promotions 

**Use and Security**  
Personally identifiable information that is submitted knowingly and voluntarily by visitors to our site is retained by Fox Home Entertainment, and is not sold or otherwise transferred. However, Fox may share personally identifiable information with other parts of our company. Fox Home Entertainment maintains appropriate safeguards to ensure the security, integrity and privacy of such information. In addition to using information about you to respond to your specific requests, we may use it in aggregate form to make our site better and more responsive to the needs of our visitors. This statistical compilation of non-identifiable information may also be used by Fox Home Entertainment or provided to others for marketing, advertising or research purposes. 

**Use of Cookies**  
Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to an individual's computer hard drive for record keeping purposes. Cookies make use of a website easier by saving passwords, purchases, and preferences while a user is at the site. 

Fox Home Entertainment does not use cookies to retrieve information from your computer other than the information that was originally sent in a cookie. We do not share information obtained through cookies with any third party or use such information for marketing or promotional purposes. 

Most browsers will automatically accept cookies. You can set your browser to reject cookies or to alert you when they are being sent. However, some parts of the site may not function properly if you do so. 

**Links to Other Sites**  
This site may be linked to other parts of our company, or to web sites operated by others. Fox Home Entertainment is not responsible for the content or practices of third party websites that may be linked to this site. 

**Privacy Policy Changes**  
Fox Home Entertainment reviews its privacy policy periodically, and it may be subject to change.
