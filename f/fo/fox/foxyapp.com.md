Furthermore, we appreciate your choice to share certain information with us as it enables us to improve our products and services. We understand our role in safeguarding your data, and take this role seriously. As such, we follow two relentless principles. First, user data collection should be limited to the extent necessary to enable the proper functioning of a given product or service. Second, user data collection, sharing, and use should be transparent.

Our data collection, sharing, and use policies, as set forth below, were crafted in furtherance of the two principles enumerated above. Should you have any questions or concerns about any privacy-related issues, or the subject matter of the Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to get in touch with us at the following Email address: Info@Foxyapp.com

You may opt-out of, and stop, all information collection by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.

We will retain User Provided Information for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time after. **IX. Children.**

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of thirteen (13). No children under the age of thirteen (13) may use the Application unless such use is with informed parental consent. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has used the Application, or provided us with information, without parental consent, he or she should contact us as soon as possible at Info@foxyapp.com. We will delete such information from our files within a reasonable time.

**X. Security.**

We are concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. We provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect information we process and maintain. For example, we limit access to this information to authorized employees and contractors who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve our Application. Furthermore, we encrypt your information whenever needed to prevent unauthorized disclosure. Where certain identifying information is not necessary, we de-identify your information prior to disclosure. Please be aware that, although we endeavor to provide reasonable security for information we process and maintain, no security system can prevent all potential security breaches.
