**FoxTrot.com Online Privacy Policy** FoxTrot.com and _uclick_ LLC (" _uclick_ ") are committed to respecting the privacy rights of all visitors to our web site. By using this site, you signify your assent to the _uclick_ LLC Online Privacy Policy described below. 

**

Collection of Information

**__

_uclick_ does not require visitors to reveal any personally identifying information in order to access FoxTrot.com.

Although very limited information may be required to participate in certain promotions, technical support, sales, e-cards, chat rooms or message boards offered through our web site, participants are requested to provide information on a voluntary basis only. No information will be shared with outside parties. 

**

Children’s Policy

**

We encourage parents and guardians to take an active role to protect the privacy of their children and to prevent inappropriate use of information about their children. _uclick_ will not collect any information through our web site from persons 12 and under without parental/guardian permission. **If you are 12 or under, you must have your parent or guardian's permission to provide any information to _uclick_**. _uclick_ will remove any information provided by children from the system upon the request of the child or the parent or guardian. [Click here to view the form, print it and mail it to _uclick._](https://web.archive.org/privacy/parents.html)

**

Cookies

**

Cookies are small pieces of electronic information which a web site can store on a visitor's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Cookies allow web site operators to "personalize" the site to match your individual preferences. The use of cookies is standard on the Internet and many major web sites use them. You can choose to set your browser to notify you whenever you are sent a cookie. This gives you the option to decide whether or not to accept a cookie. If _uclick_ uses cookies, it will be to help provide you with a better experience on our site. Cookies are never required to use our site. And we never store personally identifiable information in a cookie. Sites linked to the FoxTrot.com web site may also send cookies to users. _uclick_ does not control such activities.

**

Links

**__

_uclick_ may create links to the web sites of other parties, including its promotional partners and affiliate partners. We make every effort to link only to sites that meet similar standards for maintaining each individual's right to privacy. Children 12 and under, however, should get parental permission before linking to other sites. Sites that are not associated or authorized by _uclick_ may also have links leading to <www.foxtrot.com>. _uclick_ cannot control any of these links and is not responsible for any content appearing on sites linked to or from _uclick's_ site, or for any personal information gathered at those sites.

**

Data Integrity and Security

**

The importance of security for all personally identifiable information associated with our visitors is of the utmost concern to us. We have and will continue to take reasonable measures to assure the accuracy of all information our visitors share with us, as well as any information collected from other sources. This includes procedures to avoid any unauthorized access, alteration, misuse or disclosure of that information.

You always have the right to unsubscribe from our mailing list, or delete personally identifiable information we have collected about you or your children. _uclick_ also must reserve the right to delete information or to change its privacy policy, at any time.
