# Privacy | Food4Rhino | Rhino

**1\. Privacy Policy**

Your personal information is treated by  **food4Rhino**  ([McNeel Europe](http://www.en.emea.mcneel.com/ "McNeel Europe")) with the maximum confidentiality and with the sole purpose to improve our products and services according to your needs. It is just much easier to help if we know what is important to you and have a way to contact you.

*** What we do with your information**

1\. Keep it confidential. We share your information only  with the developers of any Rhino plug-ins, Grasshopper add-ons or textures you might have downloaded from food4Rhino website, as agreed when accepting  the [terms and conditions of use](http://www.food4rhino.com/terms-and-conditions-of-use).  
2\. Improve this web site so that it better fits your needs.  
3\. Improve our products so that they better fit your needs.  
4\. Build an electronic newsletter subscription list so that we can notify subscribers of update information on our products, services and support.

*** What we do NOT do with your information**

1\. Sell or provide your information to anyone outside this organization, with the exception of the developers of any Rhino plug-ins, Grasshopper add-ons or textures you might have downloaded from food4Rhino website, as agreed when accepting  the [terms and conditions of use](http://www.food4rhino.com/terms-and-conditions-of-use).  
2\. Provide your information to our resellers, distributors, or OEMs.

*** Updating your information**

To change your e-mail address, just [edit your account](http://www.food4rhino.com/user/login).

*** Deactivate account**

To deactivate your account, [contact us](mailto:food4rhino@mcneel.com) with the subject “Deactivate account”.

**2\. Cookies**

This site uses first-party and third-party cookies to provide users with a better experience and service.

First-party cookies are used for three different goals:

  * To maintain the user session if the user is identified to make use of the services provided.
  * To detect functional and technical capabilities of the user navigation and, specifically, to detect if JavaScript is enabled.
  * To detect if the user has accepted this terms of use statement relating to cookies.



In the two first cases, cookies are deleted when logging off or closing the browser.  
In the last case, cookies will expire after a year of their creation.

Third-party cookies analyze the user experience and collect statistical data anonymously.

These cookies are managed by the Google Analytics service.

This service is managed by Google, which provides treatment services statistical data anonymously, and therefore it does not collect personal information.  
This company may use the data to improve their own services, either in their own products or in third-party products.  
You can check all the information concerning this application and its data protection policy at Google Analytics Privacy statement.  
This anonymous information helps us improve our services based on the visitors experience.

By browsing or using our services you agree to our use of cookies.

However, at any time, the user can prevent the generation of cookies and delete them by selecting the appropriate option in the browser:

  * [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en)
  * [Firefox](http://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
  * [Safari](http://support.apple.com/kb/ph5042)
  * [Internet Explorer](http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/internet-explorer/delete-manage-cookies)
  * [Opera](http://help.opera.com/Windows/12.10/en/cookies.html)



If cookies are deleted, it means that the user does not want to use this website. Any subsequent visit will imply the acceptance of the terms described above.
