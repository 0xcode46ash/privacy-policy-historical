# Football Tactics and Lineups History

  
  


### Privacy Statement

    We at Football-LineUps.com, want everyone's visit here to be enjoyable and safe, Football-LineUps.com respects your privacy and is committed to protect the personal information that you share with us. 
    If we make any changes to this Policy, they will be posted on this page and will be effective when posted. 
    For each visitor to our website, our server automatically recognizes the visitor's IP address only - not the e-mail address. 
    In spite of Football-lineups.com being a non-commercial site, we use second and third party advertisers for all of the advertising that appears on our site to help us maintain our work online. 
    These advertisers, as far as we know, do NOT automatically collect any personal information from you... Any information collected is anonymous -- like what country you are from, what state/province you are from, etc. 
    You can browse through our website without giving us any information about yourself. When we do need your personal information is when you choose to make changes to the web site database. 
    This privacy statement applies solely to personal information collected on this Web site.
