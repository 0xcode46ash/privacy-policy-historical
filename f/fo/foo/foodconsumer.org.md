# foodconsumer.org - Privacy

Your privacy is highly regarded here at foodconsumer.org.  We do not collect any personal information such as your name, address, email address or telephone number.  Rest assured that you are free to use the website anonymously.

However, advertising companies such as Google may use cookies to serve ads when you visit this website. Advertising companies may use certain non-personal information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).  You may also opt out of an ad-serving program [here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp). Please note that opt-out of an ad-serving program does not eliminate ads from the websites you visit. Instead, the ads served may be less interesting to you.

If you subscribe to our [newsletter](http://www.foodconsumer.org/newsite/newsletter.html), you can be assured that we will not sell or give your personal information such as your email address to any organization. 

Subscribers can unsubscribe at any time.
