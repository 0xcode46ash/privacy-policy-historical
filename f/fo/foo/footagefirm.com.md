**Footage Firm Privacy Policy**  
  
---  
  
FootageFirm.com is a safe and secure website, and your personal information will never be sold, shared or distributed to a third party, or in any way used for unsolicited marketing purposes, without your permission.

Your information will only be used by Footage Firm to announce service and product updates. If you do not wish to receive any communication from us, you may unsubscribe from updates at any time.

Footage Firm uses a secure checkout system provided by transaction processing company 2checkout.com. Your credit card information is never saved or stored. Once your order is processed all billing information is erased immediately.

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20090616182448id_/http%3A//www.footagefirm.com/contact.htm).
