# FOREX.COM - []

**FOREX.COM PRIVACY POLICY**

1\. FOREX.COM is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. If you register your interest with FOREX.COM about any of our services, FOREX.COM will need to obtain some personal information about you, which may be stored on FOREX.COM's computer. FOREX.COM will typically need to know your name, address, e-mail address, telephone number, services sought and so on. Additionally, any CV or resume that you mail to FOREX.COM will be filed and stored on the FOREX.COM computer system. This information allows FOREX.COM to provide you with the services provided by us as shown on http://www.forex.com.

2\. In addition to the above, the personal information which you provide us with, together with information on services that you have accessed via us in the past may be used by FOREX.COM, for the purpose of notifying you about any of its services. If you would rather not receive this wholesome material, please indicate this in your first email to us. Otherwise, by submitting your information you consent to its use in this way. 

3\. If you would like FOREX.COM to delete personal information relating to you or any CV or resume, or any other information about you, that you have sent us please send an email to support@forex.com with specific details. FOREX.COM reserves the right not to delete such personal information if there is any legitimate reason why such information ought to be retained by FOREX.COM. 

4\. Save as otherwise provided in this privacy statement or save as otherwise required by law, FOREX.COM does not disclose personal information it holds about you to any third party for marketing purposes or for any other reason other than that described at http://www.forex.com or by implication in the information you send us. In the event that FOREX.COM agrees to sell or otherwise transfer all or any part of its business to a third party, FOREX.COM may transfer any personal information that it holds about you to that third party.

5\. If FOREX.COM changes its privacy policy, FOREX.COM will publish the altered policy on this page so you may be aware of such changes and you will be given a chance subsequently to ask FOREX.COM not to use your information.

6\. Because the Internet infrastructure is global, and it is not possible to predict the routes that information sent over the Internet will take, the information you provide may be transferred temporarily via a route which takes it outside the European Economic Area as it passes between you and us. By submitting your information you consent to this transfer as well as allowing FOREX.COM to provide appropriate information about yourself to our partners whether they are operating within the EEA or not.
