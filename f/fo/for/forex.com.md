# Forex.com - Your Gateway to the Forex Market - Privacy Policy

At Forex.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We will not sell or share your personal information with third parties, regardless of whether you are a client, a user of our demo software, or just a visitor to our site. 

Whenever we collect personal information from you, we will always explain to you how we intend to use that information. For example, we will use the information you give us to process your requests and transactions, or to provide you with information about relevant products and services. 

We emphasize to our employees the confidential nature of user information and the high level of importance we place on maintaining this confidentiality. Furthermore, any companies we hire to provide support services must comply with our privacy policies. 

We do reserve the right to disclose personal information in specific circumstances, such as to fulfill our legal and regulatory requirements, to collect or report debts owed to us, or to protect our rights or property. 

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of any other web site, including sites to which we provide a link for informational purposes. 

If you wish to correct any inaccurate or outdated personal information, or if you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, email us at [info@forex.com](mailto:info@forex.com).
