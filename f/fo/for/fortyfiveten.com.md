# Forty Five Ten | Privacy Policy

**PRIVACY POLICY**

Forty Five Ten Retail LLC (“Company”, “we”, “us” or “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website located at fortyfiveten.com (the “Site”). This Privacy Policy (this “Policy”) explains how we may collect, use, and share information gathered from you as you visit the Site, and is a binding agreement between you and the Company. If you do not agree to the terms of this Policy, you must immediately cease your use of the Site. We may update this Policy from time to time, and your use of the Site constitutes your express acceptance to the terms of the then-posted version of this Policy.

**Information Collection**

“Personal Information” means any information that can be used to identify you individually. We collect Personal Information you voluntarily provide us, for example by signing up to receive our newsletters. The Personal Information we collect on the Site may include, without limitation, your: name, mailing address, and email address. You may refuse to provide Personal Information, but some parts of the Site (for example, the newsletter signup page) may not be fully available or functional without submitting such information.

We do not knowingly collect Personal Information from children under 13 on the Site. If you are younger than age 18, you may use the Site only with the permission and involvement of your parent or guardian. If we discover that we have collected Personal Information from children under 13, we will take commercially reasonable measures to promptly delete such information from our systems.

In addition to Personal Information, our servers automatically collect certain anonymous, technical information that does not identify you personally (“Anonymous Data”). Anonymous Data we collect includes, without limitation, information regarding your IP address, device type, operating system, browser, and other Site usage details such as page views, clicks, and duration of page visits. The Site may also include tracking technologies such as “cookies” that automatically helps us provide a tailored experience when you visit the Site. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the Site may not be fully available or functional without cookies.

**Use of Information**

We may use your Personal Information for the purpose for which you submitted it (for example, to sign you up for our newsletter, or to respond to your inquiries). We may also use Personal Information to protect the security of the Site and its systems, to investigate and respond to any suspected breaches of the Site or its systems, and to cooperate as we deem reasonable with legal requests and proceedings. If at any time you wish to opt-out of future newsletters or other promotional communications, you may click “unsubscribe” on the email or otherwise contact us at orders@fortyfiveten.com, and we will use commercially reasonable efforts to promptly implement your request.

We may use Anonymous Data for any purpose we deem appropriate, including without limitation to maintain and improve the Site and our current and future products and services.

**Sharing of Information**

We may share Personal Information with our affiliates. We may also share Personal Information with third parties: (i) in connection with a court order, subpoena, government investigation, or when otherwise required by law; (ii) in the event of a corporate sale, merger, acquisition, or similar event; or (iii) in connection with their services regarding the operation, maintenance, or improvement of the Site on our behalf. We will not sell or share any Personal Information to third parties except as set forth in this Policy. We may share Anonymous Data to third parties for any purpose we deem appropriate.

**Third Party Sites**

The Site contains links to third party websites. We cannot control, and take no responsibility for, the content or privacy practices of such third party websites. Please carefully review the privacy policy and any other applicable terms for such third party websites.

**Contact**

If you have any further questions concerning this Policy, or would like to correct, update, or delete such information, please contact orders@fortyfiveten.com. ****
