# Forex Lovers - Forex Trading Strategy

We will not keep, nor disclose, your personal information of any kind to any other company or organization.

Our members' data are securely protected using the best technology the Internet has to offer. 

We currently use PayPal to process payments. PayPal is the most popular and secure payment processor in the world. For more information on PayPal, please visit their website at [**www.PayPal.com**](http://www.paypal.com/).

**Advertisements provided by Google**

ForexLovers.com uses Google as a third-party vendor to serve ads when you visit this web site. Google may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by Google, click [here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
