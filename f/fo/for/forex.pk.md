# Privacy Statement of Forex.pk - A Foreign Exchange Trading Portal of Pakistan

We respect and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy lets you know how your personal information is processed and used at **FOREX.pk**. We take extraordinary steps to use your personal information only in ways that are compatible with this Privacy Policy.

  
Information Collection  
**FOREX.pk** and it's partner are the sole owner of the information collected from our website. We do not, or will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in our privacy statement below.

  
Log Files  
**FOREX.pk** keeps log files on broad fields to analyze trends, track user movements, and gather data for demographic locations. This is not attached in anyway to visitor's personal identification.

  
Sharing  
At this time, **FOREX.pk** does not share any information with advertisers, merchants, or any other third party.

  
Links  
Links to other sites outside of **FOREX.pk** do not apply to this privacy statement. We are not responsible for privacy policies of other websites linked to **FOREX.pk**. Please read the privacy statement of each website linked to **FOREX.pk** for information and data use for visitor's personal identification. Our privacy statement applies solely to **FOREX.pk**.
