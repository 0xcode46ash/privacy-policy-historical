# Privacy Policy - The Form Assembly

## Privacy Policy

#### Collection of User Information

The Form Assembly only collect information for the purpose of rendering the services advertised. When registering on the Form Assembly, you need to provide a username and a password. Providing an email address is _optional_. If you choose to disclose it, we may use it to contact you or send you information about our services. 

The Form Assembly uses cookies and logs activity as part of its normal operations. 

**We do not sell or share any collected information with third parties.**

#### No Spam Policy

We don't spam and we don't allow our customers to spam either. Our [terms of service](https://web.archive.org/terms-of-service.php) prohibits all users from engaging in any activity involving spam. Violations of this policy may result in immediate termination of service and legal action.
