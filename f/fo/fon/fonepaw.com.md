# Our Privacy Policy - FonePaw Software

Information Collected 

In some cases, FonePaw may require you to provide personal information, such as your name, address, E-mail, etc. We won't share your personal E-mail, Contact Number, and Name with any third party. 

About Cookies 

As using cookies, FonePaw can provide you with simple and personalized experience of internet. Cookies are small data files that FonePaw can recognize you when you come back to FonePaw and offer valuable and customized service for you.  
We gather the technologies and information without any personal details then automatically save them in log files, which include Internet Service Provider (ISP), Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type and so forth. Customers are allowed to delete related cookies optionally. However, before that, you should finish the order information when you back to FonePaw website again. FonePaw will not disclose your individual information to any website or make any profit. 

Security Procedure 

We try to implement safety measures through various practical industrial standard for preventing the loss, misuse, manipulation and so forth of significant information we have.  
Please remember that all of the information you transferred or disclosed may be used and collected by others, or be illegal intercepted by third party. Any of data transmission over the internet can't be absolutely guaranteed. But we still try our best to protect any transferring data of you. 

Data Analytics 

FonePaw will use integrated report of a third party and analysis tool to know the behavior of users and improve customer experience on the website. Once you using the contents of website, that means you content to transmit such information to FonePaw. But we promise that all of your personal information will not be disclosed or sold to any third party and organization. 

Your Rights: 

You are supported to contact us anytime:  
To verify whether we grasp your personal information, or access to such privacy information.  
To require us to modify any inaccurate personal information.  
To inquire for some policies and terms related to personal identity or your personal information that you submit.  
( Declaration: If there is any material alteration about this policy, we will also post here, so the users will always know what kind of personal information we collect, how to use your personal details and in what circumstances may disclose such information. In view of this policy may change every now and then, so please consult the policy regularly at FonePaw, especially before you provide any supplementary personal information. If you still go to use the website of FonePaw after releasing the changed privacy policy, you have accepted such changes acquiescently. ) 

Modifications 

FonePaw reserves the right to change the Privacy Policy from time to time and will provide notice of material changes on our website. Your continued use of the Site, or any materials or services accessible through it, after such notice has been posted constitutes your acceptance of the changes.
