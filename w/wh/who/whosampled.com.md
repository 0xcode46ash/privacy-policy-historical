# WhoSampled - Privacy Policy

WhoSampled.com has developed this Privacy Policy in order to demonstrate its firm commitment to the privacy of your data. We strongly believe that your personal data should stay private.

WhoSampled.com is committed to safeguarding the privacy of your data whilst providing you with a personalised, fun, and valuable service. This Privacy Policy explains when and how personal information is collected, used, disclosed and protected with respect to WhoSampled.com. By using WhoSampled.com, you consent to the privacy practices described in this Policy, as modified from time to time by us.

## What this privacy statement covers:

We collect personal information that identifies you when you sign up with WhoSampled.com and each time you use the service. We will keep this information for a reasonable period or for as long as the law requires.

WhoSampled.com is not directed to children under 13 years of age. Users who are under 18 confirm that they have their parents or guardian's permission to use our website.

If these terms are unacceptable to you for whatever reason, you should discontinue use of the website immediately. By using the website, you implicitly agree to our policies and terms. These policies and terms may change or be updated from time to time and it is your responsibility to be aware of any changes. The latest version will always be on our website.

## Personal information we collect to set up your WhoSampled.com account:

WhoSampled.com will collect personal and other details from you when you register as a user. These details will include, but may not be limited to:  
\- Name  
\- E-mail address  
\- Background information

By registering with WhoSampled.com, you consent to use of this information by WhoSampled.com. You also consent to the sharing of this information and any other information you may have voluntarily posted with other users when appropriate.

## Additional information we collect automatically when you use WhoSampled.com:

When you use WhoSampled.com we automatically receive and keep information about how you use the software and service. This includes your account history and preferences.

We use this information to ensure service quality, to develop and tell you about new features of the software and service, and to customize the content you see.

## How we share your personal information with third parties:

WhoSampled.com will not share, trade or sell your information with other companies or parties not associated with its proper use within the website.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

We may disclose your personal information to third parties if required to do so to comply with legal process or requirements, or to protect the rights, property or personal safety of others.

## How you can edit or delete account information:

As a registered WhoSampled.com user, you can review and update your account details online using your username and password. When you update information, we usually keep a copy of the previous version for our records.

## Security of your personal information:

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of information, we have put in place physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to protect the information we collect.

## Use of cookies:

We may use "cookies" on our website to help us personalize your WhoSampled.com experience.

Cookies are small files that are placed on your computer through your web browser. Each time you access our website, cookies automatically collect certain information about you, including computer and connection information, browser type and version, operating system and platform details, the time of accessing the website and other information. We use cookie information in support of the activity you are carrying out, for anonymous user analysis and for research and development.

Most browsers will let you turn off cookies. If you are not sure whether your browser has this capability, please check with the software manufacturer or your Internet service provider.
