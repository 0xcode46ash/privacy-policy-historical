# WhoIsHostingThis.com Privacy Policy

WhoIsHostingThis.com respects our users. Our privacy policy explains what we do with any data we collect.

We may use your IP address for several reasons: to help diagnose server problems, to administer our website, to help identify you and to gather broad demographic information using webstats packages, like Google Analytics. This uses cookies.

We may collect demographic and profile data to tailor your experience. Based on this information, we may display products and content we feel you might interest you. We may request data such as contact information, email address and demographic data.

Any information you provide is used only to send you information and promotional material you specifically request (eg, a hosting voucher claim). You can opt out at any time.

We do not spam, rent or sell our mailing list. Your personal data is only passed to a third party if a) you explicitly request information from a third party (eg, to claim a special offer on hosting) or b) when required by law.

Demographic and user data is collected to so we can make sure we offer the best user experience possible.

This site contains links to other websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites.

Please [contact us](http://www.whoishostingthis.com/contact/) if you have any questions about our privacy practices.
