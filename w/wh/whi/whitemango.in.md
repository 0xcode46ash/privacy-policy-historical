# White Mango

## Privacy Policy 

StartEdge Venture is firmly committed to your privacy. We want you to feel secure for the personal information you provide to us while using our site which we would treat with utmost care. This Privacy Policy throws light on our information gathering and dissemination practices.

In general, using our site as a site visitor may be considered anonymous: we do not engage in any activity that will personally identify our site visitors. We may collect personal information when you voluntarily submit it to us, for example if you: • Register for any inquiry • Register to inquire about career opportunities The personal data collected will not be sold to anyone outside and will be solely used by StartEdge Venture and its group of companies for a one-on-one contact with the registrants. Any information that we have collected or stored about you may be transferred to a future purchaser of our company and/or its assets or to respond to inquiries. 

StartEdge Venture reserves the right to modify or update our Privacy Policy at any time without notice, so we encourage you to review this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your Personal Information, we will post an alert on this page.  
Cookies A "cookie" is a small piece of data that a website can send to your browser, which may then be stored on your system. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it or not.  
We sometimes collect anonymous information from visits to our website such as source of traffic to the website, time spent on each page etc. to help us provide better customer service. We may use this data to analyze trends and statistics and to help us provide you with a better customer service. Cookies can be used to provide you with tailored information from a website.

### External Links

This website would contain links to other websites. StartEdge Venture is not responsible for the privacy practices or the contents of such websites, nor do we take any responsibility for the opinions of third parties expressed on or through our website. 

### Deletion of your record

This site gives users the following option for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service. To delete your record from our database, send us an email at [info@StartEdgeVentures.com](mailto:info@StartEdgeVentures.com) or [info@whitemango.in](mailto:info@whitemango.in)

### Changing or Modifying Information

This site gives users the following option for changing and modifying information previously provided. You can send us an email at [info@StartEdgeVentures.com](mailto:info@StartEdgeVentures.com) or [info@whitemango.in](mailto:info@whitemango.in) to request a change. 

### Contacting the Website

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this website, you can contact us using the Contact Us form or at [info@StartEdgeVentures.com](mailto:info@StartEdgeVentures.com) or [info@whitemango.in](mailto:info@whitemango.in)
