# Privacy

Trophy Properties Real Estate, LLC, and Whitetail Properties, LLC value the privacy of our visitors and the personal information that you share with us.  We believe that protection of customer information is one of our fundamental responsibilities to you.  Because privacy and consumer trust are important to us, we provide this privacy statement to explain our policies to you. 

##  **Information That We Collect**

We gather the following types of information in order to improve our service to you:

  * Contact information you provide such as, your Name, Address, Email Address, Phone Number, Mailing Address, etc.
  * Tracking information which our web server automatically recognizes each time you visit one of our sites or communicate with us by email , including your domain name, your email address, and what pages you visit. 
  * Information about property that you are interested in purchasing or selling. 
  * Other information that you submit, via email, telephone, website postings or other similar means related to your use of our site, including.



##  **The Way We Use and Share Information**

Depending on your use of the website and the services you request from us, we may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  * To assist you with your sale or purchase of hunting land or farmland
  * To enable our agents, brokers, and affiliated entities to contact you and provide personal service, personalized content, and/or search results.
  * To process and respond to inquiries and to contact you about services you have requested.
  * To alert you to properties listed for sale, or the results of recent sales that may be of interest to you
  * To improve the features, content, and navigability of our websites.
  * To alert you to new website features, promotions, and services, and to deliver other information to you, including information or offers from third party affiliates, that may be of interest to you.
  * To solicit your feedback about our services.



In order to facilitate your sale or purchase of property, we may share your personal information with third parties involved with your sale or purchase (or potential sale or purchase) of real estate. These third parties include, but are not limited to, third-party real estate agents and brokers, insurance providers, lenders, attorneys, title companies, appraisers, and inspectors. 

We may also share your information in response to a subpoena, legal order, or official request.  We also reserve the right to share your information if we reasonably believe you have acted in violation of the Terms of Use for our website, or when we believe that the sharing of information is necessary to protect your safety or the safety of others. 

In the future, we may sell some or all of our assets. In the event of a sale of our company or its assets including our database, customer information will be transferred. We will take steps designed to require buyers to honor our privacy policy.  

##  **Links and Third Party Advertising**

Our website may contain links to third-party advertisers and websites. You are not required to visit any third party websites in order to utilize our website. The use of personal information on third-party websites is governed by the privacy policies of those websites, which may be different from the privacy policy for this website.  If you choose to visit a third party website, Whitetail Properties encourages you to review the privacy practices and content of such websites before submitting personal information.

##  **Updating and Deleting Your Information**

You may update your information or ask that we delete the information you have provided to us at any time by sending an email to [info@whitetailproperties.com](mailto:info@whitetailproperties.com) or by submitting updated information to our website. 

##  **Use of Cookies**

A "cookie" is a small text file or text identifier placed on your computer to assist us in providing customized services. We use cookies to provide you with a better experience and to become more familiar with our customers through the study of analytics. Some portions of our sites require the use of cookies for proper function.

You may manage how your browser handles cookies by adjusting its privacy and security settings. Browsers are different, so refer to instructions related to your browser to learn about cookie-related and other privacy and security settings that may be available.

##  **Security**

Any request on our website for personal data includes the use of security measures that are designed to prevent inadvertent disclosure of your information.  We continually monitor and update the security measures to protect against evolving threats. 

##  **Updates and Questions**

This policy was last updated July, 2012. 

We may from time-to-time revise this Privacy Policy.  The date of last revision will be available on this page, so please be sure to check back periodically. Your continued use of the website after changes have been posted to the Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such changes.

By visiting our website, you are acknowledging and accepting this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [info@whitetailproperties.com](mailto:info@whitetailproperties.com).
