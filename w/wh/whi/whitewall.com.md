# Privacy Policy

1) To complete and execute your order we process a range of your personal information in accordance with the conditions of the German data protection laws (title, first- and surname, address, email address and phone number, payment details). We save the contract text and send you an email containing the details of your order. You can view our AGB (General Terms & Conditions) [here](https://web.archive.org/terms-and-conditions "T&C") at any time, and can view your previous orders in your WhiteWall account. On registering for the newsletter your email address will be used for private advertising purposes, until you unsubscribe from the newsletter. You may have already given your explicit consent to the following text ("Subscribe to newsletter (You may unsubscribe at any time)"). We would like to make you aware that you are able to revoke your consent at any time with immediate effect for the future.

2) In addition, in order to ensure maximum data protection, all payment details (e.g. account and credit card details) will automatically be deleted from our systems after the order and the payment have been processed and such information will have to be re-entered in the event of future transactions.

3) In accordance with federal data protection laws you have the right to access, for free, information regarding your saved data as well as, where applicable, the right to adjust, block or delete this information. This can be done either through your password-protected area [My WhiteWall](https://web.archive.org/myWhitewall/show), or by contacting our customer services under [info@whitewall.com](mailto:info@whitewall.com).

4) The WhiteWall website uses cookies in order to allow you to use all features and functions of our website fully. Most of the cookies used will automatically be deleted after the conclusion of the browser session. Other cookies are retained and will enable us to recognize your computer upon your next visit and will thus help us to improve our services to you.

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. (“Google”). Google Analytics uses “cookies”, which are text files placed on your computer, to help the website analyze how users use the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the website, compiling reports on website activity for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however please note that if you do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of this website. By using this website, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above.

5) Of course third parties will not be allowed to use cookies to collect, process or use the data of our visitors. The default settings in all major browsers accept cookies. You can generally disable the use and storage of cookies by selecting respective browser settings. However, by altering your settings, you can choose to automatically block cookies from all web sites, or only our website but this may make certain features of our services inaccessible to you.

6) To ensure the best possible search results on our Art Market we use the Google translation service Translator. This service helps to ensure that your search request is automatically translated into different languages which are then sent back to our website. Your search requests will not be saved, and will not be used in connection with other information.
