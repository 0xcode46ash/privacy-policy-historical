# PRIVACY POLICY

**PRIVACY POLICY**

W Concept is committed to protecting your privacy and takes great care to safeguard information in our possession. 

Your preferences for use of your information are our highest priority. 

W Concept guarantees the information you enter will not be disclosed to any outside organization or third party. 

We may use contact information to contact you about new products, features or special promotions. 

If you do not want to receive such communications you may remove yourself from the mailing list by logging into your account and opting out of the mailing list. 

Please email contact@wconcept.com

**# Security**

W Concept uses cutting edge technology to ensure your checkout process and the content saved in your account are secure. 

Payment information, email address, shipping and billing address and all other information are secured by bluewisesoft , a leading internet security company.
