# WEAU TV-13 Privacy Policy

**Privacy Statement for WEAU-TV**

WEAU-TV 13 has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: WEAU.com. 

Information Automatically Logged 

We use your IP address only to gather broad demographic information, unless clearly stated otherwise, and with your permission. We will not disclose information without your consent, except as required by Federal Communications Regulations or proper legal paperwork or other legal processes. 

Cookies 

Our Site uses cookies to deliver content specific to your interests. The information is stored in your browser, not on our Web server. We do not collect or log this information on our server. However, we do not control the use of cookies from our advertisers or vendors, and these cookies are not subject to the weau.com privacy policy. 

Surveys 

WEAU.com may post optional surveys in which we ask for Personally Identifiable Information, with your permission. These surveys are optional, and are not required in order to use the site. If we ask for Personally Identifiable Information, we will clearly state if we intend to share it with others. 

External Links 

This site contains links to other sites. WEAU TV-13 is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

Children's Guidelines 

We care about children and understand that they have special privacy needs. Therefore, we have adopted the following privacy policy regarding children: 

Minors may participate in our interactive site; however, a minor may not appreciate the consequences of providing personal information and must obtain a parent's or legal guardian's consent to participate in our interactive site or download files. 

Contacting Us 

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this site, you can contact: [info@weau.com](mailto:info@weau.com) or use our online [feedback form](https://web.archive.org/web/20021207194048id_/http%3A//www.weau.com/feedback.asp).
