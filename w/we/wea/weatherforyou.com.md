## Privacy Statement for Weather for You

**Weather for You** has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: [Weather for You](http://www.weatherforyou.com/)

#### Information Automatically Logged

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site.

#### Cookies

Our Site uses cookies to deliver content specific to your interests.  If you request to have a particular weather forecast set as your default forecast, that information will be saved in a cookie.

#### Advertisers

We use outside ad companies to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information.

#### Registration Forms

Our site's registration form to request receiving weather forecasts and current conditions requires users to give us their email address.

Contact information from the registration forms are used only for the purpose requested (sending weather forecasts or current conditions).

Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

#### Order Forms

Our site uses an order form for customers to request advertising information.  We collect contact information (like their telephone number and postal and email addresses).

Contact information from the order forms is used to provide information about our company. The customer's contact information is also used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary.

#### External Links

This site contains links to other sites. **Weather for You** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

#### Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.
