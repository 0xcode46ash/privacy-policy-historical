## Cookies and Web Beacons

websitesetupguide.com does not use cookies. Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site or on their advertising pages. Our advertising partners include Commission Junction and Google Adwords.

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to generate the advertisements and links that appear on websitesetupguide.com and on advertising partners. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

websitesetupguide.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers. You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. websitesetupguide.com's privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.
