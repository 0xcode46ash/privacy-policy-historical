## What data do we collect?

  * Webtrends collects information directly from visitors to its digital properties when they choose to participate in our offers and programs or otherwise provide information directly to us.
  * The information you provide may be combined with data automatically collected using Webtrends own services and from other companies.
  * Change cookie settings >>
  * [Learn more >>](http://webtrends.com/privacy-policy#information-we-collect)



## How do we use your data?

  * We use the information we collect to provide you with the services you request.
  * Your data is also used to facilitate and enhance your digital experience and may be used to create a profile of your activity for marketing purpose. _You may request changes to your contact preferences by emailing_[ _comprefs@webtrends.com_](mailto:comprefs@webtrends.com).
  * [Learn more >>](http://webtrends.com/privacy-policy#your-information-choices)



## Do we share your data?

  * Webtrends does not sell, rent or lease Webtrends customer information to third parties.
  * Your data may be shared with service providers who work on our behalf. It may also, with your consent only, be shared with marketing partners or other independent third parties. _You may opt-out of our sharing your information with independent third parties by emailing_[ _comprefs@webtrends.com_](mailto:comprefs@webtrends.com).
  * [Learn more >>](http://webtrends.com/privacy-policy#sharing-of-information)



## Do we use third party tracking?

  * We use our own products, and products of third parties acting on our behalf, to analyze, optimize and improve our sites, including our products and services.
  * Change cookie settings >>
  * [Learn more >>](http://webtrends.com/privacy-policy#information-we-auto-collect)


  * Webtrends is a third party service provider used by our customers to analyze, optimize and improve their sites, products and services.
  * All data we collect on behalf of our customers through Webtrends products and services is their property and subject to their privacy policies.
  * To understand your choices for cookies set while visiting our customers’ web sites and digital properties, please consult these customers’ privacy statements and cookie settings.
  * Webtrends uses customer data for the purpose of providing services to that specific customer.
  * Webtrends does not share information collected for one customer with our other customers.
  * [Learn more >>](http://webtrends.com/privacy-policy#information-we-collect)
