# Privacy Statement

[ ](http://www.dpbolvw.net/click-3523406-10574334)

## Privacy

For every visit to this website, we record visitor's IP Address, location, OS, Browser Type, Visit Length and other computer specific information. This type of information is used to learn about user preferences and to make this site more useful and friendly.

## Email Privacy

We highly respect our visitors' privacy. Hence we **DO NOT** sell, rent or share your email address thru which you might have contacted us or entered during the registration process (not applicable to all sites) in our website network.

We do not indulge in SPAM (Unsolicited Commercial Emails).

## Cookie Management and Web beacons

We may access and set cookies using Web beacons, also known as single-pixel GIFs, which are invisible graphical images. These Web beacons tell us useful information regarding our site such as which pages users access.

## Third Party Advertisements

To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies (such as Google's Adsense) may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information. It could also access and set cookies in your browser, use web beacons as mentioned above. For further information, consult the privacy policy of [Google (Adsense)](http://www.google.com/privacy.html)

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.  
Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.  
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).  


## Contact us for more info

If you have any privacy related issues, you may use the "Contact us" Form, to communicate with us.

## Policy - subject to change

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and we will use for these new purposes only data collected from the time of the policy change forward. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our Web site periodically.
