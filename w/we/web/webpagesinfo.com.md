# Privacy Policy - Webpagesinfo

## Privacy Policy

The Webpagesinfo.com ("WebPagesInfo") Privacy Policy covers WebPagesInfo's treatment of personal or personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") that may be collected when you are on the WebPagesInfo web site and when you use WebPagesInfo's services. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that WebPagesInfo does not own or control, or to individuals that are not under WebPagesInfo's supervisory control.

#### Collection and Use of Personal Information.

WebPagesInfo may collect Personal Information when you use certain services on the WebPagesInfo web site or when you visit cookie-enabled web pages. WebPagesInfo may also log your IP address when you use the WebPagesInfo web site. WebPagesInfo uses this Personal Information to fulfill requests for our services or products, to contact users regarding changes to our site or our business, and to customize the content that you might see on the WebPagesInfo web site. WebPagesInfo may also use "cookie" files to better serve your needs by creating a customized web site which fits your needs.

#### Sharing and Disclosure of Personal Information.

WebPagesInfo will not sell or rent your Personal Information to any individual, business, or government entity. WebPagesInfo will share your Personal Information ith other entities should you request WebPagesInfo to share such information, or if WebPagesInfo is required to respond to court orders, subpoenas or other legal process.

#### User Accounts.

You have the ability to edit your User Information at any time, and may delete your account at your convenience. Under no condition will WebPagesInfo share your user account information with another individual, business or government entity, unless WebPagesInfo is required to respond to court orders, subpoenas or other legal process. WebPagesInfo allows users to set up accounts to update information on web domains, such updates are public.

#### Content rating and parental controls.

WebPagesInfo.com does not offer any content or services for use by children. If you are under 18, parent or guardian is required to supervise your use of WebPagesInfo.com.

#### Encryption.

WebPagesInfo uses all economically reasonable security measures in protecting user data. All user account pages are protected with Secure Socket Layer ("SSL") encryption. All user accounts must be accessed with usernames and passwords. You are advised not to share your username and password with any other person.

#### Amendments.

WebPagesInfo reserves the right to amend this policy at any time. WebPagesInfo will contact registered users by e-mail, and shall also post a notice of changes on its web site, when and if the terms of this policy are amended.

#### Contact.

You may contact WebPagesInfo directly by e-mailing WebPagesInfo Contact Email should you have any questions regarding this policy.

#### Report a policy violation

You can report a policy violation using [this form](https://web.archive.org/report/).
