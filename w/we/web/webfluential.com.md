# Webfluential | Privacy

### 1\. Introduction

  1. Webfluential respects your privacy and is committed to Processing and protecting your Personal Information lawfully in accordance with the Electronic Communications and Transactions Act, 25 of 2002. This Privacy Policy governs Webfluential’s policies and practices with respect to the Processing of Personal Information and other data collected by Webfluential from the Website in connection with the Services. 
  2. By using the Website you agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy and consent to the Processing of your Personal Information and data as set forth in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to and accept this Privacy Policy, you should not use the Website. 



### 2\. Definitions

  1. “Personal Information” means Personal Information as defined in section 1 of the Electronic Communications and Transactions Act, 25 of 2002. 
  2. “Privacy Policy” refers to this privacy policy. 
  3. “Processing” means using or accessing information in any way, including, but not limited to, collecting, collating, storing, analysing, updating, modifying, disclosing and deleting information. 
  4. “Services” means the Website, the global influencer marketing platform operated by Webfluential, Webfluential’s mobile applications, and services provided by Webfluential in relation thereto. 
  5. “Webfluential” refers to Webfluential Global Limited, a company incorporated in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Mauritius, with its registered address at Level 3, Alexander House, 35 Cybercity, Ebene 72201, Republic of Mauritius. 
  6. “Website” refers to, and includes any part or element of, [www.webfluential.com](https://web.archive.org/pages/home) and any of its sub-domains. 



### 3\. Processing of Personal Information

  1. In order to use the Services, you will be required to register an account with Webfluential. During this registration process, you will be asked to provide Webfluential with Personal Information, such as your name, email address, phone number, your paypal or local bank account details, information regarding your social media account etc. 
  2. This Personal Information is used by Webfluential to create a profile for you in order to provide the Services. Should you elect to not share this Personal Information, you will be unable to complete the registration process and use the Services. 
  3. Webfluential will not share, rent or sell your Personal Information with third parties except under the following circumstances: 
    1. when you have consented or directed us to share the information; 
    2. with service providers, affiliates and partners who perform services on Webfluential’s behalf; 
    3. the transfer of Personal Information as an asset of Webfluential’s business in the event that it is involved in a merger, acquisition or the sale of all or a part of its assets; 
    4. as may be required by law; and 
    5. when, in the sole opinion of Webfluential, it is necessary to do so to protect its rights or your safety or the safety of others, or to investigate and/or prevent fraud or a breach of security. 
  4. Webfluential will store and use your Personal Information only for so long as your account for the Services is active and as may be necessary to comply with any applicable laws, resolve disputes and to enforce its agreements. 



### 4\. Safety and Security

  1. Webfluential uses all reasonable technical and organisational measures to secure the integrity of the Personal Information under its control and prevent the loss of, damage to or unauthorised destruction of your Personal Information and unlawful access to or Processing of your Personal Information. When you enter sensitive information (such as a credit card number or banking details) on our registration forms, we encrypt that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). 



### 5\. Cookies

  1. When visiting the Website, Webfluential may place one or more cookies (a small file that is stored by your browser to recognise you and store your preferences) on your computer. Webfluential uses these cookies to track your behaviour and user settings on the Website in order to improve the Services by personalising the information delivered to you in accordance with your preferences. You may adjust the settings on your browser to refuse cookies but some of the Services may not work if you do so. 
  2. Webfluential also uses third parties to advertise on the Website and these third parties may use cookies or other means to help measure the effectiveness of their advertisements. 



### 6\. Links to Other Sites / Third Parties

  1. The Website may include links to other websites and/or contact forms to third parties which may collect your Personal Information. Please be aware that this Privacy Policy does not extend to such other websites or third parties and Webfluential is not responsible for the Processing of your Personal Information by such sites or parties. We recommend that you refer directly to these parties regarding their privacy policies before supplying your Personal Information to them. 



### 7\. Correction of Personal Information

  1. If your Personal Information changes (such as your email address), you undertake to correct, update or amend your Personal Information by editing your account profile accordingly. 



### 8\. Consumer Rights 

  1. Webfluential may use your information to contact you about promotions and services. You are entitled, at any stage, to opt out of these communications by deactivating your account for the Services or by clicking on the "Unsubscribe" option on the communication. 



### 9\. Changes to Privacy Policy 

  1. We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice to you. Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on our website and become effective at the time of posting. 
  2. In the event that material changes are made to this Privacy Policy that affect the Processing of your Personal Information, we will notify you of these changes by means of a notice on our Website.
