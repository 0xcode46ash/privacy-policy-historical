# Web Templates, Flash Templates, Flash Intro Templates, OsCommerce Templates, Website Templates Design - WebsiteTemplates

WebsiteTemplates Privacy Policy 

  1. a) This privacy policy covers WebsiteTemplates treatment of personally identifiable information collected through a website owned and operated by WebsiteTemplates. 

b) This privacy policy does not apply to the practices of companies that WebsiteTemplates does not own or control, or of persons that WebsiteTemplates does not employ or manage, including any third-party content contributors bound by contract and any third-party websites to which WebsiteTemplates websites link. 

  2. a) You can visit the websites of WebsiteTemplates without revealing any personal information. However, WebsiteTemplates needs certain personal information if you wish to purchase our products, register for an reseller account or use certain WebsiteTemplates services. 

b) Where required, this information may include your personal contact information and/or your company contact information. WebsiteTemplates will use this information to reply to your inquiries, to provide you with requested products and services, to set up your member.s account, and to contact you regarding new products and services. 

c) By accessing the services of WebsiteTemplates and voluntarily providing us with the requested personal information, you consent to the collection and use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy. 

  3. a) WebsiteTemplates automatically receives and records non-personal information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, cookie information and the page you requested. WebsiteTemplates may use this information to customize the advertising and content you see and to fulfill your requests for certain products and services. However, WebsiteTemplates does not connect this non-personal data to any personal information collected from you. 

b) WebsiteTemplates also allows third party companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Again, these cookies are not connected to any personal information. Third party cookie usage is subject to their own privacy policies, and WebsiteTemplates assumes no responsibility or liability for this usage. 

  4. a) WebsiteTemplates may disclose your personal information to third parties who work on behalf of WebsiteTemplates to provide products and services requested by you. We will share personal information for these purposes only with third parties whose privacy policies are consistent with ours or who agree to abide by our policies with respect to personal information 

b) Artvertex may otherwise disclose your personal information when: 

    1. We have your express consent to share the information for a specified purpose;
    2. We need to respond to subpoenas, court orders or such other legal process;
    3. We need to protect the personal safety of the users of our websites or defend the rights or property of WebsiteTemplates;
    4. We find that your actions on our websites violate the WebsiteTemplates Terms of Use document or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services.
  5. a) If you do not consent to the collection, use or disclosure of your personal information as outlined in this policy, please do not provide any personal information to WebsiteTemplates. If you have provided personal information to WebsiteTemplates and no longer consent to its use or disclosure as outlined herein, please notify WebsiteTemplates. 

  6. a) Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet can be considered 100% secure. However, your WebsiteTemplates Information protected for your privacy and security.

b) Further, WebsiteTemplates retains your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes identified above or as required by law. 

  7. a) WebsiteTemplates may at any time, without notice to you and in its sole discretion, amend this policy from time to time. Please review this policy periodically. Your continued use of WebsiteTemplates websites after any such amendments signifies your acceptance thereof. 

  8. If you have questions or suggestions about this privacy policy, or your own personal information, please e-mail us.
