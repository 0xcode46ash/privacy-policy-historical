# Starfield Technologies Privacy Policy

STARFIELD TECHNOLOGIES, INC. PRIVACY POLICY

Starfield Technologies, Inc. respects your privacy. We have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to you, our customer. This privacy statement discloses the privacy practices for this site, www.starfieldtech.com. 

I. What information is collected?

Personal Information:  
In order make suggestions or request product or partnership information, we require personal information from you, including your name, address, phone number, and email address.

Clear Gifs (Web beacons/Web bugs)  
Our site does not use clear gifs. 

Cookies  
Our site does not use cookies.

Non-personal information:  
We use information gathered about you from our site statistics (for example, your IP address) to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. We also gather broad demographic information from this data to help us improve our site and make your browsing and purchasing experience more enjoyable. This information is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

II. How information will be used?

Resale and Disclosure of Information:  
We maintain a strict “No-Spam” policy. We will never give any information about you to others without your express permission. We do not rent our mailing list to third parties. Our site uses forms in which you give us contact information (like your name, address, and email address) so you can place orders, request information and support, and make product suggestions. We use your customer information only when it will be important for us to contact you regarding functionality changes to our products, our Web site, new Starfield Technologies services, and special offers we think you'll find valuable. If you would rather not receive this information, you may opt out of it by following the instructions in Section III, below.

Information Disclosure Required by Law:  
The only exceptions to our disclosure policy occur when:   
We are required by law, in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law.  
_Or_  
We must comply with a legal process served on our Web site. 

  
Email:  
We use email messages to communicate with you, to confirm your placed orders, and to send information that you have requested. We also provide email links, as on our "About Starfield Technologies" page, to allow you to contact us directly. We strive to promptly reply to your messages.

The email and/or postal mail you elect to receive may include offerings from trusted Starfield Technologies advertising partners. We never sell or share your personal information for these offerings.

The information you send to us may be stored and used to improve this site and our products, or it may be reviewed and discarded. However, your information will only be shared with third parties with your explicit permission. 

III. User choices in receiving and distribution of information

As a customer of Starfield Technologies, you may alter or delete your customer information or opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at any time. You have the choice of opting out of receiving promotional emails and mail, while still receiving non-promotional emails that deal with your account. IV. Changes in our Practices

If we change our information handling practices or other privacy aspects, we will post those changes on this privacy statement, at least 30 days prior to their implementation. We will also notify you directly via email. 

V. Your Consent to this Policy

By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of your data by Starfield Technologies as specified above. Your visit and any dispute over privacy is subject to this Policy.
