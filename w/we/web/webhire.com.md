# Privacy Statement

|  |    **Webhire's Privacy Policy**

If you request to receive Webhire's Free Email Updates, we ask for certain information, including your email address, so that we can send it to you.  The email address you provide us is used only for the purposes of sending you these updates, and you can cancel your request at any time.

When you sign up to purchase a product or receive additional product information, Webhire and its partners collect personal information. To learn more about how Webhire uses your information, please [contact ](https://web.archive.org/web/20010602185947id_/http%3A//www.webhire.com/contact/contact.asp)us directly. To learn more about how our partners use your information, please see their privacy policies.
