# Weichert Rental Network | Apartment Listings | Apartment Guide | Apartment Search

**Privacy Statement**

**WE ABSOLUTELY POSITIVELY DO NOT SELL ANY LISTS OR PERSONAL INFORMATION TO ANY 3RD PARTY COMPANIES.**

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, but not the e-mail address (where possible). The information we collect is used to improve the content of our services, used to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual consumer, used to notify consumers about updates to our Web site and used by us to contact consumers for marketing purposes - sending new properties \- anything related to finding you a new apartment.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please unsubscribe by logging in and clicking on "unsubscribe". We do not make our customer e-mail list available to other nonaffiliated organizations. We do not sell lists of consumer information to third party companies. We offer products and services of consumer interest. We will not share your name/information with any nonaffiliated companies or organizations. If are not interested in any of our products or services you will not be contacted and you can unsubscribe to our service at any time. 

Access to your personal information is limited to employees and participating properties, which are reasonably believed to need such information to provide their services to you. We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you. Your Account Information is password-protected. In certain areas we uses industry-standard SSL encryption to protect data transmissions.
