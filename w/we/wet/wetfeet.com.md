# Privacy Statement

WetFeet.com has created this privacy statement to demonstrate our strong commitment to privacy. The following statement explains our information-gathering and -dissemination practices for our website, www.wetfeet.com.

**Your privacy**  
When we gather information from insiders for Insider Guides and other career research products, we guarantee them complete anonymity. They are understandably concerned about their privacy, and we go to great lengths to ensure that their identity is never released to anyone outside of WetFeet.com.

WetFeet.com values your privacy too. We only request information absolutely essential to the filling of your order or to understand you better so that we can improve our products and services. We do not release customers' or members' names or contact information to third parties.

**What information we collect and how we use it**  
WetFeet's site uses your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website. Your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information.

WetFeet's site also uses cookies to keep track of the orders you make when shopping at our site. Cookies allow us to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site. 

The WetFeet website registration form requires users to give us contact information, including name and e-mail address. We also ask you to provide us with some additional information, such as your educational background, to better match our information, products, and services with your needs. 

When you make a purchase with WetFeet, we collect your financial information including credit card numbers on the order form. Contact information from WetFeet's registration forms is used to send orders to contact you when necessary. 

We also prvide you with the opportunity to receive selected information about career topics through our E-Insider electronic newsletter and other free communications. As a registered user, you may opt out of receiving future mailings by changing your subscription preferences on our site.

WetFeet.com enables you to transfer your résumé electronically to us for use in our [Résumé Review Corner](https://web.archive.org/web/19991128082328id_/http%3A//wetfeet.com/about/advice/resumereview/home.asp). If you submit your résumé to WetFeet.com, you grant us the right to transmit your résumé and contact information to the sponsor of the Résumé Review Corner. WetFeet.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of that company. You also grant WetFeet.com the right to post your résumé (with your name and contact information changed) on our website or in other media. 

**Privacy policies of other websites**  
WetFeet's site contains links to other career-related sites. WetFeet's site at http://www.wetfeet.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.

**Security**  
The WetFeet site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. 

**Choice**  
WetFeet's site provides users the opportunity to opt out of receiving communications from WetFeet and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor.

WetFeet gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service:

1\. You can change your subscription preferences in your member registration  
2\. You can e-mail your request to [orders@WetFeet.com](mailto:orders@WetFeet.com). 

**Correct/update**  
WetFeet's site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided. 

1\. You can change your information in your member registration  
2\. You can e-mail your request to [orders@WetFeet.com](mailto:orders@WetFeet.com).
