# Wegmans Privacy Policy

### Wegmans Privacy Policy

OUR PLEDGE TO YOU  
Since 1916, we've been in the business of trying to be the best at serving the needs of our customers. We see the Internet as a great way to do just that. 

You have our word that the information gathered through this site will only be used on behalf of giving you, our valued customer, our very best. We will hold all such information in the strictest of confidence, even within Wegmans, and pledge never to sell your name, address, or purchase information to anyone else. We guarantee it. 

Danny Wegman
