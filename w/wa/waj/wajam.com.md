## A SPECIAL NOTE FOR PARENTS AND LEGAL GUARDIANS:

Wajam respects the U.S Children's Online Privacy Protection (COPPA) Act of 1998 and the guidelines of the Children's Advertising Review Unit (CARU) of the Council of Better Business Bureau, Inc. in each of its websites for children. You can help by spending time online with your children and monitoring your children's online use. Please help us protect your child's privacy by instructing them never to provide personal information on this website or any other without your permission.

If you are under 18, please be sure to read this policy with your parents or legal guardians and ask questions about things you do not understand. Children under 13 (U13) are prohibited from using the Services provided by Wajam.

  1. Information Collection. 
    * We only collect personal information:
      * that you provide to us directly via the Website or the Wajam Application when you fulfill the registration form, sign up for our Services and provide us feedback or comment. The collection is limited to the following information: name and email address;
      * that we receive from our social networking partners (e.g., such as Facebook, Twitter or Google+), if you decide to subscribe to the Services through them; and/or
      * that you decide to make available to us and your contacts (e.g., pictures) either directly or through our social networking partners.
    * Wajam may also collect the following usage information, whether you are accessing the Website or use the Wajam Application as a registered or non-registered user: your IP address, Wajam cookie information (see below), the date and time of your visit or use of the Wajam Application, the type of Internet browser or hardware device (computer or mobile/smartphone) you use, URLs of the pages you visit in order to give you personal search results and the URL of any other website that referred you to our Website. This information is not linked to you as an individual and is not usually regarded as personal information unless you register for our Services, in which case it becomes personal information (as it is linked with the personal information contained in your account). We use your Mac Address, Drive Serial Number and Default Browser to assign you a unique identifier. Wajam may also collect system information to diagnose and debug software issues. We collect a list of all add-ons you've installed to Firefox, Chrome, Internet Explorer and Safari in order to detect and solve add-on compatibility issues.
    * We do not collect any other personal information, period.
    * If you choose to use our service to tell a friend [e.g. email a friend] about Wajam search results, we will ask you for your friend’s email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email inviting him or her to visit the site. Wajam stores this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email.
    * Your friend may contact us at [privacy@wajam.com](mailto:privacy@wajam.com) to request that we remove this information from our database.

These services providers have access to your personal information only for purposes of performing these tasks on our behalf;
      * our advertising partners so that they can provide advertisements or promotional materials on or through the Website and/or the Wajam Application that are more relevant to you; and

    * These providers and partners are prohibited from sharing or selling this information, and may not use this information for any purpose not enumerated in this section.
    * In addition, if Wajam merges with another company, is subject to a corporate reorganization, sells or transfers all or part of the Website and/or the Wajam Application, its business assets or shares, or in the event of bankruptcy, it is possible that the personal information we possess may be sold, transferred or otherwise shared as part of that transaction. Third parties are prohibited from sharing or selling this information, and may not use this information for any purpose not enumerated in this section. You can opt out of this usage by visiting your account settings (see below in Advertising and Opting Out).
  4. Cookies, Third Party Cookies & Tracking Technologies. 
    * A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. We do link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site. Wajam may set and access certain cookies on your computer for the following reasons: 
      * to facilitate your "signing in" on the Website or the Wajam Application;
      * so that we can provide you with customized content;
      * to keep track of preferences you specify while you are using our Services;
      * to provide general internal and user analytics;
      * to conduct research to improve the content of the Website, the Wajam Application and the Services; and
      * to assist in identifying possible fraudulent activities.
      * If you configure your browser to reject cookies, you may not be able to use certain or all of our Services.
    * Third Party Cookies. The use of cookies by our partners, affiliates, tracking utility company, service providers is not covered by our privacy statement. We do not have access or control over these cookies. Our partners, affiliates, tracking utility company, service providers use session ID cookies to make it easier for you to use our Services.
    * Tracking Technologies. We use tools such as Google Analytics, a third-party tracking service, which uses cookies to track non-personally identifiable information about our visitors to our site in the aggregate to capture usage and volume statistics. Some of our business partners (e.g., Google Analytics) use cookies on our site. We have no access to or control over these cookies. This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by Wajam only and does not cover the use of cookies by any third-party.
    * Flash Cookies.We use local shared objects, also known as Flash cookies, to store your preferences such a volume control or high game score, or display content based upon what you view on our site to personalize your visit. Third Parties, with whom we partner to provide certain features on our site or to display advertising based upon your Web browsing activity, use Flash cookies to collect and store information.  
Flash cookies are different from browser cookies because of the amount of, type of, and how data is stored. Cookie management tools provided by your browser will not remove Flash cookies. To learn how to manage privacy and storage settings for Flash cookies click here: <http://www.macromedia.com/support/documentation/en/flashplayer/help/settings_manager.html>
