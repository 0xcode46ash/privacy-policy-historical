# Privacy Policy | Watchfinder Ltd

We will use your personal information in the following ways: 

  1. To provide you with information about the products and services we offer. 
  2. To provide you with a more personalised service. 
  3. To conduct market research. 
  4. To run competitions and promotions. 
  5. To provide you with other offers and promotions from carefully selected companies.



We collect your personal information when watch enquiries are made. 

You might be asked for your personal information if you want to take advantage of services such as forums, subscriptions, or product enquiries. In the case of product enquiries (through the watchfinder service or through the current stocks facility) your email address, including details of the watch you selected, will be stored so that we can return a written quote by email or send offers on similar watches or offers that might be relevant to you. By submitting a request you give permission for watchfinder to contact you using either telephone or email. 

You agree that we may use Personal information provided by you in order to conduct appropriate anti fraud checks. Personal Information that you provide may be disclosed to a credit reference or fraud prevention agency, which may keep a record of that information 

In each case we will only use the personal information you provide to deliver the services you have informed us you wish to take part in. 

Watchfinder.co.uk Ltd will also email you with the details of other relevant offers from companies it owns. 

Links within this site to other websites are not covered by this policy.
