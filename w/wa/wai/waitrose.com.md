The John Lewis Partnership may use the information provided by you within this site to improve our services or to offer additional products, services or information from companies within the Partnership. For your information, the businesses within the John Lewis Partnership include our John Lewis Department Stores, Waitrose Supermarkets and our mail order companies of Waitrose Direct and Findlater Mackie Todd wines.

The John Lewis Partnership respects the privacy of its customers, and does not share any personal information with third parties. Please note that you do not have to provide any information to use this site, but if you do choose to withhold essential data you will not be able to access certain sections or facilities within the site. If you do not wish to be sent further information but wish to make full use of this site, please click the box indicating this wish on the registration form.

You have the right to request a copy of any information we hold on you at any time, and to have that information corrected if it is inaccurate. If you wish to view any information you have submitted, please contact Company Secretary, John Lewis Partnership, 171 Victoria Street, London SW1E 5NN.
