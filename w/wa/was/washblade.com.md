# Privacy Policy - Washington Blade: The Gay and Lesbian News Source of Record - D.C. and National Gay News, Entertainment and Opinion

  
  
Window Media may ask you to register and provide information that personally identifies you, including when you register as a subscriber to receive e-mail newsletters or respond to a contest or marketing campaign. The information gathered is used to enhance the user experience on our site and to identify frequent visitors. Information collected will not be sold, distributed or otherwise disseminated to outside sources. We may use your personal information for three primary purposes:

  * To improve the content of our web site, products and services
  * To alert you to product upgrades, updates and renewal information
  * To share special offers, product announcements and updated information, and other new services from Window Media.



You may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by clicking UNSUBSCRIBE or by contacting our Webmaster at [webmaster@Window-Media.com](mailto:webmaster@Window-Media.com) We will be sure your name is removed from our list.

Additionally, if you do not want Window Media to contact you for marketing purposes, by email, postal mail, fax and/or phone, you may opt out by sending e-mail to the Webmaster. Simply place the word UNSUBSCRIBE in the subject field and provide us with your name and the information you wish to unsubscribe. 

Window Media will take appropriate steps to protect the information you share with us. When you provide us with a Credit Card number (and other sensitive information), Window Media will take commercially reasonable steps to protect this information through a secure connection with your web browser. Credit card numbers are only used for payment processing and are not retained for marketing purposes.

Window Media is protected by federal copyright law and material published in print or online may not be reproduced in whole or part without the permission of the publishers. Window Media assumes no responsibility for unsolicited materials submitted for publication. Window Media is also not responsible for claims made by advertisers or materials provided by advertisers or their agents. 

If you have questions or comments regarding Window Media privacy practices, you can contact us at [Webmaster@Window-Media.com](mailto:webmaster@Window-Media.com) and we will be sure to address your needs.
