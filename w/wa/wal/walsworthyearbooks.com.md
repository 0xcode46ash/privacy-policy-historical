# Privacy Statement

**Walsworth Publishing Company's Privacy Promise**

Walsworth Publishing Company promises never to sell, rent, barter or share with any third party personally identifying information collected during your use of the Site without your prior permission.

From time to time we may reveal general statistical information about our Site and visitors, such as number of visitors, number and type of goods and services purchased, etc., but we will never reference you by name or specific address.

Your trust and confidence are our highest priority.

**Information We Collect**

We do collect and store the names, addresses, and other personally identifying information of customers of the Site who make requests. This information is saved in a secure environment and is kept for our records and to make it easier for you to use our services on return visits.

**How We Use the Information We Collect**

Information we collect is used to build features that will make the services available on the Site easier to use. This includes better customer support and timely notice of Site updates.

Information collected by Walsworth Publishing Company is stored in a secure database, accessible only by Walsworth Publishing Company and its associates.

From time to time, Walsworth Publishing Company may contact you to request feedback on your experience using the Site, to assist us in improving the Site, or to offer special promotions to you, as a Site user.
