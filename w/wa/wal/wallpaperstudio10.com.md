# Privacy Policy | Tens of thousands HD and UltraHD wallpapers for Android, Windows and Xbox

Updated: 09-04-2017

1\. Wallpaper Studio 10 is a website and application as services (“Services”) created, developed and owned by good2create.

This document outlines the types of information collected by our Wallpaper Studio 10 “Services”. Wallpaper Studio 10 provides a photo hosting service to you, subject to the following Privacy Policy, which may be updated from time to time without notice to you.

This Privacy Policy applies only to information that you provide to us through the “Services”, and when you access, download, purchase or use any information, services, products or content appearing in the “Services”.

By using the “Services”, you are accepting and consenting to the practices described in the Privacy Policy.

2\. User Accounts  
No user account is required in order to browse or download the content we provide. User account is only required to upload your wallpapers.

3\. User Provided Information  
We obtain certain information when you provide it to us: for example, if you setup your account profile in our “Services”, you may provide your name, email address, your age, photograph, and your country. Some of that information can be used by good2create to identify or contact you directly and is sometimes referred to as personally identifiable information. We don't sell your personally identifiable information to third parties.

Whenever you upload an image your personal information became public except your name and email address. Other personal information (profile photo, age, country, gender) can be set to public or private on your profile. 

In addition to your personal information, any images that you upload to the “Services” will be publicly available. You upload and share your Personal Information and content in the “Services” at your own risk. While we go to great lengths to create a safe and enjoyable experience in the “Services”, we cannot control the actions of other users with whom you may choose to share your content and/or profile information. In addition, we cannot anticipate other users’ ability to circumvent privacy settings. You understand that, even after removal of your Personal Information and content from the “Services”, copies of your information may remain viewable in cache or archive on the local computers of users who may have downloaded or stored your information.

Even with all the appropriate precautions we take to protect your information, we recommend you refrain from sending private information or Personal.

4\. Information from Use of our Services  
Good2create also gathers non-personally identifiable information from Users of our Services. Non-personally identifiable information is information that usually cannot by itself be used to identify a particular person or entity, and may include: the Internet Protocol (IP) address, unique device identifiers, anonymous IDs for advertising, operating system, mobile network information and non-precise device location information (e.g., city or region).

5\. User generated Content  
We offer our Users the opportunity to upload images. Such postings are governed by our Terms of Service. Users agree that the uploaded images will be publicly available and can be collected and used by others.

6\. Social authentication provider login  
When you choose to login to Wallpaper Studio 10 with any of the social authentication providers (Microsoft, Facebook or Google+) we can access certain information you've added on the mentioned social providers in order to prefill your profile information and identify you. 

7\. Third-Party Advertisers  
Wallpaper Studio 10 serves ads provided by a range of third-party advertising networks. The practices of our advertising partners are not directly covered by Wallpaper Studio 10’s privacy policy. We recommend that you read their policies. For your convenience we provide a list our advertising partners below. Unfortunately, we cannot guarantee it is always comprehensive and up to date.

8\. Access and changes to personally identifiable information; account termination  
You may at any time review or change your personally identifiable information by going to your account settings. Upon your request via e-mail, we will delete your account and contact information from our active databases. Please note that we may be obligated to keep some of all of your information on our servers even after you've deleted your account. 

9\. Security  
We use reasonable efforts to protect information from misuse. However, we cannot guarantee that only authorized persons will view your information. We are not responsible for third party circumvention of any privacy settings or security measures. 

10\. Children's Privacy  
We do not direct the “Services” to persons under 13 years of age, and requires Users to be 13 years or older. Accordingly, we do not knowingly collect or maintain on the Site personally identifying or other information from persons under 13 years of age. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with Personal Information without their consent, he or she should contact us through our “Services” Settings’ page. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with Personal Information, we will delete such information from our files.

We recommend that minors over the age of 13 ask a parent or guardian for permission before sending any information about themselves to anyone over the Internet. 

11\. Data hosting  
The information collected by good2create is hosted in the United States of America and is subject to U.S. state and federal law. If you are accessing our Services from other jurisdictions, please be advised that you are transferring your personal information to us in the United States where data protection and privacy laws may be less stringent than the laws of your country. By using our “Services”, you consent to the transfer and use of your personal information in accordance with this privacy policy.

We welcome your questions, comments, and concerns about this Privacy Policy and our privacy practices. Please send us feedback pertaining to these matters or any other issue you may have with Wallpaper Studio 10.
