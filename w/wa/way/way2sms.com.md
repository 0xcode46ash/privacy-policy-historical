# Way2SMS - FREE SMS any where in India

**Way2SMS takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn    more about our privacy policy. **

What This Privacy Policy Covers This policy covers how Way2SMS   treats personal information that Way2SMS collects and receives. Personal  information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your   name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise   publicly available.

**Information Collection and Use** **General**

Way2SMS collects personal information when you register with Way2SMS

  When you register we ask for information such as your name, emai l  address, birth date, gender, Mobile, Location etc.,

Way2SMS automatically receives and records information about every   message on our server logs .

We Record every Contact you register in your address book .

Way2SMS! uses information for the following general purposes: to   customize the advertising and content you see, Providing More value in the   form of Inserted ads in every message you send to your contacts.

  **Information Sharing and Disclosure**

We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or   with Way2SMS under confidentiality agreements. These companies may  use your personal information to help Way2SMS communicate with you  about  offers from Way2SMS and our marketing partners. However, these  companies do not have any independent right to share this information.

We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate,   prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud,   situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any personviolations of Way2SMS 's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law

We transfer information about you if Way2SMS is acquired by or merged   with another company. In this event, Way2SMS will notify you before  information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different   privacy policy. 

Way2SMS displays targeted advertisements based on personal information.

Way2SMS inserts targeted ads in every message (SMS) you send to your   contacts. Also you may even receive targeted SMS ads according to your  profile.

You may receive SMS Advertisements or offers from our Trusted Partners
