# Privacy Policy - way2tnpsc.com

Our Way2tnpsc.com assures your privacy. We will not collect any personal information or details about you, unless you choose to afford your information needed to us. When you visit our website, web server automatically collects your domain, IP address, volume, date and time, pages you visit during the access. Here your email address or any personal information about you is not identified. If you chooses to provide us any personal information e.g: your email address, that will be used only to respond to your inquiry. We will not distribute this information to any organization.  
**Links:**  
We have links to other sites . Please be aware that way2tnpsc.com are not responsible for any privacy practices of such sites.   
**Advertisements:**  
We serve ads for third -parties in our website. We are not responsible if such ads collects any of your relevant information.  
**Cookies:**  
Cookies placed on your device when you browse improves our website to be efficient and free to use. It also helps to share our pages in social networks. Strictly, no sensitive or personal information is collected through cookies without your consent. If you wish, you can deactivate your cookies in your browser.  
Your privacy is important to us.
