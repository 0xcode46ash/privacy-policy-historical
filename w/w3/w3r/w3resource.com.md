# About privacy policy of w3resource

1\. Any part of the content is not intended to collect personal information.

2\. Except in certain examples of JavaScript and PHP, we do not use cookies to store user information. For those said cases, the only purpose is to execue the example properly and bring the actual result to the learner.

3\. IP address of user's machine is logged for statistics only.

4\. Security settings are implemented to prohibit misuse / abuse. 

5\. Log files are used for server administration only.

6\. If links of other web sites are used, w3resource do not have any reponsiblity of their privacy policies.

7\. You can mail to w3resource2011@gmail.com if you want to know any further information regarding our privacy policy.
