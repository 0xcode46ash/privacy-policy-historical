# WMSPanel privacy policy - WMSPanel

This page describes the privacy practices for information collected by the WMSPanel web service. This is the basis for our customer relationships. We (the WMSPanel team) will post here all to the changes for privacy policies.

### What information does WMSPanel collect?

WMSPanel web service collects the data from the servers where our customers installed service agent as described below.

For Wowza Media Server WMSPanel collects server name, server IP addresses, server performance metrics, IP address of each connection, traffic consumed by all connections, server configuration, virtual hosts names, applications names, application instances names, streams names; the IP addresses of connections are used for statistics processing only and ARE NOT stored within the service after the statistics are processed. WMSPanel DOES NOT process or collect the content which is stored and transmitted on customer's server.

For Windows Media Services WMSPanel collects server name, server IP addresses, IP addresses of connections, traffic consumed by visitors; the IP addresses of connections are used for statistics processing only and ARE NOT stored within the service after the statistics are processed. WMSPanel DOES NOT process or collect the content which is stored and transmitted on customer's server.

For Nimble HTTP Streamer WMSPanel collects server name, server IP addresses, IP addresses of visitors, traffic consumed by visitors, server configuration; the IP addresses of connections are used for statistics processing only and ARE NOT stored within the service after the statistics are processed. WMSPanel DOES NOT process or collect the content which is stored and transmitted on customer's server.

WMSPanel web service also requires cookies to record current session information. A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive.

WMSPanel team is the sole owner of the information collected on the WMSPanel web service. WMSPanel customer is allowed to browse the data collected on this customer's servers via all means provided by WMSPanel web service. WMSPanel team will not sell, rent or expose in any other way this information to anyone else.

### What does WMSPanel do with the information it gathers?

The information collected from customer's servers is processed and stored in order to provide the user experience described in WMSPanel promotional materials such as the web site, the team's blog and any other online and offline documentation.

WMSPanel team members will follow up by email with people who filled out contact form or signed up for account within the service. Using the email addresses provided at registration, WMSPanel team members may send personal promotional emails about new products, product enhancements and special offers. Upon written request, WMSPanel team will remove your personal information from our contacts database.

### With whom does WMSPanel share the information it gathers?

WMSPanel team does not share information collected by WMSPanel web service to outside third parties. This relates both to customer's personal information and the data collected from customer's servers.

### How does WMSPanel handle the financial information?

WMSPanel service does not store or process financial information of its customers. All information regarding customers' billing is processed and stored by FastSpring payment gateway. Please refer to [FastSpring terms of service](http://www.fastspring.com/purchasing-through-fastspring.php) to see how your financial information is handled and protected. 

### What are WMSPanel service' security measures for information it gathers and tracks?

WMSPanel takes precautions to protect your information. When WMSPanel gathers sensitive information, this information is protected both online and offline. Whenever we collect sensitive information, such as streaming media meta-data, that information is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secure way via SSL encryption. You can verify this by looking for a closed lock icon at the bottom of your web browser, or looking for "https" at the beginning of the address of the web page. WMSPanel agent is sending information using SSL encrypted connection. While we use encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted online, we also protect your information offline. Only employees are granted access to personally identifiable information. The computers and servers in which we store customer's identifiable information are kept by Rackspace hosting provider. Read [Rackspace security management ](http://www.rackspace.com/security/management/) web pages to learn more about how our and your data is protected. 

  
Please [contact us ](https://web.archive.org/contact) if you have any questions about this privacy statement. [Read our terms of service ](https://web.archive.org/terms_of_service) to get familiar with terms of using the WMSPanel service.
