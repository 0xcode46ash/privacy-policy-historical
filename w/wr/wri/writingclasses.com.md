# Our Privacy Policy at WritingClasses.com

What do we do with the information you provide to us?  
 _Hopefully get you into the writing class that's right for you..._

None of the information from registering will be sold or made available to other parties. Your name and address will be kept only for internal use by Gotham Writers' Workshop. You may receive announcements by e-mail so that we can keep you informed of new class offerings and special events. If at any time you would like us to delete your information from our database, feel free to contact us. Please be sure to include your full name and address so we can identify you correctly.
