# Wildlife Reserves Singapore

Thank you for reading our privacy policy statement (“Policy”).

  1. If you are only browsing this website, we do not capture any data that enables us to identify you individually.
  2. If you choose to send us an e-mail or make an application through this website for which you provide us with your personal information, we will keep such information confidential and retain only as necessary.
  3. Appropriate security safeguards are in place to protect your personal data. Only authorized employee will have access to your personal information, which may be used for the purpose of serving you better or improving our website.
  4. We will also not disclose, transfer, share, sell or rent personally identifiable information to any third party. However, we may send you certain communications relating to our products and services, announcements or special promotions. You may opt out from receiving such communications by replying to the e-mail with “unsubscribe” or by such other means prescribed in the communications. 
  5. This website may contain links to third party sites which are not controlled by us. We are not responsible for the contents, privacy practices or other operating procedures of these other websites. We encourage you to consult the privacy notices of those websites.
  6. We may amend this Policy from time to time and will post any such amendments on this website so as to keep you updated. 
  7. By accessing this website and any of its pages you are agreeing to the terms set out above.
