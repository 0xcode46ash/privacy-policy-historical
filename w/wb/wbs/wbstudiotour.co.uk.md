## Overview

Welcome to the Warner Bros. Group Privacy Policy.  This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding information collected, used, disclosed and otherwise processed when you visit a Warner Group website or use other Warner Group products and services, why we collect this information, how we safeguard it and the choices you can make about the way such information is collected, used and disclosed. Together we will refer to such websites, products and services as the “Sites”.  By providing us with personal information, you signify your consent to this Privacy Policy.  This Privacy Policy applies regardless of the means or device you use to access the Sites.

The Sites are operated by Warner Bros. Technical Operations Inc. (a Delaware, USA corporation) on behalf of Warner Bros. Entertainment Inc. (a wholly owned subsidiary of Time Warner Inc., a Delaware, U.S.A. corporation), and Warner Bros. Studios Leavesden Limited (a company incorporated in England) ("we," "us", "our"). The companies listed in this paragraph and in the attached [list](http://www.warnerbros.co.uk/privacylist.html) are all part of the Warner Group.
