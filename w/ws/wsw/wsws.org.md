# Privacy Statement - World Socialist Web Site

## Privacy Statement

The _World Socialist Web Site_  respects your privacy. You can visit our website without giving us any information about yourself, but sometimes we do need information to provide the services that you request.

The _World Socialist Web Site_  does not distribute, sell or rent any information collected on this site. Any information you provide will be held with the utmost care and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. If you have any questions, please feel free to call or email us at [webmaster@wsws.org](mailto:%20webmaster@wsws.org).
