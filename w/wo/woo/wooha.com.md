# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy - July 1, 2004**

We take protecting your privacy very seriously here at "WOOHA" Sell It Online, LLC. Your personal information is ALWAYS kept confidential. The information that we collect from our customers is done so for tracking and customer satisfaction purposes ONLY. As a customer, you entrust your information to us and expect that information to remain secure. This is why we will NEVER sell or disclose your information, email, phone number or other personal information to any third party sources.

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy please feel free to contact us via email at privacy@wooha.com to inquire further. As always, we will respond to you within 24 hours.

Thank you from the "WOOHA" Team.
