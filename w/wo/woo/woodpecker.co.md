# Privacy policy

|  Effective: January 1, 2015 Last Updated: August 10, 2015

At Woodpecker.co (henceforth also referred to as “Woodpecker”,“we,” “us,” “our”), we take great care about how our users’ personal information is used and shared, and we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how Woodpecker.co collects and uses the personal information you provide on our “Website” Woodpecker.co as well as in the “Service” we provide at https://app.woodpecker.co.  
Before you use Woodpecker, please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using or accessing the our Website and/or Service, you are accepting the practices described in our Privacy Policy and you consent that we will collect, use, and share your information in the following ways.

## WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

When subscribing on our Website, you may be asked to enter your: name and/or e-mail address. You may, however, visit our Website anonymously.  
When starting a trial of our Service, you may be asked to enter your: first name, last name and e-mail address.  
When subscribing for premium Woodpecker account, you may be asked to enter your first name, last name, e-mail address and appropriate payment information.  
When using our Service both during the trial period as well as during the premium subscription, we also receive and store any information you choose to provide us with respect to your customers (“Prospect” or “Prospects”). The Prospects’ information we receive and store typically includes names and email addresses of your customers, but will consist of any information you choose to provide us with.  


## WHAT DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION FOR?

To personalize your experience (your information helps us to better respond to your individual needs).  
To improve our website (we continually strive to improve our website offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you).  
To improve our customer service (your information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs).  
To process transactions (your information, whether public or private, will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any other company for any reason whatsoever, without your consent, other than for the express purpose of delivering the requested Service).  
To send periodic emails (the email address you provide for order processing, will only be used to send you information and updates. If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, we include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email.)  


## HOW DO WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION?

Your account is protected by a password for your privacy and security. You are responsible for preventing unauthorized access to your Woodpecker account and all the personal information by selecting and protecting your password appropriately and limiting access to your computer or device and browser by signing off after you have finished accessing your account.  
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of all personal information you provide us with.  
We offer the use of a secure server. All supplied sensitive/credit information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our payment gateway providers database only to be accessible by those authorized with special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential.  
After a transaction, your private information (credit cards, social security numbers, financials, etc.) will not be stored on our servers.  
All the data you provide us with will be processed by: Woodpecker.co LTD (sp. z o.o.) residing in Europe – Poland – Wroclaw, 31/1 Maczka STR Zip code: 52-201 in a way that is crucial to agreeing on a contract, as well as its fulfillment. Transaction data, in that personal data, can be transferred for the benefit of PayLane Sp. z o.o. located in Europe – Poland – Gdańsk at Arkońska 6/A3, zip code: 80-387, KRS: 0000227278, on the terms that it will beneficial on in service connected to order payments. A user has the right to see the content of his/her data and any corrections made to it. Sharing of information is voluntary, however, at the same time it is crucial to using the Website as well as the Service.  


## DO WE USE COOKIES?

Whenever you interact with our Website and/or Service, we automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, “cookie” information, and the page you requested. “Cookies” are identifiers we transfer to your computer or mobile device that allow us to recognize your browser or mobile device and tell us how and when pages in our Website and Service are visited by you. If you click on a link to a third party website, such third party may also transmit cookies to you. This Privacy Policy does not cover the use of cookies by any third parties.  


## DO WE DISCLOSE ANY INFORMATION TO OUTSIDE PARTIES?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe the release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.  


## CHILDREN’S ONLINE PRIVACY PROTECTION ACT COMPLIANCE

We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our Website and Service are both directed to people who are at least 18 years old or older.  


## CHANGES TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. If we decide to change our Privacy Policy, we will post these changes on this page.
