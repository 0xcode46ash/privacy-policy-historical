# WOOLWORTHS - Help

  
Woolworths.co.uk does not sell or trade any of your personal details to third parties.Woolworths.co.uk is a member of the Woolworth Group PLC. A list of companies in the Group is available on request. We, the Group, are committed to providing users with an Internet experience which respects their privacy. We adhere strictly to the requirements of the UK Data Protection Act 1998.

**Security**

Any information, including account and credit card details, entered by you during the registration/order process is encrypted by our secure server software and held by us in that way, to prevent unauthorised access to your details. 

Any information you provide to us off-line or held by us in that format, is also stored in a manner approved by Data Protection Legislation and we enforce strict internal security procedures to ensure compliance and prevent unauthorised access.

**How we may use your information**

You may, from time to time, receive other information from Woolworths.co.uk or Woolworths Group PLC perhaps as a result of your entries to competitions, promotions run by our site, or from the range of purchases you make. 

This information is used by Woolworths.co.uk and Woolworths Group PLC solely to improve the service we provide to you, such as informing you of offers or recommendations that suit the pattern of purchases you tend to make, or allowing us to make alterations to the look and feel of our site to properly reflect your changing needs and preferences. 

Other than as necessary to fulfil your order, we will not provide your personal information (i.e. information you supply that identifies you) to any third party without your consent or unless we are required to do so by law. 

In addition you will always be given the opportunity to remove your approval by 

[contacting us](https://web.archive.org/help/contactus.jhtml). We may also pass on some general statistical information about our user base, sales patterns, traffic volumes and related matters to reputable third parties but these details will not include information that personally identifies the individual user.
