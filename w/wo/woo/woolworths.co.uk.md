**WOOLWORTHS.co.uk PRIVACY POLICY**

**Who we are**

Streets Online Ltd is a member of the Woolworths Group, which includes MVC Entertainment Ltd, EUK, Woolworths plc and VCI. We, the Group, are committed to providing users with an Internet experience which respects their privacy. We adhere strictly to the requirements of the UK Data Protection Act 1998. 

Streets Online have an up-to-date entry under the Data Protection Register. This can be seen at: <http://www.dpr.gov.uk/>, when you reach this page simply use the search function provided and type 'Infront Ltd' (our registered corporate name) to locate our specific page.

**About this statement - Your consent**

The purpose of this statement is to explain to you how the Group may use your personal information. By registering as a user of the services provided by Streets Online (whether to order goods or services or otherwise) and by using the Streets Online web sites (Streetsonline.co.uk, alphabetstreet.co.uk, audiostreet.co.uk, dvdstreet.co.uk,. videostreet.co.uk, gamestreet.co.uk and informers.co.uk) generally you agree to this use. 

**Information you provide**

·       Web usage information to develop and improve our websites. 

·       Registration information to keep you informed. 

·       Name, address, e-mail, credit card, product and order details. 

·       Other data, from time to time, to help us provide you with improved products and services.

·       Cookies information and information regarding your use of the site (see below). 

**How we use personal information**

·       To process your order and administer your account (including fraud prevention). 

·       To enable us to review, develop and improve the products, services, special offers and events that we offer. 

·       To provide access to privileged areas of the website and provide additional information. 

·       To administer contests, competitions and news email service. 

·       To carry out market research and personalise your visit to our website.

·       Where you have requested it, to send you details of other Group products, offers and services by email or post that may be of interest to you. If, after you have registered, you do not want to receive marketing information from the Group, then you can unsubscribe at any time by either:

(a)  contacting the Group directly via the unsubscribe facility included in communications that may be sent to you; or

> (b)  changing your preferences in Your Account details

·       Where you have ordered Digital Services, we reserve the right to remotely access your computer in order to assess whether Digital Service content and licences have been successively delivered. We may also request a test/file or other content in order to undertake this exercise in which case we will contact you with further details nearer the time. ** __**

**Who do we pass personal information to?  
**  
We may provide information about you as follows: 

·       To employees and agents of the Group to administer any accounts, products and services provided to you by the Group now or in the future.

·       In some cases the Group may use a technical partner to conduct some processing of your personal information on its behalf, but this is always subject to strict contractual control and is in compliance with UK Data Protection Legislation.

·       Other than as necessary to fulfil your order (including in the delivery of goods and services you have ordered to you by third parties), we will not provide your personal information (i.e. information you supply that identifies you) to any third party without your consent or unless we are required to do so by law. 

·       Some general statistical information about the user base, sales patterns, traffic volumes and related matters may be passed on to reputable third parties but these details will not include information personally identifying the individual user.

·       To other companies associated with the Group so that they can send you details of their products, offers and services that may be of interest to you.  We will not pass your personal data unless you have consented to this use.  You can change your preferences via Your Account details at any time.

**Data Security**

We endeavour to take all reasonable steps to protect your personal information. All the data collected by us is stored on a secure server. The secure server software encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. 

[ **Your use of this site**

In order that we can monitor and improve the site, we may gather certain information about you when you use it, including details of your domain name and IP address, operating system, browser, version, cookie details and the web site that you visited prior to our site, this is anonymous aggregated data.  
**Cookies**

Cookies are small pieces of information stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. We use cookies to allow us to understand who has seen which pages and advertisements, to determine how frequently particular pages are visited and to determine the most popular areas of our website. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, though you do not have to. If you wish to disable cookies please refer to your user manual although please remember that by disabling cookies you may not be able to place an order on this website.

For more information about cookies and your rights, please refer to: [www.allaboutcookies.org](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/)

**3rd party information**

We may supplement the information that you provide to us with information that we receive from third parties, for example, collected from other members of your household. 

**Access to your information**

You may access and update all of your data via: [https:www.woolworths.co.uk/common/account/secure/index.jhtml](https://web.archive.org/web/20040607085324id_/http%3A//www.woolworths.co.uk/ww/content/www.woolworths.co.uk/common/account/secure/index.jhtml)  or by updating your details in Your Account. This is password protected and you are the only person with access to this data. 

**Other links**

Please be aware that our site may link to other web sites which may be accessed through our site. We are not responsible for the data policies, content or security of these linked web sites.
