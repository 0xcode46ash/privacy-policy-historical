## Privacy Policy

### **Personal Information**

We do not collect personal information. The server collects statistical data as every server - IP, OS type, Browser type, language etc. We use the server statistics only for statistical purposes. If you purchase our product(s) or service(s) - we will use your personal information only for communication purposes.

### **Personal information / credit card security**

We don't sell directly, so - we will never see your credit card info. The payment process is secured by the payment service provider.

We use your addresses only for communication between us (as provider/publisher) and you (as customer/client). 

### **Email security**

We do not send unsolicited email. We only write you if you write us. We do not collect email addresses. There are 2 possible situations we can have your email address - if you are our customer and/or if you contact us providing your email address. If you purchase our products or services, we will use the email address provided in the purchase process for sending you important nnotes about updates, upgrades and bugfixes. If you give us your email address when asking question - we will use this email to answer you. We never give your email address to third party.

### Cookies

A cookie is a small piece of information that is sent to your browser when you access a site. There are two kinds of cookies, session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie is a line of text that is stored temporarily in your computer's memory. Because a session cookie is never saved, it is erased from the memory of your computer as soon as you close your browser. A persistent cookie is a more permanent line of text that gets saved by your browser to a file on your hard drive.

This site uses session cookies only. We do not use persistent cookies.

You do not need to have session cookies enabled to view static web content on www.wonderwebware.com. In other words -- if you disable cookies in your browser you will still access this site. 

We has set our software so that your browser will only return cookie information to the domain where the cookie originated (in this case, wonderwebware.com). No other site can request this information. Note: Regardless of the particular uses for cookies on wonderwebware web sites, we will not share any cookie information with any third parties.
