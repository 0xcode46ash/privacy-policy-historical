# Worldwide Capital - Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy

Confidentiality extends to all the information that our company has received about users, including any information received during their visit to our website.  
 **  
Personal Information**

Worldwide Capital obtains personal information about you when you register on our website, when you contact our support team and when you are on our website.

When you register at Worldwide Capital, we ask for your name, country, and other optional data. Along with your answer to a secret question, all this information may be needed to restore access to your account in case you lose or forget your password. We automatically receive and record technical information from your browser: IP address, cookies and the page you request.

 **Cookies Information**

Worldwide Capital may write cookies to your computer and subsequently use them. You can delete your cookies following the instructions in your browser.

 **Changings in Privacy Policy**

Worldwide Capital may amend the Privacy Policy without any special notice. The amended Privacy Policy shall come into effect from the moment it is deployed.
