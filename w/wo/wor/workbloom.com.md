# Privacy Policy

|  |  **Editor's Note**  
---  
We need your feedback.  If you have suggestions on how we can improve, if you see a typo, or if there is a broken link, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20110314181805id_/http%3A//workbloom.com/contact-us.htm).  
   
  
   
   
   
   
|  Privacy Policy  
---  
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click **[ here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp)**.  
   
   
   
   
|  |  ** Privacy Contact Information**  
---  
If you have any questions, concerns, or comments about our privacy policy, you may contact us by e-mail at [info@workbloom.com](mailto:info@workbloom.com). 

  We reserve the right to make changes to this policy.  Any changes to this policy will be posted.
