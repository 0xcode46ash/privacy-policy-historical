# Privacy policy

At World First we pride ourselves on offering the highest levels of security, transparency and integrity when it comes to protecting our clients' interests.

That's why World First UK Ltd ("World First UK") and World First Markets Ltd ("World First Markets") are both registered as Data Controllers with the Information Commission Office.  To make sure, please feel free to check our registration [here](http://www.ico.org.uk/esdwebpages/search/).

World First UK and World First Markets are part of the World First Group companies ("the Group") which also includes World First USA, Inc. (based in the USA) and World First Pty Ltd (based in Australia).

This policy applies to information that the World First Group may collect about visitors to our website, companies and individuals who register for our services and continue to make use of our services and any other person who corresponds with us over the phone or in writing.

Our aim is to give you reassurance that any personal information will be used fairly and only for the purposes for which it was collected.

Please review this Privacy Policy prior to registering an account and providing us with your personal information.  Client confidentiality is our main concern and through good practices we strive to ensure that:

  * only information which is necessary for adherence to the regulations and/or improvement of our service is collected;
  * personal information is collected fairly; and
  * personal information is stored securely.



The Data Protection Act 1998 ("DPA") regulates the collection, processing and storing or personal data.  When your information is processed by a data controller you are referred to as a data subject. 

If you have any further questions about data protection with World First, please don't hestiate to get in touch with your dedicated dealer and we will do our level best to alleviate any of your concerns.
