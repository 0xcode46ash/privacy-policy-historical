# WOMF™ Help - Privacy Policy

## Policies

  1. WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd and its licensors know and respect that you care how information about you is used and shared; WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly.
  2. By registering on the WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd websites, you are accepting the Conditions and practices described.
  3. WOMF™ is an online application that helps people find local services they can trust by soliciting and aggregating reviews. To accomplish this, WOMF™ users write reviews about local services that are posted to the WOMF™ Site. These reviews can then be accessed and read by other WOMF™ users. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, a "user" is defined as someone using the WOMF™ Site, and a "friend" is defined as someone you have explicitly invited to share reviews with you.
  4. To fulfill the purpose of the WOMF™ Site, WOMF™ needs to display a limited amount of personal information, so that users are able to evaluate the trustworthiness of a particular review.
  5. By using WOMF™, you permit the collection and use of your personal information as outlined below in the Privacy Policy. You should visit this page periodically to review any changes to the Privacy Policy.
  6. When/if you voluntarily disclose personally identifiable information (e.g., user name, e-mail address) via reviews, messages, forums, postings, or in other areas of the WOMF™ site, that information, along with any substantive information disclosed in your communication, may be collected and correlated and used by third parties and may result in unsolicited messages from other posters or third parties. These activities are beyond the control of WOMF™. Please do not post any personal information on the WOMF™ site or in other areas that you expect to keep private.
  7. As a registered WOMF™ user, you can modify your personal information at any time by accessing the "Registration" section of the site.
  8. Upon your request, WOMF™ will remove you and your personal information from the site; however, it may be impossible to remove your entry without some residual information being retained by WOMF™. Registered users who wish to delete their registration should do so through the local radio station website, hosting the WOMF™ site. 
  9. Information WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd collects: 
    1. User submitted account information such as name, age and email address to identify you and may send you notifications related to the use of the service.
    2. User submitted location information such as city, state, or area code.
    3. User submitted reviews, opinions, experiences and ratings of local businesses and service providers.
    4. Advertiser submitted advertisements, photos, logo's, specials, reviews, opinions and incentives to contact their local businesses
    5. E-Lert collects words nominated by you for monitoring of the site.
    6. Some information collected is not explicitly submitted by the user as WOMF™ logs your browser-type related information, IP address, requested URL, referring URL, and timestamp.
  10. How WOMF™ Uses Your Information 
    1. Information such as your full name and any personally identifiable information you included in your reviews at the time of review placement may be shown to other users primarily in conjunction with reviews you have contributed.
    2. WOMF™ may use your email address to send you updates or news regarding the WOMF™ Site, but you may choose not to receive email of this type by accessing the "Registration" tab of the WOMF™ site and updating your notification settings.
    3. Browser-type related information, IP address, requested URL, referring URL, and timestamp data help WOMF™ diagnose problems with the WOMF™ server, administer WOMF™, and otherwise provide the highest possible level of service to you. WOMF™ may also use this information to perform statistical analyses of user behavior and characteristics in order to measure interest in and use of the various areas of the site and to inform advertisers of such information as well as the number of users that have been exposed to or clicked on, their advertising banners.
  11. How E-Lert Uses Your Information 
    1. Information such as your full name and any personally identifiable information you included in your comments at the time of comment placement may be shown to other users primarily in conjunction with comment you have contributed.
    2. E-Lert may use your email address to send you updates or news regarding the WOMF™ Site, but you may choose not to receive email of this type by accessing the "Registration" tab of the WOMF™ site and updating your notification settings.
    3. Browser-type related information, IP address, requested URL, referring URL, and timestamp data help E-Lert diagnose problems with the E-Lert server, administer E-Lert, and otherwise provide the highest possible level of service to you. E-Lert may also use this information to perform statistical analyses of user behavior and characteristics in order to measure interest in and use of the various areas of the site and to inform advertisers of such information as well as the number of users that have been exposed to or clicked on, their advertising banners.
  12. Transfer of Information 
    1. Transfer of Information
    2. WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd will disclose information about individual users to governmental or judicial authorities or law enforcement agencies, or to other individuals or entities in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal processes.
    3. WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd may share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities or suspected fraud, or to enforce or apply the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy.
    4. WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd reserves the right to transfer your personal information in the event of a transfer of ownership of WOMF™ or E-Lert, such as an acquisition by or merger with another company. In such an event, WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
    5. WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd will notify you when information about you may be provided to third parties in ways other than explained above, and you will have the option to prevent such information sharing.
  13. Links 
    1. The WOMF™ Site may contain links to third party websites to which WOMF™ has no affiliation. WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd do not share your personal information with those websites and is not responsible for their privacy practices. WOMF™ suggests you read the privacy polices on these third party websites.
  14. Use of Cookies 
    1. Like most websites, WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd require cookies to function properly. Cookies are small text files that reside on a user's computer and identify you as a unique user. WOMF™ uses cookies to refine the WOMF™ Site, E-Lert service and simplify the user experience. For example, the use of cookies allows registered users to enter the WOMF™ Site and E-Lert service without explicitly typing in their email address and password every time. You should be aware that WOMF™ and E-Lert cannot control the use of cookies by advertisers or any information advertisers may gather from their use of cookies. If you do not want information collected through the use of cookies, there is a simple procedure in most browsers that allows you to deny or accept the cookie feature; however, you should note that cookies may be necessary to provide you certain features (e.g., customized delivery of information) available on the WOMF™ site and/or E-Lert service.
  15. Security 
    1. Your WOMF™ membership and/or E-Lert account is password-protected.
    2. WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd try very hard to protect the WOMF™ and/or E-Lert users' information. WOMF™ and E-Lert use industry standard measures to protect your information that is stored within the respective database.
    3. WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd limit the access to your information to those employees who need access to perform their job function such as customer service personnel.
    4. If you have any questions about the security at E-Lert and/or the WOMF™ Site, please contact WOMF™.
    5. Although WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd take appropriate measures to safeguard against unauthorized disclosures of information, WOMF™ cannot assure you that personally identifiable information that WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd collects will never be disclosed in a manner that is inconsistent with this Privacy Policy.
    6. You hereby acknowledge that WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd is not responsible for any intercepted information sent via the Internet, and you hereby release WOMF™ and E-Lert from any and all claims arising out of or related to the use of intercepted information in any unauthorized manner.
  16. Children 
    1. Children are not permitted to use this site.
    2. WOMF™ does not knowingly solicit information from any child. Should WOMF™ find that a child has accessed the Site their access will be terminated.
  17. Terms and Modifications to this Privacy Policy 
    1. By using the WOMF™ Site, you signify your agreement to the terms of the Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, you should not use the site. WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd may modify this Privacy Policy at any time. If a material change is made to this Privacy Policy and/or the way WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd uses WOMF Australia Pty Ltd & WOMF Elert Pty Ltd users' personally identifiable information, then WOMF™ will post a prominent notice of the nature of such change on the first page of this Privacy Policy. You are responsible for reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically to ensure that you are aware of any changes to it. Your continued use of the site indicates your assent to any changes to this Privacy Policy.
  18. Security 
    1. This site is encrypted with Comodo 128 bit ssl

    1. a. All transactions are processed in local currency. Please see your regions local WOMF™ site for further information.
