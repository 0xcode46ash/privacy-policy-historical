# Wotif.com: Privacy

## What Information We Collect

In an effort to keep our service as simple and easy as possible we do not create, use or store profiles of our customers. We only record PII and other information that we reasonably require to do business with you. On all Wotif pages that collect PII, we specifically describe what information is required in order to provide you with the product or service you request.

Wotif collects PII when you:

  * _Make a booking_  
Your full name, email address and phone number are taken each time you make a booking with Wotif. We will also collect credit card information, including card number, card type, cardholder name and expiry date to pay for accommodation purchased through Wotif.
  * _Tick 'Remember my details for next time' on booking page_  
Your full name, email address, phone number, credit card type, and name on card are retained when you tick 'Remember my details for next time' whilst making a booking.
  * _Subscribe to our newsletter_  
Wotif collects email addresses from people who subscribe to the Wotif newsletter by either entering their email address in the "Newsletter" section of the site or by opting to be sent the newsletter at the time of making a booking.
  * _Surveys, promotions and competition information_  
Occasionally, Wotif may offer site visitors or newsletter receipients an opportunity to participate in a survey, promotion or competition. PII collected by Wotif when you participate in a survey, promotion or competition may include your full name, email address, telephone number and street address.
  * _Contact Wotif.com_  
Wotif collects PII such as your full name, email address and/or telephone number whenever you submit a query or suggestion, or request assistance with a booking.



Other information we collect:

  * _Statistical information_  
When you visit the Wotif website we record general information about your visit for statistical purposes. These statistics do not contain any PII. 
  * _Cookies_  
We may, from time to time, use "cookies" to store your preferences, record session information and collect information on how you visit and access our Web pages. We collect information on the Web pages visited by our customers in order to help us to continue improving our services. 

"Cookies" are pieces of information that our Web page transfers to your computer's hard disk for record-keeping purposes. Cookies can make the Web more useful by storing information about your preferences on a particular site. The use of cookies is an industry standard and many Web sites use them to provide useful features for you. Cookies in and of themselves do not personally identify you, only your computer.




## How We Use Your Information

Wotif uses your PII only for the purpose of responding to and fulfilling your requests for our products and services. Information collected on Wotif may be used to:

  * _Purchase Wotif products or services_  
Information collected when you purchase from Wotif is used to charge your credit card and deliver your booking confirmation electronically. We may also contact you about your stay for customer service purposes or to make any changes or cancellations to your booking.
  * _Display your details when you next book with us_  
Wotif will only display your details if you have ticked 'Remember my details for next time' whilst making a booking with us. This is done to reduce the information you need to type when you next make a booking. Your full name, email address, phone number, credit card type, and name on card are the only details remembered for next time.
  * _Send newsletter email_  
Wotif will only send newsletters to people that have opted in to receive them. Our newsletters provide information about the latest deals available on the web site and occassionally offer incentives for completing surveys or participating in competitions or promotions.
  * _Respond to your questions or suggestions_  
We will use your email address and/or telephone number to contact you when you submit a question or suggestion.



## Who We Share Your Information With

Wotif does not disclose any information about customer bookings to anyone except in the following circumstances:

  * _Accommodation suppliers_  
Wotif provides the guest name, telephone number and email address for each booking made to the relevant accommodation supplier. Our accommodation suppliers require this information for the booking and if they need to contact you about the booking. Although Wotif offers accommodation from reputable accommodation suppliers, we have not placed specific limitations on these suppliers from using or disclosing your information without your permission. Therefore, we encourage you to review the privacy policies of any suppliers whose accommodation you purchase on Wotif. 
  * _Legal obligations_  
Wotif may be required by law to disclose information provided by you to Wotif for the purposes of obtaining products or services from Wotif. Wotif may also disclose information against someone who poses a threat to Wotif's interests (such as customer fraud) or whose activities could cause harm to others.



## How You Control and Access Your Information

Wotif believes that site visitors and customers should have control over the collection and use of their PII. You have control and access to your information as follows:

  * _Newsletter subscription and unsubscription_  
If at anytime you would like to either change your email address with us or unsubscribe from our newsletter you can: 
    1. Go to the "Newsletter" section of our site from the home page and follow the instructions to change your email address or unsubscribe; or 
    2. Send us an email to [newsletter@wotif.com](mailto:newsletter@wotif.com?subject=\[newsletter%20subscription%20changes\]) advising that you would like to unsubscribe or change your email address. Be sure to include details of your old and new email addresses for changes.
  * _Booking information_  
If at anytime you would like to alter the PII you provided to Wotif in your booking, please [contact us](http://www.wotif.com/ContactUs.jsp).
  * _Remember my details for next time_  
If at anytime you would like us to discontinue remembering your details for next time, you may untick 'Remember my detail for next time' the next time you make a booking or [contact us](http://www.wotif.com/ContactUs.jsp).
  * _General_  
If at anytime you wish to enquire about any of your PII that is stored by Wotif please [contact us](http://www.wotif.com/ContactUs.jsp).



## How We Protect Your Information

To ensure the confidentiality of your PII Wotif uses leading security technology and procedures that are regularly reviewed and updated. 

Wotif has also obtained a digital certificate from Thawte Server CA (part of the VeriSign group), an industry leader in the protection and management of data transmission and storage. 

Our systems are configured with industry standard data encryption and firewall technology. When you send PII to Wotif over the Internet, your data is protected by Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to ensure safe transmission.
