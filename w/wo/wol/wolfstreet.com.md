# Privacy Policy | Wolf Street

The information WOLF STREET (the site) gathers when you interact with it falls into two categories:

**1) Personal information** : The email address and name you enter when you log into the site to post a comment or when you sign up for email updates. If you wish to not reveal your name, youâ€™re encouraged to sign in with an alias and an anonymous email address.

**2) Non-personal information** , such as tracking information collected by us (to verify login and user ID across various pages) and third parties (for advertisement purposes). This includes information about the device with which you access the site, such as the IP address, browser type, and browser language.

The site uses cookies and web beacons for log-in purposes, to verify user ID across different pages, and to help with managing third-party ads. Third-party advertisers use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to gather user information across the Internet and serve up personalized ads.

The site also uses third-party services, such as Google Analytics, that measure and analyze web traffic and provide essential analytical data, for example, pageviews per day and percentage of visitors located in a certain country. These analytical services use cookies and other technologies to collect the traffic data.

You can disable cookies on your browser. If you do, the site will function properly, but you cannot log in and post a comment. For more information on how to block cookies or manage them in other ways, please see: <http://www.allaboutcookies.org/manage-cookies/>
