# Privacy policy - Wowroms.com

####  Privacy Policy 

all the information we collected in the website wowroms.com will be kept confidential and will not sold or used in any way.

**collection and use of information**  
we will collect if you become member your email adresse and country and we will use your email to send newsletters in the future.  
Your e-mail address will never be given or sold to a third party and will only be viewed by wowroms.com.

**Third Party Cookies**  
our third-party advertiser may recognize or place a unique cookie on your browser.

**Changes to this policy**  
We may modify this privacy policy at any time, so please review it frequently. Changes and clarifications, we will notify you here that it has been changed.
