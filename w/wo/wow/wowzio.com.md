# 

Wowzio has established this privacy policy to explain to you, our users, how your information is protected, collected and used, which may be updated by Wowzio from time to time. Wowzio will provide notice of materially significant changes to this privacy policy by posting notice on the Wowzio site. 

## 1\. Protecting your privacy

  * We don't share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. 
  * We don't send you unsolicited communications for marketing purposes. 
  * We do not post your email anywhere on our site, to help prevent 3rd party harvesting and spam. Please make sure that you do not post your email or any other contact information in your profile. 
  * Account information is password-protected. Keep your password safe. 
  * Wowzio, or people who post on Wowzio, may provide links to third party websites, which may have different privacy practices. We are not responsible for, nor have any control over, the privacy policies of those third party websites, and encourage all users to read the privacy policies of each and every website visited. 



## 2\. Data we collect

  * We collect your email address at the time of registration, for purposes such as login, sending self-publishing and confirmation emails, authenticating user accounts, providing subscription email services, registering for forums, etc. 
  * We may collect personal information if you provide it in profile, registration, feedback or comments, post it on our interactive forums, or if you contact us directly. Please do not post or provide any personal information on Wowzio's that you expect to keep private. 
  * Our web logs collect standard web log entries for each page served, including your IP address, page URL, and timestamp. Web logs help us to diagnose problems with our server, to administer the Wowzio site, and to otherwise provide our service to you. 
  * We use [Google Analytics](http://www.google.com/analytics/) for tracking and reporting. For more information, please see <http://www.google.com/analytics/>



## 3\. Data we store

  * We store blog posts as well as thumbnails of images in blog posts, to present and publish them in our widgets. If you are a blog owner, and would like us to stop doing this, please send us an email, clearly identifying yourself to info [at] wowzio [dot] com. Once we have verified ownership of the blog, we will deactivate all widgets related to your blog, and stop further distribution of your blog's content. 
  * All feedback is stored in our database, even after "deletion," and may be archived elsewhere. 
  * The commenting system we use is from [js-kit](http://js-kit.com/), comments you leave on Wowzio are stored on js-kit's servers. 
  * Our web logs and other records are stored for the past 6 months. 
  * Registered users can access and update their account information through the account page. 
  * Although we make good faith efforts to store the information in a secure operating environment that is not available to the public, we cannot guarantee complete security. 



## 4\. Archiving and display of Wowzio pages by search engines and other sites

  * Search engines and other sites not affiliated with Wowzio - including [archive.org](http://archive.org/), [google.com](http://www.google.com/), and [search.yahoo.com](http://search.yahoo.com/) \- archive or otherwise make available Wowzio's pages and content.



## 5\. Circumstances in which Wowzio may release information

  * Wowzio may disclose information about its users if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to respond to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal process. 
  * Wowzio may also disclose information about its users to law enforcement officers or others, in the good faith belief that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to enforce our [Terms of Use](https://web.archive.org/docs/tos), respond to claims that any posting or other content violates the rights of third-parties; or protect the rights, property, or personal safety of Wowzio, its users or the general public. 



## 6\. International Users

  * By visiting our web site and providing us with data, you acknowledge and agree that due to the international dimension of Wowzio we may use the data collected in the course of our relationship for the purposes identified in this policy or in our other communications with you, including the transmission of information outside your resident jurisdiction. In addition, please understand that such data may be stored on servers located in the United States. By providing us with your data, you consent to the transfer of such data.



## 7\. Feedback

  * We welcome your feedback on this document on our [feedback page](https://web.archive.org/docs/feedback).
