# Privacy Statement

|  The privacy of our visitors to Words of Wisdom, http://www.wow4u.com is important to us. This information will help you make an informed decision about sharing your personal information with us. At www.wow4u.com, we recognize that privacy of your personal information is important. Here is information on what types of personal information we receive and collect when you use visit www.wow4u.com, and how we safeguard your information. **Personal Information**  
At www.wow4u.com, collection of personal information from visitors is solely done on a voluntary basis. **Cookies**  
Cookies may be used only to help with your online experience where the only purpose is to tell the web server that your return visit is recognized and to tell the web server that you have returned to visit. You have the option of disabling cookies by modifying your web browser fully within your own control.   
Some third party advertisements, like Google for example, use cookies to serve ads on this site. **Release Of Information**  
If www.wow4u.com is sold, the opt in information collected through your voluntary disclosure may be transferred to the new owner only as part of the sale and only as a method to ensure that the service you expect is continued. In that event, you will receive notice of such sale and we will seek agreement to the best of our ability that the new owner respects and honors opt-out requests. **Newsletter List**   
All emails registered for our newsletter is kept private. The email list is never sold or distributed to anyone. The list is maintained by Google groups and is a private list. The list is used solely for the purpose to send out our newsletter weekly. We never send the newsletter to anyone who has not requested it. **Questions on our Practices**   
If you have any questions regarding our privacy practices, please feel free to email us at: wowmotivation XX gmail.com (delete spaces and XX, insert @ symbol) **Updates**  
We reserve the right to update this policy and if we do so, we will indicate on our site that the policy has been updated. We would encourage you to review our policy periodically. **Agreement To Terms**  
If you do not agree with the terms as set out herein to www.wow4u.com's Privacy Policy as posted hereon, please refrain from using this site or any of it's offered services. 

Your use of this site and any of its services constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Policy.
