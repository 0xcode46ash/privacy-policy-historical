# Wisevid - Privacy Policy

**The purpose of this page**

This page explains Wisevid's commitment to your privacy and to the protection of your personal data collected and processed when you use the Website, under the terms and conditions set out in the [General Terms of Use](http://www.wisevid.com/terms) page.

**Data that we collect**

We collect and subsequently process the data that you voluntarily provide on the [registration form](http://www.wisevid.com/register) of the website including your user name, a valid email address, and your date of birth. The only Data automatically collected by the website is your IP address.

**Why we process**

Data indicated on the Website as mandatory is required in order to use the website's features (video uploads, comments, playlists, etc). 

**Who has my data besides Wisevid?**

Wisevid does not share or sell your data what so ever.

**Data retention**

Data is stored on the website's host and is kept only as long as necessary for the purposes set out above. After that point, data is kept only for statistical and backup purposes and shall not be used for any other reason.

**Youth Restrictions and Parental Control**

Children under the age of 13 are not permitted to register with Wsievid. 

**Your rights**

You are entitled to access and correct your Data by requesting to do so via the Contact us section, or manually doing so if possible on the My Account section.
