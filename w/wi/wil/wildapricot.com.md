# Integrated, Web-based Association Membership Management Solution: Free Trial!

### Privacy Policy

We have a firm commitment to safeguarding the privacy of our customers.

We collect the e-mail addresses and other personal information during Wild Apricot account sign-ups, from email inquiries, forum registrations and forum postings, blog comments. The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web pages and the quality of our service, or to respond to inquiries, and is not shared with or sold to other organizations for commercial purposes, except to provide products or services you’ve requested, when we have your permission, or when it is mandatory to to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by law.

We do not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other companies except to provide products or services you’ve requested or when we have your permission. 

### INFORMATION GATHERING AND USAGE

Bonasource Inc. only uses your personal information to conduct business with you. Your personally identifiable information will not be rented, sold or shared with any third party except as otherwise agreed by you.

When you register for a Wild Apricot account we ask for information such as your name, organization name, and email address. Also, you can import your existing database of members as well as add members manually or have them apply for membership online. If you have events open to the public, registration information is collected for your internal use. When registering for events, your site visitors will provide attendee information and for payment information according to the selected payment method (e.g. Paypal online payment) 

We do not contact your members or event registrants except as related to operating the Wild Apricot system on your behalf, for example: event notifications and reminders, membership renewals, security and other notifications.

By opening an account, you agree that, with respect to other users’ personal information that you obtain through Wild Apricot or through a Wild Apricot -related communication or Wild Apricot-facilitated transaction, Wild Apricot hereby grants to you a license to use such information only for Wild Apricot-related communications that are not unsolicited commercial messages. We do not tolerate spam. Therefore, without limiting the foregoing, you are not licensed to add the name of someone who has registered for membership or for an event to your mailing list (email or physical mail) without their express consent.

Bonasource Inc. uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provision, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement.

Bonasource Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices of each Wild Apricot website setup by each Account Holders. It is up to the Account Holder to protect the privacy of their members and event registrants.

### COOKIES

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive. Cookies are required to use our service. We use cookies to record user session information. 

### CLIENT DATA STORAGE

Bonasource respects the intellectual property rights of others. Whereas Bonasource Inc. owns the data storage, databases and all rights to the Wild Apricot application, you retain all rights to your data and we will never sell or share your data with any third-parties, contact your members directly, or use your data for our own business advantage. We will also not utilize your data in our databases to compete with you, market to your members, advertise to, or contact them for any other means of profit. We respect the privacy of our clients, and the privacy of their data. 

### SECURITY

The security of your personal information is important to us. The Service has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. We do not store credit card information in our database. We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on our Web site, you can email us at support @ wildapricot.com.

We may record your computer’s IP address with your order to help us identify fraudulent credit card use. We may share this information with the financial institution that issued the credit card with which an order was placed, or with law enforcement authorities if we determine, or are notified, that the use of the credit card was fraudulent.

### DISCLOSURE

Bonasource Inc. may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as when it believes in good faith that the law or legal process requires it or when your actions violate the Terms of Service.

### CHANGES

Bonasource Inc. may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about material changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the account holder’s email address or by placing a prominent notice on our website. 

### LINKS TO OTHER SITES

This site may contain links to other sites. Bonasource Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such other Web sites.

### QUESTIONS

Any questions about this Privacy Policy should be addressed to support @ wildapricot.com
