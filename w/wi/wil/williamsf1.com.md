# Privacy

AT&T Williams Privacy Policy 

  * 1\. General Information 
    * 1.1 The website at [http://www.attwilliams.com](http://www.attwilliams.com/) (the "Website") is managed Engineering Limited ["AT&T Williams"]. AT&T Williams is responsible Website and this Privacy Statement applies to that Website only.
  * 2\. AT&T Williams Privacy Statement 
    * 2.1 AT&T Williams and its service providers will use any personal information collected from the Website in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998 ('the Act'). Further information about the Act can be found at the website of the Information Commissioner (www.informationcommissioner.gov.uk).
    * 2.2 All information will be kept secure and will be treated as confidential. This personal information will only ever be used for communicating with you, about AT&T Williams and we will not sell your information to any third parties. 
    * 2.3 When you visit the Website, a small text file known as a cookie will be downloaded onto your computer. This is a common practice which allows AT&T Williams to provide you with a better Website by keeping track of the areas of the Website you visit, for example your areas of specific interest. A cookie may allow AT&T Williams to recognise repeat visits and recall information which you have previously supplied to AT&T Williams, such as your name. You may adjust how your computer deals with cookies using your internet browser. Disabling cookies however may mean that some functionality may be affected. For further directions on how to disable cookies please click here: http://allaboutcookies.org/manage-cookies/stop-cookies-installed.html 
  * 3\. What information do we collect and why? 
    * 3.1 The information collected on this site includes email addresses and/or other contact details and information which users voluntarily provide in order to register for one of our fan clubs, for promotions, and to receive information from AT&T Williams and/or our partners and for us to provide you with information, products or services that you request from us or which we feel may interest you. AT&T Williams will not disclose voluntarily-provided details to third parties or use the information in any other way without prior consent.
  * 4\. Your rights 
    * 4.1 You have the right to access your information held by AT&T Williams and where necessary, to have it amended. For more details, please contact: Data Protection [Comms Office, Williams F1, Grove, Wantage, Oxon. OX12 0DQ]. You can also request not to receive marketing information from AT&T Williams by sending a blank email to [enquiries@williamsf1.com].
  * 5\. Links to other websites 
    * 5.1 The Website contains links to other sites that are completely unrelated to AT&T Williams. This privacy statement does not apply to these other sites nor are we responsible for the content and practices of thsse websites.
  * 6\. Changes to our privacy statement 
    * 6.1 This privacy statement may change at any time, so you may wish to check it each time you visit the Website.
  * 7\. Data Controller 
    * 7.1 The Data Controller for the purposes of the Act is AT&T Williams [Comms Office, Williams F1, Grove, Wantage, Oxon. OX12 0DQ].
