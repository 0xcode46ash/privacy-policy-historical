# William Hill Poker - Privacy Policy

The following describes the information gathering and dissemination practices for the web sites operated by William Hill Credit Limited (www.williamhillpoker.com). Your data will be processed by William Hill Credit Limited and by other companies in the William Hill Group. Please note that this site contains links to other sites and that William Hill is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such sites. By using the William Hill web sites you acknowledge that you have read the terms of this Privacy Policy and that you consent to the use of your personal information for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. 

If you do not wish to provide the personal information on the basis set out in this Privacy Policy you should not complete the personal details in the spaces provided. However, in that case, we regret that you will not be able to make any further use of the William Hill web sites. 

**What type of information we collect and how it is used :**

William Hill collects personal information (name, address, email address, etc.) during the registration process. Your personal information is used only for demographic statistics and to display appropriate individualised advertisements and content. 

William Hill may periodically send members written communications to announce important service changes, new features, technical issue updates and news about other William Hill products and services. If you do not wish to receive such written communications from us please notify us by email at [customerservices@williamhill.co.uk](mailto:customerservices@williamhill.co.uk)

William Hill will not send you unsolicited information, including email, regarding any third party commercial offers or advertisements. 

Emails and other contacts between you and our Customer Services Department are logged and processed through RightNow contact management solution. RightNow provide a secure hosted environment for all contact data. The data is not accessible to or shared with, any parties external to William Hill. RightNow can be found at [www.rightnow.com](http://www.rightnow.com/)

**How we protect your information :**

William Hill keeps all of your personal information private and does not provide or sell this information to any third party. Personal information collected by William Hill is stored in secure operating environments that are not accessible by the public. 

William Hill will not disclose your personal information unless acting under a good faith belief that such action is necessary to : 

  * conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process  
Or 
  * act to protect the interests of its members or other qualified bodies 

William Hill complies with the United Kingdom Data Protection Act 1998 and the Data Protection Principles set out in that Act in the collection and processing of personal information. 

**Contacting us :**

If at any time you believe that William Hill has not adhered to these principles, please notify us by email at [customerservices@williamhill.co.uk](mailto:customerservices@williamhill.co.uk) and we will use all commercially reasonable efforts to promptly determine and correct the problem. 

This privacy statement applies specifically to the [www.williamhillpoker.com](http://www.williamhillpoker.com/) web site. Questions regarding this statement should be directed to [customerservices@williamhill.co.uk](mailto:customerservices@williamhill.co.uk)
