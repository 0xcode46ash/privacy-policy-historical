# Will Bontrager Software LLC Privacy Policy

###  Will Bontrager Software LLC Privacy Policy 

Will Bontrager Software, LLC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to privacy. 

The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the web sites maintained by Will Bontrager Software, LLC (the "Web Site" or "Web Sites"). 

**Links**

The Web Sites contain links to other web sites, which may be information, advertising, or other links. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. 

We only recommend things we believe in. As remuneration for the time and research involved to provide quality links, we generally use affiliate links when we can. Whenever we link to something not our own, you should assume they are affiliate links or that we benefit in some way. 

**Third Party Cookies**

Third parties may be placing and reading cookies on your browser, or using web beacons to collect information, in the course of ads or other content being served on the web sites. Will Bontrager Software, LLC has no authority over, and can not control, how third parties use cookies or information gathered therewith. Most browsers have options allowing users to block or otherwise manage cookies. 

**Ads**

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit the Web Sites. These companies may use information about your visits to the Web Sites and other sites on the Internet in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here. 

**Children**

The Web Sites are designed for the use of adults. Any children the Web Sites must be accompanied by responsible adults and any interaction with the Web Sites must be performed by adults. 

**Data Collection On The Web Sites**

We may compile or mine information collected by the Web Sites for statistical, demographic, visitor usage, diagnostic, planning, administrative, content creation, anonymous statistics, site design, and/or other purposes. 

We may use cookies to customize Web Sites or provide a better user experience. 

We may keep a record of information provided during the use of forms on the Web Sites and information provided by other methods. Email addresses of form users may be used for communication initiated with the form, or to send messages or content related to the purpose of the form. Unless such is within the purpose or intent of the form, no email addresses provided on our forms are added to any of our subscription or notification lists. 

The Web Sites may collect and aggregate information, including anonymous information. Domain names, users' system data, IP addresses, and other information may be logged. When personal information is provided, that information may be logged. 

No personal information collected by the Web Sites will be voluntarily released to any person or entity not involved with the maintenance or support of any of the Web Sites, unless a Web Site is sold or ownership otherwise transferred, in which case all information and records pertaining to that Web Site may be transferred to the new owner(s). Will Bontrager Software, LLC would not be responsible for the privacy practices of the new owner(s). 

**Contact**

If you wish to unsubscribe, choose to opt-out, or wish to be removed from any Web Site's subscription or other database records, use the method provided with emails you receive or, failing that, use a contact link at Will Bontrager Software, LLC's [WillMaster.com Web Site](http://www.willmaster.com/) and provide us with all information we may require to remove you from our records. 

**Questions**

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, the practices of any of the Web Sites, or your dealings with any of the Web Sites, use a contact link at Will Bontrager Software, LLC's [WillMaster.com Web Site](http://www.willmaster.com/) and provide us with all information we may require to understand your questions or concerns and to respond to you.
