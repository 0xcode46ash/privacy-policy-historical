# Wim's BIOS Page Privacy Statement

  


[Please read our Privacy statement](https://web.archive.org/privacy.jsp)

| 

Wim's BIOS Page, and its advertisers and affiliates are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users.

Wim's BIOS Page does not collect any personally identifiable information about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address, except when you sign up for the message board. When you sign up for the message board this information will be explicitly asked and not retrieved in some without you knowing it. Most information that is requested is optional, only a nickname and a email address is required. It's your choice how much information you want to give. The information you give us will not be sold or given away to any 3rd party company, website or individual.

Wim's BIOS Page does obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information such as the Internet Service Provider you use to connect to the Internet, your browser type, or the type of computer operating system you use (Macintosh or Windows, for example). The information collected by Wim's BIOS Page is used for the purpose of targeting ads and measuring ad effectiveness on behalf of our advertisers.
