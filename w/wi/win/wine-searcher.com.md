# WineSearcher's Privacy Statement

Email addresses collected for notifying wine availability are used solely for that purpose. To receive the newsletter, enter your email address in the form on the [home page](https://web.archive.org/index.lml?ID=null).

We never spam.

Personal information collected during the registration process for the "Pro Version" is solely for legal requirements of running a service business over the Web. This information is never passed to other organizations.

The site uses cookies solely to improve usability.

All searches on the site are recorded, and can be re-used in [summarised form](https://web.archive.org/stats.lml?ID=null).
