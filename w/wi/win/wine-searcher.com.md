# WineSearcher's Privacy Statement

Personal information collected during the registration process for the "Pro Version" is solely for the legal requirements of running a service business over the Web. This information is never passed to other organizations. Credit Card information is not stored on our server.

Email addresses collected for notifying wine availability are used solely for that purpose. To receive the newsletter, enter your email address in the form on the [home page](https://web.archive.org/index.lml?ID=null).

We never spam.

The site uses cookies to improve solely to usability. All searches on the site are recorded, but only in a [summarized form](https://web.archive.org/stats.lml?ID=null) that cannot be related back to indvidual users.

If you have any queries about privacy, please send them to our NZ office.
