# About & Privacy Policy - Windows Lifestyle

Windows Lifestyle is a blog primarily targeted to Microsoft Products users. It basically covers tips and tricks, news and information related to Microsoft products and Windows operating system.

You will also find reviews and suggestions related to Windows Phones, Laptops and other Computer Accessories.

This blog was founded by **Nitin Agarwal**. He was a Microsoft Most Valuable Professional (MVP) in Windows Expert – Consumer category for four times. He is using this blog as a medium of sharing what he knows and learn about Microsoft products in his day to day life.

If you want to reach him personally for any of your queries or just want to say Hi, shoot him an email at nitin[at]sprouts.media.

You can find him on Twitter [@nitinagarwal198](https://mobile.twitter.com/nitinagarwal198), [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/nitinagarwal1988?_rdr) and [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/nitinagarwal1988/) as well.

He has also authored an eBook [The Complete Windows 7 Shortcuts eBook](http://www.thewindowsclub.com/the-complete-windows-7-shortcuts-ebook-released). Apart this he contributes at many Microsoft featured tech communities and helps Windows and PC users in solving their issues.

The latest eBook authored by him is [The Ultimate Guide to Speech Recognition in Windows 7](http://www.thewindowsclub.com/the-ultimate-guide-to-speech-recognition-in-windows-7-released). This eBook is mainly written to help Windows users to use voice commands efficiently to control PCs.
