At Winferno Software, we understand your need to protect your personal data and are firmly committed to guarding the confidence you have placed in our company. When you subscribe to Secure IE Bulletin or purchase Winferno Software, we may ask you to provide some personal information (such as your email address). We use the information we collect for these three reasons:

  * To improve the content of our web site, products and services
  * To alert you to product upgrades, updates and renewal information regarding your product
  * To share special offers, product announcements and updated information, and other new services from Secure IE.



You may receive periodic emails from us regarding our new products, upgrades or services. If you do not wish to receive these mailings, simply send a blank email to [unsubscribe@winferno.com](mailto:unsubscribe@winferno.com) and we will promptly remove you from our lists.

Winferno Software does not share or sell your email address with any third party.  
  
---  
  
None of the Winferno software programs contain banner advertising and they do not log, or in any way track, the web sites you visit. Our programs do not have the ability to "phone home" or connect to any web site without your direction.  
  
Winferno Software does not store your credit card information and all online purchases are processed on secure e-commerce servers, using 128-bit encryption, the highest form of encryption security available.
