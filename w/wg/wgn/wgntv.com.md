# WGN-TV

WGNTV.com does not knowingly collect or solicit personally-identifiable information from or about children age 12 or under except as permitted by law (i.e., to respond to a one-time request from a child or to allow a child to participate in a sweepstakes, in which case we collect no more information than is necessary to allow the child to participate and we delete all such information at the conclusion of the sweepstakes). If we discover that we have received any information from a child age 12 or under in violation of this policy, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe WGNTV.com has any information from or about anyone age 12 or under, please contact us at the address listed below:
