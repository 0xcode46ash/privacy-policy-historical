# Privacy Policy

Khojle respects your privacy and assures you that any information provided by you to Khojle is protected and will be dealt with according to this Policy. To avail of the Services offered on khojle.com, you may have to provide certain information to us. This includes your:

  * 1\. Username
  * 2\. Password
  * 3\. Valid Email address
  * 4\. Name
  * 5\. Birth Date
  * 6\. Postal address/Contact details
  * 7\. Mobile phone number



When you use the web platform or mobile platform of Services to advertise/buy, khojle.com may display your contact information, i.e., mobile phone number or email ID as the case may be, to prospective buyers/sellers so that you can close the best possible deal for your product/service by directly communicating with the buyer/seller. It is, thus, possible that at certain times, you may receive communication which may possibly be unwanted/offensive/malicious/obscene or communication which may otherwise be intrusive to you.

In the event that you receive such communication or even otherwise, you understand and acknowledge that Khojle will not be liable in any manner.

When you visit Khojle.com, certain information may also be automatically collected and stored, including:

  * 1\. The IP address of your computer, browser type and language.
  * 2\. The date and the time during which you accessed the site.
  * 3\. The address of the website which you may have used to link to Khojle.com



When you visit Khojle.com, cookies may be left in your computer. A cookie is a small text file that uniquely identifies your browser. The cookies assigned by the servers of Khojle may be used to personalize your experience on Khojle.com. Additionally, cookies may also be used for authentication and security purposes.

Cookies may also be assigned by the advertisers of Khojle when you click on any of the advertisements which may be displayed on Khojle.com in which case such cookies are controlled by these advertisers and not Khojle.

Khojle may also use software applications for website traffic analysis and to gather statistics, used for advertising and for determining the efficacy and popularity of Khojle com among others.

Information gathered by Khojle is stored securely using several information security applications including firewalls. However, security is always relative and Khojle cannot guarantee that its security measures are absolute and cannot be breached.

Also, data which is transmitted over the Internet is inherently exposed to security risks or threats. For instance, information transmitted via chat or email can be compromised and used by others. Therefore, Khojle cannot guarantee any security for such information.

When you register with Khojle, your account is protected by means of login information which includes a username and a password that is known only to you. Therefore, you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login information including your username and password. If you become aware of or reasonably suspect any breach of security, including compromise of your login information, it is your responsibility to immediately notify Khojle.

Khojle communicates with you through email address that you would have specified at the time of registration. Khojle may use this email address to send you emails related to the proper functioning of your account for instance when you receive a response to a advertisement/classified you have posted on Khojle or to send you newsletters that you have subscribed or to send you occasional announcements about changes in Khojle's policies or promotions introduction of new services. It is possible that such emails may contain commercial messages of advertisers.

Khojle.com may contain links to other websites. Such websites are governed by their own privacy policies and Khojle does not exercise any control over them. It is your responsibility to read and understand the privacy policy of such websites when you follow a link outside Khojle.

Khojle has a policy of not sharing any personally identifiable information with anyone other than entities specifically authorized by Khojle which may include advertisers and sponsors of Khojle. However, Khojle may use cumulative non-personal information for auditing and analysis purposes with the aim of improving its services.

Additionally, Khojle may share personally identifiable information if sharing of such information is necessary:

  * 1\. to comply with legal processes or governmental request, or
  * 2\. to enforce the Terms of Use and this Privacy Policy, or
  * 3\. for prevention of fraud, or
  * 4\. for issues involving information security, or
  * 5\. to protect 
    * a. your rights;
    * b. the rights of Khojle; and
    * c. the rights of the general public



Khojle confirms that this Privacy Policy is only a description of its operation regarding user information. This Policy is not intended to and does not create any legal rights in your favour or in the favour of any other person. Khojle reserves the right to change this policy at any time without giving you prior notice.
