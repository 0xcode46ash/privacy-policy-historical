# Kyero.com Privacy Notice - updated 26-7-2012

## Related: [terms and conditions](https://web.archive.org/info/terms) | [refund policy](https://web.archive.org/info/refund) | [cookie policy](https://web.archive.org/info/cookies)

We know that you care how information about you is used, stored and shared and we appreciate your trust in us to do that carefully and sensibly. This notice describes our privacy policy. By visiting Kyero.com, you are accepting and consenting to the practices described in this privacy notice

## Information We Collect

We receive and store only information you enter on our web site or give us in any other way. You can choose not to provide certain information although you might not be able to make use of some of the features at Kyero.com.

We **NEVER** store credit card information on any Kyero.com controlled server. All credit card information is securely processed by WorldPay or SagePay.

Like many web sites, we use cookies to personalise your experience of Kyero.com and for aggregate statistical analysis. The information we collect is used to improve the content of Kyero.com, not shared with other organisations for any purposes whatsoever.

Information about our customers is an important part of our business and we do not sell or give it to others under any circumstances.

## Protection of Kyero.com and Others

We release account and other personal information when we believe such release is appropriate to comply with law, to enforce or apply our conditions of use and other agreements or to protect the rights, property or safety of Kyero.com, our users or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organisations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction. This does not include selling, renting, sharing or otherwise disclosing personally identifiable information from customers for commercial purposes in a way that is contrary to the commitments made in this privacy notice.

## Information Security

We maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards in connection with the collection, storage and disclosure of personally identifiable customer information. Our security procedures mean that we may occasionally request proof of identity before we disclose personal information to you. It is important for you to protect against unauthorised access to your password and to your computer. Be sure to sign off when you finish using a shared computer.

If you choose to visit and use Kyero.com, your visit, use of it and any dispute over privacy is subject to this privacy notice and our conditions of use, including limitations on damages and applicable legislation. If you have any concern about privacy at Kyero.com, please [open a support ticket](http://support.kyero.com/Tickets/Submit) and we will try to resolve it.
