# the University of Health Sciences

> By accessing or using the University of Health Sciences' Web Site, you agree to the terms of the University of Health Sciences Online Privacy Policy, as outlined below. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not access or use this site.
> 
> **Collection of Personal Information  
>  ** When you engage in certain activities on this site, such as enrollment and event registration, the University of Health Sciences may ask you to provide certain information about yourself by filling out and submitting an online form. If you elect to engage in these online activities, the University of Health Sciences may require that you provide us personal information, such as your name, mailing address, e-mail address, and other personal identifying information. You may also be asked to provide a credit card number.
> 
> When you submit personal information to the University of Health Sciences, you understand and agree that the University of Health Sciences and its subsidiaries, affiliates and trusted vendors may transfer, store, and process your profile. The University of Health Sciences collects this information in order to record and support your participation in the activities you select. The University of Health Sciences recognizes and appreciates the importance of responsible use of this information. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.
> 
> Your personal information is password-protected to prevent unauthorized access to it. This password is encrypted. We recommend that you do not divulge your password to anyone. 
> 
> **Use of Cookies**  
>  When you visit the University of Health Sciences, you can surf the site anonymously and access important information without revealing your identity. In order to improve our site, we use "cookies" to track your visit. A cookie is small amount of data that is transferred to your browser by a Web server and can only be read by the server that gave it to you. It functions as your identification card, recording your passwords and preferences. It cannot be executed as code or deliver viruses.
> 
> Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. While the University of Health Sciences uses cookies to track your visit, our Web Site and our Web servers automatically log the IP/Internet address of your computer, this information does not identify you personally and you remain anonymous unless you have otherwise provided the University of Health Sciences with personal information.
> 
> Please note that the University of Health Sciences is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of any other website that the University of Health Sciences may link to.
