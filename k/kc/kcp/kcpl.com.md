# KCP&L, Kansas City electric company

**This site is best viewed with an 800 x 600 display setting.**

You have our assurance that: 

  * We are committed to ensuring your privacy online. 
  * We only use your personal information to help us service your account, to improve our services to you, to provide you with products you have requested, and to inform you about additional products or services that may be of interest to you. 
  * We protect your account information by placing it on the secure portion of our web server. 
  * This Web site does not collect personally identifying information about you except when you specifically and knowingly provide it. 
  * The kcpl.com site does use "cookies" for session management purposes. However, they are automatically deleted when you end the session, or exit the site. 



The Kansas City Power & Light site contains links to other Web sites. Kansas City Power & Light and its family of companies are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these Web sites.

Kansas City Power & Light reserves the right to modify its Web site privacy and security policies without notification. If you have questions in the future you may want to return to this page to view the current policy.

If you have questions about our Web site policies or procedures contact us by [e-mail](mailto:comments@kcpl.com).

**E-mail contact and surveys**   
We may occasionally contact you via e-mail or an online survey. Typically, this will be for the purpose of learning your preferences or satisfaction with online or off-line services, and to provide useful and timely information. You always have the choice of disregarding these communications. If you prefer to never receive them you also have the option of opting out. **Opting out **  
Any newsletters, surveys or promotional offers we make to you via e-mail will include a clearly visible option you may select to cancel any such future communications. You may also select to opt out under User Preferences within the AccountLink online billing program. **Security**   
The Kansas City Power & Light Web site is secure for our customers, shareholders and the public. All information presented to you, and any information that you may provide through AccountLink® or online forms is protected by SSL (Secure Sockets Layer), an Internet standard for providing secure communications.

The Web site is constantly monitored to ensure proper operation of security and other systems to protect customer and other data. Unauthorized attempts to modify any information stored on this system, to defeat or circumvent security features or to use this system for other than its intended purposes are prohibited and may result in criminal prosecution. 

**Use of personal information **  
KCP&L respects the privacy of the users of this Web site. We may collect certain personal information about you or your business in order to provide customized information and services through AccountLink® which are stored on the secured access portion of our Web site. This is done through methods such as an online survey, sign up for online services, or the registration process required for online access to your account information. In order to make changes to your personal information or to cancel your access [contact AccountLink](mailto:alinfo.kcpl.com). Follow these links to read more about [AccountLink](http://www.kcpl.com/residential/billing_acctlink.html) and [online bill payment](http://www.kcpl.com/residential/billing_payinfo.html). 

We do not share your contact information (e-mail address, name, address, phone), demographic or firmographic information (such as gender, age, business type, etc.) with third parties for marketing purposes without your prior consent.

 **Cookies **  
Definition: A cookie is a piece of code stored in your computer's memory containing information about your visit to a site. The code uniquely identifies the user during a session or on future visits to a site so they don't have to input their information each time they return. 

This Web site employs the use of cookies to facilitate your log in to access personal account information, or other online applications and to track usage behavior for the purpose of improving our content and services. 

**Childrens' Privacy **  
This Web site contains energy-related facts and information that could be of educational interest to children. Kansas City Power & Light, and our family of companies, does not gather personal information online from or about children. We encourage all children to seek consent of their parents or guardian before providing any information, in any form, about themselves or their household to anyone on the Internet.
