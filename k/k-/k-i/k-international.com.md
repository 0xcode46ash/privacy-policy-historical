# About K International plc Translation Services

  | 

### Privacy policy

**Our commitments to you  
**K International plc respects and protects the privacy of the individuals that use our websites. Personal information about you is not disclosed to any third party without first receiving your permission, as explained in this privacy policy.

**Personal information**  
Personal information is information such as your name, birth date, e-mail or mailing address that can be used to uniquely identify you. You can access any K International plc website and browse it without providing your personal information. However in order to participate in some of the activities available, or to benefit from services offered on the Websites, you must provide personal information (including, but not limited to, your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, and credit card number if you place an order with us). 

Contact information such as name and e-mail address is used to provide you with the services that you have chosen on this website including responding to your comments and requests for services. We may also use your personal information to send you marketing or promotional materials including e-mails although you will have the opportunity when inputting your personal information or at any time afterwards to ask us to refrain from doing so. K International plc also uses personal information about you to improve our marketing and promotional efforts; to statistically analyse website usage; to improve our content and product offerings; and to customise our websites' content, layout and services. We may also use personal information collected to contact you in connection with any inappropriate use of this website, non-compliance with any Terms and Conditions of Use, or in connection with a complaint filed by another user. You have a right to access the personal information that is held about you by K International plc or to ask us to amend or update it. To obtain a copy of your personal information, please e-mail info@k-international.com. 

**Information sharing and disclosure**  
Personal information collected on this website is used primarily for the purpose it is requested. K International plc will not sell or rent your personal information to anyone. We may share personal information collected on the websites with our partners and third parties but only with your consent. When you purchase our services, K International plc will collect credit card information for invoicing purposes. This information is processed for K International plc by a third party partner and by giving that information you consent to our supplying it to the third party for those purposes. That third party and K International plc use security techniques, including encryption and secure servers, to protect against any unauthorized access to transmitted information used for invoicing purposes. Neither K International plc nor our third party partner stores, uses, or shares, credit card information for any other purpose. We may also disclose personal information when required by law or if in good faith we believe that such action is necessary in order to conform to the requirements of law or comply with legal process served on the website. 

**Anonymous information  
**K International plc may collect certain aggregate data called web log information (such as your web browser, operating system, pages visited, etc.) and use cookies (see below) when you visit any of our web pages. For instance, when you visit one of our websites, our web server will automatically recognize some non-personal information, including but not limited to the date and time you visited our site, the pages you visited, the referrer (the website you came from), the type of browser you are using (e.g., Netscape, Internet Explorer), the type of operating system you are using (e.g., Windows '95 or Mac OS), and the domain name and address of your Internet service provider (e.g., America Online, Earthlink). A "cookie" is a small text file, stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive, which can be read by our system when you return to our site. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent that if you wish. Whilst K International plc uses cookies to track your visit to our website, this information does not identify you personally and you remain anonymous unless you provide K International plc with personal information. We may combine non-personal information collected automatically through cookies with any personal information previously submitted by you.
