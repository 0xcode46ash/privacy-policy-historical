# KFFL - The Hottest NFL, MLB and Fantasy News!

By using KFFL, you agree to KFFL's Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, we request that you do not visit the KFFL website found at http://www.kffl.com/ and http://www.kfflsports.com/, the NFLnews.com website found at http://www.nflnews.com/ and MrFootball.com website found at http://www.mrfootball.com/. 

On the Internet, there is a wealth of information. Unfortunately, at times, it may also contain personal details about you that you don't want everyone to know. Your real name, street address, phone number or email address are at times available on the Internet. KFFL strives to help keep our viewers and customers personal information private while utilizing our services. 

Please keep in mind that whenever you supply personal information (via message boards, chat features), it is information that can be collected and viewed by people you do not know. KFFL, like every other website online, cannot 100% guarantee the security of any information you disclose while you're online and you do so at your own risk. 

KFFL's general policy is not to collect personally identifiable information. Items such as your name, address, email address and other personally identifiable information about our visitors is only collected when you knowingly and voluntarily supply them to KFFL. Non-personal information, through the use of IP numbers and cookies, may be collected by KFFL at any time for the use of optimizing our web site. This information, such as the browser you utilize, domain name of your Internet service provider and operating system may be utilized for internal purposes at KFFL. 

Cookies are pieces of information that an Internet site transfers to an individual's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. They make web-surfing easier for you by saving preferences, passwords and other unique items that you set. KFFL never saves passwords or credit card information in cookies. Cookies are used as an industry standard and you will find them at most major Internet websites. Cookies help a company see which areas are the most popular and which are not. This data helps on improvements and updates to Internet sites based on the data of total number of visitors and pages viewed. 

As a user, you have the capability in most Internet browsers to allow or reject the use of cookies. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or indicate to you when a cookie is being sent. However, without cookies, you will not always be able to utilize the full function of a website and pages may be considerably slower. 

Advertisers at KFFL and it's various domain names may occasionally supply a cookie to your hard drive. These cookies are not under the control of KFFL and the use of advertising cookies sent by third-party servers is standard in the Internet industry. 

KFFL, at times, will link to other Internet sites. Sites not found at http://www.kffl.com/ and http://www.kfflsports.com/, http://www.nflnews.com/ and http://www.mrfootball.com/ are beyond our control. These links include advertising banners, KFFL's links page, our writer's personal pages and other various internet sites we link to.

Your Acceptance of these Terms. By using KFFL, NFLnews.com or MrFootball.com you signify your agreement to KFFL's Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not utilize our sites. If you continue to utilize the sites following the posting of changes to KFFL's Privacy Policy terms it will mean you accept those changes. 

KFFL reserves the right to modify its Privacy Policy at any time.
