# Copyright Notice

## Privacy Policy Statement

This is the web site of KOHL’S Department Stores.

Our postal address is:  
N56 W 17000 Ridgewood Drive  
Menomonee Falls, WI, 53051

We can be reached via e-mail at **webmaster@kohls.com** or you can reach us by telephone at 414.703.7000.

Our web server does not automatically recognize information regarding the domain or email address of individual visitors. However, we do collect information on the pages visitors access or visit. We also collect information volunteered by visitors, such as survey information and/or site registrations. The information we collect is used for internal review and is either discarded or used by us to contact consumers for marketing purposes such as those described below.

If you provide us with your email address online you may receive periodic email from us with information on sales, new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please contact us at the email address **remove@kohls.com** address and tell us that you do not want to receive email from us. Please provide your email address and specify that you want KOHL’s to discontinue email mailings to you.

If you provide us with your postal address online you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on sales, new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not want to receive such mailings, please contact us at the email address **remove@kohls.com**. Please provide your exact name and address and specify that you want KOHL’s to discontinue postal mailings to you.

If you provide us with your telephone number on-line, you may receive telephone contact from us with information you have volunteered on-line.

We will not use this information for telemarketing purposes nor will this information be shared with other organizations.

This website provides links to several other websites. While we choose our links carefully, we cannot take any responsibility for the content of linked sites, or for the privacy policies of sites we link to.

If you have additional questions regarding Kohl’s Department Stores privacy policy, please contact us at **webmaster@kohls.com**.
