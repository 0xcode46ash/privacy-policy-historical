# www.KOB.com - Privacy Policy Statement

[](http://www.addthis.com/bookmark.php?v=20)   Print Story 

This is the web site of KOB-TV, LLC. 

Our postal address is   
4 Broadcast Plaza SW   
Albuquerque, NM 87104 

We can be reached via e-mail at [cjones@kob.com](mailto:cjones@kob.com) or you can reach us by telephone at (505) 243-4411. 

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name.  The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded. 

With respect to cookies: We use cookies to store visitors' preferences and track return visitors. 

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads on sites on which we advertise.  Third-party advertising technology (1) uses information derived from your visits to this Web site and the sites on which we advertise to serve ads to you and (2) places a unique third-party cookie or cookies on your browser.  In addition, we may use Web beacons, provided by third-party advertising companies, to help manage and optimize our online advertising.  Web beacons enable us to recognize a browser's cookie when a browser visits this site, and to learn which banner ads bring users to our Web site.  To "opt-out," please visit <http://www.networkadvertising.org/consumer/opt_out.asp>

Information collected in contact forms such as email and phone numbers are used only for internal purposes and are not shared with third parties.  Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line will only receive telephone contact from us to answer questions or information requests.
