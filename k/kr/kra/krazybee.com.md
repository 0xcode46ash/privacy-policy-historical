# KrazyBee - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Finnovation Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. ("Company") is concerned about the privacy of its users (hereinafter referred to as "You" "Your" "Yourself") accessing its website located at www.krazybee.com ("Website") and has provided this Privacy Policy ("Policy") to familiarize You with the manner in which Website collects, uses and discloses Your information collected through the Website.

### The Companyâ€™s Principles to protect Privacy

## (a) Security

Wherever Your Personal Information may be held within the Company, the Company intends to take reasonable steps to protect it from unauthorized access or disclosure. The Company is committed to protecting the security of Your personal information. We use a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect Your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

## (b) Choice

You may choose whether or not to provide personal information to the Company either online or offline. If You choose not to provide the personal information we request, you can still visit most of the Website, but You may be unable to access certain options, offers, and services that involve our interaction with you. If You chose to have a relationship with the Company, such as a contractual or other business relationship, the Company will naturally continue to contact you in connection with that business relationship.

## (c) Notice

Where the Company collects personal information on the Website, the company intends to do so for a legitimate purpose and the same purpose may be asked by You to the Company's designated employees. Unless the transfer of personal information is legally required, the Company does not intend to transfer personal information without Your permission to third parties who are not bound to act on the Company's behalf.

## (d) Access/Accuracy

To the extent that You do provide the Company with personal information, the Company wishes to maintain accurate personal information. Where the Company collects personal information from You on the Website or through offline channels, its aim is to provide a means for the Company to contact You or vice versa. Hence, the Website has provided means to update or correct that information. If for any reason those means are unavailable or inaccessible, You may send updates and corrections about Your personal information to info@krazybee.com and the Company will make reasonable efforts to incorporate the changes.
