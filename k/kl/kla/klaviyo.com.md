# Privacy Policy | Klaviyo

#### Last updated: May 22, 2018

We created this Privacy Notice to explain how we collect, use, disclose and otherwise process personal information in connection with operating our business.

How we collect information

We collect information by various means:

  * Directly from individuals
  * From our clients or their service providers
  * From third party vendors, data brokers, or business partners
  * From recuiters



[Tell Me More](https://web.archive.org/privacy/policy#how-we-collect-information)

Information we collect

The information we collect may include:

  * Contact and demographic information
  * Consumer shopping history details that our clients transfer to us to enable us to perform our services
  * Cookies and other usage information we collect by automated means



[Tell Me More](https://web.archive.org/privacy/policy#information-we-collect)

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  * Provide services to our clients
  * Analyze the use of, improve, and promote our products and services
  * Comply with legal requirements



[Tell Me More](https://web.archive.org/privacy/policy#personal-information)

Information sharing

We may share the information we collect:

  * With our service providers and business partners
  * As required by legal requirements
  * in connection with a merger; acquistion or another corporate transaction



[Tell Me More](https://web.archive.org/privacy/policy#information-we-share)
