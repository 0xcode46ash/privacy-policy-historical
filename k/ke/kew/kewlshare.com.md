# KewlShare : Upload, Share & Earn Money with Your Files

When you register with us, we may ask you to provide us with certain personal information, including, but not limited to, your name and email address. 

**Logging IP Addresses:**

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server does not recognize any information regarding the domain or e-mail address. IP addresses are logged and used by KewlShare for measuring usage. 

**Collecting e-mail Addresses:**

We collect the e-mail addresses of KewlShare Delivery recipients and senders for the purpose of logging and measuring usage. KewlShare does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with nonaffiliated companies. 

**Certain Exceptional Disclosures:**

We may disclose your information if necessary to protect our legal rights or if the information relates to actual or threatened harmful conduct or potential threats to the physical safety of any person. Disclosure may be required by law or if we receive legal process. 

**Links to other sites:**

Our website has links to other sites. When you click a link to another site, you are no longer on our site and are subject to the privacy policy of the new site. 

**Use of Cookies:**

KewlShare uses cookies to store the preferences of users. 

**File Transfer Security:**

We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site. Files stored for delivery are only accessible by KewlShare and through the clickable link generated for your recipient. All files stored for delivery are deleted when they expire. 

**Policy Changes:**

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time without prior notice. The policy posted on this website will be the current version dated with the last revision date. You are responsible for regularly reviewing these terms and conditions.
