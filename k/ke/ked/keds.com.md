# Keds Shoes - Official Site - The Complete Keds Collection. Classics, Skimmers, Wedges & More

|  |  | **Privacy Agreement**  
At keds.com, we are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information. The data generated by your visit to keds.com is provided to keds.com and its agents in an effort to better serve your needs and improve your shopping experience. Keds may use this information to send you communications regarding orders placed or advanced notice of events/promotions, new products or services.

 **Security Statement**  
Maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information is important to keds.com. All payment information including credit card related data is considered and treated as confidential information. We utilize Secure Server Software (SSL) to encrypt all payment information, including names, address, credit card numbers, so that it can not be read as the information travels through the internet. SSL is considered to be among the most secure ways to do business through the internet. You can be sure that SSL is protecting you and your information by looking for the SSL symbol at the lower left hand corner of any window in which you are asked for personal information. When SSL is active, it will display (depending on your software) a closed lock or an unbroken key, letting you know that the information you input will be secure.

| **About Keds**  
Since 1916, Keds sneakers have represented authenticity, quality, performance and style. [Leading actresses, athletes and style icons](http://www.keds.com/text/true/news.jsp) throughout the years have worn their Keds canvas sneakers. Today, Mischa Barton continues this trend as the spokesmodel for Keds canvas sneakers. Keds has a myriad of canvas shoe styles from the [classic canvas champion sneaker](http://www.keds.com/shopping/product/detailmain.jsp?itemID=591&itemType=PRODUCT&iMainCat=110&iSubCat=14&iProductID=591) and vintage inspired [wedges](http://www.keds.com/jump.jsp?itemType=CATEGORY&itemID=212) to [ballet flats](http://www.keds.com/jump.jsp?itemID=211&page=1&itemType=CATEGORY), [sandals](http://www.keds.com/jump.jsp?itemID=145&itemType=CATEGORY&iMainCat=110&iSubCat=145), [Nanette Lepore](http://www.keds.com/jump.jsp?itemType=CATEGORY&itemID=176), and [sporty sneakers](http://www.keds.com/jump.jsp?itemType=CATEGORY&itemID=215). The future for Keds shoes will continue to bring fashionable canvas styles for Women, Men and Kids. 

At Keds.com you will find every style of Keds canvas shoes for Women, Men and Kids that are produced. Even the hard to find wide widths, small and large sizes of sneakers, sandals, flats, wedges, and ballet flats. If you can't find your favorite shoe at Keds.com, please [contact us](http://www.keds.com/custserv/customerservicemain.jsp?cid=7&showrequire=1) for assistance!
