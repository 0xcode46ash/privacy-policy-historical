# privacy policy

Privacy Policy  
This is the privacy policy of keralapals.com. For more information on our privacy policy  you can contact us at keralapals @ yahoo.com . This statement provides you with types of data we collect from keralapals.com and how we are using it.

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes about your browser, IP address and your search term you used to come here . We collect no information on consumers who browse our Web page.

Cookies information:  We use cookies to store preferences to record information like what page users visit, browser type. and other information users sends .

Ad servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and click stream information.
