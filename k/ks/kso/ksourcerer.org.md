# Privacy | Services Collection Agency

Your privacy is very important to us. This privacy policy of servicescollectionagency.com describes the information we gather, how we share that information and what we do to protect your information. Use of the site indicates agreement with this privacy policy. 

What Information Do We Gather?

Information we automatically collect via industry standard web log files includes the URL from which you visited, the page you are visiting, your computer browser information and your IP address. We may also use automatic data collection devices such as "cookies". We also may use 3rd party analytics programs.

As a prospective creditor using offerings on our site (such as our Request for Free Collection Agency Quotes service) to receive information from or about collection agencies, you are required to provide additional information so servicescollectionagency.com can deliver these services to you. This includes contact information (name, company name, email and telephone number). We may also ask you for feedback about our service, which is collected and stored to allow us to continually improve our offerings.

How Do We Use Your Information?

servicescollectionagency.com uses the information we gather to put creditors and collection agencies into contact with one another, to provide other features and functionality of this site, to improve your experience on our site through analysis and communication, for other purposes described in this policy, and for purposes disclosed to you when you provide information.

To Facilitate Free Quotes: Creditors or collection agencies will receive emails and other communications from us to fulfill or facilitate the purpose for which the information was provided or a request to use a service on this site. You cannot opt out of emails that are necessary to provide our services. By using servicescollectionagency.com's services offered on this site, you agree that we may use your personal information to contact you and deliver information to you related to those services.

Other Communications: servicescollectionagency.com may send you email and other communications related to this site, your use of our services and the status of your account, which may include site updates and responses to your inquiries. In addition, servicescollectionagency.com sends periodic surveys and other communications, all of which you can opt out of receiving.

How Do We Share Your Information With Others?

Prospective Creditors: To facilitate our business of matching creditors and collection agencies, prospective creditors using our services will be provided information about collection agencies relevant to the creditor's request or inquiry using such services. This can include collection agency contact information, company background information and, where appropriate, ratings or reviews.

Collection Agencies: Similarly, collection agencies whose products or services are relevant to a prospective creditor's request, and/or marketers, resellers and other service providers performing marketing, fulfillment and/or similar services on behalf of such collection agencies with respect to the prospective creditor's request, may be provided with that prospective creditor's information. We contractually limit use of the creditor's information (including all forms of contact information submitted) by the collection agency to communicating with the creditor, or permitting its marketers, resellers or other service providers to communicate with the creditor, about their desired purchase.

Agents: From time to time, we may use the services of third-party companies to help facilitate our business activities. These include marketing service providers. These companies are subject to confidentiality agreements with us and are prohibited from using the information we provide for any reason except to perform the functions that servicescollectionagency.com has outlined for them.

Business Transfers: It is possible that our business may be merged with or sold to another business entity. During such a transaction, business information is typically one of the assets shared in the combined entity. In the event that this occurs, your personal information will be protected by the servicescollectionagency.com privacy policy in effect at the time. If your personal information will be used contrary to this policy, you will receive notice as described in the "Notification of Changes" section below.

Legal Requests. servicescollectionagency.com is interested in protecting its members and the community at large. We will release account and other personal information to legal authorities when we believe it is appropriate to comply with the law, rules or regulations or to protect persons or property. In addition, servicescollectionagency.com may choose to release information about you to relevant parties in relation to investigations of fraud, piracy or other unlawful activity.

Information Security

Your information is stored on servers that are protected against unauthorized access through the use of safeguards such as "firewalls," encryption and Secure Socket Layers. We utilize many security techniques to protect your personal information. However, it is impossible to guarantee a perfect security system.

Children

Children (persons under the age of 18) are not eligible to use servicescollectionagency.com's services.

Legal Disclaimer

Although we have attempted to address the various privacy-related issues you are likely to encounter, we cannot ensure that all of your private communications and other personal information will never be disclosed in ways not otherwise described in this privacy policy. Therefore, although we use industry standard practices to protect your privacy, we do not promise, and you should not expect, that your personal information or private communications will always remain private from unauthorized access.  
Also, servicescollectionagency.com contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy policy applies to information collected by servicescollectionagency.com alone.

Notification of Change

We may amend this privacy policy for minor changes at any time by posting the amended terms on the Site. All minor amended terms shall be effective upon posting.
