# Kiwibank Privacy Policy

This website contains applications that collect personal information about you. Kiwibank will collect and use this information for the purpose for which it is provided. We will hold the information securely and will only use it in developing and running Kiwibank and/or providing you information about accounts and services that we think might be of interest to you (including from our selected business partners). If you ask us not to provide you with this information, we will comply with your request. You may ask us to show you the personal information we hold about you and to make corrections to it.
