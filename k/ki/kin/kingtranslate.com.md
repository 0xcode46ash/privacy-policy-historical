# King Translate - Privacy Policy

### Last modified April 29, 2012

Koyote-Lab, Inc. ("KingTranslate") takes your privacy very seriously. Please read the following to learn more about King Translate's Privacy Policy ("Policy"). This Policy details how KingTranslate receives, collects and uses information in connection with its Website located at [www.KingTranslate.com](https://web.archive.org/). 

Unless otherwise defined in this Privacy Policy, capitalized terms shall have the same meaning ascribed to them in the Terms of Use.

## Collection and Use of Information

**Information You Provide:**

You can use the Website and access the Software and Service without providing any Personal Information (defined below). However, in connection with other activities through the Website, you may be asked to provide certain personally identifiable information, which may include your name, email address, age, gender, address, telephone number(s), areas of interest, login name, login password and other self-identifying information (all "Personal Information"). It is entirely optional for you to engage in these activities. KingTranslate collects this Personal Information primarily for the following purposes: (i) personalize your experience of the Service; (ii) to manage and maintain users' personal preferences; and (iii) to email you with news, updates and offers. If you no longer wish to receive communication from KingTranslate, please follow the "unsubscribe" instructions provided in any of those communications (or see section below on Change or Removal of Information).

**Information We Automatically Collect:**

KingTranslate does not automatically collect any Personal Information on or through the Website, except as may be provided by you in connection with your voluntary communications described above. However, when you use the Website, KingTranslate may also automatically collect certain information about your computer to facilitate, evaluate and verify your use of the Website. For example, KingTranslate may use "cookies," "web beacons" and similar devices to track your activities on the Website. Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to an individual's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. A web-beacon is a small transparent gif image that is embedded in an HTML page or email used to track when the page or email has been viewed. Cookies make web-surfing easier by saving preferences and browsing patterns while you are on the Website. Cookies may be used to analyze Website usage, improve content, customize the Website's content and layout and for other customer service purposes. KingTranslate's use of cookies or similar devices is not linked to any Personal Information.

When you visit the Website, KingTranslate may automatically receive and record certain non-personally identifiable information on its server logs from your browser, including your IP address, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), cookie information, and the webpage that you visit. KingTranslate collects non-personally identifiable information for general purposes, including but not limited to analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking user movements, conducting research, and providing anonymous reporting to internal and external clients. KingTranslate will not link any Personal Information, including e-mail addresses, with the aggregate data of its users. Please be aware that some non-personally identifiable information such as Uniform Resource Locators ("URL's) or Internet Protocol ("IP") addresses could become Personal Information when combined by third parties with the ISP's records. KingTranslate does NOT do this with your information.

## Sharing and Disclosure of Information

KingTranslate does not rent, sell, or share any information about the user with any third-parties without your permission. KingTranslate may disclose your information if KingTranslate believes such action is necessary to: (a) comply with the law or legal process served on KingTranslate; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of KingTranslate (including the enforcement of our agreements); or (c) act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of KingTranslate services or members of the public. KingTranslate may share aggregated non-personally identifiable demographic information about users with third party advertising and content companies. This information does not identify individual users and KingTranslate does not link aggregate user data with Personal Information.

KingTranslate may use certain trusted third party companies and individuals to help it provide, analyze and improve the Website, Software and Service (including but not limited to data storage, maintenance services, database management, web analytics, information processing and improvement of the Website's features and services. These third parties may have access to your information only for purposes of performing these tasks on behalf of KingTranslate and under obligations similar to those in this Privacy Policy.

## Third party advertisers and websites

KingTranslate does not actively share Personal Information with third party advertisers for their direct marketing purposes unless you give KingTranslate your consent. Advertisers, however, may obtain public information about you from your user profile and other public sources. Remember, third parties will have access to your Personal Information if you provide it to them. For example, if you want to buy offers, goods or services from through third party advertisers on the Website, the transaction may be facilitated on the third party's website. You will then have to provide the third party with the information required (such as for example name, shipping address, credit card info etc), which will them be subject to their specific privacy policies. If you access another website or promotion and/ or service through a website operated by any entity other than KingTranslate, that site is referred to as a "Third Party Site". Unless KingTranslate clearly indicates otherwise at the time of collection, KingTranslate does not share Personal Information with Third Party Sites. Please be aware however that these Third Party Sites may collect information about you. Please see the Third Party Site's privacy policy to better understand what information is collected, how it is used and shared, and your choices regarding control. KingTranslate has no control over these linked Third Party Sites, each of which has a separate privacy and data collection practice, independent of this Privacy Policy. KingTranslate does not monitor, control, or endorse the information collection or privacy practices of any Third Party Site. As such, KingTranslate is not responsible for, or have control over, the content or the privacy policies of these Third Party Sites. You must read the privacy policies of each and every website that you visit when linking from the Website. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected by KingTranslate.

## Children's Privacy

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. We operate our website in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). The Website is intended solely for users who are of adult age in their jurisdiction, and any use of, or access to the Website or Service by anyone under the age of majority is unauthorized, unlicensed, and in violation of the Terms of Use and this Privacy Policy. By using the Website and Service, you represent and warrant you are of adult age in your jurisdiction and that you agree to abide by all of the terms and conditions herein. KingTranslate never knowingly collects or maintains information at or on our Website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of the Website is directed at or structured to attract anyone under 13.

## Security

KingTranslate takes reasonable precaution to protect your information from misuse, loss and unauthorized access. Although KingTranslate cannot guarantee that your information will not be subject to unauthorized access, KingTranslate has physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the your information. Your information is protected by secured networks and access is limited to a few authorized employees and personnel. However, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.

If you contact KingTranslate by e-mail or a "contact us" or similar feature on the Website, you should be aware that your transmission might not be secure. A third party could view information you send by these methods in transit.

In the unlikely event that KingTranslate believes that the security of your Personal Information in our possession or control may have been compromised, KingTranslate may seek to notify you that development if you have provided KingTranslate with your email address or other method communicating with you. If a notification is appropriate, KingTranslate will endeavor to do so promptly as possible under the circumstances.

ADDITIONALLY, YOU UNDERSTAND THAT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION MAY BE TRANSFERRED TO AND PROCESSED IN COUNTRIES (INCLUDING THE UNITED STATES) WHERE LAWS REGARDING PROCESSING PERSONAL INFORMATION MAY BE LESS STRINGENT THAN IN YOUR COUNTRY.

## Data Retention

KingTranslate will retain your information for as long as needed to provide you services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy. KingTranslate may retain and use your information as necessary to comply with its legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Consistent with these requirements, KingTranslate will try to delete your information quickly and upon request.

## Business Transitions

In the event that KingTranslate goes through a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition by another company, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, Personal Information will likely be among the assets transferred. KingTranslate will post a notice on this Website of any such change in ownership or control of the Personal Information KingTranslate may have prior to such transfer.

## Changes to Policy

From time to time, KingTranslate may revise this Policy. KingTranslate will post the revised Policy on the Website, so that users can always be aware of what information KingTranslate collects, how the information is used and under what circumstances such information may be disclosed. If KingTranslate makes a change in its data collection policy that would result in a materially more permissive use of your Personal Information, KingTranslate will obtain your prior affirmative consent before implementing the change with respect to your data if we have your contact information or we will provide more prominent notice. You should also visit this page periodically to review any changes to the Privacy Notice.

## Change or Removal of Information

If you have provided KingTranslate with Personal Information, you can review, update, correct, edit or delete your Personal Information at any time by contacting us at [info@KingTranslate.com](mailto:info@kingtranslate.com).
