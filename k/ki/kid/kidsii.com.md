# Kids II

On-Line Privacy Policy of Kids II

**Collecting Information from You** : Kids II feels it is important that you understand the type of information we collect from you, as well as how that information is used, disclosed, and protected. Please be aware that we do not use cookies or cookie technology to collect personal information about you. However, for administrative purposes, we do collect IP (Internet Protocol) addresses so that we can count the total number of visits on our Website. We do not use these IP addresses to associate personal identification or contact information with you. 

**Participating in On-line Activities** : We do collect personal information from you if you participate in one or more of activities offered on the Website, such as

  * entering our on-line surveys
  * participating in an on-line contests
  * sending comments or questions via email
  * requesting technical support or information via email
  * applying on-line for job positions
  * subscribing to mailings or notices concerning Kids II or its products and services



In each of these instances, we may ask for your name and e-mail address, as well as other relevant personal information. Unless otherwise noted in the applicable section of the Website, your participation in any surveys, contests, sweepstakes, subscriptions or other on-line activities is completely optional. 

**Changing Your Information** : At any time, you may change any information you have submitted to Kids II and may direct that we delete the information already provided by you. Kids II will honor any such requests and provide confirmation that such action has been taken.

**Use of Information** : Kids II does not sell personal information to third parties. The information collected is used to notify contest winners, to respond with answers to inquiries, or to send notices relating to consumer studies of our products if registered with our Product Advisory Board. In addition, personal information provided by visitors who are over the age of 18 may be used by Kids II for internal marketing purposes.

**Security** : Kids II has in place a variety of safeguards, both physical and electronic, to guard your personal information. We use state of the art tools and techniques to protect against unauthorized access to our systems.
