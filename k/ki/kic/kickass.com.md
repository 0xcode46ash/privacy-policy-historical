# Privacy Policy Accursed Toys

**
* We collect no personal data without your knowledge and consent. 
* We share no personal data with any outside parties and never have. 
* We have no personal data from visitors to share even if we wanted to share it, which we don't. 
* Any statistical data collected from our server logs cannot be used to identify an individual visitor and is only used internally to help us make our sites more responsive to visitors. 
* We have no technology that would allow us snoop on individuals during their visits or after they leave. 
* E-mail addresses from correspondence with any of our sites are not sold, reused or repurposed in any way. 
**
* **Maintaining your privacy is important to us.**
