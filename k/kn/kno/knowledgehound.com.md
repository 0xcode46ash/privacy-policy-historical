# Knowledge Hound Website Privacy Policy

KNOWLEDGE HOUND WEBSITE PRIVACY POLICY

* * *

Knowledge Hound, LLC is committed to respecting the privacy of our users. This policy details what information may be collected during a visit to our site and what we do with that information. 

An IP address is a unique identifying number that an Internet appliance uses during its time on the Internet. Knowledge Hound uses IP addresses to help diagnose problems, to administer and improve our site and to collect demographic and usage information in the aggregate. We may provide aggregate information to potential advertisers. We may share collected information with another company in the event that we merge with or are acquired by another company. 

We may display advertisements from individual advertisers, third party ad networks and/or affiliate networks. These ads may place cookies (small data files) on users' hard drives. These cookies are not placed, tracked or retrieved by us. We do not have access to the information gathered by the cookies. Knowledge Hound currently does not use cookies.
