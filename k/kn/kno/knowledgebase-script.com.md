# Private Policy - phpkb Professional Knowledgebase Software Script

**Privacy Policy**

We, at **_Chadha Software Technologies_** ([www.chadhaajay.net](http://www.chadhaajay.net/)) understand and respect that your privacy should always remain strictly under your control. It is because of this philosophy that you can be confident that the information you provide to us will not be accessible to people whom you have not authorized. Neither will it be sold or otherwise distributed to third parties other than in accordance with this policy without your consent. Knowledgebase-Script.com is not a source of unsolicited email.

**Use of Your Personal Information**

We will use your email address to contact you from time to time and we will use your email address for security reasons to confirm that you are who you say you are. We may use your contact information to send the phpkb's Monthly Newsletter to you but you will have the control over the choice to recieve newsletter from us. You may discontinue the email newsletter subscription whenever you desire.

We also collect other data, which may include your IP address and what browser you are using. We use this information to statistically analyze site usage and to customize our site's content, layout and services. The resulting information will allow us to improve our site and better understand and serve our users. It is compiled and analyzed on an aggregated basis only.
