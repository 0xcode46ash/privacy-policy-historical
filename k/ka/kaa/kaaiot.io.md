# Privacy Policy

## KaaIoT Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Apr.10, 2017

KaaIoT Technologies LLC. (“KaaIoT Technologies”, “us,” “our,” or “we”) HAS CREATED THIS PRIVACY POLICY DESCRIBING OUR POLICIES AND PROCEDURES FOR THE COLLECTION, USE AND DISCLOSURE OF USER INFORMATION. PLEASE READ THE FOLLOWING TO LEARN MORE ABOUT OUR PRIVACY POLICY, AND HOW WE TREAT INFORMATION COLLECTED FROM OUR VISITORS AND USERS.

This Privacy Policy is effective to all visitors and users of this website, other websites we operate and on which we have incorporated this Privacy Policy, and all products and services offered through our websites. This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures for collection, use, and disclosure of user Information. The terms “you” and “your” refer to visitors, users or viewers of the Website.

BY ACCESSING AND USING THE WEBSITE YOU AGREE THAT YOU HAVE READ AND UNDERSTAND THE TERMS OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY AND THAT YOU AGREE TO BE BOUND BY THESE TERMS.

### Changes to Privacy Policy

KaaIoT Technologies reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time, at our sole discretion, and without notice to you. Your continued use of this site following any changes to this Policy shall constitute your acceptance of the changes and the revised terms.

### Information we Collect

We may collect Personally Identifiable Information voluntarily provided by you when you contact us, sign up for our newsletter, participate in webinars, create a user account, download materials, participate in promotional activities, or provide such information through other electronic means. Personally Identifiable Information we collect may include information such as your name, email address, physical address, and telephone number. We are committed to security and implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personally Identifiable information.

We may also collect certain non-personally identifiable information, such as your computer’s IP address, browser information, cookie information, domain name, your geographic location, pages viewed and the number of times viewed. We may also collect information about your use of the website using third party tools such Google Analytics, but only for the purpose of improving our website, user experience, and our services. You may also voluntarily provide us with other non-personally identifiable information, such as your affiliated companies. Unless otherwise required by law, all non-personally identifiable information is not used to personally identify you and is only used by us to improve the content and functionality of our Site.

### How we Use your Personally Identifiable Information

We may use your Personally Identifiable information to:

  * Personalize your experience (your information helps us to better respond to your individual needs);
  * Improve our website (we continually strive to improve our website offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you);
  * Improve customer service (your information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests, inquiries and support needs);
  * Create and use email lists to send periodic emails to provide you with new product information, technical updates and information about support services;
  * Perform marketing surveys to better understand the needs of prospective clients and partners.



### How we Share your Information

We do not sell your Personally Identifiable Information to outside parties, however, there are certain circumstances in which we may share your Personally Identifiable Information information:

  * If required by law or by court order, or if such disclosure is necessary and permitted by law to protect ourselves or third parties;
  * In the event of a corporate merger or sale of corporate assets, your Personally Identifiable Information may be transferred as part of such transaction;
  * Partners, service providers and relevant third parties may receive Personally Identifiable Information in connection with their performance of services on our behalf. All such parties shall be required by contract to keep all information received confidential and to only use it for the purpose for which it was provided to them.



### Cookies

We may use a standard technology called a “cookie” to collect information about how you use and interact with our websites. Cookies reside on your computer and help the Site recognize your computer’s or mobile device’s browser as a previous visitor in order to build up a profile of how you and other users use the website. If you do not want information collected through the use of cookies, you should be able to modify your browser preference to disable cookies.

### Products

KaaIoT Technologies LLC. delivers end-to-end IoT solutions for connected products. We may collect certain information when you download, deploy, or use our products. You may be required to register before downloading our products. To register you may be required to provide Personally Identifiable Information. You may also choose other information such as your affiliated companies, but such information will not be required for registration. We also may automatically collect non-personally identifiable information, such as your computer’s IP address, browser information, cookie information, domain name, your geographic location, and pages viewed / number of times viewed, etc. to improve the functionality of KaaIoT website. All information shall be maintained in a manner consistent with this Privacy Policy.

### Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act), and do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.
