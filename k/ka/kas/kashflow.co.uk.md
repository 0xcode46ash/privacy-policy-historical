# KashFlow - Welcome

**Statement of Privacy  
** Key One Limited is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. This Key One Limited Statement of Privacy applies to the KashFlow.co.uk web site and governs data collection and usage at all KashFlow sites and services worldwide. By using KashFlow.co.uk websites, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

  
**Collection of your Personal Information  
** KashFlow.co.uk collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. Information collected buy the KashFlow.co.uk site may be shared within Key One Limited Personal and demographic data may be stored and processed outside of your country. By using KashFlow.co.uk you consent to any such transfers outside of your country.

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by KashFlow.co.uk and may be shared with other Web sites within the Key One Limited. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by Key One Ltd for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the KashFlow.co.uk Web sites.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through KashFlow.co.uk, or other public online forums, this information may be collected and used by others.  
Key One Limited encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from IRIS so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. Key One Limited is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of the Key One Limited and KashFlow family of Web sites.

**Use of your Personal Information  
** Key One Limited Web sites and their operational service partners collect and use your personal information to operate Key One Limited Web sites and deliver the services you have requested. Kashflow.co.uk may also use your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Key One Limited. Key One Limited may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your experience and opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

Key One Ltd does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Key One Limited may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, or provide customer support.. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Key One Limited, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Key One Limited keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within KashFlow.co.uk, in order to determine which services and facilities are the most popular. This data may be used to deliver customized content and advertising within KashFlow.co.uk to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Key One Limited will not disclose your personal information, without notice, unless required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Key One Limited or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Key One Limited, KashFlow and KashFlow.co.uk; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Iris Software Limited, its web sites, or the public.

**Use of Cookies**  
KashFlow.co.uk uses "cookies" to help you personalise your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to enhance your user experience. No financial or personal information is stored on these cookies.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you will not be able to use the KashFlow.co.uk Service.

**Control your Personal Information**  
Key One Limited will not share any personal or commercial information collected via the KashKlow.co.uk website with any third parties. You may choose not to receive marketing material from Key One Limited during the sign up process, and through your preference. You may also stop the delivery of future promotional e-mail from Key One Limited by following the specific instructions in the e-mail you receive. The instructions explain how to stop receiving such e-mails.

KashFlow.co.uk may send out periodic e-mails informing you of technical service issues, product surveys, new feature announcements and news about Key One Limited products and services. These e-mails are considered essential to the provision of the service you have requested. You will not be able to choose to unsubscribe to these mailings, as they are considered a part of the service you have chosen.

**  
Security of your Personal Information**  
Key One Limited secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. KashFlow.co.uk secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information is transmitted over the internet it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.

  
**Changes to this Statement**  
Key One Limited will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. Key One Limited encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how Key One Limited is protecting your information.

  
**Contact Information**  
Key One Limited welcomes your comments regarding this Statement of Privacy. If you believe that Key One Limited has not adhered to this Statement, please contact Key One Limited by e-mail. We will use commercially reasonable efforts to promptly determine and remedy the problem.
