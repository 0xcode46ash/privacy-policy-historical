# Urban clothes Rave girl store Rave clothing store at Karmaloop

**PRIVACY POLICY ::**  
Karmaloop collects customer information so that we can offer our valued customers the products that they want. We respect your privacy and assure you that we will maintain and use this information responsibly.

When you order products or register on our site, we need you to provide your name, email address, mailing address, day telephone number and credit card billing information (only when ordering products). Karmaloop logs the user's Internet Protocol Address (IP). An IP is a number that is automatically assigned to you personal computer every time you browse the Internet. Information from IP addresses is used for system administration, to examine overall traffic trends, troubleshooting, etc. This information is used only in the aggregate form. IP addresses are not linked to any personally identifiable information, meaning the user session is tracked, but the user is anonymous.

Personal information that you provide to us directly is maintained in private files on our secure web server and our internal systems. Karmaloop will not sell or release your email address or any other information to any other parties.
