# Privacy Policy - Kafto.com

## PRIVACY

##  Kafto.com takes your privacy seriously! Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy. 

Kafto.com may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your Kafto.com account or by placing a prominent notice on our site! Kafto.com collects personal information when you register with Kafto.com, when you use Kafto.com content, when you visit Kafto.com pages or the pages of certain Kafto.com partners, and when you enter advertising promotions. Kafto.com may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies. 

When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, ZIP code, and other personal interests. When you register with Kafto.com and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us. 

Kafto.com collects information about your visits to Kafto.com and with some of our business partners, including information about your use of it. Kafto.com automatically receives and records information from your computer and browser, including your IP address, Kafto.com cookie information, software and hardware attributes, and the page you request. 

Kafto.com uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, to promote product or services, to fulfill your requests for products and services, to improve our services, to contact you, to conduct research, and to provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients. 

  * Kafto.com may provide personal information to the advertiser when you interact with or view a targeted ad. However, by interacting with or viewing an ad you are consenting to the possibility that the advertiser will make the assumption that you meet the targeting criteria used to display the ad. 
  * Kafto.com works with vendors, partners, advertisers, and other service providers in different industries and categories of business. 
  * Kafto.com advertisers include adult content providers, adult material, financial service providers (such as banks, insurance agents, stock brokers and mortgage lenders) and non-financial companies (such as stores, airlines, and software companies) etc.
