# The Official James Bond 007 Website | Privacy Policy

### Privacy Policy  


#### 007.com Privacy Policy

###### Last updated: November 2011

#### Overview

###### This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding information collected, used, disclosed and otherwise processed when you visit the 007.com website or use other Eon Productions products and services, why we collect this information, how we safeguard it and the choices you can make about the way such information is collected, used and disclosed. Together we will refer to such websites, products and services as the “Site”.  By providing us with personal information, you signify your consent to this Privacy Policy.  This Privacy Policy applies regardless of the means or device you use to access the Site.  
The Site is operated by Eon Productions Limited of Regina House, 124 Finchley Road, London, United Kingdom NW3 5JS ("Eon Productions").

#### The Personal Information we collect

###### You can visit our Site without telling us who you are or providing us with any personal information. However, when you visit our Site, our web server automatically records certain information, such as your IP (Internet Protocol) address and other related information such as the name of your ISP (Internet service provider), your browser type, operating system and language, the website from which you visit us, keywords and any browser add-on technologies that are available for content delivery, the pages on our Site you visit and in what sequence, and the date and length of your visit. 

#### Cookies and web beacons

###### We, our third party service providers, and partners, may send "cookies" to your computer or use similar technologies to enhance your online experience at our website and across the Internet.  
"Cookies" are small pieces of information stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive.  Cookies identify you as a unique customer and store your personal preferences as well as technical information including click through and click stream data. Click stream data tells us which site pages you have visited and in what order. Cookies manage and measure the performance of promotional messages displayed on or delivered by or through the Site. We use cookies and other technical information to analyse traffic on the Site and to track user trends, to determine how frequently particular pages are visited, to determine the most popular areas of the Site and for technical reasons connected with your use of our Site. This helps us to enhance your online experience and to improve the design and content of the Site to suit your needs. Cookies can either be permanent (i.e., they remain on your computer until you delete them) or temporary (i.e. they last only until you close your browser).  
We may also use "web beacons" or similar   


###### technology that monitor your use of our Site and allow us to understand which pages you visit on our Site. These may also be referred to as “Clear GIFs”. Web beacons (or web bugs) are small strings of code that provide a method for delivering a graphic image on a web page for the purpose of transferring data, such as the IP (Internet Protocol) address of the computer that downloaded the page on which the web beacon appears, the URL (Uniform Resource Locator) of the page on which the web beacons appears, the time the page containing the web beacon was viewed, the types of browser that fetched the web beacon and the identification number of any cookie on the computer previously placed by that server.  
By accepting this Privacy Policy, you specifically agree to our use of cookies and web beacons as described in this Privacy Policy. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. However, if you prefer, you may adjust your browser to reject cookies from us or from any other website.  Please consult the "Help" section of your browser for more information. However, please note that certain areas of our Site can only be accessed in conjunction with cookies or similar devices and you should be aware that disabling cookies or similar devices may prevent you from accessing some of our content.

#### Security

###### Although absolute security cannot be guaranteed on the Internet, please be assured that we have in place appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal information against accidental or intentional manipulation, loss, misuse, destruction or against unauthorised disclosure or access to the information we collect online. While we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any personal information we record from your visit to our Site, we will continue to maintain and improve these security measures over time in line with legal and technological developments.  
We may disclose information that we have recorded about you to third parties to comply with applicable laws and regulations. We also reserve the right to disclose information to third parties, in accordance with applicable law, where a complaint arises concerning your use of our Site, where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person and where we believe, in our sole discretion, that your use is inconsistent with the Privacy Policy or our Terms of Use.  
We may share information about visitors to the Site in an anonymous and aggregate form with third parties including business partners, and sponsors, to understand customer trends and patterns and manage and improve our business relationships.  
It is possible that we or one of our divisions or affiliates could merge with, be sold to or be acquired by another third party. To the extent legally permissible and in conformity with

###### the conditions of this Privacy Policy, we may share the information we hold that relates to you with the resulting entity.

These third parties can obtain personal information that you voluntarily submit to them to make use of the offer. We are not responsible for the collection and use of your personal information by these third parties. However, you will be informed, at the point of collection of such personal information, if these third parties will obtain such information, how it will be processed and to whom it will be transferred.

These third parties may view, edit, or set their own cookies. The use of these technologies by such third parties is subject to their own privacy policies and is not covered by this Privacy Policy. For more information about third party ad servers at our Site and your ability to opt-out of targeted marketing from such third parties, please visit www.youronlinechoices.co.uk and also visit the Network Advertising Initiative.
