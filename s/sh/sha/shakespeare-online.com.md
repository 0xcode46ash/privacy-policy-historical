# About Shakespeare Online Copyright and Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click [here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp).

**Notice of Copyright**

All information provided by _Shakespeare Online_ is owned by _Shakespeare Online_ (excluding outside links and public domain work) and any user is permitted to store, manipulate, analyze, print, and display the information on _Shakespeare Online_ only for such user's personal use. In no event shall any user publish or redistribute or otherwise reproduce any _Shakespeare Online_ graphics or written content in any format to anyone, and no user shall use any _Shakespeare Online_ information in connection with any business enterprise.

Full citation of any original material on this site is required if the material is used on other web sites or in research papers, essays, articles, or any other print medium. The following is a sample citation:  
Mabillard, Amanda. "Horatio." 

_Shakespeare Online_

. 2000. (day/month/year you accessed the page) < http://www.shakespeare-online.com/playanalysis/horatiochar.html >.
