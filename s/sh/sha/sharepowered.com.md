# Privacy Policy - Sharepowered

Sharepowered Privacy Policy

Your privacy is extremely important to us.  This policy describes how Sharepowered.com uses your information and how we use the information we collect from users browsing our site.  This Privacy Policy does not govern our use of any information you send to us outside of our website services, such as emailing and telephone communication. Information we collect on our site may be stored in international countries, including servers in the United States. By using our websites, you explicitly consent to such use of your information and agree to the terms of this privacy policy.

Our Information Sharing Practices – At Sharepowered.com, we will never share your personal information for monetary gain of any kind.  The only information we collect for third parties is information to display advertisements on our site.  We will never sell or exchange information to any other site.

Automatically Collected Info – We automatically collect certain information for administration and maintenance of our website.  Each time you visit our websites we may automatically collect your IP address, browser and computer type, access time, the web page you came from, the URL you go to next, and the web page(s) that you view during your visit. We also utilize third-party advertisement reports that display advertisement statistics and traffic as well.

Cookies (Yum!) – We may set and access cookies on your computer. Upon your first visit to our website, a cookie is sent to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser.  A “cookie” is a small file containing a string of characters that is sent to your computer and stored on your hard drive when you visit a website. Many websites use cookies to provide useful features for their users. Each web site can send its own cookie to your browser. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent; however if you reject cookies, you will not be able to take full advantage of our Services. We use cookies to track your traffic on our site and gauge your preferences.  No personal identifying information is collected with these cookies.

We also may allow certain analytic services and providers of applications used on the Services (e.g., sharing buttons) to collect non-personally Identifiable information by placing cookies on our website that will track certain performance of our services, such as web traffic, click-throughs, etc., in order to assist us and our service providers in better understanding the interests of our users and to better serve those interests.

Security – We take security very seriously at Sharepowered.com.  Although we utilize the latest security measures on our sites, your information is not 100% guaranteed.  In the event of a data loss catalyzed by an external event, we will not be held responsible for loss of data.

Links- We do not affiliate with 3rd party websites that are linked from our sites.  Many of our webpages contain hyperlinks to external sites.  We take no responsibility for the content or policies of those sites and the links are provided for informational, editorial, and entertainment value only.

Advertising – We may use other companies under agreements with us to serve advertisements on our site.  These advertisers may use cookies to verify which effective ad placements to display.  These advertisers are not affiliated with Sharepowered.com and our sites and use their own methods for collecting information. By using our sites, you agree to these advertiser’s Terms of Services stated on their websites.

Targeted Advertising – Sometimes we may provide targeted advertising on our sites.  The advertising is determined by the collection of information from cookies.

Minor Privacy – Our services are not intended for and may not be used by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 and we do not target the services to children under the age of 13.  All minors need parents’ permission before logging onto our website.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we ever change the way in which we treat your user information, we will inform you of such change via email or by posting a notice on relevant areas of our website.  Any updated version of this Privacy Policy will be effective as of the date noted.
