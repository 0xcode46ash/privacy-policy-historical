# Shiur.com - Your Online Yeshiva! - Torah | Shiur | Torah Portion | Judaica | Audio Shiur | Ask A Rabbi | Jewish History | Jewish Tradition

**

  
**Shiur.com** has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: Shiur.com 

**Information Automatically Logged**   
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our web site. 

**Cookies**   
Our Site uses cookies as part of the technical implementation of various site features including management of the user's session state. Cookies are not accessible by, nor are they shared with 3rd parties. 

**Advertisers**   
We may use outside ad companies to display some of the ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our web site, any cookies received with outside banner ads are collected by an outside advertising agency, and we do not have access to this information. 

**You information**   
Our site also collects demographic and profile data. This information is used to tailor our visitor's experience at our site, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences. This anonymous information does not personally identify specific users and is also used for statistical purposes to help determine how many people are visiting this website. 

**Newsletter**

If you would like to be removed from our newsletter, please do so from our homepage. 

If you have any other questions about privacy practices, please let us know by sending an email to [admin@shiur.com](mailto:admin@shiur.com).
