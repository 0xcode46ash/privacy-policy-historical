# Resize Images online

## Shrink Pictures

**Privacy Policy**

During your usage of this website some personal information may be collected, such as resized images and email addresses.

Our website is automated and purges any temporary files, including resized images within 12 hours.

You can optionally send resized images as an email attachment.  We do not collect or store any email address entered into the forms on our website for any purpose.  The only time you will receive an email from our site is when someone resizes an image and enters an email address.

We do collect statistical information about the options chosen to resize pictures.  This information is collated and used by us to improve the tools we provide for your use.

Above all, we respect your privacy and never pass on any personal or identifiable information to any 3rd Party.
