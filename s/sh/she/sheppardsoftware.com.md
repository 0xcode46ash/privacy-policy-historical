# Sheppard Software - Online Games & More

**www.SheppardSoftware.com** is dedicated to educating and entertaining its visitors through the use of our dynamic Flash games and quizzes. We are pleased to provide teachers, parents, and learners of all ages with the following privacy statement, letting you rest easy that your information will never be used for any purposes other than making your learning experience better and more rewarding. 

Sheppard Software collects certain technical information from your computer each time you request a page. This information is collected from your computer's Web browser and may include your IP address, operating system, Web browser software (e.g. Netscape Navigator or Internet Explorer), screen resolution, and referrer Web site. None of the information collected during your visit is personally identifiable, nor will it be used to track your activities anywhere outside the site.

Additionally, no email addresses or contact information submitted via our contact page will ever be sold, distributed, or shared with any third parties.

Your privacy is important to us. If you should ever have questions regarding the acquisition or use of information that appears on the site, please feel free to [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20071212113023id_/http%3A//www.sheppardsoftware.com/contact.htm) with your comments.
