# ShermansTravel.com -- Screened Listings, Deal Reviews, Objective Editorial

  
The following information explains our policy regarding any personal information you may supply to us when visiting ShermansTravel.com. You can generally visit our web site without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information, including your e-mail address. 

ShermansTravel.com does not collect personally identifiable information about individuals except when such individuals specifically provide such information on a voluntary basis, such as when requesting our e-newsletter. 

ShermansTravel.com will not share e-mail addresses nor any information submitted by a user when accessing our web site with anyone outside ShermansTravel.com nor will ShermansTravel.com disclose user information to any third party. Our web servers may automatically recognize your domain name when you visit our site, and this information is aggregated and used to measure statistics regarding the use of our site, such as the number of visitors, average time spent on the site, pages viewed and similar information. This information is likewise not distributed to or shared with third parties. 

ShermansTravel.com may use the information it gathers for its own internal purposes. Our goal in collecting personal information online is to continually improve the content of our web site and to offer consumers with the travel products that best meet your preferences and desires. ShermansTravel.com may use the personal data you provide online to send you information about new products and services, or offers that we feel may be of interest to you. 

ShermansTravel.com reserves the right to change this policy at any time. This statement and the policies outlined herein are not intended to and do not create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party. By using the ShermansTravel.com site, you signify your agreement with the terms of our privacy statement. 

If you have any questions about ShermansTravel.com privacy policy, please contact the us at: info@ShermansTravel.com
