# Privacy | She Wears Many Hats

**Your privacy is important.  
**

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy this notice is provided explaining the online information practices here at SheWearsManyHats.com and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, it’s made available on the homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested. By visiting SheWearsManyHats.com you are consenting to this privacy policy. This policy may be updated periodically. It is your responsibility to review this policy from time to time to be aware of any updates and changes.

 **Collection of Personal Information  
**Any personal information that you choose to provide on this site is never shared with any unrelated third party unless otherwise stated in this privacy policy. This is a public website and as such you should be aware that any time you submit a comment any information shared in a comment can be seen and collected by any visitor to this site.

 **Cookies  
**We may use “cookies” to allow us to store a small piece of data on your computer about your visit to our Web site to improve the quality of our service. The purpose of cookies is to help us learn how to make our site more useful and what areas may need improvement. You may accept or decline cookies by changing the settings on your browser. If you choose to disable cookies, some areas of this site may be diminished and or not work at all.

 **IP Addresses and Tracking  
**When visiting SheWearsManyHats.com, the IP address used to access the site will be logged along with the dates and times of access. This information is purely used to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement and gather broad demographic information for internal use. Most importantly, except where noted in this privacy policy, any recorded IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

 **Security  
**Security measures are maintained to protect your personal information from misuse, unauthorized access, or disclosure. While we make every effort to protect your personal information, no exchange of data online can be 100% guaranteed as secure. You acknowledge that any personal information voluntarily shared through SheWearsManyHats.com could be accessed or tampered with by a third party. You agree that we are not responsible for information you have shared through SheWearsManyHats.com that has been tampered with or taken without our knowledge or permission.

 **Links to Third Party Websites**  
Links on this site are for your use and reference. SheWearsManyHats.com is not responsible for the privacy policies on these websites. You should be aware that the privacy policies of these sites may differ from those here.

 **Third Party Advertisements**  
Third-party advertisements are used to serve ads on this website. Third parties may be placing and reading cookies on your user’s browser, or using web beacons to collect information in the course of these ads being served.

You may choose to opt out of these cookies. For more information about your choices please visit here: <http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/>

Gourmet Ads primary ad server is Appnexus. You can opt out of cookies from them by visiting <http://www.gourmetads.com/about/privacy-policy> and then “opt out.”
