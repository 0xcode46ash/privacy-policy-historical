  * We can be reached via e-mail [here](http://www.shopping-bargains.com/aboutus/contact_us.cfm) or you can reach us by telephone at 662-320-2028.
  * For each visitor to our Web site, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, but not the e-mail address.
  * _With respect to web browsers_ : we collect only the domain name, but not the e-mail address of visitors to our Web site, aggregate information provided by the web browser software (when available, such as country, browser type and version, screen resolution, javascript status, etc.), aggregate information on what pages visitors access or visit, and information volunteered by the visitors, such as survey information and/or email newsletter registrations (these items are completely optional). This information is used to better offer content that is accessible to our readers in the format that works best with their web browser and computer configurations.
  * _With respect to our email newsletters_ : Our email newsletters have only one (1) required field (the email address). All other information collected is optional. We collect data on email delivery rates, open rates and click rates to better improve our services.
  * The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web site and is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.
  * _With respect to cookies_ : We use cookies to record past activity at our site in order to provide better service when visitors return to our site.
  * If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please do so using the unsubscribe option at the bottom of each e-mail newsletter that we send.
  * _With respect to ad servers_ : We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies. We do, however, employ Google AdWords on select pages.
  * From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our Web site periodically.
  * Upon request we provide site visitors with access to a description of information that we maintain about them. Consumers can access this information by visiting [here](http://www.shopping-bargains.com/newsletter.cfm).
  * If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us via e-mail [here](http://www.shopping-bargains.com/aboutus/contact_us.cfm) or at the above address or telephone number.
