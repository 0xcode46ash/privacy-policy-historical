# Privacy Policy

**Personal data**   
Your personal data will not be registered by Shop2market. This with the exception of the Shop2market platform, that requires a personal login. Personal information made available for the use of the Shop2market platform are saved for your own purposes. For the use of this information we comply to the guidelines of the IAB, please see the e-mail code of conduct. Your information is not made available or sold to third parties. 

**Automatically collected impersonal information**   
Shop2market can collect data from impersonal nature, such as browser type or IP address, the operating system you are using or the domain name of the website through which you go to the website or through which you leave it. Also this information is not made available or sold to third parties. 

**IP adressess**
