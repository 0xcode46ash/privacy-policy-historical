# show2me :: Privacy Policy

We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected, and although we are not subject to Australia’s Privacy Policy Regulations, we do apply them as a Best Practice and to demonstrate our commitment to securing our users personal information because yesHEis.com and show2.me are both owned by CVC Network Ltd registered in Australia.

Australia’s Privacy Policy regulations are some of the strictest in the world and complying with Australia’s regulations also satisfies regulations of other jurisdictions too, including, New Zealand, UK, US, Canada, Hong Kong and Singapore, and websites hosted on associated country domains, “.com.au”, “.co.nz”, “.co.uk”, “.com”, etc.

User data is securely stored on our servers in the UK and USA, and whenever we use 3rd party services we take reasonable steps to ensure they comply with privacy requirements.

We collect various anonymous information used internally by us, solely for the purpose of measuring visitor traffic, and trends.

We may update our Website features and Privacy Policy from time to time to remain relevant and up to date, however relevant Privacy Policy changes only apply to new personal information collected after the change date.

You have the right to request access to your personal information that we have stored by contacting us, but we may charge a reasonable fee to process these requests.

We currently store only personal information that users supply, and we store communications between users similar to the way an email system stores communications.

We make use of cookies to enable traffic gauging and identify logged in users so that they can access their messages and information. We do not store any of your personal information in cookies. You can set your browser to block cookies however this may obstruct certain functionality of the Website.

We do not sell advertising on our Websites and as such do not disclose user personal information to advertisers, however Media that we link to on external services may use cookies and also serve their own adverts over which we have no control. If you have any queries about these external services please directly contact those services.

We may disclose your personal information within our subsidiaries or holding company in order to facilitate our service to users.

We may disclose personal user information in order to comply with legal requirements or to protect the rights, property, or safety of ourselves, our users, or others.

CVC Network Ltd welcomes your comments or questions regarding this Privacy Policy and how we protect your personal information that we store. Please contact us via our Contact Us page and mention Privacy Policy in your enquiry.
