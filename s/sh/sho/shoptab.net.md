# Privacy Policy - ShopTab

Watershed Software, LLC ("ShopTab") is serious about protecting your privacy and recognizes that you care how information about you is used and shared. We appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly. This notice (this "Privacy Policy") describes how we may collect personal information about you, how we may use it, what choices you have regarding it, and how we protect it. This Privacy Policy is incorporated into and is a part of the Terms of Use of this ShopTab Web site (Facebook App or the "Site") and we encourage you to become familiar with the terms and conditions contained herein. By accessing and using this Site and the ShopTab Service, you agree that you have read and agree to this Privacy Policy, and that you accept and consent to the privacy practices described in this Privacy Policy.

#### What Information Do We Collect? 

ShopTab may collect various types of information from or about you. During the Site registration process, you supply us with basic contact information (such as name, address, telephone number, e-mail address and company name) that we use for billing and contact purposes. This information is generally entered into fields in the registration forms. Should you purchase any ShopTab Services on or through the Site, the information that you supplied during the registration process may be used to track details about that purchase. We may also collect information regarding the origination source of visitors, their site activities and purchase behavior for our internal use only. We do not collect any information from your customers. ShopTab does not have access nor any ability to use any of the personally identifiable data that may be collected by the third-party e-commerce application to which ShopTab links. ShopTab may, however, use anonymous, aggregated data to evaluate the effectiveness of its technology. ShopTab is not responsible for any data collected by Organizations using other third-party technology to complete the ordering process. 

#### How Do We Use The Information That We Collect?

ShopTab uses the information that it collects for the following purposes: to fulfill your requests for services, to contact you, for billing purposes, to respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims, to share information in order to investigate, prevent or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, for violations of ShopTab's terms of use or as otherwise required by law, to improve the services that ShopTab makes available on the Site, to notify you of changes made to the Site or new products and services made available on or through the Site, to evaluate your needs and customize Site content delivered to you according to those needs, to facilitate the processing of any purchases you make through the Site, to send you promotional material from ShopTab and some of our affiliates, and for other legitimate and lawful business purposes of ShopTab]. ShopTab does not share information collected about you with third parties. It is our policy not to sell lists containing personal information about Site users, registrants or subscribers. 

#### How Do We Protect Information Collected About You?

ShopTab places a high value on protecting your personal information. ShopTab takes commercially reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Please be aware that despite using commercially reasonably measures, no security system is impenetrable. If you use a password on our Site, you are responsible for keeping it confidential. Do not share it with any other person. If you believe your password has been misused, please contact us immediately. 

#### Choices About the Use of Your Information.

You may choose to remove the Application from your Facebook account by clicking on "Edit Page" in the admin toolbar and selecting "Update Info " form the drop-down menu. Then select the “Apps” tab on the left rail. Click the "x" next to the ShopTab Application's name, then client "remove" again to confirm the deletion. 

#### Linked Sites

For your convenience, some hyperlinks may be posted on the Site that link to other web sites not under the control of ShopTab. We are not responsible for and this Privacy Policy does not apply to the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to seek out and read the privacy policy of any web site that you visit. In addition, when you initiate a transaction on a web site that our Site links to, even if you reached that site through our Site, the information that you submit to complete that transaction becomes subject to the privacy practices of the owner of that linked site. You should read their privacy policies to understand how they use and protect personal information that they collect. ShopTab is not responsible for the privacy or information practices of its suppliers or any third party Web sites. 

#### Changes to Privacy Policy

From time to time, ShopTab may change its privacy practices because of changes in relevant and applicable legal or regulatory requirements, the business or business practices of ShopTab, or in its attempts to better serve your needs, or for any other reason in ShopTab's sole discretion. We encourage you to review this Policy whenever you visit our Site or use our Service to view the current Privacy Policy.

If you wish to contact ShopTab about privacy related issues, you may do so by sending an email to : [privacy@shoptab.net](mailto:privacy@shoptab.net)
