# Shop for shoes at Shoebuy.com

[Customer Service](https://web.archive.org/sb/customer): Privacy Policy

  
Shoebuy respects your privacy. We use any information we receive to improve our relevance to you and to improve the shoe buying experience. When you order, we ask for an address, credit card number and expiration date. Shoebuy's secure server takes all confidential information and encrypts it during transmission, which ensures your information remains private and protected. 

We will not sell, rent or loan identifiable personal information to any third party. The only information we may share is non-personal, summary or aggregate customer data with partners or third parties. In addition, we offer the Shoebuy.com Safe Purchase Guarantee to ensure your comfort when buying shoes on the Shoebuy.com website. 

At Shoebuy, we always appreciate your business. If you'd like to provide feedback about the Shoebuy.com website or recommend a way we can improve the shoe buying experience, [click here](https://web.archive.org/sb/contact/feedback.jsp).
