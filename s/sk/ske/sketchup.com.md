# SketchUp

Privacy Policy 

At @Last Software, Inc. (“@Last”), we take our customers’ privacy very seriously. Therefore, we want to provide you with explicit information regarding how we collect, gather, and identify information during your visit to this site. This information may be expanded or updated as we modify our site.  We recommend that you review this policy from time to time to see if it has changed so that you can remain informed about what information we collect, how we use it and when and how we disclose it. 

**What Organization Is Collecting the Information.**  
@Last Software, Inc. owns and operates the www.sketchup.com web site and is the company that collects personal information governed by this privacy policy.  All information will be collected and stored in the United States.  Our mailing address is 821 Pearl Street, Boulder, CO 80302, our telephone number is 303-245-0086, and our general information email address is [info@sketchup.com](mailto:info@sketchup.com).  @Last Software will own all information that it collects.

**How we gather information about our customers:**  
We collect information about our customers through a variety of methods. These methods include forms on our Web site, our software or service registration and licensing procedures, attendance or participation in product training or trade show events that @Last participates in, industry groups and organizations and publications, and through direct communication such as phone conversations. 

In our effort to provide you with products or services we may at times ask you to submit information about yourself. You never have to answer these questions. However, if you should elect to withhold requested information, we may not be able to provide you with some of these services dependent upon the collection of this information. 

Some of our web pages use "cookies" so that we can better serve you with tailored information when you return to our site. Cookies are identifiers that a web site can send to your browser and are stored on your computer to facilitate your next visit to our site. It may do such things as store the fact that you have registered for a seminar so that the next time you visit the site we recognize you as a previous registrant. We may also use clear GIFs on some pages within the our web site.  Clear GIFs and cookies make your internet experience quicker and more convenient. You can, if you wish, set your browser to notify you when you are sent a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it. You can also set your browser to turn off cookies. If you do so, however, some areas of our site may not function properly.

**How we use information about our customers:**  
We may use your information on an aggregated basis to do such things as operate our site, enhance our site, and sell and deliver advertising.  We may also use this data, both aggregated and personally identifiable, to deliver products and services, detect use of our software, deliver informational and promotional electronic mail, to make sales calls, and enhance your on-site experience by displaying content and marketing messages that we believe will be of interest to you.

**Submissions:**  
@Last welcomes customer input and feedback and provides various services whereby such information can be made available to users of our Web services, such as FAQ’s, Tips and Tricks, Case Studies, User Gallery and other services. Any user material, information or other communication transmitted or posted to our Web sites or services, or otherwise provided to @Last ("Submission") is understood by both parties to be non-confidential and non-proprietary. @Last has no obligation with respect to any Submission and may freely copy, reproduce, incorporate, distribute or use such Submission as it wishes. Submissions that are pornographic, violent, obscene, threatening, defamatory, libelous or violate the law in any way, are strictly prohibited. 

**Protection of User Information:**  
@Last will not monitor, edit or disclose information about a user or a user's private content, except where @Last may do so in the good faith belief that such action is reasonably necessary: (1) to comply with the law; (2) to comply with legal process; (3) to enforce the Terms; (4) to respond to claims that such content violates the rights of third parties; (5) to protect the interests of @Last or others. All material submitted to our web sites and services, regardless of form or medium, becomes the property of @Last. 

@Last does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. @Last may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that we feel may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party.  Notwithstanding the foregoing, you acknowledge that if @Last is acquired, the company’s assets and all customer information in its possession will become the property of the acquiring company.

@Last may provide your personal information (including credit card information) to @Last's agents for purposes of processing and/or fulfillment of transactions, including product orders that are conducted through this site. 

**Control of Your Personal Information:**  
As a valued customer, @Last offers you the choice to “opt-out” of receiving certain marketing communications. You may stop the delivery of future informational or promotional e-mail from @Last by following the specific instructions in the e-mail you receive. The instructions explain how to stop receiving such e-mails. Please note that due to lead times on certain mailings and other contacts, you may continue to receive contacts for a period following your "opt out" notification. 

@Last wants to ensure that the information it collects from its customers is accurate. **General Notices:**  
We may send out periodic e-mails informing you of technical service issues, product surveys, new feature announcements and news about our products and services. These e-mails are considered essential to the provision of the service you have requested. You will not be able to choose to unsubscribe to these mailings as they are considered a part of the service you have chosen. 

**Security of your Personal Information:**  
We have established and maintain reasonable security procedures to protect the confidentiality, security and integrity of your personally identifiable information.  “Perfect security,” however, does not exist on the Internet.

Although your privacy is very important to us, due to the existing legal regulatory and security environment, we cannot fully ensure that your private communications and other personally identifiable information will not be disclosed to third parties.  For example, we may be forced to disclose information to the government or third parties under certain circumstances, or third parties may unlawfully intercept or access transmissions or private communications.  Additionally, in the unlikely event we need to investigate or resolve possible problems or inquiries, we can (and you authorize us to do so) disclose any information about you to private entities, law enforcement or other government officials as we, in our sole discretion, believe necessary or appropriate.

**Protecting Children:**  
@Last is committed to the safety of our children. @Last products are not marketed to anyone under the age of 18 years old (a “Minor”) and @Last does not knowingly collect personal information from Minors. @Last is committed to protecting the privacy of minors and if it becomes aware that such information was collected, it will treat that information as sensitive. Minors may not participate in any discussion boards, sweepstakes or contests, nor make any public postings or submissions on our Web site except through their parents or teachers.  Students Minors seeking to participate in contents are required to do so through their parents or teachers, and, should the submission be displayed on our Web site their personal information will be limited for their protection.
