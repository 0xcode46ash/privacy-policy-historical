# SkreemR Mp3 Search - Privacy

Skreemr respects your privacy. Our privacy policy is simple and unwavering:  


Skreemr does not store server logs that record your IP address or how you use the site. Skreemr stores only non-identifying statistics to analyze trends with our site and to help diagnose problems.  


Skreemr does not require or collect any personal information for the use of the services on this site. If Skreemr adds additional services to the site in the future that would require some minimal personal information (such as username, password and email address), Skreemr will not sell, trade or otherwise disclose your personal information to other companies.  


**Third Party Advertising**  
We use CPX Interactive INC and other third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please click [here](http://www.cpxinteractive.com/privacy_policy.htm)

**Third Party Cookies**  
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique “cookie” on your browser.
