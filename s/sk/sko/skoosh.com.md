# Skoosh - Privacy Policy

Skoosh conforms to the Data Protection Act of 1998, and any person providing information to Skoosh. is protected by the laws therein.   


Any or all Personally Identifiable Information collected from this website is used by Skoosh for contact and marketing purposes only. On the personal details page of this site, you have a yes/no box where you can opt out of receiving our mailings. Information collected is kept on record by Skoosh for future reference. Any information collected is not viewed by/given/sold/rented to any third party, and remains strictly confidential. The Personally Identifiable Information that Skoosh collects is:   
\- full name   
\- home address   
\- telephone number  
\- e-mail addresses 

These details are used only to send requested/important information to customers, and for our own marketing research. Credit card payments over the internet are secure and are managed using secure server technology (SSL encryption). Our card acquirer is Barclays Merchant Services, the information is sent using 128bit SSL encryption. **N.B. We do not store your credit card details**.

Any concerns that you may have with regards to security can be addressed to the IT Manager at [info@skoosh.com](mailto:info@skoosh.com)
