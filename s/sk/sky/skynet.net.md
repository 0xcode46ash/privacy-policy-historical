# Sky Net | Worldwide Express

By entering any data or information to this site, you authorize SKYNET to use it under the standards and principles of our privacy policy. Such Policy is part of these Terms and Conditions and we encourage you to read them carefully as follows: 

1\. Information - Visitor

In an automatic form, our system gathers information regarding SKYNET use volume , this info is used to determine the number of users we have.  
Only, regarding this info, SKYNET reserves the right to provide such information to our partners and SKYNET agents. We will not deliver any customer personal information.  
Information  
To close business between SKYNET and customer, SKYNET requires acceptance of “Service Agreement.” Also, SKYNET requires that customer provides his name, address, type of computer, e-mail address, credit card number and expiration date, and type of services desired by customer. Aditionally, we (SKYNET) require some information regarding customer interests and needs, this information will be optional.  
Disclosure  
SKYNET will not disclose customer personal information; such as name, mailing address, addresses, credit card numbers and expiration dates. This will be confidential and secret information.
