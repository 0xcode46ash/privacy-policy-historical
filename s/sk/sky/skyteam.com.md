# SkyTeam - Caring more about you

|   |   
  |   
**SkyTeam is always conscious and respectful of your personal privacy.**

**Last updated:** January 2004

SkyTeam recognizes the importance of protecting your personal information. 

This Privacy Policy will answer your questions about the kind of information we collect and how we use it. We have also included information on how to prevent your personal information from being collected or shared. SkyTeam reserves the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. If we modify or update our Privacy Policy, we will post the revised policy here, and any modifications will apply to all information collected by SkyTeam, including previously collected information. 

If you have any questions or comments related to the Privacy Policy, contact SkyTeam using; [skyteam@eprize.net](mailto:skyteam@eprize.net)

We collect information about your visits, such as service provider, browser type, operating system, pages accessed on our web site and the date and time of access to optimize your user experience and enhance your time spent with SkyTeam online. We also receive information telling SkyTeam what site you came from

We may retain your name, and possibly other personal information, such as mailing address, e-mail address or credit card number when you provide this information to us. We may ask you to voluntarily provide SkyTeam with information regarding your personal or professional interests, demographics, experience with our products, and contact preferences. This information may be combined with information about you which we gathered from our SkyTeam members. We use the information to design offers customized to your interests. For example, you may receive a special offer for your most frequently traveled destinations or be asked to participate in a survey.  
  
   > |  | 

We will not sell your name or other private profile information to third parties, and do not intend to do so in the future. We routinely share your information with our SkyTeam member airlines and their subsidiaries. SkyTeam may also send you promotional offers in connection with its promotional partners if you indicate that you wish to receive offers from them. From time to time, we may engage third parties to process information on our behalf; however, SkyTeam requires that these third parties comply with SkyTeam's Privacy Policy and principles when processing this information. We may share anonymous, non-personal aggregated information about all our users with third parties.  
  
   > |  | 

SkyTeam uses cookies, tags, and other similar technologies to improve your experience on our site. These technologies allow SkyTeam to enhance your visit to SkyTeam.com, provide you with information tailored to your needs and facilitate effective site administration.

You may elect to refuse cookies. Please refer to your browser Help instructions to learn more about cookies.   
  
    > |  | 

If a child under 13 in the United States has provided SkyTeam with personal information without parental or guardian consent, the parent or guardian may e-mail us, and we will remove the information and unsubscribe the child from any promotional contact opportunities. Likewise, SkyTeam takes appropriate measures to comply with similar local or national legislation in other countries.  
  
   > |  | 

We only contact you regarding promotional opportunities if you indicate you desire to receive offers from SkyTeam. A third party may act on SkyTeam's behalf and conduct a promotion for SkyTeam or assist SkyTeam in managing its marketing database. If you have indicated that you wish to receive promotional offers from SkyTeam, you may opt out of receiving future SkyTeam e-mail, telephone and postal marketing communications by contacting SkyTeam at [skyteam@eprize.net](mailto:skyteam@eprize.net) or mailing SkyTeam at ePrize, P.O. Box 9052, Farmington Hills, MI 48333-9052. If you have questions about removing your name from any of our subscription lists, you may contact SkyTeam at [skyteam@eprize.net](mailto:skyteam@eprize.net). Please allow 3-4 business days for any changes to take effect.  
  
   > |  | 

We ask that you keep your information as up-to-date as possible so you may get the maximum benefit from SkyTeam.com. Go to [skyteam@eprize.net](mailto:skyteam@eprize.net) to make your changes or mail them to ePrize, P.O. Box 9052, Farmington Hills, MI 48333-9052.  
  
   > |  | 

This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of each site that collects information from you. SkyTeam's Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected by SkyTeam.  
  
   > |  | 

SkyTeam uses appropriate physical, electronic and managerial safeguards to protect the information you provide to us.
