# SkyscraperPage.com Privacy Policy

[](https://web.archive.org/web/20001205095600id_/http%3A//skyscraperpage.com/index.htm)  
|    
  
---|---  
  
SkyscraperPage.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.  


The forum on this website collects personal information when a person registers to be a member. This information is is avaliable to anyone who views a members profile. It is not required to provide personal information though. Messages in the forum may be saved for many years time, the forum can become an archive of information supplied by or about a person. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in the forum becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.  


The Amateur Highrise Photography Page does display personal info. A person who submits photos may have their name and/or email displayed, photo date and description text.  


Personal information collected through contests this website holds is used to contact the winner of the contest and to mail the prize to them. The name if the contest winner is announced on this website.  


Information colleted thru the Demographic Survey is shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis.  


The forum uses cookies for login info. Some ads shown on this website may also use cookies.  


This site contains links to other sites. SkyscraperPage.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.  


If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this website, please [email us](https://web.archive.org/web/20001205095600id_/http%3A//skyscraperpage.com/files/input.htm).  


The Privacy Policy of our ad agency: [Burst Media Privacy Policy](http://www.burstmedia.com/release/privacy.asp).
