# Welcome to SgtSearch! Search should always be this simple.

**Banner Ads-**  
SgtSearch.com works with a third party that serves ads to this site. To find out more about how Flycast manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to the [FlyCast Privacy statement pages](http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html). 

**Email Accounts-**  
As you sign up for our email services, some demographic information is collected for internal use only. For an example- If you are a male and you told us you want to learn more about college, we may use this information to send you information on that topic. We never sell, rent, lease, or share personal demographics with third parties. Our overall site demographics may be used and shared at times to serve our community as a whole. Example- Our site is 50 percent male and 50 percent female users. 

**Cookies**  
Our weather technology utilizes cookies ONLY when YOU WANT to set a custom weather page with SgtStorm. The rest of our site is cookie free, and we track NO user information unless implied within our site. 

**Bottom Line Privacy**  
SgtSearch.com adheres to the highest standards when it comes to our visitors internet experience. Our privacy policy is not lengthy as we are a public service site, with little user data collection. As we grow and enter into working relationships with internet pioneers of privacy such as FlyCast, it is our way of proving our NO TOLERANCE limit to breach of personal privacy. 

**Changes to policy**  
As we add new services that may use cookies or new tracking technology, here is where you will find it first. SgtSearch.com is set on making your visit with us rewarding for both parties alike, and we look forward to any questions you may have about internet privacy. **If you are an internet privacy provider and would like to contact us about joining your services, we would love to hear from you. Please email privacy@sgtsearch.com with details and your website address.** SgtSearch out...
