# SGI - Company Info: Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

At SGI, your privacy is important to us, and we would like to provide you with answers to the following questions regarding our privacy policy. 

**I. What information does SGI collect about me when I visit sgi.com and how is that information used?**

For the purpose of updating and improving our web site to serve our customers more effectively, we collect general information about you when you visit sgi.com, including your domain name, the type and version of browser and operating system that you use, how much time you spend browsing our site and which pages you visit. However, unless you voluntarily provide personal information to us, such as your name or email address, we do not collect such information about you. We use personal information provided by you only to perform services that you request, such as adding your email address to a mailing list or answering your technical support questions. 

**II. Does SGI share any information about me with third parties?**

No. We will use our best efforts not to transfer information that personally identifies you to anyone else without your knowledge and approval at the time that you submit such information to us. 

**III. Will I receive unsolicited email as a result of visiting sgi.com?**

No. We will only contact you in connection with services that you request, such as confirming that you have been added to a subscription list or responding to your email queries. 

**IV. Does sgi.com use cookies?**

Occasionally, we use cookies to help you navigate our site more efficiently. For example, we may use cookies to tell us whether you have already visited a page on our site.
