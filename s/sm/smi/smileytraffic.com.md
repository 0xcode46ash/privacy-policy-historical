Personal Information  
Is NEVER EVER given out to anyone, this is collected to set up your account and for payment or contact with Smileytraffic.com.

Cookies  
Smileytraffic.com uses cookies to store session information so you will stay logged in and database can accurately add credits properly to your account.

Advertisers  
Most advertisements on Smileytraffic.com are provided by outside companies and may contain cookies. These cookies are collected by their respective companies and are not any part of this website.

IP Addresses and Logging  
Smileytraffic.com server logs information about visitors, including IP addresses, date/time visited, referring website, length of stay, etc. This information is used for site statistics only.

Customer Information & Credit Card Transactions  


Limited information is supplied to Smileytraffic.com in the event of a sale. This includes your name, IP Address and website domain name. This information is not sold or distributed to third parties.
