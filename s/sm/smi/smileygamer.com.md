SmileyGamer.com respects the privacy of Internet users.  
In general, we receive and store any information you enter on our Web site or give us in any other way. You can choose not to provide certain information. We use the information that you provide for such purposes as responding to your requests, and communicating with you. This information will not be shared with third parties.

**Consumer information**  
During the process of delivering an ad to you, no personally identifiable information about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address is collected. However, some types of non-personally identifiable information such as the Internet Service Provider you use to connect to the Internet, your browser type, or the type of computer operating system you use are obtained. The information collected are used for the purpose of targeting ads and measuring ad effectiveness on behalf of our advertisers.
