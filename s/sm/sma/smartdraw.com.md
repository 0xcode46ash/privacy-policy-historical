# SmartDraw Privacy Policy

**1)** **Your Information is Secure**  
****  
We will never release your e-mail address or personal information to any other company. 

**2) Unsubscribe from our Mailings at Any Time**  
****  
If at any time you no longer want to receive information about free updates or product upgrades from us, just let us know.
