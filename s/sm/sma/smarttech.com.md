# SMART Technologies Inc.

  
The following information explains the policy of SMART Technologies Inc. (together with its subsidiaries, collectively "SMART") regarding any personal information you may supply when visiting any of SMART's Web sites. SMART's goal in collecting personal information online is to provide you with a personalized Web experience and focused customer service. SMART strives to deliver relevant information that meets your needs and ensures that your visit to SMART's Web site is easy and efficient. 

SMART collects personal contact information only (i) when you register to receive personalized information on its Web site or from SMART Customer Service; (ii) to gain entry into one of SMART's secure sites; and (iii) to enter one of SMART's promotions. When you register for certain sales promotions SMART may share your personal information with a SMART channel partner that it feels can help meet your needs. If you don't want your personal information distributed to others, or if you would like to be removed from any of SMART's subscription lists, please [contact SMART](mailto:info@smarttech.com). SMART will not sell or rent your information to any other organization without your explicit permission.

**IP Addresses**  
SMART collects IP addresses for system administration and internal tracking. When you visit SMART's Web site, our servers log the IP addresses only. SMART does not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable. 

**Cookies**  
Some of SMART's Web pages use "cookies" so that we can better serve you with more tailored information when you return to SMART's Web site. Cookies are pieces of information that are transferred to your computer's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. The information stored will make it faster and easier for you to use SMART's Web site because it allows you to use the Web site without having to re-enter information on each visit.

SMART does not use cookies to retrieve information from your computer that was not originally sent in the cookie, and it does not use information transferred through cookies for any promotional or marketing purposes. Some SMART Web pages use cookies to track usage patterns in order to improve the usefulness and effectiveness of SMART's Web site. This information is not shared or sold to any third parties whatsoever. 

By using SMART's Web site, you signify your agreement with the SMART Privacy Policy. SMART reserves the right, at its discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time. Your continued use of this Web site following changes to these terms means you accept those changes.
