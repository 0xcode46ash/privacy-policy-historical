# Privacy Policy

[](https://www.smartbets.com/)

Your privacy is important to us. With that in mind, we have developed this policy in order for you to understand how we collect, use, communicate, disclose and make use of personal information. We are committed to maintaining and applying these principles in order to ensure that the confidentiality of your personal information is protected and assured.

You have access to your personal information through your settings on smartbets. Here you can request changes to your personal information.  
Should you have any questions regarding your personal information do not hesitate to reach out to us through our support form on smartbets or the contact form in our apps.

## Cookies

We use cookies at smartbets. The aim of cookies is to collect anonymized information about our users in order to make the website experience better (e.g. by remembering login information, etc.) and improve the website and community. The cookies we use are Google Analytics, Visual Website Optimizer, and user login sessions at smartbets.com.

These cookies give us annonymized information about browser type, geography, referral, operation system, time of visit, and number of page views. You can see all cookie details concerning your specific computer here. By using smartbets.com, you accept that we may make use of these cookies.
