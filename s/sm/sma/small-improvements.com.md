# Small Improvements - Privacy Policy

#### Small Improvements is committed to protect the privacy of our customers. This Privacy Policy outlines the information collected as well as it may be used.

  1. When you register with Small Improvements, information is needed such as your name, company name, email address. 
  2. Small Improvements uses collected information for purposes such as processing and fulfilling your order, billing, service improvement, research, and other general purposes.
  3. Small Improvements will not make collected information available to any other parties. We will not sell, rent, exchange or share your personal information with any third-parties without permission.
  4. Small Improvements will share the collected information in order to respond to investigation, court orders, legal process, or to investigate, prevent or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, or situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Small Improvements's terms of service, or as otherwise required by law.
  5. All of your data remain private and confidential.
  6. Small Improvements will send a welcome email, and emails regarding the status of a person's performance review, or 360 degree reviews, or objectives, or the like. Administrators will at times receive Small Improvements service related announcements. All emails can get disabled by the recipient in their settings page.
  7. The Small Improvements website uses [Google Analytics](http://www.google.com/intl/en/analytics/index.html) to collect information about its visitors. Once a user logs in to Small Improvements, Google Analytics gets disabled for the user as long as she is logged in, and no data is collected.
  8. Small Improvements uses a technology called "cookies" to store session information. A cookie is a small amount of data, which includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from our web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. Small Improvements will set and access Small Improvements cookies on your computer, cookies are required to use the Small Improvements service.
  9. Small Improvements may update this Privacy Policy, and will notify account holders of significant changes in the way we treat any personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your Small Improvements account. We may also place a prominent notice on our site.
  10. For questions related to this Privacy Policy, contact support@small-improvements.com
