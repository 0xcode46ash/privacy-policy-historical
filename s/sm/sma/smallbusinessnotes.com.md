Small Business Notes is committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. Below is information outlining our continued support for the responsible management of information. 

Small Business Notes does not sell or share email addresses or other personal information that people using this site might provide. Unless otherwise directed by the person submitting a comment, email addresses will only be used as necessary to reply to questions or to follow up on legitimate complaints about misuse of the service. 

As a fundamental part of operating the site, usage logs are created, archived, and analyzed for aggregate information about usage patterns. No personal information is contained in these reports. Small Business Notes reserves the right to share this aggregate information when appropriate. 

The usage logs also contain IP addresses that could be used by law enforcement personnel to track a specific user. Small Business Notes will cooperate with law enforcement personnel by providing this information in the event that the site becomes part of a legitimate criminal investigation. 

Small Business Notes links to many external sites that provide related content. We have no control over the privacy policies of these sites. 

Small Business Notes also provides some commercial services in association with third parties, including [Amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/redirect?tag=antiimperialiint&path=subst/misc/policy/privacy.html) and other respected retailers and content and service providers. Please see the privacy statements hosted at their sites. 

Small Business Notes uses third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please read the privacy statements posted by the [Sonar Network](http://www.sonar.com/naiprivacy), [Burst! Media](http://www.burstmedia.com/release/privacy.asp), [Fastclick](http://fastclick.com/about/privacy2.html), [BeFree](http://www.befree.com/docs/includes/privacy.htm), and [LinkShare](http://www.linkshare.com/privacy.html). In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.
