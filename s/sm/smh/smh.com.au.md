# smh.com.au - Privacy Policy

  


  
Any and all information collected - including membership forms or email lists - at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will nntial and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to us will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to.

We analyse our website logs to constantly improve the value of the website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

We maintain several email lists to keep subscribers informed about areas of specific interest. Individuals must request to join our mailing lists by signing up through our website. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone.

Click [here](http://www.members.f2.com.au/help/privacy_policy.html) for further information. 

The Sydney Morning Herald is committed to the Australian Press Council's [Privacy Standards](https://web.archive.org/news/apc.html).
