# Santa Monica Daily Press

**Privacy Policy**

Information we collect from users of the site will be stored electronically for publication marketing and newsletter marketing purposes for those users that have opted to receive such information.

**Contact Details**

We will use your contact details (your name, job title, telephone and fax number and your e-mail and postal addresses) to provide any services requested by you and also to tell you about new products, offers, and changes to the site. These details will be used by us or partners representing our services to provide you with information about other services and products related to our services. We will not sell or rent personal information collected about you. The customers that utilize our website frameworks on their domains are solely responsible for the handling of information collected therein and must be contacted directly regarding their handling and policies of such information, interactions, data collection, and storage.

If you do not wish to be contacted, would like to be removed, or have your information updated or corrected from our database, please express your preferences by emailing us. 

Additionally, you may opt-out of any newsletter or promotional information by reply via opt-out links included in our email communications or by emailing us. 

**Voluntary Information**

We may ask for further information about you as part of online surveys, requirements to use or access certain information or functionality or to determine suitability of other promotional products and offers to be sent to you. This information will be used to enable us to improve our website, client sites, or online services.

**Cookies**

We make use of cookies to gather valuable information about how users move around the site. This enables us to understand how the site is used and to make improvements. The information we report by use of cookies does not include personally identifying information.

**Statistical Information**

Website usage information will be used for the purposes of marketing and reporting. Usage information on our site and client sites will not include personally identifying information.

**Contact Us**

You may write or contact us for any questions or requests related to our policies.
