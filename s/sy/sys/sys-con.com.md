# Privacy Information @ SYS-CON Media

TOP LINUX LINKS YOU MUST CLICK ON

SYS-CON Media ** Does Not Disclose Your Personal Information** to outside parties under any circumstances. We do ask you if you are interested in receiving relevant information or offers from other i-technology vendors via e-mail. You may choose to receive this information or easily "unsubscribe" from each magazine's "Opt In List" not to receive any e-mails from SYS-CON outside of your monthlys newsletter.

If you choose to receive additional information other than your monthly newsletters, this information is sent to you directly by SYS-CON. If you choose not to receive other information from us, this will not affect your regular "Digital Edition" or "Archives" subscriptions. If you choose to discontinue your "Digital Edition" or "Archives" subscriptions, you may also do this by simply unsubscribing to "Digital Edition" or "Archives" any time during your subscription period. 

When you send us your "unsubscribe" request to each of our offerings such as "Digital Edition," "Archives" or "Buyer's Guide", your record gets permanently removed from these databases as we receive your remove requests. 

Thank you for making SYS-CON Media the world's leading i-technology resource and please let us know how we can serve you better!
