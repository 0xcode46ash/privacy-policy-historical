# Syncapse: Privacy

January 14, 2008

At Syncapse Corp. we work hard to ensure the privacy of our users. This statement explains the information we collect, how we use that information, and some of the measures we have implemented to ensure our users' privacy.

This privacy policy applies to the Syncapse.com website and does not cover those applications developed by Syncapse Corp. The applications developed by Syncapse Corp. are documented in their own privacy policies.

  * Syncapse collects names and email addresses from visitors who subscribe to our newsletter. Users wishing to unsubscribe can follow the instructions included in each newsletter or simply call us to have their name removed from our list.
  * Syncapse collects contact and payment information from visitors who make purchases from our website.
  * Syncapse's server automatically records the details of your visit whenever you access our website including the URL accessed, your IP address, browser type, language, and date/time of your request.
  * We do not use cookies to track user information, but employ a third party application (Google Analytics) that may use cookies to track users.


  * We protect the private information of our users carefully. We have appropriate measures in place to protect against the loss, misues, or alteration of information that we collect from visitors of our website.
  * We use personal information only for the purpose it was originally given to us for.


  * From time to time, we make our customer e-mail list available to reputable organizations who we believe have products or services that are of interest to our users. If you would prefer us not to share your information, please let us know by calling us.
  * Telephone numbers and postal addresses are not shared with third parties.


  * Our privacy policy may change at some time in the future. All changes in our privacy policy will be posted on our website.
  * If you are concerned with how these changes affect the use of your information, please check our web site periodically for privacy policy updates.



If you feel that this site is not following its stated privacy policy, you may contact The DMA's Committee on Ethical Business Practices at mgoldberger@the-dma.org.
