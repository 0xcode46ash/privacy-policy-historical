# Synergent Corporation - Privacy Statement

The privacy of our website visitors is important to us, and therefore, we have adopted the following privacy policy.

If you have any questions and/or comments about this policy, please contact 

[Jon Paradise](mailto:jparadise@mainecul.org).

  1. **Institution Information Areas**  
For our staff only, Synergent collects and stores information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, user IDs and passswords of institutions who register to access the "affiliate-only" areas of the website. This information is used to improve the quality of the website information and services to credit unions.
  2.  **General Information Areas**  
To track website use, measure site traffic, and improve site navigation and information, Synergent collects domain names, dates and times of website visits.
  3.  **Online Registration**  
This information is never sold, given or disclosed to third parties.
  4.  **Email**  
Synergent may use feedback information from emails sent to Synergent to respond to inquiries, comments or suggestions. This information is never sold, given or disclosed to third parties.
