# Privacy Policy - Svpply

## Purpose of this Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Svpply treat the personal information that we have collected about you when you visit and use our service.

## What data do we collect?

We receive and store any information you enter on our Web site or provide to us in any other way. This includes but is not limited to registration and profile information, IP addresses, referral pages, service behavior, and traffic data. We do not track your information or behavior on any third party locations or services unrelated to Svpply.

## What do we plan to do with this data?

Your data serves two overt functions: (1) By storing and tracking this information, we hope to gain a better understanding of site usage behavior and to subsequently improve the content and offerings on our site. (2) On an aggregate, non-personally identifiable level we may use this data for market research products and reports. 

## Your data and third parties

We do not sell or rent any of your personally identifying data to third parties for direct marketing purposes. We do not collect specific member address information, anyway, so this is a moot point.

We may share or disclose information about you to other companies or individuals when: (1) we must comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal or regulatory process, (2) in the event of a sale, merger, transfer, or exchange of control of Svpply to another party.

## What information can I access?

You can access your personal information on our Web site and correct, amend or delete information that is inaccurate. Upon logging in visit your user profile setting page located at: <https://svpply.com/settings/>. Upon request, we are happy to provide access to any site behaviour data that we have collected about you. All requests for such data will be fulfilled within a 14-day period.

## Communications with Members

When you register with Svpply, you will be automatically opted-in to receive email and in-system notices, which may include announcements, newsletters and recommendations. You are welcome to elect out of receiving those messages at any time by clicking here.

## Public sharing of your data

Svpply allows the public and other members of the site to browse your public profile and saved items. Svpply allows other users to browse your username, followed members, number of items saved, general place of residence, member number, invitee, personal website, favorite category and favorite price range. number of saved items, and number of people who have followed your posts. Svpply also allows for the RSS syndication of all of its public content within the Web site.

## Removal of User and User data

We will not remove posts on a per-post basis save in the exception of Copyright protection or dispute, see our Terms of Service for more on our Copyright policies. If you would like your user profile removed from Svpply, contact us here. Your public profile and URL will be deleted within 72 hours.
