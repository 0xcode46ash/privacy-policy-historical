# Student Job Search | Privacy Policy

I certify that the information I have given is true and correct.

I acknowledge this information is being collected for Student Job Search to offer an effective job service.

I understand that it will be used primarily to enable SJS to assist me to obtain employment / employees. It will also be used within SJS for statistical purposes and for student / employer satisfaction surveys conducted by an independent agency to assist SJS in better serving our clients.

I agree that SJS may give my contact details to my past, current or prospective employers or employees.

I understand that SJS may be required by law to provide information on filled jobs to the Ministry of Social Development if they request it, in order to check students' eligibility to receive the unemployment benefit. I agree to the information being given to the Ministry of Social development.

If I wish to inspect my personal information held by SJS I understand that at my request SJS will show me the database screen display of my file.  

I have read, understand and agree to abide by the SJS terms and conditions.
