# Steinhafels Furniture - Privacy Policy

At Steinhafels Furniture, we respect and share your concerns regarding privacy. We keep information entered during ordering and registering confidential. 

You may also create an account through steinhafels.com at any time by providing your email address and designating a unique access password. You do not need to create an account in order to visit our site. For your privacy and security, we do not store credit card numbers in your account. 

We never sell or rent your personal information to any third parties under any circumstances. In some instances, we partner with other companies to provide our products and services to you. Some of these companies are given access to some, or all, of the information you provide to us and may use cookies 

(see definition below)  on our behalf. These companies are restricted from using your information in any way other than providing products and services through our web site. 

As a customer or subscriber, you may occasionally receive e-mail communications about new Steinhafel products or special offers we think you’ll like and requests for feedback. 

Cookies are pieces of information that are stored by your hard drive to remember aspects of your visits to a particular web site. We may use cookies to help us improve your shopping experience by tracking which areas of our site are most and least popular. We never use cookies to store confidential information like passwords or credit card information. We may also collect certain technical information from your computer. This technical information may include your Internet Protocol (IP) address, your computer’s operating system, browser type and the address of a referring website, if any. We will use such information only to investigate and identify unauthorized activities.
