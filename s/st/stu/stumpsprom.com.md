# StumpsProm.com: Customer Service: Site Security & Privacy

### Site Security & Privacy

Stumps is very concerned about site security on the Internet today. That is why we have made sure that **we have followed and surpassed all industry standards** necessary for a secure site. These include encrypting all data that you give to us, processing your credit card information in a safe and responsible matter, processing your order soon after it is received and not sharing any information that has been given to us by you, our customers. We want you to feel confident when ordering from **Stumps** , and we are doing everything we can to continue to operate a secure site. If you have any other questions concerning site security, please feel free to [e-mail us](mailto:csr@stumpsprom.com).

**Privacy Statement**  
Our customer's privacy is our number one concern. That is why we take every precaution to make sure that all customer information is kept completely private. These would be specials, contest information or short messages containing information that we think you would want to know.
