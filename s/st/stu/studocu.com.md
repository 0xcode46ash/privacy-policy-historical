# Privacy Policy - StuDocu

* * *

**Privacy Policy**

  
StuDocu.com (StudeerSnel Holding B.V.) takes the privacy of its Users very seriously and will process and use information about you in a manner that is in conformity with current security standards.. StuDocu.com is committed to respecting the privacy of its Users and the confidentiality of your personal data, traffic data and communications content and conforms to the applicable Dutch legislation and regulations in relation thereto. This Privacy Policy is designed to in understanding how StuDocu.com uses and safeguards User-information.

In the event that the words written in capitals are not defined in this Privacy Policy they are defined in article 1 of the [Terms of Use ](https://web.archive.org/web/20151223220305id_/http%3A//terms/7)

**StuDocu.com    
**StudeerSnel Holding B.V. is a company which offers an online Platform which gives users the possibility to exchange Study Material among themselves, hereafter " **StuDocu.com** ". StuDocu.com has its registered offices at (1053BA) Amsterdam at Wenslauerstraat 16-2, 1053 BA Amsterdam and is registered under Company Registration Number 56829787.

This Privacy Policy applies solely to personal information that is acquired on the Websites that fall under the corporate responsibility of StuDocu.com. StuDocu.com urges you to be careful when you enter any personal information online.. StuDocu.com neither accepts responsibility or liability for personal information disclosed to other users by yourself on our Website or Platform. Please be aware that whenever you voluntarily post public information on our Website or Platform that information may be accessed by the public.

  
The Platform may contain hyperlinks which, if clicked, will navigate you to the websites of third parties. StuDocu.com has no control over the websites of third parties. It is possible that a different privacy policy applies to the use of these websites. This Privacy Statement only applies to personal data obtained by means of the Platform. StuDocu.com accepts no responsibility or liability for the (operation or contents of) third party websites.

**Personal information  
**Personal data is information that directly identifies you and may be used to contact you online or offline. We collect personal data during your use of our Service, for example during your (profile)registration on StuDocu.com.This information may include your name, email address, phone number, mobile number, home address, gender birth date and credit card of bank account information. Some of the requested information during your Profile-registration is optional. You can always amend the information you have submitted in you Profile.We will only collect and transfer personal information to the extent reasonable necessary to fulfil your requests.

**Non-personal information  
** StuDocu.com is also able to collect non –personal identifiable information including IP Address, profile information, aggregate user data and browser type, from their Users. 

StDocu.com also collects Automatically Generated Information from all users.This information consists of your surfing behavior on the Platform, such as the web pages you visit, how long you stay on certain web pages, what you download and/or upload and the use you make of your Profile. 

We also use cookies to passively collect demographic information, personalize your experience on our Website and monitor advertisements and other activities. Cookies are small files downloaded to your computer to track movements within websites. Also, we use cookies to deliver content specific to your interest and for other purposes. We may link cookie information to personal information. You can disable cookies, however some areas of the Website will not function properly if you do so. 

This data is used to manage the Website and Platform, track usage, improve the Service and for security and monitoring purposes. We will only collect and transfer non-personal information to the extent reasonable necessary to fulfil your requests.

**Use by StuDocu.com**  
StuDocu.com offers a Platform which enables users to exchange Study Material among themselves. In order to ensure that the Study Material can indeed be offered and paid for on the Platform, StuDocu.com must use your personal data. 

StuDocu.com also wishes to keep you informed on the development of its Platform. In case of an upgrade, update or any other change to the Platform, StuDocu.com will let you know. This also applies to information regarding payments. It is not possible to opt out of these messages, as they are crucial for the performance of the service. 

Furthermore, by sending you the StuDocu.com newsletter, StuDocu.com will keep you informed of promotions it organizes. You will receive the StuDocu.comnewsletter on a regular basis. If you no longer wish to receive the StuDocu.com newsletter, you may unsubscribe by editing your personal settings within your profile. The possibility to unsubscribe is also included by a direct link in every individual StuDocu.com newsletter. If you unsubscribe, StuDocu.com advises you to regularly check the Platform or social media for new promotions. 

In the Terms of Use you grant StuDocu.com the right to publish and copy your personal data (such as your username and Profile picture) and Study Materials in order to promote the Platform. This means, inter alia, that StuDocu.com can use certain personal data for the promotion of the Platform, for instance by stating who is the most successful user, what Study Material has been downloaded the most or what Study Material or which users are new. 

**Use by Third Parties**  
Accept as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy , StuDocu.com will not disclose personal information to any third party unless we believe that disclosure is necessary:  
1) To conform to legal requirements or to respond to a subpoena, search warrant or other legal process received by StuDocu.com, such as an infringement on Intellectual Property rights of others, whether or not a response is required by applicable law;  
2) To enforce the StuDocu.com agreements with you, to manage the website or to protect your rights;  
3) Tot protect the safety of members of the public and Users of the Service.  
4) StuDocu.com also uses Google Analytics, a web analysis service provided by Google Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses 'cookies', which are text files placed on your computer as described in paragraph 3, to help the website analyse how you use the site. The information generated by the cookies about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the website, compiling reports on website activity for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however please note that if you do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of this Website. By using this Website, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above  
5) In the event of StuDocu.com's further growth and development, one or more parts of the assets of the company may be transferred to a third party or StuDocu.com may merge with a third party. In that case your personal data will also be transferred.

Your information may be transferred to countries both in and outside the European Union. StuDocu.com cannot assure that countries outside the European Union have an adequate level of protection.

**Protection  
** StuDocu.com shall take suitable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data.  StuDocu.com shall not retain personal information longer than required in order to perform its Services or as required under relevant legislation. 

**Modification**  
StuDocu.com reserves the right to modify the provisions in this privacy policy if necessary. We will inform you of these changes on the Website and the Platform.  

**Contact  
**You can always inspect and, if necessary, amend the personal data in your Profile. If you wish to receive an overview of all the personal data about you stored by StuDocu.com, you can send StuDocu.com a request for inspection. StuDocu.com shall send you an overview of stored personal data within four (4) weeks. Subsequently you can ask StuDocu.com to correct, supplement, delete or protect the collected personal data. StuDocu.com will inform you within four (4) weeks whether it will honor your request to correct supplement, delete or protect your personal data. The abovementioned requests can be sent to jacques@StuDocu.com.com.

If you have any further questions regarding this privacy statement, please send an email to jacques@StuDocu.com.com.
