# Privacy Policy Statement

**Privacy Policy Statement**

We welcome you to the website of Iowa Student Loan Liquidity Corporation (Iowa Student Loan), a private nonprofit corporation. We understand that you, our customer and website visitor, are concerned about the privacy and security of your personal information. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it, which is why we have adopted the following privacy policy. This policy lets you know more about the ways we might collect, process and use your nonpublic personal information.

The information contained in the privacy policy may change over time without notice to you. Please check back periodically for updates.

**
  * Collection and Release of Information 
**
****

> ****
> 
> ****Iowa Student Loan recognizes the importance of respecting the privacy of our customers, applicants and website visitors. We want to assure you that Iowa Student Loan uses information gathered about you only for the limited purposes permitted by law. We collect nonpublic personal information about you from the following sources: Information we receive from you on loan applications or other forms; information about your transactions with us or others; information we receive from consumer reporting agencies; and information we receive from nonaffiliated third parties, such as your school and any affiliated loan guarantee agencies.
> 
> We only collect information we need relative to processing loan applications, administering loan accounts, providing services to customers, providing customers and website visitors with information about our products, services and upcoming events, and to fulfill any legal and regulatory requirements. We do not release or disclose any nonpublic personal information about you to anyone, except as permitted by law. 
> 
> Our web server automatically recognizes the Internet service providers of our website visitors. Additionally, we collect information about (i)websites from which our visitors connect to our site, (ii)the browsers visitors use, and (iii) the page(s) consumers access on our site. We compile and sort this information in order to develop group profiles of our website visitors, to assist with systems administration, and to resolve problems.
> 
> We collect the electronic mail addresses of those who communicate with us via electronic mail, and information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations. We do not automatically collect any of this information from our website visitors.
> 
> If you supply us with your electronic mail address, postal address and/or telephone number on-line, we may contact you or send you information about our new products and services, or upcoming events.
> 
> We do not intend to collect information from children under 13 years of age. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal identifying information from a child under the age of 13, we will promptly take all reasonable measures to delete the data from the system. ****

**
  * Opt-out Policy
**
****

> ****
> 
> If you do not want us to contact you or send you information about available products or services or upcoming events, you may send us an electronic message at [marketng@studentloan.org](mailto:marketng@studentloan.org) or advise us during any telephone call we may make to you. Please provide us with your exact name and address. If possible, provide us with your name as it appears on any mailings you have received from Iowa Student Loan.

**
  * Employee Responsibility
**
****

> ****
> 
> Each Iowa Student Loan employee is personally responsible for maintaining consumer confidence in the company. We provide training designed to educate employees about the meaning and requirements of these privacy policies. We conduct internal audits of our compliance with the privacy policies. Employees who violate these policies or other company policies and practices are subject to disciplinary action, up to and including dismissal.

**
  * Business Relationships
**
****

> ****
> 
> We expect the companies we select as our business partners and service providers to honor our privacy principles when they handle information about our customers. We do not sell or rent our customer information to any outside company or organization.

**
  * Secure Technology
**
****

> ****
> 
> We use state-of-the-art technology to keep your personal information (including account information) as secure as possible.

Iowa Student Loan is committed to preserving the privacy of our customers. We have described the manner in which we collect and use nonpublic personal information. In the event you have concerns, questions or comments about this policy, please contact us at the addresses or telephone number provided above.
