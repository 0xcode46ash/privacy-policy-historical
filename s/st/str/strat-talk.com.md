# Fender Stratocaster Guitar Forum

_Current as of Febuary 1, 2011_

Because protecting your privacy is very important to STRAT-TALK.COM, we have established this Privacy Policy, which explains what information we collect from our visitors, how we use that information, and how you can view or control use of the information you submit. Please note that this policy may be revised from time to time, so please check it frequently. We will post notice of any changes to this policy.

### Personal Information

STRAT-TALK.COM asks for personal information only when we need information that personally identifies you (“Personal Information”). For example, STRAT-TALK.COM asks for your name, email address, and location when you register, and collects additional information for surveys or other promotions sponsored by STRAT-TALK.COM and/or its promotional partners. We use your Personal Information to operate STRAT-TALK.COM’s services, and to help identify you and gather broad demographic information.

### Cookies

STRAT-TALK.COM or its advertisers may place a “cookie” in the browser files of your computer. Cookies are small pieces of information or files that are stored on a user’s computer by the Web server through a user’s browser for record keeping purposes. The information can be retrieved to personalize, monitor, or regulate use of the site. Cookies allow STRAT-TALK.COM to automate access and data entry functions of the site, such as login information, and tailor the site to a user’s preferences or interests. In addition, cookies allow STRAT-TALK.COM to track usage of the site to determine those areas of the site that are useful or popular (and those that are not), thereby enabling STRAT-TALK.COM to improve and update its service.

### Direct Mailings and “Opting In”

STRAT-TALK.COM may send you information about various products and services that may be of interest to you. When you register for this website you automatically “opt in” to receive such communications during the registration process. Each such email will have an unsubscribe link to remove yourself from the mailing list.

### Public Forums

STRAT-TALK.COM offers User Reviews & Ratings and Discussion boards. STRAT-TALK.COM users should be aware that when they voluntarily disclose Personal Information to other users or third parties on the STRAT-TALK.COM system, through email, or outside of the STRAT-TALK.COM system, that information can be collected and used by others and may result in unsolicited messages from other members or parties. While STRAT-TALK.COM strives to protect your Personal Information, STRAT-TALK.COM cannot guarantee the security of any information that you transmit to STRAT-TALK.COM and you are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of any passwords or other account information.

### Links to Other Websites

STRAT-TALK.COM may provide links to its partners and other Internet sites. These sites have separate data and privacy practices independent of STRAT-TALK.COM, and STRAT-TALK.COM disclaims any responsibility or liability for their policies or actions. 

You can view and make changes to the information you have previously provided to STRAT-TALK.COM, by visiting your [User Profile.](http://www.strat-talk.com/forum/usercp.php)

### Security

To protect the security of your Personal Information, STRAT-TALK.COM limits access to user data to authorized STRAT-TALK.COM personnel. STRAT-TALK.COM will NOT disclose any Personal Information except as described above or in its Terms & Conditions. STRAT-TALK.COM may disclose user information, including demographic data, in an aggregated form that does not identify individual users in order to describe its services to prospective partners, advertisers and other third parties and for other lawful purposes. STRAT-TALK.COM may disclose information about you if we have a good faith belief that we are required to do so by law or legal process, to respond to claims, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of STRAT-TALK.COM or others.

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, or if you believe STRAT-TALK.COM has not adhered to these principles, please contact us at: [webmaster@strat-talk.com](mailto:webmaster@strat-talk.com)
