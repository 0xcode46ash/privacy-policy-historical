# Stride Rite Children's Shoes - Official Site - Baby, Toddler & Kid's Shoes - Standard & Wide Widths

Thank you for visiting striderite.com, a Stride Rite Corporation site. Your privacy is important to us. We understand that when you choose to provide us with information about yourself, you trust us to act responsibly. This policy explains how we collect information from you and how we use it. Please read on for more details about our privacy practices.

**What Information Does Stride Rite Collect?**

Stride Rite does not collect personally identifiable information about individuals except when the information is knowingly provided. Generally, this information is requested in connection with an online transaction.

From time to time at striderite.com, you can enter contests, vote in polls, request product information, or subscribe to our Online Program. The types of personally identifiable information that may be collected at these pages include your name, mailing address, e-mail address, birth date, and information about your interests in and use of various products, programs and services.

At some other pages in the site, you can submit information about other people. For example, you might submit your child's name and birth date so we can send them an electronic birthday card or greeting. The types of personally identifiable information that may be collected about other people at these pages include their name, sex and birth date.

We also may collect certain non-personally identifiable information when you visit our web pages such as the type of browser you are using, the type of operating system you are using and the domain name of your Internet service provider. 

**How Do We Use The Information?**

We may use the information you provide about yourself to respond to your inquiries about our products, offerings, and services, and to offer you other products, programs or services we think you will find valuable.

We sometimes use this information to communicate with you, such as to notify you when you have won one of our contests or when we make changes to our web site or subscriber agreements, or to fulfill a request by you for information about a product or store location.

We use the information that you provide about others to enable us to send them an electronic greeting or online birthday card.

We sometimes use the non-personally identifiable information that we collect to improve the design and content of our site. We also may use this information in the aggregate to analyze site usage, as well as offer you other products, programs or services.

We may store and disclose personal information as allowed or required by applicable law, including making disclosures that are necessary or advisable to: (a) protect the rights, safety or property of users of our web site, the public, or Stride Rite; and (b) conform to legal or regulatory requirements. 

**Does Stride Rite Use "Cookies"?**

Yes. "Cookies" are bits of electronic information that a web site can transfer to a visitor's hard drive. Cookies, by themselves, do not tell us your e-mail address or other personally identifiable information. 

We use cookies to help make our site better by showing how and when you use the site. Stride Rite does not use information transferred through cookies for any promotional or marketing purposes, nor is the information shared with any third parties. 

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent that if you would rather not store the information for future use. 

**Does Stride Rite Disclose The Information It Collects To Outside Parties?**

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer personally identifiable information that visitors voluntarily provide to us. However, the data collected may be provided to other parties in an aggregate form for marketing, advertising, or other uses. 

Our site sometimes offers contests, sweepstakes, or promotions that are sponsored by or co-sponsored with identified third parties. By virtue of their sponsorship, these third parties may obtain personally identifiable information that visitors voluntarily submit to participate in the contest, sweepstakes, or promotion. Stride Rite has no control over the third-party sponsors' use of this information. 

If a visitor at anytime chooses to opt-out from any future mailings or contact from Stride Rite, please let us know by e-mail at striderite@striderite.com and include your name and e-mail address. Another option is to visit the Stride Rite Online web page and answer 'No' to the receive periodic updates question. 

**Special Note to Parents And Children**

This is a general audience site. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personally identifiable information from children under 13 except as permitted by law. If we discover that we have received any information from a child under 13 in violation of this policy, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe Stride Rite has information from anyone under 13, please contact us at [striderite@striderite.com](mailto:striderite@striderite.com). 

Unless otherwise disclosed during collection, Stride Rite does not provide any personally identifying information, regardless of its source, to any third-party for any purpose whatsoever. Information provided by a parent voluntarily about their children under 13 may be used internally. 

Children - Before sending any information about yourself over the Internet to us or anyone else, be sure to ask your parents for permission. 

Parents - Stride Rite recommends parents to take an active role in their children's use of the Internet. We encourage you to talk to your children about safe and responsible use of their personal information while using the Internet.

**Security Statement**

Maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information is important to striderite.com. All payment information including credit card related data is considered and treated as confidential information. We utilize Secure Server Software (SSL) to encrypt all payment information, including names, address, credit card numbers, so that it can not be read as the information travels through the internet. SSL is considered to be among the most secure ways to do business through the internet. When SSL is active, it will display (depending on your software) a closed lock or an unbroken key, letting you know that the information you input will be secure.
