# Privacy Policy - Stylus Inc

Privacy Policy

Our commitment to privacy

To better protect your privacy we would like to explain our online information practices here. We have explained the choices that you can make about how we use the information you give us.

On some of our pages we collect personal information like Name, Address, Email id and company details. We use this information only to aid us in completing the project that you may be contacting us about. We use email ids to reply to mails that we receive. And we do not share this information with any third-party.
