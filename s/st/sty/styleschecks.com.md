# Privacy Policy ... The Styles Check Company

This is the web site of The Styles Check Company, Inc.

Our postal address is   
P.O. Box 2  
Bel Air, Maryland 21014-0002

We can be reached via e-mail at [customerservice@styleschecks.com](mailto:%20customerservice@styleschecks.com)   
Or you can reach us by telephone at 1-800-356-0353

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes the consumer's domain name and IP address. We collect only the domain name and IP address, but not the e-mail address of visitors to our web page, the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail and aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit. We also calculate the length of time each visitor spends on our page. 

The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded and used to improve the content of our Web page. 

We never make the e-mail addresses of those who access our site available to other organizations. If you have submitted your e-mail address to us, but do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending email to us at the above address and telling us that you do not want to receive e-mail from our company. 

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed on-line. Please provide us with your correct phone number. This information is never shared with other organizations.
