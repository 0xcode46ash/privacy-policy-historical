# Privacy Policy - StocksToTrade.com

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how StockstoTrade, Inc., (“StocksToTrade,” “us,” or “we”) will collect, use, disclose, transfer, and store your personal information. This Policy applies to any StockstoTrade websites, applications, brands, services and products (collectively, the “Services”) that do not have a separate privacy policy or that link to this policy. By using our Services and/or registering for an account with us, you accept the terms of this Policy.

Please review this Policy to familiarize yourself with our privacy practices, and if you have any questions please feel free to contact us.

**1\. Types of Information We Collect**

We collect both personal and non-personal information from you when you visit or use our Services, or contact us. We collect the following types of personal information:

  * Registration information when you create an account with us, including your name, date of birth, and contact information, such as your email and phone number
  * Information you send to us via message, chat, or similar functionality
  * Information you provide on our blog or website via comments
  * Transaction information when you request information or purchase a product or service from us
  * Location information when you visit or use our Services, such as through your IP address or mobile device, where we are permitted by law to process this information
  * Any information collected from social media features and widgets, such as the share button on our blog
  * Analytics information through cookies, web beacons and similar technologies when you visit our Services
  * Information from third-party partners, such as information from a partner we jointly offer services with or from an advertiser



  
**2\. How We Use Your Information**

We use the personal information we collect from you to:

  * Provide, develop and improve our Services
  * Create aggregate information (e.g., creating a report on trends in the usage of our website)
  * Provide you with a personalized experience on our Services
  * Communicate with you about your account or transactions with us, and send you information about our Services or changes to our policies
  * Provide you with customer service
  * Detect, investigate and prevent activities that may violate our policies or be illegal



**  
3\. How We Share Your Information  
**

We may share your personal information in limited circumstances, including:

  * When you allow us to share your personal information with a third-party
  * When we cooperate with our affiliates and other non-affiliated third-parties that offer products or services that we or they believe may be of interest to you, however such third-parties are prohibited from using your personal information for purposes other than those requested by us or required by law
  * When we have a good faith believe that disclosure is required by law
  * When companies perform services for us that require the disclosure of personal information, however such companies are prohibited from using your personal information for purposes other than those requested by us or required by law
  * In the course of a sale, merger, transfer, exchange or other disposition (whether of assets, stock or otherwise) of all or a portion of our business, in which case the personal information we have collected or otherwise acquired may be one of the assets transferred
  * To ensure the security and safety of our customers and third-parties
  * To protect our rights and the rights of our customers and third-parties, or to take precautions against liability
  * To enforce our Terms of Use or rules
  * To comply with a legal process or investigation



**  
4\. Your Choices  
**

We give you the ability to control your choices with regard to our collection, use and disclosure of your personal information. If you would like to exercise your choices, please contact us. Your choices include the ability to:

  * Correct, update or delete your registration account
  * Change your choices for subscriptions, newsletters and alerts
  * Choose whether to receive communications from us regarding offers and promotions for our products and services, or products and services that we think may be of interest to you
  * Request access to the personal information we hold about you and what we amend or delete it



**5\. International Transfers of Information**

StocksToTrade provides Services worldwide. By using our Services, you consent to the transfer and storage of your personal information outside of your home country, including the United States, for the purposes described in this Policy. The privacy protections and the rights of authorities to access your personal information in such countries may not be equivalent to those afforded to you in your home country.

**6\. Cookies & Do Not Track **

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on your computer, and that your browser provides to a website each time you return. We use cookies to help our Services identify and track visitors, their usage of our Services, and their website access preferences. We also have third-party cookies on our Services that may collect personal information about you and your activities over time or across different websites.

Visitors of our Services who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to “Do Not Track” cookies before using our Services, with the drawback that certain features of Services may not function properly without the use of cookies.

  
**7\. Changes to this Policy  
**

We may change this Policy from time to time. We encourage you to frequently check this page for any changes to this Policy. If there are any material changes to the policy, we will notify you by email at the last email address you provided us, by posting notice of such changes on our sites, or by other means consistent with applicable law. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.

**  
8\. Links to Third-Parties**

We may provide links to content or advertisements that may be created, offered, sponsored or affiliated with third-parties. We cannot guarantee the privacy of your information once you provide it to a third-party. We strongly recommend that you review the privacy and security, and Terms of Use/Acceptable Use policies of third-parties before sharing your information with them. If you disclose information to others, or if you are directed to a third-party website, their privacy policies and practices apply to your information.

  
**9\. Children’s Privacy  **

Neither StocksToTrade nor any of its Services are designed for, intended to attract, or directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users in this age group.  


**10\. Security of Your Personal Information**

We take commercially reasonable efforts to maintain security protections in accordance with industry practices to protect personal information from loss, alteration, misuse, destruction and unauthorized access or disclosure. However, you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password and account, and you are fully responsible for all activities that occur under your account. If you allow third-parties to access your StockstoTrade account, we are not responsible for anything that happens as a result of that access. If you believe your account has been abused, please contact us immediately.
