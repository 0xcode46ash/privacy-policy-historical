# Stouffer's - Privacy Statement

Thank you for visiting Stouffers.com. We value your visit to our Web site, and we hope you enjoy it. We know privacy is important to you, and want you to be comfortable as you explore our site, and perhaps share in the interactive nature of the site. We do not share personally identifiable information collected at this site with third parties. Below is a summary of this site's information collection and use practices. 

_Please note: the privacy practices set forth in this Privacy Policy are for this Web site only._

**Personal Information Use**  
We collect names and e-mail addresses when voluntarily submitted by our on-line visitors. This personal information is only used to fulfill your request. That is, to respond to your questions and comments in our Feedback section or, if you elect, to receive e-mail updates about our site and products. If you elect to receive e-mail updates, your information may be shared with other Nestl√© business units and you may receive product information from them, as well. 

Information is kept secure. Only our authorized employees and authorized agents (who have agreed to keep information secure) have access to stored information. If upon receipt of an e-mail update from us, you decide you no longer want to receive updates, you may elect to be dropped from our mailing list. 

**Tracking/Cookie Technology**  
Cookie or tracking technology is useful for gathering information such as browser type and operating system, tracking the number of visitors to our Web site, and understanding how visitors use our Web site. Personal information cannot be collected via cookies. 

**General**  
We have no intention of collecting personal information from children at this site. 

We reserve the right to make changes to this policy. Any changes to this policy will be posted.
