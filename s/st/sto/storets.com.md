# STORETS- Womens Clothing & Fashion Online Store - Get the latest fashion at the discount price

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy the following notice explains the information we collect, how it is used, how it is safeguarded, and how to contact us if you have any concerns.

  
**As part of the order process, the following information  can be collected from shoppers:**

Name, Billing Address, Telephone Number, Email Address, Credit/Debit Card Number, eBay ID, Paypal Account, etc.

 **How That Information Is Used:**

The information you collect is used such as to fill order, to contact buyers if they have questions, to refund, to confirm payment, or to send email with special offers.  
  
 **Our Commitment To The Security of Your Data  
**  
We operate a technical and organisational measures provided by our hosting server to ensure that your personal data is kept secure and to prevent unlawful or unauthorised disclosure, misuse, alteration, damage or accidental loss or destruction.
