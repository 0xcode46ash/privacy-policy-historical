# Web Privacy Statement | St. Lawrence University

St. Lawrence University (the University) respects the privacy of the people who use [www.stlawu.edu](http://www.stlawu.edu/). This privacy statement informs visitors about information this website collects, who may access that information, and what the University may do with the collected information. The University reserves the right to make changes to this web privacy statement.

The University uses cookies and web analytics services to track user activity on the institutionâ€™s main website. This information is used to track usage, analyze trends in user behavior, and to personalize the experience for individual users. Users may accept or reject cookies by configuring their browser security settings appropriately.

The University may occasionally collect information via the website. The University is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell information to outside entities, but we reserve the right to share it with strategic partners who are bound to confidentiality agreements with the University.

If you have questions about this statement, or about how the University uses any information collected via its website, contact [University Communications](http://www.stlawu.edu/university-communications).
