# STMicroelectronics | Privacy Policy

"At STMicroelectronics, our culture begins and ends with the customer and service is our number one priority. On-line, our aim is to provide an environment where information is easily targeted, speedily accessed and efficiently retrieved.  
Consistent with usual business practices, ST does not request personal information from visitors prior to accessing any section of the Company's public web sites. Identifying information (name, address, etc.) is required only when website visitors give feedback to ST or when a specific question is asked. Apart from the internal of these communications, personal information is not provided in any form to any organizations outside of ST."
