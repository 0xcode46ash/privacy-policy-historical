# Halloween Costumes - Adult and Kids Costumes, Online Halloween Costume

[](http://www.starcostumes.com/categories/50-Percent-Off-SALE.aspx)

Star Design Costumes is committed to maintaining your privacy. Some parts of our site use cookies, and our Website logging software does record information about your browser, geographic location and operating system, but this information will only be used for aggregate reporting purposes and will not be tied specificially to you.

Star Design Costumes collects information for the purposes of contacting you regarding your feedback and orders, including your address, phone number and e-mail address. This information is only used for those purposes, and we will not sell or otherwise give this information to a third party for commercial purposes.
