# StarPulse.com - Privacy Policy

**  
Privacy Statement for Starpulse.com**

**** Starpulse.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: Starpulse.com 

**Information Automatically Logged**

We use only standard server statistics like your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. For example, an IP would be used to gather broad and general demographic information, so we can make our service better. We evaluate what browser versions our users are using, country of origin, referring links and general browser info, so we can continue to provide cutting edge features and content compatible with the demands of our user base and their respective technology profiles. 

**Advertisers**

We do use some outside advertising company's to display some of the ads on our site, these ads may contain/use cookies, cookies received in conjunction with these banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information. While Starpulse.com does use some cookies in other parts of our Web site, these cookies are used to support the respective technology and not for purposes of acquiring personal user information (that is our promise to you)! We respect and value you as a visitor to Starpulse.com and hope that you will visit us again and again.

**Registration Forms**

Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their email address). The customer's contact information is also used to send promotional material from us and some sponsors and site partners. StarPulse.com uses a third party email service bureau to deliver all 3rd party opt-in programs. Information that is self-reported is neither shared nor co-mingled with any other data that may reside within this database. All registrants have given their permission to receive information from StarPulse.com or selected partners of StarPulse.com. A 1-by-1 clear GIF may be embedded within any HTML promotion in order to determine if the email has been opened. At any time, registrants may choose opt-out of receiving future mailings. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time; see the choice/opt-out section below. 

**Contests**

We run contests on our site in which we ask visitors for contact information (like email address). Contact information from the contests is used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary (like when they WIN or need to receive newsletters and announcements) Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time; see the choice/opt-out section below. Specific contest rules and regulations can be found on the bottom of the contest page, rules may vary from contest to contest depending on Sponsor and respective guidelines. 

**Message Boards**

Starpulse.com LLC expressly disclaims any and all, opinions expressed in our community areas, including but not limited to those posted on our (message boards, and email services). The opinions posted to these areas are those of individuals and not the opinions of Starpulse.com LLC. 

**External Links**

This site contains links to other sites. Starpulse.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

**Security**

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. 

**Choice/Opt-Out**

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving promotional/marketing information from our partners, and us .   


**Contacting the Web Site**

Contacting the Web Site, If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact: [support@starpulse.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20021020045616id_/http%3A//www.starpulse.com/help/support@starpulse.com)
