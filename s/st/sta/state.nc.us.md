# PORTAL PRIVACY STATEMENT FOR INDIVIDUALS

**PORTAL PRIVACY STATEMENT FOR INDIVIDUALS**  
**Without Personal Accounts**  
Thank you for visiting the _NC @ Your Service_ Portal and reviewing our privacy policy. State agencies, and commercial sites such as Yahoo!, that you can reach from the _NC @ Your Service_ Portal have different privacy statements. Some of the statements are placed on their home pages. Some do not have privacy statements at all. When visiting sites without published policy statements, you may wish to send an email to the Webmaster inquiring about the privacy policy and statement for the specific site. **Information Collected and Stored Automatically **  
When you visit our website to browse, read pages, or download information, we automatically collect and store **only** the following information:  


  1. The Internet domain and IP address from which you access our portal; 
  2. The date and time you access our site; 
  3. The pages you visit; and 
  4. If you linked to _NC @ Your Service_ from another Web site, the address of that Web site. 

_NC @ Your Service_ uses this information to help us make our site more useful to visitors--to learn about the number of visitors to our site and the types of technology our visitors use.
