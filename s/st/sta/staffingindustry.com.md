# Privacy Policy | Staffing Industry Analysts

Staffing Industry Analysts is committed to maintaining the privacy of its members and web site users. When you purchase our products or services or complete a registration on this web site, we do collect personal information so that we can serve you well. Staffing Industry Analysts does not sell, share, or rent this personal information to third parties.

To fulfill our commitment to client service, Staffing Industry Analysts uses third-parties such as credit card processing companies, shipping companies, mailing houses, hotels, event coordinators, and others. If you purchase one of our products or services, or register for one of our events, your personal information may be given to these parties, but only to enable the fulfillment of our commitment to you.

To provide customizable and personalized services, Staffing Industry Analysts uses cookies to store and track information about members and users of our web sites. A cookie is a piece of computer code that enables web servers to "identify" visitors. With respect to cookies, you have choices – through your browser you can accept all cookies, be notified when a cookie is set, or reject all cookies. However, if you choose to reject all cookies, you will be unable to access some of the proprietary content and useful features on our web sites.

Staffing Industry Analysts monitors how members and web site visitors use our web sites. We use this information to help us better understand what our clients need and/or how clients are using their memberships, which enables us to improve or augment our products and services. We may also share this information on an aggregated basis with third parties (for example, to disclose web site traffic numbers to advertisers).

You may correct or update the personal information we have about you at any time. Go to your user profile on our web site, or contact [memberservices@staffingindustry.com](mailto:memberservices@staffingindustry.com).

If you no longer want to receive e-mail communications from Staffing Industry Analysts, you may unsubscribe by clicking the "Unsubscribe" button at the bottom of each e-mail we send to you, or by following the instructions to "opt out" appearing on that e-mail.

This privacy policy is effective as of June 15, 2007. Staffing Industry Analysts reserves the right to change the terms of this privacy policy or to modify the features of our web sites at any time. Please check this page periodically for changes, although we will notify you if there is a material change in our privacy practices with respect to the use of your personal information, including posting a message on our home page stating that the privacy policy has been changed.

This privacy statement applies solely to Staffing Industry Analysts’ web sites. If you have any questions about this privacy policy or any of Staffing Industry Analysts’ privacy-related practices, please contact us at [memberservices@staffingindustry.com](mailto:memberservices@staffingindustry.com).
