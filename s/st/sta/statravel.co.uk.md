Your privacy is of utmost importance to us and we are committed to maintaining the privacy of registered users. Please take time to read through our privacy statement to ensure you are aware and accept our policy. top 

  
When you decide to register via the website, we ask you for personal details such as your name, email address, age, whether you are a student and the region in which you live. This information is then used to:

i) email you weekly newsletters and special offers (if you choose to receive them)  
ii) allow you to enter any competition that appears on the website. top

If you have booked with us recently you will automatically be sent one newsletter from us - at this time you can choose to continue to receive them or remove your registration in one click. 

You are entitled to a copy of your information held by us. If you would like to see this please ask us. 

  
We run promotions with third parties at specific times of the year - however, we will not share your data with these third parties unless you choose to opt-in via the form for that specific promotion - this will be made very clear. 

Third parties advertise on our website and in our newsletter, however your data is kept on our records only, no other company will be given this data by STA Travel. We ensure that we manage all data under the guidelines of the Data Protection Act and all information is processed in accordance with the principles laid down by the Act. top

  
If you wish to unsubscribe, please do so at any time by [clicking here](http://www.statravel.co.uk/c_aboutus/removeme.asp) and we will remove you from our mailing list. Alternatively you can unsubscribe at any point via the newsletter. top 

  
The ads appearing on parts of this web site are delivered to you, on our behalf, by Adlink. Information about your visit to this site, such as the number of times you have viewed an ad (but not your name, address, or any other personal information) is used to serve ads to you on this site. 

  
In the course of serving advertisements to parts of this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognise a unique cookie on your browser, but it will not collect any personally identifiable information about you.
