# Privacy Policy - Statistics Views

Wiley [Registered office address: The Atrium, Southern Gate, Chichester, West Sussex, United Kingdom. PO19 8SQ, registered company number 0180277] recognizes the importance of protecting the information it collects from Users in the operation of StatisticsViews, and takes reasonable steps to maintain the security, integrity and privacy of this information in accordance with the Wiley Privacy and Data Protection Policy.

By submitting your information to Wiley you consent to the practices described.   
Wiley will ensure that any identifiable personal information it collects through, for example, the registration and purchasing process, will be adequate, relevant and not excessive for purposes of operating StatisticsViews; it will be kept accurate and up-to-date based on information provided and will be deleted when no longer needed. Except where necessary in connection with services provided by appropriate intermediaries, who will be required to comply with the confidentiality provisions of this policy, Wiley will not disclose any personal information identifying Users to any third party, unless required by law or to enforce the [Terms and Conditions](http://stats.cwslive.wiley.com/view/0/terms.html) of Use for StatisticsViews, or unless we have received your consent.   
Any private communications or information supplied to Wiley by a User may be used by Wiley for internal business and research purposes and to help enhance and develop StatisticsViews but will not be shared with third parties unless we receive your consent.   
Wiley may disclose to carefully chosen third parties navigational and transactional information in the form of anonymous, aggregate usage statistics (including "hits" on StatisticsViews and the products herein) and demographics but only in forms that do not reveal a User's identity or confidential information, except as required by law as needed in connection with legal proceedings, or unless we have received your consent.   
Links may be provided from StatisticsViews to other websites. In using such links, Users should be aware that each website will vary in terms of its privacy and data protection policies, and Wiley does not take responsibility for the privacy policies of, and usage of, personal information collected by others including, without limitation, those of any partner or affiliate.   
You should be aware that identifiable personal information--such as your name or e-mail address--that you voluntarily disclose on bulletin boards or in chat areas that are accessible to other Users could be collected and disclosed by others. Wiley cannot take any responsibility for such collection and disclosure.  
Please note that Wiley's privacy and data protection policy is reviewed periodically. Wiley reserves the right to modify its privacy and data protection policy at any time without notice. Any changes to the policy will be posted on this page. Your continued use of StatisticsViews will be deemed conclusive acceptance of such modification. Any comments or questions concerning this policy should be addressed to: Privacy Policy @ Wiley.com.
