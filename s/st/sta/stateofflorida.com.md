# State of Florida.com - Privacy Policy

We at State of Florida.com recognize the importance of protecting your personal information.

**You have our assurance that:**

  1. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy online.
  2. This website does not collect personally identifying information about you except when you specifically and knowingly provide it.
  3. We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information.
  4. When we do collect personal information, we use this to help us better serve your needs, such as through the provision of services or information that may be of interest to you.
  5. This website is not primarily aimed at children. In addition, in order to protect our children, this website does not knowingly collect or display the individually identifying information of users who are under the age of 13. 

State of Florida.com contains links to other websites. State of Florida.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites.

State of Florida.com uses WebTrends Live to analyze traffic to this website. WebTrends Live does not create individual profiles for visitors. Unlike some tracking services WebTrends Live does not have a database of individual profiles for each visitor. WebTrends Live only collects aggregate data. For more information about WebTrends Live privacy policy, please click [here](http://www.webtrendslive.com/privacy_policy.htm).
