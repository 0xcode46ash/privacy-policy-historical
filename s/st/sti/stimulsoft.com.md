# Stimulsoft Privacy Policy. :: Stimulsoft

**This Privacy Policy was last modified on August 30, 2012.**

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of Stimulsoft. On this page you will find information about our policies with respect to Personal Information and Anonymous Information that is collected through Stimulsoft's websites and through other communications with us. Please visit this page frequently to learn of any changes to our Privacy Statement.

 **Information Collected  
**  
You may visit Stimulsoft's websites without identifying yourself or revealing any personal information. Our Stimulsoft's websites does collect information such as domain names, IP address, browser type, operating system, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by Stimulsoft for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service and for site trends and statistics. This information does not personally identify you.

 **Use of Personal Information**

Stimulsoft do not sell, rent, or give your personal information to any third party. And we do not use your information for any purpose outside the scope of communicating with you in the most efficient manner. We may use your personal information to deliver services, such as newsletters, events, training or software that you request or purchase. We may also use any information you have provided as reasonably necessary to provide customer support to you.

 **Cookies**

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Stimulsoft uses cookies to help Stimulsoft identify and track visitors, their usage of Stimulsoft's websites, and their website access preferences. Stimulsoft visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Stimulsoft's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Stimulsoft 's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

 **Information Security**

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. These include a variety of security technologies and procedures, including internal reviews of our data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures and appropriate encryption and physical security measures. Access to your personal information is restricted to those Stimulsoft employees, contractors and agents who need to know that information in order to process it and provide personalized customer support to you. These individuals are bound by confidentiality obligations and may be subject to discipline, including termination, if they fail to meet those obligations.

 **Third Party Services**

At these Stimulsoft's websites, there may be links to companies with whom we have a relationship. There companies include service providers (who, in providing services such the hosting and processing mechanisms of our websites have access to the information contained within their own systems) and resellers and affiliate sale partners (who collect customer and transaction information on our behalf). Stimulsoft is not responsible for the privacy practices of our business partners. We encourage you to read their privacy statements, as they may differ from ours.

 **Privacy Policy Changes**

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will attempt to provide a more prominent notice (including, for certain services, email notification of privacy policy changes).

 **Contacting Us**

Any questions about this Privacy Policy should be addressed to [support@stimulsoft.com](mailto:support@stimulsoft.com)
