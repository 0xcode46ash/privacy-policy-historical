# Spunky Jones SEO Blog - Privacy Policy

  *   * **The Spunky Jones Blog** , has created this email privacy policy to demonstrate our commitment to protect the privacy of your personal information.

**How we Protect your Privacy:**  
We respect the privacy of our patrons and use appropriate security measures to protect their personal information/confidentiality against the loss, misuse and alteration of data used by our system.

**Sharing and Usage:**  
You can be assured that we will never share, sell, or rent personal information without your advance permission or unless ordered by a court of law. Information submitted to us is only available to employees managing this information for purposes of contacting you or sending you emails based on your request for information, and to contracted service providers for purposes of providing services relating to our communications with you.

**Privacy Policy Changes:**  
This policy was created on March 11, 2009.

**I promise not to sell your personal information to anyone.**  
Spunky Jones.
