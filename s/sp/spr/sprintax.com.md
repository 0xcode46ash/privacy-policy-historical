# Sprintax.com | Privacy Policy

Sprintax software has been developed and is fully owned by taxback.com.

Our privacy policy outlines how Sprintax uses and safeguards all the information you provide when you use this website. Sprintax is wholly committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. If we request certain information which you can be identified by when using our website, you can rest assured that it will be used only in accordance with this privacy statement.

Sprintax may alter this policy occasionally. As such, please review this policy from time to time to ensure that you are satisfied with any changes. This policy is effective from 31 July 2013.

### What information we collect

We may collect the following personal information from you:

  * Contact Details including name, email address, mailing address and phone number
  * All Financial Information necessary to file a tax return on your behalf
  * Social Security Number or equivalent, depending on your tax country of residence
  * Demographic information such as age, education, gender, interests and zip code



### How your information is used

We require this information to assess your needs, to provide you with a constantly improving service for other reasons, including:

  * Administering your account
  * Sending you occasional marketing and promotional communications, such as emails, newsletters, SMS text messages
  * Providing the service you have requested
  * Using your information to improve our products and services



We will never sell your information

### Choice/Opt-Out

If you no longer wish to receive our newsletter or marketing emails, you can unsubscribe by following the unsubscribe instructions included in our emails. 

### Refer-A-Friend

If you choose to refer a friend to us through one of our multiple referral avenues, we will ask you for your friend's name and email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email inviting him or her to visit the site. Sprintax retains this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email and tracking the success of our referral program. 

Your friend may contact us at [info@sprintax.com](mailto:info@sprintax.com) to request that we remove this information from our database. 

As is the case with most web sites, we automatically gather information about your computer such as your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and operating system. 

### Information Obtained from Third Parties

If you provide us with personal information about others, or if others give us your information, we will use that information solely for the specific reason for which it was given to us. 

### Information Sharing

We will not share your personal information with third parties except where your prior consent is given or unless we are required to do so by law to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. 

We may provide your personal information to companies that provide support services, such as shipping documents to you. These companies are authorized to use your personal information only as necessary to provide these services to us. 

We may also disclose your personal information when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to guard our rights, ensure your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or reply to a government request. 

If Sprintax is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a clear notice on our web site of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information, as well as any decisions you may have regarding your personal information to any other third party with your prior consent to do so. If you believe that any information we are holding about you is incorrect or incomplete, please write to or email us as soon as possible, at the address below. We will promptly correct any information found to be incorrect. If you choose to become a client of Sprintax and you store personal information online with us, then you can log into your account at anytime and update your personal information. 

### Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your computer hard drive. Cookies help analyse web traffic and enable web applications to respond to you as an individual. The web application can tailor its operations to your needs, likes and dislikes by gathering and remembering information about your preferences. 

Overall, cookies help us provide you with a better website, by allowing us to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not. A cookie in no way gives us access to your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us. 

This site uses cookies. We link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. However, this may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website. 

Some of our business partners or third parties use cookies or other tracking technologies on our site. Data is only collected in the aggregate. We have no access to or control over these cookies. 

### Links to Other Sites

Should you decide to leave our site by clicking on a link which leads to a third party site, we cannot be held responsible for the activities of said party, nor can we accept responsibility for any use of your personal information by such third parties. These sites are not governed by this privacy policy. We encourage you to review the privacy statements of any other service provider from whom you request services. 

### Security

The security of your personal information is important to us. As such, all sensitive information (such as social security number) on our forms is encrypted during the transmission of that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). 

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure and we follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of Internet transmission or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, however. As such, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. 

If you have any questions about security on our Web site, you can contact us as outlined below. 

### Additional Policy Information

Our web site offers publicly accessible blogs or community forums. You should be aware that any information provided in these areas may be seen, collected and used by others who access them. We also have testimonials on our sites, for which if a user posted personal information with this, we have received permission. 

### Notification of Privacy Statement Changes

We may update this privacy statement to reflect changes to our information practices. Should we make any material changes we will notify you by email (sent to the e-mail address specified in your account) or through our Site prior to the change becoming effective. We encourage you to periodically review our privacy policy for the latest information on our privacy practices.
