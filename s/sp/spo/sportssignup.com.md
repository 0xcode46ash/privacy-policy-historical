# Sports Signup

 

League Sports Services is committed to safeguarding your privacy online. We have posted this privacy policy to help you understand how your personal information will be treated as you use our web site. This policy may change from time to time, so please check back periodically. 

#### Personal Information

Our system collects information about your family on behalf of the sports league your family is participating in.  We act as an agent in collecting information from you for them, and likewise provide various reports and notification to the sports leagues about participants. 

The information we collect will NOT be shared with anybody other than the authorized representatives from the sports league you are participating with. 

Personal information is stored in Microsoft's SQL Server Database, which supports industry standard security.  The Database is hosted by ActiveHost, a top-grade hosting company that is a Microsoft Certified Partner, and uses latest practices for ensuring data security and database availability.  Although League Sports Services will use all reasonable efforts to safeguard the confidentiality of your personal information, transmissions made by means of the Internet cannot be guaranteed to be secure. League Sports Services has no liability for disclosure of  personal information due to errors in transmission or unauthorized acts of third parties. 

#### Mailing Lists

Emailing you is a vital component to our system, as this is the primary way your sports league can communicate with you.  Thus you can expect to receive periodic emails from the sports league regarding sign-up deadlines, meeting notices, etc. 

Will will NOT provide your email to any other third party without your express permission.

#### Credit Card Information

When you register through our system and pay via a credit card, the payment information is collected on a secure web page hosted by VeriSign, the leader in secure payment processing, and not stored in our system.

If you have any other questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at [privacy@leaguesportsservices.com](mailto:privacy@leaguesportsservices.com).
