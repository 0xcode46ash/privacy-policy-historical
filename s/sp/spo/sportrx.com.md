# Privacy Policy | SportRx

  * SportRx respects your privacy and the security of the data that visitors provide.
  * The purpose of the information we collect is strictly for the use of providing you with the best possible customer service.
  * SportRx does not sell, share, or disclose any personal information with any third parties. Any information you give us is held with the utmost care and security, and will only be used to fulfill your order.
  * SportRx.com uses "cookies" to gather online browsing patterns so that we may serve up the best possible advertisements that are relevant to your interests. If desired, you can manage and delete cookies through most web browsers. Visit the [ NAI website](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/) for further consumer opt-out information.
  * If you wish to opt out of all communication from SportRx, please email us at contact@sportrx.com.
  * If you have any questions about this privacy statement, practices, or your dealings with the SportRx website, please do not hesitate to [ contact us](http://www.sportrx.com/contact).
