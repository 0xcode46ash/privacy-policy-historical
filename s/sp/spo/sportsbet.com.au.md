# SportsBet - Licensed Sports Bookmaker

Sportsbet and this Privacy Statement may be subject to applicable legislation, regulations and codes of practice. Sportsbet is committed to keeping the highest level of scrutiny and trade practises possible to ensure the safest possible environment for all of our customers. Sportsbet complies with the National Privacy Principles and takes seriously its obligation to safeguard personal information about its customers. Sportsbet is committed to complying with the National Privacy Principles contained in the Privacy Act. 

Sportsbet collects personal information for the purpose of providing services to our customers. We may also use contact details for the purpose of sending information about products and services to our customers. The only circumstances in which external organisations may be given access to your personal information is when a related company or contractors and service providers are acting on behalf of Sportsbet and are helping us operate our business or provide a service to you on our behalf. 

Sportsbet will take reasonable steps to protect personal information we collect and ensure that it is accurate, complete and up-to-date. Your information is held on secure servers. Sportsbet employees, privy to such information, are bound by confidentiality agreements in respect of client's details and trading habits. 

If you have any further inquires or concerns in regards to the security and privacy of Sportsbet please feel free to [contact us](http://www.sportsbet.com.au/ContactUs.asp).
