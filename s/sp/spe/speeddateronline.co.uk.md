# SpeedDater Online - Privacy policy for SpeedDater and SpeedDater Online.

**SpeedDater Ltd respects and understands the importance of your privacy and will never sell, share, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any third parties without your express permission. All details are kept on the SpeedDater Ltd database.**

When registering with SpeedDater Ltd you will be required to provide your email address or other personal information. This information is used for 

(a) adding you to the SpeedDater Ltd mailing and text alert list (this list has an opt-out facility) and/or 

(b) registering you for a SpeedDater Ltd event. When your information is collected, it will only be used to advise you of forthcoming SpeedDater Ltd Events and for correspondence relating to any events for which you have booked. If you register with SpeedDater Ltd your information is added to our email mailing list. 

We never pass on email addresses to other SpeedDaters or people registered with the SpeedDater or SpeedDater Online websites.
