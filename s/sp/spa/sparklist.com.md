* * *

SparkLIST.com uses your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information.

This site contains links to other sites. SparkLIST.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. SparkLIST.com recommends you refer to each Web site's Privacy Statements.

SparkLIST.com uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect visitor's contact information (like their email address) and financial information (like their account or credit card numbers). The customer's contact information is used to get in touch with the visitor for billing purposes only. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services.

SparkLIST.com uses subscription forms for individuals to request information. We collect visitor's contact information (like your email address) to provide you with the information that you request. We will periodically send you email referring to news and updates to the SparkLIST.com.

Each client will be notified via email, telephone or postal mail relating to any changes regarding the use of sensitive client information by SparkLIST.com. Written or active email consent by the individual user will be required.  
**  
Sharing Information**  
SparkLIST.com does not share, sell or rent your personally identifiable information to outside third parties. Confidential information will only be disclosed due to law or court demands. **

Supplementation of Information

**  
SparkLIST.com does not supplement your information with any outside third parties. **

Security

**    
The SparkLIST.com web site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Sensitive data, such as credit card information, collected through SparkLIST.com's website is transferred via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) server only. SparkLIST.com employees are made aware of all security policies and are maintaining and protecting your personally identifiable information. **

Choice / Opt-In

**  
SparkLIST.com provides opt-in opportunities for all email lists that you choose to receive. We use your email addresses to confirm your subscription and validity of the provided email address. Your reply to this email is necessary for completing your subscription to any email list. **

Choice / Opt-Out

**  
SparkLIST.com provides its users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us. We offer users the following options for removing their information from our database, to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service.

2\. telephone: 920.490.5901 **

Correct / Update

**  
Change the email address that you have subscribed with by first unsubscribing with your current email address. Then, resubscribe with your new email address. All email list welcome messages contain the information necessary for subscription and removal from their appropriate list. SparkLIST.com also provides the following contact information for modifying information previously provided.

1\. email: [support@sparklist.com

Unsubscribe

**  
SparkLIST.com provides unsubscribe / opt-out opportunities for all email lists that you choose to receive. All email list welcome messages contain the information necessary for removal from the list. Removal from all lists is instant and automated by our servers.
