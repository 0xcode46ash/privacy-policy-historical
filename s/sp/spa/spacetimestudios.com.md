# Spacetime Studios - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2010

Spacetime Studios recognizes that you care about the personal information that is held about you and how it is used. We are committed to respecting and protecting the privacy our users. This privacy statement applies to all personal data, submitted by you, to the Spacetime Studios web site. This statement discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices of Spacetime Studios in connection with www.spacetimestudios.com (the "Site").

If you choose to create an account and register on the Site, you need to submit a chosen name and contact information, more specifically an e-mail address. No extremely personal information, such as Social Security or Credit Card numbers are stored by us.

The Site also stores and reads cookies to and from your computer. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser to enable our forums and general website features to recognize your browser for when you return. By using the Site, you agree that both the webserver, and any trusted 3rd party we chose, may place a tracking cookie on your machine and access it at any time during your session. In general, cookies are safe and are not a security issue when being used from 'trusted' internet sites, such as Spacetimestudios.com. For information on how to manage cookies in your browser, please visit [AboutCookies.org](http://www.aboutcookies.org/Default.aspx?page=2)

The Site will not sell, transmit or distribute any data held without your prior permission or consent, unless required to do so under relevant United States legislation. All other information provided by you goes into a "public profile" and is entirely optional, all information held in that profile is held there at your own risk. This information is edited by you via your Control Panel in the forums.

You have a legal right for the personal information held on the Site to be updated if it is incorrect or deleted if you choose. Most of this can be done through your Control Panel in the forums. If you are having issues with this, please contact us if you need help.

The Site will take all reasonable effort to ensure that all personal information about you submitted is maintained secure on our server. However, we make no warranty to this.

This site is intended for individuals over the age of 13. Any information collected is intended to be of individuals over the age of 13. According to the [Childrenâ€™s Online Privacy Protection Act](http://www.ftc.gov/bcp/edu/microsites/coppa/intro.htm) (also known as COPPA), our site does NOT cater to children. "Operators of commercial websites or online services directed to children under 13 that collect personal information from children" require a COPPA agreement and privacy policy. Since we do not cater to this age group, no COPPA compliance exists on the Site.

The Site may also contain links to other sites. If you access a third party site from a link on this Site, any information you disclose on that site is not subject to this Privacy Statement. It is possible that these links may be used by third parties or others to collect personal or other information about users of the Site. Spacetime Studios is not responsible for the privacy practices of such Web sites, such third parties, or the content of such sites. It is solely your obligation to review and understand the privacy practices and policies of these other Web sites and of these third parties.

This Privacy Policy may be modified at any time without notice. It is your responsibility to check this page for updates or changes. If you have any questions regarding your privacy, please [Contact Us](http://www.spacetimestudios.com/sendmessage.php).
