# Privacy Policy

[Home](https://web.archive.org/) >> [Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/privacypolicy/) >> Privacy Policy

  
Southern Polytechnic State University is committed to protecting the privacy of our constituents.  We collect no personal information from visitors to our website unless they proactively submit such information. Any information submitted via the website will only be used for the purposes stated on the submission page. Release of personal information is governed by Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act of 1974 (FERPA). 

As with most websites, our web server logs standard information about visits to the site.  These logs do not identify personal information unless a visitor logs into a password-restricted area.  All such information collected is used only for maintaining the performance and security of our servers, and is not shared with any other parties unless required by law.
