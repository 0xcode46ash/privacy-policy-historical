# Privacy Statement

SPS Commerce has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: [www.spscommerce.com](http://www.spscommerce.com/). 

Browser Information Collected on the Web Site

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. SPS Commerce analyzes our web site logs to constantly improve the value of the materials available on our web site. Our web site logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site. 

Collection of Information

As a communications medium, SPS Commerce uses an order form for customers / prospects to request information, products, and services. In doing so, we collect information from you (name, address, email, etc.). We use this information only to respond to you and to keep you updated on SPS Commerce happenings. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone.
