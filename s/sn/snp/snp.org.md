# Privacy Statement - SNP2015

* * *

### Your personal data

The information you supply us will be treated with the strictest confidence in accordance with the Data Protection Act. Information may be stored on a computer database and used to keep you informed of SNP news, campaign and other opportunities to support the Party. If you prefer not to receive any future information, please contact us.

* * *

### Joining the Party online

We collect the required information in order to check you meet the conditions of and help you fulfil the opportunities provided by membership of the SNP. At your option, we ask for additional information such as gender and date of birth to help us provide a better membership service to you.

* * *

###  **Supporting the SNP**  

If you submit contact details to the SNP as a supporter, you agree to receiving communications from the party by postal channels, SMS and email. You will be able to opt out of these communications free of charge at any time. If you wish to volunteer, your details will be passed on to local organisers.

* * *

### **Online surveys**

If you complete a survey on our website, we will not publish your personal information but many provide a summary of the results of the survey at a regional or national level on an anonymised basis.

* * *

### Donating to the SNP

The information we ask for is used to process your donation and, if you donate more than £500, to check that you are on a UK electoral register. If you donate more than £1,500 to an SNP unit (e.g. local branch or Constituency organisation), or more than £7,500 to the SNP in the course of a calendar year, your name and the amount of your donation will be reported to the Electoral Commission for publication in their public register of donations.

* * *

### Links to other sites

Our site contains links to other sites and servers. The SNP is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.

* * *

### How we protect your information

Our website has security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of the information under our control. Our server is located in a locked, secure environment, with a guard posted 24 hours a day. When you contribute online, we use a secure server to protect your credit card number and other personal information during transmission. Credit card details remain encrypted until they are transferred to a computer not directly connected to the internet.

* * *

### Cookie

A cookie is a tiny text file that is stored on your computer. We may use technology to track the patterns of behaviour of visitors to our site. This can include using this "cookie" file which would be stored on your computer. You can usually modify your browser to prevent this happening. We may store the internet address you connect to our computer with, the time and date you connected, browser information and the pages you visited. This information is used only to provide us with a broad statistical breakdown of our site usage and to monitor site security.

* * *
