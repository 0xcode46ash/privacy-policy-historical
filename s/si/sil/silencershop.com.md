# Privacy Policy | Silencer Shop

Our privacy policy is simple:

  1. **All customer information will be kept confidential.** We won’t sell or give away any information you give us; including your name, e-mail, phone number, or address.
  2. **All online transactions are conducted through a secure server in order to protect your data, and we don’t save credit card information.**
