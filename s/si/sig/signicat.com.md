# Privacy policy

This privacy policy applies to visitors to the website of Signicat AS.

We respect your privacy. Any information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to Signicat will not be used in ways that you have not consented to.

Signicat handles personal information in accordance with the Norwegian Personal Data Act.
