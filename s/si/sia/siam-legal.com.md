# Privacy Policy | Siam Legal International

Www.siam-legal.com is a website which deals with commercial services, legal disputes, visa applications, and other legal services in Thailand. This Privacy Policy is indicative of our commitment to secure the privacy of our visitors. It outlines how we protect the information given to us. It describes how we collect and use such information.

### Use of Information

Our Privacy Policy covers all online information consisting of personal identifiable or non-personal identifiable information shared with us while visiting our website. It does not cover offline information.

The personal information supplied to the website is used to provide services, carry out the transactions expected of us, process requests and notify clients of any material change to this Privacy Policy.

The non-personally identifiable information, on the other hand, shall be used to manage the website. It may be used to analyze data for easy navigation and access on the website.

Hence, the use of the website signifies your agreement with all the terms of the Privacy Policy.

Should you not want us to use your personal identification, do not submit any information to the website or to any of our lawyers.

### Privacy Notice

This website reserves the right to amend or supplement the contents and/or information without prior notice. Any changes made to the website shall become effective on the date of posting. The use of the website after such posting constitutes consent thereto. Thus, it is advised to review the Privacy Policy of the website before using it.

In case of revision and information is required, a notice may be sent informing you of the reason for the change, of the need for the information we sought from you, and the choices granted to you regarding its use.

### Information Collected by Third Parties

As our website is linked to other websites, this Privacy Policy does not apply to other linked websites. It is advised that you consult their own privacy policy as they have different policies regarding the use of their respective sites. Consequently, we have no control over the information submitted by or in possession of third parties.

### Security

The website does not guarantee the security of information given to it. Although reasonable measures are made to protect your personal information against unauthorized disclosure or use, all transmissions are still at your own risk.

### Governing Law

The laws of Thailand govern this Privacy Policy without regard to its conflict of law provision.
