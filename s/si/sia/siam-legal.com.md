# Privacy Policy | Thailand Law Firm

www.siam-legal.com (Site) is a site which deals with visa application, commercial services, legal disputes and other legal services in Thailand. This Privacy Policy is indicative of our commitment to secure the privacy of our visitors. It outlines how we protect the information given to us. It describes how we collect and use such information. 

## Use of Information

Our Privacy Policy covers all online information consisting of personal identifiable or non-personal identifiable information shared with us while visiting the Site. It does not cover offline information. 

The personal information supplied to the Site is used to provide service, carry out the transaction expected of us, process request and notify clients of any material change to this Privacy Policy. 

The non-personally identifiable information, on the other hand, shall be used to manage the site. It may be used to analyze data for easy navigation and access on the Site.

Hence, the use of the site signifies your agreement with all the terms of the Privacy Policy. 

Should you not want us to use your personal identification, do not submit any information to the site or to any of our lawyers.

## Privacy Notice

The Site reserves the right to amend or supplement the contents and/or information without prior notice. Any changes made to the Site shall become effective on the date of posting. The use of the site after such posting constitutes consent thereto. Thus, it is advised to review the Privacy Policy of the Site before using it.

In case of revision and an information is required, a notice may be sent informing you of the reason for such changes, of the need for the information we sought from you, and the choices granted to you regarding its use. 

## Information Collected by Third Parties

As our Site is linked to other sites, this Privacy Policy does not apply to such other sites. It is advised that you consult their own privacy policy as they have different policies regarding the use of their respective sites. Consequently, we have no control over the information submitted by or in possession of third parties.

## Security

The Site does not guarantee the security of information given to it. Although reasonable measures are made to protect your personal information against unauthorized disclosure or use, all transmissions are still at your own risk.

## Governing Law

The laws of Thailand govern this Privacy Policy without regard to its conflict of law provision.
