# Simply Scripts - Read Hundreds of Scripts and Screenplays On Line!

Simply Scripts Privacy Statement

Simply Scripts respects your privacy.  All information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned or otherwise disclosed.  Any information you give to Simply Scripts will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to.  

Disclosure of information to third parties:  Simply Scripts does not and will not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms, email lists, or emails sent to the [webmaster](mailto:simplyscripts@att.net) asking questions or requesting information.

Browser information collected on Simply Scripts:  Simply Scripts collects, through [hitbox.com](http://hitbox.com/), click stream data such as web surfer's IP address, web pages on Simplyscripts.com which have been viewed by a web surfer, date and time, domain type, and originating url.  Simply Scripts does not collect personally identifiable information unless it is given to Simply Script through an email.  That information is and will not be disclosed to a third party.

Use of Cookies:  Simply Scripts does not use cookies.  HOWEVER, affiliates of Simply Scripts may use cookies.  Listed below are all of Simply Scripts affiliates with links to their Privacy Policies.

Privacy of emails and email lists:  Any and all emails sent to Simply Scripts are held in the strictest confidence.  The contents of the emails will not be shared, sold, reused, rented, loaned or otherwise disclosed to a third party without the consent of the sender.  While Simply Scripts does not maintain an email list, if, at a future time Simply Scripts does elect to create an email list, that list will not be shared, sold, reused, rented, loaded or otherwise disclosed to a third party without the consent of the sender.  In addition, Simply Scripts will insure that our list server software is configured such that it will not divulge the email addresses of list subscribers to anyone, including other subscribers. 

If you have any questions regarding Simply Scripts' privacy policy, please  notify the [webmaster](mailto:simplyscripts@att.net)
