# Privacy Policy | Sizzling Bit

At SizzlingBit.com, the privacy of our visitors and readers is very important to us. Your privacy is guaranteed here.

We know that the privacy of your personal information is important. Below are the details on what types of personal information we receive when you use and visit SizzlingBit.com, and how we protect your information. We never sell, share, release or rent your personal information to any third parties.

You are free to read and utilize the resource available here at SizzlingBit.com, but not allowed to copy the content.

#### Log Files

Like many other Web sites, SizzlingBit.com makes use of analytical tool such as Google Analytics. It collects the data and information such as, Internet Service Provider (ISP), Internet Protocol (IP) address, the type of web browser (like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Opera, Internet Explorer), and referring/entry/exit pages. These data and information are NOT sold, shared, released or rented to any individuals, third parties or any other organizations, and strictly kept for increasing the performance of website.

#### Cookies, Web Beacons and DoubleClick DART Cookie

We use third party advertisements on SizzlingBit.com to serve ads when you visit our website. These advertisement companies may use cookies and web beacons. Google, as a third party advertiser uses cookies to serve ads on SizzlingBit.com.

Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to SizzlingBit.com and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).

SizzlingBit.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of third-party advertisement companies for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. SizzlingBit.com's privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or websites.
