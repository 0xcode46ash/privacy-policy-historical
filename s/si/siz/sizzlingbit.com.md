# Privacy Policy

At SizzlingBit.com, the privacy of our visitors is very important to us.

We know that the privacy of your personal information is important. Below are the details on what types of personal information we receive and collect when you use and visit SizzlingBit.com, and how we protect your information. We will never sell, share, release or rent your personal information to any third parties.

#### Personal Information

We only collect the data you provide to us when you post any comment or visit our site. The information we get may include your name, your email address, your Internet Service Provider (ISP), your Internet Protocol (IP) address, the web browser you used to visit our site (like Chrome, Firefox, Opera, Internet Explorer), which pages you accessed or visited of our site, and the date/time you visited our site. These information will never be sold, shared, released or rented to any individuals, third parties or any other organizations.

#### Cookies

To support our site, we use third party advertisements on SizzlingBit.com. Some of these advertisers may use cookies and web beacons. Google, as a third party advertiser uses cookies to serve ads on SizzlingBit.com. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to SizzlingBit.com and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
