# Privacy Policy

  


Sizzled Core value our readers and visitors privacy. However for maintaining statistics and keeping away spammers we do need to collect and preserve small piece of information of people who visit our site.

#### Privacy Policy:

  * We use analytical tool such as Google Analytics to understand where our customers and visitors are coming from. In this we collect information such as IP Addresses and Operating system used. However these data are not shared with a third party and strictly kept for increasing the performance of website.
  * The site is hosted at bluehost.com ( hosting company) but no data is shared with them either.
  * The blogging platform used by Sizzled Core is WordPress which stores a small amount of information in users computer when he or she comments. This is of very small size and no way is a malware or script which hacks a computer for information.
  * You are free to read and utilize the resource available here but now allowed to copy the content.
