###  [Privacy Policy](http://www.scrapslive.com/2000/05/privacy-policy.html)

Thank you for visiting scrapslive.com. This privacy policy tells you how we use personal information collected at this site. Please read this privacy policy before using the site or submitting any personal information. By using the site, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy. These practices may be changed, but any changes will be posted and changes will only apply to activities and information on a going forward, not retroactive basis. You are encouraged to review the privacy policy whenever you visit the site to make sure that you understand how any personal information you provide will be used.

 **Collection of Information  
**We collect the email addresses submitted by our visitors for email newsletters. The email address you provide is only used for sending out email newsletters and other updates and is not not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.  


 **Cookie/Tracking Technology  
**Cookies are placed on the browsers by the [statcounter](http://www.statcounter.com/) website statistic service used by Scrapslive.com to track the site's performance. Also the third parties (The advertising used in this site) may place cookies on your browser to collect information in course of ads being served in our website.  


 **Commitment to Data Security**  
Your email addresses are kept secure. All emails and newsletters from this site allow you to opt out of further mailings. 

**Privacy Contact Information**  
If you have any questions, concerns, or comments about our privacy policy you may contact us using the information below:

By e-mail: mail@scrapslive.com

We reserve the right to make changes to this policy. Any changes to this policy will be posted.
