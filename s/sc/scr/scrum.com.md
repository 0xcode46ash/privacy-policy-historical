# Scrum.com - Rugby News, Results, and Action, including the Rugby World Cup, from Scrum, the best Rugby Football site in the World : Privacy Policy

Scrum.com respects your privacy, so we will never send you unsolicited emails. We do not generally pass on any of your personal details, or sell mailing list data, to third parties except that we may disclose your details to third parties in the following limited circumstances

•  where you consent to us doing so. So, for example, if you agree to receive promotional material from a third party as part of a site promotion or competition, your personal details may be disclosed to the third party concerned; or

•  to organisations that carry out functions on our behalf; or

•  as part of any business re-organisation, purchase or sale in which your details are one of the assets being transferred as part of the re-organisation, purchase or sale.

In each case, to the extent practicable, your personal details will remain subject to the terms of the privacy policy which is current at that time for this site, except to the extent that you agree otherwise.

You will only receive news from Scrum.com if you sign-up to receive our newsletter or otherwise agree to receive rugby related information from us elsewhere on this site. To subscribe, [**please click here**](http://devb.sotic.net/1552_58.php). You can unsubscribe at anytime by [**clicking here**](http://devb.sotic.net/1552_58.php).

A harmless item called a "cookie" and website statistics are used by Scrum.com, allowing us to record visitor numbers, number of pages viewed and referral source. These items do not record any personal data. This data simply helps us to administer and enhance the site and service.

We may amend this privacy policy at any time. If we make any material changes in the way we collect, use, and/or share your personal details, we will notify you by sending you an e-mail at the last e-mail address that you provided us, and/or by prominently posting notice of the changes on our site.
