# SciQuest : Privacy Statement

Each employee agrees to keep all customer information confidential. In addition, SciQuest, Inc. respects your right to privacy online. 

SciQuest, Inc. will ask for personal information online such as your name, email address, mailing address, etc. when you request information. Choosing to provide this information over the Internet will assist the company in offering you strategic procurement solutions. 

We fully comply with both the TCPA and Can-Spam Acts. All emails sent by SciQuest, Inc. allow you to be removed from our lists and, once removed, you will no longer receive emails.
