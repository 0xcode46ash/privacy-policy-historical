# Privacy Statement | SciQuest

Each employee agrees to keep all customer information confidential. In addition, SciQuest, Inc. respects your right to privacy online.

SciQuest, Inc. will ask for personal information online such as your name, email address, mailing address, etc. when you request information. Choosing to provide this information over the Internet will assist the company in offering you strategic procurement solutions.

We fully comply with both the TCPA and Can-Spam Acts. All emails sent by SciQuest, Inc. allow you to be removed from our lists and, once removed, you will no longer receive emails.

SciQuest does not share personal information with third-parties and does not store information about your visit for use other than to analyze content performance through the use of cookies, which you can turn off at anytime by modifying your Internet browser’s settings. SciQuest is not responsible for the republishing of the content found on this blog on other Web sites or media without permission.

**Blog Comments**

SciQuest reserves the right to edit or delete any comments submitted to the [Open Kitchen blog](https://web.archive.org/blog/ "Open Kitchen") without notice that it determines to be inappropriate, including the following:

  1. Comments deemed to be spam or questionable spam
  2. Comments including profanity
  3. Comments containing language or concepts that could be deemed offensive
  4. Comments that attack a person individually
