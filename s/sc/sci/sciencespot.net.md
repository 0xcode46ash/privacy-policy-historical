# The Science Spot: Google Search Results

## Privacy Policy

(Last update: June 2013)

### Information we gather

The following information is gathered through Site Meter from our website visitors: IP address and location if available, with the exception for the Kid Zone pages.Â 

### How we use the information we gather

SiteMeter uses the information from the regular site pages (excluding the Kid Zone pages) to enhance your visitor experience and compile aggregate data to determine our usage reports.

### The people that are given access to this information

The Site Meter usage reports are only available to the site administrator.

### The security measures we have in place to protect your personal information

To safeguard your personal information, we do not share information with any third party with the exception for advertisers as outlined below.

### Our use of cookies

Third party advertisers on our site, such as Hot Chalk Network and Google AdSense, may use cookies for tracking purposes. 

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to visitors based on their visit to sites they visit on the Internet. Website visitors may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy. For more information about opting out of Google AdSense cookies, please visit <http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html>.
