# Privacy Policy Scholarship Fellow

The Scholarship informational content uploaded on scholarshipFellow.com is solely for educational informational purposes and scholarshipfellow.com is not in any way affiliated with any of the scholarship agencies shared on our site.

We review all the Scholarships information before uploading to our site from authentic resources.

* * *

We scholarship fellow team respects the clause that the information we collect from our users like their first and last names with email addresses are purely confidential and we do not share this info. We scholarshipfellow.com also abide by the Federal Trade Commission rules for not asking for the information from Children of under 13 years in age.

## Information Collection by ScholarshipFellow and its Use

Details such as first and last name of our website visitors majorly students hunting for scholarship opportunities at scholarshipfellow.com are collected in two ways. Either using Opt-in banner feature or using sidebar widget. This information is purely provided by scholarship fellow visitors voluntarily for sending them regular emails regarding most new scholarship or fellowship updated published on our website.

Our team and Scholarship fellow board takes user information very seriously and we do not share this information with any third party. In any case, if we are required to share user information with any app or company associated or affiliated with us for purpose of educational or informational plans then schoarship fellow will notify users first.

### Change in Privacy Conditions Serviced on Scholarship Fellow

If there is any minor or major change associated with user information or privacy concerns at scholarship fellow platform then we are going to notify our prestigious site visitors via email.

In case they wish you discontinue Newsletter services of Scholarship Fellow then they have an option in email to click on “Un-Subscribe” button in order to stop receiving updates from us.

#### Other websites capture User Data of Scholarship Fellow or not?

Scholarship Fellow do not allow any third party service or an app to capture user data from our server. This user data in servers of Scholarshipfellow on our site about global fellowships or scholarships is purely used to notify users in form of our daily Newsletter updates.

##### Privacy Concerns of Social Media Featured Apps

Our website scholarship fellow has interactions with social media sites and the privacy concerns are solely dependable on how the social media uses user’s information. Cookie feature on scholarship fellow is enabled to let it function properly. If you wish to discontinue our scholarship fellow Newsletter updates then any time use unsubscribe button in email you receive from us and you’ll never hear back from scholarship fellow team again.

Scholarships shared through our website using social media sources contains our links and not in any way shares contact information of scholarship agencies of the users or visitors of our website. We highly secure our scholarship fellow social media campaigns to ensure the privacy of our visitors and provide authentic sources of information with application processes short methods to helpout new students from all over the international audience.
