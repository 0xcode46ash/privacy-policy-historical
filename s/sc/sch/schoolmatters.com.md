# SchoolMatters - Privacy Notice

Contacting us via e-mail or registering for Standard & Poor's schoolmatters.com updates reveals your e-mail address and any other information you include such as phone number and/or mailing address. We will use this information to help us process your registration, fulfill your request or respond to your inquiry. All of your personal information will be stored in a secure database in the United States of America. Access to this database is limited to authorized persons. 

Occasionally, we use data collected about customers and prospects to inform them about products or services from Standard & Poor's our parent, The McGraw-Hill Companies. 

If at any time you would like your name removed from lists that are shared for promotional reasons within Standard & Poor's, with other units of The McGraw-Hill Companies, or with third parties, simply send a written request to [schoolmatters@sandp.com](mailto:schoolmatters@sandp.com) or to Privacy Official 47-3-4, Standard & Poor's, 55 Water Street, NY NY 10041. 

You can also send a written request to [schoolmatters@sandp.com](mailto:schoolmatters@sandp.com) if you would like to confirm the accuracy of the information we have collected from you, or if you have questions about the uses of this information. 

For more information about The McGraw-Hill Companies Privacy Policy, please visit <http://www.mcgraw-hill.com/privacy.html>.
