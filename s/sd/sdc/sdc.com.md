# SDC's Privacy Statement

|   
---  
****SDC**** | SDC's Privacy Statement  
**SDC** is committed to protecting your privacy. This SDC Privacy Statement applies to SDC's family of personals web sites and governs data collection and usage at all SDC personals sites and services; it does not apply to non personals online or offline SDC sites, products or services. Please read the SDC Privacy Statement below and also any supplementary information at the end of this statement for additional details about particular SDC sites and services that you may use.

SDC DOES NOT SELL, lease, or disclose any personal information collected such as your email address and holds this information in the strictest privacy to be used only in the intent that it was disclosed. 

SDC is an Adult audience web site company, intended for users of legal age in their prospective state. Some Personal information of all users is collected, used and disclosed as described in this Privacy Statement.   
  
****SDC**** | Collection of your Personal Information  
At some sites, SDC collects personal information, such as your e-mail address. SDC may also collect demographic information, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites. Information collected by SDC is used for SDC services and websites only. 

If you choose to sign up for a paid subscription service, we will collect additional personal information, including your credit card number and billing address, in order to create a SDC billing account. You can always add to or update the information in your billing account through the Tools --> Membership area. 

SDC also collects certain information about your computer hardware and software. This information may include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by SDC for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide help regarding use of SDC's sites   
  
****SDC**** | Use of Cookies  
SDC.com uses "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies **cannot** be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.. 

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. For example, We use cookies (and you can turn this off on the login page) to "remember" you each time you login. This saves you time from typing in your user name and password each time you visit us. 

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.   
  
****SDC**** | **Use of Web Beacons**  
SDC Web pages do not contain electronic images known as Web beacons - sometimes called single-pixel gifs except to collect web site traffic data.   
****SDC**** | **Changes to this Statement**  
**SDC** will occasionally update this Privacy Statement to reflect company and customer feedback. When SDC posts changes to this Statement, you will see the word "updated" next to the Privacy Statement link on the front page of SDC. If there are material changes to this Statement or in how SDC will use your personal information, SDC will prominently post such changes prior to implementing the change. SDC encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how SDC is protecting your information.  
  
****SDC**** | Security  
To secure site integrity, SDC employs measures, including but not limited to security audits, use of encryption tools and software, and other reasonable security measures and procedures. 

Internal access to users' private and nonpublic personal information is restricted to site administrators **ONLY.**  
  
****SDC**** | Enforcement  
  
In the event that SDC becomes aware that site security is compromised or nonpublic user information has been disclosed to unrelated third parties as a result of external activity, including but not limited to external security attacks, SDC shall take reasonable measures which it deems appropriate, including but not limited to internal investigation and reporting, and notification to and cooperation with law enforcement authorities, notwithstanding other provisions of this Privacy Statement. 

If SDC becomes aware that a user's personal information provided to SDC has been disclosed in a manner not in accordance with this Privacy Statement, SDC shall make reasonable efforts to notify the affected user, as soon as reasonably possible and as permitted by law, of what information has been disclosed, to the extent that SDC knows this information.   
  
****SDC**** | No Guarantees For Factors Beyond SDC's Control  
  
While this Privacy Statement expresses SDC's standards for maintenance of private data, it is not in a position to guarantee that the standards will always be met. There may be factors beyond SDC's control (e.g., "script kiddies, crackers and other malcontents, hurricanes, tornados, act of god, plague, scarlet fever, loss of power, loss of mind, body and soul, rabies, mad cow syndrome, foot and mouth disease, west nile virus ") that may result in disclosure of data. As a consequence, SDC disclaims any warranties or representations relating to maintenance or nondisclosure of private information.
