# Privacy Policy

As part of our commitment to offer unrivaled levels of service to our customers, we pledge that the information collected through this site will be used only to add value to your shopping experience. The Sultan Center does not sell or lease personal information (name, address, Civil ID number, bank account, credit card number, telephone number, or other information by which you can be personally identified) to any other company, person, or agency.*

The information you provide helps us understand your shopping preferences so that we can personalize your online experience and offer savings awards and other benefits to you. TSC may use this data to give you personally tailored coupons, offers, or other information that may be provided to TSC by other companies. Additionally, TSC may create compilations of information that is not personally identifying and provide this data to other companies to help them understand the demographics of our customers.

Please remember that you may be asked for information by entities other than TSC when clicking on an advertisement or hypertext language linking sultan-center.com to other sites. TSC does not exercise control over any information you give to any other entity, even if that information came from the TSC website.

By using this site, you are agreeing to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy Statement. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE WITH THIS PRIVACY STATEMENT, DO NOT USE THIS SITE. We reserve the right to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this statement at any time, at our discretion. Any changes will be posted on the site. Please check this page periodically for changes.

_*TSC may disclose personal information in response to subpoena, court order of a specific request by a law enforcement agency, or as required by law._
