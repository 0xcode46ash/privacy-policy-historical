# Disclaimer & Privacy Policy | SUU

Although the authors of this web site have made every reasonable effort to be factually accurate, no responsibility is assumed for editorial/clerical errors or error occasioned by honest mistake. All information contained on this web site is subject to change by the appropriate officials of Southern Utah University without prior notice. Material on this web site does not serve as a legal or equitable contract between Southern Utah University and any other party.

Any information submit via website forms will be used for the purposes stated on the form. SUU respects user privacy and does not sell information to third-party sources.

The site uses a few external social collaboration tools and user analytics tracking software, but no personal information is collected or stored by SUU in the implementation of these utilities.

Southern Utah University's official logos may not be used or reproduced without permission. Individuals, organizations or companies that wish to use the official logos must contact the [Office of Marketing Communication](https://web.archive.org/web/20150512211105id_/http%3A//suu.edu/mc/index.html) for permission.

SUU strives to make the website comply with technical standards for accessibility. If a user encounters difficulties, please contact the [Disability Support Office](https://web.archive.org/web/20150512211105id_/http%3A//suu.edu/ss/support/disability/index.html).

This website contains links to other sites. SUU is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.
