Information Collection and Use  
Sunbelt Rentals collects information in several ways from different areas of our web site in order to provide you with personal service. This information may also be used to evaluate and enhance your experience with Sunbelt Rentals and your visit to our web site.

Personal Information  
The types of information we collect to personally identify you include your name, address, telephone number, and email address. Sunbelt Rentals collects this information only when you volunteer this information on applications, contact forms, Sunbelt Rentals mail signup, employment applications, and other forms located on this web site. By entering information into any one of these areas, you grant Sunbelt Rentals permission to contact you about selected products, companies and events we think may interest you. If you later decide you do not want to be contacted, please let us know by mail, phone or email at [customerservice@sunbeltrentals.com](mailto:customerservice@sunbeltrentals.com)

Certain areas of our site are only accessible upon disclosure of personally identifiable information. Therefore, if you do not wish to volunteer your personal information in these areas, access will be denied.

Sunbelt Rentals also uses a browser feature called “cookies” to recognize you as a repeat visitor each time you visit our web site. You always have the option to refuse cookies by turning them off thorough your web browser options. However, if you refuse cookies, you may not have access to all areas or features of SunbeltRentals.com.

Non-personal Information  
Non-personal information may also be collected which provides Sunbelt Rentals with information regarding your visit to our web site. This information may include your browser version, service provider, IP address, and domain type. Sunbelt Rentals uses this information to understand how visitors access SunbeltRentals.com. This helps us build and optimize the best web site for our audience.

Use of third parties  
In certain cases, Sunbelt Rentals may use third parties to fulfill orders, process payments, monitor site activity, conduct surveys, and administer emails. If you do not want your information collected by us or by third parties for these purposes, you can decide not to participate in these activities.

Links to other sites through SunbeltRentals.com  
Our web site may link to other web sites that may be of interest to you. These web sites have their own privacy, security, and terms of use statements. Sunbelt Rentals is not responsible for the policies or content of linked sites and we encourage you to review their policies before providing them with personal information.

Data Security  
Sunbelt Rentals is committed to providing reasonable and appropriate technical, administrative and physical safeguards to protect against foreseeable hazards such as data loss, destruction, misuse and improper disclosure.

Children’s Policy
