# Registration and Privacy Policy

|  |  | 

##### ****

Thank you for visiting Sunset's website.

By completing the form below, you are eligible to be on our mailing list for for advance notice of Sunset events, special offers, and promotions. Your registration is optional. See our Privacy Policy below.

|  |  | **Privacy Policy**

The Sunset Publishing Corporation (Sunset) Website is maintained by Sunset Publishing Corporation, 80 Willow Road, Menlo Park, CA 94025. You can reach us by e-mail at webmaster@sunsetpub.com.

The Sunset Website does not collect personal information about individuals except when knowingly provided by such individuals. Any personal information (e.g., name, e-mail address, etc.) provided by such individuals may be used in connection with Sunset Magazine, Sunset Books, the Sunset Website, and for such other purposes as may be indicated at the point of collection (or otherwise detailed in Sunset’s Privacy Policy). From time to time, such information may be provided to reputable third parties for marketing information-gathering purposes. To correct or update your personal information and/or if you do not want us to share your personal information with third parties, please let us know by e-mail at offlist@sunsetpub.com, and include your name, address, and e-mail. While Sunset will use reasonable good faith efforts to require third parties to adhere to its privacy policies, it does not bear any responsibility for any actions or policies of such parties. Please note that Sunset’s website may contain links to other sites that may have different policies, and Sunset does not bear any responsibility for any actions or policies of such other sites.

KIDS: Please be sure to ask your parents for permission before sending any information about yourself over the internet.
