# Supload.com - Free Image Hosting, Free Audio Hosting, Free Music Hosting, Free Video Hosting, Ringtone Hosting, Free Movie Hosting

Supload.com will only collect small details about users which are required for security and image hosting functions. the details collected are as follows;

user's IP address  
web browser type   
file & filename which is being uploaded   
time of the file being uploaded

all information is kept secure on our servers. in the event that law enforcement officials request any information, Supload.com will provide any information logged to the respective law enforcement agencies per their request. Supload's continuing effort to secure user privacy means that your information will never be sold or distributed to any non law enforcement third parties.
