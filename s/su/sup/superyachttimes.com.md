# Superyacht Times

SuperYachtTimes.com respects every user's privacy. When you register at SuperYachtTimes.com, we ask for your name, e-mail, country, profession and company name.

By using this website, you understand that all information collected from your order will be collected indefinitely; and may be stored and used in jurisdictions whose privacy laws may be different and less protective than those of your home jurisdiction. SuperYachtTimes.com may use the information collected for a variety of purposes including direct marketing via e-mail.

SuperYachtTimes.com reserves the right to disclose your personal information if required to do so by law, or in the good faith belief that such action is reasonably necessary to comply with legal process, respond to claims, or protect the rights, property or safety of our company, photographers, customers, or the public.

SuperYachtTimes.com uses as much effort as possible to ensure the accurateness and reliability of the content, but make no representations or warranties as to the contents accuracy, or reliability. There are links to other websites which are beyond our control. You waive any and all claims against SuperYachtTimes.com for any and all issues regarding these links.
