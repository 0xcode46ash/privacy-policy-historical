# SuperPages.ca: About Us & Help: Privacy Policy

  |  Dominion Information Services Inc. - Disclosure Notice

Dominion Information Services Inc. (" **Dominion** ") will only collect, use and disclose information that is personally identifiable to you (your " **Personal Information** ", provided that Personal Information does not include your name, address and telephone number as it appears in a telephone directory) in accordance with our Privacy Policy which is available by contacting 1-877-685-3474 or by [email](mailto:callcentre@dominioninfo.com) (Service en français, composez sans frais: 1-800-308-8050) or as required or permitted by law. This disclosure notice contains a short summary of our Privacy Policy.

  * establish and maintain responsible commercial relations with you and to provide ongoing services; 
  * understand your needs and preferences; 
  * develop, enhance, market or provide products and services; 
  * manage and develop our business and operations; 
  * use in connection with the operation of our business and billing and collection; 
  * co-ordinate with telephone or other communication companies; 
  * facilitate, only if and to the extent necessary, the financing, sale or transfer of our business in whole or in part; and 
  * meet legal and regulatory requirements.

Collection, Use and Disclosure.

Unless you otherwise consent, your Personal Information is collected, used and disclosed only in connection with the purposes identified above or as may be required or permitted by law.

Your Personal Information, regardless of the format in which it is held, shall be protected from loss, theft, unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use modification or destruction through appropriate security measures. Where commercially practicable, Dominion shall use contractual arrangements to protect Personal Information disclosed to third parties. We keep your information for only as long as may be necessary or relevant for the identified purpose of collection or as may be required or permitted by law.

You decide when to provide us with your Personal Information. However, your decision to withhold information may limit the services we can provide to you. You may "opt out" of receiving certain informational or other materials from Dominion.

You may access, verify and amend your Personal Information held by Dominion by calling us at 1-877-685-3474 or by [email](mailto:callcentre@dominioninfo.com) (Service en français, composez sans frais: 1-800-308-8050).

If you have any concerns or complaints regarding our Personal Information practices, please contact us as soon as possible. We shall use all reasonable efforts to address your concerns or complaints. If you are not fully satisfied with our response, we will help to further escalate your complaint. We may be liable under applicable federal statutes for a violation of our Privacy Policy.
