# SuperCounters - Fastest and most reliable free hit counter

Collection of User Information  
SuperCounters may periodically collect user information from it's visitors. 

**Cookies** \- Cookies are used to track you on the website, and in the login process. 

**Registration** \- During the registration process, we collect some information from you. We will not sell, give or share your personal information with anyone, unless required to by law. We will only contact you for problems or changes with your counter, for announcements about our service, or other matters related to Supercounters.
