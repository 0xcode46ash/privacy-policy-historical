# Sur La Table - The Art and Soul of Cooking

Privacy Policy  
   
Sur La Table is committed to protecting your privacy while you use our website, www.surlatable.com. Our privacy policy statement describes how we gather information and what we do with that information.  
   
General Information  
   
  
This site contains links to other sites. [www.surlatable.com](http://www.surlatable.com/) is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these other websites. 

Each customer's computer has an IP address (Internet Protocol). We use your IP address to diagnose problems with our server and administer our website. Your IP address is used to identify your computer (numerically) and your shopping cart as well as gather broad demographic information.  
  
   
Cookies  
   
  
Our site uses cookies. Cookies contain information about you and your preferences. Cookies are small text files (alphanumeric identifiers) a website uses to recognize repeat customers and enhance their experience when they return to the website. Only the information that you provide, or the choices you make while visiting a website, can be stored in a cookie. For example, a cookie cannot determine your e-mail address unless you choose to enter it in to the appropriate field. 

Allowing a website to create a cookie does not give that or any other site access to the rest of your computer, and only the site that created the cookie can read it. Cookies allow you to take advantage of the best features offered by websites. We use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site. You can set your browser (Internet Explorer or Netscape) to notify you when a site is using a cookie and to refuse it if you'd like. Click [here](http://www.surlatable.com/general/faq.cfm#cookie_info) for more information.   
  
   
How We Collect Information   
   
  
Sur La Table gathers information about customers through:

  * Our product order form
  * Our catalog registration form (opt-in choice)
  * Online surveys (opt-in choice)
  * Promotional contests (opt-in choice)
  * Newsletter registration form and campaigns (opt-in choice)
  * Culinary program registration form (opt-in choice) 

  
   
How We Use This Information   
   
  
  
The information we obtain from you is used for these purposes:

  * To send you items you have ordered
  * To contact you if necessary (about your purchase, to clarify information provided, etc.)
  * Billing for your purchases
  * To send you information about our company and promotional material from some of our partners
  * Sur La Table occasionally makes our mailing list available to other services. We want you to receive only the e-mail, newsletters and catalogs that you want. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings. See contact information below.

  
   
Security   
   
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. For your protection, we do not store any credit card information after your transaction has been completed. This information must be re-entered each time you purchase from our site.
