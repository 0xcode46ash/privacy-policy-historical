# Survey Police

This is the web site of SurveyPolice.com, and is owned and operated by Fortuna Internet Solutions Inc.

This Policy is effective on April 1, 2007 and was last updated on November 11, 2007.

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. Because we gather certain information regarding our users, you have the right to know exactly what information is being gathered and how it is used. This privacy policy is considered to be part of SurveyPolice.com's Terms of Use Agreement. By using this website, you are also consenting to SurveyPolice.com`s privacy practices, including the use and disclosure of any personally identifiable information that you have opted to share, as per this privacy policy.

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, but not the e-mail address. We do use cookies and coding for website analytics software on this website. We do collect aggregate information on what pages users access and user-specific information on which pages are accessed and how.

The information we collect is used to improve the content, layout, and usability of our website. This information is not sold to anyone, including commercial partners.

To become a member of this website requires that you register and supply personally identifiable information including your name, date of birth, country of residence, e-mail address and other information, which is stored by SurveyPolice.com.

The information you provide to us through this website is used by SurveyPolice.com to operate this website and provide you with the information you request and to possibly communicate with you. We may, for example, use the information you provide to us to target ads or send e-mails to you including opt-in newsletters and website alerts. Your information may also be used to contact you if necessary, such as in the case of assisting you with usage of this website or by using our complaints feature on our website. You may opt-out of receiving our newsletter at any time.

Members of this website have the option of providing ratings, making comments, or filing complaints. Your ratings, comments and complaints are made publically viewable and your first name may be published along with your rating, comment, or complaint. SurveyPolice.com may also choose to edit your comments or complaints for things including but not limited to grammar, spelling, and search engine keyword strategies, which will be made publically viewable on this website.

At any time (except for administrative e-mails referenced above), Members of this website have the ability to opt-out of receiving our newsletter. To unsubscribe from our newsletter simply click on the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of email we have sent you, and your newsletter subscription preferences will be noted.

SurveyPolice.com does not send spam, or unsolicited e-mails to any of our users. We also do not sell or provide e-mail addresses to any unauthorized third party. If you receive e-mails from our partners because you have opted to agree to receive additional information during the registration process for becoming a Member of this website, please note that these e-mails do not originate from SurveyPolice.com or Fortuna Internet Solutions Inc. Please visit the website of [Opt-Intelligence](http://www.opt-intelligence.com/) for more information on who is handling your information when you "opt-in" to these offers.

Our website contains external links to commercial partners. We are not responsible for the content of these websites, nor their privacy practices. Please refer to the privacy policy listed on their website to find out more information about what information they collect and how they use it.

This Privacy Policy may be revised, changed or updated at any time. When this Privacy Policy is modified, the date with the latest revision will be clearly shown. Please visit this page often to see if this Privacy Policy has been updated since the last time you visited this website.
