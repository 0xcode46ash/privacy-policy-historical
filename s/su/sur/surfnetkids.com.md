# Surfing the Net with Kids: Privacy Policy

"Surfing the Net with Kids" respects your privacy and does not distribute, sell or publish any private information, including email addresses. Subscriber lists are never rented or sold, and are used only to receive requested information directly from "Surfing the Net with Kids." 

Demographic information is used only in aggregate to provide advertisers with more information about the "Surfing the Net" audience. Any email addresses posted on the site (in user submitted site reviews or on the bulletin board) are optional and are posted ONLY with the user's permission. 

IP addresses and cookies are used only to accurately serve banner ads, to assure that when you click on an ad, you are sent to the correct site. 

"Surfing the Net" is not responsible for the content of sites outside of its network.
