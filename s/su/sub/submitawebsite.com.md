# search engine submission and free registration to hundreds of engines

Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Always Respected

Submitawebsite is a reputable search engine placement company which has been offering free and paid services to the Internet community since 1997. Information gathered is used to define submission standards, to review client web sites and to offer updated improved services to our visitors including information from other reputable organizations whose products or services may be of valuable resource to you. At any time if you wish not to receive future e-mail correspondence from Submitawebsite, please e-mail message to unsubscribe@submitawebsite.com

* * *

About Cookies

You may have read about "cookies," nuggets of information that are placed by a Web site in a storage place on your own computer. Submitawebsite uses cookies only to control the correct function of our forms and to ensure that you don't see the same ad too many times in a single session. Our cookies do not contain any personal identity information or track any user activity.  


* * *

Safety

Submitawebsite does not offer or service any sites of illegal or pornographic nature.  


* * *

Kids  
  
Be sure to ask your parents for permission before you send any information about yourself like your name, address or e-mail address over the Internet and remember to ask permission before buying anything on-line.
