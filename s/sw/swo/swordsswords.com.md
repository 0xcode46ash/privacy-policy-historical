# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

At [www.swordsswords.com](http://www.swordsswords.com/), a subsidary of Wholesale Gallery Inc., we are committed to protecting your privacy online. Our Privacy Policy demonstrates our firm commitment to protecting the privacy of our customers. We use the information that we collect about our customers to fulfill product and service purchases and to create a more personalized online experience.   
When you shop at [www.swordsswords.com](http://www.swordsswords.com/), a subsidary of Wholesale Gallery Inc., we collect transactional information or Personally Identifiable Information from you such as your name, telephone number, e-mail address, billing address, shipping address, credit card or other payment information and a password.   
We at [www.swordsswords.com](http://www.swordsswords.com/), a subsidary of Wholesale Gallery Inc., do not sell or rent your transactional information to others.  
[www.swordsswords.com](http://www.swordsswords.com/), a subsidary of Wholesale Gallery Inc., uses customer information only for the purposes of filling orders and contacting purchasers to inform them of their order status. Also, our company does not tolerate credit card fraud because we put an emphasis on keeping our customers safe. This includes using the highest level of SSL Encryption when accepting orders on our web site.  
Also, [www.swordsswrods.com](http://www.swordsswrods.com/), a subsidary of Wholsesale Gallery Inc.,  will never ask you to provide sensitive information through email because one of the biggest threats to the security of our customers' accounts is fraudulent email and Web sites, called spoof email and spoof Web sites. Both, spoof email and spoof Web sites, are used to obtain personal and account information.   
We do not store any sensitive customer information (including credit card data) because all orders are manually reviewed and checked by our in-house fraud analyst. [www.swordsswords.com](http://www.swordsswords.com/), a subsidary of Wholesale Gallery Inc., will remain committed to protecting your privacy online.
