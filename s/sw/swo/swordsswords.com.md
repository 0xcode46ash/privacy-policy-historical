# Privacy Policy

Swordsswords takes pride in being a service-oriented company and Swordsswords will work hard to earn your repeat business. This webpage is officially owned and established by Swordsswords as the Privacy Policy, and any changes made to this policy will be updated here on this page, except where clearly expressed otherwise. 

This means that Swordsswords enforces strict protocol to ensure that any Personally Identifiable Information of every customer is kept confidential beyond the usage stated within this Swordsswords Privacy Policy. At every step of your order processing, from the secure browsing of our site to receiving your purchase, you will be able to see and appreciate the level of customer service and security that makes Swordsswords.com a trusted online store. 

Swordsswords has put forth the following policy in order to protect customer/individual interests, Personally Identifiable Information, non-personally identifiable information, Swordsswords as a company, and trusted affiliates for operating the business. 

Personally Identifiable Information:

At SwordsSwords we are committed to protecting your privacy online. The Swordsswords Privacy Policy demonstrates our firm commitment to protecting the privacy of our customers. Swordsswords will use the information that we collect about our customers to fulfill product and service purchases and to create a more personalized online experience. When you shop at SwordsSwords, Personally Identifiable Information is collected from you such as your name, telephone number, e-mail address, billing address, shipping address, credit card or other payment information, an encrypted password, and other personally identifiable information. All personally identifiable information from the Swordsswords website is transferred via SSL encryption (HTTPS) to a secure server and access to this information is strictly forbidden for any reason other than: (1) processing a transaction, (2)order processing, (3) customer change or removal of information, (4) protecting from encrypted data loss, (5) improvements to the website shopping experience, (6) contacting a customer for business relations (7) to personalize customer experience (8) to offer opt-in promotional opportunities with your consent (9) to contact you with shipping and order fulfillment informational emails and contact. Personally Identifiable Information is only trusted to parties who assist in: (1) operating the Swordsswords website, (2) conducting business and/or services to the customer, (3) associated services to assist in operation and agree to keep the above information confidential. 

Stored and Encrypted Password information is not retrievable by Swordsswords employees, and all password resets require the confirmation through the customers registered email address. 

Swordsswords does not sell or rent Personally Identifiable Information to any 3rd parties. If you wish to have your information removed from the Swordsswords website or systems, you may contact Swordsswords by calling 1-877-579-6737. 

Non-Personally Identifiable Visitor Information:

Swordsswords may collect Non-Personally Identifiable Visitor Information for the use of analytics, upgrades, benchmarks, marketing, advertising, and for other trusted uses. A Swordsswords Email Newsletter sign-up is optional, and anyone can enter their own email address to opt-in. However, this is for opt-in purposes only, and by using this feature, you agree that you have entered a valid email address that you have consent to use. It has come to our attention that a wrong email address may be entered without consent, and Swordsswords provides options to Unsubscribe from the Swordsswords Email Newsletter. To unsubscribe from Swordsswords Email Newsletters, click the “UNSUBSCRIBE” link at the bottom of a promotional email, or send a request via email to promo@swordsswords.com. Swordsswords provides customers with a strictly enforced “DO NOT MAIL” list, which is irreversible except by the receipient who has requested to be on the “DO NOT MAIL LIST”. 

Payment Information Processing:

Credit Card information and other payment information is not saved on the Swordsswords website system, and is only used to process a transaction through the authorized Swordsswords Payment Gateway. All supplied payment information on the Swordsswords online systems is transferred via Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) and encrypted into the Swordsswords Payment Gateway. Access to this information is forbidden except by those authorized with special access to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential. After a transaction, your private information (credit cards, financials, etc.) will not be kept on file for more than 60 days. 

When placing an order on Swordsswords website, you will be required to enter your payment information for each order placed. This is a precautionary measure that ensures additional security to Swordsswords customers in that the Swordsswords accounts and website will not be used fraudulently by Swordsswords or it's trusted affiliates. 

Swordsswords and affiliates are periodically audited to ensure company/site security and compliance with laws, regulations, and governance. Swordsswords secure data is encrypted by Network Solutions Certificate Authority: 

 

How we use cookies:

Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that www.swordsswords.com transfers to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser to allow Swordsswords systems to recognize your browser and to provide personalized logins and to store items in your Shopping Cart between visits. The Help portion of the toolbar on most browsers will tell you how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie, or how to disable cookies altogether. However, cookies allow you to enjoy a convenient and enriched shopping experience and Swordsswords recommends that you leave them turned on. 

California Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

Because Swordsswords value your privacy we have taken the necessary precautions to be in compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. Swordsswords, therefore, will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent. 

As part of the California Online Privacy Protection Act, all users of our site may make any changes to their information at anytime by logging into their HTTPS encrypted "My Account" webpage and going to the 'Edit Profile' page. 

Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance 

Swordsswords is in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Swordsswords website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older. _*Please also note that we require a customer be 18 years of age or older._
