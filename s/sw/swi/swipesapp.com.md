# Privacy Policy

                                      Privacy Policy
We at Swipes Incorporated regard your privacy and right to confidentiality of data as very
important. Your data is collected and used in accordance to the relevant provisions of the
American data protection legislation. In this Privacy Policy, we are informing you which of
your data do we collect and for what purposes. The policy regards information disclosed by
the users of the website: http://swipesapp.com and the app - Swipes app.
Controller
Swipes is a global service. However, by accessing the app, you consent to the processing
of your data in the United States as the Service is operated in the United States. Your data
is protected by the laws of United States of America. If you have any questions or concerns
about data protection, you are welcome to contact us by email at this address:
support@swipesapp.com or mail us at:
Swipes Incorporated
16192 Coastal Highway, Lewes
Delaware, 19958
United States of America
Subject of data protection
The subject of data protection is personal and non-personal identification information. We
may collect personal details such as your name, your email address, and the use-data that
you have provided in various ways - subscription on the website, to a newsletter,
registration to the app or respond to a survey, in connection to our products and services.
We will ask you for appropriate name and email address but the submission of data is
voluntarily. You can always refuse to supply personal identification information, however
that may prevent you from engaging in certain website and/or app related activities.
Automatically collected data
For statistical purposes, we may also collect non-personal identification information about
your interaction with our website and app. The internet browser is automatically
transmitting the following data:
   • your date and time of access,
   • your browser-type and version,
   • the operating system you use,
   • the URL of the website you have previously visited, the quantity of data transmitted
This data is not assigned to an identifiable person and is stored separately from personal
data.
Data entered by the users
You can use the Swipes product with a registration via email and password or with a
Facebook registration. We use your email data to be able to contact you, send you

newsletters (from which you can sign out at any point) and to respond to your wishes,
questions and criticism. You can access Swipes on iPhone, iPod, iPad devices, however
there is no synchronization between them at the moment. We are now building this feature
to ensure your best experience with the daily planner on the various devices you have. For
now, your data is solely stored on your storage medium.
Transfer of data
Your data will not be transferred to third parties without asking for your consent in advance.
Data transfer to third parties without informing you separately beforehand can occur in the
following exceptional cases as explained below:
    • In case of legal investigation of Swipes Incorporated, personal data will be transferred
      to the criminal investigation authorities and, if appropriate, to injured third parties. We
      will only do this if there are concrete indications of illegal and/or abusive behavior.
    • We also depend on the use of external service providers to supply us services for
      storing data, customer service, advertising and in the future - processing payments. In
      this cases, information is transferred to the suppliers to enable the services. However,
      we select the providers carefully for their transparency of privacy.
    • Swipes Incorporated may go through a change of legal form. We may also form, sell or
      buy subsidiaries, divisions or parts of the company. In such transactions, the user
      information together with the part of the company to be transferred, will be passed on.
      This processes will be done in accordance with this Privacy Policy and the relevant
      data protection laws.
Changes in your data
If you want to delete your account, please inform us at support@swipesapp.com. We will
also delete all data associated to your account. We will be grateful to hear your reasons for
leaving us, so that we can constantly improve the service.
How we protect your information
We adopt appropriate data collection, storage and processing practices and security
measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction of
your personal information, username, password, transaction information and data stored on
our servers.
Sensitive and private data exchange between the website/ the app and its Users happens
over a SSL secured communication channel and is encrypted and protected with digital
signatures.
Changes to this privacy policy
Swipes reserves the right to alter the Privacy Policy at any time. When we do that, we will
revise the updated date at the bottom of this page. We encourage Users to frequently
check this page for any changes to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the
personal information we collect. You acknowledge and agree that it is your responsibility to
review this privacy policy periodically and become aware of modifications.
