# Swiggy | Swiggy.in

We ( **Bundl Technologies Private Limited, alias “Swiggy”** ) are fully committed to respecting your privacy and shall ensure that your personal information is safe with us.

**COLLECTION OF INFORMATION**

When you place an order on our website, we collect your information including email address, mobile number and address so we can deliver reliably. Signing up to get registered will also involve collecting the same information.

**USE OF INFORMATION**

We use the details filled in during order checkout/ signup to ensure that the orders are delivered reliably in time, and to keep you posted regarding any delays and send you invoices.

The information collected to register you as a member will be used to improve the customer experience while re-ordering or to provide rewards to customers regularly using our platform.

**STRICT CONFIDENTIALITY POLICY**

We will never sell, trade or pass on your personal information to any third party, including our partner stores from where you are ordering. We will only share information when it is essential to comply with law, enforce our site policies or protect others or ours rights, property or safety.

**USAGE POLICY**

All photos are the property of Bundl Technologies Pvt Ltd/ our partner restaurants and shall not be used for commercial purposes without mentions/credits.
