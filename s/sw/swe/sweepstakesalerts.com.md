# SweepstakesAlerts.com - Privacy Policy

The following sets forth the policies for the collection and use of personally identifiable information (“Information”) by Sweepstakes Alerts (“SweepstakesAlerts.com”) in connection with its operation of its Web site located at www.SweepstakesAlerts.com, ( the “Site”) and other areas around the web as well as offline (the Site and all other operations together are known as the “Business”), and supersedes any previous statement of such policies. SweepstakesAlerts.com has created the below policy to explain the types of information it collects from users, how it uses this information, with whom and for what purposes it shares the information, and how users can interact with SweepstakesAlerts.com in connection with the information collection and use. 

SweepstakesAlerts.com is owned and operated by Dynamic Data Strategies, LLC. 

## Collection of Information.

In connection with the Site, SweepstakesAlerts.com may collect Information in the following ways:

  * Through forms filled out by a user.
  * Through Information provided by a user in connection with prize promotion entries.
  * Through the maintenance and analysis of Web server logs.
  * Via communications between a user and SweepstakesAlerts.com



SweepstakesAlerts.com may also connect non-personally identifiable information from users via “cookies” (small text files placed by SweepstakesAlerts.com on user computers), single-pixel GIF image files (also called “Web beacons”), Web server log analysis and other similar technological means. Such information may be used to track site trends and enhance the user experience, and may be shared with third parties. 

To the extent third parties may place advertising or other content on the Site or through a promotion of SweepstakesAlerts.com, such third parties may utilize cookies or other technological means within the advertising to collect and utilize non-personally identifiable information. SweepstakesAlerts.com is not responsible for information collected by third parties, nor for the collection or use of Information by other sites to which the Site or any promotion of SweepstakesAlerts.com is linked.

## Types of Information Collected by SweepstakesAlerts.com.

The following types of Information about a user are among those that may be collected by SweepstakesAlerts.com in connection with the Business:

  * Name
  * Postal address
  * E-mail address
  * Telephone number
  * Cell phone number
  * Date of birth
  * IP Address
  * Referring site
  * Friends
  * Photos and Videos
  * Other technical information collected by the Site’s servers.

  


## Children.

SweepstakesAlerts.com does not knowingly collect or retain information in connection with the Site from children under the age of 13. SweepstakesAlerts.com does not ask children under age 13 for personal information. 

## Use of Information.

SweepstakesAlerts.com may use Information collected in connection with the Site in the following ways:

  * To send users promotions in connection with SweepstakesAlerts.com.
  * To send users promotions on behalf of a third-party. Such promotions will only be sent by SweepstakesAlerts.com on behalf of the third-party.
  * To provide requested information, products and services to users via the Site or through other online or offline channels.
  * To provide newsletters to our users. 
  * To improve the user experience with the Site.
  * In connection with the operation of the Site.
  * For users to obtain information and offers for products and services offered by SweepstakesAlerts.com.



In order to do the foregoing, SweepstakesAlerts.com may provide the Information to trusted third parties, including but not limited to selected third party vendors as well as third party contractors providing services to SweepstakesAlerts.com to assist with the operation of the Site and other communication services. SweepstakesAlerts.com will use commercially reasonable efforts to limit use of the Information by such third parties to the specific uses set forth above. SweepstakesAlerts.com also utilizes electronic and physical security to reduce the risk of improper access to or manipulation of Information during transmission and storage, but cannot guarantee the security or integrity of the Information and shall have no liability for breaches of security or integrity or third-party interception in transit.

We may also share your information with affiliated and non-affiliated third-parties to offer products or services that may be of interest or benefit to our customers

SweepstakesAlerts.com may also disclose Information when it determines it is necessary to comply with applicable laws or regulations, in connection with a merger, acquisition, asset purchase or sale of or by SweepstakesAlerts.com, or to protect the interests or safety of SweepstakesAlerts.com, its customers, or other visitors to the Site.

SweepstakesAlerts.com may share information to respond to subpoenas, court order, or legal process, or to establish or exercise it legal rights or defend against legal claims. We will transfer Information about you if SweepstakesAlerts.com is acquired by or merged with another company.

## Opting Out from Offers from SweepstakesAlerts.com.

At any time, a user may opt out from receiving future offers from SweepstakesAlerts.com by following the instructions contained within each marketing communication. Such opting out will not apply to any communications from third parties to whom SweepstakesAlerts.com may have provided Information regarding the user. Third parties’ use of the Information is subject to such parties’ own privacy policies, for which SweepstakesAlerts.com shall not be responsible.

## Access to Information. 

All users who provide their Information to SweepstakesAlerts.com may modify and correct such Information through their personal profile.
