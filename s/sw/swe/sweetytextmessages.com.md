# Privacy Policy

Sweetytextmessages.com website aspires to provide a pleasant browsing experience for visitors through providing a favorable Privacy Policy.

## Collection of Information

The Site monitors the traffic through collecting non-personal information (the type of the computer / browser, IP addresses, demographics).

## Cookies and 3rd Party Advertisements

The Service uses cookies to personalize the browsing experience. The Users may set browser settings and refuse cookies. Google uses the DART cookies, which assemble non-personal information and permit the service of ads. Review the Google ad and content network privacy policy. Third party companies provide ads on the Site, they use cookies to collect non-personal information.

## Security

The Site uses diverse electronic and operational tools to prevent the unauthorized access, the usage or the alternation of the Users’ transmissions.

## Links to Other Websites

Sweetytextmessages.com resource incorporates links to other sites, the Service is not responsible for security, the Privacy Policy of other websites.

## Limitations to Privacy

The Service is not responsible for any damages which might occur as the result of Users’ online activities, and due to the data of third party websites.

## Changes

The Site changes the Privacy Policy at any time without notifications, the updates appear on the Site.
