# SQL Maestro Group - Privacy policy

## Privacy Policy

* * *

We do not share or sell your personal information with anyone else, we keep it secret. 

**IP Address**  
We may log your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our web site. This information is primarily used for general traffic tracking and security. 

**Cookies**  
Our site does not use cookies. 

**Newsletter**  
We allow visitors to sign up for our free newsletter by providing an email address. The email address you provide is used only to send you the free newsletter. These information is not shared with any third parties. 

**External Links**  
This site contains links to other sites. **SQL Maestro Group** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. 

**Security**  
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.
