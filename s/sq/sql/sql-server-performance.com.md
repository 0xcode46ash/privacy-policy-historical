# SQL-Server-Performance.Com Privacy Policy

SQL-Server-Performance.Com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: SQL-Server-Performance.Com.

We collect the default information that is sent from your browser to our website when you visit, which includes, but is not limited to: dynamic information, click-stream information, http information, and search terms. We summarize the information we collect, but do not maintain individual records about our visitors. We use this information to help us better understand our website's visitors. This summarized information is used internally by us and is never given or sold to anyone.

Our site does not use cookies directly. But some of the outside services we employ on our website, such as our website traffic measurement tool ([www.webtrendslive.com](http://www.webtrendslive.com/)) and sponsors ([www.burstmedia.com](http://www.burstmedia.com/)), use cookies. If you have a question about cookies used by our outside services, please contact their web site directly.

This site contains links to other sites, including sponsors. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. You will need to check with these website directly to view their privacy policies.

Disputes arising in connection with the privacy statement will be resolved by our web site's customer service representative. Errors or wrongful actions arising in connection with our privacy policy can be remedied by contacting: [webmaster@sql-server-performance.com](mailto:webmaster@sql-server-performance.com).

#### E-Mail Newsletter

If you subscribe to our e-mail newsletter, we will not give or sell your e-mail address to anyone. To unsubscribe from our newsletter, see the instructions at the bottom of your most recent newsletter.
