# Sam Ash Music 1-800-4-SAMASH

We appreciate your visit to our website. We fully respect your right to privacy and the need for you to have control of your personal information. It is our goal to make this site function at a level that best serves the needs of our visitors.If you visit certain areas of this site you may be asked to provide personal information in order to receive a catalog, make a purchase, enter a contest, or subscribe to a newsletter.

We will use this information to keep you informed of information that is of interest to you. If you make a purchase online your credit card number will only be used to process your payment - not for any other reason whatsoever. If you have provided us with demographic information that information will not be shared with anyone else (except that information provided in applying for the Sam Ash Credit Card is subject to use by HRSI and subject to its privacy policy).

**We never sell or rent our email lists or mailing lists to anyone, for any reason, except other Sam Ash companies and successors and assigns.** Occasionally we may partner with one of our vendors to provide you with information about a new product, a special sale, a clinic or workshop. 

If for any reason you wish or stop receiving **postal** mail or change your address, please go [[here](https://web.archive.org/catalogsignup/)].

If you want to correct your mailing list information or inform us of a change of address for **email** , please go [[here](https://web.archive.org/profile/)].
