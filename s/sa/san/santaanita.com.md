# Privacy Policy - MI Developments

  | 

> This website is provided by MID, subject to the following privacy statement. By using this website, you agree to the privacy policy. MID may change this statement from time to time, and your continued use of this site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new privacy policy.
> 
> MID has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to preserving your privacy online. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: www.midevelopments.com.
> 
> ## Cookies
> 
> The MID site uses minimal cookies to deliver content specific to your interests. The cookie information does NOT include personal data such as name, age, phone number, e-mail address or mailing address. If you would prefer, you can set your browser to reject cookies.
> 
> ## Request Forms
> 
> The MID site information request form and/or email alerts requires users to give us contact information. Contact information from these forms is used to deliver information about our company as requested.
> 
> ## Collection of Information by Third Parties
> 
> This website may contain links to third party websites and portions of this website, including but not limited to the Investor Relations section, may be operated by others whose information practices may be different than MID’s. Visitors should consult the other sites’ privacy statements as MID may have not control over information that is submitted to, or collected by, these third parties.
