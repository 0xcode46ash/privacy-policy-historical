## Information We Collect and How We Use It

When a website visitor contacts SaaS _hr_.com for additional information related to SaaS _hr_.com products and/or services, he or she is requested to provide specific contact information. This contact information includes a contact name, email address, and phone number, which is used by SaaS _hr_.com to respond to the inquiry. 

Each user, including SaaS _hr_.com partners and their end-user client companies, where appropriate, who provided his or her contact information may opt-out of receiving specific mailings by sending an email request to be removed from future mailings. Unless otherwise agreed to by electronic signature, contact information gathered is not shared with outside organizations or sold to third parties. 

## Access to Personally Identifiable Information

SaaS _hr_.com partners and their end-user client companies are able to update their personally identifiable information. If SaaS _hr_.com partners and their end-user client companies request SaaS _hr_.com to delete their personal information, either by postal mail at the contact information listed below, or by using electronic signature, SaaS _hr_.com will do so provided that deletion of data will not prevent SaaS _hr_.com from complying with its legal obligations. SaaS _hr_.com will respond to such requests within 30 days. 

SaaS _hr_.com will retain partner and/or end-user company information for as long as such partner account is active. SaaS _hr_.com will retain and use user information as necessary to comply with SaaS _hr_.com's legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce SaaS _hr_.com agreements. 

## Communications from the Site

SaaS _hr_.com will occasionally send users information on its products, services, special deals, promotions, or industry research information. Out of respect for user privacy, SaaS _hr_.com presents users with the option to not receive these types of communications by requesting so in writing, by electronic signature, or by using the unsubscribe mechanisms included in promotional emails sent by SaaS _hr_.com. 

If a partner, eligible employee, or representative wishes to subscribe to SaaS _hr_.com newsletter(s), SaaS _hr_.com will use the name and email address provided by such party to send the requested material. If a partner, eligible employee, or representative wishes to unsubscribe from SaaS _hr_.com newsletters, such party may request to do so in writing, by electronic signature, or by using the unsubscribe mechanisms included in all promotional emails sent by SaaS _hr_.com. 

## Legal Disclaimer

SaaS _hr_.com reserves the right to disclose user personally identifiable information as required by law and when SaaS _hr_.com believes that disclosure is necessary, in order to protect its rights and/or to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on SaaS _hr_.com website. 

## Business Transitions

In the event SaaS _hr_.com goes through a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition by another company, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, users' personally identifiable information might be among the assets transferred. Users who are on the current list of recipients of communications referenced earlier will be notified via e-mail or prominent notice on the SaaS _hr_.com website for 30 days of any such change in ownership or control of users personal information. 

## Security

The security of user personal information is important to SaaS _hr_.com. SaaS _hr_.com follows generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to SaaS _hr_.com, both during transmission and once the company receives it. However, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, SaaS _hr_.com cannot guarantee its absolute security. 

## Cookies

A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user's computer for record-keeping purposes. SaaS _hr_.com uses cookies on its website. SaaS _hr_.com does not link the information users submit while on the SaaS _hr_.com site stored in cookies to any personally identifiable information. 

SaaS _hr_.com uses session ID cookies to enable session tracking for SaaS _hr_.com customers who login using its website. A session ID cookie expires when a user closes their browser. SaaS _hr_.com does not use cookies to monitor a user navigating the website outside of its business applications. 

If a user rejects cookies, the user may still use SaaS _hr_.com's web site, but the user's ability to login and use the SaaS _hr_.com business application may be limited. 

## Log Files

As is true of most websites, SaaS _hr_.com gathers certain information automatically and stores it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser types, internet service providers (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating systems, date/time stamp, and click stream data. 

SaaS _hr_.com uses this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze web site visitor trends and to track users' movements on the outside-facing website. 

## Links to Other Sites

This website contains links to other sites and or other online services that are not owned or controlled by SaaS _hr_.com. Website visitors are advised that SaaS _hr_.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. 

SaaS _hr_.com encourages each website visitor to be aware when leaving the SaaS _hr_.com site, and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. 

This privacy statement applies only to information collected by SaaS _hr_.com. 

## Customer Testimonials

SaaS _hr_.com posts customer testimonials on the SaaS _hr_.com website, which may contain personally identifiable information. SaaS _hr_.com does obtain each customer's consent via an electronically signed or digitally scanned consent form prior to posting the customer's name along with their testimonial. If a customer wishes to have his or her testimonial changed or removed, the customer may do so in writing. 

## Changes in this Privacy Statement

SaaS _hr_.com may update this privacy statement to reflect changes to its information practices. If SaaS _hr_.com makes any material changes, it will notify its partners and their end-user client companies, where applicable, by email (sent to the e-mail address specified in users' accounts) or by means of a notice on SaaS _hr_.com's website prior to the change becoming effective. SaaS _hr_.com encourages all users to periodically review this page for the latest information on SaaS _hr_.com's privacy practices.
