# Sax on the Web > Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy

### Copyright

We respect your privacy! As a rule no visitor information is collected by the   _[Sax on the Web](http://www.saxontheweb.net/index.html)_   service. Any and all information you will volunteer at this site will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to the _Sax on the Web_ pages will be held with care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. Please see our [server provider's](https://www.cari.net/support-center/legal-and-policies/privacy.html) privacy policy. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to [contact us](http://www.saxontheweb.net/Resources/contact.html).

### Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:

Sax on the Web is a free service for saxophone players and devotees. Several individuals contribute to this service knowing that we do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site.

### Cookies

This website uses cookies to ensure you get the best experience on our website.
