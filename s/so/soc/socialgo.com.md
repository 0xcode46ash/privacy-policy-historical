# SocialGO - Privacy Policy

**General Information**  
SocialGO LTD takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following information to learn more about our privacy practices.  
This policy covers what personally identifiable information SocialGO LTD collects and how it is treated. Personally identifiable information is information that can be used to identify visitors to the http://www.socialgo.com Web Site. This information may be limited to an IP address or extend to personally identifiable information such as your name, address, email address, or phone number. 

This policy only applies to http://www.socialgo.com and does not extend to the practices of other Web Sites that we may link to, companies that we do not own or control, or to people that we do not employ or manage.

**Information Collection**

Web Site and System Administration  
Research and Development

We keep records of all of our subscribers for billing and service maintenance reasons. 

Our Web Site generates server log files automatically. These log files are used to generate statistical information and error reports to ensure the Web Site runs with a minimum of disruption. They are not actively used to identify individual visitors.

**Information Recipients - Who has access to the information collected?**

Ourselves and/or our entities acting as our agents or entities for which we are acting as an agent. 

**Information Retention - How long is this information retained?**

Information entered into social networks will remain during the life of the network. If a network is cancelled or deleted then the data will remain on our servers for a maximum of 12 months during normal backup cleanups.
