# soccerstats.com - Privacy Policy

    |  |    |    
  


When you visit this website, we do not collect any personally identifiable information about you such as your name, address, phone number, or e-mail address. We use third-party statistical services to gather statistics on the use of the website. Such services collect some types of non-personally identifiable information such as the Internet Service Provider you use to connect to the Internet, the IP address accessing the website, your browser type, the type of computer operating system you use, or the screen resolution that you use. Such information is used for the purpose of improving the users' browsing experience on our website, and to analyse trends by aggregating statistical site usage data. We may also collect data sent to us by e-mail or through the contact form on the Website, including the IP address from which a message was sent via the contact form.   
You acknowledge and agree that we may preserve and may also disclose information collected if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that any such preservation or disclosure is reasonably necessary to comply with legal process, enforce the terms and conditions of use, respond to claims that any content violates the rights of third-parties, or protect the rights, property, or personal safety of other users or the public.   
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit the Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites through the use of cookies in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [advertising opt-out page](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).   
Cookies may also be used by third-party statistical services to track visitor activity on the website and update traffic statistics. This information is collected solely to improve the service and statistical purposes and is not used to identify individuals. One way you can stop all or some cookies from being stored on your computer, and therefore, stop Web sites and advertisers from storing and using certain non-personally identifiable information collected about your visits, is to configure the cookie blocking options in your internet browser configuration. Most web browsers (such as Internet Explorer and Mozilla Firefox) allow to configure settings to disable all cookies or to enable or disable them one by one. For more information please consult the user manual or help file for your particular web browser.
