# SoccerSTATS.com - Privacy Policy

When you connect to this Website, we do not collect any personal information about you such as your name, address, phone number, or e-mail address. However, we may or may not obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information such as the Internet Service Provider you use to connect to the Internet, the IP address accessing the website, your browser type, the type of computer operating system you use, or the screen resolution that you use. Such information is used for the purpose of improving your browsing experience on our website, and to aggregate statistical site usage data. We may also collect data sent to us by e-mail or through the contact form on the Website, including the IP address from which a message was sent via the contact form.   
You acknowledge and agree that we may preserve and may also disclose information collected if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that any such preservation or disclosure is reasonably necessary to comply with legal process, enforce the terms and conditions of use, respond to claims that any Content violates the rights of third-parties, or protect the rights, property, or personal safety of other users or the public. We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit the Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites through the use of cookies in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. 

Blocking cookies:   
One way you can stop cookies from being stored on your computer, and therefore, stop Web sites and advertisers from storing and using certain non-personally identifiable information collected about you, is to block all cookies by disabling cookies in your Internet browser configuration. This setting is meant to block all cookies from any website. Depending on your Internet browser, you can block all cookies in the following way: 

Microsoft Internet Explorer 6.x   
1\. Menu: Tools-Internet Options   
2\. Select Tab: Privacy   
3\. Pull "slider" to "Block All Cookies," which is the highest setting 

Netscape Navigator 4.x   
1\. Menu: Edit-Preferences   
2\. Select Category: Advanced   
3\. Find section: Cookies   
4\. Select your choice
