# PRIVACY POLICY | Social Lead Freak

**We Respect Your Privacy**

We respect your privacy. In order to better protect your privacy, this notice has been posted to explain our online information practices and what you can do about the methods in which your personal information may be collected and used. We have done our best to make this information as easy for you (the site visitor) as possible. It is located in the right sidebar of the homepage, as well as every other page on this site.

**Collection of Personal Information**

We will not share your personal information with anybody.

**Links to Third Party Websites**

Some links to other sites have been included on this site for your use and reference. We are not responsible for the privacy policies of the websites linked to. Please be aware that any privacy policies of sites other than this one may differ from ours considerably.

**Amendments to this Privacy Policy**

This privacy policy may be altered at any time, at our discretion without notice.
