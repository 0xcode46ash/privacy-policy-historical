# Privacy Notice : Sony Middle East & Africa

**Sony Gulf FZE - Privacy Notice**

Sony Gulf FZE knows that you care about how information about you is used and shared and we appreciate the trust you have placed in us. This notice describes our privacy policy. By submitting information about yourself, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy notice.

Your personal details are being collected by Sony Gulf FZE No other use of the information will be made without your prior consent.

For the purposes described above, Sony Gulf FZE may share the information with the various departments in our company and / or any associated company in the Sony Group either in United Arab Emirates or globally. The information may also be shared with our distributors and advertising agencies. Transfer of information will only be done to those companies, with whom Sony Gulf has an agreement that controls usage of information in a manner similar to the conditions mentioned in this privacy notice. Sony Gulf FZE will be obliged to release personal information, when required by law. No other disclosure of your information will be made without your prior consent.

If you are under 13 years of age, you may submit your personal details only with involvement of a parent or guardian.

If you would like to review, update, modify or delete your personal information; limit or revoke any consent previously given or stop receiving any communication from Sony Gulf FZE, please inform us by sending an e-mail to Sacdhelpdesk.Sogul@ap.sony.com.

Sony Gulf FZE will delete in an un reproductive manner any personal information that is no longer necessary or upon a deletion advise by you.
