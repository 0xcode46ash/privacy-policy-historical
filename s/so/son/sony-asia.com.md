# .:Sony Asia Pacific:.

This web site ("Web Site") is managed by Sony Electronics Asia Pacific Pte. Ltd. For each visitor to this Web Site, our Web server automatically recognizes and stores the visitor's IP address/Domain name combination. Your computer provides this information automatically each time you log on, unless you have activated blocking technology available in some browsers. We also may combine your IP Address/Domain name combination with other information which you provide us. You provide this information when, for example, you send us an e-mail, fill out a registration or the feedback form, request for customer support or answer a survey. 

  
Data collected during your visit to the Web Site may be used for (1) providing customer support for Sony products, (2) providing you with information on our latest software downloads & updates to our Web Site, (3) reviewing to improve the Web Site contents or layout, (4) conducting surveys, (5) marketing activities related to Sony products and services. 

For the purposes described above, our company may share the information with any associated company in the Sony group either in Singapore or overseas. The information may be shared with third parties we deem appropriate to fulfil the above mentioned purposes. However, in such case, we will endeavor to ensure that the recipients do not use it for any purpose other than the limited purpose determined by us within the scope of your consent and we will endeavor to ensure that they secure the information from unauthorized use. The information may only be disclosed to other third parties in exceptional cases required by laws or regulatory authorities. 

If you would like to inspect, correct, update or delete the information stored in our database, or if you have any question or comment about our privacy policies, please contact us by sending an e-mail via the [feedback form](http://www.css.ap.sony.com/feedback/).

By submitting your information to us, you are consenting to the uses we have described above. If you do not consent to these uses, we suggest you opt out where available, activate blocking technology if available in your browser, or choose not to submit your data or inquiry through the [feedback form](http://www.css.ap.sony.com/feedback/).
