# SOAP OPERA DIGEST

**Soapdigest.com Privacy Policy**

Your privacy on the Internet is of the utmost importance to us. Because we gather certain types of information about the users of Soapdigest.com, we feel you should fully understand the terms and conditions surrounding the capture and use of the information. This privacy statement discloses what information we gather, how we use it, and how to correct or change it. We reserve the right to revise this privacy policy upon reasonable notice to our visitors. 

Questions regarding this statement should be directed to Soapdigest.com at [soapdigest@primediamags.com](mailto:jason_bonderoff@primediamags.com) for clarification. 

**Frequently Asked Questions:**   
1) Gathering Information   
2) Use of the Information   
3) Sharing of the Information   
4) Opt-out Policy   
5) Other Privacy Issues & Concerns 

> **1) Gathering Information:**   
>  Soapdigest.com gathers information about users through optional, voluntary participation in message boards, as well as certain contests and sweepstakes. This information enables Soapdigest.com to better tailor our content to our visitors. 
> 
> In addition, Soapdigest.com also gathers information from consumers who subscribe to Soap Opera Digest on-line and purchase goods from the Soap Opera Digest Store. 
> 
> This site, Soapdigest, uses the services of Engage for the serving and/or targeting of ads, promotions and other marketing messages. To do this, Engage collects anonymous data through the use of cookies. To learn more about Engage, including your ability to opt out of the Engage system, go to <http://www.engage.com/privacy>. 
> 
> **2) Use of the Information:**   
>  Soapdigest.com may perform statistical analyses of user behavior in order to measure relative consumer interest on various services (for product development purposes) and to inform advertisers as to how many consumers have responded to an advertisement and seen or "clicked" on their advertising banner. In performance of these statistical analyses, we will disclose information to third parties only in aggregate form. Personal information on individual subscribers and advertisers will not be provided to any third party except regarding the fulfillment of magazine subscriptions or good purchased from the Soap Opera Digest Store. 
> 
> Soapdigest.com may send out emails to users that have opted for an online service to announce special offers, services and announcements from our site and/or advertisers. 
> 
> **3) Sharing of the Information:**   
>  Soapdigest.com shares the information it tracks, in aggregate form, with advertisers and other partners. We do not release information about any individual subscriber to third parties except to the extent necessary to fulfill magazine subscriptions or goods purchased from the Soap Opera Digest Store. 
> 
> Soapdigest.com occasionally makes the list of postal addresses volunteered on its Web site via email registration, contests, sweepstakes and surveys available to other carefully screened associations or companies whose products or services we think you may find of interest. We pre-screen all use of our information for our customers. 
> 
> **4) Opt-out Policy:**   
>  Soapdigest.com gives users options whenever necessary. Such choices include:   
> 
> 
> \-- Opting not to receive electronic newsletters when registering for a site.   
>  \-- Opting not to participate in certain interactive areas, which completely alleviates the need for any information from our users.   
>  \-- Opting out of Adsmart cookies, which improve the quality of your visit to the site, will erase the unique user ID associated with your current Adsmart cookie.   
>  \-- Adsmart makes available both its policies and information practices at adsmart.net and has voluntarily opened its practices and systems for third party auditing. 
> 
> **5) Other Privacy Issues & Concerns:**   
>  Soapdigest.com is not responsible for the content or privacy policies of Web sites to which it may link. Please keep in mind that whenever you voluntarily disclose personal information online -- for example, on message boards, through email or in chat areas -- that information can be collected and used by others. In short, if you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return. Furthermore, while we strive to protect your personal information, Soapdigest.com cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us, and you do so at your own risk! You are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your password and/or any account information. Please be careful and responsible whenever you're online.
