# Privacy Policy

Softsia.com has created this privacy policy in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.

You can use Softsia.com without revealing any personal information. When you request pages from softsia.com, our server may recognize the browser's domain name and IP address. We collect the domain names and IP addresses in order to analyze trends, administer the site, and track user's movement for aggregate use. This information is used solely for statistical purposes and not to identify individuals. 

## Third-Party Advertising

We use third-party advertisers to serve ads when you visit our website. Our third-party advertiser may places or recognizes a unique "cookie" on your browser. However, we have no access to or control over these cookies. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. 

## Software Submission

All software programs are submitted by the author, if specific submission violates the copyright, please contact us and we will remove it as soon as possible. We do not sell, share, or rent submitted information to other companies or individuals. 

## Links

This site contains links to other sites. Softsia.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. Each user is responsible to virus check any program downloaded from the site before installation. Softsia.com is not responsible for any loss or damage resulting from users' downloading or use of any such programs or files.

If you have any additional questions, please feel free to [contact us](http://www.softsia.com/contact.php)
