# SolarCity - Privacy

**SolarCity is committed to protecting your privacy.**  
When you browse the SolarCity site, we may collect data about your visit in the form of "cookie" technology. This technology does not identify you as such, but rather aggregates visitor information to help us track traffic patterns and web server information.

We only collect personal information when you provide it to us by filling in an online form or registering for an event. We may use the personal data you provide to seek your feedback or to send to you the information you have requested. We do not share any personal information we collect without your permission, nor do we sell or rent lists of any type.

If you wish to change or delete any of the personal information you have entered into any of our online forms, or if you have additional questions about our privacy statement, please contact us at: [privacypolicy@solarcity.com](mailto:privacypolicy@solarcity.com). 

To UNSUBSCRIBE from receiving emails, please send us an email at [unsubscribe@solarcity.com](mailto:unsubscribe@solarcity.com?subject=Unsubscribe). Requests to unsubscribe from SolarCity e-mails may take 10 business days to process.
