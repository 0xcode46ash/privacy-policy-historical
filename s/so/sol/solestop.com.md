# Solestop! Buy Brand Name Shoes Online!

We do require certain personal information and shipping address information for the processing of orders. Solestop.com respects your privacy. Solestop.com does not sell, rent or loan any personal information that is identifiable to any unrelated third parties. Any personal information given to us will be held with the utmost care and security and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented. 

Your identity and personal information will be kept anonymous unless used internally. 

* * *

**How Do I Know My Credit Card and Personal Information is Secure?**

All personal information and credit card information in the order process is completed using a secure server. Solestop.com makes use of Secure Socket Layers (SSL) to encrypt all data provided by the customer. We take all necessary precautions, given today's technology, to ensure your personal and credit card information is kept safe and private. 

**Does Solestop.com use 'Cookies'?**

When you shop at Solestop.com, and depending on how you have your browser configured, a small file called a 'cookie' may be stored on your computer. This 'cookie' essentially contains a unique personal identification code that is used in order to save your Shopping Cart and review its contents the next time you bring it up. If you have 'cookies' turned off within your browser settings, your Shopping Cart contents will not be remembered each time you visit the site. 

**Will Personal Information Be Disclosed To Any Other Parties?**

Under current Solestop.com policy, no personal information is currently sold or made available to any other person or party outside of the Supenrikeman.com team. However, this policy may change in the future. If this policy does change, Solestop.com will announce any plans to do so and will ask you if you choose to opt in. We will make the assumption that you do not want to opt in, and if you do you would have to respond to our request. If no response is made, none of your personal information will be shared with any other person or party other than the Solestop.com team. 

Solestop.com reserves the right to cooperate with any local, provincial or federal officials in any investigation where personal information of customers or users is required. As well, we will provide personal information of customers and users for any reports about lawful or unlawful users' activities that may be required by any authoritative body. We reserve the right to change, modify and update this Privacy Policy at any point in time. 

We reserve the right to disclose any personal information in full or in part in order to satisfy any law, regulation or government request.
