# Solmar Hotels & Resorts privacy policy

We will not sell or rent your information to third parties for marketing purposes and will only disclose your information in accordance to our privacy policy/and or with your permission. Your information will only be shared with third parties for services or features offered on [www.solmar.com](http://www.solmar.com/) that you have selected to use and only when absolutely necessary and under confidential restrictions.

We use safe, secure technology and other privacy protection programs to keep your information secure.

Personally identifiable information is for most part only shared with third parties in order to complete a transaction or activity you initiated on our sites.

All information you provide us is held in strict confidence. Privacy policies contained herein apply to services available under the domain and sub-domains of [www.solmar.com](http://www.solmar.com/)
