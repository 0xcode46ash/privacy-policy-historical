SOLAR IMPULSE SA
EPFL Scientific Park
CH-1015 Lausanne
Switzerland
Introduction
Solar Impulse takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following Privacy Policy carefully
to learn more about Solar Impulse's use of your personal identifying information that Solar
Impulse collects when you visit Solar Impulse's website (hereinafter the "Website"). Solar
Impulse reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time without notice to you.
You are therefore responsible for regularly reviewing this Privacy Policy.
This Privacy Policy covers Solar Impulse's treatment of personal identifying information that
Solar Impulse collects when you are on the Website and when you use services on the
Website, notably the services related to the "Supporters Program".
This Privacy Policy does not apply to the practices of any companies or legal entities that
Solar Impulse does not own or control or to any persons that Solar Impulse does not employ.
Information collection and use
Solar Impulse does not collect personal identifying information when you access the Website
homepage and browse the Website without disclosing your personal data (except cookies;
see below under "cookies").
Solar Impulse collects personal identifying information when you register on the Website,
when you use certain products or services on the Website.
When you register, Solar Impulse asks for your name, e-mail address, and some other
personal information. Once you register with Solar Impulse and/or sign in to our products or
services, you are not anonymous to Solar Impulse.
Solar Impulse also automatically receives and records information on its server logs from
your browser including your Internet Protocol address (IP address), which identifies your
computer, Solar Impulse's cookies information and the page you requested.
Solar Impulse uses the collected information for two main purposes: (i) for the functioning of
the "Supporters Program" and (ii) for the exchange of information within Solar Impulse.
Information sharing and disclosure
Solar Impulse will not sell, rent, or communicate by any other way, your personal identifying
information.
Subject to the exceptions below, Solar Impulse promises that it will not disclose your
personal identifying information to third parties.
                                                 1


Solar Impulse may send personal identifying information about you to third parties only
when:
(i)     Solar Impulse needs to share your information to provide the product or service you
have requested;
(ii)    Solar Impulse needs to send the information to third parties who work on behalf of
Solar Impulse to provide a product or service to you (these third parties do not have any
rights to use the personal identifying information Solar Impulse provides to them beyond
what is necessary to provide a product or service to you);
(iii)   Solar Impulse responds to courts or authorities; and
(iv)    Solar Impulse finds that your actions on the Website violate Solar Impulse Website
Terms and Conditions or any other Solar Impulse's guidelines or rules applicable for specific
services.
Cookies
Solar Impulse may set and access Solar Impulse's cookies on your computer. A cookie is a
small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to
your browser from a website's computer and stored on your computer's hard drive.
Solar Impulse uses its own cookies for number of purposes, mainly to:
(i)     access your information when you sign in, so that we can provide you with
customized content;
(ii)    estimate and report the total audience size and traffic of the Website; and
(iii)   conduct research and development to improve the Website contents and services
and achieve and maintain a high service level.
Children's privacy
Solar Impulse does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
Solar Impulse does not take specific steps to protect the privacy of children who disclose
their personal identifying information to Solar Impulse.
Access to your personal identifying information
Solar Impulse gives you the ability to modify or to consult the personal identifying information
of your profile by sending us an e-mail including in the title of the e-mail the reference to
personal identifying information at supporters@solarimpulse.com.
Solar Impulse allows you to challenge the data that it holds about you and you may have
such data rectified, amended or completed.

Security
If you communicate with Solar Impulse by e-mail, you should note that the secrecy of
Internet e-mail is uncertain. By sending sensitive or confidential e-mail messages or
information which are not encrypted, you accept the risk of such uncertainty and possible
lack of confidentiality over the Internet.
In certain areas, Solar Impulse uses industry standard SSL encryption to protect data
transmissions.
Privacy compliance
Solar Impulse is subject to Swiss data protection laws when collecting and processing
personal identifying information.
Questions or suggestions
If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please send us an e-
mail at contact@solarimpulse.com.
