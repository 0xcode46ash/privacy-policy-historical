# Solis Company Privacy Policy

_This site is hosted by Yahoo! Store. Yahoo! hosts the store pages, ordering system, and order data. Yahoo! automatically collects order information but may only use this information in the aggregate. If you accessed this store through Yahoo! Shopping, Yahoo! also automatically collects information about your shopping experience. Please see the[Yahoo! Privacy Policy](http://privacy.yahoo.com/privacy/us/) for more information about how Yahoo! uses this information._Privacy is taken very seriously at Solis Company! Our privacy policy is simple. We will not provide, rent, or make available in any way, our customer information to any parties outside of Solis Company. Period. All customer information is stored offline in an encrypted format in a secure data environment. No personally identifiable information is collected on visitors to our site.

Customer order information, i.e. Bill To and Ship To addresses, credit card information and phone numbers, are used to process the order only. No unsolicited mailings or phone calls are made. If you are uncomfortable providing your phone number, please enter 555-555-5555 in the phone number field.

Customer e-mail addresses are used to send shipment updates. 

Information you provide to Solis Company during the ordering process is never shared, sold or rented by us to others for solicitations of any kind... THAT'S IT !

Please contact Steve Haas at SRH70@HOME.COM with any questions or concerns.
