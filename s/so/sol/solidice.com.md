# SolidICE

SolidICE takes the protection of the personal information of its visitors and registered users seriously.

SolidICE will take all necessary precautions to prevent the unauthorized access to any and all personal information collected from visitors and registered users.

Information which may be collected from visitors and registered users includes but may not be limited to:  


  * The ip address of a user.
  * Information collected by Google Analytics.
