We are commited to keep all information of our visitors private. All informations are gathered in the log files of the server tracking the IP Address. This website takes every possible precautions to protect our visitors information. All personal information of the visitors are protected both online and offline.

When an information is providing by the visitors we keep those information private and only use them for our business to improve our services and products. You can contact us using the form providing and those information are only used for our internal purpose or for further communication with the visitors.

The information posted by visitors on the website such as Name, Email Id, Address, Phone No etc are kept personal within our company. These information can only be given out to third parties only in certain circumstance such as : 

  * Comply with the law or with legal process  

  * Protect and defend our rights and property  

  * Protect our members or registrants from misuse or unauthorized use solutionsinfini.com site or  

  * Protect the personal safety or property of our members, registrants, or the public.  

  * When customer have given us permission we may make your e-mail address available to third parties and to our employee for communcation purpose.
