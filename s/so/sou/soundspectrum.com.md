# Terms of Use

  
     Protecting your privacy is important to SoundSpectrum, Inc. (SoundSpectrum). We are committed to keeping your personal information confidential. If you use our services or download our software, please read this Privacy Policy to learn how we handle your personal information. 

Why collect information?   
SoundSpectrum collects and uses your personal information to provide you with easy access to our products and services, and to keep you up to date on the latest product announcements, software updates, special offers, and other information we think you'd like to hear about. This may occasionally include providing you with information from other technology companies about products and services that can add value to your SoundSpectrum products. From time to time, we may also use your personal information to contact you to participate in a market research survey, so that we can gauge customer satisfaction and develop better products.

SoundSpectrum uses industry-standard Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption on all web pages where personal information and payment information is required. To make purchases from SoundSpectrum, you must use an SSL-enabled browser such as Internet Explorer or Netscape Navigator. This protects the confidentiality of your personal and credit card information while it is transmitted over the Internet. 

The SoundSpectrum website may use "cookie" technologyto identify your browser with a unique, random number. The cookies we may use do not reveal any personal information about you. Cookies help us understand which parts of our website are the most popular, where our visitors are going, and how long they spend there. 

In some of our email to you, we may use a "click-through URL." When you click one of these URLs, you may pass through a web server before arriving at the website that is your destination. This allows us to track click-throughs to help us determine your interest in particular topics and to help us improve our customer communication. 

SoundSpectrum does not knowingly solicit personal information from children or send them requests for personal information. 

SoundSpectrum's website may contains links to other sites. SoundSpectrum does not share your personal information with those websites and is not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to learn about the privacy policies of those companies directly from their web sites. 

The SoundSpectrum Customer Privacy Policy is subject to change at any time. We encourage you to review the privacy policy regularly for any changes.
