# About SRS Solutions - SRS Solutions

## Introduction

By using the SRS website, you consent to the collection and use of information by SRS Solutions as specified below. If we change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. 

As with every service we provide, our goal is to provide you the most relevant and useful information possible. To help with that goal, we may collect and use information about how our customers use these communications. If we collect such information, we will use it only on an aggregate basis (i.e., not tracked to you), unless we have first notified you and obtained your consent. 

## Submitting Information

When you submit any of the web forms available on this website and the SRS network, you agree that any information including the contact information you enter and submit will be used by SRS Solutions' representatives to identify and contact you for the purpose of your inquiry. The information will under no circumstances be shared with third parties unless we have first notified you and obtained your consent. 

## Cookies

SRS Solutions may send a "cookie" to your computer. A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. A cookie does only store textual data and cannot be executed or run. Therefore, it cannot read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. SRS uses cookies to track which sections of the SRS Network you have visited and how you used them. You can choose whether to accept cookies or if you want to be notified when a cookie is sent by changing the settings of your browser. If you choose not to accept cookies, your experience at our website and other websites may be diminished and some features may not work as intended.
