# Privacy | SEIKO WATCH CORPORATION

Seiko Watch Corporation shall make every reasonable effort to protect personal information provided by users of this website ([www.seikowatches.com](http://www.seikowatches.com/)) and their privacy. 

Normal use of this website does not require users to provide personal information such as name and address. Certain pages on this website such as those for making inquiries to Seiko Watch Corporation, however, request limited personal information. In such case, please provide the necessary information.

Seiko Watch Corporation will not disclose any personal information to third parties without your consent. When, however, disclosure is required by law or when requested by a public agency such as a court or a police body, Seiko Watch Corporation may disclose personal information without your consent. 

Seiko Watch Corporation implements appropriate and reasonable levels of security to prevent risks such as unauthorized access to personal information as well as loss, tampering, or unauthorized disclosure of such personal information. In addition, access to databases containing personal information is limited to authorized persons within the company, and strict management and protection of personal information is implemented.
