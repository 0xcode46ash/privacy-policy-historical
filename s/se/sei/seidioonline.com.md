# Seidio, Inc.

Security remains the primary concern of on-line consumers. The e-commerce site of Seidio.com at http://www.seidioonline.com ,where you are currently visiting, has been certified for the implementation of the strongest 128-bit SSL and is a VeriSign Secure Site ensured by the VeriSign Secure Site Seal displayed, which is to make our customers feel comforrtable passing private, confidential and sensitive information and data (e.g. credit card numbers) to our site.

During the check out process for your online shopping, after you have saved your online order, you will be requested to provide your credit card information under an SSL session. All information sent to this site under such SSL session is 128- bit encrypted, protecting agasint disclosure to thirds parties. 

| 

Seidio.Com is an online shopping marketplace designed to deliver the best possible online shopping experience to you, our users. It is our policy to respect and protect the privacy of our customers and the users of this site. Seidio.Com is continually improving this site by adding new functionality and features. Because of these changes, this privacy statement is subject to change without notice. We encourage you to review these terms of use periodically for changes. If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, you may contact us at [customer@seidio.com](mailto:customer@seidio.com). 

| 

This site collects information from its users when the user registers or places an order on this site. If we have trouble processing an order, request, or completing a delivery, the representatives of Seidio.Com will use this information to contact the user. The purpose of your email address is for the use of sending you the status report on your order. Credit card information is used to bill you for products or services. We also collect your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website. Your IP address is used to assist us in identifying you and your shopping cart.
