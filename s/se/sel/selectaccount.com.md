# Privacy Statement

## For your protection

These notices describe how medical and financial information may be used and disclosed, and how you can get access to this information. Please review these notices carefully.

SelectAccount has always been committed to maintaining the security and confidentiality of the information we receive from our members. Whether it's your medical information, financial information or identifiable information (such as your name, address, phone number or member identification number), we maintain careful safeguards to protect you against unauthorized access and use. 

You should know that we are required by law to provide you these notices about our legal duties and privacy practices. We hope that these notices will clarify our responsibilities to you and provide you with a good understanding of your rights.
