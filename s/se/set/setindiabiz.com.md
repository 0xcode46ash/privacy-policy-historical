# Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy

“Setindiabiz.com”, (hereinafter referred to as the ‘website’) is sensitive to privacy issues on the Internet. We maintain the best standards and market norms in our customer’s information privacy, and in relation to secure transactions on Setindiabiz.com. We believe it is important for our customers to know, how we collect, use and disclose Personal Identifiable Information, hereinafter referred to as ‘PII’ which we receive from them, through the internet. This Privacy Policy does not address the collection, use or disclosure of information by any of our affiliates and/or by third parties. 

By using the Site, the User hereby consents, expresses and agrees that he has read and fully understands the Privacy Policy of “setindiabiz.com” as stated on the website. The users further consents that the terms and contents of the said Privacy Policy are acceptable to them, and are binding as per the provisions of the Information Technology (Intermediaries guidelines) Rules, 2011, duly formulated under the Information Technology Act, 2000. If you do not agree to the terms and conditions of the said Privacy Policy, please do not use, access the Site, in any manner whatsoever.

In this Privacy Policy, references to “You”, “User”, shall mean the end user accessing the Website, its contents and using the Services offered by the setindiabiz.com, through any of means, which shall include, website, telephone calls and emails. Further, “setindiabiz”, “website”, “we”, “our”, “us” shall mean and include, ‘setindiabiz.com’, of which the users are willing to avail the services.

## What type of information we collect from Users? What is PII?

Personal Identifiable Information, also “PII”, is information that identifies the user as an individual, and is used by us or our affiliates, so as to reach you, via electronic or other means. In order to avail the services provided through our site, such as, to provide you information on the services we offer through our site, to see the available listings, access blogs etc, we require the users to furnish PII. We shall collect:

  * Profile Information, i.e., data which is used to contact the users, such as, complete name, telephone/mobile number and e-mail address and postal address.
  * Financial Information i.e., credit card details, including validation code, other payment account number and passwords.
  * Demographic Information, i.e., hometown, zip codes, age, gender, history of services availed.
  * Public Information, includes, User ratings, reviews, user name.



Our web-servers may track the non – personal information about the visitors, which shall include, but not limited to, your computer type, OS version, Internet browser, domain name, webpage and/or complete URL from which users’ has entered our sites. This information is accumulated to measure the number of visits, average time spent on the site, pages viewed, etc. Setindiabiz uses this information to measure the use of our site, and permit us to improve our services and thus, we could serve in a better manner.

We collect personal information only when users voluntarily provide it to us and/or when you intent to see or use our services or want to learn more about them. Usually, such personal information collected by us, is used to respond to your inquiry, process an order or allow users to access specific account information.

## Use of Information

All information shared with us on our website, is available only with “setindiabiz”. We do not share personal information of our users with any Third Party, except on a confidential basis to its agents, consultants, associates, representatives, employees, in cases wherein they need to know to complete the task assigned to them and who are bound to preserve the confidentiality thereof, to any person outside its organization, any personal information.

We use personal information to customize the service to you. In addition to this, we use the information to promote services, to inform you about our products, and services, that we believe may be of your interest, and for product improvement purposes. We shall also use the information; to diagnose problems as advised at time of information collection; to solve issues for our safety purposes and to protect us against error, fraud, and crimes; and to manage the terms and conditions of our website.

Further, to send you important information, with respect to changes to our terms, conditions, and policies and/or other administrative information. We shall also use your personal information, for our internal purposes, such as data analysis, audits, developing new products, improving our website, enhancing our services, recognizing usage trends and determining the effectiveness of our promotional campaigns.

We may share this information with other corporate affiliates and entities for the same reasons mentioned above. But, the information shall not be sold to them for consideration to third parties.

At times, we shall conduct online surveys for better understanding the needs and profiles of our visitors. When we conduct a survey, we shall try, but are not obligated, to let you aware of, how such information is used.

You recognize and understand that there is no obligation on you to provide your personal information to us, and any and all personal information provided by you to us, is with your full consent, own will and desire to make available such personal information. You shall also understand that we are under no compulsion to authenticate the source from which your personal information is provided to us, and such information shall be deemed to be provided by you, until and unless you demonstrate us within a period of fifteen days from the date of providing of such information to us, to our satisfaction, that such information was provided to us without your free consent.
