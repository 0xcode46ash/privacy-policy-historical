## Search Engine Marketing Group Privacy Policy

Search engine marketing group is committed to protecting your privacy.

We will only collect the minimum amount of information that is required to provide satisfactory service to our clients. 

  * I **nformation collected:** the information we collect may include but is not limited to your name, address, telephone number, fax number, email address credit card number(s) as well as general information about your business and website. Search Engine Marketing Group will not disclose or sell your information to any third party. We value your business and are committed to protecting your privacy. 

  * **Use of cookies:** Your browser has a built in facility for storing small files called “cookies” our website uses cookies specifically for tracking statistical data. These files are completely harmless and will not adversely affect your computer in any way. However you can disable cookies as a optional browser setting but doing so may limit the use of many websites you visit. 

  * **Links:** Search Engine Marketing Group may link to various sites of relative content from time to time. Search Engine Marketing Group does not control the content of any third party site that we link to. We may do this as a convenience but this does imply our support or endorsement of any third party content/website.
