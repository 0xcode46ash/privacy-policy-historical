The following discloses is our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website. This policy may change from time to time, so please check back periodically. 

semmo.NET™ does not collect information form our visitors who use our services. We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this visit Google's privacy page.

In short, if you disclose personal information online that is accessible to others, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return. 

Ultimately, you are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your passwords and any information about yourself. Please be careful and responsible whenever you are online.
