# Learn About and Get in Contact with Us

See My Personality offers a number of free online tools for both entertainment and education. All tools and tests are provided 100% free of charge. We treat the information we collect very carefully, please see our security and privacy policy for further information on this. We're constantly updating our tools and tests to reflect new findings in relevant research. If you have a test or research you think compliments please get in touch with us using our contact form below. 

## Privacy Policy

See My Personality is a not for profit organization which exists to further general knowledge in the field of psychometrics as well as serve as a portal for entertainment which is also enlightening. We do not and will never sell information you provide to any third parties. We do not contact users unless we have requested permission to do so via email. 

We do collect visitors statistics such as referral data and content usage in order to optimize our content. This data is anonymous meaning no personal data is stored. Once again we will never sell this data to third parties. 

### Security

See My Personality is dedicated to the security of information gathered on our website. We conduct regular security reviews and ensure our software is running the most secure and stable versions of any security updates.

## Refund Policy

If you are at all dissatisfied with any of our services, please use the form above to contact us within 7 days of purchase for a full refund.

## Delivery Policy

All products are digital and are delivered immediately upon purchase - they are also emailed to you for later use.
