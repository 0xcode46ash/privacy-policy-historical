# SecurePCCleaner - Privacy Policy

  * The use of “cookies”

At LocusSoftware Inc., your right to privacy is one of our primary concerns. This Privacy Policy describes the guidelines LocusSoftware Inc. uses to protect the information we collect about you during your visit to our Web sites.

### Information we collect

SecurePCCleaner requests personal information at different times, for instance, while you are visiting the site, or during a purchase via our Web site, phone or fax. The information we collect may include your name, address, telephone number, email address, credit card information, and personal interests. In addition, LocusSoftware Inc. periodically sponsors contests and other promotions that may require additional information in order to participate.

### What we do with this information

The information collected allows SecurePCCleaner, to provide services you have requested or to enhance your experience while visiting us. The information we collect during a purchase is necessary for us to fulfil your order, track its status, and provide the best customer service possible.

SecurePCCleaner, will not sell your private information to any outside company for its use in marketing or solicitation. We may periodically use the information provided to us to notify you of important announcements regarding special offers or important functionality changes to our Web site.

SecurePCCleaner, may also use customer information to create statistics to better understand our users and to improve our services. These statistics may also be disclosed to potential advertisers and/or business partners. Such statistic data are always aggregate and never include details with names or other private information unless we obtained a person’s permission beforehand.

### The use of "cookies"

The cookies we use to track or maintain specific information about our customers are absolutely friendly, and are required to find out some of your preferences, allow you to log in, check your order status, etc. while your visit our Web site, or to provide better services in general.

### Your consent

By visiting and purchasing products via SecurePCCleaner,Web site, you authorize LocusSoftware Inc. to collect and use the information described above. We may periodically modify our Privacy Policy and will post any changes on this page so you can always be up-to-date on the information we collect and how we use it.
