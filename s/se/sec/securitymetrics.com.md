# SecurityMetrics - Privacy Policy

SecurityMetrics, Inc. is aware of the privacy concerns of its customers. Our policy for collecting and using personal information is detailed below.

**PRIVACY POLICY**  
SecurityMetrics, Inc. policy in relation to information collected through registration, testing, and/or any other means is to respect and protect the privacy and confidentiality of our users. SecurityMetrics, Inc. does not disclose, rent, or sell email addresses, security test results, or any other information that we may receive to any third party, unless specifically requested by the customer.

Customers may request removal of all records from our database including registration records and test results, and receive confirmation by email that the data has been removed from our systems.

**CHANGES**  
We reserve the right to make changes to this policy at any time without notice. If the change involves a reduction in privacy of any kind, a notice will be sent to all affected parties. Since the inception of SecurityMetrics, Inc. and the first version of this privacy policy, no such change has been made.

Last revised: 3 May 2001
