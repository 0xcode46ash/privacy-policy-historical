# Secure Pay | Disclaimer

SecurePay is committed to respecting your privacy and the security of your personal information.  


We keep all such information confidential but cannot be responsible for the remote risks of interception over the Internet or for information illegally obtained by hackers or otherwise. 

On rare occasions, we may disclose specific information upon governmental request, in response to an administrative or court order, or as otherwise required by law. 

We may also share information with companies assisting in fraud protection or investigation.   


We do not provide information to these agencies or companies for marketing or commercial purposes. 

This statement is not intended to and does not create any contractual or other legal right in or on behalf of any party.
