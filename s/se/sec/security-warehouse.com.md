# Privacy Notice : Zen Cart!, The Art of E-commerce

  
Best-China-Security-Supplies.com recognises that privacy is important. This document outlines the types of personal information we receive and collect when you use Best-China-Security-Supplies.com services, as well as some of the steps we take to safeguard information. We hope this will help you make an informed decision about sharing personal information with us.

 **Scope**

This notice confirms the Best-China-Security-Supplies.com Privacy Policy, which describes in detail the privacy practices that apply to Best-China-Security-Supplies.com products, services and websites worldwide. 

**Personal information and other data we collect**

  * Best-China-Security-Supplies.com collects personal information when you register for Best-China-Security-Supplies.com service or otherwise voluntarily provide such information. 
  * We may combine personal information collected from you with information or third parties to provide a better user experience, including customising content for you. 
  * Best-China-Security-Supplies.com uses web server, cookies and other technologies to enhance your online experience and to learn about how you use Best-China-Security-Supplies.com services in order to improve the quality of our services. 
  * Best-China-Security-Supplies.com servers automatically record information when you visit our website or use some of our products, including the URL, IP address, browser type and language, and the date and time of your request.   




**Uses  
**

  * We may use personal information to provide the services you've requested, including services that display customised content. 
  * We may also use personal information for auditing, research and analysis to operate and improve Best-China-Security-Supplies.com technologies and services. 
  * We WILL NOT share aggregated non-personal information with third parties outside of Best-China-Security-Supplies.com. 
  * We WILL NOT use third parties to assist us in processing your personal information. 



**Your choices**

  * We offer you choices when we ask for personal information, whenever reasonably possible. 
  * You may decline to provide personal information to us and/or refuse cookies in your browser, although some of our features or services may not function properly as a result. 
  * We make good faith efforts to provide you access to your personal information upon request and to let you correct such data if it is inaccurate and delete it, when reasonably possible. 



**More information**

For information about specific Best-China-Security-Supplies.com services, privacy practices or if you have general questions, please write to us at [support@best-china-security-supplies.com](mailto:support@best-china-security-supplies.com).
