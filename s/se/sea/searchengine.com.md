# Privacy Policy | BoxOnline is a Swiss based incubator for startup technology ventures

Your privacy is extremely important to us. Following is our policy regarding how we protect the privacy of the personal information you provide us. BoxOnline.com collects information when you register with BoxOnline.com. When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, post code or your address. BoxOnline.com automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, BoxOnline.com cookie information and another accessible information.

BoxOnline.com uses information for the following general purposes: improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

BoxOnline.com does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except under the following circumstances: -We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with BoxOnline.com under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your information to help BoxOnline.com communicate with you about offers from BoxOnline.com and our marketing partners. -We respond to court orders or legal process.

BoxOnline.com does not provide any individually identifiable information to any advertiser.

Cookies  
BoxOnline.com may set and access BoxOnline.com cookies on your computer. BoxOnline.com lets other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages and/or network to set and access their cookies on your computer. You may block cookies from within the preferences area of your browser however, doing so will limit your interaction with BoxOnline.com when cookies are required such as during the checkout process or an email opt-in signup.

Surveys  
From time to time, we or a company selected by us may offer you the opportunity to participate in surveys. You may be asked to provide membership information to participate. All surveys are optional.

Contests  
We may from time to time offer you the opportunity to participate in sweepstakes, product giveaways and similar services. You will be asked to provide membership information to participate in any sweepstakes or giveaways. We or a company selected by us administers the sweepstakes and contacts the winner. All sweepstakes are optional.

Advertising  
We use our own advertising as well as third party advertising agencies. We cannot be held responsible for anything contained within our partners, our members, or our advertisers sites.

Financial Information  
We use Paypal for all online transactions. BoxOnline has no access to your financial information nor do we store any financial information – Paypal is one of the most secure forms of online transaction available and is used for your protection. Please review [Paypal’s Privacy Policy](http://www.paypal.com/cgi-bin/webscr?cmd=p/gen/ua/policy_privacy-outside) prior to making a purchase should you have any concerns.

Analytics  
In addition to analysis of our server logs, we use two third party services for our web tracking and web analytics. We use Add Free Stats and Google Analytics. We also use our own server software to determine the accuracy of all third party information. Please review [Add Free Stats’ Policy](http://www.addfreestats.com/privacy.html) and the [Google Privacy Policy](http://www.google.com/intl/en/privacy.html) for specifics.

By transmitting any suggestions, comments, information, identification, material, or other content (“content”) to us, you automatically grant us the royalty-free, perpetual, irrevocable, non-exclusive right and license to use, reproduce, modify, adapt, publish, translate, create derivative works from, distribute, redistribute, transmit, perform and display such content (in whole or part) worldwide and/or to incorporate it in other works in any form, media, or technology now known or later developed for the full term of any rights that may exist in such content. Further, we are free to use any ideas, concepts, know-how, techniques, and suggestions contained in any communications you send to this Site for any purpose whatsoever, including but not limited to creating, manufacturing, and marketing products and/or services using such information.

General  
You can edit your BoxOnline.com account Information at any time. Communications from BoxOnline.com, such as service announcements, administrative messages and the BoxOnline.com Newsletter, are considered part of BoxOnline.com membership and you may not opt out of receiving them. We limit access to information about you to employees who need to come into contact with that information to provide services to you or in order to do their jobs. Your BoxOnline.com Account Information is password-protected.

Changes to this Privacy Policy  
BoxOnline.com may update Privacy Policy from time to time without notice to you. Please check these Privacy Policy periodically for changes. By using BoxOnline.com web site after changes have been made to Privacy Policy, you agree to the changes.
