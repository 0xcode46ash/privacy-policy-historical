# SearchEngineOptimising.com Submission Programs Privacy Policy from Babylon Technologies Ltd

SearchEngineOptimising.com is a trading style of Babylon Technologies Ltd. 

Introduction

This statement discloses the privacy practices for Babylon Technologies Ltd ("Babtech"), which owns and operates the following sites on the World Wide Web: [searchengineoptimising.com](http://www.searchengineoptimising.com/), [ineedhits.co.uk](http://www.ineedhits.co.uk/), [babtech.co.uk](http://www.babtech.co.uk/), [feelConfident.co.uk](http://www.feelconfident.co.uk/), [condomia.co.uk](http://www.condomia.co.uk/) and [condoms-direct.com](http://www.condoms-direct.com/). When signing up for any of our services, you will be asked to provide information about you. This information is collected to ensure that we receive payment for using our services and also so that we can communicate with you when necessary. It is extremely important that you understand that any information that identifies you will never be shared with anyone without your consent. Our privacy statement as set forth below is written so that you can understand exactly what we do and don't do with your information.

While we will never share information that may be used to identify you, we do expect our policy statement will change as new ways to protect your confidentiality and new uses for information emerge. To that end, we value your comments about our privacy statement and invite you to e-mail us with suggestions and concerns.  
Overview

Babylon Technologies Ltd (Babtech) is committed to safeguarding your privacy on-line. Babtech does not collect any information about you other than information which you provide when you sign up for services either from Babtech or from one of the members of Babtech's Partnership programs.

Babtech will use the information it collects to communicate with you and to administer to your account. At no time will Babtech disclose any information that it collects from you without your approval including Credit card information or other personal financial information that may be gathered by Babtech during the course of your account activity to any third party. The sole use of this information is for Babtech to receive payment for the services.

Babtech is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement.

Note that if you sign up for service from one of our Partnership programs, you hereby authorise and consent that we may share with the Partner the following information concerning you: e-mail address, name, title, entity, address and phone number (collectively "Contact Information"). No financial information will be shared. Further, all of our Partners have privacy policies that protect against the disclosure of personally identifiable parties to third persons. This Policy may change from time to time and any and all changes will be posted on our Web page in this section. Please read this policy carefully and review it periodically to keep informed of any changes.

What information is collected?

Only two types of information are collected: Contact Information and Financial Information. As stated above, the purpose of collecting this information is to allow Babtech to administer to your account and provide better service to you.  
Personal Financial Information

None of a user's personal financial information (such as a credit card number and expiration date) is ever distributed, sold or rented to any party for secondary purposes. The personal financial information of our Subscribers is used only for internal administrative purposes (i.e. appropriate billing).

Contact Information

We collect personal information that may include names, address, phone number, e-mail address, billing address, etc. This Contact Information is never sold, rented or distributed to third parties for secondary purposes and is used for administrative purposes (such as billing or contacting you regarding the services we provide). We will share Contact Information we collect only with those of our Partners with whom you have an account or whose services you have consented to use. We require that all our Partners have their own Privacy Policy in place.  


Where do we collect information from you?

We collect information from:  
Registration and Account Set Up

When you sign up for services from Babtech, you will be asked to provide certain basic Contact Information. After this is entered you, we will establish your account. You will be given the opportunity to provide additional Contact Information once your account is established. In addition, Financial Information will be collected to process payment for the services we provide.  
Customer Service

If you contact Babtech either via e-mail, telephone or letter, we may keep a record of the correspondence.

How is this information used?

The information we collect is used to contact you regarding our services and to ensure that we get paid for providing services to you.  
Who is collecting information?

Only Babtech is collecting information while you are on our Web sites or using our services.

Our Web sites may also provide links to third parties and their Web sites. We are not responsible for third party Web sites and how that Web site may handle the collection or distribution of information gathered at those sites. We recommend that you review the privacy policy of any Web site you decide to visit. We do not control, are not responsible for, nor are we recommending the usage of any other Web site. The links provided on our Web sites are there because a person or entity has paid or contracted for the right to have a link.

With whom is your information shared?

Generally, we do not share any information we collect. We will never share any Personal Financial Information, except in the very limited circumstances when we have your consent or when we are required to disclose pursuant to a court order or if requested by a law enforcement agency or other legal requirement. As stated above, we will share Contact Information with those of our Partners with whom you have an account or whose services you have consented to use.  
Does Babtech monitor e-mails or other communications?

We do not monitor in advance user's communications, e-mails or other content distributed through the Internet. By using our service you acknowledge that Babtech is unlikely to be aware of violations of law or other misconduct occurring over the Internet. As a user of our service, you bear full responsibility for the material received and sent via e-mail. We may have the capability to review these communications while in use and be required to disclose personal or other information when required by law, court order, governmental agency or law enforcement authority.

How may you update information?

We allow you to update all of your Contact Information simply e-mailing us at the following address: [support@searchengineoptimising.com](mailto:support@searchengineoptimising.com). You may only change your e-mail address by e-mailing us from the new location and verifying the change.  


Does Babtech use any filtering software?

No.  


Does Babtech allow users under the age of 18?

No. We believe that it is especially important to protect minor's privacy on line and encourages parents and guardians to spend time online with their children to participate and monitor their Internet activity. If you are under 18, we recommend that you get your parent's permission before disclosing any information about yourself. Babtech will not knowingly allow anyone under the age of 18 without the written permission from a parent or guardian.

The parent of a child under 18 can review and have deleted their child's account.  


Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. Once the user closes their browser, the cookie simply terminates. For instance, by setting a cookie on our site, the user would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our site. If a user rejects the cookie, they may still use our site. Cookies can also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our site.  


Referral program

We may offer a referral program in the future. If a user or subscriber elects to use our referral service for informing a friend about our site, we ask them for the friend's name and email address. We will automatically send the friend an email inviting them to visit the site. We store this information for the purpose of communicating our products and services. The referred individual may contact us at (e-mail address) or (telephone number) to request the removal of this information from our database.

Presently, we do not use cookies for administration of the site, such as remembering logins. None of our business partners use cookies on our site.

We may from time to time send e-mails to our Subscribers regarding our services. Also, we plan to develop a periodic electronic newsletter that we will send to all of our interested Subscribers. Out of respect for the privacy of our Subscribers, the receipt of such communications is handled on an op-in basis. Subscribers are given the opportunity to 'opt-in' to receiving these types of communications on our Web sites.

Subscribers should not share their password or access code with others or place that information where other may gain access. You are responsible for making sure that this information is kept confidential.

PLEASE NOTE: Internet data transmission is not always 100% secure and we cannot warrant that information you transmit utilising Babtech's services is always 100% secure. Because security is important to Babtech and our users, Babtech pledges to take all reasonable measures to ensure the security of our services and system.
