# Nowyo.com - Privacy

## What information do we collect?

When you use our website we collect certain information from you. If you register on our site, place an order, or if you fill out a form you may be asked to enter your personal information such as first and last name, phone number, address, email address, credit card information, etc. 

## What do we use your information for?

The information we collect from you is used to process transactions, to personalize your experience, to help us respond to your needs as an individual, to improve our website, to make future transactions quicker and save you time, and to help us improve our customer service. Your personal information will not be sold or given to any other company without your consent, other than for the normal business purposes of transferring a product such as a domain name or providing a service as you have requested. We will use your email address to send you news and updates pertaining to your order. If you decide to join our mailing list you will receive emails about discounted domains and other products and services. If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from future mailings we always include unsubscribe links at the bottom of every mailing. 

## Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell your personally identifiable information to any outside parties. We would only release your information if doing so would be mandatory to comply with a law or to enforce site policies to protect the rights of others or our own. If we decide to sell non-personally identifiable information to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes it will only be trends and statistical information as opposed to information that is identifiable to an individual. 

## Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.

## Your Consent

By using our site, you consent to our web site privacy policy. 

## Changes to our Privacy Policy

In the future if we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on our website on this page. We will also post the date of the most recent update. This policy was last modified on 4/13/2011.
