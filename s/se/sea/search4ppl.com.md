# Privacy policy - Search For People

The use of our website signifies that you understand our Privacy Policy. Please read it carefully. 

When you use [Search For People](http://www.search4ppl.com/) website for the first time or when you search for yourself you may find some information related to you that already exists. That information has been indexed from **public free sources** all-over the web. If you want to delete this information, contact directly us. You will always be able to delete this information from your public profile on Search For People. 

When you use Search For People, we or our partners send one or more **cookies (small file containing a string of characters)** , in order to uniquely identify a userâ€™s browser. We use cookies to improve the search results, store user preferences and track user trends. We may allow our business partners to use cookies on Search For People for the same purposes identified above. We may also use service providers acting on our behalf to use cookies for the purposes identified above. 

**Emails** : When you send email or other communication to [Search For People](http://www.search4ppl.com/), we may retain those communications in order to process your inquiries, respond to your requests and improve our services. 

**Analytics:** We use third party web-analytics providers to help us understand the use of our Services. Analytics collects the information sent by your browser as part of a web page request, including cookies and your IP address, that collect is made through third party services which also receives this information and their use of it is governed by their Privacy Policy. 

## Data security

We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. These include internal reviews of our data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures. 

We restrict access to personal information to [Search For People](http://www.search4ppl.com/) employees, contractors and agents who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve our services. These individuals are bound by confidentiality obligations and may be subject to discipline, including termination and criminal prosecution, if they fail to meet these obligations. 

## Third Party Resources

Current Policy only applies to the [Search For People](http://www.search4ppl.com/) website and services. We do not exercise control over the sites that our service use. These other sites may place their own cookies or other files on your computer, collect data or solicit personal information from you. We encourage you to read such privacy policies of any third-party sites. It is the sole responsibility of such third parties to adhere to any applicable restrictions on the disclosure of your personally identifying information, and [Search For People](http://www.search4ppl.com/) and its affiliates shall not be liable for wrongful use or disclosure of your personally identifying information by any third party. 

## Changes to this Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version of the policy will govern our use of your information and can be accessed on current web-page. By continuing to access or use the Services after those changes become effective, you agree to be bound by the revised Privacy Policy. 

If you have any additional questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us any time through email: [misc@search4ppl.com](mailto:misc@search4ppl.com).
