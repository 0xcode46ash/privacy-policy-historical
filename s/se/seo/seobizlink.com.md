# Privacy Policy

We honor your privacy. Any information we gather from you, including your email address, will merely be used to send you the data and information you have requested. This data and information includes search engine (SE) Ranking reports, marketing proposals and monthly promotional newsletters. 

We will not give or sell your personal data and information. If you give us your email and allow to send more information, you may opt out of receiving any additional email messages at any time, by unsubscribing from our email list. So be sure you understand the privacy policy of any site and trust them before providing any personal information.
