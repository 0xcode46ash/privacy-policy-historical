# Privacy Policy | Sleepopolis

_Sleepopolis is supported by readers. Product links on Sleepopolis are referral links. If you use one of these and buy something Sleepopolis makes a little bit of money. Need more info?[See how it all works here](http://sleepopolis.com/how-sleepopolis-works/)._

Sleepopolis does not share personal information with third-parties nor does Sleepopolis.com store information collected about your visit for use other than to analyze web site performance through the use of cookies, which you can turn off at anytime by modifying your Internet browser’s settings. The owner is not responsible for the republishing of the content found on this blog on other web sites or media without permission.

**Blog & Social Comments Policy**

Sleepopolis reserves the right to edit or delete any comments submitted to this blog or via our social platforms without notice due to;

  1. Comments deemed to be spam or questionable spam
  2. Comments including profanity
  3. Comments containing language or concepts that could be deemed offensive
  4. Comments that attack a person individually
  5. Comments deemed to be irrelevant or off-topic



Disagreements are encouraged and more than welcome. However, if you do disagree please do so respectfully. Nasty, rude, or mean remarks are removed and repeat offenders will be banned.

**Giveaways Policy**

All giveaways on Sleepopolis are hosted on the Gleam platform or directly on Sleepopolis.com. If you enter a giveaway by email you may be contacted by Gleam and/or the company sponsor of the giveaway. All parties are required by federal law to provide an easy unsubscribe option. If you do not wish to receive email from these parties please unsubscribe using the link at the bottom of each email they send.

Some giveaways include physical products. To sign up for these giveaways you may have to submit your name, address, email, and other basic contact and mailing information. Sleepopolis does not share this data with any 3rd parties. We only use this for the purpose of getting you your product.

**California Privacy Rights**

If you are a California resident, California Civil Code Section 1798.83 provides you the right to receive (a) information identifying any third party(ies) to whom we may have disclosed, within the preceding calendar year, your personal information for that third party’s direct marketing purposes; and (b) a description of the categories of personal information disclosed.  To obtain such information free of charge, please write to us at the address below, or [submit your request here](http://sleepopolis.com/contact/).  We will respond to these requests within thirty (30) days.  Requests that come to us by other means may result in a delayed response.

Mailing Address:  17209 N 45th St, Phoenix, Arizona 85032

**Persons Under Age 18**

We do not knowingly permit the website to be used by any person under the age of eighteen and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children younger than thirteen years of age.
