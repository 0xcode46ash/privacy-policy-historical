# Slim-Fast Online: Privacy Policy

|  In order to foster your privacy online Slim•Fast Foods Company has developed the following privacy policy. This policy outlines the specific manner in which your personal information will be used at Slim•Fast Online. It is important that you read it thoroughly. 

This policy addresses: 

  1. What personally identifiable information  
is collected from you;  

  2. What cookies are and how they are used;   

  3. Slim•Fast's policy against sharing your information with third parties;   

  4. What choices are available to you regarding collection of your information;  

  5. How you can edit, update or delete your information;  

  6. The kind of security precautions that are in place to protect your information;  

  7. How Slim•Fast protects children's privacy; 
  8. What else you should know about your online privacy.  




* * *

  
**What personally identifiable information does Slim•Fast collect from me?**

Slim•Fast Online registration requires the submission of your name and e-mail address. During registration, you are also asked for such personal information as your address, gender, height, weight, and phone number. The personal information provided to us enables us to customize user sessions more effectively. 

Your personal information may be requested from time to time when you submit your Slim•Fast success story; when you respond to one of our online surveys or polls; when you submit a suggestion for improving Slim•Fast and Slim•Fast Online; or when you report a difficulty with either our website or one of our products. 

* * *

  
**What are cookies and how does Slim•Fast Online use them?**

A [cookie](http://www.zdwebopedia.com/cookie.htm) is a small piece of information that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. Cookies have several purposes. They function as tracking tools by informing us when you visit Slim•Fast Online. This ability to identify users and learn how they use our site enables us to provide more effective customizable and personalized services. Cookies are invoved in our auto sign-in option through which you don't have to re-enter your password each time you visit Slim•Fast Online. In addition, cookies allow us to monitor user trends and patterns. The information provided by cookies permits us to continually evaluate our website in order to make improvements to our content and to offer information which users find relevant. All membership related areas in Slim•Fast Online require that you accept cookies. You can change the settings of your browser to either notify you when a cookie is set, or to refuse to accept the cookies. However, if you refuse to accept cookies, you will lose some of the interactive capabilities (i.e. membership, weight charts) of Slim•Fast Online. 

* * *

  
**Does Slim•Fast share my personal information with third parties?**

When you submit personal information on the Slim•Fast website you are sharing that information with Slim•Fast alone. Slim•Fast will not disclose, trade or sell a user's personally identifiable information to any other party. 

Slim•Fast may also disclose account information in special cases when we have reason to believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be causing injury to or interference with (either intentionally or unintentionally) Slim•Fast's rights or property, other Slim•Fast Online users, or anyone else that could be harmed by such activities. Slim•Fast may provide records or information to law enforcement officials or other government entities if required to do so by law. 

* * *

  
**What are my choices regarding the collection of my information?**

If you do not want your information collected please do not submit it to us. 

If you are concerned with the use of cookies at Slim•Fast Online, you can modify your browser preferences to notify you when a cookie is set, or to reject all cookies. However, if you choose to reject all cookies, you will not be able to use services including (but not limited to) Slim•Fast Online membership, newsletters, weight charting, chat, and message boards. You will still be able to browse and search for reference information without cookies. 

* * *

  
**How do I edit, update, or delete my personally identifiable information?**

If you need to access, update or change your [Slim•Fast Online membership profile](https://web.archive.org/profile/index.asp) at any time you may do so by using your Slim•Fast Online password. If you do not want to receive e-mail including newsletters and notices regarding our products and services, you can edit your [Slim•Fast Online membership profile](https://web.archive.org/profile/index.asp) to no longer receive e-mail. 

If you have forgotten your password, please [click here](https://web.archive.org/profile/password_help.asp) to receive a reminder by mail. For all other problems signing in to Slim•Fast Online, please e-mail our [consumer services department](https://web.archive.org/contact/contact.asp) describing the specific situation for which you need help. If you would like for us to remove your personal information from our records, please contact our [consumer services department](https://web.archive.org/contact/contact.asp) with your full name and registered e-mail address. We will use reasonable efforts to delete your information from our existing files. 

* * *

  
**What security is in place to protect my information?**

Your Slim•Fast Online account information is password-protected. This means that only you and Slim•Fast have access to your personal information. As a precautionary measure we suggest that you do not give out your password to anyone. Also, if you share a computer with someone else or are using a computer in a public place like a library, remember to sign out of your Slim•Fast Online account and close your browser window when you finish your work. 

Since your privacy is a major concern for us, Slim•Fast will do everything in its power to ensure the security of your personal information on our systems. However, you should be aware that data transmission over the Internet is not 100% secure. 

* * *

  
**What about children's privacy?**

Slim•Fast believes that children should obtain their parent's consent before sending personal information to anyone online. Slim•Fast discourages children under 18 years old from using this site without adult supervision. Slim•Fast will not send any direct e-mail offers to users who indicate they are under the age of 18. 

* * *

  
**What other privacy issues should I know about?**

Please keep in mind that whenever you voluntarily disclose personal information online on message boards, through e-mail, or in chat areas - that information can be collected and used by others. If you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return. Please be careful and responsible whenever you are online. 

* * *

  
**Additional Information**

Questions regarding this statement should be directed to the Slim•Fast [consumer services department](https://web.archive.org/contact/contact.asp). 

Since our privacy policy may be modified occasionally, it is in your best interest to check back frequently for any policy changes or updates.
