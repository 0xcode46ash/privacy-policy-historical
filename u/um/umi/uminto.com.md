# Privacy Policy of Uminto.com

## Privacy Policy

Privacy needs to be protected in this present electronic world and we at **http://www.uminto.com** respect that. You as well as we understand that we need to divulge some information to the world for availing services.

#### General Information

We request you to give us general information like email address, mobile number, gender etc. Other information like photo, city, state, country, address etc are optional, and is left to the discretion of the user when he opts for optional premium services. 

#### Important Information

##### Your Password

We protect your password and we state that we do not share your password with anybody. It is also your own responsibility to protect your password. 

##### Cookies

We have cookies on our website which enable us to give you fast service and customized service. However, you have the option to deny the cookies feature but by doing this you will also not be able to use some of the services. 

It is also stated that we may have other cookies from other websites on our website over which we don't have any control and hence we are not liable for privacy trespass by such website. It is they who should be liable for such trespass of privacy. 

**Ad Serving**

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/). 

E-mail, SMS & Phone Calls from Us and Others 

**By registering on http://www.uminto.com you give us the consent to contact you through email, SMS (Short Message Service) and through tele communication (mobile, landline or any other medium) for all purposes. The purpose includes information on products and offers, contest and prizes, marketing and advertising communications and all other reasons. By registering you are giving us the consent to do so.**

#### Changes in Privacy Policy

We will regularly update our website and if any change is required in Privacy policy then we will notify you of such change by email or posting on the website. 

#### Contact Us

You can contact us any time by [clicking here](https://web.archive.org/contact-us.asp).
