# American Immigration Center: All the Information about Latest INS Updates on US Immigration Procedures

## Privacy Statement

The American Immigration Center is committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect on this web site mainly to process orders, make shopping at American Immigration Center possible and to enhance your overall shopping experience. We do not forward, sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We may choose to do so in the future with trustworthy third parties but before we do, we will amend our privacy policy. 

Our most common method of shipping is UPS. If you provide us with your e-mail address we will enter it into the UPS terminal. This will allow UPS to confirm by e-mail your pick up, scheduled delivery, and tracking number. The American Immigration Center is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other Web sites. If you wish us not to use your e-mail address for UPS shipping confirmation, please let us know by placing a note on your order or by letting us know by contacting us over the phone at 505.891.1555. 

When you sign up for our Immigration Newsletter Subscription we need an e-mail address-which we use to send the information you requested about immigration news, company news, and update briefs. We also monitor customer traffic patterns usage and interaction with our web site to help us develop and improve the design and layout of the American Immigration Center. 

When you place order online, we offer the use of a secure server. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. 

By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by the American Immigration Center. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. 

If you have any comments or suggestions we want to hear them.   
Please e-mail us at: [feedback@us-immigration.com.](mailto:feedback@us-immigration.com)
