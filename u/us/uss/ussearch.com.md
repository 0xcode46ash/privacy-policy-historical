# Privacy

**US SEARCH.com Inc. Visitor Privacy Policy**

US SEARCH is committed to respecting the privacy rights of all visitors to our Web site. The following policy describes how US SEARCH protects information it receives from visitors to its Web properties.

**What information does US SEARCH collect and how does US SEARCH use it?**

  * When you order services or register on US SEARCH properties, we may need you to provide your name, e-mail address, mailing address, phone number and, when ordering services, credit card information.


  * Visitors will also be given a choice when ordering or registering whether they would like to be included on US SEARCH's mailing list, or whether they would prefer not to receive such mailings from US SEARCH. If you are included on US SEARCH's mailing list, we may notify you about important functionality changes to the Web site, new services, and special offers we think you'll find valuable. 



**How does US SEARCH protect visitor information?**

US SEARCH implements a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. User personal information is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of employees who have specific access rights to such systems. When you place orders or access your personal information, we offer the use of a secure server. All sensitive/credit information supplied by users is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.

**Does US SEARCH disclose the information it collects from its visitors to outside parties?**

US SEARCH does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer outside the company personally identifiable information that visitors voluntarily provide in any registration or order form. This information is used to better understand visitors' use of US SEARCH's site and to support related transactions made on the site.

**Does US SEARCH use "cookies"?**

Cookies are bits of electronic information that a Web site can transfer to a visitor's hard drive to help tailor and keep records of his or her visit to the site. Cookies allow Web site operators to better customize visits to the site to the visitor's individual preferences. The use of cookies is standard on the Internet and many major Web sites use them. US SEARCH may use cookies to help provide its visitors with a better experience on our site, however, we do not store personal identifying information in cookies.

**Your consent**

By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of the information you provide according to this Privacy Policy. If we decide to change our Privacy Policy, we will post these changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.

**Access To Personal Identifying Information**

US SEARCH provides access to the personal identifying information collected through the purchase of our services by offering customers the opportunity to review and correct inaccuracies of such information. In order to review or correct personal identifying information, a customer must send a written request to US SEARCH including their name, complete address, purchase date and order reference number. The company will process the request within 30 days of receipt. US SEARCH will not process third party requests. Requests should be mailed to:

> US SEARCH.com Inc.  
>  5401 Beethoven Street  
>  Attn: Legal Department  
>  Los Angeles, CA 90066

**Does US SEARCH use Web Bugs?**

US SEARCH utilizes third parties to record information regarding the use of its site. The information recorded is limited to the sources and advertisements which refer customers to our site. The information recorded by these third parties is disclosed to US SEARCH only and not to any other parties. US SEARCH does not record customer information regarding activities outside of our Web properties
