### Disclosure of Information

UserZoom does not collect personally identifiable information. All responses to survey questions are stored against an anonymous ID number. However, it may be necessary for us, at certain times, to reveal statistical information regarding the UserZoom site to third parties. Personal information is only collected if you specifically provide this information on a voluntary basis. We make every effort to ensure that your information is maintained in a safe and secure environment.

Visitor information collected via online forms and surveys is made available only for internal use by UserZoom, for research and marketing purposes.

UserZoom may use, distribute or sell the use of user experience anonymous data obtained from measuring any websites on the Internet that either is publicly accessible without special passwords to any user with a web browser or requires a special password that UserZoom legitimately obtains for its own account. 

If anyone contracts UserZoom to collect measurement data that requires a password known only to them, we will not distribute or otherwise use that performance data without their permission.

### Third-Party Sites

Please be aware that some of the Web sites you may choose to link to from our site may collect Personally Identifiable Information and other information about you. UserZoom strongly urges our users to be aware that if you use these third party links, you will leave the UserZoom site. The practices of these Web sites are not covered by this Privacy Policy.

### Cookies

"Cookies" are small text files stored on a study participants hard drive; not on our site. UserZoom only uses cookies to prevent participants from taking the same survey multiple times. We do not use "tracking cookies."

Use of General Clients Data   
UserZoom will not reveal to any customer what URLs or transactions another customer is measuring. UserZoom will not reveal to any customer any proprietary consulting reports.

### General Security Policy

UserZoom is aware of your privacy concerns and strives to collect only as much data as is required to make your experience as efficient and satisfying as possible, in the most unobtrusive manner as possible.

### Changes to this Privacy Policy

If we change this policy, we will publish changes on our site before those changes take effect. Please review this site periodically to keep informed of our most current privacy policy. As always, if you do not agree with our Privacy Policy or any changes to it, you should cease using this site. If you continue to use this site, you will be deemed to have accepted those changes.

The foregoing policies are effective as of January 1, 2005. UserZoom reserves the right to change this policy at any time. This statement and the policies outlined herein are not intended to and do not create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party. 

### Cancelation of your subscription

If you do not want to receive email, phone or postal mail communications from UserZoom, please let us know by sending email to us at the above address and telling us what kind of communication you do not wish to receive from our company. Please provide us with your exact name and address, as well as the email addresses to which you want us to stop sending communications.
