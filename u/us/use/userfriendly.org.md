**Privacy Policy of userfriendly.org**

We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to us will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. 

**userfriendly.org** does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including survey forms or email lists. 

**userfriendly.org** occasionally analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the value of the content available on our website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site. 

**userfriendly.org** uses DoubleClick's DART system to serve banner ads. The DART system collects cookies. Cookies collect certain non-personally-identifiable information about users. DART does not collect any personally-identifiable information, such as name, address, phone number or email address. For information about non-personally identifiable information that DART collects for the purpose of targeting ads and measuring ad effectiveness, please refer to <http://www.doubleclick.net/company_info/about_doubleclick/privacy>

**userfriendly.org** may be undertaking a voluntary registration system for users in late 1999. Individuals must affirmatively request to join our mailing lists by signing up through a form on our web site. We will not sell, rent, loan, trade or lease these addresses on our list to anyone, though we will use this mailing list to keep members informed of upcoming events and to market **User Friendly** services and products. 

If you have any additional questions, please contact us at [info@userfriendly.org](mailto:info@userfriendly.org)
