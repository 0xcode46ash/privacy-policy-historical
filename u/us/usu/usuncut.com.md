Privacy Policy

This privacy statement covers all of the Instablogs sites. Please read this statement carefully before using proceeding to access Instablogs Sites. Your use of Instablogs Sites indicates your agreement to abide by the Terms in effect.

Information Collection

Instablogs Network is the sole owner of the information collected on the Instablogs Sites. Instablogs Network may collect information from our users at several different points on the Instablogs Sites.

Comments

In order to enhance their use of the Instablogs Sites, users can choose to leave comments on particular articles of interest. To leave a comment, users must submit information including a valid email address. Instablogs Network uses this information to screen out users who leave comments prohibited by our terms and conditions of use, and will not pass this information to any other organization.

Mailing List

Instablogs Network may launch a mailing list for some or all of the Instablogs Sites.
