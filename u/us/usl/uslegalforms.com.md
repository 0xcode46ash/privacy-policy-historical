# U.S. Legal Forms, Inc. - Privacy Policy

U.S. Legal Forms, Inc. (USLF), respects your privacy! Any and all information you provide will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to the USLF will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. If you have any questions, please let [us know](http://www.uslegalforms.com/help/ "Contact USLegalForms.com").

 **Information collected on the website:**   
USLF does not collect information about it's users.  USLF website logs **are not personally identifiable** , and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

**Use of cookies:**   
We do not use cookies to gain information from you.  We do use cookies to manage the shopping cart and affiliate sales. The cookies only assist you use the site.

**Privacy of our email lists:**   
USLF will not share your email address with any third party. USLF does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on other list to anyone.
