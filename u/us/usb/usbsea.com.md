# Privacy Policy-Reday

|   | 

Reday cares about your privacy. We know that you care how information about you is used, and we appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly. This notice describes our privacy policy. By visiting usbsea.com, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Notice.

**Collection of information**  
Reday collects personally identifiable information that you specifically and voluntarily provide on the Site. Personally identifiable information is information that identifies you as a specific individual, for example, your company name, address, contact person, telephone number, fax number, email and etc. usbsea.com includes both public areas, open to all visitors, and private areas that are open only to those visitors who choose to register.  
  
 **Use of information**  
We may use the information you provide to us to fulfill requests for products, confirm your orders, service your account or for other administrative purposes, We may also access your information to resolve disputes, to troubleshoot problems, to enforce agreements between us.  
  
We may also use the information you provide us in order to administer promotions or surveys, helps us develop new products and services based on your needs.

**Reday protect your privacy**  
personally identifiable information about our customers is an important part of our business, and we do not sell, trade or rent it to others. Also we do not share customers' information with other parties.

Your personal information is protected by the password you created when you created an account at usbsea.com (or another password you chose after changing a previous password). Please keep this password confidential. No Customer Service Associate or any other representative of Reday will ever ask you for your password. The confidentiality of your password is yours to protect. You may change it anytime by going to My Usbsea. **Notice of changes to this privacy policy  
** Reday reserve the rights to change this privacy policy at any time. However, if any changes do occur, we will publish the changes to our web site and post a notice on the homepage that a new privacy policy has been implemented. We will also take reasonable steps to contact you personally to let you know of the new changes.
