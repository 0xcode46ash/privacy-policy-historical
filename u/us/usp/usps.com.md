# USPS - Privacy Notice Highlights

|  |  |   
---  
  
|   
---  
|  ScopeThis statement applies to USPS visitors and customers of USPS.com.  |   
|  |  |   
---  
| You do not have to register or provide personal information to visit our site.  
| We collect personal information directly from you and from your transactions with us. Providing it is voluntary, but otherwise, we may be unable to process your request or transaction.  
| We use certain web tools, such as cookies, to support the website and measure site usage, which do not identify you personally without your express consent.  
| We only market other USPS products/services to you with your consent, based on your marketing preferences.  
| Create or change your preferences by accessing your account on USPS.com and editing your profile.  
|  |   
---  
| We use your information to provide you USPS products and services.  
| We do not sell customer or mailing lists to marketers or any outside parties.  
| We only disclose your information as permitted by the Privacy Act, including the following limited situations: to a congressional office on your behalf; to financial entities regarding financial transaction issues; to a USPS auditor; to entities, including law enforcement, as required by law or in legal proceedings; to contractors and other entities to fulfill your product or service; or with your consent.  
| You may access information we maintain about you by contacting the program for the product/service you ordered.  
| 

|   
---
