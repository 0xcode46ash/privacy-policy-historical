# USPharmacist.com > Privacy Policy

Welcome to [www.uspharmacist.com](http://www.uspharmacist.com/)! This site is owned by Jobson Medical Information LLC ("JMI"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding collection, use, and disclosure of personal information that you may provide to us through this Web site.

**What personal information do we collect on this site, and how do we collect it?**

  * When you subscribe to U.S. Pharmacist®, we ask you for your first and last name, title, company name, address, phone number, fax number, and e-mail address.
  * When you sign up to take CE activities on this site, you create a "CE student profile," which includes your name (first, middle, last), address, phone, e-mail, and fax number, as well as the numbers, states and expiration dates of your licenses.
  * If you sign up for our Continuing Education E-Mail Newsletter, we ask you for your first and last names, address, e-mail address, cell phone number, and home fax number.



**How do we use the personal information that we collect?**

  * We use the personal information that we collect on your online subscription form as follows: 
    * Our fulfillment house, which currently is Hallmark Data, stores all the information that you enter on the online subscription form and makes that information available to the our circulation department. 
    * Our circulation department may share your name and address with our direct mail vendors (who are engaged on a job-to-job basis), who may send you renewal mailings, and with our list vendor, which currently is Statlistics, who may include your name and address in lists that it rents to third parties. We also may share your name and address with MBNA America Bank, N.A., with whom we have a credit card program. MBNA may use that information to send you offers for a U.S. Pharmacist® credit card. 
    * If you provide your e-mail address, we may send you subscription renewal forms by e-mail and may forward your e-mail address to our list vendor, which currently is Statlistics, to use to send you e-mail promotional messages for other pharmacy products and services offered by JMI and other companies. We also may use your e-mail address to send you newsletters about developments and opportunities in the pharmacy and pharmaceutical industries. ****
    * If you provide your telephone number, we may share it with telemarketing companies (who are engaged on a job-by-job basis) who may use it to ****solicit renewals of your subscription, and with our list vendor, which currently is Statlistics, who may include the phone number along with your name and address in lists that it rents to third parties.   You do not have to provide your telephone number to subscribe to U.S. Pharmacist®.
    * If you provide your fax number, you give consent for us to send you fax advertisements for JMI information products and services at the fax number you have provided. We also may share your name and fax number with our fax vendor, which currently is Express Messaging, who may send you subscription renewal forms by fax. You do not have to provide your fax number to subscribe to U.S. Pharmacist®. ****
  * We use the information that we collect when you register for CE activities as follows: 
    * We use your name and address to grade your post-activity test and to send you a statement of credit for the CE activity. If PHE uses a third-party company to grade your post-activity test and issue statements of credit, we will give your name and address to that company for that purpose only. 
    * If you sign up for our Continuing Education E-Mail Newsletter, JMI will use your e-mail address to send you the newsletter as well as JMI’s _U.S. Pharmacist_ ® eConnect® newsletter and e-pharrm/alert®, a promotional service of JMI’s subsidiary ?alert Marketing, Inc. 
    * On occasion, the commercial supporter of a CE activity will ask us for a list of the people who participated in that activity, so that it may document the first level of outcomes-based evaluation in CE ( _i.e._ , who attended, which medical specialties/practices were represented, how this compares to the target audience, and whether the activity needs to be repeated because significant numbers of the target audience did not attend).  In that event, we will provide the supporter with your name, title and affiliation, but we will request in writing that the supporter not contact you directly for any purpose. ****
  * If we sell, assign, or otherwise transfer the business of this Web site to which your personal information relates, we will transfer your personal information to the buyer and require the buyer to agree to treat it in accordance with this Privacy Policy. 
  * If we are required to disclose your personal information by any applicable law, court order, or government regulation, we will comply with such laws, orders or regulations.



**Do any third parties collect information on this site?**  
BPA Worldwide Interactive audits our Web site traffic using technology provided by Nielsen Online. Such audits enable us to tell our advertisers how many people are visiting the site and seeing their advertisements.  Click here <http://www.nielsen-online.com/privacy.jsp?section=leg_scs> to learn about how the Nielsen technology works and the kind of information that it collects. ****

**Do you use cookies (and what are they)?**  
Cookies are pieces of information that a Web site places on your computer to facilitate and enhance your communication and interaction with that site by making it easier for you to navigate the site.  That is how we use cookies on this site.  We do not use cookies to retrieve personal information from your computer.  Cookies (and Java-Script) must be enabled for you to enter and use this site.  The Nielsen technology that BPA uses includes cookies to gather statistical information which is designed to assist in understanding what users find interesting and useful on this site.

**What about non-confidential information that I transmit to you?**               
Any communication or material you transmit to us by electronic mail or otherwise, including any data, questions, comments, suggestions, or the like is, and will be treated as, nonconfidential and nonproprietary. We may use anything you transmit to us or post on this Web site for any purpose, including but not limited to, reproduction, disclosure, transmission, publication, broadcast, posting and/or ranking. Furthermore, you expressly agree that we are free to use any ideas, concepts, know-how, or techniques contained in any communication you send to us without compensation and for any purpose whatsoever, including but not limited to, developing, manufacturing and marketing products and services using such information.

**How can I correct my personal information?**  
To keep your personal information accurate, current, and complete, if you are a U.S. Pharmacist® subscriber, please contact Hallmark Data Systems at 1- 877-529-1746 or [uspsubscriptions@halldata.com](mailto:uspsubscriptions@halldata.com).  If you are not a U.S. Pharmacist® subscriber, please call our customer service at 1- 800-825-4696. 

**How can I be sure that my Information is secure on your Web site?**  
We take reasonable steps to protect your Information as you transmit it from your computer to our site and to protect your Information from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. You should keep in mind that no Internet transmission is ever 100% secure or error-free. In particular, e-mail sent to or from this site may not be secure, Moreover, where you use passwords, ID numbers, or other special access features on this site; it is your responsibility to safeguard them. 

This site may contain links or references to other Web sites in addition to the ones we have already mentioned. Please be aware that we do not control such other Web sites and that, in any case, this Privacy Policy does not apply to those Web sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of every Web site you visit.

**How will I know if this policy has changed?**  
If this Privacy Policy changes, the revised policy will be posted on this site. Please check back periodically, and especially before you provide any personal information. This Privacy Policy was last updated on **June 3, 2009**.
