# Privacy Policy @ Ultimate-Guitar.Com - your #1 source for tabs!

* **Third Party Advertising**
  
We use [DoubleClick](http://doubleclick.net/) and other third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site about goods and services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please click [here](http://www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy). 
* **Also please note this:**
  
\- A 3rd party cookie will be placed on your browser when you`re browsing the site.  
\- Information collected on this site is used to serve ads on this site.  
\- You can choose not to have your info shared with 3rd party advertisers (ie. through an "opt-out").
