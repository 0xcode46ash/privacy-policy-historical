## Which data is being logged?

  * If you only browse our site: Your IP

  * If you purchase a product: Your IP, name, e-mail address, country and payment information (but NOT your credit card number). Your password is stored on our server in an encrypted format.

  * If you sign up for our newsletter: Your IP and e-mail address




## Why do we store user information?

The main reason to store your user data is to give you access to the products you purchased. We also use your e-mail address to notify you about updates, new releases and news. Your information is never shared or sold to any 3rd parties. Your information is all safe with us.

##  Right of information

At any given time **you** have the right to request the information we stored about **you** cost free.

## How we handle your information

Unless required for legal investigations or if it's in the best public interest (e.g. suspected credit card fraud) we do not share your information.

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, please don't hesitate to [contact us](http://backlink2seo.com/contact_us.html).
