**_Visitor Privacy_**  
Our web server automatically recognizes and collects only the visitor's domain name. Visitor email addresses are not collected. The information collected is only used for internal review and discarded. 

**_Information/Product Requests_**  
Provide your mailing address and/or email address on-line to receive any information or products you request. Telephone numbers provided on-line will be called only if we have questions about your request. 

Occasionally you may receive mailings or emails from Uline or our special partners with information on new products, services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive these offers, please contact us by mail, email, or phone.
