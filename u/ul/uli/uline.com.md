# Uline: Privacy Policy

This is the web site of Uline Shipping Supplies. 

Our mailing address is:   
Uline Inc.  
2200 S. Lakeside   
Waukegan, IL 60085 

We can be reached via e-mail at [customer.service@uline.com](mailto:customer.service@uline.com) or by phone at (800) 958-5463.

For each visitor to our web page, our web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name. We automatically collect only the domain name, but not the e-mail address of visitors to our web page. The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded. 

If you supply us with your postal address and/or email address on-line, you will receive the information or products for which you asked. You may also receive periodic mailings or emails from us or our special partners with information on new products, services or upcoming events. 

If you do not wish to receive these offers, please contact us via one of the means provided above. People who provide us with their telephone number on-line will be contacted via telephone only if we have questions about additional information provided. 

If you have any questions about the above statement, please contact us at [customer.service@uline.com](mailto:customer.service@uline.com).
