# uwad.com : Privacy Policy

For each visitor to our Web pages, our Web server automatically recognizes only the visitor's browser type and domain name, but not the e-mail address (unless this has previously been provided). 

We collect only the domain name from which a visitor has come to our site, and aggregate information about which of our pages visitors access, together with any additional information that may be volunteered by our visitors, such as information provided by survey and feedback, mailing list and forum registrations, and all such other information that is freely given by our visitors. 

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web site, to customize the content and/or layout of our pages for each individual user, to notify consumers about updates to our Web site and/or relevant news and information items connected with our web site and its areas of specialist interest, and to contact visitors occasionally for marketing purposes, with their prior consent. 

No information relating to individual visitors to our site is shared, divulged or sold to any third parties, individuals or organizations for any form of commercial purpose. 

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending email to us at the address below advising us that you no longer wish to receive any email from us, or by unsubscribing from mailing lists. Forums etc. in the manner outlined when such subscriptions are first activated, and restated from time to time thereafter, or alternatively by contacting your forum moderator, or by sending an Email message to the uwad.com Webmaster at:

_[support@uwad.com](mailto:info@uwad.com)_

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed on-line. 

**Spam Policy:**

YOU SPAM, YOU OUT. We have zero tolerance spam policy.

  1. Ultimate Web Advertising TOU explicitly forbids spamming. Prohibited spam or otherwise impermissible marketing activities in your membership may include, but are not limited to, the following: 
    1. Sending mass email to recipients who haven't requested email from you, and/or sending email with a fake return address 
    2. Promoting a service with inappropriate links, titles, descriptions, or use of any content for which you do not have the appropriate rights. 
    3. Promoting your affiliate web site by posting multiple submissions in public forums that are identical. 



If you're not sure if something constitutes spam, please exercise caution. Ultimate Web Advertising may take action, at our discretion, if we believe your store has engaged in impermissible marketing activities, spamming, or other violations of these Terms Of Use.
