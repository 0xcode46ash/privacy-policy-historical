# Privacy statement

Our privacy policy

  
We consider the privacy of our clients to be of utmost importance, and guarantee the following: 

  1. We do not make any client data available to third parties.

  2. We do not share data among clients.

  3. We do not sell client lists.

  4. We do not distribute advertising or promotional material to our clients on behalf of third parties.




Dr Tax-UFile.ca Inc. is fully committed to protecting confidentiality and privacy. Our policies on information gathering and disclosure practices for the Web site [www.ufile.ca](http://www.ufile.ca/) are as follows: 

  * We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to manage our Web site.

  * We use cookies to monitor your session on our site only. 

  * The various forms used on our site (registration, online surveys, etc.) may require you to provide contact information (name, email address) and unique identifiers. 

We may use this customer contact data to send you information about our company. 

Contact information might also be used to get in touch with you if necessary. 

Unique identifiers (such as social insurance numbers) are only used to verify your identity. 

  * We distribute material only to those who have opted to receive emails from us on a periodic basis. 

You can always opt out of receiving future mailings, or have the contact information updated by sending an email to our [webmaster@ufile.ca](mailto:webmaster@UFile.ca) or by visiting _http://www.ufile.ca/TaxNewsAndTips/Newsletter.asp_.

  * Financial information is required for the sole purpose of billing you for using our service. 

  * Demographic and profile data (such as your place of residence and your language) may also be collected to help facilitate your use of our Web site.

  * Your tax information is part of your own file and no one has access to that information except you. That information is encrypted and stored on our secure servers. 

  * This site contains links to other Web sites, such as various government sites. UFile.ca is not responsible for the privacy practices or contents of these sites. 

  * Our [technical](https://web.archive.org/web/20031207053232id_/http%3A//www.ufile.ca/Help/FAQ_tech.asp) FAQ's describe the security measures that we take to protect the integrity of your information and to offer the highest security standards available. 

  * This site is not for children. We do not collect information from them, nor do we offer products or services that might be of interest to them. 




If you have any questions about this privacy statement, this site, or your dealings with UFile.ca, please contact our [webmaster@ufile.ca](mailto:webmaster@UFile.ca).
