# Urban Terror Forums Privacy Policy

> Privacy Policy
> 
> Silicon Ice collects minimal statisical information from the users of the Urban Terror forums. No personal information is stored. Our statistical reporting system records our server requests. This system stores the users IP & the date of access.
> 
> Silicon Ice does not sell any user information stored on the system. Silicon Ice does not give out any user information to any 3rd party companies.
