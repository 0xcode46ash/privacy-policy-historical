# UrbanDictionary.com: Privacy Policy

**Urban Dictionary** is a slang dictionary with [your definitions](https://web.archive.org/insert.php). _Define your world._

**Urban Dictionary Privacy Policy**

> **Urban Dictionary will never give out your email address or send you a message you did not request.**
> 
> **Urban Dictionary records session-related data** including operating system, session length, IP address, cookie values and date and time of access. We use this information to identify trends that help us improve Urban Dictionary. This information is not used to identify you.
> 
> **Urban Dictionary collects marketing information,** including age, gender and income, on our voluntary [user survey](https://web.archive.org/survey.php). Survey responses are not personally identifiable. We use information collected on the survey only in aggregate form.
> 
> **Urban Dictionary requires email addresses** for several features, including "recommend for deletion," "send this word to a friend," "mailing list" and "define a new word." We do not send unsolicited mail to these addresses and will not give or sell them to third parties.
> 
> **Urban Dictionary's[online store](https://web.archive.org/store.php)** uses PayPal for secure credit card transactions. We never see or store your credit card information.
> 
> **You can contact Urban Dictionary** through our [feedback form](https://web.archive.org/feedback.php?subject=privacy+policy).
>
