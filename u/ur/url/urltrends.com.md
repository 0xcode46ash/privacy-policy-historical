**What about “cookies”?**

“Cookies” are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computers hard drive. Our cookies are safe and do not contain any personally identifying information. Most web browsers except cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent this.

**What happens to information collected by UrlTrends.com**

Whenever UrlTrends.com handles personal information, regardless of where this occurs, we take steps to ensure that your information is treated securely and in accordance with the relevant Terms of Service and this Privacy Policy.

UrlTrends.com does not trade, sell, or rent your personal information to customers, sales, traffic patterns, and related site information to reputable third party vendors. Any information disclosed from statistics is general and does in no way disclose personal identification details.

UrlTrends.com may send you ane email to indicated any important functionalty changes to the magazine site. You many opt out of Newsletters and special offer mailouts at any time. UrlTrends.com endeavours to forward only genuine opportunities that are of interest including fabulous compeitition & free giveaways.

Ecommerce, shopping and telephone related services ordered via this site and associated 3rd party providers, information collected may be used for marketing purposes of similar, related and other products and services.

Responsibility for what is posted in the discussion groups or other public forums lies with each user – YOU. You alone are responsible for the content of your messages, and the consequences of any such message. You agree not to use the sites in any way to send or submit materials.

**In summary**

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect from you to enhance your UrlTrends.com experience. We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to others.

**Your consent**

By using our web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by UrlTrends.com.
