# Universal Campus Credit Union

When using UCCU's electronic services, you don't have to worry about the security of your personal and account information. We want all your personal information kept secure and confidential.

We use the information you give us to verify who you are, to keep your account with us secure, and to complete your transactions or business. Your personal information is private and we respect your privacy.

Your information stays here. We have never sold and never will sell information about you to any third party.

Our digital certificate was issued by [Verisign.](http://digitalid.verisign.com/server/about/dataSheet.htm) We use 128 bit SSL encryption to send and receive information.
