###  Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Udemy coupons uses cookies to help Udemy coupons identify and track visitors, their usage of Udemy coupons website, and their website access preferences. Udemy coupons visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Udemy coupons’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Udemy coupons’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

###  Ads

This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Udemy coupons and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
