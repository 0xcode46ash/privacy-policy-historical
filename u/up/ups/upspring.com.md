# PRIVACY - Upspring.com

## PRIVACY POLICY

* * *

Upspring is provided to you by Worldspring, LLC ("we" or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. You can visit Upspring.com (collectively, the "Site") without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. By using this Site you agree to be bound by the policy set forth herein.

We may make changes to the Site or policies and conditions that govern the use of the Site at any time. We encourage you to review the policies periodically for any updates or changes.

Any use of the web site is at sole risk of the user. Your continued access or use of the Site shall be deemed your acceptance of these changes and the reasonableness of these standards for notice of changes. 

>   
> 
>   1. **Accuracy**
> 
> We do our best to maintain the accuracy of information you supply us.
> 
>   
> 
>   2. **Protecting Your Privacy**
> 
> • Your account information is password protected. Please help us by keeping your password safe. We do not publish your account information or share it with other individuals or organizations.
> 
> • Be aware that as users when you voluntarily display or distribute personal information (such as your phone number or email address on the Site), that information shall be made available to the public and can be collected and used by others. This may result in unsolicited messages from third parties over which Worldspring, LLC is not responsible. Please review our Terms of Use before using our Site.
> 
> • We do not knowingly rent or sell your information to any third parties without your consent.
> 
>   
> 
>   3. **Information We Collect**
> 
> In several areas of our site, we ask you to provide personal information about you by filling out and submitting a member profile. We ask for name, address, email address etc. in order to create an account. We also collect user submitted reviews, ratings, tags and photos of local businesses and service providers.
> 
> In addition, we collect your Internet Protocol (IP) address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our site. Your IP address is also used to help identify you when you visit our site.
> 
>   
> 
>   4. **How We Use Your Information**
> 
> If you elect to include personal information in your profile, we will use such information to provide the service. We will allow you to view, correct or remove your personal information. We use your personal information as necessary to provide products and services you have requested, to contact you about Upspring's products and services, as directed by you. If you choose to use our invitation service to recommend a friend about Upspring, we will ask for your friend's name and email address. Upspring will send a one-time email to your friend. Your friend may contact us at anytime to request for removal of this information about our database.
> 
> We may use the information provide to target ads that we think you will be interested in. We may, for example, use information you provide to send emails, newsletters and alerts to you. You may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time. We may occasionally send you administrative emails concerning changes to our User Agreement or other important policy in which may affect your membership at Upspring.com. You may not opt-out of any such administrative emails.
> 
>   
> 
>   5. **Security**
> 
> We will take reasonable steps to protect the security of the Personal Information we collect from you. Although we make good faith efforts to store the information in a secure operating environment that is not available to the public, we cannot guarantee complete security.
> 
>   
> 
>   6. **External Links**
> 
> Upspring has links to other Web sites. Worldspring, LLC is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of other Web sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of those other Web sites so you can understand how they collect, use and share your information.
> 
> Upspring welcomes links to our web site. Web site owners are free to establish a hypertext link to this site provided that we are not required to pay any sponsorship fees.
> 
> If at anytime our policy ever changes, we will post it in this page so that you will always understand how we use, collect and disclose your personal information.
> 
>
