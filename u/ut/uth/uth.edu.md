# Internet Privacy Policy for The University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston

The University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston maintains the UTHSC-H web site as a public service. The following is the privacy policy for the www.uth.tmc.edu and www.uthouston.edu sites:

1\. Due to the University's diversity in teaching, research and patient care, many different web authors publish information on this site. 

2\. To improve the usefulness of our web site, we collect traffic information from our site's server logs. This information does not identify the visitor individually. The information we collect consists of: 

  * The name of the domain from which the visitor accesses the Internet.
  * The Internet Protocol (IP) number.
  * The date and time that the web page was visited.



3\. Some pages use cookies to maintain sessions or to automate navigation. Most UTHSC-H sites do not use cookies or any other similar technology to collect information about our visitors. 

4\. We collect the e-mail addresses of those individuals who communicate with us via e-mail or who give us their e-mail address. We may redirect your message to another government agency or person who is in a better position to answer your question.

5\. We collect information voluntarily provided by those individuals who communicate with us via e-mail or who submit forms on our web site.

6\. We use e-mail addresses and volunteered information to send news, notices of upcoming events, and tax information to those who request it. We will not market the information we collect.

7\. This site contains links to other sites. The University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites.

8\. The information we collect may be subject to public disclosure under the Texas Public Information Act.
