# Privacy Policy

#### _Online Highways_ Is Committed To Your Privacy

The following policies apply to all Web sites published by Online Highways LLC, including but not limited to Online Highways (ohwy.com), India9.com and u-s-history.com. Your privacy on the Internet is of the utmost importance to us. Individually identifiable information about you is not willfully disclosed to any third party without first receiving your permission, as explained in this privacy policy.

#### Cookies

When you visit this site, we may a "cookie" to your computer. A cookie is a piece of data stored on a site visitor's hard drive which helps us improve your access to our site, customize the user experience, and identify repeat visitors to our site. Online Highways does not disclose its cookies to third parties except as required by a valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena, statute, or court order.

Most browsers are set up to accept cookies by default. You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser, or you can have it inform you each time a cookie is sent. Be aware that some parts of this website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

#### Information Collection, Use and Sharing

Online Highways may share aggregated demographic information about our visitors with advertisers, business partners, sponsors, and other third parties. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person.

This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personally identifiable information.

By using this site, you consent to the collection and use of information by Online Highways as specified here. If we decide to modify our privacy policy, we will post changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
