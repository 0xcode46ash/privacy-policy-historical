# UK Trade and Investment :: Privacy Policy

**_Privacy Policy and Data Protection Act 1998_**

This Privacy Policy covers the treatment of personal data which you submit to us. (Personal data is defined as any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual.) We will process any personal data you provide in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998. 

**_Collection of personal data_**

In order for you to request or submit information to us, we may ask you to provide personal data such as your name, postal address, telephone, fax number and e-mail address. When you submit personal data to us, you understand and agree that this data will be supplied to UK Trade & Investment, and the Department of Trade and Industry who will take appropriate steps to ensure that personal data is protected from unauthorised use and kept in a secure manner either electronically or on manual records. 

When you submit personal data to us you understand and agree that: 

  1. The data is to be collected for the purpose of supporting and developing UK inward investment. This may involve the information being used by UK Trade & Investment, Department of Trade and Industry and Foreign and Commonwealth Office of which UK Trade & Investment is a part. It may also involve the transfer of data outside the European Economic Area to our overseas offices. 
  2. In order to provide you with further information and support the personal data may be passed to UK Trade & Investment partner agencies – The Regional Development Agencies in England and the Development Agencies for Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland. 



**_Use of Cookies_**

We do not use cookies to track information about your visit.

**_Change to privacy policy_**

We may revise this Privacy Policy at any time by posting such revised Privacy Policy at www.invest.uk.com. Your continued use of the site will mean you accept those revisions. 

**_Data Controller_**

The data controller is the Department of Trade and Industry and the Foreign and Commonwealth Office. You can write to the Department of Trade and Industry’s nominated Data Protection Officer at Room LG140, 1 Victoria Street, London, SW1H 0ET.
