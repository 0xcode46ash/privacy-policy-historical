# Privacy Policy | UKTODAYNEWS.COM

UK Today News can be reached via e-mail at contact@uktodaynews.com

Our Web server does not automatically recognize any information regarding the domain or e-mail address of each visitor who comes to our web page.

We, at UK Today News, collect no information or details on visitors who browse our web pages.

This is the information we collect :

Cookies: We do not set any cookies.

Ad Servers: We, UK Today News, try as much as we can to get you relevant offers that may be of interest to you, the visitor. UK Today News has relationships with other companies and we allow these companies to place ads on our the web pages of www.uktodaynews.com. Due to your visit to our website,there is a likelihood that ad server companies can collect some information like your domain type, your IP address as well as your clickstream information. 

For further information, we suggest that you check the the privacy policies of: Google Adsense, and Infolinks. 

If, for any reason you feel that www.uktodaynews.com is not following this stated information policy, please contact us at the e-mail address provided above.
