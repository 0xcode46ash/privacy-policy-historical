# Privacy Policy of Unlock4Modem - unlock4modem.in

**Privacy Policy**

We receive and collect visitors information, as and when you visit our site, but we respect the privacy. The information include your IP address, your ISP (internet service provider), the browser you used to visit our site (Internet Explorer, Firefox, Opera, Google’s Chrome etc), the time you visited our site and which pages you visited throughout our site.

We never sell your personal information to third parties.
