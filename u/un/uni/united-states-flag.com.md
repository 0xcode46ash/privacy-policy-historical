# Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. We will not sell or rent your information to anyone. The information you provide us will be used to communicate the status of your order.

We may also send you information, via email, on promotional offers at our store in the future. All such messages have an unsubscribe link, and any request for removal will be respected and acted on immediately. We limit these offers to 4 per year.

To learn more about us, [click here.](https://web.archive.org/web/20121015152528id_/http%3A//www.united-states-flag.com/info.html)
