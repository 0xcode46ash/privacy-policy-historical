# Unishippers - Privacy Statement

Unishippers privacy policy covers treatment of personally identifiable information that Unishippers collects when you are on Unishippers' web site and when you use our services. This policy also includes personally identifiable information that our business partners share or may collect on Unishippers' website.

**Sharing and Disclosure**  
Unishippers will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone. Unishippers may send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when:

  * We have your consent to share information or need to provide your information to provide a service requested by you.
  * We need to send the information to companies (including franchisees) who work to provide a service to you.
  * We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process.
  * We find that your actions on our websites violate the legal terms and conditions of using this web site, or any of our usage guidelines.
  * We are using your information for Unishippers' marketing purposes. To remove yourself from our marketing list please contact webmaster@unishippers.com



**Changes to this Privacy Policy**  
Unishippers may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in this policy we will notify you by posting an announcement on our web pages at least ten (10) days before the substantial change takes effect. Other than as expressly stated above, your personally identifiable information is an asset of Unishippers and may be treated as such for all purposes.
