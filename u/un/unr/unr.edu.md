# University of Nevada, Reno Web Site Gateway Privacy Policy

The following policy describes the information gathering and dissemination practices for the University of Nevada, Reno Web site gateway ([www.unr.edu](http://www.unr.edu/)) and its associated top-level pages: 

**Information gathering**  
The University of Nevada, Reno logs IP addresses ("Internet Protocol" address or internet location) to identify: (1) usage levels of specific areas of our site; (2) origin of our site's audience; and, (3) browser and operating system of site visitors.  We do not associate IP addresses to individuals. As such, visitor sessions will be tracked, but visitors will remain anonymous. 

The University of Nevada, Reno Web Management Team logs search terms entered into the University of Nevada, Reno search engine ([www.unr.edu/search](http://www.unr.edu/search/)). This data is not associated with individual visitors. 

Other than information provided by visitors via e-mail, the University of Nevada, Reno Web site gateway does not collect personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email, address.)

**Use of information**  
The University of Nevada, Reno Web Management Team uses the information described above to tailor content to visitors, ensure broad access to our site regardless of software, monitor site performance and generate aggregate statistical reports. At no time do we disclose site usage by individual IP addresses.

The University of Nevada, Reno does not provide or sell any personally identifiable information to outside entities.

**Security**  
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of data collected. 

**Other site areas, external links**  
University of Nevada, Reno Web site gateway contains links to other independently run Web sites within the university's network and to sites outside the www.unr.edu domain. The Web Management Team is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
