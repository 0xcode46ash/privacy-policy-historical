### Cookies

Cookies are pieces of information that any website may transfer to your browser that are then stored in your computer�s system. Cookies are returned to the website that deposited them when a visitor returns to that site. Our site uses cookies to keep track of your session, to save passwords, and to make sure you are not subjected to repeated advertising. We may also use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests.

Most web browsers are set up to accept cookies. You can, however, reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. If you choose to decline cookies, some of the functionality of the website may be impaired.
