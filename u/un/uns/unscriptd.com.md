# Privacy | Unscriptd

### 1\. Our commitment to privacy

Wikisport Pty Ltd ACN 153 220 225 ( **Wikisport Pty Ltd t/a Unscriptd, we, us, our** ) and its subsidiaries, related entities and affiliates respect your privacy and we are committed to compliance with our obligations under the Privacy Act1988 (Cth) ( **Privacy Act** ).

Please take a moment to read our Privacy Policy as it describes what happens to your personal information (being information or an opinion about you, whether true or not, which identifies you or from which your identity is reasonably identifiable) that is collected via:

  1. the Unscriptd application designed for Apple iOS and Android OS devices and any other software or documentation which enables the use of this App (the **App, Unscriptd App** ), and
  2. the website at www.unscriptd.com (the **Website** ),  
(together, the **Platform** )



### 2\. Definitions

Website means the website at www.unscriptd.com

See our Terms of Use at [here](https://web.archive.org/terms "terms") for the definition of any terms not defined in this Privacy Policy. 

### 3\. Personal Information

Where practicable, we will deal with you anonymously or under a pseudonym. However, due to the nature of our services, where you do not wish to provide us with your personal information, we may not be able to provide you with requested products or services.

**3.1 Personal Information**

"Personal information" is information or opinion about an identified individual, or an individual who is reasonably identifiable.

We collect and hold personal information that is reasonably necessary for, or directly related to, one or more of our functions or activities.

**3.2 Sensitive Information**

"Sensitive information" is information or opinion about an individual which is:

  1. personal information which is also information or an opinion about the individual's racial or ethnic origins, political opinions, political association membership, religious beliefs or affiliations, philosophical beliefs, professional or trade association membership, trade union membership, sexual orientation or practices, or criminal record; or
  2. health, genetic or biometric information about the individual.



We will only collect sensitive information (including health information) about you with your consent, except where we are required or permitted by law to collect your sensitive information without your consent.  You acknowledge that any such information is provided on a purely voluntary basis. 

Please note that the personal information you provide to us may reveal or allow others to identify aspects of your life that are not expressly stated (for example, your name may reveal your ethnic origins). 

### 4\. Collection of your personal information

**Unscriptd** will collect and hold your personal information in a fair and lawful manner, and not in an unreasonably intrusive way.  Where it is reasonably practical to do so, we will collect your personal information directly from you.  However, in certain cases we will collect personal information from third parties, including from publically available sources.

**4.1 Direct collection**

We may collect the personal information you directly give us through some of the following means:

  1. when completing the registration process for the **App** ;
  2. from **App** account opening forms and other related correspondence (whether in writing or electronically);
  3. while conducting customer satisfaction and market research surveys;
  4. whilst administering any of our services;
  5. when you sign up to receive our daily newsletter;
  6. when you enter any promotions or competitions we run from time to time; and
  7. as otherwise required to manage our business.



**4.2 Automatic collection**

We may collect personal information automatically through some of the following means:

  1. we may receive location data passed to us from third-party services or GPS-enabled devices you have set up;
  2. we may collect information about how you use the **App** to assist us to provide you with more tailored services, and to enable us to develop and improve our services;
  3. we may also collect information from the devices and networks that you use to access the **App** to help us develop and secure our services; and
  4. we may use 'cookies' to store and sometimes track information about you, but only on an anonymous basis.



### 5\. Types of personal information we collect

We usually obtain personal information directly from our users via the **Platform** and from maintaining records of information provided in the course of ongoing customer service.  

To use our **Platform** , you will need to provide us with at least your name, your email address and your password.

You may provide further information to allow us to tailor our services. This information can include (but is not limited to) your date of birth; your title, your gender; your postcode; your contact details including mobile phone number and your address.

**5.1 Use Information**

We may collect information about how you use the **App** to assist us to provide you with more tailored services, and to enable us to develop and improve our services.

When you use the **App** , the information we may collect can include (but is not limited to):

  1. the functions you have used;
  2. how you have shared your content;
  3. third party content you have received;
  4. advertisements you have clicked on; and
  5. third party websites you have access to via the **App**. 



We may also collect information from the devices and networks that you use to access the **App** to help us develop and secure our services. The information we may collect can include (but is not limited to):

  1. the internet protocol (IP) address of your computer of the proxy server that you use to access the Internet;
  2. your computer operating system;
  3. your type of web browser;
  4. your mobile device;
  5. your mobile operating system;



the name of your ISP or your mobile carrier. 

We obtain some of this information from third party analytics companies whom we have allowed to access a database of de-identified material of ours de-identified database in order to provide us with this information.

**5.2 Cookies**

We may use 'cookies' to store and sometimes track information about you, but only on an anonymous basis.  A cookie is a small amount of data sent from a server which is stored on your computer's hard drive.  You can set your browser to refuse cookies or to ask you before your browser accepts them. Certain parts of our **Platform** may require a cookie to be activated and, if you choose to reject the cookie, a particular feature may not be available to you.

### 6\. Our purposes for handling your personal information

As a general rule, **Unscriptd** only processes personal information for purposes that would be considered relevant and reasonable in the circumstances. **Unscriptd** will only collect information that is necessary for the purposes described n this Privacy Policy, or other purposes specifically communicated to you. **Unscriptd** uses fair and lawful methods to collect personal information.

Unscriptd collects, holds, uses and discloses personal information to:

  1. provide the **Platform** to you;
  2. enable access to information to you;
  3. enable you to share content;
  4. comply with its legal and regulatory obligations; and
  5. otherwise to manage **Unscriptd's** business. 



### 7\. Use and disclosure of personal information

The primary purposes for which we use and disclose personal information include:

  1. providing you with information available on our Platform;
  2. maintaining user records; and
  3. **App** registration procedures;



We may also use and disclose your personal information we collect for a secondary purpose.  A secondary purpose is one which is related to the primary purpose of collection.  Some of the secondary purposes for which we may use your personal information include:

  1. marketing our services to you;
  2. **Platform** development;
  3. market research; and
  4. to keep you informed of any changes to our business or other offerings that may be of interest to you.



We will not use or disclose your personal information for any other purpose unless you have consented to that use or disclosure.

We may also disclose your personal information to our professional advisors or contractors, to help us run our business. Each of our advisors and contractors is bound by obligations of confidentiality.

Your consent to this use and disclosure is required in order to provide you with access to and services via the **Platform**. If you do not consent to this disclosure, we may not be able to provide the services offered to you in connection with the **Platform**.

**7.1 De-identified Information**

We may use your personal information in de-identified form and to assist us in running our business. We may also provide de-identified information drawn from the Unscriptd App, usually in aggregated form, to third parties. This information may include (but is not limited to):

  1. locations of users;
  2. advertising that users have clicked on; and
  3. functions accessed by users.



**7.2 Value-added messages**

We may use your personal information, including your contact details, to provide you with information about products and services, including those of third parties, which we consider may be of interest to you.

We will not disclose your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

You may opt out at any time if you no longer wish to receive direct marketing information. You can make this request by contacting our Privacy Officer.

### 8\. Accessing  and correcting your personal information

You may contact our Privacy Officer to request access to the personal information that we hold about you and/or to make corrections to that information, at any time.  On the rare occasions when we refuse access, we will provide you with a written notice stating our reasons for refusing access.  We may seek to recover from you reasonable costs incurred for providing you with access to any of the personal information about you held by us.

**Unscriptds** not obliged to correct any of your personal information if it does not agree that it requires correction and may refuse to do so.  If **Unscriptd  **refuses a correction request, we will provide you with a written notice stating our reasons for refusing.

### 9\. Protection of personal information

**Unscriptd** maintains appropriate physical, procedural and technical security for its offices and information storage facilities so as to prevent any loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification of personal information.  This also applies to disposal of personal information.

**Unscriptd** further protects personal information by restricting access to personal information to those who need access to do their job.

### 10\. When does this Privacy Policy not apply?

This Privacy Policy only applies to personal information collected or held by us.  It does not cover third party websites referred to in or linked from the **Platform**.  Those sites may have their own privacy policies.

### 11\. Transfers of personal information overseas

Either:  
We do not disclose any of your personal information overseas.  
OR  
As the internet is a global environment, using the internet to collect and process personal information necessarily involves the transmission of that information worldwide.

### 12\. Resolving personal information concerns

If you have any questions, concerns or complaints about this Privacy Policy, or our use of your personal information, please email our Privacy Officer at privacy@unscriptd.com 

Once a complaint has been lodged, the Privacy Officer will respond to you as soon as possible.  
You may also lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner by telephone: 1300 363 992 or email: [enquiries@oaic.gov.au](mailto:enquiries@oaic.gov.au).

### 13\. Changes

We reserve the right to change the terms of this Privacy Policy from time to time, without notice to you. An up-to-date copy of our Privacy Policy is available on the Platform.
