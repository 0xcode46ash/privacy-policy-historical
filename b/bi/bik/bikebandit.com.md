**_  
BikeBandit.com Privacy Notice_**

This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for BikeBandit.com. This privacy notice applies solely to information collected by the BikeBandit.com web site. It will notify you of the following: 

  
  
1\. What personally identifiable information is collected from you through the BikeBandit.com web site, how it is used and with whom it may be shared.  
2\. What choices are available to you regarding the use of your data.   
3\. The security procedures in place to protect the misuse of your information.   
4\. How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information. 

**_  
Information Collection, Use, and Sharing_**

BikeBandit.com is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. BikeBandit.com only has access to and collects information that you voluntarily give us via email or other direct contact from you. BikeBandit.com does not sell or rent this information to anyone.

BikeBandit.com will use your information to respond to you, regarding the reason you contacted us. BikeBandit.com will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request, e.g. to ship an order.

Unless you ask us not to, BikeBandit.com may contact you via email in the future to tell you about specials, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy.

_**Your Access to and Control Over Information**_

You may opt out of any future contacts from us at any time. You can do the following at any time by contacting us via email at [service@bikebandit.com](mailto:service@bikebandit.com):

\- See what data BikeBandit.com has about you, if any.   
\- Change/correct any data BikeBandit.com has about you.  
\- Have us delete any data BikeBandit.com has about you.   
\- Express any concern you have about our use of your data.

  
_**

Security

**_

BikeBandit.com take precautions to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the BikeBandit.com website, your information is protected both online and offline.

Wherever BikeBandit.com collect sensitive information (such as credit card data), that information is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secure way. You can verify this by looking for a closed lock icon at the bottom of your web browser, or looking for "https" at the beginning of the address of the web page.

While BikeBandit.com uses encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted online, BikeBandit.com also protects your information offline. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, billing or customer service) are granted access to personally identifiable information. The computers/servers in which BikeBandit.com stores personally identifiable information are kept in a secure environment. 

  
**  
If you feel that BikeBandit.com is not abiding by this privacy policy, you should contact us immediately via email at [service@bikebandit.com](mailto:service@bikebandit.com).**
