# SearchBiblio.com - Privacy Policy

[](https://web.archive.org/) |   
---|---  
  
It is [SearchBiblio.com](https://web.archive.org/)'s strict policy to respect the privacy of our users. In cases in which we must collect information from our users, it will only be done as necessary, and with the full knowledge of the user. In no case will we share any personal information with any party outside of SearchBiblio.com, or otherwise make it available outside the scope of our company.

We do collect information and data about searches and site usage, but it will always be used and/or studied in a summary form, especially should we ever share that information with any external party. In no case will we use the data in such a manner that it links a particular search to a specific user.
