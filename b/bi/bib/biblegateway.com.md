# BibleGateway.com: A searchable online Bible in over 35 languages and 50 versions.- Privacy policy

The Bible Gateway respects your privacy, and will never share your email address or other personal information with any third party. 

The Bible Gateway uses [cookies](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTTP_cookie) to store your Bible Gateway preferences. Any information collected in this manner, or which you provide to us, is used solely for the purpose of improving your Bible Gateway experience, and will never be shared with outside parties. 

The [full version](http://www.gospelcom.net/home/privacy/) of our privacy policy goes into more detail. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please [let us know](mailto:privacy@gospelcom.net).
