# BizBash Los Angeles | Events * Meetings * Marketing * Style * Strategy * Ideas

We respect your privacy! The following section explains the type of information we may collect during the normal operation of our Web site and how BizBash Media would use and protect such collected information. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us at 646.638.3600. 

Any personally identifying information which might be collected or made available at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to BizBash will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to or been advised of at the time of collection.

Resale or disclosure of information to third parties: BizBash Media does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected through BizBash.com, with the exception of our Connect feature browser information collected on BizBash.com: BizBash.com analyzes our Web site materials and logs of your visit(s) and usage to constantly improve the value of the content and tools available on our Web site. Our Web site logs are not personally identifiable, with the exception of our Connect feature. 

Privacy of our email lists:   
BizBash Media maintains several email lists to keep visitors and clients informed about breaking news. Individuals must affirmatively ask to join our mailing lists by contacting BizBash Media staff by clicking here. Individuals may also change information or opt-out of our email list by clicking here. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone but our affiliated companies. In addition, we configure our list server software to refuse to divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone but authorized BizBash Media personnel. 

Security:   
BizBash Media handles your information securely. BizBash Media employs security measures to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information we store and transmit. Access to our servers is limited. Our BizBash Media security policy ensures that our personnel maintain the highest standards in working with the data on our servers.
