# Bitstrips: Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 1, 2008 and was last updated December 3, 2009.

### **Privacy Policy**

Please read the Bitstrips.com (the **Service** ) Privacy Policy carefully. It is important that you know how Bitstrips Inc. ( **Bitstrips** ) collects and uses your personal information. By visiting and/or using the Service, you are accepting the practices detailed in this Privacy Policy.   


**Information Collection and Use**   


"Personal Information" means information about an identifiable individual.    


Registration Information

If you choose to create a Bitstrips.com account, you need to submit certain personally identifiable information during registration, such as your username and email address or other contact information. Bitstrips may use your contact information to communicate with you about offers from the Service or its marketing partners. We may also contact you about account updates and Bitstrips-related news.   


Automatically Collected Information

Whenever you interact with or through the Service, Bitstrips and its authorized agents collect certain information, such as your IP address, browser type, geographic location, Service cookie information and the page(s) you request. We study and use this information on an aggregate level to help resolve technical problems, analyze trends and operate the Service.    


We may attempt to use automatically collected information to identify users in instances where we believe our Terms of Use for the Service have been violated. To the extent that this automatically collected information may be used to identify you, we consider it Personal Information.    


**Cookies**

Bitstrips uses cookies that are saved on your computer in order to provide the Service according to your preferences, and to permit Bitstrips to understand how the Service is being used. Some cookies are only stored for the duration of your browser session; other cookies remain stored on your computer after each browser session. Bitstrips cookies do not contain any Personal Information and are analyzed in aggregate.   


Most browsers allow you to control your cookie settings and to delete your cookies, as you choose. However, some of the Service or its services may be unavailable to you.   


**Information Sharing**

Bitstrips does not rent or sell your Personal Information.   


We may share your Personal Information with third party individuals or third party companies for the following purposes:

  * Executing Service hosting and maintenance;
  * Displaying your Bitstrips Profile Info page;
  * Allowing other users to view your Content, as defined in the Bitstrips Terms of Use;
  * Delivering marketing messages through the Service or to your email address;
  * Complying with laws, law enforcement and legal claims against you; or 
  * in the event of the sale of more than half of the assets in or ownership of Bitstrips. 



**Deleting Information**

You may delete your Bitstrips account by emailing [_privacy@bitstrips.com_](mailto:privacy@bitstrips.com).   


**Policy Regarding Children**

Bitstrips is not intended for use by children under the age of 14. If you are under the age of 14, you are not allowed to provide to Bitstrips Personal Information without consent from your parent or legal guardian.   


**Conditions of Use**

Before using the Service, you must read, understand and agree to this Privacy Policy and the Bitstrips Terms of Use.   


**Updates**

This Privacy Policy will be updated from time to time. Please check back regularly to stay informed of changes.   


**Contact Us**

If you have any questions or comments regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our Privacy Officer at [privacy@bitstrips.com](mailto:privacy@bitstrips.com).
