# bimmerfest - Bringing The BMW Community Together - Privacy Policy

  


###  You are visiting a performanceIX site 

Protecting your privacy is important to us. We strive to keep your personal information confidential. By ensuring that you are aware of and understand the performanceIX Customer Privacy Policy, we can provide you with better service. Please take a moment to read the following policy to learn how we handle your personal information. 

###  Information we gather 

When you visit a performanceIX website, we only gather typical non-personal information about your computer. 

We gather things like: 

> **Ø** Operating System  
>  **Ø** Browser type  
>  **Ø** Connection Speed  
>  **Ø** Computer speed and size  
>  **Ø** Monitor resolution  
>  **Ø** IP settings

###  Cookies 

Sometimes we use cookies, which we feel better enable the site and make it more user friendly. Should you decide to disable or reject our cookies, some services may not function as we had intended. 

###  What we do with your information 

The information we gather is used in brainstorming sessions to help us make better decisions regarding the performance of our site. **WE NEVER SELL, LEASE, or RENT ANY INFORMATION WE GATHER TO THIRD PARTIES.**

###  Legal Issues 

Unless some certified legal action requests information, we will not reveal any information we know about our you or any members nor we will use your information for any ill intent.
