# Privacy Policy

                                           Privacy Policy
BioLegend, Inc. values your privacy and makes every effort to safeguard your personal information.
Detailed below is the type of information we collect, why we collect it, and our methods for securing any
confidential information obtained.
Information Collected and How It Is Used
BioLegend, Inc. may need to collect your contact and billing information to process your order or assist
with technical and customer service. In addition, we may use this information for targeted promotions
and to inform you of changes to products, web experience, or other updates that may affect you.
BioLegend, Inc. uses cookies to enhance your experience on our website. This allows you to customize
and personalize your preferences without being required to re-enter information on every page or
during every visit. This also allows product choices to be stored in your cart. We may use statistics
obtained from cookies to determine how users typically use our site and increase its functionality.
Protecting Your Information
BioLegend, Inc. takes protecting the information of those who use our site seriously. We will never sell
your personal information to a third party and will never share your information unless it is necessary
for the completion of your order or request. We adhere to the guidelines we have established to keep
your information secure and continually strive to update our policies as new technology and information
becomes available.
