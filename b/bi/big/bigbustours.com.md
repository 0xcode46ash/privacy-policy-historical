# Big Bus Tours - International - privacy

The content on this website is maintained by the Big Bus Company Ltd trading as Big Bus Tours.

This privacy policy, together with the Terms and Conditions sets out the basis on which any personal data we collect from you, or that you provide to us, will be processed by us. Please read this privacy policy carefully. By visiting this website you are accepting the terms of this privacy policy. 

It is the stated policy of The Big Bus Company Ltd that we will act in accordance with current legislation and aim to meet current best practice with regard to the processing of personal data. 

The Big Bus Company Ltd will not store your credit card information or share your credit card information with any other party. 

  
You may visit this site anonymously as often as you like without providing any personally identifying information (such as your name, address or email address). The Big Bus Company Ltd will not collect personally identifying information from you except when you specifically provide it, and will protect the information received. 

With your consent The Big Bus Company Ltd may gather personal information from you through website sales, surveys, competitions and newsletters. Your name is not generally linked to cookies unless you specifically provide your name as part of surveys, competitions and newsletters. The Big Bus Company Ltd will not share it with third parties without obtaining your consent first.
