# BigTen.org: Privacy Statement.

**PRIVACY POLICY**

BigTen.org. ("BigTen.org") wants you to know how the personal information that you provide to us will be treated. We want to make the time you spend on our site as fun, interesting and informative as possible so we have developed this privacy policy to answer some of the most frequently asked questions about online privacy and to inform of our practices. 

WHAT INFORMATION DOES BigTen.org COLLECT? 

BigTen.org collects information from you at different points on our site, including but not limited to, when you enter sweepstakes or contests, participate in surveys, opt to participate in information exchanges and dialogues and make purchase through our online store. In most instances, we typically ask you for your name, address, e-mail address, and other information necessary to administer the sweepstakes, send information or complete the purchase transaction. 

DOES BigTen.org USE COOKIES? 

"Cookies" are data that are sent to your browser and stored on your computer's hard drive. Cookies allow us to track the activity of our users on our site and note their preferences. Your cookie may be set or updated at different points during your visits to our site. The use of a cookie allows us to deliver customized messages to you when you access our site based on the information that is stored in your cookie. 

Cookies used by BigTen.org do not contain any personally identifiable information, but they do enable us to know how you browse our site and link to others through our site. If you do not wish to accept Cookies, it is possible to configure your browser to reject them. 

HOW DOES BigTen.org USE YOUR INFORMATION? 

BigTen.org uses the information that you provide to us or that is collected through cookies in several different ways. As we explained above, the information that we collect is used to interact with you and provide you with offers and advertisements that may be of particular interest to you We also use it to provide you with services, allow you to interact with others and to shop and order products online. We may also collect data regarding the number of visitors we receive, track traffic patterns, track our users visits to businesses and features on our site and other related site information. We will then use this data to enhance our site to meet the needs and interest of our users. 

WITH WHOM DOES BigTen.org SHARE YOUR INFORMATION? 

BigTen.org does not sell, rent or trade your personally identifiable information to others. However, we may provide aggregate, non-personally identifiable information collected from our users to reputable third-party vendors. 

It is also important for users to be aware that BigTen.org may disclose personal information if required by law or if it in good faith believes that such disclosure is necessary to comply with the law, to enforce our terms of use or to protect or defend the rights or property of BigTen.org, our users or others. 

WHAT HAPPENS WHEN I LINK TO ANOTHER SITE FROM BigTen.org? 

Our site may from time to time includes links to third party Web sites as a service to our users. BigTen.org is not responsible for the content or information or the privacy policy and practices such Web sites which very widely throughout the Internet. 

HOW DO I CONTACT BigTen.org? 

We at BigTen.org welcome the opportunity to hear your questions or comments about our site or privacy policy. Please e-mail us at bigten@winmill.com or write to us at BigTen.org., 2525 Green Tech Drive, Suite B, State College, PA 16803. 

CONSENT TO PRIVACY POLICY AND NOTICE REGARDING CHANGES 

By using BigTen.org, you consent to the use of your information by us in conformance with the above privacy policy. Our privacy policy may change from time to time and those changes will be posted on this page for your information. Therefore, we recommend you review this page on a periodic basis. In addition, your use of this site is subject to our Terms of Use. Please click to review.
