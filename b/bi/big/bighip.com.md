# About BigHip - Our Dedication to Our Customers

Netricsoft, Inc. is strongly committed to protecting the privacy of its consumers. Throughout cyberspace, we strive to provide a safe and secure environment for all users of our products and services. 

The purpose of this BigHip privacy policy is to inform you, as a welcome visitor to BigHip.com, about the type of information we may gather about you, how we may use this information, whether we disclose it to any entity, and the options available to you regarding our use of, and your ability to correct, this information. 

The following divulges our usage practices and tactics in capturing information for Netricsoft, Inc. BigHip offers several opportunities for visitors to join promotional and informational mailings, or to receive assistance in evaluating our email solutions and services. We use this contact information to send mailings about our company, products, and services. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time, and their email addresses will be removed from our database within 72 hours. 

Unsolicited commercial email is forbidden by Netricsoft, Inc. 

| 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and administer our web site. We also track browser types in order to develop an understanding of our visitors' needs, related to web site design. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information. All captured information helps us determine whether our solutions fit your needs, and is used to send updates regarding our company, products, and services. An opt-out option is included at the bottom of every email, and you may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time. Your email address will be removed from our database within 72 hours. 

| 

Client-side cookies are used to verify the login status of customers using products or services linked directly with our website. Client-side cookies are also used to track point-of-entry to point-of-registration, for those users participating in our affiliate signup programs, or to track and measure the success of a particular marketing campaign. The usage of cookies while on our site is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information. All set cookies are temporary; once the user closes their browser, the cookie simply terminates. If a user rejects the cookie, they may still use our site; however, the user may have limited access to the site. 

| 

BigHip is the sole owner of the information we collect. We do not sell, share, or rent this information to others without your express permission, or unless ordered by a court of law. Information submitted to us is only available to the BigHip employees who manage this information, for purposes of contacting you or sending you emails based on your request for information. 

| 

BigHip takes every precaution to protect our users' information. When users submit sensitive information via the website, it is protected both online and off-line. BigHip enforces rigorous internal policies among its employees to maintain the security and privacy of BigHip.com visitors, and is constantly re-evaluating and deploying new technologies to enhance security. 

| 

BigHip provides its users the opportunity to opt-in and opt-out of receiving communications from us. This is made available during sign-up for our email lists, in all email messages delivered from our company, and by emailing us at [support@bighip.com](mailto:support@bighip.com). Once a user opts-out, he or she will no longer receive any future communications from BigHip.

Users who no longer wish to receive our newsletter and promotional communications may unsubscribe at any time, by clicking on the unsubscribe link included in all BigHip mailings, or by sending an email to [unsubsribe@bighip.com](mailto:unsubsribe@bighip.com). Users of our site are always notified when their information is being collected by any outside parties, so they can make informed choices as to whether or not they should proceed with services that require an outside party. 

| 

In the event that BigHip decides to alter its privacy policy, all changes will be listed here. We are committed to keeping our users informed and up-to-date on the type of information we gather, how we might use that information, and whether we will disclose it to anyone.
