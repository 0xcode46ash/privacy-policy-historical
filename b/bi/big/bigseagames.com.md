# Privacy Policy | Big Sea Games

Last modified: July 21, 2009 

Big Sea Games ("Big Sea Games") is owned and operated by Big Fish Games, Inc. ("Big Fish Games"). Big Sea Games and Big Fish Games have created this Privacy Policy to demonstrate our commitment to your privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices. **By visiting Big Sea Games, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Notice.**

## INFORMATION THAT WE MAY COLLECT

  * **Personally Identifiable Information:** When you create a Big Sea Games account (a "Big Sea Games Account"), we will collect your e-mail address and ask that you create an online username. If you contact our customer support team online, we will ask for and collect your first and last name.
  * **IP Address:** When a user visits Big Sea Games, we may collect the IP address that requested the display of this Web site.
  * **Sign Up and Survey information:** Any information you provide to us as part of your use of Big Sea Games will be stored as entered.
  * **Cookies:** Cookies are small data files that are stored on your computer by Web sites. Through your Web browser, cookies are only used by the Web site that wrote the cookie. Data stored in a cookie from Big Sea Games is encrypted and only accessible by Big Sea Games or other Big Fish Games Web sites. Personal information stored in our cookies is limited to your username and the email address associated with your account.
  * **Other Information Collected:** The Web browser or client software that you use to access Big Sea Games may transmit certain geographic information and information regarding the capabilities or features of your computer and/or software to us. We may also track other types of information, such as user behavior and gameplay, how long our users have been members of or participating in Big Sea Games, the last time a user visited Big Sea Games, and whether a user has won any sweepstakes, contest or other promotion featured on Big Sea Games.



## USE OF COLLECTED INFORMATION

We use your username, e-mail address, and password to uniquely identify you as a Big Sea Games user. We may use or display your username for the purposes of rankings, competitions or sweepstakes, chat identification, and/or game high scores. 

We may use your contact information to process our newsletters and/or e-mail campaigns. Your contact information will allow us to notify you about new features, enhancements and the resolution of site problems, handle and/or route your customer support questions and issues, and inform you of upgrade opportunities, sweepstakes, contests, prizes, and/or special events. 

We use cookies to enhance your online experience. The information in cookies enables you to automatically sign in when you visit Big Sea Games, and offers other operational site advantages. 

We may use other identifying information collected to calculate aggregate statistics about our user community or for security purposes. 

## SECURITY OF COLLECTED INFORMATION

We take all reasonable precautions to safeguard the privacy of your data. All data that we collect is stored on one or more secure database servers that cannot be accessed from outside our corporate network. Outside of our network, the only access to this data is that provided through our Web sites. Through our sites, access to personal information is completely controlled by user-defined e-mail addresses and passwords. Within our company, access to data is secured through a private user ID and password known only to key personnel who maintain the database. All employees of Big Fish Games are legally bound by a nondisclosure agreement that prevents the disclosure of any corporate or customer information relating to any Big Fish Games Web site, including Big Sea Games, to third parties. 

## E-MAIL/OPT-OUT

When you create a Big Sea Games Account, you are automatically registered to receive one or more of our newsletters, even if you previously opted out of receiving newsletters at the time you downloaded any software from us. At any time, you may opt-out of receiving these newsletters by selecting the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any newsletter. 

## LINKS TO THIRD PARTY SITES

Web sites that link to or from Big Sea Games may collect personal information about you. This Privacy Policy does not apply to, and cannot control the activities of, other Web sites. 

## SHARING INFORMATION

We will not resell your information to any third parties. We will not share your information with any third parties, except that we may disclose your personal information to third parties who perform services for us. We do not control the activities of such third party vendors. While we cannot guarantee that they will adhere to the same privacy and security procedures as Big Sea Games, we will take all reasonable precautions under the circumstances to ensure the protection of your personal information. We may also disclose your information if there is a violation of the Big Sea Games Terms of Use or if we have a good faith belief that the law requires it. We may also provide other aggregate, non-personally identifiable information about our user community to partners with whom we do business. 

## CHAT & MESSAGING

Big Sea Games may operate message boards and/or chat areas where users can exchange ideas and communicate with each another. When posting to a message board or chat area, the information is being made publicly available in an online environment. Any information posted is the sole responsibility of the individual user. 

## CHILDREN

Big Sea Games does not offer products for purchase by children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 13, you may use and access Big Sea Games only with the involvement of a parent or guardian. 

Big Sea Games respects the privacy of parents and children and is committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act. If a parent believes that his or her child has submitted personal information to Big Sea Games, he or she can contact us via e-mail at privacy@bigseagames.com or postal mail at the address listed below. We will promptly delete the information upon learning that it relates to a child under the age of 13. We recognize the importance of safeguarding the privacy of children and encourage parents to check and monitor their children's use of online activities regularly. 

## CONTACT US

To review, modify or remove the information you have provided to us, or to contact us with any questions or complaints, please e-mail us at privacy@bigseagames.com or write to us at: Big Sea Games Legal-Privacy Department, 333 Elliott Avenue West, Suite 200, Seattle, WA 98119 

## AGREEMENT WITH BIG SEA GAMES POLICIES

By visiting or participating in Big Sea Games, you signify your understanding of and agreement with this Privacy Policy, and to our [Terms of Use](https://web.archive.org/terms/). If you do not agree with our policies, please do not use or participate in any of the products or services featured on Big Sea Games. 

## CHANGES TO PRIVACY POLICY

We may modify this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we post changes to this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "last modified" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to periodically review this Privacy Policy to be informed of how your information will be protected.
