# Big Boss Financial

## Privacy Policy

Purposes of using the personal information This privacy policy sets out how Big Boss Financial Limited uses and protects any information that customers gave Big Boss Financial Limited when they use our service. 

Information of inquiry and disclosing the personal information  
Upon customer's request, we respond to the notification and disclosure of using personal information held by us. We also respond to modify and add the contents, delete account, suspend the use and stop offering information to the third party (hereinafter called "the disclosure").  
For inquiry about the disclosure, please see the below "Contact detail for enquiries about Privacy of Personal information: 

Acquisition of personal information by unrecognized method  
We assure that we will not use Cookie or Web Beacon which is hard to be recognized by customers for collecting personal information. 

Offering personal information to third party  
Big Boss Financial Limited will not disclose customers' personal information to third parties except the following situations;  
* If the customer agreed to do so.  
* If it is required to do to protect human lives, bodies or properties and also hard to get the authorization by the customer.  
* If it is required to disclose or provide by law  


Measures in Safety Management of Personal Information  
For the collected personal information, we are committed to ensuring that we take secure measures to prevent against information leak, damage and lost.   


Note for personal information we collect  
Required information is optional for customers to fill in. However, please note that if required information is not filled in or with wrong information, we may not provide desired services or responses.
