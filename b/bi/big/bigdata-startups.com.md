# BigData-Startups | Privacy Policy | BigData-Startups

### **BigData-Startups-com – Privacy Policy**

At BigData-Startups.com (“BigData-Startups”) we appreciate your readership and support and place a high value on your personal privacy. We respect the personal nature of the information you provide us on our website and faithfully recognize the importance of protecting this information. In adopting this privacy policy (this “Privacy Policy”), our intent is to balance our legitimate business interests in collecting and using your personal information with your reasonable expectations of privacy.

### **When does BigData-Startups collect my personal information?**

You may provide personal information to BigData-Startups by entering and submitting your data in any of the forms on the website. At no time will you be required to share any personal information to gain complete access to our site. BigData-Startups is an open community but email addresses, names and sometimes other information are requested to participate in some of our member-only features.

In addition to basic contact information, our analytics trackers may also collect other information. This may include the operating system you are working on, the Internet browser you are using, and the screen resolution of your computer.

### **How does BigData-Startups use my personal information?**

Depending on the portion of our site that you have subscribed to or interacted with, we may use your information in a number of ways.

  * To send you a (daily) digest of BigData-Startups news stories or newsletter. This subscription is entirely opt-in by the user.
  * To respond to an inquiry or to notify you if needed.
  * When commenting via the Facebook Connect, we do not get access to your email address nor do we have any access to your personal data or private information.
  * To perform data analytics and advertising targeting.



Apart from the data collected in the Big Data Decision Model, we will not sell individual personal data or email addresses or allow them to be used for mailings you have not asked to receive. Users that want to make use of the full features of the Big Data Decision Model are required to accept additional terms and conditions.

To maintain a site that is free of charge, we display advertisements on our website. We also use information you give us through surveys to help our advertisers target the audience they want to reach and to provide you with potentially interesting advertisements.

Unless you give consent, at no time does any party other than BigData-Startups has access to your personally identifiable information and unless we have your consent. No other party than BigData-Startups will ever contact you. The only exceptions to the foregoing are that we may disclose personally identifiable information (i) in response to legal process, for example, in response to a court order or subpoena, (ii) in response to a law enforcement agency’s request, or where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take actions regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of another person, violations of our terms of use, or as otherwise required by law, or (iii) in the event we are acquired by or merged with another company.

Non-personally identifiable information (such as stated above) may be shared in aggregate form with our advertisers, business, or marketing partners but your personally identifiable information will never be disclosed to them.

Third parties and websites that have links on our site may also collect or use information about you. This Privacy Policy does not cover the information practices of other companies.

### **BigData-Startups Comment Policy**

We never censor comments based on political or ideological point of view or of a critical nature. We reserve the right to delete those comments that are off-topic, “spammy” in nature, abusive, use excessive foul language, or include ad hominem attacks.

Once a comment is made public on the BigData-Startups website, we will not remove it by request. As a user, you are responsible for your own communications and are responsible for the consequences of their posting.

Comments will only be considered for removal (i) in response to legal process, for example, in response to a court order, or (ii) in response to a law enforcement agency’s request, or where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take actions regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of another person, violations of our terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.

All comments and other content contributed to BigData-Startups shall adhere to the following policy, and may be edited or removed at BigData-Startups’s discretion if it is deemed to be in violation of this policy:

  1. **Personal Attacks:**  Please don’t engage in any personal attacks, persistent trolling or mindless abuse.
  2. **Hate speech:**  Racism, sexism and homophobia will not be tolerated.
  3. **Language and Threats:  **Please watch your language and respect other people’s views, beliefs and emotions.
  4. **No Spam**
  5. **Smear Tactics:  **Although we acknowledge criticism of our articles and our writers, we will not allow misrepresentation. We will distinguish between constructive arguments and smear tactics.
  6. **Relevancy:**  Please keep conversations relevant.
  7. **Quality:**  We encourage you to take responsibility for the quality of the conversations in which you’re participating.



Although we have a hands-on approach to community engagement, we do sometimes miss problem commenters or trolls. We appreciate your efforts and help to keep the BigData-Startups community environment inviting, insightful and constructive.

### How you can contact us

If you have questions about either this Privacy Policy please email us using our [feedback](http://www.bigdata-startups.com/contact/ "Contact") form. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will update the Privacy Policy modification date below.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of November 23, 2012 and was last updated on September 4, 2013.
