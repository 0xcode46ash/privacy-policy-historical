# Bigger Bolder Baking Website Privacy Policy - Gemma’s Bigger Bolder Baking

**Bigger Bolder Baking**

**Privacy Policy**

Welcome to BiggerBolderBaking.com (the “Site”). Taste Buds Entertainment LLC (the “Company”, “we”, or “us”) values your privacy and security and provides this Privacy Policy (the “Privacy Policy”) to describe the ways in which we may collect, use, and share your personal information. By using or accessing the Site in any way, whether or not you register as a user (a “User”), you agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy.

We may revise this Privacy Policy at any time by updating this document. The most recent version of the Privacy Policy will be posted on the Site under the link “Privacy Policy.” Every time you access the Site you agree to be bound by the current Privacy Policy as posted.

**LAST MODIFIED: JUNE 1, 2016**

  1. **Information we Collect  **
    1. We may collect, store, and use the below personal information from you when you access or visit the Site: 
      1. **Information we automatically collect.** This includes information about your browser, computer or mobile device, and interaction with the Site. We generally collect this through the use of cookies, which are small text files placed on your computer. Cookies do not give us access to your computer but help us optimize the Site experience for you. We may also obtain this information by using clear .gif files, log files, or other tracking devices.
      2. **User account registration information.** This includes any information you submit to the Site when you register a User account, including your name, email address, and other contact and profile information.
      3. **User Content and other information you submit to the Site.** The Site allows members to submit User Content, as that term is defined in the Terms of Use. We will collect all User Content you submit.
      4. **Information you submit when contacting the Company**. If you contact the Company through email, social media, or a contact form, we will collect the information you submit.
      5. **Information collected by advertisers**. Third party advertisers, including Google, may use cookies (small text files that track your browsing history) to serve ads based on your browsing history or interests. For Google based ads, you may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting the Ads Settings portion of your Google account.
  2. **How we Share and Use Personal Information**
    1. We generally use personal information in order to improve the Site and to provide new and unique content submitted by Users. In particular: 
      1. We may share User Content as provided for in the license described in the Terms of Use posted on the Site. This includes derivatives of the User Content. When sharing User Content, we may also include your public User account information.
      2. If you create a User account we may use that information to send you messages about the Site or products we think would be useful to you. You may opt out of Site emails by following the unsubscribe instructions in any email we send. Please note that you may be unable to unsubscribe from administrative messages without deleting your User account.
      3. We may use your personal information in order to optimize and customize your site experience as well as to identify and provide information, offers, products, or services that may be of interest to you.
      4. If you voluntarily participate in any of our surveys, contests, or promotions, we may request additional personal information from you. We may use this information to contact you about the survey, contest, or promotion, to select and notify winners, and offer you products and services or information about the Company.
      5. We may use your personal information to respond to your inquiries, provide customer service, and manage your account with the Site.
      6. We may disclose your personal information if required to do by law or to defend or prosecute a legal action.
  3. **Security  **
    1. The Site is designed to be a community based Site that provides valuable information to Users and visitors alike. Because of this, the Site may contain links to third party websites or applications. Some of these links may be submitted by Users. We do not control the third party websites or applications and can make no guarantee as to the safety or security of our visitors when they leave our Site. Please use caution when accessing any third party links.
    2. We value the security and privacy of our visitors and Users and utilize a variety of electronic and physical measures to protect your personal information from security breaches. However, we cannot guarantee the safety of your personal information and cannot guarantee that networks across which you may transmit information are secure. Therefore, the Company shall under no circumstance be liable for any inadvertent disclosure or breach of your personal information.
    3. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If we determine that we have collected personal information from a child under the age 13, we will promptly delete that personal information. If you believe that we might have any personal information from a child under the age of 13, please contact us at [info@biggerbolderbaking.com](mailto:info@biggerbolderbaking.com).
    4. We encourage you to contact us with questions or comments. You may reach us at [info@biggerbolderbaking.com](mailto:info@biggerbolderbaking.com). You may also contact us at this address if you believe the personal information we have (as it relates to you) is inaccurate or if you wish to inquire about opting out of certain collection practices.
