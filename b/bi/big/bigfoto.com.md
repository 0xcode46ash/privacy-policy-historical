# Privacy, no spam marketing

BigFoto.com respects the privacy of individuals using the site. All information submitted to BigFoto.com is not to be sold or used in any way that violates your right to privacy. Personal information and email addresses are collected for the sole purpose of responding to inquiries. The use of spam marketing techniques is never endorsed by BigFoto.com.

  
We use third-party advertising companies, such as Google, to serve advertisements when you visit our website.   
These companies may use non-personally identifiable information regarding your visit to this   
and other websites, such as the user IP address, pages viewed, date and time of your visit,   
and number of times you have viewed an advertisement (but not your name, address, e-mail   
address or other personal information), to serve advertisements on this or other websites   
that may be of interest to you. In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our   
third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser. If you would   
like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this   
information used by these companies, [ Click Here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).  
As a third-party provider, Google uses cookies to serve advertisements on our website. Google’s use of DART-cookies enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our and other websites on the Internet. Users may deactivate the use of Dart cookies by visiting this website: <http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html>
