# Privacy | Penny Auctions - BidRivals | USA

## General

BidRivals takes great care to protect the privacy of any personal information provided to us by you. The main aim of this policy is to inform you how we will collect and use this information. Please read it carefully before you provide us with any personal information.

## What is Personal Information?

Personal Information is any information collected from you during registration process (name, surname, address, e-mail address and phone number) and your future activity at BidRivals. We will also ask for your payment details but these will be held by our third party payment providers. We do not have access to your payment details.

## Changing/updating personal information 

You can view, change and update most of your personal information at any time by logging on to your BidRivals account. Due to security reasons some personal information can only be changed by contacting our customer support team.

## Closing account

You can close your account at any time by contacting our customer support team.  All of your personal information will be removed from view and access to your account will be blocked as soon as reasonably possible. BidRivals is obliged by Law to store any personal information from closed accounts, this information can be used to prevent illegal activities, enforce our Terms and Conditions, resolve any disputes or problems and assist legal authorities with any investigations. 

## Use of Personal Information

We use your personal information in order to provide you with a fast and efficient service. Collected information is used for direct contact with you, payment processing, keeping track of all of your auctions as well as informing you about the latest products and promotions. By opening an account with BidRivals you also allow us to use your personal information in order to prevent any potential illegal activities and to enforce our Terms and Conditions. 

## Sharing Personal Information

In the event that we are under a duty to disclose or share your personal data in order to comply with any legal obligation, or in order to enforce or apply our Terms and Conditions and other agreements, or to protect our rights or property (this includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for the purposes of verification, fraud protection and credit risk reduction), your personal information might be disclosed to an applicable governmental, regulatory or enforcement authority.

## Use of cookies

 To make your visit on BidRivals website as easy and user friendly, BidRivals is collecting information (cookie) sent from your web browser. This information is used for picking the right site and language of your account, keeping track of all your auctions and to improve our site and offered promotions. If you do not want us to collect cookies you may do so by turning off the cookie collection in your browser settings, please refer to your individual browser instructions. Please be aware that switching off cookies collection may restrict your full usage of BidRivals site.

## Security Measures

Your personal information provided during registration process is password protected and accessible only by you. It is your sole and exclusive responsibility to ensure that your login details are kept securely. Please note that you must not disclose your login details to anyone. BidRivals will never ask you for your password over e-mail or phone. BidRivals is not responsible for any abuse or misuse of your account by third parties due to your disclosure, whether intentional or accidental.

## Consent

By registering an account with BidRivals and taking part in our auctions you accept this privacy policy and you give consent to BidRivals to share your personal information with above mentioned parties. If you do not consent to sharing parts of your information with third parties please contact us immediately or close your BidRivals account.
