# BoostInsider - Boost Your Business Through The Insiders

First of all, you retain full ownership of, and absolute liability for, your channel and your content. You retain complete creative direction and control over your channels and your contents. BoostInsider will not touch your content on any social media channels.

Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:

To categorize your social followers base and assign personalized and focused influencing campaigns to you.   
Your personal information will be kept within our website and will not be disclosed to any third party. 

To improve our website   
We continually strive to improve our website offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you 

To process transactions   
Your information, whether public or private, will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any other company for any reason whatsoever, without your consent, other than for the express purpose of delivering the purchased product or service requested. 

To send periodic emails   
The email address you provide may be used to send you information, respond to inquiries, and/or other requests or questions.
