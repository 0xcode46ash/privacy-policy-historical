# Privacy Policy

bodybuildingforyou.com does NOT collect any personal information about you. 

We do NOT ask you for your phone, address, or any other type of personal information.

We will NEVER discolse or sell your email address to a third party.

In short, you have NO worries about your privacy with BodybuildingForYou.com

bodybuildingforyou.com through various advertising providers may use interest based advertising to serve ads on its web pages.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
