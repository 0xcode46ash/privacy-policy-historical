# Security and Privacy - Borrowell

**Security**

At Borrowell, the security of your information is of the utmost importance. In order to protect your personal information from unauthorized access and use, we use security measures that meet or exceed industry standards for financial institutions.

We have implemented administrative, technical and physical measures to safeguard the personal information in our custody. We restrict access to any personal information on a need-to-know basis and limit access to employees who require access to fulfil their job requirements.

**Privacy**

We know how important your privacy is to you. We will never share or disclose your personal information to a third party without your consent.

The lenders funding your loan will not have access to any personally identifiable information about you. We will provide them with access to anonymized information for statistical purposes.

To learn more about how we protect your information, please review our [Privacy Policy.](https://www.borrowell.com/legal/privacy-policy/ "Privacy Policy")

### At Borrowell, getting a loan is as easy as 1, 2, 3.

  * Apply online in a few minutes. Qualified applicants will  get an obligation-free quote instantly.

  * You’ll securely provide us with a few pieces of information for verification purposes and to set up your account.

  * After you’ve completed all the steps, your money will be directly deposited into your bank account as soon as the next business day.
