# Bountii - Privacy Policy

Bountii Privacy Policy  
---  
Revised September 27, 2007  
At Bountii we recognize the importance of your privacy. This document describes the types and uses of personal information we collect when you use Bountii's services.  
Types of Information We Collect  
On some of our pages we allow you to enter your ZIP code to include shipping and tax costs in product prices that are listed on Bountii.  
We set and access cookies on your computer.  
Bountii's servers automatically record information when you visit our website. This information includes your IP address, browser type, and time of your page request.  
Uses  
We use personal information to provide the pages and services that you request.  
Bountii also uses personal information to analyze site usage and improve the quality of our services.  
We do not share any of your personal information with third parties outside of Bountii.  
If you have additional questions regarding your privacy and Bountii, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20071220044509id_/http%3A//bountii.com/contact.php).
