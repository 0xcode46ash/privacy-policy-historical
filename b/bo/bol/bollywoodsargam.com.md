# Privacy Policy - We are commited to your privacy - Bollywood Sargam

  
**Bollywood Sargam Privacy Policy**

Bollywood Sargam has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: BollywoodSargam.com

**Advertisers**

We are solely dependend on our advertiser for revenue. All advertising is managed by our staff. We use Cookies only for showing contents based on your choice and preferences. There may be some ads that uses cookies for which we have no control. If you do not want cookies, please disable them. 

**Personal Information**

This Web site does request personal information like name and email address for feedback, greetings and joining our mailing list for newsletter. Apart from this we do not collect any information that personally identifies you or allows you to be personally contacted. Your name can be displayed on welcome page, The information stays on your computer and is not send back to our server. We do not track your activities on this website.  
We do not display any personal information without your consent and permission on this Web site, and also we do not share any personal information with any third parties nor do we use any personal information for any purposes. We do not sell, rent any of our visitors information including there email addresses used for feedback or mailing purpose

**External Links**

This site contains links to other sites. Bollywood Sargam is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
