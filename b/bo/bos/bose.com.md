# Bose®: Customer Care, Privacy Statement

|  |  |  |  | At Bose® Corporation we believe that your interaction with us should result in extraordinary and lasting experiences. For that to happen, it's important to establish and maintain your trust. Bose respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information and using it responsibly.  
---  
When we ask for personal information for any reason on this Web site, we will provide a link to this policy statement. Ensuring that you understand how personal information is obtained and used is important to us. The following is an overview of some of the means by which we may obtain customer information:  
•   Online transactions and surveys, electronic resumes and e-mail  
•   Bose® Retail Store Music Theater tickets  
•   Product registration (online and via U.S. mail) and warranty cards  
•   Contest and sweepstakes entries  
•   Telephone, mail and Bose store transactions  
•   Bose credit card applications  
•   Bose music shows/events  
  
For a detailed description of what type of information we obtain at each point of contact, how we use the information and how you can control its use, we invite you to examine our complete corporate privacy policy—the topics addressed are outlined on the left-hand side of this page.  
This policy may be revised from time to time. We will post all changes on this Web site. This policy is specific to Bose Corporation's operations in the United States, and applies to information you submit online as well as through other channels.
