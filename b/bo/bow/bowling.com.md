# bowling.com - Discount Bowling Balls, Bowling Bags, Bowling Shoes, and Bowling Equipment from the #1 online source

Privacy Policy

We will not rent or sell your name, address, email address, credit card information or personal information to any third party without your permission. That pretty much says it all. Your information stays at bowling.com and goes nowhere else. 

If you have purchased from our store or subscribed to one of our newsletters, or entered a contest, we may occasionally update you via email of special opportunities. 

How does bowling.com protect my credit card information?  
bowling.com uses the latest technology to protect your credit card information and we do not release it to any outside organization. When you check out, you'll see the small lock  at the bottom of your browser indicating a secure transaction. We use **SSL Encryption Technology** and automatically switch to secure mode before placing your order, and your personal information is protected with SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption technology.
