_PRIVACY POLICY_**

**Privacy Statement for Box Office Mojo**

Box Office Mojo has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: **Box Office Mojo**. 

We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site.

Our site may use cookies in order to serve you personalized content and enhanced navigation features. If you do not accept cookies, you will not have access to customized content or navigation features.

This site contains links to other sites. **Box Office Mojo** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

We may use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. Ads may contain cookies, and appear to be coming from our site, but in reality they are coming from our partners who are serving ads on our site.

We may, from time to time, ask users for personal information and/or their opinions about our Web site or the subject matter on our Web site (e.g., in the form of polls, specific feedback requests, etc…). All such areas of the site requesting this information are completely optional. Information gathered from these sources are used for general marketing purposes and to enhance our Web site.

**Your Contact Information**

You may, in the course of using our site, choose to provide us with personal information about yourself including name and e-mail address.  We will not sell or give away your name, e-mail address, mailing address or phone number to any third parties.

**Public Forums**

This site may make games, chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. You may not use a false e-mail address, impersonate any person or entity (fictional or real), or otherwise mislead as to the origin of your posted content. 

**Choice/Opt-Out**

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor. 

This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service. 

  1. Send your request to info@boxofficemojo.com.
