# Policy - Big Brand Deals ­ Outlet, Sale, Clearance & Auctions

**Privacy Policy**

At Big Brand Deals, we believe that your personal information is so sensitive, that we've worked hard to create a personalized shopping experience where you don’t provide us with any of these details, period.  Since you shop and communicate directly with the merchants and makers that you know and trust, we are never provided with any of your personal information.  Go ahead, shop with confidence knowing that YOU are in control! 

If you choose to share your E-mail address with us to subscribe to our E-mail newsletter and/or special offers, we will not share your E-mail address with any third-party.

Big Brand Deals does not use cookies or other passive means to collect information about users, other than to track general statistics related to overall traffic to this Web site/newsletter/special offers and our affiliates.

Thanks for visiting us!
