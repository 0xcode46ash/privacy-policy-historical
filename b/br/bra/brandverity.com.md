# Privacy Policy | BrandVerity

## Privacy Policy

BrandVerity provides subscription and contract brand monitoring services. 

### Use of General Customer Data 

BrandVerity will not share, sell or reveal any personal information provided by visitors, including but not limited to your name, email address or other personally identifiable information. We may contact you from time to time to provide you account-specific information or to provide you related information about products or services. 

### Use of Alerting Data

BrandVerity will not share settings used to create and prioritize email alerts. The filter patterns, whitelists and blacklists that you create are considered alerting data. BrandVerity may use, sell or share aggregate alerting data, but will not do so in a way that would allow individual account settings to be determined. 

### Use of Monitoring Data 

From time to time, BrandVerity may use, sell or distribute monitoring data that BrandVerity collected during the normal course of business. 

### Web Visitor Information Collected

We collect and store personal information in a number of ways when you visit our web site. For example, we collect personal information when you: (1) register and set up an account; (2) build alerts; (3) fill out an online form or survey; (4) contact us by e-mail. When you visit our web site, some information is also automatically collected, such as your computer's Internet Protocol (IP) address, your computer's operating system, the browser type, the address of a referring web site, and the time and date of your visit as well as the pages that you visited.
