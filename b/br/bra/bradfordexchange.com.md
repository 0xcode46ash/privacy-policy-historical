# collectiblestoday.com-Customer Service

| 

  
  
Shopping online at collectiblestoday.com is extremely safe. collectiblestoday.com uses the secure Oracle Application Server to protect your ordering information as it comes to us via the Internet. This means that all information, such as name, address and your credit card number enter in the chekout section of our online store is encrypted. VeriSign provides authentication that you are in direct communication with collectiblestoday.com. 

  
  
As an AOL Certified Merchant, we have agreed to abide by AOL's privacy policy. To read AOL's privacy policy, [click here. ](http://www.aol.com/info/privacy.html)

  
  
collectiblestoday.com is a network of companies who specialize in varioushigh quality collectible products such as collectors plates, dolls, figurines, ornaments and music boxes. On occasion you may receive offers from our affiliates for exceptional products that we believe you may enjoy. Our affiliated companies include: 

* The Bradford Exchange
* The Ashton-Drake Galleries
* Ardleigh Elliott & Sons
* Hamilton Collection
* Van Hygan & Smythe
* Hawthorne Village
* Bradford Editions
* StudioHummel

We also occasionally share customer information with carefully screened non-affiliated companies that offer products and services that we believe you may enjoy. If you object to our sharing information about you as a customer with these non-affiliated companies, please 

[email us](https://web.archive.org/ct/cs/cs_contactus.jsp).

  
  
Privacy Policy:  
collectiblestoday.com is the Internet marketing arm of The Bradford Group. We at the Bradford Group are committed to protecting our customers' privacy. We know that you are concerned about how your information is used and shared, and we appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly. The following notice describes our privacy policy.

Information That We Collect From Every Visitor:  
We collect information about the site pages, items, stores, and articles you view. This is not used as personal information, but as aggregate information (group information) that helps us learn more about how people use and react to our site. We also store information about the items you add to your cart so your cart selections are not lost between visits.

Information You Give Us:  
When you place an order, we will need your name, phone number, e-mail address, mailing address, credit card number and expiration date so that we can process and contact you about the order. We will keep a record of this information and the items you purchase. This allows us to process and fulfill your orders and return requests, and to send you information that's of interest.

You do not have to register at our site to place an order, but ordering is much simpler when you are registered. You will chose a login name and receive a password that will allow you to update your personal information at any time in the future. When you log on with your password, your name, address and other contact information will automatically be entered into the correct spaces of your order form. Also, you can log on with your password, and track the status of orders you have given us. Our personal registration services are provided free of charge, and you are under no obligation to purchase anything.

Periodically, collectiblestoday.com will ask customers to participate in contests and surveys. If you participate, the information we collect may be associated with your account, and used to improve your overall customer experience.

Email:  
When you order an item, a catalog, or have a customer service request, we send you an email to confirm that we received your request or to answer your inquiry. These messages are generated automatically, and are sent regardless of no-email requests. If you prefer not to receive other types of email, you can indicate your preference when your receive an email from us. We have an opt-out feature on every email that we mail out. Or, you can respond to an email and request to be removed. You can also use the 

contact us feature on our site or phone us at 1-877-268-6638

Information Sharing:  
We share information about your individual orders with the sister corporation that packs, ships and bills your purchases, and with outside companies on a confidential basis as needed to obtain approval to charge your credit card, to verify your address, and to deliver your order.

We release account and other personal information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with law; enforce or apply our Conditions of Use and other agreements; or protect the rights, property, or safety of collectiblestoday.com, our users, or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection, collecting unpaid purchase balances, and reducing credit risks. This protects you from price increases related to uncollected debt.

Our Wish List service, as well as our business referral program, are provided by outside companies. If you chose to use these optional services, then they may share your information with us, but are not authorized to contact you or provide your information to others without your permission.

We share customer names, addresses, and email addresses with other companies within the Bradford Group, and occasionally with other carefully screened and reputable 3rd parties for marketing and promotional use. You may opt out from receiving email, as explained above. If you prefer not to receive postal mail from us or other companies, you may use the contact us feature on our site or phone us at 1-877-268-6638 to tell us that you don't want your information shared, or that you don't want to receive postal mail from us.

We collect and analyze the aggregate (group) shopping and site usage patterns of our visitors. We use this information to develop a better site for our customers, and reserve the right to disclose it to third parties on an aggregate basis. Site usage patterns for individual customers are protected from this disclosure.

Automatic Information:  
This is information provided by cookies. Cookies are small alphanumeric identifier files that store information. They enable our systems to recognize your browser and to provide important features that personalize our site to make your shopping faster and easier. For example they store your cart information between your visits.  
You can prevent your browser from accepting cookies through its help feature, but that prevents you from shopping on our site. We recommend that you take full advantage of all the features we've designed to make shopping easy and fun.

Children:  
We do not use collectiblestoday.com to knowingly solicit data from, or market to, children under age 13.

Customer Information Transfer:  
As we continue to develop our business, we might sell or buy businesses and assets, including customer information.

Visit us Often:  
Please visit this Privacy Policy frequently, as our business changes all the time, and we update this policy to reflect those changes. By visiting our site you agree that we may collect and use information as stated in our privacy policy. If we change our privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page so you are always informed about what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
