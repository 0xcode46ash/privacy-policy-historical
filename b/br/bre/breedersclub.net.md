**_BreedersClub.net_**  
  
---  
  
**Privacy Policy**  
  
**How we handle your information**  
  
BreedersClub.net provides a classified advertising service, newsletter and other services. Users of this web site should be aware of the following use of their information.

We will not give or provide your financial information (such as credit card or other financially related information) to any third party other than our merchant services provider. Our merchant services provider requires certain information in order to transact business with you. 

When you register as a user of BreedersClub.net or communicate with us in any manner, we do not release your e-mail address and/or any other personal information to any third party.

When you register for our classified ad service, much of the information provided by you to us will be posted in your classified ad and will be available for viewing by the public. Your classified ad will not show your street address. Your email address will not show unless you choose to include it in the description portion of the ad. If you choose to have your telephone number included in your ad, that will be viewable to those visitors viewing your ad.

Should you have any questions about our privacy policy, please e-mail your questions: [click here](https://web.archive.org/web/20051214112045id_/http%3A//www.breedersclub.net/html/contact.htm).
