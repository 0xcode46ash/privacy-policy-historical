**_PetsOnSale.com and BreedersClub.net_**  
  
---  
  
**Privacy Policy**  
  
**How we handle your information**  
  
PetsOnSale.com and BreedersClub.net provides a classified advertising service, newsletter and other services. Users of this web site should be aware of the following use of their information.

We will not give or provide your financial information (such as credit card or other financially related information) to any third party other than our merchant services provider. Our merchant services provider requires certain information in order to transact business with you. 

When you register for our newsletter or communicate with us in any manner, we do not release your e-mail address to any third party.

When you register for our classified ad service, much of the information provided by you to us will be posted in your classified ad and will be available for viewing by the public. Your classified ad will not show your street address. Your email address will show and be available to the public in order for them to contact you. If you wish, you may delete your email address after posting it.

Should you have any questions about our privacy policy, please e-mail your questions: [click here](https://web.archive.org/web/20031203050921id_/http%3A//breedersclub.net/html/contact.htm).
