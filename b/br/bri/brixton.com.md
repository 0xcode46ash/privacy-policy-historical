# Alebra Technologies Inc. Privacy

|  |   


Alebra Technologies Inc. is committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors to our Web site. Please read the following policy to understand how and why your personal information is collected and how it is used by our Web site and our business. This policy may change from time to time, so please check back periodically.

* * *

Alebra may use information collected about you to communicate with you about products, services, and future promotions. Alebra may selectively share customer information, including names and mailing addresses, with reputable third parties and organizations whose products and services we think may interest you, but only with your consent.  If we contract with vendors to assist us in processing information that we collect from you, we require those vendors to maintain the confidentiality of the information we provide and to use the information only for the purposes identified by Alebra. 

If you do not want the information you provide Alebra to be shared with nonaffiliated third parties for marketing purposes, or if you do not want to receive correspondence from Alebra, you may opt out as described below. 

* * *

For online event registration, you will be required to provide contact information as well as additional information so that we can tailor the event to the interests and needs of attendees.  Any time we request information such as an address, we transmit that information in an encrypted format using industry-standard secure socket layer (SSL) encryption. This protects the information from interception and unauthorized use.   


* * *

If you contact Alebra via the Contact Us mechanism that appears throughout this site, you will be required to supply information so that we may properly assess your request and respond to you in a timely manner. 

* * *

Cookies are small amounts of data that are sent to your browser and stored on your computer's hard drive. Alebra may use cookies on this site to allow us to better analyze our site traffic patterns and to provide improved content, products and services  Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent that if you would rather not store the information for future use. If you decide not to accept cookies, you can still use most of the features of our web site. 

* * *

In addition to the uses outlined above, Alebra may, from time to time, use your personal data to send you information about news and events that may be of interest to you. However, we value your interest in and relationship with Alebra and we are committed to protecting your information from unauthorized use. Therefore, we will not share or sell your information to any unauthorized third party without your consent. 

Alebra uses industry-standard practices to protect your information during transmission to us and once it is stored in our systems. However, neither data transmission over the Internet or data storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. When you transmit data over the Internet, you do so at your own risk. In many cases - such as event registration - other options may be available to you such as telephone, mail or fax.

* * *

lebra provides you with the opportunity to opt out of receiving unsolicited marketing mail or to authorizing Alebra to share your personal information with unaffiliated third parties and other organizations. You may have your personal information removed from our database for marketing purposes and request not to receive future Alebra communications or services. To do so, please contact us using one of the options shown on our Contact Us page. 

* * *

Comments, suggestions, feedback, questions and e-mail messages that you send to Alebra shall be deemed to be non-confidential. Alebra shall retain all rights of ownership to such information or materials. Alebra shall have no obligation of any kind with respect to such information or materials and Alebra shall be free to use, display, disclose, reproduce, copy, transform, create derivative works, and distribute the information and materials to others without limitation. Alebra shall be free to use any ideas, concepts, or know-how provided in such information or materials for any purpose whatsoever.  

* * *

By using the Alebra Web site, you consent to the then current Privacy Policy of Alebra Technologies Inc.  We may change our privacy policy at any time and without notice.  If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are aware of them.  Your continued use of the Alebra Web site following the posting of changes to these terms means that you accept these changes.   
If you believe that we have not complied with this privacy policy, please notify us via e-mail at [info@alebra.com](mailto:info@alebra.com).
