# Brighthand - Privacy policy

Brighthand respects your privacy. You will NEVER have to register or provide any personal information in order to browse our website and use the Brighthand discussion boards.

If you believe your privacy has been invaded, please contact us immediately and we will make all attempts to rectify the situation.

**Information about You**

Brighthand may from time to time ask you to provide information about yourself to us on a voluntary basis, through surveys, polls, and as a requirement for entry into Brighthand contests. This information may include your name, address, telephone number, Zip Code, e-mail address, sex, age range, the types of devices you use, planned purchases, and what kinds of features you prefer in a PDA.

Brighthand aggregates this information to determine trends, preferences, and demographics about the handheld community in general. We will use this aggregated information to study the trends of our user community. Occassionally we may provide this aggregate data to the sponsors of Brighthand.

**Your e-mail address**

No one enjoys unsolicited e-mail, or spam. However, Brighthand may use your e-mail address to send you information about Brighthand that we think you might be interested in. You can easily opt-out from receiving e-mail from Brighthand by letting us know by e-mail, mail, or phone (preferably e-mail.)

You can contact us at:

  * **E-mail:** [optout@brighthand.com](mailto:optout@brighthand.com)
  * **Mail:** Brighthand, 6311 Renaissance Way, Atlanta, GA 30308
  * **Phone:** 678-592-HAND (4263)



Brighthand will NEVER sell or share your e-mail address with any outside source without your permission.
