**PRIVACY POLICY**

The BRITA Products Company´s Web servers collect the domain names of visitors to our Web sites. This information is aggregated to measure number of visits, average time spent on the site, pages viewed, etc. BRITA uses this information to measure the use of our sites and to improve the content of our sites.

BRITA´s Web sites may contain links to other sites, and we make every effort to only link to sites that share our high standards and respect for privacy. However, we are not responsible for the content or the privacy practices employed by other sites.

BRITA only collects personal information from our visitors on a voluntary basis. Personal information may include name, title, company, address, e-mail address, phone number, etc. Unless specifically stated, we do not require this information to obtain access to any part of our sites. The personal information we collect is used by BRITA and/or its subsidiaries only for the specific purpose for which it is submitted, as indicated on our Web site. We do not sell or rent or transmit personal information to other organizations.

If you would like to correct your information or remove your name from our records, please e-mail us at [clorox@casupport.com](mailto:clorox@casupport.com) or write us at:

The BRITA Products Company  
P.O. Box 24305  
Oakland, CA 94623-1305

Please give us your full name, e-mail address and phone number in case we have questions.

BRITA does everything it can to keep all information from our online visitors as secure as possible against unauthorized access and use. All information is protected by BRITA's security measures, which are periodically reviewed.

BRITA is committed to the principles of protecting your privacy on the Internet. Our privacy policy follows the Coalition for Advertising Supported Information and Entertainment Goals for Privacy for Marketing on Interactive Media. However, BRITA reserves the right to change, modify, add to or remove portions of this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for any changes.
