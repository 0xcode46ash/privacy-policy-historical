# Brittany Ferries - Security & Privacy

Security & Privacy

At Brittany Ferries we believe that all our customers and other visitors to our site have a right to privacy.

Any personal information you provide will not be passed on to any other parties and you will not receive unsolicited mail from other sources. We will contact you from time to time with details of products we believe will be of interest to you. If you do not wish to receive this or similar information then it is easy to opt out.

All information you submit to us - including all personal and financial details - is transmitted in encrypted form. This encryption process ensures that all information is coded before it is sent across the Internet.
