# Privecy Policy - Bring The Bacon Home

Thank you for reviewing Bring The Bacon Home privacy policy. This site is owned and operated by www.BringTheBaconHome.com (Hereinafter referred to as "BTBH", "We", "Our" or "Us"). 

**WHAT INFORMATION DOES BTBH COLLECT FROM OUR USERS AND HOW DO WE COLLECT IT?**  
BTBH only contacts individuals who specifically request that we do so or in the event that they have signed up to our membership, or have purchased one of our products. BTBH collects personally identifying information from our users during online registration and online purchasing. Generally, this information includes, name, phone number and email address for registration to receive products and membership materials and name, postal address and credit card information when purchasing our products. All of this information is provided to us by you. 

**HOW DO WE USE THIS INFORMATION?**  
This information is used for internal marketing purposes only. BTBH does not sell, exchange, or release your personal information (name, email, phone, mailing address, credit data, etc.). Your information is not shared with any other third party or company outside of billing and shipping purposes. 

**WHAT ARE COOKIES AND HOW DO WE USE THEM?**  
The use of cookies is an industry standard and many web sites use them. Cookies are stored on your computer and not on this site. If you do not wish to receive cookies, or want to be notified of when they are placed, you may set your web browser to do so, if your browser so permits. 

**HOW DO WE STORE YOUR INFORMATION AND HOW CAN YOU UNSUBSCRIBE?**  
Your information is stored at the list server that delivers BTBH updates. Your information can only be accessed by those who help manage those lists in order to deliver email to those who would like to receive BTBH updates. You can remove yourself at any time from our updates by contacting support.
