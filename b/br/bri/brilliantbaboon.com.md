# Privacy policy | Brilliant Baboon

**Privacy Policy**

Marimedia Ltd. and brilliantbaboom.com (“ **we** “, “ **us** ”, the “ **Company** ” and/or the “ **Site** ”, respectively) provides this Privacy Policy to inform you about its policies and procedures regarding the collection, use and processing of information when you visit www.brilliantbaboon.com and/or create an account on the Site.

This Privacy Policy may be amended or updated from time to time. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy, our Terms of Service available at <http://brilliantbaboon.com/terms-of-service/> and DMCA Policy available at [http://brilliantbaboon.com/dmca-policy/](http://brilliantbaboon.com/terms-of-service/)  regularly for any changes. Any term not defined herein is defined in the Site’s Terms of Service.

BY ACCESSING OR USING THE SITE OR SERVICES YOU AGREE TO THE TERMS AND CONDITIONS SET FORTH IN THIS PRIVACY POLICY, INCLUDING TO THE COLLECTION AND PROCESSING OF YOUR INFORMATION (AS DEFINED BELOW). IF YOU DISAGREE TO ANY TERM PROVIDED HEREIN, PLEASE DO NOT ACCESS OR USE THE SITE OR SERVICE IN ANY MANNER WHATSOEVER.

  1. **Use of Cookies.**



Like many websites on the web, we use “Web cookies” and “Adobe Flash Local Shared Objects” to collect information. A cookie is a small data file that we transfer to your computer for record-keeping purposes and it enables us to identify your preferences when you re-visit the Site and keep your specific settings. We use “session ID cookies” to enable certain features of the Site, to better understand how you interact with the Site and to monitor web traffic routing, and aggregate usage of the Site. If you do not want to accept our cookies you can instruct your browser not to, by changing its settings, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the website you visit. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all portions and functionality of the Site.

  2. **Non-Personally Identifiable Information**.



When you access the Site, we may collect the following non-personally identifiable information (“Non-Personal Information”):

    * **Information regarding your use of the Site** ; Information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website or online service.
    * **Links and Third Party Sites** ; The Site may contain links in a format that enables us to keep track of whether you have followed them. Furthermore, the Site may contain information in the form of, but not limited to advertisements, third-party links to third party.


  3. **How We Use Information.**



We use Information for the above purposes and in order to (i) monitor and analyze access and use of Site and for the technical administration of the Site, (ii) improve the Site, (iii) generate and derive useful data and information concerning the interests, characteristics and website use behavior of our users, and (iv) verify that visitors and users to the Site meet the criteria required to process their requests.

  4. **Information Sharing and Disclosure.**


    * **Information** **Related to Advertising**. To support and enhance the Site and services, we may serve advertisements, including third parties advertisements, through the Site. These advertisements are sometimes targeted and served to particular users. Advertisements served through the Site may be targeted to users who fit a certain general profile category may be inferred from information provided to us by a user, may be based on the Site usage patterns of particular users, or may be based on your activity on Third Party Services. We do not provide Personal Information to any ad networks for use other than in connection with the Site.
    * **Submission of Information**. Any information you choose to provide in an application, a comment or a submission should reflect how much you want other users to know about you. We recommend that you guard your anonymity and sensitive information and we encourage users to think carefully about what information they disclose in their applications, comments or submissions.
    * **Aggregate Information**. We may share aggregated information, including reports generated as a result of your access and use of the Site and Service for industry analysis, demographic profiling and other commercial purposes and to deliver targeted advertising about other products and services.
    * **Service Providers**. We may employ third party companies and individuals to facilitate the Service needed to the maintenance of the Site including, without limitation, maintenance, hosting and database management services, web analytics and administration. We may need to share personal information with our service providers in order to provide services to you. We do not authorize our service providers to use the personal information or other information we share with them beyond what is necessary to assist us.
    * **Compliance with Laws and Law Enforcement**. We cooperate with government and law enforcement officials and private parties to enforce and comply with the law. We will disclose any information about you to government or law enforcement officials or private parties as we, in our sole discretion, believe necessary or appropriate to respond to claims and legal process (including but not limited to subpoenas), to protect our or a third party’s property and rights, to protect the safety of the public or any person, or to prevent or stop any activity we may consider to be, or to pose a risk of being, illegal, unethical, inappropriate or legally actionable.
    * **User content**. Some features of the Services allow you to provide content to the Services, such as written comments. All content submitted by you to the Services may be retained by us indefinitely, even after you terminate your account. We may continue to disclose such content to third parties, as described in this Privacy Policy.



We take much caution in implementing and maintaining the security of the Site and your information. We employ industry standard procedures and policies to ensure the safety of our users’ personal information (when applicable), and prevent unauthorized use of any such information. Although we take reasonable steps to safeguard information, we cannot be responsible for the acts of those who gain unauthorized access, and we make no warranty, express, implied or otherwise, that we will prevent such access. If you feel that your privacy was treated not in accordance with our Policy, please contact us directly at admin@brilliantbaboon.com and our privacy officer shall investigate promptly.

  6. **Our Policy Towards Children.**



We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information (“ **Personal Information** “) from children under 13 without the consent of the parent or guardian. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with Personal Information without their consent, he or she should contact us at admin@brilliantbaboon.com. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with Personal Information, we will immediately delete such information from our files.

  7. **Contact Us.**



If you have any concerns or questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@brilliantbaboon.com

We may amend this Privacy Policy at any time.

**Effective as of March, 2015**
