 **Brookstone's Online Information Practices and Privacy Principles:**

 **B** rookstone respects the privacy needs and concerns of our customers. It is our goal to provide you with an enjoyable and satisfying shopping experience, and to meet and exceed your expectations. To accomplish this goal we will at times ask visitors to our Web site to complete surveys, register on the site, and will notify customers who have registered with us of merchandise and services that we believe will be of interest. 

  * We do not participate in bulk e-mail solicitations ("spam").
  * We do not rent or exchange the names, addresses or e-mail addresses of our registered Web site guests.
  * We collect information on the times and ways registered individuals use our Web site, in order to make improvements to the site.
  * Whenever we ask for information from visitors to our Web site, we will seek to clearly disclose the use of that information.
  * We will use your e-mail address only to contact you about merchandise or services you have indicated are of interest to you, and any such communication will include instructions on how to opt out of future communications.
