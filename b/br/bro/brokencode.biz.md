# Privacy Policy – Brokencode™

The [privacy](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/privacy) of our visitors to [www](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/www).brokencode.biz is important to [us](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/us).

At [www](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/www).brokencode.biz, we recognize that [privacy](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/privacy) of your personal [information](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/information) is important. Here is [information](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/information) on what types of personal [information](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/information) we receive and collect when you use and visit [www](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/www).brokencode.biz, and how we safeguard your [information](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/information). We never sell your personal [information](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/information) to third parties.

**Log Files**  
As with most other websites, we collect and use the data contained in log files. The [information](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/information) in the log files include your IP (internet protocol) address, your ISP ([internet service provider](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/internet-service-provider), such as AOL or Shaw Cable), the browser you used to visit our site (such as [Internet Explorer](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/internet-explorer) or Firefox), the time you visited our site and which pages you visited throughout our site.

**Cookies and[Web](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/web) Beacons**  
We do use cookies to store [information](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/information), such as your personal preferences when you visit our site. This could include only showing you a popup once in your visit, or the ability to login to some of our features, such as forums.

We also use third party advertisements on [www](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/www).brokencode.biz to support our site. Some of these advertisers may use [technology](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/technology) such as cookies and [web](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/web) beacons when they advertise on our site, which will also send these advertisers (such as [Google](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/google) through the [Google](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/google) [AdSense](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/adsense) program) [information](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/information) including your [IP address](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/ip-address), your ISP , the browser you used to visit our site, and in some cases, whether you have Flash installed. This is generally used for geotargeting purposes (showing New York [real estate](http://www.brokencode.biz/tag/real-estate) ads to someone in New York, for example) or showing certain ads based on specific sites visited (such as showing cooking ads to someone who frequents cooking sites).

You can chose to disable or selectively turn off our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings, or by managing preferences in programs such as Norton Internet Security. However, this can affect how you are able to interact with our site as well as other websites. This could include the inability to login to services or programs, such as logging into forums or accounts.
