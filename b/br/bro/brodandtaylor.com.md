# Privacy Policy - Brod and Taylor Folding Proofer

Thank you for visiting the brodandtaylor.com website.  We respect your privacy and want to protect your personal information.

You may give us personal information about you, such as your name, your contact email and other contact details on our website. This privacy policy describes how we collect and use your personal information.

**How and where we collect personal information:**

– electronic newsletter opt-in forms

– discussion boards and forums

– chats and blogs

– customer registration

**How we use your personal information:**

The information you provide is confidential. We will never rent, sell, share or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties. We may contact you from time to time regarding your purchases or the services you have subscribed to.

We may also send you promotional announcements from time to time about new products and services that you might find useful.

If you choose not to receive information from us, you may request removal of your contact information from our database at any time by emailing us at contact@brodandtaylor.com or by phoning us (in the U.S.) at (800) 768-7064.
