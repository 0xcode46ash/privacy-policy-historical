# Privacy Policy | Browsefordeals

Browsefordeals recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of personal and financial information. We have instituted reasonable policies and security measures to protect the information you provide for us. If you give us your personal information, then with your consent, we will use this information to keep you informed of our special promotions, otherwise, we may use the information you provide for us only to review our performance and to improve our services.

We do not sell, rent or disclose our client’s names, addresses, email addresses or other personal information to any non-Browsefordeals entity. We may share customer information within our Group, so that you may be informed of other products that may be of interest to you. 

Cookies are pieces of information a web site can send to your computer’s browser for record-keeping purposes. We use cookies to ascertain which web pages are visited and how often, to make our web site more friendly, and to give you a better experience when you return to the website. 

Our site may contain links to and from the websites of our partner networks, advertisers and other third parties. If you follow a link to any of these websites, please note that they have their own privacy policies and that we do not accept any responsibility or liability for these policies. Please check these policies before you submit any personal data to these websites.
