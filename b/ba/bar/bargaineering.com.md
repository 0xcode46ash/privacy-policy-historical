# » Privacy Policy

[Bargaineering](http://www.bargaineering.com/) and [Blueprint for Financial Prosperity](http://www.bargaineering.com/articles) take online privacy extremely seriously and we will actively protect the the privacy of the users of the Bargaineering web site and those who subscribe to the mailing list.

**Subscription Lists & Email Addresses**: We only collect the names and email addresses of subscribers to the email newsletter and hold them confidentially. We will not release or disclose any specific subscriber information under any circumstances unless demanded by law. General aggregate information about the list itself (total number of subscribers, etc.) may be disclosed.

**Cookies:**

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site.
  * Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet.
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).



**Web Server Traffic** : We record information about how people access the web site and this includes your IP address (or DNS), your browser, and your operating system.

**Email** : Email sent to the site is considered property of this site but we will attempt to receive your consent if we decide to publish it.

**Comments** : Comments left on the site are the responsibility of the commenter. We reserve the right to remove comments or edit them due to errors (usually HTML related errors, the WP Markdown plugin has some quirks in it). We won’t censor comments. As for email addresses included in the comments, please see the **Subscription Lists & Email Addresses** entry.

**External Links** : The web site contains many links to other sites and due to the constantly changing nature of the Internet, we make no promises as to the reliability of those sites or their privacy policies.
