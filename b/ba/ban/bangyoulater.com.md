# - BangYouLater.com

  * This document details important information regarding the use and disclosure of user data collected on Bangyoulater.com
  * The security of your data is very important to us at Bangyoulater. We ensure that any information or data given by users will be confidential and will never be given out to any second or third party for commercial or marketing purposes without your consent.
  * This site strictly limits its membership and viewing privileges to adults 18 years of age and over. All persons who do not meet its criteria are prohibited from accessing or viewing the contents of this Site.



DATA COLLECTED

  * Personal Information: Non-Registered users can watch videos without registration and without any information being collected. However the visitor's IP address will be recorded in the episode that there is any embezzlement of information and/or content.
  * Registered Members: Registration is required for accessing privileges such as video downloading. The following personal information is requested at the time of registration: username (required), and email address (required). Additional personal information will not be required but may be added if desired by the user for contact and dating criteria.
  * Content Uploaded to the site: Any personal information or video content that you voluntarily disclose online becomes publicly available and can be collected and used by other members and non-members.
  * Cookies: While visiting Bangyoulater.com, we may send one or more cookies to your computer that identifies your browser session. Bangyoulater.com uses both session cookies and persistent cookies. However if your settings prohibit persistent cookies, some video streaming may not function properly.
  * Log File Information: When you visit Bangyoulater.com, our servers automatically record information that your web browser sends such as your web request, IP address, browser type, browser language, referring URL, platform type, domain names and the date and time of your request.
  * Emails: If you contact us, we may keep a record of that communication.



USES

  * Your Personally identifiable information submitted to Bangyoulater.com is used to provide to the user the website's features and special personalized features.
  * Your chosen username is displayed to other Users alongside the content you upload such as videos and comments. Other Users can contact you through the private messaging system.
  * Any videos that you submit to Bangyoulater.com may be redistributed through the Internet and other mediums for public viewing.
  * We will not use your email address or other personally identifiable information to send commercial or marketing messages without your consent.
  * We may use your email address without further consent for non-marketing or administrative purposes (Website changes, emergencies etc.)
  * We analyze combined user traffic information to help streamline our marketing and hosting operations to better serve our customers.



DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION

  * It is the under duty to do so Bangyoulater.com may release data to comply with any legal obligation, Terms Of Service and protection of property rights. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations including the police and governmental authorities for the purposes of protection against fraud or any other kind of illegal activity whether or not identified in the Terms Of Service. It is Bangyoulater.com's policy, whenever possible and legally permissible, to immediately notify you upon an obligation to supply data to any third party.
  * Should you deliberately upload any illegal material Bangyoulater.com, we shall forward all available information to all relevant authorities and this without notice.



SECURITY

  * Upon registration to Bangyoulater.com, it is your duty to remember your username/password. We ask you not to disclose such information with anyone.



YOUR RIGHTS

You are entitled as a right to access and modify your data by doing so directly on the website. If you run into any problems, don't hesitate to contact us at info@bangyoulater.com
