# RIGHTWARE’S PRIVACY AND COOKIES POLICY – Rightware

Rightware Ltd. (“ **Rightware** ”) appreciates your interest in our products and our website. As Rightware respects your privacy rights and recognizes the importance of protecting the information received from and about you, we have introduced this privacy policy (“ **Privacy Policy** ”) for the purpose of informing You on the collection, use and protection of your personal information while visiting our website and downloading our products.

The information will be used and collected by Rightware Oy, a company duly organized under the laws of Finland, having its registered office at Niittykatu 6 C, 02200 Espoo, Finland. If you have any questions or comments regarding the handling of your personal information please contact us at info (a) rightware.com.

## 1\. Non-personal data collected

Rightware does not collect any identifiable data about you as user of Rightware website.

However, Rightware may use tools or third party analytical software to collect and use certain non-personal data such as website ID and hardware ID which does not enable Rightware to identify you. Rightware may use the collected non-personal data for purposes of analyzing the usage of Rightware website and its products, and managing, providing and further developing products. Rightware will not disclose any of such non-personal data to third parties.

## 2\. Personal data collected

Rightware collects your personal data under this Privacy Policy only when you register to download products offered for download in Rightware website. [If you establish the account with data provided to Rightware by a third party, such as a social networking service, Rightware will receive some of your personal data from such third party service provider.] The following personal information is collected upon your registration:

  * Name
  * E-mail address
  * Phone number
  * Title/Job function
  * Company
  * Address



Rightware shall use the personally identifiable information received from you at the registration for purposes of marketing its products and services to you and notifying you on the issues regarding the products you have downloaded from Rightware. No personally identifiable information shall be disclosed to any third parties.

Your personally identifiable information is retained as long as you are a registered user of the Rightware product you have downloaded. Your personally identifiable information will be destroyed after you have informed Rightware that you no longer are a user of its product.

## 3\. Security

We have security measures in place to protect against unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of the information we hold. When you enter sensitive information on our site, we encrypt that information using secure socket layer (SSL) technology. We restrict access to personal information to a small number of Rightware employees.

We are committed to safeguarding the security of user information that is retained on our servers. Nevertheless, determined individuals may uncover such information and disclose it. For this reason, we cannot guarantee against unauthorized access to the information we hold about you.

## 4\. Cookies

Our website uses cookies. Your consent to our use of cookies in accordance with the terms of this Privacy Policy will be asked when you first visit our website.

A cookie is a file containing an identifier (a string of letters and numbers) that is sent by a web server to a web browser, and stored by the browser. The identifier is then sent back to the server each time the browser requests a page from the server.

Cookies can be used by web servers to identity and track users as they navigate different pages on a website and to identify users returning to a website.

Cookies may be either “persistent” cookies or “session” cookies.  A persistent cookie consists of a text file sent by a web server to a web browser, which will be stored by the browser and will remain valid until its set expiry date (unless deleted by the user before the expiry date). A session cookie, on the other hand, will expire at the end of the user session when the web browser is closed.

We use both persistent cookies and session cookies on our websites.

We may send you the following cookies:

  * PHPSESSID which contains session information
  * LocalStorage which contains information you have earlier filled in our site



How we use cookies. We use “first party” cookies to:

  * Count return visits to our site.
  * Report anonymous statistical information on website usage.
  * Personalize the website and to deliver content specific to your interests.



You may set your browser to block all cookies, but our website may not function properly if you set your browser to block all cookies.

Some pages of our website may serve “third party” cookies to your PC or device. Typically, these cookies are served by other companies and are used to:

  * Serve and track targeted advertising.
  * Track the use of embedded content from other sites such as Google Maps and YouTube.
