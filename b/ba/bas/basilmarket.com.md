Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. The following policy outlines what data we collect and how we use it. 

**Content**  
All content aside from MapleStory images is copyright 10 BasilMarket.com. Use of any BasilMarket.com content without permission is strictly forbidden.

**Data Collected**  
In an effort to protect your privacy, BasilMarket.com only collects data which is absolutely essential for your membership. To this effect, the only data we collect is your email and your IP address. Both items are necessary to recover your account and/or account password in the event that you forget your password.

**Data Set**  
In order to maintain a 'logged in' status, we store a cookie on your computer. Without this cookie you would have to login each time you visited the page. The cookie is encrypted and does not contain any revealing information.

**Data Sharing**  
From time to time when dealing with advertisers we share aggregate demographic information about our users (ie, 30% Female, 70% Male). We do not share specific information about specific users.

**Data Protection**  
Your privacy is important to us, and we strive to protect your private information.

If you have any further questions about this policy, feel free to use the contact form at the bottom of the page.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp).
