# BulkPowders.co.uk : Bodybuilding, Sports and Nutritional Supplements

All personal contact information for an account holder is securely stored on our servers, and will not be divulged to any third party under any circumstances. All information regarding an account holder's purchases will also not be shared to any party; for your own privacy and security, credit card information is not stored server side in our database. We ensure that the privacy of each customer is protected fully. If there are any questions, please contact the [Webmaster](mailto:sales@bulkpowders.co.uk).

Your online transaction takes place in real-time as you place an order, and all the information is encrypted with the industry standard Secure Socket Layers technology by the Protx payment gateway. By the time we receive the order, the transaction is authorized, and once the funds have cleared the item will be dispatched.

BULK POWDERS makes a commitment to protecting your privacy and promises only to use information collected about you in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998.

When you place an Order, we collect certain information and you may submit personal data to BULK POWDERS (for example your name, phone number, postal address, email address). We may also record which products you are interested in and which products you purchase.

We also monitor customer traffic patterns and site use to enable us to improve the service we provide.

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart. Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart.

This information will be kept securely in accordance with our internal security policy and may be used to:

1\. Process your Order;

2\. Provide you with an up to date, efficient, and reliable service;

3\. Open and run your account;

4\. Tell you about changes to our website and to let you know about any new products;

5\. Administer prize draws;

6\. Provide a more personal shopping experience.

By making an Order and submitting your data you agree to this use.

We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent.

**"Cookies"**

"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored on your computer's hard drive by your browser. These allow us to store your account information and provide features to make your shopping on our website easier. Most browsers allow the cookie function to be turned off. If you want to know how to do this look at the help menu on your browser.

BULK POWDERS has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.
