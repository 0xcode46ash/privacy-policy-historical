# Budget Truck Rental: Customer Service

| 

Budget Rent A Car System, Inc. (“Budget”) respects the privacy of every individual who visits our website at www.yellowtruck.com or Budgettruck.com (the "Website"). Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy to inform you as to how we collect, use and to whom we may disclose the information you entrust to us. 

Information We Collect and How We Use It 

You can visit the Website without telling us who you are or providing us with any information. However, we may collect the domain names, IP addresses, host names, and pages requested (not the e-mail addresses) of all visitors to the Website to measure the number of visits, pages visited, average time spent on the Website, etc. We also may collect the e-mail addresses of those who send us e-mail messages. We use this information to continually improve the convenience and functionality of the Website for our customers. 

If you choose to provide us with personal information on the Website, for example by renting a truck, purchasing other goods or services, generating a quote for one of our trucks, or responding to a questionnaire or survey, you are agreeing to share that information with us and with our subsidiaries, affiliates, dealers, and business partners. We may use personal identifiable information about you to improve our marketing and promotional efforts, to statistically analyze Website usage, to improve our content, and to customize the Website's services, content and layout. For example, we may use the information to ensure the proper delivery of information you requested and to provide personalized service. 

When you request a free quote, we need to know your name, e-mail address, phone number, moving from city/state and/or zip code, moving to city/state and/or zip code, moving date, and whether you need a car carrier or towing equipment. This allows us to process and present a quote and to notify you of any changes. If you provide a telephone number, Budget Truck Rental representatives may contact you about information or questions on your truck rental quote or reservation. 

Like many other Internet websites, the Website may use "cookie" technology to measure Website activity and to customize information to your personal tastes. A "cookie" is an element of data that a website can send to your browser, which then stores the "cookie" on your hard drive. When you return to the Website we can recognize you from the information stored in the "cookie" and can tailor information to suit your individual preferences. The goal is to save you time and provide you with a more meaningful visit. You may set your browser to notify you when you receive a "cookie," or to refuse to accept "cookies." However, please note that if you choose not to accept "cookies" from the Website, you may limit the functionality we can provide to you at the Website. 

Collecting Information From Children 

We do not intend to collect personal information from children who identify themselves as being under 18 years of age. Children should not provide personal information on the Website.

Our Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and to ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. Our secure server uses Secure Sockets Layer software ("SSL") which allows us to receive information that your Web browser encrypts and transmits to us. SSL is supported by Microsoft Internet Explorer 4.0, 5.0, and other popular browsers. SSL encryption technology helps to prevent the unauthorized viewing and downloading of your personal information by encrypting your credit card number, name, address, and phone number before they travel over the Internet 

Sharing Information 

We do not intend to sell your personal information we collect to third parties.. 

Budget may also share your information in some of the following ways, unless you tell us otherwise:

**Business Partners & Sponsors:** Budget may disclose some or all of your Personal Information to business partners or sponsors to offer you services or products.  Many promotions offer opportunities to request additional information from sponsors. By you requesting more information about such Promotions, you give Budget permission to transfer your personal information to the sponsor so they can fulfill your request. 

**Third Party & Aggregate Data:** From time to time, you may be offered the opportunity to receive materials or special offers from third parties.  If you opt to receive information from these third parties, Budget will share your name and e-mail address with such third parties. Under confidentiality agreements, Budget may match personal information with third party data. Also, Budget may keep track of what portions of the Web Site users are visiting and other user statistics, and aggregate this information to help us create a better experience for users of our web site. This is all done without knowing your name or other personal information in order to describe our viewers and services to prospective partners, advertisers, and other third parties, and for other lawful purposes. 

**Other:** Budget  may also disclose account information in special cases when we have reason to believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be violating our Terms of Use, or may be causing injury to or interference (either intentionally or unintentionally) with any of Budget’s rights or property, other users, or anyone else. Budget may disclose or access account information when we believe in good faith that the law requires it and for administrative and other purposes that we deem necessary to maintain, service, and improve our products and services. 

  


Other Information Collectors 

Except as otherwise expressly discussed in this Privacy Statement, this document only addresses the use and disclosure of information we collect from you. Our Website may include links to the websites of our business partners, vendors and advertisers. To the extent that you disclose your information to other parties, you are subject to the privacy practices of that party. We cannot determine for you whether these other parties have Privacy Statements similar to ours, and therefore, we cannot be responsible for the content or the privacy practices employed by any other party. 

Your use of the Website constitutes your consent to our collection and use of this information. If we change our Privacy Statement, we will update this page of the Website so you will always be aware of how we use your information. 

**Questions and Contact Information**

If you have any questions, concerns or comments about this Privacy Statement, please contact us.
