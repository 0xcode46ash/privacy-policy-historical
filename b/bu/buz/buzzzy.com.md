# Buzzzy - Privacy Policy.

### Privacy Policy

Buzzzy does not collect personal information such as names, contact details and email addresses, unless this information is provided by you explicitly and intentionally. 

Buzzzy monitors website traffic patterns and collects statistical information, such as the date, time, IP address and the browser used in order to improve the service quality and offer a better user experience. 

Website usage statistics may be provided by Buzzzy to third parties, clients, partners, advertisers and suppliers but they will not contain any specific personal data.
