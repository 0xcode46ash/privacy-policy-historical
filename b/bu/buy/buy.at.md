# Free web redirection to free websites from austrian domains

## Privacy Statement for Rename Limited

Rename has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. 

The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for our service.

### Information Gathered

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information.

Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (e.g. your E-mail address) and customer data. 

The customer's contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. You will receive an E-mail to confirm registration, and subsequent E-mails on a reasonable basis when service improvements are announced and for selected marketing purposes.

At no time however will your personal contact information be given or sold to 3rd parties, all offers will be made only directly from Rename Ltd. 

This site contains links to other sites. Rename is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

### Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All user accounts are password protected, and we endeavour to keep these protected at all times. Users are responsible for keeping passwords secret, and we will only send lost passwords back to the e-mail of the registered account user.

#### Choice/Opt-Out

You may select the delete option in our login service and your account details will be terminated once you have deleted all active Renames.

### Change/Modify

You may select the relevant change options (i.e. personal and account details) in our login service and your details will be appropriately modified.
