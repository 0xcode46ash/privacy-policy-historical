# Privacy Policy Business Directories

This Web site takes every provision to protect our users’ information. When users submit sensitive information via this Web site, their information is protected both online and off-line. Your information is kept and handled on dedicated servers located within the United States that are protected by physical and technological security measures. We use many procedures to safeguard your personal information from unsanctioned access.  While we go to these  lengths to protect your privacy, we in no way guarantee, and you should not have the expectation, that your private data or interactions of information over the Internet will always remain private, due to outlying factors. We may divulge personal information to respond to legal requirements, administer our policies, respond to claims that content infringes upon the rights of others, or to protect anyone’s rights, property, or safety.

####  About 

This local business directory is customized and designed towards promoting local businesses and constructing business to customer relations. We are constantly evolving and updating our site to meet the needs of both the consumer and customer.
