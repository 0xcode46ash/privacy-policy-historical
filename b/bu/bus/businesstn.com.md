# Privacy Policy and Cancellation Policy

**Privacy Policy**

**_Business Tennessee_** magazine does not sell our subscribersâ€™ email addresses, phone numbers or mailing addresses. 

We do not allow access to our subcriber databases by any third party, except for technical maintenance and magazine mailing/fulfillment.

If you chose to purchase items through our site, we may forward your information to third parties for services such as credit card processing and order fulfillment. 

We do not share personal information. We may require you to register to view certain features, or to subscribe to **_Business Tennessee_** , and request personal information such name, address, phone number, email address in order to fulfill your request. 

Completion of other information requests, such as company name, primary business, job title, gross annual revenues or sales for your company, number of employees, and your purchasing influence is optional. This information is used to evaluate our readership demographics to better serve you. We may provide the collective data to advertisers but we do not personally identify users.

 **REFUND AND CANCELLATION POLICY**

**_Business Tennessee_** magazine refund subscriber fees, on a pro-rated usage basis, upon written request for cancellation from you. We do not refund fees for individually purchased articles appearing in Business Tennessee magazine.
