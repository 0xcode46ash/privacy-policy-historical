## [Privacy Policy](http://www.businesspundit.com/privacy-policy/ "Permanent Link to Privacy Policy")

At Business Pundit, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information is gathered from our users and how that information is used by us. By using the site, you are accepting and agreeing to the practices described in this privacy policy. You are advised that this policy may be changed at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page. You are encouraged to review this policy each time you visit this Web site.

Note that this policy does not apply to sites that Business Pundit does not own or control, including any external sites to which we link. When you click a link that takes you to a different site, this privacy policy no longer applies. We are not responsible for the privacy policies of sites to which we link.

**What Information We Collect and How it Is Used**

Each user that visits Business Pundit has some information collected about him/her. This information includes referring URL, IP address/domain name, browser type, screen resolution, language, operating system, and color palette. Certain user behaviors are also tracked, including click paths and time spent on pages. This information is mostly aggregated non-personal information and is used primarily to analyze user behavior and track referring URLs.

**Distribution of Information Collected at Business Pundit**

We reserve the right to disclose the information we collect with government agencies or other third parties helping us to investigate or prevent fraud. We may do so when (1) we are permitted or required to do so by law; or (2) we are preventing or attempting to prevent actual or potential fraud or unauthorized actions; or (3) we are investigating past fraud.

We also reserve the right to disclose the information we collect with third parties for marketing purposes.
