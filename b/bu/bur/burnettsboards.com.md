# FINE PRINT | Burnett's Boards - Daily Wedding Inspiration

_**Full disclosure**_ : Burnett’s Boards is a personal blog written and edited by me. I do occasionally accept some forms of cash advertising, sponsorship, sponsored giveaways, paid insertions, or other forms of compensation, and want you as readers to be aware of this. Burnett’s Boards abides by word of mouth marketing standards and [FTC guidelines](http://ftc.gov/opa/2009/10/endortest.shtm) regarding disclosure of compensation. I believe in honesty of relationship, opinion, and identity, and will never feature content or advertising that is not representative of me or my blog. **When compensation received influences advertising content, topics, or posts made in this blog, this will be fully disclosed. All content, Shop vendor space, or posts for which I received compensation will be clearly identified as sponsored content.** As the owner of this blog, I am occasionally compensated to provide my opinion or recommendation on products, services, web sites, and various other topics. Even though I may receive compensation for some posts or advertising, **I always give my honest opinion, findings, beliefs, or experiences on those topics or products.** The views and opinions expressed on this blog are purely mine. This blog does not contain any content which may present a conflict of interest. For **easy identification of sponsored posts** this phrase (or one similar to it) will be at the bottom of the post: _This is a partnered post._

To find out more information please visit Google DFP privacy policy here:[http://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/](https://connect.emailsrvr.com/owa/redir.aspx?C=sq4rFt9JwEqELV2lhatI77ec0VqNQNAIvPrbdOLth0OUmXw2OpNvy3LaN_vox0tfsuo88UOUr1c.&URL=http%3a%2f%2fwww.google.com%2fintl%2fen%2fpolicies%2fprivacy%2f)._

_**Privacy policy**_ : The privacy of my visitors is extremely important.  **I will never share your personal information** (e.g., email address) with third-parties. I am not responsible for any republishing of content found on this blog on other web sites or media without my permission. Like many other web sites, www.BurnettsBoards.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. **IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.  **

_**Comment policy** :_ Comments are welcomed and encouraged on this site, but there are some instances where comments will be edited or deleted as follows:  **Comments deemed to be spam or questionable spam will be deleted.** Including a link to relevant content is permitted, but comments should be relevant to the post topics. Comments containing language or concepts that could be deemed offensive will be deleted. Comments that attack a person individually will be deleted. You retain copyright to any comments you post. I reserve the right to edit or delete any comments submitted to this blog without notice.
