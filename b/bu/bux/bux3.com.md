# Bux3 - view. click. make money.

## Privacy Policy

This is the web site of Bux3, Ltd. 

We can be reached via contact form. 

**Cookies:** With respect to cookies: We use cookies to store visitors preferences. 

**Mail:** Under no circumstances do we ever ask for your password, account information or anything related to extreme-ads.net via e-mail. We do not send unauthorised emails to our members. 

**Information we collect:** We accumulate and store all user inputted information as well as your IP address browser information & date and time of the record conception upon registration. We never treat users differently in anyway related to sensitive information. 

**Account security:** User information such as your e-mail and paypal addresses will never be revealed. 

**3rd party privacy:** For the privacy acts of 3rd party links to other sites which include banner, popup and text link adverts as well as general advertising links that may be displayed all throughout the Bux3 website we do not take responsibility 

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail at the above address.
