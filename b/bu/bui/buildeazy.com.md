**Privacy Policy**

**General**   
We understand your personal information is important.   
BuildEazy respects your privacy and any information collected by BuildEazy will not be passed onto any third parties. 

**Information collected by third parties**   
Google and the DoubleClick DART cookie.   
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site.   
Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to the users based on their visit to this site and other sites on the Internet.   
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy/ads/). 

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website.   
These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/). 

**Email**   
If you contact BuildEazy, we may keep a record of that correspondence. We do not collect any email addresses (other than opt-in subscribers) of those who communicate with BuildEazy via email. 

**Subscribers**   
You join the BuildEazy Mailing List (become a subscriber) to receive a free newsletter every time we publish a new DIY article or topic of interest. Newsletters may, from time to time also include content as deemed suitable by BuildEazy.   
Your name and email address will not be used for any other purpose.   
You may unsubscribe at any time by following the simple instructions in the emails forwarded to you. Your name or email address will not be disclosed to any third party. 

**Linking to BuildEazy**

You are welcome to link to any BuildEazy™ free plans or projects without permission.   
You my also copy a short excerpt from that particular plan or project to use solely as a description or a summary.
