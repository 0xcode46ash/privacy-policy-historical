# BuildDirect - Privacy Policy

At BuildDirect, you can be comfortable and confident when you’re working with us as you transform your properties with high quality building materials. As we work together, we’re aware that you’re trusting us with your personal information. We take this trust very seriously. 

**What the Privacy Policy Covers:**

Builddirect is dedicated to preserving the security of your personal information at every stage. Attached at the bottom of this page is the BuildDirect Privacy Policy, which outlines:

  * What kinds of information we collect from you
  * How we use that information
  * Who we share it with, and who we don't
  * How BuildDirect protects your personal information
  * How you can access or correct your personal information that we hold
  * How you can opt out of including your information in our secure database



[Read the BuildDirect Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/pdf/privacy-policy-nov-2011.pdf) for details.
