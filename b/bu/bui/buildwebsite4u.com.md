# Build Web Site For You - Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give us will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. 

### Cookies and AdSense

Third parties, such as Google AdSense, may place and read cookies on your browser, or use web beacons to collect information, while serving ads on BuildWebSite4u.com. 

A 'cookie' is a small file containing a string of characters that is sent to your computer when you visit a website. When you visit the website again, the cookie allows that site to recognize your browser. Cookies may store user preferences and other information. You can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some website features or services may not function properly without cookies. 

### External Links

This site contains links to other 3rd party sites. BuildWebSite4u.com is not responsible for the privacy policy or the content of these sites. 

BuildWebSite4u.com may present links in a way that allows us to track whether these links have been followed. We use this information to improve the quality of this site.
