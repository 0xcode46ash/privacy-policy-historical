**Cookies**

BuildaJoomlaWebsite.com uses cookies to store session information so you will not see the same advertisement repeatedly.

**Email Addresses**

BuildaJoomlaWebsite.com has an opt-in email list if you have decided to opt-in by filling out the proper form. Then you will receive emails from BuildaJoomlaWebsite.com every once in awhile. We hate spam and will never rent, sell, trade, or giveaway your email address. 

**External Site Links**

BuildaJoomlaWebsite.com links to other websites on the internet. Content and Privacy Policies are the responsibility of their respective owners.

**Advertisers**

Most advertisements on BuildaJoomlaWebsite.com are provided by outside advertisement companies and may contain cookies. These cookies are collected by their respective companies and are not accessible to BuildaJoomlaWebsite.com.

**IP Addresses and Logging**

BuildaJoomlaWebsite.com server logs information about visitors, including IP addresses, date/time visited, referring website, length of stay, etc. This information is used for site statistics only.

**Customer Information & Credit Card Transactions**

Limited information is supplied to BuildaJoomlaWebsite.com in the event of a sale. This includes your name, IP Address and website domain name. This information is not sold or distributed to third parties. Credit card payments are processed through [Paypal.com](http://www.paypal.com/). This information is collected solely for PayPal to securely process the order, and is not accessible to BuildaJoomlaWebsite.com.
