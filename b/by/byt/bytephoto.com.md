# Digital Camera Photography : reviews, news, photo sharing, forums

BytePhoto.Com does not collect any user specific identifiable information other than what the user specifically volunteers. For example, this information may be volunteered when you register. We do not use this information for any purpose other than its intended use i.e. to allow posting in the forums, uploading photos to the gallery and to allow sending of the newsletter to the email address you maintain in your user profile, etc.) We don't resell names or any individual data. We may report demographic information in the aggregate, but don't share specific information on individual members 

BytePhoto.Com collects some non user specific information such as the type of browser and operating system used. This information is used to assist us internally to provide the best site design and format for our users.

BytePhoto.Com uses cookies to store session information to allow you appropriated access to forums, gallery etc.

BytePhoto.Com logs information about visitors, including IP addresses date/time visited, referring website, length of stay, etc. This information is used for site statistics only.

Limited information is supplied to BytePhoto.Com in the event of a sale. This includes your name, IP Address and the email address that you provided to PayPal.com when making your purchase. This information is not sold or distributed to third parties. Credit card payments are processed through PayPal.com. This information is collected solely for PayPal.com. To securely process the order

BytePhoto.Com links to other websites on the internet. Content and Privacy Policies are the responsibility of their respective owners.

If you have any questions about our privacy policy please contact admin@bytephoto.com
