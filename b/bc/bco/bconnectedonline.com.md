**II. INFORMATION WE COLLECT ABOUT YOU**

General Information  
When you use the Internet, your web browser or client software may transmit certain information to the servers that host the web sites you visit. The information may include the unique number assigned to your server or Internet connection, the capabilities and features of your computer, your geographic location, and your movement within and interaction with the web site, including what pages you look at and what information you download. When you visit Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites, Boyd Gaming Corporation servers collect such information. Boyd Gaming Corporation uses this information to generate aggregate statistics about visitors to Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites.

Personally Identifying Information  
Boyd Gaming Corporation only collects personal information that you voluntarily provide to us or voluntarily provide to other entities who share that information with us. "Personal Information" is information through which you can be identified. The Personal Information that Boyd Gaming Corporation collects through Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites or obtains from its other entities depends on the nature of your online activity, such as whether you make reservations, plan a meeting, sign up for Email List, sign up for a players card, sign up for Bowling League, participate in Online Contests, apply for employment online, make purchases online or request a brochure. Personal Information that Boyd Gaming Corporation may collect from you directly or through other entities, includes your name, address, e-mail address, telephone number(s), fax number, birth date, gender, credit card information, purchase and reservation information and other information that you voluntarily provide. The Personal Information is collected by e-mail, through online forms, and through your activities on Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites.

**III. HOW WE USE THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT**

Boyd Gaming Corporation may use the information collected by us or other entities: (1) for marketing purposes, including, but not limited to, notifying you of special promotions, offers and events at any of our properties; (2) to process and fulfill reservations and make a record of reservations; (3) to enable us to contact you for confirmation or customer service after reservations and purchases are made or after you sign up for or participate in certain activities; (4) to respond to inquiries, complaints and other communications; (5) to cross-reference with other Personal Information that we have acquired about you or may acquire about you through other sources; and (6) for profiling or demographic purposes.

**IV. DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION WE COLLECT**

Except as provided below, Boyd Gaming Corporation will not sell, trade, or disclose to third parties any Personal Information obtained through Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites or obtained from other entities, without your consent. Boyd Gaming Corporation may disclose Personal Information: (1) to comply with a court order, subpoena, search warrant or other legal process; (2) to comply with legal, regulatory or administrative requirements of any governmental authorities; (3) to protect and defend Boyd Gaming Corporation, its subsidiaries and affiliates, and all of their officers, directors, employees, attorneys, agents, contractors, and partners, in connection with any legal action, claim or dispute; (4) to enforce the terms of use of Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites; (5) to prevent imminent physical harm; (6) to businesses that Boyd Gaming Corporation may use for purposes of performing functions on behalf of Boyd Gaming Corporation, such as companies that process credit card transactions; and (7) in connection with the sale, assignment, or transfer of any Boyd Gaming Corporation property or Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Site.

**V. CHOICE REGARDING PROVIDING INFORMATION**

General information is collected whenever you browse Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites. The Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites do not provide any means for opting out of providing general information when you browse Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites. An example of general information would be noting the domain name of the web site from which you linked to our site or advertisement.

You can browse Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites without providing any Personal Information. However, providing Personal Information is required to use some of the services offered through the Boyd Gaming Corporation web sites, including, but not limited to, making hotel reservations, making purchases, requesting information and participating in sweepstakes.

You can opt out of receiving certain communications and offers. Opt out options will be indicated when they are available. 

**VI. ACCESS**

The general or Personal Information collected through Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites or provided by other entities is not currently accessible to you, except that confirmation is provided for some transactions and services, such as reservations and purchases.

**VII. SECURITY**

General Information and Personal Information collected by Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites is stored on secure servers. The secure servers are protected by firewalls and other standard security procedures. The secure servers are not generally accessible by unauthorized third parties, but could become accessible in the event of a security breach.

**VIII. AGE RESTRICTIONS AND THE CHILDREN'S ONLINE PRIVACY PROECTION ACT**

Pursuant to law, persons under the age of twenty-one (21) are not permitted to gamble in casinos and are not permitted to loiter in the gaming areas of any Boyd Gaming Corporation properties. Similarly, Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites are general audience sites intended for persons over the age of 21. Accordingly, the provisions of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act do not apply to Boyd Gaming Corporation Web Sites.
