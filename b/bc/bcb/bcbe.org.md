# BCBE - About our Site - Privacy Statement

privacy statement

Baldwin County Board of Education has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: bcbe.org. 

This site contains links to other sites. **bcbe.org** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

Our online surveys and contact forms ask visitors for contact information (like their email address). The visitors's contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Other data collected may include, but are not limited to: _physical address or telephone number_. 

Security 

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Backups of our contact database information are made and kept on-site in a secure area, and all files are guarded by an encrypted password authentication system. 

Choice/Opt-out 

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about the visitor. 

This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service. 

  1. You can send email to [webmaster@bcbe.org](mailto:webmaster@bcbe.org)
  2. You can send mail to the following postal address:  BCBE Technology, 1091 B Ave., Loxley, AL 36551 
  3. You can call the following telephone number: (251) 972-6850 

Change/Modify 

This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided. 

  1. email [webmaster@bcbe.org](mailto:webmaster@bcbe.org)
  2. send mail to the following postal address:  BCBE Technology, 1091 B Ave., Loxley, AL 36551 
  3. call the following telephone number: (251) 972-6850 ext 7840
