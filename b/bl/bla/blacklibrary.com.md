# Privacy Policy | The Black Library | Bringing the worlds of Warhammer and Warhammer 40,000 to life.

**PRIVACY POLICY**

Games Workshop is committed to protecting your privacy. Games Workshop will only use the information that Games Workshop collect about you lawfully (in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998).

Games Workshop collect information about you for 2 reasons: firstly, to process your order and second, to provide you with the best possible service.

Games Workshop will not e-mail you in the future unless you have given us your consent.

Games Workshop will give you the chance to refuse any marketing email from us. The type of information Games Workshop will collect about you includes: 

  * your name 
  * address 
  * phone number 
  * email address 
  * credit/debit card details 



Games Workshop will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent. 

The information Games Workshop hold will be accurate and up to date. You can check the information that Games Workshop hold about you by emailing us. If you find any inaccuracies Games Workshop will delete or correct it promptly.

The personal information which Games Workshop hold will be held securely in accordance with our internal security policy and the law.

If Games Workshop intend to transfer your information outside the EEA (European Economic Area) Games Workshop will always obtain your consent first.

Games Workshop may use technology to track the patterns of behaviour of visitors to our site. This can include using a "cookie" which would be stored on your browser. You can usually modify your browser to prevent this happening. The information collected in this way can be used to identify you unless you modify your browser settings.If you have any questions/comments about privacy, you should [email](mailto:orders@blpublishing.com) us
