# BLABBERMOUTH.NET

BLABBERMOUTH.NET PRIVACY POLICY 

Last Updated: October 1, 2010 

The owner of BLABBERMOUTH.NET (hereinafter "Blabbermouth") understands your interest in your privacy. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy, and how it applies to the collection and use of information gathered when you visit our site. Your use of this website ("Site") represents your agreement to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you don't agree with any of these terms, please stop using the site. From time to time, our Privacy Policy may be updated and, when it is, we will change the "Last Updated" date above and post the changes to this page. Each visit to the Site by you shall signify your agreement to abide by the Privacy Policy that is in existence at the time of your visit. By visiting and using the Site after an update you signify your agreement to be bound by the updated terms, so please check this page from time to time. 

What information do we collect? 

A. Non-personally identifiable information 

Blabbermouth and/or its website hosting service provider (hereinafter, "we" or "us") may automatically receive and record certain non-personally identifiable information about you as you visit and navigate the Site. Non-Personally Identifiable Information does not reveal your identity but refers, instead , the types of information set forth below in this subsection A, namely: 

Server Log Files. Your Internet Protocol ("IP") address is a number automatically assigned to your computer to enable information to be routed to it as you browse the Internet. We may save log files which record your IP address when you visit our Site. 

Environmental variables. Environmental variables provide us with technical information about your computer, such as computer type and Internet browser type. 

Cookies. Cookies are tiny files that may be saved on your computer that we can read each time you visit our Site. Most or all Internet browsers provide a procedure for you to prevent the use of cookies. If you wish to prevent our cookies, please consult the documentation for your browser. 

Pixel tags. Pixel tags are image files that send information to us about how you use our website. 

In addition we may keep logs which record the search terms you enter into our "Search News" and "Search CD Reviews." These logs may be associated with cookies. 

B. Personally identifiable information 

In addition to the non-personally identifiable information that we may automatically collect, we may collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide. Specifically, when you sign up with the Site to obtain log in privileges, we ask you to supply your email address, a password, your date of birth and your location. Once you log in, you are not anonymous to us. Accordingly, when you log in (1) your otherwise non-personally identifiable information and (2) the reviews, comments, and other information that you may choose to submit to our Site may both become associated with your log in information. 

In addition, if you choose to submit news, we will collect your email address and, if you want to receive credit for the submission, you will be asked to provide your name or nickname, as well any applicable website you wish to provide. We may combine information about you that we collect with information we obtain from business partners or other companies. 

Uses of Collected Information 

Because non-personally identifiable information collected at the Site does not personally non-personally identifiable information with any affiliates and/or other third parties, for any purpose. In general, the following types of information may be used for the purposes stated: 

Server Log Files. We may use your IP address stored in our server log files together with the IP addresses of all visitors to our Site for various administrative and marketing purposes, such as to measure how many people are visiting our website, the pages visited in a session, to help diagnose any problems that Site visitors may have receiving Site information. 

Environmental variables, including, but not limited to, information about your browser type and version, operating system and type of computer you use to access the Site, may help us to provide our website to you in the best format by enabling us to tailor what we send to your particular configuration of hardware and software. 

Cookies, small text files, may help us to recognize you when you return to the Site and is useful in order to help personalize your visits and make navigation easier. In many cases, browser settings and third party software may be used by you to limit or eliminate cookies stored on your computer. 

Pixel tags can help us to track your activity on our website by recording, for example, what website pages you visit. Pixel tags may be associated with cookies. 

Certain advertisements displayed on the Site are hosted by third parties, who use non-personally identifiable information for behaviorally and/or demographically targeted advertising. No personally identifiable information is shared by us with advertisers in this process. Further, to the extent that third party software that resides on your computer or allows streaming collects any information ( non-personally identifiable or personally identifiable), such collection is not controlled by us or this Privacy Policy. 

Generally, personally identifiable information is used for the purposes for which you have provided it (for example, to provide you credit for a news item which you submitted). Your Username and password are used to validate that you are a registered user and authorized to submit a post to the forum. We may also use personally identifiable information for our own internal business analytical purposes. We are not responsible for personally identifiable information, such as your email address, which is disclosed by you in your profile, or when you post comments or other information as permitted by our Site and which are visible to others visiting the Site. If you voluntarily disclose personal information when you submit news or a review, or otherwise voluntarily provide personal information on any other publicly accessible areas on this Site, you should not consider such information as private and it is possible that such information can also be collected and used by others and may result in unsolicited messages from other people. 

Opting in to receive or share information 

We may also provide opportunities for you to opt-in to (1) receive marketing messages from us or from related companies or our advertisers. If you choose to opt-in, we may share your personally identifiable information with such parties to allow them to contact you with information that they believe may be of interest to you. 

In situations where you are given an opportunity to opt-in you will also be provided with a procedure to opt-out of future communications. 

Sharing of Information 

Except as set forth in this Privacy Policy we do not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances: 

A. We may provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with us under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help us communicate with you about offers from us and/or our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information with others; 

B. We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, third party complaints or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; 

C. We will share information when we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, and in connection with suspected fraud, intellectual property infringement, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Blabbermouth's Terms of Use Agreement, or as otherwise required or permitted by law; 

D. We reserve the right to transfer any and all information that we collect from Site users to any affiliated entity or a third party in the event of any reorganization, merger, sale, joint venture, assignment, transfer or other disposition of all or any portion of Company's business, assets or membership interests (including without limitation in connection with any bankruptcy or similar proceedings). 

E. We reserve the right to send you certain communications relating to the Blabbermouth service, such as service announcements, administrative messages, newsletters, notifications of updates to our Terms of Uses Agreement or this Privacy Policy, that are considered part of your Blabbermouth account, and will always offer you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving such communications.

This Privacy Policy applies to our website at www.blabbermouth.net and to its sub pages. 

Links on our website may lead to pages of third parties, such as Roadrunner Records, to Warner Music Group, to our advertisers, to third-party stores and others. This Privacy Policy does not apply to them and any information you provide to them will be subject to their respective privacy policies. 

More generally, this policy does not apply to the practices of companies that we do not own or control, or to people that we do not employ or manage. We may use Google Analytics to aggregate certain information about Site visitors and we refer you to Google Analytics' privacy policy regarding their use of such information. We may let other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to Blabbermouth's cookies. 

Nevertheless, we do work with third party service providers for purposes of hosting and administering this Site and both non-personally identifiable and personally identifiable information may be stored on the server of our service provider. 

Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information And Preferences 

Currently, other than changing your password, there are no preferences for a registered user to select. There is no option for account deletion, however, once logged in, a registered user can change his or her password. 

Data Retention 

The data discussed above that is provide by you may be retained by us for as long as we deem it useful or necessary. Should a specific time period for data storage be mandated by pertinent law or regulation, we reserve the right to comply with same. 

Not Targeting Children 

This Site is not intended for children and we currently do not knowingly collect or aggregate personally identifiable information regarding children younger than 13 years old. If you are under 13 years old, please note that you should not provide any personally identifiable information.

Information sent over the Internet and information stored on our servers connected to the Internet cannot be 100% secure. In addition, to improve the ease of use of our Site, any personally identifiable information (such as your password) that you may provide when using our Site is sent in unencrypted form. Accordingly you have the obligation to keep your password confidential, and if you are concerned that your password or other personal information may be disclosed by providing them to us, you should not provide it.
