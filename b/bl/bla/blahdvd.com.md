# Shop @ BlahDVD for the cheapest DVDs Films Movies Music CDs UK

**Privacy & Security**

**Your Personal Information**

All information you give us is kept completely confidential. 

We will require your name, address and contact details. We do not allow any other party access to this information unless it is necessary to fulfil your order, or unless we should be required to do so by law. We do not sell or pass your information to any other companies for marketing purposes.

If you choose, we may also use this information to contact you from time to time with offers and Product news from Blahdvd.com. 

You can change your personal details or subscribe / unsubscribe to our free newsletters at any time from “Update details” in the “Account” section which can be found at the top of any page on the site.

To complete your order you will have to make payment using a credit or debit card.  
For your added security all payment transactions are processed automatically by HSBC Bank PLC, therefore Blah.com are not party to your personal credit card or bank details.

  
**Site Security**

To protect the integrity of the site and customer confidentiality the latest security and firewall protection is used.

The site is a Verisign Secure Site. To ensure that this is a legitimate Verisign secure site, please check the following:

1\. That the original URL you are visiting comes from www.blahdvd.com  
2\. The status of the server certificate is valid, by clicking on one of the Verisign logos displayed on the site.

**  
Consent to the use of information in the manner described.**

By using the forms on this website and disclosing your personal details you are consenting to the collection, storage and processing of your personal information in the manner described. Should any changes be made to this policy you will be notified by email and relevant pages on the site. At this stage you would have the opportunity to amend your account should you object to any proposed changes prior to their implementation.
