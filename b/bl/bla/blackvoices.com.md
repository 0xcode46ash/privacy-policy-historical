# Black Voices

### blackvoices.com and your privacy

**Information Collection**

When you browse Black Voices you do so anonymously. Personal information - including your e-mail address - is not collected. We do however, collect information on a strictly voluntary basis, in which personal information is supplied by you for purposes such as correspondence, services like "chat," "E-mail," "classifieds," site registration, making a purchase, participating in online surveys, or entering contests. 

Like many other commercial websites, Black Voices may utilize a standard technology called a "cookie" to collect information about how our site is used in order to better serve our online customers. Please read About Cookies for more information. 

**Information Use**

If you have provided Black Voices with your E-mail address, or have done so in the past, it may be used on occasion to send you E-mail offers, notify you of updates to our website, or assist reputable, well-established organizations, that serve as partners and affiliates, to contact you regarding services in which you may have an interest. All offers are carefully developed to ensure that they meet our standards. Our goal is to target offers to people who we believe will find them of value. 

Should you prefer to receive information via U.S. mail, and supplied a postal address at Black Voices, the address will be used to send only the information you specifically requested when supplying your postal address. 

Persons who voluntarily supply Black Voices with a telephone number in order to process orders they have placed online may receive a telephone call from us in order to follow-up on customer service issues, or provide answers to questions. Your name and telephone number will be removed from any shared lists that Black Voices may provide to other organizations. 

**Declining E-mail Offers**

Black Voices provides users with an easy way to decline receiving E-mail offers. 

Although most customers indicate that they appreciate receiving notification of new website additions, contest results, and carefully tailored offers, we recognize the importance of providing you with privacy choices. At any time, you may request to discontinue receiving these offers from us by replying to the E-mail stating your preferences. All E-mail offers you receive from Black Voices will inform you of how to decline receiving further E-mail offers. 

**Children**

Black Voices does not knowingly solicit data from children, nor do we knowingly market to children. 

**Company Commitment to Privacy**

Black Voices is owned by the Trinbune Company. In keeping with the Tribune Company's long-standing leadership in consumer privacy protection, we are actively involved with current industry initiatives to preserve individual privacy rights on the Internet and in all aspects of electronic commerce. Black Voices' Internet Privacy Statement is applicable only to our United States customers and visitors to our website at this time.
