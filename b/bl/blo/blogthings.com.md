# Blogthings Privacy Policy

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click the following links for more information: [Google Advertising Privacy Policy](https://www.google.com/policies/technologies/ads/) and [Commission Junction Privacy Policy](https://se.cj.com/privacy.html).

We collect anonymous usage data in order to optimize the performance of Blogthings.com. **We do not use an outside service for the collection of this usage data and we never share any of this information.**
