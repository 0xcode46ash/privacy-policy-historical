# Blogger Den

Privacy of every individual who visits the website is important for us.

When you visit the site, We will not collect any of your personal data (for example, contact information such as your name, address or phone number) unless you give it to us.

If you contact us by email, fax, post or telephone we will use your contact details in order to handle your issue, request or order. We will also keep your details to be able to contact you in the future notifying of any news or developments relating to www.bloggerden.com.

If you do not wish us to keep your details for these purposes, please let us know. You can do this when you first contact us or at any time after this by using our Contact Us page.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads on our site. These companies may use information (not including your contact information) about your visits to this and other websites to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
