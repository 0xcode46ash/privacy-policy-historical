# BlogPatrol Privacy Statement - The Counter for Blogs

******Privacy Policy**

  * We will never give, sell, or rent your email address to anyone else. Period.
  * We will only ever email you in relation to your account with BlogPatrol.
  * The information collected by BlogPatrol about your site visitors is for your use and information only. This information will never be sold, rented, or given to any other parties.
  * If you have any other questions or concerns about your privacy, please [_email us_](mailto:privacy@blogpatrol.com).
