# BlogTack.com » Privacy Policy

Privacy Statement 

This privacy statement discloses the privacy practices for this website. 

Information 

Respective owners deserve copyright of contents and information available on this site. We will not sell or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. We collects information from user and other sources available at several different sections on our website. If any contact commenced with the reference of this site, there is no responsibility from the site owner. And we never give assurance of any kind of dealing.

Registration and Inquiries 

In order to use some portions of this site, a user must complete the registration form. During registration a user need to give their contact information, such as name, email address. This information is used to contact the user about the interest on our site for which they have expressed. 

Cookies our site 

Cookie is a piece of script to stored data on the user”s hard drive containing information about the user. We never use a cookie to link to any personally identifiable information while user on our site. Once the user closes their browser, the cookie simply terminates. 

Google Ads 

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. 

Google”s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet. 

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy. 

Log Files 

We use IP addresses to analyze visitor”s tendency, movement, and site performance and gather information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to any personal information. 

Links 

This website contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users or visitors to read the privacy statements of any website before visit. 

Special Offers 

We may send all new members a welcoming email. Established members will occasionally receive information on products, services, special deals, and a newsletter. 

Site and Service Updates 

We may also send the user site and service announcement updates, which contain important information about the service. We communicate with the user to provide requested services and in regards to issues relating to their account via email. 

Correction/Updating Personal Information: 

If a user no longer desires to keep his / her information with this website please inform us to remove. If company information or personal information or content changes please inform us to correct. We will endeavor to provide a way to correct, update or remove that personal data provided to us.
