# Privacy Policy | Ways To Make Money Online

Privacy Policy for www.BlogStash.com

I take everyone’s privacy very seriously, just like I would like others to take my privacy seriously. That’s why I think it is important to let you know that some of your personal information is collected and used, just like on most other websites and blogs. And that is why I wrote it in a simple to understand language for everyone to understand everything easily.

Here are a few ways in which your personal information may be collected and used:

**Cookies**  
As you know, I make some money on BlogStash by selling advertisement spaces. Some of these advertisers may use cookies which helps them serve ads that are interesting to you.  Some advertisers may also use technologies to gather information that helps them rate the effectiveness of their advertisements. But, the information gathered don’t include things like your name, address, email address, or phone number.

**Log Files**  
Loge files are used on almost all websites and blogs to gather information and data that helps the owner in tracking and analyzing things like visitors movement on the site, IP address, type of browser and etc. But, these information are not personally identifiable at all, and will not be shared with anyone else.  
****  
If you don’t like the way your information are stored and used, you can manually disable your cookies, If you don’t know how to do this, simply go to your browsers’ respective websites, thee you will find detailed information and step by step guide for disabling your cookies.

If you would like more information, or have any questions about the privacy policy of BlogStash.com, please feel free to contact me at **satrap@blogstash.com**. Thank you for vising BlogStash.com. and I hope you enjoy reading my posts.

Satrap
