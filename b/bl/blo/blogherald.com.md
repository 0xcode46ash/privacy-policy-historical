# The Blog Herald: more blog news more often

####  The Blog Herald Privacy Policy

The Blog Herald provides this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: blogherald.com

**Use of Cookies**

We do not use cookies on this site, however other sites and advertisers that we link to may do so.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We may provide aggregate statistics about our readers and traffic patterns, and related site information to reputable third-party vendors. However, these statistics will not include personal identifying information. 

This site contains links to other sites. The Blog Herald is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites.  
**  
Financial Information**

We collect financial information (such as your credit card information) on an as-needed basis to bill you for products and services you purchase through our site or affiliated sites and to fulfill your orders. If we have trouble processing an order, the contact information discussed immediately above will be used to get in touch with you. We will not sell, share or rent any financial information collected from you except as necessary to fulfill your order. All financial information provided to YNOTMasters is maintained on our secure servers. 

**Online Surveys**

Our online surveys do not record any identifying information. 

**Public Forums**

This site makes comment facilities available to its readers. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. 

When you post to our comment section, your IP is recorded. Note that any info you make available to the public *may* be picked up by search engine spiders. The Blog Herald takes steps to curtail this via META tags and/or robots.txt but cannot control individual spider compliance with industry standards in correctly using the provided tags. 

**Security**

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. 

**Choice/Opt-Out**

This site provides readers the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from The Blog Herald after registering to recieve information. Please email remove@blogherald.com should you wish to opt-out

**Correct/Update**

Whilst the Blog Herald endevours to be accurate in its reports, it is happy to consider letters of reply and corrections.
