# Blogmedia™.com :: Terms of Service -- TOS

Blogmedia.com--Everything you need to start a blog™.

:: Privacy Policy

1\. We do not sell, rent or trade any personally identifiable information you provide when using Blogmedia.com ("Blogmedia"). 

2\. We do not collect any personally identifiable information about you (such as your name or email address) when you use Blogmedia™ unless you specifically decide to provide such information in order to enhance your Blogmedia™ experience. If you choose to provide an email address to subscribe to our newsletter we will not sell, rent, or trade such information.

3\. You are not required to provide an email address to browse Blogmedia™. When you visit Blogmedia™, we place a small text file -- called a "cookie" -- on your computer that allows us to identify your Web browser and gauge bandwidth usage. We use cookies to improve the quality of our service, and optionally store your preferences and settings. Importantly, a cookie does not allow us to obtain any personally identifiable information (such as your real name and address) unless you have specifically provided such information as part of personalizing your Blogmedia™ home page.

4\. When ordering products or services from Blogmedia™.com your name, a valid email address, telephone number, billing and/or mailing address are required to process your order and also to provide you with customer service and technical support. We will not sell, rent, or trade such information.

5\. If Blogmedia™ is ever sold or substantially all of the assets relating to Blogmedia™ are transferred to another entity, we will transfer the information you have provided to the acquiring entity in order to ensure continuity of your personalized service. We recognize and welcome our obligation to comply with the law and this policy is subject to that obligation. We will, therefore, disclose personally identifiable information in response to a request from law enforcement officials or as otherwise required by law, or if we believe it is necessary to protect someone's safety, or enforce our Terms of Service. If we decide to materially change this privacy policy, we will prominently post those changes on this page.
