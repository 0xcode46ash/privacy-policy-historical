# Gay Directory and Searchable Guide of Gay/Lesbian/Bi/Transgender Links by BGLAD

We are committed to protecting our visitor's personal privacy where possible. We do not use any information about where you personally go on this or our partner sites and we do not provide that information to others.

We do not give out, or share, any personal information on anyone who is a member, user, on our mailing list, writes us, participates in discussions, surveys etc. We do not share, sell or give away any mailing list information without prior permission of those on our mailing list or subscribers.
