# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**  
Beyond.com takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy. 

**What this Privacy Policy Covers**  
This Privacy Policy covers Beyond.com's treatment of personally identifiable information that Beyond.com collects when you are on the Beyond.com site, and when you use Beyond.com's services. This policy also covers Beyond.com's treatment of any personally identifiable information that Beyond.com's business partners share with Beyond.com or that Beyond.com may collect on a partner's site.

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Beyond.com does not own or control, or to people that Beyond.com does not employ or manage. 

**Information Collection and Use**  
Beyond.com collects personally identifiable information when you use certain Beyond.com products or services, when you visit Beyond.com pages, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. Beyond.com may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners. 

Beyond.com also automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, Beyond.com cookie information and the page you requested.

Beyond.com uses information for three general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, to fulfill your requests for certain products and services, and to contact you about specials and new products. 

**Information Sharing and Disclosure**  
Beyond.com will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone. However, from time to time, we will provide your email addresses to our shopping mall partners

Beyond.com may also send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when: 

  * We have your consent to share the information; 
  * We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested; 
  * We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; 
  * We find that your actions on our web sites violate our Terms of Service or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services.



**Cookies**  
Beyond.com may set and access Beyond.com cookies on your computer. 

Beyond.com allows other companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to Beyond.com's cookies. 

Beyond.com uses web beacons to access our cookies within and outside our network of web sites and in connection with Beyond.com products and services.

**Security**  
In certain areas, Beyond.com uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.

**Changes to this Privacy Policy**  
Beyond.com may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages. 

**Questions or Suggestions**  
If you have questions or suggestions please send an email to: [privacy@beyond.com](mailto:privacy@beyond.com).
