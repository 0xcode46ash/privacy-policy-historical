# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is incorporated by reference into the Bellashoot Terms of Service. The term “Bellashoot” includes Bellashoot, Inc., our affiliates, and Bellashoot.com. It explains how Bellashoot may collect, use, and disclose information it obtains through the Bellashoot Service.

The “Bellashoot Service” (collectively, the "Service") is defined as any website, mobile application, or Internet service under the control of Bellashoot, whether partial or otherwise, in connection with providing a platform for Bellashoot’s services.

**Policy Scope.** Our Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share and protect information in relation to our web site, mobile services, and any software provided on or in connection with Bellashoot services, and your choices about the us and collection of your information. 

By using our Service you understand and agree that we are providing a platform for you to post content, including photos, videos, comments and other materials ("User Content"), to the Service and to share User Content publicly. This means that other Users may search for, see, use, and/or share any User Content that you make publicly available through the Service, consistent with the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use. Our Policy applies to all visitors, users and all others who access the Service ("Users").

**Personal Information Defined.** Bellashoot considers “Personal Information” to include information that alone or when in combination with other information may be used to readily identify, contact, or locate you, such as: name, address, email address, and/or phone number. Bellashoot does not consider Personal Information to include information that has been anonymized so that it doesn’t allow a third party to identify a specific individual easily. 

#### Personal Information

The Bellashoot Service collects some Personal Information when you register. To register with the Bellashoot Service, you must supply your email address, username and password. We may also collect Personal Information User Content (e.g., first & last name, gender, phone number, photos, videos, comments, and other materials) from you in other ways when you use the Bellashoot Service, such as from your posted content or through your contact with our support team.

#### Location Information

The Bellashoot Service may access your GPS location through your mobile device so you can easily tag your photos to services & stores near you.

#### How to find your friends on Bellashoot:

If you choose, you can use our "Find friends" feature to locate other people with Bellashoot accounts either through:

  * your contacts list - If you elect to find your friends through your device's contacts list, then Bellashoot will access your contact list to determine whether your contact is using Bellashoot.
  * third-party social media sites - If you choose to find your friends through a third-party social media site, then you may be prompted to connect to the third-party service understanding that any information that such service may provide to us will be surely governed by this Privacy Policy.
  * a search of names and usernames on Bellashoot - If you choose to find your friends through a name or username search on Bellashoot then simply type a name to search.



Please note: If you choose to invite someone to the Service through our "Invite friends" feature, you can select a person directly from the contacts list on your device and send a text or email from your account. You understand and agree that you are fully responsible for any and all charges that apply to communications sent to or from your device, and because this invitation is coming directly from your account, Bellashoot does not have access to or control this communication.

#### Cookies, Automatic Data Collection, and Related Technologies.

Additionally, the Bellashoot Service collects and stores information that is generated automatically as you use it, including anonymous usage statistics. To collect this information, the Bellashoot Service may use log files, web beacons, cookies, and other similar technologies.

For example, the Bellashoot Service uses cookies to store users’ preferences, website and/or app activity session information. “Cookies” are bits of information that are stored by your browser or app on your computer or mobile device. This technology allows for the storage of user preferences, user ID, and password. You can turn off the “cookie” function in most current Internet browsers; however, most of the features of the Bellashoot Service may not function properly without them.

The Bellashoot Service automatically collects other system information, such as your web request, IP (Internet Protocol) address, browser language, browser type, domain name(s), referring pages, exit pages and URLs, platform type, number of clicks, pages viewed, landing pages, the order of these page views, the amount of time spent on particular pages, the time and date of your request, and the unique identity of your browser. This information is used to administer the Bellashoot Service, analyze trends, improve the design of the Bellashoot Service, track user movement, and otherwise enhance the Bellashoot Service.

By using the Bellashoot Service, you are authorizing us to gather, parse, and retain data related to the provision of the Bellashoot Service.

#### How We Store Your Information

##### Storage and Processing:

  * Your information collected through the Service may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country in which Bellashoot, a company in the same group of companies as Bellashoot or Service Providers maintain facilities.
  * Bellashoot may transfer information that we collect about you, including personal information across borders and from your country or jurisdiction to other countries or jurisdictions around the world. If you are located in the European Union or other regions with laws governing data collection and use that may differ from U.S. law, please note that we may transfer information, including personal information, to a country and jurisdiction that does not have the same data protection laws as your jurisdiction.
  * By registering for and using the Service you consent to the transfer of information to the U.S. or to any other country in which Bellashoot, a company in the same group of companies as Bellashoot or Service Providers maintain facilities and the use and disclosure of information about you as described in this Privacy Policy.
  * We use reasonable safeguards to help keep the information collected through the Service secure and take reasonable steps (including requesting a unique password) to verify your identity before granting you access to your account. However, Bellashoot cannot ensure the security of any information you transmit to Bellashoot or guarantee that information on the Service may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed.
  * You are responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your unique password and account information, and for controlling access to emails between you and Bellashoot, at all times. If the social media services you connect to Bellashoot with make changes to their services, your privacy settings may also be affected, as we are not responsible in any way for the privacy, functionality, or security measures of any other organization.



#### How We Use Your Information

##### What do we use your information for?

Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:

  * To personalize your experience, remembering info so you don’t need to re-enter it each time you visit (your information helps us to better respond to your individual needs).
  * To develop new features to improve our website through testing & monitoring metrics such as traffic, demographics & number of visitors (we continually strive to improve our website offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you).
  * To improve customer service & fix technical problems (your information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs).
  * To administer a contest, promotion, survey or other site feature.
  * To send periodic emails personalized to your interests.



#### Sharing of Your Information

##### Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties (or the group of companies of which Bellashoot is a part) without your consent, except as noted in this Policy. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

#### What happens if there’s a change in control of the company?

If we sell or otherwise transfer part or all of Bellashoot or our assets to another organization (e.g., in the course of a transaction like a acquisition, merger, bankruptcy, liquidation, dissolution), your information such as User Content, name and email address, and any other information collected through the Service may be among the items transferred or sold.

#### As Required By Law and Similar Disclosures

Bellashoot may access, preserve, and disclose your Personal Information, other account information, and content if it believes doing so is required by law or if those actions are reasonably necessary to:

  * comply with legal process, such as a court order or subpoena;
  * enforce this Privacy Policy or Bellashoot’s Terms of Service;
  * respond to claims that any content violates the rights of third parties;
  * respond to your requests for customer service;
  * respond to law enforcement;
  * investigate and prevent unauthorized transactions or other illegal activities; or
  * protect the rights, property, or personal safety of Bellashoot, our users, or the public.



#### Your Choices About Your Information

##### Your account information and profile/privacy settings:

  * Update your account at any time by logging in and changing your profile settings.
  * Unsubscribe from email communications from us by clicking on the "unsubscribe link" provided in such communications. As noted above, you may not opt out of Service-related communications (e.g., account verification, purchase and billing confirmations and reminders, changes/updates to features of the Service, technical and security notices).
  * If you have any questions about reviewing or modifying your account information, please contact us directly at support@Bellashoot.com.



##### How long we keep your User Content:

  * Following termination or deactivation of your account, Bellashoot may retain information (including your profile information) and User Content for a commercially reasonable time for backup, archival, and/or audit purposes.
  * If you have any questions about termination or deactivation of your account, please contact us directly at support@Bellashoot.com.



#### Children’s Privacy

We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not knowingly collect maintain or solicit any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older. Please DO NOT use or access the Bellashoot service in any manner at any time if you are less than 13 years of age. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child under the age of 13 has obtained a Bellashoot Service account, then you may alert Bellashoot at admin@Bellashoot.com and request that Bellashoot delete that child’s Personal Information from our systems.

Bellashoot may modify or update this Privacy Policy from time to time, so please review it periodically. We may provide you additional forms of notice of modifications or updates as appropriate under the circumstances. Your continued use of Bellashoot or the Service after any modification to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such modification.

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page. We may provide additional forms of notifications of appropriate modifications or updates. Your continued use of Bellashoot or the Service after any modification to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such modification.

If you would like to update or correct any information that you have provided to Bellashoot through your use of the Bellashoot Service or otherwise, or you have suggestions for improving this Privacy Policy, please send an email to [info@bellashoot.com](mailto:info@bellashoot.com)
