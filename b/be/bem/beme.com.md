# What's happening in your world

**1\. This Privacy Policy**   
IPC Media Limited and IPC Electric Limited ("we", "us" and "our"), the producers of this BEME.com web-site ("Web Site"), are committed to protecting your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully as it contains important information about the use of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect about you, and how we collect and use your personal information, including setting out the circumstances where we provide it to third parties. Please also read the [**Terms & conditions **](https://web.archive.org/displayarticle.asp?ChannelID=1&Contentitemid=15271)**** **2\. What personal information do we collect and what do we do with it**   
2.1   
To take advantage of some of our services (such as obtaining a private e-mail address, clippings service, personal horoscope or monthly planner) you will be required to register with the Web Site. We do need to collect your name, username, e-mail address, password, home post code, and date of birth. In addition, we may collect certain optional information such as sex, marital status, nationality, profession, country of residence, internet access availability and education or training background to assist us in making our service more tailor-made for you. The personal information will be collected when you register with the Web Site or when you choose to use one of the services described above on the Web Site. 

2.2  
When you enter a competition, contest or other promotional feature, we may ask for your name, age, address and e-mail address so we can administer the contest and notify winners. Please also read the [**Competition Rules**](https://web.archive.org/displayarticle.asp?ChannelID=1&Contentitemid=15445) ****

2.3   
We will use the registration information [and the information we gather from our affiliates in relation to your purchases] to personalise your experience in the Web Site and to help us select goods and services for inclusion in the Web Site's shop which may be of interest to you. We will also work with carefully selected partners to let you know about other merchandise that might be of interest to you. 

2.4   
We also monitor traffic patterns and usage of our Web Site to help us improve our Web Site design and lay out. We may pass this information to our partners or other third parties but it will not contain any personally identifying information. 

2.5   
We may use your personal information to notify you of other special offers, competitions, quizzes, other promotions or news we are running from time to time on the Web Site or we may pass your details to trusted third parties so that they can also provide you with details of special offers, competitions, quizzes, other promotions or news. If, at any time, you would rather not receive any such information, please send us your full name and address to websupport@BEME.COM. In those circumstances we will need to keep your information on file so that we can deal with any complaints from you as to fulfilment of your orders by any of our affiliates etc, but we will not email you or contact you by telephone, fax or post with any further offers, competitions, quizzes, other promotions or news nor provide your personal information to any third parties. 

2.6   
The Web Site may contain various "opt-out" boxes on the pages where you provide your personal information. If you do not want us to provide your personal information to trusted third parties as may be described on the pages of the Web Site or herein, then you should tick the relevant box(es) on such pages of the Web Site and we will not transfer it to those parties. 

**3\. Your Consent**

3.1  
For the purposes of the Data Protection Act 1998, you acknowledge and agree that: 

3.1.1   
in the course of registering and using this Web Site and any services offered via this Web Site (including the Web Site shop and any competitions), your personal data will be captured electronically or otherwise and transmitted to us or to any third parties as set out above or to third parties who host our web servers; and

3.1.2   
we may send your personal information outside of the European Economic Area for processing. 

3.2   
By using this Web Site, you expressly agree to the collection and use of your information as set out above for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. 

**4\. Our Responsibilities - How does BEME.COM protect customer information?**   
We follow security procedures as required by the UK Data Protection Act 1998, in the storage and disclosure of information which you have given us, to prevent unauthorised access. At times we may let you know about the products or services provided by carefully selected third parties. We will only deal with partners who comply with the UK Data Protection Act 1998, or with whom we are satisfied have appropriate measures to protect data. However in some cases a breach of security may be made by a third party for which we cannot accept responsibility. 

**5\. What are 'Cookies' and how do we use them?**   
Cookies are tiny files planted on your computer's hard drive by web servers. Our cookies do not contain any personally identifying information but they do enable us to recognise your web browser (e.g. Netscape of Internet Explorer) and so, each time you log into BEME.com, your computer will be recognised by our web server. You can set your web browser not to accept cookies. 

**6\. Changes to Privacy Policy**   
IPC reserves the right, without notice to you, to change this Privacy Policy at IPC's sole discretion. IPC will give you as much notice as possible prior to making any amendments, but prior notice may not always be feasible for a variety of reasons. We will post any new Privacy Policy on the Web Sites and it will become effective from the time of posting. Please visit this Privacy Policy on a regular basis to make sure you have read the latest version and you understand what we do with your personal information.
