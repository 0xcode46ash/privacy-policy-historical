# Privacy Policy | BestCompany.com

Thanks for visiting our site! We are dedicated to visibility with how we use any personal information collected on this site. Please read this privacy policy before using the site or submitting any personal information through posting a comment or review of a company. By using the site, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy. These practices may be changed in the future, but if any changes are implemented, they will be posted and will only apply to activities and information going forward, not retroactively. We encouraged you to review the privacy policy at your own time and pleasure when you visit the site to make sure that you understand how any personal information you provide will be used.

### Cookie/Tracking Technology

This site uses cookies and other tracking technology to allow us to enhance your experience. Cookie and tracking technologies are useful for gathering information such as browser type and operating system, tracking the number of visitors to the Site, and understanding how visitors use the Site. Cookies can also help customize the Site for visitors. Personal information is not and cannot be collected via cookies and other tracking technology.

### Third Party Advertising

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

### Collection of Information

We collect personally identifiable information, like names, email addresses, etc., when voluntarily submitted by our visitors. The information you provide is used to fulfill your specific request of posting your comment, and not for any other use.

### Distribution of Information

We will only share collected information with third parties when we are legally required to do so, or when you request us to do so for the purpose of validating your review. Information will never be provided to third parties for marketing purposes.

### Comments and Reviews

We reserve the rights to share your information you provide on our site with credit score companies listed on our site for the sole purpose of assisting communication between current/past clients, and providing a platform for companies/agencies to inform or reconcile any negative past or current situations. We do not sell or rent any of of your information at any time to any other company.
