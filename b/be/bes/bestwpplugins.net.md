# BestWPPlugins.net | Privacy Policy

_Last updated May 13, 2011_

BestWPPlugins.net (“BWP”) has established this privacy policy to demonstrate our commitment to the privacy of the users of our websites. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy, and how we treat personally identifiable information collected from our visitors and users.

 **WHAT THIS PRIVACY POLICY COVERS  
**

This privacy policy covers BestWPPlugin.net’s treatment of personally identifiable information collected by BWP through a website owned and operated by BWP.

This privacy policy does not apply to the practices of companies that BWP does not own or control, or of persons that BWP does not employ or manage, including any third-party content contributors bound by contract and any third-party websites to which the BestWPPlugins.net website may link.

 **COLLECTION & USE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION  
**

You can visit the website of BWP without revealing any personal information. However, BWP needs certain personal information if you wish to purchase certain products, receive the BestWPPlugins.net newsletter, or use certain BWP services.

Where required, this information may include your personal contact information and/or your company contact information. BWP will use this information to reply to your inquiries, to provide you with requested products and services, and to contact you regarding new products and services.

By accessing the services of BWP and voluntarily providing us with the requested personal information, you consent to the collection and use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy.

 **COLLECTION & USE OF NON-PERSONAL INFORMATION  
**

BWP automatically receives and records non-personal information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, cookie information and the page you requested. BWP may use this information to customize the content you see and to fulfill your requests for certain products and services. However, BWP does not connect this non-personal data to any personal information collected from you.

 **INFORMATION SHARING & DISCLOSURE  
**

BWP may disclose your personal information to third parties who work on behalf of BWP to provide products and services requested by you. We will share personal information for these purposes only with third parties whose privacy policies are consistent with ours or who agree to abide by our policies with respect to personal information.

 **BWP** **MAY OTHERWISE DISCLOSE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION WHEN:**

(a) We have your express consent to share the information for a specified purpose.

(b) We need to respond to subpoenas, court orders or such other legal process.

(c) We need to protect the personal safety of the users of our websites or defend the rights or property of BWP.

(d) We find that your actions on our websites violate the BWP Terms of Use document or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services.

 **CONSENT**

If you do not consent to the collection, use or disclosure of your personal information as outlined in this policy, please do not provide any personal information to BWP. If you have provided personal information to BWP and no longer consent to its use or disclosure as outlined herein, please [notify us](http://bestwpplugins.net/contact "Notify Us").

 **SECURITY**

Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet can be considered 100% secure. However, your BWP Information is protected for your privacy and security. In certain areas of our websites, as identified on the site, BWP may use industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.

We also safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, through access control procedures, network firewalls and physical security measures.

Further, BWP retains your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes identified above or as required by law.

 **CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY  
**

BWP may at any time, without notice to you and in its sole discretion, amend this policy from time to time. When it does, BWP will also revise the “last update” date at the top of this Privacy Policy. Please review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the BestWPPlugins.net website after any such amendments signifies your acceptance thereof.

 **QUESTIONS OR SUGGESTIONS**

If you have questions or suggestions about this privacy policy, or your own personal information, please [contact us directly](http://bestwpplugins.net/contact "Contact Us Directly").
