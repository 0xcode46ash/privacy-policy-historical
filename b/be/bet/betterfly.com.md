# Privacy Policy | Betterfly.com

Date of Last Revision: September 3, 2011

Welcome to Betterfly.com™, a website that serves as an online community and platform for people who want to learn, look, and feel better and the people who can help them. Betterfly.com is operated by Betterfly, Inc., a Delaware Corporation (hereafter referred to as “Betterfly”, “we”, “us”, or “our”).Your privacy is important to Betterfly. This Privacy and Information Security Policy ("Privacy Policy", "the Policy") is designed to help you understand how we collect and use information. This Policy is part of and incorporated into the Betterfly Terms of Use.

**Acknowledgement and Acceptance of the Privacy Policy**

BY PROVIDING PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION TO US OR BY USING THE SITE AND THE SERVICE, YOU AGREE TO THIS POLICY AND THE PRACTICES DESCRIBED IN THE POLICY. BETTERFLY RESERVES THE RIGHT TO CHANGE THIS POLICY AND WILL PROVIDE NOTIFICATION OF ANY MATERIAL CHANGE OR AMENDMENT TO THE POLICY BY NOTICE POSTED TO THE SITE. You are advised to consult the Policy regularly for any changes. If you do not agree with these terms, you should not use the Site and the Service. By using the Site and the Service, you are consenting to our processing of your information as set forth in the Policy. "Processing" means using cookies on a computer or using or touching information in any way, including, but not limited to, collecting, storing, deleting, using, combining and disclosing information.

Betterfly operates its Site and Service for Users who have the option of registering for two types of profiles: (i) A “Betterist Profile” for a Betterist who posts a description of a service offering available for booking through the Site and Service (a “Listing”) for an appointment between themselves and a single Service Client (a “Private Lesson”) or an appointment between themselves and more than a single Service Client (a “Group Class”) or (ii) a “Service Client Profile” for a Service Client who seeks Services via a Listing from a Betterist.
      1. Users have the option of registering for a (i) Betterist Profile, (ii) Service Client Profile or (iii) Betterist Profile and Service Client Profile sharing a single Account. 
      2. Betterfly provides Users with access to a collection of on-and offline resources, including, but not limited to web page hosting services, on- and off-line advertising services, promotional pricing services, and the ability to contact other Users to seek/offer services. Unless explicitly stated otherwise, any new features that enhance the current Site or Service shall be subject to these Terms of Use.
      3. There is no cost for using the basic sevices of the Site. From time to time, there may be premium content available via subscription or surcharge, and those areas will be clearly marked.
      4. Betterfly facilitates meetings between Users, but Betterfly will not be liable for any cancellations, postponements, and the like by any User of the Site or Service, and all refunds, make-goods, alternatives, or rain checks are as agreed upon by the Users and not by Betterfly.
    2. _Information Collected During Registration_. In the registration process, Betterfly may collect registration information from its Users, including name, user name, password, and contact information such as email, phone number, mailing address ("personal information") or credit card (“financial information”) that may identify you. Each of these different types of information is used to provide Users with access to the Site and the Service, including, but not limited to, your profile page, other User's profile pages, advertising, promotional pricing services, ability to contact other Users of the Service, and participating in the Service. Registration information is used to authenticate users and recall their profile pages. 
    3. _Information Collected When Visiting the Site_. In using the Site and the Service, we collect and store information of Users in order to provide communications and the Service. This data is gathered from a variety of sources and enables Betterfly to create personalized webpages, provide the Service to Users, and verify that the webpages are controlled by those who represent to claim them.
      1. When you visit the Site, servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include information such as your IP address, browser type or the domain from which you are visiting, the webpages you visit, the search terms you use, and any advertisements or links on which you click. For most users accessing the Internet from an Internet service, the IP address will be different every time you log on. Betterfly uses Log Data to monitor the use of the Site and the Service, and for Betterfly's technical administration.
      2. Information, including, but not limited to, your screen name, full name, profile photo, and any other personally identifiable information you have included in your profile page, reviews, and use of the Service may be shown to other Users of the Service. Your full name and email address may also be used when you send an email through the Site and the Service. You should be aware that when you voluntarily disclose personally identifiable information via your profile page, reviews, messages, forums, postings, and in using the Service, that information, along with any substantive information disclosed in your communication, can be collected and used by third parties and may result in unsolicited messages from other posters or third parties. Such activities are beyond the scope of Betterfly. PLEASE DO NOT POST ON THE SITE OR ANY OTHER AREAS ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION THAT YOU EXPECT TO KEEP PRIVATE.
      3. Financial information, including your credit card, that is entered into the Site is encrypted. Betterfly does not store your credit card information on its servers. Online Payments are sent through a secure financial gateway and we endeavor to protect the security of your payment information during transmission by using the Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology.. Betterfly may, however, collect contact information such as name, address and phone number for billing purposes.
      4. Information pertaining to your use of Betterfly Bucks and means of purchasing Betterfly Bucks or redeeming earned available Betterfly Bucks. This information includes, but is not limited to, your mailing address used to remit earnings, your banking account information, an Employer Identification Number (EIN) or Social Security Number (SSN) where applicable by law, and any tax information you provide to us.
      5. Additional information we ask you to submit to authenticate yourself or if we believe you are violating site policies (for example, we may ask you to send us an ID or bill to verify your address, or to answer additional questions online to help verify your identity or ownership of an item you list).
      6. Other supplemental information from third parties (for example, if you incur a debt to eBay, we will generally conduct a credit check by obtaining additional information about you from a credit bureau, as permitted by law).
    4. _Announcements, Messages, and Newsletters_. Betterfly will send you a welcome/confirmation email when you register for the Site and the Service. Receipt of this email is required to use the Site and the Service. Betterfly may periodically email you with administrative messages. Betterfly offers subscriptions to Betterfly email newsletters and information. To opt-in to such email newsletters, Users will be asked for information, such as your email address and the newsletter(s) you wish to receive. This email address will only be used for this feature. You may change your subscription or unsubscribe at any time by following the instructions contained in the email.

      2.     6. _Optional Information_. Betterfly may also collect and analyze from Users certain information regarding the use of the Site and the Service. Information collected may include, but is not limited to, Site traffic volume, frequency of visits, type and time of transactions, type of browser and operating system, IP address, requested URL, referring URL, etc. This information is logged to help diagnose technical problems, administer the Site and the Service, and improve the quality and types of Service delivered.   3. **Cookies**
    1. _Cookies May Be Necessary_. Like most websites, the Site requires cookies to function properly. Cookies are small text files that reside on a User's computer and identify you as a unique user. We use cookies to refine the Site and the Service and to tailor the User experience. You should be aware that Betterfly cannot control the use of cookies by advertisers or any information advertisers may gather from their use of cookies. You can configure your browser to accept all cookies, reject all cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. However, you should note that cookies may be necessary to provide you certain features (e.g., customized information and use of the Site and the Service) available on the Site. If you reject all cookies, you may not be able to use the the Site and the Service that require you to "sign in" and you may be unable to take full advantage of the Site and the Service.
  4. **Tracking Technologies**
    1. _Web Beacons_. Web Beacons/ Web Bugs (clear gifs, pixel tags or web bugs) are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of web users or to access cookies. Unlike cookies which are stored on the user's computer hard drive, web beacons are embedded invisibly on the web pages (or in email) and are about the size of the period at the end of a sentence. Web beacons may be used to deliver or to communicate with cookies, to count users who have visited certain pages and to understand usage patterns. Betterfly may also include web beacons in promotional (or other) email messages or newsletters to determine whether messages have been opened and acted upon. Betterfly may also receive an anonymous identification number if you come to Betterfly.com from an online advertisement displayed on a third party website.

    1. Betterfly does not target its offerings toward, and does not knowingly collect any personal information from users under 13 years of age. Betterfly recommends that prospective users between the ages of 13 and 17 years of age ask their parents or other legal guardians for permission before sending any information about themselves to anyone over the Internet.
