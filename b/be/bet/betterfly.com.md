These Terms of Use constitute legally binding terms and apply to each user of the Service, including registered and unregistered Users ("you", "your").

Your privacy is important to Betterfly.com ("Betterfly", "we", or "us"). This Privacy and Information Security Policy ("Privacy Policy", "the Policy") is designed to help you understand how we collect and use information. This Policy is part of and incorporated into the Betterfly.com Terms of Use.

BETTERFLY.COM RESERVES THE RIGHT TO CHANGE THIS POLICY AND WILL PROVIDE NOTIFICATION OF ANY MATERIAL CHANGE OR AMENDMENT TO THE POLICY BY NOTICE POSTED TO BETTERFLY.COM. You are advised to consult the Policy regularly for any changes. If you do not agree with these terms, you should not use Betterfly.com and the Service. By using Betterfly.com and the Service, you are consenting to our processing of your information as set forth in the Policy. "Processing" means using cookies on a computer or using or touching information in any way, including, but not limited to, collecting, storing, deleting, using, combining and disclosing information.

Users may create profiles; read and write reviews; explore videos and blogs; book appointments; showcase their skills; meet clients; and utilize Betterfly's online tools including its payment service.
      1. A "User" is any guest of Betterfly.com, registered or non-registered, who utilizes Betterfly's website. A "Betterist" is the online persona of a User who elects to help other Users become better at something by posting service offerings to their "Betterist Site".

Betterfly.com provides Users with access to a collection of on-and offline resources, including without limitation web page hosting services, on- and off-line advertising services, promotional pricing services, and the ability to contact other Users to seek/offer services. Unless explicitly stated otherwise, any new features that enhance the current Site or Service shall be subject to these Terms of Use.
      3. Subject to the restrictions on use and on posting set forth in this Agreement, you may: use Betterfly.com to solicit reviews and business contact information; forward reviews to people you know; invite people you know to join Betterfly.com; and view the portions of the information, data, text, sound, photographs, graphics, video, messages, and other materials available on the Site or Service.
      4. Betterfly.com facilitates meetings between Users, but Betterfly.com will not be liable for any cancellations, postponements, and the like by any User of the Site or Service, and all refunds, make-goods, alternatives, or rain checks are as agreed upon by the Users and not Betterfly.com.
    2. _Information Collected During Registration_. In the registration process, Betterfly.com may collect registration information from its users, including name, user name, password, and contact information such as email and mailing address ("personal information") that may identify you. Each of these different types of information is used to provide Users with access to Betterfly.com and the Service, including, but not limited to, your profile page, other User's profile pages, advertising, promotional pricing services, ability to contact other Users of the Service, and participating in the Service. Registration information is used to authenticate users and recall their profile pages.
    3. _Information Collected When Visiting Betterfly.com_. In using Betterfly.com and the Service, we collect and store information of Users in order to provide communications and the Service. This data is gathered from a variety of sources and enables Betterfly.com to create personalized webpages, provide the Service to Users, and verify that the webpages are controlled by those who represent to claim them.
      1. When you visit Betterfly.com, servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include information such as your IP address, browser type or the domain from which you are visiting, the webpages you visit, the search terms you use, and any advertisements or links on which you click. For most users accessing the Internet from an Internet service provided the IP address will be different every time you log on. Betterfly.com use Log Data to monitor the use of Betterfly.com and the Service, and for Betterfly.com's technical administration.
      2. Information, including, but not limited to, your screen name, full name, profile photo, and any other personally identifiable information you have included in your profile page, reviews, and use of the Service may be shown to other Users of the Service. In addition, Betterfly.com may sell your reviews to third parties, in which case your provided information (e.g., screen name, first name and last name, profile photo, and any personally identifiable information you have included in your reviews, profile page, and use of the Service) may be shown to users of such third party websites. Your full name and email address may also be used when you send an email through Betterfly.com and the Service. You should be aware that when you voluntarily disclose personally identifiable information via your profile page, reviews, messages, forums, postings, and in using the Service, that information, along with any substantive information disclosed in your communication, can be collected and used by third parties and may result in unsolicited messages from other posters or third parties. Such activities are beyond the scope of Betterfly.com. PLEASE DO NOT POST ON BETTERFLY.COM OR ANY OTHER AREAS ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION THAT YOU EXPECT TO KEEP PRIVATE.
      3. Information pertaining to your use of Betterfly Bucks and means of purchasing Betterfly Bucks or redeeming earned available Betterfly Bucks. This information includes, but is not limited to, your mailing address used to remit earnings, your banking account information, an Employer Identification Number (EIN) or Social Security Number (SSN) where applicable by law, and any tax information you provide to us.
      4. Additional information we ask you to submit to authenticate yourself or if we believe you are violating site policies (for example, we may ask you to send us an ID or bill to verify your address, or to answer additional questions online to help verify your identity or ownership of an item you list).
      5. Other supplemental information from third parties (for example, if you incur a debt to eBay, we will generally conduct a credit check by obtaining additional information about you from a credit bureau, as permitted by law).

Receipt of this email is required to use Betterfly.com and the Service. Betterfly.com may periodically email you with administrative messages. Betterfly.com offers subscriptions to Betterfly.com email newsletters and information. To opt-in to such email newsletters, Users will be asked for information, such as your email address and the newsletter(s) you wish to receive. This email address will only be used for this feature. You may change your subscription or unsubscribe at any time by following the instructions contained in the email.
    5. _Using Service Features_.
      1. Betterfly.com and the Service include features in which Users may share information and send personalized messages to other Users. If you use this feature, Betterfly.com will ask you for the information needed to send the message and automatically send such messages and information.       2.     6. _Optional Information_. Betterfly.com may also collect and analyze from Users certain information regarding the use of Betterfly.com and the Service and Betterfly.com activity.   3. **Cookies**
    1. _Cookies May Be Necessary_. Like most websites, Betterfly.com requires cookies to function properly. Cookies are small text files that reside on a User's computer and identify you as a unique user. We use cookies to refine Betterfly.com and the Service and to tailor the User experience. You should be aware that Betterfly.com cannot control the use of cookies by advertisers or any information advertisers may gather from their use of cookies. You can configure your browser to accept all cookies, reject all cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. However, you should note that cookies may be necessary to provide you certain features (e.g., customized information and use of Betterfly.com and the Service) available on Betterfly.com. If you reject all cookies, you may not be able to use the Betterfly.com and the Service that require you to "sign in" and you may be unable to take full advantage of Betterfly.com and the Service.
  4. **Tracking Technologies**
    1. _Web Beacons_. Web Beacons/ Web Bugs (know as clear gifs, pixel tags or web bugs) are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of web users or to access cookies. Unlike cookies which are stored on the user's computer hard drive, web beacons are embedded invisibly on the web pages (or in email) and are about the size of the period at the end of a sentence. Web beacons may be used to deliver or to communicate with cookies, to count users who have visited certain pages and to understand usage patterns. Betterfly.com may also include web beacons in promotional (or other) email messages or newsletters to determine whether messages have been opened and acted upon. Betterfly.com may also receive an anonymous identification number if you come to Betterfly.com from an online advertisement displayed on a third party website.

Each registered User is assigned a unique user name and password which is required to access their user information. Your Betterfly.com Account Information is protected by your password for your privacy and security. It is the User's responsibility to protect the security of their login information. Firewalls and other security technologies are employed to prevent interference or access from outside intruders; however we cannot guarantee the security of User account information.     1. Betterfly.com does not target its offerings toward, and does not knowingly collect any personal information from users under 13 years of age. If you are under 13 years of age, do not attempt to register for Betterfly.com and the Service or send any information about yourself to Betterfly.com. Should Betterfly.com collect any personal information from a user under 13 years of age, it will comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act. Betterfly.com recommends that prospective users between the ages of 13 and 17 years of age ask their parents or other legal guardians for permission before sending any information about themselves to anyone over the Internet.
