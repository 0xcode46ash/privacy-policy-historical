# Privacy Policy

We promise to look after your personal information and to treat your details with respect. We’ll never sell or give away your personal information. However, we’d like to contact you via email now and again to keep you up to date with our latest catalogues and promotions. If you don’t want to be kept up to date, just [let us know](https://web.archive.org/customer-service/contact-us "Contact Us").

We do keep hold of the personal information you provide and any other information relating to you, to use for the purposes of managing our business and our relationship to you, such as improving how our website works, market research, or for the prevention of fraud. Out of necessity we may occasionally share information with relevant and trusted organisations to do this.
