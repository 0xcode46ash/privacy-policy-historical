# Married Dating at BeDiscreet.com

BeDiscreet is a trading name of IDIGITAL Ltd. 

BeDiscreet is committed to protecting your privacy. Our aim is to ensure that you can spend your time on the site looking for and making contact with other members. Our privacy policy is intended to ensure that you can do that without having to worry about confidentiality. 

1\. All acceptable information you publish or post on your Profile will be displayed on your Profile. We will remove any bad language or obscenities in the interests of a pleasant environment for all members. We will only post pictures and images which meet the guidelines set out in our membership terms and conditions. 

2\. Information you give us that is not for display on your Profile, such as your name, email address or date of birth, will be kept strictly private. Whenever we ask for such information we make it clear that it is not for display. 

3\. We will never sell, rent or otherwise share your name, email address, or other personal information with any other business or for any marketing campaign other than by BeDiscreet without your permission. 

4\. We are required to give information to legal authorities if they have the proper authorisation such as a search warrant or court order. Other than a valid request from a legal authority your information is secure on the BeDiscreet web site .

5\. Your individual use of BeDiscreet and your interaction with others through BeDiscreet will be kept strictly private. 

We take our PRIVACY POLICY very seriously. We want you to have confidence whenever you use BeDiscreet. If you have questions or concerns, please email us at [admin@bediscreet.com](mailto:admin@bediscreet.com)
