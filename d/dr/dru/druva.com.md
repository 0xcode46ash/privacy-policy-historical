# Privacy Policy and Terms of Use

At Druva, we believe it is important to protect the privacy of our customers and guests. This Privacy Policy explains and summarizes how we collect and use your personal information.

In general, you can visit the website without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself. We may collect domain name and/or IP address, browsing behavior, and/or other non-personally identifiable information regarding what pages are accessed, through the use of cookies or other browser tools.

The information that is collected by Druva will be used only for internal business purposes to better serve your needs and the needs of our other customers. We will not disclose any personally identifiable information to any third party without your prior consent unless we believe that disclosure is necessary: (1) to conform to legal requirements or to respond to a subpoena, search warrant or other legal process received by Druva, whether or not a response is required by applicable law; (2) to protect our rights; or (3) to protect the members of the public. We will not sell any such information.

The website may contain links to third-party sites, however, please be aware that we are not responsible for and cannot control the privacy practices of these other sites. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our website, and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information.

If you have questions or concerns about the privacy policy, please send an email to [privacy@druva.com](mailto:privacy@druva.com "privacy@druva.com")
