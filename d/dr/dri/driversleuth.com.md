# Privacy Policy | Driver Sleuth

The terms below discloses the privacy policy for the website DriverSleuth.com and applies only to the information collected by this site. By accessing the site and downloading any software from DriverSleuth.com you are bound by these website terms and conditions. We are very serious about your privacy and have no intention of selling your information for any reason. 

## Collected Information

We collect your personal information to further assist our company in delivering a high level customer service and experience when interacting on our website. Non-identifiable data such as your time on the site, pages that you visit, and IP address help us deliver the best user experience that we can offer. Personal information is only collected by forms on the website and never without your knowledge. This can include name, email address, telephone number etc....

## How we Use the Information

If you contact us for any reason, we will use this information to respond to your inquiry. Also, unless you request not to, information may be sent to you to inform you of contests, promotions, discounts, and software updates or changes. 

## Use of Cookies

Cookies are commonly used throughout the web and are used on the DriverSleuth.com website. A cookie is a small piece of text data placed on your hard drive by DriverSleuth.com's servers to better serve our visitors and customers. Use of cookies have no way to determine the identity of the visitor. Cookies are harmless and cannot access your computer or place malicious software on your PC. Most browsers enable you to allow or disallow cookies when you visit a website.

## Security

DriverSleuth takes appropriate security measures to ensure your data and information if collected is safe and secure. We use standard internet technologies to keep your data from intruders and unwanted access.
