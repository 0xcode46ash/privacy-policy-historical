# Drinkstuff.com's privacy policy

|  | 

### Our privacy policy

We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to Drinkstuff will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to [let us know](https://web.archive.org/contactus.asp).

****Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:****

> Drinkstuff does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms, email lists or addresses.
> 
> We do not condone the use of spam and our regular newsletters have a full opt-in/opt-out functionality as seen on the bottom of every page on Drinkstuff.

****Browser information collected on the website:****

> Drinkstuff analyses the website logs to constantly improve the value of the materials available on website. Our website logs **are not personally identifiable** , and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.
