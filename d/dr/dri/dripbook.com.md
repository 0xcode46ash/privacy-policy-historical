# Dripbook | Privacy Policy

Dripbook.com recognizes that its customers, visitors, users, and others who use www.dripbook.com or other web sites owned or operated by Dadaphile, Inc. (the “Dripbook Sites”) (collectively or individually “Users”) value their privacy. This Privacy Notice details important information regarding the use and disclosure of User information collected on the Dripbook Sites. Dripbook.com provides this Privacy Notice to help you make an informed decision about whether to use or continue using the Dripbook Sites. 

This Privacy Notice is incorporated into and is subject to the Dripbook.com Terms of Use. Your use of the Dripbook Sites and any personal information you provide on the Dripbook Sites remains subject to the terms of this Privacy Notice and our Terms of Use 

Please note that any video, image, or other content posted at the direction of Users onto the Dripbook Sites becomes published content and is not considered personally identifiable information subject to this Privacy Notice.
