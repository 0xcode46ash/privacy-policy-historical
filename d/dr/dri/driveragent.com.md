# driveragent.com - Policies

### Privacy Policy

DriverAgent.com is the property of TouchStone Software Corporation.

TouchStone Software Corporation will not sell or rent our customer list to a third party.

This page describes the privacy policy observed by DriverAgent.com. We value your repeat business and respect your privacy, therefore this page describes our collection and use of information provided by you when visiting DriverAgent.com.

If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact DriverAgent.com at [web_master@touchstonesoftware.com](mailto:web_master@touchstonesoftware.com)

### Use of Personal Information

We at DriverAgent.com respect your privacy. Our marketing department observes "100% opt-in" business practices. This means we use exclusively our own (100% opt-in) customer list.

We will use the limited information we collect about you only as a means to provide you with better products and interactive services.

When you purchase DriverAgent.com products, get free information (e.g., newsletters or white papers) or free application software from our web site, you may be asked to "register." 

During registration we may ask for your name, phone number, email address, company address.

Also during registration, we will provide you with the option to receive email regarding product updates, special offers and other notices. Regardless of which option you pick, you will receive a one-time confirmation email after registration.

You can change your mind and opt-out at any time. Our electronic messages include simple instructions to "unsubscribe."

Our customer list is regarded as our most important asset. Though we will skip the details regarding firewalls, secure sockets layer (SSL), encryption and other techniques, we will say that we protect the information you provide against unauthorized access or release, with the same or more protection as afforded all essential DriverAgent.com information assets and confidential information.

DriverAgent.com will not sell or rent our customer list to a third party. We may occasionally share non-personal, aggregated customer data with other third parties (e.g., traffic reports).

### Your Consent

By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by DriverAgent.com, as described above.
