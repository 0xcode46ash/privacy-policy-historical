DFS GALLERIA INTERNET PRIVACY POLICY 

What our Privacy Policy Covers 

  * This Privacy Policy covers any personal information that DFS Group L.P. and its affiliated companies ("DFS") obtain from you when you visit our site. 



What Information is collected 

  * For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the visitor's domain name. 
  * We collect aggregate information on what pages on our site visitors access as well as information volunteered by them in response to our invitations to provide that information, such as survey responses and site registrations. 
  * We use cookies to store individual language preferences in order to provide better service when visitors return to our site. 



How the information is used 

  * The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web site. 
  * We also use the information volunteered by visitors for this purpose to send offers and news via email or through SMS messaging. 
  * We do not share information we collect with other organizations for commercial purposes. 



Email 

  * If you do not wish to receive email from DFS in the future, please click the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of our communications. 



SMS 

  * If you do not wish to receive SMS messages from DFS in the future, please contact us at the above address. 



Protecting your information 

  * We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we collect from you at our site. 



Access to your information 

  * Upon request we provide any site visitor who establishes his or her identity to our satisfaction with access to information that we have collected from them. 



Contact 

  * If you feel that this site or DFS representatives are not following the procedures in this policy, you may contact us at the above address.
