At Dolly, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy notice has been established to explain our practices of information collection. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at any time any information might be collected.  
The information collected on the Site is used strictly for processing orders, credit applications, requests for information and other related items on behalf of our customers and partners. We do not sell or trade any customer/partner data with any firm, for any reason. We have gone to great lengths to ensure such data is protected and secure.  
• When you submit an order with Dolly, Inc., we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing address, and any other pertinent data. This allows us to process documents completely and accurately.  
• We use your use name/password login information to pre-populate your address and billing information, as well as to provide relevant information to you while visiting the Site.  
• We will monitor traffic patterns and Site usage to help us develop effective programs and content for our customers and partners.  
• We may also use the information we collect to occasionally notify you about functionality changes to the Site, new services, and special offers we think you’ll find valuable. If you would rather not receive this information please contact us.  
• We will not sell trade or rent your information.   
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have incorporated a combination of physical, mechanical and managerial procedures to ensure your safety. We offer the use of a secure server, using Secure Socket Layer 128-bit data encryption (SSL) software. In partnering with VeriSign, this protects against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you. SSL is endorsed by the World Wide Consortium and is the de facto standard for secure data transmission. All information that you input is encrypted prior to being sent to us and all of the data we collect is protected against unauthorized access.  
“Cookies” are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive. Our cookies do not contain any personally identifying information, but they do enable us to provide features such as remembering your shopping cart in the event of interupted service, or remembering your details on return visits. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent that. Even without a cookie, you can still use most of the features in our store, including placing items in your shopping cart and purchasing them.   
No. Dolly, Inc. does not sell, trade or rent your personal information to others.   
By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Dolly, Inc. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.  
We always welcome your questions and comments, including those that concern your privacy. Feel free to Email us, call us during regular business hours 8 a.m. - 6 p.m. E.T. weekdays at 1-937-667-5711 and ask for customer service online services, or send a letter to Dolly, Inc. Our details can be found [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20071212033623id_/http%3A//www.dolly.com/contact.asp).
