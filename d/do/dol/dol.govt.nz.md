# Privacy Statement - New Zealand Department of Labour

[Home](https://web.archive.org/web/20041010032031id_/http%3A//www.dol.govt.nz/index.asp) > Privacy Statement

Privacy Statement  


Providing Visitors with Anonymous Access 

You can access our website home page and browse our site without having to disclose your personal data. 

Data Collection and Purpose Specification 

We do, however, collect some personal data that you may volunteer while using our site and its services. We do not collect or use personal data for any purpose other than to send out information and publications requested by you. 

We do not disclose your personal data to other organisations outside the Department of Labour, including to other government institutions and authorities. 

If we wish to use our visitors' personal data for new purposes, we would give them the opportunity to consent to those new purposes. 

Automatic Collection of Information 

We do not use cookies to store personal data. 

Confidentiality/Security 

We have implemented security policies, rules and technical measures to protect the personal data that we have under our control from: 

  * unauthorised access 
  * improper use or disclosure 
  * unauthorised modification 
  * unlawful destruction or accidental loss 



All our employees and data processors, who have access to, and are associated with the processing of personal data, are obliged to respect the confidentiality of our online visitors' personal data. 

Access to the personal data we may hold about you 

For those who have supplied their personal contact information to order and receive publications or use other online services from within our organisation, you can request our web manager to provide you with the personal data we might hold about you and make any changes you require. 

Privacy Support 

If you have an enquiry or concern about our privacy policy, please [contact us](mailto:info@dol.govt.nz?subject=Privacy).
