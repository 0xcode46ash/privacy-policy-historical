# Privacy Policy for Download Free Games

  


## Privacy Policy

Download-Free-Games.com is a web property of its parent company, Alpha Internet Consulting LLC, a Washington State Corporation.

The postal address for this website is:   
Download Free Games  
613 S. Ivory St  
Spokane, WA 99202

We can be reached via e-mail at games@download-free-games.com 

**Emails**

Email subscribers to our newsletters and game clubs will never have their email addresses given away, sold, or will be spammed in any way. All subscribers always have an option to unsubscribe at any time. All newsletters are double opt-in, meaning subscribers must confirm their desire to be on the list by clicking a link in an email sent to their sign up address. This is done to protect all email addresses from being mistakenly or fraudulently added to our list by 3rd parties. 

**Site Visitors**

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name and IP address, but not the e-mail address (where possible).

We only automatically the domain name, but not the e-mail address of visitors to our Web page. Email addresses are collected if visitors double opt-in to our Free Game Alert Service. Email addresses are then only used by Download Free Games and not sold, rented, given, or shown to any other company for any use whatsoever. Our use of double opt-in email addresses is to notify visitors of new games added to the site.

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web page, not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes. 

**Cookies**

Cookies are used on many download links to track referrals from this site. All full version purchases made from these downloads are then credited to Download Free Games from software developer partners on a revenue sharing basis.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail at the above address or if you are a subscriber to our Free Game Alert Service, you may follow the directions on the bottom of any of our Alerts and unsubscribe. 

**Ad Servers**

With respect to Ad Servers: We are currently partnering with Google to deliver ads. We cannot be responsible for the quality of ads or the operation of sites that buy ads through Google.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact The DMA's Committee on Ethical Business Practices at mgoldberger@the-dma.org.
