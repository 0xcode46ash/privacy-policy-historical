# DownEast Basics Clothing Catalog

**It's your information!**

DownEast Basics (DEB) is absolutely committed to your privacy.

DEB is the sole owner of any information collected on this website. 

For security reasons, DEB may record your IP address with each form submission. However, DEB will not, without customer consent, share your information with other entities. 

If you have any questions please feel free to contact us.
