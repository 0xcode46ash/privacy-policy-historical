# Doulos Privacy Policy

The Doulos website does not automatically capture or store personal information, other than logging the user's IP address and session info such as the duration of the visit and the type of browser used. This is recognised by the Web server and is only used for system administration and to provide statistics which Doulos uses to evaluate use of the site.

We use cookies to help us provide you with the regional information most relevant to you when browsing our Training course section. We do not use cookies for collecting user information from the site.

You will be asked for contact information if you download any of the free resources from our website. This information helps us to understand the audience for these free resources and inform users of updates.

We will only use this information to send you further information on our products and services if indicated by you on the download form that this is acceptable.
