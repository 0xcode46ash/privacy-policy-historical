# Domain Search Privacy Policy

DomainSearch.com has a very simple privacy policy - we respect your privacy. Any information collected through our website or contact form is kept strictly confidential.

We offer a free website, providing the public with domain name availability information, whois informaiton, links, and tools. This site is supported by advertising. To better serve you, various third party ads may place and/or read cookies on your browser or use web beacons to collect information. You can manage your cookie settings (acceptance options, on/off settings, or removal) within your browser settings.

For detailed information on managing cookies, view your browser's help menu. For more information on how to opt out of cookies, visit www.WorldPrivacyForum.org.
