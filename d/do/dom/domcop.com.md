# DomCop Privacy Policy

Axeman Tech Pvt Ltd, d/b/a DomCop (DomCop) understands how its users value the privacy of their personal information. This Privacy Policy will give you information about what is collected through your use of this website, the choices you have about how this information is used and the security involved for the information we collect when you register for a DomCop account.

DomCop may contain links to other sites and once you enter another website, please be aware that DomCop is not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. The only information we normally collect is non-personal information through the use of cookies or web beacons. This information is not traceable back to any individual and cannot be used to identify an individual, e.g. the keyword phrase you searched on to find this website. However, if you decide to contact us, then you will be submitting personally identifiable information in the form of your email address along with anything else you volunteer.

**Log Files**  
Like many other Web sites, DomCop makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. 

**Cookies and Web Beacons**  
DomCop does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser. 

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include Commission Junction.

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on domcop.com send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see. 

Domcop.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers. 

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. Domcop.com's privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites. 

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites. 

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at support@domcop.com.
