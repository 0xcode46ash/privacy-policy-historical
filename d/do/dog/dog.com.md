# Dog.com: Discount Pet Supplies, Wholesale Dog Supply

|    
|    
|  Dog.com respects your privacy and we are committed to protecting it. Dog.com believes in keeping your information confidential. We only collect information from you when you specifically and knowingly provide such information. Any information we collect is used for the purpose of providing you with a more satisfying and personalized shopping and browsing experience. 

Dog.com does not sell, or rent any personal information to other companies or third parties. 

Dog.com collects and uses information in the following ways: 

  * **Order Fulfillment** \- When you order from our site, we request your, name, email address, mailing address, telephone number, credit card number and expiration date. This information allows us to process, fulfill and alert you to the status of your order. Dog.com may use this information about your purchases to enhance your shopping experience by notifying you of special promotions, functionality changes and new services that we think you will find valuable. You may choose to remove yourself from this list at any time. To be removed from this list, send and e-mail to [unsubscribe@dogsoutfitter.com](mailto:unsubscribe@dog.com) and title your e-mail "No Promotions." We may also monitor customer traffic patterns and site usage to help us develop the design and layout that best suits our customers. 
  

  * **Surveys, Site Features** \- We may ask you for additional information about you and your behavior and/or about your pet or business. We may request this information, but do not require that you complete this information. This information is requested to help us provide the best possible site and personalized experience on Dog.com. This information will be kept confidential and will be used to help us make Dog.com better for you. 
  

  * **Preferred Customer Offers** \- When you sign-up for the Dog.com Preferred Customer Offers we will ask you for your name and email address. This information is required. We may also ask you some questions in regard to your pet. Our Preferred Customer Offers will contain content and product highlights, and special promotions. As part of these offers and associated promotions, Dog.com may send you information about services and products from partners and other companies that we think may be of interest to you. Please note, that these e-mails are sent by Dog.com and we do NOT give these companies or partners any personally identifiable information about our customers or users. You may choose to remove yourself from our regular email Preferred Customer Offers and/or our partner emails. To be removed you may send an email to [unsubscribe@dogsoutfitter.com](mailto:unsubscribe@dog.com). The title your email should be "No Newsletter," "No Dog.com Promotions," "No Partner Promotions," or combination thereof that you desire. 
  

  * **Catalog** \- When you sign-up to receive the Dog.com Catalog, a monthly publication, Dog.com will ask for your name, address and email address. Your name and address are required to send you the Dog.com Catalog. The email address is optional and may be used to send you special promotions and other items we think may be of interest to you. You may remove your name from our catalog list at any time. To remove your name from our Catalog List, please send an email to [unsubscribe@dogsoutfitter.com](mailto:unsubscribe@dog.com) and title the email "No Catalog." 
  

  * **Technical Information** \- Dog.com does place a feature on your web browser called a "cookie" to assign each member a unique number. This unique number allows Dog.com to track the items in their Shopping Cart and to save past purchase information. This enhances your experience by saving your Shopping Cart information so that you have the ability to view old orders. Cookies do not allow Dog.com to track the items to collect additional information other than the information that is provided by the member at the time of registration. In addition to cookies, Dog.com may collect technical information which does not allow us to identify you personally but pertains to your use of the Internet. For example, we may collect the type of Internet Browser you are using or type of e-mail messaging capabilities that you have. This information enables us to enhance the content from Dog.com.

Dog.com may share non personal, non-individual aggregate information or summary statistics about our customers, sales, traffic patterns and related site information to reputable third-party vendors. This information will never include any personally identifiable information. Dog.com is very protective of the privacy of its customers. If at any time Dog.com is required by law or by a legal process to release customer information, we must cooperate fully.

**Security**

Dog.com is committed to offer the highest levels of protection for your personal information. We use secure technology, rigorous privacy protection controls and stringent restrictions on employee access in order to safeguard your personal information, including credit card information. 

All Shopping Cart information including credit card information and passwords is encrypted with SSL technology (Secure Sockets Layer) in secure databases protected by a firewall. SSL technology (Secure Sockets Layer) is the Standard for encrypted data transmission on the Internet between a Web server and a client (browser). 

Once Dog.com receives your credit card information, you can choose to have us store it for future orders on a secure, non-networked computer that cannot be accessed through the Internet. You will only need to supply us with the credit card information once. For our customers who still prefer not to transmit their card number over the Internet, we also offer the option of phoning or faxing your credit card number. Simply print you basket order using your browser's print function and call us at 1.888.FOR.DOG7 or fax us at 570.384.4833. You may then delete the items from your basket or exit your browser.   
  
**Consent**

By using this Dog.com web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Dog.com. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post any changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we disclose it. 

If you are under 18 years of age, please get parental permission before using the Dog.com web site. If you have additional questions about our Privacy and Security Guarantee, please contact us at [service@dogsoutfitter.com.](mailto:service@dog.com)
