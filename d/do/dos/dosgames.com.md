**User Information** Your privacy is important to us. That is why no personal information is collected about you by dosgames.com. Our hit tracking service (currently sitemeter.com) tracks general information about all visitors to the site, such as how many people use each webbrowser, what is the most popular screen resolution, etc. No information about specific visitors is ever stored. This information is used to ensure that the site is compatible with the majority of visitors. **Cookies** dosgames.com uses no cookies and will not put any on your computer. **DOS Games Email service** The dosgames.com free email service is hosted by a third party, bigmailbox.com. Information collected during signup for the dosgames.com email service is handled by this third party and is subject to their privacy policy, located [here](http://mail.bigmailbox.com/users/dosgamescom/signup.cgi?sw=1&tt=full). **Disclosure** dosgames.com openly volunteers its practices of collecting information, and reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any time. Any questions regarding our privacy policy can be directed to [webmaster@dosgames.com](mailto:webmaster@dosgames.com).
