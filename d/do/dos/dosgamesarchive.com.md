**Our commitment to privacy**  
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available from every page of this website. 

**The information we collect**  
This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on DOS Games Archive. In our online shop you can order products. The types of personal information collected at these pages are: 

  * Name
  * Address
  * E-mail address
  * Phone number
  * Credit card information



**The way we use information**  
We use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order only to complete that order. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order. 

We use the information you provide about someone else when placing an order only to ship the product and to confirm delivery. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order. 

We use return e-mail addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties. 

We never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses. 

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals. 

Our hit tracking service tracks general information about all visitors to the site, such as type webbrowser, operating system, screen resolution, etc. No information about specific visitors is ever tracked or stored. This information is used to ensure that the site is compatible with the majority of visitors. 

This site uses the services of ad services, which include [Burst! Media](http://www.burstmedia.com/), [Google AdSense](http://www.google.com/adsense/) and/or others for the serving and/or targeting of ads, promotions and other marketing messages. To do this, the advertiser(s) may collect anonymous data through the use of cookies. 

**Our commitment to data security**  
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. 

**Disclosure**  
DOS Games Archive openly volunteers its practices of collecting information, and reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any time. 

**How to contact us**  
Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send us an email at [webmaster@dosgamesarchive.com](mailto:webmaster@dosgamesarchive.com).
