# Privacy Policy | DoNanza

DoNanza (“donanza.com” or the “Company”) is committed to protecting the privacy of your information. This Privacy Policy describes DoNanza's website's privacy practices. The DoNanza website is owned, maintained and operated by CrowdIT Ltd., a private company incorporated under the laws of the State of Israel. Reference herein to DoNanza referrers to CrowdIT as well.

This Privacy Policy covers the information practices of the webs that link to this Privacy Policy: [http://www.donanza.com](https://web.archive.org/); [http://donanza.me](http://donanza.me/) (collectively referred to as “donanza.com’s Websites” or “the Company’s Websites”).

DoNanza, [http://www.donanza.com](https://web.archive.org/), is an online marketplace for on-demand web applications ("BizApps") that run on the Company’s Website platform. The BizApps Applications may be posted by DoNanza and/or by third parties.

When BizApps applications are provided by DoNanza, this Privacy Policy applies. When BizApps applications are provided by third parties (even if such BizApps are linked to from the DoNanza Website), the privacy policy of such third parties shall govern the use of such BizApps, and not this Privacy Policy.

The Company's Websites may contain links to other websites over which DoNanza has no control (the "External Websites"). The links to such External Websites as provided by DoNanza without taking any responsibility to the content or security of such External Websites. You acknowledge that DoNanza is providing these links to you for your convenience, and that Donanza is not and under no circumstances shall be responsible for the information practices or the content of such External Websites. The Company encourages you to review the Privacy Policies of such other websites to understand their information protection and use practices.

By using or accessing the Company's Website, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you should first contact DoNanza's support staff at [contact@donanza.com](mailto:contact@donanza.com).

DoNanza offers a variety of applications and services that are collectively referred to as the “Services.” DoNanza collects information from individuals who visit the Company’s Websites (the “Visitors”) and individuals who register to use the Services (the “Customers”).

When expressing an interest in obtaining additional information about the Services or registering to use the Services, DoNanza requires you to provide the Company with personal contact information, such as name, company name, address, phone number, and email address (the “Required Contact Information”). A blogger or site owner whishing to install DoNanza Project Box on his/hers site, will be required to provide his/hers blog/site URL, as well.

When purchasing the Services, DoNanza requires you to provide the Company with financial qualification and billing information, such as billing name and address, credit card number, and the number of employees within the organization that will be using the Services (the “Billing Information”). DoNanza may also ask you to provide additional information, such as company annual revenues, number of employees, or industry (the “Optional Information”). Required Contact Information, Billing Information, and Optional Information are referred to collectively as “Data About donanza.com Customers.”

As you navigate the Company’s Websites, DoNanza may also collect information through the use of commonly-used information-gathering tools, such as cookies and web beacons (the “Web Site Navigational Information”). Web Site Navigational Information includes standard information from your web browser (such as browser type and browser language), your Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, and the actions you take on the Company’s Websites (such as the web pages viewed and the links clicked). For additional information about the collection of Web Site Navigational Information by DoNanza and others, please click [here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/faqs.asp).

You may have the option to sign in to your DoNanza account by using third party social networking websites such as facebook and twitter (the "Social Networks"). If you choose to sign in through a Social Network, we may use the information you already provided to such Social Network in registering for an account of such Social Network and which you have allowed such Social Networks to share with DoNanza, including without limited to you first name, your user profile picture, your friends and contacts list, the networks to which you belong (the "Social Submitted Information"). You expressly authorize us to collect and use your Social Submitted Information in connection with your use of the DoNanza Services. You further acknowledge and agree that DoNanza may maintain copies of your Social Submitted Information indefinitely, or delete any or all Social Submitted Information, at our sole discretion.

You may upload any content on the Company's Website at your own risk. DoNanza cannot control the actions of other Customers with whom you may choose to share your pages and content (the “Content”). Therefore, we cannot and do not guarantee that Content uploaded by you will not be viewed, changed or even harmed by unauthorized persons. DoNanza is not responsible for circumvention of any privacy settings or security measures contained on the Company's Website. You understand and acknowledge that, even after removal, copies of Content posted on the Company's Website may remain viewable in cached and archived pages or if other Customers or Visitors have copied or stored your Content.

The Company uses Data About donanza.com Customers to perform the Services requested. For example, if you fill out a “Contact Me” web form, the Company will use the information provided to contact you about your interest in the Services.

The Company may also use Data About donanza.com Customers and/or Content you upload and/or Social Submitted Information for marketing purposes. For example, the Company may use information you provide to contact you to further discuss your interest in the Services and to send you information regarding the Company and its partners, such as information about promotions or events.

DoNanza may also use Web Site Navigational Information alone or in combination with Data About donanza.com Customers to provide personalized information about the Company. For additional information about the use of Web Site Navigational Information, please click [here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/faqs.asp).

DoNanza uses commonly-used information-gathering tools, such as cookies and web beacons, to collect information as you navigate the Company’s Websites. This section describes the types of Web Site Navigational Information that may be collected on the Company’s Websites and how this information may be used.

Donanza.com uses cookies to make interactions with the Company’s Websites easy and meaningful. When you visit one of the Company’s Websites, donanza.com’s servers send a cookie to your computer. Standing alone, cookies do not personally identify you. They merely recognize your web browser. Unless you choose to identify yourself to donanza.com, either by responding to a promotional offer, opening an account, or filling out a web form (such as a “Contact Me” or a “30 Day Free Trial” web form), you remain anonymous to the Company.

Donanza.com uses cookies that are session-based and persistent-based. Session cookies exist only during one session. They disappear from your computer when you close your browser software or turn off your computer. Persistent cookies remain on your computer after you close your browser or turn off your computer.

If you have chosen to identify yourself to donanza.com, the Company uses session cookies containing encrypted information to allow the Company to uniquely identify you. Each time you log into the Services, a session cookie containing an encrypted, unique identifier that is tied to your account is placed on your browser. These session cookies allow the Company to uniquely identify you when you are logged into the Services and to process your online transactions and requests. Session cookies are required in order to use the Services.

Donanza.com uses persistent cookies that only the Company can read and use to identify browsers that have previously visited the Company’s Websites. When you purchase the Services or provide the Company with personal information, a unique identifier is assigned you. This unique identifier is associated with a persistent cookie that the Company places on your web browser. The Company is especially careful about the security and confidentiality of the information stored in persistent cookies. For example, the Company does not store account numbers or passwords in persistent cookies. If you disable your web browser’s ability to accept cookies, you will be able to navigate the Company’s Websites, but you will not be able to successfully use the Services.

Donanza.com may use information from session and persistent cookies in combination with Data About donanza.com Customers to provide you with information about the Company and the Services.

Donanza.com uses web beacons alone or in conjunction with cookies to compile information about Customers and Visitors’ usage of the Company’s Websites and interaction with emails from the Company. Web beacons are clear electronic images that can recognize certain types of information on your computer, such as cookies, when you viewed a particular website tied to the web beacon, and a description of a website tied to the web beacon. For example, donanza.com may place Web beacons in marketing emails that notify the Company when you click on a link in the email that directs you to one of the Company’s Websites. Donanza.com uses web beacons to operate and improve the Company’s Websites and email communications.

Donanza.com may use information from Web beacons in combination with Data About donanza.com Customers to provide you with information about the Company and the Services.

Donanza.com uses local shared objects, also known as Flash cookies, to store your preferences or display content based upon what you view on our site to personalize your visit. Third parties, with whom the Company partners to provide certain features on our site or to display advertising based upon your web browsing activity, use Flash cookies to collect and store information.

Flash cookies are different from browser cookies because of the amount of, type of, and how data is stored. Cookie management tools provided by your browser will not remove Flash cookies. To learn how to manage privacy and storage settings for Flash cookies [click here.](http://www.macromedia.com/support/documentation/en/flashplayer/help/settings_manager.html#117118)

When you visit DoNanza’s Websites, the Company collects your IP addresses to track and aggregate non-personal information. For example, donanza.com uses IP addresses to monitor the regions from which Customers and Visitors navigate the Company’s Websites.

DoNanza does not have access to or control of the cookies that may be placed by third party advertisers. The information provided to third parties does not include personal information, but this information may be re-associated with personal information after the Company receives it.

Donanza.com also contracts with third-party advertising networks that collect IP addresses and other Web Site Navigational Information on the Company’s Websites and emails and on third-party websites. Ad networks follow your online activities over time by collecting Web Site Navigational Information through automated means, including through the use of cookies. They use this information to provide advertisements about products and services tailored to your interests. You may see these advertisements on other websites. To learn more about these and other advertising networks and their opt-out instructions, [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp).
