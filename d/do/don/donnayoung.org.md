# Terms of Use, Penmanship

## Privacy

###  [donnayoung.org](http://donnayoung.org/)

### Site-CD (YoungMinds) Customers

Donna Young and Paypal will request your name and address for the purpose of fulfilling a site-cd order. Donna Young keeps a copy of a customer's email address, name, and address for the purpose of customer support. Donna Young does not sell or otherwise share any personal information.

### Donnayoung.org Yahoo Group

Visitors who join the donnayoung update yahoo group will receive an email occasionally usually every two months, more or less. The email addresses are not sold or shared in any way.

### General Privacy 

The server that hosts donnayoung.org collects, like all servers do, non-personally identifiable information for the purpose of site statistics.

I reserve the right to send correspondence in the case of illegal/unethical activity such as copyright infringement or bandwidth theft.

### Google Ads

Donnayoung.org uses google ads.

Information about Google's cookies: 
  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site. 
  * Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet. 
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
