#### Donatos Privacy Statement

Donatos respects your privacy. That's why the individual identifying information you provide to us will be used solely for purposes of serving you better. "Individual identifying information" is information that enables us to identify you, such as your e-mail address, name, title and address.

#### How We Use The Information You Give Us 

Donatos uses the individual identifying information you give us in order to be able to selectively send you communications that we think would be of particular interest to you. That said, we will always give you the opportunity to opt out of receiving such direct marketing contact. With regard to electronic mail, no such communications will be sent without your permission and will be discontinued if you revoke this permission.

#### Who We Share Your Information With

When you provide your personal information to Donatos (such as name, address, phone number, company name, e-mail, etc.) we will not give or sell your individual information to any outside company for its use in marketing or solicitation. We will strictly maintain the confidentiality of your personal information. Additionally, internal practices help protect your privacy by limiting employee access to and use of any data you choose to share with Donatos. If you have any questions or comments about our privacy practices, would like to stop receiving information from Donatos, or would like to correct or update information provided to Donatos via its Web site, please contact us by sending an e-mail to [info@donatos.com.](mailto:info@donatos.com)
