# DMi Partners

DMi Partners recognizes the importance of protecting your personal information. Please print a copy of this Privacy Policy to add to your records, so that you can refer to our commitment to your privacy at your discretion. 

## Information We Collect

DMi Partners collects the following types of information: 

  * Personally Identifiable Information we collect from you on applications and forms completed on DMiPartners.com as well as from additional sources used to process your request. 
  * Information while you browse DMiPartners.com. While you view our site, our system tracks the referring url, the pages you view, the pages you download and your IP address. Additionally, DMiPartners.com and/or a marketing partner may place a "cookie" on your computer that tracks your use of DMiPartners.com. Please see section below for more information on cookies 



## Cookies

DMiPartners.com and/or a marketing partner may place a "cookie" on your computer that tracks your use of DMiPartners.com. A cookie is a string of data that is stored on your hard drive, which enables DMiPartners.com to determine the number of unique users accessing the various pages on DMiPartners.com. 

## Sharing of Your Information

We may share all of the information we collect with companies that perform marketing services on our behalf or to financial institutions with whom we may have joint marketing arrangements. Such companies and institutions may choose to contact you to discuss the interest you have expressed via your submission of a form. By submitting the form you are consenting to receive communication, including e-mail and telephone calls from these companies and institutions, even if your number appears on the Do Not Call list. 

## Responsibilities and Representations for Linked Web-Sites

Because DMiPartners.com can not control the content of sites linking to or from DMiPartners.com, we are not responsible, nor can we make representations to the content of those web-sites or their individual privacy policies. 

## Updates to Our Privacy Policy

From time to time, DMiPartners.com may need to make edits or changes to their privacy to reflect changes in business practices or compliance with new regulations. DMiPartners.com will provide notice of any revised policy to current customers and those customers whose nonpublic information we share as described previously. DMiPartners.com reserves the right to edit or change this policy at any time.
