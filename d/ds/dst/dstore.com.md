# dStore.com.au - Australian Online Department Store

  | 

**Privacy Policy:**  
dStore is committed to protecting your privacy. dStore does not sell, rent, or loan any identifiable information regarding our customers to any third party. Any information you provide is held with the utmost care and security, and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented. We do not send unsolicited mail and we always provide the opportunity to refuse marketing email from us. 

**Information we collect:**  
For us to process your order, we need to know your name, email address, delivery address and credit card details. We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent. Cookie technology is used to track the patterns of behaviour of the visitors to our site. This data allows us to better target our product offerings and enables us to provide you with the best possible online browsing experience.
