# Definitivedeals.com - Your definitive source for all online deals, offers, coupons, savings, bargains & discounts

Privacy Policy Statement for DefinitiveDeals.com

We respect user's privacy and make every effort to protect the privacy of the visitors to this website. 

**Contact**   
The administrator for this site can be reached via email at [admin@definitivedeals.com](mailto:admin@definitivedeals.com?subject=privacy).  All efforts will be made to respond to any inquires within 48 hours. 

**Information that is collected**   
Information such as domain name, browser type and statistics for links and deals that are being viewed are collected by this website. DefinitiveDeals only uses this information internally to improve the content and navigation of this site. Information that is volunteered by visitors such as e-mail addresses, contact information, comments etc. is also collected but are never shared with third parties. 

**Links to other websites**   
DefinitiveDeals links to several outside sites such as merchants, advertisers, partners and sponsors. When leaving DefinitiveDeals and browsing external sites, you are encouraged to review the privacy policy of those sites as it may or will differ from this privacy policy. DefinitiveDeals is not responsible for the privacy practices or policies of any third party web sites and this privacy policy statement does not apply to those Web sites. DefinitiveDeals makes no representation to the accuracy or existence of other websites' privacy policies. 

**E-mail addresses**   
DefinitiveDeals only collects and stores e-mail addresses that are submitted voluntarily to us by our visitors. The e-mail addresses are used to send out newsletters other related information and are **never** shared with outside parties. Providing your e-mail address to DefinitiveDeals is voluntary and is not required to browse the site. If you have previously submitted your e-mail address to DefinitiveDeals but no longer wish to receive e-mails from us, please send an e-mail to [remove@definitivedeals.com](mailto:remove@definitivedeals.com?subject=remove) and we will promptly remove your e-mail information. 

**Cookies**   
DefinitiveDeals do not set or make use of cookies. However, other merchants and organizations that are linked to on this website may set cookies when visiting their sites. 

**Data Security**   
DefinitiveDeals takes reasonable precautions to keep all personal information secure. There are managerial and physical procedures in place to prevent unauthorized access to data. 

**Changes to the Privacy Policy**   
DefinitiveDeals reserves the right to change and/or amend this privacy policy at any time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
