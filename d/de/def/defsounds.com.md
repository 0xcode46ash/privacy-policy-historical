### General Policy

At DefSounds.com we value your privacy. We hold absolutely no personal information about our visitors unless provided. We do not collect IP addresses however an email address is required for registration. We do not and will not sell any personal information provided by you to anyone. 

Here are some tips on how to protect your privacy while browsing the internet:  
[12 Ways To Protect Your Online Privacy](http://www.eff.org/Privacy/eff_privacy_top_12.html)

### What information does Defsounds.com collect?

Defsounds.com only collects information given by the user. In order to participate in discussions in our [forums](http://forums.defsounds.com/) and post comments on articles, a valid email address is required. This email address will not be sold or given to any third party. 

### What is my information used for?

Your e-mail address is used primarily for our Newsletter, however you have the option to turn the Newsletter future off in your account settings. Your e-mail address is also used for certain support tasks such as password recovery. 

### With whom is my information shared with?

Defsounds.com does not share your information with anyone, however it is important that you use discretion when revealing personal identifiable information when posting article comments or taking in part in our forums as it may be collected and use by an unknown and unauthorized third party. Such activities are beyond our control. 

For any other questions please [contact us.](http://www.defsounds.com/contact)
