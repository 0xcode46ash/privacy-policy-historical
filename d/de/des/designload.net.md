# DesignLoad.net - Website Templates, Designs, Logos, Powerpoint, Interfaces, Buttons, Arrows and more!

Creditcard transactions  
All Creditcard payments on our site are processed through **2checkout or Worldpay** merchant services. Creditcard information collected is stored only at 2checkout/Worldpay. Your creditcard is charged after the authorization procedure complete /we may give you a call to confirm your order/.

Site registration  
Registration process requires users to submit contact information. This data is used to identify the customer when logged into the system as well as for invoicing purposes. Your contact information is needed during the authorization procedure and it needs to be filled out correctly.

Our company will not sell or give client list to any other party.

IP Addresses and Logging  
Our server logs information regarding visitors to our site, such as IP address, date/time, pages accessed etc. This information is used to generate reports for statistical purposes.

Profile update  
When logged in, you can change your data in member area anytime.
