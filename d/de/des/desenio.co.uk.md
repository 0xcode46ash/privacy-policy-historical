# PRIVACY POLICY

**PRIVACY POLICY**

Through your registration and / or order, you agree to Desenios right to store and use information about your purchases, as well as the contact information you provide. We treat your information with the utmost care and ensure that your customer data is only used by Desenio. The data is stored and used to enable us to fulfill our commitments to you. This means that information about you will be disclosed even to our billing partner Klarna and current logistics partner. Desenio will not disclose your personal data to other third parties. The data is stored and is also used for marketing purposes, but only for Desenio. This means that, unless you object to it in written, Desenio may use your personal information to contact you via email with newsletters or direct marketing. Of course you have the possibility to at any time notify us that you object to the processing of your personal data for marketing purposes. Contact our customer service.
