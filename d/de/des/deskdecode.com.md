# Privacy policy | DESKDECODE.COM

This privacy policy has been created to serve better for those who are concerned with how their personal information is being used by our website. At deskdecode.com we are very serious about our privacy policies. Any information provided by you including name, email address, website etc. will never be provide to anyone outside deskdecode.com

**Personal information collection**

We don’t ask you for personal information unless we truly need it. We only collect information when you enter information on our site. We only subscribe your email address when you enter your email address in our Subscription page as based on your website experience. We also provide you a proper link about unsubscribing our email Subscription with every mail sent by us. We don’t share your personal information with anyone except deskdecode.com admin.

**Log Data**

Like most website operators, we are tracking your information to create a log file. This log file contains your IP address, type of browser, country, date/time, no. of pages open by you and submitted comment by you. We are collecting this information to get secure our website form hackers. If any user try’s to hack our site’s information, we create some restrictions for that IP address to protect our daily user’s information.

**  Cookies**

A “cookie” is a small text file which is placed in your computer to allow us to recognize you the next time you visit our site.

Yes, we use cookies to collect additional information data to improve our Services. Cookies help’s us to track you down when you visit again to our website and gives you a fastest performance to access our website.

**Comments**

Every comment is submitted to our Akismet anti-spam service. Your comment will not approve if you are commenting some spam link or creating backlinks of your website.

**Changes to this Policy**

We may change this privacy policy time to time which will be reflected in this page <http://www.deskdecode.com/privacy-policy/>** **. For any further questions, you may contact us at “info@deskdecode.com”.
