# Design Work Life » Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy

### Email List

The designworklife mailing list provides you with news and updates about [designworklife](http://designworklife.com/ "designworklife"), [Seamless Creative](http://seamlesscreative.com/ "Seamless Creative"), and [Seamless Paperie](http://seamlesspaperie.com/ "Seamless Paperie") via the submitted email address. Your email address will not be sold, given, or otherwise distributed to any third parties. You can opt out of the mailings at any time.

### Cookies

Third party vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to this website. No personally identifiable information is collected by these cookies. You can opt out of third party vendor’s cookies at any time by [visiting this page](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp "Opt Out").

### Questions?

Please feel free to [contact us](mailto:hello@designworklife.com "hello@designworklife.com") with any questions regarding our privacy policy.
