# DegreeDirectory.org -

### DegreeDirectory.org Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and will protect it in the ways outlined in this privacy statement. 

### Information Collection

In order to request information from a school listed in DegreeDirectory.org, you must submit a form that may include your name, address, phone number, and email address so the school or its authorized agent can contact you and provide the additional information you requested. DegreeDirectory.org will not share your information with anyone other than the school and/or the schoolâ€™s authorized agent. 

### Links to Other Sites

DegreeDirectory.org contains links to other web sites. While every effort is made to only link to quality, ethical sites, Education-Portal.com bears no responsibility for their information collection policies or any content that may be displayed on them. 

### Activity Log

DegreeDirectory.org may automatically log your site activity to analyze problems and to improve its usability. We cannot distinguish personally identifiable information from this data. 

### Cookies

DegreeDirectory.org does not use cookies to collect personal information.
