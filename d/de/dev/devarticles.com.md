# DevArticles - ASP articles, PHP articles, C# articles, VB.Net articles, MySQL articles, SQL Server articles, JavaScript articles, web developer forums

This is the web site of devArticles.com. We can be reached via email at [support@devarticles.com](mailto:support@devarticles.com).

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address.

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who post messages to our bulletin board, the e-mail addresses of those who make postings to our chat areas, information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations. The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded. It is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

Occasionally we may send you special promotion emails from our partners or companies which we feel offer value to our newsletter subscribers. We never provide these companies with your email address -- all special promotion emails are sent using our in-house mail servers and come complete with unsubscribe information.

With respect to cookies: We use cookies to record whether or not a visitor has rated any particular article already. If you do not want to receive e-mail from us, please let us know by sending us e-mail at the above address.

With respect to Ad Servers: We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies. 

With respect to security: We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact or Us via email at devart@devarticles.com.
