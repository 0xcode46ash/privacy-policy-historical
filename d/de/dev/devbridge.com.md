# Privacy Policy

## How Devbridge Group May   
Use Your Information

Devbridge Group will use your information to fulfill your requests for information, distribute newsletters and alerts to you, evaluate any job applications, and for such other purposes as you may initiate or request. We may keep any of your information on file and use it to contact you. When you submit your information, you consent to Devbridge Group using such information as described herein.

Devbridge Group will not intentionally disclose or transfer (and shall take reasonable steps to prevent the unauthorized or accidental disclosure of) your information to third parties (i.e., persons or entities that are not affiliates of Devbridge Group) without your consent, whether for such third parties’ marketing purposes or otherwise.

Additionally, Devbridge Group collects certain information that you do not visibly enter, such as your IP address, browsing pattern on the Site, click stream data, and HTTP protocol elements.

## Devbridge Group Subscriptions and Requests for Information

By downloading or subscribing to Devbridge Group’s email or submitting a Contact Us form, you agree to receive on occasion other correspondence from Devbridge Group related to the subscribed/requested insights and other news and information. You will be contacted by e-mail or otherwise only by Devbridge Group.

Your e-mail address will not be shared with any third party other than an email service provider Devbridge Group engages to send e-mails.

## Surveys

If you participate in a survey, all of our research results are reported as totals or in other aggregated form. Information about you as an individual respondent is not made public.

## Link to Third-party Sites

Devbridge Group may provide links to third-party Websites or information as a service to our users. If you use these links, you will leave this Site. Such links do not constitute or imply an endorsement, sponsorship, or recommendation by Devbridge Group of the third party, the third-party Website, or the information contained therein. Devbridge Group shall not be responsible or liable for your use thereof. Such use shall be subject to the terms of use and privacy policies applicable to those sites.

## Access to Your Information

You are entitled to know whether we hold information about you and, if we do, to have access to that information and require it to be corrected if it is inaccurate. You can do this by sending an email to [marketing@devbridge.com](mailto:marketing@devbridge.com).

You may also unsubscribe from mailing lists or any registrations on the Site. To do so, please follow instructions within the email received, email [marketing@devbridge.com](mailto:marketing@devbridge.com) or contact us at the address provided at the end of this policy.

## Security

Devbridge Group has implemented generally accepted standards of technology and operational security to protect information from loss, misuse, alteration, or destruction. Only authorized Devbridge Group personnel are provided access to contact information, and these employees and vendors are required to treat this information as confidential. However, Devbridge Group cannot guarantee that unauthorized persons will not obtain access to your information.

## Consent; Changes to Privacy Policy

By using this Site, you consent to the collection, use, and storage of your information by us in the manner described in this Privacy Policy and elsewhere on the Site. This Privacy Policy is subject to change from time to time. We will post the Privacy Policy changes and, if they are significant in nature then we will email notice of such changes. We will also keep a copy of the policy documents for future reference. This Privacy Policy was last updated on July 8, 2016.

## Questions About Our Privacy Policy

If you have any questions about Devbridge Group’s Privacy Policy or if you require any assistance in navigating or reading the materials on our website, please email [marketing@devbridge.com](mailto:marketing@devbridge.com).
