# Privacy Policy

DecorPad.com maintains the confidentiality, security and integrity of personal information we obtain from our members. To prevent unauthorized access, promote data security, and encourage appropriate use of information, we use a variety of tools to assist in the protection of your personal information. However, "perfect security" does not exist on the Internet, so we make no guarantees. We do not disclose personal information to any outside party. We will not give, sell, rent, or loan any identifiable personal information to any third party, unless legally required to do so. Any information we collect from our members will be used in a variety of ways, including to help analyze site traffic, understand member needs and trends, and to improve our services. This information is for internal use only and will not be distributed or sold to any parties outside DecorPad.

The protections described in this Privacy Policy do not apply when you provide information (including personal information) in connection with your use of these public forums. Any information you share in a public forum is public information and may be seen or collected by anyone, including third parties that do not adhere to our Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for events arising from the distribution of any information you choose to publicly post or share through our Site.

We are not responsible for the content of any websites which may be linked to DecorPad.com . These links are provided for your convenience only and you access them at your own risk. The privacy policies and the practices of websites outside DecorPad and all of its services are beyond our control. It is your responsibility to read and understand the privacy policies of any and all websites you visit on the web.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies: [Google AdSense](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html), [Google Analytics](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/) and [VigLink](http://www.viglink.com/static/policies/ftc).
