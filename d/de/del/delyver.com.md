# Privacy Policy - Delyver | Bangalore Based Home Delivery Service in Marathahalli, Whitefield, Bellandur, Sarjapur Road

PRIVACY POLICY

We respect your privacy and ensure that your personal information is safe with us.

What information do we collect from you?  
We collect information from you when you register on our site or fill out a form. When signing in to our site, you may be asked to provide your email ID. Placing an order requires us to collect the correct delivery address to which will the order will be delivered and the billing address if the delivery is to be done to a different address.

What do we use this information for?  
Any of the information which we collect will used in one of the following ways:  
• To personalize your experience to provide you information relevant to your location  
• To improve customer service and effectively respond to your queries and support needs  
• To process transactions and update you about the order status  
• To administer contest/survey/feedback about the site features  
Your email ID is used for login into our site post registration and will be used to send order related information. Your contact number will be used for sending order confirmation details and to update you about the status of the order. It will also be shared with the retailer where you are ordering from to ensure that you are informed about any updates on the order.

Do we disclose your information to outside parties?  
We do not sell, trade or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. This does not include the retailers (also referred to as ‘partners’) from where you are ordering, thereby ensuring that your order is prepared and delivered to the right address. We may also release your information when it is essential to comply with law, enforce our site policies or protect others or ours rights, property or safety.
