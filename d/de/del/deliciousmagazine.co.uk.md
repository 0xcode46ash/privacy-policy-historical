# delicious.

  
Privacy Policy

The publishers of delicious. ( Seven Publishing Limited) value your privacy. The personal information you provide through the delicious. web site will be held by us in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998. 

  1. Registering to receive our email update, or
  2. Answering any of our reader questionnaires.



The information you provide is held on a secure database and is used to improve the services to our readers and web site users. We will not disclose your personal details outside our company. Information you provide will be disclosed only in an aggregated form to our marketing partners. At no time will any reader be individually identified. The only exception to this is if we are required to share information as part of a legal process such as a subpoena or a court order. 

If at any time you wish to be removed from our database, simply send an email to [unsubscribe@deliciousmagazine.co.uk](mailto:unsubscribe@deliciousmagazine.co.uk?subject=UNSUBSCRIBE) with "UNSUBSCRIBE" in the subject line.
