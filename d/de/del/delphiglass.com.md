# Delphi - Security and Privacy Info

|  **Safe Shopping Guarantee**  
Your credit card information is safe with us! Our 128-bit encryption technology is considered the best available today for secure e-commerce transactions, so that you never have to worry about credit card safety. Period.  
---  
We guarantee that every transaction you make at DelphiGlass.com will be 100% safe. DelphiGlass.com holds a digital Super Certificate(128-bit) from **Thawte** , a trusted Certification Authority. Our secure server software (SSL) is the industry standard among the best software available today for secure commerce transactions. It encrypts **all of your personal information** , including credit card number, name, and address, so that it cannot be read as the information travels over the Internet.  |    |   
  
**Your Privacy**  
At DelphiGlass.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy. DelphiGlass.com does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. When you order, we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing address, credit card number, and expiration date. This allows us to process and fulfill your order and to notify you of your order status.

We may also use the information we collect to notify you about important functionality changes to the Web site, new DelphiGlass.com services, and special offers we think you'll find valuable from Delphi and select arts and craft supply partners.
