# Privacy at Dealitem.com!

Dealitem.com is fully committed to the security and privacy of our valuable visitors.

Personal Information

Dealitem.com Web Server recognizes only the visitors' IP address and the Browser Version. This information is used to aid us in fixing problems and to serve you better.

Mailing List

Dealitem.com does not have any mailing listat this time.

Cookies

Dealitem.com does not use cookies.

Online Offers

We bring you the best offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information.

Feedback?

Dealitem.com welcomes comments and questions on this policy. We are dedicated to protecting your personal information and will make every reasonable effort to keep that information secure. Any revisions to our policy will be posted to this site. Questions regarding this statement should be directed to [Our Feedback page](https://web.archive.org/web/20080619192318id_/http%3A//www.dealitem.com/emaillink/default.asp?feedback=1).
