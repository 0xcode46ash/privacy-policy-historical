# Privacy Policy -

Simple Debt Solutions (SDS) respects the privacy of every individual who visits its Web Site and any information collected about you will firstly be used to fulfil any service you might request and secondly improve how as a company we serve you. We do this by making appropriate use of the information.

This information will not be disclosed to anyone outside of SDS, its affiliated or associated companies, its agencies, or licensees, and other companies with which SDS has arranged services for your benefit. It will be treated in accordance with the legal provisions under English law concerning data protection.

You have the right as an individual to find out what information we hold about you and make corrections if necessary; you also have the right to ask us to not use the information. We will make all practical efforts to respect your wishes.
