# dealnews and Your Right to Privacy

**dealnews.com Inc. Privacy Policy**

The policies below are applicable to dealnews.com, dealmac.com, dealchat.com, and other sites published by dealnews.com Inc. (dealnews), 102A Wynn Drive, Huntsville, AL Â 35805, email [privacy@dealnews.com](mailto:privacy@dealnews.com).

dealnews respects each individual user's right to privacy. dealnews does not collect personal identifying information unless such information is specifically and knowingly provided from users during contests, surveys, sweepstakes, site registration, or use of public areas such as a forum. dealnews may use personal identifying information gathered from these activities to notify a user of a winning entry in a contest or sweepstakes, to tell a user about new features and services on its website, or to display advertisements or content which closely match a user's interests. dealnews does not supply personal identifying information to third parties unless the user "opts-in" for such purposes by specifically authorizing its use. dealnews is not responsible for the actions or policies of third parties.

dealnews uses reasonable precautions to keep the personal information disclosed to us secure. However, dealnews is not responsible for any breach of security. If a dealnews user knowingly provides personal information, such as a name or email address, in a forum or other public service, that user should be aware that the information provided can be collected and used by third parties. Such activities are beyond the control of dealnews.

Non-personal information and data may be automatically collected through the standard operation of dealnews's Internet servers or through the use of "cookies." Cookies are small text files a web site places on your computer's hard drive to recognize repeat users and facilitate the user's use of the site. Also, cookies allow a site to track usage behavior and compile aggregate data for content improvement, targeted advertising, and marketing and promotional purposes. Users should be aware that dealnews cannot control the use of cookies or the resulting information by advertisers or other third parties. Most browsers allow a user to deny cookies; however, users should note that cookies may be required to provide certain web site features.

ATTENTION KIDS: No information should be submitted to dealnews by children under 18 years of age without his or her parent's consent. dealnews does not monitor the age of its user audience and may collect personal information from users who are children under thirteen years of age. A parent has the option to agree to collection and use of her child's information without agreeing to that information being shared with third parties. dealnews cannot require that a child disclose more information than is reasonably necessary to participate in a particular activity, whether a contest, survey, site registration, forum, or other activity.

dealnews reserves the right to change this policy at any time by posting notice of the existence of a new privacy statement. This statement and the policies outlined herein are not intended to and do not create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party.

dealnews network works with a third party that serves ads to this site. To find out more about how Flycast manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html.
