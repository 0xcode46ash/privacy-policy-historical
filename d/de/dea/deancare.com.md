# Dean Health System Privacy Statement, Medical Disclaimer and Copyright Statement

Privacy Statement & Medical Disclaimer 

If you are currently experiencing a medical emergency use your telephone and call 9-1-1.

Privacy Statement  
Dean Health Systems Inc., is committed to protecting the privacy of its web sites users and has adopted this privacy statement to govern our conduct and document our commitment to privacy. Our health information sites do not request or collect any information that personally identifies you or allows you to be personally contacted unless you affirmatively provide express direct authorization for the collection and/or use of such information. If you expressly and affirmatively provide any personal information to us, we will not share that information with any other party. We will not contact you unless you request that we contact you. From the information we learn about you from your visit to this site, we store only the following: the IP address from which you access the Internet, the date and time, the Internet address of the web site from which you linked directly to our site, the name of the file or the words you searched, and the browser used to access our site. This information is used to measure the number of visitors to the various sections of our site and identify system performance or problem areas. This site does not use cookies. "Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. This privacy statement will be updated periodically. Last update, August 7, 2001.

Medical Disclaimer  
Information provided on this and any other Dean Health Systems Web site is neither intended nor implied to be a substitute for professional medical advice. You should not use this information on this web site or the information on links from this site to diagnose or treat a health problem or disease without consulting with a qualified healthcare provider. Dean Health Systems advises you to always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider prior to starting any new treatment or with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.

Dean Health Systems provides links to outside sources as a courtesy; however, Dean Health Systems is not responsible for information on other web sites. At Dean Health Systems we strive to assure our web site is accurate and timely, we cannot guarantee accuracy of the contents.
