# Dead Frontier - Dead Frontier Privacy Policy

  
  
**Cookies**

Deadfrontier.com uses cookies to store session information so you will not see the same advertisement repeatedly.

**External Site Links**

Deadfrontier.com links to other websites on the internet. Content and Privacy Policies are the responsibility of their respective owners.

**Advertisers**

Most advertisements on deadfrontier.com are provided by outside advertisement companies and may contain cookies. These cookies are collected by their respective companies and are not accessible to deadfrontier.com.  


**IP Addresses and Logging**

Deadfrontier.com server logs information about visitors, including IP addresses, date/time visited, referring website, length of stay, etc. This information is used for site statistics only.  


**Customer Information & Credit Card Transactions**

Limited information is supplied to deadfrontier.com in the event of a sale. This includes your name, IP Address and website domain name. This information is not sold or distributed to third parties. Credit card payments are processed through [Plimus.com](http://www.plimus.com/). This information is collected solely for Plimus to securely process the order, and is not accessible to deadfrontier.com
