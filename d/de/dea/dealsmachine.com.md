# Dealsmachine.com: Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy Statement

**What Information Do We Collect?**

Dealsmachine.com takes user privacy seriously and makes reasonable efforts to protect your privacy. Be aware that Dealsmachine.com does collect and track certain user information such as time of day, browser type, browser language, and IP address of each user. ** **

**With Whom Does Dealsmachine.com Share Information? **

We do not sell or share any personal information you provide us to anybody. Whether you send us an email or sign up for our newsletter, you can rest assured that we are not going to sell or give away any information you provided us for mailing lists or for anybody else. We are just trying to find you the best deals, and your information will be kept safe and never given out!

Dealsmachine.com contains mostly links to other sites. Please be aware that we Dealsmachine.com are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this website.

**Your Consent and Changes to the Privacy Policy**

This Privacy Policy may change from time to time. Dealsmachine.com will post those changes on this page.
