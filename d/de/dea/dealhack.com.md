# Dealhack: Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is intended to communicate to our users the type of information collected by Dealhack and how that information is used. 

**Ownership**

Dealhack.com is owned and operated by Shopping Syndicate LLC, which is a California Limited Liability Company. All references to "Dealhack" contained herein are inclusive of and concurrently refer to Shopping Syndicate LLC and dealhack.com. 

**Statistic Logs (Not Personally Identifiable)**

On our web server, we have logs that give us information about the ways users access the content on our website. We use that information to draw conclusions about how our visitors use the site. We do occasionally share aggregate site traffic information with our partners and advertisers. However, we do not share personally identifiable user information, except as indicated below. 

**Collection of Personal Information**

Dealhack does not collect personally identifiable information from users unless such information is specifically and knowingly provided during email communication, "contact us" forms, surveys or contests. 

Information submitted via email becomes property of Dealhack. We will preserve your privacy, but reserve the right to use success stories and testimonials for promotional purposes. We will not release your contact information in these testimonials. If you would like to email us your feedback or success story but would like it to remain confidential, please express your intention clearly in your email. 

**Cookies**

Non-personal information and data will automatically be collected through the standard operation of our web servers and through the use of "cookies." Cookies are small bits of text data that web browsers store based on requests from web servers. They allow us to recognize repeat users and facilitate your use of the site. Also, our cookies allow us to track usage behavior and compile aggregate data for content improvement, targeted advertising, and marketing and promotional purposes. 

Some of our business partners use cookies on our site (for example, advertisers). Please be aware that Dealhack cannot control the use of cookies or the resulting information that is collected by those advertisers or other third parties through the use of cookies. Most browsers allow a user to deny cookies; however, please note that authorizing the use of cookies may be required to provide full access and optimal interaction with certain web site features. 

**Links**

Dealhack provides links to other websites over which we have no control. If you follow those links you become subject to the privacy policy of the site you are visiting. 

**Third Party Advertising**

Dealhack posts ads on the site that are delivered to users directly by Google, LinkShare and Commission Junction. Those companies collect information about users' visits to this site, such as number of times they have viewed an ad (but not user name, address, or other personal information). They use that information to decide which ads to show users of Dealhack. For more information the cookies used by these sites and how to "opt-out", please review [Google](http://www.google.com/intl/en/privacy.html)'s, [LinkShare](http://www.linkshare.com/home/privacy.shtml)'s and [Commission Junction](https://www.cj.com/privacy.jsp)'s privacy policies. Please understand we have no access to or control over how those sites use cookies. 

**Providing Information as Required by Law**

We do not provide information about Dealhack users to other organizations or to people not employed by Dealhack. However, if we are required by law to provide personal information about our users to local, state or federal authorities, we will do so. 

**Site Security**

Dealhack takes reasonable precautions to keep the information users disclose to us secure. However, Dealhack is not responsible for any breach of security that might occur. 

**Attention Children**

No information should be submitted to Dealhack by children less than 18 years of age without their parent's or guardian's consent. Dealhack does not monitor the age of its user audience. 

**Change Notification**

Dealhack may choose to modify its Privacy Policy from time to time without notice. Should Dealhack change our Privacy Policy, we'll immediately post changes here on this Privacy Policy page. You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy from time to time to ensure that you are aware of any changes. We will always use information in accordance with the Privacy Policy that was in effect at the time the information was collected. 

Dealhack's current privacy policy was last modified on 24 August 2005 and has been continuously in effect since that date. 

This statement and the policies outlined herein are not intended to and do not create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party.
