# dealspotr - Scanning the top deal sites for the best deals

## Privacy Policy

The dealspotr Privacy Policy describes how we treat personal information when you use dealspotr's products and services, including information provided to products that use dealspotr's idealspotr technology. In addition, the following describes our privacy practices that are specific to dealspotr.

### Personal information

  * You need a dealspotr Account to create an idealspotr homepage that is accessible from any Internet-connected computer. dealspotr asks for some personal information when you create a dealspotr Account, including your email address and a password, which is used to protect your account from unauthorized access. A dealspotr Account allows you to access many of our services that require registration. 
  * You may also create an idealspotr homepage that is accessible only from your computer, with preference settings stored in a cookie. 
  * You may choose to submit additional information in order to personalize your homepage (such as a zip code to customize your weather forecast). 
  * When you use idealspotr, dealspotr's servers automatically record log information which may include information about your preference settings. 



### Uses

  * dealspotr maintains and processes personal information in order to provide your personalized homepage and other services. This information will be stored in a cookie on your machine and sent to dealspotr when you request the page if you have set up a page without an associated dealspotr Account, or transmitted to dealspotr's servers and securely stored in association with your dealspotr Account information if you are signed in to your dealspotr Account. 



### Your choices

  * You can terminate your dealspotr Account at any time. 
  * You can delete your cookie with your preferences stored by following the instructions for deleting cookies in your browser. 



### Information Sharing

  * Most dealspotr gadgets and themes are provided by third parties, not dealspotr, who may receive and process your requests and preference settings for that gadget or theme in order to provide their service. In some cases, other information including one or more of your dealspotr cookies may also be technically available to the third party, although third parties are prohibited by contract from accessing your dealspotr cookies or other information unrelated to the gadget or theme they provide. Use of your information by these third parties is not governed by this Privacy Policy. Gadgets and Themes made by dealspotr are labeled with a "By dealspotr" logo in the gadget directory.
  * Some idealspotr gadgets allow you to share information, updates and activities with your friends. These social gadgets are labeled with a special icon.
  * We may share aggregate statistics about idealspotr usage with our business partners. We will not share personally identifying information with these partners or any third parties without your consent, except under the narrow circumstances described in the [dealspotr Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/privacy.php).
