# Privacy Policy

DealTaker takes pride in providing you with a Fun, Friendly place to exchange information with other people. To keep the site interesting, we have several unique features, such as image posting, Personal Messaging, Custom Signatures, Private forums and such. 

We do not requure any personal information within the DealTaker systems, other than your email address, username and password. We do not sell emails and only rarely will send notifications of site updates through email. Although many of the features give the assumption of privacy, we do not guarantee that any other information you provide through the DealTaker systems will be held in confidence. All information is stored within a database and could potentially be compromised. In addition to other items, we do actively track IP's of all accesses to the site, including Guests.   
In addition, our Administrators & Moderators have varying levels of access to any information stored within the DealTaker systems. 

All activity you partake in within the DealTaker systems is at your own risk, and there is no guarantee of truthfulness, availability, security or stability. Administrators and Moderators have the right to edit any message for any reason. 

Any messages are your own responsibility. DealTaker will work with law enforcement or other agencies in providing information if requested. 

The DealTaker forums are for Entertainment only. 

Rules:   
1\. keep it clean, Nothing you wouldn't want your mother or kids to see is allowed here.   
2\. No Illegal Activity is allowed.
