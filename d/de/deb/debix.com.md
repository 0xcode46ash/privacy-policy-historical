### Information Collected

“Personal Information” means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, first and last name, home or other physical address, email address, phone number, or other contact information, whether at work or at home, date of birth, Social Security number, gender, password, credit card number and information about dependents. AllClear ID may ask you to provide Personal Information that will enable us to enhance your use of the Service, to enroll you for the Service, or for purposes such as facilitating correspondence with you, registering your entitlement to access certain special features, or to complete transactions. It is always your choice whether or not to provide that Personal Information. If you choose not to provide requested Personal Information, you may not be able to register for, or use certain features of the Service.

“Technical Information” AllClear ID may collect and use technical data and related information that is gathered periodically to facilitate the provision of the Service. AllClear ID may also collect technical data and related information – including but not limited to technical information about your devices, system and application software, and peripherals. We may also automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser, which could include your IP address, cookie information, browser information, and the page you request. This information is not deemed by AllClear ID to constitute Personal Information and while AllClear ID will not use it in a way that associates such information with you, such information it may be aggregated and used. AllClear ID owns this information and may use it in any manner it deems appropriate.

Your Personal Information will be used to enable us to deliver the Service to you, in support of your relationship with AllClear ID and your use of our Services, and as otherwise set forth herein.

Among other things, AllClear ID wants to help you quickly find information on our Site, leverage our tools in connection with your authorized use of our Service, and alert you to product upgrades, special offers, updated information, and other new products and services. Personal Information you provide at registration or in your Profile section may also be used to customize your experience on the Site and with the Service.

AllClear ID will use your Personal Information to communicate with you through email and notices posted on the Site and through the Service. However, users may "opt out" of receiving such information. To opt-out, please send a request to support@allclearid.com or follow the instructions set forth in the applicable communication.

### Security

AllClear ID utilizes a wide range of advanced technologies for Internet and secure interactive voice security, including multi-band, multi-factor authentication, data encryption, and database data encryption.

When you attempt to access secure areas of the Site or sign into the Service, AllClear ID will authenticate you.

AllClear ID protects your information over the Internet by using a secure Web server, which allows your Web browser programs (Microsoft Internet Explorer, Firefox, etc.) to interact with AllClear ID’s Web server via an encrypted session.

AllClear ID uses a Secure Socket Layer (SSL) connection that provides an encrypted connection between your computer and AllClear ID. The encrypted connection scrambles ordinary text or data into ciphertext to safeguard your information as it is transmitted over the Internet. The information is decrypted, or put back into a readable format, when it reaches its intended destination.

Certain Personal Information AllClear ID collects is encrypted. In addition, the Site and the Service are hosted in an environment that employs firewall and other advanced technology designed to prevent interference or access from outside and unauthorized inside intruders.
