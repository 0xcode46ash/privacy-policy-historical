# Cheapskate Monthly Online: Privacy Statement

**Your Privacy is Important to Us** Â 

Cheapskate Monthly Online has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this Website: cheapskatemonthly.com, cheapsk8.com and debtproofliving.com. 

This site does not sell, rent or give away any visitor/customer information that is collected from any forms within the site. All personal information collected is kept completely confidential and is only used to keep track of subscriptions, for product shipping and to keep our visitors/customers informed of what's happening at Cheapskate Monthly. 

This site contains links to other sites. Cheapskate Monthly Online is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

We may sometimes use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, our ad company may collect cookies received with banner ads, and we do not have access to this information. 

**Security.** This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. 

**Choice/Opt-Out.** Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about the visitor. This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service. 

You can send an email to the following address: 

[cmupdate@webwinder.com](mailto:cmupdate@webwinder.com)

**Correct/Update.** This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided. Send email to the following address: 

[cmsupport@webwinder.com](mailto:cmsupport@webwinder.com)

**Contacting the Web Site.** If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please visit our online customer support form, located at the following web page address:
