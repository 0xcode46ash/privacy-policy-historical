# DtDNS :: Privacy Statement

The following privacy statement discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this web site.

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. IP addresses are also used in the redirection of hostnames, and will be publicly available to the Internet community. Since this is an IP redirection service, IP addresses may also be used in statistical tracking.

Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name and email address) and demographic information (like their zip code, age, or income level). The customer's contact information is used primarily to contact the visitor when necessary. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. Parts of this information may be publicly available through the WHOIS lookup system we have in place.

This site contains links to other sites. DtDNS is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

**Public Forums**  
This site makes forums, message boards, and/or mailing lists available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

**Security**  
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All data is backed up in the case of an emergency or misuse.

**Choice/Opt-Out**  
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor. We also offer an option in the Account Profile to toggle the option to receive notices.

This site gives users the option for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service by using the Cancel Account option in the profile manager.

This site gives users the option of changing and modifying information previously provided by modifying it in the profile manager.
