# DVD Times - DVD News, Reviews and Features

**Privacy Statement**  
DVD Times have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for DVD Times.

**Information Automatically Logged**  
We use your IP address to monitor traffic movement through our sites, your IP address isn't normally used to specifically identify you.

**Cookies**  
Cookies may be used by DVD Times as a means of remembering your personalised settings and to remember your username making it easier for you to post messages and to help you keep track of messages that you have already viewed. Cookies may be used on the site as a means of tracking viewers in a non-specific manor to establish how users use our site.

**Advertisers**  
DVD Times mostly use outside advertisement companies to display advertisements. These advertisements may contain cookies and other modern technologies that are secure. Cookies are also used by our own advertisement system to allow advertisers to determine how well a partcular campaign has been. While DVD Times uses cookies in other parts of the web site, cookies received with banner advertisements are usually only collected by the advertisement company, and DVD Times does not have access to this information.

**External Links**  
DVD Times may contain links to other sites. DVD Times is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites, for more information on this topic, please consult the disclaimer.

**Public Forums**  
DVD Times makes commenting systems available to our users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. Similarly DVD Times cannot be held responsible for any information that is posted to these services, even though we may decide to remove it.

**Security**  
DVD Times has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. All information stored about users is backed up regularly, the information stored online, is protected.

**Choice/Opt-Out**  
DVD Times provides users with the opportunity to opt-out of receiving promotional/marketing information from us. You can send an email to admin@dvdtimes.co.uk or follow the details that are provided in the appropriate email's relating to unsubscribing.

**Contacting DVD Times**  
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of DVD Times, or your dealings with DVD Times, then you can contact: admin@dvdtimes.co.uk
