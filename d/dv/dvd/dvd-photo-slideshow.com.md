# Photo to DVD Slideshow Software - Slideshow DVD, Photo to DVD, DVD Slide Show

## Privacy Policy:

### ANVSOFT's Commitment to Privacy

ANVSOFT respects your privacy. This policy governs websites owned and operated by ANVSOFT and discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices of our corporate website.

### Information Collection and Use

ANVSOFT collects personal information including name, address and email when requesting information, registering for an event or requesting to be contacted by us through a form on our website. We gather this information in order to fulfill your request. You also have the option to opt-in to receive future email communications from us. ANVSOFT Inc. uses secure server hosted by [2CheckOut](http://www.2checkout.com/) and [PayPal](http://www.paypal.com/) that encrypt all the customer's personal information including name, address, credit card number credit card expiration date etc. before it is sent to us. Encryption works to prevent any malicious use of your personal information. Your credit card information is only used for online transition. We do not rent, sell or trade your information with any other people or nonaffiliated companies.

### Opt-out Policy

ANVSOFT subscribers who have opted-in to receive email communications from us have the ability to opt-out by following the opt-out instructions on the bottom of emails sent. You may also opt-out by contacting [unsubscribe@anvsoft.com](mailto:unsubscribe@anvsoft.com)
