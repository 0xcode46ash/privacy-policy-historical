# Privacy policy - Discussion Leaders

Personal Information means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, a home or other physical address and an email address or other contact information, whether at work or at home. In general, you can visit Web pages of DiscussionLeaders.com. without telling us who you are or revealing any Personal Information about yourself.  Some of our Web pages utilize “cookies” and other tracking technologies. A “cookie” is a small text file that may be used, for example, to collect information about Web site activity. Some cookies and other technologies may serve to recall Personal Information previously indicated by a Web user. Most browsers allow you to control cookies, including whether or not to accept them and how to remove them.  You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie, or you may choose to block cookies with your browser. Tracking technologies may record information such as Internet domain and host names; Internet protocol (IP) addresses; browser software and operating system types; clickstream patterns; and dates and times that our site is accessed. Our use of cookies and other tracking technologies allows us to improve our Web site and your Web experience. We may also analyze information that does not contain Personal Information for trends and statistics. 

At no point in time will DiscussionLeaders.com management team working under any of its products and/or services share this information for any purposes whatsoever with anyone outside of the company. In case such a need arises in the future it will be done with full disclosure of how and what data shall be shared. This will not be done without the express consent of the respective individual.

This privacy policy is intended to be a generic guideline while using the products/services extended by the management team of this website.

Management team of DiscussionLeaders.com reserves the right to change certain aspects of this policy as and when it deems fit. The users will always be notified of such changes and will be given an option to opt-out if they disagree with the revised policies.
