# Discount School Supply - Privacy Policy

| **Cart Summary**  
---  
Cart is empty.  
  
|  Discount School Supply is dedicated to safeguarding your privacy when you visit the Discount School Supply website (the "Site"). To do so, we have created the following policy to make sure that you understand how we treat your personal information. This privacy policy may be updated from time to time without notice to you.

Some personal information is gathered when you register on the Site. Discount School Supply does not sell or rent that information to anyone. However, Discount School Supply may, from time to time, use the information to: 1) contact you; 2) send you information about Discount School Supply, and/or; 3) send you promotional materials from some of Discount School Supply's partners. If you do not want to receive any information or promotional materials, please follow the unsubscribe instructions included with the promotional material.

Discount School Supply has taken security precautions to prevent the loss or misuse of any personal information you provide us on the Site.

Demographic, profile, and IP address data is also collected at the Site. This information may be shared with our partners, but is provided only on an aggregate basis, and does not identify you personally. In other words, Discount School Supply's partners never have access to your individual account information. Discount School Supply uses "cookies" to store and track information about you so that we can deliver more personalized service to you when you use the Site. You may modify your browser to: 1) accept all cookies; 2) be notified when a cookie is set, or; 3) reject all cookies. If you choose to reject all cookies, you will not be able to use the portion of the Site that requires registration to participate.

Discount School Supply's Site may maintain "links" to other Web sites. Discount School Supply is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such other Web sites.

This Site may contain "public" forums, such as "chat rooms," news groups, or message boards. If you post any information on those forums, that information becomes public information and may be collected and used by others.

If you have any questions regarding Discount School Supply's privacy policy, or if you want to modify or remove any information provided about you to Discount School Supply's, please send us an e-mail at: [service@earlychildhood.com](mailto:service@earlychildhood.com).
