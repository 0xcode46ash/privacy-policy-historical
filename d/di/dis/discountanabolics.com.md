# Security & Privacy: Discount Anabolics

** Secure Server Certificate **

All orders are processed with Equifax Secure Socket Layer (SSL), which support 128-bit encryption, an industry standard for protecting your personal information while and after your order is placed. Our SSL certificate is up-to-date and ready to protect your information while checking out. This system will protect all of your private information, including credit card number, from ever being retrieved from potentially mischievous hackers.

Don't take our word for it, verify our certificate yourself.  During the checkout process, you will be browsing through our SSL.  On your browser window, down in the right had corner, you will see a pad lock.  Double click the pad lock to reveal our certificate and its expiration date.

RMSN guarantees that every order placed will be 100% Safe and Secure.  No customer will be held liable for any unauthorized purchases made on this site.  If you feel for any reason that your privacy or security has been breached, please contact us immediately.

During the checkout process, we collect only the information we need to process your order efficiently and effectively.  RMSN will not release _any_ of your private information to _any_ third party organization or agency. Your information is kept safe and secure.  Most importantly, your information will not be used by RMSN for any unauthorized marketing.  Any phone calls or emails from RMSN will only be to collect additional information to complete an existing order.
