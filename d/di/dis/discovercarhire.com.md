# Car Hire with discount rates from Discover Car Hire

##### Privacy Policy Statement

Discover Car Hire is committed to ensuring that all customer personal details and credit card information is kept confidential, so that it cannot be read as it is sent over the internet. Booking on our website is a completely secure process. We use SSL (Secure Socket Layer) technology, which is a recognised industry standard that encrypts all your personal data. 

For each visitor on our Web page, our Web server automatically recognises information regarding the domain or email address. We collect the email addresses of those who communicate with us via email. We may also collect name, address and telephone number of those who register in our system. We collect no information on consumers who browse our Web page. If you do not want to receive any emails from us in the future, please let us know by sending an email to [support@discovercarhire.com](mailto:support@discovercarhire.com). If you provide us with your postal address on-line, you will only receive the information directly related to the purpose for which you provided us your address. Persons who provide their telephone numbers, will only receive telephone contact from us for information regarding orders they have placed on-line. 

We use cookies on our website to make sure you have the best experience we can offer. With respect to Ad Servers: We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies. From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will contact you before using your data for these purposes to notify you of the policy change and to provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. Customers may prevent their information from being used for purposes other than those for which it was originally collected by sending us an email at the address above. 

Upon request, we provide site visitors with access to unique identifier information (e.g., customer number or password) that we keep about them, reservation request information (e.g., dates on which customers made reservation request) that we keep about them. Consumers can access this information by sending us an email at the address above. Upon request we offer visitors the ability to have inaccuracies corrected in contact information, unique identifiers, communications that the consumer/visitor has forwarded to the site.
