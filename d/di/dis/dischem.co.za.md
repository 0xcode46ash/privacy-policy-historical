# Dis-Chem | Privacy Statement for the Dis-Chem Website.

**Privacy Statement for the Dis-Chem Website.**  
We created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: Dis-Chem.co.za. Information Automatically Logged We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. Submissions to our query system will log your ip automatically onto our server. We do this to prevent any attempts to hack our server or any malicious attacks. 

**Cookies**  
Our Site may use cookies. This information is collected by our server, and is used for statistical purposes as well as member log-in verification. We may also utilize cookies.. These cookies are used to monitor how many unique visits you send our ranking system. 

**Personal Information**  
We may collect limited information from members in order to contact them from time to time regarding website updates, for verification purposes, and to allow their website to be shared with our visitors. Any personal information collected on this Web site is not shared with any third parties. 

**External Links**  
This site contains links to other sites. Dis-Chem.co.za is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

**Contacting this Web Site**  
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can use this form: [contact form](https://web.archive.org/web/20130531204317id_/http%3A//dischem.co.za/contactus.asp).
