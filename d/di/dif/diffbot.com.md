# Diffbot | Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the website at http://www.diffbot.com (the “Site”). 

Using the Site, you can sign up for a service that autonomously understands any webpage and converts it into a set of entities and facts (the "Service"). 

At Diffbot Technologies Corp., we respect and protect the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your information and is part of our [Terms of Use](https://web.archive.org/terms/) when you use our Site or Service. 

### Your Consent

By using our Site or Service, you consent to our Privacy Policy. If you don’t agree, please don’t use our Site or Service. 

### What information do we collect?

When you register for the Service you'll be asked to provide certain information about yourself, such as your name, email, credit card information, and billing address. 

We may also collect non-personal information, including information that can’t be used to personally identify you as an individual, such as: 

  * anonymous usage data
  * referring/exit pages and URLs
  * platform types



We may use your public IP address to detect your location, so that we know where our potential users are coming from. Also, our Site or Service may not be available in all areas. 

### How do we use your information?

Information we collect from you might be used: 

  * To improve our products
  * To craft algorithms used within our products
  * To provide data used to generate blog posts, articles, and/or website content
  * For marketing purposes



### Do we use cookies?

Yes. Cookies are small files that include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies let us recognize your browser and remember certain information about you in order to personalize your experience of our Site and Service. 

We also use cookies to compile aggregate data about traffic to our Site so that we can improve our Site. 

We may use both persistent and session cookies; persistent cookies remain on your computer after you close your session and until you delete them; session cookies expire when you close your browser.  

### How long do we store your information?

We intend to store your information indefinitely. 

### What about links to other websites?

We may provide links to or compatibility with other websites or applications.  However, we’re not responsible for the privacy practices employed by those websites or the information or content they contain.  

### How do we protect your information?

We use appropriate physical, electronic, and other procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect. However, please be aware that the Internet is an inherently unsafe environment, and that hackers are constantly working to defeat security measures. 

Thus, we cannot guarantee that your information will not be accessed, disclosed, altered or destroyed, and you accept this risk. 

### Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personal information for commercial or marketing purposes. 

We will, if required by a valid court order, provide your personal information in a civil or criminal proceeding. 

### Not Intended for Children

Our Site and Service are not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly or specifically collect information about children under the age of 18. 

### Changes to our Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on this page. We may also, but are not required to, send you an email notice. 

### Contact Us

If you have questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@diffbot.com.
