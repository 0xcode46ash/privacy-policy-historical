# Privacy

Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

**Commitment to our Customers:**

Professional Satellite & Communications is committed to providing you with a private and secure shopping experience when using one of our online storefronts.

We are a proud member of the Better Business Bureau and firmly believe in the BBBOnLine® Reliability mission to promote trust and confidence on the Internet. For further information visit their site at http://www.bbbonline.org.

**Overall Privacy Statement:**

Professional Satellite & Communications takes your privacy seriously. Because we gather certain types of information about our users we want to help you understand the terms and conditions surrounding the collection and use of that information. This privacy statement discloses the types of information we gather, how we use it, and how we strive to protect it. These privacy practices apply to the Web site that you were viewing when you clicked through to this policy, which is operated by Professional Satellite & Communications, its partners or affiliates.

This privacy policy applies to the U.S. operations of all products and services within the Professional Satellite & Communications family.

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Professional Satellite & Communications does not own or control, or to people that Professional Satellite & Communications does not employ or manage.

Our affiliate and partner websites have their own privacy policy as well, please be sure to read their privacy policy and guide lines.

**1\. What Personally Identifiable Information Do We Collect?**

**a. Personal Information**

We collect personally identifiable information when you provide it to us, primarily for billing and shipping purposes when you purchase our products online.

Your name, address, phone number, email address, and credit card information, such as the number, name, and expiration date on your credit card information is used to fill and ship your order, bill you and contact you if we have any questions regarding your order. We also collect and archive the online customer's acceptance of sales terms related to our products.

All personal information submitted to us is visible only by you, Professional Satellite & Communications and the financial institution that authorizes your purchase. Sensitive information is transmitted securely over the Internet and dedicated landlines. 

If you do not feel comfortable providing us with this information via the Internet, you may place an order by calling our customer service department at 1-800-215-2436.

**b. Non Personal Information**

All feedback, comments, suggestions, ideas and other submissions, (collectively referred to as “Submissions”) received by Professional Satellite & Communications by electronic mail or otherwise will be treated as no confidential and non-proprietary. Professional Satellite & Communications is free to use any Submissions for any purpose, including but not limited to developing and marketing products using such information. Your transmission of any Submissions to Professional Satellite & Communications shall constitute an assignment from you to Professional Satellite & Communications of all worldwide rights, titles and interests in all copyrights and other intellectual properties in the Submissions. Professional Satellite & Communications has no obligation to pay any compensation for any Submissions or to respond to any Submissions. 

You agree that no Submissions submitted by you to Professional Satellite & Communications will violate any right of any third party, including copyright, trademark, privacy or other personal or proprietary rights, or will be or contain libelous or otherwise unlawful, abusive or obscene material. You are and shall remain solely responsible for the content of any Submissions you make. 

**2\. What Anonymous Information Do We Collect?**

Your Internet browser has a feature called "cookies" which store small amounts of data on your computer about your visit to any of our sites. However, cookies tell us nothing about who you are unless you specifically give us personally identifiable information. You do not need to have cookies turned on to visit most of our sites, although active participation in certain areas of our sites may require cookies. In addition, you may elect not to allow cookies to be collected by selecting certain options on your browser.

**3\. Information Sharing and Disclosure**

Professional Satellite & Communications does not sell or divulge your personal information to any third parties, unless of course the law requires us to do so. If however, merchandise is not in stock, we will provide your shipping information directly to our vendor so that they may ship directly to you. Like us, our vendors do not sell, divulge or disclose the personal information they receive from us to any third party. We have established internal procedures to guarantee and ensure that the information you provide to us is used only for these purposes.

We reserve the right to co-operate fully with local, provincial and federal officials in any investigation relating to any content provided by you (including personal information) or purported unlawful activities that may involve your participation.

**4\. Information Collected from Children**

This Site is a general audience Site and does not target or intend to collect personal information from children. Children are not eligible to use our services and we ask that minors (under the age of 18) do not submit any personal information to us. If you are a minor, you can use this service only in conjunction with your parents or guardians. If Professional Satellite & Communications learns that it has received or collected personal information from a child, We will take reasonable steps to provide a parent with parental notice of such collection and delete this information, if parents so request.

**5\. Changes to this Privacy Policy**

We reserve the right to change or update this privacy policy, at any time with reasonable notice to users of the Site. Any changes or updates will be effective immediately upon posting to the Site.

**6\. Questions, Comments**

Questions regarding this privacy statement, our products or regarding any other aspects of our network of Web sites, should be sent via e-mail to: support@professionalsatellite.com.

We read every message submitted and try to reply promptly to every one. We may also file your comments to improve the site and program, or review and discard the information.

If you wish to call us to report you problems, our friendly customer service staff is available 7 days a week 9am-5pm Pacific Standard Time to answer your questions or assist you in any way possible! at 1-800-215-2436.
