**TYPE OF DATA PROCESSED**

Browsing Data

The computer systems and software procedures used to operate this website during their normal operation acquire some personal data which transmission is implicit in using the Internet communication protocols.

It is information which is not collected for the purpose of being associated with specific individuals, but which might - due to its nature and through processing and associations with data held by third parties - enable identification of users. 

Included in this category of data there are the IP addresses or the domain names of the computers used by the users connecting to the website, URI (Uniform Resource Identifier) of the requested resources, time of request, method used to submit the request to the server, the size of the file obtained in response, the numerical code indicating the status of the response from the server (successful, error, etc.) and other parameters relating to the operating system and the user's computer environment.  
This data is only used to obtain anonymous statistical information on using the website and to check its correct operation.  
Data may be used to ascertain responsibility in case of hypothetical computer crimes against the website: except for this possibility, the data on web contacts do not persist for more than seven days.

Data supplied voluntarily by the user

The optional, explicit and voluntary submission of e-mails to the addresses provided in this website entails the subsequent acquisition of the sender's address, necessary for responding to his requests, and of any other personal data included in the message. Specific summary information dedicated to particular services on request will be provided or displayed on the website pages.

Cookie Policy

Cookies consist of a small amount of data containing information on your visit to websites that can be stored on your computer or other Internet-enabled devices, for instance smartphones and tablets.

We may use cookies to make it easier for you to browse our website, to adapt its contents to your interests and to personalize your experience offering faster access to the website. Cookies simplify and facilitate the interaction between the user and the website, allowing the website to remember the user’s preferences and individual activities, resulting in time-saving and an easier and more efficient browsing. Cookies can memorize information on the website (language, number of visits, font dimension..) and facilitate the supply of several services (user registration, use of the shopping basket, newsletter subscription or products upgrades etc.).

We also use cookies to create aggregate, anonymous statistics, in order to understand how users use our website and to allow us to improve the structure, the content and the services offered on this website.

If a service is not completely anonymized, you will find it listed in the Third party cookies for which you can refuse your consent. 

This website uses three different types of cookies: 

**1\. Essential technical Cookies**

These cookies are strictly necessary session cookies essential to enable a correct and smooth functioning of the website: they allow the browsing, the sharing of on line content, the session authentication and access to secure areas to make your access to the website more rapid and efficient and to maintain your preferences and credentials during the browsing and the shopping experience. Without these cookies, necessary services cannot be provided. 

**2\. Analytic cookies**

These cookies allow us to understand how users use the website and how they move around the e-shop, in order to assess and evaluate its functioning and to create the most appropriate content, tailored on our users’ preferences. For example, these cookies tell us what are the most and the less visited pages, how many users visit the website, how much time a user generally spends on the website and how users arrived on the website. By doing that, we can understand what are the optimal settings and what improvements can be made so that contents can be charged quickly and visualized in an appropriate manner. All data that is collected anonymously by these cookies and is not linked in any way to the user’s personal information. To carry out these actions, on our websites we use third parties services to anonymized collected data. 

**3\. Third Party Tracking Cookies**

These cookies are provided by third parties not controlled and not likely to become controlled by Zerogrey. Therefore, the latter may not provide any guarantee on the use of the information collected by these third party data controllers.   
These third party cookies allow to provide additional information and advanced and personalized functions. Among many features, the possibility to share content through social networks and to enjoy a personalized experience on the basis of the preferences expressed by the users through the pages visited in the past. These services are mainly provided by external operators. Should the user have an account or use the services provided by any third parties providing tracking cookies, these third parties could be able to know whether the user visited our website. The use of data collected by these external operators through the use of tracking cookies is subject to the respective policies regarding personal information that can be found in the links listed below. Any third parties profiling cookies is identifiable by the name of its operator in the list below, and can be disabled by following the instructions stated in the links below.
