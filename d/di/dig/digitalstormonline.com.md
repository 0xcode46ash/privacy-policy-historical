# Digital Storm - Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

1\. Privacy Policy  
a. GENERAL PRIVACY POLICY DETAILS.  
Digital Storm collects personal information when you place an order. When you are in the checkout process, we ask for your personal information such as, name, email address, billing/shipping address, city, state, zip code, contact phone numbers, and credit card/paypal details. Once you have placed your order, you are not anonymous to us. Digital Storm collects information about your orders to keep a history of orders. Digital Storm automatically receives and records information on your servers from your browser. Including the following, your IP address, cookie information, and your page you have requested. Digital Storm uses this information for the following purposes, direct advertising, improving services, request for new products, research, and misc. information to better serve you.  
b. INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE.  
Digital Storm does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people with any third parties.  
c. COOKIES  
Digital Storm may set and access Digital Storm cookies on your computer.
