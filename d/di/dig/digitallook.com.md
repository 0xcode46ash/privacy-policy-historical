# DigitalLook.com. UK company research and news alerts.

**•    What information do we collect?**

When you register with our Site, we will collect your name, country, company name, company type and job description, e-mail address and phone numbers. We also monitor customer traffic patterns and Site use which enables us to improve the Service we provide. 

**•    What do we do with the information?**

We will use the information provided by you to promote a more personal Service. We are required by the Data Protection Act 1984 and 1998 to follow strict procedures to protect customer information. 

As some of our computer systems are based outside the European Economic Area ("EEA"), it will be necessary to pass on some of your personal data outside of the EEA. By agreeing to the terms and conditions you accept that such transfers will take place. 

We may use this information to tell you about changes to our site and let you know about our Service and those products and services of other companies we work with. If you do not wish to receive this information you may email us at [remove@digitallook.com](mailto:remove@digitallook.com). and we will remove your account. 

We may pass the information we collect about you to other carefully selected companies whose products and services we think may be of interest to you. If you do not want us to do this please notify us at [remove@digitallook.com](mailto:remove@digitallook.com) specifying your account code and e-mail address. 

**•    What about "cookies"?**

"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored on your computer's hard drive by your browser. These allow us to store your details and provide features to make your use of the Service easier. Most browsers allow the cookie function to turn off. If you want to know how to do this please look at the help menu on your browser.
