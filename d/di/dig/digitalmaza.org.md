**Privacy Policy-DigitalMaza.In**  


This privacy policy sets out how DigitalMaza Limited uses and protects any information that you provide, whilst using DigitalMaza Limited products and services. This includes, but is not limited to, DigitalMaza.In and any associated websites and sub-domains.

DigitalMaza Limited is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Any information provided by you to DigitalMaza Limited, by which you can be identified, will only be used in accordance with this privacy policy.

DigitalMaza Limited may change this policy as and when necessary. It is your responsibility to check this privacy policy periodically to ensure you agree with any changes.

**

What information we collect

**

When using any products and services provided by DigitalMaza Limited, we may require some or all of the following information:

  1. name  

  2. address  

  3. telephone number  

  4. email address  

  5. information related to any financial transactions which may be carried out  

  6. domain / Uniform Resource Locator (URL) where the software will be installed



Additionally, whilst using the DigitalMaza.in website, any associated websites and sub-domains, some or all of the following information may be collected:

  1. Internet Protocol (IP) address  

  2. geographical location  

  3. browser type and version  

  4. operating system  

  5. referral source  

  6. length of visit, page views, website navigation and any other related browsing activity  




**What we do with the information we collect**

The information you provide to DigitalMaza Limited is required for, but not limited to, the following reasons:

  1. internal record keeping  

  2. access to the DigitalMaza.in website, associated websites and sub-domains  

  3. account management  

  4. purchase confirmation and account reset  

  5. confirmation of email address from the software license owner  

  6. sending notices of product upgrades, security alerts and patches, and other relevant communications  

  7. validating your domain / URL after purchase of the DigitalMaza forum software to ensure compliance with our licensing agreement  




**Security**

We are committed to ensuring that any information you provide to DigitalMaza Limited is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect.

**How we use cookies and IP addresses**

 ****

DigitalMaza.In, any associated websites and sub-domains, use small text files, called cookies, to store information in your web browser. The information stored may include preferences, session identification, HTTP information, and IP address.

You can set your browser to not receive cookies, but this may limit your ability to use DigitalMaza.In and any associated websites and sub-domains.

IP addresses of computers used to visit DigitalMaza.In and any associated websites and sub-domains are stored in our database. This includes when first registering for an account, managing your account details, writing a private or public message or performing any other transaction which results in data being transferred to or from your computer and the website in question.

Your IP address is not visible to the public at any time.

**Links to other websites**

 **** DigitalMaza.In, any associated websites and sub-domains, may contain links to other websites. These websites may or may not be affiliated with DigitalMaza Limited. 

DigitalMaza Limited has no control over any external websites therefore clicking any link which results in you visiting an external website will result in this privacy policy no longer applying and DigitalMaza Limited cannot be held responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide whilst visiting such sites.

**Controlling your personal information**

 ****

When using the DigitalMaza.In forum, you may choose to limit public viewing of applicable personal information through the settings located in the Privacy section of your account.

DigitalMaza Limited will not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties unless we have your explicit permission or are required by law to do so. We may use your personal information to send you security information, product updates, or other information pertaining to DigitalMaza Limited, or your account.

If you discover any of the information DigitalMaza Limited holds for you is incorrect or incomplete, that you are unable to change yourself, please contact us as soon as possible so the necessary changes can be made.
