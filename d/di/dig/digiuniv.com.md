# Digiuniv

[](https://web.archive.org/web/20131218220712id_/http%3A//www.digiuniv.com/index.html)

DIGIUNIV is a web based Education Management Software that helps customers meet   
the increasing demands, save costs and gain a competitive edge.

### Privacy Policy

Protecting Your Personal Information  
Where personal information is required, we use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption to transmit this data. This is done to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of the information you have offered. While Digiuniv strives to protect the personal information you submit to us. Once your information has been received by we will make our best effort to ensure the safety and security on our systems.

These companies will only be provided the information they need to perform the service, and they are strictly prohibited from using that information for any other purpose. One Software may occasionally present a special contest or promotion sponsored by another company. You may be asked to provide personal information in order to quality for this contest or promotion. If your personal information will be shared with the sponsor(s), you will be notified prior to your submission of personal information. Finally, while we will make every effort to preserve the privacy of our users, at times we may be required by law to disclose personal information.

**Security**  
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. When a user submits personal information, it is kept on a private server not accessible from the Internet. This prevents any malicious use of your information. 

All financial information that is submitted to our websites is encrypted and is stored on private servers that are not accessible from the Internet. Further, users can select to transmit order information over a secure connection, which increases the security of each transaction.
