# Digital-Tutors :: How can we help?

Digital-Tutors take your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

**Information Collection and Use**

Digital-Tutors collects personal information when you register with Digital-Tutors Forums. This is optional and for registration purposes only. Registration allows you to participate in the Forum activities and allows us to identify you as a unique member of the Forum.

We reserve the right to send you certain communications relating to the Digital-Tutors Forums service, such as service announcements and administrative messages, without offering you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving them. 

Registration and participation in Digital-Tutors Forums is restricted to individuals above age 13. 

**Information Sharing and Disclosure  
**

Digital-Tutors does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies. 

**Purchase Transactions  
**

All credit card transactions associated with the purchase of Digital-Tutors Training CD's are handled by [Pay Pal](http://www.paypal.com/). Please check Pay Pal's Privacy Policy for explanation of how your information is being used. The online store is also managed by bCentral who provides secure access to the shopping cart. Please visit [www.bcentral.com](http://www.bcentral.com/) for their privacy policy. 

**Cookies  
**

Digital-Tutors may set a cookie and access it on your computer. These are to remember your login information and to bypass the enter screen when accessing the forums. 

**Confidentiality and Security**

Your Digital-Tutors Forum Account Information is password-protected. 

**Changes to this Privacy Policy**

Digital-Tutors may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by placing a prominent notice on our site.

**Questions and Suggestions**

If you have questions or suggestions, please contact us at [papa@digital-tutors.com](mailto:papa@digital-tutors.com).
