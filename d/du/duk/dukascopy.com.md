# Privacy Policy Statement

**Privacy Policy Statement**

Last update: March 7, 2005

**Information about our Organisation and Web-site**

The mission of Dukascopy (Suisse) SA (hereinafter “Dukascopy”, “we”, “us”) Web-site is to offer a hi-tech trading platform to satisfy the needs of private investors, professional traders, institutional brokers, private banks, custodian banks, hedge funds, market makers, _etc_. Our privacy policy covers Dukascopy and its Web-site.

**Providing Visitors with Anonymous Access**

You can access our Web-site home page and browse our site without disclosing your personal data. 

**The services and links of our Web-site**

Our Web-site enables you to communicate with other visitors or to post information to be accessed by others. When you do so, other visitors may collect your data. 

**Automatic Collection of Information**

We automatically log personal data, but we do not link information automatically logged by other means with personal data about specific individuals. 

A cookie is a small data file that web-sites write to your hard disk for record keeping purposes. We use cookies to store data, but we do not link non-personal information stored in cookies with personal data about specific individuals. The use of cookies does not restrict user’s right to privacy in any way.

**Data Collection and Purpose Specification**

We do not collect any personal data from our visitors when they use our services unless expressly stated to the contrary. For further details see Section 16 "Privacy" of the Terms of Use. We do not collect information about our visitors from other sources, such as public records or bodies or private organizations.

We will only use data about your person in non-anonymous form if you have knowingly and expressly given this data to us.  

**Security of Data Transfers**

Please note that data is transported over an open network which is accessible to anybody. We do not accept any liability for any losses as regards the security of data transfer via Internet. Please use other means of communication if you think this is necessary for security reasons.

We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption technology in order to protect user’s information transmitted to us while using Dukascopy trading platform. This technology protects the user from having his information intercepted by third parties.

**Protection of Personal Data**

Once sent to us, personal data is stored on our servers, to which access is strictly limited. We have taken particular precautions to ensure the protection of our technical environment.

**Contact Details**

If you require any additional information or you have any further questions concerning our privacy policy please do not hesitate to contact us at [info@dukascopy.com](mailto:info@dukascopy.com)
