# DX - DX Privacy

As sales people, we understand how important your personal information and your customer database are to you, your company and your customers. We also know that protecting this sensitive information from unauthorized use or view is crucial. That's why we have built multiple security features into our system.

**What information does DX.COM collect and why?**  
We collect information you provide when you register as a DX account, use our services, send us an e-mail or call us. This information may include the following: 

  * Your name
  * Your mailing address
  * Your e-mail address
  * Your phone number
  * Your credit card number



It includes data you submit about contact records; for example, the name and address (e-mail and postal) of a client or colleague. We only use information you submit about other people in order to deliver information, products, or services specifically requested by you. We protect the data with multiple security measures. 

**Who does DX.COM share information with?**  
We will share customer information with our agents, representatives and trusted service providers and contractors for limited purposes, including fulfilling customer orders; offering certain products and services in connection with our Web site; communicating to customers; providing customer service; enhancing and improving customers' shopping experiences; enabling access to our partners' sites; and completing credit card transactions. **These third parties are not allowed to use this information for any purpose except fulfilling orders and servicing your questions.**

Aside from the purposes described above, we will never share your personal information with any other third parties unless we have your permission or you have been notified. DX.COM reserves the right to cooperate with law enforcement officials in the investigation of alleged unlawful activities of our customers related to electronic communication transmitted on our site. DX.COM will have no legal liability for such disclosures. 

We may also provide information to other organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction. We may share aggregated demographic and statistical information with our other partners. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person.
