# Privacy Policy

  
The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to inform users about the way their Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is being used online by this site. This PII is information that tends to make it possible to locate, contact or identify an individual. Please take the time to go over our privacy policy in a careful manner to understand the ways in which we go about collecting, using, protecting and or handling your PII on this website.

#### What personal information do we collect from users that visit our blog, website or app?

When a user orders or registers on this site, the user may be asked to enter such information as name, email address, credit card information or other details to facilitate your experience.

#### When is information collected?

Information is collected when a user registers or enters information on this site.

#### How is this information used?

Information collected during registration, purchases, signing up for our newsletter, responding surveys or marketing communication, surfing this site, or using other features may be used in the following ways:

      • Facilitating meeting customer needs and service requests.  
      • Adding the efficiency of the user’s site transactions.  
      • Facilitate and enabling communication with the user regarding the site.

#### How is visitor information protected?

This site is scanned and tested on a regular basis in order to apply the most current protective practices in order to secure user information.

Secured networks are employed in order to contain the users and can be accessed by a limited number of individuals who are awarded access rights, and are bound by confidentiality requirements. Sensitive/credit information you supply is encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.

Various security measures are used when an order is placed, information is entered, or other information is submitted or accessed.  
Gateway providers are used to process information and this information is not stored or processed on our servers.

#### Are 'cookies' used on this site and what are they?

Cookies are used by this site and nearly all others. The term is used to refer to small files that a site or service provider transfers to a user’s hard drive through a Web browser (if allowed by the user) which facilitates the site and/or service provider's systems ability to recognize the user’s browser and makes it possible to collect and retain certain information. As an example, this site employs cookies to remember and process the items placed into the user’s shopping cart. This site also uses cookies to discern user preferences based on the user’s site activity. This facilitates the provision of an improved service. Cookies also help to compile aggregate data regarding site traffic and interaction so that site experiences and tools can be continuously improved. This site may use trusted third parties that track this information for the site.

Your computer can be set to warn each time a cookie is being sent, or cookies can be blocked altogether using browser settings. Each browser does this in a different way. Users should consult the Help menu of their browser.

#### If cookies are disabled:

Some features will be disabled and some services may not function as designed such as Login and User sessions.

#### California Online Privacy Protection Act

CalOPPA requires commercial websites and online services to post a privacy policy. See more at: <http://consumercal.org/california-online-privacy-protection-act-caloppa/#sthash.0FdRbT51.dpuf>

In compliance with CalOPPA we agree to the following:  
Anonymous user visits if wanted.  
A link to the site privacy policy will be on the site’s home page, or on the first significant page after entering the site.  
The Privacy Policy link includes the word 'Privacy', and is easily located on the specified page.

Notification of privacy policy changes will be placed:

  * On our Privacy Policy Page



Users will be able to change personal information:

  * By logging in to their account



How are do not track signals handled by this site?  
Do not track signals are honored and this site will not track, plant cookies, or use advertising when a Do Not Track (DNT) browser mechanism is being employed by a user.

Does this site allow third party behavioral tracking?  
Third party behavioral tracking is not allowed or employed by this site.

COPPA (Children Online Privacy Protection Act)

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) allows parents to control the collection of PII of children under 13. The COPPA regulation is enforced by the Federal Trade Commission, the nation's consumer protection agency. This regulation specifies what operators of websites and online services must do to protect children's privacy and safety online.

We do not specifically market to children under 13.

#### Fair Information Practices

In compliance with Fair Information Practices the following responsive action shall be implemented, should a breach of data occur:

Users will be notified via email

  * Within 7 business days



This site agrees to the individual redress principle. This principle requires that individuals have actual enforceable rights against data users. It also requires that individuals have recourse in the courts or via a government agency which will investigate and, if necessary prosecute any non-compliance by data processors.

#### CAN SPAM Act

The CAN-SPAM Act regulates commercial email, establishes requirements for commercial messages, affords recipients the right to stop emails being sent to them, and specifies the penalty for violations.

This site collects your email address to:  
      • Deliver information, and inquiry responses  
      • Order processing, other logistics and updates pertaining to orders  
      • Marketing our services to clients after original transactions

In compliance with CANSPAM we agree to the following:  
      • False, or misleading subjects or email addresses will not be used  
      • Messages will be identified as advertising.  
      • The physical address of our business or site headquarters will be included  
      • Third party email marketing services will be checked for compliance, if used.  
      • Quickly implement and Honor opt-out/unsubscribe requests  
      • Provide a link at the bottom of each email to unsubscribe from emails

A user can unsubscribe to emails at any time by  
      • Following instructions at the bottom of each email.
