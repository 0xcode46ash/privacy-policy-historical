# Privacy Policy | Day2DayPrinting.com

## Protecting your privacy is paramount for the success of our business

We have adapted technologies and operating procedures that support privacy of our clients. We use encryption for sensitive information such as Credit Card data access.

Also to ensure your privacy is completely protected, we have adapted security clearance levels and personal background check on employees in processing and accounting.

## Registration

Day2dayprinting.com provides products and services to Public & Businesses.

In order to use Day2dayprinting.com, you do NOT need to create a user name and password.

Although, creating an account will allow you to receive our free giveaway promotional offers, check your order history, re-order with ease, keep track of your designs and shipping options etc.

During the registration you will be asked to fill out a basic form regarding your contact information, shipping information etc.

This information helps us to communicate with you regarding the process of your orders and ship them to you by using the shipping information you provided us with.

## Use of information policy

Day2dayprinting.com is the sole owner and user of the information you provide us with.

We use your provided information to send you updates regarding shipping and tracking information of your order, order process update, file approval.

Also we send you information regarding free product offering, promotions, discounts, special offers, and change of policies, newsletters.

We do NOT share, rent, lease or sell any of your information to any other company or vendors that we may use to produce products.

## Legal disclaimer

Although we strive to protect your private information from any other third party, we may share necessary information with third party vendors such as UPS, FedEx, UPSP, DHL and, other freight companies.

## Cookies

Cookies are small piece of electronic data that is being stored in user's computer. Cookies are only placed on user's computer if the browser setting allows cookies from secure sites.

We use cookies to collect and analyze visit statistics. We use the analyzed statistics to improve our site in order to provide you with superior and personalized user experience.

## Communication

We will communicate with you in regards to your orders only. Our Client Service Personnel will use your phone number, email address, cell phone number, addresses to contact you via phone calls, emails, text messages to communicate issues or updates regarding your orders.

## Links

Day2dayprinting.com may contain links to other sites; we do not take responsibility for other sites and vendors you may visit through Day2dayprinting.com.

Please read privacy statement of other sites you visit.

All of Day2dayprinting.com privacy statements and policies applies to Day2dayprinting.com website only.

## Change in policies

Day2dayprinting.com has discretion to change or update this privacy policy. However, we will notify our clients via email in regards to any changes we have made if you are still a registered member of our website.

We suggest you check our policies in a regular basis to stay informed of any updates that may have been implemented.

Continued use of our service indicated your agreement to our terms and conditions and policies.
