# B-school events, B-School competitions, Management events, Management competitions, MBA events, MBA competitions, Business quizzes, Management Quizzes, Management Videos, debates, Conferences - all at Dare2Compete.com

At Dare2Compete.com we recognize that privacy is important. This Privacy Policy applies to all of the services offered by Dare2Compete.com.  
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us through our website or write to us at **help[at]dare2compete.com**

**Scope**

The Privacy Policy covers the treatment of personally identifiable information when you use dare2compete site.  
The policy does not apply to practices of companies dare2compete do not own or control, or to the people that dare2compete does not employ or manage.

**Information collection and use**

Dare2compete site collects personally identifiable information when you register for dare2compete site account or when you use certain products or services provided by dare2compete site.  
When you register at dare2compete site we ask for your name, mobile no, email address, college etc. Once you have registered at dare2compete site and sign in for our services, you are not anonymous to us.   
Dare2compete also automatically receives and records information on shared server logs from your browser including your [IP address](http://info.yahoo.com/privacy/in/yahoo/ip), [cookie](http://info.yahoo.com/privacy/in/yahoo/cookies) information and the page you requested.  
In addition, when you use dare2compete site services, you may make some of your personal information public. If you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return and dare2compete site will not be responsible for the use or misuse of such information.  
Email communication that you send us via the email links on the Site may be shared with company representatives, partners or employees. From time to time, we may send you service-related emails, such as a welcome email when you come in touch with the Site.   
We use your email address to send you: 

  * Password reminder and registration confirmation 
  * Special offers 
  * News letters 
  * Changes in the service's policy or terms of use 
  * Event based communications such as new event information, invites, reminders etc. 



Note: We send users newsletters and updates upon registration. We send newsletters and/or promotional emails on behalf of our alliance partners. 

We use your personal information to: 

  * help us provide personalized features 
  * tailor our sites to your interest 
  * to get in touch with you in the case of password retrieval and policy changes 
  * to provide the services requested by you 
  * to preserve social history as governed by existing law or policy 



We use contact information internally to: 

  * direct our efforts for product improvement 
  * contact you as a survey respondent 
  * notify you if you win any contest; and 
  * send you promotional materials from our contest sponsors or advertisers 



**Information Sharing and Disclosure**

Dare2compete site will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone. ****  
Dare2compete will send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when:

  * We have your consent to share the information;
  * We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested;
  * We need to share the information with certain service partners in order to respond to your comments or to resolve service issues. (Unless we tell you differently, these partners have no independent right to use this information except to respond to your comments or to resolve the service issues.);
  * We respond to requests from any Indian or foreign government, security, defence, revenue, regulatory or other authority, agency or officer;
  * We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; or
  * We find that your actions on our web sites violate the dare2compete [terms of service](http://in.docs.yahoo.com/info/terms/) or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services.



**Cookies**

Dare2compet may set and access dare2compete [cookies](http://info.yahoo.com/privacy/in/yahoo/cookies) on your computer and you may not be able to disable these cookies. Dare2compete ensures that all information collected and accessed by dare2compete through the access of these cookies will be maintained in accordance with this Privacy Policy.  
Dare2compete site allows [other companies](http://info.yahoo.com/privacy/in/yahoo/adservers) that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. 

**Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences**

Dare2compete gives you the ability to edit your [dare2compete account information](http://edit.yahoo.com/config/eval_profile) and preferences at any time, including whether you want dare2compete to contact you about specials and new products. Dare2compete is not responsible and will not be liable for the accuracy, completeness, veracity and reliability of any information including any customized information which may be accessed by or provided to you, which could vary depending on your dare2compete account information and preferences including your browser preferences.

**Links**

Dare2Compete.com may present links in a format that enables us to keep track of whether these links have been followed. We use this information to improve the quality of customized content and advertising.

**Affiliated sites**

Dare2Compete.com may offer some services in connection with other web sites. Personal information that you provide to those sites may be sent to us in order to deliver the service. We process such information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. The affiliated sites may have different privacy practices and we encourage you to read their privacy policies.

**Security**

Your dare2compete account information is password-protected for your privacy and security.

**Changes to this Privacy Policy**

Dare2compete site may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.
