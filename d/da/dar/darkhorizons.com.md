# Dark Horizons | Privacy Statement

### Statement

This Privacy Policy has been created by Dark Futures Pty Limited trading as Dark Horizons Multimedia, the operators of the Dark Horizons website, in an effort to demonstrate our firm commitment to protecting your privacy on our site.

### Information Collection and Use

Dark Horizons Multimedia may use visitor's Internet Protocol (IP) addresses from our ISP, in order to help diagnose any problems with our server and for general administration of our web site. We do not collect any Personal Identifiable Information (PII) from people who browse our website.

### External Links

The Dark Horizons website contains links to other sites which are not owned or operated by Dark Horizons Multimedia and we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. We encourage you to carefully review the privacy and terms of use policies of each respective linked site individually before offering any of your personal information.

### Affiliate Programs

We are currently participating in a number of affiliate programs who may collect information in order to process orders you place with them and/or to provide other services they offer. We encourage you to carefully review the privacy and terms of use policies of each respective affiliate before offering any of your personal information.

### Advertising

We use third party advertising companies to display ads on our site via our affiliate programs. These ads may contain cookies and we do not have access to any (PII) collected in this manner. We encourage you to carefully review the privacy and terms of use policies of any of the above sites before offering any of your personal information.

### DoubleClick

This website may utilize certain services provided by DoubleClick, Inc. ("DoubleClick"). For further information concerning DoubleClick's privacy practices, including the ability to opt-out from any data collection, reporting and/or targeting activities associated with its services, please [go to Double Click](http://www.doubleclick.com/us/about_doubleclick/privacy/).

### About Children

Our site is not directed to children under 13. Children under 13 MUST not submit personal information to www.darkhorizons.com and Dark Horizons Multimedia will not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of thirteen. Any questions regarding privacy issues can be addressed to [garth@darkhorizons.com](mailto:garth@darkhorizons.com) or Garth Franklin, Dark Horizons Multimedia, P.O. Box 1935, Dee Why, NSW, 2099, AUSTRALIA.
