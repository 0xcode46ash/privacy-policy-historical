_About Datematch.com and the Information We Collect_

* * *

    Datematch.com is an online matchmaking and community service designed to provide an easy way for our members to meet other quality singles and couples on the Internet. Once members create their own unique profile, our matching technology provides them with instantaneous matches based upon their pre-selected dating criteria. None of the information we gather can be used by the public (visitors, other members, and anyone not employed by Datematch.com, its corporate affiliates, or the financial institutions we use to process membership charges) to identify a specific individual. 
_Datematch.com Affiliate Partners_

* * *

    The Affiliate Partner program is a vehicle for other Internet sites to link to the Datematch.com. No personal member information is exchanged with the Affiliate partner. 
_Information Collected/Tracked by Datematch.com_

* * *

    Datematch.com gathers two types of information: information that users submit to us and information not directly submitted by users. 
_Information submitted to Datematch.com_

* * *

    There are two types of information we collect that users submit: public and private. We define private information to be any information that allows others to contact a user other than through Datematch.com or allows the collection of information about the user other than what is displayed on the site. Public information is personal information that may be displayed on the site, such as gender, age and height, but cannot be linked to a particular user or their personal details. 
_PRIVATE:_

* * *

    This information is gathered from free trial members and members. This information includes, but is not limited to, email address, IP address, first name, last name, credit card number, address, telephone and SMS details. 
_PUBLIC:_

* * *

    To accomplish our goal of bringing compatible people together, we must gather personal information such as gender, age, ethnic background, appearance, likes/dislikes and more. This information is meant to be seen by members and visitors to the site but cannot be used to identify a specific member by personal information such as name, address, etc. 
_Information that users do not submit to us_

* * *

    This is information we collect that is not personally identifiable, such as browser type and IP address. This information is gathered for all visitors to the site. It may be used for security measures, traffic reporting or site usage tracking. This information cannot be used by the public (visitors, other members, and anyone not employed by Datematch.com, its corporate affiliates, or the financial institutions we use to process membership charges) to identify a specific individual. 
_Use of Information Obtained by Datematch.com_

* * *

    Information that users submit to us 
_PRIVATE:_

* * *

    We may use a user's email address to send news and special offers regarding our site, and with the exception of specific direct marketing emails during the free trial period, the user may choose not to receive emails of this type by using the newsletter function in Account Info OR by emailing remove@Datematch.com.  

    Email addresses may also be used to contact members with information regarding their account.  

    Email addresses may also be used in private correspondence with the user for customer service issues. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for services. All other private information submitted by the user is confidential and will not be disclosed except as required by law or to protect members of Datematch.com. All private information can be updated or deleted by the user at any time using the Account Info section.   

_Datematch.com's Affiliate Partners_

* * *

    Information submitted by the Affiliate Partner is used for establishing and maintaining a business relationship. No personal or business information is shared outside the relationship between Datematch.com and the Affiliate Partner. Affiliate Partners cannot view or access private information on members or users of our site. 
_PUBLIC:_

* * *

    Public information supplied by the user is intended to be viewed by registrants and subscribers to the site. This information is used by registrants and subscribers for searching and matching purposes. 
_Information that users do not submit to us_
_AGGREGATE:_

* * *

    We use your server, IP address, and browser-type related information in the general administration of our website. 
    Sharing of the Obtained Information by Datematch.com 
    We do not sell, rent, share, trade or giveaway any of your private information without your permission except to the financial institution that processes your credit card transactions unless required by law or for the protection of your membership. 
_Use of Cookies_

* * *

    We currently use cookies in our Chat areas and for our own advertising purposes. Please note that advertisers on the Datematch.com site may send cookies to users of the site and Datematch.com does not control or have access to this information.
