**Advertising**

DateYard uses its own proprietary advertising broadcast methods to deliver advertising messages to its users. The advertising system we developed and use does not use or provide the advertisers with any pieces of information that the user provided on-site.

You acknowledge and grant DateYard permission to use your photos* or text* on our partners' sites or in the marketing of the Service.

* Excludes any pictures, text, or recordings assigned to your backstage. Excludes your personal information, such as your email address or full name.

We may use third-party advertising companies to display ads when you visit the DateYard website. These companies may use anonymous information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to the DateYard website (and web sites operated by others) in order to provide advertisements on the DateYard website about goods and services that may be of interest to you.

We cannot guarantee, and assume no responsibility for verifying the accuracy of the information provided by other users of the Service.

You may not use the Service for any unlawful purpose. We may refuse to grant you an ID or nickname that impersonates someone else, is protected by trademark or proprietary law, or is vulgar or otherwise offensive, as determined by us in our sole discretion.

We do not share any personal information with third parties.

**Monitoring of Information**

We reserve the right to monitor all advertisements, public postings and messages to ensure that they conform with the content guidelines which may sometimes be applicable.

You shall remain solely responsible for the content of profiles, public postings, messages and other materials you may upload to the Service or users of the Service.

**Archiving and Preservation of Information**

We archive all information, including personal information that you provide while using any other functionalities on the DateYard website. We store this information on servers that are controlled by us or by third parties who agreed to comply with our commitments made under th present Privacy Policy and whom we have a contractual relationship with. Archived information shall generally not be erased unless and until your complete account with DateYard.com is deleted, and even then we may retain some or all your information on back-up files.
