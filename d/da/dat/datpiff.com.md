# Privacy Policy @ DatPiff

privacy policy   
  
This site is built and maintained by Uni Studios, having a mailing address of P.O. Box 6206, Hillsborough, NJ, 08844.

DatPiff logs IP addresses and other visitor information for statistical, and legal purposes only. This information may be used to show general statistics to advertisers. No personally identifiable information is collected and stored. DatPiff does not share any of this information with any outside parties. 

We hold your privacy above all else, as we welcome and respect our visitors. Should you have any further questions, please use the form located [here](https://web.archive.org/contact.php) to contact us.   
  
Disclaimer: All mixtapes on this site are for promotional use only. We do not sell mixtapes. Any fees paid for a "Premium Account" are to pay for bandwidth and administrative costs.
