# Privacy Policy – Children's Miracle Network Hospitals

#### Information Collection and Use

**Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals** collects personal information when you donate to **Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals**. 

When you donate, we ask for information such as your name, email address, and zip code. Once you donate to Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals, you are not anonymous to us. 

**Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals** automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, and the page you request. 

**Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals** uses information for the following general purposes: to improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.
