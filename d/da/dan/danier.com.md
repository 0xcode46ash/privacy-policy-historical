## Why Do We Need Your Personal Information?

When you register for our contests or make a purchase, we collect information such as your name, address, e-mail address and birthdate. We will use this information for warranty claims and to tell you about free gifts, coupons, prizes, exciting offers or new products related specifically to Danier Leather. Your birthdate is used to validate a winner in our win instantly contest(s). We do not share your personal information with third parties, except where they are providing us a direct service for the above; we do not provide personal information to any third parties for any other purposes.

## Removing Your Name From Our List

If at any time, you wish to be removed from our e-mailing list, all you need to do is use the hyperlink located at the bottom of each e-mail you receive, or send an e-mail to [privacy@danier.com](mailto:privacy@danier.com) with “unsubscribe” in the Subject heading.

You can also choose not to receive any other marketing communications from Danier, whereupon we will ensure that you do not receive any, offers, special discounts nor Danier promotional notices. Please communicate this in writing including your name, address, phone number & email address (if applicable) to the following addresses:

## A Word About Cookies

Cookies are used as part of the software that runs our site. When you visit, our site sends a cookie to your browser’s cookie file. This cookie file is simply a text file and our cookie is just another line of text. A cookie contains no personal information. It is only in your file during your visit to our site. It does not stay in your file after you have left. Turning off your Internet browser’s ability to access cookies will increase your security but could disable some of the Web site’s functionality.

## Links

The Danier Web site may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that Danier is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware of this when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of every Web site that collects personally identifiable information.

## Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to change our privacy policy at any time. Accordingly we recommend that you check this page periodically in order to review our current policies.
