# DAWN.COM | Others | Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy 

This Privacy Statement verifiesthat DAWN - the Internet Edition is a publication of The Pakistan HeraldPublications (Pvt) Limited. This statement discloses the privacy practices forDAWN - the Internet Edition. For further information you can contact us at: [webmaster@dawn.com. ](mailto:webmaster@dawn.com)  


DAWN - the Internet Edition has adopted a set of information managementguidelines which serve as the basis for our customer and advertiserrelationships. Accordingly, these guidelines are subject to change. Any suchchanges will be posted on this page. 

DAWN - the Internet Edition is not responsible for the content or the privacypolicies of Web sites to which it may link. 

**Feedback Form Information:** While filling a feedback form <http://www.dawn.com/fixed/feed/feed.htm>, DAWN - the Internet Edition requests that you provide your personalinformation, e-mail address, and demographic information (country, zip code,age and gender are required; household income is optional). DAWN - the InternetEdition may collect additional personal information and views from subscribersby contests, polling booths or surveys. By providing this information you agreethat DAWN - the Internet Edition may store information you provide as part ofits data-collection for future demographic study. 

**E-Mails:** DAWN - the Internet Edition maintains two mailing lists to keepits subscribers about the events and happenings in Pakistan and South Asian region.Individuals may click on the following links to subscribe to our mailing lists.

 **DAWN Internet NewsAlert** -this mailing lists contains important headline news and breaking events inPakistan. One issue is sent daily and Breaking News are sent for major newscoverage. To subscribe: <http://www.dawn.com/fixed/subs/dinasub.htm>

**DAWN Wire Service** \- this is a weekly mailing lists that gathersinformation on all major happenings. It includes National News, BusinessUpdates, Sports and Opinions of famous columnists. To subscribe: <http://www.dawn.com/fixed/subs/dwssub.htm>

**Data Security:** Toprevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriateuse of information, we have put in place physical, electronic, and managerialprocedures to protect the information we collect online. We never sell, rent,loan, trade or lease the addresses on our lists to anyone. We have configuredour list-severs in such a manner that no list subscriber cansubscribe/unsubscribe others by just putting their addresses in the onlineforms. The user has to confirm his/her subscription/un-subscription request byemail before it is applied. 

**Banner Advertising:** DAWN - the Internet Edition may perform statisticalanalyses of user behavior in order to measure interest in the various sectionsof our site and to inform advertisers as to how many consumers have seen or"clicked" their advertising banners. Personal information about youas an individual subscriber will not be provided to any third party. 

We have ads served on our site by Advertising.com. To visit their privacystatement, please click here <http://www.advertising.com/privacy/index.html>
