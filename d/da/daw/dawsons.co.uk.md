# Dawsons Music Online

[](http://www.dawsonsonline.com/live/rhtSearch.php5?search=all&page=1&searchString=june+AND+special%21&submit.x=0&submit.y=0)  
Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy Dawsons Music will not send unsolicited email to anyone who buys from our store. All enquiry forms feature opt in check boxes to allow you to choose whether to receive mail and offers from us or not. We do not disclose your email address or any other personal details to any other company. If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy then please email [flek@dawsons.co.uk](mailto:flek@dawsons.co.uk).

Cookies are used on this shopping site to keep track of the contents of your shopping cart once you have selected an item, to store delivery addresses if the address book is used and to store your details if you select the 'Remember Me' Option. We also keep a note of your address to allow ius to organise delivery.

As described above, none of this information is passed to third parties. All information is held securely at Dawsons Music Ltd on secure servers. The information is collected lawfully and in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998. Occasionally we will email you about special offers we have. However you can opt out of these at any time either by replying to the mail, or sending an email to the address above asking this.
