# FOREX Market | Daily Forex Privacy Policy

DailyForex.com respects your privacy. All information collected at this Web site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to DailyForex.com will be held with the uttermost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. 

Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:   
DailyForex.com does not sell, rent, trade, loan, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or e-mail lists. 

Browser information collected on the Web site:  
DailyForex.com analyses the Web site logs to improve the value of the materials available on our site. Our Web site logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site. 

Use of cookies:  
DailyForex.com does use cookies on its Web site, mostly in order to track traffic coming from other websites and leaving to other websites. With the cookies, we make no attempt to link them with the individuals to make any information personally identifiable. 

Updates and changes:  
DailyForex.com reserves the right to replace its "Privacy Policy" at any time. We suggest that you regularly check [http://www.DailyForex.com](http://www.dailyforex.com/)

Please e-mail: [info@DailyForex.com](mailto:info@DailyForex.com) with questions or comments.
