# Privacy Policy of DailyCostume.com


    DailyCostume.com - Privacy Policy 
    
    DailyCostume.com does not collect or identify any private or personal information from its visitors. 
    
    Third-party advertisers may place cookies on your computer. 
    
    The DailyCostume.com staff is constantly monitoring all site activity that may
     include or link to spyware, malware, or any other form of malicious software.
    
    We take your privacy and anonymity very seriously. 
    
    We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. 
    These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email 
    address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to 
    provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more 
    information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information 
    used by these companies, click here http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp
    
    If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us : dailycostume @ gmail.com
