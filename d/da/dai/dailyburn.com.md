# DailyBurn.com - Privacy Policy

**Statement**  
This Privacy Policy has been created by the webmasters at http://www.dailyburn.com, in an effort to demonstrate our firm commitment to protecting your privacy on our site.

 **Information Collection and Use**  
http://www.dailyburn.com may use visitors' Internet Protocol (IP) addresses from our ISP, in order to help diagnose any problems with our server and for general administration of our web site. We do not collect any Personal Identifiable Information (PII) from people who browse our website.

**External Links**  
http://www.dailyburn.com contains links to other sites which are not owned or operated by http://www.dailyburn.com and we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. We encourage you to carefully review the privacy and terms of use policies of each respective linked site individually before offering any of your personal information.

**Affiliate Programs**  
We are currently participating in a number of affiliate programs who may collect information in order to process orders you place with them and/or to provide other services they offer. We encourage you to carefully review the privacy and terms of use policies of each respective affiliate before offering any of your personal information.

**Advertising**  
We use third party advertising companies to display ads on our site via our affiliate programs. These ads may contain cookies and we do not have access to any (PII) collected in this manner. We encourage you to carefully review the privacy and terms of use policies of any of the above sites before offering any of your personal information.

**About Children**  
Our site is not directed to children under 18. Children under 18 MUST not submit personal information to http://www.dailyburn.com and http://www.dailyburn.com will not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of eighteen. Any questions regarding privacy issues can be addressed to [privacy@dailyburn.com](mailto:privacy@dailyburn.com).

All pictures, articles, titles and content contained on pages herein were collected freely from the internet or contracted for or produced by DailyBurn.com and are believed to be either public domain or owned outright by DailyBurn.com. If you believe you own the copyright to any images or content that appears on this site, please send an email to [privacy@dailyburn.com](mailto:privacy@dailyburn.com).
