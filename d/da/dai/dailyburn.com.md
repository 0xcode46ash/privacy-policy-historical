# Dailyburn.com: Privacy Policy

Statement

This Privacy Policy has been created by the webmasters at Dailyburn.com, in an effort to demonstrate our firm commitment to protecting your privacy on our site.

Information Collection and Use  
Dailyburn.com may use visitors' Internet Protocol (IP) addresses from our ISP, in order to help diagnose any problems with our server and for general administration of our web site. We do not collect any Personal Identifiable Information (PII) from people who browse our website.

External Links  
Dailyburn.com contains links to other sites which are not owned or operated by Dailyburn.com and we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. We encourage you to carefully review the privacy and terms of use policies of each respective linked site individually before offering any of your personal information.

Affiliate Programs  
We are currently participating in a number of affiliate programs who may collect information in order to process orders you place with them and/or to provide other services they offer. We encourage you to carefully review the privacy and terms of use policies of each respective affiliate before offering any of your personal information.

Advertising  
We use third party advertising companies to display ads on our site via our affiliate programs. These ads may contain cookies and we do not have access to any (PII) collected in this manner. We encourage you to carefully review the privacy and terms of use policies of any of the above sites before offering any of your personal information.

About Children  
Our site is not directed to children under 18. Children under 18 MUST not submit personal information to http://www.dailyburn.com and http://www.dailyburn.com will not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of eighteen. Any questions regarding privacy issues can be addressed to privacy@dailyburn.com.

All pictures, articles, titles and content contained on pages herein were collected freely from the internet or contracted for or produced by DailyBurn.com and are believed to be either public domain or owned outright by DailyBurn.com. If you believe you own the copyright to any images or content that appears on this site, please send an email to privacy@dailyburn.com.

Dailyburn.com.com is committed to user privacy in our products and services. This policy outlines our personal information handling practices. If you give us personal information, we will treat it according to this policy.

Types and uses of personal information.  
Your personal information is used in the following ways:  
Email Correspondence. Used to raise and respond to issues and inquiries of all types. Also used to distribute information about Dailyburn.com.com, our products, and services. 

Security measures

Data Retention  
Dailyburn.com will not retain your personal information longer than is necessary for the purposes for which it was collected. 

Linked web sites  
We provide links to third party sites. Since we do not control these Web sites, we encourage you to review the privacy policies posted on these (and all) third party sites. 

Children's privacy  
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have personal information on a child under the age of 13, we will delete that information from our systems. Check out the FTC's site for more tips on protecting children's privacy online. 

We may display advertisements from third parties. Third party ad-serving companies control which ads are shown. These ad-serving companies may use cookies to collect non-personally identifiable information from you. Advertisers use this information to target the ads and measure their impact. 

Third Party Advertising  
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please click here. 

Third Party Cookies  
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.

All pictures, articles, titles and content contained on pages herein were collected freely from the internet or contracted for or produced by Dailyburn.com.com and are believed to be either public domain or owned outright by Dailyburn.com.com. If you believe you own the copyright to any images or content that appears on this site, please send an email to privacy@dailyburn.com.

Contests

Dailyburn.com.com contests are only open to people ages 13 and above. Any personal information such as name, mailing address and E-Mail address collected during contest entries is not kept or distributed by Dailyburn.com.com, unless a sponsor of the contest has requested it. In this instance, the Dailyburn.com.com Member will be asked to agree to offer this information to the sponsor, and only to the sponsor, while entering the contest. No entries are ever re-sold to a third party vendor. 

Winners agree to have their name, city and state (country) displayed on the website when they accept their prize via a confirmation E-Mail. 

Contest rules vary by contest and are available on the specific contest entry form. 

Unless otherwise noted, all contests are strictly one entry per person per contest. Blatant use of tactics designed to give any individual an advantage in any given contest will not be tolerated. Registering multiple accounts, submitting multiple contest entries, entering under multiple personal names, and any other fraudulent actions will result in disqualification in the current and future contests. It is the right of Dailyburn.com.com to reclaim any awarded prize if any of these fraudulent methods were used in winning any contest.
