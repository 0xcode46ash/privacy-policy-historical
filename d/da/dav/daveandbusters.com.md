# Dave&Buster's Privacy Policy Statement

This is the web site of **Dave &Buster's, Incorporated**.  


Our postal address is   
**2481 A Manana  
Dallas, Texas 75220**

We can be reached by telephone at 214-357-9588 or by fax at 214-350-0941.

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes the visitor's domain name and e-mail address (where possible).

We collect the domain name and e-mail address (where possible) of visitors to our Web page, the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, user-specific information on what pages consumers access or visit and information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations.

The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded, used to improve the content of our Web page and not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

If visitors do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending email to us at the above address and telling us that visitors do not want to receive e-mail from our company.

**We do NOT make the e-mail addresses, postal addresses and/or telephone numbers of those who access our site available to any businesses, organizations or entities outside of Dave &Buster's, Inc.**

In short, we value the relationship we have with our clients and our visitors to our Web site. And it is standard operating procedure for Dave&Buster's, Inc., to safeguard the privacy of our clients and our visitors to our Web site.

We hope visitors understand and appreciate our insistance on a strong Privacy Policy Statement as a matter of standard corporate policy and procedure.

We can be reached via telephone or fax (214-357-9588 or by fax at 214-350-0941) to receive visitors' comments regarding our Privacy Policy Statement.
