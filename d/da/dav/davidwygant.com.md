# David Wygant - The Dating Expert that men and women trust most with their love lives

David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc.'s Statement of Privacy

David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. are committed to protecting your privacy. This Statement of Privacy applies to davidwygant.com and governs data collection and usage at all David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. sites and services; it does not apply to other online or offline sites, products or services. 

Please read the David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. Statement of Privacy. davidwygant.com is a general audience website, intended for users 18 years of age and older. Personal information of all users is collected, used and disclosed as described in this Statement of Privacy.

**Collection of Your Personal Information**

As an E-commerce website, davidwygant.com collects personal information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. Information collected by davidwygant.com is used solely for the purpose of billing and shipping your online purchases, and for sending messages and correspondence via email. 

David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. do not collect any information about your computer hardware and software. David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. encourage you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from davidwygant.com so that you can understand how those websites collect, use and share your information. 

We are not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of the davidwygant.com website.

**Use of your Personal Information**

David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. and their operational service partners collect and use your personal information to process product orders and deliver the products requested. It is possible that David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. may also use personal information to inform you of other products or services available from davidwygant.com and to send you requested information including, but not limited to, newsletters and emails about davidwygant.com. David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. do not sell, rent or lease their customer lists to third parties. David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your personal information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party.

We occasionally hire other companies to provide limited services on our behalf, such as handling the processing and delivery of mailings, providing customer support, processing transactions, or performing statistical analysis of our services. We will only provide those companies the personal information they need to deliver the service. They are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information and are prohibited from using that information for any other purpose.

David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. do not collect, use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations.

David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. may access and/or disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on davidwygant.com; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc., including their Websites; or (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of davidwygant.com or the public.

Personal information collected on this site may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country in which David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. or their affiliates, subsidiaries or agents maintain facilities, and by using this site, you consent to any such transfer of information outside of your country.

David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. abide by the safe harbor framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of data from the European Union.

**Control of Your Personal Information**

davidwygant.com offers its customers choices for the collection, use and sharing of personal information. You may go notify us of your preferences by emailing support at davidwygant.com. Please be sure to provide complete account information so we can identify you in our records.

You may also stop the delivery of future promotional e-mail from davidwygant.com by responding directly to any email you receive with a request to remove you from the mailing list.

**Security of Your Personal Information**

David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. are committed to protecting the security of your personal information. We use a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. For example, we store the personal information you provide on computer systems with limited access that are located in controlled facilities. When we transmit highly confidential information (such as a credit card number) over the Internet, we use a secure payment gateway so your information is protected.

**Changes to this Statement**

David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. If there are material changes to this Statement or in how davidwygant.com will use your personal information, David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc.will use your personal information, davidwygant.com will prominently post such changes prior to implementing the change. David Wygant, DW Productions, Wygant Creative Group and Wygant Productions, Inc. encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how davidwygant.com is protecting your information.
