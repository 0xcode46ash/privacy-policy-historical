# DBRS Policy for Clients of dbrs.com

Dominion Bond Rating Service (DBRS) is committed to protecting the privacy of its employees and clients. We recognize the need to respect and protect the personal identifiable information that is disclosed to us. As such, we have established a privacy policy which is structured around the guidelines laid out by the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). Personal information includes any factual or subjective information about an identifiable individual. It does not include the name, title, business address, or telephone number of an employee of an organization.

Our website, www.dbrs.com, collects data provided by your account administrator, as well as automatically through the use of "cookie" technology. Examples of types of information that may be collected automatically is registration information (e.g. user name and password), user preference data (e.g. a preference to track a particular industry), and page hit counts. User preferences and usage statistics fall under the classification of personal information.

The information we gather is used to enhance your visit to www.dbrs.com, and to enable us to tailor our services to your needs. For instance, DBRS uses personal information to administer subscriptions; to send you e-mail alerts based on your chosen industries or companies; to customize page content; to allocate resources to provide research on popular topics; and to keep you informed of research materials and conferences that may be of interest to you.

DBRS does not sell or share any personal information you provide to us through www.dbrs.com, or any other means. The information we collect is used solely for internal purposes, some of which are mentioned above.

You may view/edit your user preferences at any time by going to [My Settings](https://www.dbrs.com/web/sentry?COMP=2100&X=1) on the website. To provide an optimal level of service to you, DBRS's goal is to maintain accurate and up to date personal information. Should you wish to access and/or update your contact information, or to communicate any questions or concerns to us, please contact Client Services at info@dbrs.com.

If you have any questions/concerns regarding DBRS's privacy practices, DBRS's Privacy Officer can be contacted directly at [privacy@dbrs.com.](mailto:privacy@dbrs.com)
