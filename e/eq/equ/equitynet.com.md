# Desmac Corporation - Privacy Policy

This website is maintained by Desmac. The primary purpose of Desmac.com is to be an informative library for businesses needing software support, consulting, and/or development. 

  At Desmac, we are committed to preserving your privacy. This page is used to explain how Desmac protects your privacy while viewing Desmac.com. 

  Desmac.com gathers information through two ways: directly and indirectly. 

  1\. Directly   
At Desmac.com, there are a number of pages where we request your information. Examples of such pages are [ Project.asp](https://web.archive.org/web/20030923155026id_/http%3A//www.equitynet.com/project.asp) or [Apply.asp](https://web.archive.org/web/20030923155026id_/http%3A//www.equitynet.com/apply.asp). Desmac.com uses this information to better serve you in a professional, respectable manner. 

  2\. Indirectly   
Desmac.com uses "cookies." "Cookies" are small files of information used to inform Desmac.com about information while visiting our site. Desmac.com uses cookies to better understand our clients and to better serve future visitors. If you prefer, you may increase your security level, which will prohibit the placement of "cookies" by Desmac.com 

  Under no circumstances will Desmac.com sell, advertise, or market your information to a third party. 

  For more information about your privacy with Desmac.com, please contact [info@desmac.com](mailto:info@desmac.com).
