# Privacy Policy | ESL Lounge

**Your Privacy Is Our Priority**

_esl-lounge.com_ has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: **esl-lounge.com**

### Information Automatically Logged

There is information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, screen size, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by esl-lounge.com for the operation of the service, to maintain and improve quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the _esl-lounge.com_ site.

### Advertisers

We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information. 

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click [here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp). 

esl-lounge is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com

### Registration Forms

Our site's registration forms sometimes require users to give us contact information (like their name, e-mail, and postal address), and demographic information (like their zip code, age, or geographical location).

Contact information from the registration forms is used to maintain and improve quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the _esl-lounge.com_ site.

Contact information from the registration forms is also used to maintain a mailing list used by _esl-lounge.com_ to inform site users of changes to the site and is not passed on to any third parties.

Information collected from registration forms regarding the general demographic make up of _esl-lounge.com_ visitors (i.e. percentage of visitors from Canada) may be made available to third parties, but _not_ that information which is associated only with individuals (i.e. e-mail addresses, names)

Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. 

### Surveys

Our online surveys ask visitors for contact information (like email address), and demographic information (like zip code, age). 

Contact information from the surveys is used to maintain a mailing list used by _esl-lounge.com_ to inform site users of changes to the site and is not passed on to any third parties.

Information collected from surveys regarding the general demographic make up of _esl-lounge.com_ visitors (i.e. percentage of visitors from Canada) may be made available to third parties, but not that information which is associated only with individuals (i.e. e-mail addresses, names)

Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. 

### External Links

This site contains links to other sites. _esl-lounge.com_ is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
