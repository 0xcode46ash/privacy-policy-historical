# ESL Discussions - Privacy Policy

## PRIVACY POLICY

ESL Discussions.com uses the Google advertising programme to deliver advertisements to its pages when you visit. Google may use information about your visits to ESL Discussions.com in order to provide advertisements about goods and services that might be of interest to you.

Google (a third-party vendor) may use technology such as cookies (such as the [DoubleClick](http://www.google.com/policies/technologies/ads/) cookie) when it places ads on this site. These cookies will use information including your IP address, your ISP , the browser you used to visit our site, and in some cases, whether you have Flash installed to better serve their ads. Users may opt out of Google cookies by using Google's [PrivacyTools](http://www.google.com/intl/en/goodtoknow/online-safety/security-tools/). If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click on the links below:

Users may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting the Google Ads Preference Manager or by using Google's Privacy tools. (Alternatively, you can direct users to opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for interest based advertising by visiting [aboutads.info](http://www.aboutads.info/).)
