# Essay Depot - Privacy Policy

Essay Depot is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This policy is created in order to demonstrate our firm belief to protecting your personal information. 

**What information do we collect from our visitors? Why?**

Essay Depot obtains only the information that is necessary to provide greater efficiency to our website and better advertising that is appropriate to our users. We ask for the age, name, email address, and physical address information during the registration process. This information is used in order to provide the user with the greatest ease for navigating through the website. It is also used for providing the user with suitable content. The IP address is also collected during this time in order to provide for the maximum security of all our users. 

**How much personal information are you disclosing to advertisers?**

Essay Depot stands strong in its belief to keeping your information private. The information collected is only used to obtain some general statistics for our advertisers in order to provide proper advertising suitable for our users. No personal information is ever disclosed during this process.
