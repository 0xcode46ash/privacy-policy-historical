# Privacy Policy | Escape Motions

Escape Motions, s.r.o. ("Escape Motions" or "we" or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

Escape Motions’ privacy policy covers the collection and use of personal information that may be collected by Escape Motions anytime you interact with Escape Motions such as when you purchase an Escape Motions Software product, contact a technical support, participate in our community center including posting to forum, to gallery, comment on articles, and others or sign up for an e-newsletter.

#### 

Information We Collect

  


### Information You Provide to Us

When you place an order with us for our software products, the information we collect may include your name, e-mail address, mailing address, billing address, shipping address and, if applicable, EU VAT number or other tax ID. We will also keep the details of your order and of its fulfillment. We never store your credit card information.  
If you create account on our Community website, we may collect your username, e-mail address and password for your account.  
We collect your personal information because it assists us in delivering a high level of customer service. For example, your personal information helps us to keep you informed and up-to-date on the latest product updates and announcements as well as special offers that may be extended from time to time.  
If you do not want Escape Motions to contact you, you may “opt-out” at any time. You may “opt-out” by contacting Escape Motions Customer Service Department or by following the instructions included in the newsletter.

### Information We Collect from our Software

We collect information when you use the Escape Motions Software. When you use our software while your computer is connected to the Internet, we collect your registration details of the software, IP address, the version of the software and operating system details.

### Cookies

We may use cookies to collect your personal information. Cookies are unique identifiers which are used to customize your website, mobile application or social media site experience. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent or notify you whenever you are sent a cookie. This gives you the chance to decide whether or not to accept the cookie. A cookie does not tell us who you are, your email address, or any other personal information. We use the information it provides to help us improve our website, mobile application, social media site and our services, and to provide our visitors with a better visit. Even without accepting a cookie you can still access all features on the site. There may, however, be limitations on your use of some site functions.

#### 

How We Use Your Information

Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:   
\- To provide and improve our software products (your information helps us better respond to your individual problems and needs).  
\- To improve customer service (your information helps us effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs).  
\- To carry out our obligations and enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered into between you and us, including for billing and collection.  
\- To send periodic emails about our own products that may be of interest to you (the email address you provide for order processing may be used to send you information and updates pertaining your order, in addition to receiving occasional company news, updates, related product or service information, etc.) and to keep you informed about all the news from our world.  
Note: If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving emails, we include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email.  
\- To administer a contest, promotion, survey or other feature.

We are not liable or responsible for the personally identifiable or other information you choose to submit in the community center of our website such as a forum, gallery or any other publicly accessible area of the site. 

#### 

Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. However, we may release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect our or others rights, property or safety.

#### 

Accessing and Correcting Your Information

You may change your email address, password and other information for the Community center or the software by editing them in your account page. To change your ID provided during the purchase of our software, please contact us at our [support email](mailto:support@escapemotions.com).

#### 

How We Protect Your Information

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you access your personal information. While we strive to protect the personal information you submit to us, no data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. As such, we cannot guarantee or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or from our products and you do so at your own risk. Once your information has been received by Escape Motions, we will make our best effort to ensure the safety and security on our systems.

#### 

Children’s Information

We believe it is important to provide added protection for children online. We encourage parents and guardians to spend time online with their children to observe, participate in and/or monitor and guide their online activity. Escape Motions does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. We advise all children to first get their parents or guardians permission before creating an account for the Community page on our website.

#### 

Links to Other Websites

Our website, forum, gallery, social media sites and mobile applications may contain links to websites of third parties. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those other websites.

#### 

Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our privacy policy and agree to its terms.
