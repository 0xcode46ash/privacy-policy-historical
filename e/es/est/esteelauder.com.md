# Estée Lauder

When you register at the Estée Lauder Web site, we respect your privacy. Estée Lauder will not give your user information to a third party without your consent. The information you provide us is used to help personalize product information and offers for you.

Your name and address information is used for mailing you free sample offers. Your E-mail address is used only for sending you information and updates about Estée Lauder products, tips and the Web site.

On 

[_our registration form_](https://www.esteelauder.com/templates/user/signin.tmpl/policy/private.html) you can select whether or not you want to receive E-mail from us. If at any time you wish to be taken off the Estée Lauder E-mail list, please send a message to [_unsubscribe.esteelauder@messagemedia.com_](mailto:unsubscribe.Esteelauder@messagermedia.com) with the word "remove" as the only word on the first line of the body of the message. If you would like to contact a person to have your name removed from the mailing list, please send mail to [_el-tech@esteelauder.com_](mailto:el-tech@Esteelauder.com).

If you want to send feedback to us about a privacy issue or anything else, 

[_click here_](http://www.esteelauder.com/templates/email/email.tmpl).

Thank you.
