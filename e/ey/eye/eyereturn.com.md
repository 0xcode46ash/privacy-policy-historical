# eyeReturn: Privacy Policy

  
All of your individually identifying information is treated as confidential and will not be sold by us to any third parties, including our Campaign Sponsors and host website owners. 

**Access to your account is password protected**

We will NEVER release your account password to any person. In the event that you forget your password, you may generate an on-line request to have the password sent to you by e-mail at the e-mail address used in registration. **What information we need to collect:**

eyeReturn collects the minimum information from you in order to set up a personal eyeReturn account â€“ the username of your choice, and a password. This information is necessary for you to access the eyeReturn system via participating websites. 

In the event you win a reward, you will be asked for information that our campaign sponsors will need to deliver this reward. 

This information is stored on an eyeReturn database in Toronto, Canada. 

We will NEVER release your account password to any person. In the event that you forget your password, you may generate an on-line request to have the password sent to you by e-mail at the e-mail address used in registration. 

**Your Responsibilities**

eyeReturn cannot ensure your privacy if you do not do so yourself. Don't divulge your eyeReturn account password to any third party. You will not be asked for it by anyone connected to, or unconnected to, eyeReturn and its partner companies. If you are suspicious of any request for information, please contact us immediately at privacy@eyereturn.com. 

eyeReturn cannot be held responsible for any activity in your account which results from your failure to keep your own password secure.
