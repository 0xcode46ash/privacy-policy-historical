# Privacy Policy

The information you provide us such as name, email, address will only be stored in our database to provide you with the service you have requested. The Newsquest Media Group guarantees that it will not, without your explicit consent, distribute this personal information to other commercial partners.

It also guarantees that information about your patterns of usage will not be forwarded to other parties.

The information will not be kept any longer than necessary for us to deliver the services requested by you. You are free to delete yourself as a user at any time you may wish to do so.

For the purposes of the prevention or detection of offences, and/or the apprehension or prosecution of offenders, we may share any of the information that we collect with the Police, other public or private sector agencies or representative bodies in accordance with the relevant legislation. This will include public authorities, insurance companies, finance companies, automotive advertising companies and/or other agencies.

When you make a call to a vehicle dealer using one of the telephone numbers advertised on our website, this call may be recorded, and the dealer may be provided with details of the call including number, date, time and length of call. The dealer may also be given access to the recording of the call.
