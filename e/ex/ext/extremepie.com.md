# Customer Services :: Privacy Policy and Data Protection at extremepie.com

### “We care about your privacy. I promise Extremepie.com will never sell or pass on your details to a third party without your consent“

Al Gosling, CEO Extreme Group 

## Our policy in detail;

Extremepie.com is totally committed to protecting the privacy of our site visitors and customers. extremepie.com is owned and operated by Extreme Innovations Ltd (part of the Extreme Group). We will not disclose information about our customers to third parties except where it is a necessary part of providing a service to you - e.g. arranging for a product to be sent to you. extremepie.com is registered on the Data protection register. 

We will not rent or sell your name, address, e-mail address or personal information to any third party (excluding partners from whom you may have linked to our site) without your permission. 

We will not rent or sell your personal information, other than as part of a sale of the whole or a substantial part of the assets of Extreme Innovations Ltd, without ensuring that suitable arrangements are in place to protect your privacy (unless required to do so by law). 

We may analyse user data to decide which users to include in any promotional campaigns we may run. We may also use aggregate information and statistics for the purposes of monitoring Web site usage in order to help us develop the Web site and our service and may provide such aggregate information to third parties. From time to time we may provide your information to our customer service agencies for research and analysis purposes so that we can monitor and improve the services we provide. 

**Updating your details**

If any of the information that you have provided to extremepie.com changes (for example if you change your e-mail address) do let us know. If you are already a registered user, then just log-in to "your account" and make the changes and this will automatically update our records. If you are not yet a registered user, then please give us the correct new details by sending an e-mail to info@extremepie.com - if you are changing your email address, don't forget to give us your old email address too! 

**Your consent**

By submitting your information you consent to the use of that information as set out in this policy.   
• If we change our privacy policy we will post the changes on this page, and may place notices on other pages of the web site, so that you may be aware of the information we collect and how we use it.   
• We will also e-mail you should we make any changes so that you may consent to our use of your information in that way. Continued use of the web site will signify that you agree to any such changes.   
• Because the Internet infrastructure is global, the information you provide may be transferred to countries outside the European Economic Area ("EEA") that do not have similar data protection legislation during use as set out in this policy (for example, we may decide at a later date to host the extremepie.com site on servers located outside the EEA).
