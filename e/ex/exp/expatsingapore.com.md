# Expat Singapore Registration Form

**Why register with us?**

   Registering with our site has several advantages. First, you will receive a free monthly newsletter through your email, keeping you in the know about the latest happenings in Singapore as well as on our site. By registering with us, we will also be better able to help you by providing relevant information about moving to and living in Singapore.

**Prizes to be won!**

   And from September 1, 1998, everyone who registers with us will automatically take part in our [monthly contests](https://web.archive.org/web/19990223215421id_/http%3A//www.expatsingapore.com/travel/register.htm) where [Sedona Hotels International](https://web.archive.org/web/19990223215421id_/http%3A//www.expatsingapore.com/travel/promo.htm) is giving away free trips to Indonesia and other exotic destinations. If you do not wish to be considered for the contest, please indicate so at the bottom of the form.

**Our Privacy Policy**

**     **We strive for the highest ethical standards in editorial and advertising integrity. We collect information about you to: 

  * Mail you information about products and services you are likely to want, and 
  * Provide our advertisers with aggregated statistics on our visitors.



   Occasionally, we will send out information from selected companies about products and services that we think might interest you. If you do not wish to receive any information from these companies, you may opt out by indicating thus on the registration form.
