# Experian - Privacy and Information Use

##  Experian's approach to privacy and information use 

Experian is the information industry's recognized leader in balancing the interests and needs of consumers and businesses when developing new information-based services and products.

Our values approach to information use is a model for all companies concerned about doing business in an increasingly privacy-sensitive and regulation-minded business environment.

Experian is in compliance with the Direct Marketing Association's [guidelines for online marketing and privacy](http://www.the-dma.org/busasst6/busasst-onmarkprivpr6a7.shtml).

### Experian's information values

Experian's five information values are: 

  * partnership: We partner with customers to meet legitimate business needs and with consumers -- who are their customers -- to ensure appropriate information privacy.
  * fairness: We strive to meet consumer expectations about accuracy and appropriate use of consumer information, and to be open and honest in disclosing how that information is used.
  * balance: We offer only products and services that assure a favorable balance of consumer benefits when weighed against privacy expectations.
  * security: We protect information from unauthorized access or alteration by providing the highest level of security appropriate for the information's sensitivity.
  * communications: We provide education and information to help people become more knowledgeable consumers, and we listen carefully to organizations concerned with consumer and privacy interests. 



###  The information we collect on this web site

When you come to the Experian web site to browse, you do so anonymously. Experian does not collect identifying information about you. We collect only summary information about the numbers of individuals who visit our web site and what those individuals look at. We collect this information so that we can continue to improve the site and make it more worthwhile and interesting to you.

If you decide to purchase an online product, we will collect information for authentication and billing purposes. However, we do _not_ retain any credit card information you provide us.
