# Privacy Policy

Last Updated on 22nd Sep 2014

### General:

This Privacy Policy Statement applies to all of the products and services offered by FrontalRain Technologies or its subsidiaries or affiliated companies ("The Company", "FrontalRain Technologies Pvt. Ltd", "we", "us", "our", and terms of similar meaning) and is incorporated into and subject to our Terms of Service. Capitalized terms not defined herein shall have such meaning as ascribed to the term in the Terms. The Customer and/ or the Authorized User shall hereinafter be collectively referred to as the "user", "you", "your", "the Customer" and any other terms that bear similar meaning.

This Privacy Policy covers and describes how we collect, use and disclose information that we collect through the website: [www.eximdesk.com](http://www.eximdesk.com/), our subdomains and our service (Eximdesk).

### Information Recorded and Use:

### Personal Information

When you sign up for the Services or promotions that requires registration, we seek the personal information of the Customer and the Authorized User (such as name, email address and phone number). For our customers we also maintain Customer Data which is managed differently as explained in a later section. FrontalRain collects information under the direction of its Customers, and has no direct relationship with the individuals whose personal data it processes within the platform.

### Correcting and Updating Your Personal Information:

To review and update, correct or delete your personal information and to ensure it is accurate, you may login into your account to make the changes, or you may contact us at [support@eximdesk.com](mailto:support@eximdesk.com).

### Access to Data Controlled by our Customers:

FrontalRain Technologies has no direct relationship with the individuals whose personal data it processes through the Eximdesk system. An individual who seeks access, or who seeks to correct, amend, or delete inaccurate data should direct his query to the FrontalRain Technologies's Customer (the data controller). If the Customer requests FrontalRain Technologies to remove the data, we will respond to their request within 30 business days.

### Log Information:

Upon access of the Services, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website. These server logs may include information such as your web request, internet protocol address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.

### Usage Details:

Your usage details including without limitation the time, frequency, duration and pattern of use, features used and the amount of storage used will be recorded by us for among other reasons, security concerns.

### User communications:

When you send an email or other communications to FrontalRain Technologies, we retain those communications.

### Customer Data:

FrontalRain Technologies will not review, share, distribute, or reference any Customer Data except as provided herein or the Terms,or as may be required by law. Individual records of Customer Data may be viewed or accessed for the purpose of resolving a problem, support issues, or suspected violation of the Terms of Service, or as may be required by law. Customers are responsible for maintaining the security and confidentiality of their user ids and passwords.

### Contents of your User Account:

We store and maintain files, documents, to-do lists, emails and other data stored in your user account at centralized FrontalRain designated facilities. In order to prevent loss of data due to errors or system failures, we also keep backup copies of data including the contents of your user account. Hence your files and data may remain on our servers even after deletion or termination of your user account. We assure you that the contents of your user account will not be disclosed to anyone and will not be accessible even to employees of FrontalRain except in circumstances specifically mentioned in this Privacy Policy Statement and Terms of Service. We also retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

### Data Retention:

FrontalRain Technologies will retain personal data we process through the platform on behalf of our Customers for as long as needed to provide services to our Customer. FrontalRain Technologies will retain and use this personal information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

### Data integrity:

FrontalRain Technologies collects, processes and reviews all Customer Data only for the purposes for which it was collected and in accordance with this Privacy Policy or the Terms of Service or under law.

FrontalRain Technologies processes the various information you provide to offer solutions and results to you, as may be required for providing and enhancing the Services including without limitation:

    * Auditing, research and analysis in order to maintain, protect and improve our services;
    * Aggregating usage patterns, trends and other statistics;
    * Developing new services;
    * FrontalRain Technologies may also use data about customers for marketing purposes. For example, the Company may use information you provide to contact you to further discuss your interest in FrontalRain Technologies, the Service, and to send you information regarding the Company and its partners, such as information about promotions or events; and
    * On occasion we use email address or other contact information to contact our Customers to ask them for their input on our services. We may also use your email address to send you promotional emails and update you about our latest products. Out of respect for your privacy, you may choose to stop receiving our promotional emails by following the unsubscribe instructions included in these emails or you can contact us at [info@eximdesk.com](mailto:info@eximdesk.com). We may also use personal information about you to improve our marketing and promotional efforts, to analyze our website usage, to improve our content and product offerings, and to customize our website's content, layout, and services. These uses improve our website and better tailor it to meet your needs, so as to provide you with a smooth, efficient, safe and customized experience while using our website.



### Information sharing:

FrontalRain Technologies shares Customer Data and any other information with other companies or individuals outside of FrontalRain Technologies in the following limited circumstances:

    * We provide such information to our agents who have been engaged to perform functions on our behalf. We require that these parties agree to process such information based on our instructions and in compliance with this Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures. These parties are authorized to use such information only as necessary to provide these services to us.
    * To (a) satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request, (b) enforce applicable Terms of Service, including investigation of potential violations thereof, (c) detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues, or (d) protect against imminent harm to the rights, property or safety of FrontalRain Technologies, its users or the public as required or permitted by law.
    * If FrontalRain Technologies becomes involved in a merger, acquisition, or any form of sale or transfer of some or all of its assets. In this case, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our Web site of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your personal information.



However, we would like to state that no system can guarantee absolute security although, we take every reasonable precaution to assure that your data is secure in the manner provided in this Privacy Policy. All information collected from the Customer or that falls within the possession of FrontalRain is held on secure servers hosted by FrontalRain. However, the nature of internet is such that no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. However, we have assured to take such reasonable steps as laid out in these terms to protect your data.

### Cookies:

We use temporary and permanent cookies to enhance your experience of our Services. Temporary cookies will be removed from your computer each time you close your browser. Permanent cookies or persistent cookie remains on your hard drive for an extended period of time. You can remove persistent cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browser's "help" directory [(Information on cookies).](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTTP_cookie "Clicke here for more information on cookies") If you have turned cookies off, you may not be able to use registered areas of the website. We tie cookie information to your user id when you elect to remain logged in so as to maintain and recall your preferences within the website.

We may from time to time employ the services of a third party agent to analyze statistical and aggregate data about our website content and user base. This aggregated information is not personally identifiable and is employed in improving website functionality and offerings. To this end, these third parties may employ the use of cookies and, or pixel tags in providing their services. While these third party cookies are not tied to your Customer Data, FrontalRain does not have access to these cookies nor are we responsible for them.

### Social Media Widgets:

Our Web site includes Social Media Features, such as the Facebook Like button [and Widgets, such as the Share this button or interactive mini-programs that run on our site]. These Features may collect your IP address, which page you are visiting on our site, and may set a cookie to enable the Feature to function properly. Social Media Features and Widgets are either hosted by a third party or hosted directly on our Site. Your interactions with these Features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing it.

### Links from our website:

Some pages of our website contain external links over which we have no control. You are advised to verify the privacy practices of such other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of the manner of use or misuse of information made available by you at such other websites. We encourage you not to provide personal information, without assuring yourselves of the Privacy Policy Statement of other websites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our website and welcome any feedback from you with regard to those linked sites.

Except as otherwise expressly included in this Privacy Policy, this document only addresses the use and disclosure of information we collect from you. To the extent that you disclose your information to other parties through such external links, whether they are subscribers or customers or otherwise, different rules may apply to their use, collection and disclosure of the personal information you disclose to them. ### How secure is your Information:

We adopt industry appropriate data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures, as well as physical security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction of your Personal Information, username, password, transaction information and data stored in your user account. Access to your name and email address is restricted to our employees and agents who need to know such information in connection with providing Services to you and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

Salient security features from the physical and network perspectives are:

    * Physical security provided at the data center like biometric access, surveillance cameras, photo id card, etc.
    * Network level security consisting of Robust Firewalls and Intrusion Detection Systems;
    * Dedicated Personnel at the centralized FrontalRain designated facilities will monitor both the above on a 24X7 basis



Salient Security Features from an Application perspective are:

    * Service provided on https protocol ensuring encryption of data and secure transmission.
    * Comprehensive Application level security addressing issues like access level checking, SQL Injection, etc.
    * Facility to configure a password policy, to enforce stronger and more secure passwords
    * Backups of application and database on a regular basis



FrontalRain Technologies uses robust security measures to protect Customer Data from unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and help ensure the appropriate use of Customer Data. When the Service is accessed using Internet Explorer version 9.0 or later, Firefox version 14.0 or later, or Safari version 3.0 or later, or Google Chrome 21.0 or later, Secure Socket Layer ("SSL") technology protects Customer Data using both server authentication and data encryption. These technologies help ensure that Customer Data is safe, secure, and only available to the Customer to whom the information belongs and those to whom the Customer has granted access. FrontalRain Technologies also implements an advanced security method based on dynamic data and encoded session identifications, and the Company hosts its Web sites in a secure server environment that uses firewalls and other advanced technology to prevent interference or access from outside intruders. Because the Company uses the Service to maintain Data about FrontalRain Technologies' Customers, this information is secured in the same manner as described above for Customer Data.
