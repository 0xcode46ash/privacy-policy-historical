The Hearst Electronic Group Privacy Statement** ****  
  
Hearst Electronic Group ("HEG") shares your concern regarding the privacy and confidentiality of the personal information we collect during the use of our websites. The following is our policy regarding the use and protection of this information.   
  
  
We collect information about users during their registration for our websites and through their use of certain areas of the websites, including contests and polls. HEG does not reveal any personal information that users provide to us to any third party without permission. We do provide aggregated information to our advertisers in order to help them better understand our services, but HEG does not give advertisers information about individual users unless we are given permission to do so.   
  
  
  
  
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site or your dealings with HEG, you can contact: _[privacy@hearstelectroweb.com](mailto:%20privacy@hearstelectroweb.com)_ and let us know your preferences.
