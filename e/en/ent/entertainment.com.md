**Entertainment Publications, Inc. Privacy Policy**  
   
Entertainment Publications, Inc., ("Entertainment") respects your privacy. When you use our web site, we will collect certain information in order to make shopping online possible and provide you with other services. This privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") describes the information we collect, and the manner in which we may use such information. We may modify or supplement this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we decide to change this Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on this page, prior to the change taking effect.

For our online Fund-Raising Privacy Policy, please [click here](https://web.archive.org/jsp/consumer/help/safety_privacy_legal/parent_fundraising_privacy_policy.jsp).

  | Collection of Information:  
   
  | • | When you request certain services using our web site, such as registering as an Online Member or ordering Entertainment products online, we will ask you to provide personal information about yourself. This information will include name, mailing address, e-mail address, telephone number, credit card number and Entertainment card number. This information, along with the Entertainment® book edition number, is necessary to your receiving and/or being billed for the services you request (e.g., name, address and credit card information when ordering products), and you will not be able to use such services without providing this information.  
  | • | We will retain this information for as long as you are a registered Online Member. You may request to terminate your Online Membership and have your personal information removed from our database by submitting a request to [Customer Service](https://web.archive.org/jsp/consumer/help/contact_us.jsp), identifying the subject as "Privacy", and, unless we are prohibited by law, we will remove such information within a 3 month period.  
  | • | Information such as where you purchased the Entertainment book, prior purchasing history, age, marital status, household information and any other personal information are optional. We will collect information such as this only when knowingly provided by you. We utilize this information in order to provide and improve our service to you.  
  | • | When you visit our web site, we will automatically collect information regarding your use. Currently, this is done using "Cookies" - small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive and relayed back to our servers. Cookies enable such functions as tracking items you place in your shopping cart, storing and retrieving passwords and identifying you as a prior visitor, Online Member or customer of our Web site.   
  | • | Cookies do not retrieve personal information unless you have previously provided it to us. Most browsers can be set to refuse Cookies and if you wish, you can manually delete Cookies from your computer's hard drive. Currently, you can use most features of our Web site without accepting Cookies (including placing items in your shopping cart and purchasing Books), but refusing Cookies will prevent the storage of items in your shopping cart in between visits and may now, or in the future, affect certain functionality and prohibit your usage of other services.

We will only use your credit card information for the processing of our payment.
