# Privacy Policy | Entertainment.ie

This website, entertainment.ie and associated apps is owned and operated by Packed House Ltd.

In this Privacy Policy, when we refer to "entertainment.ie", we mean the digital network operated by entertainment.ie, or its parent companies, subsidiaries and affiliates.

Entertainment.ie is fully compliant with The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679, which is in effect from May 25th 2018.

## **Right to Privacy**

entertainment.ie respects your right to privacy. Any information we collect, be it non-personalised or personalised, will be treated with the highest standards of security and confidentiality, strictly in accordance with The General Data Protection Regulation. Entertainment.ie strictly only works with fully data compliant partners.

## **Collection and use of personal information**

entertainment.ie collects non-personalised and personalised data in three main ways:   


  * Information that you actively volunteer, such as account registration, email newsletter registration and similar.
  * Analytics: This allows entertainment.ie to measure the number of visits to the website and helps inform us as to how to improve the website for visitors.
  * Advertising: Advertisers on entertainment.ie use cookies to inform, optimise and serve ads based on your browsing activities and interests.



We never share this information with any third parties without your explicit consent.

For more information on how to manage your cookies please visit our [Cookie Policy.](http://entertainment.ie/privacy.asp)

  


### **_The information we collect:_**

#### **Advertising**

Advertisements on entertainment.ie can be of a non-personalised or personalised nature. The information collected by advertisers helps them to gauge the effectiveness of their campaigns and may limit the number of times you see an advertisement. For more information and details of how to manage how advertising works on entertainment.ie, read our[Cookies Policy.](http://entertainment.ie/cookiepolicy.asp)

#### **Entertainment.ie Reader Accounts ("ulike")**

You may register for a free entertainment.ie readers account (called "uLike").  
These accounts allow you to enter competitions on our website, customise your cinema and tv listings as well as receive email and text reminders.

The information we collect and store securely includes name, email address, phone number, postal address, date of birth, competition entry details, sms and email reminders, posts in comments, log in and updating account details. We need this information to send your reminders, check the validity of competition entries, send you prizes if you win, and for storing your favourite tv stations and cinemas on our systems.

You may change your account settings or opt out of uLike at any time by [logging into your account.](http://entertainment.ie/ulike/)

#### **Using Facebook to comment**

When connecting your Facebook account to entertainment.ie to comment on an article, we need to obtain permission to collect your associated Facebook name, email address and IP address.

Comments on entertainment.ie are moderated. If a comment breaks our house rules we may need to remove a comment or block a user from commenting in the future. We need this account information to provide this service.

You may [delete this data](http://entertainment.ie/ulike/) from entertainment.ie any time.

#### **Entertainment.ie Partner Listing Accounts**

You may register for a free entertainment.ie listing partner account to submit event listings.  
The information we collect and store securely includes name, email address, phone number, the venue or act you represent, the events you have submitted, log in and updating account details.

We need this information to verify the events that are submitted are valid and do not contravene our terms of use.

You may opt out of our partner listing account at any time by [deleting your account.](http://entertainment.ie/ulike/)

#### **Email Newsletters**

You may register for informational email newsletters via our website. This requires your name and email address, and optionally your county and areas of interest, to be submitted to our email newsletter software (Mailchimp), which is fully compliant with The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). [Read Mailchimp’s Privacy Policy.](https://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy/)

You may choose to opt out of email newsletters or update your preferences at any time via the unsubscribe link in the foot of any of our email newsletters or from the signup form on our website . You may also choose to opt out by contacting [info@entertainment.ie](mailto:info@entertainment.ie) at any time.

#### **Email Contact by You**

If you email entertainment.ie with feedback or questions, we will respond to you as quickly as possible. Any details you send to us, including email and name, are stored for as long as is necessary to deal with your query. All contacts are deleted once we have satisfactorily answered your question or comment.

#### **Competition Winners List**

We like to provide all readers with the names of winners of competitions. We will always request permission from the winner to display their name on the list and only display this for 3 months. Any winner has the right not to have their name published. There may be some instances of us not being able to display full names on our winners list for security or operational reasons.

## __Services We Use__

We use some third party services on our website. We only use GDPR compliant services and partners.

#### **Analytics Services**

These services help us build a better website experience. We use Google Analytics, Hotjar, Firebase and Parse.ly for this purpose. All of these services anonymise your IP and do not contain any personally identifiable information. Read their Privacy Policies by clicking on the links above. These companies do not share your data with any other companies. This information will never be disclosed to a third party, unless obliged to do so by a rule of law.

#### **Advertising**

We schedule our advertising on site and in app with Google’s DoubleClick for Publishers. Any information collected and stored is non-personalised. Some cookies are used for non personalised ads. [Read our Cookies Policy.](http://entertainment.ie/cookiepolicy.asp)

#### **Competitions & Surveys:**

#### **Wufoo**

Entertainment.ie occasionally uses Wufoo for competition entry collection. We will clearly inform you when we are using this service. Wufoo is owned and operated by Survey Monkey. [Read Wufoo’s Privacy Policy.](https://www.surveymonkey.com/mp/legal/privacy-policy/)

#### **Survey Monkey**

Entertainment.ie occasionally uses Survey Monkey to run reader surveys. We will clearly inform you when we are using this service. [Read Survey Monkey’s Privacy Policy.](https://www.surveymonkey.com/mp/legal/privacy-policy/)

## **_External Links_**

Entertainment.ie does provide links to other websites. Whilst we do check the validity of other websites and never link to any bogus websites, you should always check other website’s own Privacy Policies and Terms of Use.

We update our Privacy Policy from time to time as legislation and internal operating procedures change. Please keep an eye on this page for any updates or changes.

Contact our Data Protection Officer at [requests@packed.house](mailto:requests@packed.house) for any further clarifications or personal data requests. Whilst we aim to address any questions or queries as soon as possible, please allow up to 30 working days for a response.

For more information on data protection, please visit [www.dataprotection.ie](https://web.archive.org/web/20180629054334id_/http%3A//entertainment.ie/www.dataprotection.ie) & <https://ec.europa.eu/info/law/law-topic/data-protection_en>
