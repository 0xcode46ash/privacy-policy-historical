# Privacy Policy

Welcome to Ents24.com, a site owned and operated by Ents24 Ltd ('Ents24'). We treat your privacy seriously, and this page describes our treatment of any personally identifiable information that we collect when you are using the website and when you contact us. Ents24 is registered under the Data Protection Act in the UK. 

## Information Collection and Use

Ents24 collects information about you when your register for an account, and thereafter when you visit the Ents24 web pages. Ents24 may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners. 

**Registration.** During the registration process, a user is required to give their full name, email address and postcode. It is optional for the user to provide further information about their interests and demographic information (such as gender and date of birth), but encouraged so we can provide a more personalised experience. 

**Correspondence.** If you contact Ents24, we may keep a record of that correspondence. 

**Other Information.** Ents24 automatically receives and records information from your browser including your IP address and the page you requested. 

**Cookies.** [Read further information here about Ents24's use of cookies.](https://web.archive.org/web/20051214141805id_/http%3A//www.ents24.com/web/content/Cookies.html)

## Sharing of Information

Ents24 will not release your personally identifiable information to other parties, unless you give your consent during the registration process or at a later date (see below). 

We will share aggregated demographic information with our partners and advertisers. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify individuals. 

We may send information about you to other companies to provide services or products you have specifically requested. Unless we state otherwise, these companies do not have the right to use your information for any other purposes. 

## Editing your Account Information

If you would like to alter your account information or preferences at any time, please log into the website using the username and password you chose during registration.
