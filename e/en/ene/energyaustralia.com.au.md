# EnergyAustralia - Privacy policy

EnergyAustralia respects the confidentiality of your personal information. We use the following guidelines to protect any information you provide to us when you use the EnergyAustralia web site.

From time to time we may undertake customer surveys to collect statistics about our customers for research and product development purposes. You are free to decide whether or not to participate in these surveys.  


  * Our aim is to use any confidential information we collect from you to : 

  
\- provide you with the service you have requested,   
\- allow us to develop new products and services to meet your needs, and   
\- allow us to communicate with you effectively   


  * You are able to indicate whether or not you wish to receive this communication from us.


  * We do not sell or give to other persons or organisations lists containing your personal information.


  * We will not disclose any personal particulars we hold about you to any third person without your written consent or your implied consent. You give implied consent when you indicate to us that you wish to obtain information about goods or services supplied by others.


  * We protect your privacy by restricting access to your personal information to those staff who need it, either to process information or to provide you with the services you have asked for.


  * When you visit the EnergyAustralia site, we may consider which pages you visit to help us determine which parts of the site you value, so that we can build and develop our web site to best meet your needs.
