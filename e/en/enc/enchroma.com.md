# Privacy Policy | EnChroma

#### **Protection and Use of Private Personal Information**

Protecting your private information is our priority. This Statement of Privacy applies to the EnChroma.com and EnChroma, Inc. and governs data collection and usage. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, unless otherwise noted, all references to EnChroma, Inc. include EnChroma.com and EnChroma. The EnChroma website is an E-commerce site engaged in the business of marketing and sales of spectacle glasses, sunglasses and prescription eyewear. By using the EnChroma website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

#### **Collection of your Personal Information**

EnChroma may collect personally identifiable information, such as your name. If you purchase EnChroma’s products and services, we collect billing and credit card information. This information is used to complete the purchase transaction. EnChroma may also collect anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your age, gender, household income and Type and extent of color blindness or color vision deficiency, and prescriptions for spectacle eyeglasses. We may gather additional personal or non-personal information in the future.

Information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by EnChroma. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the EnChroma website.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through EnChroma’s public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others.

EnChroma encourages you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from EnChroma so that you can understand how those websites collect, use and share your information. EnChroma is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of the EnChroma website.

#### **Use of your Personal Information**

EnChroma collects and uses your personal information to operate its website(s) and deliver the services you have requested.

EnChroma may also use your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from EnChroma and its affiliates. EnChroma may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

EnChroma does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.

EnChroma may share data with trusted partners to help perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to EnChroma, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

EnChroma may keep track of the websites and pages our users visit within EnChroma, in order to determine what EnChroma services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within EnChroma to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

EnChroma will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on EnChroma or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of EnChroma; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of EnChroma, or the public.

#### **Use of Cookies**

The EnChroma website may use “cookies” to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize EnChroma pages, or register with EnChroma site or services, a cookie helps EnChroma to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same EnChroma website, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the EnChroma features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the EnChroma services or websites you visit.
