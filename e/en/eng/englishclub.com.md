# EnglishCLUB.net > Privacy Policy Statement

EnglishCLUB.net is firmly committed to privacy on the internet. This means that we consider that any information you give us is confidential. We are committed to keeping it confidential unless you give us your specific permission otherwise.

**Personal Information**  
We collect only information that is voluntarily provided by our visitors, and we use it only for the purpose for which it was provided. We securely log this information.

This information is not shared with or sold to any third party, and would only be made available to certain law enforcement agencies in the event of a criminal offence and a resulting court order requiring us to comply. This information is not collected for advertising purposes, and is never shared with advertisers.

**Questions or Concerns**  
If you have any questions about EnglishCLUB.net's privacy policy or practices, or about your dealings with this website, please do not hesitate to [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20001202055300id_/http%3A//www.englishclub.net/contact.shtml).

EnglishCLUB.net works with a third party that serves ads to this site. To find out more about how Flycast manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to <http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html>.
