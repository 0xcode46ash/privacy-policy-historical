# Engineering Resumes - Search | Engineer Jobs.com

[](http://www.engineerjobs.com/)

Engineer Jobs.com is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. The following explains how we handle your personal information.

Engineer Jobs.com does gather statistics about what areas of the site users visit most frequently, and what services users access the most, but that information is always in the aggregate and NO individual information is used.

Since we began operations in 1996, we have NEVER sold or given any personal information about our users to any third party.
