# Endsleigh Insurance Services Ltd

At endsleigh.co.uk we are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about you simply to provide you with information you request together with details of updates and improvements to our site. All your information is handled in accordance with the Data Protection Acts of 1984 and 1998. 

## What information do we collect and how do we use it?

We only collect the information that you actively provide us with. This is generally information required to forward a quote. This information may be stored so that we can retrieve quote information at a later date. 

## Will Endsleigh disclose the information it collects to outside parties? 

We take great care to ensure that the information will be used exclusively by Endsleigh and not passed on for any other Marketing promotions. The information you provide will be processed by Endsleigh's website agency Tamar, who will not disclose your details to any other parties. 

## Does Endsleigh keep web logs? 

Endsleigh maintains web logs in order to evaluate the popularity of its pages and sites. These will not reveal any details about you or impact on your privacy in any way. 

## How can you remove your details from our lists? 

If you decide that you do not want to receive further messages from Endsleigh, then simply click on the link at the bottom of the email you receive, which will automatically remove you from our lists. 

## Your consent 

By entering your details, you consent to collection and use of this information as described above. If we change our privacy policy, we will post any changes on these pages so that you are always aware of how we use your information. 

## Tell us what you think 

Endsleigh welcomes your comments about privacy. Please contact us with any requests or complaints on the subject.
