Privacy Policy   
Last Updated: October 20, 2006

This Privacy Policy covers this website.

Before you submit any information to our website, please read this Privacy Policy for an explanation of how we will handle your information. By using any part of our website, you agree, without limitation or qualification, to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use this website.   
  
We Respect Your Right to Privacy & Are Committed to Protecting it  
We are sensitive to your privacy concerns, and are committed to letting you know what information is being collected on our website, how the information is being used and what choices you have regarding the collection and use of the information. Please take a moment to review this Privacy Policy and contact us with any questions at [privacy@careerdegreesource.com](mailto:privacy@careerdegreesource.com).  
Privacy Policy Changes  
We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. You accept and agree to this Privacy Policy, and any changes to the same, by visiting this website. Therefore, we encourage you to review this website from time to time to review any possible changes to our Privacy Policy. Any updates we make will be clearly noted at the top of this page.  
What information do we collect about you and why?  
We gather personally identifiable information that you expressly provide when you enter information onto a form on our website including, without limitation, your name, mailing address, e-mail address, and other unique personal identifiers (“Personal Information”). It is your decision to provide us with this Personal Information as a way for us to provide a service back to you.

We also collect ANONYMOUS information, which includes any information that is NOT tied to your Personal Information, such as your IP address (“Anonymous Information”). You are not required to register or submit any information on our website in order for us to collect Anonymous Information about you. Further, we may receive information about you from other sources including, but not limited to, service providers for tracking and marketing purposes (“Source Information”). Examples of Source Information may consist of website referral data or information related to the browser type or connection speed you are using to access our website. Personal Information, Anonymous Information, and Source Information are collectively referred hereinafter as “Information.” 

We collect Information about you to (a) provide products, programs, and services that you have requested; (b) operate and improve the features, offerings and content presented to you by Us; (c) personalize the content provided to you; (d) communicate with you and respond to your inquiries; (e) conduct research about your use of the features, offerings and content from Us; (f) improve our product; (g) “pre-populate” forms; and (h) help offer you other products, programs, or services that may be of interest to you.  
  
Cookies   
In addition to the Information that you provide to us or that we obtain about you, We will also collect certain information arising out of your use of this web site, or augment the data We collect with other information from other online or offline sources. We use “cookies" and the Information to enhance your experience and present you with personalized offers. Cookies are simply data created by a Web server that is stored on a user's computer. It provides a way for the Website to keep track of a user's patterns and preferences and, with the cooperation of the Web browser, to store them on the user's own hard disk.  The cookies contain a range of URLs (Web addresses) for which they are valid. When the browser encounters those URLs again, it sends those specific cookies to the Web server. For example, if a user's ID were stored as a cookie, it would save that person from typing in the same information all over again when accessing that service for the second and subsequent time during the same browser session.

You can opt-out of the use of cookies by having your browser disable cookies or warn you before accepting a cookie. Look for the cookie options in your browser in the Options or Preferences menu.
