# Privacy Policy | Eddy Articles - Free Articles Directory | Submit Articles

A Privacy our visitors is essential for us.

We recognize that privacy of your personal data is important. Below follows some Information about what kind of information we collect when you visit our site. We never sell your information to third parties.

Data entry by registration

In general, the site EddyArticles can be visited without having to reveal any personal information. There are, however, areas of the site where it is necessary that users provide their personal data to register on the site.

You can choose to register on the site and provide personal data such as name, date of birth, e-mail, etc..

The data provided by users will only be used to perform the services selected by the user for which it was provided not being added to another list, made available to other entities or used to send information unrelated.

Cookies and web beacons

The site EddyArticles may use cookies and web beacons when a user has access to the pages. Cookies can be used are associated (if any) only with the browser of a particular computer.

Cookies are used on this site can be installed by the same, and they come from different servers operated by him, or from the servers of third parties that provide services and install cookies and web beacons (for example, cookies are employees to provide advertising services or certain content through which you view the advertising content or certain pre-time). You can search the hard drive of your computer as instructed by the browser itself.

The user has the ability to configure your browser to be advised on the computer screen, on the reception of cookies and to prevent their installation on the hard disk. The information relevant to this configuration are available in manuals and instructions to the browser itself.

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us here.

Thank you for your attention.
