# A Note About Security and Privacy

**Security and Privacy**

Thank you for shopping at www.eddiebauer.com. We've done our best to ensure the privacy and security of your transactions with us. Your credit card number and other personal data will be treated with the highest standards of safety, security features, and confidentiality. 

**Secure Shopping**   
Orders placed on our site are encrypted using SSL technology, so rest assured that your transactions are protected. With SSL implemented on both the client and server, information sent via the Internet can be trusted to arrive privately and unaltered to our server only. In order for SSL security to work, you must use an [SSL-enabled browser](https://web.archive.org/siteinfo/frame_browser_req.asp). For more information about SSL, see [Netscape's website](http://search.netscape.com/newsref/ref/internet-security.html). 

**Please note that email is not encrypted and is not considered a secure means of transmitting credit card numbers.**

If you still have any concerns about ordering electronically, please call **1-800-789-1386** to simply place your order over the phone. Have the 10-digit item number, size and color ready for each item you wish to order.

**Your Privacy Online**   
At Eddie Bauer, we are committed to ensuring your privacy as a customer. You can be confident that Eddie Bauer does not sell or exchange names, or any other information about our online customers, with third parties.

As a visitor to www.eddiebauer.com, our website automatically recognizes your Internet service provider, but you cannot be identified as an individual. For customers who make a purchase at www.eddiebauer.com, we do collect information on which pages are accessed at our site. We use this information to improve your overall shopping experience at www.eddiebauer.com. We also keep track of information volunteered during the order and registration processes - and we keep this information confidential. In an effort to offer you the best - and most current - shopping opportunities, we may contact you with news about Eddie Bauer products, services and events. If, for any reason, you would like to be taken off our email list, please contact us via email at [**imedia@eddiebauer.com**](mailto:imedia@eddiebauer.com).

**AOL Customers**  
As a certified AOL merchant, we have agreed to abide by AOL’s privacy policy. To read AOL’s privacy policy, [click here](http://legal.web.aol.com/policy/aolpol/privpol.html). If you have additional questions concerning our privacy practices, please contact us at [imedia@eddiebauer.com](mailto:imedia@eddiebauer.com)

**Our Catalog Mailing List**   
For print catalog customers, we may, on occasion, share a portion of our mailing list with other reputable organizations. If you do not want your name and address shared with other organizations, please contact us at 1-800-426-8020. If you do not wish to receive Eddie Bauer catalogs or prefer receiving a limited number of Eddie Bauer catalogs, please contact us at 1-800-426-8020. You may also contact us via email (please include your name and mailing address) at [**imedia@eddiebauer.com**](mailto:imedia@eddiebauer.com).
