# Privacy Policy - Education Articles

EdArticles.Com publishes information that you provide with your article including your name and any information you provide "About the Author." Other information is used only for the purposes of EdArticle.Com. We live by the Golden Rule when making decisions regarding user information. If you have any doubts about whether you want your information available on the Internet, simply do not submit an article.
