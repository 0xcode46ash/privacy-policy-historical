# Ordering Information: Privacy Policy

Edgepark Medical Supplies is strongly committed to safeguarding your online privacy, and has taken steps to protect the information you share with us. Edgepark Medical Supplies ensures the integrity of all credit card transactions between you and Edgepark with the most secure encryption technologies available today. Furthermore, we have managerial procedures in place to help maintain the accuracy of your information. Edgepark Medical Supplies will make a sincere effort to respond to your requests to correct personal information inaccuracies in a timely manner. In accordance with standard practices, we highly recommend that you keep your username and password confidential.

To provide you with a more personal and customized experience, Edgepark Medical Supplies collects certain personal information from you. Some personal information is gathered when you register to receive certain Edgepark services or products. At other times, we may ask you for information so you may participate in activities such as sweepstakes, contests, or other promotions. The better we know you, the better we can tailor our services to meet your needs. Under no circumstances will Edgepark Medical Supplies share your personal information with other interested parties without your express written consent. You may at any time declare that your personal information should be used for research purposes only, and we shall act accordingly.

Edgepark Medical Supplies reserves the right to change this statement at any time by posting revisions on the Edgepark web site. This privacy policy is not intended to, and does not, create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party.
