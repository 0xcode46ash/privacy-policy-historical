# Euroleague Basketball

**PRIVACY POLICY**

Thank you for visiting WWW.EUROLEAGUE.TV, FIBATV AND LIVEBASKETBALL.COM ("the Websites") powered by [WWW.LIVESPORT.TV](http://www.livesport.tv/), part of the Perform Group, [www.performgroup.com](http://www.performgroup.com/)

This Privacy Policy explains how Perform Group Limited (as owner of LIVEBASKETBALL.TV -subject to domain name change), Euroleague Properties SA (as owner of EUROLEAGUE.TV), Fédération Internationale de Basketball (as owner of FIBA.TV), together with other companies within the Perform Group Limited's, Euroleague Properties SA's and Fédération Internationale de Basketball's group of companies ("the Companies"), use information we collect about you and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. 

The Companies provide an online video service offering a selection of live sports events, edited highlights, extended highlights and other content (collectively, "Content") available on the Websites. Our video service, including the Content and our video player, and any other features, tools, applications, materials or other services offered from time to time by the Companies in connection with its business are referred to collectively as the "Services". The Websites are based in the United Kingdom.

By your use of the Websites you consent to be governed by this Privacy Policy.

1\. PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION

You can visit the Websites without disclosing any information about yourself. However, to purchase any live events viewable on this Website, participate in any contest or message feature, download any content, take part in any other interactive feature of the Websites, or receive information from the Companies, such as newsletters or email regarding upcoming the Services, you must register and provide accurate information about yourself to the Companies, as the Services may not work properly without such information.

Additionally, the Websites contain links to third-party websites that are not operated by the Companies. These third parties may collect data from you and share some of that data with us. The Companies are careful when choosing its partners, sponsors, and affiliates, but the Companies are not responsible for any misuse of personal information by such third parties. We recommend that you view the privacy policy posted on each of the Websites that you use to confirm that the Companies' policies are acceptable to you.

When using the Services, we may ask you to provide all or some of the following information: your name, your address, your email address, banking details, your telephone number, and your age ("Personal Information"). We may also ask you to provide your thoughts or answer some questions about your favourite Companies' events, personalities, and other subjects of interest to sports fans. Your Personal Information is password protected, and any Personal Information you provide to the Companies is protected from unauthorised disclosure by reasonable technological measures. Information provided to Companies is not, however, transmitted via SSL encryption technology. We recommend that you do not divulge your password to anyone. Personal Information may be used for marketing and promotional communications by the Companies, its partners, sponsors, affiliates, successors, and assigns. The Companies will, not, however, sell or lease your personal information to third parties not associated with the Companies, its partners, sponsors, and affiliates.

If you do not wish to receive marketing and promotional communications from us or our partners, sponsors, and affiliates, you can choose to opt-out when providing your Personal Information to the Companies. You can also opt-out by updating your Websites' user profile. 

Please note: If you do decide to opt-out of receiving information, the Companies will be unable to keep you informed of any new products, services, or special offers that may interest you and will have an impact on our ability to inform you of any live events important information as part of any subscription service you may have.

2\. AGGREGATED INFORMATION

To evaluate the Websites and ensure that we are providing Content and Services of interest to you, we may collect and store statistical information regarding how you use the Websites, the name of your Internet Service Provider, the websites that referred you to the Websites, and the time and date you view the Websites. We may also use Personal Information to develop demographic information regarding Websites users. Such aggregated information will not identify you personally. We may share this aggregated, statistical information with our partners, sponsors, affiliates, and other third parties.

We may on occasion combine information we receive online with other records to enhance our ability to notify you of our products and services that we think will be of interest to you. If you do not want to receive these notifications please send an e-mail to [customerservices@performgroup.com](mailto:customerservices@performgroup.com) letting us know.

3\. COOKIES

From time to time the Websites may place a cookie on your computer. A "cookie" is a small line of code that is stored in your browser when you visit a cookie-enabled website. A cookie allows us to track and keep records on user activity. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you set your browser to reject cookies, it is possible that some parts of the Websites will not function. Moreover, in order to use the Services, your browser must accept cookies.

4\. DISCLOSURE FOR LAW ENFORCEMENT PURPOSES

The Companies reserve the right to access and disclose Personal Information to comply with applicable laws and lawful government requests, to operate its systems properly, or to protect itself or others. The Companies may attempt to obtain the prior consent of the individual before disclosing the Personal Information, but have no obligation to do so.

5\. CONTACT AND NOTICES

Although absolute security cannot be guaranteed on the Internet, please be assured that we have in place security measures to protect the confidentiality of your information. We will continue to maintain and improve these measures over time in line with legal and technological developments.

You can contact us at any time at [customerservices@performgroup.com](mailto:customerservices@performgroup.com) to see the personal information we have collected through the Websites that relates to you. To protect your privacy and security we will take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access.

If we decide to change this Privacy Policy, the changes will be posted on this page so that you are always aware of the information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we disclose it. We will not use or disclose personal information provided to us in ways unrelated to the ones described above without informing you and providing you with an opportunity to opt-out of these unrelated uses.

If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy please feel free to contact us at [customerservices@performgroup.com](mailto:customerservices@performgroup.com)

6\. PARENTS & GUARDIANS:

These Websites are general audience Websites. We do not knowingly collect and/or share Personal Information from children under the age of thirteen on the Websites. However, your child cannot use some of the interactive features of the Websites without providing Personal Information. We therefore recommend using the Websites with your child if he or she is interested in participating in an interactive feature of the Websites.

If, in the future, the Companies collect Personal Information from children, it will do so in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998
