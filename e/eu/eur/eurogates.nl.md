# Privacy policy

|  | Study Abroad|  |  |    
---|---|---|---|---  
  
We respect and protect the privacy of our users. 

Eurogates promises _never_ to sell, exchange or release your personal information without your consent to any third parties.   


Personal information includes name, e-mail address, and answers to our questions.   


Eurogates does not monitor individual traffic patterns on our site - only aggregate reader behaviour is collected.   


We use this information only to help us improve our [site Eurogates](http://www.eurogates.nl/ "www.eurogates.nl") to make it more useful for our visitors.
