# EUdict | European dictionary

EUdict take your privacy concerns seriously. We do not collect any personal information from users browsing this website. A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a Web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. Cookie is used only for keeping your last searches. If you want you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies but then you will not be able to see (and pick from) the list of the last searched terms. When you recommend EUdict to someone using form on the page, email address and entered name are stored in Gmail account. We agree that this information will not be made available to any third party without the explicit consent of users. EUdict uses analytic software to collect data about web browsers and computers but this information contains no personal information about any visitor to our site; that is, no names, addresses, telephone numbers, e-mail addresses, and the like. Although we took all the preventive measures to make this site safe, we are not responsible for any damage or loss related to use of the content found on this website. EUdict reserves the right to change these terms of use and use of the web site at any time and shall not be liable for any possible consequences resulting from those changes. The mentioned changes take effect on publication on this web site.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click [here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
