# Privacy Policy | Online Fashion Clothing Wholesale : Ezytred Malaysia

The Privacy policy applies when you visiting the site [www.ezytred.com](http://www.ezytred.com/) and/or using the products and services on the site. As customer’s privacy is value to us, we therefore have a privacy policy for you to understand on how we collect and use your personal data. By using this site, you consent to our use of email as a means of such notification. 

**Use of Personal Data**

In providing your personal data, we will use your data for the following purposes.

• We will process your online order and delivery.

• We will send you an email notification whenever order is placed and delivery is done.

• We will contact you in the event of any problems with product and delivery of your items.   

• We may send you from time to time (by email) about our products, services and details of promotion. You may unsubscribe the newsletter if you do not wish to be contacted for these purposes. 

• To verify and carry out financial transactions in relation to payments you may make online. For example, payments that you make through our website will be processed by 3rd parties’ payment gateway such as Ipay88, e-GHL and etc.

The personal information we collect may include your:

• Name

• Telephone Number

• Mobile Number

• Delivery Address

• Billing Address

• Email Address

We will ensure that any personal data we collect is accurate, complete, protected and reliable for its intended use. If you wish to update your personal data, you may edit your information online, or contact us by email or phone, and we will assist you.

**Data Disclosure**

We never pass on your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. Data that is forward to third parties such as shipping agents only use in connection with the delivery of goods. Apart from where you have consented or disclosure is necessary to achieve the purpose for which it was submitted, personal information might be disclosed in certain situations where we believe in good faith that the law requires disclosure.

**Security**

In order to protect your personal data submitted to our site from loss and unauthorized access, we docontinually adapt our security measures in line with current technologies and our systems. Besides, our employees who provide services related to our information systems are obliged to respect the confidentiality of any personal information held by us.

**Cookies**

Cookies are data that is saved during subsequent visits to the sitethat can be retrieved from your computer or mobile device. We used third-party cookies to collect statistics from analytics and advertising party, such as Google Analytics, Google Adwords. If you do not want your personal datato be collected through the use of Cookies, you may do the necessary setting for Cookie feature at the web browser. This data may include:

• Your computer's IP address

• The pages within Ezytred that you visit

• The time spent on those pages, items and information searched for on our site, access times and dates, and other statistics

**Changes to the Privacy Policy**

[www.ezytred.com](http://www.ezytred.com/) reserves the rights to modify the privacy policy statement at any time. You may review our privacy policy statement time to time to keep you update on the policy change.
