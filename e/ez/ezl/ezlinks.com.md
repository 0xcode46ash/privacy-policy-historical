# Privacy Statement

**Privacy Statement for**  [www.ezlinks.com](http://www.ezlinks.com/)   


EZLinks Golf, Inc. has created this statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: www.ezlinks.com. 

Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information.

Our site's registration form requires customers to provide us with contact information (i.e. name, email address), and financial information (i.e. account or credit card number).  We use customer contact information from the registration form to send the person information about our company and promotional material from some of our partners. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the customer when necessary. Financial information that is collected is used to check the customers' qualifications for registration and to bill the customer for products and services when applicable. A customer's financial information is disclosed ONLY to the company that is providing EZLinks' credit card processing service, or the golf course operator should a golfer or group of golfers not fulfill their reservation commitment(s).  A customer's financial information is not disclosed to any other entity.

This site may contain links to other sites. **http://www.ezlinks.com** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such sites. 

Our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We may collect customers' contact information (i.e. email address) and financial information (i.e. account or credit card numbers). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders to our customers. The customer's contact information is also used to get in touch with the customer when necessary. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the customer for products and services.

We periodically run contests on our site in which we ask visitors for contact information (i.e. email address). We use contact data from our contests to send customers promotional material from some of our partners. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the customer when necessary.

We may use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. Ads may contain cookies, and appear to be coming from our site, but in reality are coming from our partners who are serving the ads on our site. Our site does not use cookies. We do not have access to any information about you from cookies you might receive while visiting our site.

_Security_ :

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Our secure certificate is provided by [VeriSign](http://www.verisign.com/).

_Choice/Opt-Out_ :

Customers or visitors can choose to completely remove all information about themselves from the EZLinks database.  Please understand that once this step is taken, EZLinks cannot recover the data.  If you choose to use any of EZLinks' services that require you to provide information to EZLinks, you will have to reenter ALL data; including address, credit card information, etc.  This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database. 

  1. Send email to [remove@ezlinks.com](mailto:remove@ezlinks.com)
  2. Call 888-775-4657 



_Correct/Update_ :

This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided. 

  1. Send email [info@ezlinks.com](mailto:info@ezlinks.com)
  2. Call 888-775-4657
