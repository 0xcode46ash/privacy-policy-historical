# Eish SA - South Africa's favourite clicktory!

|  Privacy Policy 

* * *  
  
---  
  
Please read our Privacy Policy and by continuing to use Eish SA, you agree to abide by the terms of this document. 

Eish SA is committed to directing you to the website/websites, of your choice, for your individual/business needs. Eish SA recognises the importance of protecting the privacy of personal/business information collected about our users and or advertisers.

If you do not agree to the Eish SA privacy policy, please discontinue to use our service, however if you continue to use Eish SA, we will accept that you do agree to what is stated in this document.

**Your personal** **information  
**  
Eish SA collects information from you for the various services we have to offer on this website. Your name and email address will be required from you in order for you to register for certain of those services. 

When you are asked by Eish SA to submit your personal information, you are sharing that information with Eish SA only and we regard that information as priveleged and private. 

 **How we use any collected information** **  
**  
We collect your information to ensure our users a more informational online experience and thus informing them of the services you have to offer.

Eish SA publishes advertisements on behalf of advertisers and the information we have collected from our advertisers assists us in reaching their target market.

**Links to other websites**

Eish SA users should be aware that  you will be directed to other websites that are beyond our control. There are links to other websites from Eish SA pages that take you outside of our service. This will happen when you click on a banner advertisement, an informational link and a category link. These websites are not part of Eish SA and if you choose to visit any of the formentioned websites you do so at your own risk. 

 **E-mail**  

Eish SA will use the e-mail address provided when you register for the use of our services. Eish SA will send promotional e-mails/letters to its users and its advertisers.

 **Pornography**

Eish SA will not accept or include any informational links, advetisements or website links of a pornographic nature.  

By continuing to use Eish SA you agree to the terms of this document.

For any queries regarding our privacy policy please do not hesitate to contact us on: [info@eishsa.co.za](mailto:info@eishsa.co.za)

Thank you.
