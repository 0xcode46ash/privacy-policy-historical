# E-DIARY - Favourite events at your fingertips! - Privacy

**Privacy Policy** **1\. PRIVACY POLICY STATEMENT**

> We take the privacy and security of our customer information very seriously, and we are committed to safeguarding your privacy online.
> 
> Our strict E-CAL contact standards ensure only that relevant and specific information is communicated to our customers.
> 
> E-CAL is owned and managed by E-DIARY Pty Ltd (EPL). EPL is bound by the Spam Act 2003 and the National Privacy Principles (NPP) (Australia) set out in the Privacy Act 1998 (Commonwealth) (the "Act"), EPL will only collect, use or disclose personal information about you in accordance with the Act and our strict standards. Please read the following to understand how your personal information will be treated as you use E-CAL. For a copy of this statement email [support@e-diary.com](mailto:support@e-diary.com)

**2\. PERSONAL INFORMATION**

> "Personal information" means the information or an opinion (including information or an opinion forming part of a database), whether true or not, and whether recorded in a material form or not, about a person whose identity is apparent, or can be reasonably ascertained, from the information or opinion.

**3\. INFORMATION GATHERING  
 **  
\- IP ADDRESS

> We may gather your IP address to help diagnose problems with our service, and to gather broad user information.

\- INFORMATION SUBMITTED IN REGISTRATION AND PREFERENCES

> We require you to register in order to access the E-CAL service, and we store this information securely. We aim to improve the service we offer, and this information allows us to tailor our service to best suit you. 
> 
> The E-CAL publisher(s) to which you subscribe have access to your information, and if you have provided consent, they may contact you with additional communications suiting your profile. Participation is voluntary, and you will only be contacted in accordance with the law, and subject to our strict privacy and contact standards.

**4\. CONSENT**

> You may provide consent to receive additional communications suiting your profile by checking the appropriate 'opt-in' box on E-CAL pages and applications.

**5\. DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION TO THIRD PARTIES**

> We recognise that some of the information you supply may be personal information. We will not disclose your personal information to a third party except as described in this privacy statement or where this is otherwise consistent with the law.

**6\. DESTRUCTION OF YOUR INFORMATION**

> We will take reasonable steps to destroy or de-identify any personal information about you which we hold, when it is no longer needed by us.

**7\. ACCESS AND UPDATING**

> We recognise that you may want to update your personal information. We will provide you with access to that information in accordance with the Act. If you wish to access or update your information, please email [support@e-diary.com](mailto:support@e-diary.com)

**8\. SECURITY**

> We will take reasonable steps to protect personal information we collect from you from misuse, loss or unauthorised access, modification or disclosure.

**9\. CONTACTING US**

> If you have any questions about this privacy statement, or your dealings with us, please email [support@e-diary.com](mailto:support@e-diary.com)
