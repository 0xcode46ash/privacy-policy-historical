# Trade WoW Accounts Buy Sell - EpicNPC - Privacy Policy

EpicNPC respects your privacy. This page explains our policy relating to the personal data that is received by us.

**Information Collected**  
EpicNPC forum software automatically records information when you visit our website, including your IP address. Additionally, at the time of registration, we retain data on your email address and any other information that you might submit to us which is optional. We also use cookies to store your account information for a convenient browsing experience. 

Information collected during verification will only be released to an outside source if accompanied by a subpoena. Location information such as the city and country members reside in is available to all members. The name of the internet service provide (ISP) is also shown, but IP address remains private.

**Uses**  
Under limited circumstances we may share personal information with third parties as well, including when complying with legal matters and also when ensuring the security of our site and related services. EpicNPC reserves the right to release information publicly if a scam is reported against a user.

**Your Choices**  
Personal information fields in your profile, outside of the necessary information to process and approve your account, are entirely optional and at your discretion to provide. EpicNPC reserves the right to keep any information and images posted by it's members on it's website.

For more information on privacy concerns, please email us by using the 'Contact Us' page below.
