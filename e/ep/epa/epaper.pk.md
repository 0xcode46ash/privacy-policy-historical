# Privacy Policy

Today is Monday, April 4, 2016

Epaper.pk cares about how your personal information is used and shared, and we take your privacy seriously. Please read the following terms to learn more about our privacy policy. By visiting and registering at Epaper.pk.com portal you are accepting the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy. 

  * We will never sell your personal information, or use it for any other purpose other than logging in/out, demographics for our knowledge, and any voluntary feature of the site.
  * We use cookies with minimal information in them. For Example we use cookies set to remember your preferences when you visit, i.e favorite tabs, location etc
  * We use Google Analytics, which is covered by [Google's Privacy policy](http://www.google.com/intl/en/privacy.html)
  * We serve ads that have their own privacy policy. You can click on the ad button that usually appears alongside the banner (blue triangle) to see more details that is covered by the ad-placement agency, over which we do not have any sort of control neither do we have access.
  * We do not allow registration as we do not have any active profiles database at this moment.



**In details**

  1. **What does Epaper.pk Privacy Policy Covers?**  
We do no collect any information whatsoever regarding you including name, location, email or anything related since we do no have any active registration system as of today.
