# Easy WebContent - Privacy Policy

### Easy WebContent Privacy Statement 

Your privacy is important to us.   
If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should contact Easy WebContent by email at [orders@easywebcontent.com ](mailto:orders@easywebcontent.com)

###### The Gist of our Privacy Policy: 

  * \- We respect your privacy and it is important to us. 
  * \- Your information will not be distributed sold or shared outside of our organization or our intermediary secure transaction processors. 
  * \- We may once in a while contact you directly to discuss our services or partners.
  * \- You have the ability to opt out of our marketing by unsubscribing via link at bottom of our newsletters.



###### In Detail: 

Easy WebContent Privacy Statement   
At Easy WebContent we are committed to protecting your privacy. Because we gather important information from our visitors and customers, we have established this Statement as a means to communicate our information gathering and dissemination practices. We reserve the right to modify or update this Statement at anytime.   


Collected Information On Web Site:   
We require customers who register to use the services ("Services") offered on our Site to give us contact information, such as their first name, last name, company name, address, phone number, and e-mail address, and billing information, such as billing name and address, credit card number. At the time you express interest in attaining additional information, or when you register for the service, we may also ask for additional personal information, such as your title, fax number, or additional company information, such as number of employees, or your industry. Customers can opt out of providing such additional information by not entering it when asked. This applies to non-required field in our registration/contact forms. 

Easy WebContent uses the information that we collect to set register and set-up Service(s) for individuals and their organizations. We may also use the information to contact customers to further discuss additional services and/or special offers and events at our company that may further interest or benefit you. Customers may be invited to receive an email newsletter by providing an email address. Customer email addresses will not be distributed or shared with third parties. Customers can opt out of being contacted by us, or receiving such information from us, at any time by sending an email to [orders@easywebcontent.com](mailto:orders@easywebcontent.com). 

We may also request your e-mail address or mailing address for purposes of adding you to our mailing list, or by your request to have someone contact you for additional information about our service, jobs, or partner opportunities. Whenever we request the identity of any visitor, we will first indicate the purpose of the inquiry before the information is requested or sent to us. 

Easy WebContent always maintains a very strict "No Spam" policy. This means that means we do not intend to sell, rent, or otherwise give your e-mail address to a third-party, without your consent. 

Customers are also asked to provide an email address when registering for the Easy WebContent, in order to receive a username and password for their account. We may also email information regarding updates to the Easy WebContent or within our company, and may send a Customer Newsletter. 

All financial and billing information that we collect through the Site is used solely to check the qualifications of prospective customers and to bill for our Services. This billing information is not used by Easy WebContent for marketing or promotional purposes. Easy WebContent uses a third-party intermediary to manage the credit card processing. This intermediary is solely a link in the distribution chain, and is not permitted to store, retain, or use the information provided, except for the sole purpose of the credit card processing and/or recurring transactions related to your subscription with us. In addition we may consider using Paypal or Google Checkout as payment option where they are solely used to process and collect payment for our services. Other third parties, such as content providers, may provide content on the Easy WebContent web Site but they are not permitted to collect any information, and Easy WebContent does not share any user information with these parties. 

Customers who have registered for the Easy WebContent service may be using the Site to host data and information ("Data"). Easy WebContent will not review, share, distribute, print, or reference any such Data except as provided in the Easy WebContent Terms of Use, or as may be required by law. Individual records may at times be viewed or accessed only for the purpose of resolving a problem, support issue, or suspected violation of the Easy WebContent Terms of Use, or as may be required by law. Of course, customers are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality and security of their user registration and password. 

Easy WebContent may also collect certain information from visitors to and customers of the Site, such as Internet addresses, and IP addresses. This information may be logged to help us diagnose and pinpoint any technical problems, and to administer our Web Site in order to constantly improve the quality of the Service provided. We may also track and analyze non-identifying and aggregate usage and volume statistical information from our visitors and our customers and provide such information to third parties. 

Security   
Our Site has security measures in place to help protect against the loss, misuse, and alteration of the Data under our control. When our Site is accessed using Firefox, Netscape Navigator, or Microsoft Internet Explorer versions 5.5 or higher, Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology protects information using both server authentication and data encryption to help ensure that Data is safe, secure, and available only to you. Easy WebContent also implements an advanced security method based on dynamic data and encoded session identifications, and hosts the Site in a secure data center environment that uses a firewall and other advanced technology to prevent interference or access from outside intruders. In addition, Easy WebContent provides unique user names and passwords that must be entered each time a customer logs on to access their account. Lastly, the Easy WebContent system utilizes an auto-log out option where after a certain period of inactivity a session is ended and user must login to access account. Such safeguards help minimize and potentially prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of Data.   


What are Cookies and what about Cookies?   
Cookies are small pieces of data that are transferred to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser from our Web Server. A cookie cannot read data from your hard disk or read cookie files that may have been created from other sites. The Easy WebContent, Inc. website utilizes cookies as a means of providing personalization features to our visitors. For example, we utilize cookies to remember settings that you may have made at our website. Standing alone, cookies do not identify you personally. They merely recognize your browser. 

You can choose whether to accept cookies by changing the settings of your browser. Typically, by accessing the browser's help feature you can obtain information on how to prevent your browser from accepting all cookies or to notify you when a cookie is being sent. If you choose not to accept these cookies, your experience at our website and other web sites may be diminished and some features may not work as intended. 

Third-Party Sites   
The Site may contain links to other web sites. Easy WebContent is not in any way responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these other web sites. Customers and visitors will need to check the policy statement of these others web sites to understand their policies. Customers and visitors who access a linked site may be disclosing their private information. It is the responsibility of the user to keep such information private and confidential. 

Opt-Out Policy   
Easy WebContent offers its visitors and customers a means to choose how we may use information provided. If, at any time after registering for information or ordering Services, you change your mind about receiving information from us, you can opt-out of the mailing list via the unsubscribe link at the bottom of the email newsletter sent to you. 

Correcting & Updating Your Information   
If you are a customer and need to correct or update your information, please login to your Easy WebContent account and within your control panel you can update your billing, contact, and company information. It is the customers' full responsibility to keep their information up to date from their Account area. 

To discontinue the Easy WebContent Service you must cancel service through your Easy WebContent control panel. You can login at [www.moreedits.com](http://www.moreedits.com/)
