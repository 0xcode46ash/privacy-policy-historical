**Privacy:**  
EasyWeddings.com.au is committed to protecting your privacy. We are also committed to providing you with the very best experience. For us to enhance your experience at our site we must gather certain personal information about you that helps us customise our content to your tastes and preferences. 

**Your information:**  
EasyWeddings.com.au collects information in several ways from different parts of our Web site. EasyWeddings.com.au may collect some personal information when you initially access our site. We may ask for your name, e-mail address and details referring to your wedding. The more information you share with us, the better we are able to enhance your experience on our site.

**How we collect your information:**  
EasyWeddings.com.au uses some "Cookies" to track and record your preferences and activity on our Web site. Cookies are small bits of data that are sent to your browser and stored on your computer's hard drive, they are updated occasionally as you access the many different parts of our Web site.

EasyWeddings.com.au records details regarding your wedding and guests of your wedding, this information is stored within secure databases. We also record IP addresses when you participate in either the forum or use the planning tools, to ensure site content is always of a high standard and if any malicious content is recorded we can then ban certain individuals. 

Some advertisers or Web sites that have links on our site may collect personally identifiable information about you when you access or utilise those links. EasyWeddings.com.au does not control the collection or use of such information, and the practices of those advertisers are not included in this Privacy Policy. 

**What do we do with your information:**  
The information we collect may be used to provide such capabilities as personalised services. We may occasionally contact you with information regarding special updates at EasyWeddings.com.au. 

**Sharing of your information:**  
EasyWeddings.com.au does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with others, unless required to do so by law. 

You Acknowledge that by using our Web site, you agree to this Disclaimer and Privacy statement. 

This policy may change from time to time so please revisit this page occasionally.

We hope you enjoy your experience using EasyWeddings.com.au and good luck with all the planning to come.
