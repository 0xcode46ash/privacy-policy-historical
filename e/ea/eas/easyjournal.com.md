# Easyjournal - Privacy Policy

[](https://web.archive.org/register/type/default.asp) |  |  |

| 

Please note that this policy may be occasionally updated. Return to this page to check for modifications. 

Easyjournal will not sell, rent, or redistribute your personally identifiable information to anyone, unless one or more of the following criteria are met: 

  1. We have your explicit consent to share the information.
  2. We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested.
  3. We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process.
  4. We find that your actions on our web site violate the privacy or personal rights of other Easyjournal users.

If you subscribe to Easyjournal PRO, Easyjournal will record limited transactional details (e.g. transaction ID) but will not use this information for any other transaction without your prior knowledge and/or explicit consent. Transactional data are stored and transmitted for billing and bookkeeping uses only. Easyjournal does share transactional information with third party payment networks for processing purposes only. These payment networks require transactional details (e.g. name, credit card number, address) in order to process purchases made on the Easyjournal website. 

Easyjournal will occasionally share general site usage information with partners. Such shared information will not contain any personal data. Shared information may contain number of users, user regions, users per hour, most accessed areas, and/or other types of general user information.
