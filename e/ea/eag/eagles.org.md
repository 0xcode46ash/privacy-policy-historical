Privacy Policy

**THIS IS A SECURE SITE  
USE OF YOUR CREDIT CARD IS STRICTLY PROTECTED FROM  MISUSE.**

We are very strongly committed to our supporter’s privacy, and we have established important procedures in our privacy policy to protect the information you share with us. 

The only information that we collect and retain in our off line secure database is your name, mailing address, e-mail address and giving history. This information is used to provide our members with newsletters and updates about current eagle issues. 

We use up-to-date encryption technology to safeguard your private information during electronic commerce transactions. 

When you provide your credit card number through our secure server your number is only used once and then the information is erased from our system. 

We will NEVER redistribute your credit card information to ANY third party. 

We will NEVER reveal, sell, give away or trade your personal information (name, address, phone number, email, etc.) to ANY external organization that is not directly related to your transaction, unless authorized by you or required by law. 

If you wish to review or correct information collected by us please either call the American Eagle Foundation office at 865-429-0157 or write American Eagle Foundation, P.O. Box 333, Pigeon Forge, Tennessee 37868 or e-mail us at eaglemail@eagles.org 

Your privacy is our priority. We will always conduct business in a manner consistent with our promise to keep your information secure, using it only for purposes of processing your membership, donation or product order.
