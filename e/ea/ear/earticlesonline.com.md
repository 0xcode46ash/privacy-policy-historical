# eArticlesOnline.com Privacy Policy

Your Article Directory Privacy Policy

### _New User / Opt-In Emails_

Your email is used to verify you as a new user by sending you an email you have to click on a link to verify you are you. You may opt-in to get reports on new articles, etc, whenever needed.

###  _Links_

This site, and in particular articles, contains links to other sites. eArticlesOnline.com is not responsible for anything dealing with these other web sites.

###  _Cookies_

A cookie is a small harmless piece of data stored on your computer to help us track you on the site when signed in. No personal information is contained in it.

###  _Information Sharing_

We will never share, sell, give, lease, rent, or in any way give you information to any third parties. Information will only be disclosed due to law demands by a court.

###  _Legal Disclaimer_

Information will only be disclosed when ordered by the law or court.
