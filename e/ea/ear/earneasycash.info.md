_We do not collect personally identifiable off-line contact information. We do not distribute to third parties any personally identifiable information. Advertisers do not have access to view any of your account information or email address._  
**Information Collected**  
EarnEasyCash has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. EarnEasyCash uses your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information which we use to send targetted ads based on your country of residence. EarnEasyCash's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like name and e-mail address). EarnEasyCash sends only site news and announcements to your registered email address. You may opt-in to receive paid advertisements to your email through the member account menu. You may opt-out of site news emailings by deleting your account or requesting your account to be deleted.  
All profile data such as Sex, Date of Birth, etc is completely optional and does not affect site experience or earnings.

**Other Sites**  
_This site contains links to other sites._  
EarnEasyCash is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. EarnEasyCash is not responsible for the information you choose to disclose at other websites.

**Public Forums:**  
EarnEasyCash uses a [forum](http://www.eecash.info/) to facilitate customer response and support. The forum requires a seperate registration and it is optional. Be cautious when disclosing or posting information in a pubic forum because it becomes publicly viewable.

**Security:**  
EarnEasyCash has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. Some personal information such as passwords are encrypted.

**Children's Guidelines:**  
EarnEasyCash does not accept memberships from users under 13 therefore EarnEasyCash does not collect any personal information of minors/children.

**Choice/Opt-Out:**  
EarnEasyCash provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us. You may either delete your account from your account menu or you may also [Contact Us](http://www.earneasycash.info/index.php?view=contact) and request your membership and account information to be deleted at any time. If you request an account deletion through email, please allow a reasonable amount of time before deletion. Upon deletion, all of your information is deleted from the database. No information is saved.

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with EarnEasyCash, you can [Contact Us](http://www.earneasycash.info/index.php?view=contact).
