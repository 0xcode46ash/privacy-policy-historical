# Privacy Policy | Earth Trekkers

### Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to Earth Trekkers. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that we collect and the ways in which we use that personal information. By visiting our website, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy forms part of our [Terms and Conditions of Use](https://www.earthtrekkers.com/terms-of-use/) document.

#### Collection of your Information

We collect your information when you voluntarily enter it into our website. This can include signing up for our newsletter, making a purchase, commenting on a post, or otherwise entering information on our website. **Â **

#### Use of Your Information

We use your information when answering comments on posts, sending out emails or our newsletter, and for providing our services.

We will not use or disclose information that could be used to identify an individual member in any circumstances except to ensure the proper functioning of our business and the website, to communicate promotional offers and special events to you, to allow our trusted third parties, including our advertising partners, to communicate promotional and special offers to you, and where the law requires us to do so.

#### Securing Your Information

Please note that no information transmitted over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure. However, we will protect your personal information as best as possible but we cannot guarantee the security of any information that you transmit to us, or receive from us. The transmission and exchange of information is carried out at your own risk.

#### Links to Third Party Websites

This website contains content with links to third party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies of these websites. We have no liability for the content and information on these third party websites.

#### Use of Cookies

Like most websites, we use cookies. Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored on your computer. They are designed to record your browsing history, log in information, and your web surfing history. Personal information is not collected by cookies, however, if you previously provided personal information then cookies may be linked to this information.

In some cases, we use third-party advertising companies to show you ads when you visit our website. These companies may use your stored cookies to provide you advertisements about goods and services that are of an interest to you.

You can disable the use of cookies by changing the setting in your web browser.

#### Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@earthtrekkers.com.
