# Privacy Policy | Email Sender,Email Marketing,Email Marketing Software

At EmailSender we take security seriously. We respect your privacy too. We work hard to store your information securely. We have no direct relationship with the individuals whose personal data our system processes. An individual who seeks access or seeks to correct, amend, or delete inaccurate data should direct his query to us using the [contact](http://www.emailsender.org/contact/ "contact") form. If the Client requests EmailSender to remove the data, we will respond to their request immediately. We will retain personal data we process on behalf of our Customers for as long as needed to provide services to them. We will retain and use this personal information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

**

What information do we collect?

**

We really have no control over what information you are asked to supply.

What information to provide is determined by our customers .

However, in general, this information usually include name, email addresses,addresses,telephone,etc,etc

**

As a subscriber on our customers’ email list

**

Be reassured that your information is securely stored.

We do not contact you.

We do not sell or share your information with anyone else.

**

As our customer

**

Your subscribers information is secured with us

We do not contact them.

We do not sell or share your information with anyone else.

please use the contact form above to do so.
