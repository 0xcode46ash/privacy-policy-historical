# Privacy Statement | eMagazines Marketing Insight

## Privacy Statement for eMagazines.com

eMagazines.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: [eMagazines.com](https://web.archive.org/)

**BY ACCESSING THIS SITE YOU ARE ACCEPTING THE TERMS OF THIS PRIVACY STATEMENT.**

## Email Communications

When you register or order at eMagazines we send you a courtesy confirmation. 

All communications will contain simple removal instructions. 

## Information Automatically Logged

We use your IP address to help identify you. 

## Order Forms

Our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect contact information (like your email address), and demographic information (like your zip code). 

Contact information from the order forms is used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary. 

Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. 

## External Links

This site contains links to other sites. eMagazines.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

## Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. This site is tested and certified daily by hackersafe.com to pass the FBI/SANS Internet Security Test. 

## Choice/Opt-Out

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving promotional/marketing information from our partners, and us. 

You can send opt out requests via email to suppportticket[at]emagazines.com. 

## Data Quality/Access

This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided: 

You can send email to suppportticket[at]emagazines.com.
