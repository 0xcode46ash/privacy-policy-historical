# Privacy Statement

Emergency Reporting respects your privacy and takes great care to safeguard information in our possession. Emergency Reporting does not share customer information without permission, unless we are legally required to. We restrict access to your information to those individuals who need it to provide you with services. We use your information to support and enhance our business relationship. For instance, to set-up your account, provide service and support, and share service and company news with you. We do not sell your personal information. Any third-party companies we must use to provide you with services are required to keep your information secure and confidential. 

For more information about our privacy policy, call us toll free at: 1-866-773-7678 or by email at [info@emergencyreporting.com](mailto:info@emergencyreporting.com).
