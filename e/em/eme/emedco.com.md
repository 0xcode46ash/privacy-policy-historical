# Emedco.com Privacy Policy

## Privacy

Emedco.com strives to protect and respect the privacy of its visitors. We are dedicated to maximizing your on-line experience while ensuring your personal data is never compromised.

## Cookies

Emedco.com may use & "cookie" technology to obtain non-personal information from on-line visitors in order to improve their on-line experience. A “cookie” is an element of data that is sent to your browser, which may then be stored on your hard drive so we can recognize you when you return.

## Personal Data

Emedco.com only gathers personally identifiable data, such as names, addresses, e-mail addresses, etc., when voluntarily submitted by a visitor. For example, personally identifiable information may be collected in order to fulfill an order, respond to a request for literature, or to download demonstration software. This information is used only by Emedco.com or its partners who are involved in the operation of these sites for internal purposes.

Emedco also sometimes makes available our mailing list to other companies who might be of interest to our customers. If you would prefer not to receive information from these companies, please let us know.

Emedco.com does not rent, sell or share e-mail addresses. E-mail addresses are used for routine business communications between Emedco and our customers, and to send product and information updates to those customers who have requested to receive them.

Emedco reserves the right to change this policy at any time. Be sure to check this page periodically for changes.
