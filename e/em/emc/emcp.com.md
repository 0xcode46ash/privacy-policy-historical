# Privacy Policy | EMC Publishing, LLC

We value our customers and promise that we will keep your personal information confidential. We collect information about our customers only to conduct business and offer products and services that might be of interest to you. Your account information is not shared with companies nor sold to any organizations outside of EMC/Paradigm/JIST. Please notify us if you wish to be removed from our email campaigns.
