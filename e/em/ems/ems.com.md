# Privacy & Security : Eastern Mountain Sports (EMS)

[Customer Service](http://www.ems.com/customer/customer_service.jsp?FOLDER%3C%3Efolder_id=1408474396003267) > **Privacy & Security**

## Collecting Personal Information

When you place an online order we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing address, credit card number, and the card's expiration date. This information allows us to process your order, and to notify you of your order status.  
\- When you make a credit card purchase in one of our stores, we need to know your credit card number, and the card's expiration date.  
\- When you sign up for our e-mail newsletter, we need only your e-mail address.  
\- When you sign up to receive notice about our sales or store events through the mail, we need your name and mailing address.  
\- When you enter a contest or provide information for other promotional events, we will ask for the information needed to administer the contest and notify the winner.Return to top

## Saving Personal Information

When you register online with EMS, we give you the option of saving your personal account information so you don't have to enter it each time you visit our site.

To serve you better, we may combine information you give us online and in our stores about your shopping activity and product interests. We may use this information to enhance and personalize your shopping experience with us, and provide you with service and products tailored to your specific needs and interests. You may also receive periodic mailings from us about new products, services, discounts, or special promotions or events as a result of purchasing online, or providing your phone number at the register. If you do not want to receive such mailings, please call 888-463-6367, e-mail us, or copy your mailing label and customer number with the letters "DNM", and mail to: EMS, Mailing Preferences, One Vose Farm Rd., Peterborough, NH 03458.Return to top

We occasionally provide your name and postal address (but not your e-mail address) to a select group of companies whose products you may find of interest. If you do not want us to share your information with other companies, please call us at 888-463-6367, [click here](mailto:customerservice@ems.com), or copy your mailing label and customer number with the letters "DNR", and mail to:  
EMS, Mailing Preferences  
One Vose Farm Rd.  
Peterborough, NH 03458.

On rare occasions, we may also have to provide information to a third party:  
\- When we believe in good faith that the law requires it (i.e. when someone is suspected of being involved in illegal online activities).  
\- To protect the rights or property of EMS or its affiliates.  
\- Under limited circumstances specifically described to you when we collect the information, such as in the rules of sweepstakes or other promotions.

## Site Security

At EMS, we promise that your information is secure. Our Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software is the industry standard and among the best software available today for secure online commerce transactions. It encrypts all of your personal information, including credit card number, name, and address, so that it cannot be read as the information travels over the Internet. We are also a VeriSign site; click on the VeriSign logo to verify our membership and commitment to your security. Most browsers will show a lock icon in the bottom status bar on secure pages that ask for your name, password, address, or credit card.Return to top

## What's A "Cookie"?

A cookie is a small file stored on your computer by a web site to give you a unique ID. A cookie generated by our site contains no information about you. It contains only a numeric ID. 

EMS.com uses cookies to  
\- Allow you to retrieve your shopping cart on your next visit, as long as it is within a 12 hour period.  
\- Estimate the number of shoppers on our site.  
\- Determine the browser our visitors use so the site can be designed to work properly with the most common browsers. provide aggregated, statistical user information to third parties without releasing individual information. For example, we may disclose that "30% of our customers have bought hiking boots" in order to describe our site or services to trading partners, advertisers, or other third parties.Return to top

## Banner Network Services

EMS uses the services of several banner networks which send advertising in the form of an advertising banner to web surfers that have accessed the EMS website. In order to provide this service to EMS, these networks may use "cookies," beacons, and other technologies to collect anonymous and non-personally identifiable information which may include your web surfer's IP address, web pages which have been viewed by a web surfer, date and time, domain type, and responses by a web surfer to an advertisement delivered by us. We use different types of technologies to collect this type of information, including cookies and web beacons.
