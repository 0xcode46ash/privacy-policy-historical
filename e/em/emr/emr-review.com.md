# Privacy Policy

Gibraltar IT LLC (Gibraltar) collects email addresses of people who send us email and who sign-up for our services (the “Service”). Such information may contain personal data about you including your email address, name, address, phone numbers, credit card numbers, etc. We will not disclose such personal information without your permission. However, certain information collected from you and about you is used within the context of providing the Service. The information we collect is not shared with or sold to others except under the circumstances below, and your use of the Service is deemed to provide us permission to disclose the following:

  * In order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Gibraltar’s terms of service, or as otherwise required by law; 
  * In order to provide the products or services you've requested; 
  * If Gibraltar is are acquired by or merged with another company, we will transfer information about you to the new owner entity. In this event, Gibraltar will notify you by email or by putting a prominent notice on the Gibraltar web site before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy. 



## Limitations

This privacy policy only addresses the use and disclosure of information we collect from you. Although you may interact with software suppliers as a result of visiting our site, or link to other sites or third parties through use of our website, Gibraltar are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties or the content of linked sites. We encourage you to read the posted privacy policy whenever interacting with any website. In addition, please be aware that despite our best intentions and the guidelines outlined in this privacy policy, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## What we do with your information

Gibraltar uses the information we collect for the following general purposes: products and services provision, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research.

## Security

The security of your personal information is important to us. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on our Web site, you can send email to us at [info@gibraltarit.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20140628114807id_/http%3A//gibraltarit.com/policies/info@gibraltarit.com)

## Disclosure

By using the Service, you acknowledge that Gibraltar IT LLC may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with court orders requiring us to do so or when your actions violate the Terms of Service. Gibraltar may also include a screen shot of any site using its service to advertise its service or include as part of a gallery of sites using Gibraltar.

## Data Storage

Gibraltar owns the data storage, databases and all rights to the Gibraltar application however we make no claim to rights in your data.

## Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. Gibraltar uses cookies to record current session information and Remember Me functionality. Gibraltar requires you to log in to your account after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing the content of your account. Session cookies are removed upon termination of session while Remember Me cookies remain until cleared by the user or browser.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy statement at any time, so please review it frequently. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you here or by means of a notice on our homepage so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it.
