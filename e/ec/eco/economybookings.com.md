# www.economybookings.com Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

www.economybookings.com (hereinafter referred to as "we") is committed to protecting your privacy online. You are important to us, and we will take special care to maintain your trust. In order to fully understand your rights, we encourage you to read this Privacy Policy. Please direct questions regarding this Policy to the Administrator of this website.

**When does www.economybookings.com collect my personal information?**

www.economybookings.com gathers information from users in three ways: voluntary registration, cookie technology and IP addresses. 

Voluntary Registration

When you register with www.economybookings.com we ask for certain necessary personal information. Other specific services may have additional registration requirements and may require you to provide additional information. 

Cookies

www.economybookings.com uses cookies to store and track your preferences to provide you with a more personalized website. 

IP Address

www.economybookings.com may also log IP addresses for the purposes of systems administration or to report information in aggregate form to our advertisers and other sponsors.

If you register with our site, from time to time, www.economybookings.com may email you with informative messages about our services or third party products and services we believe may be of interest to you.

**Whom does www.economybookings.com share information with?** ****

www.economybookings.com may share user information with specific third parties for the purpose of providing additional services. In particular, www.economybookings.com has several service partners who provide services or products associated and as part of such operations, these companies may have access to your personal information. In addition, www.economybookings.com also discloses aggregated user statistics in order to describe our services and reach to prospective partners, advertisers and other third parties. However aggregate data is not traceable to you specifically. Except for these situations, we will not disclose your personal information to any third parties except to the extent necessary to provide you with a requested service, or under circumstances required by law. We will disclose your personal information when we believe in good faith that any applicable law, regulation or legal process requires it, or where we believe disclosure is necessary to protect or enforce our rights or the rights of another user. 

Specific uses of personal information that vary from this Privacy Policy (if any) may be found in other areas of the www.economybookings.com-website or privacy policies applicable to that particular service. To the extent that you are notified of uses of your personal information which are different from those described in this Privacy Policy during your enrollment in any particular service, contest or promotion, the uses of which you are specifically notified will govern that service, sweepstakes, contest or promotion. Please note, however, that we do not control the privacy policies of our advertisers and other third-party sites to which we provide banner ads and links. If you click-through a banner ad or link and submit your information to an advertiser, you will be subject to their own privacy policies. www.economybookings.com assumes no responsibility or liability for these third-party policies or the actions of nonaffiliated third parties. 

**What else should I know about my privacy?**

You should be aware that if you voluntarily disclose personal information online in a public area, that information may be collected and used by others. We do not control the actions of our visitors and registered users. If you disclose personal information in a publicly accessible place in the www.economybookings.com Site, you do so at your own risk and you understand that your submitted information may be collected and used by others to send you unsolicited messages, advertisements or for other purposes. 

Please remember that you are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of any password you use to register, and you should always be careful and responsible whenever disclosing information online. 

Although www.economybookings.com uses reasonable efforts to safeguard the privacy of your personal information, transmissions made by means of the Internet cannot be made absolutely secure. www.economybookings.com assumes no liability for disclosure of data due to errors in transmission or unauthorized third parties. 

**Acceptance of Privacy Policy** ****

By using the www.economybookings.com Site and services, you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree or are not comfortable with any policy described in this Privacy Policy, your only remedy is to discontinue use of the www.economybookings.com Site. We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Your continued use of any portion of the www.economybookings.com Site following notification or posting of such changes will constitute your acceptance of those changes. If you have questions or concerns about our specific policies, please send us an email.

**Privacy Policy**

www.economybookings.com respects your privacy and shall not, without your consent, disclose your personal details, which may include, name, address, travel information and all medical conditions that you disclose to us to any other party other than those parties required to have access to those details to provide the Booking and Travel Services to you.
