# Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting ebicycles. Because your privacy is important to us, we've provided this notice describing our personal policies regarding online information practices and how your information is collected and used at this site.

Total EV is committed to zealously protecting the privacy of our users. As a registered respondent, your information is completely confidential. Any information given to us by users is held exclusively by Total EV, and will be used only to confirm registration, conduct product testing, and forward industry news. This information will not be shared with outside parties unless the user gives explicit consent, or mandated by law.
