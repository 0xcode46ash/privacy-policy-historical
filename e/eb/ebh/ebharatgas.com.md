# ebharatgas.com

Bharat Petroleum Corporation Ltd. (BPCL) values the privacy of information pertaining to its ebharatgas.com users. Bharat Petroleum will not use or disclose information to any outside sources about individual visits to the ebharatgas.com site or information that registered users may give, such as name, address, email address or telephone number and personal details.

Bharat Petroleum will not use information provided by registered users of the ebharatgas.com site without their permission and will provide the means for them to manage and control the information that they have provided.
