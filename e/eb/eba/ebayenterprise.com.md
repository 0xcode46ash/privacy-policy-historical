# Privacy at eBay Enterprise | eBay Enterprise

### Privacy at eBay Enterprise

The following privacy statements address our information collection practices with respect to certain types of information that we receive: 

  * [The Web Site Privacy Statement](https://web.archive.org/web/20130625011350id_/http://www.ebayenterprise.com/privacy/web_site_privacy.php) addresses the types of information gathered through this Web site, [www.ebayenterprise.com](https://web.archive.org/), how such information is used, and with whom such information is shared.
  * [The Safe Harbor Privacy Statement](https://web.archive.org/web/20130625011350id_/http://www.ebayenterprise.com/privacy/safe_harbor_privacy.php) addresses how we collect, use, and disclose personally identifiable information that we receive in the United States from the European Union and Switzerland.
