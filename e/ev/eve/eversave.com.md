# Privacy Policy | Eversave

Below you'll find all kinds of Privacy Policy information that lawyers want you to know.  Take a look, and if there's something you don't understand, please don't hesitate to email us via [this link.](http://www.eversave.com/contact-request.jsp?cc=1)

This is an updated Privacy Policy. This new Privacy Policy applies to all users who were notified about this updated Privacy Policy in accordance with the old privacy policy and new users who register on our site on or after the Effective Date. If you do not agree to our practices, please do not register, subscribe, create an account, purchase a Save, or otherwise interact with us at www.eversave.com (or the mobile and touch versions), and any individual, business partner-specific, merchant-specific, city-specific, or other area-specific sites we offer.

**Effective Date: March 22, 2012**      [Click here to view the previous Privacy Policy.](https://web.archive.org/privacy-policy1.jsp)

 

This Eversave.com privacy policy (the "Privacy Policy") is intended to inform you of our policies and practices regarding the collection, use and disclosure of any information you submit to us through www.eversave.com (“Eversave.com”). This includes "Personal Information," which is information about you that is personally identifiable such as your name, e-mail address, mailing address, telephone number, date of birth, billing information, unique referring url and other non-public information that is associated with you, as well as "Anonymous Information," which is information that is not associated with or linked to your Personal Information and does not permit the identification of individual persons. This is an updated Privacy Policy.

**Regarding Children**  
Children under the age of 13 are not permitted to use Eversave.com and we do not intentionally collect or maintain Personal Information from those who are under 13 years old. Protecting the privacy of children is very important to us. Thus, if we obtain actual knowledge that a user is under 13, we will take steps to remove that user's Personal Information permanently from our databases, unless we are legally required to obtain such information. We recommend that children between the ages of 13 and 18 obtain their parent’s permission before submitting information over the internet. By using Eversave.com, you are representing that you are at least 18 years old, or that you are at least 13 years old and have a parent’s or guardian’s permission to use Eversave.com.

 

**Collection of Information**

 

**Personal Information that You Provide**  
“Personal Information” includes information that alone or when in combination with other information may be used to readily identify, contact, or locate you (e.g., your full name, address, telephone number, email address, and location).

We collect Personal Information that you submit to us voluntarily through Eversave.com. We also collect Personal Information that you submit in the process of creating or editing your account and user profile on Eversave.com. For example, our registration and login process requires you to provide us with a valid email address and your zip code. You may also elect to provide us your name and mobile number at that time. In addition, when you link your account to our loyalty program, we collect information about your transactions and redemptions at participating merchants.

When you personalize your profile and use the features of Eversave.com, we will collect any information you voluntarily provide, and we may also request optional information to support your use of Eversave.com, such as your year of birth, gender, mailing address, the age of your children and their gender, home phone number, a password of your choice and other demographic information.

We may also collect information, personal or otherwise, you voluntarily provide during your use of Eversave.com. in the form of the content that you submit, such as photos, reviews, ratings, postings, comments, testimonials and other information you choose to submit within the Eversave.com community.

We may also collect information about you and your friends who use Eversave.com, including from any social network or other site you may have connected from, in order to provide you with a more personalized experience. For instance, we may collect your user ID or profile information that you have permitted to be displayed through Eversave.com in order to display you as a friend or in association with your profile and collections.

When you purchase a Save, you will need to submit your credit or debit card number, expiration date, authentication codes or other payment information so that our service providers can process your payment for the Save and is retained by us to expedite your purchase of future Saves. Your card information is also used in connection with our loyalty program, if you participate, so that you can take advantage of offers made available in the program. This information is not used for other purposes.

When you sign up for, or win, any contests, sweepstakes, or other activities that we make available on Eversave.com or via third parties\vendors, Facebook or other platforms, we will collect the information designated along with such activity, which may include your contact information such as your address and mobile or telephone number.

If you choose to sign up to receive information about products or services that may be of interest to you, we will collect your email address and other related information.

If you are a merchant that is interested in participating in Eversave.com, we will collect your Personal Information when you complete our contact sheet, which information we will store in our database.

 

If you are not currently registered as an Eversave.com user and you click on "Sign in Using Facebook," you will first be asked to enter your Facebook credentials and then be given the option to register and join Eversave.com. You can edit your Facebook privacy settings for Facebook or disconnect this service by visiting the Facebook Application Settings page.  If you choose to use our referral service or buy a Save for a friend, we will ask you for your friend's name and email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email with the gift you bought for them or inviting him or her to visit the site. Eversave.com stores this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email, sending the gift, and tracking the success of our referral program. Your friend may contact us via [this link.](http://www.eversave.com/contact-request.jsp?cc=1) to request that we remove this information from our database.

 

**Personal Information from Other Sources**  
We may receive Personal Information about you from other sources with which you have registered, companies who we have partnered with (collectively, "Partners") or other third parties, including billing processors. We may associate this information with the other Personal Information we have collected about you.

 

**E-mail and E-mail Addresses**  
If you send an e-mail to us or fill out our "Feedback" form through Eversave.com, we will collect your e-mail address and the full content of your e-mail, including attached files, and other information you provide. We may use and display your full name and email address when you send an email notification to a friend through Eversave.com or the social network from which you have connected to Eversave.com (such as in an invitation, or when sharing your content). Additionally, we use your email address to contact you on behalf of your friends (such as when someone sends you a personal message) or notifications from a social network or other website with whom you have registered to receive such notifications. We may use this e-mail address to contact you, for things such as notifications of sales, promotions and other related information. You may indicate your preference to stop receiving further promotional communications as further detailed below.

 

**Information Collected Via Technology**  
As you use Eversave.com, certain information may also be passively collected and stored on our or our service providers’ server logs, including your Internet protocol address, browser type, and operating system, and when you use our mobile sites and apps, certain information including your geographic location, phone information and operating system.

We also use Cookies and navigational data like Uniform Resource Locators (URL) to gather information regarding the date and time of your visit and the solutions and information for which you searched and viewed, or on which of the advertisements displayed on Eversave.com you clicked. This type of information is collected to make Eversave.com more useful to you and to tailor the experience with Eversave.com to meet your special interests and needs.

We also engage in remarketing to market our sites across the web. When a user visits our site, a cookie is dropped on the user. Users with this cookie may be targeted across advertising networks to receive relevant advertisements, including from us. You may opt out of this behavioral ad serving using the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) opt-out page (http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp).

 

An "Internet protocol address" or "IP Address" is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer when you use the Internet. In some cases your IP Address stays the same from browser session to browser session; but if you use a consumer internet access provider, your IP Address probably varies from session to session. For example, we, or our service providers, may track your IP Address when you access Eversave.com to assist with ad targeting.

 

"Cookies" are small pieces of information that a website sends to your computer’s hard drive while you are viewing a website. We may use both session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent Cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience with Eversave.com. Persistent Cookies can be removed by following your Internet browser help file directions. In order to use our services offered through Eversave.com, your web browser must accept Cookies. If you choose to disable Cookies, some aspects of Eversave.com may not work properly, and you may not be able to receive our services.

We may also enable advertisers and ad servers to promote third-party products and/or services by placing advertisements on Eversave.com. These advertisers and ad servers may use Cookies and/or "Web Beacons" (which are usually small, transparent graphic images) in order to monitor information related to served advertisements. Clicking on such advertisements will direct you to the website of a third-party and the advertiser. This Privacy Policy does not cover the privacy practices of any advertisers or ad servers.

 

**Use and Disclosure of Information**  
In general, Personal Information you submit to us is used by us to provide you access to Eversave.com, to improve Eversave.com, to better tailor the features, performance, and support of Eversave.com and to offer you additional information, opportunities, promotions and functionality from us, our partners or our advertisers. Additionally, we do share your content preferences and other information with the social network with which you have connected to Eversave.com, along with those companies and persons you have asked or allowed us to share your information with.

 

We may provide your Personal Information to merchants and third-party service providers who work on behalf of or with us to provide some of the services and features of Eversave.com, including our loyalty program, and to help us and them communicate with you.

 

Examples of such services include sending email, analyzing data, providing marketing assistance, service and administer loyalty programs, processing payments (including credit card payments), providing customer service and running and implementing contests, sweepstakes and other similar activities. We require our third-party service providers to promise not to use such information except as necessary to provide the relevant services to us.

 

We may share some or all of your Personal Information with third party partners who contribute prizes, sponsor or participate in our contests, sweepstakes and similar activities. We may share some or all of your Personal Information (other than credit or debit card information) with Merchants whose Saves you have purchased for their commercial purposes and to provide the Save as well as so they can track your redemption activity and provide customer support . If you do not want us to use or disclose Personal Information collected about you in the manner identified in this Privacy Policy, you should not use Eversave.com.

Additionally, we may create Anonymous Information records from Personal Information by excluding information (such as your name) that would otherwise make the Anonymous Information personally identifiable to you. Generally, we aggregate this information and use it in statistical analysis to help us analyze patterns in the use of Eversave.com. This Privacy Policy does not limit our use or disclosure of any Anonymous Information in any way, and we reserve the right to use and disclose Anonymous Information to our partners, advertisers and other third parties in our discretion.

We may share some or all of your Personal Information with Eversave.com affiliated companies that are under a common control, in which case we will require them to honor this Privacy Policy. We may also share some or all of your Personal Information with unaffiliated companies, such as merchants, for their own marketing and other purposes, but only if you consent to our doing so. We will seek your consent in those instances where we want to share your information with unaffiliated companies for their marketing purposes.

In the event we go through a business transition such as a merger, acquisition by another company, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your Personal Information may be among the assets transferred. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur and are permitted by this Privacy Policy, and that any acquirer of ours or that acquirer’s assets may continue to process your Personal Information as set forth in this Privacy Policy. Your Personal Information will remain subject to this Privacy Policy unless and until changed in accordance with its terms.

 

We may disclose your Personal Information if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to (a) comply with relevant laws or to respond to subpoenas or warrants served on us; or (b) to protect and defend our rights or property, you, or third parties. You hereby consent to us sharing your Personal Information under the circumstances described herein.

 

**The Ability of Others to View Your Information**  
Helping you to protect your information is important to us. It is up to you to make sure you are comfortable with the information you choose to provide us and the information you choose to publish. You understand that when you use Eversave.com, certain information you post or provide through Eversave.com, such as your name, profile, comments, testimonials, and reviews, may be shared with other users and posted on publicly available portions of Eversave.com, including without limitation, social media applications and other public forums in which you choose to participate. Please keep in mind that if you choose to disclose Personal Information when posting comments or other information or content through Eversave.com, this information may become publicly available and may be collected and used by others, including people outside the Eversave.com. We will not have any obligations with respect to any information that you post to parts of Eversave.com available to others, and recommend that you use caution when giving out personal information to others through social media networks or otherwise.

We offer you choices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of your Personal Information. You have a right at any time to stop us from contacting you for marketing purposes. When you receive promotional communications from us, you may indicate a preference to stop receiving further promotional communications from us and you will have the opportunity to "opt-out" by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in the promotional e-mail you receive or by contacting us directly via [this link.](http://www.eversave.com/contact-request.jsp?cc=1)

 

Despite your indicated email marketing preferences, we may send you administrative emails regarding Eversave.com, including, for example, administrative and transactional confirmations, and notices of updates to our Privacy Policy if we choose to provide such notices to you in this manner.

You have the right to request a copy of [and /or have deleted?] the Personal Information that we hold about you. If you would like a copy of some or all of your Personal Information, or to have such Personal Information deleted from our records, please contact us via [this link.](http://www.eversave.com/contact-request.jsp?cc=1). We want to make sure that your information is accurate and up-to-date. You may ask us to correct or remove information which you think is inaccurate. You may also change any of your profile information by editing it in the profile settings page. Please note that we cannot change or delete information in the databases of third parties with whom we have already shared your Personal Information.
