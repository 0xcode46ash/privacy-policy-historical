# Privacy Policy | EveryCarListed.com

All vehicle listing information, including price, mileage, other data, and images, are subject to verification by the seller of the vehicle.

If you have questions about our posted privacy policy, you should contact us by clicking [here](http://www.everycarlisted.com/contact) or contacting us at 903.593.5400.

**Information Collection and Use  
**If you have questions about our posted privacy policy, you should contact us by clicking [here](http://www.everycarlisted.com/contact) or contacting us at 903.593.5400. everycarlisted.com is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others. EveryCarListed.com, which powers this web site, has no liability or responsibility for content on everycarlisted.com or other web sites linked to or from everycarlisted.com.

**Ad Placement  
**On our on-line ad placement form (when available) a user must provide contact information (like name and email address) and financial information (like credit card number, expiration date). This information is used for billing purposes and to fill customer's orders. This contact information is used to get in touch with the user if there is a problem processing the order.

**Links  
**Please be aware that Every Car Listed is not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites. Please read the privacy policies on each individual site.

**Security  
**When asked for sensitive information (such as social security numbers), that information is encrypted and protected. A lock icon on the bottom of Web browsers such as Netscape Navigator and Microsoft Internet Explorer becomes locked while on a secure page.

**Notification of Changes  
**If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on our Homepage so our users are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under circumstances, if any, we disclose it.
