### General

We use cookies on our site to maintain your login session (whenever you click "remember me" when logging in) and to store your style preferences. For more information on cookies, [click here](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTTP_cookie)

  


### Linking your accounts

We offer you the option to link your Evo-Web account with a number of third parties. This is only ever done through the secure methods offered by these third parties. The permissions you grant to us are only used for actions you choose to generate i.e. nothing will automatically post or submit content to these third parties on your behalf. We do not access or store any other information that you may submit to these third parties.

  


### Google advertising

We use Goodle AdSense to serve ads when you visit the site. Google and its partners may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and would like to know your options in relation to not having this information used by these companies, **[click here](http://www.google.co.uk/privacy_ads.html "Google ad and content network privacy policy")**.

We also use Google Analytics to track user activity and analyse our web traffic. For more information on how Google uses this data and how you can control it please read: **[How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps](http://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/partners/)**

You are also able to review your opt-out options for Google Analytics at the [Google Analytics Opt-out page](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/).
