# Privacy Policy - eFestivals.co.uk

_published_ : Mon 23rd Jul 2012

We take the privacy of registered members very seriously and observe strict compliance with the Data Protection Act 1998 - your personal details are fully protected.

Data collected from use of the website is compiled into summary form to gain information about the use of the website. We may also use the information in our members database for analytical and statistical studies although your details will never be separately identifiable in these types of studies. This information is then used to improve our service to you.

We use every means possible to ensure that your data is securely stored.

**Use of cookies**  
A cookie is a piece of data stored on your hard drive containing information taken from the interaction between your web browser and some features of this website. We do not store personal data collected from cookies at all - they are used only to improve your use of the website, such as for remembering your login details for when you return to our website.

We have relationships with carefully-selected and monitored suppliers who may also set cookies during your visit to be used for remarketing purposes - in other words to show you different products and services based on what you appear to be interested in.

For more information about cookies, please see [http://www.allaboutcookies.org](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/) or try googling for more information.

To adjust your cookie use on eFestivals please [see here.](https://web.archive.org/cookies.php)

If you have any queries regarding the personal information we may hold, please e-mail [webmaster@efestivals.co.uk](mailto:webmaster@efestivals.co.uk).
