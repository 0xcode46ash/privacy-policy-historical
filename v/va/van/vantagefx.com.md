Spam Act
Spam is a generic term used to describe electronic ‘junk mail’- unwanted
messages sent to a person’s email account or mobile phone. In Australia,
spam is defined as ‘unsolicited commercial electronic messages’.
‘Electronic messaging’ covers emails, instant messaging, SMS and other
mobile phone messaging, but not cover normal voice-to-voice
communication by telephone.
Vantage FX complies with the provisions of the Spam Act when sending
commercial electronic messages.
