## Our Commitment to Privacy:

At Varsity Tutors LLC your privacy is important to us. Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed to any third party. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our home page and at every page where personally identifiable information may be requested. If you have any questions regarding Varsity Tutors LLC's online privacy policy, please do not hesitate to ask us by sending an e-mail to [ privacy@varsitytutors.com ](mailto:privacy@varsitytutors.com) . 

## The Information We Collect:

In a few areas of our website, we may ask you to provide information that will enable us to enhance your site visit and enable us to contact you. In each of those instances, we may ask for your name, e-mail address, phone number, address, as well as other similar personal information that is needed to register or authenticate you as a user. 

We use e-mail addresses when replying to any e-mail we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose. Varsity Tutors LLC does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses we obtain to anyone. Varsity Tutors LLC will not use, share, or sell personal information provided to us online or by phone to any outside organization without your consent. Varsity Tutors LLC wants to keep your personal information accurate. If at any time you notice an inconsistency or inaccuracy in your personal information, please contact us at [ info@varsitytutors.com ](mailto:info@varsitytutors.com) . 

## Links:

This website contains links to other websites. Please be aware that Varsity Tutors LLC is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other websites. We encourage our users to be aware when leaving our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that we link to. Every effort will be made to make the user aware of when they are leaving the Varsity Tutors website. 

## How to Contact Us:

Should you have any questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send us an e-mail at [ privacy@varsitytutors.com ](mailto:privacy@varsitytutors.com) .
