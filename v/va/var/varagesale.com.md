# Privacy Policy

(last update: Jan 2012)

### Intro

We take privacy very seriously. Rest assured that we treat your information with the uttermost respect.

### Information we gather

The following information is gathered from our website visitors:

  * Your name
  * Your IP address
  * Your email address
  * Your gender
  * Your location (city, state/province, country only)



### How we use the information we gather

We use this information to enhance your visitor experience. For example, to send you notifications by email, or to determine which communities you might be interested in. It will not be given sold to third-parties **under any circumstance.**

### The people that are given access to this information

Your personal information is accessible by our server administrator.

### Our use of cookies

Our website makes use of cookies which are small digital files that are stored in your web browser that enable us to track your return visits to our website and therefore improve your experience.
