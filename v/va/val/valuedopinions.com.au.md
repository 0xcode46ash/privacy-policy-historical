# Privacy Policy | Valued Opinions AUS

**Effective Date: November 12, 2015**

[](https://privacy.truste.com/privacy-seal/Research-Now-Group,-Inc-/validation?rid=98d7d3ce-e81d-48ed-a077-3e151b4123ee "TRUSTe Privacy Certification")

### **Information Collected to Provide a Personalized Experience**

  * • We will ask you to provide us with information about yourself.
  * • We may supplement information that you provide with information received from other organizations to create segmentations.
  * • We keep track of your interactions with our websites.
  * • You can also sign up to participate in our advanced [Cookies](https://www.valuedopinions.com.au/privacy-full/?subsid=101462515#c12835 "Opens internal link in current window") program.



### **Uses of Information**

  * • We will not send any unsolicited commercial emails (“spam”) to you or sell your contact details to third parties. 
  * • We use the information we collect to provide you with relevant market research surveys. 
  * • We use the information we collect for market research purposes. 
  * • We keep the information you provide.



### **Third-Party Involvement**

  * • We may share information about you with trusted third parties in order to enrich your profile, so as to provide you with more relevant market research surveys.
  * • We may need to involve third parties for the fulfilment of rewards.
  * • Should you express consent during the sign-up process, Research Now may share information about you with your recruitment sponsor.
  * • All information is shared anonymously without identification, unless you consent to provide Personally Identifiable Information (PII).



Additional details: [Third Party Websites ](https://www.valuedopinions.com.au/privacy-full/?subsid=101462515#c12839 "Opens internal link in current window")

###  **Your Privacy Choices**

  * • All program members (“Members”) must opt in. 
  * • You can unsubscribe (opt-out) from our membership programs by contacting [Member Services](https://www.valuedopinions.com.au/help/contact-us/?subsid=101462515 "Opens internal link in current window")
  * • You can choose to unsubscribe from our advanced cookie program.



Additional details: [Opting Choices](https://www.valuedopinions.com.au/privacy-full/?subsid=101462515#IXc "Opens internal link in current window")

### **Information Protection**

  * • Our privacy policy undergoes an independent review by TRUSTe. 
  * • We comply with market research industry standards according to CASRO and ESOMAR.



Additional details: [Security](https://www.valuedopinions.com.au/privacy-full/?subsid=101462515#c12843 "Opens internal link in current window")
