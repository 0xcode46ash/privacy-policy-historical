# Privacy policy | Data Protection | Valued Opinions AU

**Effective Date: 30/09/2016**

[](https://privacy.truste.com/privacy-seal/Research-Now-Group,-Inc-/validation?rid=98d7d3ce-e81d-48ed-a077-3e151b4123ee)

### **Information Collected to Provide a Personalized Experience**

  * • We will ask you to provide us with information about yourself.
  * • We may supplement information that you provide with information received from other organizations to create segmentations.
  * • We keep track of your interactions with our websites.
  * • You can also sign up to participate in our advanced [Cookies](https://web.archive.org/web/20170219101231id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.com.au/privacyFull.html#/#Cookies "Opens internal link in current window") porgramme.



Additional details: [ Information Collected ](https://web.archive.org/web/20170219101231id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.com.au/privacyFull.html#/#information "Opens internal link in current window")   
 

### **Uses of Information**

  * • We will not send any unsolicited commercial emails (“spam”) to you or sell your contact details to third parties.
  * • We use the information we collect to provide you with relevant market research surveys.
  * • We use the information we collect for market research purposes.
  * • We keep the information you provide.



Additional details: [ How We Use the Information We Collect ](https://web.archive.org/web/20170219101231id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.com.au/privacyFull.html#/#useofinfo "Opens internal link in current window")   
 

### **Third-Party Involvement**

  * • We may share information about you with trusted third parties in order to enrich your profile, so as to provide you with more relevant market research surveys.
  * • We may need to involve third parties for the fulfilment of rewards or to obtain services inherent or connected to our business activities.
  * • Should you express consent during the sign-up process, Research Now may share information about you with your recruitment sponsor.
  * • All information is shared anonymously without identification, unless you consent to provide Personally Identifiable Information (PII).



Additional details: [ Third Party Websites ](https://web.archive.org/web/20170219101231id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.com.au/privacyFull.html#/#websites "Opens internal link in current window")   
 

### **Your Privacy Choices**

  * • All program members ("Members") must opt in.
  * • You can unsubscribe (opt-out) from our membership programs from your account page. You can also contact [ Member Services ](https://web.archive.org/web/20170219101231id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.com.au/contact.html)
  * • You can choose to unsubscribe from our advanced cookie porgramme.



Additional details: [ Opting Choices ](https://web.archive.org/web/20170219101231id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.com.au/privacyFull.html#/#opting "Opens internal link in current window")   
 

### **Information Protection**

  * • Our Privacy Policy undergoes a yearly review by an independent 3rd party.
  * • We comply with market research industry standards according to CASRO and ESOMAR.



Additional details: [ Security ](https://web.archive.org/web/20170219101231id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.com.au/privacyFull.html#/#data "Opens internal link in current window")
