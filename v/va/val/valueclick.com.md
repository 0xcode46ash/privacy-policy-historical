# ValueClick - About Us - Privacy Statement

If you would like more information on how to opt-out of the DoubleClick cookie, please [CLICK HERE](http://www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy/opt-out.asp?asp_object_1=&).

**VALUECLICK INC'S PRIVACY STATEMENT**  
Internet consumer privacy is of paramount importance to ValueClick, our advertisers, and the Web publishers in our network. The following statement outlines ValueClick's policies and principles regarding online privacy. ValueClick, Inc. is committed to protecting the privacy of consumers, publishers, and advertisers, and to do our part to maintain the integrity of the Internet. 

Because of the changing nature of consumer needs and our business itself, ValueClick, Inc. reserves the right to modify this policy statement at any time without notice. As such, and since we want to always make certain that our privacy policy is clearly understood, 

_we recommend that you review this Privacy Statement periodically to note any changes that may have occurred_. Please forward any comments you may have regarding this policy to us at [privacy@valueclick.com](mailto:privacy@valueclick.com). 

**  
CONSUMER PRIVACY**  
ValueClick, Inc. serves advertising to websites using two different delivery methods; the first is through ValueClick's standard delivery system, the second using DoubleClick's DART delivery system. Internet consumers may encounter one or both advertising systems as they travel through the Internet. Each system has its own capabilities and purpose. Internet consumers should make note of both systems and the type of information each collects. 

_  
**Internet Consumer Data Collected by ValueClick, Inc's Standard Delivery System.**_

**Personally Identifiable Information (PII)**  
When an Internet consumer clicks on one of ValueClick, Inc.'s served advertisements originating from ValueClick's standard delivery system, ValueClick, Inc. 

_does not_ collect any personally identifiable information regarding the consumer who clicked on the ad (e.g. name, address, social security number, e-mail address).   
**  
Non-Personally Identifiable Information (Non-PII)**  
When an Internet consumer clicks on one of ValueClick, Inc.'s served advertisements originating from ValueClick's standard delivery system, ValueClick, Inc. _does_ collect Non-Personally Identifiable Information (Non-PII), which is used solely for the purpose of delivering ads and reporting results. This is known in the industry as "ad delivery and reporting data". Ad delivery and reporting data includes type of browser, operating system, IP address, domain type, page(s) visited, date and time of when an ad has been responded to. 

ValueClick does not use the collected NPII for any other purpose than to deliver ads and report results, and by itself this information cannot be used to identify any specific user. 

ValueClick, Inc. retains all ad delivery and reporting data indefinitely, for accounting and auditing purposes. 

_**Internet Consumer Data Collected by DoubleClick's DART Delivery System**_

**Personally Identifiable Information (PII)**  
When an Internet consumer clicks on one of ValueClick, Inc.'s served advertisements originating from DoubleClick's DART delivery system, DART 

_does not_ collect any personally identifiable information regarding the consumer who clicked on the ad (e.g. name, address, social security number, e-mail address). 

**Non-Personally Identifiable Information (Non-PII)**  
When an Internet consumer clicks on one of ValueClick, Inc.'s served advertisements originating from DoubleClick's DART delivery system, DART 

_does_ collect non-personally identifiable information (Non-PII). This information includes the type of browser, operating system, IP address, domain type, page(s) visited, and the date and time of when an Internet consumer has responded to an advertisement. 

Non-personally identifiable data is collected to assist in ad targeting and to measure ad effectiveness on behalf of our advertisers and web publishers who specifically request it. 

**_Consumer Choice Concerning Data Collection_

Advertising Originating from ValueClick's Standard Delivery System -   
Use of Cookies - Remove and Disable option 

**  
When a consumer views an ad served by ValueClick's standard delivery system they are issued a "Cookie". A cookie is a tiny file that contains a number. The number is recorded and stored in ValueClick's system; the cookie is stored on the consumer's computer in the web browser cookie file. ValueClick only uses this information for delivering ads and reporting results. Cookies do not harm Internet consumers' computers and cannot collect any information about a person, only their computer. 

Because ValueClick only collects information for delivering and reporting advertising, consumers who wish to "opt-out" or refuse a ValueClick cookie being issued to their computer have the option of disabling cookies within their web browser software, which means that they will no longer receive cookies without their approval. Internet consumers can also delete any cookies that currently reside on their computer. Internet consumers should refer to their respective browser's user manual for instruction on performing this action. It is important to note that disabling the cookie option in your web browser will block your computer from receiving cookies from __all__ websites using this technology. 

**Advertising Originating from DoubleClick's DART Delivery System -  
Use of Cookies - Opt-Out Option **  
When a consumer views an ad served by DoubleClick's DART delivery System they are issued a DART "Cookie". A DART cookie is a tiny file that contains a number. The number is recorded and stored by DoubleClick, the cookie is saved and stored on the consumer's computer in the web browser cookie file. When a consumer with a DART cookie arrives at any subsequent website using DoubleClick's DART advertising delivering system, DART will recognize the previously issued cookie. 

Cookies are used by DART to record what advertising is interacted with so that ad delivery produces information relevant to the consumer's interests. 

For further information and details about DoubleClick's DART delivery system, the type of data collected, and how it is used, please visit DoubleClick's website at; 

[http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy_policy/ ](http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy_policy/)

In accordance with consumer privacy initiatives, companies collecting data related to consumers Internet activities must give them the right to "opt-out" of (i.e. choose not to) having this information collected. Consumer's wishing to "opt-out" of DoubleClick's DART cookie being issued to their computer can go to DoubleClick's website for instructions and procedures for doing so. 

[http://www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy/opt-out.asp?asp_object_1=&](http://www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy/opt-out.asp?asp_object_1=&)

 _ **Client Data (i.e. Web Publishers and Advertisers)**_

**Non-Personally Identifiable Client Data.**  
ValueClick, Inc. does not collect non-personally identifiable information from our clients. Non-personally identifiable information is also known as "demographic" data and includes information such as age, gender, and income range. 

**Personally Identifiable Client Data**  
ValueClick, Inc. collects personally identifiable information from all websites (and specifically their owners) wishing to join our network. The collection of this information is a part of normal business practice. Clients wishing to join the ValueClick, Inc. advertising network will be asked to provide personally identifiable data through the host sign up and registration process at our web site. Examples of personally identifiable data that may be requested include name, address, phone number, e-mail address, social security number, tax ID number, etc. ValueClick, Inc. will never request or collect data from a client on subject areas including sexual orientation, political or religious affiliations, or medical data. 

ValueClick, Inc. 

_does not_ use any personally identifiable data about a client for profiling purposes. All personally identifiable information that ValueClick, Inc. collects from clients is principally intended for administrative needs (e.g., accounting and pay-out), for which purpose such data are required. 

Periodically ValueClick, Inc. may desire to communicate with our network of hosts via email newsletters or other avenues to offer strategic marketing information and other communications to assist in business related activities. In such instances ValueClick, Inc. will access clients' personally identifiable information. In the event that a client of ValueClick, Inc. wishes not to have their personally identifiable information used for the aforementioned purposes, those clients can elect to opt-out. For more information on opting-out please see the "Client Choice Concerning Data Collection" section below. 

All personally identifiable data provided by individual clients will be kept in strict confidence by ValueClick, Inc. and will be treated with the same level of respect and care that ValueClick, Inc. treats its other proprietary information and trade secrets. ValueClick, Inc. does not sell, rent, exchange or barter any personally identifiable information to or with any third party for financial gain or marketing purposes. ValueClick, Inc. may, from time to time, provide such information to certain third-party administrative vendors for efficiency purposes in providing administrative services such as billing, delivery and payouts. Any third-party vendor so used, will be required to enter into a confidentiality and non-disclosure agreement with ValueClick, Inc. in accordance with the policies stated here. 

_**Client Choice Concerning Data Collection**_

**Personally Identifiable Client Data - Opt-Out**  
Because ValueClick, Inc. is a strong proponent of online privacy we are committed to making sure that clear notice and choice is provided to all clients before any personally identifiable data are used for any purposes other than for what was originally intended. 

When a client joins ValueClick, Inc.'s network and registers for ValueClick, Inc.'s services, personally identifiable information is required as a part of doing business. ValueClick, Inc.'s primary intent in collecting personally identifiable information is for use in administrative purposes including accounting and payouts. However, ValueClick, Inc. may periodically use personally identifiable information for marketing and communication purposes targeted to our clients. Clients wishing not to receive such communications from ValueClick, Inc. may do so by "opting-out" during the registration and application procedure process. A client may change their "opt" status at anytime during their tenure with ValueClick, Inc. by accessing their online account. 

**Security Precautions**  
ValueClick, Inc. makes every effort to maintain the security of its network and the data we collect. Any data that is stored on ValueClick, Inc.'s server is treated as proprietary and confidential and is not available to the public. ValueClick, Inc. has a strict internal security policy with respect to the confidentiality of customer and other data, limiting access only to those employees or third parties who have a need to know such information for the purpose of effectively delivering ValueClick, Inc. products and services. 

**Access to Data**  
ValueClick, Inc. allows clients who have provided personally identifiable data, access their data to ensure that it is correct and to make any changes at anytime through their ValueClick customer online account. 

_**The ValueClick Network**_

**ValueClick and Client Website Privacy Policy Statements**

In accordance with the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) ValueClick, Inc. requests that website publishers belonging to our advertising network create privacy policy statements. 

ValueClick, Inc. asks that websites in our network comply with Online Privacy Alliance (OPA) guidelines by disclosing any and all information that is being collected from Internet consumers on their website, including data gathered from banners and other forms of advertising being served on their website. We also ask that they provide Internet consumers with conspicuous access to their statements. 

Furthermore, ValueClick, Inc. requires that all websites within our network using DoubleClick's DART delivered advertising 

_must_ have privacy policy statements readily available to consumers with information about what types of information is being collected. They _must_ also provide links to our website which provides information and access to "opt-out" procedures. 

If you have particular concerns about any website advertising with ValueClick, Inc. with respect to privacy issues, please contact [privacy@valueclick.com](mailto:privacy@valueclick.com).

**Children's Privacy**  
As of April 21, 2000 the children's Online Privacy Protection Act became effective. The regulations apply to the online collection of personal information from children under the age of 13. ValueClick, Inc. is very sensitive to the issue of children's privacy and makes every effort to protect the privacy of children using the Internet. ValueClick, Inc.'s web site, products, and services are not developed for or directed at children. Our policy is not to permit anyone under the age of 13 to do business with us. If you believe your child has provided ValueClick, Inc. with any personally identifiable data by registering at ValueClick, Inc.'s web site, please contact [privacy@valueclick.com](mailto:privacy@valueclick.com) to have them removed. 

We also request that all websites using our advertising network disclose in their privacy statements any and all information regarding the collection of information from children.
