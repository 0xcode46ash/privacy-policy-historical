# New Mass. Media - Advocate*Weekly Newspapers - Privacy Policy

  1. Your e-mail address will not be shared outside of New Mass. Media for marketing purposes without your consent. 
  2. Users' profile information will be used to create personalized content, services and advertising on our web sites. In addition, we may use your profile to generate aggregate reports and market research. 
  3. New Mass. Media may occasionally use cookies to uniquely identify individual browsers (not users), typically to aid in the prevention of biased poll results. At no point will New Mass. Media store personal contact information in cookies. 
  4. At any time, you may e-mail [feedback@newmassmedia.com](mailto:feedback@newmassmedia.com) if you would like have your contact information deleted from our records. 
  5. New Mass. Media does not provide functionality to perform reverse searches which find individual names from address, e-mail, or phone number information
