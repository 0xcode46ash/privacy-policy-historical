# Value Line

### Privacy Statement for Value Line, Inc.

**Information Collection:**  
This privacy statement demonstrates Value Line, Inc.'s commitment to your right to privacy and explains the privacy policy governing research.valueline.com (the " **Web Site** "). It is our general policy to collect and store only personal information that our members and visitors knowingly provide. 

We require registration to use this Web Site. When you register on the Web Site, you are only required to give a user name, password, and e-mail address. However, we may also ask for additional information such as personal (address, phone, email), demographic (gender, age), and financial information (income, account, credit card data). We may use any information you provide, which is stored in a database, for our own marketing or research purposes. 

If our privacy policy changes, we will notify all users by posting the new policy on the Web Site. 

**Minors:**  
This site is not intended for minors. We do not accept registration from minors. 

**Information Disclosure and Use:**  
Unless otherwise provided, we may use the personal information we collect from you as follows: 

· 
  * to ensure that our Web Site is protected against unauthorized access;
· 
  * to market and improve our products and services and to notify you of updates to the products and services; and
· 
  * to use email to diagnose problems with the server or to administer the Web Site.



We do not provide information relevant to your use of the Web Site, including information gathered during registration or from a survey, contest or order form, to any third party. 

**Choice/Opt-Out—Internal Use:**  
If you do not want us to use the registration or supplemental information you provide for our internal marketing or research purposes, you may opt out and elect not to have the information shared, irrespective of whether you register for a user account, respond to a survey or questionnaire, or disclose information requested by us through any other means. AN OPT-OUT BOX WILL APPEAR WHEN YOU PROVIDE ANY REQUESTED INFORMATION. If you have previously registered to use the Web Site and wish to prevent our internal use of your information, please contact us as specified below. 

**Cookies:**  
Cookies are files that contain information created by a web server that can be stored on your hard disk for future use. We use cookies only to facilitate automated activity, store and track passwords, and determine appropriate solicitations by tracking your movement through the site. Cookies are not used to disseminate significant information about you over the Internet or to analyze any information that you have knowingly or unknowingly provided. Cookies are destroyed whenever you log off the Web Site, shut down your browser or reboot your computer. Your web browser will allow you to disable cookies, but you will not be able to successfully log on to and participate in our Web Site if you do so. Therefore, you will always remain in guest status if cookies are disabled. 

**Privacy:**  
This Web Site contains links to other web sites. We have no control over and are not responsible for the privacy policies of such sites. 

**Integrity/Security:**  
We use encryption and other technology to store personal information gathered online in an attempt to provide a secure environment that is protected from unauthorized third party access, alteration, theft, or misuse. However, we cannot guarantee the security of the information you transmit to us. 

**Contact:**  
You may change or delete information that you have already provided by contacting [researchcenter@valueline.com](mailto:researchcenter@valueline.com). 

**Redress:**  
If you feel we have handled your personal information in a manner that does not comply with this privacy statement, please contact: [researchcenter@valueline.com](mailto:researchcenter@valueline.com)
