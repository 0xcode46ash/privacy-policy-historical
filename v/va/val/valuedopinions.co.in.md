# Valued Opinions Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: 04/03/2015**

[](https://privacy.truste.com/privacy-seal/Research-Now-Group,-Inc-/validation?rid=33f65110-9879-47b7-851d-9bfa80f20696)

### **Information Collected to Provide a Personalized Experience**

  * • We will ask you to provide us with information about yourself.
  * • We may supplement information that you provide with information received from other organizations to create segmentations.
  * • We keep track of your interactions with our websites.
  * • You can also sign up to participate in our advanced [Cookies](https://web.archive.org/web/20160327051432id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.co.in/privacyFull.html#/#Cookies "Opens internal link in current window") program.



Additional details: [Information Collected](https://web.archive.org/web/20160327051432id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.co.in/privacyFull.html#/#information "Opens internal link in current window")  
 

### **Uses of Information**

  * • We will not send any unsolicited commercial emails ("spam") to you or sell your contact details to third parties.
  * • We use the information we collect to provide you with relevant market research surveys.
  * • We use the information we collect for market research purposes.
  * • We keep the information you provide.



Additional details: [How We Use the Information We Collect](https://web.archive.org/web/20160327051432id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.co.in/privacyFull.html#/#useofinfo "Opens internal link in current window")  
 

### **Third-Party Involvement**

  * • We may share information about you with trusted third parties in order to enrich your profile, so as to provide you with more relevant market research surveys.
  * • We may need to involve third parties for the fulfilment of rewards.
  * • Should you express consent during the sign-up process, Research Now may share information about you with your recruitment sponsor.
  * • All information is shared anonymously without identification, unless you consent to provide Personally Identifiable Information (PII).



Additional details: [Third Party Websites ](https://web.archive.org/web/20160327051432id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.co.in/privacyFull.html#/#websites "Opens internal link in current window")  
 

### **Your Privacy Choices**

  * • All program members ("Members") must opt in.
  * • You can unsubscribe (opt-out) from our membership programs by contacting [Member Services](https://web.archive.org/web/20160327051432id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.co.in/contact.html)
  * • You can choose to unsubscribe from our advanced cookie program.



Additional details: [Opting Choices](https://web.archive.org/web/20160327051432id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.co.in/privacyFull.html#/#opting "Opens internal link in current window")  
 

### **Information Protection**

  * • Our privacy policy undergoes an independent review by TRUSTe.
  * • We comply with market research industry standards according to CASRO and ESOMAR.



Additional details: [Security](https://web.archive.org/web/20160327051432id_/https%3A//www.valuedopinions.co.in/privacyFull.html#/#data "Opens internal link in current window")
