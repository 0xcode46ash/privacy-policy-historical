# Vacation Express : Privacy Statement

Â 

**Vacation Express** is committed to respecting your privacy. Our Web site is designed so that you can visit on the Web without identifying yourself or revealing any personal information. Once you opt to provide personally identifiable information (any information by which you can be identified), it will only be used to support your customer relationship with us. 

Vacation Express will not sell, rent, or lease your personally identifiable information to third parties, unless we have your permission or we are required by law to do so.

Vacation Express is committed to protecting the security of your information. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. We use encryption when collecting or transferring sensitive data such as credit card information. Credit card numbers are used only for payment processing and are not retained for other purposes.
