What does VnnMall do with the the collected information? We respect your privacy and believe that your personal information—including your purchases, email and residential addresses, and credit card information—belongs to you. The data we collect is used to give us demographic information about the entire VnnMall audience to improve the VnnMall service and help us sell the advertising and products that support the site. The personal information you provide lets us fill your orders, provide customer service assistance, and inform you about updates to the service and notify you of products and services of VnnMall.com. If at any time you would no longer like to take part in the e-mail program, simply e-mail us via the address that is provided at the bottom of any e-mail that we send to you with the UN-SUBSCRIBE command and you will be taken off of the list within 24-48 hours.
