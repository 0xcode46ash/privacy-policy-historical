# Privacy Policy

### Privacy Policy of Vodafone Egypt

This Privacy Policy describes the type of information necessary for Vodafone Egypt (hereinafter "[Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/)") to gather from you in order to process your inquiries and/or provide you with the services and information described and offered on the Vodafone.com.eg web site. 

By visiting [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/), you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Notice. 

The purpose of gathering information from the Members serves the purpose of providing an enhance informative experience at VODAFONE.COM.EG, here are the types of information [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) gather: 

  1. **Information the Members provide:**  
[Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) receive and store any information you enter on our [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) site or give us in any other way. You can choose not to provide certain information, but then you might not be able to take advantage of many of our features. [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) use the information that you provide for such purposes as responding to your requests, and communicating with you.
  2. **Automatic-Information:**  
[Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with the web site. For example, [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) use "cookies," and [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) obtain certain types of information when your browser accesses [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) The Members provide said information while using the E-mail communication features on [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/). 
  3. **E-mail-Communications:**  
To help [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) make e-mails more useful and interesting, [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) often receive a confirmation when you open e-mail from [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) if your computer supports such capabilities. 
  4. **Cookies:**  
The "help" portion of the toolbar on most browsers will tell you how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie, or how to disable cookies altogether. Cookies allow you to take full advantage of some of Vodafone.com.eg's features, and [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) recommend that you leave them turned on while browsing [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/). 
  5. **Sharing the Information:**  
Information about our Members is an important part of our business, and Vodafone Egypt is not in the business of selling it to others. Vodafone Egypt share Members information only with the subsidiaries of Vodafone, further control measures are described below. 
  6. **Affiliates:**  
Vodafone Egypt work closely with affiliated businesses. In some cases, these businesses operate stores at Vodafone Egypt or sell offerings to you at [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/). 
  7. **Protection of[Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) and Others: **  
[Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) release account and other personal information when [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) believe release is appropriate to comply with law; enforce or apply our Terms & Conditions and other agreements; or protect the rights, property, or safety of [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/), our users, or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction. 
  8. **Your approval:**  
Other than as set out above, you will receive notice when information about you might go to third parties, and you will have an opportunity to choose not to share the information. 
  9. **Members's Choice:**  
As referred to above, Members can always choose not to provide information, even though it might be needed to take advantage of such [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) features. When you update information, [Vodafone.com.eg](http://www.vodafone.com.eg/) usually keep a copy of the prior version for our records. 
  10. **Compliance:**  
If you feel that your rights under the terms and conditions above were violated, please contact our support at this address [customer-serviceeg@vodafone.com](mailto:customer-serviceeg@vodafone.com).
