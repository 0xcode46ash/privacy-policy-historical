**Information Collected**

Personal information is collected by Vodafone Ghana. We collect personally identifiable information about you (your "Data") through:

·         _The use of inquiry and registration forms._

·         _The use of or registration with our products or services_

·         _The provision of your details to us either online or offline_

Other elements of your Data that we collect may include:

·         _Name_

·         _Company name_

·         _Home, shipping and billing addresses, phone and fax number_

·         _Mobile telephone number_

·         _E-mail address_

·         _IP address and browser information_

·         _Market research data such as customer usage patterns_

·         _Use and Disclosure of Personal Information_

We use your Data for purposes, which may include:

·         _Providing our customers with a personalized service._

·         _Processing orders, registrations and inquiries._

·         _Conducting market research surveys._

·         _Running competitions._

·         _Measuring interest in and improving our website, products, and services._

·         _Providing you with information about products and services we offer._

We use the information you provide when placing an order to complete that order. We do not share this information with outside parties except;

1.      To the extent necessary to complete that order or to provide services where the outside party is acting on our behalf and has no independent right to use the information we provide (e.g., third-party shipping, 

2.      To improve service provision, develop new products and enhance our value proposition, or

3.      To successors in title to our business, or 

4.      In accordance with legal and regulatory requirements, or 

5.      As necessary, at Vodafone Ghana’ssole discretion, to protect the perceived rights, safety and property of Vodafone Ghana, users of our websites, and the public.

Other than as set out above, you will be notified when Personal Information about you will be shared with third parties, and you will have an opportunity to choose not to have us share such information.

**Security Policy**

Vodafone Ghana has appropriate measures in place to help ensure that our users’ Data is protected against unauthorized access or use, alteration, unlawful or accidental destruction and accidental loss. Although we make reasonable efforts to protect your Personal Information from loss, misuse, or alteration by third parties, you should be aware that there is always some risk involved in transmitting information over the Internet. There is also some risk that criminals could find a way to thwart our security systems.

**Transfer of Data**

The Internet is a global environment. Using the Internet to collect and process Personal Data necessarily involves the transmission of Data on an international basis. When we collect personal information from you on our website, we comply with the international data protection laws and safeguards. 

**Changes to this Policy**

This Statement takes effect from 1 July 2017. From time to time we may make changes to this Privacy Statement to reflect any changes to our privacy practices in accordance with changes in legislation, best practice or website enhancements. We will notify you about material changes to this Privacy by placing a prominent notice on our website.
