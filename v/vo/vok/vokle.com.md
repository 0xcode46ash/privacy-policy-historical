# Vokle - Privacy Policy

Last modified on November 1, 2008. 

Welcome to the Vokle Inc. (“Vokle” or “Vokle.com”) applications, platform, web site ( [ www.vokle.com ](https://web.archive.org/ "Vokle.com") ), and any other web sites, applications, or services operated or produced by Vokle Inc. Vokle values the privacy of the users, subscribers, pro-members, and others who visit and use Vokle (collectively or individually, “You” or “Users”). 

This Privacy Notice is incorporated into and is subject to the Vokle Terms of Service. Your use of the Vokle and any personal information you provide through the Vokle are subject at all time to this Privacy Notice and the Terms of Service. 

By using Vokle, you expressly consent to the information handling practices described in this notice. 

**The Information Vokle.com Collects**

User-Provided Information 

You may provide what is generally called “personally identifiable” information (such as your name, email address, zip code, birthday, personal bio, profile photo, profile comments, live video streams, credit card number and billing information) to Vokle if you form an account or attempt to access certain functionality or content at Vokle. 

Automatic Information 

When you access Vokle, we may send one or more session and/or persistent cookies, which are small text files containing a string of alphanumeric characters, to your computer. A session cookie disappears after you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains after closing your browser and may be reused by your browser on subsequent visits to Vokle.com. Persistent cookies can be removed. Please review your web browser “Help” section to learn the proper way to modify your cookie settings. Additionally, some of Vokle’s business partners and advertisers may use cookies on our website. We have no access to, or control over, these cookies. 

Furthermore, Vokle and its authorized agents automatically receive and record certain "traffic data" on their server logs when you visit the site to help diagnose problems with its servers, analyze trends and administer the website. This traffic data includes, but is not limited to, your IP address, a unique user ID, cookies, version of software installed, system type, the content, live chats and pages that you access at Vokle.com as well as the dates and times that you visit the Vokle.com. 

**The Way Vokle Uses Information**

  * Vokle.com uses the information that you provide or that we collect to operate, enhance, and provide all of the features and services offered by Vokle, as well as to track user-generated content and Users to the extent necessary to comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act. 
  * We will use your email address, without further consent, for administrative communications such as Vokle.com system updates, customer service, terms of service infringement or defamation issues, or to contact you regarding any content that you have submitted to or downloaded from the Vokle. 
  * Vokle may use automatically collected information and/or cookies to: (a) automatically update the website on your system; (b) remember your sign-in information so that you will not have to re-enter it every time you access the Vokle.com; (c) track your entries, submissions, and status in any promotions or other activities; (d) monitor aggregate site usage metrics such as total number of visitors and pages accessed. 



**When Vokle.com Discloses Information**

Rest assured that Vokle not sell your personal information to anyone and that we will share your personal information only as described below. 

  * Please be aware that any personally identifiable information that you voluntarily choose to display on any publicly available portion of Vokle – such as when you submit news content or provide profile information – becomes publicly available, searchable, and may be collected and used by others without restriction. 
  * Vokle will allow third parties access to all Vokle data this is already publicly available through the Vokle website. Vokle will keep user passwords and profile settings private. 
  * Vokle allows for the RSS syndication of all of its public content, and basic user information (content submitter ID, date of submission, and URL access to profile pages) within the Vokle website whereupon third party websites will have full access. 
  * Vokle personnel and authorized consultants and/or contractors may have access to user information if necessary in the normal course of business. 
  * Vokle may disclose automatically collected and other aggregate non-personally-identifiable information with interested third parties to assist such parties in understanding the usage and demographic patterns for certain programs, services, content, advertisements, promotions, or other functionality of Vokle. 



* We may disclose some limited user information to affiliated companies or other businesses or persons to: provide web site hosting, maintenance, and security services; conduct data analysis and reports; offer certain functionality; and assist Vokle in improving, enhancing, and expanding Vokle.com. We require that these parties process such limited information in compliance with this Privacy Policy and reasonable confidentiality measures. 

* Vokle may disclose user information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with state and federal laws or respond to a court order, judicial or other government subpoena, or warrant in the manner required by the requesting entity. 

* Vokle also reserves the right to disclose user information that we believe, in good faith, is appropriate or necessary to take precautions against liability; protect Vokle from fraudulent, abusive, or unlawful uses; to investigate and defend ourselves against third-party claims or allegations; to assist government enforcement agencies; to protect the security or integrity of the Vokle; or to protect the rights, property, or personal safety of Vokle, our users, or others. 

**Your Choices**

You may decline to share your personally-identifiable information with Vokle, in which case Vokle will not be able to provide to you some of the features and functionality found on the website. You may update, correct, or delete your user information and preferences by clicking on the “Settings” link under the “Home” section of the navigation bar. A user must be logged in to make such settings changes. You are responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your unique password and account information at all times. 

**Advertisers**

Vokle allows third party companies, servers, and advertisers to serve advertisements within the site. These third-party companies may automatically collect general user information such as your IP address and other log file information when sending advertisements that appear within the Vokle.com directly to your browser. Such third-party advertisers may also use other technologies (such as cookies and javascript) to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and to personalize their advertising content. 

Vokle.com does not provide your personally identifiable information to these third-party advertiser without your consent. However, please note that if an advertiser asks Vokle to display an advertisement to a certain demographic or audience segment (for example, males age 18 to 25 who have participated in certain promotions) and you respond to that advertisement, the advertiser may conclude that you fit the description of the audience that they were trying to reach. 

This Privacy Policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites. Vokle reserves the right the add or remove third-party advertisers in its discretion and Vokle may not at all times list such updated advertisers in this Privacy Policy. 

**Our Commitment to Data Security**

Vokle uses commercially reasonable physical, technical managerial safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of your personal information. We cannot, however, ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to Vokle, and you do so at your own risk. Once we receive your transmission of information, Vokle.com makes commercially reasonable efforts to ensure the security of our systems. However, please note that this is not a guarantee that such information may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed by breach of any of our physical, technical, or managerial safeguards. 

If Vokle.com learns of a security systems breach, then we may attempt to notify you electronically so that you can take appropriate protective steps. Vokle.com may post a notice on the site if a security breach occurs. Depending on where you live, you may have a legal right to receive notice of a security breach in writing. To receive a free written notice of a security breach you should notify us at [ privacy@vokle.com. ](mailto:privacy@vokle.com "Send a privacy inquiry to Vokle.")

**Our Commitment to Children’s Privacy**

If you are under 18 years of age, then please do not use or access Vokle at any time or in any manner. 

Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important. For that reason, Vokle does not knowingly collect or maintain personally identifiable information from persons under 18 years-of-age. If we learn that Vokle has collected information from a child under the age of 18, we may delete that information as promptly as possible. 

If you are the parent or legal guardian of a child under 18 who has become Vokle member, then please contact Vokle.com at [ privacy@vokle.com ](mailto:privacy@vokle.com "Send a privacy inquiry to Vokle.") to have that child’s account terminated and information deleted. 

**International Visitors**

For Users visiting Vokle from other non-U.S. territories, please note that any data you submit to Vokle will be transferred outside the European Economic Area or such other non-U.S. territory for use by Vokle and its affiliates for any of the purposes described herein. In addition, because Vokle operates globally, we may make information we gather available to worldwide business units and affiliates. By providing any data at Vokle, you hereby expressly consent to such transfers of your data to the United States or other countries. 

**In the Event of Merger or Sale**

In the event that Vokle is acquired by or merged with a third-party entity, we reserve the right, in any of these circumstances, to transfer or assign the information that we have collected from Users as part of that merger, acquisition, sale, or other change of control. 

**Changes and Updates to this Privacy Notice:**

Vokle may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time, at its sole discretion without further notice to you and this will be reflected by a “last modified” date at the top of the page. Please revisit this page to stay aware of any changes. In general, we only use your personal information in the manner described in the Privacy Notice in effect when we received that personal information. Your continued use of Vokle constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Notice and any future revisions. 

For revisions to this Privacy Notice that may be materially less restrictive on our use or disclosure of personal information you have provided to us, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you and obtain your consent before implementing revisions with respect to such information. 

**Vokle Contact Information:**

Please contact Vokle with any questions or comments about this Privacy Notice, your personal information, and our third-party disclosure practices or your consent choices by email: [ privacy@vokle.com. ](mailto:privacy@vokle.com "Send a privacy inquiry to Vokle.") We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days of its receipt.
