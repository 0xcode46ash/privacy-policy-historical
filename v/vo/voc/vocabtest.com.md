# Vocab Test.com's Privacy Policy

**Our Commitment To Privacy**   
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the way your information is collected and used. 

**What Information We Collect:**   
We do not request any personal information from our users except _optionally_ for their email address when creating personal tests. This information is not stored on VocabTest.com and is therefore never used again or sold to anyone. 

**The Way We Use Information:**   
We use your email address to send you login information for your records. Once the email has been sent, we no longer keep it for our records -- It is destroyed. Once again, the email address is _optional_ , you are not required in any way to provide us with your email address when creating personal tests. 

**Third Party Advertising Networks**   
VocabTest.com uses one or more third party ad serving networks to serve ads at this site. In the course of delivering an ad to you, the third party ad serving party may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser and use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services that may be of interest to you. 

**How To Contact Us:**   
Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send us an email at _wordmasters@vocabtest.com_.
