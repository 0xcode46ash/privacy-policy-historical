# VocabTest.com Privacy Policy

**Our Commitment To Privacy**   
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the way your information is collected and used. 

**What Information We Collect:**

 _Users Without Accounts_

: We do not require any personal information to access this site without an account. 

_User Accounts_

: To register as a user you are required to provide your name, email address and birthday. 

**The Way We Use Information:**

 _Users Without Accounts_

: We may ask you for an optional email address or name for submitting or creating tests. This information is destroyed immediately after sending out the email. 

_Users With Accounts_

: Account information is used for resetting passwords and auto-filling final tests with your real name. We will not send you unsolicited spam or sell your email address. The only information that will be public is your **username**. Your personal information is safe with us! 

**Third Party Advertising Networks**   
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here:   
<http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp>

**How To Contact Us:**   
Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send us an email at [wordmasters@vocabtest.com](mailto:wordmasters@vocabtest.com).
