# Vois - Social networking, social commerce, buy, sell, meet, connect, share, find friends, bands, music, entertainment, business, love, personals, classifieds, communities and people like you!

|    
---  
  
**Effective January 1, 2007:**

**Data Collection by Vois.com**   
Vois.com collects user submitted data such as name, email address, gender, age, personal interests, financial information, location, education and occupation in order to assist users in finding and communicating with each other. Additionally, Vois.com logs information including IP address, profile information, aggregate user data and browser type, from users and visitors to the site. User IP addresses are recorded for security and monitoring. This data is used to manage the website, track usage and improve website services. This non-personally-identifiable information may be shared with third-parties to provide services and advertisements to members.   
  
   
  
**Cookies and Advertisers**   
Vois.com like many websites uses cookies. Cookies are instruments which are used to store information. As with any website you may elect to choose to configure your browser to accept or reject cookies, or notify you when a cookie is turned on, however, in order to use Vois.com you must enable your cookies. Vois does not link the information it stores in cookies to any personally identifiable information about you which you may submit while on our site, however Vois does allow third party advertisers that are serving advertisements on our website to set and access cookies on your computer. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you advertisements and then send you targeted advertisements that the advertiser believes will be of most interest to you. In this way, ad servers may compile information about where you or anyone else using your computer saw their advertisements and determine which ads are clicked on. This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by Vois.com but does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers or links to their websites.   
  
   
  
**Links to Other Websites**   
Vois.com links to other websites. Vois.com is not responsible for the privacy policies and/or practices of those other sites for which it links. Our privacy policy only governs information collected on Vois.com.   
  
   
  
**Legal Requirements**   
Vois will not disclose personal information to any third party unless we believe that disclosure is: (1) by legal requirement or to a subpoena, search warrant or other legal process received by Vois.com, whether or not a response is required by applicable law; (2) to protect our rights; or (3) to protect the safety of our members or the public. Vois also reserves the right to transfer personal information to a successor in interest that acquires rights to that information as a result of the sale of Vois or substantially all of its assets to that successor in interest.   
  
   
  
**Email / Email Options**   
Regarding email addresses - Email addresses are used for inviting friends, adding friends and site updates or news. Users within Vois.com communicate with each other through the Vois.com service, without disclosing their email addresses and as such a Member's email address is not shared or displayed. We may use a user's email address to send site updates, company news or news regarding Vois. Users may elect not to receive email of this type by changing their "email" settings to "Do not send me update emails" in the user "Account Settings"   
  
   
  
**Searching**   
To facilitate searching on Vois, Vois.com allows users to search for other members using name, email address and schools and/or companies.   
  
   
  
**Posting to any Public Forums**   
Whenever you choose to post to any public forum on Vois such as Web Logs (Blogs), Bulletin Boards, Message Boards, Classifieds or anywhere else on Vois.com that information is public and can be used by people to send you unwanted or unsolicited communications.   
  
   
  
**Editing Information**   
Vois.com Members may edit or remove any of their personal information at any time by logging into their account and accessing features such as Edit Profile.   
  
   
  
**Your Privacy and Security**   
Vois.com member accounts are secured by member-created passwords. At Vois.com we take measures to protect information stored within our database and restrict access to member information by allowing only those employees who need access to perform their job functions, such as our customer service personnel and technical staff. Although we take measures to protect your Privacy and Security we cannot guarantee the security of your member account information which may occur through unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure or other factors known or unknown.   
  
   
  
**Contests, Promotions and Sweepstake**   
In the event that Vois.com or a partner sponsors, a contest, sweepstake or promotion users may be requested to provide personal information including name, email address or home address in order to participate. In this event, we then may transfer the personal information received from you during this promotion, sweepstake or contest to those ad partners that you have agreed to supply with this information. During any contest, sweepstake or promotion it will be made clear who is collecting the personal information and whose privacy statement will apply.   
  
   
  
**Changes in Our Privacy Policy**   
We may make changes to our privacy policy. If we make changes, we will make our users aware the changes so users will know. Users are bound by any changes to our Privacy Policy after those changes have been posted.   
  
   
  
**Contacting the Web Site**   
All questions regarding our privacy policy can be sent to us at: privacy@Vois.com (313 NW 3rd Delray Beach, Florida)   
  
   
  
**Invitations and Other Communications to Non-members**   
Vois members can invite friends to join the service by sending invitation emails via our automated invitation system. Vois.com stores the email addresses that members provide so that the respondents may be added to the friend's list of the member sending the invitations, and also to send reminders of the invitations Vois.com does not sell these email addresses or use them to send any other communication besides invitations, invitation reminders (up to three (3) per email address). Recipients of invitations from Vois.com may contact Vois.com to request the removal of their information from our database.
