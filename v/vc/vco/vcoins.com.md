# privacy

## **Privacy Statement  
for  
VCoins - The Ancient Coin Mall**

VCoins has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.  The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: VCoins - The Ancient Coin Mall.

  * **Your personal information will never be sold or otherwise provided to any third party. You may receive information directly from VCoins or our member dealers if you have indicated that would like to receive that information. You may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time -- see the choce/opt-out section below.**
  * We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. 
  * Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart.
  * Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (such as name and email address).  If the user has agreed, we use this contact information to send the information about our company and occasional promotional material from some of our member dealers.  The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary.  Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.
  * This site contains links to other sites.  VCoins is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
  * Our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services.  We collect visitor's contact information (like their shipping and email addresses). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders to our customers.  Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.
  * Our online surveys ask visitors for contact information (such as email address). If the user has agreed, we use contact data to send information about our company and occasional promotional material from some of our member dealers.  The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary.  Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.
  * We run contests on our site in which we ask visitors for contact information (such as email and mailing addresses).  If the user has agreed, we use contact data to send information about our company and occasional promotional material from some of our member dealers. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.



##  **Public Forums**

This site may make chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users.  Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

##  **Security**

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

##  **Choice/Opt-Out**

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from our member dealers or us.

This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service. 

  * You can send email to [admin@vcoins.com](mailto:admin@vcoins.com)



##  **Correct/Update**

This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided. 

  * You can send email to [admin@vcoins.com](mailto:admin@vcoins.com)



##  **Contacting the Web Site**

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact VCoins at [admin@vcoins.com](mailto:admin@vcoins.com).
