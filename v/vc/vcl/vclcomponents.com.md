# VCL Components - Delphi and C++ Builder Components

  
| 

|  VCLComponents.com values Your privacy.

**Personal Information**  
VCLComponents.com collects personal information about our users during registration, from our use of "cookie" technology as you use the service. 

VCLComponents.com uses reasonable precautions to keep any personal information disclosed to us secure and we never disclose such information to third parties without your express permission. We only ever use personal information for the exact purpose we stated when you gave it to us. However, we are not responsible for any breach of security or for any actions of any third parties which receive the information. VCLComponents.com also links to other sites. We are not responsible for their privacy policies or how they treat information about their users. 

**Aggregated Information**  
We also collect aggregated tracking information derived mainly by tallying page views throughout our sites. Such information enables us to better tailor our content to visitors' needs and to help our advertisers better understand the demographics of our audience. Because VCLComponents.com derives its revenue mainly from advertising, providing such aggregated demographic data is essential to keeping our service free to users. 

**Contact VCLComponents.com**  
Please contact us at admin@vclcomponents.com for more information about our privacy policy if required.
