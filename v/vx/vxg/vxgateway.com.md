**Data Collection**

To open your Account, we need to obtain certain data information from you to satisfy the minimum requirements as specified in the registration forms and KYC - KYB compliance. If we request required field information we expect the information to be accurate. For high value transactions, we will always request you provide further information we will register and confirm our acceptance of your request, we may choose to accept or reject such request. This applies to all account type including Governments and financial institutions.

**Use of Data**

The data is used to operate your Account and to enable us to provide the services. We need to verify information with banks, government agencies and entities not specified to ensure we minimize the risk for all parties involved in the transaction. We will use best efforts at all times to ensure best in class industry practices are utilized to perform the service. 

**Data Control**

We provide you with a profile management area that must be accurate at all times, transactions may be refused based on a mismatch for transactions from issuing and receiving parties that are not current. 

**Communication**

Once your application has been accepted we will send a confirmation email (personal accounts only), in the case of a business, merchant, central or commercial bank and government your application takes a minimum 5 days to process.

We will send emails we deem fit that you need to be made aware of from time to time, if we cannot communicate with you then you void all rights in using our service.
