# VX Gateway

**A.VX Gateway Inc PRIVACY STATEMENT**  
VX Gateway is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that provides you with a safe and secure online exchange.  
  
**B. USE OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION**  
VX Gateway and its operational service partners collect and use your personal information solely to operate the VX Gateway Web site and deliver the services you have requested.  VX Gateway does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. VX Gateway may, from time to time, contact you about a particular offering in a legal arena that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name) is not transferred to any third party. 

VX Gateway may share data with trusted associates to assist us with statistical analysis. Such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to VX Gateway and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information. 

  
**C. SECURITY OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION**  
VX Gateway secures your personal information from unauthorised access, use or disclosure. VX Gateway secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorised access, use or disclosure. Personal information (such as debit card numbers) is transmitted only through the use of Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encrypted protection.
