# Velocity Micro - Privacy Policy

## Your Privacy is Safe with Us!

We support ethical E-commerce practices. **Velocity Micro** will _not_ sell/trade/rent/give/copy information about you to anyone. 

Any data we may collect about you voluntarily will only be used to enhance or tailor our product offerings and to inform you of our product changes.

 _Call us toll free 800-303-7866 with any questions you have regarding your privacy on our site._ You may submit your e-mail address (see top left) and other information about yourself to be included on our monthly mailing list and special offers list. To remove yourself from this list, send a request to [info@VelocityMicro.com](mailto:nfo@velocitymicro.com) and ask to be removed from future e-mailings, or call us toll free 800-303-7866. **Velocity Micro** has launched a new website with improved e-commerce capabilities allowing for a more efficient shopping experience for our customers. Information we collect on our customers will only be used to facilitate quicker check-out for our return clients. 

**Velocity Micro** , however, will never willfully sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transmit any personally identifiable information about you to any third party without first receiving your permission.  **Velocity Micro** has built its ebusiness through the TRUST of its CUSTOMERS and our COMMITMENT TO THEM. Any transaction, agreement, information, or other communication between you and **Velocity Micro** is solely between you and **Velocity Micro**.
