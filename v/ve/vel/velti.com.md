# Privacy

Velti is committed to preserving your right to privacy when you visit our website. In general you can visit the Velti site without revealing any information about yourself or your company. If and when you provide information for future contact, we guarantee not to pass it on to anyone else. Velti gathers information and statistics regarding web site traffic but does not collect individual information on users. The visitors on the site will always remain anonymous.
