# Privacy

This site is committed to protecting the privacy of its customers. This privacy policy applies to the use of our website including the services and products provided through our website.

How your information is used

You supply certain information when you sign up with us as a customer. This information includes your name, the name of your business, your postal address, email address, telephone number, fax number and how you heard of us. This information will be used in the management of our database and to enable us contact you about the service you have requested.  
We may also use your email address to send you one special offers. If you wish you may opt out from receiving these offers.

In addition, when you use our website we may store your IP address and may use this information to administer our website and help to diagnose any network or server problems. Our website does not use cookies at present but we may introduced the use of cookies in the future to make your use of Velnet Search a better experience. If you do not want Velnet Search or any other site to store cookies in your browser you may configure your browser NOT to accept cookies

  
Information we share with others

Velnet Search will not divulge your personal information to a third party. The following circumstances are however an exemption:

In order to register a domain name we are required to send your name or/and business name, postal address and email address to organisations that formally record the registration; this information may be available to others via a "Whois" search;

If we are required to do so by law or a statutory body;

To a financial institution with whom we hold an account for the purpose of verifying credit status and / or validating financial transactions.

Velnet Search is not responsible for the privacy policy of other companies or website.

  
Credit Cards

WorldPay processes credit card payment for Velnet Search. WorldPay collects your credit card details over a secure server to guarantee your privacy. Velnet Search does not collect or store your credit card details.

  
Links to other sites and banner advertisements

Velnet website contains advertising and links to other websites. No personal information is passed to other websites. Velnet Search is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of linked websites. We would encourage you to read the privacy statements of other websites if you are concerned about your privacy.

  
Consent

Use of the Velnet Search website and services indicates your consent to the collection and use of information as described in this document. Any changes to the document will be posted on this page.
