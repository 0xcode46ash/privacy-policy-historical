# Privacy

### [Privacy](http://www.vegkitchen.com/privacy/)

Any information submitted by visitors on this site is confidential and secure. VegKitchen.com adheres to guidelines that protect visitor privacy online and prohibit data-collection practices. Information will not be shared with any other web sites or advertisers, and we do not sell or rent personal information to third parties.
