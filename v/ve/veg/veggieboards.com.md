# VeggieBoards! - powered by vBulletin

Worthless Productions provides this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering process and dissemination practices for this website: VeggieBoards.com 

**Information Requested**

Registration is required to view or post on our message boards. Information requested during the registration process includes: user name, password, e-mail address, location, occupation, homepage, interests, and ICQ number. Only the user name, password, and e-mail address are required. All other information is optional and will be visible to all other members. Thus, if you don't feel comfortable completing certain fields, just leave them blank. Even though we require an e-mail address for registration you can opt to have your e-mail address hidden from public view by editing your profile. 

We also offer a directory of websites, businesses, organizations, and resources that may be of interest to our visitors. Some of the sites are submitted by our content team, some by our visitors. Information requested during the sign up process includes: site name, url, description, category, contact name, e-mail address, and a password. All of the information requested on the submission form is required, and with the exception of the contact name and password, visable to anyone viewing the directory. If the website is submitted by our content team we only include information that is readily available on the website we are submitting. 

**How Information Is Used**

We do not sell, rent, or make available your personal information to others. We use customer information we collect only for the purposes of website maintenance and administration, account maintenance, content delivery, and statistical purposes. We also utilize certain products and services that are offered by a third party. These companies may collect personal information. 

  * [PayPal](http://www.paypal.com/) \- to process payments. 



**Cookies**

Our message board software utilizes cookies to help identify you and to customize your experience. The cookies make your use of the site easier, make the site run more smoothly, and help us to maintain a secure site. 

**External Links**

This website contains links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. 

**Public Forums**

This site makes message boards and a website directory available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. 

**Data Quality/Access**

Please ensure the information within your account is accurate and updated as needed. To review, modify, or delete the information in your VeggieBoards! message boards profile please visit the following url: [ http://www.veggieboards.com/boards/usercp.php?s=](http://www.veggieboards.com/boards/usercp.php?s=)

To review, modify, or delete the information in your VeggieBoards! website directory listing please e-mail us at [support@veggieboards.com](mailto:support@veggieboards.com). 

**Contacting This Website**

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this website, or your dealings with this website, you can contact:
