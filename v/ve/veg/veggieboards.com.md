# VeggieBoards.com - Vegetarian and Animal Rights Bulletin Board

Worthless Productions provides this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering process and dissemination practices for this website: VeggieBoards.com 

**Information Automatically Logged**

When you register on our site, we collect personal information from you such as your name and e-mail address. Each new registration is also assigned an account name and password. All of the information provided on the registration page (with the exception of your password) will be viewable by anyone visiting the bulletin board. Thus, if you don't feel comfortable about completing certain fields, just leave them blank. If at any time you would like to edit the information you have provided you may do so by following the link at the bottom of this statement. In addition, your passwords are not encrypted and can be seen by the BB administrators. Do not use a password that you would be afraid to reveal to anyone. 

**How Information Is Used**

We do not sell or rent your personal information to others. We use customer information we collect only for the purposes of website maintenance, account maintenance, and statistical purposes. We also utilize certain products and services that are offered by a third party. These companies may collect personal information. 

  * [HitBox](http://www.hitbox.com/) \- to measure website traffic. 
  * [PayPal](http://www.paypal.com/) \- to process payments. 
  * [MoreOver.com](http://www.moreover.com/) \- provides news feeds. 
  * [SearchBucks](http://www.searchbucks.com/) \- search engine. 



**Cookies**

We use cookies to help identify you when you visit our site and to customize your experience. The cookies make your use of the site easier - allowing you to access our bulletin boards without logging in each time, make the site run more smoothly, and help us to maintain a secure site. 

**External Links**

This website contains links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. 

**Public Forums**

This site makes chat rooms, forums, message boards, opt-in mailing lists, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. 

**Data Quality/Access**

Please ensure the information within your account is accurate and updated as needed. To review, modify, or delete your information please visit the following url:  
[ http://www.veggieboards.com/cgi-bin/ubbs/ubbmisc.cgi?action=editbio](http://www.veggieboards.com/cgi-bin/ubbs/ubbmisc.cgi?action=editbio)

**Contacting This Website**

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this website, you can contact: 

[support@veggieboards.com](mailto:support@veggieboards.com?Privacy%20Policy)
