# Privacy Policy :: Vector Open Stock

Vector Open Stockhaving a principal place of business at Orinoco 5230, Montevideo, Uruguay (hereinafter, referred to as “we”, “our, “us”) collects from the individual user (hereinafter, referred to as “you” or “your”) certain information through the website [http://www.vectoropenstock.com](http://www.vectoropenstock.com/) (hereinafter, referred to as “the Website”).This Privacy Policy contains our policies and procedures governing the collection and handling of information that identifies an individual user or that could be used to contact or locate him or her (hereinafter, referred to as “Personal Information”). This Privacy Policy applies only to the Website. It does not apply to any other websites. 

### 1\. Personal Information collected by us and the purposes of collection

1.1 We collect Personal Information when you contact us using our contact form. If you contact us using our contact form we collect the following Personal Information from you: (1) your name and (2) your email address. This information is used solely for the purpose of replying to your inquiries. 

1.2 When you register an account to use the Website, we collect the following Personal Information from you: (1) your name and (2) your email address. Additionally, you may provide us with information, such as (1) your website name and URL, (2) your occupation, (3) country, and (4) short biography. This information is used solely to enable you to sign in to the Website and access member-only features. 

1.3 If you apply to become a Contributor to the Website, we collect the following Personal Information from you: (1) your name, (2) your email address, (3) your telephone number, (4) your address, and your PayPal ID. We collect this information in order to pay you the sums due by the Company to you and to send you our Contributors newsletter. If your application to become a Contributor is rejected your personal information will be permanently deleted. The term “Contributor” is defined in our Terms and Conditions available [here](https://web.archive.org/terms-and-conditions/). 

1.4 We use the Personal Information collected from you for the following purposes: (1) processing orders; (2) sending updates of terms, products, promotions, and challenges; (3) for improving the quality of our services, and (4) to contact you in relation to your use of the Website.

### 2\. Security

2.1 We employ information security tools to protect Personal Information collected by us. However, even with the use of such tools, we cannot guarantee the security of Personal Information. By using the Website, you acknowledge and agree that we make no such guarantee, and that your use of the Website is at your own risk. 2.2 If you purchase images from us, we use trusted secure payment processors to take payment on our behalf. We do not store credit card details nor do we share customer details with any 3rd parties. 

### 3\. Cookies

3.1 By using the Website, you are agreeing that we can place cookies on your device.

3.2 Cookies in general are text files containing small amounts of information which are downloaded to your device when you visit a website. Cookies are then sent back to the originating website on each subsequent visit, or to another website that recognizes that cookie. Cookies are useful because they allow a website to recognize a user's device. 

3.3 Cookies perform different functions, such as allowing you to navigate between pages efficiently, remembering your preferences, improving the user experience, and ensuring that adverts you see online are more relevant to you and your interests.

3.4 We use cookies to help you navigate the Website efficiently and perform certain functions. For example, cookies allow us to remember your log in details to save you from having to type their username and password every time they visit us, or move from page to page.

3.5 If you do not want to accept cookies, your web browser has settings which allow you to control how you browse websites. These include settings to manage your cookies. Check your browser’s help information or visit the following links for further information:

  * Firefox
  * Chrome 
  * Opera



### 4\. Accessing and correcting your Personal Information

4.1 If you would like to access, modify, or delete your Personal Information stored on our servers, please contact us at the address mentioned in Section 9 of this Privacy Policy.

###  5\. Amendment of this Privacy Policy

5.1 We may change this Privacy Policy at any time by posting a new version on the Website. The new version will become effective on the date it is posted.

### 6\. Retention Period

6.1 We will retain your Personal Information for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required by law.

### 7\. Children

7.1 We do not use the Website to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us at info (at)vectoropenstock dot com. We will delete such information from our files within a reasonable time.

### 8\. Your consent

8.1 By using the Website, you are consenting to our processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us. The term “Processing” includes, but is not limited to, collecting, storing, deleting, using, combining and disclosing information. 

### 9\. Contacting Us
