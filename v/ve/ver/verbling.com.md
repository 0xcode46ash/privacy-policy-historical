## Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important, so Fluency Forums Corporation has created the following Privacy Policy to let you know what information we collect when you visit our Site, why we collect it and how it is used. This Privacy Policy explains the data collection and use practices of verbling.com; it does not apply to other online or offline Fluency Forums Corporation sites, products or services. The terms "you," "your," and "yours" refer to the customer/purchaser utilizing our Site. The terms "Fluency Forums Corporation," "we," "us," and "our" refer to Fluency Forums Corporation and its subsidiaries and affiliates. This Privacy Policy is governed by our Terms and Conditions available at [http://verbling.com/tos](https://web.archive.org/tos). By using this web site, you consent to the data practices prescribed in this statement. We may periodically make changes to this Privacy Policy that we will include on this page. It is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy frequently and remain informed about any changes to it, so we encourage you to visit this page often. 

#### What Information Is Collected?

"Personally Identifiable Information" means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, home or other physical address, an email address, phone number or other contact information, whether at work or at home. Fluency Forums Corporation also automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookie information and the page you request. You can choose not to provide us with certain information, but then you might not be able to take advantage of many of our features 

#### How Is Such Information Used?

We will occasionally email you updates about technology, announce new features, remind you of events, or inform you about other important news. You always have the option to unsubscribe to these messages. 

#### Is The Information Shared or Disclosed?

Fluency Forums Corporation does not rent, sell or share personal information it collects about you to or with marketers. Information collected from you is only used to complete and support your purchases from and use of the Fluency Forums Corporation Site and to comply with any requirements of law. Fluency Forums Corporation may disclose personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (1) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Fluency Forums Corporation or this web site; (2) protect and defend the rights or property of Fluency Forums Corporation; or (3) act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Fluency Forums Corporation, its web sites or the public. If Fluency Forums Corporation should ever file for bankruptcy or merge with another company, we may sell the information you provide to us on this Site to a third party or share your Personally Identifiable Information with any company with whom we merge. 

#### Access.

If you would like to correct, amend or delete any information on the site, you can email us at founders@verbling.com. 

#### International Transfers.

Personal Information collected on this web site may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country in which Fluency Forums Corporation or its affiliates, subsidiaries or agents maintain facilities, and by using this web site, you consent to any such transfer of information outside of your country. 

#### Children.

This Site is not intended for or directed to persons under the age of 13. Any person who provides their information to Fluency Forums Corporation through the Account Login page for new customers, Signup Page, or any other part of the Fluency Forums Corporation Site represents to Fluency Forums Corporation that they are 13 years of age or older. 

#### Cookies.

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser in your computer when you visit our Site. We use cookies to improve our Site and make it easier to use. Cookies permit us to recognize users and avoid repetitive requests for the same information. We mostly use "session cookies" that are automatically deleted after each visit. Cookies from our Site can not be read by other Sites. Most browsers will accept cookies until you change your browser settings to refuse them.
