# Privacy Statement - Verseoftheday.com - A ministry of Heartlight, Inc.

**Privacy Policy**

* * *

Verse of the Day keeps all of our user information (from mailing  
lists, correspondence) **_strictly confidential_** , unless granted personal,  
individual permission to publish such information.

This means that we will not exchange your e-mail address with **_anyone,_**  
**_for any reason_** outside of The Verse of the Day Organization.  
 

As with most sites, we collect general information on visits to our  
 pages. This information is used only for statistical purposes.  
_None of this information is linked back to you as an individual._

If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact us  
 at: [info@verseoftheday.com](mailto:info@verseoftheday.com) for further details  
of our security procedures and policies.

Sincerely,  
_Ben Steed_  
Operator of Verse of the Day
