# Privacy

**Privacy Statement**

Vera Bradley's web servers collect the domain names of visitors to our web sites. This information is aggregated to measure number of visits, average time spent on the site, pages viewed, etc. Vera Bradley uses this information to measure the use of our site and to improve the content of our site.

Vera Bradley's web site may contain links to others sites, and we make every effort to only link to sites that share our high standards and respect for privacy. However, we are not responsible for the content or the privacy practices employed by other sites.

Vera Bradley only collects personal information from our visitors on a voluntary basis. Personal information may include name, title, company, address, e-mail address, phone number, etc. Unless specifically stated, we do not require this information to obtain access to any part of our site. The personal information we collect is used by Vera Bradley only for the specific purpose for which it is submitted, as indicated on our web site. 

We do not sell or rent or transmit personal information to other organizations.

If you would like to correct your information or remove your name from our records, please write us at:

Vera Bradley  
Attention Marketing Department  
2208 Production Road  
Fort Wayne, Indiana 46808

Please include your full name, e-mail address and phone number in case we have questions.

Vera Bradley does everything it can to keep all information from our online visitors as secure as possible against unauthorized access and use. All information is protected by Vera Bradley's security measures, which are periodically reviewed.

Vera Bradley reserves the right to change, modify, add to or remove portions of this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for any changes.
