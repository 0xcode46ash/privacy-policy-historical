# Vertain | Privacy Policy

|   
Privacy Statement 

Our Privacy Policy, described below, reflects our firm commitment to maintain the privacy of the organizations and individuals that we serve. **We do not disclose to the public or to third parties any information about our clients, including their identity.**

Benchmark Data   
Data for our industry benchmarks (e.g., the [eTailer Index](https://web.archive.org/m.q?req=peti) and the [eService Index](https://web.archive.org/m.q?req=pesi)) is collected at our own expense, and is displayed on our Web site as a public service. The organizations listed in these benchmarks may or may not be subscribers to our services.  Information Gathered   
Our site offers forms where we gather visitors' contact information (such as name, and e-mail address). This information helps us provide better service to our visitors, for example, e-mailing of information and alerts. Visitors may also send us e-mails. We read every e-mail sent to us, and may keep some of the e-mails for future reference.  Information Usage    
We use visitor contact information gathered from contact forms or e-mails to respond to inquiries and send information, alerts and other data. Visitors may opt-out of receiving such information; see the choice/opt-out section below.  Choice/Opt-Out    
If you wish to opt-out of receiving communications from us, please request this by e-mailing us at [help@vertain.com](mailto:help@vertain.com) Correcting, updating or deleting contact information    
If you wish to correct, update or delete contact information from our databases, please request this by e-mailing us at [help@vertain.com](mailto:help@vertain.com) |
