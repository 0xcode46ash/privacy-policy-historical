Privacy Policy 

At Veremedia, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Any information we collect, such as email addresses, is submitted at the visitor's will. We do not and will not share any personal information with any third parties unless by governmental order. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Veremedia does not own or control, or to people that Veremedia does not employ.

**What Information Is Collected?**

Veremedia Network contains more than 1,000 websites to which we deliver advertisements and which you - the web surfer - may occasionally visit. During your visit to the Veremedia Network, Veremedia and/or our third party vendors may collect certain information, as follows.

**Personally Identifiable Information**

"personally identifiable information" or "PII" means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, home or other physical address, an email address, phone number or other contact information, whether at work or at home. 

To access certain websites in the Veremedia Network, we may ask you to register and provide certain PII. Veremedia is committed to making sure that you have clear notice and choice before sharing any PII and that you have opted-in to our use of such information. Web surfers who have provided PII may elect to have their data deleted from our files, or kept from being used for purposes other than for the requested service, by following the procedures set forth below in the "Access" section below.

**Non-Personally Identifiable Information**

Veremedia also automatically receives and records anonymous information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website which is part of the Veremedia Network. These server logs may include your Internet Protocol address, browser type, page you request, one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser, and responses by a web surfer to an advertisement delivered by us or our third party advertising vendor. 

Veremedia also collects non-personally identifiable information about a web surfer visiting and/or registering on one or more websites owned by Veremedia or participating in the Veremedia Network. Examples of such non-personally identifiable data include age, gender, and interests. 

As a rule, all of the foregoing information is anonymous and cannot, by itself or in combination, be linked to identify a specific person.   
Web surfers may elect not to provide non-personally identifiable information by following the cookie opt-out procedures set forth below. 

**How Is Such Information Used?**

**Non-personally identifiable information**

Veremedia will only use your non-personally identifiable information on an aggregate basis (i.e. information would be collected into groups, in order to better target advertising and content across the Veremedia Network and to rank advertising performance. We may also share such aggregated information with our subsidiaries and third party partners for similar use. Veremedia will not sell your non-personally identifiable information to third parties for direct marketing purposes.

**Personally identifiable information**

At some sites in the Veremedia Network, we collect PII from individuals and companies that wish to enter into business relationships with Veremedia, for instance web publishers who seek to become a part of Veremedia's Network. We use such information for purposes of administering the Veremedia Network program, including maintaining account information, sending payments, etc. 

Veremedia may disclose personally identifiable information to third parties which seek the disclosure of it, and which make a legally-compliant request for its disclosure. If Veremedia should ever file for bankruptcy or merge with another company, or share your PII with any company with whom we merge or that acquires all or substantially all of our stock or assets to permit the continued use of your PII for the purposes you consent to under this Privacy Policy.

**Policy Prohibiting Use of Adware or Spyware**

Veremedia adheres to a policy of not using or partnering with any downloadable applications that are downloaded or collect personally identifiable information from the user without notice and consent.

**Access**

Veremedia allows visitors to the Veremedia Network who have provided us with their PII to access, correct, amend or delete any information they have provided by sending us an email at the contact email address listed below.

**Choice**

**Non-personally identifiable information**

Veremedia uses technology, including technology from its third party advertising vendors, which includes cookies, "web beacons" and/or "action tags". A "cookie" is a small text file stored by your browser on your computer when you visit the Veremedia Network. A "web beacon" or "action tag", is a small image (usually a 1x1 pixel) embedded in a web page or email. We use cookies, web beacons and action tags to collect non-personally identifiable data to provide better and more relevant services and messages to that user. Cookies, web beacons and action tags permit us to recognize users and avoid repetitive requests for the same information. Cookies from the websites in the Veremedia Network can not be read by other websites. Most browsers will accept cookies unless you change your browser settings to refuse them and opt-out of the use of Veremedia cookies and those of our third party advertising technology vendors. However, some Veremedia Network features will not function properly if your cookies are disabled. For more information on cookies and how to manage and delete them, please visit: [www.allaboutcookies.org/manage-cookies/](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/manage-cookies/)

**Personally identifiable information**

When you sign up for a particular service that requires registration, we ask you to provide certain PII. If we use this PII in a manner different than the purpose for which it was collected, then we will seek your consent prior to such use. If we propose to use PII for any purposes other than those described in this Privacy Policy and/or in the specific service notices, we will offer you an effective way to opt out of the use of personally identifiable information for those other purposes. You can decline to submit PII to any of our services when requested or permitted to do so, but in that case Veremedia may not be able to provide those services to you. 

**The Security of Your Information**

Veremedia uses appropriate physical, managerial, and technical safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of your personally identifiable information However, please note that this is not a guarantee that such information may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed by breach of any of our physical, technical, or managerial safeguards.

**International Transfers**

Personally identifiable information collected on the Veremedia websites may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country in which Veremedia or its affiliates, subsidiaries or agents maintain facilities, and by using this web site, you acknowledge and agree that you may be transferring your personally identifiable information outside of the country in which you have contributed it and that you consent to any such transfer of information outside of your country. These countries may not have similar data protection laws to those that apply in the European Economic Area (EEA). However, if we transfer your information outside of the EEA in this way, we will take steps to ensure that your privacy rights continue to be protected in compliance with applicable data protection law and this Privacy Policy. Details of companies and countries processing your PII on our behalf will be provided on request, by writing to one of our contact addresses at the end of this Privacy Policy.

**Children**

Veremedia's websites, products and services are not intended for or directed to persons under the age of 13. For that reason, Veremedia does not knowingly collect or maintain PII or non-personally identifiable information on the Veremedia websites from persons under 13 years of age. If you are under 13 years of age, please do not use or access the Veremedia websites at any time or in any manner. Any person who provides their information to Veremedia through the login page for new customers or website users or any other part of the Veremedia Network represents to Veremedia that they are 13 years of age or older. If Veremedia learns that PII of persons under 13 years of age has been collected on the Veremedia websites without verified parental consent, then Veremedia will take appropriate steps to delete this information.

**Your Consent to this Privacy Policy**

By using the Veremedia Network, you agree to this Privacy Policy. This is our entire and exclusive privacy policy and it supersedes any earlier version. 

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. When we do, we will also revise the "last updated" date at the Privacy Policy. For changes to this Privacy Policy that may be materially less restrictive on our use or disclosure of the personally identifiable information you have already provided to us, we will attempt to obtain your consent before implementing such revisions with respect to such information, or notify you either by placing a prominent notice on the home page of our web site or by directly sending you a notification. For all other changes, it is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy frequently and remain informed about any changes to it, so we encourage you to visit this page often. Your continued use of one or more websites within the Veremedia Network constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Policy and any updates to it.
