# Privacy policy

Thank you for visiting our website, vbtutor.net. Our privacy policy describes how we collect and use personal information at our website . We are committed to protect and safeguard your privacy while visiting our web site. We may change our privacy policy from time to time but any changes will be updated in this page. 

**Collection of Personal Information**

Our website vbtutor.net collects personally identifiable information such as name and email address when visitors voluntarily fill up forms to give feedback in our guest book or participate in our mailing list. This information is only used to improve our website and to provide better services to our visitors .

**The use of Cookies**

Our website uses cookies for gathering non-personal information such as browser type, operating system and number of visitors to our site. Cookies are also employed by our advertisers such as Google to serve and display advertisements on our website,vbtutor.net If you do not wish to view those advertisement, you may configure your web-browser to disable the cookies. 

**Dissemination of Information**

Please be assured that we will not share, distribute and sell your personal information to outside parties.The information collected is solely used to serve our visitors better.

**Data Security**

We assure you that your personally identifiable information is kept secure all the time through the implementation of a variety of security measures.

**Contact Information**

If you have any questions about our privacy policy you may contact us using the information below:
