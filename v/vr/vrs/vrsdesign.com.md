### __Our Commitment to Privacy

At VRS DESIGN, we value our customers’ right to privacy.   
VRS Design’ Privacy Policy is to help our customers understand how we collect, secure, and use the information our customers provide to us at vrsdesign.com.   
We recommend that our customers review our Privacy Policy if they are concerned about how the information is used.  


### __Terms

#### Online Account Registration

When you register on vrsdesign.com, you make shopping faster and easier. If you have registered an account with us, your billing address, shipping address, and account information only need to be entered once and will be securely stored in our database. You will be able to access your account online to modify or delete information. For non-registered users, we request for email, billing, and shipping information for each purchase. 

#### Information Collected by vrsdesign.com

When you shop at vrsdesign.com, we collect information we need to complete your transaction. Information we collect may include:

Your Name / Shipping Address / Billing Address / Telephone Number / Email Address

We do not ever share our customer's personal information.

#### Billing Information

#### Shipping Information

We use this information to send your order that you had placed.

#### Email Information

We use this information to send your order that you had placed.

#### Cookies:

Cookies are small data texts files that web browsers save when you visit some websites. We use cookies to make your shopping experience on vrsdesign.com more convenient and more personalized. For example, you can retrieve an earlier shopping car with the saved cookies on your web browser.
