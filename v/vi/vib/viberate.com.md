# Privacy policy | Viberate - smarter way to explore live music

### PRIVACY POLICY

#### Intro

This privacy policy sets out how Viberate uses and protects any information that you give us when using Viberate.com. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. All information provided by you will only be used in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Privacy Policy may be changed, revised or amended by Viberate by posting the changes under this section. You are advised to periodically visit this page to review the current Privacy Policy.

  


#### Collected Information

Viberate may collect the following information: \- Contact information including email address and phone number. \- Demographic information such as postal code, age, gender, preferences and interests. \- Other information relevant to user segmentation (profession, musical tastes). \- Your logo, contact and other information and links to your social media channels (if you are an artist) or logos, contact and other information and links to social media channels of the artists, labels or venues you represent. \- Login credentials for third-party websites. We also aggregate information on what Viberate.com visitors (whether logged into the Service or not) access while browsing the website. However, you are the one in control of our possibility to collect and retain your personal information. You may contact us to delete any/all of your personal information and you can completely delete your account at any time. In the case of account deletion, all your personal information will be deleted in 14 days.
