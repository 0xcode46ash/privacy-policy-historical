# VividSmoke.com

**Privacy Policy:** Vivid Smoke is committed to protecting private information collected on our website. This privacy policy is intended to provide you with the information on how we collect, use and store the information that you provide, so that you can make appropriate choices for sharing information with us. 

**The information we collect:**

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the VividSmoke.com website. On some pages, you can order products, make requests, and register to receive materials. The types of personal information collected at these pages are: 

Name   
Address   
Email address   
Phone number   
Credit/Debit Card Information   
(etc.)


On some pages, you can submit information about other people. For example, if you order a gift online and want it sent directly to the recipient, you will need to submit the recipient's address. In this circumstance, the types of personal information collected are:

Name   
Address   
Phone Number   
(etc.) 
  
Vivid Smoke will NOT, in any way, shape, or form, disclose your information outside of the activities you participate here within. Occasionally, information that we collect, such as your email address may  be used to send out newsletters, promotions or discount offers. As such, it is voluntary for the customer to receive offers through Vivid Smoke.

Vivid Smoke uses secure and SSL technology for areas that require payment transactions or personal information received by the customer.
