# Video Game Countdown - Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 4, 2008**

Video Game Countdown knows that you care about how your information is used. We strive to collect only information that is necessary for you to enjoy this website. The following is our privacy policy.

### What information does Video Game Countdown gather?

We gather information that you give us, such as username, password, and email address. We also use cookies to store information while you are visiting Video Game Countdown. Cookies are data stored on your hard drive, such as a cookie that stores what products are in your shopping cart. 

### Does Video Game Countdown Share the Information It Receives?

We are not in the business of selling or sharing customer information. Any information you provide will only be used at Countdown Network, LLC websites and Amazon.com. The information given to Amazon.com is limited to items such as what is in your shopping cart.

 _We are an Amazon Associate in regards to purchases and preorders. Once you begin a checkout, Amazon`s privacy policy applies._
