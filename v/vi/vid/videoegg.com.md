 **VideoEgg Website Privacy Policy**

This privacy policy covers how VideoEgg, Inc. (“VideoEgg”) collects and uses information at its website located at: www.videoegg.com and generally for its other services such as, the ad network service commonly known as the VideoEgg Ad Platform Online Program (the “Program”). To get a better understanding of the Program and how VideoEgg collects and uses information in connection with the Program, click [here](https://web.archive.org/privacy/description).

Please note that this privacy policy only applies to information VideoEgg collects at this site and through our Services and not any websites, webpages, blogs, applications, widgets or other experiences operated by any third party including our affiliates, advertisers, providers and partners.

We know that some of the words and concepts used in this privacy policy may be difficult to understand. To help you, we’ve included **a brief glossary of terms** which can be accessed by clicking [here](https://web.archive.org/privacy/glossary). 

This privacy policy does not apply to: (i) people employed by VideoEgg or to entities owned or controlled by VideoEgg; and (ii) any websites, webpages, blogs, applications, widgets or other experiences offered by our participating Affiliates and Advertisers.

**Collection and User of Personally Identifiable Information.**  
In general, VideoEgg does not collect or use personally identifiable information on this site, except as set forth below. 

_Registration for Services._ This site includes registration pages for certain of VideoEgg’s Services, including the Program. VideoEgg may collect personally identifiable information from people who want to register to participate in the Program as an Affiliate. A description of VideoEgg’s data collection and use practices in connection with the Program are described in the description of the Program which can be accessed by clicking [here](https://web.archive.org/privacy/description).

_User Communications._ When you send email or other communication to us, we may retain those communications in order to process your questions, respond to your requests and improve this site or VideoEgg’s Services. 

**Collection and Use of Non-Personally Identifiable Information on this site.**  
When visiting this site, VideoEgg or its providers collect non-personally identifiable information that allow us to better understand how visitors interact with the site. VideoEgg implements certain technologies to enable us to collect such information. An explanation of those technologies is described below. 

_Cookies._ VideoEgg uses both session and persistent cookies on this site. We use cookies to assist us in understanding how our site is used and improving our site. 

Our use of cookies on this site is anonymous and does not allow us to gather personally identifiable information about you.

If you do not want us to maintain cookies while you are visiting this site, you can configure your browser’s settings to limit the acceptance of or disable cookies. Please note that some aspects of the site may not properly function or display if you limit or disable cookies.

_Web Beacons/Click-stream Data._ VideoEgg uses web beacons in conjunction with cookies to collect click-stream data that helps us better understand how visitors move from page to page within our site. As with cookies, our use of web beacons is anonymous and does not allow us to gather personally identifiable information about you. 

_Passive Browser Information/Server Logs._ VideoEgg collects passive browser information through its web servers. We use the passive browser information and server logs to help us understand how many visitors come to the site, where they may come from and ultimately to help us make decisions about how to organize this site.

_Demonstrations._ VideoEgg from time to time will offer visitors to the site the ability to see demonstrations of different Services offered by VideoEgg. To the extent you view demonstrations, we may collect non-personally identifiable information in connection with the demonstration of the applicable Service. Our collection, storage and use of non-personally identifiable information in connection with such demonstrations is subject to the terms applicable to such Service. 

**Information Sharing.** We make efforts to ensure data security on our systems to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. However, no data transmissions over the Internet can be guaranteed to be secure. As a result, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us and you do so at your own risk.

**Information relating to children.**  
VideoEgg holds all personally identifiable information in confidence. There are instances in which personally identifiable information and non-personally identifiable information may be shared with third parties.

_Non-personally identifiable information._ In connection with this site, we may share non-personally identifiable information with our family of companies or other trusted businesses or persons for purposes of processing non-personally identifiable information or providing other services on our behalf.

_Personally identifiable information._ VideoEgg only shares personally identifiable information with other companies or individuals outside of VideoEgg as follows: (i) if VideoEgg has your consent for the sharing of personally identifiable information; or (ii) to assist in the fulfillment of requests for services that you’ve requested from VideoEgg (for example, we may use a third party to help send e-mails in response to requests you’ve made). Note that any third party that VideoEgg chooses to share personally identifiable information will be required to enter into a confidentiality agreement with VideoEgg. VideoEgg may share your personally identifiable information to respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, to establish or exercise our legal rights or, defend against legal claims and if in our judgment in such circumstances disclosure is required or appropriate. In addition, VideoEgg may share your personally identifiable information if we believe it is necessary in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of the terms and conditions that may apply to our Services, or as otherwise required by law. 

If VideoEgg sells all or part of its business or Services, information you have shared with VideoEgg or VideoEgg has collected (both personally identifiable and non-personally identifiable) may be one of the business assets that will be transferred. The transferred information will remain subject to the promises made in this privacy policy or subsequent policies to which you have consented. 

**Data Security.**  
VideoEgg make efforts to ensure data security on our systems to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. However, no data transmissions over the Internet can be guaranteed to be secure. As a result, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us and you do so at your own risk.

**Information relating to children.**  
This site is not structured to attract children under 13 years of age. Our Services prohibit use of the Services by anyone under 13 years of age, and require any person providing access to the Service to anyone under 13 years of age to certify that he or she has the consent of the parent or legal guardian of such person and to provide reasonable verification required by VideoEgg. VideoEgg does not knowingly collect or maintain personal information of anyone under 13 years of age, and if we discover that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will promptly delete it. Parents and legal guardians may request us to review, delete or stop the collection of personally identifiable information of their child by contacting us at the email or postal addresses listed below.

**Collection and Use of Non-Personally Identifiable Information in the Program.**  
_Other Third Party Sources_ Videoegg may supplement the information it collects with other non-personally identifiable information it obtains from third parties.

**Modifications to Privacy Policy.**  
This privacy policy may be modified by VideoEgg in its sole discretion at any time without prior notice to you. VideoEgg will post any revised privacy policy on this site. Your continued access of this site and your use of Services after the privacy policy has been modified and posted will signify your acceptance of such modifications for information provided by you.
