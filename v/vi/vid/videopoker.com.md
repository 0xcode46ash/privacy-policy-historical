# VideoPoker.com Privacy Policy

## Information Collection and Use 

We collect a limited amount of personal information when you register on **VideoPoker.com**. This information is used to keep you informed about new games coming into the marketplace and to occasionally send you information regarding video poker and our Web site. 

## Requirements to Join VideoPoker.com

We require very little information when you join: your e-mail, your personally chosen password, your name, your zip code and confirmation that you are at least 21 years of age. 

## Use of your information

Any personal information that you provide to our Web site shall stay safe, private and secure. We do not resell personal information to outside sources. Like almost every other Web site, our systems may log information when you visit including your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. Our Web site may place cookies on your computer to store information about your visits.

## External Links

This site contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these other Web sites. 

##  Questions or Suggestions 

If you have questions or suggestions please send an email to [comments@videopoker.com](mailto:comments@videopoker.com).
