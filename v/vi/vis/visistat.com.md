# KickFire Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy 

Welcome to the VisiStat, Inc. (doing business as KickFire (“KickFire”) Privacy Policy, effective as of April 20, 2017. By accessing any and all pages on this website and all affiliated websites, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. We respect your privacy and take it seriously. So please read this Privacy Policy carefully. Your use of this website means that you consent to KickFire’s collection, use and disclosure of information we receive from or about you. This Privacy Policy is governed by this website’s Terms of Use, including limitation of KickFire’s liability to you, so please review them as well. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy or KickFire’s Terms of Use, you may not use this website.

KickFire may, from time to time, change this Privacy Policy so we also ask that you occasionally review it and always review it before providing new personally identifiable information as defined below. Each of these changes will take effect 30 days from the date on which it is first published on this website. This website collects the following information from you:

We collect your personally identifiable information on this website only if you voluntarily share it with us. By “personally identifiable information” we mean that which could identify you as an individual. Examples of this are an individual’s name, home address, telephone number, billing and shipping information, credit card information, other personal contact information and personal e-mail address.

We collect other information that’s not personally identifiable information as defined above. This includes, without limitation, information that’s publicly available, the IP address of the requesting computer, its internet browser and operating system, the date, time and duration of your visit, path taken through this website, clickstream information and the web pages which you visit.

Here’s what we do with it:

Our lawful use, copying, retention and disclosure of any information or data that is not personally identifiable information as defined above is not restricted or affected by this Privacy Policy.

Personally identifiable information is treated differently. We may use it in conjunction with the reasons you sent it to us and may use, copy, retain and disclose it for other lawful purposes, including sharing it with our affiliates, associates and partners such as advertisers, linked sites and independent contractors except that we will not ever resell it unless in aggregate or similar form that does not identify you as an individual.
