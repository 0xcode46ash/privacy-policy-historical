# Myrtle Beach Hotels, South Carolina Resorts & SC Travel - Myrtle Beach Marketing Cooperative

### Internet Privacy Statement

  
**Contact Information:**

e-mail: [enews@mbchamber.org](mailto:enews@mbchamber.org "enews@mbchamber.org")

**Data Collection for Statistics:**

We use data-tracking software and devices (which may include "cookies") to aide us in determining visitor behavior. Our data-tracking software collects the following information: 

  * The name of the domain from which you access the Internet 
  * The web site from which you linked directly to our web site, if you have used another web site to reach our site 
  * The operating system and web browser you use to view pages 
  * The date and time you access our web site 

This information is used solely for research purposes and helps us in improving our site for our visitors.

**Opt-in e-mail Newsletter and Visitor Information Form Requests:**

Information obtained by an e-mail newsletter request form or a visitor information request form will be used for the sole purpose set forth in the form. E-mail newsletters will be mailed monthly and the information provided on this form will help us determine what information you are interested in receiving. This form collects your name, address and e-mail address and the topics of interest to you. This information is not shared with third parties.

Visitor Information request forms are received and information is mailed. Providing your e-mail address on this form will include you in a one-time e-mail mailing about information on the Myrtle Beach Area. You will then have an opportunity to opt-out of these monthly messages. The names and addresses collected from the Visitor Information form is provided to third party chamber member subscribers and information from these parties will be included in the visitor information pack sent by the chamber.

**Consent:**

By visiting the Myrtle Beach Area Chamber of Commerce Web site, you expressly consent to the collection and use by the Myrtle Beach Area Chamber of Commerce of personally identifiable information according to the Internet Privacy Statement above.

**Opt-out:**

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please send an e-mail to [enews@mbchamber.org](mailto:enews@mbchamber.org "enews@mbchamber.org")and request to be taken off of all e-mail correspondence.

If you have sent a visitor information request form and no longer wish to be receive this information, please contact the chamber at one of the following:

**Myrtle Beach Area Chamber of Commerce, 1200 N. Oak Street, P.O. Box 2115, Myrtle Beach, SC 29578 - Tel: (843) 626-7444 or Toll Free: (800) 356-3016**

e-mail: [enews@mbchamber.org](mailto:enews@mbchamber.org "enews@mbchamber.org")

Please provide us with your exact name, mailing address and e-mail address, if applicable.
