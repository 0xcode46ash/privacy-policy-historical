This privacy policy explains how we may collect, store, use and disclose any personal information that you provide to us when using this website. _**Collection**_  
We may collect your personal information (including, but not limited to, your name, email address, and country) depending on your dealings with us.  
We may collect personal information directly from you when you email us.

_**Automated Collection**_  
When you visit this website, we may use automated monitoring software to collect and store certain information about your visit. However, this automated collection will not collect your name, address, email or any other information that can be used to personally identify you.

_**Use and Disclosure**_  
We may use your information to: 

  1. Contact you
  2. Analyze usage of this website
  3. Improve the content of this website



We will not sell, rent or lease your personal information to third parties. Your personal information will be made available internally for the above purposes and we may also disclose your personal information to third parties, who have agreed to treat your personal information in accordance with this privacy policy, for similar purposes only.  
By providing us with your personal information, you consent to our using and disclosing your personal information in the manner set out above.

We will only use or disclose personal information that you have provided to us, or that we have obtained about you: 

  1. For the above mentioned purposes.
  2. If you have authorized us to do so
  3. If we have given you notification of the intended use or disclosure and you have not objected to that use or disclosure.
  4. If we believe that the use or disclosure is reasonably necessary to assist a law enforcement agency or an agency responsible for national security in the performance of their functions.
  5. If we believe that the use of disclosure is reasonably necessary to enforce any legal rights we may have, or is reasonably necessary to protect the rights, property and safety of us, our customers, or others.



If we are authorized, required, or permitted by law to disclose the information.
