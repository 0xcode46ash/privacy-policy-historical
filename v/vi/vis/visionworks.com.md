# Visionworks Marketing & Communications

Your privacy is important to Visionworks Marketing & Communications. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

 **The Information We Collect:** This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the Visionworks Marketing & Communications Web site. On some pages you can place orders or request custom quotes. The types of personal information collected at these pages are: name, business name, phone, fax, address, e-mail address.

 **The Way We Use Information:** We use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order or requesting a custom quote only to complete that order or fulfill that quote. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order. We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties. We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our Web site and to share with advertisers. This information may indicate statistics/numerical data or other demographic, but we will not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals. Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.

 **Our Commitment To Data Security:** To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

 **How To Contact Us:** Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please call us at 573-449-8567 or [send us an email.](mailto:marketsmart@visionworks.com)
