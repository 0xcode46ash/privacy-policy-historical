# Privacy Policy | Vin DiCarlo

**Privacy Policy**  
This is the privacy policy of the web site www.VinDiCarlo.com.  Your privacy is guaranteed, and your private information will never be shared with the public. By entering your email on our website, you simply grant us permission to contact you at any point in the future with free advice relating to dating and attracting women, as well as our own offers, or offers extended to you from companies with whom we are affiliated.

We include a simple 1-click unsubscription link in each email, and you can unsubscribe at any time. Please allow up to 10 days from the date of unsubscribing for us to update our records and remove you from our mailing list. For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes information of your browser, IP address, City/State/Country.  The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded, used to improve the content of our Web page, used to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual visitor. We reserve the right to sell any email addresses entered into this website at any point in the future. With respect to cookies: We use cookies to store visitors preferences, record user-specific information on what pages users access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors’ browser type or other information that the visitor sends. With respect to Ad Servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we may have relationships with other companies like Google (www.google.com/adsense) that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information. To contact us, you may do so by writing an email to support[at]vindicarlo.com. For further information about the privacy policy of Google, please visit: http://www.google.com/privacy.html.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.
