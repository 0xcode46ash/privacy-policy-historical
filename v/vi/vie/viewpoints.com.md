### What We Collect

Viewpoints collects personal information when you register with the Viewpoints Site, when you use our products and/or services, when you visit the Viewpoints Site pages or the pages of certain Viewpoints partners, when you enter promotions and sweepstakes via the Viewpoints Site and when you participate in market research studies sponsored by Viewpoints. Viewpoints may combine information about you we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.

When you register with Viewpoints, we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, ZIP code and/or password among other contact requirements. The registration form information requests may change from time to time. When you register with Viewpoints and sign in to our service, you are not anonymous to us.

Any additional information, including personal information, you include in a user profile or post to the Viewpoints Site will be publicly available and may be accessed by visitors to the site. Any information you choose to include in a user profile or post to the Viewpoints Site is provided at your sole discretion.

Viewpoints automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP Address, Viewpoints cookie information, and each page you request.

Viewpoints uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

Children: Because the Viewpoints Site is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18, Viewpoints does not knowingly collect any personal information from persons under the age of 18 and will automatically block registration of any person if they indicate they are under the age of 18. If we learn that any user is under the age of 18, we will terminate the account and delete the user's information. Inherent in the Viewpoints service is the responsibility of parents and/or guardians to ensure that their children's privacy is protected.

Viewpoints uses third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

### Cookies

We use "cookies" to personalize your viewing of the Viewpoints website, to enable you to access your account and to facilitate the automatic log-in process. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. Viewpoints lets other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies and not this one. Advertisers and other companies do not have access to Viewpoints' cookies.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of www.viewpoints.com or the other websites you visit.

Viewpoints' pages and/or HTML emails may contain single-pixel gifs called "web beacons" that allow us (i) to count users who have visited a certain web page or opened an email, (ii) to conduct research on partner websites for auditing purposes, or (iii) to access your www.viewpoints.com "cookie" to identify you when on third party websites. These web beacons may be used to report aggregate demographic or usage information to our partners. Viewpoints' web pages may also contain web beacons placed there by third parties to help determine the effectiveness of our joint promotional or advertising campaigns. No personally identifiable information about you is shared with partners except as otherwise set forth in this Privacy Policy.
