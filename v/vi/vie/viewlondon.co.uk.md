# Viewlondon - London for Londoners

      ViewLondon Privacy Policy

Viewlondon has developed this Privacy Policy in order to demonstrate its firm commitment to the privacy of your data. Viewlondon is committed to safeguarding the privacy of your data whilst providing a personalised and valuable service. If you have any requests concerning your personal information or any queries with regard to these practices please contact Viewlondon by electronic mail on [info@viewlondon.co.uk](mailto:%20info@viewlondon.co.uk) Viewlondon has a designated privacy officer to ensure your interests are constantly reviewed. 

Information Collected and its Use 

Viewlondon collects personal information from you in order that the Viewlondon service can be offered as effectively as is possible. The data will be used also for the following purposes: 

  * Sending out details of viewlondon competitions.
  * Sending out a weekly viewlondon newsletters.
  * Building marketing profiles.

The data controller will be Viewlondon Limited whose company number is 03855218 and whose registered office is MSP secretaries Limited 22 Melton Street London NW1 2BW. 

In order to ensure that you are fully aware of how data will be used outside of the Viewlondon website services there is an [information on data use page](https://web.archive.org/web/20021207193552id_/http%3A//www.viewlondon.co.uk/full_register.asp#DataUse) where you can opt-out of your data being used in certain ways. If you do not want data to be used in the ways outlined please tick the opt-out boxes positioned next to the descriptions of use. 

If consent is given by you Viewlondon will use the information outside of the uses outlined above for the following purposes:- 

  1. Marketing purposes for Viewlondon.
  2. Viewlondon may market third party products and services.
  3. Viewlondon may pass your data to carefully selected third parties who then in turn contact you for marketing or other purposes.

******Security**

Viewlondon is concerned for the security of data, which it holds and employs a system of user name and passwords on its computer system to ensure that access to the systems is limited to those with authorisation. Viewlondon maintains physical security around its premises to ensure that access cannot be gained to the premises by anyone who is not authorised. 

Viewlondon is constantly reviewing the security measures required to prevent third parties from accessing any data that it holds. 

  
  
**Transfer Of Data**

Viewlondon is constantly reviewing and expanding the area of the world which they wish to cover and therefore it may be necessary to transfer your data outside the European economic area.   
  
**Disclosures**

With the exception of certain uses set out in this Privacy Policy and in accordance with the [information on data use page](https://web.archive.org/web/20021207193552id_/http%3A//www.viewlondon.co.uk/full_register.asp#DataUse), Viewlondon will not disclose any information they keep about you to any third parties unless requested by the police or any regulatory or government authority to provide your information and or information concerning your use of this website. Furthermore Viewlondon will disclose your information and or details of your use of this website if it is required to do so by law or requested to do so by a third party during the course of an action for the infringement of their rights which Viewlondon reasonably believe to arise from your use of this website. 

**Responsibility with You** You should be aware that when you disclose personal information on any public bulletin board or chat areas of this or any other website used as a result of use of this website that information can be collected and used by anyone who views that website and may result in unsolicited messages from other participants or other parties. Viewlondon is not responsible for any such messages. When using any other website if you pass them any information that information will be held in accordance with that website's privacy policies. Please take the time to review these third party privacy policies to ensure that you have no objection to them holding your personally identifiable data. 

  
  
**You have control**

If at any stage you would like to remove, check or amend the information held by Viewlondon about you please send an electronic mail to [info@viewlondon.co.uk](mailto:%20info@viewlondon.co.uk) and Viewlondon will take the necessary action within a reasonable time.   
  
**Change to Privacy Policy**

If Viewlondon changes this Privacy Policy the amended Privacy Policy will be posted on this website and the revised Policy will take effect once posted. Your continued use of this website after the posting will signify your agreement to the new terms. 

If Viewlondon substantially changes the Privacy Policy you will be notified by e-mail to provide you with an opportunity to opt-out of having your data held in accordance with the amended Policy. 

  
**Transmission of Data**   
  
Whilst Viewlondon have taken all reasonable steps to ensure that the information you provide will be kept secure from unauthorised access the internet is not a secure environment and Viewlondon cannot guarantee that the information will be secure during transmission by you to this website.   
  
**Data Protection Commissioner**

The data protection commissioner is aware of Viewlondon's data processing as Viewlondon has notified the Commissioner in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998.
