# Legal Notices

ViewSonic.com is committed to protecting your privacy. We maintain the privacy of your information using security technologies and adhere to policies that prevent unauthorized use of your personal information. By using our Website, you consent to the collection and use of your information. 

**What information does ViewSonic.com collect and how is it used?**

To process some of your requests, we need to know your name, email address and mailing address. This allows us to process your request. 

We might request additional information from you in other areas or in the future. You may choose not to provide this information. We might also ask for information about your interests, or for feedback about our site so that we can improve our site and service. Providing us with this information is always optional. 

In addition to the information we collect directly from you, we also monitor and track usage of our Website, viewing and traffic patterns, customer behavior, and other similar information. This information is compiled into a statistical aggregate. 

We use this aggregate information as well as all of your personal information for internal business and administrative purposes including improvements of our Website, our product and service offerings, our operations, or our customer service. We also might use your personal information from time to time to send you notices about special offers, new products, services, promotions, and other similar information unless you have indicated to us that you do not wish to receive such information. 

Any communication or material you transmit to ViewSonic over this Website, by electronic mail or otherwise, including any data, questions, comments, or suggestions will be treated as, non-confidential and non-proprietary. ViewSonic may use anything you transmit or post to its affiliates for any purpose, including but not limited to reproduction, disclosure, transmission, publication, broadcast and posting. Furthermore, ViewSonic is free to use any ideas, concepts, know-how, or techniques contained in any communication you send to this Website for any purpose whatsoever, including but not limited to developing manufacturing and marketing products using such information. 

**Does ViewSonic.com disclose the information it collects to outside parties?**

ViewSonic does not currently sell or otherwise provide its customer lists or similar information to third parties for their use. 

**ViewSonic.com Privacy Policy Changes**

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will pose these changes here so that you will always know what information we gather, how we might use that information, and whether we will disclose it to anyone.
