Viewpoint Forum Privacy Statement 

_Viewpoint Forum is part of MarketVision Research, a full-service marketing research firm headquartered in Cincinnati, OH._

MarketVision Research, Inc. is a full-service marketing research firm headquartered in the U.S. in Cincinnati, OH. As part of our business, we routinely collect data for the purpose of uncovering insights of relevance to our clients on a variety of topics. To the extent data collection (typically in the form of surveys) involves consumers or business professionals, the results are only used in aggregate with other data and with no personally identifiable information attached. 

We respect your privacy and confidentiality and will never make your personal information available to outside parties. Your information is used only as a way to group responses in providing feedback to our clients, and none of your specific information or responses are looked at on an individual basis. 

MarketVision Research and Viewpoint Forum, our online panel and survey platform, strictly enforce the rules set forth by CASRO concerning the interviewing of respondents 13 years of age or younger, the [Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998](http://www.coppa.org/ "Visit coppa.org \(opens in a new window/tab\)") (COPPA) of 1998. If a survey requests the participation of a minor household member, under the age of 14 years, you can be assured that the rules outlined by COPPA will be followed. 

Your participation in any research with MarketVision Research or Viewpoint Forum is voluntary. If, at any time, you wish to discontinue your participation please simply email us at [pverrill@mv-research.com](mailto:pverrill@mv-research.com "This will open your email application") or [julie@viewpointforum.com](mailto:julie@viewpointforum.com "This will open your email application") and we will remove you from future participation. 

Notification 

  * Any and all data collected by MarketVision Research or via Viewpoint Forum is done so by permission of the respondent providing data.
  * Any and all data collected by 3rd party suppliers are contractually obligated to comply with MarketVision's policies for data handling/management.
  * The nature of any research is communicated upfront and with full transparency.
  * A notification will be sent to respondents via email to gain their cooperation. If at any time you wish to no longer be emailed for surveys or other research purposes, or any purpose at all, you will be removed from future notifications.
  * Any references to deadlines for completion and incentives to be earned are clearly communicated in this notification.



Choice 

  * MarketVision Research and Viewpoint Forum strictly abide by CASRO rules of participation. You can opt-out of any and all participation at any time with no penalty or hesitation. Your participation is entirely voluntary. To opt-out, email either [pverrill@mv-research.com](mailto:pverrill@mv-research.com "This will open your email application") or [julie@viewpointforum.com](mailto:julie@viewpointforum.com "This will open your email application").
  * If there's an opportunity to participate in a non-survey research activity, and if this opportunity includes any sort of media or self-identification, you will always be asked for permission to participate in said activities PRIOR to collection of data. You can continue participation in other research activities and choose not to participate in anything that identifies you in special research requests, with no penalty or hesitation. All participation is by permission only.
  * You may discontinue your participation in research with MarketVision Research or Viewpoint Forum at any time.
  * Any communication requesting participation by others in the household will be clearly communicated to the primary research participation, with no requirement for participation. Data remains secure via all CASRO guidelines.



Onward Transfer 

  * All information collected by MarketVision Research is kept secure and confidentially, and not reported using any personally identifiable information.
  * Any vendors or 3rd parties working with or for MarketVision Research are contractually obligated to comply with MarketVision Research guidelines for confidentiality and data security, and in compliance with CASRO requirements.
  * All third party resources are required to comply with the Safe Harbor privacy principles.



Access 

  * You have the right to know what information has been collected as a result of your research engagement with MarketVision Research and/or Viewpoint Forum. If at any time you have questions about what's being collected, email us at [pverrill@mv-research.com](mailto:pverrill@mv-research.com "This will open your email application") and we can respond accordingly.



Security 

  * MarketVision Research retains data only on secured servers in secured facilities.
  * We will never rent or sell your data for any purpose. Data is used strictly for research purposes.
  * Any personally identifiable information is secure and data is reported only in aggregate. In the rare exception that we would like to share information that could identify you as a participant, your permission will be acquired PRIOR to collection of data and there is no requirement to participate.



Data Integrity 

  * All research data is kept strictly confidential and never shared for any reason outside of the expectations for the research.
  * All personally identifiable information that we collect is done so by permission only, and data stored on secure servers.
  * No personally identifiable information is ever shared without your permission.
  * All personal information we collect is relevant for the purpose for which it is to be used and that to the extent necessary, we take reasonable steps to ensure data is reliable for its intended use, accurate, complete and current.

CASRO Safe Harbor Program 

MarketVision Research is dedicated to ensuring your personal privacy. We strictly adhere to CASRO guidelines for proper data protection, and are contracted with CASRO's Safe Harbor program to ensure security of data between the U.S., Switzerland, and the EU. As part of our participation in the CASRO Safe Harbor program, we are compliant with the seven privacy principles and fifteen frequently asked questions (FAQs) of the U.S.-EU and U.S.-Swiss Safe Harbor Frameworks as developed by the U.S. Department of Commerce in consultation with the European Commission and the Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner of Switzerland. To learn more about the Safe Harbor program, and to view MarketVisions' certification, please visit <http://www.export.gov/safeharbor/>.
