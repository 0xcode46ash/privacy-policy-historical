# Victoria Online: Privacy

  


 on Victoria Online. 

Personal information is information that identifies a particular person. A person’s name is personal information. On its own, some information does not identify a particular person. However, a combination of such information may identify a particular person. This combination of information is also personal information.

We do not collect any personal information unless it is voluntarily provided.

You acknowledge that you may not be able to use certain parts of this website without providing your personal information. Generally, however, we will not ask you to provide any personal information unless it is necessary and appropriate.

If any part of this website requests you to provide personal information, you will be informed of the purpose for which that information is collected and will be used and/or disclosed.

Generally, any personal information collected through this website will be made available only to government personnel whose duties require them to inspect or use the information. Sometimes, this information may also be made available to private contractors or consultants engaged to provide services to, or on behalf of, government.

Any personal information collected through this website will also be disclosed if required by law (eg to the police, the courts).

If you wish to access any personal information we may have collected about you, you may request such access by contacting our Privacy Officer on 03 9655 6224 during business hours. You may also contact our Privacy Officer in relation to any other concerns you may have about the privacy of personal information when using this website.

 

Last updated 23 December 2003
