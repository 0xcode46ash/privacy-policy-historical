# VirtualPROMOTE Privacy Policy Statement

[](https://web.archive.org/web/19981202223011id_/http%3A//www.virtualpromote.com/index.html)  
  
This is a web site operated by **SmartDesk, Inc.**. 

Our postal address is   
**24102 Snipe Lane  
Laguna Niguel, CA 92677**

We can be reached via e-mail at [webmaster@virtualpromote.com](mailto:webmaster@virtualpromote.com)  
Or you can reach us by telephone at 714-360-9915 

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address.

We collect aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit and information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations.

The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded, used to improve the content of our Web page and not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending email to us at the above address and telling us that you do not want to receive e-mail from our company. 

When you supply your name and/or e-mail address when registering to access any of the interactive services on this site, we do not gather that data for any other use than to identify you when you access those services. However, we do not have any control over address harvesters that may invade our web site for the purpose of harvesting addresses for use by other parties.

When you register to be added to one or more of the e-mail lists maintained by us, we do not share that information with any other person or business.
