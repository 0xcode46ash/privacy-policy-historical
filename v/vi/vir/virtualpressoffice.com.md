# Virtual Press Office - Privacy Policy

The following discloses Virtual Press Office, Inc.'s policy for information-gathering and dissemination of personal and non-personal information collected when you visit our site.

Cookies are used to keep track of your registration information, and may also be used to deliver content specific to your interests, and to save any password you provide so it doesn't need to be entered repeatedly. 

Our Web site's registration form requires your contact information (e.g., name, address, organization, and email address). This information may be shared with relevant companies that post content to the site to help assess your interest in their news, product literature and background information. This information may also be used to contact you.. 

You may also choose to sign up for the Release On Demand service, a news profiling system that delivers specific categories of content via email. Your contact details may be passed on to companies that have provided content distributed via Release On Demand. 

We further reserve the right to transfer or otherwise distribute any information we collect from or about you to third parties or to a successor-in-interest. 

We may change the provisions of this policy from time to time and urge you to check back here periodically. Your continued use of any of the websites or pages within the scope of this policy will be your acceptance of the version of the policy in place at the time of your visit.
