# Virtools Privacy Statement

Virtools and Your Privacy

Virtools is committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. Our goal is to give you as much control and information as possible regarding your personal information and the way we use itin the course of our business. We want to be a leader in providing our customers with comprehensive privacy information.

You can visit Virtools on the Web without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself. You can subscribe and unsubscribe yourself from our bi-monthly newsletter directly from our website at any time.

Virtools will never sell, rent or disclose your personally identifiable information to outside parties without your consent unless compelled by law or court order to do so. With your permission, Virtools may send you e-mail regarding our new products, promotions or upgrades directly or via your country's distributor.
