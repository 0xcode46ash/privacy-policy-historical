# 'The Smarter Way To Play' - Privacy Statment

|   
---  
  
E-Lottery collects contact (name, address, phone numbers, email address), personal (date of birth) and payment related information from users applying for membership through the site. This information is used by our staff and/or the system to process the membership application and contact the user with notifications from E-Lottery, including winnings notification. 

E-Lottery is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We do not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement.  
  
  
  
---  
  
Users' membership information (such as postal address, phone numbers, email address, etc) can be updated via the Member Details page located on the member's website. Alternatively the member can place a request for changing this information or terminating his/her membership with E-Lottery via email or telephone. The member can also request to have their details completely removed from our system.  
  
  
  
---  
  
E-Lottery collects information on our site visitors collectively, including which sections of the site are most frequently visited, how often and for how long. This data is always used as aggregated, non-personal information. E-Lottery utilizes this information to improve and enhance our services by monitoring the areas on the site which are most popular to you. The data collected is not linked to personally identifiable information.  
  
  
  
---  
  
A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. By setting a cookie on our site, the user should not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our site  
  
  
  
---  
  
If we change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this web page so our users are always aware of what information we collect and how we use it  
  
  
  
---
