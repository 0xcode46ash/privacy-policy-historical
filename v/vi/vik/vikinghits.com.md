**VikingHits Privacy Policy**

VikingHits will not share, sell, or give away any information regarding their members including but not limited to their name, email address, and other private information. 

VikingHits reserves the right to use member email addresses for the purpose of periodically sending out information relevant to any and all aspects of VikingHits membership. 

VikingHits may require the use of cookies to store login session information. 

VikingHits merely serves as a directory of other websites and is therefore not responsible for the privacy practices and/or content of those websites. 

Financial information is used solely to bill the user for products and/or services. All personal, credit card, and bank account information is transferred by separate institution (i.e. PayPal.com or Payza) over secure SSL encrypted Internet connections and is not stored on VikingHits servers.
