# 13dots - SwishMax And Photoshop Tutorials - Privacy

### 13dots.com Privacy Policy

 **13dots.com respects your privacy.**

13dots.com is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should you be asked to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, then you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement and will not be released, sold, or rented to any entities or individuals outside of 13dots.com. 13dots.com may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. This policy is effective from 13th May, 2004

 **Registration**

To use the 13dots.com website we ask you to complete a registration process. During registration a user is required to give their valid e-mail address for verification purposes. A username is also required, which can be a nickname if you wish to remain anonymous. This information is to identify the user, and for other users to interact with the user. None of this information will be sold, transmitted, or in any other way sent to any third parties. You also may be asked for your location, instant messenger names, skills etc but these are only optional and you may leave them empty. 

**Cookies**

Using cookies is optional, but strongly recommended as they identify the user when he/she logs in. Cookies are used to track topics on our Forums, showing you which topics have new replies since your last visit and to automatically log you in when you return. If your computer is unable to use the cookie system to browse the board correctly, then the board will automatically add in a session ID to each link to track you around the board. A "cookie" is a small data text file that is placed in your browser and allows 13dots.com to recognize you each time you visit this site(customization etc). Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and 13dots.com does not use cookies to collect personal information. Cookies may also be used by 3rd party content providers such as newsfeeds. 

**External Sites And Members Blogs.**

13dots.com contains members blogs and links to other sites. Please be aware that 13dots.com is not responsible for the written content of each blog posted at this site, nor the privacy practices of external internet sites. You are advised to read the privacy policy of external sites before disclosing any personal information.

 **Remember The Risks Whenever You Use The Internet**

While we do our best to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee the security of any information that you transmit to 13dots.com. You are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of any passwords or other account information. In addition, other Internet sites or services that may be accessible through 13dots.com have separate data and privacy practices independent of us. 13dots.com disclaims any responsibility or liability for their policies or actions. 

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, please do not hesitate to contact us!
