# Privacy and Shipping Information

**SECURITY/PRIVACY**

Your privacy is very important to us. We never disclose your name, e-mail address, or any other personal information provided to us. All information given on forms is optional, except where this information is required to process your order.

All required information is kept internally and is held in strict confidence. We never share your personal information with any third parties.

Using SSL encryption security technology, your online order is processed securely. When placing your order, you will be taken to our secure server prior to being asked for your credit card information.

We do not solicit by phone, email or snail mail. We will contact you only if your order information is incomplete or incorrect.

**SHIPPING**

All orders are processed immediately and are discreetly shipped the following day.

North American orders are shipped by UPS or FEDEX GROUND. Delivery time is 3 to 5 business days. 

PO Box, APO/Military and International orders are shipped by USPS GLOBAL PRIORITY MAIL. 

International orders are delivered within 15 business days. 

Tracking information will be provided through email by the designated carrier. 

Please note that holidays may delay delivery time.
