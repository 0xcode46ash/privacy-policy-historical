# Privacy Policy

All the legal jargon is below, but to summarize the following information it is important to affirm that we never, ever will sell your e-mail address, or any information about you whatsoever to anybody. We wouldn't want someone to do that to us, so we won't do it to you. All ID information is kept on file solely to verify eligibility. All information is destroyed when no further verification is necessary. 

By visiting Xcaret web site you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Xcaret highly respects the privacy of each customer. We appreciate your business and will handle your information very carefully. The following is our privacy policy. 

Xcaret respects the privacy/confidentiality of its users and the importance of the information they entrust through its online services. Personal information gathered by Xcaret will not be sold. The information we receive through our web sites allows us to personalize and improve the shopping experience of our valued customers. The types of information we gather include the following: 

Information You Give Us. Is solely for the purpose of completing or improving our service. We receive and store the information you enter on our Xcaret web site or give us in any other way. You are free to choose not to provide certain information, but that might reduce your ability to take advantage of the full range of our features. We use the information that you provide for such purposes as responding to your requests, customizing future shopping for you, improving our stores, and communicating with you. For example, you provide information when you search for a product; place an order; communicate with us by phone, e-mail, or otherwise; or complete a contest entry form. As a result of those actions, you might supply us with such information as your name, address, and phone numbers; credit card information; people to whom purchases have been shipped, including addresses and phone number; e-mail address and content of e-mails sent to us. 

Information Gathered Automatically. Is solely for the purpose of completing or improving our service. We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with us. For example, like many web sites, we use "cookies," and we obtain certain types of information when your web browser accesses our Xcaret web site. Examples of the information we collect and analyze include, but not limited to, the IP address of your computer; login; e-mail address; password; computer and connection information such as browser type and version, operating system, and platform; purchase history; the full URL click history to, through, and from our web site, including date and time; cookie number; and products you viewed or searched for. 

E-mail addresses collected. Are solely for the purpose of contacting you regarding our service. To help us make e-mail more useful and interesting, you may receive a confirmation when you open e-mail from our Xcaret web site if your computer supports such capabilities. 

Information Supplied by Others. Is used to correct records on hand. We might receive information about you from other sources and add it to our account information. Examples of information we receive from other sources include updated delivery and address information from our carriers or other third parties, which we use to correct our records and deliver your next purchase or communication more easily; account information, purchase or redemption information; search term and search result information. 

Sharing of Information by Xcaret. Occurs when the information is necessary to complete the delivery of products or service. Information about our customers is an integral part of our business, and web Xcaret site does not sell your information to others. We share customer information in the following ways only: Third-Party Agents. Third parties perform services on the behalf of Xcaret web site, including order fulfillment, sending postal mail and e-mail, removing redundant information from customer lists, data analysis, and processing credit card payments. These parties have access to personal information needed to perform their functions, but are not allowed to use it for other purposes. 

Information May be Used to Comply With the Law. We release account and other personal information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law or protect the rights, property, or safety of Xcaret web site, our users, or others. This includes, but not limited to, exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction. However, this does not include selling, renting, sharing, or otherwise disclosing personally identifiable information from customers for commercial purposes contradictory to the details already set forth in this Privacy Notice. 

Cookie Information. A cookie is a small piece of information that many web sites use to help make shopping online easier. It does not cause hazard as a spyware. The information is stored on your computer's hard drive to enable our systems to recognize your browser and to provide features such as personalized greetings, customized item lists, etc. Your web browser has a Help feature that will tell you how to prevent your browser from accepting cookies, how to have the program notify you when you receive a cookie, or how to disallow receipt of cookies altogether. However, these cookies allow our Xcaret web site to offer you most of our best features, and we recommend that you leave them turned on.

To ensure this we use a safe server under the SSL protocol (Secure Socket Layer) which receives the information you send us and encrypts your information for your protection.
