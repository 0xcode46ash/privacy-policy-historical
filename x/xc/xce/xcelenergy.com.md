# Privacy Policy | MaritzCX

**USA** (EN) ([ES](https://www.maritzcx.com/privacy-policy-us-es/)) |  **Canada**  ([EN](https://www.maritzcx.com/privacy-policy-canada/)) ([FR](https://www.maritzcx.com/privacy-policy-canada-fr/)) ([ES](https://www.maritzcx.com/privacy-policy-canada-es/)) |  **Europe**  ([EN](https://www.maritzcx.com/gb/privacy-policy/)) ([DE](https://www.maritzcx.com/de/privacy-policy/)) ([ES](https://www.maritzcx.com/gb/privacy-policy-es/))

MaritzCX Research LLC, its parent organizations, subsidiaries and affiliates (collectively, “MaritzCX”) is committed to building user trust and confidence by promoting, and complying with, business practices that help protect the privacy of our customer and user data. This policy provides our commitment to your privacy by describing our information, practices and policies to ensure compliance with the Privacy Shield privacy framework, as defined by the U.S. Department of Commerce in cooperation with Switzerland and the European Commission.

The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) has jurisdiction over MaritzCX’s compliance with the Privacy Shield program.

MaritzCX has met the requisite seven privacy principles to participate in the Privacy Shield program. For more information about the Privacy Shield program and its benefits visit <https://www.privacyshield.gov/welcome>.

A list of the seven principles and how MaritzCX meets these requirements follow:

NOTICE AND CHOICES

**Children**

MaritzCX is a family-friendly company and does not publish information that is not suitable for children or minors. Examples of pornography, offensive language, graphic pictures, or violence are not published on any MaritzCX site. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of our receipt of personal information of a child under 13 we will take steps to promptly remove such information.

**Information Collection & Use**

MaritzCX will only collect your personal information (such as your name, email, address or phone number) if you provide it voluntarily. The information collected through this website is used by MaritzCX for marketing and/or to contact the visitor to resolve questions and/or concerns.

ONWARD TRANSFER

**Information Sharing and Disclosure**

MaritzCX reserves the right to share your information when required by subpoena, court order, applicable law, government statute, regulation or other legal process. Additionally, we may share your information when we have a good faith belief that the disclosure is necessary to prevent or respond to fraud, defend our websites or Services against attacks, or protect our property and security, or our customers and users. At times, we may append your contact information by purchasing updated address, phone and other corporate information from national list companies. We do this to help ensure relevancy and accuracy in our marketing, promotion and sales efforts. Customers that use MaritzCX products to collect personal information must agree to abide by the same principles followed by us. Additional information describing the procedures and policies that we follow to protect your data can be found in the MaritzCX Data Confidentiality Compliance Policy document (available upon request to [privacycompliance@maritzcx.com](mailto:privacycompliance@maritzcx.com?subject=Compliance%20Policy%20Document%20Request)).

**Legal Disclaimer**

We reserve the right to disclose personal information as required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on our site.

MaritzCX is liable for onward transfer of personal information to third parties.

ACCESS AND DATA INTEGRITY

**Your Account Access**

Usernames and passwords are stored in our databases. All passwords are encrypted. Account information is kept secure and used solely by you to access your client account, and to facilitate communication. Company administrators have permission to make corrections or changes to their account information as needed. User information is stored only when a user voluntarily gives this information for use by a client company. No information is released outside of MaritzCX unless with the authority of the user or as required under applicable law.

**MaritzCX Partners (Technology and Resellers)**

The privacy and security of the data of all of our partners is considered in the architecture of our proprietary platform. The data of all MaritzCX partners is logically isolated from the data of all other MaritzCX partners. The data is stored in a single tenant manner to prevent cross-contamination of any data in the platform. Support personnel of the platform are trained in data privacy policies and procedures that prevent disclosure of a partner’s information. The platform is architected to support the services of multiple partners without conflict.

SECURITY

**Features**

MaritzCX has implemented a number of security features to help prevent the unauthorized release of or access to personal information. Although the company strives to create and maintain a secure and reliable site for you, the confidentiality of any communication or material transmitted to or from MaritzCX via the website or email cannot be guaranteed.

**Cookies**

A cookie is a small string of text that a Web site can send to your browser to assist in distinguishing your browser from a previous visitor by saving and remembering any preferences that may have been set while browsing the particular site. Currently MaritzCX uses cookies only for session-state management and has no current plan to use cookies for any other purpose. We will revise this policy statement accordingly if we elect to begin using cookie technology for any other purpose, so you may wish to check this policy periodically for updates.

ENFORCEMENT

**Opt Out**

You may unsubscribe or opt out of receiving electronic communications from us at any time. We contact customers, prospects, and survey respondents with different types of communication: operational, marketing, and research. To opt out of our marketing communication, go to the [unsubscribe page](http://info.maritzcx.com/UnsubscribePage.html) and enter your email address. Customer operational email preferences can be customized within the platform. When you unsubscribe or opt out of a research program, you will no longer receive any unsolicited electronic communication related to the same program offering. Clicking on an unsubscribe link in the email invitation will take you to a web page where you can complete the opt-out process. Certain programs will provide a contact for support or other means to remove your name from our contact list. Please allow up to 10 business days of our receipt of your request for the unsubscribe or opt out process to take effect.

**Dispute Resolution**

If you have any dispute or claim arising out of or relating to this Privacy Policy, you should report your claim to us at [privacycompliance@maritzcx.com](mailto:privacycompliance@maritzcx.com).

MaritzCX has committed to attempt to resolve privacy complaints in accordance with the EU-US Privacy Shield principles.  If you do not receive timely acknowledgment of your complaint, or if your complaint is not satisfactorily addressed, please visit <https://www.jamsadr.com/eu-us-privacy-shield> for more information and to file a claim with the JAMS EU-US Privacy Shield dispute resolution program.  The services of JAMS are provided at no cost to you.  Under certain circumstances, EU individuals may seek redress from the Privacy Shield Panel, a binding arbitration mechanism.

MaritzCX commits to cooperate with EU data protection authorities (DPAs) and comply with the advice given by such authorities with regard to human resources data transferred from the EU in the context of the employment relationship.
