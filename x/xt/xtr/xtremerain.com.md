# Privacy Policy - XtremeRain

Here are privacy policies. If you want to know more, You should contact us.

##  **XtremeRain Website Privacy Policy**

###  **Cookies and Web Beacons:**

XtremeRain uses cookies to collect and store a little bit information about visitors. We use Google Analytics and WordPress Stats. It may collect your IP address, browser information, device information, operating system information etc. We respect your privacy.

###  **DoubleClick Cookie:**

We use Google ads. Google uses the DART cookie. It helps to serve ads to users based on their visit to www.xtremerain.com and other sites on the Internet.  
You may know more details about it from here [http://www.google.com/policies/technologies/ads](http://www.google.com/policies/technologies/ads/)

Some of advertising partners like Adsense may use web beacons and cookies on our site. Our advertising partners include:

###  **Google Adsense:**

Adsense automatically receive your IP address, browser information and device information. You can fully read it from the link given above.

###  **Other Third-Party Ads:**

Sometimes Adsense may server other third-party ads which also follow google’s rule. So, These policies are same. You can search on google for more details.

###  **Amazon Affiliate Program:**

We are a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for us to earn fees by linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites.

###  **Other Affiliate Ads and Links:**

We also use Avangate, Impact Radius and Clever Bridge affiliate links and banners to monetize our site.

###  **Disqus Comment:**

We use Disqus as our comment platform. It collects your email address, Name and IP address. When you comment on any of our posts, We will get these information. We are committed to keep that private.

###  **Subscribers Information:**

We collect and store subscribers email addresses and other basic details to send relevant weekly newsletters to them. We respect your privacy. We never sell or provide your information to third-party.

##  **XtremeRain Android Apps Privacy Policy:**

Some apps will require several permissions like Accessing Network, Account Information, Internet Connectivity, Read and Write External storage etc. All of your data will be kept secret.

##  **Want to Remove Your Information or Access Your Information?**

We only collect some basic information. As we use third-party services like google analytics, we can’t give access to all data or remove. But, You can contact us for details via this [contact us](https://www.xtremerain.com/contact-us/) page.
