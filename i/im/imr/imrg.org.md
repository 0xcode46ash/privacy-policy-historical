# Privacy Policy - IMRG

  


### 

### Privacy Policy - IMRG Site

### 

### Introduction

IMRG Limited (we and us) are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.

This policy (together with our website Terms of Use) sets out the basis on which we will process any personal data we collect from you, or that you provide to us. Please read it carefully. If you have any questions please do not hesitate to contact us at market@imrg.org

For the purpose of the Data Protection Act 1998, IMRG Limited is the data controller of any personal information that you provide to us through this website ([www.imrg.org](http://www.imrg.org/) \- the Site).

### Information we may collect from you

We may collect and process the following data about you that you submit:

a) information that you provide when becoming a registered user (including, for example, your name, address, telephone number and email address)

b) information provided when filling in forms or boxes on the site, for example at the time of requesting or subscribing to any service 

c) information that you contribute to the Site including, for example, information posted or submitted to the site as a registered user

d) information from surveys that we use for research purposes, if you choose to respond to them

e) communications you send to us, for example to report a problem with or submit a comment regarding the Site

We may also collect and process information about you that you may not actively submit, for example details of your visits to the Site (including, but not limited to, traffic data, location data and the resources that you access).
