**_Privacy Policy_**  


We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at IMSDb will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to IMSDb will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to let us know. 

**_Resale or disclosure of information to third parties_** :  


IMSDb does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or email lists. 

  
**_Browser information collected on the website_** :   


Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site. 

  
**_Use of cookies_** :   


We do not use cookies on this site. 

  
**_Privacy of our email lists_** :   


We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone.
