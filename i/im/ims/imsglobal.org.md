# Welcome to IMS Global Learning Consortium: Privacy Statement

**IMS Privacy Statement**

_Non-Member Access_  
IMS Global Learning Consortium, Inc. respects your privacy. We do not release any information about non-member access to our public site outside of the IMS organization.

The information you provide us, for example, name, email and organization is used for research, and if you request it, to update you on happenings within IMS. No identifying data is disclosed to any third party for any purpose, except where mandated by law. Data that we collect is used only for server administration and research. Any information you release will be held securely to the best of our ability.

We will take appropriate steps to protect the information you share with us from unauthorized access or disclosure.

_Member Access_  
We use member-related information for the purposes of administering the IMS organization and website and communicating and collaborating with our members. 

Members' interactions with the private site are authenticated. Consequently, we have the capability to identify users and their activities within the site. This information will not be discolosed to any third party for any purpose, except where mandated by law.

  1. By requesting membership and a password you are added to our newsletter mailing list and your identity is relayed to all IMS Members. 
  2. We monitor membership and communicate with members frequently and announce their membership to the public at large. 
  3. When IMS documents are released, if you are an active participant your name, email and company will be released publicly with your approval. 
  4. We will strive to inform members, prior to the release of any information concerning them or the organization they represent. 



If you have any questions or comments about our privacy policy, contact [webmaster@imsglobal.org](mailto:webmaster@imsglobal.org)
