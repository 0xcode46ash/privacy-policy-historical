Privacy

This Privacy Policy describes and explains the information collection practices of iManage, Inc. ("iManage") for the iManage website (the "Site"). Questions regarding this Privacy Policy should be directed to iManage by sending an e-mail to [legal@imanage.com](mailto:legal@imanage.com). This Privacy Policy supplements the iManage Website Terms of Use and is thus incorporated into and governed by all of the provisions of the Terms of Use. In the event of any conflict between the Terms of Use and the Privacy Policy, the provisions of the Terms of Use shall govern.

  1. Collection of Information  
We may collect personal information such as your name, address and e-mail address, as well as your company size and your position in the company. We use cookies on the Site to deliver content specific to your interests. We allow third party advertisers to display advertisements on the Site, and these advertisements may contain cookies.

  2. Use of Information  
We collect your personal information for internal promotional and marketing purposes only. We may use this data to send you information about iManage, to inform you of products and services that may be of interest to you, and to notify you of special offers.

  3. Disclosure of Information  
iManage does not sell, trade or rent your personally identifiable information to others. We may chose to do so in the future. If we do, you will have the opportunity to "opt-out" of such policy. iManage may release your personal information when we believe, in good faith, that such release is reasonable necessary to (i) comply with the law; (ii) enforce or apply the terms of our agreements; or (iii) to protect the rights, property or safety of iManage, our users, or others.

  4. Updating or Changing Information  
You may update or change your personal information by contacting your iManage Sales Representative.

  5. Linking  
iManage has partnerships or other relationships with third parties and the Site provides links to their websites. iManage is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. 
  6. Protection of User Information  
The Site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. The information collected by iManage is stored in a secure operating environment that is not available to the public.

  7. User Consent  
By using the Site, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information by iManage in accordance with this Privacy Policy. iManage will post any changes to the Privacy Policy on this page. Your continued use of the Site after any such change is made constitutes acceptance of and agreement to be bound by the terms of the Privacy Policy as modified.
