# iMedix - Privacy policy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains the accepted privacy practices for our website at [www.iMedix.com](http://www.imedix.com/) (" **iMedix** "). It describes the ways we collect and use your personal information on iMedix and the rights and options available to you with respect to your information. This policy is incorporated to the iMedix's Terms of Use (the " **Terms of Use** " or the " **Terms** "). 

[](http://www.truste.org/ivalidate.php?url=www.imedix.com&sealid=101) iMedix Inc. is a licensee of the TRUSTe Web Privacy Seal Program. TRUSTe is an independent, non-profit organization whose mission is to build user's trust and confidence in the Internet by promoting the use of fair information practices. This privacy statement covers the Web site [www.iMedix.com](http://www.imedix.com/). Because this Web site wants to demonstrate its commitment to your privacy, it has agreed to disclose its information practices and have its privacy practices reviewed for compliance by TRUSTe.

If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact us at [support@imedix.com](mailto:support@imedix.com). If you do not receive acknowledgement of your inquiry or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, you should contact TRUSTe at <http://www.truste.org/consumers/watchdog_complaint.php> TRUSTe will then serve as a liaison with us to resolve your concerns. 

**Personal Information that we collect**  
When you register with iMedix, we may ask you to provide us with personal details such as your name and e-mail address. We collect further information when you use any of the services on iMedix (the "Services"). We collect personally identifiable information at various points on our site. We collect information such as your name, email address, and date of birth when you register on our site, report inappropriate content, send feedback, invite a friend, and use our BLOG among other services.

We also record the frequency and scope of your use of the Services, the duration of your sessions, the web pages that you visit, information that you read, content that you use or create, advertisements that you view or click on, your communications with other users and third parties on iMedix, the Internet protocol (IP) address and the name of the domain that serve you to access iMedix, or any of the Services, and the geographic location of the computer system that you are using to log-in on iMedix through the use of "cookies" and "log files" (See "Cookies" and "Log Files" below). 

**Cookies**  
We use cookies on iMedix. Cookies are packets of information sent by our servers to your web browser and then sent back by the browser each time it accesses our servers. The cookies contain a variety of information, such as the content of search queries, iMedix's pages you have accessed, session durations and IP addresses. We use cookies for various purposes, such as to save you the need to re-enter your user name and password each time you log-in on iMedix, to facilitate the use of services on iMedix, to collect statistical information with respect to the use of iMedix, to verify information, to customize services on iMedix to your personal preferences and for information security purposes. 

Some of the Cookies may expire when the session ends and you exit your browser. Other cookies are saved on your computer's hard drive for the purposes above. If you wish to block our cookies, then please use the help button in your browser and follow the necessary instructions. However, bear in mind that disabling cookies may complicate or even prevent your use of iMedix, or certain features thereof. 

Some of our business partners (e.g., advertisers) use cookies on our site. We have no access to or control over these cookies. See "Third Party Advertisers" below.

This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by [www.imedix.com](http://www.imedix.com/) only and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

**Log Files**  
As is true of most Web sites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data. 

The information we gather via log files may be anonymous or in some instances personally identifiable. If you are not a registered user, than we will not knowingly make use of any information which personally identifies you, except as specifically indicated in the policy with reference to anonymous tracking data collection.

Further information may be collected when we exchange communications with you, for example, if you submit a request to our customer support, or when you contact our designated Copyright Agent. 

We use this information to analyze trends, to administer the site, to track users' movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. 

We do link this automatically-collected data to personally identifiable information as indicated above for the purposes of troubleshooting and statistics. 

We use a third-party tracking service that uses cookies to track non-personally identifiable information about visitors to our site in the aggregate to capture, e.g. usage and volume statistics.
