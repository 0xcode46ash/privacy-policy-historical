# 

How we use your personal information:

The information you give us is absolute safe with us. We will never rent, sell, share or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties.

We might contact you from time to time regarding your purchases or the services (like forums and announcement lists) you have subscribed to.

We may also send you promotional announcements from time to time about new products and services that you might find useful.

You may also opt out from receiving information from us at any time and ask us to remove your contact information from our database.
