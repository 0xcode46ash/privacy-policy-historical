# Importsouth.com

Welcome to ImportSouth.com by Vizionx, LLC! (Hereafter referred to as ImportSouth.com). ImportSouth.com wants you to feel comfortable and secure when you use our services. To that end, we are committed to protecting your privacy. 

Be assured that ImportSouth.com will not share private information with anyone! You also play a role in guaranteeing your privacy and safety. Please exercise common sense when providing personal information to ImportSouth.com and its users. Don't reveal sensitive information to other. Make sure you are totally comfortable when meeting or exchanging phone numbers with other members. Refrain from revealing your address. Never share sensitive business or financial information. 

ImportSouth.com's primary goal in collecting personal information is to provide our members with the most useful and best and services. Be advised that Findapix will not sell or distribute confidential personal information to any institutions. Your profile information will be solely used to generate for market research. 

PRIVATE INFORMATION is any information that allows others to contact a user other than through ImportSouth.com or allows the collection of information about the user other than what is displayed on the site. Private information is gathered regular members. This information includes, but is not limited to, email address, rectd dress, first name, last name, credit card number, and address. 

PUBLIC INFORMATION is personal information that may be displayed on the site, such as gender, age and height, but cannot be linked to a particular user. Public information is personal information such as gender, age, ethnic background, appearance, and more. This information is meant to be seen by members and visitors to the site but cannot be used to identify a specific member by name, address, or other profile information. 

ImportSouth.com makes forum's available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information online. ImportSouth.com also offers a internal messaging system, and be advised that when you are using this system, whatever you are transmitting will be in the public information. 

We have designed ImportSouth.com to be a simple, safe, fun and discreet way to meet others. Ultimately, you are responsible for the manner in which you use our site. If you have any questions or comments about our services or policies, please contact us at admin@importsouth.com.
