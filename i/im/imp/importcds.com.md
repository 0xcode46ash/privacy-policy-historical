Privacy Policy

|   
  
---|---|---  
  
• Why do you want to know my name and e-mail address?  
Your email address allows you to receive information about your order. It is also your login and it allows us to easily locate your order information if you do not have your order number. 

  
• Why do you want to know my credit card number and  
street address?  
Your credit card number is necessary to process your order. We would need to know the street address that your credit card company has on file in order to authorize the credit card. This is to protect you, the consumer, from any unauthorized charges.

  
• How do you make sure nobody steals my credit card  
information?  
All of our transactions are processed through and our registered user accounts are password protected.

  
• How do you use this information?  
The information we gather from you is for processing your order. 

  
• Do you sell my information to anyone?  
We will not sell, distribute or release any names, email address, phone numbers or other forms of contact information regarding customers or contacts of ImportCDs.com. 

  
• Can I see and change the information you have about me, personally?  
Yes, you can change all your information by logging in to your account and updating your account information. 

  
• How can I start or stop receiving e-mail from you?  
If you wish to stop receiving email from us, you can use our [contact form](https://web.archive.org/web/20060516173219id_/http%3A//importcds.com/ordered_from.asp) and put "unsubscribe" in the subject line.

  
• Who can I contact if I have a question about my privacy?  
You can contact us using our [contact form](https://web.archive.org/web/20060516173219id_/http%3A//importcds.com/ordered_from.asp) for more information.
