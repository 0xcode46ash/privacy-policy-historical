# Privacy Statement | iMoney

Unless you are registered for online services from iMoney, you remain anonymous when you browse this website. We do not collect personal information (e.g. your name, address, phone number or email address) if you are only browsing this website.

iMoney may use “cookies” to collect information about how our website is used. Information, such as the server your computer is logged onto, your browser type (for example, Internet Explorer or Google Chrome), and whether you responded to advertisement on the iMoney website or email is collected and tracked, however, this is done in an aggregated manner and is not linked to you personally.

### Types of Information Collected

If you want to apply for, or take advantage of our online comparison services, we may require you to provide us with some personal information, including your name, age and contact information. Where applicable, we may also require that you provide us with your personal financial information, including information on your job, income, assets and liabilities. iMoney keeps your personal and financial information for only as long as it requires to provide you with our services.

### Information Use

If it is deemed appropriate, iMoney may share your personal information with third party providers whose products are listed on the iMoney website. iMoney may also share your personal information with authorised representatives of these third party providers. At no point will iMoney sell, distribute, or lease your personal information to third parties unless we have your permission, or are required to do so under the law. iMoney may use your personal information to send you promotional information which may benefit you.
