# ITV - Formula One

To enhance your Web experience, this site uses anonymous identifiers ("cookies") to collect anonymous preference and interest visitor information. This information is provided to Engage Technologies, Inc. ("Engage") and is used by Engage to create anonymous interest profiles. This information does not include and is never merged with personally identifiable information (such as name, address or email address) or any information that is considered sensitive or controversial or that would compromise your privacy. Engage profiles are accessed by Engage-enabled Web sites and marketers to tailor site content, promotions and advertising and to perform marketing research. Consumers can opt-out from these profiles at any time. For more information on Engage, including your option not to have this information used for this purpose, go to [www.engage.com](http://www.engage.com/).
