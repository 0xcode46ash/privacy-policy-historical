# Privacy Policy | It's All Tech

It’sAllTech.com takes the privacy of our readers extremely seriously. We will never sell your information to third-party companies, nor will we use it for anything other than security purposes and statistical information.

**Personal Information**

As with most other websites, every time you visit It’s All Tech, your IP address, Internet Service Provider, the Web browser you used to visit our website (for example Internet Explorer, Firefox, Safari etc.), the time you visited, and which pages you visited are logged into our databases. This is purely for statistical information and security purposes. None of this information, including your name and email address, will ever be passed to third-party companies, sold to third-party companies, or rented to anyone outside of It’s All Tech.com.

**Third-party  advertisements**

We use Google AdSense as our main advertisement provider for It’s All Tech. This service uses cookies to display advertisements on the Web page. Similar services on It’s All Tech may also take your location to display relevant advertisements. We will **never** see this information.
