INFORMATION GATHERING AND USAGE 

  * The contents of your mon.itor.us page is stored and maintained on Sourcio servers in order to provide the service.
  * When you sign in to access your personal page from anywhere we ask for information such as your email address and password.
  * Mon.itor.us will never sell, rent or share your personal information.
  * We are sending confirmation message once when you create new contact.
  * Only confirmed contacts are used to sent Notifications 
  * Notification is sent twice: one when failure occurs, and second when it recovers



COOKIES

  * A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive.
  * Cookies are required to use the mon.itor.us service.
  * We use permanent cookies to record your session information
