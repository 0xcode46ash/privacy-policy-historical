# Online Privacy Policy

| | 

  
**Online Privacy Policy**

By using the ItWorksweb site, you agree to the ItWorks Online Privacy Policy, as outlined below. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use this site.

**COLLECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION**

At times, particularly when you order products, download software, or enter contests from this web site, ItWorks may ask you to provide certain information about yourself by filling out and submitting an online form. It is completely optional for you to participate in these programs. If you elect to participate, ItWorks may require you to provide your contact information.

ItWorks collects this information in order to record and support your participation in the programs you select. If you order a product, for example, the information is used to register your license and rights, if any, to technical support, upgrade discounts, or other benefits that may be made available to registered users. If you enter a contest, information is collected to validate the entry and contact you regarding the contest prize or awards. ItWorks also uses information that you provide as part of our effort to keep you informed about product upgrades, special offers, and other ItWorks products and services.

ItWorks recognizes and appreciates responsible use of this information and does not share or provide information to other companies or organizations.
