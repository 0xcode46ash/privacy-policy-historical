# Privacy Policy - Isagenix.com

### Collection of Information

Isagenix International is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our website, mobile or handheld device application visitors and customers. Information we collect is used solely to improve our services and enhance our overall customer experience. Any information you provide will never be sold to any third parties or mass marketers. We may collect non-personal information about the ways that visitors use the Isagenix International website, mobile or handheld device application for tracking information, statistics, and marketing research purposes. The information we obtain allows us to review activity and how to improve our website, mobile or handheld device application for our customers. Visitors who use our website, mobile or handheld device application never need to provide personal information if they choose not to. 

### How We Use Personal Information

Isagenix International may seek personal information from you to provide you with a service, place an order, or fulfill a request. We will never discuss payment information (e.g. credit card numbers) with anyone but you. Isagenix International is a Direct Sales Company and as such, we may provide personal information as needed to your personal Isagenix Independent Associate or Upline Sponsor to provide them credit for orders and Associate enrollments. All Isagenix Independent Associates must abide by this Privacy Policy.

### Website, Mobile or Handheld Device Application Changes and Associate Replicated Website Changes

We may add to, modify, or delete parts of our website, mobile or handheld device application as needed. For security purposes, we reserve the right to add to, modify, or delete parts of Associate Replicated Websites as needed. For example: We do not allow certain claims to appear on any Associate Replicated website and may remove them for the security of the company.

### Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of information stored on your hard drive, not on our website, mobile or handheld device application. We may use cookies to store your Associate password so that you do not have to re-enter it continually or to remember in what language you prefer to see the website, mobile or handheld device application. Cookies may also allow us to research and improve our website, mobile or handheld device application to match your needs. We do not use cookies to research your activity on other websites, mobile or handheld device application. You may always decline our cookies if your browser permits. We encourage you to add [http://www.isagenix.com](http://www.isagenix.com/) as a trusted site in your browser if possible and to decline cookies from all non-trusted sites.

### Security

We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption where there is private or confidential information displayed on our website. SSL encryption ensures that your information is protected and will not be accessible by third parties.

### Limits

Isagenix International cannot be liable for any information obtained by outside parties from your use of this website, mobile or handheld device application or by your activities as an Isagenix Associate. We cannot guarantee absolute privacy from any hypertext links on our website, mobile or handheld device application leading to other websites, mobile or handheld device application. We encourage that you be careful providing information to any third parties other than Isagenix International.

### Acceptance of Privacy Policy

By using this site, you agree to the terms of our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please discontinue your use of this website, mobile or handheld device application. Isagenix International reserves the right to change our Privacy Policy at any time.Â
