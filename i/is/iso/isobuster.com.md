# IsoBuster Privacy Policy - CD, DVD, Hard Disk Data Recovery Software

Privacy Policy

This web site is owned and operated by Smart Projects.

The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to explain how we treat data obtained through your visits to our web site or when you use or buy our products or services.

For each visitor to our web site we may use limited information about the visitor to present the most relevant content. Visitor systems are not probed in any way to retrieve personal information, only the information sent by the browser can possibly be used. In other words, just tracking information such as IP, which web browser, version of your web browser etc. This sort of information may also be stored and used for statistical purposes, to understand and serve our visitors best.

We only collect personal information that is freely and knowingly volunteered by the visitor or customer, such as via newsletter subscriptions and/or product or service registrations, support requests etc.

The personal information we collect is used to notify consumers about updates to our web sites or products, and/or to deliver and follow up on purchased goods or services and/or to respond to posted queries or remarks. The provided information will not be shared with others. We do NOT resell nor otherwise provide your details to others. Should Smart Projects ever be acquired by, or merged with, another company then that other company will also respect these guidelines with respect to personal information that was gathered before the merger or acquisition.

With respect to cookies: A cookie is a small amount of data that is transferred via your browser to your system (in the so called cookies folder) by a Web server and it can only be read by the server that sent it to you, but Smart Projects does not use cookies to identify you personally. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it. We use cookies, depending on the situation, to store choices made on the web site, such as language information, affiliate tracking, coupon tracking etc. In other words, to provide you a with a pleasant experience and possibly make your life easier.

Software produced and/or provided by Smart Projects may check the servers from time to time, to see if there is a more recent version of the software available. This is never done without your knowledge. A message asking for your approval is shown first. You can disable checking or disable the approval request in the options of the software. During the short online query, after your approval, the software may send along very limited information about your system, such as OS and OS version, Software version, License information etc. to be able to present you with the correct update for your product. No personal information is harvested nor sent along with these online queries. Parts of the information may also be stored and used for statistical purposes, to understand and be able to serve our customers best.

Smart Projects reserves the right to change the Privacy Policy from time to time and will update this information on the web sites. Your continued use of the sites, or any materials, products or services accessible through it, constitutes your acceptance of the changes.

The present Privacy Policy and/or any dispute that might arise between Smart Projects and/or its affiliates and you are governed by the applicable law of the Smart Projects office. All the disputes will be handled initially on an amiable basis and in the event that such a dispute cannot be closed the dispute shall be settled by the arbitration of the competent courts from the territorial jurisdiction of Smart Projects' office.
