# :: Privacy Policy ::

  
Privacy is very important to us, and we maintain the privacy code of any personal data made available to us. Below are the guidelines we have adopted for protecting any information supplied to us during an online visit to iSell Pty Ltd. 

We protect any personal information supplied to us:

To make the most of an online experience, we will request some information from our customers to enable us to conduct follow-ups. Examples, of this includes a request for email notification. Except for information required to complete a purchase order, all other requests for information are kept to a minimum.

Although iSell reserves the right to contact a customer for normal business communication, we shall respect the confidentiality of any personal information. iSell Pty Ltd has no desire or intention to infringe on a customer's privacy while using our website, and we shall not sell, distribute, trade, or otherwise disclose any of your details to outside parties or organisations without your consent.
