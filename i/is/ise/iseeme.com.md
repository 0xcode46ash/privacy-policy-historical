# I See Me! Personalized Children's Gifts

_This site is hosted by Yahoo! Store. Yahoo! hosts the store pages, ordering system, and order data. Yahoo! automatically collects order information but may only use this information in the aggregate. If you accessed this store through Yahoo! Shopping, Yahoo! also automatically collects information about your shopping experience. Please see the[Yahoo! Privacy Policy](http://privacy.yahoo.com/privacy/us/) for more information about how Yahoo! uses this information._

At I See Me! Inc., we take our customers' privacy and security very seriously. We do not and will not sell, give or trade the information you provide us to personalize the book. This information is used solely for the purpose of personalizing the book. If you have provided us your email address, we'll get back to you with timely special offers. We do not and will not sell, give or trade your email address without your prior written consent. To opt out, please send your request to customerservice@iseeme.com. We store your purchasing history, which we may use to enrich our product offerings or customize your buying experience.
