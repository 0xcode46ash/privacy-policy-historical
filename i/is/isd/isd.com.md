# WECA Web Site Legal Terms & Conditions

|   
  
  
  
|    | 

WECA is committed to respecting and protecting your right to privacy. 

In general, you may always visit our site and browse without giving us any personal information. There are times we must have your personal information, such as your name, address, email address and phone number in order for us to grant you an access tour to protected sites, respond to your inquiry or provide you with product support. 

Any personal information provided by you will be used for WECA's purposes only. We will not share your information with individual or other companies unless required by law. However, WECA may need to provide your name and address to third parties that WECA uses for providing products and/or services you requested. If you do not want us to use your personal information to contact you, please let us know and we will not use that information for such purpose.
