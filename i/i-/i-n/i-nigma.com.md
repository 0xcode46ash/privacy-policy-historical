# i-nigma - Privacy Policy

|  | 

  1. We respect your privacy.
  2. We seek to comply worldwide with the Data Protection Act of 1998 and take all reasonable care to prevent any unauthorized access to your personal data and information.
  3. You acknowledge that 3GVision collects information in order to provide the i-nigma software and operate the i-nigma service, e.g. information that you have provided to us, or that we have obtained from your use of our products and services (such as the date, time and location of downloading the i-nigma handset software, or browsing activity when using i-nigma).
  4. Such information collected may be stored and processed in the United Kingdom, or any other country in which 3GVision or its customers or partners operate. By using the service, you consent to any such transfer of information outside of your country.
  5. We may pass on such information to your mobile phone service provider to secure collection of fees, or to governmental agencies, including but not limited to courts and district attorneys' offices, in connection with the prevention and detection of breaches of network security, unlawful acts or breaches of our contract terms.
  6. We may also use personal information to identify attractive audiences and media for advertisers.
  7. We will never sell such information to any third party.
  8. We will take all reasonable steps to ensure that all such information is stored securely.
  9. If you have any concern about privacy violations, please contact us.
