# Privacy Policy and Information Handling for ID.me

**Doc Ref: IDme.S.00.030**  
**Version: 4.0.3**  
**Last updated: 2015-11-04**

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. You acknowledge that you have read, understand and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy by accessing or using this website (Website). Exit this page and do not access or use the credentialing service (Service) or Website if you do not agree to these terms.

This Privacy Policy provides information regarding the data that we collect and handle from you when you visit this website (our “Website”) or use our mobile applications. Our Privacy Policy tells you how we use, share and protect this information, what choices you have about how this information is used, and how you can access and update this information.

Our site offers a credentialing service (hereafter referred to as “Service”). The Service and Website offer eligible Users, who create an account with an electronic identification resource (or “ID.me Account”), verification of their status as a member of an affinity group, create accounts with websites that use the ID.me Account and access certain exclusive benefits.

## Information You Provide Directly to ID.me

In order to provide our Service, you may need to provide us with information about yourself. For example:

  * You will be asked to provide email and a password to create an account
  * You will be asked for additional attributes in order for us to verify your identity and attributes of your identity, as well as to provide you with an enhanced customer experience.
  * During verification on our site, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, social security number, birthdate or other details to help us verify your eligibility. In some case you may be asked for your credit card number for identity verification purposes.



In addition, we may combine personally identifiable information that you have provided to us with other information that is publicly available or that we receive from other sources.

## When do we collect information?

We collect information from you when you register on our site, fill out a form, authenticate using a third party service provider, or enter information on our site.

## How do we use your information?

We may use the information we collect from you when you register, verify yourself, sign up for our newsletter, respond to a survey or marketing communication, browse the website, or use certain other aspects of our Website:

  * To verify your identity your transactions.
  * To send periodic emails regarding your order or other products and services.



## Information We Collect Automatically

In addition to information you choose to provide to us, we also use standard Internet technologies to collect certain additional information. For example:

**IP Address.** When you come to our Website, we collect your Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, which identifies the computer or service provider that you use to access the Site. We may use IP addresses to collect information regarding the frequency with which specific visitors visit various parts of our Site. We may combine this information with personally identifiable information that we have collected to make our Website and our communications to you more targeted to your interests.

**Cookies.** Our Website also uses cookies. Cookies are small text files that are stored on a user’s computer and allow websites to remember information about users. We use cookies for a variety of purposes in order to enhance the quality of our Site. We use transient (also called “session ID”) cookies to provide continuity from page to page. A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser. We also use persistent cookies. Persistent cookies allow our Website to recognize your browser when you return after your first visit. Cookies allow us to personalize your return visits to our Site and to save you time during certain activities. You have the choice to set your browser to accept all cookies, reject all cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. It is up to you whether to allow us to send you cookies. However, if you do not accept cookies from us, your ability to use some areas of our Website will be limited.

**Web beacons and other technologies.** The Website uses other tracking tools, including web beacons. Web beacons are images on the Website (or in emails that the Website sends to you) that help us determine what parts of the Website you have visited, whether you have opened an email that we have sent to you, or otherwise to measure the effectiveness of the Website.

## How does ID.me use the Information?

ID.me may use information we collect about you for a variety of business purposes, including:

  * to maintain and manage your account, deliver Services that you have requested, and enhance your experience on our Website;
  * to process and track your orders and provide the Services you request;
  * to communicate with you about the Website or other products or services in which you may be interested or which are new offerings, or to respond to your inquiries or comments;
  * to perform business analysis or for other purposes to improve the quality of our business, the Website, and the products and services we offer;
  * to verify your eligibility to receive discounts and benefits from organizations and registration authorities, such as government agents with access to your group affiliation credentials, military records, military affiliation(s), dates of active and reserve duty service, and information related to your accounts with financial institutions;
  * to communicate with Third Party Service Providers to accomplish or perform administrative tasks; or
  * in accordance with your consent.



## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We take very seriously our responsibility to maintain your privacy with respect to your information. We will therefore share that information only with the following entities, and only under these circumstances:

  * with entities that provide services on our behalf, including Website development, maintenance and support, verification, email distribution, and promotions management, only to the extent that these entities need the information to perform their services or as required by law, unless any additional use or sharing of the information is separately permitted under another provision of this Privacy Policy;
  * with identified entities or relying parties whose products or services you specifically use or request to receive while you use the Website;
  * when we believe that doing so is required by law, may be necessary to protect any person’s property, rights, or safety, or to investigate a potential violation of law, will help to enforce any terms of use or other legal agreement, or will help resolve disputes that arise in the ordinary course of business, or in the event of a corporate transaction;
  * in aggregated or other non-personally identifiable form;
  * in the event of merger or acquisition, or filing for protection of bankruptcy (user information may transfer to a successor entity in connection with a corporate merger, consolidation, sale of assets, bankruptcy or other corporate change; user information submitted prior to any such transfer would remain subject to terms of this Privacy Policy; user information submitted after transfer to a successor entity may be subject to new Privacy Policy terms); or
  * in accordance with your consent.



## What does ID.me do with my data?

You can elect to provide all or only some of the information requested by the Website during the registration process; however, if you decline to provide any of the above information, you may not be able to register for an ID.me Account or to access certain benefits of the ID.me Service (including certain deals, cash back rewards, offers or opportunities offered by Third Party Websites) as they may be conditional upon certain eligibility requirements. We may also use the collected information to troubleshoot, resolve disputes, accomplish administrative tasks, contact you, enforce our agreements with you, including the Website’s Terms of Service and this Privacy Policy, comply with applicable law, and cooperate with law enforcement activities. In addition, we reserve the right to audit your eligibility (e.g., group affiliations such as active duty military and student status), to audit user accounts for fraud (e.g., verify that purchases are delivered to registered user addresses), and to prevent duplicate ID.me Accounts from being created with the same information.

## How does ID.me use data to better serve members of America’s Armed Forces and personnel covered by the Service Members Civil Relief Act (SCRA) (50 U.S.C. App 501 et. seq)?

ID.me provides services to Financial Services companies and Third Party Websites in order to facilitate their compliance with the provisions of SCRA. By joining ID.me, you agree that ID.me may use your personal information at any time to assist Financial Services companies and Third Party Websites to determine your eligibility or your family member’s eligibility for SCRA benefits and protections. Source of this information my include, but not limited to, publically available websites, physical documentation, financial information, ID.me’s network or third parties that have a relationship with ID.me.

We may use Third Party Advertising Companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. ## How do I correct or update my information with ID.me?

We believe that you should have the ability to access and edit the Personally Identifiable Information and Sensitive Information you provide us. ## May I opt-out of receiving ID.me e-mails?

Yes. You may opt-out of Promotional e-mails, by e-mailing feedback@id.me, uncheck the checkbox in your account settings next to “I want to subscribe to ID.me offers and discounts” or click “unsubscribe” button at the bottom of any of our e-mails.

You expressly consent to receiving Administrative and Promotional e-mails when you register for an account with, log into, or otherwise utilize the Website or Service or verification service, including through Third Party Websites. ID.me will send the Administrative and Promotional e-mails to the e-mail address you provided.

Administrative e-mails relate to your activity with ID.me, including e-mails regarding your account, requests and or inquiries by you, earned cash back rebates, and purchases of products and services (of which information may be provided by the Third Party Website to ID.me). The primary purpose of Administrative e-mails will be transactional or about your relationship with ID.me or the Third Party Website through which you accessed the Service. The Administration e-mails may contain a mix of transactional and/or relationship information with minor content component commercial information regarding new products or services. You may not opt-out of Administrative e-mails.

Promotional e-mails will provide information regarding the Website or Service products, goods and/or services, including sales and other offers by Third Party Websites, such as cash back rebate promotions, employment and educational opportunities, and/or the products and services of our Third Party Websites, Affiliates and Service Providers.

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In addition, all sensitive information you supply is encrypted via Transport Layer Security (TLS) technology.
