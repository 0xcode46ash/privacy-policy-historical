# Privacy Policy

At iPhoneDownloadBlog.com, we recognize that privacy of your personal information is important.

**iPhone Download Blog Privacy Statement**

What follows is the Privacy Statement for iPhoneDownloadBlog.com. Please read this statement regarding our blog. If you have questions please ask us via our [contact form](http://www.iphonedownloadblog.com/contact/).

**Email Addresses**

You may choose to add your email address to our contact list via the forms on our websites. We agree that we will never share your email with any third party.

**Ownership of Information**

iPhoneDownloadBlog.com is the sole owner of any information collected on our website.

**Comments/Message Boards**

iPhoneDownloadBlog.com contains comment sections (a.k.a. message boards). We do not actively monitor these comments and the information on them is for entertainment purposes only. If we are alerted to something we deem inappropriate in any way, we may delete it at our discretion. We use email validation on our message boards in order to reduce “comment spam.” These email addresses will not be shared with any third party.

**Cookies, Web Beacons, and Similar Technologies**

iPhoneDownloadBlog.com may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to manage users’ site preferences and ensure the site’s functionality. This data is not shared with any third party. Accessing our website is not dependent on accepting cookies and all major browsers allow you to disable cookies if you wish.

**Third Party Cookies**

iPhoneDownloadBlog.com and its advertisers may also use ad network providers and other service providers, such as Google.com and Kontera, to help present customized content and advertisements on iPhoneDownloadBlog.com. These providers may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies on your computer to help present, better target, and measure the effectiveness of their content and advertisements, using data gathered over time and across their networks of Web pages to determine or predict the characteristics and preferences of their audience.

iPhoneDownloadBlog.com may share certain non-personally identifiable information about you as an iPhoneDownloadBlog.com user (such as user age and zip code) with certain ad network and service providers, including Google.com and Kontera, to help them deliver more relevant content and advertisements through their networks. The use of cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies by Google.com, Kontera, and other ad network and content providers is subject to their own privacy policies, not that of iPhoneDownloadBlog.com.

**Traffic Reports**

Our industry-standard traffic reporting records IP addresses, Internet service provider information, referrer strings, browser types and the date and time pages are loaded. We use this information in the aggregate only to provide traffic statistics to advertisers and to figure out which features and editorials are most popular.

**Legal proceedings**

We will make every effort to preserve user privacy but iPhoneDownloadBlog.com may need to disclose information when required by law.

**Links**

iPhoneDownloadBlog.com frequently links to other websites. We are not responsible for the content or business practices of these websites. When you leave our website we encourage you to read the destination site’s privacy policy.

**Notification of Changes**

When iPhoneDownloadBlog.com makes changes to this privacy policy we will post those changes here.

**Contact Information**

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, please [contact us](http://www.iphonedownloadblog.com/contact/).
