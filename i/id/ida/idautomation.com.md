# Privacy and Security Statement for IDAutomation.com, Inc.

**IDAutomation.com, Inc.** has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses information about the privacy and security for this website: 

#### Privacy

**We do not sell, share, trade, or in any other way, make your name, email address, or other information available to any other party.** Your order information will be treated as **strictly confidential** and will only be used to process your order and to notify you about future product updates for the product you order. **IDAutomation.com, Inc.** offers a monthly newsletter that is sent by email. Your email address will only be added to this list under the following conditions:

  * If during the order process, the check box that states " _Yes! Please keep me informed about product updates, upgrades, special offers and pricing via email_ " is enabled. 
  * If you subscribe to the newsletter from the following URL: <http://www.idautomation.com/about-us/newsletters/>
  * If a critical update is available for the product you order, you will be added to the mailing list so we can email you the update notification. 



If your email address is added to this list and you do not wish to receive future newsletters from us, you may remove the newsletter subscription via unsubscribe information in the bottom of the newsletter. 

#### Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All information on our online order forms is encrypted before transmission to prevent possible interception by a third party. The encryption used with our online order forms is designed to provide government level security for your credit card transactions. Your transmissions in this area are absolutely secure and private.

This site contains links to other sites. **IDAutomation.com, Inc.** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

#### Contacting Us

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you may **[contact us](http://www.idautomation.com/about-us/contact-us.html)**.
