# Privacy Policy for iDrop News

iDrop News appreciates our readers and values your privacy. We will never rent, share, or sell iDrop News’ subscriber list. The policy below describes how we treat the information we collect when you visit our site and/or register for iDrop News and any information shared with us by our business partners. Please review this carefully, and let us know if you have any questions.

 **Your Information**

We will not disclose your personal information, specifically your email address, to any third party without your consent. Our goal is to consistently provide you with quality iDrop News material.

 **Other Information**

In order to make improvements to our site and tailor it to our audience, we may also request other information from you from time to time. These requests could include, but are not limited to, information on income level or age. We may also share this information with third parties in aggregate, anonymous form (For example, we might inform an advertiser that, “Our average subscriber earns $55,000/year, loves Apple products, and spends 1/2 of their income on Apple gadgets”). No third party will receive information from us that personally identifies you or anyone else in any way.

 **Usage Information**

It is important for us to gather information on how our site is used: How much time do users spend on our site, where did they come from, and where do the go once they leave? All of this information is collected in aggregate form. None of it is personally identifiable. This information is collected only to help us to, among other things, handle traffic on iDrop News, and to make sure that our service reaches you in the most efficient manner possible.

 **Children Under the Age of 13**

Our Website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. No one under age 13 may provide any information to the Website. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, do not provide any information on this Website or on or through any of its features/registration on the Website. If we learn we have collected or received personal information from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information. We explicitly do not allow any user to register for our site without confirming that they are 13 or older.

 **How to Unsubscribe**

You can unsubscribe from receiving our emails at any time. You can unsubscribe by clicking on the “Unsubscribe” link in the footer of our email and following the instructions. You can also unsubscribe by clicking on the “[Unsubscribe](https://www.idropnews.com/unsubscribe/)” link on this site and following the instructions.

You can unsubscribe from receiving our browser notifications at any time. Visit our notifications page by clicking on the “[Notifications](https://www.idropnews.com/push-notifications/)” link on this site and follow the instructions.

 **Third Party Advertising**

In order to support our operations, we do attempt to deliver the most relevant content in forms of display/banner ads and content links. If these ads are not fitting to you and you are not interested, please stop visiting this website (and unsubscribe from our list). We’d appreciate your feedback letting us know the real reason why you’re leaving by contacting us at contact@idropnews.com.

 **Cookies**

[Cookies](https://www.idropnews.com/cookie-policy/) are small text files placed on your computer, in order to help websites like ours retrieve information vital to providing the best possible service. Our cookies help us recognize you and simplify your user experience. iDrop News will never use cookies to obtain any information from your computer not related to our site. Visit our [cookie policy](https://www.idropnews.com/cookie-policy/) page to learn more.

 **Third Party Cookies**

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser.

 **Links to Other Sites**

Aside from our unique and original content that we deliver, we sometimes reference relevant information from other websites and provide links to these sites. We are not responsible for any content that appears on these websites. For questions about these sites you may visit from iDrop News, please consult their individual privacy policies.

 **Security  **  
We follow the most secure web practices (HTTPS) to make sure our users’ data, behaviors, and patterns are kept anonymous. We take data security seriously and that’s why we manage all our data internally.

 **Modifications  **  
We may make changes to this policy in the future. If any important changes occur, we will notify you through one of our newsletters and notifications.

 **Contact Us**

If you have questions about this policy, or about the website in general, please write to us at contact@idropnews.com or [visit here](https://www.idropnews.com/contact-us/) to find other ways to get in touch with us.
