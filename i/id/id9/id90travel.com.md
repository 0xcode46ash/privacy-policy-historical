December 10, 2014

In addition, we may also share your information with third parties that provide Services on our behalf, or with whom we have contracted to offer certain Services directly and on their own behalf (collectively, “Third Party Providers”) through their own web sites (“Third Party Websites”).

BY USING ANY OF THE SERVICES OFFERED THROUGH THE ID90 TRAVEL WEBSITES, YOU ACKNOWLEDGE AND AGREE THAT ID90 TRAVEL MAY COLLECT AND USE CERTAIN INFORMATION MORE PARTICULARLY DESCRIBED BELOW IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS PRIVACY POLICY, AND THAT YOU HAVE READ, UNDERSTAND, AND AGREE TO BE BOUND TO ALL THE TERMS OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY.

### Information We Collect and How We Use It

In order to access certain features and functionality provided through the Services, we may require you to register and/or create an account with us. If you do so, we may collect certain personally identifiable information from you including without limitation, your first and last name, mailing address, e-mail address, telephone number and date of birth. We may also request credit card or other payment account information for transactions as part of the Services, but do not store or maintain your credit card information on our servers.(For purposes of this Privacy Policy, such personally identifiable information is sometimes referred to as “Personally Identifiable Information” or “PII”).

ID90 Travel Websites relies upon one or more third party credit card processors to process credit card payments for transactions effected through the ID90 Travel Websites, and is not responsible for data breaches or privacy practices of those third party credit card processors.

You can elect to provide all or only some of the PII requested by the Services. However, if you decline to provide certain PII, you may not be able to register for an account or access certain features and functionality available through the Services.

In addition to using your PII to provide you with access to and use of the ID Travel Websites and Services, we may also use PII to troubleshoot, resolve disputes, accomplish administrative tasks, contact you, enforce our agreements with you, comply with applicable law, and cooperate with law enforcement activities. In addition, we reserve the right to audit your eligibility or to audit User accounts at any time for possible fraudulent activity.

“Non-Personally Identifiable Information” or “Non-PII” is anonymous information that does not reveal PII about any particular Users. This type of information may include things like the Uniform Resource Locator (“URL”) of the website you visited before coming to ID90 Travel Websites, the URL of the website you visit after leaving any of the ID90 Travel Websites, the type of browser or mobile device you are using to access the Services, your Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, language, unique device identifier, approximate geographic location, and time zone. We, and/or our Third Party Providers may automatically collect this information when you access and use the ID90 Travel Websites and/or Services through the use of electronic tools like cookies and web beacons or pixel tags or location based capabilities on the particular device you’re using to do so. We may use Non-PII to troubleshoot, administer the Services, analyze trends, gather demographic information, comply with applicable law, and cooperate with law enforcement activities. We may also share this information with our Third Party Providers to measure the overall effectiveness of our online tools, content, and programming.

Cookies are small files containing strings of characters placed onto a User’s device to uniquely identify the User’s browser. When you use or access the ID90 Travel Websites or any of the Services, we may send one or more cookies to your computer, to improve your user-experience, as well as the quality of the Services provided through the ID90 Travel Websites, by storing user preferences and tracking user trends, such as how people search. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can set your browser to disable cookies. However, some features of the ID90 Travel Websites and/or Services may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Log information - When you use or access the ID90 Travel Websites, our servers automatically may log and record information that your browser or mobile device sends to them. These server logs may include information such as your web request, Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.User communications - When you send email or other communication to ID90 TRAVEL, we may retain those communications in order to process your inquiries, respond to your requests and improve our services.

Third Party Websites – The ID90 Travel Websites sometimes offer goods, services and deals through websites operated by third parties which are not operated or controlled by ID90 Travel (previously defined above as “Third Party Websites”). PII that you provide to Third Party Websites may be sent to ID90 TRAVEL in order to deliver the Services. While any information we receive from Third Party Websites will be processed in accordance with this Privacy Policy, your interactions with the Third Party Websites will likely be subject to the their privacy policies.

### User Choices on Collection and Use of Information

As discussed above, you can always choose not to provide User Information, but if you do so, you may not be able to use all or certain parts of the Services offered through the ID90 TRAVEL Websites.

If you register for an account or otherwise submit User Information through the Services, we will send you administrative email messages. We may also send you Administrative E-mails and Promotional E-mails regarding your account activity and transactions with us, as well as updates about the Services “Administrative Emails” relate to a User’s interactions with the Services, and include emails regarding particular question, issues, concerns or other matters relating to your account. You cannot opt-out of Administrative Emails without cancelling your account. “Promotional Emails” are electronic messages advertising the ID90 Travel Websites and/or Services, as well as sales and offers of related products and services by Third Party Websites, Third Party Providers and Affiliates. If you do not want to receive Promotional Emails from us, you may elect to opt-out of receiving Promotional Emails at any time after registering by e-mailing us at customersupport@id90travel.com, by writing to us at the address contained herein, or by hitting the “unsubscribe” button at the bottom of any of e-mails you may receive from ID90 Travel or its Affiliates.

### Protection of User Information

We take various security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. These include internal reviews of our data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures, as well as physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access to systems where we store User Information.

We restrict access to PII to ID90 TRAVEL employees, contractors and agents who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve the Services. These individuals are bound by confidentiality obligations and may be subject to discipline, including termination, if they fail to meet these obligations.

Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet or any wireless network can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we employ commercially reasonable security measures to protect data and seek to partner with service providers which do the same, we cannot guarantee the security of any information transmitted to or from ID90 TRAVEL Websites, online tools or mobile applications, and are not responsible for the actions of any third parties that may receive any such information.

Account Information and other password-protected areas of ID90 Travel Websites can be accessed only with a valid user name and password. Each password owner is responsible for keeping the password confidential and safe, as ID90 TRAVEL has no control or responsibility for this type of User Information. If the password may have been stolen or might otherwise be misused, it is the responsibility of the subject User to notify ID90 TRAVEL immediately for further action.

### Public Areas of Our Websites

We may provide areas on ID90 Travel Websites where you can publicly post information about yourself, can communicate with others such as discussion boards or blogs, or can submit reviews and/or media content. By engaging in such conduct, you acknowledge that any such information you supply may be accessible by other consumers and companies and may appear on other websites or web searches, and therefore this information could be read, collected, and used by others throughout the world. For example, if you post your email address along with a posting or review, you may receive unsolicited messages from other registered users. We have no control over who reads your postings or what other users may do with the information you voluntarily post, so please use caution when posting any PII. We do obtain consent from our Users to post their name along with their reviews.

### Children

Minors (those under the age of 18) may not use the ID90 Travel Websites. We do not knowingly collect User Information from anyone under the age of 18, and no part of ID90 Travel Websites are designed to attract anyone under the age of 18. Because we do not intentionally collect any information from children under the age of 18, we also do not knowingly share or disclose such information to third parties. If you have reason to believe that a child under 18 years of age has provided us with information, please contact us at customersupport@id90travel.com and we will take appropriate action under applicable law.
