# Privacy Statement

We at Upside Wireless Inc. demonstrate a firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.

This site contains links to other sites; Upside Wireless Inc. com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.  
IP addresses of accesses to the server are used to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. We may use cookies to add functionality to some areas of our site.  
Our online forms ask visitors for personal information (like their names, addresses, email addresses). We also may run contests on our site in which we ask visitors for similar information. Data collected in surveys and contests will be used internally and will be handled confidentially.  If you, during the signup process, allow us to put your name on our mailing list we will treat your personal information with utmost care and not share it with anybody else.    
If you, during the signup process, allow us to put your name on our external mailing list,  we will share it only with a selected group of partners only then when they can provide you with valuable information, product or service. 

## Public Forums

This site may make discussion forums available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.  

## Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.
