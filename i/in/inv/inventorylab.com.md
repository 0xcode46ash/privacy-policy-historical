# Inventory Lab - Terms Of Service

## Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy 100%. Any information given to InventoryLAB is strictly for account and services use only. This document applies strictly to information collected by our website and application. 

We collect your email account, contact information, amazon credentials and etc only for the sole purpose of using our service in an effective manner. Absolutely NO information is shared with outside parties and/or affiliates to InventoryLAB.

We do not use cookies for any reason outside of recognizing your computer to use InventoryLAB. Cookies are NEVER used for any other method such as tracking your behavior while using the service. 

We do send Emails to the your account only for the sole purpose of informing you of updates/ and or special offers and emails will only be sent by InventoryLAB, not 3rd parties or affiliates.

Any further questions to our privacy policy can be emailed to [admin@inventorylab.com](mailto:admin@inventorylab.com)
