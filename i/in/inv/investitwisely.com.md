# Privacy Policy | Invest It Wisely

Your privacy is important to Invest It Wisely. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Invest It Wisely collects, and the ways in which Invest It Wisely uses that personal information. We never sell your personal information to third parties. This privacy policy may be updated; please check this page occasionally to keep up to date with any changes.

#### Log Files

We collect and use the data contained in log files, which are generated automatically by the server. The information contained therein includes your IP address, ISP, browser, pages visited, as well as the time visited.

#### Cookies

Cookies are used to store information, such as personal preferences. Third party advertisers on the site may also use cookies and web beacons, and may include Google Analytics in addition to Google Adsense. In order to disable this behavior, you can deactivate our cookies or third party cookies via your browser settings.

#### Third-party policies

Data controlled by third parties is under the control of their respective privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third party. Please see the policies below for more information:
