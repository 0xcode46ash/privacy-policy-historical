# Privacy Policy | Investor Trader

You are at the website of **InvestorTrader**. The owner of this site can be reached at _paul at investortrader dot co uk_.

**InvestorTrader** does not collect individual data on its users.

**InvestorTrader** collects aggregate data for statistical purposes using Google Analytics and WordPress Stats. The information collected on subscribers to the site is maintained for internal purposes only. This information is never sold, rented or made available to any third parties.

**InvestorTrader** partners with Google, as a third-party vendor. Google uses cookies to serve ads on **InvestorTrader**. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to **InvestorTrader** users based on their visit to **InvestorTrader** and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.co.uk/privacy_ads.html).

All content appearing on this site remains the property of InvestorTrader.

No content may be reproduced without the express permission of InvestorTrader.

For any questions regarding this site please contact the owner at _paul at investortrader dot co uk_.
