# InvisionFree - Your Free Hosted Invision Board!

**Privacy Policy  
  
**Effective December 1, 2002

Invision cares about protecting your privacy. The following is a description of how InvisionFree takes your privacy seriously.

We collect the following information:  
Name   
Address   
Email address   
Phone number   
Credit/Debit Card Information   
(etc.) 

We use the information collected to complete orders. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order.   
We don't not distribute your email address to third parties. We will not send spam to your address ever.

We will not share any information you give us with third parties.

  
We protect any information you give us from unauthorized access.

We do not collect personal information from children that we know are under the age of 13.

Any questions can be emailed to privacy@invisionfree.com
