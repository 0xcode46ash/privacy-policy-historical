**Website Privacy Policy Statement**

We can be reached via e-mail at:

[webmaster@www.bargainproperty.com](mailto:webmaster@www.bargainproperty.com) 

or you can reach us by telephone at 1 805 968-2020.

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, but not the e-mail address (where possible). We collect only the domain name, but not the e-mail address of visitors to our web page and aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit.

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web page, used to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual consumer and not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

If you provide us with your postal or e-mail address, you may receive periodic electronic and/or postal mailings, containing information we believe you may find useful, including notices of new services offered by BPN and its strategic partners, upcoming events, published newsletters and press releases. If you do not wish to receive this information, we will be happy to remove your name from our database. You may notify us by electronic mail at the above address. Please specify if you do not wish to receive any future electronic contacts and/or postal mailings. 

We do not share our mailing lists with any other organizations and any personal information you provide MPA will not be disseminated to third parties. We will not contact visitors to our Website by telephone. 

Information we provide is compiled from a variety of public data sources. We do our best to ensure accurate, timely, and complete information. We cannot, however, guarantee the accuracy of information obtained from external sources. If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at the above telephone number.
