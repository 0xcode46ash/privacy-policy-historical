# IndiaStudyChannel.com Privacy Policy

We collect personal information from site visitors. This information will be used only for authenticating members, statistical reports. Registered member's information will be displayed in various appropriate pages in this website. 

We do not sell or share your personal information and email address to anyone else. 

Visitors of this site may be able to view your contact information and contact you directly. If you like to hide any of your personal information from others, please do not submit it while registering.
