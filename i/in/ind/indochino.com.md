# Privacy : Indochino.com

|  |  | Privacy Policy  
---  
Your information is safe, we take your personal privacy security very seriously.  
Only 3 people will see your info:  
Paypal - They deal with your credit card information.  
Our Employees - We need to put your address on the package before we send it to you.  
Our Tailors - When Jackson Xue, our head tailor, is cutting your suit, he needs the following: Ikea fabric scissors, four meters of fabric, your measurements, and about 2 hours.
