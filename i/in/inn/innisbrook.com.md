# Innisbrook's Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy 

When you use our website, we will automatically collect certain information about your use of our site. This information may be used to analyze our site usage and to improve our marketing efforts and services.

When you use our website to place an order, or for your child’s school fundraising[](http://www.bbbonline.org/cks.asp?id=102042614314923196)

program, we will need to collect certain information from you. We will ask you to provide your name, mailing address, e-mail address, telephone number and your credit card number. We will also need your child’s name and grade to award prize credits for all orders placed online. We will use this information to notify you of special promotions and new product introductions.

Your school’s chairperson for the fundraising committee will have the ability to access a list of students who earned prize credits through online orders. The area from which this information is received is password protected. The list contains students’ first and last names, the total amount of items purchased on each student’s behalf and the prize credits earned.

This information, as well as all other information collected, will not be shared with any third parties except under the circumstances outlined below.

  
WE will NOT disclose your personal information except under the following circumstances:  
• If required by law.

• If deemed necessary by us in order to protect the property and personal safety of us,   
visitors to our site and third parties.

• To third parties assisting us in processing services requested by you, and for billing and   
collection purposes.

By using our website and providing us with information, you are consenting to our collection and use of your information described above. You may request to have all of your information and your child’s information collected and removed from our database by contacting our customer service department at 1-877-525-5608. We will remove your information in a timely manner, unless we are prohibited by law from doing so.

[](https://seal.verisign.com/splash?form_file=fdf/splash.fdf&dn=WWW.INNISBROOK.COM&lang=en)  
All personal customer information and payment information is collected using  Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption. All customer information is encrypted before being transmitted to us over the Internet. Additionally, all customer information on our server is secured against unauthorized access. Your credit card informatiion will be used only to process your payment.

  
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, or if you believe this policy has been violated, please notify our customer service department at 1-877-525-5608. We value your input and will do everything possible to protect you, your child and your privacy when you use our website.
