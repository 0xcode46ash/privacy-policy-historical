# Privacy Policy | INNOVERTEK

### Customer & Subscriber Contact Information

INNOVERTEK Corporation holds all of its customers account information & subscribers with utmost confidentiality. Under no circumstances do we sell any information that we collect from our subscribers or our customers.

In order to provide a superior service & communication we will collect information such as name, email address, telephone number, business address & some business information.

We use contact information to provide service updates, educational information, notifications and alerts to our customers & subscribers

As standard operational procedure we send promotional information via email. By following the unsubscribe link you can freely opt out which means you will no longer receive communication from INNOVERTEK Corporation.

On occasions we may contact you via the telephone in order to offer additional information and updates about our products & services.

To avoid receiving telephone calls simply inform the Business Automation Specialist to add your contact information to our do not call list, this will prevent ant further communication via the telephone.

### Customer Business Data

Our customers’ data is stored within dedicated encrypted servers and remains completely protected and strictly confidential, at all times.

INNOVERTEK Corporation pride itself on highest levels of security across our internal servers and employees.

Customers information remains the property of our customers and therefore will not be contacted, sold, shared affiliated with in or under any circumstance.

### Associated Links

INNOVERTEK Corporations website may on occasions contain links to enable you to visit other websites of interest easily. However, once you have used these links to leave www.biggerbetterbusiness.com, you should note that we do not have any control once you have left our domain.

Therefore, we cannot be responsible for your protection and privacy or any information or payments that you provide whilst visiting other domains and such domains that are not governed by this privacy statement. You should exercise caution and look at the privacy statement applicable to the domain in question.

### General Information

You may request details regarding the information we hold within our servers or any third party servers, under the Data Protection Act 1998.

If you would like a copy of the information we hold please send anrequest email to datasupport@innovertek.com

We mayrequire a small administration fee in order to complete this request.

### Contact Our Legal Department

For further information please email legal@innovertek.com

INNOVERTEK Corporation is committed to its customers & subscribers strict confidentiality
