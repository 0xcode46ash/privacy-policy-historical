# InfoEdge: Privacy Statement and Practices

**Cookies**

InfoEdge, like most websites, places a cookie on your machine enabling us to log certain information about the origin of your entry, navigation through the site, and exit pages. This information is used to create a more personal experience and to improve the overall utility and usability of our site for future visitors. If you do not wish to accept cookies, you may disable this on your web browser using its security options. Your ability to browse our website will not be diminished. 

However, **cookies must be enabled in order to complete an online research purchase**. If you prefer not to enable cookies, you may always contact us by phone to complete your purchase. 

Additionally, we use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like to opt out of this cookie or get more information, please visit the [Google ad and content network privacy policy here.](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)

**Registration**

To access certain content on our site, including Free Research, White Papers and Sample Pages, you may be required to submit contact information so that we may communicate with you regarding your interest in our research. If you do not wish to hear from us, simple use the UNSUBSCRIBE link at the bottom of every email you receive. You may also write to us with your remove request: 

InfoEdge   
Attn: Customer Service

Cambridge, MA 02139 

**Safeguarding Your Information**

InfoEdge will not share your contact information with third parties without your permission. Some free content on our site requires that you consent to share your contact information with the publisher. In these cases, you will be notified before you download the free content. If you agree to share your information, the publisher will be provided with your contact information as well as the name of the research that you downloaded. 

**Links to Third-Party Sites**

Occasionally, InfoEdge provides links to third-party websites. The privacy policy applies only to InfoEdge.com. 

**The Directory of Top Computer Executives**

The Directory of Top Computer Executives is published by Applied Computer Research. While InfoEdge sells the Directory, we are not in any way involved in its creation. Our customer data is NOT included in the Directory. The contact information in the Directory is compiled through extensive telephone surveys conducted by Applied Computer Research.
