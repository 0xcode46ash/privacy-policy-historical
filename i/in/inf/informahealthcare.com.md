##### What Information Do We Collect?

We collect the domain name and e-mail address (where possible) of visitors to our Web page, the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, user-specific information on what pages consumers access or visit and information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information, and/or site registrations. When you undertake to request a count on this site we ask for your name, e-mail address, company name, address, post code, phone number, job title, industry, and business interests. Once you have registered a count with us and signed up to our services (paid or otherwise), you are not anonymous to us. In order to tailor our subsequent communications to you and continuously improve our products and services, we may also ask you to provide us with information regarding your professional interests, experience with our products, and more detailed contact preferences.

##### Use of Personal Information

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web page, to notify consumers about updates to our Web site, and may be compiled and shared with other companies within the Informa Group to contact consumers for marketing purposes. It may also be shared with reputable third party organisations to help them contact consumers for marketing purposes.

##### Use of Cookies

A cookie is a small text file written to a user's hard drive that contains the User ID. The cookies do not contain any personal information about users and in themselves only record those areas of the site that have been visited by the computer in question, and for how long. The use of cookies is now widespread by sites in order to track traffic flows. We use cookies to enable us to offer certain time-saving features to our users such as, one-time log in, global-site shopping cart functionality and pre-populating name and address fields. If a user sets up his browser to reject the cookie, he or she may still use the site although functionality will be impaired. You can find out more about the use of cookies on [www.cookiecentral.com](http://www.cookiecentral.com/)

##### Log Files

We use cookies and log files to administer the site, track users' movements and actions, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. The information gathered can thus be used to improve the site and the user experience.

##### User's Right to Opt-out

From time to time, we may make the e-mail addresses of those who access our site available to other reputable organisations whose products or services we think you might find interesting. If you do not want us to share your e-mail address with other companies or organisations, please let us know by sending an e-mail to [database@informa.com](mailto:database@informa.com) or by calling or writing us at the address below. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending an e-mail to [database@informa.com](mailto:database@informa.com) or by calling or writing us at the address below. You can, however, have your name put on our do-not-share list by sending an e-mail to [database@informa.com](mailto:database@informa.com) or by calling or writing us at the address below. Please provide us with your exact name and address. We will be sure your name is removed from the list we share with other organisations. Persons who supply us with their telephone and fax numbers on-line may receive contact from other Informa Group companies and us with information regarding new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such telephone calls or faxes, please let us know by sending an e-mail to [database@informa.com](mailto:database@informa.com) or by calling or writing us at the address below. You can, however, have your name put on our do-not-share list by sending an e-mail to [database@informa.com](mailto:database@informa.com) or by calling or writing us at the address below.
