# | InfoCuts | Privacy Policy

Infocuts.com is a community based news site and each user can create his/her own profiles. Users profiles are publicly access by any one. Infocuts.com has personal information page which contain Real Name, Private Email, Public Email,Homepage,Location and Occupation. User private email is not displayed in your profile page. You can also upload your image. Each user must be very careful before giving such information in your profile. All this information is not mandatory. **Infocuts.com is not responsible for any misuse of this information and users can't put any legal obligations in this regards.**

## Types of Information

We Collect two types of information, first one is "personally identifiable information,". This information includes your name,email address, telephone number, age, background, or other personal information. User provide this information in their profile section. This information is accessible to all users who view your profile. Don't provide any information which you don't want to share. Infocuts.com is not responsible for any sort of misuse of the information. The Other one is "non-personally identifiable information". This information is only use for the analysis. We are currently using "Google Analytics" to track this information. 

## Third Party Cookies

Google and other third party vendor use DART cookies. These cookies are just used to serve advertisement on infocuts.com. Mostly all advertisement providers also use JavaScript to serve ads. Users can disable cookies and JavaScript from their browser settings. 

## Links to Other Sites 

External links on the infocuts.com are not controllable by our team. As infocuts.com is a community based site and users can give or share links with other users. Infocuts.com will be not responsible for any damage by the External links on the infocuts.com. We recommend that visitors must properly check the external link before clicking on the link. 

##  Links on Other Sites

Infocust.com is not participating in any link exchange programs. Infocuts.com is not responsible for the infocuts.com links on any other sites. 

## Children’s Privacy

Infocuts.com is recommended for above the age of 13.if we come to know that we have personal information about a child under age of 13, we will delete such information from our systems. 

## Terms of Use

By using Infocuts.com you agree to follow the terms of use and privacy policy of the site. The terms shall take effect immediately on your first use of Infocuts.com. If you don’t accept and follow the terms of Infocuts.com, please do not access, use and contribute to Infocuts.com. 

## Special Note for Parents

Infocuts.com is not intended for use by children, especially those under age of 13. Minors between the ages of 13 and 17 must not provide any personal information like address and telephone numbers. Children must get permission from their parent(s) or legal guardian(s) before participating into any information and comments on infocuts.com 

## Copyright Complaints

Infocuts.com respects other intellectual property. If any material on Infocuts.com (like Images) has copyright issue, please contact us with proper prove that you own the material. We will remove the material within 24 hours after receiving your email. All copyright complaints will send on copyright(at)Infocuts.com 

## Right to Accept or Reject News

Infocuts.com has a complete right to publish or reject the public news. 

## Rules to Submit News

We don't accept News with   
1- Sexual content  
2- Profane content  
3- Duplicate content  
4- Wrong information   
5- Personal opinion  
6- Hate speech or hate graphics content  
7- Personal product Promotions content  
8- Old News  
9- News less than 200 Words.

## Sharing of Information

We may share your information with our current or future affiliates, parent company, subsidiaries, or other related entities that perform services on our behalf such as data storage, web hosting, or other services. 

## How We Protect Information

infocuts.com security and privacy policies are periodically reviewed and enhanced as necessary. Our User data is protected by industry standard firewall and password protection systems. Only authorized individuals have access to the main database. 

## Changes to this policy

Infocuts.com reserves the right to change or update the policy at any time without informing its users. It’s highly recommended that you check this page periodically for changes. You continue visit the site following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes.
