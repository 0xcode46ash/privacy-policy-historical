# Privacy Policy - Infineon Technologies

Infineon strongly believes in protecting the privacy of the personal information you share with us by using our websites. Therefore, we also believe it is important to outline our understanding of privacy and to explain to you our Privacy Policy. 

**1.)** **Scope of this Privacy Policy**   
The Privacy Policy applies to all personal information collected or used by the Infineon Group (hereinafter “Infineon”) from Infineon websites. 

**2.)** **Registration**   
Some pages of Infineon under the URL [www.infineon.com](https://web.archive.org/cms/ "Infineon Product Startpage") are password protected and require a registration by you if you want to access them. Upon registration within these pages, you voluntarily provide us with some personal information of you. Once you are registered you are no longer anonymous to Infineon - you are given a user name and are able to take advantage of Infineon's offers. In addition to the registration, we may ask you for specific areas of interest if you choose to subscribe to the Infineon Design:)Link. 

You can review, change, or delete the personal information that you have inserted on our websites. The information on your login and personal information is accessible to you only after you have logged in with your user name and password. You can change your password on your Login Data page and your address on your Personal Data page. 

**3.)** **Purpose of Use**   
Infineon's goal in collecting personal information is to customize our network of sites for our users. We offer personalized, interactive communication. By providing personal information to us, you will help us to offer more responsive customer service and to improve our websites. 

**4.)** **Safety of personal information**   
Whenever Infineon handles personal information, your information will be treated securely and in accordance with our Privacy Policy. Infineon protects your personal information by applying high security standards. 

Infineon's technological safeguards and company policies ensure that your personal information will be treated with the utmost care and respect. Infineon does not distribute your personal information to third parties 

**5.)** **Links to other websites**   
The Infineon websites may contain links to websites of third parties. Infineon is not responsible for the content of those sites. 

**6.) Cookies**   
A cookie is a small piece of information sent by Infineon's web server to help your browser identify who you are, and to allow Infineon to offer customizable and personalized services. Infineon websites use cookies. Please ensure that your browser's preferences have been set to accept cookies. If you reject all cookies, you will be unable to use some of our services. We use cookies to deliver better and personalized service. Infineon collects anonymized usage information on this website by using the tool Adobe SiteCatalyst. Therefore we use third party cookies. These are used for website optimization and are not linked with any personal information without the explicit approval by the website user. However, if you do not want to send this information you can opt out [here](http://infineon.d3.sc.omtrdc.net/optout.html?locale=en_US "Opt Out"). 

**7.)** **Contact**   
In case of any queries, you may contact Infineon as described in “Ask Infineon!” at the top of this Website. 

This Privacy Policy became effective on 2008-12-08.
