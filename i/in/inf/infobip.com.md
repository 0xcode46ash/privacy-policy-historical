# INFOBIP Smart Messaging System

**Privacy Policy**

**General**  
InfoBip Privacy Policy covers the collection and use of personal information that is collected by InfoBip when you visit our website and register to receive our guide. Your privacy is a priority for InfoBip, and we go to great lengths to protect it. 

**Situations when InfoBip will disclose your information**

InfoBip will disclose your personal data to any other third party with your consent, only if: 

  * It is required by law,
  * It is necessary in order to provide you with a service which you have requested,
  * We suspect that your use of InfoBip services is in breach of their terms of use,
  * To protect the InfoBip rights or property, or any InfoBip customer,
  * To lessen a serious threat to a person's health or safety.



The use of your data by the distribution partners is ruled by their own privacy policy rules. We invite you to contact them directly.

  **How InfoBip stores your information and keeps it secure**

InfoBip will take all reasonable steps to keep secure any information it holds about you and to keep this information accurate and up to date. Your information is stored on secure servers that are protected in controlled facilities. In addition, our employees and the contractors who provide services linked to our information systems are obliged to respect the confidentiality of any personal information held by InfoBip. However, InfoBip will not be held responsible for events arising from unauthorized access to your personal information. For more information about our privacy practices, you can send us an e-mail or write to us.
