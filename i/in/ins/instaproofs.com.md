### Data Collection

When visiting Web sites, certain information about you and your computer can sometimes be collected. This section explains what information Instaproofs, Inc. collects, and how it is used.

We collect the following information about photographer accounts:

  * Name data
  * Personal contact data
  * Job title data
  * Business contact data
  * Clickstream data
  * Misc data



We collect this information for the following reasons

  * Completion and Support of Current Activity
  * Web Site and System Administration
  * Research and Development
  * Affirmative Customization
  * Individual Analysis
  * Individual Decision
  * Contacting for Marketing of Services
  * Historical Preservation



The information collected by Instaproofs, Inc. from photographers is used for contacting them when it is needed, and it is used for letting Instaproofs know how to pay the photographer, when payment is due.

### Cookies

Cookies are a technology which can be used to provide you with tailored information from a Web site. A cookie is an element of data that a Web site can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it.
