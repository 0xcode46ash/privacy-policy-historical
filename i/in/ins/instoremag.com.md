# Privacy Policy

## **Privacy Statement**

INSTORE and SmartWork Media recognize the importance of the privacy issue in regard to online usage. Information which you provide to us either directly (through online forms or via email) or indirectly (data normally collected on traffic to this site) is covered by our privacy policy.

## **Privacy Policy**

INSTORE and SmartWork Media hold in confidence all information (such as names, addresses, and phone numbers) given to us by customers and visitors to this site. We will not sell or release this information to any outside organization without your knowledge and consent. INSTORE or SmartWork Media might occasionally contact you with news regarding products or special offers which we feel could be of interest to you.

You can always request more information regarding our privacy policy or ask that we remove your name from our mailing lists by contacting us via email at [feedback@smartworkmedia.com](mailto:feedback@smartworkmedia.com). We reserve the right to retain certain customer information relating to billing and account status.
