# | InstaMed Communications |

**General Information**  
InstaMed Communications (InstaMed) respects the privacy of our customers, business partners and employees and we recognize the requirement to protect all Personal Information that you send us. InstaMed has therefore established this Privacy Statement so that you may understand what information we collect and how that information is used. This Privacy Statement applies to InstaMed Communications and all of our affiliated companies.

**What Personal Information does InstaMed collect on its web sites?**  
Personal Information is information that can be used to identify or contact a specific individual, such as a name, address, telephone number or email address. InstaMed may collect certain Personal Information from customers, business partners, employees and candidates for employment. InstaMed does not collect Personal Information when you visit our web sites unless you choose to provide such Personal Information.

**How does InstaMed use Personal Information collected on its web sites?**  
InstaMed uses Personal Information to consider candidates for employment opportunities within InstaMed. Except as provided in this Statement, InstaMed does not use your Personal Information for a purpose other than the stated purpose for which such information is collected. Personal Information given to InstaMed may be transferred across state and country borders for data consolidation, storage and simplified information management.

**With whom does InstaMed share Personal Information collected on its web sites?**  
InstaMed will not share Personal Information with any other third parties without your permission, unless required by, or in connection with, law enforcement action, subpoena or other litigation, or applicable law. InstaMed will not sell, trade or lease your Personal Information to others. 

**Sensitive Information**  
Sensitive Information is defined as Personal Information specifying medical or health conditions, racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs, trade union membership, or information specifying the sex life of the individual. InstaMed does not request or receive Sensitive Information from visitors to our web sites. 

**Security of Personal Information**  
InstaMed is committed to taking reasonable efforts to secure the personal data you choose to provide us. To protect the privacy of any Personal Information you may have provided, InstaMed employs industry-standard controls including physical access controls, Internet firewalls, intrusion detection, and network monitoring. In addition, access to sensitive data as defined in this Statement is limited to those individuals and agents having a need to know.

**Other Information/Logging and Cookies**  
InstaMed currently does not collect and/or log Internet Protocol addresses, Internet domain names, the web browser and operating system used to access the InstaMed web site, the files visited, the time spent in each file, and the time and date of each visit. InstaMed does not use cookies on its web sites. 

**Verification**  
InstaMed will engage in periodic self-assessment to verify that it continues to be in compliance with this Privacy Statement.
