# Privacy Policy | Internet Solutions HK

[](https://web.archive.org/web/20130218162620id_/http%3A//www.internetsolutions.hk/index.html)

### Privacy Policy

Please read carefully

#### Responsibility

Internetsolutions.hk responsibility to our customers privacy is described in this section. Unless required by law or as otherwise provided herein, Internetsolutions.hk will take commercially reasonable steps to ensure your privacy rights. Internetsolutions.hk is committed to developing relationships with customers that are based on trust and never will intentionally defy that trust.

#### Confidentiality

With the exception of trusted business affiliates and/or associates who work on behalf of or in connection with Internetsolutions.hk and can be subject to specific confidentiality agreements, Internetsolutions.hk will not provide or sell to any third party your personal information and will keep all customer information confidential. However, Internetsolutions.hk will share information in the following situations:

\- To avert, scrutinize, or take action regarding illegal activities, any situations involving threats to the physical safety of a person, potential fraud, violations or suspected violations of our Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by Federal or Massachusetts law.

\- Certain customer information (contact information) is transferred to WHOIS during domain registration to our affiliated third-party domain registrar ResellerClub or eNom Inc.

\- If Internetsolutions.hk is merged with or acquired by another company, Internetsolutions.hk may transfer the information about the customers to that company and will notify their customers about the change.

\- Internetsolutions.hk will respond to court orders, subpoenas, or other legal process, and will make use of the customers information as necessary to exercise or establish Internetsolutions.hk's legal rights or defend against any legal claims.

#### Encryption

When customers make an online payment to Internetsolutions.hk all information is protected because all the transmission of data is encrypted. Our Online Payment partners: Authorize.net, Paypal and Alipay use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL), which is the industry standard for encrypting all personal information (name, address and credit card numbers).
