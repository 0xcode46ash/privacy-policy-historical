# Integral7 - Company - Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy

At Integral7, we respect the importance of your personal information and the need to maintain the security and confidentiality of that information. We are committed to safeguarding the privacy of the information we maintain about you. This Privacy Policy details Integral7’s commitment to protecting your privacy, including what information we collect, how we use that information, how we safeguard that information and what you can do to control your information.

**What Information We Collect and How We Collect It  
**When you sign up for an Integral7 service, we collect contact information from you that typically includes your name, address, phone number, and email address. When necessary, information about the credentials you hold may also be collected. If you link to the Integral7 site from your candidate account with a credential sponsor and opt in to proceed to the Integral7 site, we may automatically collect your contact information, the credential sponsor name and the credentials you hold. We may ask for additional personal information, such as title, company name, or the industry you work in. You can opt out of providing this additional information by not entering it. 

When you visit our website, our system may use cookies and other technologies to enhance your online experience. These tools enable us to recognize you when you return to our site, maintain your browser session while you browse, as well as help us provide you with a better, more personalized experience when accessing the Integral7 services.

You can update or remove your personal information at any time by contacting Integral7 at [accounts@integral7.com](mailto:accounts@integral7.com).

**How We Use Your Information  
**You are in control of your information.

We may use or disclose your information under the following circumstances: 1) to provide the services you have requested; 2) to inform you of our other products or services; 3) to verify or complete a transaction or event; 4) to verify, on your behalf, your credential status to an authorized third party (i.e. employer or credential sponsor); 5) to assist an authorized third party in performing functions on behalf of Integral7 as part of the services provided to you by Integral7; 6) when forced to comply with law, regulation, or a court or government order or request; 7) in preventing fraud or imminent harm, and ensuring the security of our network and services; or 8) if you or any authorized person give us permission to do so.

If you do not want your data to be shared with third parties, you can choose not to allow the transfer by not using the third party service. ****

We may share aggregated non-personal information with third parties. Aggregate information is statistically relevant information based on the overall population of the user community. This information cannot be traced back to a specific individual.

**Safeguarding Your Information – Maintaining Your Trust  
**Any information provided to Integral7 is treated with care and discretion. We maintain a number of physical and electronic security features as well as procedural safeguards to protect your information. We conduct periodic reviews of systems, including security and privacy measures in order to provide protection for your information. We constantly re-evaluate our security and privacy policies and adapt them as necessary to deal with new challenges.

Authorized users access the system through commonly used web browsers.  Access requires a valid username and password.  All application pages are secured via SSL.

Integral7 servers reside in a secure, tier-one data center “vault.”  Physical access is highly restricted.  Servers and system logs are monitored for suspicious activity and are periodically tested by external attack simulations.  The data center hosting Integral7 software holds the SAS-70 Type II Compliance data center certification that meets compliance regulations for Sarbanes-Oxley, HIPAA, 21 CFR Part 11, CISP and Federal Government requirements like OMB A-130, Security Act and NIST Standards.

We restrict access to personal information by our employees and agents. Additionally, our employees are required to acknowledge their responsibility to maintain the confidentiality of your information. These individuals are bound by confidentiality obligations and may be subject to discipline, including termination and criminal prosecution, if they fail to meet these obligations. 

**Your Control of Your Information  
**Integral7 respects your decisions about the collection and use of your information.  By accessing and using this web site you consent to the use of your personal information by Integral7 in the manner specified in this Privacy Policy.

We are committed to keeping accurate, up-to-date records to help ensure the integrity of the information we maintain about you. If you hold an account with an Integral7 service and need to update or change your personal information you may do so by logging in to your account and updating the applicable fields. If you do not hold an account with Integral7 but believe we hold information on you that you would like to verify and/or modify, please contact us at [accounts@integral7.com](mailto:accounts@integral7.com) or (888) 754-3600.

**Third-Party Sites  
**Some of the Integral7 sites contain links to other, third-party sites whose information practices may be different than ours. This Privacy Policy only addresses the information gathering practices of Integral7.  To the extent that you disclose personal information to other web sites, or link to other web sites, you are subject to the other web sites' privacy policies.  We encourage you to review a third party web site's privacy policy before you disclose any personal information.

**Changes to the Privacy Policy**   
Please note that Integral7 may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. By continuing to use this web site, you agree to be bound by any such revisions and should periodically review the current version of this Privacy Policy for your own edification. 

**How to Contact Us**  
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel fee to contact us at [info@integral7.com](mailto:info@integral7.com). 

If you have a security-related concern, please contact us at (888) 754-3600. We will work closely with you to ensure a rapid and personal response to your concerns.
