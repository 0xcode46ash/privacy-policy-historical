# Mortgages, home loans and interest rates- Interest.com

Interest.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to privacy. 

User privacy is important to Interest.com. The success of our business depends upon our ability to maintain the trust of our users. Below is information regarding Interest.com's commitment to protect the privacy of users. 

**Information Collected during usage of Interest.com**  
Interest.com, along with thousands of other Web sites, uses cookies to enhance your Web viewing experience. Interest.com's cookies do not damage your system or files in any way. 

Here's how it works. Interest.com assigns you a unique number and records that number in the cookie file of your computer. Then, when you visit Interest.com, Interest.com reads this number to track your progression through the website and your response (your actual clicking on) to any advertisements. 

This process does not collect any personally-identifiable information about you, such as your name, address, phone number or email address. Interest.com does, however, collect non-personally identifiable information about you, such as the server your computer is logged onto, your browser type (for example, Netscape or Internet Explorer), and whether you responded to the ad delivered. 

Such non-personal information is used by Interest.com to structure our website and enhance our content to better serve the needs of our visitors. This non-personally identifiable information collected by Interest.com is used for the purpose of measuring ad effectiveness on behalf of Interest.com's advertisers. 

**Personal information that may be collected:**  
Interest.com may collect personal information from you if you decide to apply for a loan or fill out a pre-qualification form with one of our advertisers. This information will be used by the applicable institution in determining whether or not to extend financing to you. 

Interest.com may use some of the information provided in these forms for generating a demographic profile of our customer base, but Interest.com will not use the information provided in these forms for solicitation purposes or will not distribute this information to any other third party (with the exception of the institution whom which you submitted the form). 

Interest.com may at times request personal information from you for special surveys or promotional offers. If you provide personal information for such requests, the information may be used for solicitation purposes. You will be notified at the point of request if this to be the case. 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address may be used to gather broad demographic information. 

We may use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we may use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are use for broad demographic and geographical information and administer by the third-party ad company. 

**Public Forums**   
This site may make chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. 

**Security**   
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. 

**Links**   
This site contains links to other sites. Interest.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

**Children's Guidelines**   
This web site meets the guidelines of TRUSTe's Children's program. 

  1. We do NOT collect online contact information without prior parental consent or parental notification, which will include an opportunity for the parent to prevent use of the information and participation in the activity. Without prior parental consent, online information will only be used to respond directly to the child's request and will not be used for other purposes without prior parental consent. 
  2. We do NOT collect personally identifiable offline contact information without prior parental consent. 
  3. We do NOT distribute to third parties any personally identifiable information with out prior parental consent. 
  4. We do NOT give the ability to publicly post or otherwise distribute personally identifiable contact information without prior parental consent. 
  5. We do NOT entice by the prospect of a special game, prize or other activity, to divulge more information than is needed to participate in the activity.
