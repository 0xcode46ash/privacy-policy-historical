# Interweave.com Privacy Policy

****

**  
Interweave.com Privacy Policy**

We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of the users of our sites. Please read this policy carefully. As we continue to grow and improve our site we may need to change this policy from time to time. All changes will be posted here so that you will always know what information we gather, how we might use that information, and whether we will disclose that information to anyone. Please refer back to this policy regularly.

Interweave.com is maintained by Interweave Press, Inc. and follows the Interweave.com privacy policy, detailed below. If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please send us an e-mail at [privacy@interweave.com](mailto:privacy@interweave.com)

Personal Information

We may request personal information from you in connection with your use of, or participation in, our contests, surveys, forums, subscription registrations, chats, bulletin boards, discussion groups, requests for suggestions, visitors' requests for free products or services or additional information, and in connection with other activities, services or resources we make available on our site. In all of these cases, we will collect personal identification information from you only if you voluntarily submit such information to us.

We will never intentionally disclose any personal identification information about you to any third party without having received your permission.

Interweave.com uses cookies to create a better website. Cookie technology, through the storage of information on your hard drive, allows you to store your password making entering our site a quicker experience and shows us the most popular areas of our site so we may augment them. You are free to decline our cookies, but parts of our site may not work properly if you do so.

Personal information given out in a forum or chat room can be collected and used by third parties. Interweave.com cannot guarantee the security of the information you post in these venues. Please be mindful before offering any personal information in an open forum.

Interweave.com offers many service and resources in the form of links to screened third parties. Even though we visit and approve of these sites Interweave.com has no control over the content or links that appear on these sites. These sites do not fall under the Interweave.com Privacy Policy.
