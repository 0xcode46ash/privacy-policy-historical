**General  ** **Clause**

  


Intichat is a social discovery website offering an online community to meet and communicate with family and friends. This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you (the site user) of Intichat's policies and practices regarding the collection, use and disclosure of any Personal Information that you submit to us through our Sites.

  
We collect Personal Information that you submit to us voluntarily through our Sites.

  


1)     We may collect your name, email address, your gender, date of birth and postal code.

  


2)     When you [save](http://hi5.com/friend/displayPrivacy.do "Powered by Text-Enhance") other Personal Information in your profile we will collect any such information you enter.

3)     When you enter Personal Information throughout the Intichat network using one of the features of the Intichat network, for example blogs, forums, pages, or events, we will collect any such information you enter ("Publicly Posted Information").

4)     When you contact us by sending us an email, we will collect your email addresses and any information contained in the email you send us.

  
Once you register with Intichat and sign in to our Services or you are responding to an invitation to join Intichat, you are not anonymous to us. If you are browsing the Sites without being invited or registering with Intichat, you are anonymous to us, but we will collect the information listed below under Personal Information Collected via Technology. If you choose to use our invitation service to tell a friend about our site, we will ask you for information needed to send the invitation, such as your friend's email address. We will send your friend an email inviting him or her to visit the site. Intichat stores this information to send these invitations, to register a friend connection if your invitation is accepted, and to track the success of our referral program. Your friend may contact us at support@Intichat.com to request that we remove this information from our database.

**Email Notification Opt-Out Clause**

Users can manage their mailing preferences to specify which type of email notifications you want to opt out of. Once you are logged in you can change these setting you click “account” link upper right Hand corner then click on the “Privacy Settings” link then click the “:notifications” link, simply by checking either “yes” or “no” boxes next to each notification, indicating which type of email you would like and would not like to receive. Canceling one's account will also prevent a user from receiving future communications from Intichat. However, you may still receive a user generated invitation from your friends asking you to join Intichat.

**No Spam Clause**

We at Intichat.com are opposed to spam. Our No Spam Policy and Terms of Service prohibit all users from engaging in any activity involving spam on our service. Violations of these policies may result in immediate termination of service and legal action against any spammer. Intichat's Terms of Service expressly prohibit users from uploading, posting, emailing, transmitting or otherwise making available junk mail, commercial advertisements, or other form of commercial solicitation on the Intichat website or through Intichat's services. Any violation of our policies, by sending unsolicited email advertisements to or through Intichat.com computer systems, will use or cause to be used Intichat.com servers, located in Florida. Accordingly, any unauthorized use of Intichat.com computer systems is a violation of our policies and certain federal and state laws, including the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (18 U.S.C. sect 1030 et seq.), Such violations may subject the sender and his or her agents to civil and criminal penalties.

  
**Privacy Policy Defined Clause**

  
"Personal Information" is information about you that is personally identifiable to you, like your name, address, email address, or phone number, as well as other non-public information that is associated with the foregoing.

"Anonymous Information" means information that is not associated with or linked to your Personal Information; Anonymous Information does not permit the identification of individual persons. We collect and use Personal Information and Anonymous Information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Intichat does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or as otherwise described in this privacy policy.   
We provide your Personal Information to third party service providers who work on behalf of or with Intichat under a binding confidentiality agreements to provide some of the services and features of the Intichat community and to help us communicate with you. These service providers may not use your personal information to communicate with you about offers and services from Intichat.. Our marketing partners may use your personal information to communicate with you about offers and services from Intichat and our marketing partners. However, Intichat nor its marketing partners do not have any independent right to share this information.  
If you decide to use one of the additional services that are offered by our partners, we may forward Personal Information to these partners to enable them to provide the services that you requested.  
We also provide information to third-party advertising companies, as described in the next section.  
Please be aware that the handling of your Personal Information by our partners or the third-party advertising companies is governed by their privacy policy, not ours.  
If you would like to know (1) the categories of Personal Information about you that we disclosed in the prior calendar year to third parties for the purposes of the third parties’ marketing and/or (2) the names and addresses of such third parties, please contact us.  
We will disclose information in response to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims. We may disclose information, if we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding suspected illegal activities or fraud, situations involving the physical safety of any person, violations of Intichat's terms of use, or as otherwise required by applicable law.   
If our company or our assets are acquired by another company, that company will possess the Personal Information collected by us and it will assume the rights and obligations regarding your Personal Information as described in this Privacy Policy.

**Personal Information Collected Via Technology Clause**

When users visit particular pages within Intichat, a UserID may be placed in the URL, allowing customized features for the user. As you browse the Sites, certain information may also be passively collected and stored on our server logs, including your Internet protocol address, browser type, and operating system. We also use Cookies and navigational data like Uniform Resource Locators (URL) to gather information regarding the date and time of your visit and the solutions and information for which you searched and viewed, or on which of the advertisements displayed on the Sites you clicked. This type of information is collected to make our Services and solutions more useful to you and to tailor the Intichat community experience to meet your special interests and needs. If you search the Intichat community by using a toolbar that Intichat, or a third party provides, we may collect Anonymous Information about you automatically, but we will not collect Personal Information.   
An "Internet protocol address" or "IP Address" is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer when you use the Internet. In some cases your IP Address stays the same from browser session to browser session; but if you use a consumer internet access provider, your IP Address probably varies from session to session. We track your IP Address when you login to the Sites.

"Cookies" are small pieces of information that a web site sends to your computer's hard drive while you are viewing a web site. Intichat may use both session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent Cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience on the Sites. Persistent Cookies can be removed by following your Internet browser help file directions. If you choose to disable Cookies, some areas of the Sites may not work properly.  
Other third parties may place advertisements and cookies on webpages within the Intichat network. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one.

**Regarding Children Under the Age of 13 Clause**

  
If we receive information suggesting that a user who has registered with Intichat is under the age of 13, we require the user to provide evidence confirming that the user is at least 13 years old. Our registration process is designed to restrict children under the age of 13 from becoming users of Intichat and using our site.  If the user fails to provide such information within 48 hours, we immediately terminate the user's account and nullify all personally identifiable information displaying on the site. We do not intentionally gather Personal Information about visitors who are under the age of 13. We also do not currently have age filters on the site itself so any user 13 years of age or older can view and use our sites in every capacity.
