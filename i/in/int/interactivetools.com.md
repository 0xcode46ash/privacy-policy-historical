# interactivetools.com | Privacy Policy

Information we collect such as name, email address, mailing address, credit card number etc., is required for processing your order. We do not give, sell or trade your personal information to others. 

If you choose to submit a customer review of one of our products we ask for your first name and last name initial (i.e. Tom S.). Although, you can choose not to have your name displayed with your review. 

To further protect customer information we use a secure server. All information you submit is encrypted through the use of secure server software before it is sent to us. As well, all data collected here at interactivetools.com is password protected to prevent unauthorized access. 

interactivetools.com does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.
