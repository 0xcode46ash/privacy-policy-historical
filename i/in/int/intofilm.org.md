# Privacy policy - Into Film

## Introduction and General Terms

Into Film is committed to protecting you and your family's personal information when you are using our services. We want our services to be safe and enjoyable environments for our audience. This Privacy Policy relates to our use of any personal information you provide to us through the Into Film website.

In order to provide you with the full range of Into Film services, we are sometimes required to collect information about you.

We are legally obliged to use your information in line with all laws concerning the protection of personal information, including the Data Protection Act 1998

## The Data Protection Act

Under the Data Protection Act, we have a legal duty to protect any information we collect from you. We use leading technologies and encryption software to safeguard your data, and keep strict security standards to prevent any unauthorised access to it.

## What information do we collect?

We collect three kinds of information from visitors to the Into Film site:

1) Data you give to us when you sign up to the newsletter or contact us directly

2) Anonymous site usage information using Google Analytics

3) On occasion, Into Film will conduct competitions and surveys via an authorised data capture provider.
