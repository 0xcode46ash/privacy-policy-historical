# Privacy | ihavegot.com

The information we collect

information in your profile, everything you add, shouts, pictures, links 

messages sent to other users, shouts and send a comments or review to another user's everything 

Technical information regarding your internet browser and computer 

We do not sell or disposing your information to the third party companies. 

Private information

settings and administrative data such as your username and password.

your privacy settings, "Who Can See?" settings for your profile, basic and contact information and friends.

your time-zone for displaying correct time of events to you.

Who Can See your information?

All members can set privacy settings for their information such as: 

Profile basic information
Profile contact information
Frinds list
For each everything (Something, Someone and Other things) they want to add.


[Go to "Who Can See?" Settings](https://web.archive.org/web/20170602120856id_/http://www.ihavegot.com/settings/)
