# Untitled Page

Microsoft is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy statement explains data collection and use practices of the IE Add-ons Web site (the "Site"); it does not apply to other online or offline Microsoft sites, merchandise or services. By accessing the Site, you are consenting to the information collection and use practices described in this privacy statement. 

**Your personal information—We don’t collect it** We do not collect on this Site, information that personally identifies you or allows us to contact you The IE Add-ons Site offers the ability to post reviews of merchandise featured on the Site. As the author of a review, you will be identified by a nickname that you choose. In order to help prevent your personal information from being gathered and used by others for inappropriate or harmful purposes, you should be cautious about including personal information in your nickname or merchandise reviews. When you click on a link to purchase or download an item listed on the Site, or if you upload an IE add-on, you will be redirected to a different site. Any personal information you provide to such sites will be collected by those sites and governed by those sites' privacy statements. the IE Add-ons Site does not receive any of that personal information. We receive only aggregate reports from those sites. 

**Enforcement of this privacy statement** If you have questions regarding this statement, you should contact the IE Add-ons Site by e-mail. 

**Changes to this privacy statement** We may occasionally update this privacy statement. When we do, we will also revise the "last updated" date at the top of the privacy statement. For material changes to this privacy statement, we will notify you either by placing a prominent notice on the home page of our web site or by directly sending you a notification. We encourage you to periodically review this privacy statement to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the personal information we collect. Your continued use of the service constitutes your agreement to this privacy statement and any updates.
