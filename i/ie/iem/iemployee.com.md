# Privacy

We at Asure Software feel that our users should understand completely our policy that governs data capture and use. **Safe Harbor**

** The United States Department of Commerce and the European Commission and the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commisioner have agreed on a set of data protection principles and frequently asked questions (the "Safe Harbor Principles") to enable U.S. companies to satisfy the EU law and Swiss Federal requirement that personal information transferred from the EU to the United States be adequately protected. Consistent with its pledge to protect personal privacy, Asure Software adheres to the Safe Harbor Principles. **

**Scope**

** This Safe Harbor Privacy Policy (the "Policy") sets forth the privacy principles that Asure Software follows with respect to transfers of personal information from the European Union (EU) to the United States and from Switzerland to the United States according to the US.‐EU Safe Harbor Framework and the U.S-Swiss Safe Harbor Framework, in any format including electronic, paper or verbal. **

**Definitions**

** The following definitions shall apply throughout this Policy: 

“Asure Software” Shall mean Asure Software and its product lines: ADI Time, iEmployee, Legiant, collectively referred to as “Time and Labor Management” and NetSimplicity, as well as its successors, subsidiaries, divisions and groups in the United States. 

"Agent" means any third party that uses personal information provided to Asure Software to perform tasks on behalf of and under the instructions of Asure Software. 

"Personal information" means any information or set of information that identifies or could be used by or on behalf of Asure Software to identify an individual. Personal information does not include information that is encoded, anonymized, aggregated or publicly available information that has not been combined with non‐public personal information. 

"Sensitive personal information" means personal information that reveals race, ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs, or trade union membership, or that concerns health or sexual orientation. In addition, Asure Software will treat as sensitive personal information any information received from a third party where that third party treats and identifies the information as sensitive. 

“Payroll Data” means information relating to compensation, deduction- voluntary or otherwise, tax information – to include information found on W-2’s, banking information and any other data that may be found on paystubs or payroll checks. **

**Privacy Principles**

** The privacy principles in this Policy are based on the Safe Harbor Principles. 

**Notice** **  
** When Asure Software collects personal information directly from individuals in the EU and Switzerland ortheir respective employers, it will inform them about the purposes for which it collects and uses their personal information, the types of non‐agent third parties, if any, to which Asure Software discloses that information and the choices and means, if any, that Asure Software offers individuals for limiting the use and disclosure of their personal information. Notice will be provided in clear and conspicuous language when individuals are first asked to provide personal information to Asure Software, or as soon as practicable thereafter, and in any event before Asure Software uses the information for a purpose other than that for which it was originally collected. If Asure Software receives personal information from its subsidiaries, affiliates or other entities in the EU and Switzerland, it will use such information in accordance with the notices such entities provided and the choices made by the individuals to whom such personal information relates. **

**Choice**

** Asure Software will offer individuals the opportunity to choose (opt‐out) whether their personal information is (a) to be disclosed to a non‐agent third party, or (b) to be used for a purpose other than the purpose for which it was originally collected or subsequently authorized by the individual.For sensitive personal information, Asure Software will give individuals the opportunity to affirmatively and explicitly (opt‐in) consent to the disclosure of the information to a non‐agent third party or the use of the information for a purpose other than the purpose for which it was originally collected or subsequently authorized by the individual. Asure Software will provide individuals with reasonable methods to exercise their choices. **

**Data Integrity**

** Asure Software will use personal information only in ways that are compatible with the purposes for which it was collected or subsequently authorized by the individual. Asure Software will take reasonable steps to ensure that personal information is relevant to its intended use, accurate, complete, and current. **

**Transfer to Agents**

** Asure Software will obtain assurances from its agents that they will safeguard personal information consistently with this Policy. If Asure Software has knowledge that an agent is using or disclosing personal information in a manner contrary to this Policy, Asure Software will take reasonable steps to prevent or stop the use or disclosure. **

**Access and Correction**

** Upon request, Asure Software will grant individuals reasonable access to personal information that it holds about them, and Asure Software will take reasonable steps to permit individuals to correct, amend, or delete information that is demonstrated to be inaccurate or incomplete. Updates and access is often from individuals’ employers’ upstream human resources management system/record keeping system from which Asure Software receives all personal information from. **

**Security**

** Asure Software will take reasonable precautions to protect personal information in its possession from loss, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction. **

**Enforcement**

** Asure Software will conduct compliance audits of its relevant privacy practices to verify adherence to this Policy. Any employee that Asure Software determines is in violation of this policy will be subject to disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment. **

**Dispute Resolution**

** Any questions or concerns regarding the use or disclosure of personal information should be directed to the Asure Software Privacy Officer at the address given below. Asure Software will investigate and attempt to resolve complaints and disputes regarding use and disclosure of personal information in accordance with the principles contained in this Policy. For complaints that cannot be resolved between Asure Software and the complainant, Asure Software has agreed to participate in the dispute resolution procedures of the panel established by the European data protection authorities as well as the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner to resolve disputes pursuant to the Safe Harbor Principles. **

**Changes to the Safe Harbor Privacy Policy**

** This Policy may be amended from time to time, consistent with the requirements of the Safe Harbor Principles. Asure Software will provide appropriate public notice about such amendments. The most recent version of this Policy will be available at [www.asuresoftware.com](http://www.asuresoftware.com/). **
