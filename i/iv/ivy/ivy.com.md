# Ivy.com Privacy Policy

**Ivy.com Privacy Policy**

Without question, the Internet provides Ivy.com students with an incredible number of educational opportunities. However, we realize how important it is to be responsible when presenting these opportunities to students, and we are committed to keeping our visitors and their privacy safe on the web. Our mission is to help every student that comes to our site reach his or her potential through high-quality, personalized educational services.

Ivy.com takes students' privacy rights seriously and does not share any student information gathered from this website with unaffiliated organizations. Website logs and cookies are only used by Ivy.com webmasters to track site usage and course progress and to ensure that only registered students can access the course. Ivy.com and its affiliates may mail or email students and their parents about test preparation or about other programs and opportunities. Please notify [ webmaster@ivy.com](mailto:webmaster@ivy.com) to be removed from any mail or email list.

**Legal Notice**

The free practice test, free lesson, and discounted prices are promotional offers that Ivy.com may terminate at any time. All individual user data collected through this site is confidential and will be used for internal purposes by Ivy.com and its affiliates only.

**Personal Information**

We ask for student registration in order to set up your test preparation file. Throughout the course, students will log into their own unique account to start their online instruction, take practice tests, review their scores, and ask questions of ivy.com tutors.

What security precautions are in place to prevent the misuse of my information? The importance of security for all personally identifiable information associated with you is of utmost concern to us. We exercise great care in providing secure storage of your personal information. All credit card transactions take place over an encrypted connection on our secure servers.

Your account information is password-protected, so that you, and only you, have access to this personal information.

We recommend that you do not divulge your password to anyone. Also remember to sign out of Ivy.com and to close your browser window when you have finished your work. This is to ensure that others cannot access your personal information and correspondence if you share a computer with someone else or are using a computer in a public place like a school, library, or Internet cafe.

Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your personal information, Ivy.com cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or from our online products or services, and you do so at your own risk. Once we receive your transmission, we make our best effort to ensure its security on our systems.

**What about student's privacy?**

Naturally, the safety of students is very important to us. Ivy.com online programs have been developed in compliance with both the Children's On-Line Privacy Protection Act of 1998 and the Children's Advisory Review Unit (CARU) of the Better Business Bureau. We also adheres to the following policies for student users:

We encourage parents and guardians to spend time online with their children and to participate in the interactive activities offered on the site.  Unless otherwise disclosed during collection, we do not provide any personally identifying information, regardless of its source, to any unaffiliated third party, for any purpose whatsoever. · Parents have the ability to change any preferences chosen by their students or change/delete any information provided by their students.

For additional information, Ivy.com refers you to: [www.privacyrights.org/fs/fs21-children.htm](http://www.privacyrights.org/fs/fs21-children.htm).
