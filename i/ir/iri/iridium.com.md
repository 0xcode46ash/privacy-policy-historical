# Iridium | Privacy Policy

### Our Commitment to Privacy. 

At Iridium Satellite LLC ( **“Iridium** ,” “ **we** ,” or “ **us** ”), we respect the privacy of your information and have provided this explanation about our online information practices to show our commitment to protecting your privacy. By using this website (the “ **Site** ”) or the services provided through this Site (the “ **Services** ”), we may collect information about you, and use and disclose such information, as described in this privacy policy (“ **Privacy Policy** ”). Most areas of the Site are intended for consumers. One area of the Site is restricted to use by our service partners (the “ **Extranet** ”). This Privacy Policy describes Iridium’s collection, use and disclosure of Personal Information and Anonymous Information (each as defined below) in both the consumer areas of the Site and the Extranet. “ **Personal Information** ” means your contact information (e.g., your name, address, email address, or telephone number) and any other non-public information that is associated with your contact information. “ **Anonymous Information** ” means information that is not linked to Personal Information and does not enable contact with any identifiable person.

THE SITE AND SERVICES ARE HOSTED IN THE UNITED STATES. IF YOU ARE A USER ACCESSING THE SITE OR SERVICES FROM THE EUROPEAN UNION, ASIA, OR ANY OTHER REGION WITH LAWS OR REGULATIONS GOVERNING PERSONAL DATA COLLECTION, USE, AND DISCLOSURE THAT DIFFER FROM UNITED STATES LAWS, PLEASE BE ADVISED THAT THROUGH YOUR CONTINUED USE OF THE SITE OR SERVICES, WHICH IS GOVERNED BY UNITED STATES LAW, THIS PRIVACY POLICY, AND OUR TERMS OF SERVICE, YOU ARE TRANSFERRING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION TO THE UNITED STATES AND YOU CONSENT TO THAT TRANSFER. ADDITIONALLY, YOU UNDERSTAND THAT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION MAY BE PROCESSED IN COUNTRIES (INCLUDING THE UNITED STATES) WHERE LAWS REGARDING PROCESSING PERSONAL INFORMATION MAY BE LESS STRINGENT THAN IN YOUR COUNTRY.

### Children Under 18.

Protecting the privacy of children is very important to us. We do not collect or maintain Personal Information from people we actually know are under 18 years old, and no part of the Site or Services is designed to attract people under 18 years old. If we later obtain actual knowledge that a user is under 18 years old, we will take steps to remove that users Personal Information from our databases. 

### The Information We Collect.

We collect Personal Information and Anonymous Information about you.

  
| 

Personal Information  
In the consumer areas of the Site, we collect Personal Information when you knowingly provide it to us. In order to use, access, or receive certain Services, you may be required to complete the applicable registration form accessible on the Site. For example, to receive a newsletter, you must provide your name and email address. To send a satellite message, you will be required to provide the number to which you are sending the message, your email address and a message. To change the satellite area in which you are located, you will be required to login with your subscriber number. In addition, we collect Personal Information when you participate in surveys and/or sweepstakes, or when you correspond with us. 

To gain access to the Extranet, you will be required to provide your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, citizenship and the service partner with which you are affiliated (the “ **Service Partner** ”). In order to expedite your registration, and for verification purposes, it is possible that we may get your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and/or citizenship from the Service Partner. While using the Extranet, you may provide to us certain information on behalf of the service partner (“ **Service Partner Information** ”).   
  
---|---  
  
| 

Anonymous Information Collected Via Technology.   
As you use the Site or Services, certain Anonymous Information may also be passively collected and stored, such as your Internet protocol address, browser type, and access times. We may also use Cookies (as defined below) and navigational data like Uniform Resource Locators (URLs) to gather information regarding the date and time of your visit and the solutions and information for which you searched and viewed. “ **Cookies** ” are small pieces of information that a website sends to your browser while you are viewing a website. We may use both session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent Cookies (which stay on your browser until you delete them) to improve your experience on the Extranet (e.g., by keeping you logged in and remembering your preferred language). Persistent Cookies can be removed by following your Internet browser help file directions. If you choose to disable Cookies, you may be logged out of the Extranet and your language preference will revert to English on the next webpage reload.  
  
### The Ways We Use Information. 

We use Personal Information that you submit to us to administer your account, control access to the Extranet, communicate with you, respond to your requests, provide Services, improve the quality of the Site and Services, ensure compliance with export control laws and provide you with other information that may be useful or interesting to you. Such information may include regular newsletters and marketing emails, including contest entries and surveys. You can opt out of receiving newsletters from the Site by clicking the “Unsubscribe” link at the bottom of each email. If you are an Extranet user and receive marketing emails from us, you can opt out of receiving future marketing messages by modifying your profile. Please note that even if you unsubscribe or optout, we may still send you Site and Service related emails (e.g., emails related to your transmission of satellite messages).

We may also use your Personal Information to create Anonymous Information records by excluding information (such as your name or email address) that makes the information personally identifiable to you. We may use these records, and other Anonymous Information, to analyze usage patterns so that we may enhance the Site or Services. We use Service Partner Information in accordance with the terms of use posted on the Extranet.

### The Ways We Disclose Information. 

We may disclose Personal Information 

  * To third party vendors who help us make the Site or Services available, but only if they are contractually obligated to use the information in compliance with this Privacy Policy;
  * To third parties to whom you ask us to send Personal Information, including those to whom you send a satellite message;
  * To certain Service Partners so that they may approve your access to their proprietary information;
  * To our parent company, any subsidiaries, joint ventures, or other companies under a common control (collectively, “ **Affiliates** ”), in which case we will require our Affiliates to honor this Privacy Policy;
  * To a company that merges with, acquires, or purchases the assets of, Iridium, in which case such company may continue to process your Personal Information as set forth in this Privacy Policy; or
  * If we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to (a) resolve disputes, investigate problems, or enforce our terms of use; (b) comply with relevant laws or to respond to requests from law enforcement or other government officials relating to investigations or alleged illegal activity; or (c) protect and defend the rights or property of Iridium, you, or third parties, we can (and you authorize us to) disclose Personal Information without subpoenas or warrants served on us.



We may disclose Anonymous Information to: 

  * Third party vendors who may help us enhance or provide the Site or Services; or
  *  Third parties to whom you ask us to send Personal Information.



**How You Can Update, Correct or Delete Your Information.**

  
| 

Updating and Correcting.   
You may update your Personal Information by emailing us at [iridium.online@iridium.com](mailto:iridium.online@iridium.com) from your email address on record or, if you have access to the Extranet, by editing your profile on the Extranet. Subsequent uses or disclosures of information by us will reflect those changes, but we have no ability to update any information previously disclosed to any third party. Your Personal Information is necessary in order for us to send you newsletters or provide you access to the Extranet; therefore, if you delete such necessary information we may not be able to provide you certain Services.  
  
---|---  
  
| 

Deleting.  
If you wish to have your Personal Information removed from our databases, you may contact us at [iridium.online@iridium.com](mailto:iridium.online@iridium.com) from your email address on record and let us know what Personal Information you want deleted. You may request deletion of your Personal Information by us, but please note that we may be required (by law or otherwise) to keep this information and not delete it (or to keep this information for a certain time, in which case we will comply with your deletion request only after we have fulfilled such requirements). When we delete Personal Information, it will be deleted from the active database, but may remain in our archives.   
  
### Third Party Practices.

The Site or Services may contain links to other websites, including those of our partners. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such other websites. The privacy policy of a partner listed on our Site may differ from ours. We encourage you to read the privacy policy for each third party website you visit before responding to any offers.

### Changes to Our Policy.

This Privacy Policy is subject to revision from time to time. We will post any revised version of the Privacy Policy on this Site. If we make any material changes to it, we will also send you an e mail to the last e mail address you provided to us (if any). Any amended Privacy Policy will be effective as of the date stated in the revised policy.

Any changes to this Privacy Policy may affect our use or disclosure of Personal Information collected prior to the changes. If you do not agree to any of the changes, you must notify us prior to the effective date of the changes that you wish to have us delete your Personal Information. Continued use of our Services following notice of such changes shall indicate your acknowledgement of such changes and agreement to be bound by the revised terms and conditions. 

### Contact Us. 

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at [iridium.online@iridium.com](mailto:iridium.online@iridium.com).
