# Privacy Policy

f you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us: [ Contact ](http://contact.igrezadecu.com/contact.html)

We are strongly committed to protecting your privacy online. therefore igrezadecu.com does not request nor collect any personally identifiable information. Your IP address may be used to gather broad demographic information and general visiting paterns.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit igrezadecu.com. These companies may use information ( Not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. Igrezadecu.com does not accept any responsibility about the advertisements which are published at the website.
