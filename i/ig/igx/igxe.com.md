Privacy Policy

IGXE Inc. pledges to not send out customer information, subject matter or data sent to us unless required to do so by the law. We will collect any and all required information necessary to complete a transaction and make it as safe as possible for both IGXE.com and our customers. Such information may include, E-Mail, Name, Telephone Number, Address and other information. At no times will this information be sent to outside parties unless required by law.

Information or personal sensitive data sent through Live Chat, IM's, Phone Calls, E-Mails, Text Messages or any other type may be recorded and collected by 

IGXE Inc. We strongly recommend reading this policy statement so you understand what information we will collect, use or share if it is necessary. We are not responsible for outside websites or outside information that interacts or links to our company.

Information not noticeable by users may include IP address, Browser Type, Domain Names, Access Time and referrals to our website can be collected and archived. The information is used to improve safety, as well as improve the quality of service 

IGXE.com offers.

Our company uses the information provided to us to complete transactions requested by users. We may also use personal information to inform you of other products available and special promotions.

We do not sell, rent or lease client or customer information lists to any outside or third parties. Information such as e-mail, name, address, telephone number etc. will never be given to any other parties. We may share data with trusted partners to help build a better quality website, or improve service to our customers. All outside third parties are prohibited from using personal information except to provide these services to the Company, and they are required to not send any of the information provided to outside parties.

We will disclose personal sensitive information without notice, only if required by law or in other important matters that are issued by the high officials at 

IGXE Inc. As an established business we are required to use your information for such matters listed above. 

The purpose of the Privacy Policy is for you the customer to understand the use of your information.
