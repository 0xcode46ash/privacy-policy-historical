# igirlgames.com's privacy

Content Games/videos are unrated and the possibility exists some games may contain violence, suggestive themes, crude humor, blood, gore, sexual content, simulated gambling, and/or use of strong language.   


Our Privacy

No information is sold or released to anyone about visitors to Our site or apps without prior consent or a court order. In other words, no information is released to anyone unless you give permission first. 

Our site or apps does not attempt to collect personal information from children. Note that Our site or apps cannot always determine that a user is a child. Our site or apps does not maintain databases of children or adults.

When visiting any Internet site, your unique address called an "IP address" is recorded. Our site or apps does not release any information about the collection of this address to any third party except under court order. Our site or apps archives the log files in order to create aggregate statistical reports, detect errors on the web site, and for security reasons. The log files are eventually deleted.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](https://www.networkadvertising.org/). 

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. Our site or apps's service is free for all to use, and prior registration is not needed.

Our site or apps will explicitly ask when it needs information that personally identifies you. All personal identifiable information Our site or apps collects are only gathered with your voluntary act. Our site or apps keeps all information private and does not, and will not, share it with any third parties except under these circumstances:

\- When such action is necessary to conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process.

\- When such action is necessary to protect and defend the rights or property of Our site or apps.

Our site or apps uses cookies as part of tracking process for advertisement banners so you do not see the same ad repeatedly, but cookies will not be used to personally identify you without your prior consent.

Our site or apps uses subscription forms for individuals to subscribe to its ezine . Our site or apps will use your email address to provide you with the ezine, which is usually mailed daily. Our site or apps will never send you any unsolicited email outside of the ezine.

Our site or apps does not and will not share, sell or rent your email address to outside third parties. Neither does it supplement your information with any outside third parties. The contents of Our site or apps's mailing list will only be disclosed due to law or court demands. 

Unsubscribing from Our site or apps's ezine is simple and can be done by following the instructions at the end of every email sent.
