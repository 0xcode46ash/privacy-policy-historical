# iFIT

iFIT.com respects your privacy. We have established this policy to define when we collect personally identifiable information from visitors to iFIT.com and what we do with such information. 

**Information Collection and Use**

  * iFIT.com collects personally identifiable information when you register for certain iFIT.com personalized services and when you enter promotions and sweepstakes. iFIT.com may also receive personally identifiable information from business partners. 
  * When you register with iFIT.com, we ask for your email address. When you register for free iFIT.com personalized health and fitness programs, we ask for your email address, age, gender, weight, height, and personal goals and interests. When you use a credit card to pay for any of iFIT.com's services or products, we ask for your name, address, credit card and credit card-related information. Once you give us your name and address -- and log on to our services -- you are not anonymous to us. 
  * iFIT.com automatically receives and records information in our server logs from your browser including your IP address, cookie information and the page(s) you request. 
  * iFIT.com may use information in three general ways: to respond to your requests for information, services and products; to contact with reports about your personalized services and about special offers; and, to customize both the content and advertising you see on iFIT.com pages. 



**Information Sharing and Disclosure**

  * iFIT.com DOES NOT sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone. 
  * iFIT.com DOES send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or individuals when: 
  * you have given us your consent to share such information; 
  * it is necesary to share your information in order to provide a product or service you request; 
  * it is necessary to send information to companies who work on behalf of iFIT.com to provide a product or service you request. (Unless we advise you otherwise, such companies do not have the right to use personally identifiable information except in ways necessary to assist us in the fulfillment of your request.); 
  * it is necessary, in our judgment, to respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; or 
  * in our judgment, your actions relative to the iFIT.com web site violate our Terms of Service or any of our usage guidelines for iFIT.com services or products. 



**Cookies**

  * iFIT.com may place and access iFIT.com cookies on your computer. 
  * iFIT.com may allow companies that present advertisements on iFIT.com pages to place and access their own cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies. Such advertisers do not have access to iFIT.com cookies. 



**Editing Your Personal Information and Preferences**

  * iFIT.com allows you to edit your personal profile and preferences at any time. 



**Security**

  * Your iFIT.com personal profile information is password-protected for your privacy and security. 
  * iFIT.com may use industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect certain data transmissions. 



**Changes to this Privacy Policy**

  * iFIT.com may change this policy from time to time. If we make a substantial change, we will post a prominent notification on iFIT.com web pages.
