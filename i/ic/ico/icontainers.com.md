# Privacy Policy | iContainers US

_Last update: April 2012_

iContainers USA Inc. has created this website to allow SME’s and indivuduals to quote and book online seafreight and arifreight services with origin or destination to the United States in FOB or CIF conditions depending on what customer needs.

The following discloses our Privacy Policy information gathering and dissemination practices for the icontainers.com website.

**By visiting, quoting and booking through[www.icontainers.com](http://www.icontainers.com/) you agree to these conditions. Please read them carefully.**

## Information and cookies

The information collected is used to market and improve the services we offer, to improve our website content and to contact customers or vendors with service updates, technical updates, or for other marketing purposes. All the information from your accounts is prívate and is only use to improve your experience and provide international transport services.

When you log in, quote and book in iContainers.com or send e-mails to any iContainers account you consent to receive communications from us electronically. We will communicate with you by e-mail or by posting notices on your user intranet. You agree that all agreements, notices, disclosures and other communications that we provide to you electronically satisfy any legal requirement that such communications be in writing.

You may not use iContainers services for any illegal purpose. We may cease providing any and we may terminate your right to use iContainers at any time. Your rights to use iContainers will automatically terminate without notice from us if you fail to comply with any of these Conditions of Use or any other Service Terms.

We store Cookies from users in order to personalize and better understand their needs and offer them a better experience using the website. Our site also uses cookies to help us recognize returning customers and better administrate the repeat functionality of our website. The information we learn from customers helps us personalize and continually improve your experience.

iContainers also receives and stores any information you enter on our Web site in order to fulfill shipment requirements. All these information is only used by iContainers or iContainers suppliers to formally compile with maritime regulations and international trade best practices. This information includes shipping and receiving addresses, product descriptions and the date and time of the shipment. All of this information is required in order to correctly process a shipment request.

Access is restricted by the use of an email address and password by user. Users can change, modify or cancel their data using their intranet or sending and email to [it@icontainers.com](mailto:it@icontainers.com). Customers are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of the account and password and for restricting access to their computers, and they agree to accept responsibility for all activities that occur under their account or password.

Users may check the privacy statement periodically to view any changes or updates. If a customer wants to cancel its account an email to it@icontainers.com will be required.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this website, you can contact the company at email: [infousa@icontainers.com](mailto:infousa@icontainers.com) or [info@icontainers.com](mailto:info@icontainers.com).
