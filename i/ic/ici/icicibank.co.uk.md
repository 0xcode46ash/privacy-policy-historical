# ICICI Bank UK :: Privacy Policy

|   
---  
  
| 

**Data Protection** ICICI Bank is the data controller of your information.   
In this clause "Group" means the ICICI Bank Limited group of companies. Your information may be stored on a Group database and may be used by us and any Group company for the purposes set out in this clause. 

" **Your information** " includes any information which we or any Group company holds now or at any time in the future and which comes from or relates to:   
  
---  
|  | Applications made to us or any Group company;   
---|---  
| Dealings with us or any Group company;  
| Third parties (such as, credit reference agencies, fraud prevention agencies or other third parties associated with you);   
| Products and services supplied to you by us or any Group company;   
| Your account(s), including the way you use and manage your account(s),  
| 

Your transactions (including details of the goods or services and the supplier to which the transaction relates) and the payments which are made to and from your account(s) (including the method by which made such payments are made e.g. by card or other non-cash method).   
  
  
  
**We and other Group companies will access, use and analyse your information for the following purposes;**  
|  | To manage, maintain and develop our relationship with you;   
---|---  
| To consider any applications made by you; :   
| To manage your account(s);   
| To give you statements and provide our services,  
| To make financial risk assessments including money laundering checks, fraud prevention and regulatory compliance and reporting;   
| 

Unless you have indicated otherwise on your application form, to decide which of our Group companies and third party products, services and events may be of interest to you and to provide you with information in relation to such products, services and events;   
  
| Making credit related decisions about you;   
| For assessment and analysis purposes including behaviour scoring and market and product analysis and   
| To develop and improve our services to you.   
  
  
  
From time to time the way in which and purposes for which we process your information may change. Where you continue to use your account(s) for 2 months after we have notified you of such a change, unless you have written to us to tell us that you do not consent to that change, you will have consented to such change. **We will not disclose any of your information outside the Group except:**  
  
We will deal with your information in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998.   
In order to provide the services which you have requested or may request from time to time your information will be transferred Group companies and third parties which are in other countries. Such countries may not have laws to protect your information. If we transfer your information to a Group company or third party which is based in another country, we will make sure that such Group Company or third party agrees to apply the same levels of protection as we are required to apply to information held in the UK and to use your information only for the purpose of providing the service to us.   
You may access your information (in return for a reasonable fee) and request details the entities with whom we have shared your information by writing to Operation Manager, ICICI Bank UK Ltd. 21 Knightsbridge London SW1X 7LY. You may correct factual errors in your personal information or request that we remove personal information about you from our records by sending us a request detailing the error or your instructions to remove information from our records. To protect your privacy and security, we may take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access to your information or making the requested alterations.  
Where you borrow or may borrow from us, we may give your personal information including details of your account and how you manage it to credit reference agencies. If you borrow and do not repay in full and on time we may tell credit reference agencies who will record the outstanding debt.
