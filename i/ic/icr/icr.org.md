# Privacy Policy

The Institute for Creation Research will not provide information about you to another organization.   


If you no longer wish to receive our free publications, please [click here](http://www.icr.org/change) to unsubscribe.   


No information is collected than is typically collected in server logs by web sites in general. If you choose to provide additional information, such as your address, this will allow us to deliver to you the resources that you have requested. 

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact our [contact us](mailto:webmaster@icr.org?subject=Privacy%20Policy%20Question&body=Regarding%20your%20privacy%20policy%20at%20http%3A//icr.org/home/privacy%2C%20).

Thank you.
