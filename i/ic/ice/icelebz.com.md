# Privacy Policy : iCelebZ.com

_**iCelebZ.com Privacy Policy**_

iCelebZ.com is sincerely committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. Below is the information mentioned of our continued support for the responsible management of consumer information.

_Consumer Information_

  
We share aggregate information (not individual data) with our advertisers to help them better understand our services and to monitor the reach of their advertising on the service.     
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp). 

iCelebZ.com also links to a wide variety of other sites. We are not responsible for their privacy policies or how they treat information about their users. 

If you have any questions concerning the privacy policies of iCelebZ.com which are not covered in this statement, please send email with your questions to [webmaster@icelebz.com](mailto:webmaster@icelebz.com?Subject=Privacy%20Policy) with the words "Privacy Policy" in the subject field.
