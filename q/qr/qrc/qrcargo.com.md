# Qatar Airways Cargo

  
Privacy Policy  
  
General  
  


Your privacy is very important to Qatar Airways Cargo and we understand how important it is to you. Qatar Airways Cargo aims to be as clear and open as it can be about what it does with your information. Once you choose to provide us with any information by which you can be identified, then it will only be used in accordance with this privacy policy. This privacy policy applies to the entire website. Please view this policy every time you use the website as its terms may change and by using this website you are deemed to have read and agreed to the most recent version of this policy.

When you use this website and have not advised Qatar Airways Cargo to the contrary, you are agreeing that Qatar Airways Cargo may use your personal information for the purposes set out in this policy.

Qatar Airways Cargo is a company based in Doha, State of Qatar and it utilises support and other functions which may require the transfer of your personal data to and from the European Economic Area from time to time. By using this website you are acknowledging and agreeing to the processing of your data in countries where the data protection laws are not as extensive as in the European Economic Area. Please refrain from supplying your data and contact us if you do not agree to the transfer of your data in the manner set out in this policy.

Security  


Qatar Airways Cargo is committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, Qatar Airways has put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information it collects on-line. Qatar Airways Cargo uses encryption when collecting or transferring sensitive data such as credit card information.

  
Links  


This website may contain links to enable you to visit other websites. However, once you have used these links to leave this website, Qatar Airways Cargo does not have any control over that other website. Therefore, Qatar Airways Cargo cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide whilst visiting such sites and such sites are not governed by this privacy policy.
