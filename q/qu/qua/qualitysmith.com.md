# About Us

Privacy

The privacy of our customers has always been of utmost importance to us at QualitySmith. The following statement explains our policy regarding the personal information we collect about you. 

  


  * You do not have to give us personal information to visit our site.
  * We will collect personally identifiable information (name, email address, or other unique identifier) only if specifically and knowingly provided by you.
  * Personally identifying information you provide will be used only in connection with QualitySmith programs and services or for such other purposes as are described at the point of collection.
  * Information is collected for statistical purposes and QualitySmith sometimes performs analyses of user behavior in order to measure customer interest in the various areas of our site.
  * We do not give, sell or transfer any personal information to a third party.
  * We may enable "cookies." A "cookie" is a file placed on your personal computer's hard drive by a Web site that allows it to monitor your use of the site.
