# Privacy: quirm.net

## Privacy

We take privacy concerns very seriously and are committed to the protecting the online privacy of our web site visitors. Although we may monitor web traffic, no personally identifiable information is extracted in this process and the statistics gathered are used for internal analysis only in order to improve our services and our web site.

We value the privacy of our visitors and understand that they do not want their names or email address details to be passed onto third parties. We will not communicate personal information of this type, in any form, to any third party without the express permission of the individual(s) concerned.

### Cookies

Cookies are pieces of data — stored in the cookie directory of your computer — that are often created when you visit a website. This site uses WordPress which requires the setting of **non-tracking** cookies in order to function effectively. These cookies **cannot** be used to track your movement across this site or elsewhere. You can set your web browser not to accept cookies but, if you do this, you will not be able to use this site — or any other WordPress site — effectively.

### Deleting Cookies

Information on deleting or controlling cookies is available at [AboutCookies.org](http://www.aboutcookies.org/). Please note that by deleting our cookies or disabling future cookies, you may not be able to access our site.

### Log Files

Most site server’s generate log files that record visitors’ use of the site. We can analyse log file information in order to make changes to the layout of the site and to the information in it, based on the way that visitors move around it. Log files do not contain any personal information and they can not be used to identify any individual patterns of use.

### Application of this Policy

This policy applies solely to our web site. Where we offer links to other sites, it should be understood that we do not endorse, nor can we be held responsible for, the privacy policies of those sites.

We welcome any [questions and comments](http://quirm.net/contact/) regarding this site’s privacy policy.
