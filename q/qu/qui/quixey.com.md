# Quixey Privacy and Terms

## Privacy Policy

Effective: December 19, 2013

This Privacy Policy describes Quixey’s policies and procedures about the use of information that we collect and receive from users of our products and services available via our Site and/or mobile apps. For purposes of this Privacy Policy, “Site” means our website available at [www.quixey.com](http://www.quixey.com/); “Service” means the services provided through our Site, platform, and through mobile apps. We will not share your Personal Information with others except as described in this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy does not apply to any third-party services that you may access through our Services. For purposes of this privacy, “Personal Information” means information about you that can be used to contact or identify you as an individual, such as your name, phone number, email address and/or physical address. In some instances Quixey acts as a service provider for our Clients. In these instances. Quixey collects information under the direction of its Clients, and has no direct relationship with the individuals whose personal data it processes.

**TRUSTEs Privacy Seal**

Quixey Incorporated (“Quixey”) has been awarded TRUSTe’s Privacy Seal signifying that this Privacy Policy and practices have been reviewed by TRUSTe for compliance with TRUSTe’s program requirements  including transparency, accountability and choice regarding the collection and use of your Personal Information. The TRUSTe program covers only information that is collected through the Site and the Service. TRUSTe’s mission, as an independent third party, is to accelerate online trust among consumers and organizations globally through its leading privacy Trustmark and innovative trust solutions. If you have questions or complaints regarding our privacy policy or practices, please contact us at privacy@quixey.com. If you are not satisfied with our response you can contact [TRUSTe here](http://watchdog.truste.com/pvr.php?page=complaint&url=).

**Information We Collect**

We may collect information in different ways:

**Information You Decide to Provide** :  We do not collect Personal Information about you through the Site unless you provide it to us voluntarily.  For instance, if you decide to contact us by email through the Site, we may collect your name, company name, email address and telephone number and other Personal Information that you decide to submit to us. Similarly, if you desire to have access to certain sections of the Site (e.g., because you decide to participate in one of the “Quixey challenges”), you may be required to become a registered user and to submit certain Personal Information to us to allow you to use certain features of the Site.

**Information Collected Through Your Use of the Services:** When you use our Services we automatically collect certain information about the Services that you use and how you use them.  This information may include:

  * **Device Information:** We may collect device-specific information such as a device identifier, user settings, the operating system, information about other software apps you may access on your device, as well as other information about your use of our Services. We may collect your location and/or use various technologies to determine your location, such as sensor data from your device that provides information on nearby Wi-Fi access points and cell towers. We may also index and collect information about the mobile applications that you have downloaded onto your mobile device, such as names, application identifiers and usage statistics.
  * **Email Address** : When you use our Service on a mobile device, we may collect your email address.
  * **Connection Through Social Networking Sites** : We may allow you to connect to our Service using Facebook Connect or other social networking site authentication option. When you connect through a social networking site, we may collect information sufficient to link any applicable profile data to information available to Quixey about you. These services will authenticate your identity and provide you the option to share information about your activities on Quixey’s Site or Service with others within your social network
  * **Log Files:** When you use our Services, our servers automatically receive and record certain information  from your browser and compute, such as your web request, Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, browser type, referring / exit pages and URLs, number of clicks, domain names, landing pages, pages viewed.
  * **Cookies and Anonymous Identifiers:** We may send one or more “cookies” – small data files – to your computer to uniquely identify your browser and improve your use of our Services.  A cookie may convey anonymous information about how you use our Services, but does not collect Personal Information about you. A persistent cookie remains on your computer after you close your browser so that it can be used by your browser on subsequent uses of our Services.  Persistent cookies can be removed by following your web browser’s directions. A session cookie is temporary and disappears after you close your browser.  You can reset your web browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent, however, some features of our Services may not function properly if the ability to accept cookies is disabled.  We may also use clear GIFs (also known as web beacons) which are used to track the online usage patterns of our users anonymously.  No personally identifiable information is collected using these clear GIFs.  In addition, we may also use clear GIFs in HTML-based emails sent to our users to track which emails are opened by recipients.  This information is used to enable more accurate reporting and make our Services better for our users. ****



**Information From Other Sources** :  We may receive information about you from other sources, such as entities that can help us improve the quality or personalization of our Service to you.

**How We Use the Information We Collect**

We use the information we collect through our Services to provide, maintain, protect and improve those Services; to develop new services; to protect our servers, and users; and to offer you personalized content– like giving you more relevant search results.  For example, we may use the information we collect:

  * to provide our Services and any information you request, and to process and complete any transactions;
  * to respond to your emails, comments, and requests; and to provide customer service;
  * to personalize and improve our Services and your experience when you use our Services;
  * to increase the functionality and user friendliness of our Services;
  * to monitor and analyze usage and trends of our Services and third-party services;
  * to send you confirmations, updates, security alerts, and support and administrative messages and otherwise facilitate your use of, and our administration and operation of, our Services;
  * to send or display, or facilitate the sending or displaying of, advertisements tailored to your interests; and
  * for the purpose for which the information was collected.



In addition, if you decide to provide information about yourself via the Site, such as your name, phone number and email address, or if you use our Service on a mobile device we may use your email address to send you Service-related notices (including any notices required by law, in lieu of communication by postal mail). We may also use your Personal Information to send you marketing messages. If you do not want to receive such messages, you may opt out by following the instructions in the message.

**Information We Share with Others**

We may share the information we have collected from you:

  * when you give us your consent to do so, including if we notify you that the information you provide will be shared in a particular manner and you provide such information;
  * with service providers who are working with us in connection with the operation of our Services (these service providers may have access to your Personal Information but only to the extent necessary to perform services on our behalf and are subject to this Privacy Policy and obligated not to disclose that information or use it for any other purposes.);
  * if you decide to use certain third-party services that require the sharing of information;
  * when we reasonably believe that we are legally authorized or required to do so or that doing so is reasonably necessary or appropriate to comply with a law, regulation or to respond to lawful requests or legal authorities, including responding to subpoenas, warrants or court orders;
  * if we believe that disclosure is reasonably necessary to detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues;
  * to enforce or apply this Privacy Policy, our Terms or other policies or agreements or to protect our rights or property; and
  * in connection with any merger, sale of company assets, financing or acquisition, or in any other situation where user information may be disclosed or transferred as one of our business assets.



We may also share aggregated or anonymous information (about you but doesn’t identify you):

  * for usage analytics to help us better understand who is using our Services and how;
  * to improve the relevancy of the ads you see on our Services and to tailor then to your interests; and
  * for industry analysis, demographic profiling, research, analysis and other similar purposes.



**Information Collected and Used by Third Parties**

**Third Party Analytics:** We may use analytics providers to help us understand the use of our Services. These providers will also collect the information sent by your mobile device or browser, and their use of that information is governed by their applicable privacy policy. We use Google Analytics and have enabled Google Analytics Demographics and Interest Reporting. We use the reports we receive from Google Analytics to analyze usage patterns of Quixey’s Service and aggregate Quixey user demographics.  Google provides access to your [Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) to enable you opt out of Google Analytics features. For web browsing, Google also provides a [browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/) that users can download and install to prevent their data from being collected and used by Google Analytics.

**Third Party** **Advertisers:** We may allow third-party advertisers to serve advertisements on our Services. These third-party advertisers may use technology to send the advertisements and links that appear on our Services directly to your browser or mobile device. They may also use other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons) to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and to personalize the advertising content. We do not provide any personally identifiable information to these third-party advertisers without your consent. Our Privacy Policy does not cover the information practices of other companies and organizations who advertise on our Services, and who may use cookies, web beacons and other technologies to serve and offer relevant ads. To learn more about behavioral advertising or to opt-out of this type of advertising for participating ad networks, you can visit the [Network Advertising Initiative](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/) or the [Digital Advertising Alliance](http://www.aboutads.info/choices).

**Third Party Services:** Our Privacy Policy does not apply to the products and services you select that are offered by other companies or individuals, including products or applications that may be displayed to you in search results; products and services that may include our Services; or other products and applications linked from our Services.

**How We Protect Your Information**

We use physical, managerial, and technical safeguards that are designed to preserve the integrity and security of your information. We cannot, however, ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or guarantee that your information on our Services may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed by breach of any of our physical, managerial, or technical safeguards. You should not provide any sensitive information about you or others via the Site.

**Your Choices About Your Information**

You may decline to submit Personal Information through our Site, in which case we may not be able to provide certain services to you.  You can update or correct your Personal Information through the applicable Services or by contacting us. You may opt out of information collection on your mobile device by uninstalling the Quixey mobile app.  If you uninstall the Quixey mobile app from your mobile device, the identifier will continue to be stored.

When acting as a service provider, Quixey has no direct relationship with the individuals whose personal data it processes. An individual who seeks access, or who seeks to correct, amend, or delete inaccurate data should direct his query to Quixey’s Client (the data controller).

Quixey will retain Personal Information and the data we process on behalf of our Clients for as long as needed provide services to our Client. Quixey will retain and use this Personal Information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

We may need to process your Personal Information on our servers in countries around the world, which may not be located in the country where you live.

Quixey complies with the U.S. – E.U. and U.S. – Swiss Safe Harbor Framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use and retention of personal data from European Union member countries and Switzerland.  Quixey has certified that it adheres to the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles of notice, choice, onward transfer, security, data integrity, access, and enforcement. To learn more about the Safe Harbor program, and to view Quixey’s certification, please visit <http://www.export.gov/safeharbor>.

**Children’s Privacy**

We do not knowingly collect or solicit Personal Information from anyone under the age of 13. If you learn that your minor child has provided us with Personal Information without your consent, please contact us.  If we become aware that we have collected Personal Information from a child under age 13 without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information.

**Changes to Our Privacy Policy**

Any information that is collected is subject to the Privacy Policy in effect at the time such information is collected.  If we change our privacy policies and procedures, we will post those changes on this page to keep you aware of what information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we may disclose it.  Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.  By continuing to access or use our Services after those changes become effective, you are agreeing to be bound by the revised policy.

If you have any questions about our data collection practices or this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [privacy@quixey.com](mailto:privacy@quixey.com).
