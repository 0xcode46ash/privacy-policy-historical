# Policies

  
|  |   |   
---|---|---  
  |   
Quickbeds.com recognises the importance of protecting the privacy of all information provided by our users. It is for this reason that we make every effort to ensure the confidentiality of the information you provide us with. The information you provide us with will assist in the development of our service. We are committed to your privacy and therefore will not sell or exchange any personally identifiable information obtained from you to a third party unless you consent. Quickbeds.com periodically sends out e-mail newsletters and promotional e-mail to our users about services and products offered by Quickbeds.com. Users can indicate that they do not wish to receive e-mail information from Quickbeds.com or its advertisers by de-registering where indicated on the homepage.
