# Privacy Policy – QuietAgent

Last updated: February 1, 2010

QuietAgent, Inc. is committed to protecting the privacy of our Website users. This Privacy Policy is designed to assist you in understanding how QuietAgent, Inc. (”QuietAgent”, “we”, “us”, “our”) uses and safeguards information users provide while using QuietAgent.

Privacy Policy Summary

  * We collect very limited personal information
  * We never sell, rent, loan or otherwise provide access to our users’ information to any party
  * We only use personal information for improving our user’s experience with our website
  * We encrypt all personal information when it is sent over the Internet
  * We encrypt personal information stored in our database so it is not readblae by a human
  * We adhere to the International Safe Harbor Privacy Principles



**1 Personal Information We Collect:** We may collect limited personal information from our users, including their name and contact information such as their email address and phone number(s) (“Personal Information”), in order to make a user’s experience with our products and services more enjoyable. In order to invite others to join QuietAgent, users may enter their email addresses. This information will be used by us to send your invitation, which may include a message from you. The email addresses of people who you invite will be used only to send your invitation and reminders.

**2 Access to Information:** QuietAgent does not sell, rent, loan, trade, lease or otherwise share or provide access to our users’ personally identifiable information to any party whatsoever.

QuietAgent offers an “Anonymous Public Profile” feature which allows users to publish their anonymous QuietAgent profile to the public Internet. The default setting for a user’s Anonymous Public Profile is set to “public.” Users can review and change the default setting by editing their My Account settings, however QuietAgent does not warrant how often third-party search engines will update their cache.

**3 Information We Do Not Collect:** We will not knowingly collect nor make available a user’s:

  * Age
  * Gender
  * Family or marital status
  * Sexual orientation
  * Color, race, nationality or ethnic origin
  * Religious or ethical beliefs
  * Disability(ies)
  * Country of Birth
  * Social Security or other personal identification such as Federal Tax ID, etc.
  * Any other information about our users from other online or offline sources



**4 Updating Information:** Users may, at any time, log into their account and update their Personal Information, by visiting the “My Account” section of the Website.

**5 Usage Tracking:** We may also automatically track certain information based upon a user’s usage patterns on the Website in order to make their experience with products and services more enjoyable. This information may include what they click on, the preferences they set while on the Website, what pages they visit on the Website, and the URL that a user has just come from and the URL a user may go to next (whether this URL is on our Website or not), a user’s computer browser information, and their IP address.

**6 Cookies:** Our Website uses a feature on Internet browsers called a “cookie”. Cookies are small files that Internet browsers place on a computer’s hard drive. We use cookies for enhancing a user’s experience when they use the Website, for items such as remembering a user’s login name and general Website preferences.

**6.1 Third Party Cookies:** Our Website may use Third Party Cookies from time-to-time, to show our users related products and services offered by another company. These companies may collect, store and use information about our user’s visits to our and other websites, however, we will never provide such third-parties with our user’s name, phone number(s), address or other identifying information.

**7 Use of Personal Information:** You agree that QuietAgent may use your Personal Information to contact you and deliver information to you that, in some cases, is targeted to your interests (such as relevant job postings and services), or provides administrative notices or communications applicable to your use of the Website. By accepting this Privacy Policy, you expressly agree to receive this information. If you do not wish to receive these communications, we encourage you to opt out of any further receipt by following the opt-out provisions provided in each such communication, and / or via the “My Account” section of the Website.

**8 Data Transmission Security:** We comply with industry standards of data transmission security to help protect your information. Personally identifying and / or credit card information you enter or update on the Website is encrypted using industry standard 1024 bit SSL encryption technology before being sent over the Internet. For more information on this encryption service, you can visit [http://www.thawte.com](http://www.thawte.com/).

**9 Encrypted Data Storage:** You may have heard of attacks on databases in the past, where personal information was stolen from an online database. QuietAgent encrypts any personal identifying information using an industry standard “One-Way” data storage encryption. Data stored in our database, such as name, address, phone, email, password, etc, is unreadable to a human and must be decrypted using the secret keys built into our technology. For more information on database encryption, visit: <http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/cc278098.aspx>

**10 Safe Harbor:** QuietAgent has certified its adherence to the U.S. Department of Commerce’s Safe Harbor Principles in connection with the U.S. implementation of the European Union Data Protection Directive. For more information about the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles, please visit: <http://www.export.gov/safeharbor/>

**11 No Guarantee:** You should be aware that when using any website and the Internet in general, that there is always an element of risk that someone can and will access your information. We are absolutely committed to protecting your privacy, however we do not promise, and you should not expect, that your Personal Information or private communications will always remain private. As a user of our Website you understand and agree that you assume all responsibility and risk for your use of the Website, the Internet in general, and the information you post or access to, on or from this Website.

**12 Your Agreement:** You agree that by using the Website, that you are, by doing so, accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Furthermore, you agree that QuietAgent may from time-to-time update this Privacy Policy by providing such revised terms to you or by publishing the revised Privacy Policy on the Website. Any such modifications will supersede all prior versions after the revised version has been posted on the Website or otherwise made available as described above, and your continued use of the Website after posting or availability constitutes your agreement to the revision.

**13 Dispute Resolution:** Any controversy or claim arising out of or relating to this contract, or the breach thereof, shall be determined by arbitration administered by the International Centre for Dispute Resolution in accordance with its International Arbitration Rules. <http://www.adr.org/sp.asp?id=22090>
