# Quid

At Quid®, we are committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information.  It’s yours, it matters to you, and we get that.  This Privacy Policy is to let you know what information we use and collect from users of [www.quid.com](http://www.quid.com/) and other Quid URLs and websites, the Quid software platform (“Quid Platform”), any of our services (“Services”), and on behalf of our clients. The Quid Platform shall be included in the definition of Services for purposes of this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy is subject to the [Terms of Service](https://quid.com/terms-and-conditions) posted on the Sites.  By using our Sites or Services, you expressly consent to Quid’s collection, storage, use and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

#### E.U. SAFE HARBOR

Quid complies with the U.S. – E.U. Safe Harbor framework and the U.S. – Swiss Safe Harbor framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of personal data from European Union member countries and Switzerland. Quid has certified that it adheres to the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles of notice, choice, onward transfer, security, data integrity, access, and enforcement. To learn more about the Safe Harbor program, and to view Quid’s certification, please visit <http://www.export.gov/safeharbor/>.

#### INFORMATION COLLECTED FROM OUR SITES AND SERVICES

  1. **Automatic Collection:** We use automated mechanisms such as cookies to keep track of users’ interactions with the Sites and the Services.  This may include your Internet protocol address, referring website address, browser type and access times.  We refer to this type of date as “anonymous” user information.  A glossary that explains each of these italicized terms is included at the end of this Privacy Policy. We also use third party analytics such as Google Analytics to gather and analyze aggregated anonymous user information.
  2.  **Volunteered:  ** If you contact us or register to receive our newsletter, open an account with us, purchase products or services, send us an email, or share personal information with us in any other way, we collect the information you share.  Depending on what information you choose to provide, this may include your name, email address or billing information.  You do not have to share this information with us, but without it, you may not be able to access certain content or features or use certain Services.
  3.  **Posted:  ** If you post information on public areas of the Sites (or elsewhere on the Internet) that information may be collected and used by anyone, including Quid.



#### HOW DO WE USE THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT?

The short answer is that we generally use this information to help Quid provide you with better service, which in turn helps cover the cost of providing our Sites and our Services and third party websites and services.  Among other things, the information we collect can enable Quid to:

  * customize the content and advertising you see
  * fulfill your requests for Services and validate your user rights
  * contact and communicate with you
  * conduct research and analysis on behalf of Quid and/or Quid’s clients
  * provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients
  * provide you with additional information we think is of interest to you



#### WHEN DO WE SHARE OR DISCLOSE YOUR INFORMATION?

Quid will not sell or disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as specified in this Privacy Policy.

  * **Anonymous Information.**   The Site may track the total number of visitors to the Site, the number of visitors to each page of the Site, browser type, IP addresses, external websites linked to and other usage statistics collected and used in aggregate form collected through the Site and we may analyze this data for trends and statistics in the aggregate but such information will be maintained, used and disclosed in aggregate form only and it will not contain personally identifiable information. We may use such aggregate information to analyze trends, administer the Site, track users’ movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. We may freely share anonymous user information with others, such as clients, advertisers, sponsors and business partners.
  *  **Personally Identifiable Information.**   In more limited circumstances, we may share personally identifiable information we have collected.  For example:
    * We might share personally identifiable information during due diligence or in connection with a sale, merger, consolidation, change in control, transfer of substantial assets, reorganization or liquidation.
    * If you give us permission, we may share your personally identifiable information with third parties who may send you marketing and promotional information.
    * Your personal information may be transferred to anyone who is helping us make the Sites and Services available and functional, like technical agents, payment processing vendors, other subcontractors, and our affiliates, agents and consultants.
    * As in any transaction, if you provide your credit card information to us for purchases, your credit card company will be provided with relevant information about us, services purchased, cost and other information necessary to process the transaction.
    * We may disclose your personal information if permitted or required by law, or where we reasonably believe such action is necessary in order to protect or defend our interests or the interests of our employees or users or business partners, such as to protect, establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims, or when we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to comply with the law, prevent imminent physical harm or financial loss, or investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, threats to our property, suspected fraud, or violation of [Quid’s Terms of Service](https://quid.com/terms-and-conditions).



#### CHILDREN UNDER 13

The Sites are not intended for children under age 13, or for anyone under age 18 without involvement of a parent or guardian.  The Sites may include unmonitored content posted by users or sourced from other third parties and such content may be unsuitable for children.   We do not knowingly collect or distribute information from or about children under 13.

#### THIRD PARTIES

Third parties may directly gather anonymous user information from users of our Sites through automated means such as cookies.   For example, companies who place ads on our Sites may set their own cookies or use other automated mechanisms that gather anonymous user information.   This Privacy Policy does not cover how third parties gather and use such data. 

In addition, the Sites and Services may contain links to third party websites and content, over which Quid has no control.  These third parties’ privacy policies may be different from our Privacy Policy, and you access such linked websites or third party content at your own risk.

#### SECURITY

We take your security seriously and take reasonable steps to protect and secure your personal information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure, including through secure socket layer, password protection and, for certain transmissions to our Services, encryption. Any information collected by Quid is stored on servers that Quid manages, using standard security procedures and practices appropriate to the nature of the information.  Although we take reasonable security measures to protect your personal information, please be aware that no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.  As a result, Quid cannot guarantee or warrant the security of any information you transmit on or through the Sites or Services and you do so at your own risk. We are not responsible for circumvention of any privacy settings or security measures contained on the Site.

**Glossary**

  * _Cookie:  _ an alphanumeric identifier that we may transfer to your computer’s hard drive through your browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser and tell us how and when pages in our site are visited and by how many people.  A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser.  A persistent cookie remains on your hard drive for an extended period of time.
  *  _Internet protocol address:  _ an individual “name” that is assigned to each computer on the Internet, so that each computer can be told apart.
  *  _Referring website address:  _ the domain name or URL of the webpage you were visiting right before you came to the Sites.
  *  _Browser:_   is the software application on your computer that lets you “see” websites.  Common browsers are Microsoft Internet Explorer, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Opera and Apple Safari.
