# Quick Bookmarks - Privacy Policy

##  Quick Bookmarks Privacy Policy

We collect your personal information (nicknames and email addresses), when you register to use Quick Bookmarks website and services.

In order to provide you this service, we need to store on our servers your bookmarks, notes and all the data that you insert in your personal account. 

We may also, at times, collect yout IP addresses and information regarding your use of our website and services.   
We may also collect information related to the referring URL, access times and browser type. 

Other users of the system are absolutely not able to access to your personal information and data.  
We will not share yout personal information, Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, personal bookmarks and usage information with third parties. 

In order to offer and provide optimized and personalized services, we use cookies to store and sometimes track information about our users. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to a user's browser from a web server and is eventually stored on a user's computer hard drive. 

Visit your Quick Bookmarks Settings to edit your profile and other information. When you're signed in, Quick Bookmarks page headers will include a link to the Settings page.   
You can delete your Quick Bookmarks account and all the information about you sending an email to support@quickbookmarks.com. 

Quick Bookmarks reserves the right to modify all or some of this Privacy Policy at any time without notice.  
If we change our Privacy Policy, the new policy will be available in this place. 

We will use information only in accordance with the Privacy Policy under which the information was collected unless we have received explicit authorization from the appropriate user(s) to do otherwise. 

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can send an e-mail to [ support@quickbookmarks.com](mailto:support@quickbookmarks.com).
