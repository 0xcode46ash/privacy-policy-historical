# Voucherzap.co.uk Privacy Policy

#### Voucherzap.co.uk Privacy Policy

At voucherzap, the security of your personal information is of vital importance to us, and we therefore treat it with the greatest of care. This policy describes how personal information is used on this website and outlines the ways in which we ensure it is safe. For more information, please contact us at info@voucherzap.co.uk.

#### Log Files

At voucherzap.co.uk, log files are used to help us discover information about the types of browser people are using, the date they have visited us, where they have come from and which pages on our site they are visiting, along with the IP address of each visitor. This information can not be traced back to you in any way and is only used to pass on information to our partners in order for the site to work correctly, and to help us analyse trends on the site enabling us to make improvements. 

#### Cookies

Detailed information about what cookies are and why we use them can be found here: [Cookie Policy](http://www.voucherzap.co.uk/cookies/).
