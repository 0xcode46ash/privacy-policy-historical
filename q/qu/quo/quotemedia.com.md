**1\. PRIVACY POLICY**

1.1 QuoteMedia is strongly committed to protecting the privacy of users of its interactive products and services.

1.2 This privacy policy applies to the QuoteMedia.com Inc. site and all of the Internet accessible QuoteMedia branded services, which we may offer from time to time, referred hereinafter as QuoteMedia. The purpose of this privacy policy is to inform you, our visitor, what types of information we may gather about you when visiting our site and how we may use that information. We will also inform you whether we disclose your information to anyone and if so, under what conditions, as well as the choices you have regarding our use of the information.

1.3 In general, our service automatically gathers certain usage information, such as; numbers and frequency of visitors to QuoteMedia and the areas visited. This collective data helps us determine how much our customers use various areas of the site, so we can improve our site. We use technology called "cookies" that tells us how and when pages in our site are visited and by how many people.

1.4 QuoteMedia cookies do not collect personally identifiable information and we do not combine information collected through cookies with other personally identifiable information to tell us who you are, your screen name, or e-mail address. Most browsers are set to accept cookies. You can set yours to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If you do so please note that some part of our site may not function properly.

1.5 We may provide information to other partners about how our members use QuoteMedia. We do this to provide you the best Web experience possible and to improve the site in general.

1.6 In the event that you register to use a specific service, such as Real Time Quotes or Portfolio, we will need certain information, such as your name, internet address, billing address, etc, and in some cases, a credit card number in order to provide you with the service or product. We may also use the information to let you know about additional products and services in which you might be interested. You can choose not to receive such information by simply letting us know when you sign up for the product or service. If you did not choose this option at the time you provided us the information, you may opt out at any time by sending an e-mail stating your preference, or to opt out at info@quotemedia.com, **SUBJECT OPT OUT**

 **2\. LINKS TO OTHER SITES AND PRIVACY**

2.1 Users will find advertising or other content on our site that link to a site or services of our partners, suppliers, advertisers, sponsors, licensors, and other third parties. In many cases, the links represent cooperative projects or are mutual links established with persons or entities connected with our site. Although we initially visit the sites or services that are so linked, we do not control the contents or links that appear on these sites. In addition, these sites or services, including their contents and links may be constantly changing. These sites and services may have their own privacy policies or no policy at all, as such; we accept no liability for their policies. 

2.2 Our web site uses a third party, DoubleClick, who is our Web Advertising Partner. In the course of delivering an ad to you, DoubleClick may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser, and may collect certain non-personally identifiable information about your visits to this and other Web sites. This information is used to better select ads that meet your preferences. We do not share this information with any other third parties. **3\. CREDIT CARD SECURITY/PRIVACY POLICY**

3.1 Protecting your privacy and your information is a top priority. We have taken careful measures to secure both your information and our premises. We exercise great care in the transfer of information from your PC to our servers. Our secure sever software encrypts information, ensuring that all Internet transactions remain private, unless sent by an unsecured means such as e-mail. It is not advisable to send credit card information at anytime via unsecured means, especially e-mail.

**4\. POLICY CHANGES**

4.1 Should we make changes to our privacy policy or disclaimer we will post such changes under the appropriate heading, here on our site, so that you will always know what information we gather and how we might use that information.

**5\. SALE OF INFORMATION**

5.1 We will not sell any personal identification information about you as an individual user such as your name, street address, telephone number or credit card number to any third party. As previously noted, we may share with our partners information about how visitors use QuoteMedia's site in order to make it more relevant to the individual user.

5.2 Please note that if you give out personal information online, through a bulletin board or chat room, that information can be collected and used by third parties. We strive to protect our users' privacy but we cannot guarantee the security of information you post in these forums. If you choose to disclose personal information in such forum it is at your own risk

**6\. RELEASE OF INFORMATION**

6.1 We will release specific information about you, or your account, to comply with any valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena, statute or court order. .

7.1 By using the QuoteMedia site you signify your acceptance of our Privacy Policy and Disclaimer. If you do not agree to this policy or disclaimer please do not use the site. Your continued use of the QuoteMedia site following the posting of any subsequent changes to these terms will mean that you have accepted such changes. Questions regarding our privacy statement and disclaimer may be directed to info@quotemeia.com.
