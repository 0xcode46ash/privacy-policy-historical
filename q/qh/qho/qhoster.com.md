# Privacy Policy

**Presentation**

QHoster understands your concerns, at the use and disclose of your personal information. We respect the confidentiality right of Internet users and do not collect your personal information without your consent.

**What data is collected?**

QHoster collects your information when you register on our site www.QHoster.com, or you make a purchase. 

When ordering or registering on our site, you may be asked to enter name, email address, postal address, telephone number, fax number or your credit card. Your normal visits to our site are anonymous.

**What your data is been used for?**

All the data that QHoster collects from you may be used in any of the following ways:

  * To better satisfy your needs


  * To improve our products and services based on feedback from you


  * To respond more effectively on your requests for service and maintenance


  * Your personal information, whether publicly available or not, will not be sold, exchanged, transferred or rented to anyone for any reason, without your consent, except with explicit purpose of the supplier of the product or service that you have bought


  * Running a contest, promotion, survey or other feature of the web site


  * To send you periodic emails



The email address that you provide for order processing, can be used to send you information and news related to the order, in addition to receiving corporate news, updates, related to the product or service, information etc..

**How do we protect your data?**

We implement security measures to maintain the security of your personal information, when you place an order or access your personal data.

We offer the use of a secure server, in which all submitted sensitive data is transferred via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into the databases, accessible only to those having access rights to such systems and who are required to keep the data secret.

After successful transaction, your personal data (credit cards, financial data, etc.), won’t be stored on our servers.

**Do we use "cookies" ?**

Yes (cookies are small files, that a site or service provider sends to your computer, through your web browser, if you allow this to happen). This enables web sites or service providers to recognize your browser, capture and remember specific data.

We use "cookies" to help us remember and process the products in your shopping cart and at the same time to understand and retain your preferences for future visits to our web site.

**Do we distribute your information to third parties?**

QHoster does not sell, trade or transfer your data to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties, who help us work on our web site www.QHoster.com, our business or service, extent that these outside parties are obliged to keep such data confidential. We may also disclose your personal information, when we believe it is appropriate to law enforcement policy on our site, or to protect our or other rights, property or safety.

**Relations with third parties**

At our discretion, we may include or offer products or services of third parties, on our web site www.QHoster.com. These third-party web sites have separate and independent from ours, Privacy policy. Therefore, QHoster is not responsible for the content and operation of third-party sites. However, we welcome any comments on these web sites.

**Protecting children's online data**

We are in compliance with COPPA (Law on protection of children’s online data). QHoster does not collect data from persons under the age of 13. Our web site, products and services are aimed at people who are aged at least 13 years or older.

**Online privacy policy**

This online privacy policy applies only to the information collected through our web site www.QHoster.com, not to information collected by other means.

**Terms and conditions**

You can read the Terms of service, limitations and responsibilities while using our services at "[Terms of Service](https://web.archive.org/web/20120513210727id_/http%3A//www.qhoster.com/tos.html "Terms of Web Hosting Services")"

**Your consent**

By using www.QHoster.com, you agree to our Privacy policy.

**Changes in Privacy policy**

If any changes in Privacy policy, they will be posted on this web site without prior notice.
