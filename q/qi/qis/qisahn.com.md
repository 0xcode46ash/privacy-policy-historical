# Privacy Notice : Qisahn.com

We collect information from customers so that we can make fast deliveries to you. Repeat customers will not need to go through the trouble of retyping their information.

We use the information you provide only to complete orders. We do not share information about you with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order.

We will require your date of birth to verify your age while you sign up. If you are under the age of 13, you may use Qisahn.com only with the involvement of a parent or guardian.

We value your privacy. If you have any further queries regarding our privacy, please contact us at: [ shop@qisahn.com](mailto:shop@qisahn.com)
