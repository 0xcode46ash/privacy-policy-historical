# Privacy Policy for visitors to tlsbooks.com

Privacy Policy for visitors to tlsbooks.com.

Tlsbooks.com and its affiliates may request certain personally identifiable information for the purpose of fulfilling an order or acknowledging a donation. We will not knowingly disclose this information to others. We may also collect non-personally identifiable information for the purpose of enhancing the website experience for our visitors, compiling site statistics or supporting an activity engaged in on this website. Examples of the information we may collect and analyze include but are not limited to your Internet protocol (IP) address, name and e-mail address. We may also collect computer and connection information such as browser type and operating system. We reserve the right to release any personal information we may have acquired from you in the event of unlawful activities (such as copyright infringement) or obscene or threatening actions by you. We will not send e-mail request or contact you via e-mail unless you have contacted us and would like a reply. We reserved the right to send correspondence in the case of illegal/unethical activity such as copyright infringement or bandwidth theft.

We may utilize third party vendors and/or advertisers on this site. These vendors and/or advertisers may include but are not limited to Adobe.com, PayPal.com, and Google.com. You understand that third party websites may utilize cookies and will have their own privacy and security policies. It is your responsibility to understand these policies. tlsbooks.com may modify this privacy policy any time and you may view these terms on our website.

Update 4/7/09  
We are currently using third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (NOT INCLUDING your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/).

The following information about Google and the [DoubleClick DART cookie](http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/faq.aspx) may be of interest to our visitors:

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.
  * Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
