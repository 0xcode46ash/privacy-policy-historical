**IMPORTANT** : BY SUBMITTING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION YOU GIVE YOUR CONSENT THAT ALL PERSONAL INFORMATION YOU SUBMIT MAY BE PROCESSED IN THE MANNER AND FOR THE PURPOSES DESCRIBED IN THE FOLLOWING PRIVACY POLICY.

### PRIVACY POLICY

Our mission is to provide a novel approach to secure cloud storage. Tresorit will allow you to share files, and collaborate with your partners, colleagues and friends with cryptographic end-to-end security guarantees. As our objective is to operate such a high-quality service perfectly, we attach great importance to the protection of your Personal Data and your right to self-determination and make every necessary precaution in order to safely handle your Personal Data.

This Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as: “Policy”) provides our policies and procedures for collecting, storing, using, processing and disclosing your personal information and also forms an integral part of the [End User License Agreement](http://tresorit.com/free-version-end-user-license-agreement) (hereinafter referred to as: “EULA”).

The provisions of this Policy are in conformity with Act CXII of 2011 on the Right of Informational Self-determination and Freedom of Information (hereinafter referred to as: “Data Protection Act”) and Directive 95/46/EC on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data.

#### 2\. Scope of Data Management

Tresorit may collect, record, store, process and transmit your Personal Data and other information only with your consent. By using our services, you give your consent to Tresorit to perform Data Management solely in accordance with this Policy.

We make all the necessary measures to safeguard and protect your Personal Data and other information you provide to us or we may obtain from your usage of our services. We think privacy is a high priority, therefore this Policy primarily focuses on the authenticity and integrity of Data Management, while the implementation of our scheme tries to take the best available technical and organizational measures in order to obviate any kinds of breach.

We NEVER collect or store your files, encryption keys and passwords in an unencrypted or invertible form. Files and some corresponding encryption keys can only be decrypted by the people you explicitly shared with.

The scope of our Data Management includes the followings:

Personal Data and other information you give us: Tresorit’s account creation process requires giving us certain Personal Data and other contact information. The collected Personal Data may include your e-mail address, first name, last name and a certificate created in relation with such information. The visibility of your Personal Data is restricted: your first and last name, e-mail address, storage account username will be accessible only by us and the people you send an invitation to, or from whom you accept an invitation. In case you accept somebody’s invitation, the inviter will be able to access your Personal Data to the same extent. You are free to decline any invitation, and if you do so, the inviter will not get access to your Personal Data. Please note that your Personal Data remains non-encrypted, in a clear text format.

Stored Content: The files you upload and store using our services will be encrypted in such a way that neither we nor any third parties can access its content and its encryption keys or files in a readable form. Our encryption process is designed to provide access to such data up to the extent allowed by you, and nobody else is able to decrypt your Stored Content. This Policy does not grant us any right to your files or intellectual property, we and the Data Processor mandated by us perform only Data Procession tasks which are needed for providing the services as explained below. You understand that in order to provide the services Tresorit may perform Data Management such as access, transmit across networks and modify the location of your Stored Content solely in an encrypted form.

Monitoring / Website / Cookies: Improving our services is really important for us, therefore we collect information for monitoring and debugging purposes. Please note that these types of information are collected automatically as a result of your use of the services or through the use of our website and web analytics services as described below. Such information may include your internet protocol address, your unique software ID, the type of browser you use, the site you visit immediately prior to visiting our website etc., but we never collect unencrypted or invertible passwords, encryption keys or files. When you are using our services or visiting our website, your activities and information regarding these activities are also logged, while the website might use cookies in order to collect information for improvement purposes. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by websites that you visit. These text files can be read by these websites and help to identify you when you return to a website. Cookies can be “persistent” or “session” cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your computer, when you have gone offline, while session cookies are deleted, as soon as you close your web browser. You are free to decline these cookies by applying the appropriate browser settings but please note that by doing so, you may experience interference during the use of our website only.

Communication between our users: As a part of our services, Tresorit delivers invitations, for the purpose of which Tresorit stores and accesses such communication between you and the person who invites you or whom you invited. All Personal Data and other information regarding invitations (e-mail address, name, tresor name and its unique URL, storage account and username of the inviter / invited person), data entered into our forms and any other communication you send us are subject to our Data Management, therefore these types of Personal Data and other information remain in a non-encrypted form until deleted in accordance with Section 5 below.

#### 3\. Purpose of Data Management

As we collect, store, transmit and process various types of Personal Data and other information, we feel that it is essential to make clear the purposes for such operations. Please note that by creating a Tresorit account and using our services you acknowledge and agree with the following objectives and purposes.

In general, we use the submitted information for functional and improvement reasons. It is also important to underline that we want to prevent and take action against any activity that is or may be in breach of the [End User License Agreement](http://tresorit.com/free-version-end-user-license-agreement), this Policy or the effective legal provisions. In light of these principles, we detail the purposes of Data Management for the following specific types of data as follows:

Information you give us / Invitation messages: Your e-mail address and your first / last name are used for identification and contact purposes, as they are necessary to create and maintain specific Tresors, as well as keeping up the communication channels among us and our users. The same provision applies to every invitation message sent out, thus we can track our user groups, and improve our service from time to time.

Monitoring / Website / Log Data: There are mainly statistical reasons behind the storage of such information regarding the visitors of our website, their browser types, internet protocol addresses or unique software IDs. By storing and analysing such information, we will be able to create in-depth analysis about our service, which is essential for improvement and debugging purposes. Please note that any data stored or collected for statistical purposes will not be disclosed to any third parties.

#### 5.  Amendment, deletion and destroying of data

Personal Data

As a registered user, you can view your Personal Data stored by us at any time as well as edit or delete these data from our database if your Personal Data have changed, or if you decide to stop using Tresor, without limitations.

You understand and accept that the deletion of your Tresorit account does not mean the immediate deletion of all of your Personal Data stored by us. For identification purposes, Tresorit reserves the right to store your e-mail address for 1 year after your user account has been deleted. Same provision applies to the invitations you have sent out, as Tresorit will store such information for 90 days after their validity has expired. Upon the expiry of the aforementioned deadlines, Tresorit destroys such data in a way that those will not be available again to anyone.

You also understand and accept that your Personal Data necessary for sending and receiving invitations may remain accessible to persons to whom you sent an invitation, or from whom you received and accepted an invitation, even if your Tresorit account is deleted or the invitation you sent or accepted has expired, or you stop sharing your Stored Content with such persons.

Stored Content

As a registered user, you can access, edit or delete your Stored Content at any time without limitations. Once your Tresorit account is deleted for any reason, your Stored Content will be automatically deleted as well.

You understand and accept that in line with the functionality of our services, in case you make any change to your Stored Content, at least 10 previous versions of your Stored Content might remain accessible to you and to the persons with whom you shared such previous versions. You may increase the number of the previous versions of your Stored Content which remains so accessible when setting your Tresorit account. You also understand and accept that previous versions of your Stored Content exceeding this number or your Stored Content which is chosen to be deleted by you or due to the termination of your Tresorit account can be restored for 90 days from the date of such deletion only by you. After the expiry of such deadline your stored content will be destroyed in a way that those cannot be restored and will not be available again to anyone, including you.

You understand that once you shared all or a part of your Stored Content by using Our Service with any person who accepted your invitation, such Stored Content goes out of your Control and remains accessible by such person to the extent you granted such person access, even if you select to delete or remove your stored content from tresor. Therefore we ask you to pay special attention with whom you share your Stored Content.
