# Trygames - Free Downloadable Games

**_Privacy Policy_**

Trymedia Systems is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. The following information explains our privacy practices. 

**Collection, Use and Disclosure of Information**  
Trymedia Systems collects the domain name and/or IP address (i.e., the location of your computer on the Internet) of each user that visits the Site for system administration and troubleshooting purposes. We do not link this information to any personally identifiable information, which means that your session on the Site will be logged, but you will remain anonymous to Trymedia Systems. In addition, some areas of the Site may permit you to submit contact and other information to Trymedia Systems. Your contact information may be used to provide you with information such as press releases and other news concerning Trymedia Systems and its products and services, changes to the Terms and Conditions, and other information Trymedia Systems believes you may find valuable. By electing to submit contact or other information to Trymedia Systems, you are consenting to such communications by Trymedia Systems. You may opt-out of receiving such communications from Trymedia Systems (see "Opt-Out" section below). Trymedia Systems does not share your contact information with any third party without your consent, except a court or governmental authority if reasonably required by law. 

**Choice / Opt-Out**  
It is up to you whether or not you provide your contact information to Trymedia Systems. Once you have provided Trymedia Systems with your contact information, it may send communications regarding its products and services, the Site or otherwise, as noted above. Trymedia Systems provides you with the opportunity, at any time, to opt-out of receiving such communications, and/or to request the removal or your contact or other information from Trymedia System's database, by indicating your desire to opt-out:   
1\. In an e-mail sent to info@trymedia.com; or   
2\. By mail to the postal address located below 

**Correct / Update Information**  
If any of the contact or other information you have provided to Trymedia Systems changes or is incorrect, you may notify Trymedia Systems via e-mail at [info@trymedia.com](mailto:info@trymedia.com) of your new, correct information. 

**_General Compliance with Laws_**  
You shall comply with all applicable laws, statutes, ordinances and regulations pertaining to your use of the Site. 

**_Applicable Law_**  
This Site is operated by Trymedia Systems from its offices within the State of California, USA. The laws applicable to the interpretation of these Terms of Use shall be the laws of the State of California, USA, and applicable federal laws, without regard to any conflict of law provisions. Company makes no representation that the Content in this Site is appropriate for access from other locations. Those who choose to access this Site from other locations do so on their own initiative and are responsible for compliance with local laws. You agree to submission of any dispute to the state and federal courts located in San Francisco, California, USA.
