# Privacy Policy - Traders Log

Traders Log (TL) is committed to protecting your privacy.  We will not disclose personal information about you to any third party without your express consent.

In the operations of TL we collect information through subscription processes.  All information collected by us will enable us to better serve you, conform with regulatory requirements, and keep you informed about special offers and/or other pertinent information. We also gather regular statistical information regarding site usage.  This information is used for analytical purposes only.  This information provides us with vital statistical information required to refine our site and improve the experience of our users. In addition, these statistics allow us to develop accurate demographics for the benefit of our advertisers and partners. At no time is any advertiser provided with an individual’s contact information unless that user grants their permission beforehand. To optimize the user experience TL may make use of cookies technology. Please note that nothing in a cookie allows anyone to identify you individually.

By registering with TL, you grant us permission to send you our various newsletter and product offers.  At times, we might also send you occasional trading-related messages from our partners that we feel are applicable. All email services are accompanied by a simple-to-use automated unsubscribe facility.
