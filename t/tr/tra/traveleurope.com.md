# Privacy policy

By using **Traveleurope.com** website, you accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy. 

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to take account of changing requirements such as changes in technology or legislation, and request that you refer back to this Policy frequently to become aware of any such revisions. In addition, we recommend that you review the privacy policy of any website accessed through the **Traveleurope.com** website as these sites may be subject to different privacy practices. **Traveleurope.com** is not responsible for these websites or for the information made available thereon. 

## Confidentiality

**Traveleurope.com** does not collect any Personal information through this website. 

**Traveleurope.com** provides information about travel products from hotel, airline, car rental and other suppliers. Such products and services can be purchased through links to third party websites as **Traveleurope.com** merely acts as an information provider, therefore we encourage you to review the information privacy practices of any and every website whose products you purchase through links from this website. 

**Traveleurope.com** is not responsible for these suppliers and their websites or for the information made available thereon. 

## Cookies

We are constantly researching the latest in technology to ensure that we are able to give you the most effective, informative and enjoyable experience on our site. A cookie is a small text file that our site may send to your hard drive when you are logged on to our site. They uniquely identify a single computer and provide us with statistical and other analytical information collected on an aggregate and non-individual specific basis helping us to improve website browsing and users' experience. 

We do not use cookies to store personal information. 

At any stage you may decide to disable cookies. If you want to know when our site is using a cookie from your hard drive, you can set your browser to send you an alert. If you prefer to use our site without utilising cookies, you can set your browser to not accept them at all, however, they will make your life easier. Many web sites are employing this cookie technology and cookies are never to be used to store information of a private nature, such as credit card numbers. 

Also, you can be assured that the information stored in any cookies initiated by **Traveleurope.com** cannot be accessed by any other web sites. Please also refer to your browser Help instructions for further details.
