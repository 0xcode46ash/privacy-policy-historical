# Privacy policy -TradeTracker, the no 1 in performance-based online marketing solutions

## Privacy policy

For TradeTracker, it is of crucial importance that your personal data are protected. TradeTracker guarantees that: 

  * Your personal data will never be sold to or traded with no matter what party;
  * The infra structure and software of the affiliate program have been made and protected in such a way that unauthorised parties cannot acquire personal information with regard to participants in TradeTracker;
  * You will have the possibility to determine at any time if you want to keep receiving information and want further communications;
  * Your data will never be released to third parties, other than for determining the accuracy of the data provided by you and verification of the transactions carried out by you;
  * The used cookies (a file that is stored on your PC to recognize you and gain automatic access to the information that is important to you) will only be used to collect visitor information, and that these cookies do not contain personal data. The collected information exclusively relates to data that cannot be traced back to persons. The collected information is not stored and is only kept as compiled data for statistical analyses.
