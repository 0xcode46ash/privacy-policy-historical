# Privacy Policy

TravelCLICK strives to offer its visitors the many advantages of Internet technology and to provide an interactive and personalized experience. We have established information handling practices that we believe are consistent with the highest standards and best practices of companies doing business on the Internet. 

Summary:

  * You can always visit our site without revealing who you are or providing any personal information. 
  * We will not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, phone number without your knowledge and permission. 



We utilize cookie technology to collect data to identify first time versus return visitors to our site. This information allows us to provide faster data retrieval for return visitors. The information we collect is used to contact current and future customers regarding news and "best practices" in the hospitality industry, and other information that only TravelCLICK can provide due to its exclusive joint partnerships and proprietary database. 

We collect personally identifiable information from users through voluntary submissions. We use good faith efforts to make it clear why the information is being collected and what it will be used for. 

Should you sign up for our email list and then change your mind and no longer wish to receive e-mail or periodic mailings from us in the future, please let us know by sending an e-mail to [Unsubscribe@travelclick.net](mailto:Unsubscribe@travelclick.net) with your email address in the subject line, and allow four weeks for the processing. 

Thank you for sharing our concern with privacy issues.
