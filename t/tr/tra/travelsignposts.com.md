We respect the privacy of those who visit our web site and those who become subscribers. In accordance with this, we have created this policy in order to demonstrate our privacy commitment to our users:

#####  What this Privacy Policy Covers

  * This Privacy Policy covers our treatment of personally identifiable information that we collect when you are on our site, and when you use our services. This policy also covers our treatment of any personally identifiable information that third parties share with us.
  * This policy does not apply to the practices of organizations that we do not own or control, or to people that we do not employ or manage.



#####  Information Collection and Use

  * We collect personally identifiable information when you register for an account, when you use our services, and when you visit our pages. We may also receive personally identifiable information from third parties.
  * When you register with us, we ask for your name and email address, and you may also supply other personal details and interests if you want to. Once you register with us and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.
  * We use this information for three general purposes: to customize the content you see, to fulfill your requests for certain services, eg forum features, and to contact you about services if you request it.
  * We also automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, cookie information and the page you requested. This information is not linked to your account and will not be used to identify you.



#####  Information Sharing and Disclosure

  * We will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.
  * We will only send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when: 
    * We have your consent to share the information; 
    * We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; or 
    * We find that your actions on our web sites violate the Terms of Service



#####  Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences

  * We give you the ability to edit your Account Information and preferences at any time, including whether you want us to contact you about new services.
  * You may request deletion of your account by sending an email to accountremove@ travelsignposts.com.



#####  Security

  * Your Account Information is password-protected for your privacy and security.
  * In certain areas we use industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.



#####  Public Forums

This site makes chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas become public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information [9].

##### Purchasing on the Web site

Travel Signposts is an Associate of Amazon.com and information provided during all purchases made on this website from Amazon is protected by Amazon's Security and Privacy policies. 

We also offer our own e-books for sale through Digibuy, a subsidiary of Digital River, whose privacy and security policies strictly protect any information supplied to them during the purchase process.

To purchase items from this website, a purchaser needs to complete an order form that gives us contact information (like their name, email address and delivery address), and financial information (like their credit card numbers) to Amazon and/or Digibuy. Credit cards details are protected by SSL encryption. All information is stored in our partners' secure databases and is only accessible by authorised staff. 

##### Third Parties

This site provides links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, usability or the content of such Web sites. 

#####  Changes to this Privacy Policy

The privacy policy is subject to modification from time to time. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes here so that you will always know what information we gather, how we might use that information and whether we will disclose it to anyone. Any significant changes to our privacy policy will be announced on our home page. If you do not agree with the changes in our policy you have the ability to remove your account information. This can be done by sending email to account_remove@travelsignposts.com.

#####  Questions or Suggestions

If you have questions or suggestions complete a feedback form or send an email to privacypolicy@travelsignposts.com.
