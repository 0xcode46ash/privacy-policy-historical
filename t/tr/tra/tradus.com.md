# Tradus - Privacy Policy

Unless personally identifiable information is voluntarily submitted , the only information that Tradus plc (and its subsidiaries) collects from visitors to this website are the date and time of their visit, the pages visited, the name of the visitors Internet service provider and the website that referred the visitor. We use this data to measure site activity, to generate statistics and to improve our services and the information provided by our site.

Any information which may identify the visitor which is voluntarily provided will be used only to fulfil the visitor's request or for the purposes indicated on the page where the information is requested. We never provide any information (personal or non-personal) to any other company for that company's independent use.
