# Trade-Schools.net - Privacy Policy

Trade-schools.net is owned and operated by [Beeline Web Site Promotions, Inc. (Beelineweb.com)](https://www.beelineweb.com/).

Beelineweb.com does not sell or rent any personal data submitted by visitors to any unaffiliated third parties. Beelineweb.com respects the privacy of users visiting our site and will abide by all applicable laws concerning the release of personal information.

The information submitted in the forms on our site is sent only to the school or college that you are requesting information about.

As an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases.

This site uses analytics systems to help improve usability and the user experience. The analytics system may record mouse clicks, mouse movements and scrolling activity, as well keystroke information that you voluntarily enter on this website.

For further information, please [_contact us_](https://www.trade-schools.net/contact.asp).
