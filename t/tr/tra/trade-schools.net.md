### UNIVERSITIES, COLLEGES & TRADE SCHOOLS

Privacy Policy

](http://www.trade-schools.net/)   
  
[Beeline Web Site Promotions, Inc.](http://www.trade-schools.net/) does not sell or rent any personal data submitted by visitors to any unaffiliated third parties. Beeline Web Site Promotions, Inc. respects the privacy of users visiting our site and will abide by all applicable laws concerning the release of personal information. 

The information submitted in the forms on our site is sent only to the school or college that you are requesting information about.

Additional information regarding AdSense ads which are displayed on certain pages of our site: 

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on certain pages of our site.
  * Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to this site and other sites on the Internet.
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
