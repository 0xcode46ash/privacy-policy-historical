# Privacy policy : TraffUP

We respect your privacy and do everything we can to safeguard your personal information. 

### Collection and use of personally identifying information

TraffUP collects your email address, name and potentially other information volunteered when you either register or communicate with us. Also when visiting TraffUP, our system may record your activity, IP address, browser information and information about referring site.

TraffUP uses the information gathered for the provision of services to you and for improving your experience with the website.

We will never share your information with a third party. Should you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please feel free to [contact us](http://traffup.net/contact/).
