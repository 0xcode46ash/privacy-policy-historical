#### Cookies

  * carview may set and access tradecarview cookies on your computer.
  * We use data collection devices called "cookies" on certain pages of the Site to help analyze the Site page flow, measure promotional effectiveness, and promote trust and safety. "Cookies" are small files placed on your hard drive that assist us in providing our services. We offer certain features that are only available through the use of a "cookie". We also use cookies to allow you to enter your password less frequently during connecting with the Site. Cookies can also help us provide information that is targeted to your interests. You are always free to decline our cookies if your browser permits, although in that case you may not be able to use certain features on the Site and you may be required to reenter your password more frequently during connecting with the Site.
