# Transport Exchange Group Privacy Policy

In this policy "we", "us" and "our" refers to Transport Exchange Group Limited (registered number: 3464353) whose registered office is at Newstead House, Pelham Road, Nottingham NG5 1AD and "you" and "your" refers to any visitor to our family of websites which include www.courierexchange.co.uk, www.haulageexchange.co.uk, CXMobile.com and others ("the Websites").

By accessing the Website and providing us with your personal details you agree to accept and be bound by this policy.

  
**  
1\. PRIVACY POLICY**

> **1.1** We may request certain personal information from you in order to provide services to you through the Website. You are responsible for the accuracy and truthfulness of this information and you warrant and undertake to us that all such information is true and correct.   
>  **1.2** We will use your personal information only for the purposes of providing services to you. We will treat your personal information as confidential and we will not disclose it to any third party except:  
>  **1.2.1** with your prior agreement;  
>  **1.2.2** as necessary for providing the goods and services to you;   
>  **1.2.3** as required by law; or  
>  **1.2.4** to any successor to or purchaser of the business and assets of our business.  
>  **1.3** We reserve the right to request supporting documentation to verify personal information at our absolute discretion.  
>  **1.4** You must notify us immediately of any change in your personal details.  
>  **1.5** Please be aware that we may have links to other websites that may collect personally identifiable information about you. This privacy statement does not cover the information, practice or policies of other such websites and you should check these with the provider of such websites.

**2\. COOKIES**

> **2.1** Cookies are small pieces of software that are automatically downloaded to your computer when you visit the Website. They are started automatically whenever you next access the Website. Cookies are used widely on many web sites.  
>  **2.2** We store cookies in order to recognise if you have been to the Website before so that we can monitor how many people are using the Website and if we are getting repeat visits. We do not use cookies to store any personal information about you.  
>  **2.3** We also use a “temporary cookie” each time you log on. This enables us to show you personalised views of information and also means that you don't have to re-enter your password every time you move between different parts of the Website. This temporary cookie is erased completely as soon as you close down your browser.  
>  **2.4** Not all browsers support cookies, although Internet Explorer and Netscape Navigator do. Some browsers permit you to disable cookies if you wish and details of how to do this should be available on your browsers help facility.
