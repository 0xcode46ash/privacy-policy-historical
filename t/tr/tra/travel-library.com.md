# Rec.Travel Library

The Rec.Travel Library was founded to help individuals share travel information and stories with each other, and believes in the rights of individuals on the Internet to liberty and to privacy. We make no attempt to track any personal or individual information about any visitor to this web site, and we do not engage in spamming. 

**Information Automatically Logged**

Certain information about all web server traffic is automatically logged by all web servers. This information consists of your IP address, the page you requested, the time of the connection, the type of web browser you use, and the address of the page containing the link that sent you here. We use this information in aggregate to find the total number of visitors to each page of the web site and what web sites bring visitors to us, and also to help identify errors (such as broken links) within our web site. 

**Advertisers**

We use an outside advertising company, Burst! Media, to display ads on our site. Some of these ads may contain cookies and may collect certain kinds of personal information. Burst! Media have published their own [privacy statement](http://www.burstmedia.com/privacy.htm) describing how they use the information about visitors which they receive through their advertising service. The Rec.Travel Library has no access to the information that Burst! obtains through its banners. 

**Personal Information**

The Rec.Travel Library does not request any personal information from you nor collect any personal information about you. If you choose to contact us by email, we keep a copy of your email message only while corresponding with you about that particular issue. We do not keep any kind of mass-mailing list, and addresses or other information are not released to any outside organizations or individuals. 

**External Links**

This site contains many links to other sites. We have no knowledge of or responsibility for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

**Contacting us**

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please contact Brian Lucas at: [lucas@travel-library.com](mailto:lucas@travel-library.com)
