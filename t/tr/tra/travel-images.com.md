# Privacy Policy - Travel-Images.com - stock phototgraphy

Travel-Images.com has created this privacy policy within our firm commitment to privacy and data protection. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for the pages under the main domain http://www.travel-images.com.Â  We reserve the right to change this policy at any time without previous notice. Any new version of this policy will be made available on this site.

**Information Automatically Logged**   
We do not log IP addresses or other demographic data on our visitors.

**Cookies**   
Our Site does not use cookies. See more under 'Advertisers'.

**Forms**   
Some of our site's forms require users to send us contact information (like their name or email) for orders or information requests. This information is not used for any other purpose. Contact information from the forms is not shared with other companies. This information is only used on an aggregate basis.

**External Links**   
This site contains links to other sites. Travel-Images.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

**Public Forums**   
This site makes message boards and guest books available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

**Security**   
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.

**Advertisers**   
We use outside ad companies to display ads at Travel-Images.com. These ads may contain cookies and/or web beacons to collect data in the ad serving process. We do not have access to this information.

**Judicial orders**   
Travel-Images.com only recognizes jurisdiction to Portuguese and European Union courts. We reserve the right to disclose information under a direct court order.
