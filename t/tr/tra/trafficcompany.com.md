# TrafficCompany.com

> **Use of Personal Information**  
>  This site gathers minimal information about you and your site. The purpose of this information is used solely for contacting you. We don't give away, sell, or distribute this information to any third parties who many want to collect this information from us. We also do no use your Email address to spam, all your information is safe with us. Our contests collect information required for you to partake in the contests, we do not ask for any information we don't need. 
> 
> **Use of Your Email Address**  
>  Your Email address will never be used for spam, or distributed to third parties, we use it only to contact you about anything updated with the site, if you so request. We would like to let you know your Email address is safe with us. 
> 
> **Use of Cookies**  
>  Our site do not use cookies for any reason.
> 
> **Questions?**  
>  If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, you're encouraged to contact us at [webmaster@trafficcompany.com](mailto:webmaster@trafficcompany.com).
