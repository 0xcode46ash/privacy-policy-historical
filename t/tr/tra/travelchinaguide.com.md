# Travel China Guide: Privacy

  
**TravelChinaGuide Respects Your Privacy** TravelChinaGuide is committed to protecting your privacy and pledges to earn your trust. We will only use your information for purposes of enhancing your travel.

All personal information you give us is kept confidential. We will not sell or disclose any information that identifies you to a third party without your consent.

**Your Personal Information** TravelChinaGuide does not collect any personal information about you except when you specifically provide such information. For example, as you use our site and provide us additional information, such as travel reviews, travelogues, volunteer suggestion, we will collect the information like your email or address (if provided). Because we want to take precautions to ensure the integrity of your personal information, all of your data is stored in our secure databases. Access to that information is strictly controlled and limited to certain individuals within TravelChinaGuide. Please remember that any information you share in the public areas on TravelChinaGuide, such as Travel Gossip (A column where you can share your travel experience with others) becomes public. You should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. **Your Questions and Comments** TravelChinaGuide is committed to protecting your privacy. We encourage you to send us comments or questions regarding this policy.
