# Privacy Policy | Tricycle Magazine

Tricycle is very concerned about your privacy and the security of personal information that may be submitted by you to our website. Any information you give us will be held with the utmost care and security and will not be used in ways you have not consented to. Tricycle does not collect any personal information except when it is specifically and knowingly provided.

Tricycle does not sell, rent, share or release any personal identifying information.

Items entered as comments on Tricycle articles, blogs or in the forums are the property of Tricycle.
