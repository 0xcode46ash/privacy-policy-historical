# Privacy Policy | Triumph Motorcycles

Triumph respects your right to privacy.

The Triumph website database is used by Triumph (and third parties acting on its behalf) for client administration and marketing related purposes.

Any information provided by you to Triumph Motorcycles through this website will be used for the purpose of providing you with the services you have requested from Triumph Motorcycles, to update and enhance Triumph Motorcycles’ records and to advise you of other Triumph products, services and events which may be of interest to you.

We will not pass your information to any other parties unless this is made clear to you at the time you supplied it.

All employees and contractors who have access to your information or are associated with the handling of that data are legally obliged to respect your confidentiality.

If you do not wish us to use your details in this way, or wish to change or amend your details or contact us for any reason, please contact Triumph Motorcycles at the address below:

Triumph Motorcycles (America), Ltd.   
385 Walt Sanders Memorial Drive   
Suite 100   
Newnan, Georgia 30265

If you would like to see the information we hold about you or would like to be removed from any of our mailing lists, please write to our marketing department at the above address.

In accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998 Triumph Motorcycles Ltd is registered as a ‘Data Controller’ on the public Register of Data Controllers as maintained by the Information Commissioner.
