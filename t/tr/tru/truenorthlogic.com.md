**Please read the following to learn more about our Privacy Policy.**

Therefore, please be aware that we are not responsible for, and do not control, the privacy practice or content of such linked sites, including their use of any information that is collected when visitors to our site click through links to those sites or link to our site through those other links. To the extent to which you disclose your personal information on other websites, even those to which you have accessed from this site, different rules may apply to their use and disclosure of personal information.

Names and brands mentioned on the Truenorthlogic website may be claimed as the property of their respective owners.

Truenorthlogic may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by placing a prominent notice on our site.
