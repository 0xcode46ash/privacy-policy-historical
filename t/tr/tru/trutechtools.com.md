# Privacy Notice : TruTech Tools, Tools for "Tru" Technicians

We realize that making purchases at Turtechtools.com, or any other website, requires trust on your part. We value your trust, and pledge to you, our customer, that we will work to protect the security and privacy of any personal information you provide to us, and that your personal information will only be used as set forth in this Privacy Policy.

 **The Information You Provide**  
The security of your personal information is very important to us. We collect personal information that you choose to voluntarily provide. This information includes your email address when you request information or make a purchase. We may also ask for personal information when you contact a Customer Service Representative. This allows us to protect your confidentiality by verifying your identity.

When you make a purchase using a credit card, we do not collect your credit card information. All credit card information is managed by Pay Pal. We only collect information that allows us to ship your order. This includes the name, address and phone number of the intended recipient(s).

From time to time, True Tech Tools may conduct surveys and contests via our website, email or other media. If you choose to participate, we may request your name, email address, mailing address and other personal information.

 **How We Protect the Security of Your Personal Information**  
The security of your personal information is important to us. Every order you place at Trutechtools.com via PayPal and is protected with 128-bit encrypted Secured Socket Layer (SSL) technology. This ensures that your personal information is kept private, confidential and secure. Because PayPal manages the financial transaction, your financial information is never shared, so Trutechtools.com never sees your credit card or bank account numbers. Plus, Paypal’s experienced fraud prevention team helps fight fraud before it happens.

 **Cookies**  
Trutechtools.com does not use permanent cookies. However, online shopping does require per-session cookies. As soon as you close your browser, these temporary cookies are deleted. You can browse Turtechtools.com with cookies disabled, but you will be unable to purchase items.

Tru Tech Tools not allow third party cookies. If you come to Trutechtools.com through one of our affiliate websites, they may place a permanent cookie on your computer. This cookie is only used to track click-through rates from our affiliates.

 **Additional Computer Information**  
We collect certain technical information from your computer each time you request a page during, a visit to Trutechtools.com. This information may include your Internet Protocol (IP) address, operation system, browser type and the address of a referring website. We do not sell or rent this information, the information we collect is only used to enhance our customers' shopping experience, improve our internal searches and to develop aggregate information about our customer base and usage patterns.

 **How We Use Your Personal Information**  
Information we collect on Trutechtools.com may be used to enhance your shopping experience in the following ways: 

  *  Confirming your orders 
  *  Delivering the merchandise that you purchase online 
  *  Responding to your customer-service inquires or requests 
  *  Communicating special offers and featured items to you (if you choose to receive such notices) 
  * Finding and stocking the products you want 
  * Keeping you up to date with news letters
  * Customizing your shopping experience 



Tru Tech Tools may also use the information collected to notify visitors of changes to the site and for any other legitimate and lawful business purpose. Tru tech Tools may choose to share information with third parties, such as suppliers, financial institutions, and third party service providers, and with any successor to Tru tech Tools interest. Tru Tech Tools may disclose information about any site visitor when required by law.

Aside from the purposes described above, we will never share your personal information with any third parties unless we have your express permission or under special circumstances, such as when required by law. We may share aggregated demographic and statistical information with our partners. This is not linked to any personally identifiable information.
