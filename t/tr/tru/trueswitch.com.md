# Service Description

Esaya makes it a priority to protect and secure its users’ information.  Esaya will NOT:

  * Permanently store any personally identifiable information about its users.  Personally identifiable information may include name, title, address, etc.
  * Sell, rent or loan personally identifiable data to any third parties or organizations.
  * Disclose personally identifiable data to third parties except to the extent necessary to provide you with a requested service, or under circumstances required by law.



No Esaya employee can view or retrieve your passwords or other personal information.

Esaya will disclose your personal information when we believe in good faith that any applicable law, regulation, or legal process requires it, or where we believe disclosure is necessary to protect or enforce our legal rights or the legal rights of another user.

Esaya may share aggregate or summary information regarding its customers’ usage patterns with partners and other third parties. Esaya will archive information for the appropriate time period specified by current US Privacy laws and regulations.

If you register for TrueSwitch.com's services, you expressly consent to the transfer of your personal information across data networks worldwide for the processing of data for the express purposes of rendering the associated services to the TrueSwitch.com user.
