# Turbo Squid -- Help

Privacy Policy  
---  
  
Privacy for our users is a high priority at Turbo Squid, Inc. Thus, we do not share any personal information obtained online without permission from the submitting user, and we never will.   
  
All information gathered by Turbo Squid, Inc. is used solely for the purpose of enhancing the functionality and level of service we are able to provide our users.   
  
We store only partial credit card information in our database to ensure security and all transactions use SSL security for encryption. Any saved cards utilize your computer to complete the information.
