# Privacy Policy - Turquoise Network

Turquoise Network respects your privacy and understands the importance of protecting your personal information.  We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party.  All personal and payment information provided will be used strictly for processing your order(s).Please note that we may contact you with questions or follow-up information about orders you have placed.  Also, you may receive periodic mailings from us with information about new products, promotions, surveys, and so forth--of which you may unsubscribe from at any time.
