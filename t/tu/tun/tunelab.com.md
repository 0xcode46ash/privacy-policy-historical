# tunelab™ | Privacy Policy

TuneLab Music has developed this privacy statement to illustrate our desire to protect you and display our commitment to privacy. It is important that you read the following privacy policy. Agreement with the policies set forth in this document is a condition of use for this site. be sure to understand the information that we gather and for what purposes before entering the site.

**Personal Information**  
We will only collect personal information from you, such as your full name, street address, telephone number, and e-mail address, that you voluntarily submit to us upon joining TuneLab Music or anytime there after. This information will be used to for contests, and other promotional activities. Your personal information will only be shared to comply with any valid legal process such as search warrant, subpoena, statute, or court order. Please note that if you give out personal information online, through discussion threads, that information can be collected and used by third parties. We cannot guarantee the security of any personal information posted in forums or chats. You disclose such information at your own risk.

**Children under the age of 13**  
TuneLab Music does not accept contest entries or memberships from children under the age of 13 in compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act(COPPA). COPPA prevents this web site from collecting and/or distributing information from children without the consent of their parents. In order to comply with these standards, we will suspend all registrations and entries by children under the age of 13 until reliable forms of parental consent can be implemented. For more information on COPPA please visit <http://www.ftc.gov/privacy>.

**Cookies and IP addresses**  
TuneLab Music accesses cookies and logs IP information when you visit TuneLab Music.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).

**Message Boards / Comments**  
Upon registration to our site/message board, you will be subject to interaction with other members of the TuneLab Music community. Users are solely responsible for anything contained in their messages and their message board sessions. We do not verify, endorse, or otherwise vouch for any content in these sessions. Users may be held legally liable for the content of their posts if they are in violation of any law. Users are advised that the messages contained on our site are by no means ‘private’ and may be disclosed to legal authorities under certain circumstances. We reserve the sole and explicit right to release current, or past member information in the event that the information is subpoenaed or when it is deemed necessary and/or appropriate. Repeat offenders of these guidelines may be suspended or have access denied to our site.

**Messaging System**  
TuneLab Music has a “private messaging” system that allows members to directly communicate with other members without being visible to the public. Use of this system CAN and WILL be monitored to ensure it is not being utilized for illegal activities of any sort, to include file-sharing. Misuse of the messaging system for any illegal means will result in instant account termination without warning. Users may be held legally liable for the content of their messages if they are in violation of any law. Users are advised that the messages contained on our site are by no means ‘private’ and may be disclosed to legal authorities under certain circumstances. We reserve the sole and explicit right to release current, or past member information in the event that the information is subpoenaed or when it is deemed necessary and/or appropriate.

**Security Policy**  
Although we take your security very serious, “perfect” security does not exist on the internet and our efforts in this regard must necessarily be limited to what is practical in light of our equipment and technology.

**Changes to this Policy**  
We reserve the right to amend this policy at any time without notice. We encourage and recommend that you periodically review this statement to be informed of how we are using and protecting your information. Please direct all comments and questions to [privacy@tunelab.com](mailto:privacy@tunelab.com).
