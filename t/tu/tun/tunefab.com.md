# Privacy Policy | TuneFab

This Privacy Policy covers the collection, use, control and security of customer's personal information that may be collected by TuneFab. When you interact with TuneFab, such as visiting our website, purchasing TuneFab products or contacting us, we will collect your information. So, please take a moment to read the following to know what information is gathered, how the information is collected, what the purposes are for we use the information and how we safeguard your personal information. Your privacy is a priority at TuneFab, and we go to great lengths to protect it.

Use of your Personal Information

To solve your tech questions or recover your License Code;

To deliver services such as newsletters or products that you request or purchase;

To alert you to product upgrades, special offers, updated information and other new services from TuneFab, if you so request;

To analyze and improve our software program.

Control of your Personal Information

Once we collected your personal information, TuneFab will not share this information with any others without your permission, except for the limited exceptions already listed. They will only be used for the purposes stated above.

Security of your Personal Information

We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. These include internal reviews of our data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures, as well as physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access to systems where we store personal data.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Occasionally, we may change this privacy policy for some purpose. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent, and we expect most such changes will be minor. Regardless, we will post any Privacy Policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice like email notification. But we still encourage you to periodically review the TuneFab Privacy Policy for the latest information on our privacy practices.

Contact Information

Please send any questions or comments on our privacy practices to [support@tunefab.com](mailto:support@tunefab.com).
