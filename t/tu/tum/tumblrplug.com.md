Welcome to Tumblrplug.com, Privacy Policy.

At Tumblrplug.com, The data collected is only temporary and not stored long term. We do not submit,give,or sell any data collected to a third party. We do not collect any personal information such as personal names,email addresses or any information that is personally identifiable.

Tumblrplug.com does use temporary session cookies. We do also display third party ads (Google Adsense). Google does use cookies to serve ads on Tumblrplug.com. To find out more about google ads visit http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html
