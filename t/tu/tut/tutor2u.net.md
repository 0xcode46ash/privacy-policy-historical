# Tutor2u - Terms and Conditions

The following provides detail about our Privacy Policy. tutor2u treats your privacy seriously. In general, we will only use the personal information that we collect from you to identify personal preferences and match your needs with relevant tutor2u products, keep you informed about website developments and process any orders that you may make either through our website or by sending orders through the post.. 

Your use of this web site signifies your consent to us collecting and using personal information about you as specified below in accordance with this Policy. Should we choose to change our Policy for any reason, the changes will be posted here, so that you are always kept informed of how we collect and use your personal information, and when we may disclose it.

You may provide personal information when communicating with us. if we provide opportunities to purchase goods or services from Tutor2u, you may order a product or a service and give your name, e-mail address, delivery address, credit or debit card number and expiry date so that the order can be processed and your products (or services where appropriate) delivered to you. Sometimes you may be asked for your telephone number. If you enter a competition or promotion we will ask for your name, address and e-mail address. 

We will collect information about your educational status when you respond to specific requests (e.g. user surveys) or when you complete feedback or enquiry forms where such information is needed by us to tailor our response to you.

It may be that some of the personal information you give us is sensitive personal data within the meaning of the Data Protection Act 1998. Any such information ("sensitive information") will only be disclosed with your express consent. 

We may use information collected about you to personalise your visits to our web site and recommend goods or services to you. We also use the information to help us develop the design and layout of our web site to ensure that our site is as useful and enjoyable as possible. 

We may use information collected about you to let you know about functionality changes to our web site or changes to our terms and conditions of use. 

We may provide aggregate statistics about our sales, customers, traffic patterns and other site information to third parties, but these statistics will not include any information that could personally identify you. 

tutor2u reserves the right to access and disclose individually identifiable information to comply with applicable laws and lawful government requests, to operate its systems properly or to protect itself or its users. 

When you order products from our online store, tutor2u will offer the use of a secure server. The secure server software encrypts the information that you input before it is transmitted to netbanx - our credit card payment bureau. In addition, we have strict security procedures covering the storage and disclosure of your information in order to prevent unauthorised access and to comply with the Data Protection Act 1998. This means that sometimes we may ask you for proof of identity or a password before disclosing any personal information to you. 

A cookie is a piece of information that is stored on your computer's hard drive by your web browser. On revisiting this site our computer server will recognise the cookie, giving us information about your last visit. Most browsers accept cookies automatically, but usually you can alter the settings of your browser to prevent automatic acceptance. If you choose not to receive cookies, you may still use most of the features of our web site
