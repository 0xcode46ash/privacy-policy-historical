# Twitterlive | Privacy

## Privacy Policy

Disclaimer:

This web site is not affiliated with [twitter](http://www.twitter.com/) in any way. We just make use of their API.

How we collect and use information:

We respect our site visitor's right to personal privacy. We want you to feel secure when visiting our site and are committed to maintaining your privacy when doing so. To that end, we collect and use information through our website only as disclosed in this privacy policy. We collect information that you voluntarily submit to us in order to access our Twitterlive.net service (the "Site") such as your email and password.

Disclosure:

We will never sell or share your email address or personal information, and we do not send unsolicited emails of any kind. We may collect indirect information such as IP address for demographic purposes, type of internet browser in order to provide you with a better browsing experiense, and date and time of the request for statistical analysis.

Children's Privacy:

In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, We do not knowingly collect or save any information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that a member is under age of thirteen (13) we will delete the member's data from our database and files.

Cookies:

The Twitterlive.net Web site use "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time, tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. These cookies do not track user usage for marketing purposes.  
You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. However, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Web sites you visit.

Links to other Sites:

Twitterlive.net contains links to other web sites. If you follow any of the links you find on Twitterlive.net, you have to abide by their terms of use and are subject to their privacy policies.

Aditional Information:

If we make any changes in our Privacy Policy, we will post an updated Privacy Policy on our Site, which is effective immediately. The new policy will apply to all users of the Site and will replace any prior policies that are inconsistent with it. If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20121015185529id_/http%3A//www.twitterlive.net/Help/Contact.aspx).
