# Privacy Policy - tweetangels.com

TweetAngels.com Statement of Privacy

TweetAngels.com is committed to protecting your privacy. This Statement of Privacy applies to TweetAngels.com Web site and governs data collection and usage at all TweetAngels.com sites and services; it does not apply to other online or offline sites, products or services. Please read the TweetAngels.com Statement of Privacy. TweetAngels.com is a general audience web site, intended for users of all ages. Personal information of all users is collected, used and disclosed as described in this Statement of Privacy.

Collection of your Personal Information

As an E-commerce Web site, TweetAngels.com collects personal information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. Information collected from TweetAngels.com is used solely for the purpose of billing and shipping your online purchases. TweetAngels.com does not collect any information about your computer hardware and software. We DO NOT SELL OR TRANSFER YOUR INFORMATION AT ALL. Your Name, Address, Email, Phone Number & all information collected is fully confidential.

Use of your Personal Information

TweetAngels.com and its operational service partners collect and use your personal information to process product orders and deliver the products requested. It is possible that TweetAngels.com may also use personal information to inform you of other products or services available from Tweetangels via Email only.

TweetAngels.com DOES NOT SELL, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. YOUR INFORMATION IS 100% CONFIDENTIAL & SAFE.

TweetAngels.com may access and/or disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on TweetAngels.com or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of TweetAngels.com, including its Web site; or (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of TweetAngels.com or the public.

Personal information collected on this site may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country in which TweetAngels.com or its affiliates, subsidiaries or agents maintain facilities, and by using this site, you consent to any such transfer of information outside of your country. B TweetAngels.com abides by the safe harbor framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of data from the European Union.

Control your Personal Information

TweetAngels.com offers its customers choices for the collection, use and sharing of personal information. You may go notify us of your preferences by emailing support@tweetangels.com. Please be sure to provide complete account information so we can identify you in our records.

You may also stop the delivery of future promotional e-mail from Tweetangels. by responding directly to any email you receive with a request to remove you from the mailing list

Security of your Personal Information

TweetAngels.com is committed to protecting the security of your personal information. We use a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. For example, we store the personal information you provide on computer systems with limited access that are located in controlled facilities. When we transmit highly confidential information (such as a credit card numbers) over the Internet, we protect it through 128 bit encryption through Paypal.

Changes to this Statement

TweetAngels.com will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. If there are material changes to this Statement or in how TweetAngels.com will use your personal information, TweetAngels.com will prominently post such changes prior to implementing the change. TweetAngels.com encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how TweetAngels.com is protecting your information.
