# Legal Information

**_Privacy Policy_**

We greatly value your privacy and I can guarantee you that when you are on TweakXP.com none of your private information will ever be asked of you.  Nor secretly collected.  The only information that we collect is statistical information that is collected by our web server statistic program.  This allows us to find out anonymous information such as what country most of our users are coming from, what version of browser you are using, how you found our website, etc.  We never share any of this information with third parties and have no intension of doing so in the future.
