P R I V A C Y   P O L I C Y

**Information Gathered**  
I at www.tamilmovies.net greatly value your privacy while you surf at omy site. So we do not intentionally collect any personal information from you like name, address, e-mail address etc from my site.

Nonetheless for a limited number of days we do store on our server certain voluntarily provided information like sender/receiver’s name, E-mail etc related to our CardCITY free greeting card service. Such a temporary storage (your information will automatically delete within 60 days) of such information is necessary because it is integral part of that service. When you send one of CardCITY greeting cards, the information you provide to CardCITY is used to customize your greeting with the recipient's name and with your message, and to deliver an email notification to the recipient. We treat our cards as private correspondence between the sender and the recipient and will not disclose the contents of a card, other than to the intended recipient, unless we are required to do so by law.

To monitor the activities on www.tamilmovies.net our ISP or a third party counting service that we subscribe to may gather certain non-personal information (Server Variables) related to www.tamilmovies.net visitors like their I.P address, browser type, HTTP referrers, Demographic information etc. Nevertheless, in respect to www.tamilmovies.net visitors, no personal information such as personal names, email addresses etc. are being tracked by them.  


**Cookies**  
As I intend to offer you a worry free surfing experience I also do not use any cookies in www.tamilmovies.net site that are set directly from our servers.

**Chat Rooms and Forums**

If you disclose your e-mail address in a chat room or forum, you may receive unsolicited e-mails or "spam." I cannot safeguard the privacy of any such information you choose to disclose online in this manner and you must do so carefully and at your own risk
