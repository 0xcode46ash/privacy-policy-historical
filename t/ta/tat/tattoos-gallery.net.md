Tattoos Gallery is very concerned of its visitors privacy. Therefore, we have created a short and strict privacy policy statements, with no fluff.

Tattoos Gallery servers automatically track visitors' IP addresses, visited pages and referrer pages. We also record information offered to us by our users voluntarily.

This information is used to improve our services and overall user experience.

We will never share or sell your personal information without your consent, unless required by law.
