# Texila American University Privacy Policy | TAU Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and the privacy of our students, graduates, faculty, and staff. 

We are strongly committed to protecting your privacy. We value the trust that you place in us by providing to us your personal data and we are committed to taking all reasonable precautions to protect your personal data from misuse and keeping it secure by complying with all applicable data protection laws and regulations in Hong Kong, as amended from time to time. 

####  Your consent

By using our services and website, you are consenting to our collecting, using and disclosing your personal data in accordance with this Privacy Policy. 

We will only collect, use or disclose your personal data where it is fair and lawful to do so. In most cases, we will ask for your consent explicitly. 

####  Collection of your Personal Data 

Texila American University (tauedu.org) collects the information you knowingly and voluntarily provide (using your mouse and keyboard) when you use one of our Sites. 

The information we collect depends on how you use our Sites, but, in many cases, it will identify you personally. For example, if you submit an application, we collect the information you provide as part of the online application process. 

Likewise, we collect the information you provide when you request information, create an account, or contact us with questions or comments. 

If you post photographs or comments on one of our Sites —for example, to your public profile or on one of our blogs— we collect that information also. All postings must comply with the requirements for User Generated Content as set forth in our Terms of Use 

#### Purpose of collection

We use the personal data and contact information collected from you for the following purposes   


  * To follow up with you after you have expressed an interest in our programs. 
  * To improve our programs and services, 
  * handling and responding to your inquiries, suggestions or complaints; 
  * To better understand the needs and interests of learners, alumni, and the other constituencies we serve. 
  * Any other purposes directly related to the above. 



#### Use of Data

We intend to use your personal data for marketing activities from time to time, including   


  * marketing our products and services, activities, contests, promotional offers (benefits and privileges) 
  * special events and any other information which we think may be of relevance, and special events of our business partners or third party service providers; 
  * Conducting researches and evaluation.



Such Marketing Activities may be provided to you in various methods in accordance with the personal data you provide to us, including advertisement, postal mail, telephone call, email, text or messages on your wireless or mobile device.

#### Transfer of Personal Data

We may share Personal Information collected through our Sites within our companies and third party service providers as necessary on a need-to-know basis to fulfill any of the Purposes. These third parties are required by contract to use the Personal Information we share with them only to perform services on our behalf and to treat your Personal Information as strictly confidential.

#### Cookies

We use cookies to automatically collect information about you when you visit our website (such as the type of the internet browser and operating system you are using and the domain name of your internet service provider). Cookies are small pieces of information that your web browser stores on your computer when you visit a website. Our cookies identify your computer to us and allow us to personalize your website experience and recognize you when you return. Information collected from these cookies may be used for the Purposes to serve you better.

Most browsers automatically accept cookies. You may choose to disable cookies on your computer by changing your browser’s settings. If you disable cookies, you may not be able to use all features and services offered on our website.

#### Security

We take measures to prevent leakage of personal information in our website. Since, we cannot guarantee against any loss, misuse and unauthorized disclosure. Our servers and databases are protected by firewalls. We use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software to encrypt data as it travels between your computer and our servers. 

PayDollar is a leading, secure and reliable international payment service provider to banks and online merchants since 2000. Customers' payment details are securely transmitted to the acquiring bank, card and payment companies for real-time transaction authorisation using 256-bit Extended Validation Certificates (EV) SSL transaction encryption. PayDollar supports CVV/CVC check as well as 3-D secure authentication of Visa and MasterCard namely: Verified By VISA, MasterCard SecureCode for added security protection for both customers and merchant.

#### Third party Links

This privacy policy only applies to Texila American University websites, but not to other website that link with us. When you click on links and/or ad banners that take you to either third parties’ websites or websites of companies associated with us, you will be subject to the privacy policies of those parties. Whilst we support the protections of privacy on the internet, we do not accept responsibility for any actions taken by third parties outside our web domain.
