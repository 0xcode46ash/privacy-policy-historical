# Tactics Arena Online - Privacy Policy

| 

**PRIVACY POLICY**   


In order to join the Tactics Arena Online game server, users are required to provide a unique username & password. To participate in the forums, a unique username, password and e-mail address are required as well as the agreement to conform to the rules of the forums. Forum members must be over the age of 13 or else they will need parental consent to register. Neither the Administrators nor the Moderators participating in the forums and game server are responsible for the privacy practices of any user. 

Remember that all information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to share any of your personal information. Any user who finds material posted by another user objectionable is encouraged to contact us via email.   
We are authorized by you to remove or modify any data submitted by you to the game server & forums for any reason we feel constitutes a violation of our policies, whether stated, implied or not. This site may contain links to other web sites and files. We have no control over the content and can not ensure it will not be offensive or objectionable. We will, however, remove links to material that we feel is inappropriate as we become aware of them. 

Cookies must be turned on in your browser to participate as a user in the game server & forums. Cookies are used here to hold your username and password and viewing options, allowing you to login. You, the user, are 13 years of age or over.   
You agree that you will not post any copyrighted material that is not owned by yourself or the owners of these forums. In your use of these forums, you agree that you will not post any information which is vulgar, harassing, hateful, threatening, invading of others privacy, sexually oriented, or violates any laws. 

**Information Collection and Use** Flash Player Studios is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. Flash Player Studios collects information from our users at several different points on our web site. 

**Basic Membership** In order to play Tactics Arena Online, a user must first create a new account. During registration of an account, a user is required to create a unique username and password. Since new accounts are free, it is the user's responsibility to remember their username and password. 

**Gold Account Membership** A $5 USD charge every month is required to maintain a Gold Account Membership. All monetary transfers are to be completed using the PAYPAL merchant account system. For more information about this please visit www.paypal.com, there are much more advantages with having a Gold Membership, for more details refer to <http://www.tacticsarena.com/info/goldaccounts.html>

**Spam?** You think you hate spam, well we hate it just as much if not more. We do not sell out email addresses to anyone. Not our advertisers, marketing companies, people with fetishes for email addresses. Not anyone. We will not give out your email address to anyone. Period. 

**Choice/Opt-out** Users who no longer wish to receive our newsletter or promotional materials from Flash Player Studios may opt-out of receiving these communications by changing your own personal settings, located in your Personal Profile Section.
