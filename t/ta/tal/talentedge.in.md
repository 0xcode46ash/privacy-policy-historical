# Privacy Policy | Talentedge

At Arrina Education Services (TALENTEDGE), we are committed to protect the privacy of all those who visit our website. For us, your visit is the beginning of our relationship. It is our keenest intent to ensure that you feel confident that the information we collect, and that you choose to share, will be treated with care. We want our customers and potential customers to feel comfortable when visiting out website and when contacting us through Internet based media.

 **PRIVACY POLICY**  
Normal Web Site Usage During normal web site usage we do not collect or store personally identifiable information like name, mailing address, email address, phone number. The information we may collect and store is the name of your Internet service provider, the web site that referred you to us, the pages you request and the date and time of those requests. This information may be solely to generate statistics and measure site activity to improve the usefulness of customer visits and the service experience as a whole. This information may also be shared with third parties in order to provide these services or to analyze, store, or aggregate the information so collected.

 **Collection of personally identifiable information**  
There may be instances when using our website you may need to provide personally identifiable information in order to uneable you to receive details on a specific training program, franchise model or similar such instances. Any information you provide may be used by Arrina to improve the services, we provide. This information, like name, address, country , city, course enquired, email address, contact number and possibly additional information (comments) are collected and stored in a manner appropriate to the nature of the data required by Arrina. However, we will respect your request if you tell us that some information so collected should not be used as a basis for further contact.

 **Electronic Mail sent to us**  
Internet users may decide to send us personally identifying information, like message containing information about a training program attended and/or service received from us. We will use this information for the purpose identified in the email and to enhance our understanding of you, in order to improve our service to you. Privacy Statement Revisions  
This Privacy Policy can be changed at the sole discreation of Arrina from time to time. The revised privacy statement will only apply to data collected subsequent to the effective date.
