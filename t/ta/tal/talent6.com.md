# Talent6 Community

Talent6 understands the importance of privacy for our members. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at [privacy@talent6.com](mailto:privacy@talent6.com).

In order to register as a member of Talent6, you have provided certain personal information including your name, e-mail address, username and password, contact address, phone number, and credit card information. Talent6 retains and uses this information for the following reasons: 

1\. To inform you about casting calls and provide other content we feel may be of interest to you.  
2\. To collect membership fees.  
3\. To maintain the security of our website.  
4\. To adjust and improve our marketing techniques and customized content, products and services.  
5\. To comply with applicable law in response to a subpoena, court order, or other legal process whether or not a response is required under applicable law; or it is necessary to share the information in order to enforce Talent6ts terms of use or investigate or prevent illegal activity or possible harm to the physical safety of any person.  
6\. Unless you choose to decline this aspect of our services, your information may be shared with third parties - including our marketing partners. Talent6 may use the information that you provide to work with third parties to bring you retail opportunities by e-mail, direct mail and telemarketing. Talent6 may sell or rent user, member, or former member information, including name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, payment information, as well as aggregate statistical information, to a third party or marketing partner. If you prefer that Talent6 not reveal any of your information to a third party, please follow this link to set your privacy information preferences.   
[Set your privacy preferences](http://www.talent6.com/non_disclosure.php)
