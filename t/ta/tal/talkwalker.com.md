# Privacy Policy - Talkwalker

1\. INTRODUCTION

The terms and conditions of this privacy policy (hereinafter the “Privacy Policy”) apply to all Services that Licensee may subscribe to under Talkwalker’s Terms of Service (available at: [www.talkwalker.com/en/terms-of-service/](http://www.talkwalker.com/en/terms-of-service-us-canada/)).

In this Privacy Policy, all capitalized terms shall have the same meaning as set forth in the Terms of Service, unless otherwise stated.

For the purpose of providing the Services and Trial Services, Trendiction collects personal data within the following conditions.

Licensee expressly agrees that it has read this Privacy Policy carefully, that it has understood its meaning and is thus perfectly informed of and expressly accepts its content.

2\. DATA COLLECTED

When Licensee creates an Account in order to use the Talkwalker Free Search Service and/or Trial Services, Trendiction asks Licensee to provide contact information such as name, first name, telephone number, e-mail address, company name of Licensee, etc.

When Licensee acquires Services, additional information will be requested from Licensee by Trendiction such as billing information and address.

In this Privacy Policy, the personal data as mentioned above are referred to as “Licensee’s Personal Data” or “Its Personal Data”.

Trendiction may also collect personal data of users surfing on Trendiction’s websites such as these users’ IP addresses.

3\. USE OF DATA

3.1  Purpose

Trendiction uses the personal data collected for the following purposes:

\- Identification and authentication of Licensee when Licensee accesses its Account;

\- Providing the Services to Licensee;

\- Billing purposes;

\- Improving the quality of the Services provided;

\- Market research purposes;

\- Marketing and advertising purposes in the conditions set out in Article 5 below.

Third-Party Platforms may collect personal data concerning Licensee if Licensee connects to such Platforms via the Services. Trendiction is not responsible for the manner in which these Platforms handle any personal data that may be disclosed to them. Licensee is invited to refer to the appropriate Third-Party Platform’s privacy provisions in order to verify how its personal data is used by such Platforms.

Trendiction’s Services provide URL links towards third-party websites. Trendiction is not responsible for the manner in which these third-party websites handle any personal data that may be disclosed to them. Licensee is invited to refer to the appropriate third-party website’s privacy policy in order to verify how its personal data is used by such third-party website.

3.2  Transfers of data

Licensee’s Personal Data will not be shared with third parties, except as provided below.

Trendiction may resort to subcontractors that are located outside the territory of Luxembourg for Service optimization purposes.

In this context, Trendiction may resort to:

\- Subcontractors located in the United States solely for the purposes of providing Trendiction with client relationship management services and marketing and  emailing services;

\- Subcontractors located in the European Union for the purpose of providing Trendiction with payment services.

4\. COOKIES

Cookies are small amounts of data sent on Licensee’s and any Trendiction websites user browser and stored on their hard drive that may not allow identification of the party receiving the cookies, unless such party has already provided Trendiction with personal information allowing such identification, e.g., by creating an Account.

Trendiction uses cookies in order to provide Licensee with a tailored experience while using the Services.

In this regard, Trendiction uses:

\- Session cookies that enable Trendiction to process and memorize Licensee’s transactions and requests during a given session on its Account. These cookies are necessary in order to use the Services;

\- Persistent cookies that enable Trendiction to remember Licensee’s information in order to provide easier and more convenient access to the Services, tailored information, web content and promotional messages to Licensee, and that are used for market research purposes.

Trendiction warrants that any information collected via these cookies will not be shared by Trendiction with any third party.

Licensee or any user of Trendiction’s websites may set their browser parameters in order to select the types of cookies they wish to receive or not receive from Trendiction.

5\. MARKETING PREFERENCES

Trendiction may use Licensee’s Personal Data to contact Licensee to discuss the Services it has subscribed to or to send Licensee information concerning Trendiction and/or the Services and/or promotional activities via newsletters, messages, calls and letters to the e-mail address, telephone number or postal address provided by Licensee.

By accepting this Privacy Policy, Licensee thus expressly accepts that Trendiction uses the e-mail address, telephone number or postal address provided by Licensee in order to contact Licensee for the purposes above.

Except as mentioned in this Privacy Policy, Trendiction does not use Licensee’s Personal Data for any other kind of marketing or promotional purposes.

Licensee’s may opt out of being contacted by Trendiction at any time by using the opt-out link provided in any newsletter sent to Licensee or by contacting Trendiction in this regard at the following e-mail address: contact[at]talkwalker.com

6\. RIGHTS OF LICENSEE

Licensee can update or correct any of Its Personal Data at any time by contacting Trendiction at the following e-mail address: contact@talkwalker.com.

Licensee is entitled to request Trendiction to provide Licensee, free of charge, with a list of all of Licensee’s Personal Data stored by Trendiction by sending an e-mail in this regard to the following e-mail address: contact@talkwalker.com.

Trendiction undertakes to provide Licensee with such list within a reasonable timeframe.

7\. HOW TO CONTACT US

Licensee can contact Trendiction for any questions concerning this Privacy Policy at contact@talkwalker.comor by postal mail at Trendiction SA, 14, rue Aldringen, L-1118 Luxembourg.

8\. MISCELLANEOUS

This Privacy Policy shall be governed and construed in accordance with the laws of the Grand-Duchy of Luxembourg. The courts of the judicial district of Luxembourg-City have exclusive jurisdiction in relation to any dispute arising out of or in relation with this Agreement.

Trendiction may alter terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy, in whole or in part, by  notifying Licensee of such alterations beforehand via an appropriate method of information .
