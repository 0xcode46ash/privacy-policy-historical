### Data protection and privacy

TalkTalk Members meets the conditions of the Data Protection Act 1998 (DPA). We will not send marketing material unless you specifically opt-in and subscribed receive it. With your consent, if required, we may pass your account information on to our internal support teams so that they can investigate and help with any issues you have brought to our attention. We will never pass your details to companies outside The Carphone Warehouse group.

TalkTalk Members clearly shows which pieces of data are mandatory in order to complete the order and which are optional. We do not collect sensitive information (this is defined in the DPA 1998).

TalkTalk Members states that all information is accurate and up to date. Any inaccuracies are amended as soon as they become apparent. We will only hold personal information for as long as it is needed for the purpose it was collected.

### Unsolicited Email

TalkTalk Members will not send e-mail to customers unless they have first given their consent.

### Changes to Privacy Policy

Please note that this policy will be reviewed, and may change, from time to time. The revised policy will be posted to this page so that you are always aware of the information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we disclose it.
