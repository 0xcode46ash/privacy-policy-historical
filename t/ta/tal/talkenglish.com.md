# Privacy Policy

**Your Personal Information**

We value your privacy and we will do everything possible to keep your information private. We will not share your information to other companies without your permission.

 **Security**

All the information we collect from you during sign up is used only by talkenglish.com. The information is used to serve you better. In order to sign in, we provide verification of each member through the usage of a user ID and password. If you are using a public computer or a kiosk machine, we will automatically sign you out when reaching the maximum time limit without any activity.

 **Cookies**

We use "Cookies" to store information on your computer. "Cookies" are used to customize the site for your country, language and preference. Our "Cookies" do not collect any other information from your computer.

 **Regarding 3rd party Advertisers:**

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site. 
  * Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet. 
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html). 



We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.
