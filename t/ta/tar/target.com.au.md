# Privacy

|   
Target's Privacy Statement  
  
Your privacy is very important to us and Target will always act to protect your information. Target takes this responsibility seriously and is very careful about how and when your personal information is collected, used and shared.

  This statement tells you in general terms what our commitment is, why we need to collect personal information, who we may share that information with (outside of Target) and how you may access personal information that we keep.

Target's Privacy Commitment  
  
   
What personal information do we collect  
  
The personal information we collect is in most cases supplied directly by you when you give us your details, place an order, seek a refund without a receipt, or provide feedback to us.

  The type of service or interaction you wish to receive will determine the specific type of information we need to collect. This may include the following: Name, address, phone and other contact details (fax, email etc), merchandise information (purchases, orders etc), and method of payment.

  Sometimes we collect other information such as where you shop or what your areas of interest are, so that we can improve our services offering to you. Through business partners such as FlyBuys and GE Capital Finance, who administer our store cards, we may also collect transaction information.

Who we share your information with  
  
Target does not intend selling your personal information to other parties. However, we will often need to pass personal details such as your name and address to companies who perform parts of our service delivery. This includes organisations such as Coles Myer, GE Capital, Fly Buys and major delivery companies, external (non-Target) call centres, mail houses and third party fulfilment contractors. In these circumstances, Target will ensure that these third parties undertake to protect your privacy.Access  
  
If you would like to access your personal information, write to our customer service department at  
Replied Paid 11, PO Box 41, North Geelong 3215 or call them toll free on 1800 814 788. We prefer to receive written requests as in many cases this assists us to verify your identity.Direct marketing   
  
If you do not wish to be contacted by Target (about marketing, special offers or other contacts), please call our Customer Service Department on 1800 814 788.Online Advertising  
  
GE Capital Finance Australia and CML use pixels, or transparent GIF files, to help manage online advertising. These GIF files are provided by our advertising management partner, DoubleClick. These pixel or transparent GIF files enable DoubleClick to recognize a unique cookie on your Web browser, which in turn enables us to learn which advertisements bring users to our website. The cookie was placed by us, or by another advertiser who works with DoubleClick. With both cookies and Spotlight technology, the information that we collect and share is anonymous and not personally identifiable. It does not contain your name, address, telephone number, email address or internet service provider details. 

For more information about DoubleClick, including information about how to opt out of these technologies, go to [www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy](http://www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy).
