# Privacy Policy - TargetWoman

TargetWoman, an Online Women Publication dedicated to Women, is deeply committed to protecting individual privacy. We respect the privacy of the individuals who visit our portal. We declare here that we will not sell, distribute, lend or part with the collected personal information - if any. This is in consonance with our avowed policy to protect individual privacy. The following is a brief declaration of the information gathering and dissemination practices that this web site follows. 

**Normal Information:**  
As is normal with all websites, our server collects the usual browser information available when a browser or user agent requests any information from our servers. These are standard in nature and will consist of the following:

  


  * User IP address 
  * Browser type 
  * Date and time 
  * Requested File 
  * Referrer 
  * Browser capability (for some pages) 



This information is not personally identifying or identifiable. It cannot be used to identify a specific individual. This information helps in improving our services in future by providing accurate statistics to advertisers in the form of aggregated data. If there are any exceptions, there will be a clear disclosure regarding the same. This will give you an opportunity to choose not to participate or provide this information. Our directory section contains links to other websites. When you enter any of these websites, it must be noted that their policies may be materially different from our Privacy policy. 

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click [here](http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/faq.aspx). 

**Personal Information** :   
The information we may collect for submitting to our directory - User name,Email and any other relevant information are strictly for weeding out any spam. We do not sell or redistribute this information to anyone. 

**Contact Information** :  
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this site, you should contact the Webmaster through our [contact](http://www.targetwoman.com/contact.html) page. 

The personal information collected from the visitors of our website, including their identity, remains confidential. We respect the law(s) on confidentiality applicable to this website (US) and we will never pass on these data to any third party, unless required by law. Our website does not collect any cookies. 

**Changes to our Privacy Policy** :  
We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time for any reason. Notice of any such changes will be posted as an updated Privacy Policy on this site.
