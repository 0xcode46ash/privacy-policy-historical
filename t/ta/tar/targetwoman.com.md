# Privacy Policy - TargetWoman

TargetWoman, a directory dedicated for Women, is deeply committed to protecting individual privacy. We respect the privacy of the individuals who visit our directory. We declare here that we will not sell, distribute, lend or part with the collected personal information - if any. This is in consonance with our avowed policy to protect individual privacy. The following is a brief declaration of the information gathering and dissemination practices that this web site follows. 

  


**Normal Information:**  
As is normal with all websites, our server collects the usual browser information available when a browser or user agent requests any information from our servers. These are standard in nature and will consist of the following:

* User IP address 
  

* Browser type 
  

* Date and time 
  

* Requested File 
  

* Referrer 
  

* Browser capability (for some pages) 
  


This information is not personally identifying or identifiable. It cannot be used to identify a specific individual. This information helps in improving our services in future by providing accurate statistics to advertisers in the form of aggregated data. If there are any exceptions, there will be a clear disclosure regarding the same. This will give you an opportunity to choose not to participate or provide this information. Our directory contains links to other websites. When you enter any of these websites, it must be noted that their policies may be materially different from our Privacy policy. 

  


**Personal Information:**   
The information we may collect for submitting to our directory - User name,Email and any other relevant information are strictly for weeding out any spam. We do not sell or redistribute this information to anyone. 

**Contact Information:**  
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this site, you should contact the Webmaster through our [contact](http://www.targetwoman.com/contact.html) page.
