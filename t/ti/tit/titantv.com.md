# Decisionmark - Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy

This web site is provided by **Decisionmark Corp.**.

Our postal address is   
**200 Second Ave. SE Suite 300  
Cedar Rapids, IA 52401**

We can be reached via e-mail at [webmaster@decisionmark.com](mailto:webmaster@decisionmark.com)  
You can reach us by telephone at 319-365-5597

Decisionmark Corp. believes in your right to know what information is collected during your visit to our site and how the information is used and safeguarded.

The Decisionmark web server automatically recognizes only the domain name, but not the e-mail address, of anyone who visits our site. We collect aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit. This information is used to generate reports that help Decisionmark assess the value and interest in its web site.

We collect the e-mail addresses for users who post messages to our bulletin board and who communicate with us via e-mail, as well as information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and site registrations.

User e-mail addresses may be used by us to:

>   * customize the content and layout of our page for each consumer
>   * notify consumers about updates to our web site; or
>   * contact consumers to notify them of new Decisionmark products and services or upgrades to existing products
> 


If you DO NOT want your e-mail address to be used for the above purposes, please let us know by contacting us via our e-mail address, telephone number or mailing address listed above and specifying that you do not want to receive e-mail from Decisionmark. 

If you supply us with your postal address online, you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending e-mail to us at the above address, calling us at the above telephone number or writing to us at the above address and specify in your request that you do not want to receive postal mailings from our company.

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers online may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed online. They also may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such telephone calls, please let us know by sending e-mail to us at the above address, calling us at the above telephone number or writing to us at the above address. In your request, please provide us with your correct phone number and specify that you do not wish to receive telephone calls from us.

From time to time, we may make our customer list available to other companies or organizations whose products or services we think you might find interesting. This list can include your e-mail address and, if you have provided it to us online, your postal address and your telephone number. If you do not want us to share your e-mail address, postal address and/or telephone number with other companies or organizations, please let us know by sending e-mail to us at the above address, calling us at the above telephone number or writing to us at the above address. In your request, please specify what information you do not want to be shared, your e-mail address, postal address and/or telephone number, and provide us with your exact name, postal address and telephone number. We will be sure your name is removed from the list we share with other organizations.
