# Privacy policy | Ticketline

We know that you care how information about you is used and shared and we appreciate your trust in us to do that carefully and sensibly. This notice describes the privacy policy of The Ticketline Network Limited. 

By visiting, registering and using this site you expressly consent and accept to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. 

## Data Protection

  * We will not send you unsolicited emails (spam).
  * We will not share your personal information for marketing without your consent.
  * Any personal information which we get from you or gather about you from your use of our site will be used to contact you by post, phone, email or SMS when necessary in connection with transactions entered into by you on the site. 
  * Please note that by ordering with Ticketline you are consenting to your personal information being shared with an event organiser or promoter or venue for the particular event for which you are buying tickets. You should contact them if you would from them. Information will only be passed to event organisers for certain events where this is requested. 
  * Financial information that is collected from you is used to authorise payment and bill you. Your consent is given to provide your financial or personal information to those third parties necessary to process your transactions with us, such as credit card companies, banks and those companies that handle shipping and fraud protection. 



## Cookies

A cookie is a small file that is automatically issued to your computer when you enter most websites (including this one) and which is stored by the browser on your computer's hard drive. Cookies identify your computer, not the individual user of your computer. Ticketline do not use cookies to collect personal information about you. Cookies are only used to track your order progress and user preferences and to improve your overall experience using the website. 

Below is a description of the cookies that Ticketline use: 

### Required Cookies

These are cookies that are required for the website to work properly when you browser it or try to order tickets. 

  * #### Session Cookie

This cookie is used to identify you as you proceed through the order process and to store your user preferences. The cookie will expire once you close your browser unless you tick “Keep me signed in for today”, in which case the cookie will be stored on your computer for 24 hours after which it will expire. 
  * #### Traffic Management Cookie

This cookie is used to ensure the website operates smoothly, giving you the best possible experience by sharing our web traffic between multiple servers. 
  * #### Cookie Options Cookie

This cookie is used to save whether or not you consent to other cookies being used. It is not used to track or invade your privacy, but instead there for us to remember your privacy settings. If you delete this cookie your Ticketline privacy options will reset back to default and you will be shown a message at the bottom of the window asking you if you'd like to change your privacy options. 



### Analytics Cookies

  * #### Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous data of how many visitors our website has and how the website is used. This information is used solely by Ticketline to monitor traffic and help us improve your overall experience. 


  * #### Third Party Websites

We use embedded share buttons for some third party social networking websites, such as Facebook and Twitter. These allow our users to share artists and events with their friends. These sites may set a cookie if you login to their service. Ticketline does not set or control these cookies and you should check the relevant third party website for more information about these.

Note: you can turn off social features, apart from when on a Ticketline facebook application where the facebook cookies are required for the application to work. 




If you wish to restrict cookies that are being set by the Ticketline website you can do this via your browser settings. For more information about cookies and how they work on different browsers please visit [www.aboutcookies.org](http://www.aboutcookies.org/). 

Please be aware that restricting cookies to the Ticketline website will inhibit your ability to sign in or purchase tickets. 

## Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. We use industry standard 128 bit Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption for your transactions with us. 

It secures all of your personal information, including credit card number, name, and address, so that it cannot be read as the information travels over the Internet.
