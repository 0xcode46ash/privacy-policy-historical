# Policies : Ticket Nest

The information we collect from you is used solely for completing your sale. It is relayed via encrypted data feeds to our Ticket Outlet processing your order. We do not, under any circumstances, give your personal information to anyone else. 

TicketNest.com is firmly committed to protecting your privacy while you are visiting our Web site. The following Privacy Policy discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for [www.TicketNest.com](http://www.ticketnest.com/). 

**INFORMATION THIS SITE GATHERS/TRACKS**   
We use your IP address and browser type to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. This means that a user's session will be tracked, but the user will remain anonymous. Your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information. This means that although your IP address may be associated during your session it is not permanently associated with any of your personal information. 

**INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE**   
_Personally Identifiable Information (PII):_   
We share PII with Third Parties to perform functions on our behalf. Examples include fulfilling orders, processing credit card payments, delivering packages, sending email and postal mail, and analyzing data for the purposes of data scrubbing and marketing assistance. We do not share PII with Third Parties for any unknown or unrelated uses. 

We reserve the right to disclose PII as required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on our Web site. 

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this privacy statement, the homepage, and other places we deem appropriate so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it. We reserve the right to modify this privacy statement at any time, so please review it frequently. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you here, by email, or by means of a notice on our homepage.

**Agents**  
This Web site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We employ other companies and individuals (Agents) to perform functions on our behalf. 

We use Agents to fulfill and distribute orders, to bill you for goods and services, and to assist us in marketing efforts. They have access to PII needed to perform their functions but are prohibited from retaining, sharing, storing, or using PII for any other purposes. 

_Aggregate Information (non-personally identifiable)_   
We use Third Party Service Providers to assist us in analyzing the aggregate information about the visitors on our site. This information, which helps us optimize the usability of our site, is collected via Service Provider cookie or log files. We do not link any aggregate information to PII. 

_Cookies_   
We use cookies to keep track of your shopping cart, customize your future shopping, and to assist your needs. Upon your request, we can use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so that you do not have to re-enter it each time you visit our site. 

**REGISTRATION**   
You can browse our site without entering any personal information and, therefore, remain anonymous during your visit. However, we hope that all of our visitors will register with us so that we can learn more about our valuable customers. Our site's optional registration form requires users to give us contact information (such as their name and email address). We save customer contact information from the registration form, so customers you not have to re-enter their billing and shipping information the next you shop with us. This information is also used to contact the customer if necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving any communications from our company at any time. Please review our Opt-Out Policy below. 

**LINKS**   
This site contains links to other sites. TicketNest.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. We encourage you to read posted privacy statements whenever interacting with any Web site. We will not share any specific information that you provide to us without your explicit permission to share it. We may share broad demographic information with others. We will never release your personal information (including but not limited to your name, email address, street address, and phone number) without your express permission. Users have the ability at any time to change their preferences with us; see our Opt-Out Policy below. 

**ORDER FORM**  
This Web site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We employ other companies and individuals to perform functions on our behalf. Examples include processing credit card payments, fulfilling orders, delivering packages, sending email and postal mail, and analyzing data for the purposes of data scrubbing and marketing assistance. They have access to personal information needed to perform their functions but may not use it for other purposes. Users may opt-out of receiving communications from our company at any time; see our opt out policy below.

**SERVICE-RELATED ANNOUNCEMENTS**   
We will send you strictly service-related announcements on rare occasions when it is necessary to do so. For instance, we will send you emails with information related to your order. Generally, you may not opt-out of these communications, which are not promotional in nature. If you do not wish to receive them, you have the option to deactivate your account; see our Opt-Out Policy below. 

**SECURITY**   
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. All transactions are placed on our secure server and are encrypted with full 128-bit encryption. All transactions must be placed with a secure browser for customers to take advantage of this secure service. 

**OPT-OUT POLICY**  
You may choose to opt-out of receiving future communications from TicketNest.com and our partners. If you do not want to receive our communications, simply send an email to us at [sara@ticketnest.com](mailto:sara@ticketnest.com)[](mailto:sara@ticketnest.com) with "Unsubscribe" in the subject line. We also offer an opportunity to opt-out of certain communications in our member information area or you may contact us at the contact information listed at the bottom of this policy. You are not able to opt-out of emails related to a specific order (confirmation emails). 

**CORRECTING/UPDATING INFORMATION**  
This site gives users options for changing and updating information previously provided. If your personally identifiable information changes, you may correct or update it by emailing Customer Care at [sara@ticketnest.com](mailto:sara@ticketnest.com)[](mailto:update@ticketnest.com), or by contacting us by telephone or postal mail at the contact information listed below.
