# TFCU: Disclaimer and Website Privacy Policy

**Disclaimer**

The information and materials contained in these pages, and the terms, conditions, and descriptions that appear, are subject to change without notice. No one has permission to copy or republish any information found on this Web site and any pages thereof, unless otherwise indicated.  Information from this website is invalid unless it is read and is presented in its entirety.

**

Website Privacy Policy

**

TFCU is committed to protecting the privacy of its members and other users of its website. We have adopted the following privacy policy for your protection:

**

General Website Pages

**

TFCU collects and stores information on the domain you use to access its website, the Internet address of the site from which you linked directly to our site, and the date and time of your visit to our site. This information is used to measure the number of visitors to the various pages on the site, to help TFCU make improvements to the information contained on the site, and to better serve our members through special marketing and service programs.

**

Applications

**

Only business related information is requested on any TFCU on-line application. This information is used only for review of qualifications for the loan, account, or service for which you are applying. Any information gathered during member application is not given or sold to third parties.

**

Home Branch

**

Personal identifying information is collected when a member logs on to Home Branch. This information enables TFCU to regulate entry and to measure member usage. Any personal identifying information gathered during member registration is not sold to third parties. Home Branch is a secured site and requires a browser that supports 128-bit encryption.

**

E-mail

**

Personal information contained in e-mail sent by members and non-members may be used by TFCU staff to answer questions, to follow up on suggestions or complaints, or to improve the level of service TFCU provides. Please be advised that e-mail may not be secure against interception by unauthorized individuals. Therefore, if you wish to communicate sensitive or personal information, you may want to send it by postal mail.

**

Cookies

**

TFCU currently uses cookies only in conjunction with Home Branch to estimate the number of users and the time of use. We do not link the information we receive from cookies with any other information, such as your name or address. We set these cookies when you enter the site and erase them when you leave the site. 

If you have any questions about TFCU’s website privacy policy, e-mail **[comments@tinkerfcu.org](mailto:%20comments@tinkerfcu.org)**.
