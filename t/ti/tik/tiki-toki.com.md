# Privacy policy

This Privacy and Cookie Policy explains how Webalon Ltd (the owner of www.tiki-toki.com) uses personal information we collect and how you can limit our use of that personal data.

### 1 What information do we have on you?

1.1 We don't collect any information directly from general visitors to our website. However, our advertisers (see below) and analytics service may gather information on visitors.

1.2 We store the username, email and password (the latter in an encrypted state) of users with accounts on www.tiki-toki.com. This information is used mainly for core site functionality such as verifying log-in credentials, tracking whether users are logged in, and supporting the timeline creation and editing service.

1.3 Our site also uses cookies to track whether the user is logged in and to support our core timeline creation service (see below).

### 2 We may use this information to:

(i) Check whether you are logged in.

(ii) Send you an email with a reset password if you request it or your account is compomised in anyway.

(iii) Email you updates about our products. 

### 3 Cookies

3.1 Cookies are small files stored on your computer when you visit certain websites. You can find out more about cookies here: http://www.cookiecentral.com.

3.2 Tiki-Toki uses cookies for the following purposes:

(i) Check whether you are logged into our website.

3.3 You can turn off cookies in your browser but this will prevent you using our timeline creation service, though you will still be able to view timelines.

### 4 Third party advertising cookies

4.1 Some of the timelines created by users of free accounts will have adverts displayed under them. These adverts are provided by third parties, who may use their own cookies to track visitors to our website.

4.2 Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website. Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting the [ads preference manager](https://www.google.com/settings/ads/onweb/).

4.3 Alternatively, users can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for interest based advertising by visiting [aboutads.info](http://www.aboutads.info/).

### 5 Personal information

5.1 We may disclose your personal information if we are approached by a potential buyer of our business, if we are required to do so by law or in good-faith believing such action is necessary to comply with the law.

5.2 We never sell personal information, or pass it to third parties for marketing purposes without your permission.

### 6 Contact us

6.1 If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us by email at hello@tiki-toki.com.
