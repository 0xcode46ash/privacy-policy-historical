# Privacy policy - Time4vps.eu

Time4vps has created this privacy statement to demonstrate commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices. 

### General Information

When you sign up for our service, we will ask you to provide contact information such as your name, address (city, state/region, zip code), phone number, e-mail addresses, business data (VAT number if it is), and payment information such as credit card number, expiration date and CVV/CVC2 number (if Credit Card payment method apply). 

If there is necessity to verify customer identity, we may ask for passport or other identity approval documents. 

### Automatically logged information

When you visit our Web site we will capture your IP Address, time and duration of visit, and time and duration of the pages on our Web site that you view. We may tie this information to the your personally identifiable information. 

### Use of Personally Identifiable Information

Our site uses a secure order form for customers to request services. We will use your personally identifiable information only as follows: 

  * Contact information from the order forms is used to get in touch with the customer when necessary;
  * Billing information that is collected and used to bill the user for services;
  * Unique identifiers are collected from web site visitors to verify the user's identity;
  * All collected information used for billing and service provisioning purposes, no other reason;
  * Third parties who perform services on our behalf (such as payment processing), subject to the third party agreeing with us that it will keep your personally identifiable information confidential.



### Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. We use strong SSL encryption (256-bit) to ensure your privacy and data transmission safety. 

If customer have any questions about this privacy statement or customer dealings with our web site, customer can contact us: info@time4vps.eu .
