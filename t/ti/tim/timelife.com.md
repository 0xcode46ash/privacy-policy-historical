# Time Life - Privacy Policy

  


## Time Life Privacy Policy

This web site is maintained by Time Life Inc., 2000 Duke Street, Alexandria, VA 22314.

Time Life Inc. has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.

Our site uses forms allowing customers to request information, products, and services. On these forms, we collect visitor's contact information (like their email address, home address, and telephone number). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders. The customer's contact information is also used to get in touch with the customer when necessary to respond to a customer request.

We may also send promotional information regarding our products, and occasionally send material from some responsible third parties to our customers. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.

**Supplementation Information**

This site supplements the information that you provide with information that is received from third parties.

**Choice/Opt-Out**

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving promotional emails from us and some responsible third parties at the point where we request information about the visitor. If you do not wish to receive this kind of information, you can un-select the check box on the order and customer service form. Promotional email you do receive from us, will include instructions if you should decide you want to opt-out at a later date.

If you wish to remove yourself from our customer database for printed mail and telephone promotional communications, you can send mail to the following postal address:

ATTN: Time Life Inc.  
1450 E. Parham Rd., Richmond, VA 23280

**Correct/Update**

This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided.

Send mail to the following postal address:

1450 E. Parham Rd., Richmond, VA 23280

OR

Use the Customer Service form provided in the Customer Service section of the site.
