# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy  
  
This website features Google Ads. Barhorst Creative websites that display Google Ads use third-party advertising companies to serve those ads. 

These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. 

If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, 

[click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).

Barhorst Creative does not sell or lease email addresses or any other information collected through the use of our online forms to any third-party. 

Barhorst Creative is located in Beavercreek, Ohio and is a provider of graphic design services, stock photography and other content designed to help businesses advertise and market their products and services. 

Barhorst Creative is a member of the 

[Greater Dayton Ad Association (GDAA)](http://www.greaterdaytonadassociation.org/) , the [American Advertising Federation (AFA)](http://www.aaf.org/) and the [Dayton Creative Syndicate (DCS)](http://www.creativesyndicate.org/).
