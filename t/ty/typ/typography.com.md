# Privacy Policy | Hoefler & Frere-Jones

This Privacy Policy is applicable to Hoefler & Frere-Jones' internet website (the "Website"). By visiting this Website, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use this Website. The Hoefler Type Foundry, Inc., DBA Hoefler & Frere-Jones, including its subsidiaries and affiliates is collectively referred to as "H&FJ" hereafter.

**Privacy.** H&FJ recognizes and respects the privacy of users of this Website. When you choose to provide H&FJ with information about yourself, H&FJ will act in a responsible manner. H&FJ may store and disclose personal information as allowed or required by applicable law, including making disclosures that are necessary or advisable to (a) protect the rights, safety or property of H&FJ or others and (b) conform to legal or regulatory requirements. Without your express approval, however, H&FJ will not trade, sell or rent your personal information but may collect and/or provide aggregate statistics about its Website and users to other parties.

**Confidentiality.** If you use this Website, you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account and password and for restricting access to your computer, and you agree to accept responsibility for all activities that occur under your account or password. H&FJ does sell products for children, but it sells them to adults, who can purchase with a credit card. If you are under 18, you may use this Website only with involvement of a parent or guardian. H&FJ reserves the right to refuse service, terminate accounts, remove or edit content, or cancel orders in its sole discretion.

**Child Online Privacy Protection Act (15 U.S.C. §§ 6501-6506).** H&FJ has no intention of collecting any personally identifiable information from individuals under 13 years of age. If a child has provided personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email address, physical address, birth date, social security number) a parent or guardian of that child should contact us. We will use reasonable efforts to delete the child's information from our existing files.

**"Cookies."** As part of using this Website, information commonly known as "cookies" may be placed on your computer. These cookies contain information about you and your experience with this Website, necessary for the propagation of content, and the navigation of this Website and its storefront. The use of cookies is an industry standard and many Websites use them. If you do not wish to receive cookies, or want to be notified of when they are placed, you may set your web browser to do so, if your browser so permits.

**Links.** This Website may contain links to other websites. H&FJ, however, does not control the privacy policies of these sites. This Privacy Policy applies to information collected and maintained solely by H&FJ.

**Changes.** H&FJ may, without notice, change, modify or restrict access to this Website, or the content of the pages at any time.
