# Privacy Policy - Tyloon.com

We at Tyloon respect and guard your privacy; it is our goal to protect and bring better services to our clients and users. We acknowledge that privacy is an important issue; therefore, at Tyloon we have generated our services for your protection. Our Privacy Policy lists the types of personal information that can or may be gathered while using Tyloon and its services. 

This policy informs you how Tyloon will utilize your personal information that we, at Tyloon, may collect and/or receive, including the information related to your use of Tyloon services. Personal information is your information that is used to personally identify you by name, address, email address, or phone number, information that is not publicly available or known.

** GENERAL INFORMATION:  **

Tyloon will collect personal information when and if you are required to register with Tyloon. When you use you visit Tyloon, its associated Local Yellow Pages or the pages of certain Tyloon partners, Tyloon may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from its business partners or its companies. 

You agree that when you register, your general such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests may be asked or requested. Should you desire to become an advertiser or to display your business; only for some financial purposes may we request your business tax information or social security number and if necessary any financial information required for gathering of necessary information for establishing remittance of payment. 

Tyloon gathers and uses information for the sole purpose of the following: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfilling your requests for products and services, to improve our services, making contact with you, our internal research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients. 

** Information Sharing and Disclosure**

At Tyloon we will not rent, sell, or disclose your personal information about you with any other person or companies not associated or connected to Tyloon; except as in the following conditions: 

Your information is trusted to our partners and associated who work on behalf or with Tyloon. (only under our confidentiality agreements.) Your information is then only provided to assist Tyloon with communicating with its own marketing partners and associates. It is understood that this information is not to be independently provided for outside information or to be distributed.

Only when necessary, we respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; Tyloon believes that when obligatory to share information in order to investigate, inhibit, or cooperated with law enforcement agencies in the knowledge of illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, Tyloon will provide necessary information or documents otherwise required by law.

We at, Tyloon, do not and will not tolerate or be associated with any adult advertising, solicitation or displays. We hold to our code of not be involved with the adult only industry, its associates, products or services.

It is important to know that this Privacy Policy will change from time to time. If and when changes are made, we expect them to be minor; however there may be changes that are more substantial. However, we will post those changes and if the changes are substantial we will also provide a more significant notice.
