# tpww.net - [ privacy policy ]

**privacy statement**

This policy is to show the privacy throughout our site. Once you click on a link or move to another site you will be subjected to a new policy.

**Advertisements**

Our advertisements may contain cookies, which allow advertisers to track information about which sites you go to. For more information please visit our advertisers' official websites. 

**Personal Information**

TPWW currently does not require any personal information to be given out. Our staff will never ask anything personal.

**External Links**

We do have several links to other sites. We are not associated with these sites in any way, unless stated otherwise. 

**Public Forums**

We do have a forum which allows people to have discussions. All information is optional and posting is at your own risk. 

**Chat Rooms**

TPWW staff will never ask for personal information. Children under 13 years of age must have parent's permission.
