# Tee Turtle

Terms of service

**Privacy Policy**

**1\. Your personal information** is safe here. We will not release or sell any of the data you submit to the site. We store your address and contact information to make it easier for you to check out in the future, but for your security we do not keep records of credit card information.

**2\. You decide what is enough.** We will never send you more e-mail than you want. If at any point you feel you are getting spammed, shoot an email to our service team and we will make sure to take care of the problem for you.

**3\. Our website uses cookies** to make your shopping experience quicker and easier. If you are having trouble with shopping functions, make sure you have cookies enabled.

**Terms of Use**

**1\. You must be 13 or older to shop**. Otherwise, get a parent involved. Do not give any personal information if you are under the age of 13.

**2\. We reserve the right** to cancel and refund an order at any time if we feel that the information provided is incomplete or inaccurate. Please make sure to fill in your information correctly. If there is an issue, contact service@teeturtle.com immediately. 

**3\. All confirmations on orders** will be sent only electronically.
