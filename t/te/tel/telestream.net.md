# Telestream Company - Privacy Policy

## **Here's what you can expect from us to protect your privacy at Telestream.net**

Your privacy and security are very important to us at Telestream and we understand that it is very important to our customers as well. When we ask you to register or to provide personal information when you visit certain areas of our website, it is for the sole purpose of supporting and enhancing our relationship with you, for example, to download software, provide service and support, and share product and company news and offerings with you. Occasionally it is necessary for us to collect information about your software version, CPU type, OS version and other system specific data. We use this information only for the purpose of providing you with appropriate software updates and/or support. We strive to protect the security of your personal data and will _never_ sell your personal information to other companies or organizations. 

If you choose not to register or provide personal information, you can still visit the Telestream website; however, you will not have access to areas that require personal identification. 

Telestream is the sole operator of the Telestream Website. However, our website contains links to other sites. _Telestream assumes no responsibility for the content or the privacy policies and practices of such websites._ Telestream encourages you to read the privacy statements of these linked sites since their privacy practices may differ from ours.
