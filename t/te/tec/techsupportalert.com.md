# This Site's Privacy Policy | Gizmo's Tech Support Alert

This is the Privacy Policy that applies to this site. It explains how we handle personal information and comply with the requirements of the privacy laws. If you have further questions relating to this policy, please contact us. 

**Collecting information about you**

We collect personal information from site registrants as well as  subscribers to our newsletters and RSS feeds. The main types of information we collect are contact details, such as email addresses, names and addresses. We collect most of this information directly from individuals when they register at our web site.  
or subscribe to our newsletters and RSS feeds.

**Using and disclosing your personal information**

Our purpose in collecting information about you is to deliver the site services you requested, newsletters and RSS feeds to which you subscribe, to provide any services that you request and to operate our business efficiently.

This website and also newsletters and RSS feeds to which you subscribe may contain advertisements but we will not disclose any personal information you have provided to these advertisers or any other outside parties without your permission except where:

  * It is permitted by this Privacy Policy;
  * It is believed necessary to provide you with goods or services you have requested;
  * Some or all of the assets and operations of this website and/or newsletter business are transferred to another party in connection with a merger or sale;
  * It is necessary to protect the rights, property or personal safety of our employees, our other customers, or any member of the public.



If we collect or use personal information in ways other than as stated in this Privacy Policy, we will do so in accordance with the requirements of the privacy laws.

**Unsubscribing**

You can unsubscribe from any of our newsletters and RSS feeds at any time.

Every issue of our newsletters explains how to subscribe and unsubscribe. You may also unsubscribe by sending an unsubscribe request that lists each email address receiving unwanted copies of the newsletter to [editor@techsupportalert.com](mailto:editor@techsupportalert.com "editor@techsupportalert.com")

Virtually all RSS client software allow you to unsubscribe yourself and will we do nothing to prevent or hinder that process.

**Access to your personal information**

If at any time you want to know exactly what personal information we hold about you, you are welcome to email us at the email address shown above. We will always attempt to make this information available to you within 14 days.

If at any time you wish to change personal information that we hold about you because it is inaccurate or out-of-date, please contact us at the email address shown above and we will amend this information. If you wish to have your personal information deleted, please contact us and we will take all reasonable steps to delete it, unless we need to keep it for legal, auditing or internal risk reasons.

**Cookies**

We sometimes use cookie technology on our website to provide information and services to website visitors. Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to your computer's hard disk for record keeping purposes and are an important tool for facilitating online transactions. They are also useful to determine overall traffic patterns through our website.

The cookies used on this site are mostly self-destructing session cookies. These cookies and the information they contain are erased when users exit this website. The only enduring cookies used on this site are those created when users voluntarily elect to have their login details remembered.

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies. If you do not wish to receive any cookies, you may set your browser to refuse cookies, but this may mean you will not be able to take full advantage of the services on the website. In particular you may not be able to access the paid premium content area of this website

**Consent**

By providing your email address and other personal information, you consent to the use of your personal information, including your email address, in accordance with this Privacy Policy and the privacy laws.

**Changes to This Privacy Policy**

Our Privacy Policy may be reviewed periodically and we reserve the right to change it at any time.

In the event of any changes you will be notified at least 21 days in advance of the intended change.

You will be informed of any change by a notice in the newsletters or RSS feeds to which you subscribe and/or a special email notice to the email address you have provided. We will promptly post any updated versions of our Privacy Policy on our website. As email is not a 100% reliable vehicle for notifications we recommend you periodically check the privacy policy on the website for any possible changes.
