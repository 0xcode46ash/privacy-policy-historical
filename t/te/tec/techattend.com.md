# Privacy Policy

Tech Attend cares how information about you is used and shared.  This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which we collect, use, maintain and disclose information collected from users of techattend.com.

You may be asked for an email address in case of subscribing to our newsletter. We will never share your email address to any third party. You may, however, visit our Site anonymously.

We may collect non-personal identification information about you whenever you interact with our Site. This information is required only for optimal delivery of our content to the visitor’s computer.

Our Site may use “cookies” to enhance your browsing experience. Your web browser places cookies on your hard drive for record-keeping purposes and sometimes to track information about you. You may choose to set your web browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If you do so, note that some parts of the Site may not function properly.

If you decide to opt-in to our newsletter, you will receive emails that may include news, updates, related product or service information, etc. If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, we include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email.

We promise to NEVER send you any spam. Please report to us if you receive any.

Tech Attend will NEVER ask for your Credit Card details, and we request that you do not enter it on our Site.

Tech Attend has the discretion to update this privacy policy at any time.

For further information please [contact us](http://www.techattend.com/contact/)
