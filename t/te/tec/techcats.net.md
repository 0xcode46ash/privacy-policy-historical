# Privacy Policy | TechCats

We **TechCats  **will respect the privacy of our visitors as one of the most extreme important factor. This document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by **TechCats  **and how it is used.

  * To reproduce quotes 
  * To reproduce screen shots of any page of a **TechCats  **site To include original **TechCats  **material in mash-ups, mixes, parodies, caricatures, etc for movies, TV, print or online. 



**RSS feed use**

**TechCats** provides feeds for the Site at <http://feeds2.feedburner.com/TechCats>****

If you are using all or substantially all of a feed, we ask that you do not modify it in any way, including the stripping out of advertisements. 

  * Full ad-supported feeds are intended only for personal use in RSS readers. 
  * For all other feed uses, including reproduction on websites, you should only use the Excerpt feeds, unless otherwise agreed with the company. 
  * Please do not splice the RSS feeds into or otherwise redistribute them via third-party RSS providers. 
  * Please do not add content, including any advertisements or other promotional content, to the RSS feeds. 
  * **TechCats  **reserves the right to object to your presentation of the RSS feeds and the right to require you to cease using the RSS feeds at any time. 
  * **TechCats  **further reserves the right to terminate its distribution of the RSS feeds or change the content or formatting of the RSS feeds at any time without notice to you. 



**Web Syndication Terms of Use**

  * Internal links in the Site content must not be removed. 
  * The Site logo and/or URL should appear on each page displaying site content. 
  * The format or branding of the headlines, text and other information provided in the RSS feeds must not be modified. 
  * “More From…” links back to original Site at the end of each post must be included on content pages and may not be removed. 



**Advertiser Cookies**

  * [Google](http://www.techcats.net/category/google "Google"), as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site. 
  * Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the [Internet](http://www.techcats.net/tag/internet "Internet"). 
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html). 



**TechCats  **has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers. You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about [how to](http://www.techcats.net/category/how-to "how to") opt-out of certain practices. 

**TechCats  **privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites. If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
