# Privacy Policy - Technically Personal!

TechPP.com values the readers and visitors privacy. However for maintaining statistics and keeping away spammers we do need to collect and preserve small piece of information of people those who visit our site.

Privacy Policy:

  * We use analytical tool such as Google Analytics, Woopra to understand where our customers and visitors are coming from. In this we collect information such as IP Addresses and Operating system used. However these data are not shared with a third party and strictly kept for increasing the performance of website.
  * The site is hosted at knownhost.com ( hosting company) but no data is shared with them either.
  * The site uses a blogging software called as WordPress which stores a small amount of information in users computer when he or she comments. This is of very small size and no way is a malware or script which hacks a computer for information.
  * You are free to read and utilize the resource available here but not allowed to copy the content.
  * We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you through cookies. TechnicallyPersonal has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers. You may disable cookies on your browser but it affects your interaction with this site. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
  * Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
