# Privacy Policy

Tech-Pro Privacy Policy  
---  
  
### Personal information

Tech-Pro Limited values your privacy. The only information we may hold about you is your name and email address. The only way we can obtain this information is if:

  * you purchase a Tech-Pro branded product;
  * you send us an email.



Tech-Pro Limited will not use information received in this way for any purpose other than to identify registered users of our products or to reply to your emails. Tech-Pro Limited will not give or sell this information to any third party.

### Cookies and other tracking technologies

In common with all other websites, this website logs usage information to enable us to monitor the effectiveness of the website. The information logged includes such items as your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser software and operating system, referring web page (the page containing the link you clicked), pages accessed, and dates and times that you access the site. This website does not log any information that would enable us to identify you as an individual. We may analyze the information we collect for trends and statistics, or we may employ third party analysis services to collect and analyze site usage information for us.

Some pages of this site may use cookies for technical reasons, to improve your browsing experience. Some third party sites linked from this site may place cookies on your computer to record that you came from this site, in order to credit Tech-Pro Limited with commission on any sales that may subsequently result.

### Third party sites

Electronic commerce (e-commerce) transactions made through this site are made using third parties such as PayPal, Element5 or eSellerate. Tech-Pro Limited never receives your credit card details or other personal or financial information. These third party e-commerce providers have their own privacy policies: please consult them if you require more information.
