# Privacy Policy

Tech-Pro Privacy Policy  
---  
  
### Personal information

Tech-Pro Limited values your privacy. The only information we may hold about you is your name and email address. The only way we can obtain this information is if:

  * you purchase a product;
  * you sign up for our newsletter;Â 
  * you send us an email.



We detest spam as much as you do. Tech-Pro Limited guarantees that we will not use information received in this way for any purpose other than to identify registered users of our products, send out newsletters or to reply to your emails. Tech-Pro Limited will never give or sell this information to any third party.

### Cookies and other tracking technologies

In common with all other websites, the web server on which this site is hosted logs usage information, including such items as your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser software and operating system, referring web page (the page containing the link you clicked), pages accessed, and dates and times that you access the site. The web server does not log any information that would enable us to identify you as an individual. Tech-Pro Limited may analyze the information we collect for trends and statistics, or we may employ third party analysis services to collect and analyze site usage information for us, in order to improve the effectiveness of the site.

Some pages of this site may use cookies for technical reasons, to improve your browsing experience. Some third party sites linked from this site may place cookies on your computer to record that you came from this site. The main reason for this is to allow Tech-Pro Limited to receive a commission on any sales that may result from links to products mentioned on this site.

###  E-commerce

Electronic commerce (e-commerce) transactions made through this site are made using third parties such as PayPal, Element5 or eSellerate. Tech-Pro Limited never receives your credit card details or other personal or financial information, and will never ask you for them. The third party e-commerce providers have their own privacy policies: please consult them if you require more information.
