# Privacy Policy for Apptitude Apps | uncategorized

JUnite (and it’s derived apps - The Blog App, Easysocial Mobile, JGive) (“ **JUnite** ”) are smartphones applications created by Apptitude, a business unit of Tekdi Technologies Pvt. Ltd. (“ **Apptitude** ”) that allows it’s users to use their Joomla site credentials to log into the app and use several features of the Joomla CMS and supported extensions on their smartphones. The JUnite app is available on various Mobile Operating Systems including iOS and Android.

 BY USING THE SERVICE, YOU REPRESENT AND WARRANT THAT YOU HAVE READ AND UNDERSTOOD, AND AGREE TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY. IF YOU DO NOT UNDERSTAND OR DO NOT AGREE TO BE BOUND BY THIS PRIVACY POLICY, YOU MUST IMMEDIATELY CEASE USING THE SERVICE.

**Public Social Networks, Blogs, Following Users and Posting Comments**

JUnite makes Social Networks, Blog posts and comments available to users of the app. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information on any forum.

Upon your request to login to a website via JUnite, it may transmit information you provide (such as username, name or email address and your password) directly to the requested website, we do not retain or store on our servers any of that information, as well as information pertaining to your interactions with forums. We do not store on our servers any email addresses, usernames or passwords. Once you successfully logged into a website, all communications within the app are direct communications between your mobile device and the respective website you are using.

  **Information Collected**

The JUnite mobile app does not collect any personally identifiable information. As outlined in the previous section, all information that you enter in the app is direct communication between your mobile device and the website you are intending to log into.

**Analytics Service Providers**

We may allow certain third-party analytics service providers to include anonymous identifiers on the Application. We may also share portions of our log file data, including IP addresses, with these service providers for analytics purposes. These service providers aggregate information about what you see and interact with in the Application.

None of your Personally identifiable information is shared with any Analytics providers.

  **Links**

The app may contain links to websites, or applications not operated by JUnite or Apptitude. The app may also carry advertisements from third party companies not associated with Apptitude. Apptitude is not responsible for the privacy practices of websites, forums, or applications or other services operated by third parties that are linked to or integrated with the Service or for the privacy practices of third party advertising companies. Once you leave the app via such a link, access a blog, a third party application or click on an advertisement, you should check the applicable privacy policy of the forum, third party, or advertiser site to determine, among other things, how they will handle any Personal Information they collect from you.

**Widgets**

We may include a widget from a third party on the Website or on the Application that allows interaction or content sharing by users. “Widgets” are mini-programs that provide specific services or content from a third party. These Widgets, such as a Facebook “Share” or “Like” button, may be visible to you on a profile page or blog entry you visit. The third party providing the Widget may offer controls for you to manage the Widget’s appearance and functionality. Your interaction with a Widget typically allows the third party to collect some information about you through the third party’s cookies or its registration information. In some cases, the third party may recognize you through its Widgets and cookies even when you do not interact with the Widget. **Changes to this Privacy Policy**

From time to time we may modify this Privacy Policy to reflect best practices or changes in the law, changes in the features of the Service, or technology, and such modifications shall be effective upon updating this policy or making an update to the App.

Your continued use of the Service after we post a revised Privacy Policy signifies your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy. Therefore, it is important that you review this Privacy Policy regularly to ensure you are updated as to any changes.
