# TestPassed.com Privacy Policy

**We respect your privacy. We do not share or sell your information to any third parties.**

When you are ready to place an order, you have to register with us. We do not use your personal information such as your name and your mailing address anywhere besides processing your order and providing you with highest quality customer service. Our website is protected with Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) Encryption to insure that none of your personal information is compromised. 

**Creating an Account**

The registration information provides us with your name, your mailing address and your phone number which we use for customer service purpose only. Creating an account also allows for an easier shopping experience by letting you order much more quickly and efficiently without entering your personal information again. Your email may be used for our promotions and announcements. You may opt-out by calling us.

**Ordering**

When you are placing an order, we ask you for your credit card information. Your credit card information is acquired by our credit card processors strictly for the transactions that you place. Your credit card information does not go anywhere besides our credit processor, and your financial institution. If we have difficulty processing the order, your contact information is used to get in touch with you.

**Cookies / Usage Tracking**

Cookies, which are a bits of data saved on your computer. Cookies provide us with general non-personal information to allow us to track visitors, and site viewing patterns. We keep the aggregate visitor / usage information without any kind of personal data for statistics and traffic analyzation. This allows us to improve our site and tailor it to your needs.

**Links**

This website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for content and privacy practices of those sites, and cannot guarantee anything. We suggest you be aware of leaving our site and review the privacy policy of other sites. 

**Security**

All of the transaction and personal information is encrypted with 128-bit Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption which safeguards the information sent to and from us. Please note the small lock icon on the bottom-right of the browser window when youâ€™re placing an order. That icon shows that your browser is in a secure mode with our site. We do not keep your credit card information nor do the credit card processors. Our computers are protected against hackers, and our employees abide by the highest privacy standards. If you have any questions about security of our site, please contact us.

**Special Offers**

Our established members may receive special offers and discounts. If you wish to opt out from these mailings, please contact us.

We strive to provide you with the best shopping experience possible. If you have any questions, feel that our privacy policy is lacking, or would like to remove your account from our database, please give us a call.
