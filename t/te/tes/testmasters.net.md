TestMasters is committed to protecting the privacy and security of the users of its website. THE INFORMATION COLLECTED ON THIS WEBSITE ABOUT YOU IS SUBJECT TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY. BY DISCLOSING THE INFORMATION TO US, YOU AGREE TO THE TERMS OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY. 

  
_Collection and Storage of Personal Information_

We will collect certain personal information, including your name, address, telephone number, educational background, year(s) of graduation and e-mail address, when you inquire about our standardized test courses and/or ask a question online. We maintain this information in our student database.

_  
Use of Information_

This information is used only to provide the service or product requested and to enable us to mail to you TestMasters informational materials and to contact you at your request. The information is also used to register you for a class. We will not disclose your personal information to any third parties not affiliated with TestMasters. However, the information you provide may be used to enforce the rights of TestMasters in a court of law of competent jurisdiction.

  
_Non-Personal Identification Information And The Use Of Cookie Technology_

We collect non-personal identification information through the use of "cookie" technology and/or IP address tracking. Non-personal identification information might include the browser you use, the type of computer, the operating systems, the Internet service providers and other similar information. Our system also automatically gathers information about the areas you visit on our sites and about the links you may select from within our site to other areas of the World Wide Web or elsewhere online. We use such information in the aggregate to understand how our users as a group use our website. This enables us to know which areas of our sites are favorites of our users, which areas need improvement, and what technologies are being used so that we may continually improve our website. We may share this aggregate statistical information with our website developers, but we will not share any individual names, personal navigational or other personal information. 

  
_Your Rights_

Upon written request, we will remove your personal information from our student database or change and/or correct personal information that you state is erroneous, within seven days of your written request. However, if you take our course, purchase our books, or receive private tutoring and/or private tutoring packages, we will not remove your personal information from our student database.

Acceptance of These Terms_

By using the TestMasters website, you signify your acceptance of our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part the entire policy, please do not use our website. Your continued use of the TestMasters website following the posting of changes to these terms will signify your acceptance of those changes.
