During the process of delivering various services to you, TexAgs does not collect any personally identifiable information about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address. However, TexAgs does obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information such as the Internet Service Provider you use to connect to the Internet, your browser type, or the type of computer operating system you use (Macintosh or Windows, for example). The information collected by TexAgs is used for the purpose of targeting ads and measuring ad effectiveness on behalf of our advertisers. 

In some cases, you may voluntarily provide personal information such as, but not limited to, email addresses, zip codes, age, etc. This information is primarily used to allow user-specific access to various areas and functions of TexAgs. In addition, TexAgs may combine this information with other users' information and review it in an aggregate form to understand the type of individuals who view specific content areas inside the TexAgs website. 

_TexAgs.com works with a third party that serves ads to this site.
