# * Privacy :: #1 Source for Magento Themes & Magento Templates *

#### You're currently on:

This privacy statement discloses our practices for the gathering and use of information from visitors to our own Web sites, from people who register for services that we offer online, and from visitors to other sites where we collect information pursuant to our customer contracts.

## Your Acceptance

By using this site, you agree to the TemplatesForMagento Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use or register at our site. We reserve the right, at our discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for changes. Your continued use of the TemplatesForMagento site following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes.

## The Information we collect

We collect the domain name, ip address of visitors to our Web page, the e-mail addresses of those who post messages to our bulletin board, the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, the e-mail addresses of those who make postings to our chat areas, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, name and address, telephone number.

We use cookies to enrich your user experience and to allow you to visit multiple pages of our site without having to login at every page. We do not use cookies to identify, monitor, or track any personally identifiable information. Our cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information about you. By modifying your browser preferences, you have the choice to accept all cookies, to be notified when a cookie is set, or to reject all cookies. For more information on how to modify your browser settings to block or filter cookies, see <http://www.cookiecentral.com/faq/>.

Our site may contain links to other sites. Please note, however, that these other sites may have privacy policies that differ from those of TemplatesForMagento. We have no control over any personal information you choose to provide those sites.

To refer a friend, you will be asked to provide their email address so that we can send them a one-time invitation on your behalf to join our website. All referral email addresses are securely protected.

## User registration

Our membership registration process requires that you provide us with basic contact information such as name, email, address and birthdates. Additional information, including mailing address, are required in order to disburse payment. Without such details, we will not be able to minimize fraud and/or ensure transaction authenticity. We will never sell or otherwise share this information with any other organization.

Minors under 18 years of age are not allowed to participate in any of the services and/or programs offered by TemplatesForMagento. No information should be submitted to, or posted at, any TemplatesForMagento Web sites by visitors under 18 years of age. We encourage parents and guardians to spend time online with their children and to participate and monitor the interactive activities of their children.

## The information we collect is used for

  * To improve the content of our Web page
  * To allow us to ship your free gifts
  * To enable us to respond to you or to process, validate, and verify service requests and to perform the requested services
  * To tailor the registrant's experience on our sites, showing them content we think might interest them or altering the content according to their preferences.
  * We will use your e-mail address to send out occasional updates and offers.
  * We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site



If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail at the above address. If you supply us with your postal address on-line you will only receive the information for which you provided us your address. Except for purposes stated expressly in this Privacy Policy, TemplatesForMagento will not use your personal information.

## Disclosures Required By Law

There is a limited number of situations requiring us to disclose your personal information to others without first receiving your consent: when ordered to do so by a subpoena or court order, if you violate or breach an agreement with us, if we believe conduct by you will harm the property or rights of TemplatesForMagento or those of TemplatesForMagento's users, under exigent circumstances to protect the physical safety of TemplatesForMagento, its employees, users, or the general public, in connection with the sale of all or substantially all of our company's assets or the sale of an entire business division, or to validate credit card numbers.

## Aggregate Information

TemplatesForMagento may share aggregate statistical information gathered from the registration process with advertisers, business partners, sponsor or other third parties, but none of them receive individual identifying information about you collected at our sites.
