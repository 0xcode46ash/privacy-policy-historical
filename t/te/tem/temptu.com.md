# Privacy Policy | TEMPTU

Security Statement 

We only use your name and address information to mail you promotional items, news and offers. 

Email Privacy Matters 

We use your email address only to send you information and updates about temptu.com. You can unsubscribe at any time you wish to be taken off the Temptu.com emailing list. 

Cookies and Your Computer 

When you log on, the cookie tells us whether you have visited us before or are a new visitor. It doesn't track any personal information, give us the means to contact you, extract information from your computer or deliver viruses. Furthermore, your address or credit card information is never stored in a cookie. Cookies are simply the most efficient way for us to be able to offer you an enjoyable online shopping environment. 

Disclosure of Personal Information 

Any information you provide to us at this site when you establish or update an account, shop online or request information (i.e. name, address, email address, telephone number and credit card information), is maintained in private files on our secure Web server and our internal systems. To ensure compliance with federal law, Temptu.com does not maintain information provided by children under the age of 16. If you are visiting Temptu.com from a location outside of the U.S., your connection will be through and to servers located in the U.S. Your orders placed through Temptu.com will be accepted and fully processed in and shipped from the U.S. and all information you provide will be securely maintained in our Web server and internal systems located within the U.S. 

Links 

For your convenience, our Website may contain links to other sites. temptu.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Websites. 

Security 

temptu.com believes in providing a safe and secure shopping experience for all of our clients. We provide stringent and effective security measures on our website. It is our policy to never send private information, such as your credit card number, via email. In fact, this is a practice we recommend you adopt in all of your Internet activities. We designed temptu.com to accept orders only from Web browsers that permit communication through Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology; for example, 3.0 versions or higher of Netscape Navigator and versions 3.02 or higher of Internet Explorer. This means you'll be unable to inadvertently place an order through an unsecured connection. 

Third-Party Ad-Servers 

We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads on our behalf. These companies may employ cookies and action tags (also known as single pixel .gifs or web beacons) to measure advertising effectiveness. Any information that these third parties collect via cookies and action tags is completely anonymous.
