# Privacy Statement – Tennis Australia

We collect personal information to provide our products and services. When you provide personal information you agree that this will be used by Tennis Australia and other Australian Tennis Organisations under the terms of the tennis [privacy policy](http://www.tennis.com.au/about-tennis-australia/reports-and-policies/policies "Policies") and that you may be sent information.

Personal information may also be disclosed under the terms of the tennis privacy policy  to third parties such as our service providers, who may be located overseas including in the USA and the Netherlands. The tennis privacy policy contains information about how you may access and seek correction of your personal information, how you can complain about a breach of your privacy, and how the complaint will be dealt with. It also contains information about how you may opt-out of receiving direct marketing communications.
