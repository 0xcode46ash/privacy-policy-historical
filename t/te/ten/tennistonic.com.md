# Privacy Policy

**What This Privacy Policy Covers**   
This privacy policy relates to use of the Tennis Tonic web site at www.tennistonic.com  
This privacy policy covers how Tennis Tonic (hereafter named 'we') will treat personal information that we collect.   
We will not send you any junk emails.   
You can decide if you want to receive emails by changing your Notification Settings in the Setting area  
You can decide also which information you want to show by changing your Privacy Settings: Profile information in the Setting area.  
You can also decide which information you want to show in your personal profile.  
Children under 16 are not permitted to use the TennisTonic service, and so this privacy policy makes no provision for children's use of the site.

**We do not sell, rent, or otherwise provide your personal identifiable information to any third parties for marketing purposes. We also provide you with the means to control whether or not your contact information is viewable to other Users through your profile.**
