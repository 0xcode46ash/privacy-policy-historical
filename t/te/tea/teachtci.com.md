# History Alive! Site Security

  1. Teachers’ Curriculum Institute will never rent, sell, or exchange personally identifiable information about its customers. Teachers’ Curriculum Institute may provide information to regulatory authorities and law enforcement officials in accordance with applicable law.
  2. Teachers’ Curriculum Institute does NOT use any third-party advertising network, nor share any information with any profiling companies. 
  3. Teachers’ Curriculum Institute gathers only the customer information that we need to administer our business, provide superior service, and communicate offers on merchandise and services if you choose to receive them.
  4. Teachers’ Curriculum Institute takes reasonable efforts to ensure that the customer information it maintains is accurate, timely, and secure. When credit card information is transmitted on-line, Teachers’ Curriculum Institute uses industry-standard security measures called SSL (secure socket layer) encryption. Once a credit card order is processed, the credit card information is not stored on the server.  




Teachers’ Curriculum Institute values the trust its customers place in the company. If you have additional questions or would like further information on this topic, please feel free to e-mail us at [info@historyalive.com](mailto:info@historyalive.com) Teachers’ Curriculum Institute may update this policy from time to time. Please periodically check the policy for changes.
