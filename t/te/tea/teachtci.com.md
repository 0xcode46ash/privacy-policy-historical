# History Alive! Site Security

TCI will never rent, sell, or exchange personally identifiable information about its customers. TCI may provide information to regulatory authorities and law enforcement officials in accordance with applicable law.

TCI does NOT use any third-party advertising network, nor share any information with any profiling companies. 

TCI gathers only the customer information that we need to administer our business, provide superior service, and communicate offers on merchandise and services if you choose to receive them.

TCI takes reasonable efforts to ensure that the customer information it maintains is accurate, timely, and secure. When credit card information is transmitted on-line, TCI uses industry-standard security measures called SSL (secure socket layer) encryption. Credit card information is also stored in encrypted form for your protection.

TCI values the trust its customers place in the company. If you have additional questions or would like further information on this topic, please feel free to e-mail us at [info@historyalive.com.](mailto:info@historyalive.com) TCI may update this policy from time to time. Please check the policy periodically for changes.
