##  Privacy Policy

At Tejji we recognize that privacy is important. This Privacy Policy applies to all of the services offered by website [www.Tejji.com](http://www.tejji.com/). 

###  Information we collect and how we use it

We offer a few services that do not require you to register for an account or provide any personal information to us, such as Typing Test. In order to provide our full range of services, we may collect the following types of information: 

  * **Information you provide** – When you sign up for a Tejji account, we ask you for personal information (such as your name, email address, account password etc.). 
  * **Cookies** – When you visit Tejji, we send one or more cookies – a small file containing a string of characters – to your computer or other device that uniquely identifies your browser. We use cookies to improve the quality of our service 
  * **Log information** – When you access Tejji  services, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website. 
  * **User communications** – When you send email or other communications to Tejji, we may retain those communications in order to process your inquiries, respond to your requests and improve our services. 



The personal information are used for following purposes

  * Ensuring the technical functioning of our network.
  * Developing new services. 
  * The information related to score is shared with other user of the Site.



The personal information may be processed in any part of the world.
