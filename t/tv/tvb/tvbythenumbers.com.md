# Privacy Policy - TV Ratings, Nielsen Ratings, Television Show Ratings | TVbytheNumbers.com

**Privacy Policy for TVbytheNumbers.com**

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at webmaster@tvbythenumbers.com.

At TVbytheNumbers.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by TVbytheNumbers.com and how it is used.

**Log Files**  
Like many other Web sites, TVbytheNumbers.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

**Cookies and Web Beacons**  
TVbytheNumbers.com does not use cookies.

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include Google Adsense and others.

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to determine the advertisements and links that appear on TVbytheNumbers.com send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on TVbytheNumbers.com .
  * Google’s use of the [DART cookie](http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/faq.aspx) enables it to serve ads to TVbytheNumbers.com users based on their visit to TVbytheNumbers.com and other sites on the Internet.
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the[ Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).



TVbytheNumbers.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. TVbytheNumbers.com’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.

Doubleclick/Boomerang tags

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. The third-party advertising technology that we use on this Web site uses information derived from your visits to this site to target advertising within this site. In addition, our advertisers may use other third-party advertising technology to target advertising on this site and other sites. In the course of advertisements being served to you, a unique third-party cookie or cookies may be placed your browser. In addition, we use Web beacons, provided by third-party advertising companies, to help manage and optimize our online advertising. Web beacons enable us to recognize a browser’s cookie when a browser visits this site, to learn which banner ads bring users to our Web site and to deliver more relevant advertising. [To “opt-out,” please click here.](http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/index.aspx)

NAI/ValueClick Media

Users may opt-out from ValueClick information collection and use, [by going to the NAI’s opt-out page here.](http://news.valueclickmedia.com/rd4/ck/13205-82055-17336-8?m=4-366&e=hbt3fbc1f57)

**Email addresses, URLs supplied by you while commenting**

When you post a comment at TVbytheNumbers.com you may supply an email address or a URL. In no case will anyone’s email address be shared (disclosed, given, sold or traded) with any third party. Any supplied URLs are publicly accessible to other visitors to TVbytheNumbers.com.
