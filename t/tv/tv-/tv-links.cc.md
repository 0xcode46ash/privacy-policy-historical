# TV Links - Privacy Policy

No personal information is collected or saved on this website.

Some non-personal information is however collected in order to show targeted ads. Such information may include the type of browser you use and whether you responded to the ad delivered. The non-personally identifiable information collected by tv-links.cc is for the purpose of targeting ads and measuring ad effectiveness on behalf of tv-links.cc advertisers. And none of these information is stored.
