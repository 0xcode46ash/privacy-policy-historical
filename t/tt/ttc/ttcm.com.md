# Privacy Policy

Please note that this privacy policy covers the website (hereafter the “Website”) operated by TTCM Traders Trust Capital Markets Limited in Cyprus (hereafter the “Company”).

Your privacy is important to us. The purpose of this policy is to describe what type of data will be collected and the method by which it will be collected from users of the Website, and to explain all the reasonable precautions taken by the Company to keep such data secure. By opening an account or by using the Website, users give the consent to the collection and the use of personal data/information by the Company as explained in this privacy policy. If you do not agree to this statement, do not proceed to further web pages of the Website.

**Data transfer over the Internet**  
  
Data sent via the Internet can be accessed by unauthorized third parties, potentially leading to disclosures, changes in content or technical failures. Data sent via the Internet may be transmitted across international borders even though both sender and receiver are located in the same country. Therefore, your data may be forwarded to a country with a lower data protection level than that which exists in your country of residence. The Company accepts no liability for the security of the data whilst in transit via the Internet to the Company.

**Collection and processing of data**  
  
The Company does not collect personally identifying data from the Website except when specifically and knowingly provided by the users. Personal data shall be held on the contact and administration databases of the Company, either as a result of the trading  carried out, or being carried out by the user, or because the Company may use, subject to any applicable foreign laws, personal data to market products and services it believes might be of interest to users.

The e-mail address of a user may be used by the Company in relation to its products and services. If users do not wish to receive such communication, please send an e-mail to the Company at the following e-mail address: [info@traders-trust.com](mailto:info@traders-trust.com?subject=From%20website).

The Company may also collect data that is statistical and that does not, by itself, identify a specific individual or entity and which depends upon your visiting the Website. For example, this data may include the domain from which you accessed the Website, the date and time you accessed the Website and the Internet address of the website from which you linked directly to the Website. This data is used in aggregate form for the compiling of statistical information and the monitoring of the usage and performance of the Website.

**Cookies**  
  
The Company uses cookies to collect information. A cookie is a small data file that a website stores on your computer’s hard disk for the purpose of keeping records when users visit the Website. The Company does not use cookies to retrieve information which was not originally sent in a cookie. The Company does not use information transferred through cookies for any direct promotional or marketing purposes.

**Personal data**

Users must inform the Company by e-mail, phone, fax or post if any personal data changes. Please find our contact details[ here](http://forex.progressivetechnologies.com.cy/en/html-10-Contact_info.html). 

**Data security**  
  
The Company follows strict security procedures in the storage of your personal data in order to prevent unauthorized access. However, the Website contains links to other websites. The Company does not control and is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these other websites. The Company shall take all reasonable steps in order to prevent your personal data being seen by any third parties other than those which have been engaged by the Company to provide services to you.

The Company does not sell, license, lease or otherwise disclose your personal data to any third party for any reason. The Company will only disclose your personal data to other third parties if compelled to do so for legal or regulatory purposes.

**Agents**  
  
The Company uses a card-processing company for deposits to a client’s account. This company does not retain, share, store or use personally identifying data for any other purposes.

**Update**  
  
The Company reserves the right to update/modify this privacy policy at any time without prior notice. Users are responsible for reviewing this privacy policy regularly.

This Privacy Policy was issued in March 2010.
