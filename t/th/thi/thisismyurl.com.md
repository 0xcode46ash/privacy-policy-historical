# Privacy Policy :: Christopher Ross

## Christopher Ross respects your privacy and security.

The web site is protected to ensure that your information is not lost, misused, or abused.

## Cookies

Small text files stored on your computer hard drive are known as Cookies, these files store a variety of information used regarding your interaction with the Christopher Ross (6084770 Canada Inc.) (here in after referred to as “The Company”) web site and the information collected is intended to enhance your web experience while visiting the site.

## Forms

The Company may collect information through user-submitted forms locate on our web site. Information such as contact details may be required in order for our company representatives to provide users with information. Any information voluntarily provided is used in the strictest confidence by The Company and will not be distributed to third parties except when required by law.

## Age of Consent

The The Company web site is not marketed to people under the legal age of majority. If you are not of legal age, please do not submit any information to The Company.

## Questions

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy statement or this web site, if you wish to review or correct any contact information you may have provided us, or if you wish to opt out of any future company mailings, [please contact Christopher Ross](http://www.thisismyurl.com/about/contact/).
