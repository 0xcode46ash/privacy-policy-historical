# My Privacy Statement

Your privacy is very important to me, in most cases I do not ask for your personal contact details and you are free to visit my web site without disclosing any information about yourself. There are times when I ask for information, these guidelines will help ensure you understand my policy towards this information.

I do not rent or sell, share or distribute any personal information about you in any way, shape or form with any person outside my office. If information you require involves a third party, I will always request your permission before distributing information about you.

In the case of minors, your parents permission will be required prior to sharing any information about you.

Your information will be shared with proper authorities where requested through official legal channels, in the case of illegal activities, suspected fraud or threats of violence towards any person or property.

Any corporation, business or property owned in whole or in part by me may access information collected on this site.

**Internet Technologies**  
Anonymous information known as metrics are collected and stored in our Web log files to help identify areas of my web site that need improvement.

In some cases, cookies are used to track various activities on the site or to help the site maintain information.
