**thingama job.com** is strongly committed to protecting the privacy of all visiting job seekers that post personal information on the site. We understand the sensitivity felt by job seekers when conducting a job search via the Internet and we want to provide a safe and secure environment. **thingama job.com** firmly believes in our job seeker's right to privacy and security; therefore we have implemented a privacy statement aimed at your protection.

**Personal Information**

**thingama job.com** collects personal information from its users on a voluntary basis. This personal information is strictly to facilitate the job searching process and help us find you a suitable job match.

**Disclosure**

**thingama job.com** requests certain personal information and identification. We will not under any circumstances sell this personal information to anyone, or distribute data to parties who are unrelated to the purposes of this site. We will not display or disclose any information without user consent, unless required to do so by law or to protect the rights and property of others. Though all users should know that when voluntarily displaying personal information (for example, phone numbers or e-mail addresses), that data can be collected by others. In these cases, **thingama job.com** is not responsible.

**Cookies…what are they?**

Technology referred to as cookies makes the web more useful by storing information about a user's preferences on a particular site. Cookies are used only to help web sites provide more useful information to its visitors. However, cookies cannot be used to personally identify a user, instead they can identify the user's computer.

**Questions**

If at any time, you have any questions or concerns about the **thingama job.com** site or our policy, please feel free to contact us at 1-877-42-THING (1-877-428-4464) or submit an [online help form](https://web.archive.org/web/20000619184346id_/http%3A//www.thingamajob.com/help_form.html).

Thank you again for visiting **thingama job.com**.
