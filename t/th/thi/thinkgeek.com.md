# ThinkGeek :: ThinkGeek :: Privacy Policy

ThinkGeek is committed to the privacy of our customers. ThinkGeek, Inc. is a wholly-owned subsidiary of SourceForge Inc. The following is an Overview of the Privacy Statement of ThinkGeek, Inc. and SourceForge Inc. 

## GEEKNET, INC. UNITED STATES/EUROPEAN UNION SAFE HARBOR PRIVACY STATEMENT ("PRIVACY STATEMENT") OVERVIEW ("OVERVIEW")

**(Last Updated July 29, 2010)**

### Scope

  * This Overview highlights the Geeknet, Inc. ("Geeknet") United States/European Union Safe Harbor Privacy Statement, found at: <http://geek.net/privacy-statement>. Geeknet, Inc. is the parent company of ThinkGeek, Inc.



### Information Collected 

  * When you use the Sites, SourceForge may ask you to provide certain personal information. Geeknet may also collect certain non-personal information concerning your use and activity related to the Sites.
  * The information Geeknet collects may be combined with information collected other Geeknet sites including without limitation [geek.com](http://www.geek.com/), [sourceforge.net](http://sourceforge.net/) and [slashdot.org](http://slashdot.org/).
  * Geeknet uses cookies and other technologies to keep track of your interactions with the site to offer a personalized experience.



### Uses of Information 

  * Geeknet uses the information it collects to provide the services requested by you. Geeknet services may include the display of personalized content and advertising.
  * Geeknet uses your information to inform you about the products or services offered by Geeknet and its subsidiaries and affiliates, and to send you relevant information related to Geeknet.
  * Geeknet does not sell, rent, or lease its customer lists to third parties. In order to help provide its services, it occasionally provides customer information to other companies that work on its behalf.



### Your Choices

  * You can choose to stop delivery of promotional e-mail from Geeknet by following the instructions included in each such email.
  * To view and edit your personal information, go to the [Data Integrity and Access section of the Privacy Statement](http://web.sourceforge.com/privacy-statement#data_inegrity_and_access).



### How to Contact Us

  * For more information about Geeknet privacy practices and its contact information, please see the [Privacy Statement](http://web.sourceforge.com/privacy-statement).



### Special Note to Users of [ThinkGeek.com](http://thinkgeek.com/)

  * Display names of [ThinkGeek.com](http://thinkgeek.com/) users are not publicly available. However, if you elect to post a comment to a blog or post in a forum on [ThinkGeek.com](http://thinkgeek.com/), then you will be assigned a nickname that will be publicly available.
