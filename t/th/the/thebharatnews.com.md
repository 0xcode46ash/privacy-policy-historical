# PRIVACY POLICY | The Bharat NewsThe Bharat News

thebharatnews.com is fully committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors to its website.

**Personal Information:**  
thebharatnews.com does not collect or save any personal information. thebharatnews.com does not use forms and all emails are completely deleted once they have been handled.  
We do not place cookies on the computers of visitors to our site.  
We will not disclose any personal information to any third party for any reason.  
We do not maintain any databases storing any personal information about our visitors.  
Since we do not maintain any personal information about our visitors, we will never send you any advertisements or unsolicited information about this site.

**Aggregate Information:**  
thebharatnews.com uses a web statistics program to track aggregate usage of this site. This program is used to enable us to improve and expand the site. It does not allow us to personally identify individual users to the site.  
thebharatnews.com does not provide any aggregate information to third parties concerning visitors to this site.
