# The People's Republic of Animation - Film, Advertising and Video Games - Privacy

Some information is automatically collected whenever the PRA internet site is accessed. Additional information may be voluntarily supplied - eg. through on-line forms and e-mails for specific service delivery purposes. PRA will only use such information collected for the purpose for which it was supplied and such information will not be disclosed to any third party unless required by law.
