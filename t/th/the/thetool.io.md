# Privacy Policy (english language) - TheTool

## Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the personal data you provide to Shutapp Projects, S.L. (hereinafter ‘Shutapp) through the website https://thetool.io/ (hereinafter, TheTool). By accepting this Privacy Policy you agree to the following.

All the personal data collected by Shutapp through Thetool shall be incorporated to a filing system owned by Shutapp for its processing, by automated means or not, according to the purposes described below:

a. Personal information given when signing up at TheTool will be used by Shutapp to manage the registration and enable the user to make use of the tools and functions available. They will also be used as contact form with the users.

b. Personal information provided by users to Shutapp through any contact form or any other means of contact (email addresses, telephone numbers, fax number, etc) published in TheTool will be used to properly manage their doubts, queries or suggestions.

By accepting this Privacy Policy you give Shutapp your consent to send you commercial communications by email, containing new services and relevant information related to Shutapp or the Website, unless you have explicitly object to receive such information. You may withdraw your consent at any time by informing Shutapp by e-mail at info@thetool.io

This Platform provides technology for implementing files called cookies in the equipment you use to navigate. The use of this technology may be blocked at any time by means of your browser setup options. If you want more information in this respect, you can access our [Cookie Policy](https://web.archive.org/cookies-en).

Please note that at any time you may exercise your rights of access, rectification, cancellation and challenge. To do this, it will suffice to write to us at Shutapp Projects S.L., Cl. Provença 314, principal 2, 08037 – Barcelona, or to contact us by e-mail at info@thetool.io. Please attach a copy of your Spanish Identity Card (DNI) or similar official identification document, to avoid unauthorised data access by third parties.
