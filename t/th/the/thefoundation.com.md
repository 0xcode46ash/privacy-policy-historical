# Digital Ambit Privacy Policy

### Collecting your Personal Information

We will only ask you for information that personally identifies you or allows us to contact you if you initiate a request to be contacted by Digital Ambit, LLC. In this capacity an email address will be the only information required. All other information is optional. This information will be used to provide you with the support or information you request.

### Use of Your Personal Information

The information collected will be used to complete the request initiated by you. Your personal information will be removed from our system within 48 hours of completing your information or support request.

### Newsletters and other Periodic Information

Digital Ambit, LLC does provide periodic email messages to patrons who have elected to recieve them. Electing to recieve our periodic information is by opt-in only. If you have chosen to opt-in to recieve periodic messages from us we will quickly and accurately honor all requests for removal and have made the process of unsubscribing as easy as is possible.

### Updates to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy at any time. If we do it will be to better protect your privacy. If we change our privacy policy we will update the modified time that is seen in the footer of this page.
