# Privacy

This Privacy Policy is made on behalf of [www.thehomepage.com.au](http://www.thehomepage.com.au/). All references to **"TheHomePage"** , **"we"** , **"us"** or **"our"** in this document are references to TheHomePage, ABN 26 472 874 961.    
This Privacy Policy governs personal information you may supply to us or the information that is being collected automatically from your web browser. By using this site ( **"the Site"** ) you agree to this Privacy Policy and you agree with collection, use and disclosure of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.   
We reserve the right to modify or remove some parts of the Privacy Policy at any time. 

  **Use of IP addresses**   
TheHomePage receives your IP address from your web browser. IP addresses are recorded in our server log files and database automatically. We use your IP address to resolve any problems with our server as well as for statistical information. 

  **Use of cookies**   
Cookies are small files stored in your computer's memory or hard disk.   
TheHomePage may set and access cookies on your computer.   
We use cookies to make your access to the Site easier. When you log in you can access pages on the Site without supplying your user name and password again, this is achieved by use of "session cookies". 

  **Information collected on Registration Forms**   
We collect personal information about you, such as: your e-mail address, title, name, phone numbers, mobile number and address when you fill out our forms. This information is collected in order for your contact details to be available to property vendors, property buyers or estate agents.   
We also use your email, IP and demographic information to tailor your experience at our site and to show you the content that we think you may be interested in and to display content according to your preferences. 

  **Links to other sites**   
The Site may contain links to other web sites and these sites may have a different privacy policy to TheHomePage. You should check their privacy policy before disclosing your personal information. TheHomePage is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such sites, nor are we responsible for the use or disclosure by others of information you provide to any external sites. 

  **Use of information**   
TheHomePage does not sell, rent or share your personal information with other persons or companies except to provide products and services you requested, when we have your permission.   
We will use your personal information for the purpose of improving the Site and services we provide to you. We may use your personal information to analyse your needs and site usage. We may also use it to contact you and deliver you information about our products and services that we think may interest you.   
By adding a vendors email address to the site, you agree to automatically subscribing them to our TheHomeFront e-newsletter. Your vendor is free to unsubscribe from this at their own accord.

  **Disclosure**   
Your personal information may be disclosed in special circumstances where it is required by law, or if we have reason to believe it is necessary to identify or bring legal action against anybody damaging, injuring or interfering.   
We may aggregate personal information and disclose this information in non-personally identifiable manner to third parties for marketing and other purposes.

  **Security**   
We take all reasonable steps to protect any information collected from your computer or provided by you. Whilst we make our best efforts to ensure your information is stored securely, TheHomePage will take no responsibility for any breach of security on the Site. 

  **Access to information**   
The information you provide to us is accessible by you through the Site. You will need to use your e-mail address and password to view or modify this information.   
You are solely responsible for accuracy of information you provide to us. You must ensure that your personal information is kept accurate and up to date. TheHomePage will not be responsible for any information you provide.

We may make changes to the Privacy Policy from time to time.
