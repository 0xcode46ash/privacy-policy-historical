# Privacy Policy

We at theNetFool.com are committed to respecting the online privacy of each of our site visitors. To this end, we have established this Privacy Policy in order that you understand the care with which we treat your personal information.

Latest Update: 11:49:00 PM EST, OCTOBER 15, 2007.

Any private information collected by theNetFool.com is used for navigation on the site or to improve the functionality of the site. At no time is any of this information shared with third parties.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail at jim@thenetfool.com.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact the webmaster at jim@thenetfool.com..
