# Real Estate Marketing - Contact Management - Transaction Management - The Wise Agent

At **The Wise Agent** , privacy and security are paramount: We are committed to protecting your individual privacy and your information; and we are committed to keeping our network secure from outside infiltration.

If you would like more information about The Wise Agent, or would like to register for our online services, please complete the registration process. This information will be sent to our corporate office by email. We store your private information securely and will not share it with any third parties. 

Upon termination of The Wise Agent's services, you may request to have us assist you in downloading your client information to Outlook or Outlook Express. We understand that your information is valuable to you. At that time, we will remove your information from our servers. 

When you email us we keep your email address and add it to our mailing list. From time to time, we will contact you with product updates. If you do not wish to receive such updates, please let us know in your email. All product update emails sent to you will have instructions on how to take yourself off our mailing list.
