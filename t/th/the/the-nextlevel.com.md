# TNL - Privacy Policy

  
****

**Registering:**

Any personal information which is recorded via TNL Forums is only requested so that persons can become registered members of our forum and thus participate with our gaming community. This information will only be used in conjunction with your TNL Forum account. No personal information will be disclosed to anyone without explicit permission.

* * *

  
****

**Tracker Data / Stats:**

TNL utilized an tracker under the service provision of Extreme Tracker which is located on our index homepage. The data collected is so that we can obtain information as to where our visitors are referred, and above all to inform us of the frequency of our daily traffic. However, in respects to the personal information such as individual names, email addresses, etc. are not tracked through Extreme Tracker.

**Third Parties:**

TNL works in conjunction with Future Games Network as an active affiliate which serves ads to our site. We also work in conjunction with Twin Galaxies via a link exchange program and Future Games, as an affiliate traffic incentive for TNL.
