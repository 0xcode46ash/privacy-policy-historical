# Blue Box Media | India's premier Online Media Company

## OUR PRIVACY POLICY 

### Please read this carefully before visiting our websites. 

## Last Updated : November 11th, 2014

Our Privacy Policy governs the manner in which we collect information regarding you, when you visit our media properties. It also governs various advertising schemes on our website and also as to how those advertisements may/may not interfere with your browsing experience.

1) We collect and receive information about you and your device when you give it to us directly, when you use our Services, and from certain third-party sources.

2) The information we collect and receive is used to provide our Services, to improve our Services, and to offer effective advertising, which helps us keep most of our Services free. Also, if you provide us with your details via our newsletter services, we use them to send you relevant news and headlines, so as to keep you updated.

3) Blue Box Media (or its media ventures) may share your personal information in limited circumstances, including when we have your consent to do so or when sharing is necessary to protect Blue Box Media (or its media ventures) or comply with the law. Our agents and contractors may have access to your information, but only to perform services for Blue Box Media (or its media ventures). We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may, however, share non-personally identifiable information with select business partners.

4) You can opt-out from our newsletter services by clicking on the "Unsubscribe" link beneath it. Your information will be permanently deleted from our servers within 24 hours of your request. You can sign back to our services again, by subscribing.

5) Advertisements displayed on our website (either via our own advertising service or via third party advertising service) are meant so that Blue Box Media (or its media ventures) may continue providing you free content on its website. Advertisements on our media properties are targeted, based upon your browsing experiences.

6) Hosting of Sponsored Content on our media ventures is extremely limited. However, in cases where a content is sponsored, we will duly mention the same, for your information.

7) For any discrepencies found within this Privacy Policy, you can address your concerns on pr(at)blueboxmedia.co with the subject line being "PRIVACY POLICY".

8) This Privacy Policy is subjected to changes, without any prior notice. However, you WILL be notified of the changes on all our media ventures.
