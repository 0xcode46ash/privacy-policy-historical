# Privacy Policy - Themeit - Free Themes

**The following is an outline of** **www.themeit.net** Privacy Policy.

•[General](http://www.themeit.net/general-commerce-shape5-joomla-template.html)

At **www.themeit.net** we know that your number one concern is that we meet [the](http://www.themeit.net/x-v3-0-1-wordpress-theme.html) security and privacy standards you expect. You should be aware of the following safety and privacy issues when you browse our website.

**What information is collected by www.themeit.net?**

Standard use of the Site

In general, you can browse the Site without telling us who you are or revealing any [personal](http://www.themeit.net/bliss-v3-0-1-themeforest-personal-minimalist-blog-theme.html) information. The only information we gather during a general navigation on the Site is the information intended [for](http://www.themeit.net/native-android-app-wordpress-site.html) the server logs: IP (Internet Protocol) address, domain name, browser type, operating [system](http://www.themeit.net/tour-operator-v3-3-wp-theme-reservation-system.html) and information such as the site from which you are connected, [pages](http://www.themeit.net/crea8social-v3-0-php-social-networking-platform.html) visited, and the dates and times of those visits.

[Collect](http://www.themeit.net/ideafeed-v2-0-0-interactive-user-feedback-system.html) of Personal Information

If you subscribe to a newsletter, log on, [request](http://www.themeit.net/woocommerce-request-quote-v1-21.html) information, send any comments or if you join a discussion group or electronic mailing list, you will need to provide some personal information : name and e-mail address, for example. Such information is collected only [with](http://www.themeit.net/tour-operator-v3-3-wp-theme-reservation-system.html) your permission and is stored in [different](http://www.themeit.net/landingsumo-20-landing-pages.html) databases and mailing lists belonging to www.themeit.net.

The registration of personal information leads to the submission of a cookie* on certain parts of www.themeit.net. By setting this cookie, www.themeit.net will remember your personal details at the next visit so you do not have to re-enter the same information. This helps us to improve the quality of services that we offer.

Participation in electronic discussion groups means that other participants in the group that you have chosen (including those who are not Site Administrators) will be aware of any personal information that you choose to disclose. In some open discussion groups the information is public.

**What does www.themeit.net do with the information it collects?**

Standard use of the Site

The information collected during the navigation in the “www.themeit.net” area is used to analyze trends and the use of the site in [order](http://www.themeit.net/tour-operator-v3-8-wp-theme-reservation-system.html) to improve its usefulness. No personal information is attached there.

Personal Information

www.themeit.net may use such information to:

  * Contact you – either in response to a request or a suggestion, or to send you [news](http://www.themeit.net/voice-v1-2-1-clean-newsmagazine-wordpress-theme.html), documents, publications, and so on;
  * Confirm your entries on the site;

  * “Remember” your online [profile](http://www.themeit.net/personal-best-blog-cv-video-wordpress-theme.html) and preferences;

  * Statistical analysis performing.




**What will happen if I do not want to provide personal information?**

Providing personal information on the Site is optional. If you do not want to deliver information of this kind, you can always surf the Site, but you will not be able to access the entire [Service](http://www.themeit.net/lawbusiness-attorney-lawyer-wordpress-theme.html) and its Information.

Removal or modification of information

You can delete or modify your personal information at any [time](http://www.themeit.net/time-v2-6-3-themeforest-responsive-wordpress-theme.html) by returning to the [page](http://www.themeit.net/view-html5-responsive-single-page-template.html) where you registered them or by contacting the coordinator of the page. If the page in question does not provide any information on this subject, you can contact privacy(at)themeit(dot)net for more information.

If you are not the issuer of personal information that concerns you and want to have it removed, you can send your request to privacy(at)themeit(dot)net

Security

The personal data entrusted to the Site will not be sold or transferred to any third [party](http://www.themeit.net/soundwave-v1-9-music-vibe-wordpress-theme.html). Any information provided to www.themeit.net by [users](http://www.themeit.net/ideafeed-v2-0-0-interactive-user-feedback-system.html) of the Site is preserved with the utmost [care](http://www.themeit.net/niory-one-page-responsive-joomla-template.html) and maximum safety. It will be used only in the manner described in this privacy policy, or according to the special rules of visited sites or in a manner to which you have explicitly consented.www.themeit.net uses techniques and a variety of measures to ensure the safety of information stored in its systems and protects them from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

All our volunteers who have access to personal information or are associated with the processing of [these](http://www.themeit.net/landingsumo-20-landing-pages.html) data, are obliged to respect the confidentiality of every [user](http://www.themeit.net/ideafeed-v2-0-0-interactive-user-feedback-system.html) of the Site, which covers the personal information.

The Site provides links to sites outside the “www.themeit.net” area and could not be held responsible for the privacy practices or the content located on them.

Modifications

Any modification of these terms can be made at any time without prior publication or announcement.
