# Privacy Policy - Theater Seat Store

**WE ARE COMMITTED TO YOUR PRIVACY!**

 **Your Personal Information**

Neither TheaterSeatStore.com or InteriorMark, LLC (its parent) will ever sell or rent your information. Nor will we share it in ways other that which is disclosed in this statement without your express consent.

 **Collected Information**

Through the use of order form, we collect your contact information, including, but not limited to, addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, and financial information. This information is used solely for our purposes and we may use it for sales, billing and contact purposes and may send information about your orders, about our company, and additional promotional material from some of our partners. We use your IP address to help prevent fraud, diagnose server problems, and to administer our TheaterSeatStore.com. We use cookies (including session cookies) to maintain integrity in your shopping cart as you travel from page to page on TheaterSeatStore.com.

Demographic and profile data is also collected and is used to tailor your experience and to show you what we think you might be interested in. This information is an asset of InteriorMark and may be transferred amongst the divisions for this purpose.

 **Additional Disclosures**

We may disclose personal information when required by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law, or to comply with a legal process served on our Web site.
