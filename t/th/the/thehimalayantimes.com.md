  |  **PRIVACY POLICY**

**What we know about you:**   
The Himalayan Times takes the privacy of its users seriously. We are committed  
to safeguarding the privacy of our users while providing a personalised and  
valuable service. This Privacy Policy Statement explains the data processing  
practices of [thehimalayantimes.com](http://www.thehimalayantimes.com/index.asp). 

  
| **Information collected:** |    
---|---  
  
| Personal information is collected by The Himalayan Times through the use  
of enquiry and registration forms and every time you e-mail us your details. |    
  
  


  
| We also collect information automatically about your visit to our site.  
The information obtained in this way, which includes demographic data  
and browsing patterns, is only used in aggregate form. |    
  
**Use and Disclosure of Personal Information:  
We process personal information collected via thehimalayantimes.com  
for the purposes of:**  
---  
  
| providing a personalised service  
  
| conducting market research surveys  
  
| running competitions and  
  
| providing you with information about products and services we offer.   
  
| to build up marketing profiles  
  
| to aid strategic development  
  
| to manage our relationship with advertisers.  
  
  
We would be more than delighted to receive your comments, views  
and ideas. Help us to improve ourselves.
