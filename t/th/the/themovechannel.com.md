# Privacy Policy for themovechannel.com

We are fully committed to protecting the privacy of our users while providing a safe, secure user experience. Any information collected by us and/or any other organisation on this site, will be only that which is specifically required in order to facilitate any transaction or information you have requested, with additional optional information being collected only for the purpose of our building a profile of our users and promoting our own services to our those of our users that have opted for us to do so. We will not supply your personal details to any other person or organisation unless expressly authorised by yourself.

By using this site you are deemed to have given consent for any personal details, submitted in the ways described below, to be used in those ways indicated. 

**User registration**

Most of this site can be accessed without registering. However, there are times when we need to identify you in order to enable the correct fulfilment of a particular service. Such instances include making, managing and tracking enquiries you make regarding properties on this site, allowing you to manage your profile, email subscriptions and other personalisation settings, or using forms that can be pre-populated with your details to make them easier to use. 

We are fully committed to protecting the privacy of our users while providing a safe, secure user experience. Any information collected by us and/or any other organisation on this site, will be only that which is specifically required in order to facilitate any service or transaction. We will only supply your personal details to any other person or organisation when this is required to carry out a service requested by yourself. A full written record of all data we have stored about you is available on request.

We are registered for Data Protection - Registration number Z4966058.

**Opting out**  
Should you not wish to receive the delivery of information subsequent to registration, please opt out either at registration or by updating your personal login area. Such information includes notice of site upgrades, new products, features and relevant products. 

**Cookies**  
As part of offering and providing customisable and personalised services, TheMoveChannel.com uses cookies to store and sometimes track information about you and to store your preferences. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. All areas on TheMoveChannel.com where you are prompted to log-in or that are customisable ask that you accept cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but if you prefer, you can edit your browser options to block them in the future.
