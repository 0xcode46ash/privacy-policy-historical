# The Deadbolt - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

* * *

**TheDeadbolt.com Privacy Policy**

To publicly display our strict policy and firm commitment regarding privacy, TheDeadbolt.com has created a statement that discloses the following information with regard to protecting your privacy, information gathering, and dissemination practices of TheDeadbolt.com.

**IP Logging:**

Individual IP addresses are automatically logged at the time of visit. We use your IP addresses to execute and administer the website. Should an issue ever arise regarding the identification of a user AFTER a problem occurs, IP addresses are only used to correct or solve the problem. We do not use IP addresses for any other means beyond problem solving except for statistical analysis.

**Third Party Visitor Statistics:**

TheDeadbolt.com uses a free third-party web counter service provided by StatCounter.com to analyze visitor behavior and daily, weekly, and yearly traffic trends. Statistics collected by StatCounter include browser type, OS, country, host IP, visit length, and screen resolution. Also, as stated in the Privacy Policy at Statcounter.com, no effort is made to identify individuals without their knowledge, and information is only collected for statistical purposes only.

**Third Party Cookies:**

During the process of statistical analysis, our free third-party web counter service, Statcounter.com, sends a cookie to your computer. This information is collected solely to improve the services of Statcounter.com and for statistical purposes. As stated in the Privacy Policy at Statcounter.com, the information is not used to identify individuals and StatCounter.com maintains a strict "no-spam" policy. Your e-mail address will not be sold to a third-party by either TheDeadbolt.com or Statcounter.com.

**Advertising:**

TheDeadbolt.com may serve advertisements to this site or work with a third party to serve the advertisements. Advertisements placed on the TheDeadbolt.com website may include but are not limited to banner ads, paid links, pop-up windows, pop-under windows, buttons, and sponsorships.

Users of TheDeadbolt.com and readers of TheDeadbolt.com agree that TheDeadbolt.com has the right to run such advertisements and promotions. Your correspondence or business dealing with, or participation in promotions of, advertisers found on or through the Services, including payment and delivery of related goods or services, and any other representations associated with such dealings, are solely between you and such advertiser. You agree that TheDeadbolt.com shall not be responsible or liable for any loss or damage of any sort incurred as the result of any such dealings or as the result of the presence of such advertisers on the Services.

The Deadbolt has a third-party advertising account and relationship with Google and its AdSense program. At any time TheDeadbolt.com has the right to temporarily suspend its participation in the Google Adsense program in which you may or may not see Google Adsense ads on the site. By visiting TheDeadbolt.com, and any pages within TheDeadbolt.com containing Google AdSense advertising, you agree to, accept, and understand the following:

* Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on TheDeadbolt.com. 
* Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users of TheDeadbolt.com based on their visit to TheDeadbolt.com and other sites on the Internet. 
* Users of TheDeadbolt.com may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html). 

ZEDO is a 3rd party ad serving system that TheDeadbolt.com uses to deliver ads. You agree to, accept, and understand that ZEDO, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on TheDeadbolt.com and that ZEDO's use of cookies enables it to serve ads to users and readers of TheDeadbolt.com and other websites on the internet. In accordance with ZEDO's Privacy Policy, you agree to, accept, and understand that ZEDO uses cookies to enhance the Web experience by preventing users from seeing the same Internet ad over and over again. ZEDO does not use cookies to personally identify you in any way, and these cookies cannot damage your system or files. You can tell your browser to accept or reject cookies for all sites, or for specific sites; but remember, rejecting cookies may affect the way your favorite sites behave.

You agree to and accept that TheDeadbolt.com shall not be responsible for the security settings and/or privacy settings of any computer or device of any user or reader of TheDeadbolt.com, and you agree to and accept that The Deadbolt.com shall not be liable for any unwanted or unexpected behavior, which includes but is not limited to any initiated downloads to the user's computer of any kind, from ZEDO ads on TheDeadbolt.com. You agree to and accept that TheDeadbolt.com shall not be responsible in any way for loss, damage, support of any sort incurred as the result of the presence of ZEDO's 3rd party ad serving system and delivery of ads to TheDeadbolt.com or any advertisers on the Services.

If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by ZEDO, and to know the ZEDO Privacy Policy, click here for information on the [ZEDO Opt Out](http://www.zedo.com/company/optout.htm) and here for the [ZEDO Privacy Policy](http://www.zedo.com/company/privacy.htm).

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/).

**Personal Information:**

TheDeadbolt.com does not gather, store, or collect personal contact information. Personal contact information sent from a visitor of TheDeadbolt.com to TheDeadbolt.com, whether by e-mail, written letter, verbal communication, or any other method of communication, will not be disclosed to any other third party. The visitor's contact information may be used to respond to that particular visitor after a question, comment, or request, and may be used to contact the visitor when necessary.

At any time, in relation to contacting TheDeadbolt.com, visitors may choose to stop any and all correspondence with TheDeadbolt.com. At all times, TheDeadbolt.com gives readers the opportunity to stop receiving any and all correspondence or communication with TheDeadbolt.com.

TheDeadbolt.com contains external links to other websites. TheDeadbolt.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of any other website.

**Security:**

Security measures are in place and in effect at TheDeadbolt.com to ensure the protection of all data and the possible incorrect or inappropriate use, modification, or loss of any content on the website. TheDeadbolt.com takes every precaution to ensure that all personal information is properly protected and is kept secure against misuse, improper modification, or loss. Access to all personal information is password protected.
