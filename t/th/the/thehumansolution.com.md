#### We Care About Your Privacy

We know how important it is to keep your personal information confidential when shopping online, and are committed to protecting your privacy. The Privacy Policy outlined below informs you of what information we collect when you visit and shop at [The Human Solution](http://www.thehumansolution.com/), and how we use that information.

  


#### General

We take your privacy seriously. The information you provide to us is kept confidential and is used only for processing your order. We will never share your details with any other third-party entity unless it relates to providing you with the products or services you requested from us. We may use your email address to notify you about new products, special offers, or events, but only if you have specifically opted in for these messages.

  


#### Customer Information Collected by SquareGrove, LLC - The Human Solution

We store information you provide to us when shopping or ordering, including name, phone number, email address, and billing and shipping addresses. We use your information to process and prepare your order for delivery, to provide you with the products or services you requested, and to communicate with you if we have questions about your order. We do not sell information to any third party.

  


#### Cookies and IP Addresses

We may use your IP address to gather general demographic information and website statistics. We use browser cookies to deliver remarketing advertisements online and to gain information about customers' shopping experiences. You may adjust your browser to disable cookies, in which case our site will be unable to recognize your cart or your account, if you choose to register an account. You can always shop and purchase as a guest, without signing up for an account.

  


#### Security of Your Personal Information

We use Secure Sockets Layer ("SSL") for all financial transactions through our site, as well as SSL encryption to protect your personal details. While we make every effort to protect you, please note that it is impossible to guarantee 100% complete security, whether shopping online or offline.
