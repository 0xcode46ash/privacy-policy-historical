# Welcome to the Boston Phoenix- Boston's Alternative Source for Arts and Entertainment

**Privacy Policy**

* * *

We at thephoenix.com are committed to protecting the privacy of any personal information collected about you as we bring you information tailored to your individual needs and interests. We have established the following Privacy Policy with the goal of balancing our legitimate business interests in collecting and using information and your reasonable expectations of privacy.

We do not monitor individual traffic patterns on our site -- only aggregate reader behavior is collected.

We cannot and will not release any identifying information or personal data about you to anyone else without your consent.

We do use third party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements on this and other sites about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please [click here](http://www.sonar.com/naiprivacy).

Third-party cookies  
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.
