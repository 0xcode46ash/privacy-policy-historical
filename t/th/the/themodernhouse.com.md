# Privacy Policy | The Modern House

To ensure that your privacy is protected, we adhere to the following policy:

  * In order to respond to your requirements, when you register with this website or our office, we will need to know your name, email address, postal address and telephone number.
  * We will not sell, rent, or otherwise provide any information you give to us to any third party except as necessary to complete the business between us.
  * If at any time you wish your personal data to be amended or removed from our records, please follow the “Unsubscribe” link at the bottom of the email, or email us at: info@themodernhouse.com.
  * We will take all reasonable care to ensure that personal details are kept secure. Any personal information we gather about you through this website will not be passed to any third party or organisation.
  * Our website uses cookies to compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future. You can choose to not receive a cookie file by enabling your web browser to refuse cookies or to prompt you before you accept a cookie.
  * By submitting your information to us, you consent to its use, as set out in this policy. If at any time we change our privacy policy, such changes will be made on this page.
