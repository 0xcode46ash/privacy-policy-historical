B. PRIVACY POLICY 

Your privacy and the integrity of any information you provide us are important to us. As a result, we have created the following policy, developed pursuant to the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act, 2000 c.5. 

THE SCOPE OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY 

This policy applies to the THECOMEDYNETWORK.CA website only. It is designed to inform you of how and why TCN gathers personal and non-personal information about you. 

This website is connected by "hyperlinks" to other websites. We are not responsible in any way for the privacy practices on other websites and suggest that you review the privacy policies on those linked websites before using them. 

WE ARE ACCOUNTABLE TO YOU 

We are accountable for our privacy practices on this website. If you have any questions or concerns regarding the privacy practices on this website, please address them, in writing to TCN's e-mail address. 

OUR INFORMATION PRACTICES 

We cannot collect personally identifiable information from you without your consent. By voluntarily providing this personal information to us at various points when using our website, including, signing up for e-mail reminders, sending us a comment or suggestion, subscribing to our newsletter, using our message board or bulletin board, entering a contest or , submitting a joke, cartoon or radio clip or responding to our requests for your submissions of short stories, tabloids or newletter articles, you will be consenting to the collection, use and distribution of your personal information by TCN. TCN will collect, use and distribute information for purposes of running the contest, and as well as, distribution to sponsors which manage the contest for purposes of managing the contest and marketing (for example the sponsor directly contacting the contestant to promote products and services ) and distribution to third parties who may participate in the chatrooms and bulletin boards on the website as set out below. Additionally, T third parties collect aggregate and anonymous personal information and TCN have has access and use of that data. You are under no obligation to provide us with this information and can access many aspects of the website without providing us any personal information. By volunteering this personal information you are also agreeing to its collection, use and distribution for the purposes stated below. 

THE PURPOSE OF OUR COLLECTING INFORMATION FROM YOU 

We will use the personal information collected in the following ways: 

* To contact you if you have entered a contest.   
* To keep aggregate information on demographics and website use.   
* For web site development.   
* To, at your option, send you a newsletter.   
* To, at your option, send you e-mail reminders.   
* To respond to your comments or concerns.   
* To select you as contestant winners. 




We will not sell personal information, nor will we distribute it to third parties. By entering contests and bulletin boards or chatrooms, please be advised that you will have consented to the sharing of your personal information with co-sponsors of any contest and other third party participants in the bulletin board or chatroom. We cannot control what these third parties in the bulletin board or chatroom do with your personal information 

Personally identifiable information, or personal information, is information that identifies you, as an individual, and includes things such as your, home address, home telephone number, and e-mail address. 

If you do decide to sign up for our newsletter there is a registration form you can fill out. If you decide to register for this newsletter you will need to provide us with the following information: your name, your postal code/zip code, your e-mail address and your internet connection speed. 

At your option, there are further fields on the registration form to complete that request more personal information about you, such as your address. 

In addition, TCN collects non-personally identifiable information, which is information that does not identify you personally, but becomes available to us as a result of your visit to our website. This information includes: 

  
* Your IP address, a number automatically assigned your computer when you visit the website. Our server logs your IP address.   
* Your Internet Service Provider (ISP).   
* Your Web Browser. 


One way we use this non-personal information is for studying aggregate demographics to aid us in our web site development. 

We also use cookies on this website. Cookies are small text files that your web browser leaves on your hard drive to recognize you as a repeat user of our website, track your use of our website and target advertising. This allows for personalization of certain aspects of your visit to our site. If you do not want cookies to be used, you should access the features of your web browser for the procedure to disable the cookies. 

We may collect personal and non-personal information in a non-identifying and aggregate form to enhance our website design and to share with advertisers and for statistical purposes. For example, we may share with an advertiser information that a certain number of individuals visited a certain area on our website or that a certain number of individuals from a specific city have registered on our website, but we would not divulge to them any information that could be used to identify you as an individual. This means that we will share aggregated demographic information with our partners and advertisers. This aggregate information is not linked to any personally identifiable information. 

OPTING OUT OR MODIFYING YOUR INFORMATION: 

You can subscribe to our newsletter. However, there will be instruction on how to unsubscribe from the newsletter on our website. 

SAFEGUARDS WE HAVE IN PLACE 

We understand that personal information over the internet is particularly vulnerable to unauthorized access, and to use or destruction of personal information. To this end, TCN employs reasonable managerial and technical measures to ensure that your information is secure. For example, all personally identifiable information is maintained behind a firewall.
