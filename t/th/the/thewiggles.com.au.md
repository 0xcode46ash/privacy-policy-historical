# The Wiggles Website Privacy Notice

|  | 

  
THE WIGGLES WEBSITE PRIVACY NOTICE

1\. GENERAL POLICY  
The Wiggles respect the privacy of individuals. Generally, The Wiggles do not release personal information. However in response to a legal requirement, in the case of an emergency or in exceptional circumstances a General Manager may at his or her discretion authorise the release of the personal information. In other situations the collection, use and disclosure of personal information is governed by The Wiggles’ privacy policy, which can be found at www.thewiggles.com.au.  
Our primary concern is to protect the interests of the children who are our audience and for this reason we monitor visitors to our website.  
2\. COLLECTION   
**2.1 Information gathered by this site’s technology**  
The Wiggles may make a record of your visit and log your visit for statistical and business purposes. Such information includes your server address, domain name, IP address, the date and time of your visit, pages accessed, documents downloaded, previous site visited and the type of browser used.  
**2.2 Email**  
If you send us an email message we will record your email address. We will only use your email address for the purpose for which it was provided. We will not use your email address for any other purpose (including adding it to any mailing list) without your express approval.  
**2.3 Information provided to The Wiggles**  
If we require you to complete an electronic form on this site and in doing so it requires you to enter personal information, we will collect that information to allow us to respond to your request. We will not collect personal information that would be unlawful, unnecessary or unrelated to The Wiggles’ activities or business.  
**2.4 Cookies**  
Cookies are small files of information that save and retrieve information about your visit to our site. If you do not wish information to be gathered on a cookie it is possible to disable the acceptance of cookies by your web browser. If you want to do this you should contact your ISP.  
If this site uses a cookie to deliver contents we think will interest you, the cookie may also be used for authentification.  
**2.5 Links**  
If this site contains a link to another site, we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such other sites.  
**3\. USE**  
Personal information that you supply through this site will only be used for the purpose for which you have provided it and to assist us to conduct our business. This may include analysing information obtained on the site to determine the effectiveness of the site and to modify the site to improve the service that it provides.  
**4\. DISCLOSURE**  
We will not disclose your personal information to a third party except in accordance with this notice and The Wiggles’ privacy policy, which can be found at www.thewiggles.com.au.
