# TheUseful.com - Shop Smarter - Web Directory - Cash Prizes!

**Our commitment to your privacy**

We respect your desire for Internet security and privacy, and have taken measures to protect your personal information. You can be assured that we take every measure to create a safe and enjoyable experience at TheUseful.com. This privacy statement discloses what information we gather and how it is used. You can always correct, change or update your records at any time in the member account center.

Our membership registration process requires that you provide us with basic contact information such as name, e-mail, address and birthdate. Please be assured that none of the personal information you provide us will be shared with any external third-parties unless we have your consent. Your personal information will be used only to help us provide a better, more relevant experience.

Our site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us. We allow you to control the e-mail that you receive from us. You may select or deselect the optional e-mail from us at anytime in the member account center.

In addition, our site provides its members the opportunity to opt-in or opt-out of receiving communications from third-party sponsors. Again, we allow you to control the e-mail you receive from any third-party sponsor featured on the site. You may select or deselect the optional e-mail from third-party sponsors in the member account center.

If you request certain TheUseful.com services that are co-branded or require work performed by one of TheUseful.com's trusted partners, we do share membership information with our trusted content and commerce partners in order to fulfill your request and to provide you with the best possible member experience. Also, in the unlikely event that TheUseful.com or any of its assets are acquired by or merged with another entity, member information will be one of the transferred assets.

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. We use cookies to allow you to visit multiple pages of our site without having to login at every page. These cookies are not linked to any personal information you've provided.

Our site contains links to other sites. Please note, however, that these other sites may have privacy policies that differ from those of TheUseful.com. We have no control over any personal information you choose to provide those sites. We provide the addresses of these web sites as an avenue of information for our visitors and are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these web sites.

By using this site, you agree to the TheUseful.com Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use our site. We reserve the right, at our discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for changes. Your continued use of the TheUseful.com site following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes.

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with TheUseful.com, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/help.htm?topic=contact).
