Privacy Statement Protecting your privacy is important to Eastern States Exposition. This policy will help you to understand how we collect, use and safeguard the information you provide on our website. Information Collection Any browsing on our website is done anonymously. We do not collect personal information. You may voluntarily supply personal information, such as an E-mail, or postal address, when requesting correspondence or publications from Eastern States Exposition. We log your IP address (the Internet address of your computer) and use it to look up the host name associated with the IP. This lets us know the areas of our website that you visit, how long you spend at each location, and what organizations are using our website. We do not link your IP address to anything identifiable. The only other information automatically provided to us is the operating system and platform you are using in addition to other information the browser you use is programmed to send. Your browser supplies us with this information. Information Use Eastern States Exposition does not share, sell or use information outside of the company. The information that you supply voluntarily is used to disseminate statistical information about Eastern States Exposition and The Big E.
