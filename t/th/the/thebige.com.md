# The Big E - Privacy Policy

**Our Commitment To Privacy**

Eastern States Exposition strongly values the privacy of our customers. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make a link to it available on our homepage and most other pages of our website.

**Ad Re-Targeting**

As you browse www.thebige.com, advertising cookies will be placed on your computer so that we can understand what you are interested in. Our display advertising partners, [AdRoll](https://app.adroll.com/account/register?r=RC6KVYLFSVGHTPMYMXLPBU) & [Perfect Audience](https://www.perfectaudience.com/?ref=3f7d342a), then enable us to present you with re-targeting advertising on other sites based on your previous interaction with www.thebige.com. The techniques our partners employ do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address or telephone number. You can visit this [page](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/) to opt out of AdRoll and their partnersâ€™ targeted advertising. To opt-out of Perfect Audiences tracking please visit the [Perfect Audience Privacy Policy Page](http://www.perfectaudience.com/privacy/?ref=3f7d342a) and select the desired option(s) available to you.

**Why we collect this information**

Eastern States Exposition collects the above mentioned information to better serve our customers, track and improve our marketing performance, and to prevent fraud and abuse.

**How to delete or modify your information**

If you wish to delete or modify your information you may contact us and or delete cookies in your browser and or disable cookie tracking in your browser. Please note disabling cookies may impair your ability to use this website and other websites as intended.
