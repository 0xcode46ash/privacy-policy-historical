# The Scene

## Privacy Policy

TheScene respects your privacy and has instituted this Privacy Policy to protect you. Please read on to learn how we collect and use information obtained from this web site or from the text messages you send and receive to opt-in for services.

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy or the Terms and Conditions, feel free to contact us at [support@thescene.com](mailto:support@thescene.com)

Users of TheScene acknowledge that:

  1. TheScene messages are made available to all other members of group and are viewable both on individual mobile devices as well as TheScene.
  2. TheScene messages are logged indefinitely on TheScene servers for redundancy and back up
  3. TheScene public group messages may be read by any Internet user who logs on to TheScene.
  4. Januzian Interactive reserves the right to rent user lists to marketers to conduct mobile marketing campaigns. We do not sell numbers and will not disclose personal information, but responding to third party campaigns by opting-in, purchasing content or sharing information may result in exposing your mobile number and other personal information to third parites.
  5. TheScene makes every effort to keep user data private, and Januzian Interactive personnel does not read messages or use message content for any purpose whatsoever.



If you would like to learn more about the third party's use of this information, or to opt-out from such use, please go to [www.privacychoices.org](http://www.privacychoices.org/).

WHEN AND HOW DOES THESCENE NETWORKS COLLECT PERSONAL INFORMATION?

Registration Personally identifying information is any information that can be used to identify and contact you, including your e-mail address, full name, or telephone number (what we call your "Personal Information").

Your cell phone number is used only for the purposes of delivering those TheScene services to which you have opted-in.

Cookies When you are using the TheScene web site, in order to provide you with thorough and personalized service, TheScene Networks uses "cookies" to keep and occasionally track information about you. A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. TheScene sometimes uses cookies to track information for our users' convenience and to understand our customers better. The cookies that are gathered by TheScene are only used by TheScene. At no time do we share information from cookies with third parties.

Children Children should not submit any Personal Information without the permission of their parents or guardians. TheScene encourages all parents or guardians to instruct their children in the safe and responsible use of their Personal Information while using the Internet. TheScene will not allow children under the age of thirteen (13) to use the web site. By clicking the "accept" button, you are representing that you are at least 18, or that you are at least 13 years old and have your parents' permission to register for TheScene.

When Required by Law TheScene releases Personal Information and/or General Information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with laws; enforce or apply our Terms and Conditions and other agreements; or protect the rights, property, or safety of TheScene, our users, or others.

Aggregate Data When you access TheScene-enabled web sites, our server automatically collects certain information that is not personally identifiable, such as your IP address, pages viewed, and length of time spent on the web site. We collect and use this information, in the aggregate only, to diagnose problems with our server, to improve our sites, and to provide content of interest to you. We also aggregate data about our users and their use of the TheScene services during user sessions.

Opting-Out / Turn Off Message Delivery In the event that you elect to no longer receive TheScene services, you may always opt-out. Additionally, users on most major carriers can reply ".q" to any TheScene-enabled message to opt-out. If users encounter difficulty, they can also write to [support@thescene.com](mailto:support@thescene.com).

CONSENT TO PRIVACY POLICY By using TheScene-enabled services, you consent to the collection and use of information about you in the ways described in this Privacy Policy. TheScene reserves the right to update and amend this Privacy Policy from time to time to remain consistent with new products, services, processes, and Internet privacy legislation. We will post changes to the Privacy Policy on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. The amended Privacy Policy shall automatically be effective five (5) days after they are initially posted on the web site. Your continued use of the services after the effective date of any posted change constitutes your acceptance of the amended Privacy Policy as modified by the posted changes.
