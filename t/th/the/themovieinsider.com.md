# The Movie Insider (.com) Privacy Policy

1\. ACCEPTANCE OF POLICIES

The Movie Insider (.com) has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: The Movie Insider (.com).

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information. We use cookies to save your password on our message board (Forums) so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site.

This site contains links to other sites. The Movie Insider (.com) is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

2\. THIRD PARTY ADVERTISING 

The ads appearing on this Web site are delivered to you by our Web advertising partner. Information about your visit to this site, such as number of times you have viewed an ad (but not your name, address, or other personal information), is used to serve ads to you on this site. 

3\. THIRD PARTY COOKIES

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser. For more information, please [click here](http://www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy/). 

4\. PUBLIC FORUMS

This site makes forums/message boards available to its users. Users under 13 years of age must get parental consent before using any of these. Warnings are given. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

5\. SECURITY

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. With the exception of the information gathered on the The Movie Insider (.com) Forums, all e-mail addresses recorded by forms on The Movie Insider (.com) Forums is stored where it can not be viewed from the web.

6\. CHOICE/OPT-IN

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about the visitor.

This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service.

  1. E-mail to [contact@themovieinsider.com](mailto:contact@themovieinsider.com)



7\. CORRECT/UPDATES

This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided.

  1. E-mail to [contact@themovieinsider.com](mailto:contact@themovieinsider.com)



8\. CONTACTING THE WEBSITE

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact:   Brian Renner [contact@themovieinsider.com](mailto:contact@themovieinsider.com)   
For telephone or address contact, please send a request by email.
