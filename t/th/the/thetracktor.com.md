# The Tracktor - Amazon Price Tracker

TheTracktor.com takes personal privacy very seriously. Our privacy policy is simple. TheTracktor will not provide, rent, or make available in any way, any customer information to any other parties. Period.

To specifically address concerns of our users, we ensure the following: 

  * Customer e-mail addresses are solely used to send notification updates regarding products they have chosen to track.
  * All user information is stored in a secure data environment.
  * No personally identifiable information is collected on visitors to this site.
