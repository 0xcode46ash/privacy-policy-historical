# The Tracktor - Amazon Price Tracker

### Privacy Policy

The Tracktor takes personal privacy very seriously. Our privacy policy is simple. The Tracktor will not provide, rent, or make available in any way, any contact information of our users to any other parties. Period.

To specifically address concerns of our users, we ensure the following: 

  * Customer e-mail addresses are used solely for price notifications and if desired, our newsletter. 
  * All user information is stored in a secure data environment.
  * No personally identifiable information is collected on visitors to this site.
