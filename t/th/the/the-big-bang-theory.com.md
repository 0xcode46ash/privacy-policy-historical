# The Big Bang Theory Privacy Policy

### Privacy Policy

What we collect:

The Big Bang Theory Site, like most sites, collects log files. These log files hold **non-personal information** such as:

-IP Address.  
-Technical Information: Your browser, operating system, screen resolution, Javascript settings, Flash settings.   
-Geo-location data. This does not reveal your exact location.  
-List of all requests made to server for pages, multimedia & other file-Other analytic data may also be collected, such as the length of your stay on the site and whether you have previously visited the site before.

The Big Bang Theory Site, like many sites with forums, will collect the following **potentially personal information,** if, and only if, you provide it to us. If you do not register on our forums, or use the comment or contact system then your data won't be collected.

-User name. This may be a pseudonym, or your real name.   
-E-mail Address. The forums require an accurate e-mail address.   
-Age. The user is required to provide an accurate age.   
-Optional information such as interests, location for the purposes of forum profiles. This information is publicly shown.

What do with it:

Log files are used to analyze traffic to this website, this helps us fix problems as well as know more about our visitors. The information in our log files, which isn't personal information, are shared with a few select companies.

Personal information, such as your e-mail address, is not shared with anybody outside of this site.

Who do we share information with?

We share the anonymous data with Google Analytics, Google Adsense & ValueClickMedia.

We do not share personal information with anybody, except when required to by law.

Where is the data held?

All data submitted on this site is held on our server in the United States of America.

**By using this site, you consent for your data to be stored & processed in the United States of America where the data protection laws may differ from your country.**

How long is it held for?

We delete anonymous log files within 6 months. Information you submit to our forums or comment system isn't deleted unless you specifically request it be.

Do you use cookies?

Yes, this site uses cookies for a few things. We have "anonymous" cookies which are used by the website script to know small things like which country you are visiting from, so we can serve local content, whether you've visited before, whether you've voted on a poll or commented on a story. Our forums also have cookies, these are not anonymous cookies as they are used to keep your username logged in - only if you wish to be.

Companies who provide services for us may place "cookies" on your computer also. These cookies track viewing behaviour across this site and, potentially, other sites in their network for the purposes of advertising or statistic gathering. The information they collect through, or on behalf of, this website, is not personally identifiable.

Any questions?

If you have any questions about our privacy policy then you can e-mail me at **ADMIN@THE-BIG-BANG-THEORY.COM**.

Legal Notice.

No part of this site is targeted at minors under the age of 13 years, we expressly prohibit use of this site by people under the age of 13 or those under the age of 18 without their guardian's consent.

**This site complies with the United States COPPA and does not knowingly collect the personal information of minors under the age of 13yrs.**
