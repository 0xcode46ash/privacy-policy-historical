# PRIVACY POLICY | The Budget Mom

Protecting your private information is our priority. This Statement of Privacy applies to the www.thebudgetmom.com and The Budget Mom and governs data collection and usage. For the purposes of the Privacy Policy, unless otherwise noted, all references to The Budget Mom include www.thebudgetmom.com. The Budget Mom website is an informational and self-help site. By using The Budget Mom website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

### COLLECTION OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

The Budget Mom may collect personally identifiable information, such as your name and email address. If you purchase The Budget Mom’s products and/or services, we collect billing and credit card information. This information is used to complete the purchase transaction. The Budget Mom may also collect anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your age, gender, household income and educational background. We may gather additional personal or non-personal information in the future.

Cookies may be used to remember visitor preferences when interacting with The Budget Mom. Third party advertising companies may use cookies for tracking purposes. The Budget Mom collects general traffic information such as site visits, page views, etc. in an attempt to make The Budget Mom better.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through The Budget mom’s public comment system, this information may be collected and used by others.

The Budget Mom encourages you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from The budget so that you can understand how those websites collect, use and share your information. The Budget Mom is no responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of The Budget Mom.

### USE OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

The Budget Mom collects and uses your personal information to operate its website and deliver the services you have requested.

The Budget Mom may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

The Budget Mom does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to ANY third parties.

The Budget Mom may keep track of the websites and pages our users visit within The Budget Mom, in order to determine what The Budget Mom services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within The Budget Mom to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

### USE OF COOKIES

The Budget Mom website may use “cookies” to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text field that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. the purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize The Budget Mom pages, or register with The Budget Mom site or services, a cookie helps The Budget mom to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing address, shipping address, and so on. When you return to the same The Budget Mom website, the information your previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use The Budget Mom features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you many not be able to fully experience the interactive features of The Budget Mom services or websites you visit.
