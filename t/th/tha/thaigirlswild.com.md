# Tea Money Media Customer Support

Tea Money Media recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of personal information about our customers. As such, this Privacy Policy applies to collection, use, storage and disclosure of information by Tea Money Media.

In order to provide Internet billing services, Tea Money Media must collect, use, and share certain information about you. This policy explains what we collect, who we share it with, and how you can contact Tea Money Media in order to ensure the accuracy of the data we collect, use, and share. The information Tea Money Media collects from you helps Tea Money Media better serve you and the online community by continually updating the electronic payment services Tea Money Media provides. The data that Tea Money Media collects and maintains is as follows:

Information That You Provide to Us

Tea Money Media receives and stores all information that you enter on our sign-up pages except for security items such as Visa's CVV2. This includes:

Credit Card Sign-Up Page for Digital Products. The Web site you are purchasing an e-ticket for, your e-mail address, language preference, credit card number, expiration date, first name, last name, address, city, state, zip, country, your username and password, and your agreement to be bound by Tea Money Media's Terms and Conditions.

Check Sign-Up Page. The Web site you are purchasing an e-ticket for, your e-mail address, language preference, first name, last name, address, city, state, zip, country, your bank's ABA routing number, your account number, the type of account, your username and password, and your agreement to be bound by Tea Money Media's Terms and Conditions.

Information That We Automatically Receive

Tea Money Media receives and stores certain information whenever you download Web pages, purchase products or send us e-mail or forms. For example:

We identify the numerical IP address assigned to your computer by your Internet Service Provider.

E-mail Correspondence. Tea Money Media often requests a confirmation when you receive and open an e-mail communication delivered by Tea Money Media. We archive all e-mail sent in and out of our network, and we identify the origination IP address of all mail sent into our network. We require that you refrain from "forging headers" or sending "SPAM".

Please be aware that, as part of the Services, Tea Money Media. provides links to Web sites operated by third parties, and is not responsible for the information collection or privacy practices or the content of those third party Web sites or applications.
