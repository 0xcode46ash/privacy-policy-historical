# privacy statement

#### Information Collection and Use

In order to improve the services we provide for you, **thatsjava.com** collects your information. This information is gathered through "cookies", which helps us communicate with you when visiting our website again. The collected data is solely owned by **thatsjava.com**. **thatsjava.com** uses user's information for responding to requests, customizing services and improving communications with our users. **thatsjava.com** does not sell or share personal information with any outside parties; however, we may share generalized information about users for the purpose of improving our website. 

#### Responsibility of Use

 **thatsjava.com** is not responsible for user information collected by outside parties while visiting **thatsjava.com**. This includes information you voluntarily disclose through the use of forums, chats and e-mail, and information obtained through the use of links on our Website. 

#### Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. Once the user closes their browser, the cookie simply terminates. Cookies enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our site. 

#### Log Files

We use Internet Protocol (IP) addresses to analyze trends, administer our site, track user movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.
