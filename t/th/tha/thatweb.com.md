# ThatWeb '99 V3.0: Privacy

Questions regarding this statement should be directed to Webmaster of ThatWeb via email to [webmaster@thatweb.com](https://web.archive.org/web/19991127081207id_/http%3A//thatweb.com/mail/contact.asp?ContactMail=webmaster@thatweb.com) or via snail mail to ThatWeb.Com Private Limited, 31 Ubi Road 1, Aztech Building, Singapore 408694. **ThatWeb.Com’s Privacy Policy**  
ThatWeb.Com firmly believes in protecting the privacy of our users. 

ThatWeb.Com does not capture or store the email content or the password of our users. The email addresses logged by the system is for system management and statistical purposes. All access to data collected by ThatWeb.Com is limited. No user or third party will have access to it including our employees. All our employees do not have access rights to the data collected. It is our company policy that employees of ThatWeb.Com do not disclose or share with third party confidential information.

In general, our service automatically gathers certain usage information like the numbers and frequency of visitors to ThatWeb, very much like television ratings that tell the network how many people tuned in to a program. We only use such data in the aggregate. This collective data helps us determine how much our customers use the site, so we can continuously improve our site, and to assure that it is as appealing as we can make it for as many of our users as possible. 

To ensure total security, ThatWeb.Com reviews our web security daily.

**Information ThatWeb.Com Gathers and Tracks**  
ThatWeb gathers user information from 3 sources - when users login to ThatWeb.Com, when users participate in contests and promotions and when users request for information regarding "Advertising Opportunities".

Information collected include name, postal address, email address, country of residence and telephone number. ThatWeb.Com gathers this information to facilitate the sending of gifts/prizes and as in the case of 'Advertising Opportunities', to send the user requested information.

We gather information about our users so as to have a better understanding of them. This will enable us to tailor our services to better serve our users.

**Use of Information Gathered/Tracked**  
All information submitted to ThatWeb.Com will be kept strictly confidential and will only be used to support our users’ customer relationship with ThatWeb.Com. The email addresses and passwords which users enter when they login to ThatWeb are solely used to locate his/her server.

ThatWeb.Com does not track or store the email content or user passwords. The email contents and user passwords are flushed from our system at the end of each session and/or upon each successful or unsuccessful connection.

ThatWeb.Com only records the email addresses for system management and statistical purposes. Information gathered on our users will not be given or sold to any outside organization for its use in marketing or solicitation. 

**Sharing of Information**  
ThatWeb.Com does not share information about our users with any third party. However, ThatWeb.Com does not control the practices of our advertisers. Should you have questions about how our advertisers use information about you when you click their ads and go to their content, please contact them directly.

**Use of Cookies**  
ThatWeb.Com uses cookies. The purpose of the cookies is to pass information to user mail servers so as to retrieve/send his/her emails.

**Correct/Update Policy**  
Currently, ThatWeb.Com does not have a correct/update policy. 

**Delete/Deactivate Policy**  
Currently, ThatWeb.Com does not offer users a means by which to completely delete personal information submitted.

**Privacy Policy Changes**  
Should there be any change to ThatWeb.Com's privacy policy, we will post the change here so that users will always know what information we gather, how we might use the information and whether we will disclose it to anyone.
