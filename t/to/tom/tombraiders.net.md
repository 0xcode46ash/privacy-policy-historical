# Tombraiders.net Advertising and Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy for Tombraiders.net

This document was last updated on 5/23/2008.

##### General

Tombraiders.net respects your right to privacy. The following outlines the privacy policy for this site. If you have any questions, please feel free to email ([stellalune@tombraiders.net](mailto:stellalune@tombraiders.net)) or contact me through my [web form](http://tombraiders.net/stella/contact.html).

##### Personal Email & Opt-In Email Newsletter

There are no password-restricted areas on this site and, unless you contact me, I have no way of obtaining your email address or other personal information.

When you sign up for my [newsletter](http://www.tombraiders.net/cgi-bin/dada/mail.cgi/list/tombraiders/) or send me a message by email or through my [contact form](https://web.archive.org/web/20081217083228id_/http%3A//tombraiders.net/stella/contact.html), I do save your name (if you have provided it) and email address. I **_never_** , under any circumstances, rent, sell or share this information with any outside parties. Joining my mailing list or sending me a personal message will not cause you to receive any "spam"/junk email.

If you have subscribed to the email newsletter and would like to unsubscribe, either follow the instructions at the bottom of any newsletter message, go to the the [sign-up page](http://www.tombraiders.net/cgi-bin/dada/mail.cgi/list/tombraiders/) to unsubscribe, or send me a request by email and you will be removed immediately.

##### Log Files & Statistics

When you visit tombraiders.net our web server records information about your visit, including your IP (Internet Protocol) address, web browser type, operating system and the last page you visited before tombraiders.net. This site also uses [Google Analytics](http://www.google.com/analytics/) to track visitor statistics. This information is recorded in log files and used to analyze trends (e.g., which search engines and keywords were used to find this site, which pages are most popular, etc.), administer the site (e.g., find broken links), track users' movement through the site, and gather broad demographic information (e.g., users' country of origin). None of this information is linked to personally identifiable information or shared with any outside parties.

##### Advertising & Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data (in the form of a small text file) stored on a computer user's hard drive and containing information about the user. Cookies are used to personalize the information delivered to your web browser when you visit a particular web site. For example, some sites ask you to log in then use cookies to "recognize" you on subsequent visits. Some sites track your browsing preferences so they can display advertising that matches your interests, etc.

For more general information about cookies and how they work, please visit [Cookie Central](http://www.cookiecentral.com/faq/). 

Tombraiders.net does not use cookies. However, the advertisements on tombraiders.net do use cookies, as well as javascript code, to track which ads a user clicks and to identify tombraiders.net as the site responsible for "click-throughs" to an advertiser's site. Tombraiders.net does not have access to or control over the cookies received with ads or from visiting advertisers' sites. Users are welcome to browse our site with cookies and javascript disabled or to reject specific cookies if they choose. Check your web browser's help files for information on changing your privacy/cookie/javascript settings.

Please note that all of these companies represent numerous individual advertisers. If you have questions about specific advertisers' policies, please visit the individual sites.

Webmasters interested in joining any of these programs, please feel free to contact me for more information.

_**Update:** As of May 2008, Google also serves advertisements for various third party vendors. For details, including links to each advertising company's site, where you can find their particular policies, please visit [this page](https://www.google.com/adsense/support/bin/answer.py?answer=94149) on the Google Adsense site. Some of these advertisers do use cookies to track visitor data. As always, you are welcome to choose to disable cookies while browsing this site. This will not affect the content other than the advertising._

##### Links

Tombraiders.net contains links to other web sites, including other fan sites as well as advertisers. Tombraiders.net is not responsible for the content, security or privacy practices of these sites. I encourage users to be aware when they leave my site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information.
