# Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy

We collect the e-mail addresses of TOMWANS Delivery recipients and senders for the purpose of logging and measuring usage. TOMWAS does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with nonaffiliated companies.

We may disclose your information if necessary to protect our legal rights or if the information relates to actual or threatened harmful conduct or potential threats to the physical safety of any person. Disclosure may be required by law or if we receive legal process.

TOMWANS uses cookies to store the preferences of users.

We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site. Files stored for delivery are only accessible by TOMWANS and through the clickable link generated for your recipient. All files stored for delivery are deleted when they expire.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, e-mail address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you.

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.
