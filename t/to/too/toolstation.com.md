# Policies - Toolstation

### Privacy & security policy

When you use our website or do business with Toolstation Ltd we gather information from you. This policy explains what we do with your information and what you can expect if you provide us with your personal details. 

##### We treat your privacy with the utmost respect.

When you provide us with personal information such as your name, email address contact details or payment details you do so for the purposes of communicating with us. We do not rent or sell this information to other companies. 

If you request information or place an order with us you are added to our mailing list and may receive information from us on an ongoing basis. If at any time you wish us to stop using or holding information about you simply telephone us free on [0808 100 7211](tel:+448081007211) or send us an email to [info@toolstation.com](mailto:info@toolstation.com). 

Our systems also collect non personal information about what you are looking at and how you are using our business. This information is used to make buying from us easier. 

The Internet is not secure, however we have put in place various security procedures to make it as secure as possible. All of the forms which gather or display personal information on our site are protected by a mechanism called SSL. Firewalls are used to block unauthorised access to our servers which are themselves located in a secure location. 

Our stringent internal procedures ensure that your information is kept confidential within Toolstation Ltd.

By submitting your information to us and continuing to use our website you consent to the use of that information as set out in this Policy. If we change our Privacy Policy we will post the changes on this page. 

If you have any comments or queries regarding this policy simply telephone us free on [0808 100 7211](tel:+448081007211) or send us an email to [info@toolstation.com](mailto:info@toolstation.com).
