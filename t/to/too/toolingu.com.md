# Privacy Statement

**I.** Tooling University LLC (the "Company") is committed to protecting your online privacy while you use the services provided by the Company. We urge you to read this privacy policy in its entirety so that you will understand all of the practices and procedures that relate to your online privacy. Tooling U provides high quality web-based training on a wide variety of industrial manufacturing topics.

This Privacy Statement addresses your privacy by:

**A**. Informing you about

**i.** the type of personal information the Company collects about you through this web site;   
**ii.** how that information is collected;   
**iii.** the general purposes for which information is collected;   
**iv.** what disclosures of information will be made;   
**v.** how, and to what extent, you can limit those disclosures; and 

**B**. Assuring you that: 

i. reasonable precautions are taken to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosures, alteration or destruction;   
ii. reasonable policies and procedures are in place to insure that your personal information is kept only for the purposes for which it was gathered;   
iii. you have reasonable access to any personal information you provide; and   
iv. there is a procedure for correcting or modifying your personal information, if appropriate. 

**II.** This web site is the responsibility of the Company which can be contacted by e-mail at [ chad.schron@toolingu.com](mailto:chad.schron@toolingu.com) or by regular mail to Chad Schron, Tooling University, 15700 S. Waterloo Rd., Cleveland, Ohio 44110.

**III.** Collecting Personal Information 

Some information about your IP address, Web browser, and computer system are automatically transmitted to our web servers as an integral part of the operation of the Internet. Thus, we reserve the right to automatically collect and/or track: 

i. the home server domain names, e-mail addresses, type of client computer, and type of web browsers of visitors to our web site; and   
ii. information on what pages of the web site visitors access. 

There are times when we may ask you for other personal information including, but not limited to information collected, e.g., name, address, birth date, credit card information, etc. This information will only be requested when needed to complete a transaction for which such information is necessary. 

**IV.** Children

We do not use toolinguniversity.com or toolingu.com to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. 

**V.** Cookie Disclosure Information 

A "cookie" is a persistent client side hypertext transfer protocol file. Cookies are pieces of information that a web site can transfer to a visitor's hard drive for record keeping purposes. Cookies facilitate web surfing by saving visitor preferences and other information. The rise of cookies is a widespread industry standard and thus many major web sites use cookies. Using a cookie allows record-keeping while you are visiting a web site and saves you the extra effort of entering certain information multiple times in one site. 

Most browsers are initially configured to accept and process cookies. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies. Extensive information concerning cookies is available on the World Wide Web by searching for the word "cookies". If you have any questions concerning our use of cookies, please contact us at [chad.schron@toolingu.com](mailto:chad.schron@toolingu.com) or call toll free at 1-866-706-6601. 

We reserve the right to use cookies on our web site.

**VI.** Use of Personal Data Collected 

Personal data we collect will be used to: 

  * monitor and improve our internal operations; 
  * bill you appropriately for contracted transactions and services; 
  * properly perform the services you request; 
  * produce statistical and/or demographic data for use by advertisers, potential advertisers and other business partners; 
  * editorial and feedback purposes; 
  * marketing and promotional purposes; 
  * customize content and layout of our site; 
  * properly provide the goods you request; 
  * properly respond to your inquiries; and 
  * provide general information that may be of interest to you. 



**VII.** Disclosure of Personal Data to Third Parties 

We will not notify you before disclosing data to third parties. 

Any third party we share, rent, sell or otherwise disclose data to will be prescreened by us to insure that they are reputable and that they will only use the data for marketing purposes that we determine you might be interested in. 

We reserve the right to disclose personal data in special cases when we believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be causing injury to or interference with our web site, other users of our web site, or anyone else who could be harmed by such activities. 

We may disclose personal data when we believe in good faith that the law requires it.

**VIII.** Your Right to Opt Out 

You may opt out of having your personal information collected by contacting us by e-mail or regular mail. Please note that in order to fulfill certain obligations we may need to continue sending you notices or other important information concerning your account.
