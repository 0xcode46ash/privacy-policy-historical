# totaltravel.com - About

## Scope 

This statement provides an overview of the full totaltravel.com Privacy and Security Statement. This overview and the full privacy policy apply to all totaltravel.com websites that display or link to this statement.

## Uses of Information

  * We only use the information that your provide for the purposes you supplied it and the services you requested.
  * We use your information to inform you of other products or services offered by totaltravel.com.
  * We do not sell, loan, rent or lease your information to third parties.



## Personal Information

  * Some of our services require you to register for which we will ask you to provide personal information.
  * We do not collect information about you from other companies.
  * We use cookies and other technologies to provide our users with a more efficient service.



## Your Choices

  * You can opt out of totaltravel.com e-mail to you at any time by following the instructions in the e-mail you receive or using the unsubscribe link.
  * You may seek access to information held by us about you and you may amend or delete this information.
