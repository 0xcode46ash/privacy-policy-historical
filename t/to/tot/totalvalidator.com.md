# Privacy Statements

### Payments

All payments are processed through third party payment systems, which have their own privacy policies outside of our control. However, any information provided to us by them is dealt with as if we had collected it directly. 

### Disclosures

Total Validator does not sell, trade, or rent to third parties any information collected by us. We will not explicitly share any such information with third parties except where it may be necessary to comply with the law, apply the terms of our user agreements, or protect the rights of Total Validator. 

### Unsolicited contact

No personal information is gathered or held except for an email address purely used in order to notify people of renewals or service changes. No other form of unsolicited contact will be made.
