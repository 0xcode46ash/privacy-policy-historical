# iBingo.com Privacy Information - QuadCard Entertainment Inc Privacy Policy

**Quadcard Entertainemnt Inc Privacy Statement**

Quadcard Entertainment Inc. is the parent company of the following websites: 

* ibingo.com 
* uk.ibingo.com 
* tothegames.com 
* drawbingo.com 
* bingodestinations.com 
* billybingo.com 
* megabuxbingo.com 

Quadcard believes in earning your trust. That is why protecting your personal information and your privacy is extremely important to us. 

At Quadcard we use the latest in encryption technology to protect the information you give us. This includes all personal information you provide, such as your name, postal address, e-mail address, telephone number and credit card number. 

We are committed to protecting your privacy, your personal information will only be used by our websites for the purposes of playing the game, notification of special events or special offers. You have to option to "opt out" of these offers on your customer profile. We will not under any circumstances release your e-mail address or presonal information to anyone. 

Information directly or indirectly collected from you is for the sole use of Quadcard and under no circumstances will it be released to anyone. 

At Quadcard all your personal information is stored behind the latest Cisco PIX firewalls, we use secure 128bit SSL, certified by Secure Server Certification Authority [RSA Data Security, Inc.](http://www.rsa.com/) for all of out Web Transactions and [RC4 encryption](http://www.rsasecurity.com/rsalabs/faq/3-6-3.html), also from RSA, during game play.
