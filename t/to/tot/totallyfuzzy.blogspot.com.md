# Totally Fuzzy : Music Blog Aggregator, Music Videos: Privacy Policy

**Privacy:**

This site uses Google Analytics, which uses a cookie that collects anonymous traffic data to be able to generate meaningful traffic statistics. (Google Analytics [Terms of Service](http://www.google.com/analytics/tos.html) \- [Google Privacy Policy)](http://www.google.com/privacy.html)

  


The advertising services used on this blog are very likely to use their own cookies. We do not have access to any information that might be stored in those cookies.

You're welcome to read the terms of use of the advertising services if you want more information.

  


  * [Google Adsense (Privacy Policy)](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)
  * [Commission Junction (Privacy Policy)](https://www.cj.com/privacy.html)
  * [Linkshare (Privacy Policy)](http://www.linkshare.com/legal/privacy/)



If you should submit any personal information anywhere on this website, for submitting a blog, a review or for registration etc, that personal information, like name, email address etc, will not be collected in order to be sold or otherwise passed on to any third parties.

To protect your privacy you can use bogus information like a nickname and a webmail address.

**Privacy Policy for totallyfuzzy.blogspot.com**

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by posting a comment here. 

At totallyfuzzy.blogspot.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by totallyfuzzy.blogspot.com and how it is used. 

**Cookies and Web Beacons**  
totallyfuzzy.blogspot.com does not use cookies. 

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include Google Adsense, Commission Junction, Linkshare, . 

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on totallyfuzzy.blogspot.com send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see. 

totallyfuzzy.blogspot.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers. 

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. totallyfuzzy.blogspot.com's privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites. 

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites. 

See also [http://www.aboutcookies.org](http://www.aboutcookies.org/) or [http://www.allaboutcookies.org](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/)
