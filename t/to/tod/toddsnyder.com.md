# Privacy Policy | Todd Snyder

### At Todd Snyder we believe in protecting the privacy of our members and customers.

##### Collection and Use of Personal Information

  1. **What personal information we will collect.**  
When you purchase a product from our website, we will collect information such as your name, mailing address, phone number, email address and contact preferences. This information will be used to fulfill your order, provide you with company newsletters, or help you with any problems you may be having.
  2. **What personal information we DO NOT collect.**  
In no way will we store, save or collect your credit card information after your purchase is made. Any credit card information Todd Snyder obtains is only used to process your order and comes encrypted, meaning we don't even see the number let alone store it somewhere. 
  3. **How we may use your personal information**  
If you have signed up for our newsletter you may receive emails about upcoming products & company announcements. If you, at any time, decide you no longer want to receive emails from Todd Snyder, opting out is as easy as updating your preference by clicking on the _unsubscribe_ link at the bottom of your email.
  4. If you enter any type of sweepstakes or contest we may use the information you have provided for use in the administration of those processes.
  5. We may use personal information for internal purposes such as analysis and research to improve Todd Snyder and customer support.
  6. **How we DO NOT use your personal information**  
Any information collected by us will only be shared with third parties(shipping companies, analytics tools, etc.)in the processing of you order and to improve your experience. Todd Snyder will never sell your information to any third parties.

##### Protection of Personal information

  1. We will protect personal information by reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification. All personal and non-personal information collected by us during purchases is protected by a Secure Sockets Layer encryption.
  2. When posting to Todd Snyder's product comments or social networking service, whatever you share is visible to other users and can be read, collected or used by other visitors to the website. Todd Snyder is not responsible for the misuse of any personal information that a person submits freely on our website through the use of product comments or social networking services. You are responsible for the personal information you choose to submit in these instances. For example, if you list your name and email address in a product comment, that information is public and Todd Snyder is not responsible for the misuse of that information in any circumstance.
  3. We will make readily available to customers any changes being made to these policies by sending an email pertaining to these changes no sooner then 30 days prior to the changes taking effect.
