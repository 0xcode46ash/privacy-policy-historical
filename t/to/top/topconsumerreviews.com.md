# TopConsumerReviews.com - Privacy Policy

**PRIVACY POLICY**

TopConsumerReviews.com places a very high level of importance on the privacy of our users. We do not sell, rent, or share any information you provide to us with any third party company. As a proud member of the **Better Business Bureau** , we support honesty and fairness throughout all our activities.

You are not required to share any personal information with us. You are welcome to browse and use our detailed product review and ranking information as often, and for as long as you wish. If you find our information useful, feel free to share the site with a friend. We do not maintain any information on users of our website.

 If you decide to take advantage of the free information provided in our [newsletter](https://web.archive.org/web/20080618110518id_/http%3A//www.topconsumerreviews.com/newsletter.php), we must collect some information from you. This information is used solely to maintain correspondence between TopConsumerReviews.com and yourself. We do not rent, sell, or share any information you provide to us with any third party company.

 TopConsumerReviews.com does not utilize, nor endorse, the practice of sending unsolicited emails. You will never receive an email from us unless you have signed up for our free newsletter service. Our newsletter provides opt-in subscription and a simple unsubscribe process. We voluntarily support and maintain compliance with the Federal anti-spam legislation (CAN-SPAM) act of 2004.

If you have any questions or comments about this information please feel free to [contact us here](https://web.archive.org/web/20080618110518id_/http%3A//www.topconsumerreviews.com/contacts.php).
