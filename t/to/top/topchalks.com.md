# TopChalks: Privacy Policy

###### Privacy Policy

**Catura Broadband Systems Pvt. Ltd.** will not release, sell, rent, or trade the personal information of any of the customers of [**www.topchalks.com**](https://web.archive.org/tc/gotologin.htm) to any third party. Catura Broadband Systems Pvt. Ltd. respects and understands the importance of privacy and security of its customers. Any personal information collected by [**www.topchalks.com**](https://web.archive.org/tc/gotologin.htm), including contact information, unique identifier information, transactional and financial information, will be kept private.

Users who supply us with their telephone numbers or e-mail addresses on-line may receive telephone calls or emails from us and/or our affiliates with information regarding special promotions, new products and services or upcoming events.
