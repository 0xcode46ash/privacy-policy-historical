# Privacy Policy

**PRIVACY POLICY**

We rate protecting our user's privacy very important. Protecting your privacy and the personal information you provide us is a top priority. We do not collect any information from persons visiting our site. We may collect information necessary from anyone registering for site updates and news releases or other services offered, which require your personal information. We will never share your information with anyone other than our associated staff. We will not contact you unless you request information or services.

We will not use your personal information for  any other purposes, other than what you agreed upon when (or if) you registered for an account with us. We value your privacy and are dedicated to protecting your personal information. 

We will not sell, trade, or give away your personal information. We may share your information only with our staff or with third parties that provide services directly to us.

We may share your information with law enforcement or other government agencies as required by law. 

We will not release your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. 

**DISPUTES  
** If you choose to visit this Site, your visit and any dispute over privacy is subject to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use including limitations on damages, arbitration of disputes, and application of the law of the State of Tennessee, USA.

**CHANGES TO TO THE PRIVACY POLICY  
** To accommodate changes to our web site design, and possible legal requirements, We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time.
