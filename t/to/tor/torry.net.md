**Privacy Policy**

* * *

This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site.

**Torry's Delphi Pages** respects your privacy and we are committed to protecting your privacy throughout your use of our web site.

When you place an order online with us, we redirect you to third-party web site, provided by [ShareIt](http://www.shareit.com/privacy_statement.html). ShareIt has it own privacy policy, please read it before making an order.

We do not gather any credit card information from your product registration, we have no such technical possibility, and we do not keep any such kind of information. Moreover, we do not want to.

So, for your own privacy, please do not send us any credit card information via open channels (e.g. to our support e-mail), use this information only when placing your order via ShareIt. We do not collect any personal information from you as well.

If you subscribe to our subscription services you are required to provide your name and email address. Your name and email address will not be shared with any third party nor used for unsolicited email communication.

We use cookies on our web site only to collect general usage information, such as aggregated site-visitation statistics. A cookie is a small file that a Web site writes to your hard drive when you visit that site. A cookie is uniquely yours and can only be read by the server that gave it to you. It is a kind of your ID card and can not deliver viruses. Cookies can not read information from your hard drive or read information from other cookies. Cookies are not used to collect your personal information. You can disable cookies by using Internet Options of your browser. 

Cookies help us determine what information is most valuable to you and therefore we can improve our services and the web site.

This site contains references to third parties web pages. We do not bear any responsibility for their privacy practices. To make sure your private information will be secured with them you have to read respective privacy statements of the third parties.

We reserve the right to update privacy statement from time to time. If we change privacy statement conspicuous notice will be placed on our web site.
