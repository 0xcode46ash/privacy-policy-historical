# TourneyTime.com, LLC Privacy Statement

TourneyTime, LLC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: [http://www.tourneytime.com](http://www.tourneytime.com/). 

**Data Collection**  
TourneyTime may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. TourneyTime may use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and enhance your TourneTime experience. TourneyTime's registration form requires users to give contact information (like name, email address, etc.). This captured data will be used to identify the user to TourneyTime.com, personalize the TourneyTime.com experience, send the user information about our company, update them on upcoming events, or notify them when action is required on their behalf for their account. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. TourneyTime's member data and information will not be sold or provided to another entity, except in the event of a merger or acquisition of TourneyTime.com, LLC. 

**Content**  
This site may contain links to third party sites. **TourneyTime.com, LLC** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of any such Web sites. Our online surveys (when used) ask visitors for contact information (like their email address). We use data from our surveys to gather information about TourneyTime and it's users. Contact information may be used to address or clarify any information gathered from the survey. Occasionaly TourneyTime.com may run contests on the site in which we ask visitors for contact information (like their email address). We use contact data from our contests to send users information about our company and notify them if they are amoung the winners. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor if/when necessary and/or to send any prizes they might have won. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. 

**Public Forums**  
This site makes chat rooms, forums, and/or message boards available to its users in controled areas. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you the user should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. TourneyTime.com, LLC is not liable for any of this information. 

**Security**  
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, mis-use, and alteration of the information under our control. TourneyTime, LLC uses a secure password/user protected database that is backed up at regular intervals. 

**Choice/Opt-Out**  
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor. This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service. The users may select what information they would like to receive and what information they no longer wish to receive. 1\. You can visit the following URL: [http://www.tourneytime.com/privacy/optout.cfm](https://web.archive.org/web/20061212133157id_/http%3A//www.tourneytime.com/privacy/OptOut.cfm)

**Correct/Update**  
This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying any information they have previously provided to TourneyTime, LLC.   
1\. Login to your TourneyTime account and select "Update My Account Information" from the menu options.
