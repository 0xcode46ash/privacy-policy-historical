# TourneyTime: Privacy Statement

TourneyTime, LLC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: [http://www.tourneytime.com](http://www.tourneytime.com/). 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. We use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your username and/or password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site. Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name and email address). We use customer contact information from the registration form to send the user information about our company, update them on upcoming events, or notify them when action is required on their behalf for their account. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor if/when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. 

This site contains links to third party sites. **TourneyTime, LLC** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. Our online surveys (when used) ask visitors for contact information (like their email address). We use contact data from our surveys to send the user information about our company, or to address any information gathered from the survey. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor if/when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Occasionaly we run contests on our site in which we ask visitors for contact information (like their email address). We use contact data from our contests to send users information about our company and notify them if they are amoung the winners. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor if/when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. 

**Public Forums**  
This site makes chat rooms, forums, and/or message boards available to its users in controled areas. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you the user should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. TourneyTime, LLC is not liable for any of this information. 

**Security**  
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. TourneyTime, LLC uses a secure password/user protected database that is backed up daily. 

**Choice/Opt-Out**  
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor. This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service. The users may select what information they would like to receive and what information they no longer wish to receive. 1\. You can visit the following URL: [http://www.tourneytime.com/privacy/optout.cfm](https://web.archive.org/web/20000530084620id_/http%3A//www.tourneytime.com/privacy/optout.cfm)
