# 

At Tokyoflash.com we are fully committed to protecting the privacy of your personal details.  
We will not divulge your personal information to any third party without your consent.

  1. We use the information we collect about you to process your order and to provide a more personalised shopping experience.
  2. When you place orders, our secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information that you input before it is sent to us.

Tokyoflash.com is a **SECURE SITE**   
We fully recognise the importance of security regarding the credit card information you send us over the Internet.  
Our web server uses industry standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to encrypt all of your personal information including debit or credit card numbers, names and addresses. The encryption process converts the characters that you enter into code that is then securely transmitted over the Internet.  
When you place an order, a small padlock will appear in the corner of your browser indicating that any information provided cannot be viewed by a third party.
