# Tsogo Sun Privacy Policy

By using Tsogo Sun Gaming website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

**Collection of your Personal Information**

Tsogo Sun Gaming collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. Tsogo Sun Gaming also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your postal or ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favourites.

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by Tsogo Sun Gaming. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by Tsogo Sun Gaming for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of Tsogo Sun Gaming Web site.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through Tsogo Sun Gaming public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: Tsogo Sun Gaming does not read any of your private online communications.

Tsogo Sun Gaming encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from Tsogo Sun Gaming so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. Tsogo Sun Gaming is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of Tsogo Sun Gaming and The Tsogo Sun Gaming family of Web sites.

**Use Of Your Personal Information**

Tsogo Sun Gaming collects and uses your personal information to operate Tsogo Sun Gaming Web site and deliver the services you have requested. Tsogo Sun Gaming also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Tsogo Sun Gaming and its affiliates. Tsogo Sun Gaming may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

Tsogo Sun Gaming does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Tsogo Sun Gaming may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, Tsogo Sun Gaming may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Tsogo Sun Gaming, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Tsogo Sun Gaming does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.

Tsogo Sun Gaming keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within Tsogo Sun Gaming, in order to determine what Tsogo Sun Gaming services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customised content and advertising within Tsogo Sun Gaming to customers whose behaviour indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Tsogo Sun Gaming Web sites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Tsogo Sun Gaming or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Tsogo Sun Gaming; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Tsogo Sun Gaming, or the public.

**Use Of Cookies**

Tsogo Sun Gaming Web site uses "cookies" to help you personalise your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalise Tsogo Sun Gaming pages, or register with Tsogo Sun Gaming site or services, a cookie helps Tsogo Sun Gaming to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same Tsogo Sun Gaming Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use Tsogo Sun Gaming features that you customised.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of Tsogo Sun Gaming services or Web sites you visit.

**Security Of Your Personal Information**

Tsogo Sun Gaming secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Tsogo Sun Gaming secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.

**Changes To This Statement**

Tsogo Sun Gaming will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. Tsogo Sun Gaming encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how Tsogo Sun Gaming is protecting your information.
