# tsn.ca: Privacy

At tsn.ca we respect your privacy and want you to know about our privacy policy.

Non-personal information may be collected through use of browser cookies. A browser cookie is data which is transferred to your computer for record keeping purposes. A cookie is used to enhance your viewing experience of our site. For example, cookies identify repeat users and their preferred settings. Consult your browser documentation to learn how you can modify your browser settings. 

You may provide us with personal information when you complete forms (eg. contest entry forms) or when you decide to personalize your own tsn.ca Sportsdesk page, or enter a chat room. Please be assured that this information is not sold.

Please remember that a chat room is a public forum for discussion. The information that you choose to share in a chat room becomes public information, therefore tsn.ca is unable to guarantee the security or privacy of such information.

This site contains links to other sites. tsn.ca is not responsible for the privacy policies on other sites, nor does tsn.ca endorse the content of other sites.

tsn.ca uses a third party to serve the advertisements that you see on the pages of our website. Any information provided directly to the third party by tsn.ca is in the aggregate only. If you would like to know more about the information gathering practices and "opt-out" procedures of our third party ad server, please go to [www.doubleclick.net/privacy_policy/](http://www.doubleclick.net/privacy_policy/)

We may make changes or revise our privacy policy. Any changes will be posted here for your information. If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please email us at [webmaster@tsn.ca](mailto:webmaster@tsn.ca).
