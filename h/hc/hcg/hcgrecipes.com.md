# Privacy Policy | HCG Recipes, LLC

**Security** : We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We at [HCGRecipes.com](http://hcgrecipes.com/ "HCGRecipes.com") have put in place suitable physical and electronic procedures to protect the information we collect online.

**Your Information** : We collect information from you when you register on our site or fill out a form. When placing an order or registering on our site, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your name or email address. You may however visit our site anonymously.

**How we use your information** :  
To personalize your experience (your information helps us to better respond to your individual needs)  
To improve our website (we continually strive to improve our website offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you.  
To improve customer service (your information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs  
To send periodic emails. The email address you provide for order processing, may be used to send you information and updates pertaining to your order, in addition to receiving occasional news, updates, related product or service information, discounts and promotional materials, and free newsletter. If at any time you wish to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, we include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email.  
To administer a contest, promotion, survey, or other site feature.  
To process transactions

**Use of cookies** : We may utilize cookies to help us analyze traffic and improve our website to serve our customers and visitors better. You may choose to accept or decline cookies and you may feel free to modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.

**Third Party Links and Affiliate links** : Our website may contain links to enable you to visit other websites of interest. This privacy policy becomes inactive from the time you leave our site. Some of the third party links may contain affiliate linking and tracking information for which HCG Recipes.com may receive financial compensation.

Your information, whether public or private, will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any other company for any reason without your consent.

By using our site, you consent to our privacy policy. This privacy policy is subject to change at our discretion and any updates will be made to this page.
