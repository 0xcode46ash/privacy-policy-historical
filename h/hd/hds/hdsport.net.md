# Privacy policy - hdsport.net

hdsport.net does not collect any personal information. We are not going to ask you for your name, email, address, phone or anything else which might be considered a personal information.

hdsport.net uses cookies to improve your experience on our websites and for functionality purposes. Our cookies are not used for collection personal information.

We might show 3rd party banners (adserver javascript tags) which could collect personal information according to their privacy policy.

Our hosting provider collects information about your IP address through its web server logs. This information is not shared with third parties, except with legal entities as required by EU laws.
