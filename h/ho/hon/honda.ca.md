# Honda.ca Privacy Policy

Honda Canada is committed to ensuring that personal information collected from customers and potential customers ("customers") is appropriately protected and that customer privacy is respected.

Any personal information collected from you from this website and otherwise may be used by Honda and its related companies, their dealers and service providers to communicate marketing, product and service information to you.

Honda Canada takes appropriate security and other measures to preserve the confidentiality of this information and to ensure that it is used for the intended purposes.

By supplying your personal information to us or using this website, you agree to the terms of this policy.

If you are an owner of a Honda or Acura brand product and you wish to obtain access to your personal information or prefer not to receive marketing information from Honda Canada, please contact Honda at 1-888-946-6329 and your Dealer.
