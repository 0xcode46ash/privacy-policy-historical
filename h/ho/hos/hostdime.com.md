# Host Dime - Privacy Policy

>   
>  **Privacy** **  
> **  
>  OUR policies and procedures for handling customer information have been created with the understanding that there continues to be constant change in internet technologies. With this change also comes change in internet business methods to keep up with the technological changes. As a result, hostdime.com's policies and procedures are subject to change. 
> 
> As we strive to provide the services requested by our clients, we constantly, collect, accumulate and store information and communications that may be deemed private or sensitive to certain individuals and/or bodies. Some of this information - such as the customer's name, address, telephone number, and credit card data \- is provided to hostdime.com by its customers in order to establish service. Other information - such as the customer's account status, choice of services, and customer logs - is created and maintained by hostdime.com in the normal course of providing service.
> 
> **Data Security**
> 
> hostdime.com will protect the confidentiality of its customers' information, account information and personal communications to the fullest extent possible and consistent with the law and the legitimate interests of hostdime.com, its partners, its employees and other customers of hostdime.com's services. To protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of information that is collected from customers, hostdime.com has necessary physical, electronic, and managerial procedures in place.
> 
> **Email Address Disclosure**
> 
> Host Dime.com will NEVER divulge your email address to marketing companies period. We strive to set an example for the industry and we will NEVER sell, barter, or otherwise give away your email address. We respect you as a person and as a customer and as such, you have the right to privacy and to not be spammed.
> 
> **Disclosure of customer information and communications**
> 
> hostdime.com will not otherwise disclose its customers' personal and account information unless hostdime.com has reason to believe that disclosing such information is necessary to identify, make contact with, or bring legal action against someone who may be causing harm or interfering with the rights or property of hostdime.com, its customers, or others, or where hostdime.com. has a good faith belief that the law requires such disclosure.
> 
> hostdime.com also will not, except for reasons stated below, disclose to third parties the contents of any electronic mail or other electronic communications that hostdime.com stores or transmits for its customers. The circumstances under which hostdime.com will disclose such electronic customer communications are when:
> 
> • It is necessary in order to provide service to the customer;  
>  • It is necessary to protect the legitimate interests of hostdime.com and its customers;  
>  • It is required to cooperate with interception orders, warrants, or other legal process that hostdime.com determines in its sole discretion to be valid and enforceable; and  
>  • It is necessary to provide to a law enforcement agency when the contents are inadvertently obtained by hostdime.com and appear to pertain to the commission of a crime.  
>  • hostdime.com disclaims any intention to censor, edit or engage in ongoing review or surveillance of communications stored on or transmitted through its facilities by customers or others. hostdime.com will, however, review, delete or block access to communications that may harm hostdime.com, its customers or third parties. The grounds on which hostdime.com may take such action include, but are not limited to, actual or potential violations of hostdime.com's [Acceptable Use Policy](https://web.archive.org/aup.shtml).
