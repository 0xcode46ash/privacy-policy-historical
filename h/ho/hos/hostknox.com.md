# HostKnox.com Privacy Policy

[](https://web.archive.org/)

[](https://web.archive.org/clients/cart.php?a=add&pid=1&billingcycle=annually)

1\. Confidentiality  
HostKnox.com will not provide Customer's personal information to any third party and will keep all Customer information confidential. An exception to this are trusted business affiliates who work together with HostKnox.com and are subject to specific confidentiality agreements.  
If required by law, HostKnox.com will share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities.

2\. WHOIS Information  
Upon domain registration, the Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN) requires that certain Customer information is transferred to WHOIS (domain registration information) and made public. HostKnox.com cannot control how the WHOIS information is used by the public. The only legal way to make the WHOIS information private is by applying privacy protection to the Customer's domain.

3\. Customer's responsibility  
Although HostKnox.com will do everything possible to protect Customer's privacy, it is Customer's sole responsibility to maintain the privacy of his/her personal information.
