Content Games/videos are unrated and the possibility exists some games may contain violence, suggestive themes, crude humor, blood, gore, sexual content, simulated gambling, and/or use of strong language. 

Our Privacy

No information is sold or released to anyone about visitors to hotgirlsgames.net without prior consent or a court order. In other words, no information is released to anyone unless you give permission first. 

hotgirlsgames.net does not attempt to collect personal information from children. Note that hotgirlsgames.net cannot always determine that a user is a child. hotgirlsgames.net does not maintain databases of children or adults.

When visiting any Internet site, your unique address called an "IP address" is recorded. hotgirlsgames.net does not release any information about the collection of this address to any third party except under court order. hotgirlsgames.net archives the log files in order to create aggregate statistical reports, detect errors on the web site, and for security reasons. The log files are eventually deleted.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/). 

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. hotgirlsgames.net's service is free for all to use, and prior registration is not needed.

hotgirlsgames.net will explicitly ask when it needs information that personally identifies you. All personal identifiable information hotgirlsgames.net collects are only gathered with your voluntary act. hotgirlsgames.net keeps all information private and does not, and will not, share it with any third parties except under these circumstances:

\- When such action is necessary to conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process.

\- When such action is necessary to protect and defend the rights or property of hotgirlsgames.net.

hotgirlsgames.net uses cookies as part of tracking process for advertisement banners so you do not see the same ad repeatedly, but cookies will not be used to personally identify you without your prior consent.

hotgirlsgames.net uses subscription forms for individuals to subscribe to its ezine . hotgirlsgames.net will use your email address to provide you with the ezine, which is usually mailed daily. hotgirlsgames.net will never send you any unsolicited email outside of the ezine.

hotgirlsgames.net does not and will not share, sell or rent your email address to outside third parties. Neither does it supplement your information with any outside third parties. The contents of hotgirlsgames.net's mailing list will only be disclosed due to law or court demands. 

Unsubscribing from hotgirlsgames.net's ezine is simple and can be done by following the instructions at the end of every email sent.
