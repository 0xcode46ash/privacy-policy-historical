# Privacy Policy

As part of your registration, we collect your e-mail and ip address. This is used only to provide you with a user account, and for us to contact you if necessary. 

Your information will never be shared with or sold to any other parties except under the following circumstances: 

  * It is required by law to share the information with the appropriate agencies 
  * Hotness Rater is merged or acquired by another company. In this case, your information will be transferred to the new company and it will be subject to their privacy policy. 



We reserve the right to change this privacy policy. We will notify you of any significant changes in the way we treat personal information by e-mailing the e-mail address you provided on registration
