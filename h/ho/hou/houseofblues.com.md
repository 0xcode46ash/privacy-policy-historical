# Privacy : HOB.com

## Overview

House of Blues Digital, Inc. ("HOB") has created this privacy statement to disclose our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website, HOB.com and electronic communications. This statement answers the following questions: 

  * How is information collected?
  * How is my information used?
  * How is my information secured?
  * What if I'm a minor?
  * How can I correct and update my personal information, or unsubscribe/remove data from HOB's database?
  * Who can I ask if I have any additional questions?



* * *

## How is information collected?

You can visit HOB.com as a guest, remaining anonymous by not providing any personal information to us. Without this information, however, you will not be able to fully access all of the features and functionality of the site. We do collect some limited information about your visit but none of this information is personally identifiable to you. This information includes: your Internet location (IP address), your Internet entry point (ISP domain name), your software and its configuration used to access this site.

Also, we are provided information, including email addresses, from various partners when you enter into a transaction on their websites.

### Cookies

HOB.com uses cookies. A cookie is small amount of data, often containing an anonymous unique identifier, sent from our computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. We set cookies on your computer for a number of purposes, including to:

  * make sure you do not see the same ad repeatedly
  * deliver content specific to your interests
  * save your password so you do not have to re-enter it each time you visit our site



### Web beacons

Website pages and some email messages we send to you may contain electronic images known as web beacons. Web beacons allow us to track how many people elect to access content and features offered on such webpages and emails. We will not track or record your individual activity through web beacons for any purpose, other than as part of aggregate statistics we keep.

## How is my information used?

### Personally non-identifiable information

Aggregated, personally non-identifiable information collected by HOB.com is used to:

  * diagnose problems with our systems
  * identify and analyze trends
  * track user movement (anonymously) through our websites and electronic communications
  * better administer our online products
  * gather broad demographic information



We share aggregate demographic data with advertisers. These data are not linked to your personal information. 

### Personally identifiable information

Our site uses various forms for gathering information. Registration, survey, and contest forms all require some personal information. These forms may require you to provide:

  * contact information (e.g., name, email address),
  * demographic information (e.g., zip code, age, income level), and
  * other applicable information (e.g., credit card information).



This information is used to:

  * send prizes to contest winners,
  * send merchandise for online store purchases,
  * contact you when necessary, and
  * send information about our company or promotional material from our partners.



### Contest entrants

HOB.com regularly runs contests and giveaways sponsored, in part, by partner music labels. Participation in these contests is completely voluntary; you have the choice whether or not to disclose information required for contest registration. Information requested may include: contact information (such as name and shipping address), and demographic information (such as zip code, age level). Contact information is used to notify winners and award prizes. Additionally, we sometimes share registration information with sponsoring music labels. HOB.com is not responsible for the use of such information by these or other parties. By participating in HOB.com contests, you agree we may use your information for marketing or promotional purposes.

You may opt-out of receiving future mailings by following the instructions outlined below.

### Personalized experience

We may use your individual information in a variety of ways on our site and in our electronic communications. These methods may include: sending to you emails based on the information you provided or presenting you with particular offerings on our website based on your geographic location. When you enter a contest or take some action on our website that requires you to enter personal information, we may also use your personal information to automatically enter data into the required data fields for that contest or action.

## How is my information secured?

### Security of data during e-commerce transactions

HOB.com uses advanced security methods (Secure Socket Layer (SSL) 128-bit encryption) on all pages transmitting private financial information, such as credit card numbers. The SSL protocol ensures that your information is sent directly to HOB.com and that only HOB.com can decode it.

### Physical security of equipment storing personally identifiable information

Access to computer equipment (servers) storing personally identifiable information is restricted to authorized personnel. These personnel have access to this equipment only for administrative purposes. 

### Employee practices regarding security of personally identifiable information

Authorized personnel access users' information records for the sole purpose of system maintenance.

## What if I'm a minor?

### For minors

  * If you are under 18 years of age, you must let your parent or guardian know about this privacy policy and receive their permission before providing us any personal information. 
  * If you win a contest that has a prize your parent or guardian must mail us a signed note to the address listed in the contest rules which 
    1. grants you permission to enter, and
    2. tells us they will accept the prize for you.
  * You will be told to ask a parent before you pay for anything on HOB.com. 
  * Some areas of this website may have special rules for children under the age of 18. When you are in those areas, you must follow those rules. 
  * If your child has provided us with personally identifiable information, or if your child has used your credit card without your permission, you should contact us immediately by sending an email to [HOB Customer Service](https://web.archive.org/support/customerservice/emailform.asp). We will make all reasonable efforts to delete your child's information from our records. 



## How can I correct and update my personal information, or unsubscribe/remove data from HOB's database?

### Correct/update & choice/opt-out

HOB.com provides you the opportunity to change and modify information previously provided as well as opting-out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about you. This page gives you the option of removing information from our database in order to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our services. Corrections, updates, choice, and opt-out options can be modified by visiting the following URL: <http://www.hob.com/updates/>.
