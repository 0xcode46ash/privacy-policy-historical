**With respect to email:**  
We only collect the email addresses of those who subscribe to our newsletter. This information is _never_ sold, rented or traded to third parties. If you are a newsletter subscriber and you wish to unsubscribe, you may do so using the unsubscribe tool at the bottom of each newsletter. You can also email us and ask to be removed from the list. We do _not_ send unsolicited emails.

**With respect to postal mail:**  
We do not collect mailing addresses for any purpose. We do _not_ send unsolicited postal mail.

**With respect to phone calls:**  
Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line will only receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed online. We do _not_ make unsolicited phone calls.

**With respect to cookies:**  
By way of definition, a "cookie" is a bit of data created by a Web server and stored on a user's computer / web browser. Most computers / web browsers automatically accept cookies because they are often used to enhance the web user's experience (as is the case with any third-party cookies on our website).

We use third-party advertising services to display text ads and banner ads across our website. These ads are typically very relevant to the content of any given page, which increases the value and usefulness of our website for visitors such as yourself.

The ads generated by third parties may contain cookies that may be sent to your Internet browser through the aforementioned ads. We do not have control over cookies placed by our third-party advertisers, nor do we have access to any information their cookies may collect. However, if you prefer, you can adjust your web browser's settings so that it does not accept cookies from this or any other website.

**With respect to credit card transactions:**  
To ensure your security, we transfer the handling of all credit card transactions / purchases to our security-conscious partner, [ClickBank](http://www.clickbank.com/).
