# HomeGain -- Home valuations, find real estate agents, sell your home

We're committed to providing an online environment that is secure and private for our customers. To help ensure the security of all data transmissions -- including account viewing, submitting profiles and proposals -- we employ leading encryption technology and passwords selected by our customers. A high level of security and privacy protection is in place when you visit the HomeGain.com Control Centers.

** **Online Privacy**   
**

HomeGain.com was founded by Brad Inman, a leader in consumer real estate information and news, with a reputation built on a foundation of trust and integrity. The HomeGain.com service adheres to a philosophy based on trust and respect.   
We understand that the privacy and security of your profile information and information that personally identifies you are important to you. Our employees are responsible for adhering to confidentiality standards that are designed to protect our customers' personal information. Below is important information related to the protection and confidentiality of your personal and account information. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us at 1-888-542-0800.

** **HomeGain.com Web Site**   
**

When you browse the public areas of the HomeGain.com Web site, none of your account and personal information is obtained for storage at HomeGain.com.   
If you choose to use the HomeGain.com Control Center, which is designed for account holders only, you are required to provide your username and your password. Your password should never be shared with anyone and should be changed frequently. We use proven encryption methods to transmit account information across the Internet to our Control Center and we restrict access to the Control Center by using a login process rather than cookies or less secure means. Any related account and personal data maintained by HomeGain.com is done so to fulfill our legal and regulatory requirements and to provide you with the products and services you have requested.

** **E-mail at HomeGain.com** **

E-mail is an important communication channel for our online customers. E-mails sent automatically to agents or sellers through the function of the Control Center are kept anonymous. On occasion, we may use your e-mail address to send you communications with information about service and product information that we believe may be of interest to you. If you do not want to receive such unsolicited communications from HomeGain.com, simply [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20000620095656id_/http%3A//homegain.com/about_hg/contact). 

The ads appearing on this Web site are delivered to you by DoubleClick, our Web advertising partner. Please click here for more information on DoubleClick and its [privacy policy](http://www.doubleclick.net/privacy_policy/), information collected in the process of delivering an ad to you and your choices regarding delivery of targeted ad campaigns.
