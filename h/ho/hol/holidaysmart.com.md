# HolidaySmart Privacy Policy

**Privacy Notice**

Protecting your personal information is important to us. The information you provide to HolidaySmart is used for internal purposes only. We do not sell or make public your information. We take all measures to prevent loss, theft, or unintended use of your information. 

HolidaySmart may use your information internally for sending newsletters, product updates, sales, specials, additional services, etc. Upon your request, HolidaySmart will remove you from our mailing list. 

HolidaySmart links to outside websites that may request or collect your personal information. Your use of these sites is subject to the terms of those sites. HolidaySmart is not responsible for the policies, practices or content of the sites that we link to. 

HolidaySmart holds the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time without notice. By using our website and supplying us with your information, you agree to the terms of our Policies as listed on this site.
