# Privacy Policy - Personal Data Declaration – Holvi Support

**Privacy Policy**

Holvi Payment Services Ltd

Your privacy is important to us, and maintaining your trust and confidence is one of our highest priorities. We respect your right to keep your personal information confidential and understand your desire to avoid unwanted solicitations. We hope that by taking a few minutes to read our Privacy Policy, you will have a better understanding of what we do with the information you provide us and how we keep it private and secure.

Holvi and our marketing partners, affiliates, and analytics or service providers use cookies and other technologies to ensure everyone who uses our Services and Websites has the best possible experience. When you use our Services or visit our websites, we or a third party may place a number of cookies in your browser. Cookies also help us keep our Services and your Account safe.

When you visit the Website or use the Services, Holvi may place a number of cookies in your browser. We use cookies to keep any individual authorized to use the Services and to remember relevant information when the customer returns to the Services. We also use cookies within the Services and on our Websites to understand visitor and user preferences and improve their experience, and track and analyze usage, navigational and other statistical information. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. If you disable cookies, you may still visit the Websites and use the Services, but your ability use some features or areas of our Websites or Services may be limited.

You can generally accept or decline the use of cookies through a functionality built into your web browser. To learn more how to control cookie settings through your browser:

  * Click [here](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences) to learn more about the “Private Browsing” setting and managing cookie settings in Firefox;


  * Click [here](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en) to learn more about “Incognito” and managing cookie settings in Chrome;


  * Click [here](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/17442/windows-internet-explorer-delete-manage-cookies) to learn more about “InPrivate” and managing cookie settings in Internet Explorer; or


  * Click [here](https://support.apple.com/kb/PH17191?locale=en_US) to learn more about “Private Browsing” and managing cookie settings in Safari.



If you want to learn more about cookies, or how to control, disable or delete them, please visit http://www.aboutcookies.org for detailed guidance. In addition, certain third party advertising networks, including Google, permit users to opt out of or customize preferences associated with your internet browsing. To learn more about this feature from Google, click here.

Our Websites include third party social media features, such as the Facebook Like button and third party widgets, such as the ‘Share This’ button or interactive mini-programs that run on our Websites. These features may collect your IP address, which page you are visiting on our Websites, and set a cookie to enable the feature to function properly. Your interaction with these features is governed by the privacy policy of the third party company providing it.  


We may receive more information about you, such as name, email address, demographic information, IP addresses, location, and use of social media websites, by third parties.

Customer database

Processing of personal data to perform operations as a payment services provider and as well for customer relationship management and development. We also may use personal data for marketing purposes and to promote new services that may be of interest to you. This may be by post, telephone or email. If you do not wish to receive direct marketing from us please contact us with your request.

The register of existing or potential customers.

Identification data, such as

  * name

  * birth date
  * address
  * activity
  * start of customer relationship
  * ID documentation 
  * contact data such as phone number, email address, website and address
  * personal transaction data, such as account name and number, account status and site usage information



Information may be disclosed:

  * in case of official authorities requests based on a lawsuit or prosecution;
  * with third-party service providers we have contracted for business operations including payment, fraud prevention, validation of user credentials, secure data storage and other similar services;
  * with financial institutions, their agents or other related parties that we collaborate with to jointly create and provide you our service;
  * and at last, for legal reasons the Holvi shop owner details are publicly available in the online shop so your customers can easily contact you if needed.



Otherwise, information will be disclosed only under your consent or authorization.

Holvi ensures that these parties are properly informed of how to use Holvi data, and only use personal information in connection with the services they perform for Holvi. Holvi also shares personal information with certain financial institutions and collaborators or their agents that Holvi works with to jointly create and offer certain product. Personal data is obtained directly from the registered client or potential client. Data may be obtained from third parties such as credit bureaus and identity verification services upon agreement with the registrant. Site usage data is obtained by use of cookies. Cookies are not used to identify you personally. They enable the service to recognise your device so you do not have to give the same information several times during one task; to recognise that you may already have given a username and password so you do not need to do this for every web page requested and to measure how many people are using the service, so the service can be made easier to use and to ensure  there is enough capacity to ensure its speed and responsiveness.

Our live chat services are provided to support users as they navigate through applicable holvi.com pages (applicable pages are those with the live chat button appearing on them). Live chat support staff cannot assist with requests that are specific to accounts, any information that requires verification to disclose or process orders.

Holvi will never ask you to disclose personal details or account numbers through live chat. Please do not share any personal or account information through live chat.

The live chat service may keep transcripts of your live chat for quality assurance purposes. Chat log messages are encrypted before they are stored. Your live chat conversation information may be appended to your Holvi customer file. The live chat service is provided by a third party. Holvi does not accept any liability for any injury, loss or damage incurred by use of or reliance on the information and advice provided via the service.

The data is not kept in hard copy.
