Customer database

Processing of personal data to perform operations as a payment services provider and as well for customer relationship management and development. In addition, personal information may be used to identify the data subjects and for identification, webshop merchant management, risk management and legal compliance or administrative purposes.

The register of existing or potential customers.

Holvi Payment Services Ltd customer database includes the following data:

Identification data, such as

  * name
  * ID number
  * birth date
  * address
  * activity
  * start of customer relationship
  * contact data such as phone number, email address, website and address
  * personal transaction data, such as account name and number, account status and site usage information



Information may be disclosed:

  * in case of official authorities requests based on a lawsuit or prosecution;
  * with third-party service providers we have contracted for business operations including payment, fraud prevention, validation of user credentials, secure data storage and other similar services;
  * with financial institutions, their agents or other related parties that we collaborate with to jointly create and provide you our service;
  * and at last, for legal reasons the Holvi shop owner details are publicly available in the online shop so your customers can easily contact you if needed.



Otherwise, information will be disclosed only under your consent or authorization.

Holvi may share personal information with third-party service providers under contract who help with certain parts of our business operations including payment, fraud prevention, validation of user credentials, secure data storage and other similar services. Holvi ensures that these parties are properly informed of how to use Holvi data, and only use personal information in connection with the services they perform for Holvi. Holvi also shares personal information with certain financial institutions and collaborators or their agents that Holvi works with to jointly create and offer certain product. Personal data is obtained directly from the registered client or potential client. Data may be obtained from third parties such as credit bureaus and identity verification services upon agreement with the registrant. Site usage data is obtained by use of cookies. Cookies are not used to identify you personally. They enable the service to recognise your device so you do not have to give the same information several times during one task; to recognise that you may already have given a username and password so you do not need to do this for every web page requested and to measure how many people are using the service, so the service can be made easier to use and to ensure  there is enough capacity to ensure its speed and responsiveness.

If you click on a link to a third party site, you will leave our site that you are visiting and go to the site you selected. Because we cannot control the activities of third parties, we cannot accept responsibility for any use of your personally identifiable information by such third parties, and we cannot guarantee that they will adhere to the same privacy practices as we do. We encourage you to review the privacy statements of any other service provider from whom you request services. If you visit a third party website that is linked to our site, you should read that site's privacy statement before providing any personally identifiable information.

Upon the request to the contact person mentioned above, every data subject has the right to request to erase or supplement personal data contained in its personal data file and erroneous, unnecessary, incomplete or obsolete as regards the purpose of the processing in accordance with the Personal Data Act of Finland (523/1999)

For client and future client communication the following data will be transferred out of the territory of the member states of the European Union:

  * Name and surname
  * Electronic mail address



The use of the data is limited by user rights and all events will be kept in records.

The register is used only to persons whose work requires its use. Each user has a unique username and password. The users are also bound by professional secrecy. Registry permissions are granted and supervised by  the person responsible for managing the register.

The data is not kept in hard copy.

The Data Protection Ombudsman has the right of access to personal data which are being processed, as well as all information necessary for the supervision of the legality of the processing of personal data. The Data Protection Board has the same right.

You accept this Privacy Policy when you sign up for, access, or use our products, services, content, features, technologies or functions offered on our website and all related sites and applications. We may amend this policy at any time by posting a revised version on our website.

We value your trust and we are committed to protecting your privacy. Please contact us if you have any questions or if we can be of further service: https://support.holvi.com
