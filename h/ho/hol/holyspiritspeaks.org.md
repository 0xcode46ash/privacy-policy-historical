# Privacy Statement | The Church of Almighty God

## Privacy Policy of the Websites of the Church of Almighty God

This Privacy Policy discloses the privacy practices for the official websites of the Church of Almighty God (www.hidden-advent.org, [http://www.godfootsteps.org](http://www.godfootsteps.org/)), the gospel websites of the Church of Almighty God (www.kingdomsalvation.org, tr.kingdomsalvation.org, kr.kingdomsalvation.org, holyspiritspeaks.org), the gospel video website of the Church of Almighty God (tv.kingdomsalvation.org) and Eastern Lightning (www.easternlightning.org). The websites of the Church of Almighty God are set to preach the kingdom gospel of the end-time Christ, Almighty God, and they absolutely do not involve any commercial operations. The websites aim to provide convenience for all those who seek the truth, investigate the true way and yearn for the appearance of God. All the materials on these websites are free for reading and downloading. For convenience, these websites may include links to other websites. We do not assume the responsibility towards the practices and content of the privacy policy of such linked websites.

**Information We Collect**

1\. The information the websites of the Church of Almighty God will collect:

(1) The email address and name you fill when trying to contact us through the websites or using comment function.

(2) Logs of the web server software (Apache Httpd) when you are visiting the websites.

(3) Google Analytics will place cookies on the browser of your device and collect related information for traffic statistics.

2\. The Information Collected by Apache Httpd ([http://httpd.apache.org](http://httpd.apache.org/)) and Google Analytics (<http://www.google.com/analytics/>)

(1) Device information. Non-personal information of the computers, mobile devices and other devices you use to visit our websites, such as IP address, GPS, unique device identifiers, browser type, browser language.

(2) Browsing information. The way you enter this website (google search, shared link from facebook or typing in the URL directly) and the log of browsing this website, including the webpages you view, time you stay on each page, and the last webpage you browse. 

(3) The related information that Google Analytics collects by cookies. 

Please refer to <http://www.google.com/intl/en-us/policies/privacy/> “Cookies and similar technologies”

**Why We Collect the Information**

1\. Email Communication. We record your email information only for contacting you via email. 

2\. Statistical Analysis. We will know how users use our webistes by doing statistics to the access logs of Apache Httpd and using Google Analytics.

We will analyze the information including the daily number of visitors of the websites (IP address numbers) or the number in a period of time, the number of page views, time on each page, the website bounce rate, the user geography distribution, the percentage of access devices, the browser preferences, how the users go to our websites (by typing the address directly, by searching, or by clicking the links of the third parites like Facebook, Twitter, etc.) and the percentage. So we can analyze the site traffic, improve the user experience accordingly, and promote our sites (SEO, promotion on the third platforms like Facebook, Twitter, etc.)

**Promise and Choice**

The websites of the Church of Almighty God promise to collect only the above information from you and use them only for the above use cases. We will never release, sell, transfer, rent or share your information to any third party.

You may set your browser to block Google Analytics cookies.

If you have any questions or suggestions, please feel free to email us at [info@almightygod.church](mailto:info@almightygod.church).

**Update of Our Privacy Statement**

We may occasionally update this privacy statement. If there are any changes, we will mark “*” to the link of the “Privacy Statement” at the bottom of the websites.

This Privacy Policy was last updated in 17 January 2016.
