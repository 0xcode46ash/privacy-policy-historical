# Hairstyles for men, women, celebrity, short & prom hair styles

Hairstyles Design.com, and/or its advertising partners and network affiliates, are committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. The following statement details our ongoing support for the responsible collection and management of Internet user information.

**Internet User Information**

We do not collect any personal identifiable information about you, such as your name, address, telephone number or email address. HairstylesDesign.com does receive certain information which can not personally identify you. This latter category of information is generally referred to as non-personally-identifiable information includes information like the server or internet service provider you use, type of browser used, pages visited on our website, and general statistics on . There are some cases where you, the Internet user, may voluntarily provide personal information in response to a particular ad, or by originating an email to through our main Email address. In such cases, Hairstylesdesign.com (or a third party working with Hairstylesdesign.com) may collect this information on behalf of an advertiser. The advertiser in turn uses this information to reply to your request. 

Furthermore, Hairstylesdesign.com does not divulge any information regarding the users who use email for correspondence with the webmaster or any administrator of Hairstylesdesign.com. 

**Hairstylesdesign.com never sells any information, such as email address, to any third parties.** For inquires regarding this privacy policy, please forward to Info @ hairstylesdesign.com.
