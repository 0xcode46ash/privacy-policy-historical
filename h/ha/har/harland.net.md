# Welcome to the John H. Harland Website

INTRODUCTION/APPLICATION/SUMMARY  
Our website is intended for use by U.S. residents only and applies to personally identifiable information when you visit our website. 

This policy will explain how we collect, use and safeguard information you provide to our website.

OUR COMMITMENT TO PRIVACY  
We have issued this Policy Statement to explain our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your personal information is collected and used. 

THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT/HOW WE USE IT  
This policy applies to all personally identifiable information submitted or collected on our website.

A. Website Visitors  
When you access our website you may elect to leave your name and e-mail address. We do not retain such visitor personal information in our database unless you use our check printing service or are a business visitor. We do not disclose your personally identifiable information to others.

B. Check Printing Business  
If your bank or financial institution uses us to print your checks, it provides us with the following personal information about you so that we can print your personally identifiable checks:

  * Name
  * Address
  * Check Style
  * Account Number



If you submit a check reorder request directly to our website without a change to the foregoing information, you will need to provide us with your next check number and your e-mail address so that we can let you know that your reorder request is being processed. We use this information solely to print and mail to you your check order or reorder.

C. Other Business Visitors  
If you visit our website and wish to learn more about our market research or mortgage loan software and services, we will ask you to provide information such as:

  * Your name
  * Your title
  * Name of the financial institution by whom you are employed
  * The address of such institution, including e-mail address
  * Telephone number where you can be reached



We use this information to make personal contact with you and your financial institution so that we can provide you with additional information about our products and services either by telephone, direct mail, e-mail or personal visit.

OUR COMMITMENT TO DATA SECURITY  
We have established appropriate electronic and managerial procedures to prevent unauthorized access or use of personally identifiable information provided to us online. 

OUR COMMITMENT TO CHILDREN'S PRIVACY  
Our website is structured to avoid attracting visits by anyone under 13. It is our policy not to collect or maintain personally identifiable information from those who we know are under 13.

LINKS TO THIRD PARTY SITES  
Our website may contain links to websites controlled by third parties. Since we do not control these websites, we do not endorse or accept responsibility for their privacy practices or content.

HOW YOU ACCESS OR CORRECT YOUR INFORMATION  
You can access personally identifiable information which you have provided to our website and which is retained in our database by mailing a signed written request to:

John H. Harland Company  
P.O. Box 105250  
Atlanta, Georgia 30348

We require a signed written request to better protect your personally identifiable information. You can correct errors in your personally identifiable information which we maintain by sending a written request to correct such error together with data which reasonably establishes the existence of such an error.

HOW TO CONTACT US  
If you have other questions or concerns about this policy please click here to contact us. 

YOUR ACCEPTANCE OF OUR PRIVACY POLICY  
By using our website, you are consenting to the terms of this Privacy Policy. Please do not use our website if you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy. We reserve the right, at our discretion, to change, add or remove any part of this Privacy Policy at any time.
