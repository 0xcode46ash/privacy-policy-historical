# Privacy Policy - Hayden James

Please read the following to learn more about the Privacy Policy here. By visiting or using haydenjames.io you acknowledge that you accept the practices and policies outlined in this Privacy Policy. Haydenjames.io is committed to protecting the privacy of users. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please do not use this website.

### Information Privacy

When you submit blog comments to this site or send email using the contact form, Haydenjames.io collects the following information: your name, email and/or website address. Any such personal information you provide, will never be released, sold, or rented.

### Credit card details

Note that the haydenjames.io website will never ask for Credit Card details and request that you do not enter it on any of the forms on haydenjames.io.

### External Sites

You are advised to read the privacy policy of external sites before disclosing any personal information. Haydenjames.io is not responsible for the content of external internet sites.

### Cookies

A “cookie” is a small data text file that is placed in your browser and allows haydenjames.io to recognize you each time you visit this site. Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and the haydenjames.io website does not use cookies to collect any personal information. Cookies may also be used by 3rd party content providers such as newsfeeds or ads.

For questions or more information please [contact Hayden James](http://haydenjames.io/contact-me/).
