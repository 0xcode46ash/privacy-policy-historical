# Privacy Policy - Linux Systems Analyst | Hayden James

Please read the following to learn more about the Privacy Policy here. By visiting or using haydenjames.io you acknowledge that you accept the practices and policies outlined in this Privacy Policy. Haydenjames.io is committed to protecting the privacy of users. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please do not use this website.

### Information Privacy

When you submit blog comments, use the contact form or signup for newsletters, this website collects the following information: your name and email. Any such personal information you provide, will never be released, sold, or rented.

### Credit card details

Note that the haydenjames.io website will never ask for Credit Card details and request that you do not enter it on any of the forms on haydenjames.io.

### External Sites

You are advised to read the privacy policy of external sites before disclosing any personal information. Haydenjames.io is not responsible for the content of external internet sites.

### Cookies

A “cookie” is a small data text file that is placed in your browser and allows haydenjames.io to recognize you each time you visit this site. Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and the haydenjames.io website does not use cookies to collect any personal information. Cookies are most often used by 3rd party providers such as newsfeeds, ads or signup boxes. (so that the website does not keep showing you the same ads and/or signup boxes repeatedly.)

### Your Data Protection Rights Under The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

HaydenJames.io will use the information you provide via the newsletter subscribe form to be in touch with you and to provide updates. You can change your mind at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in the footer of any email you receive from us, or by contacting us at [in@haydenjames.io](mailto:in@haydenjames.io). We respond to all requests we receive from individuals wishing to exercise their data protection rights in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

We use MailChimp as our newsletter marketing automation platform in accordance with their [Privacy Policy](https://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy/) and [Terms](https://mailchimp.com/legal/terms/). This is by opt-in only and you can opt-out at anytime by clicking the unsubscribe link in the footer of any email you receive from us, or by contacting us at [in@haydenjames.io](mailto:in@haydenjames.io).

For questions or more information please [contact Hayden James](https://haydenjames.io/contact-me/).
