# Company Privacy Policy | Hackett

### Legal

Hackett is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to its Web Site. HACKETT analyses aggregate and non-personally identifiable data relating to usage of the Web Site. In accordance with UK data protection legislation, any personal information collected is done so with the knowledge and authorisation of users upon registration, request or submission to the Web Site. Information provided at the time of registration or submission will be used to provide the requested information and may be used for marketing research relating to HACKETT. It will not be used for any unsolicited communication to users unless so authorised by them. HACKETT will use its best endeavours to ensure that such data is kept secure at all times.  
Moreover Hackett may also occasionally sell, rent or otherwise make available any personal data submitted by visitors to its Website to other carefully selected third-parties unless not required to do so by law and not authorised by visitors. Upon request, HACKETT will remove users personal information from its database thereby cancelling their registration. Furthermore, upon request, registered visitors may access and/or correct any erroneous information.
