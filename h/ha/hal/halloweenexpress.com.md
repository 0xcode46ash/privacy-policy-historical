# Halloween Costumes

We take your business seriously and use every reasonable precaution necessary to ensure your shopping experience is safe! Halloween Express uses Secure Socket Layer (aka “SSL”) encrypting to keep your private information and credit card information as secure as possible. 

Halloween ETailers LLC is the only operator collecting or maintaining personal information through this website. The contact information for Halloween Express is: Halloween ETailers LLC | [webmaster@halloweenexpress.com](mailto:webmaster@halloweenexpress.com)

Now and in the future, Halloween Express may actively collect additional personal information and may survey its visitors on a wide variety of topics such as hobbies, personal interests, and similar topics. Additional information might also be collected by the visitors use of the site through, for example, participation in a sweepstakes or contest, when making a purchase through a Halloween Express retailer, or completing a survey. If a visitor contacts Halloween Express we may keep a record of that correspondence. In addition, Halloween Express currently passively collects information related to site usage, such as time spent on site, areas visited, sites linked to and from by visitor. In the future, Halloween Express may passively collect additional information on a wide variety of site-related usage topics, including installing "cookies" on visitors' hard drives.

Halloween Express _does not_ disclose personal information collected from visitors to third parties. Halloween Express may, in the future, disclose personal information to companies with which it is affiliated by common ownership. Halloween Express may retain third parties to review certain personal information to advise it on demographic and marketing related issues, typically on an aggregated basis, and only after such third parties have agreed to maintain the confidentiality, security and integrity of any personal information obtained.
