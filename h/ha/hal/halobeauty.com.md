# Privacy Policy | Afterglow Cosmetics

## Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions

**PRIVACY STATEMENT**

Your online privacy is of the utmost importance to us. To ensure your privacy, we secure your transaction with 128 bit encryption. In addition, we never sell or rent your personal information to any third parties under any circumstances. Your trust in our company and the security of your personal information are our primary concern. Our Afterglow Cosmetics servers only collect the IP address, domain name, and user specific information on the pages you access or visit. We also collect any e-mail or correspondence, as well as shipping information when creating an account or e-mailing us directly. ** **

**TRANSACTION SECURITY**

When you place an order online, your personal information and credit card information are encrypted using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption technology before being sent over the Internet. This technology makes it impossible for your information to be stolen or intercepted while being transferred and processed by PayPal or our Merchant Account for processing. The entire credit card transaction is done by the highly respected, trusted third-party, [PayPal Corp](https://www.paypal.com/) and our merchant account using bank level encryption. All PayPal and credit card transaction from our website are protected by high levels of encryption with the use of firewalls, as well as other protective measures to ensure that your private information is completely secure during the transaction. All information held by Afterglow Cosmetics, PayPal, and our merchant account is held as strictly confidential. ** **

**EMAIL PRIVACY**

We love and respect your email privacy. We will never rent, sell, or trade your email address. By providing your email address when requesting to receive and/or be included in exclusive Afterglow promotions, Afterglow discounts, free Afterglow eBooks and during the ordering process, you are accepting the full Privacy Policy, Terms and Conditions of our website, which includes a subscription to our eNewsletter. You may opt-out of of our eNewsletter at any time by clicking the unsubscribe button provided at the bottom of all Afterglow emails.

**PHONE NUMBER PRIVACY**

We will only contact you by phone if we have a question or concern about your order. We will not call you for any other purpose or share your phone number with other companies. ** **

**SNAIL MAIL PRIVACY**

We receive your mailing address online when you set up an account or order a product. You may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products, sales, and events. If you prefer not to receive our mailings, please e-mail us at [support@afterglowcosmetics.com](mailto:support@afterglowcosmetics.com). Please include your name as it appears on your account and your complete mailing address requesting removal.

**NOTIFICATION OF CHANGES TO THE CUSTOMER SERVICE & PRIVACY POLICIES**

We reserve the right to revise our Customer Service Policy, Privacy Policy, and Store Policies as needed. Any changes or revisions to the Afterglow Cosmetic policies will be posted here. We reserve the right to make changes at any time without notifying you, the customer. We suggest that you periodically consult this Privacy Statement & Store Policies. Please note that our rights to use your personally identifiable information will be based on the privacy policy in effect at the time the information is used.
