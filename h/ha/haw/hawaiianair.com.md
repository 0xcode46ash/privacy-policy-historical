# Hawaiian Airlines : Privacy Policy

|   
  
  


When you visit the Hawaiian Airlines web site, you can be confident that any information shared with us will always be treated confidentially and will not be disclosed to anyone outside of Hawaiian Airlines or its Preferred Partners. 

**E-mail Addresses:**  
The e-mail addresses provided to Hawaiian Airlines or HawaiianMiles will not be shared with any third parties, except as described below. If you wish to subscribe or unsubscribe from any of Hawaiian Airlines e-mail lists or need more information, please call us toll-free at 877-HA-MILES (426-4537). 

**Your Hawaiian Airlines PIN (Personal Identification Number):**  
You have a personal PIN number that serves as your personal password to your account and is designed to protect your account from unauthorized use. Hawaiian Airlines will not be responsible for any lost, stolen, or otherwise disclosed PIN numbers. Additionally, Hawaiian Airlines will not replace or reinstate any flight credits or HawaiianMiles, partner miles, or program awards that are generated or redeemed by unauthorized PIN use. Accordingly, your PIN number should be guarded and not provided to anyone. 

**Billing Information for E-Tickets (Ticketless Travel) Online:**  
Any information provided to purchase E-Tickets Online (including billing name, billing address, telephone number, fax number, e-mail, and travel details) will not be shared with anyone outside of Hawaiian Airlines, except as described below. Payment information (credit card numbers and expiration dates) is also treated confidentially and will not be shared with anyone outside of the financial institutions used to process payments. 

**Hawaiian Airlines and HawaiianMiles Special Offers:**  
Hawaiian Airlines may use the name and address information or the e-mail address you provide to send you special offers that we feel may be interesting and useful for you. In some instances, we may provide Customer lists to our Preferred Partners so you can receive special offers or ways to earn HawaiianMiles awards more quickly. Hawaiian Airlines requires our Preferred Partners to ensure safeguards are always in place to protect the privacy of any information we share. We try to be careful in our selection of these Partners to ensure your privacy and the value of their offers. If you wish to be excluded from any such special offers, or have your e-mail address unsubscribed from any electronic offers please contact us toll-free at 877-HA-MILES (426-4537). 

**Third Party Advertising Company, Avenue A:**  
We use a third party advertising company, Avenue A, to serve ads on our behalf across the Internet. Avenue A may also collect anonymous information about your visits to our Web site. This is primarily accomplished through the use of a technology device, commonly referred to as a Web beacon or an action tag, which we place on various Web pages within our Web site or that allows Avenue A to collect anonymous information. Avenue A may use information about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide ads about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by Avenue A, “[click here.](http://www.avenuea.com/privacy/default.asp)” 

You should be aware that we require the same safeguards for the information received over the Internet as we do for customer information received by telephone and mail. Only Hawaiian Airlines or our approved Preferred Partners will send you special offers.
