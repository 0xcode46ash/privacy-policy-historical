# HYLUNIA.COM - privacystatement

Hylunia International Inc. RESPECTS THE PRIVACY OF ITS ON-LINE VISITORS. Personal information that can identify you, an on-line visitor is collected only when voluntarily offered by you. Non-personal information is collected from all on-line visitors so that we can track the total number of visitors to our site and identify the type of Internet browser and operating system used by the visitor. This information enables us to continuously improve our site and our service to you.

Any changes in this privacy policy will be promptly disclosed in these pages.

Hylunia International Inc.
