# Privacy Policy

Our goal is to always protect your privacy while visiting our website.  This page will explain this websites online information practices.  We want to be completely transparent in the way your information maybe used.  You will easily be able to access the privacy page right from the top menu on our homepage.

As a part of some Google advertisement on our website, they will use cookies to serve certain ads.  In turn this enables Google to serve advertisements to visitors that are based on their search web statistics.  If you want to opt out of this form of (DART) cookie tracking you can visit this url:  <http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html>.  This tracking mechanism is subject privacy policies which belong to Google.    


Different third party ad networks may also use cookies that track visitors and their activities on this website.   Our website ([www.hyroo.com](http://www.hyroo.com/)), has no control over these third party cookies.

When you are visiting [www.hyroo.com ](http://www.hyroo.com/), the IP address that you are using to visit this website will be recorded along with the date and time you visited the website.  This is just used to analyze various trends, as well as gather demographic information which is used internally.  Any recorded IP address **IS NOT** linked to any personal and identifiable information.

This website also contains certain links to other websites that will be useful for you as a visitor.  We cannot be responsible for the policies on those particular websites.  Other websites may have different privacy policies, and you should familiarize yourself with them.

**WE RESERVE THE RIGHT TO ALTER THIS PRIVACY POLICY AT ANY TIME, AT OUR DISCRETION.**
