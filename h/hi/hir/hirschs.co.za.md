# Hirsch's - Best brands, Biggest Range, Greater Savings

Your privacy is very important to Hirsch's and therefore any information you provide to us is stored on a secure server.

Hirsch's may use your information to contact you about promotions and special offers. You are entitled, at any stage, to opt out of this service. We do not sell or rent personal information about individual members (such as name, address, email address, telephone or fax number) to third parties.

Hirsch's may disclose personal information in response to a specific request by a law enforcement agency, subpoena, court order, or as required by law.
