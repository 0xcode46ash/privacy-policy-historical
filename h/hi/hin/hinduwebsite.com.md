# Hinduwebsite.com Privacy Policy.

At Hinduwebsite.com we value your privacy more than anything else. We collect information directly from you, with your knowledge and consent, such as your name or email address, to send you any specific information or our newsletter you might  have requested or subscribed to. We do not use any hidden cookies in our pages to collect any personal information about any one. 

We have access to the number of persons who visit our site or the number of pages and URL's they browsed through. This is a general information about our visitors which is basically statistical in nature, without any reference to any particular individual. This information helps us to improve our site and make it more useful to all of you. The addresses that we so collect from our members with their consent and knowledge  will not be given away to others under any circumstances. 

However Hinduwebsite.com offers a number of services like free email service, shopping resources, merchant services, news portal services etc, through affiliate programs, which are not entirely under the control of Hinduwebsite.com. When a visitor visits these sites or joins them as a member, or avails the services and products offered from there, he or she may have to provide some information. Whatever information that is provided under such circumstances is subject to the privacy policy guidelines of the respective service providers and Hinduwebsite.com shall not be held responsible for the use or misuse of such information. The visitors are requested to read the privacy policy of each site carefully before providing any personal information about them. The visitors are also requested to read the [terms of use](https://web.archive.org/web/20010411175144id_/http%3A//www.hinduwebsite.com/utilities/termsofuse.htm) before using any of the services provided by Hinduwebsite.com.
