# Privacy

| 

**Privacy Policy** Hilton Grand Vacations Company® (HGVC®) is committed to protecting its relationship with you. As such, HGVC would like to take this opportunity to share with you its privacy practices and policy regarding the sharing of information HGVC receives from you. We understand that requesting information online involves a great deal of trust on your part; we take this trust very seriously, and make it our priority to ensure the security and confidentiality of the non-public personal information ("NPPI") you provide us. As a visitor to www.hiltongrandvacations.com, you have the right to understand our information privacy practices prior to providing hiltongrandvacations.com with any of your information. 

This HGVC Privacy Statement discloses the information privacy practices for www.hiltongrandvacations.com. The site is intended for use by visitors that are over 18 years of age. By visiting www.hiltongrandvacations.com, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy.

 **Information HGVC collects about you**

HGVC collects non-public personal information ("NPPI") about you that you specifically and voluntarily provide. NPPI information may include your full name, membership number (HHonors or other), home phone number, work phone number, street address, city, state/province, postal code, country, income range, email address, username and password. 

On all HGVC pages that collect NPPI, we specifically describe what information is required in order to provide you with the product or service you have requested. These pages may include surveys, contests, sweepstakes, travel reservation requests, and contact pages. 

This site does not contain Third Party Advertising. 

Additionally HGVC collects certain technical information from your computer each time you request a page during a visit to HGVC. This information may include your IP address, operating system, Web browser software (i.e., Netscape Navigator or Internet Explorer), screen resolution, and referrer Web site. 

**Sharing your information** HGVC does not sell or rent your NPPI to anyone. However as a subsidiary of Hilton Hotels Corporation, HGVC may share your information with the Hilton Family. HGVC may contract a reputable third party to facilitate the distribution of survey, promotion, or sweepstakes programs. Upon completion of these projects, all information is returned to HGVC. 

We do not share information about you with non-affiliates (companies outside our organization) for them to contact you for their own marketing purposes. The law permits us to share information about our current and former customers with government agencies or authorized third parties under certain circumstances. For example, we may be required to share such information in response to subpoenas or to comply with certain laws. 

**Our obligations** HGVC may be required by law to disclose information you have provided in obtaining our travel products or services. HGVC may also disclose information against someone who poses a threat to HGVC's interests (such as customer fraud) or whose activities could bring harm to others.  **Communications from HGVC**

Visitors will receive communications from HGVC when they: 

  * Request information on any/all HGVC products and services.
  * Elect to receive special promotional marketing communications
  * Win a contest or sweepstakes
  * Contact HGVC with questions/suggestions



**Request customer support or submit questions/suggestions**

When you request assistance regarding your itinerary and confirmation package, we will make every effort to respond to you via e-mail or telephone within 24 hours. 

**E-mail subscriptions** When you communicate with HGVC as stated above, you may be automatically subscribed to our e-mail list. You may occasionally receive e-mails notifying you of special promotions or other travel news. Special promotional e-mails are intended to inform you of new or unique travel opportunities or special deals that are available from HGVC. Out of respect for our users privacy we provide a way to opt-out of these communications. 

**Request to Unsubscribe**

You may unsubscribe to all HGVC e-mail at any time. To unsubscribe or "opt-out", please visit [www.hiltongrandvacations.com/optout/unsubscribe.html](http://www.hiltongrandvacations.com/optout/unsubscribe.html)

 **Changes to the HGVC Privacy Statement** This Privacy Statement was produced and posted on HGVC on April 16, 2004. HGVC reserves the right to modify or amend this Privacy Statement at any time for any reason and without notice. 

If you have questions about our Privacy Statement, please email-us at privacy@hgvc.com. 

**Contacting HGVC** We want to make sure you have control over your online travel experience as an HGVC customer, from planning your travel to protecting your personal information. 

HGVC customer service vacation guides are ready to help with your HGVC travel reservation concerns. Our vacation guides will address any questions or concerns you may have about your HGVC travel planning. We will do our best to respond to your request for assistance or other questions about HGVC within 24 hours.
