# Hitpulse.com - Traffic Exchange

Our privacy policy is a simple one. The information we collect about you will always be kept confidential and not released to anyone. We will not post your personal information, nor sell it or give it away. Any information we have about you will be used solely by Hitpulse.com or its managing company (Logiscape Technologies Inc.). Your personal information will never be given to your upline or downline for any reason.

This policy becomes void if the member is finds themself in legal trouble with authorities under which circumstances we may have no choice but to forward your personal information on to the appropriate authorites for investigation.
