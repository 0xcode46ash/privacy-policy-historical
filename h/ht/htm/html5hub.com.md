# HTML5 Hub | Privacy Policy

## Personal Information Collected:

We may collect certain personal information from you in various ways when you use our websites and applications. Types of personal contact information collected may include full names, postal address, email address, gender, phone number(s), Facebook User ID, and other social platform identifications. In addition, when visiting our Applications, some information may be automatically collected through technologies such as cookies, web beacons and web server logs. Third-party web analytics services may also be used on our applications that collect information through the same technologies. Information collected by these technologies may include your computer’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, access times, operating system, browser type and languages, referring Web site addresses, Facebook user ID, Facebook Page fan status, and the country and time zone in which your device is located. We use this information, to analyze trends, to administer the site or application, to track users’ movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user-base as a whole.

## How we use the information collected:

We may use personal information we collected about you to:

  * Provide, administer and communicate with you about products, services, events and promotions.
  * Comply with and enforce applicable legal requirements, industry standards and our policies and terms.
  * Protect against and prevent fraud, unauthorized activities, claims and other liabilities.
  * We use both your Personal information and certain non-personally identifiable information to personalize and improve your experience on our Applications.
  * We use cookies, web beacons, and web server logs information to remember information so that you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time you visit our applications,
  * Monitor the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns and monitor aggregated metrics such as total number of visitors, pages viewed, etc.



##  What data you will share:

We may share your Personal Information and non-personal identifiable information to our Application Partners (clients) so they may monitor the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns and communicate with you about products, services, events, and promotions. Our Application partner’s use of your personal information may be subject to their separate privacy policy. Application partner’s privacy policy is linked from within the Partner’s Facebook application In addition, we may share the information with our service providers who perform services on our behalf. These service providers are not authorized by us to use or disclose the information except as necessary to perform services on our behalf or comply with legal requirements. We may disclose information about you if we are required to do so by law or legal process order, in response to a request by law enforcement authorities, to take precautions against liability, to investigate and defend itself against any third-party claims or allegations, or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with state and federal laws/ We also reserve the right to transfer personal information we have about you in the event we sell or transfer all or a portion of our business or assets.

For more information please email [info@html5hub.com](http://mailto:info@html5hub.com/).
