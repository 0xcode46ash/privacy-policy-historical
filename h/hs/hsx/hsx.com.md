# HSX : About : HSX Privacy Policy

**This is the web site of the Hollywood Stock Exchange®.**

For each visitor to our Web site, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name and Internet Protocol address (where possible), but not the e-mail address. We collect the domain names and Internet Protocol addresses of visitors to our Web site, the e-mail addresses of those who post messages to our bulletin board, register for a Hollywood Stock Exchange® account, or communicate with us via e-mail. We also aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit and information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registration information. 

The aggregate and non-personally identifiable information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web site and is used for market research purposes. These purposes may be achieved directly by the Hollywood Stock Exchange® or by the transfer by HSX Holdings, Inc. of aggregate non-personally identifiable information to third parties who will be analyzing this information on our behalf or for their own purposes. Absolutely no other commercial uses will be made of this information by us or by any third parties. 

The personally identifiable information we collect is used to customize the content, product offerings and/or layout of our web site for each individual consumer, used to notify consumers about updates to and activities on our web site, used by us to contact consumers for information verification purposes, but it is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes. Hollywood Stock Exchange® will also not release any personally identifiable information for market research purposes. We may ourselves use your personally identifiable information to learn more about you and to provide you with more information on goods and services which we feel may be of interest to you. 

If you do not want to receive bulk e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending an e-mail to us at [unsubscribe@hsx.com](mailto:unsubscribe@hsx.com). 

If you supply us with your postal address on-line you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending email to us at [nopostal@hsx.com](mailto:nopostal@hsx.com). 

Please provide us with your exact name and address. We will be sure your name is removed from the lists we use for these purposes. 

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding e-commerce orders they have placed on-line. They also may receive telephone contact from us in connection with our need to verify personal information previously given, solely for our own marketing and market research purposes. If you do not wish to receive such telephone calls, please let us know by sending email to us at [nocalls@hsx.com](mailto:nocalls@hsx.com). Please provide us with your correct phone number. We will be sure your name and telephone number are removed from the lists we use for these purposes. 

Thank you for visiting the Hollywood Stock Exchange®.
