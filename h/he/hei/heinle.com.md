# Heinle - Privacy Policy

Heinle/Thomson Learning Internet Privacy Policy

At Heinle/Thomson Learning, we recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of our customers. The information that we collect at this web site is confidential and will be used for internal purposes only. We will not sell, rent or release any information gathered at this site to a third party. So that you feel comfortable while browsing our site, we have outlined below exactly what information we gather and how it is used. If you have any questions about our privacy policy or our web procedures, feel free to contact us:

Heinle  
Attn: Webmaster  
25 Thomson Place  
Boston, MA 02210  
e-mail: [reply@heinle.com](mailto:reply@heinle.com)

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web pages, to notify customers about updates to our Web site, to improve our products, to contact customers for marketing purposes, and to receive feedback from customers about the products we publish and related course and market information. It is not shared with other organizations.

For each visitor to our Web pages, our Web server automatically recognizes the consumer's domain name. In general, we collect only the domain name, not the e-mail address of visitors to our web pages. However, we do collect e-mail addresses when messages are posted to our bulletin board, when postings are made to our chat areas, when e-mail addresses are volunteered on our subscription page, survey pages and/or site registrations, and when customers communicate with us via e-mail. 

If you supply us with your e-mail address on-line you may receive periodic e-mail from us with information on new products and services. If you receive e-mail communication from us and do not want to continue receiving it in the future, please let us know by contacting us via e-mail, phone, or mail. Please provide us with your exact name and e-mail address. 

If you supply us with your postal address on-line you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by contacting us via e-mail, phone or mail. Please provide us with your exact name and address. 

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed on-line. They also may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such telephone calls, please let us know by contacting us via e-mail, phone or mail.

We use WebTrends Live to conduct anonymous web traffic analysis for our site. While WebTrends Live makes use of cookies, no personal information is ever collected or analyzed by this service.
