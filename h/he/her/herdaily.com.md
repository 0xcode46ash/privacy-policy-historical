# PRIVACY POLICY - Her Daily

Her Daily takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we treat and use all information we collect when you sign in to herdaily.com or sign up for our news blasts and alerts.

Your personal information

In order to supply you with Her Daily emails, we need some information about you. Her Daily doesn’t collect any information except that expressly provided by you, to us. We will not disclose your personal information to any third party without your expressed consent, except as required to provide you with Her Daily material you request. Her Daily will never sell, rent, or share your personal information, including your email address, to any third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Other provided information

If our site ever requests additional, voluntarily provided information from you, we will use this information to improve and tailor our content. We may share this information with third parties, such as advertisers, but only in aggregate, anonymous form. We will never provide a third party with information that personally identifies you.

Information about usage

Along with collecting the information stated above, Her Daily may gather statistics (that are not personally identifiable) relating to how our site is visited and used. Examples of these statistics could include how much time a user spends on the site. This information is collected to help us create a better, more efficient, faster website with the best, most personalized content that readers love.

Our advertising

The ads that appear on Her Daily are delivered to you by PMI 5 Media, LLC.

Links to other sites

As you can see from browsing Her Daily, we sometimes link you to other sites. When doing so, we are not responsible for any content that appears on these sites. Her Daily does not endorse the sites we link to. Each site we link to has its own unique privacy policy.

Cookies (not the yummy kind)

Like other websites, Her Daily may place or recognize cookies or small text files on your browser. This helps us retrieve information about you in order to provide the most tailored service. Cookies allow Her Daily to recognize you each time you visit the site — and simplify your user experience. We will never use cookies to obtain information from your computer if it’s not related to our site.

Third party cookies

In the course of placing advertisements to our site, a third-party advertiser may place or recognize a cookie on your browser.

Security

All of the information described above is recorded through restricted database servers.

Modifications

Her Daily may make modifications to any or all of these policies at any point in time. Be sure to check this page often if you’re interested in our policies.

Your access to Her Daily information

Upon personal request, Her Daily will happily provide you with a complete summary of the information we’ve collected about you.

Tell us what you think

If you have questions about the Her Daily privacy policy (or our site in general), please email us at talk@herdaily.com.  
If you’d like to “unsubscribe” to Her Daily emails at any point in time, click the unsubscribe link in the footer of any Her Daily email — and then follow the instructions.
