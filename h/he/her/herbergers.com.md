# Herberger's | Privacy Statement

  
Our commitment to you as our valued customer is to provide an exciting shopping experience. In order to do so, we may ask to obtain information about you so that we may improve our level of service to our customers. We welcome your opinions about the products and services that we provide. Because we respect your privacy, we always handle this information in a safe and responsible manner. To best serve you, we may share that information with other members of our Saks family of companies, but that information will not be shared outside of the Saks family of companies. We will continue to find new ways to enhance your shopping experience. [We welcome your comments, questions and suggestions](http://www.saksincorporated.com/our_stores/questions.html) about our site!
