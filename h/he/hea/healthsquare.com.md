**Privacy**

New Media Systems,LLC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.

As part of our  registration, question,  comment and suggestion forms. we  request that  users provide us certain   information (such as name and email address) and demographic information (such as zip code, age, or sex). The  contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. This information is shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis. 

**HealthSquare does not release any specific personal identifying information you may provide to us to any third parties . Please be assured that the aggregated   information cannot   be used to identify a specific visitor. **

**HealthSquare does not release any contact information you may provide to us to any third parties.**

**Please note:** HealthSquare contains links to other sites. HealthSquare is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

  


* * *

**Cookies**  


A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computer and is stored on your computer's hard drive.   


HealthSquare uses  "cookies"  solely to measure site traffic at an aggregate level. We do not use cookies to collect or store any personel information. We do not provide advertisers access to our cookies. Any cookies used by HealthSquare would expire within a year. 

HealthSquare allows  3rd party advertising networks and/or advertisers directly to display ads on our site. These ads may also contain cookies. The 3rd-party cookies that advertisers place on your hard drive may permit advertising networks to record your movements across the Internet and track over time the other sites that you visit.  


However, HealthSquare does not share the personal information that we collect with any 3rd party advertisers and we do not associate any personal information with 3rd party cookies. 

**If   you would rather not have cookies set, most browsers allow you to reject cookies or to choose which cookies to accept. **
