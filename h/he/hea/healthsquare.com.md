# New Visitor Page

  
Getting to know our visitors, help us make HealthSquare better.

**Please Note:**  


**Privacy**

New Media Systems,LLC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.

As part of our  registration, question,  comment and suggestion forms. we  request that  users provide us certain   information (such as name and email address) and demographic information (such as zip code, age, or sex). The  contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. This information is shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis. 

**HealthSquare does not release any specific personal identifying information you may provide to us to any third parties . Please be assured that the aggregated   information could in no way  be used to identify a specific visitor. **

**HealthSquare does not release any contact information you may provide to us to any third parties.**

**Cookies**  
HealthSquare uses  "cookies"  to measure site traffic and report aggregate information to advertisers such as the number of people who see their ad (reach) and the number of times people see their ad (frequency).

HealthSquare allows  3rd party advertising networks and/or advertisers directly to display ads on our site. These ads may also contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information. Nor do we share our data with them

**If   you would rather not have cookies set, most browsers allow you to reject cookies or to choose which cookies to accept. **

**Please note:** This site contains links to other sites. HealthSquare is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
