# Healthy Choice Privacy Policy

The following is a description of how we handle information we learn about you from your visit to our website. The information we receive depends upon what you do when visiting our site.

What we do not collect is personally identifying information about you when you visit our site unless you chose to provide such information to us. Again, we never share, lease or giveaway that information to third parties.

We may update this Policy from time to time, so please check in occasionally.
