# Privacy Policy - Henry's best camera store in Canada

## Henry's is committed to insuring your privacy.

We do not swap or sell our mailing lists or personal information files. We never have.

All info collected on this site is for processing of orders and/or sending eNewsletters to customers who have subscribed to receive them. This information is only accessed by authorized Henry's employees.

For information on our security policies, [click here](https://web.archive.org/Security-Policy.aspx).
