# Games Free: Privacy Policy

## GF Network

This is an official privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") for the free onlien game website gamesfree.com ("Site," "we," "us," or "our"), an Internet website owned and operated by Games Free Networks, Inc (GF). This Privacy Policy governs only the personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") collected by the Site. GF network is committed to protecting your privacy online. To further this commitment, we have adopted an Online Privacy Policy that guides how we collect, store, and use the information collected about you online. 

### Personal Information

gamesfree.com is committed to protecting your privacy online. gamesfree.com does not collect any personal information and our main goal is to provide you with daily free online games. 

### Logging

Your IP address is being logged to gather demographic information and to visualize visiting paterns (how many pages you views, gameplays, downloads, etc). We will never log individual details, or anything that might give us a clue who you are. 

### Third Party Advertising

We use third-party advertising networks to serve advertisements while you visit gamesfree.com. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. 

### Third Party Cookies

Our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser. This cookie is solely being used to track wheter or not you want to view popups, banners etc. 

### Opt Out

You can opt out most advertisers and their cookies [here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp). Please note that the nai service will create additional cookies on your machine as well. 

### Liability

It is gamesfree.com's policy to respond to claims of intellectual property violation within 48 hours. once receiving notification in accordance with any related copyright organization or owner, gamesfree.com will investigate the issue and disable or remove games where is needed.
