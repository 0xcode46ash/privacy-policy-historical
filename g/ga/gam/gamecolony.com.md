# Privacy and Security

### Your Privacy and Security Are Important To Us!

This statement demonstrates our firm commitment to player Privacy and Security on our site. 

We only use the information we collect on the site to enhance your overall gaming experience and to keep a **_CLEANER_** site. 

We **_NEVER_** share, sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any 3rd party. We are committed to making this site safer for our members and have instituted a strong and comprehensive safety and compliance program. 

  * We monitor player traffic patterns and site usage to help us develop and enhance the design of our gaming web site. 
  * We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with your connection and to gather general demographic usage information. 



Public Chat 

  * This site makes chat room available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. 



Security 

  * All of the player data we collect is protected against unauthorized access. In order to maintain data accuracy and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect on-line. All transactions are run through regularly maintained encrypted pathways to ensure security. The site and the software have **military-grade security** measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. 
  * When you access your account information, it is protected by our highly secure encoding and processing technologies, utilizing the 128 bit SSL encryption which ensures server and client (us and you) authentication through encrypted algorithms, and is the industry standard. 
  * GameColony.com utilizes the latest encryption technology to ensure that sensitive data is transferred securely over the Internet. All credit card details are stored on in full compliance with and certyified by SecurityMetrics.com. This means that using your credit card on our site could be far safer than using it to order a pizza at your local cafe... 



Restrictions Imposed on Minors 

  * This site is designed to provide entertainment services to adults. Anyone under who is considered a Minor in their respective jurisdiction (typically under the age of 18) is not allowed to participate in any GC Tournament with entry fees and may require parental consent prior to filling-out our general Registration Form(s). 
  * We DO NOT permit creation of accounts by users who submitted birthdays indicating that they were under 13. In fact, collecting personal information from anyone under 13 without parental consent is a violation of the Children's Protection Act, or COPPA which was passed by Congress in 1998.
