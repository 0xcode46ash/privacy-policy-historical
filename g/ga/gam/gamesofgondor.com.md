# Games of Gondor

PRIVACY POLICY for GamesofGondor.com 

Games of Gondor has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this web site.

INFORMATION AUTOMATICALLY LOGGED

Our systems may log information including your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our web site. We do not collect any personal information without your direct knowledge. We may at times ask if you would like to recieve our newsletter. If you opt in, some personal information may be collected at that time. Any you provide to us, may be used for marketing research within our site. We will never sell your information to any outside party. 

COOKIES

Our site may use cookies to store information about your visits. In addition, our advertisers may also store cookies for they're own purposes. While GamesofGondor uses strict requirments when selecting advertisers, they are not in return responsible for what their cookie may do. 

PERSONAL INFORMATION

Any personal information that you provided to GamesofGondor shall be kept private. 

EXTERNAL LINKS

This site contains links to other sites. GamesofGondor.com is not responsible for their content.

CONTACTING OUR WEB SITE

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this web site, you can contact us at[ webmaster@GamesofGondor.com](mailto:webmaster@gamesofgondor.com).
