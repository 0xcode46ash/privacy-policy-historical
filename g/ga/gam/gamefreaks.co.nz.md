# Privacy Policy | Gamefreaks

By using the Gamefreaks website, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined below. If you do not agree to these terms and conditions, please do not use this site.

Gamefreaks collects no personally identifying information about our users, unless you knowingly provide us with them.

We may keep your details for promotional purposes. Your information will never be sold, nor will you ever receive spam from other sites.

Any personal information can be collected and can be used by Gamefreaks. A full opt-out facility is provided on every email sent by Gamefreaks

If you have any questions, ask [superbrain@gamefreaks.co.nz](mailto:superbrain@gamefreaks.co.nz "Super Brain")
