# Game House

**_Privacy Policy_**  
Game House works with a third party that serves ads to this site. To find out more about how Flycast manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to [www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html](http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html).

Game House uses third party software which may collect and summarize statistical information, including the operating system, browser, video settings and language of the computer systems used to access the Game House website. It does not in any way attempt to collect personal information such as names or email addresses, and is not associated with information gathered during player registration. This information is used solely to determine the typical computing capability of our users in order to serve you better.

Information gathered during voluntary player registration is used solely by Gamehouse.com, and the information will not be sold to any third party. Registrant who check the box requesting promotional information may receive email on behalf of Gamehouse.com, which appears to come from another company. In these cases, the other company has not been given access to email addresses. Players may elect not to receive these emails during registration. Players who wish to no longer receive emails on behalf of Gamehouse.com may send an email to [support@gamehouse.com](mailto:support@gamehouse.com) and request to be removed from the email list.

Gamehouse.com may offer incentives to provide other voluntary information. This information is kept strictly confidential, will not be sold to any third party, and is used primarily to survey our users for advertising purposes.
