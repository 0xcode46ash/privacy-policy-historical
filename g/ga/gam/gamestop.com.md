**Privacy Policy** At GameStop.com , protecting the information you provide us is our top priority. We want your shopping experience to be enjoyable, fun and worry free. GameStop.com is committed to respecting the privacy rights of everyone who visits our website. We also recognize that our visitors, and the parents of children visiting our site, deserve a high level of assurance that any information they choose to share with us will remain private. 

**GameStop.com& Children** GameStop.com is concerned about the privacy of young children and does not knowingly collect any personal information from a child under 13. We suggest that you become involved with your child's access to the Internet to ensure that his or her privacy is protected. 

GameStop.com requires that all purchases be made either (i) by individuals 13 years of age or older, or (ii) by minors under 13 through a GameStop.com authorized affiliated program which permits parents and other guardians to both pay for the purchases of minors, and give verifiable permission for such minors to purchase items on our site and for the collection by us of certain information in accordance with the terms of our privacy policy. EACH TIME YOUR PURCHASE A PRODUCT AT GAMESTOP.COM, YOU ARE REPRESENTING TO GAMESTOP.COM THAT YOU ARE EITHER (I) AN INDIVIDUAL 13 YEARS OF AGE OR OLDER, OR (II) A MINOR UNDER 13 WHO IS PURCHASING THROUGH A GAMESTOP.COM AUTHORIZED AFFILIATED PROGRAM WHICH PERMITS PARENTS AND OTHER GUARDIANS TO BOTH PAY FOR THE PURCHASES OF MINORS, AND GIVE VERIFIABLE PERMISSION FOR SUCH MINORS TO PURCHASE ITEMS ON OUR SITE AND FOR THE COLLECTION BY US OF CERTAIN INFORMATION IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE TERMS OF OUR PRIVACY POLICY.

**Secure Shopping** GameStop.com uses encryption technology to protect the information you provide during the ordering process. Our servers are secured to protect all personal information. GameStop.com is an Authentic VeriSign Site and displays the VeriSign logo to prove it.  [](https://web.archive.org/web/20021215230536id_/http%3A//www.gamestop.com/gs/help/verisign.html) GameStop.com is a **Certified AOL Merchant**  
As an America Online Certified Merchant, we have agreed to abide by the principles outlined in AOL's Privacy Policy at the following URL: <http://legal.web.aol.com/policy/aolpol/privpol.html>

**Credit Card Information** All credit card information is stored on a secured server, and the data is available only to GameStop.com.

**Collection of Information** GameStop.com does not require that visitors reveal any personally identifying information to have access our main site. When information is required to participate in certain promotions or sales offered through our website, participants provide information on a voluntary basis only. Sometimes we may wish to collect personal information about you include: For participation in online contests, sweepstakes or other promotions 
* To create a GameStop.com Account (saves you time on future visits) 
* If you send us questions or comments online which require an e-mail address or mailing address for us to respond 
* Inclusion in our online newsletter program, which will require an e-mail address and other personal gaming and game buying habits that you wish to share with us so that we may customize messaging to your needs and wishes 

Personally identifying information, which GameStop.com collects for a particular promotional purpose or sale from either online and offline methods, will be saved on a secure server. Should any changes to our privacy policies be made, they will be posted to this website. 

**Cookies** Cookies are bits of electronic information which a website can transfer to a visitor's hard drive to help tailor and keep records of his or her visit at the site. Cookies allow GameStop.com to better tailor visits to the site to the visitor's individual preferences. The use of cookies is standard on the Internet and many major web sites use them. You can choose to set your browser to notify you whenever you are sent a cookie. This gives you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it. 

**Links** GameStop.com offers links to the websites of its promotional partners and licensed software publishers. We will make every effort to link only to sites that meet similar standards for maintaining each individual's right to privacy. However, many other sites that are not associated or authorized by GameStop.com may have links leading to [www.gamestop.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20021215230536id_/http%3A//www.gamestop.com/gs/help/www.gamestop.com). GameStop.com cannot control these links and is not responsible for any content appearing on these sites.
