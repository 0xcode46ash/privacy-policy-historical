# Privacy Policy | Games24x7.com

This privacy policy forms part of our website’s [**Terms of Service**](https://web.archive.org/web/20101225025501id_/http%3A//www.games24x7.com/player/landingpages/termsofservicepopup.html)

Games24x7 respects your privacy and assures you that any information provided by you to Games24x7 is protected and will be dealt with according to this Policy. To avail of the Facilities offered on www.games24x7.com, you may have to provide certain information to us. 

**This includes your :**

  * Username
  * Password
  * Valid Email address



**Additionally, for those of you who wish to participate in any of the paid tournaments / games / competetions organized by Games24x7,  
the following information is collected :**

  * Name
  * Birth Date
  * Telephone number
  * Postal/Contact address
  * PAN number (as applicable)



**When you visit www.games24x7.com, certain information may also be automatically collected and stored, including :**

  * The IP address of your computer, browser type and language.
  * The date and the time during which you accessed the site.
  * The address of the website which you may have used to link to www.games24x7.com



When you visit www.games24x7.com, cookies may be left in your computer. A cookie is a small text file that uniquely identifies your browser. The cookies assigned by the servers of Games24x7 may be used to personalize your experience on www.games24x7.com. Additionally, cookies may also be used for authentication, game management and security purposes.

Cookies may also be assigned by the advertisers of Games24x7 when you click on any of the advertisements which may be displayed on www.games24x7.com in which case such cookies are controlled by these advertisers and not Games24x7.

When you register your email address with Games24x7, you agree to receive email communication from Games24x7, entities specifically authorized by Games24x7 and other users.  You also agree and acknowledge that when you use our referral program for referring someone, Games24x7 will send emails to that person on your behalf and the email headers will carry your email address as the address from which such emails are sent.

Games24x7 may also use software applications for website traffic analysis and to gather statistics, used for advertising and for determining the efficacy and popularity of Games24x7 among others.

Information gathered by Games24x7 is stored securely using several information security applications including firewalls. However, security is always relative and Games24x7 cannot guarantee that its security measures are absolute and cannot be breached.

Also, data which is transmitted over the Internet is inherently exposed to security risks or threats. For instance, information transmitted via chat or email can be compromised and used by others. Therefore, Games24x7 cannot guarantee any security for such information.

When you register with Games24x7, your account is protected by means of login information which includes a username and a password that is known only to you. Therefore, you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login information including your username and password. If you become aware of or reasonably suspect any breach of security, including compromise of your login information, it is your responsibility to immediately notify Games24x7.

www.games24x7.com may contain links to other websites. Such websites are governed by their own privacy policies and Games24x7 does not exercise any control over them. It is your responsibility to read and understand the privacy policy of such websites when you follow a link outside www.games24x7.com.

Games24x7 has a policy of not sharing any personally identifiable information with anyone other than entities specifically authorized by Games24x7 which may include advertisers and sponsors of Games24x7. However, Games24x7 may use your name, Login ID and the state from where you are participating when announcing the results of any contests run on the Website. Such contests are further governed by the Terms and Conditions which shall be available on the website as and when such a contest is run on the website. Games24x7 conducts periodic analysis and survey of the traffic to Games24x7.com for market research and advertising purposes. Games24x7 reserves the right to share your registration information with Games24x7 appointed market research and advertising companies or firms from time to time for the said purposes. Games24x7 may also use cumulative non-personal information for auditing and analysis purposes with the aim of improving its services.

  * to comply with legal processes or governmental request, or

  * for prevention of fraud, or 
  * for issues involving information security, or

**1.** your rights;

**3.** the rights of the general public

This Policy is not intended to and does not create any legal rights in your favour or in the favour of any other person.
