# GateWorld - Privacy Policy

GateWorld is a fan site devoted to exploring and enhancing the science fiction fandom experience. At no time is any personal information other than an e-mail address collected from users of this site.

Some site features are only available for those users who choose to submit an e-mail address: sending us feedback or news tips or a letter to the editor; signing up for the "Gate News" e-mail newsletter; entering a contest; and registering for **GateWorld Forums**. The forums are currently hosted by Delphi Forums, and are subject to Delphi's [privacy policy](http://www.delphi.com/dir-html/privacy.html) and [terms of service](http://www.delphi.com/dir-delphi/agreement.htm).

Additional information, including full name and postal address, is required to sign up for a free "you@gateworld.net" e-mail account. Our Web-based e-mail service is hosted by Everyone.net, and your use is subject to their [privacy policy](http://www.everyone.net/main/html/p_policy.html) and [user agreement](http://www.everyone.net/main/html/agreement_enduser.html). At no time does GateWorld have access to this information, beyond the "@gateworld.net" e-mail address you create.

GateWorld is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. **Your name or e-mail address will not be sold or disclosed to any third party under any circumstances** , except in cooperation with law enforcement. The site does not use cookies, and does not track the browsing habits of individual users.

Current U.S. law requires that Web sites may not collect information of any kind -- including an e-mail address -- from children under 13 years old without their parent's consent. If you're under 13, be sure to get your parent's permission before e-mailing us, submitting a letter to the editor, signing up for the newsletter or for a gateworld.net e-mail address, or registering for the forums. GateWorld's contests are limited to fans 18 years or over.

Enjoy the site!
