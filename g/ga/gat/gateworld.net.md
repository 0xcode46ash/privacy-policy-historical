# GateWorld - Privacy Policy

GateWorld is a fan site devoted to exploring and enhancing the science fiction fandom experience. At no time is personal information collected from users of this site.

There are currently three places on GateWorld where you will be asked for your e-mail address: when sending us feedback or news tips or a letter to the editor, when signing up for the Gate News e-mail newsletter, and when registering for **GateWorld Forums**. The forums are currently hosted by Delphi Forums, and are subject to Delphi's [privacy policy](http://www.delphi.com/dir-html/privacy.html) and [terms of service](http://www.delphi.com/dir-delphi/agreement.htm).

GateWorld is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. **Your name or e-mail address will not be sold or disclosed to any third party under any circumstances** , except in cooperation with law enforcement. The site does not use cookies, and does not track the browsing habits of individual users.

Current U.S. law requires that Web sites may not collect information of any kind -- including an e-mail address -- from children under 13 years old without their parent's consent. If you're under 13, be sure to get your parent's permission before e-mailing us, submitting a letter to the editor, signing up for the newsletter, or registering for the forums.

Enjoy the site!
