# Advertising, Galt Shareware Zone Advertising

Big Brother is NOT WATCHING on our site. Visitors are welcome to visit and browse. We do not use cookies to track anyone, we do not access data on your computer. 

Galt will not collect any personally-identifiable information about you (name, address, telephone number, email address) unless you volunteer that information to us in the course of subscribing to one of our lists or ordering a product. This information is not sold or shared with any other third parties. Subscribers to our mailing list may opt out at any time and their email address will be removed from our files. If you do not want your personal information collected, please do not submit it. If you do submit your email or postal addresses online, you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new services or products. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by contacting us by email or phone. 

For each visitor to our sites, our Web server automatically recognizes your domain, the type of Internet browser you are using, the type of computer operating system you are using, and the domain name of the Web site from which you linked to our site. No other information is taken or accessed from your computer. None of this information is in any way connected to you or indentifiable with you as an individual. **Thus you are basically anonymous, and we will leave you alone!**

Thank you for visiting our site, and rest assured your privacy will not be violated by the Galt folks.
