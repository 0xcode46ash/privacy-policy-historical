**Information We Collect and How We Use It**  
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. We use cookies on occasion to serve you special content pages or track promotional programs. A cookie is a piece of text that can be entered into the memory of your browser. Some Internet services deliver cookies to visitors to their web sites. Cookies store information about your use of their service on your computer where it can only be retrieved by that same service at a later time. Cookies are used by some websites to make their services much more convenient and personalized. Cookies are stored on your hard drive, not on our site. Most cookies are "session cookies," which means they are automatically deleted at the end of a session. You are always free to decline cookies if your browser permits.

Our site uses an order form for customers to purchase products and services. We collect visitor's contact information (like their e-mail address) and financial information (like their account or credit card numbers). Contact information from the order form is used to ship orders to our customers and is used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services. Our online surveys and feedback forms may ask visitors for contact information (like their e-mail address), demographic information (like their zip code, age or income level), and profile information (like their favorite artist, video or song). Upon request from the customer, we use contact data from our surveys and registration forms to send the user information about our company. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary (contest winners, product discounts, special events, etc.). Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. The information we collect about you is used to improve the content of our Web pages and services and is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes without your permission. This site contains links to other sites. Jones Media Networks, Ltd. is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

**Site Security**  
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Firewalls, Unix System securities, and Password entry for authorized personnel guard the information stored on our servers. 

**Choices for Opting-Out**  
Our site provides users the opportunity to choose to receive communications from us at the point where we request information about the visitor. This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service.

If you have registered at our site and are receiving our email newsletter, you can opt-out of our mailing list by following this link and completing our unsubscribe form: [http://www.GACTV.com/Newsletter/unsubscribe.html](http://www.countrystars.com/Newsletter/unsubscribe.html)

If you have received any other emails from CountryStars.com or our affiliates and wish to opt-out of future mailings, please follow the instructions listed within each mailing for the appropriate way to unsubscribe from our lists.
