# Gardener's Supply: Innovative Gardening Solutions, Composters, Greenhouses, Garden Carts, Raised Beds, And More.

Security & Privacy

**SECURITY STATEMENT**  
We understand that you may have concerns about using your credit card to make purchases online. At Gardener's Supply Company, we use industry-standard 128-bit SSL encryption technology, ensuring that your information is protected and secure during its journey between your PC and our offices. To ensure your information is even more secure, we store your name, address, and billing information on computers that aren't accessible from the Internet. **PRIVACY STATEMENT**  
Our site is meant to be a service to you, and we respect your privacy. To provide the best products, information and customer service, we record several pieces of information. We record the address of the site you came from before visiting us, and any terms you may have used to search for our site. We also record the pages you viewed on our site. If you sign up for our e-Newsletters on the site, or permit us to send you our e-Newsletters via the options presented at checkout, we'll send you our next scheduled mailing. All of our e-Newsletters contain an easy option for unsubscribing. And, should we offer an on-line survey, we will also record any information you provide us in that survey. 

As an AOL Certified Merchant, we have agreed to abide by the principles outlined in AOL's Privacy Policy at the following URL: <http://legal.web.aol.com/policy/aolpol/privpol.html>. If you have any questions regarding our privacy practices, please contact us via email at [info@gardeners.com](mailto:info@gardeners.com) or call (888) 833-1412.

If you supply us with your postal address, e-mail address or phone, you might receive periodic contacts from us via the mail, e-mail or phone with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such contacts, please let us know by sending us a message via e-mail at info@gardeners.com, or by calling (888) 833-1412.
