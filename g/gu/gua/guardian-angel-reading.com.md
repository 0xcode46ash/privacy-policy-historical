## What is to be considered when data is transferred over the internet?

The internet is generally not regarded as a completely secure environment, and information sent via the internet can be accessed by unauthorized third parties. Even if both sender and receiver are located in the same country, information sent via the internet may be transmitted across international borders and be forwarded to a country with a lower data protection level than exists in your country of residence. Please note that we accept no responsibility for the security of your information whilst in transit over the internet to guardian-angel-reading.com.

## What information is collected?

We do not collect your personal data through our website unless you voluntarily provided such data that could be used to identify a person. Through its website, guardian-angel-reading.com stores several categories of personal data.

In order to receive your free trial offer, you will need to provide – on the [Free Trial Form](https://web.archive.org/free-angel-reading/) you can access using this link – the following information: title, surname, first name, date and time of birth, e-mail address and country of residence.

If you decide to order a chargeable product or electronic service, you will then need to enter the following information: full address, telephone number, credit card details.

You enter your confidential credit card details within a secure area managed entirely by the payment processors or PCI DSS-compliant suppliers working with us. Sesam does not have access to this entirely secure area under any circumstances.

There is no obligation to buy. When you visit guardian-angel-reading.com, our web server may also automatically collect your IP address. Furthermore, our web server may automatically collect generic information which does not identify you personally such as your web browser, your operating system or your Internet service provider.

## How is this information used?

All the personal data and other information which you have entered are solely used in order to provide you with the service(s) and/or products which you requested on guardian-angel-reading.com and for the purposes of technical administration, research and development of the guardian-angel-reading.com website, customer and end user administration and marketing (hereinafter “Services” and “Products”). A secret algorithm based on your surname, first name and date of birth enables us to generate your free trial offer, as well as all the personalized psychic reading products which you can order.

Your e-mail address will also be used to send you the Products and Services which you ordered on guardian-angel-reading.com. We will also use your e-mail address to send you offers for Products or Services – every 2 to 3 days – which may be of interest to you. You will also receive a free newsletter containing your horoscope and articles on subjects in the field of esotericism, clairvoyance or personal development. You can unsubscribe from this mailing list at any time by clicking on the unsubscribe link you can find in each e-mail we send you or on the [contact page](https://web.archive.org/contact-me/) you can access via this link. We will then remove your contact information from the lists used for such mailings.

Your phone number will be used to send you offers for Products or Services that might interest you through text messages twice or three times a month. If you communicate your phone number, it means that you give Sesam the authorization to send to this number text messages and phone calls recorded in advance, including promotional messages that could be sent through an automatic phone system composition. Receiving those messages or accepting receiving them doesn’t represent any obligation to buy. Please note that standard message rates and data charges from your cellular service provider apply to sending and/or receiving text messages or calls. You are solely responsible for such message rates and data charges. The costs linked to the aforesaid messages and data are only your responsibility. If you don’t want to receive more messages, you can ask us at any moment. (See «  _[How do I unsubscribe?](https://web.archive.org/general-terms-conditions-use-raw/)_ » in our General Terms and Conditions of Use).

We may transfer your personal data to third parties who act for us for further processing in accordance with the purposes for which the data was originally collected or for purposes to which you have subsequently consented. For example, sometimes a third party may have access to your personal data in order to fulfil an order, support our information technology or help compile and manage the data.

When transferring personal data internationally we make sure that we comply with applicable laws and regulations and take steps to make sure that the recipients of your information maintain an adequate level of data protection.

Sesam reserves the right to disclose your personal data in the event of a summons to appear in court, an official court order or any legal process or investigation in order to establish or exercise your rights, or to defend ourselves against claims permitted by the applicable laws and regulations. Your personal data may also be disclosed if Sesam decides that it is necessary to hold an enquiry or take measures regarding potentially illegal activities in the event of threat to the personal security of any individual, or if you are in breach of the Service’s and/or Product’s [General Terms and Conditions of Use](https://web.archive.org/legal-terms/).

Only if you ticked the box “I accept to receive offers from Padre’s partners”, could we share your personal data such as title, first name, surname, postal address and e-mail address with third party companies, partners of Sesam, whose products or services may be of interest to you. However, you can contact us at any time through the [contact page](https://web.archive.org/contact-me/) you can access via this link in order to ask us not to disclose your personal data to third parties.

Sesam’s suppliers sometimes have temporary and limited access to your personal data. This access may be granted in the context of maintenance operations or the supply of Services to Sesam. In particular, these suppliers offer services in the following areas: payment processing, e-mail routing, SMS routing, mail printing and sorting, website hosting, after-sales service processing, data storage, as well as any other service linked to the operation, maintenance and security of Sesam’s website. However, access to your personal data remains strictly limited to what is required by the supplier in order to fulfil its service contract with Sesam. Moreover, such access is strictly limited to the time required for the operation or service.

Sesam ensures contractually that its suppliers undertake to do everything within their power to ensure the protection and privacy of your personal data according to the principles defined in this document. These suppliers are also required not to use or transfer your personal data for purposes other than those defined in the service contract taken out by Sesam.

Sesam strives to maintain the reliability, accuracy, completeness and currency of your personal data in our databases and to protect the privacy and security of our databases and the data we collect. Sesam ensures the security of all your personal data, using technical and organizational security measures to reasonably protect information against unauthorized access, accidental or intentional manipulation, misuse, loss and destruction, as well as a processing procedure which complies with the applicable legislation in the country in which Sesam is based.

## Use of cookies, IP addresses and website traffic information

Sesam uses tracking technology such as cookies or tags to gather such information as outlined above (see “What information is collected” and “How do we share your personal data”) and to understand how visitors use the guardian-angel-reading.com website. Tracking technology helps us manage and improve the usability of the guardian-angel-reading.com website, for example by determining whether there has been any contact between your computer’s IP address and us in the past and identifying the most popular sections of the guardian-angel-reading.com website. In concrete terms, guardian-angel-reading.com’s server sends information to your computer which is then stored on your hard drive. The IP address of your computer is used in order to identify visits to guardian-angel-reading.com from your computer and to keep track of your browsing habits and optimize the time required to load pages. By using the guardian-angel-reading.com website you consent to the use of cookies and your IP address as specified above.

Most recent web browsers allow you to prevent this data from being stored on your computer. For more information, check your browser’s “help” function. However, please note that we do not recommend this practice, as it can reduce the quality of your browsing experience on guardian-angel-reading.com or parts of the guardian-angel-reading.com website may no longer function correctly.

Some of our pages may also contain web beacons which allow us to collect information on the volume and origin of visitor traffic to our pages as well as general demographic information about these visitors. This data is stored anonymously, it does not contain any personal information and can thus be disclosed to third parties, particularly for information or statistical analysis purposes, particularly in order to analyze the performance of our marketing promotions.
