# GunStar | Privacy Policy

Who are we?

"We" are gunstar.co.uk. We provide this website to retailers and private individuals to display products/items they are marketing, and to promote various sports. We are not sales agents. We are an internet based advertising medium. See the about us section for more information about the company.

Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how and for what purposes we use the information collected about you via www.gunstar.co.uk (referred to below as "this site"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using this site and any services we offer via this site, you are agreeing to be bound by this policy in respect of the information collected about you via this site. If you have any questions about the policy or our use of your personal information, please e-mail us at info@gunstar.co.uk.

Cookies and tracking

We use "cookies" to gather information on your visits to this website, to simplify signing in process, to track the usage of this website and allow us to make improvements to the site. Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored in the hard drive of your computer. Your browser will have the option to prevent websites using cookies, but this may reduce the functionality of this website.

Email Messages

The website generates e-mail messages as well as allowing users of this site to send e-mail messages to other users of this site. We reserve the right to monitor and keep copies of e-mails to prevent abuse of our systems and to ensure the quality of our service.  
Registering with us

If you register with us, we save the details you provide and we use them to enable us to personalise the service we offer to you.

Contact Information

We will use your contact information to provide services requested by you and of course to make you aware of site updates. To alter your contact information, please make the changes by accessing your account details and altering the relevant section.

Security, Storage and Transfer of Information

We follow strict security procedures to ensure that your personal information is not damaged, destroyed or disclosed without permission and to prevent unauthorised access to it. Our computer systems have security systems in place to prevent unauthorised access to the data held within them. However, although we make every effort to prevent unauthorised access to our systems, no computer is guaranteed to be secure and by using this site you acknowledge that.

Access to Information

Under the law you have the right to know all the information that we hold about you, whether you are a registered or non-registered user of this site. If you require to know this information, please e-mail info@gunstar.co.uk. We will make an administrative charge of £5.00 to cover costs.

Changes to this policy

We may change this policy from time to time as we add new services to this site or in response to changes in the law.
