# Privacy Policy | Guns Save Lives

## Terms

“This website” or “website” referenced below refers to GunsSaveLives.net

## Personal Information

This website may collect anonymous, non identifying browsing data through session or cookie variables for use in traffic analytics. Our hosting company may record your IP address in server logs. None of this information will be sold or traded to third parties.

Any information voluntary provided to this site through the submission of web forms, contest entries, emails or other electronic submission will never be sold, traded or transferred to a third party unless the submitter of said information specifically opts in or asks for such actions. This website may send occasional, non-solication email communications to email addresses submitted to the website. If you ever do receive an email from this website (which will never be a business solicitation, or request for money) and you wish to not receive further emails, a clear and concise opt out process will be provided.

If, at any time, you would like any personal information you have submitted removed from our database, simply [Contact Us](http://gunssavelives.net/contact-us/ "Contact Us") and let us know.

## Further Information

By using this website, you signify your agreement to our privacy policy as stated above. Note that this policy may be revised periodically without notice. Please re-read this policy prior to submitting any personal information if you have concerns about how your information is being collected and used.
