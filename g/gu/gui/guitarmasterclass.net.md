# Guitar Masterclass Privacy Policy

**Security** \- If you choose to sign up to access the whole GuitarMasterClass video lesson archive, you will be redirected to a secure http **s** server provided by **Paypal** to guarantee a secure payment process. More about security.

**Cookies** \- This site uses cookies and Ip address for tracking puposes only. Ie the information is used to measure the number of visitors at the different sections of the site, to make it more useful to our visitors.

**Personally identifiable information** \- No such information is collected in the free section of GuitarMasterClass. If you sign up to access the full archive you need to provide your email address. Your email address will not be shared with any third parties nor will it be used for any other purposes, including selling. 

During the payment process you will be asked for your name and address. GuitarMasterClass does not have access to this information, it is collected as encrypted code by Pay and Read and will not be passed on to any third parties.

**Computer information** \- GuitarMasterClass gathers your computer resolution and operating system by using a tracker. This information is only used to improve the site and make it more accessible to the visitors. 

**Contact the webmaster** \- If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, the practices of the site, or any other inquiries - you can contact the webmaster [here](https://web.archive.org/Contact-GMC-staff.htm). 

**Legal notice** \- GuitarMasterClass is not associated with any organization or group. The materials presented on GuitarMasterClass are original works created by the instructors, new arrangements by the instructors of original works from the public domain, or interpretations of works used by permission with a streaming licence from [STIM](http://www.stim.se/stim/prod/stimv4eng.nsf) (Swedish Performing Rights Society). 

GuitarMasterClass is not affiliated with guitarists/bands mentioned on the site in any form. These guitarists/bands do not endorse the site nor do they endorse the site's practices. 

You may use these files for private study, scholarship, or research. You may also use them in performances and recordings of performances. However, redistribution of these pages in any form without written permission from GuitarMasterClass is prohibited to all persons.

You may record your own takes of gmc lessons, but you may not use it for commercial purposes. 

Any disputes or legal queries shall be settled under Swedish law and by a Swedish court of law.
