# privacypolicy

We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly   
confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. A more   
detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described   
below. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to let us know.  
\--------------------------------------------------------------------------------  
Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:  
KnuckleBonz, Inc. does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information   
collected at our site, including email lists.

Privacy of our email lists:  
KnuckleBonz, Inc. maintains email lists to keep Guitar Hero(TM) members informed   
about breaking events. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our   
list to anyone.
