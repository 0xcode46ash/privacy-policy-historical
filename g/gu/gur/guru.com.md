# guru.com - privacy policy

**Guru.com Privacy Policy**  


Your privacy is extremely important to us, and we want you to feel comfortable using our site. Guru.com is committed to creating products and services that are built upon a solid foundation of trust. That's why we're developing a comprehensive privacy policy.

Any personal information that we capture will be used only to better tailor our products and services to suit your needs. We will share it only with people and companies who are directly affiliated with Guru.com. We will not sell, trade, rent, or give your personal information to third-party companies or organizations unless they are offering a product or service in conjunction with Guru.com.

In the future, we may capture additional personal information to help you find gigs. With your consent, this information will be shared with prospective employers and fellow gurus. 

Any information we present to advertisers will be in the form of aggregate statistics. Advertisers will not be given specific information about you as an individual.

Any information that you divulge in a public forum of this website is considered public information.

You may opt out of receiving email correspondence from Guru.com. You can always change your subscription status by visiting our [unsubscribe page](https://web.archive.org/web/19991109195834id_/http%3A//guru.com/newsletter_unsubscribe.cgi).

As we introduce new products and features in the future, we will post clarifications to our privacy policies here.

Many thanks for your participation!
