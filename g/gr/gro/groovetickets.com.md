**WHAT INFORMATION DOES GROOVETICKETS.COM, INC. COLLECT AND HOW IS IT USED?**  
Groovetickets.com, Inc. may collect any or all of the following three types of information: 

  1. Traffic or click-through data 
  2. Cookies, or 
  3. Contact information and other personally identifying information. 



Groovetickets.com, Inc.'s collection and use of each type of data is described below, as well as the ways (if any) in which the data may be shared with third parties. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. 

  1. TRAFFIC OR CLICK-THROUGH DATA. Data about traffic and usage is not personally identifying or identifiable, but may be supplemented or combined with personally identifying or identifiable information. Groovetickets.com, Inc. may share information it collects with certain selected third parties. For example, we may tell an advertiser that a certain number of people visited a certain area on our Web site. Groovetickets.com, Inc. collects some information regarding use of the Services by observing browser functions and the files (pages) users access. 
  2. COOKIES. Groovetickets.com, Inc. and/or its advertisers may collect information about online behavior using cookies. These cookies might contain identification information that enables us to see how our users are interacting with our site and how frequently they are returning. Groovetickets.com, Inc. cookies might contain personally identifiable information. Cookies let us know about repeat visitors by identifying from which sites our visitors are coming. Groovetickets.com, Inc. may share information about you and your usage of the Services with third parties. 
  3. CONTACT INFORMATION OR PERSONALLY IDENTIFYING INFORMATION. Our site's registration form(s) may require users to give us contact information (like their name and email address), unique identifiers (like their phone number), financial information (credit card numbers), and demographic information (like their zip code). We may use customer contact information from the registration form to send the user information about our company and promotional material from some of our partners. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary.
