# Privacy Policy - gr8 People, llc - Collaborative Recruiting Platform

* * *

gr8 PEOPLE provides informational content through the Site and generally does not use the Site for the sole purpose of collecting personal information from visitors. gr8 PEOPLE may use the data it collects for internal purposes: solving technical issues, calculating usage statistics, marketing, product development, employment and general informational improvement. gr8 PEOPLE will not disclose to third parties any personal information, except to the extent necessary to comply with applicable laws or legal processes (e.g. subpoenas), or to protect the rights or property of gr8 PEOPLE. gr8 PEOPLE will cooperate with law enforcement authorities.

We collect non-identifiable information through the use of session-based cookies. A session-based cookie is a small text file that our Site sends to your browser as an anonymous tag that identifies your computer. We use “cookie” technology strictly for session management. You can also set your browser to turn off cookies. If you do so, however, some Sites may not work properly. 

The information provided to gr8 PEOPLE through the Site is used by gr8 PEOPLE for marketing or employment purposes. Before we use it, however, we offer you the opportununity to choose whether or not to have your information used in this way.

As part of gr8 PEOPLE products and services, our customers may load data into our infrastructure. gr8 PEOPLE does not share or distribute any such customer data except as provided in the contractual agreement between gr8 PEOPLE and customer, or as may be required by law. While gr8 PEOPLE is contractually obligated to not use the personal information for our own purposes, our Privacy Policy does not reflect the privacy policy of our customers and we are not responsible for our customer’s data protection practices. If you have submitted personal information to one of our customer’s portals from a gr8 PEOPLE hosted service, contact that company to understand its privacy policy.

gr8 PEOPLE utilizes industry standard security measures to ensure the security of all data. However, the security of information transmitted through the Internet can never be guaranteed. gr8 PEOPLE restricts access to your personal information to employees who need to know that information to operate, develop or improve our products and services. Please note, however, that gr8 PEOPLE cannot guarantee the confidentiality of any communication or material transmitted to/from gr8 PEOPLE via the Site or e-mail. gr8 PEOPLE is not responsible for any interception or interruption of any communications through the Internet or for changes to or losses of data.

Users of our Sites are responsible for maintaining the security of any password, user name, or other form of authentication involved in obtaining access to password protected or secure areas of any gr8 PEOPLE site(s). In order to protect you and your data, gr8 PEOPLE may suspend your use of a site, without notice, pending an investigation, if any breach of security is suspected. Access to and use of password protected and/or secure area of any gr8 PEOPLE site is restricted to authorized users only. Unauthorized access to such areas is prohibited and may lead to criminal prosecution.

gr8 PEOPLE may include or offer third party products or services on our website. These third party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.

If gr8 PEOPLE decides to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page. In the event of a transfer of ownership of gr8 PEOPLE, such as acquisition by or merger with another company, gr8 PEOPLE will provide notice before any personal information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Visitors to our Site may at any time opt out of having their personal information used by gr8 PEOPLE by contacting gr8 PEOPLE, llc via e-mail or postal mail at the address below.
