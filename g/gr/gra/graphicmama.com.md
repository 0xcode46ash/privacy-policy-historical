# Privacy Policy | GraphicMama

## Privacy Policy

Last updated: Mar 22nd, 2016 09:14 (UTC)

##### ACCEPTANCE OF TERMS

GraphicMama respects its users' privacy. We put this Privacy Policy together to show how we collect, use and protect your personal information. By using our services and purchasing our products you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy shall be governed in accordance to international treaty provisions and other applicable laws.

##### CHANGES IN PRIVACY POLICY

We have the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. It is your responsibility to check this page and keep yourself updated with the latest edition of GraphicMama's Privacy Policy 

##### ACCESSING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

You have the right to review the personal information we store for you and make any changes you wish. You can do this from your account settings.

##### DELETING YOUR ACCOUNT

If you wish to permanently delete your account with us, please, contact us.

##### COLLECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

To be able to provide our services and share news and updates about these same services with you, we collect some personal information for you, like your name and email. To be able to complete your purchase order and bill you for the products you purchase, we collect some billing information, like your credit card number, expiration date, security code and billing address. As most modern websites do, to provide a better user experience for their clients, we also collect some technical information for you, like your browser type, operating system, IP address or geographical location.

##### INFORMATION SHARING WITH SERVICE PROVIDERS

Any personal information shared with third party providers is to be used under the regulations of this Privacy Policy. The use of your personal information by these third parties is limited to the purpose of the service they provide. For example, we share some of your personal information with the credit card processing companies that bill you for the products and services you purchase from us. We will never sell your personal information. We keep the right to disclose your personal information, if required to do so by the law. 

##### HOW YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION IS USED

GraphicMama collects and uses your personal information to:

  * administer your account;
  * provide customer service;
  * process your financial transactions;
  * share news about upcoming services;
  * conduct marketing research and analysis;
  * monitor user behaviour;



We may send you marketing emails to inform you about the launch of new products or services that may be of your interest. It is possible that some of these emails come from other businesses within the "2create Labs Ltd" or "2create Ltd". You can unsubscribe from these emails at any point, emailing us at the unsubscribe email address placed at the bottom of the messages. However, system and transnational messages, like purchase and billing confirmations, will still be send to you. 

##### DATA RETENTION

We will retain your personal information with us, as long as your account is active. 

##### PROTECTION OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

The security of your personal information is important for us and we protect it in several ways:

  * your account password is encrypted;
  * your personal information is encrypted when passed to us;
  * your personal information is stored on a secured server;
  * the information you enter on your registration or payment form is SSL encrypted;
  * our employees have limited access to your personal information;
  * all our employees sign confidentiality agreements;



Although we do our best to protect the personal information we collect and store, we should remind you that on the Internet there is no such thing as a 100% secured storage. If you have any questions concerning the security of your personal information, please, contact us. 

##### LINKS TO 3rd PARTY WEBSITES

We publish links to third parties who may have different Privacy Policies than ours. Make sure that you read those Privacy Policies before you start using their services. 

##### SOCIAL MEDIA WIDGETS

Interacting with any social media widgets on our website (i.e. a Facebook Like button) is governed by the privacy policy of the company providing the widget.

##### SINGLE SIGN-ON

On our website we use sign-in services like Facebook Connect. This may allow people from your network to see how you interact with our website.

##### CHILDREN

We don't intend to collect data from anyone under the age of 18. In case that you think that your child may have shared his/her personal information with us, please, contact us. 

##### PRIVACY COMPLAINTS

If you have any questions, concerns or complaints in regards to our Privacy Policy, please, contact us.
