# Grad Today Future in Education

##### ONLINE PRIVACY STATEMENT

**PLEASE READ THE FOLLOWING CAREFULLY**

NOTE TO MINORS - IF YOU ARE UNDER 18 YEARS OF AGE, PLEASE BE SURE TO READ THIS PRIVACY STATEMENT WITH YOUR PARENTS OR GUARDIAN AND ASK THEM QUESTIONS ABOUT WHAT YOU DON'T UNDERSTAND.

GradToday.com (the "Site") understands the importance of our users understanding our privacy policies and what we do with information we obtain through the site. Please also note that as our industry evolves, so does the privacy policy associated with it; changes may result from legal changes, government regulations, or other unforeseen occurrences. If that occurs, the revised policy will be posted immediately on the site and dated so that you know the date of the update.

**LINKS TO OTHER SITES**

Links from this site can take you to the sites of several unaffiliated companies to our Site. As a result we suggest that as you navigate the site, please ensure the URL to reflect our Site in order to ensure that your privacy is being properly regarded. We pride ourselves on only being associated with reputable associates and would hope that issues would not arise, but a cautious approach will ensure your safety.

**WHAT PERSONAL INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT AND USE**

Our Site might collect the following information: your first and last name, email address, home address, phone number, date of birth, credit card information, and certain other information, such as a Social Security Number, Our company does not require that you surrender this information as our service is purely beneficial to you, the user, but in order to serve you the best way possible, it is necessary to obtain the maximum amount of appropriate information. This information will be treated confidentially within our company, as we protect other confidential information..

**INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE**

Except as specifically stated in this statement, our policy is not to share the Personally Identifiable Information collected through this Site with any third parties. The only exception to this rule would be in instances of cybercrime in which we would divulge any necessary information to the proper authorities to prosecute any violator.

**COLLECTION AND USE OF INFORMATION FROM COOKIES**

Our Site uses "cookies" to obtain certain information when your computer's browser accesses the Site, Cookies are textual identifiers that our system tranmits to your computer's hard drive through your browser to allow our Site to recognize your browser, and to simplify future use of our Site. If you want to disable cookies, you can do so through your browser toolbar, but cookies allow you to take full advantage of this Site's features, and we recommend that you leave them turned on.
