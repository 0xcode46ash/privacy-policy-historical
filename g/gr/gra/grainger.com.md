# Grainger.com

The Information We Collect  
W.W. Grainger, Inc. ("Grainger") collects various types of information from Site visitors. Grainger collects transactional information from companies and Site visitors that purchase products from this Grainger web site ( this "Site"). The information collected may include shipping information and credit card number/expiration date. Grainger’s web server also collects general data pertaining to every Site visitor, including the IP address, the domain name, the web page, the length of time spent on this Site, and the pages accessed while visiting this Site. This information is collected to, among other things, aggregate statistical information from online and offline sources, facilitate system administration, improve the content of this Site, and customize and improve the user experience for Site visitors and customers. Grainger may also retain the content of the communications that are sent to Grainger customer service representatives. This information helps Grainger to respond to inquiries regarding this Site. Security  
Grainger places the utmost importance on protecting information transmitted via this Site and takes security precautions to protect all such information, including measures to secure and protect all customer-specific information from loss, misuse, and alteration. Grainger, however, cannot guarantee that any electronic commerce is totally secure.Use of the Information Collected  
Grainger uses the information that is collected from this Site to, among other things, better serve its customers, improve the content of this Site, and customize the shopping experience for Site visitors and customers. Grainger may also use the information collected to notify Site visitors of changes to this Site, to notify Site visitors of new products made available via this Site, and for any other legitimate and lawful business purpose of Grainger. Grainger may choose to share information with third parties. Cookies and Other Technology Information  
A cookie is a small file stored on your computer by a web site to give you a unique ID (each, a "Cookie"). Cookies help make it easier for you to use this Site and they help us provide you with the information you need - when and where you need it. Cookies provided by this Site do not contain any credit card or personal information. Grainger uses cookies to track new visitors to this Site and recognize past customers so that Grainger may present more personalized content. Grainger does not use cookies to personally identify anonymous Site visitors. Grainger does not know who you are unless you provide that information voluntarily. NOTE: If you configure your computer to refuse cookies you will not be able to use this Site. 

Grainger contracts with a third party data collection and reporting service ("Third Party Service") that collects information regarding Site visitors and their use of this Site and reports that information to Grainger. The Third Party Service uses Cookies on Grainger’s behalf to collect such information. The Third Party Service is contractually prohibited from using information obtained in the course of providing these services to Grainger in any manner other than to help Grainger provide Site visitors and customers the best possible customized experience to service your business needs. Nevertheless, Grainger respects your right to choose whether to be included in the services the Third Party Service provides. If you would like to opt out of those services completely, please "[click here](https://web.archive.org/web/20020602063513id_/http%3A//www.grainger.com/Grainger/cm_optout.jsp)."

Unsubscribing from E-Mail  
Each Site visitor can unsubscribe from receiving e-mail advertisements and special offers from this Site simply by sending a request indicating such desire to [feedback@ic.grainger.com](mailto:feedback@ic.grainger.com).
