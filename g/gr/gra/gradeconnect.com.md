# Grade Connect.Com

**Our Policy**  
Gradeconnect.com and Media Website Design, Inc. believes strongly in the user's right to privacy especially the Childs. All information collected is for the sole purpose of the operation of the website and is not sold to any entity for any reason. 

**Information Collected**   
All information collected from the student is information that would normally be available to the teacher and is used for the sole purpose of administrative tasks of the teacher in using this site such as entering grades and contacting students through e-mail. Information collected from the teachers is used for maintaining course and school integrity.
