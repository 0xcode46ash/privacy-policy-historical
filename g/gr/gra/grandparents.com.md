# Grandparents.com

## Grandparents.com Privacy Policy

Grandparents.com is committed to protecting the privacy of our online visitors. This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices governing the use of information submitted to Grandparents.com through this web site. 

## Information Usage and Sharing

Grandparents.com values your patronage and respects your right to privacy. Because we are committed to protecting your privacy, personal data relating to an identified or identifiable individual that is submitted to this web page will be collected, processed stored, and disposed of in accordance with applicable U.S. law. However, U.S. law may not provide the degree of protection to personal data, which is available in other countries. The submission of personal data constitutes the User’s acknowledgement that he/she has read and understood this notice, and agrees to its terms. In order to facilitate the purchase of goods online or to update visitors of new products or services, Grandparents.com may share certain individual information with our partners. Grandparents.com may share non-personal, non-individual information in aggregate form with unaffiliated third parties for marketing or research purposes. Should our records be subpoenaed, we will act in accordance with our legal counsel’s advice and handle each matter on a case-by-case basis.

## Cookies 

Grandparents.com uses cookie technology to increase the ease of use of the web site. When you make selections or set preferences on our site, the site will send a request to your browser to record some of your selections in a "cookie" file. The cookie is a small text file that resides on your hard drive. From time to time, our site will request the cookie from your browser so we can retrieve the stored information and you do not have to repeatedly enter information. Should you choose not to accept these cookies you may not be able to use portions of our site.

Grandparents.com never uses cookies to store credit card information, nor is the information we store in the cookie intended to be read by any other web site.

Many browsers allow users to set the preferences to show a warning each time a cookie request is received. You can also set most browsers not to accept cookies at all.

At times, some of the advertisers on our site set cookies when you select their ad banner. We do not receive the data collected by those cookies.

## Policy Changes

As Grandparents.com grows and new features are added or legal requirements change, this privacy policy may be modified. Although all information submitted to Grandparents.com is subject to the terms and conditions of this policy and any modifications, you may remove or restrict access to your account by updating your account information at www.grandparents.com Any changes to the Grandparents.com privacy policy will be posted online.

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact us at [info@grandparents.com](mailto:info@grandparents.com)
