# Gradschools.com Privacy Policy

* * *

## Privacy Policy

* * *

Educational Directories Unlimited (EDU) supports a user's right to privacy and to access our information without registration or fee. Users have unlimited access to browse through our directories and our published information for their personal use. For other uses, please see our [copyright notice.](http://www.gradschools.com/legal.html)

Our web sites contain information regarding educational opportunities. Included in these directories are e-mail addresses and a link to a standardized web form. This form is used to relay your personal information to your chosen recipient. The data in this form in not viewed, stored or utilized by EDU, its staff members or any party other than your chosen recipient. The request form is converted into an e-mail message and sent to the recipient. It is not copied, stored or resold. 

The information you are submitting is being transmitted to your selected organization via an un-encrypted e-mail message. Any messages sent via un-encrypted e-mail may be susceptible to intercept and fraud. Once received by the academic provider, EDU has no control of the information you provided and cannot account for the recipients privacy policy.
