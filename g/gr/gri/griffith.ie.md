# Cookie Policy document | Griffith College

Griffith.ie uses cookies to improve your experience on our site. By remembering certain preferences and choices we can customise information to your needs. You can decide to disable cookies via your browser at any time, but this may impair some functionality of griffith.ie

We also use cookies associated with Google Analytics. Google provides code to collect information about how visitors use our site. This information is collected anonymously and does not identify an individual. Information collected by Google Analytics includes data about where visitors have come from, what pages they viewed and how long they spend on the site. We use this information to help us improve the way griffith.ie works to provide a better user experience.

For further information on the privacy policy of Google Analytics data please visit: [Safeguarding your data](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245)
