**Personal Information**  
GreenDIYenergy.com _does not collect or store any personal information_ from website visitors.  Only after you purchase our DIY Guides are you given the “option” to give your name, email address, etc. so that we can keep you updated with important renewable energy information. 

Your privacy is critically important to GreenDIYenergy.com and if you choose to stay in touch with us by giving your name, email address, etc your information will not be made public in any way.  You can unsubscribe from any communications from us at anytime by clicking the “unsubscribe” link found at the bottom of every automated email we send.

  
GreenDIYenergy.com has put  security measures in place to protect your information.  Any personal information you give is securely held by iContact, one of the industry leaders in database security. 

Any information that you provide will never be sold, shared, exchanged, rented, transferred or given to any other 3rd party company or individuals for any reason now or in the future. We hate spam as much as you do and we will protect your information to the fullest extent of the law. 

**Cookies**  
Cookies are used to record shopping cart information. This is done through Clickbank, the World’s Largest Digital Retailer and is used for only for the ordering process. 

**Payment Information**  
All payments are processed through Clickbank, the World’s Largest Digital Retailer.  GreenDIYenergy.com never sees or has access to credit card information, this is all handled through Clickbank which uses the most advanced SSL Encrypted Security technology available so your information is always secure. See the “Security” link for more details. ****

By viewing, visiting, using or interacting with this website or any other pop-up or advertisement on this website you are agreeing and consent to our Privacy Policy.
