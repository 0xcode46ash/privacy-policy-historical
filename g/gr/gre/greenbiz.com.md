# GreenBiz.com Privacy Policy | GreenBiz.com

Our privacy policy is very simple: You need not identify yourself or your affiliation to gain access to the resources on this Web site. GreenBiz.com's policy is to safeguard the personal information and protect the identity and confidentiality of all visitors and users.

If you choose to subscribe to GREENBUZZ, our free e-letter, you need only submit your e-mail address; no additional personal information is required. We promise never to sell, rent, or swap your address with others. We won't try to sell you anything. (We may occasionally survey you to ask your opinion of how we're doing -- but no more than once a year.)

This policy extends to the e-mail addresses of colleagues or friends you provide to us for purposes of sending a sample copy of GREENBUZZ, as well as to submissions to our Ask the Experts! feature and other questions or comments submitted to the site.

For more information, [please contact us](mailto:info@greenerworldmedia.com).
