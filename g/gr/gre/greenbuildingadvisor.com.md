# Privacy Policy | GreenBuildingAdvisor.com

We at Green Building Advisor and our company as a whole, The Taunton Press, Inc., are committed to respecting your privacy. As a community-oriented website and a paid resource for valuable building information, we collect personal information to better serve you as customers. We recognize that the personally identifiable information we may collect—including name, address, email address, and credit card information—needs to be protected. This privacy policy will inform you of what personally identifiable information or personal information we may collect from you when you visit our websites, sign up for our resources, or purchase subscriptions, books, and other products from our store. The policy also explains how we use such information.
