# Privacy policy - Grameen Foundation USA

[GFUSA](https://web.archive.org/) : Privacy policy

Privacy policy

Grameen Foundation USA (GFUSA) respects your right to privacy and is committed to maintaining your confidence and trust. We do not share your personal information with other organizations for commercial or non-commercial purposes.

GFUSA does not collect any information from you without your knowledge and permission, and does not ask for personal information to access most of our website. However, you must enter identifying information to access certain features. Information you provide GFUSA is stored in a secure location.

Information collection and usage

Currently, GFUSA requires personal information on the following pages:

 **“E-mail to a friend” pages:** To forward a page from our site you must enter your name, your email address and the email address(es) of anyone you are sending it to. GFUSA includes your name and email address when we send your message. This information is not used for any other purpose.

 **E-mail newsletter signup:** Information you provide to sign up for the e-mail newsletter will be used only for e-mail communications from GFUSA, and will not be shared with any other entity.

 **Feedback page:** Contact information you send us via the feedback page is used only to reply to your request, and is not stored in our database, unless you have requested otherwise.

Donation Information and Secure Transactions

 **Transaction security:** When you donate to GFUSA via our website, your donation information is encrypted and transmitted by way of a secure connection. GFUSA uses your credit card information _only_ for the purpose of processing your donation, and it is not disclosed for any other purpose.

 **Donation page:** When you donate to GFUSA, we collect credit card and contact information necessary to verify and complete the transaction, as well as your communication preferences. We will follow those preferences in using your personal contact information to send you future mailings.
