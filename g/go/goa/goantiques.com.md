# Antiques, Art, Collectibles & More - GoAntiques

**Privacy Policy**

**Information Collection and Use**

1) GoAntiques collects personal information when you register to buy or sell with us, when you use GoAntiques services, when you visit GoAntiques pages or the pages of certain GoAntiques partners, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. GoAntiques may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.

2) When you register to buy or sell items on the site we ask for information such as your name, address, email address, phone number, cell number, fax number, user name, credit card information and notification preferences. Once you register with GoAntiques and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us. 

3) GoAntiques collects information about your transactions.

4) GoAntiques automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including GoAntiques cookie information. GoAntiques uses two types of cookies, transient and persistent, to recognize our members and remember shopping list items. GoAntiques uses transient cookies to provide continuity from page to page. Within a single visit, these cookies store information such as your wish list and shopping list to simplify checkout. They do not contain any personally identifiable information. GoAntiques uses persistent cookies to identify members after the first visit. These cookies remain on the hard drive of a PC and allow a user to be recognized when entering our site. To access any secure areas such as your account information, you must still enter your password. You can disable cookies at any time through your browser options and still view the site. The cookie itself does not tell us who you are, your email address or anything else personal. We use cookies to provide our visitors with a better experience on our site.

5) GoAntiques uses your information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.   
Information Sharing and Disclosure

1) GoAntiques does not sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except under the following circumstances: 

a) We provide limited information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with GoAntiques under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help GoAntiques communicate with you about offers from GoAntiques and our partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information. 

b) We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to any person, violations of GoAntiques’ terms of use, or as otherwise required by law. 

c) We transfer information about you if GoAntiques is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, GoAntiques will notify you if information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy. 

**Data Security and Storage**  
In order to protect and preserve the security of the data we collect, we have established procedures to safeguard the information collected throughout this website. To protect your security, we will also take commercially reasonable steps to verify your identify before granting access or making corrections.

Notwithstanding these procedures, we do not accept any liability for misuse of the site by others or from viruses, or links to other sites or to objectionable material.

We do not guarantee the preservation of data, records or other information that may expire or be removed or deleted, all without notice or liability. Seller grants GoAntiques non-exclusive publishing rights in print or electronically of uploaded images and data.   
Records Retention, Usage

GoAntiques shall have the right to retain records of all information including input, and sales pertaining to your access to the site. GoAntiques will not, however: (i) disclose transaction data comprised of prices charged, transaction volume, or identification of you to any third parties unless such data is grouped with other clients’ data and is presented in an aggregate form, or in the event that you specifically authorize such release. GoAntiques shall use commercially reasonable efforts so that such aggregate form does not permit the recipient to identify you or any of the specific transactions pertaining to any of them; or (ii) use such data to aid GoAntiques or any third party in providing services in competition with you. In the event GoAntiques discloses such transaction data to any third party, we shall make a good faith effort to ensure that your name and other personal information cannot be deduced by such third parties from such disclosed transaction data.

Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences

1) You can edit your GoAntiques Account Information, including your preferences, at any time by logging into your account.

2) We reserve the right to send you certain communications relating to the GoAntiques service, such as service announcements, administrative messages and the GoAntiques Buyers and/or Sellers Newsletters that are considered part of your GoAntiques account, without offering you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving them. We will endeavor to limit this to only messages that we deem necessary for the usage of your account.

**Confidentiality and Security**  
1) We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information in order to do their jobs. 

2) We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you. 

3) Your GoAntiques Account Information is password-protected. 

4) In certain areas GoAntiques uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.   
Changes to this Privacy Policy

1) GoAntiques may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your GoAntiques account or through our newsletters.
