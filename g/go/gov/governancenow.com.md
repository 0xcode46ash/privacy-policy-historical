# GovernanceNow.com | Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. We need your personal information only for allowing you to use certain interactive facilities on this website. This information shall not be shared with any third party for commercial purposes though it can be shared with authorities if required under law. Technical information about your computer is recorded to facilitate your visit to this site.
