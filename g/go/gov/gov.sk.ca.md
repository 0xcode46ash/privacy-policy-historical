# Government of Saskatchewan - Privacy Statement

  
The Government of Saskatchewan is committed to protecting your privacy. Its entire operations, including this web site, are subject to [ "The Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act (1992)"](http://www.saskjustice.gov.sk.ca/legislation/summaries/freedomofinfoact.shtml). This law is based on the principles contained in the Canadian Standards Association's 1996 [Model Code for the Protection of Personal Information.](http://www.csa.ca/standards/privacy/default.asp?load=code#model%20code) The simplest way to gain an understanding of the Saskatchewan Government's approach to privacy is to read the on-line [CSA Privacy Principles](http://www.csa.ca/standards/privacy/code/Default.asp?articleID=5286). 

When you visit a Government of Saskatchewan website, the server may automatically collect and retain a limited amount of data essential to the efficient operation and security of that site. An automated process may analyze this data and a statistical report may be created to help us identify usage trends and improve our service. Under no circumstances does the Government of Saskatchewan use such information to identify or track individual users.

Some Government of Saskatchewan websites may distribute a small file to your computer, called a "cookie". Such files allow us to provide you with personalized information on your future visits to our websites; they are not used for any other purpose.

When you voluntarily send us electronic mail or complete an electronic form that includes identifying information about yourself, we will keep a record of this information so that we can respond to your inquiry.

If you have further concerns about privacy in the operations of the Government of Saskatchewan please contact the [Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Office.](http://www.saskjustice.gov.sk.ca/foi/default.shtml)
