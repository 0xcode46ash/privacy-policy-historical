Number1 Appliance is dedicated to protecting the private information of our customers. To insure this privacy we will always use encryption technology to process orders. 

Number1 Appliance, through its web site, collects your information for processing your payments and arranging delivery. We do not share, sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for unknown reasons.

The ordering information you provide - including credit card information is secured using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption. This SSL technology is used to prevent such information from being intercepted and read as it is transmitted over the Internet.
