# Privacy Policy

1) GoodTherapy.org records your Name, Phone (optional), Email and Message when you send an email to a therapist. This information is kept confidential and no one but the therapist you sent the email to can see this information. If you also opted-in to the non-member newsletter, then your email & name are used to send the newsletters to you. However, GoodTherapy.org never rents, sells or gives this information to any third party.

2) GoodTherapy.org collects information entered by therapists when they register (or try to register) for membership. The information about the therapist’s practice is made public as can be viewed in any therapist profile. The other information (including but not limited to username, email address, password, etc) is kept private in a database and may be used by our internal departments if needed (such as debugging errors or analyzing outcome of marketing campaigns). However, GoodTherapy.org never rents, sells or gives this information to any third party.

3) GoodTherapy.org does not store credit card information on our servers. All transactions are processed through our SSL FirstData payment page and all data is stored on the FirstData server. You can read the Privacy Policy of FirstData here:  
<http://www.firstdata.com/en_us/privacy>

4) GoodTherapy.org collects your email address when you subscribe to the blog. This email is used to send you blogposts summaries and you can unsubscribe anytime using unsubscribe option(s) found in the email and/or on the site. GoodTherapy.org never rents, sells or gives this information to any third party.

5) GoodTherapy.org uses Google Analytics which collects information about your browsing experience on our site; this information is used in an anonymous way and not associated with a specific individual. You can read about Google Analytics’ Terms of Service & Google’s Privacy Policy on the following pages respectively:  
<http://www.google.com/analytics/tos.html>  
<http://www.google.com/privacypolicy.html>

6) GoodTherapy.org uses WhosClickingWho.com to prevent clickfraud. This service collects IP address and other data when someone clicks on GoodTherapy.org Ads from Google or its partner sites. You can read the Privacy Policy of WhosClickingWho.com here: <http://www.whosclickingwho.com/privacypolicy.html>

7) GoodTherapy.org may use other Web Analytics services and/or software to collect information about your browsing activities. This information is used to analyze the performance of the site and associated marketing campaigns. Some of the services/software like Google Analytics may store this information on their own servers.
