# Privacy - Gooya Daily

**_Gooyadaily.com_**  collects only aggregate information pertaining to visitors to its network of sites.

_**Gooyadaily.com**_  uses this information to help improve its web site and services, and to customize  
the content of its pages for its regular viewers.

**_Gooyadaily.com_**  does not sell or commercially make available specific information about its users or members other than in aggregate form, except in cooperation with law enforcement bodies in regards to violations of applicable laws.

**_Gooyadaily.com_**  maintains a database of member information which is used only for internal purposes such as advertising & technical support.

Any  _ **Gooyadaily.com**_  member may remove their information from our database at any time by sending an email to: info @ gooya . com with the word “remove” in the subject line.
