# Privacy Policy - Roost

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail and sign up for services, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, aggregate information on service usage, and information volunteered by the customer. The information we collect is used to improve the content of our website, improve our products, and the quality of our service.

It is not shared with or sold to other organizations for commercial purposes, except under the following circumstances:

It is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of [Terms of Service](http://roost.me/pages/terms/ "Terms"), or as otherwise required by law.

We transfer information about you if Notice Software LLC or AlertRocket is acquired by or merged with another company.

### Information Gathering & Usage

  * When you register for Notice Software products, include Smartloop, NativePush.com, TextPush, or BlogRocket we ask for information such as your name, email address, billing address, credit card information. [Customers who sign up are not required to enter a credit card for use of the free tier.]
  * Notice Software LLC uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provisioning, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research.
  * Notice Software LLC may use aggregated versions of collected information for the following general purposes: marketing, public relations, and research.



### Cookies

  * A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a  
web site?s computers and stored on your computer?s hard drive.
  * Cookies are required to use the Notice Software websites.
  * We use cookies to record current session information, but do not use permanent cookies. You may be required to login to AlertRocket, NativePush.com, TextPush, or BlogRocket websites after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents. You will also be required to login if you clear the cookie from your browser.



### Data Storage

Notice Software LLC uses third party vendors and hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run Smartloop, NativePush.com, TextPush, and BlogRocket. Although Notice Software LLC owns the code, databases, and all rights to the Smartloop application and all BlogRocket applications, you retain all rights to your data.

### Disclosure

Notice Software LLC may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with subpoenas or when your actions violate the [Terms of Service](http://roost.me/pages/terms/ "Terms").

### Changes

Notice Software LLC may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the email address you use to sign in to any of the Notice Software products.
