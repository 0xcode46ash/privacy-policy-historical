# Goldbely | Privacy and Security

## What This Privacy Policy Covers

This privacy policy covers the use of the web site located at [https://www.goldbely.com](https://web.archive.org/privacy-and-security) and the services provided by Goldbely, Inc. ("Goldbely"), including how treats personal information that Goldbely collects and receives. Pursuant to Goldbely's Terms of Use, you must be 18 years and older to use this site or have permission from a legal guardian. Therefore, this policy makes no provision for individuals under 18 years old. This policy does not apply to the practices of third parties that Goldbely does not own or control, or individuals that Goldbely does not employ or manage. This policy is effective on January 1, 2012. 

Information Collection and How it is Used General Information: Goldbely will not sell or disclose your name or email address to third parties without your explicit consent, except as specified in this privacy policy. Goldbely collects personal information when you register to use Goldbely services or to post content on Goldbely. At registration you are asked for your email address and possibly other optional information. If you would like to obtain a Seller account, Goldbely will ask for your business address, phone number, and payment information. Goldbely automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookies, and the pages you request. Goldbely uses information for the following general purposes: products and services provisioning, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, research, and anonymous reporting. IP addresses are automatically reported by your browser each time you view a web page. Your IP address is stored in Goldbely's user registration databases when you register with Goldbely. IP addresses may be used for various research purposes, and/or delivering unique functionality in the Goldbely Marketplace. 

## Service Announcements

Goldbely will send you a welcome email when you sign up for Goldbely's services. Goldbely will send you service announcements on occasions when it is necessary to do so. 

You may not opt-out of service-related communications, which are not promotional in nature. If you do not wish to receive them, you have the option to deactivate your account by emailing support@goldbely.com from the email address you signed up with. 

## Administrative Messages

Goldbely may periodically email you with administrative messages. Goldbely will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent. Your information may be combined with information Goldbely collects from other companies and used to improve and personalize services, content and advertising. If you don't wish to receive administrative messages or participate in these programs, simply opt-out by emailing support@goldbely.com. 

## Choice/Opt-out

If you no longer wish to receive administrative messages, you may opt-out by emailing support@goldbely.com. Please note that if you do not wish to receive administrative messages from us, those notices will still govern your use of the Goldbely website and service, and you are responsible for reviewing such notices for changes. 

Password: Keep your password secure. You are fully responsible for all activity, liability and damage resulting from your failure to maintain password confidentiality. You agree to immediately notify Goldbely of any unauthorized use of your password or any breach of security. You also agree that Goldbely cannot and will not be liable for any loss or damage arising from your failure to keep your password secure. 

Account Information: You must keep your account information up-to-date and accurate at all times, including a valid email address. 

Account Transfer: You may not transfer or sell your Goldbely account and User ID to another party. If you are registering as a business entity, you personally guarantee that you have the authority to bind the entity to this Agreement. 

## Newsletter Emails

Goldbely users are offered subscriptions to Goldbely email newsletters. To opt-in, Goldbely will ask for your email address. This email address will only be used for this feature. You may change your subscription or unsubscribe at any time by following the instructions contained in the email. 

## Friend Features

"Share with a Friend" allows members to share information about a seller or a specific item listed on Goldbely by sending a personalized email message. If you choose to use Goldbely's "Share with a Friend" feature, Goldbely will ask for the information needed to send the message, such as your friend's email address. Goldbely will then automatically send your friend an email, inviting your friend to visit the site. Goldbely does not store "Share with a Friend" information on our servers. 

## Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. You can configure your browser to accept all cookies, reject all cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. If you reject all cookies, you may not be able to take full advantage of the Goldbely service. Goldbely uses its own cookies for a number of purposes, including to: Access your information when you "sign in"; keep track of preferences you specify while you are using Goldbely's services; display local product offerings; estimate and report Goldbely's total audience size and traffic; conduct research to improve Goldbely's content and services. Goldbely cookies do not collect personal information, and Goldbely does not combine information collected through cookies with other personal information to tell us who you are or what your username or email address is. 

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

When you post a message, your Goldbely ID or alias is visible to other users. If you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return. Goldbely may be required to disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with subpoenas or when your actions violate the Goldbely Terms of Use. Goldbely may disclose personally identifiable information to parties in compliance with Goldbely's Copyright and Intellectual Property Policy, as Goldbely in Goldbely's sole discretion believes necessary or appropriate in connection with an investigation of fraud, intellectual property infringement, piracy, or other unlawful activity. In such events, Goldbely may disclose name, street address, city, state, zip code, country, phone number, email address and company name. Goldbely may share aggregated demographic information with business partners. 

Goldbely is affiliated with a variety of businesses and works closely with them. These businesses may operate stores on the Goldbely website or sell items to you from the Goldbely website. Goldbely may also provide services or sell products jointly with affiliated businesses. Due to the nature of Goldbely's business model, when an affiliated business is associated with your transaction, you will share customer information that is related to such transaction with that affiliated business. 

Goldbely employs other companies and people to perform tasks on our behalf and needs to share your information with them to provide products and services to you. Examples include but are not limited to fulfilling orders, handling billing disputes, analyzing data, providing marketing assistance and providing customer service. 

Goldbely will not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with nonaffiliated parties except as specified in this Privacy Policy to provide products or services you have requested or when Goldbely has your permission. It may be necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Goldbely's Terms of Use, as specified in this Privacy Policy or as otherwise required by law. In some cases, Goldbely may choose to buy or sell assets. In these types of transactions, customer information is typically one of the business assets that is transferred. Goldbely will transfer information about you if Goldbely is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Goldbely will notify you by email or by putting a prominent notice on the Goldbely web site before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy. 

As part of the buying and selling process, you may obtain another Goldbely user's email address, shipping address and/or other information. Pursuant to Goldbely's Terms of Use, you have a limited license to use this information. This information should only be used for Goldbely-related communications or for Goldbely-facilitated transactions. Goldbely has not granted you a license to use the information for unsolicited commercial messages. Without limiting the foregoing, without express consent from the user, you are not licensed to add any Goldbely user to your email or physical mail list. 

## Third Parties

Except as otherwise expressly included in this Privacy Policy, this document addresses only the use and disclosure of information Goldbely collects from you. If you disclose your information to others, whether they are buyers or sellers on Goldbely or throughout the internet, different rules may apply to their use or disclosure of the information you disclose to them. Goldbely does not control the privacy policies of third parties, and you are subject to the privacy policies of those third parties where applicable. Goldbely is not responsible for the privacy or security practices of other websites on the Internet, even those linked to or from the Goldbely site. Goldbely encourages you to ask questions before you disclose your personal information to others. 

## Editing and Deleting Account Information

You can change your Goldbely Account Information (except your username), at any time. Goldbely may send you certain communications relating to the Goldbely service, such as service announcements and administrative messages that are considered part of your Goldbely account. You can delete your Goldbely account by emailing support@goldbely.com. Please note that your identification, billing and contact information may remain in Goldbely's records for some period. 

Goldbely limits access to personal information about you to employees who Goldbely believes reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs. Your Goldbely Account Information is password-protected. 

If your personally identifiable information changes you may correct or update it by making the change on your Goldbely account page. You may delete or deactivate your account by emailing Customer Support at info@Goldbely.com. Goldbely will respond to your request within 10-20 business days. 

## Security

The security of your personal information is important to us. Your Goldbely account information is protected by a password for your privacy and security. You need to work to protect against unauthorized access to your password and to your computer by signing off once you have finished using a shared computer. When you enter sensitive information (such as credit card number) for registration Goldbely encrypts that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). Goldbely follows generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once Goldbely receives it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, while Goldbely strives to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, Goldbely cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on Goldbely's Web site, you can email Goldbely at info@Goldbely.com. 

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

If this privacy policy is changed, Goldbely will post changes so that you are aware of those changes. Goldbely reserves the right to modify this privacy statement at any time, so please review it frequently. If Goldbely makes material changes to this policy you will be notified here, by email, or other places Goldbely deems appropriate.
