# Privacy Policy - Gold Price OZ

### Privacy Policy

#### Privacy Policy for Goldpriceoz.com

At the website of goldpriceoz.com, we recognize that the private personal information is pretty important. This privacy policy document summarizes what types of personal information we receive and collect when you visit goldpriceoz.com. In any case, we never sell your personal information to third party.

#### Log Files

Same with many other website, we collect the log files, and use the data which contained in log files. It includes your IP(internet protocol) address, your Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), the browser that you used to visit this website, date/time stamp.

#### Cookies and Web Beacons

we do use cookies to store information about your personal preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
