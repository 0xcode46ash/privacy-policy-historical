# #1 Free online global petition hosting -

**Understanding**  
gopetition.com recognises that your privacy is very important to you and that you have a right to control your personal information. We know that providing personal information is an act of trust and we take that seriously. 

**Collection of Information**  
gopetition.com will not collect or monitor any personal information about you without your consent unless we believe it is necessary:  
1\. Because it is required by law.   
2\. To provide you with a service which you have requested.   
3\. To implement our terms of service.   
4\. To protect the rights or property of gopetition.com, any gopetition.com user, or any member of the public.   
5\. To lessen a serious threat to a person's health or safety. 

You may provide gopetition.com with information directly, such as through subscribing to email lists or identifying yourself when using particular services. The amount of information we collect about you depends on how you use our sites. The only personal information we collect is what you tell us about yourself. gopetition.com may use cookies to help maximise your enjoyment of our sites. Cookies in and of themselves do not personally identify users, although they do identify a user's browser. Please see further information on how we may use cookies below. gopetition.com may in the future introduce a membership system that will give you the option of enjoying enhanced services on our site. Again, any changes in how we use your personal information will only occur with your consent. 

**Cookies**   
Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to your computer's hard disk for record-keeping purposes. Most Web browsers are set to accept cookies. However, if you do not wish to receive any cookies you may set your browser to refuse cookies. In some instances this may mean you will not be able to take full advantage of parts of our service. The use of cookies can enhance your enjoyment of what is offered by gopetition.com, because they can help personalise your experience of our website. They can make it easier for you to shop online and can ensure that information provided to you is most relevant to your interests. Cookies can also provide us with aggregate information on how people use our websites, and which advertisements are effective, which helps us to keep improving our site. Cookies in and of themselves do not personally identify users, although they do identify a user's browser. The cookies simply operate as a unique identifier which help us to know what our customers find interesting and useful on our website, almost like a continuing questionnaire. 

**Your information**  
gopetition.com will not use or disclose any information about you without your consent, or access your communications on our websites, unless:  
1\. Required by law.   
2\. We believe it necessary to provide you with a service which you have requested.   
3\. To implement our terms of service.   
4\. To protect the rights or property of gopetition.com, any gopetition.com user, or any member of the public.   
5\. To lessen a serious threat to a person's health or safety. 

gopetition.com and its affiliates recognise the trust you place in us when you give us personal information. In order to operate the website or deliver a service we may sometimes share your personal information with an affiliate or with a service partner. We will not otherwise disclose your personal information to other companies without your consent. We need to obtain various information from our users to ensure the effectiveness of our service. The information obtained is used as a whole to gain general statistics so we can better target our advertising. We will not send you "spam" (advertising information) without first asking you whether you wish to have promotional information sent to you. If you are receiving promotional information about gopetition.com and do not wish to receive this information any longer, please go to http://www.gopetition.com/mlremove.php.   


Please allow seven days for this request to be processed.   


**Our commitment**  
gopetition.com will endeavour to take all reasonable steps to keep secure any information which we hold about you, and to keep this information accurate and up to date. Your information is stored on secure servers. In addition, our employees and data processors are obliged to respect the confidentiality of any personal information held by gopetition.com. However, gopetition.com will not be held responsible for events arising from unauthorised access to your personal information. Please notify us immediately if there is any unauthorised use of your account by any other Internet user or any other breach of security. 

gopetition.com provides links to websites outside of our service. These linked sites are not under the control of gopetition.com, and we are not responsible for the conduct of companies linked to our website. Before disclosing your personal information on any other website, we advise you to examine the terms and conditions of using their websites. 

**Advertisements on gopetition.com**  


This site, gopetition.com, uses the services of Engage for the serving and/or targeting of ads, promotions and other marketing messages. To do this, Engage collects anonymous data through the use of cookies. To learn more about Engage, including your ability to opt out of the Engage system, go to <http://www.engage.com/privacy>
