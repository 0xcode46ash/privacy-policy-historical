# Privacy & Security

### This is the website of Gobiya, LLC

323-744-1338, Please ask for Steve Martin

Any collection of information by our users is voluntary which may include, but not limited to:

  * Contact information (name & last name, name of organization, address, telephone number, email, company’s URL)
  * Inquiries and project information
  * Resumes (as provided via our resume upload system)
  * Survey information
  * Site registrations



Please provide your exact name, address, phone and URL when asked. We will and do not share this information with any other organizations. We only use this information for provision of requested or related information. If you want to stop any communications in the future from us please request using our contact form.

If you would like to correct your information please submit the changes request through our contact form.

If you feel that we are not following the rules stated above to please contact us at the information above or by our contact form.
