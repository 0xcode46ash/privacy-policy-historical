# Goby Privacy Policy - goby.com

Effective September 23, 2009

At Goby we respect your privacy. The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to let you know what information we are collecting while you are on Goby, how we are using it and with whom it is being shared. By using the Goby site, you are agreeing to the collection and use of certain information as outlined below.

###### Information we collect:

Our servers automatically receive and store certain information whenever you visit our website including your IP address, referring URL, browser type and the date/time of your visit. We also utilize cookies to enhance the web site experience and to understand how users are interacting with the Goby site. A cookie is a small text file sent to your computer in order to uniquely identify your browser. We may use both session cookies, which expire once you close your browser, as well as persistent cookies, which stay on your computer until deleted. 

###### How we use the information:

We use the information that we collect to administer the Goby site, diagnose system issues and enhance the website experience. We may use cookies to identify whether you have visited the site before, to remember any information you may have previously provided and to track your site usage in order to target search results and advertisements based on your interests. We may also merge information collected about you with that collected from other users in order to identify and analyze trends with which to continuously improve the Goby website.

###### Information shared with third parties:

Goby may use third party companies to serve advertisements on the Goby site and may share information collected about your searches queries with these companies so that the ad-serving company can present you with the most relevant, targeted advertisements based on your interests.

In the course of serving advertisements, these third party ad-serving companies may place or recognize a cookie, or another tracking technology such as a pixel, on your computer. Please note that the Goby Privacy Policy does not cover the use of information collected by any third-party ad-serving company. To learn more, or for information on how to limit the use of information collected by ad-serving companies, visit <http://www.networkadvertising.org/>

Goby may also share user trends information, on an aggregate basis, with certain third parties including its advertisers and distribution partners. 

###### Links to third party websites:

This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected by Goby.com. As a search engine, Goby contains links to third party websites which are not owned or controlled by Goby. We are not responsible for the actions or privacy practices of any sites accessed from the Goby.com site. We recommend that you read the privacy statements of each website that you access from Goby.com which collects personally identifiable information.

#### Changes to Privacy Policy:

This Privacy Policy was last updated on September 23, 2009.

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time, so please review it periodically to remain familiar with it. If we make significant, material changes to this policy we will notify you here, and via a messaging on the Goby.com home page.
