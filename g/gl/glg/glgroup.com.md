# Gerson Lehrman Group

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy is intended to describe how Gerson Lehrman Group collects, uses, shares and protects information obtained from you on this website.

**Information We Collect**
    

You may visit our website without disclosing any personally identifiable information. We collect personally identifiable information if you choose to provide it to us by requesting us to correspond with you, process an information request or otherwise by completing a form on the website. E-mail messages that you send to us, including to addresses contained on this website, are not governed by this policy.

**Cookies**
    

We may use browser cookies to collect non-personally identifiable information. Our web pages may incorporate web beacons or similar tracking technologies to allow us to track how the website is used. Such technologies are used to collect non-personally identifiable information, e.g. the name of your internet service provider, the IP address of your computer, your browser software and operating system, the identity of any linked-from or linked-to website, and other similar information. We may combine your information with similar information collected from other users to help improve the website and its services.

**Use & Disclosure of Information**
    

We use information provided to us through this website to provide you with information about our services or company in which we think you may have an interest. We do not share your personally identifiable information obtained through this website with third parties for their marketing purposes.

There is a separate [Council Member Privacy Policy](https://www.glgcouncils.com/privacy.aspx) applicable to our Council Members.

**Information Security**
    

We do employ security measures to protect against loss, misuse, and alteration of information you provide through this website and under our control. No security system is completely secure. Accordingly, we cannot guarantee the complete security of the information you provide us. We will retain your information for a reasonable period or as long as the law may require.

**Changes to This Policy**
    

Any changes to the policy will be published on this website. By your continued use of the website, you consent to the terms of the revised policy.

**Links to Other Websites**
    

This website may link to websites maintained by others. Please be aware that these third-party websites are governed only by their own privacy policies. We cannot be responsible for these policies.

**Questions, Comments or Changes to Your Information**
    

All comments, queries and requests relating to our use of your information submitted via this website are welcomed and should be addressed to Legal Department, Gerson Lehrman Group, Inc. 850 Third Avenue, 9th Floor, New York, New York 10022 (212) 984-8500 [info@glgroup.com](mailto:info@glgroup.com).
