# Privacy Policy | Glassdoor.com.au

Revised 9 Sep 2014. 

Glassdoor, Inc. ("we" or "us") receives information about you through your use of our websites, emails and mobile applications ("Glassdoor"). When you use Glassdoor, you consent to our collection, use and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy and Cookie Policy. 

This Privacy and Cookie Policy explains what kind of information we collect, how we use that information and how we may share it. We collect both personal and non-personal information (as those terms are defined in this policy) and this Privacy and Cookie Policy explains what steps we take to secure your personal information and how you can manage your choices about our collection and use of your personal information. You can view the complete Privacy and Cookie Policy [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20141010042242id_/http%3A//www.glassdoor.com.au/about/privacy-full.htm).
