# Privacy Policy - Gluten-Free Living

Thank you for visiting Gluten-Free Living. Our mission is to provide you with the information you need to live a happy, healthy gluten-free life. In carrying out our mission, we collect certain information from you including your name, postal and email addresses, telephone number and credit card information.

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining our information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

We may use the information you provide about yourself to fulfill your requests for our products, to respond to your inquiries about our products, or to contact you about your subscription or other products we offer. If you choose to submit content for publication (e.g., a “letter to the editor”), we may publish your name, city and state and other information you provide to Gluten-Free Living.

## Opt-Out Policy

If you prefer not to receive traditional mail or promotions from Gluten-Free Living or any companies who might offer services related to the gluten free life, please click here.

If you prefer not to receive on-line notices or promotions from Gluten-Free Living, or any companies who might offer services related to the gluten-free life, please click here.

Our website may contain links to other sites whose practices may be different from ours. Since we have no control over the other sites’ policies, you should consult their privacy notices.

This notice may be changed by Gluten-Free Living. The revised notice will appear on this page so that you are aware of the information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we may disclose it.

If you have any questions or concerns about Gluten-Free Living’s Privacy Notice, please send an email to [info@glutenfreeliving.com](mailto:info@glutenfreeliving.com) or write to Gluten-Free Living, 25 Braintree Hill Office Park, Suite 404, Braintree, MA 02184
