# Globex Designs Inc. - Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy

### Globex Designs Privacy Policy

Last Modified: July 21, 2008

Privacy is very important on the Internet and at Globex Designs we take all measures to ensure all our visitors' privacy is optimized. This policy applies to all pages located on the Globex Designs website.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us by visiting [the Contact Page](http://www.globexdesigns.com/contact.php "the Contact Page").

We offer several services and products that requires no registration, such as the Gmail Redesigned project. For other services such as our Client Center and the Forums we may collect the following types of information:

Information you provide - When you sign up for a new Globex Designs account we ask you for some personal information such as your name, email address and password. We do not collect or store any billing information such as credit cards, bank account numbers, or billing addresses. All transactions are done via a third party service such as [PayPal.com](http://www.paypal.com/ "PayPal").

Cookies - When you visit this site, we send several cookies (small files containing system information) to your computer that unique identifies your browser. We use cookies to improve the quality of our service by storing use preferences and tracking user trends, such as which of our resources you found most useful. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some services may not work if your cookies are disabled.

Log information - When you browse the site, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website. These server logs may include information such as your web request, IP address, browser, language and the date of your request.

Email communication - When you send in an inquery to our staff, we retain all email communication sent in order to respond to your requests in an efficient manner.

We never provide any of this gathered information to our affiliates, partners or any other third party website without clearly informing you of such actions.
