# Gptreasure.com - Privacy

When a user registers to Gptreasure.com he needs to enter: username, password, and email.  
This is the only minimal information Gptreasure collects from its registered users.  
Gptreasure.com is committed to protect its users' privacy, and will not share this information with anyone else!

In addition, there are some cases when a user gives out more information than what mentioned above.  
For instance, if there's a problem with an offer that a user tried to complete and the user needs to provide Gptreasure.com with personal information (such as print screen of an order that he placed).  
Gptreasure.com is committed to protect users' privacy

 _in any case whatsoever_

and all information Gptreasure.com gets from its users will be kept confidential.
