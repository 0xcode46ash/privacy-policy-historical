### Privacy at GSI Commerce

The following privacy statements address our information collection practices with respect to certain types of information that we receive:

  * [The Safe Harbor Privacy Statement](https://web.archive.org/web/20121211121842id_/http%3A//www.gsicommerce.com/safe_harbor_privacy.php) addresses how we collect, use, and disclose personally identifiable information that we receive in the United States from the European Union and Switzerland.
