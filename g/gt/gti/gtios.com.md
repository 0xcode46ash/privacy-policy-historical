# Privacy and security | GTI Recruiting Solutions

GTI Recruiting Solutions is committed to protecting the privacy of the users of our website, applicant tracking systems and services, in accordance with the 1998 Data Protection Act.

We may collect personal contact information from you, for example, when you apply for a job using one of our online application systems or services, or if you sign up for our newsletter. We only ever use this data with your permission and for the use specified. We never use data to carry out market research or pass it on to any persons or organisations for other purposes. We destroy any data as soon as we no longer need it. You can ask to see any data we have regarding you at any time. You can also ask us to delete it.

GTI Recruiting Solutions uses cookies on its website to collect analytical information about our users, as well as improve the user experience. No personal data is attached to these cookies and they can be disabled by altering the relevant settings in your web browser. We do not use session files to log your individual use of our website. We collect website usage data in aggregate form only. We use this data to analyse which pages on the website are most useful to our viewers and to identify which websites refer the most visitors to our site.
