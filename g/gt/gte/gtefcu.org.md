# GTE Federal Credit Union | Privacy Notice

**GTE Federal Credit Union Privacy Disclosure**

GTE Federal Credit Union recognizes that our members expect the highest level of security, privacy and confidentiality when it comes to their personal information.  We are committed to maintaining that level.  It is our policy that all personal information our members supply to us will be considered confidential.

We have not, do not, and will not disclose any member’s personal information to any outside organization for their use.  We have not, do not, and will not sell our member’s information to any outside organization.  We do share information about our members with reputable credit bureaus, with other financial institutions while conducting bona fide corporate business, and with businesses we have chosen to partner with in order to offer a broader array of products and services.

GTE Federal Credit Union collects nonpublic personal information about you from the following sources:

  1. Information we receive from you on applications or forms; 
  2. Information about your transactions with us or others; 
  3. Demographic information about you we collect from other sources; and 
  4. Information we receive from consumer reporting agencies.



 We may disclose information about you to the following types of third parties: 

  1. Financial service providers, such as those that provide mortgage, investment, credit card and insurance services on our behalf; or 
  2. Non-financial companies such as indirect lending partners; printing and mailing services firms; consulting firms; research firms, and demographic compilation firms.



We may disclose all the information we collect, as described above, to companies that perform marketing services on our behalf or to other financial institutions with whom we have joint marketing agreements.  We may also disclose information about you under other circumstances, as permitted or required by law.

If you decide to terminate your membership with GTE Federal Credit Union or become an inactive member, we will not share information we have collected about you, except as may be permitted or required by law.

Our credit union restricts access to your personal and account information to those employees who need to know that information to provide products or services to you.  We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to guard your nonpublic personal information.
