# FyberSearch - Privacy Policy

|    | 

Information that FyberSearch Collects 

  
FyberSearch may collect and save the following information: 
* Your computer's IP Address.
* The files you view while using this website and when you visited them.
* The web browser you are using.
* Your search settings.
* Information you enter into **any** forms on this website.
* The URL you were at that referred you to this website.

How the Collected Information is Used 

  
FyberSearch may use the above information in the following ways: 
* All information may be used to create statistics that will be shown to certain individuals as well as the public. These statistics will not include your email address or your computer IP address.
* FyberSearch does not rent or sale your email address or computer IP address. There may be certain times where the release of this type of information to another person and/or business is required by a court order or to complete a job that FyberSearch has assigned a person and/or business.
* You are **not** required to enter your email address when you add a URL to FyberSearch. If you do you will receive one automatically generated email. Before 12-01-05 deciding to add a URL to FyberSearch would cause you to be signed up for the newsletter but that policy is no longer in effect.
* If you create a FyberSearch [member's account](https://web.archive.org/members/) your name, email address and password will be saved and you may be emailed updates relating to the [member's area](https://web.archive.org/members/) from time to time. You have the option to [contact](https://web.archive.org/web/20060616012623id_/http%3A//www.fybersearch.com/contact.php) FyberSearch and request that your [member's account](https://web.archive.org/members/) be deleted. If you do make this request, you will no longer receive any emails.
* If you sign up to receive the FyberSearch [newsletter](https://web.archive.org/web/20060616012623id_/http%3A//www.fybersearch.com/newsletter.php) your email address will be saved and you should not be emailed anything except for the [newsletter](https://web.archive.org/web/20060616012623id_/http%3A//www.fybersearch.com/newsletter.php) as a result of signing up. This newsletter is primarily non-commercial and serves as a way for FyberSearch to communicate to interested parties about the developments we are making. If you unsubscribed but still received another copy of the newsletter it was not intentional on our part. You should immediately [alert us](https://web.archive.org/web/20060616012623id_/http%3A//www.fybersearch.com/contact.php) of the problem so we can look into it and immediately correct any problems.
* If you fill out the [show your support](https://web.archive.org/web/20060616012623id_/http%3A//www.fybersearch.com/newsletter.php) form then all information you enter will be saved and displayed to the public. Please do not enter any information you wish to keep private.
* FyberSearch will not be held responsible for any circumstances that are out of the control of FyberSearch that result in a violation of this privacy policy. Examples of these situations: people illegally breaking into this website, software malfunction and/or equipment malfunction.
  

* Please go to the [terms of service](https://web.archive.org/web/20060616012623id_/http%3A//www.fybersearch.com/terms-of-service.php) section.
  

* Please [contact](https://web.archive.org/web/20060616012623id_/http%3A//www.fybersearch.com/contact.php) FyberSearch if you have any questions or comments regarding this privacy policy.
