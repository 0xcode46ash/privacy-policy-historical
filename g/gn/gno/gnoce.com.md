# Privacy & Cookies - Fashion Charms, Bracelets, Necklaces and More

## Personal Data

We understand the importance of your personal information remaining secure. If you use this Site, you are responsible to keep the confidentiality of the information you submit. You agree to accept responsibility for all activities that occur under My Account or password. We reserve the right to refuse service, terminate accounts and to remove or edit content submitted by you in the My Account area of Gnoce.   
Moreover, Gnoce will provide you with the benefits and services if you become our members. You must consent to granting Gnoce use this information for the stated purpose, such as analyzing general market and purchasing behavior. We will not send your personal information, including your email address and more, to any third party without your permission.

## The Use of Personal Information

In general, we will use your personal information in the following ways:  
Analyze general market. Your order can help us collect the fashionable and popular trends in jewelry market.  
Improve our site. We focus on your usage and feedback to make our site much better. This is a useful method to improve our customer service as well.   
Send useful emails. When you login our site, subscribe our newsletter or place an order, the email address you provide to us can receive emails about your order and Gnoce new update.  
Notice that we will not sell, exchange or transfer your information to any third party in any conditions.

## Cookies

Just remember that cookies will not harm your PC and do not contain any viruses. Operator just uses cookies (tracking technology) with the purpose of making the websites more user-friendly, efficient, and secure. However, the cookies will not keep you from visiting Gnoce in any situation because of not using them. Your browser can be configured so that cookies can be accepted with your permission or completely blocked.
