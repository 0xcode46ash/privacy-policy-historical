# Play Free Dress Up Games at GirlDressup.Net

**1\. Privacy and information collection policy**

This privacy and information collection policy explain how we obtain information made available on the[ http://www.GirlDressup.net](http://www.girldressup.net/ "Dress up Games") website (the "Site"), and govern the information you provide us, whether actively (e.g., Personally Identifiable Information you submit to us) or passively (e.g., information we obtain through the use of Cookies). 

**2\. How We Obtain The Information We Make Available On The Site**

[GirlDressup.net](http://www.girldressup.net/ "Dress up Games") compiles information regarding websites from the publicly available "whois" databases which domain name registries and registrars publish on the Internet. We also obtain information regarding websites from other publicly available sources (e.g., Alexa). We then make that data available, along with a link to the websites, logos, thumbnail screenshots, in a centralized location at the Site. We also make available a map, which is nothing more than a spatial representation of otherwise publicly available information. 

**3\. What Information We Collect**

We do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information ("PII") about you (i.e., users) from the Site unless you choose to provide it to us. This means that you can play games in [GirlDressup.net](http://www.girldressup.net/ "Dress up Games"), you do not need to inform us who you are or reveal any PII about yourself. 

However, when you register as a member at Registration in [GirlDressup.net](http://www.girldressup.net/ "Dress up Games"), we collect Personal Information including your email address, first name, last name, and birthday, or any information that is voluntarily provided by you. 

We maintain log files which contain Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. An IP address is a numeric address that is assigned to your computer by your Internet Service Provider. We do not link IP addresses to personal information. In general, we only use log files to monitor traffic on our Web sites and to troubleshoot technical problems. In the event of abuse, however, we may block certain IP addresses. 

**4\. How We Use It**

[GirlDressup.net](http://www.girldressup.net/ "Dress up Games") is committed to protecting your privacy. We will not sell your email address to any third party vendors. The information about age is mainly used to identify minors that need to get permission from their parents or legal guardian to become members at [GirlDressup.net](http://www.girldressup.net/ "Dress up Games") (Minors in the US are defined as children under 13, in non-US minors are defined as under 16 of age.) 

We use Personal Information primarily for our own internal purposes, such as providing, maintaining, evaluating, and improving the website [GirlDressup.net](http://www.girldressup.net/ "Dress up Games"). We do not send newsletters or mass emails. 

  
We have no control over third parties to whom you may give Personal Information through the use of [GirlDressup.net](http://www.girldressup.net/ "Dress up Games"). 

  
From time to time, we also use the information you provide about yourself or others to inform you of additions or improvements to the [GirlDressup.net](http://www.girldressup.net/ "Dress up Games") services as well as conduct member surveys in order to improve our service. 

We may also use personally identifiable user information to provide targeted content and commerce opportunities to users based on their demographic and behavioral information.  


Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways or for reasons unrelated to the ones described above without also providing an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses. 

**5\. Cookie**

We may place a text file called a "cookie" in the browser files of your computer. The cookie enables us to collect information about your visits to [GirlDressup.net](http://www.girldressup.net/ "Dress up Games"). We may use this information to enhance your [GirlDressup.net](http://www.girldressup.net/ "Dress up Games") experience and may share some information with our members, vendors, sponsors or others. 

You can turn off the cookie function in your web browser, then you won't be able to register as a member or log in.
