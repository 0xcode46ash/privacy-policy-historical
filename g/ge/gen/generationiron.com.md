# Generation Iron Shop

Please read below to review our Privacy Policy on this Site. In this Privacy Policy, “generationironofficial.com”, “Generation Iron”, “GI Brands LLC”, “we”, “us”, “our” and other similar references mean Generation Iron and “you”, “your” and other similar references mean a specific user of the Site. By visiting the Site, you accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

This privacy policy is intended to assist you in understanding how we treat personal information that we collect and receive when you visit this web site, how we use that information, and the safeguards we have in place to protect that information. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable such as your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that we do not own or control or to people that we do not employ or manage.

**COLLECTING INFORMATION**

We collect personal information (such as your name, email address, mailing address, and phone and credit card numbers.) that you provide when you place an order, join our mailing list, use our products, or participate in a contest, promotion or survey. We may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies. To serve you better, the information you supply to us is added to our customer database. We automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, our cookie information, and the page you request. We use information for the following general purposes: to improve this site, to enhance your experience and improve our service, to customize the content you see, fulfill your requests for our products and services, contact you, and conduct research. Your privacy is always guaranteed when you shop with us. We will not release any of your personal information to outside parties and you will not receive unwanted emails. We reserve the right to send you certain communications relating to our service, such as service announcements, administrative messages, along with promotions, sweepstakes or surveys.

All transactions submitted to us are encrypted with 128 bit SSL security and cannot be intercepted by outside parties, guaranteed.
