# Genentech: Privacy Policy

Genentech uses your personal information for the purposes for which you gave it to us. For example, if you have expressed an interest in a particular product or program, we will use your data to provide you with the requested information or service. We may also ask for your permission to send you information on other Genentech products or services that we believe may be of interest to you.

We also may use your personal information and data concerning your use of this website for analytics purposes. For example, we may use this data to determine which parts of our site are of most interest to users, which parts are of least interest, and to modify the site accordingly.

We may share your personal information with other Genentech affiliates, partners with whom we are jointly marketing a product or service, and service providers whom we have engaged to assist us in our business functions. These entities will be required to abide by this Privacy Policy. For more information, see [Sharing and Disclosure](https://web.archive.org/privacy-policy/full-privacy-policy#sharing_disclosure).
