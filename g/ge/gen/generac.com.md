# Generac Power Systems | Privacy Policy | Generac Power Systems

Generac Power Systems, Inc. (“Generac” or “we”) respects the privacy of every individual who visits the Generac Web Site ("Site") or provides information to us over the Internet. This Privacy Statement (“Statement”) outlines what information Generac will collect and how we will treat the information.

##  Personal Information

When you visit the Site, we will not collect any personally identifiable information about you (such as your name, address, telephone number or e-mail address) unless you provide it to us voluntarily. Portions of the Site may request visitors to submit names, e-mail addresses and other personally identifiable information. By submitting personally identifiable information, you agree, without limitation or qualification, to this information being used exclusively by Generac and its authorized dealers for business purposes only. If you opt not to provide us with personally identifiable information, no material consequences will result, although you may be unable to receive specific communications on product, company, or investor relations information.

Generac may store and process personally identifiable information to better understand our visitors needs and how we may improve our products and services. However, we will not trade, rent or sell personal information gathered online about our visitors. We will not provide your e-mail address, or any other information gathered from visits to the Site, to any outside organization..

##  Contact Us

When you fill out a “Contact Us” form, your information may be sent to both Generac and its authorized dealers for business purposes. Accordingly, when you fill out a “Contact Us” form, you agree to allow both Generac and its authorized dealers to contact you and use your information to provide you with assistance which you have expressly requested, including promotional and marketing materials.

##  Non-Personal Information

Generac may collect information about you that is not personally identifiable such as domain names (e.g. aol.com, etc.), browsers, and the specific pages accessed or visited on the Site. This information is aggregated to measure the number of visits, average time spent on the site, number of pages viewed and methods by which our site was found. Generac collects this information to evaluate the use of and to make improvements to the Site.

##  Employment Opportunities

Individually identifiable information submitted in connection with employment inquiries will be used only in the employment process.

##  Statement Changes

This Statement is subject to change at the sole discretion of Generac. If we change our Statement, we will update this page to keep you informed on how we may use your information.
