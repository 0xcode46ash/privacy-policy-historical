# Privacy Policy | Genius Network

  | 

We have created this Privacy Policy to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy and security.   Our company collects information such as name, e-mail address, mailing address, phone number, fax number and billing information in different ways from our Friends, Clients and Members.  We use this information to provide you a customized experience and to keep you notified of improvements and new developments.

All information is held confidential. 

**We do not sell, rent, trade or share our client list or any client information to any other entities.   Ever.**

We hate spam!  We are committed to permission-based email practices. 

Our Friends, Clients and Members can unsubscribe from any form of communication or service at any time. 

In addition to using mail, e-mail, fax, and voice broadcast to fulfill your orders and deliver you services, we may from time to time, send special offers and promotional notices via mail, e-mail, fax, and/or voice broadcast to our Friends, Clients and Members and to prospects who have expressed interest and have requested such information. At any time, Friends, Clients and Members may “opt-out” of such offers and notifications by following the “opt-out” link on the specific offer received, or by contacting us at 480-858-0008.
