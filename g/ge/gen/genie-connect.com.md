# Privacy Policy | GenieConnect

Personal information received by GenieConnect will be kept in a secure manner and processed in accordance with the laws relating to data protection.  Except as expressly provided, any non-personal information or material sent to GenieConnect will be deemed not to be confidential.  


We shall keep information that personally identifies you or allows us to contact you (“Personal Information”) confidential. We may, however, from time to time use your Personal Information to contact you about new products and services which may be of interest to you and for other related marketing purposes.

By using this site, you agree to the terms of the GenieConnect’s privacy policy. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not use this site.
