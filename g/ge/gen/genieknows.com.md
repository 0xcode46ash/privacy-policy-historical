Genieknows.com Local Search - Privacy Policy 

|  Genieknows is committed to protecting your privacy. Protecting your privacy and your personal data is an important aspect of the way we create, organize and implement our activities on and offline.  
---|---  
  
Where this privacy policy applies

|  This privacy policy only applies to Genieknows.com Local search Beta. It does not apply to sites or pages that are linked to Genieknows.com Local Search. You will generally be able to tell whether this privacy policy applies as there will be a link to this privacy policy on that page of the site. By your use of Genieknows.com Local search and/or the submission of personal information to us, you consent to the use of your personal information on and subject to the terms of this privacy policy.  
---|---  
  
Information we collect about Genieknows.com Local search users

|  We do not collect any personal information to identify any person. The only information collected is the IP address of the user, time of the search and search queries.  
---|---  
  
What do we do with this information?

|  We use this information to improve the functionality and results of our search results, count number of users visiting our site, to deliver co-branded services and purposes of site administration.  
---|---  
|  Genieknows Local Search pages may contain Web Beacons (A web beacon is typically a transparent graphic image (usually 1 pixel x 1 pixel) that is placed on a site) to assist in delivering cookies on our site that allow us to count users who have visited those pages to deliver '[publisher](http://www.genieknows.com/publishing/)' services. 

Genieknows may also employ third party Web Beacons in order to help us compile aggregated statistics and determine the effectiveness of our promotional campaigns. We do not allow Web beacons to collect personal information about our users.   
  
---|---  
  
Changes to this Privacy Statement

|  Genieknows Local Search may update this policy to reflect the changes in the service and feedback from our customers. If there are material changes to this statement we will post a prior notice before implementing the changes. We encourage you to periodically review this statement to be informed of how Genieknows is protecting your information.
