# Geomerx - Building Powerful and Comprehensive E-Commerce for Small Business

**geo merx** will never disclose any information collected on our site except as follows:

| 1. | During the Setup process you indicate the search engines on which you want to register your site. During this registration process geomerx will distribute your URL, and all meta information you indicate you wish to disseminate to the indicated search engines.  
---|---  
  
**geo merx** will never willfully sell nor rent, whether for consideration or not, any information about you or your business to any third party. This includes all financial and personal information collected during the Sign Up and Set Up processes.

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please send an email to [service@geomerx.com](mailto:service@geomerx.com).
