# Private Policy

GEODAC Corporation recognizes your right to confidentiality and is committed to protecting the privacy of users of its web site, interactive services and products. 

**Information About You**

There are times when it is important for us to request information from you. Such requests may be part of registration, subscription requests, order forms or requests for information. We will need certain information from you to process the request. The information may consist of items such as: 

Name, Company Name, Address, Email Address, and Telephone Number.

At times, some or all of this information may be acquired over the Internet. There may also be instances where a credit card number may be transmitted to us through our website to initiate or complete a transaction. All information we ask for over the Internet is transmitted to us through an encryption process. We use industry standard SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption.
