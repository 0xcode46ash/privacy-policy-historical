# ORBIMAGE Inc. | Corporate

ORBIMAGE Inc.("ORBIMAGE," "we," "our" and the like) is committed to protecting your privacy online. To better protect your privacy, we provide you with this notice (the "Privacy Notice") explaining our online information practices for our [orbimage.com](http://www.orbimage.com/) web site (the "Site"), and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used at our Site. 

**1\. The Information We Collect**

At our Site, we offer you the opportunity to register to use our online catalogs to browse, order and receive products and services. You also can submit comments and questions about our products and services, and request to be added to our High-resolution Imagery Tips e-mail list. The types of personally identifiable information that may be collected at these pages include: name, company name, address, e-mail address, telephone number, fax number and credit card information. We also may collect certain non-personally identifiable information such as the type of browser you are using (e.g., Netscape, Internet Explorer), the type of operating system you are using, (e.g., Windows '98, Mac OS) and the domain name of your Internet service provider (e.g., America Online, Earthlink). 

**2\. How We Use the Information**

We use the information you provide about yourself to fulfill your requests for our products and services, and to respond to your inquiries about our company and product and service offerings. We also use this information to communicate with you, such as to notify you about updates to our products and services, or to assist you with any questions you may have about an order you have placed for products and services. From time to time, we may use the personally identifiable information that we collect to offer you other products or services that we believe may be of interest to you. We may also share it with select third parties to market products or services that may be of interest to you. We use the non-personally identifiable information that we collect to improve the design and content of our Site and to enable us to personalize your Internet experience. We also may use this information in the aggregate to analyze Site usage, as well as to offer you products or services. We may disclose personally identifiable information in response to legal process or when we believe in good faith that the law requires it, for example, in response to a court order, subpoena or a law enforcement agency's request. ORBIMAGE's agents and contractors who have access to personally identifiable information are required to protect this information in a manner that is consistent with this Privacy Notice by, for example, not using the information for any purpose other than to carry out the services they are performing for ORBIMAGE. 

**3\. Choices We Provide You**

We may contact you to provide you with opportunities to purchase products or services that may be of interest to you. If you prefer not to receive marketing information from this Site, just let us know by sending us an e-mail at [customer.support@orbimage.com](mailto:customer.support@orbimage.com). We may also share personally identifiable information with select third parties to enable you to learn about other products or services that may be of interest to you. If you prefer not to receive marketing information from these third parties, just let us know by sending us an e-mail at [customer.support@orbimage.com](mailto:customer.support@orbimage.com). 

**4\. Collection of Information by Others**

Our Site contains links to other sites whose information practices may be different than ours. You should consult the other sites' privacy notices as we have no control over information that is submitted to, or collected by, these third parties. 

**5\. Cookies**

We may use "cookies" to enhance your experience with our Site. Cookies are text files we place in your computer's browser to store your preferences. We cannot use our cookies to determine any personally identifiable information about you. We may use cookies to understand Site usage and to improve the content and offerings on our Site. For example, we may use cookies to customize your experience at our Web page. We also may use cookies to offer you products, programs, or services. 

**6\. Our Commitment to Security**

We maintain appropriate security measures to prevent against unauthorized access to information that we collect online.

**7\. How You Can Review Your Information**

If you would like to review the personally identifiable information that we have collected from you online, just let us know by sending us an e-mail at [webmaster@orbimage.com](mailto:webmaster@orbimage.com), and we will provide you with a copy of the information you provided. 

**8\. How to Contact Us**

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy for this Site, you may contact us at 21700 Atlantic Boulevard Dulles, VA 20166 or via e-mail at [webmaster@orbimage.com](mailto:webmaster@orbimage.com). 

**9\. Terms of Use**

This Privacy Notice is incorporated by reference in our Terms of Use, which sets forth your rights and responsibilities and the terms and conditions that govern your use of this Site and its associated services. To review the Terms of Use, please click [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20060616043447id_/http%3A//www.orbimage.com/legal/Terms.html), or the "Terms of Use" link at the bottom of each page on the Site.
