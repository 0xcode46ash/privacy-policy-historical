# Welcome to Geopharmacy

GeoPharmacy.com is committed to protecting your privacy and security. We will not disclose, sell or rent information you have provided to any third party or advertisers.

All information furnished is legally protected under doctor-patient privilege laws.

Before you place an order, we will ask you to set up your password protected account which includes your contact information (name, e-mail address, shipping address) and medical history. We use this information to provide you with a superior shopping experience and to prevent medical mistakes. You may review or revise the information we have in your account by logging into your account.

In addition, we ask that you indicate whether you would like to receive e-mails from GeoPharmacy.com keeping you informed of new products and promotions.

Email correspondence will not contain your medical information.
