# Get Ready Reserve Privacy Policy

**WHAT THIS PRIVACY POLICY COVERS**

Get Ready Reserve takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

The federal government and technology industry have developed www.onguardonline.gov to help you guard against Internet fraud, secure your computer and protect your personal information.

**How Get Ready Reserve Uses Your Personal Information**

This policy covers how Get Ready Reserve treats personal information that Get Ready Reserve collects and receives, including information related to your past use of Get Ready Reserve products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.

This privacy policy only applies to Get Ready Reserve

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Get Ready Reserve does not own or control or to people that Get Ready Reserve does not employ or manage.

**INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE**

**General**

Get Ready Reserve collects personal information when you register with Get Ready Reserve, when you visit Get Ready Reserve pages.

When you register we may ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, ZIP code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. For some financial products and services we might also ask for your address, Social Security number. When you register with Get Ready Reserve, you are not anonymous to us.

**Children**

You must be 18 or older to register with Get Ready Reserve.

**INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE**

Get Ready Reserve does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

  * We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.
  * We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of GRRâ€™s terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
  * We transfer information about you if Get Ready Reserve is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Get Ready Reserve will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.



**GENERAL**

We reserve the right to send you certain communications relating to the Get Ready Reserve service, such as service announcements, administrative messages, that are considered part of your Get Ready Reserve registration, without offering you the opportunity to opt out of receiving them.

**CONFIDENTIALITY AND SECURITY**

We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.

We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you.

**CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY**

Get Ready Reserve may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
