# Privacy Policy | Get Me A Ticket

**Privacy Policy**

**Who this applies to  
** This policy applies to individuals who have registered with Get Me A Ticket and websites which Get Me A Ticket provides competition services to.

**This policy was last reviewed  
** January 2011

**Our obligations  
** Get Me A ticket is registered as a data processor under the Data Protection Act 1998, registration number Z9141445.

We will ensure compliance with all data protection regulations.

We will only use your personal data:

\- fairly and lawfully;  
\- as set out in the Official Rules and/or this Policy;  
\- to the extent necessary for these purposes.

We may process your personal data ourselves or through data processors who will process data on our behalf (in which case we will remain responsible for ensuring the data processor complies with this Policy and all data protection regulations).

Get Me A Ticket takes your privacy seriously and takes every reasonable precaution to safeguard the personal information that you supply.

**What this applies to  
** We collect the information you choose to provide during your registration process and the information provided when entering prize draws, quizzes, competitions and answering our partner surveys which we operate on behalf of third parties.

**Uses and sharing of data  
** Get Me A Ticket collects demographic information when you register with the website, such as your name, your age and where you live. We may also ask you for information when you enter specific competitions, surveys or promotions such as information about your lifestyle including your hobbies, interests and online habits.

The following statement is prominently displayed before you can join Get Me A Ticket:

_“By submitting this form you understand and agree to accept all the_[ _ **terms and conditions**_](http://www.getmeaticket.co.uk/page/terms-and-conditions.html) _  and _[ _ **privacy policy**_](http://www.getmeaticket.co.uk/page/privacy.html) _  issues. By registering with Get Me A Ticket you agree to receive information from Get Me A Ticket, and carefully selected 3rd parties about products and services that will be selected based on your interests and information you supply to the website. You can unsubscribe from these communications at anytime.“_

Get Me A Ticket contains partners surveys that you must complete during your prize entry. These surveys may have their own privacy policies and some have their own opt-in statements, Get Me A Ticket recommends that you read and understand this information if you are answering positively.

We may use or permit our clients and carefully selected third parties to use your data to provide you with information about goods and services which we think may be of interest to you, and we or they may send you offers by email, telephone, SMS, post, fax or other appropriate means.

Your data may be provided to clients of Get Me A Ticket to be appended to information they already hold about you for the purpose of contacting you with information about their products and services.

If you do not wish to receive these communications, or you wish to alter the way in which information is sent to you, you can change your preferences by visiting this page:  
<http://www.getmeaticket.co.uk/unsubscribe>

You can also unsubscribe using the instructions provided in any email communication we send you.

**If you have any queries or concerns about the data usage please contact using the form provided on this page:  
**<http://www.getmeaticket.co.uk/contact>

**Retention and destruction of personal data**  
We will ensure that your personal data is not used unless it is accurate and up to date and we will not keep it for longer than necessary.

Once it has been decided that the information is no longer needed it will be deleted promptly.  
The personal data which we will use is the information provided at registration and the data you provide when entering competitions, quizzes, surveys and prize draws and whether you have consented to be contacted by specified organisations. You may change your consents at any time.

**Your choices/options in relation to personal data**  
You have the right to:

\- obtain access to your personal data;  
\- receive information about us and the purposes for which we process your personal data;  
\- prevent processing which may cause you damage or distress;  
\- object to direct marketing;  
\- object to purely automated decision-making in certain cases;  
\- ask us to correct any errors or delete the information we hold;  
\- opt out of any further contact

If you would like to amend/delete details or contact us in relation to the above, please contact using the contact us page: <http://www.getmeaticket.co.uk/contact>

To change any communication preferences please use the form provided here:  
<http://www.getmeaticket.co.uk/unsubscribe>

**Additional Disclosure of your information**  
We may disclose your personal information to third parties in the event that we sell or buy any business or assets, in which case we may disclose your personal information to a prospective buyer. We will also disclose your personal data if we are under a legal obligation to do so.

**Security**  
Unfortunately, the transmission of data via the internet is not completely secure. Although we will do our best to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its security. Once we have received your data we will use strict security measures to try and protect against loss, misuse, or alteration .

We also require third parties with whom we share information to keep this information secure.

**IP address and Cookies**  
We may collect information about your computer including, where available, your IP address, operating system and browser type for system administration and to report aggregate information to our client sponsors and advertisers.

For the same reason we may obtain information about your general internet usage by using cookies. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer's hard drive. They help us to improve our site and to deliver a better and more personalised service to you. You may refuse to accept cookies by activating the setting on your browser which allows you to refuse the cookies. However, this may affect your ability to access certain parts of the site.

**Changes**  
We may change the terms of this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
