# Privacy Policy | getmeaticket.co.uk - the home of free UK prize draw competitions

[ ](https://web.archive.org/user/register)

**Who this applies to**

This policy applies to individuals who have registered with Get Me A Ticket 

This policy was last reviewed: May 2008                          

**What this applies to**

We collect the information you choose to provide during your registration process and the information provided when entering prize draws, quizzes and competitions.

**Uses and sharing of data**

We may pass your personal data to the specified organisations to which you have consented and we and they may contact you to discuss selling you further goods and services. 

Neither we nor they will pass your personal data on to other third parties. 

We may process your personal data ourselves or through data processors who will process data on our behalf (in which case we will remain responsible for ensuring the data processor complies with this Policy and all data protection regulations).

We will register with the appropriate data protection authorities and comply with all data protection regulations.

We will only use your personal data:   
\- fairly and lawfully;  
\- as set out in the Official Rules and/or this Policy;  
\- to the extent necessary for these purposes.

**Retention and destruction of personal data**

We will ensure that your personal data is not used unless it is accurate and up to date and we will not keep it for longer than necessary. 

Once it has been decided that the information is no longer needed it will deleted promptly.

The personal data which we will use is the information provided at registration and the data you provide when entering competitions, quizzes and prize draws and whether you have consented to be contacted by specified organisations. You may change your consents at any time – see the Official Rules. 

**Your choices/options in relation to personal data**

You have the right to:  
\- obtain access to your personal data;  
\- receive information about us and the purposes for which we process your personal data;  
\- prevent processing which may cause you damage or distress;  
\- object to direct marketing;  
\- object to purely automated decision-making in certain cases;  
\- receive compensation for breach of a data controller’s obligations;  
\- ask us to correct and errors or delete the information we hold  
-    opt out of any further contact   
-   

If you would like to amend/delete details or contact us in relation to the above, please contact using the contact us page: [http://www.getmeaticket.co.uk/contact](https://web.archive.org/contact)

**Security**

We have appropriate security measures in place to protect against loss, misuse, or alteration of information that we have collected. 

We also require third parties with whom we share information to keep this information secure. 

We use cookies on this website. You are entitled to refuse continued storage as a result of this cookie, and may do so by contacting us using the details provided below.
