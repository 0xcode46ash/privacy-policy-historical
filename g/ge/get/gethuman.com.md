# gethuman tips

Our privacy policy is simple: we will NOT share your personal information with anyone. This includes any information you provide to us including telephone numbers, call durations, identities of companies that you contact, etc.

We hate websites and companies that share or sell our personal data, so we will never, ever, ever do that to you.

We will use your email address only to add you to the gethuman distribution list for mailing newsletters.  We may use your email to contact you if you reported a problem and we need to contact you for additional information. 

We will not share your information with anyone.
