# Jobtrain - Privacy Statement

|   | 

**Privacy Statement  **

Jobtrain is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. We want to provide a safe, secure user experience. We will use our best efforts to ensure that the information you submit to us remains private and is used only for the purposes intended.  


**Privacy Commitment**

In general, we gather information about all of our users collectively, such as the areas our users visit most frequently and what services users access the most. This information helps us determine what is most beneficial for our users and how we can continually create a better overall experience for our users.

**  
General Information Disclosure**

We do not disclose information about your individual visits to Jobtrain, or personal information that you provide, such as your name, address, email address, telephone number, etc., to any outside parties, except when we believe the law requires it. On occasion we may record and share aggregated information with our partners. Also, if you input your information on a co-branded registration page, such as that of a Jobtrain and a partner company, then your information becomes the property of both Jobtrain and the partner company. 

**  
Feedback**

Jobtrain has an area on the site where you can submit feedback. Any feedback that is submitted through this area becomes the property of Jobtrain. We may use this feedback, such as success stories, for marketing purposes, or to contact you for further feedback on the site.

**  
Privacy Commitment Changes**

If we decide to change our privacy commitment for Jobtrain, we will post those changes here so that you are always kept informed and up to date. If, at any time, you have questions or concerns about Jobtrain's privacy commitment, please feel free to email us at enquiries@jobtrain.co.uk.

Thank you for using Jobtrain.
