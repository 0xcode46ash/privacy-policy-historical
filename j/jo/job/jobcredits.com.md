# ITax Group::Privacy Policy

ITax Group, Inc. PRIVACY POLICY  
Privacy is of utmost importance to ITax Group and we are committed to respecting the privacy of the individuals who visit our web site. Following is information about ITax Group’s commitment to protect site users' privacy and to guard against unlawful use of personal data.

This Policy covers your use of ITax Group’s web site, www.itaxgroup.com, as well as ITax Group’s other sites available only to its registered partners and clients.

Contact Information  
If you have any questions or concerns concerning our online privacy policy and how we administer it, you should first contact ITax Group. Our security team can be reached by e-mail at [info@itaxgroup.com](mailto:info@itaxgroup.com?subject=Attention:%20Security%20Team) or by calling (800) 288-2100 or by mail to 2929 N. Central Ave., Suite 1800, Phoenix, AZ 85012, Attention: Security Team.

Data Collection and Dissemination  
When you visit our site, we may collect and store information provided to us as a result of your use of the web site. This information is to help us better our web site, products and services. Tax Group is the sole owner of the data collected and we will not share or sell any of your personally identifiable information to others without your approval. This data will not be traceable to you and we will always take industry standard precautions to protect your data.

Online Registrations  
In order to receive information on our services or to sign up for our free Newsletter you will be required to provide contact information such as name and e-mail address. Users may also be asked for voluntary additional information about themselves, such as the industry they are in, location, number of employees, etc. This will help us to be more responsive and specific to your particular inquiries. This information may also be used to communicate with you in the future on matters that may be of interest to you.

Opt-Out Provision/Changing Subscription Information  
Our site provides users, who no longer wish to receive communications from us, the right to opt-out of receiving any communications from us. If a user's information changes (such as zip code, phone, email or postal address), we provide a way to correct, update or delete users' personally identifiable information. Please contact the Marketing Department at ITax Group at [marketing@itaxgroup.com](mailto:marketing@itaxgroup.com) with any changes.

Links  
While ITax Group has a privacy policy in place, our web site does link to other sites. ITax Group is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of the web sites to which the user may link. All users should review the privacy policy of any web site they visit before supplying any personal information.

Notification of Changes to Policy  
If at any time we change our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on our web site.

Legal Disclaimer  
We may disclose personal information when required by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary in order to comply with the law or any legal process served upon our web site.
