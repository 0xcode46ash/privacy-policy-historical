# Privacy Policy - |

  


Your private is important to us. We will not misuse, sell or exploit any information provided to us. It will not be shared with any companies or 3rd parties.  Joy Us garden uses Mailchimp to collect names and email addresses when you sign up for our newsletter.

All advertising on this site is proved by 3rd party networks.  Joy Us garden participates in affiliate programs and receives a small commission from these sales. We provide links to products and services that we use, endorse or believe to be of value to our readers. In buying these products or services you support Joy Us garden. Thank you!

These 3rd party sites have separate privacy policies. We therefore have no liability or responsibility for the content of these linked sites. We seek to protect the integrity of our site so please let us know if you have any feedback about these sites.

Cookies are tracked (on this site) because that information is used to improve our website and content. Cookies are not harmful and cannot introduce viruses or extract personal content. If you wish to not have your information collected, you can turn off the use of cookies at any time by changing your specific browser settings.

We protect the security of your personal information by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) which encrypts information you input.

By using this site, you consent to our Privacy Policy. This policy is subject to change without notice and was last updated on August 17, 2016. If we decide to change our Privacy Policy, those changes will be posted on this page.
