# About Joyofbaking.com

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's IP address, but not the e-mail address (where possible).

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail.

The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded.

With respect to cookies: We do not set any cookies. 

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending usÂ  an e-mail [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20080514193232id_/http%3A//www.joyofbaking.com/other/emailstephanie.html) however we do not currently send out email unless we are contacted first with questions or concerns.

With respect to Ad Servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, Ad Server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information. These companies may set cookies or use web beacons. For further information, consult the privacy policies of:

With respect to Traffic Auditing: In order to accurately measure the traffic and demographics of site visitors we use independent auditors to monitor our traffic. As a result of your visit to our site, Traffic Auditing companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information. These companies may set cookies or use web beacons. For further information consult the privacy policies of:

[ Quantcast](http://www.quantcast.com/privacy.jsp)
