## Introduction

## Information We Collect

  * When you register for a Citrix Online product or free trial, we will need to collect personal information such as billing information.
  * We collect information you voluntarily supply to us on our websites, through surveys, or as feedback.
  * Information is collected during the use of some of our products as needed to provide the service.
  * We use common Internet technologies, such as cookies and web beacons, to keep track of interactions with our sites and services.
  * To learn more about Citrix Online’s information collection practices, please read our complete [Citrix Online Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/collaboration/privacy_policy_popup?popup=true&locale=en_US).



* * *

## How We Use Your Information

  * We use the information collected to provide you with the services you request.
  * We may use your information to inform you about other products or services, partners, opportunities and events offered by Citrix Online.
  * We may use your information to improve our products or websites.
  * We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.
  * Citrix Online uses industry-standard efforts to safeguard the confidentiality of personally identifiable information, including encryption, firewalls and SSL (Secure Sockets Layer).
  * To learn more about Citrix Online’s use of your information, please read our complete [Citrix Online Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/collaboration/privacy_policy_popup?popup=true&locale=en_US).



* * *

## How We Share Your Information

  * In order to conduct business it is necessary that we share some of your information with third parties, such as for credit card processing.
  * Third parties must process your personal information in accordance with our privacy policy.
  * We may need to disclose your information as required by law to protect our rights or comply with judicial proceedings.
  * To learn more about Citrix Online’s information sharing practices, please read our complete [Citrix Online Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/collaboration/privacy_policy_popup?popup=true&locale=en_US).

## Communication Choices

  * To change your email subscription status or opt out of Citrix Online’s collection, use and distribution of your information, visit our [Unsubscribe page](http://www.citrixonline.com/en_US/unsubscribe).
  * Every marketing email from Citrix contains instructions on how to opt out of receiving further marketing emails.
  * To learn more about the choices you have about the collection, use and distribution of your information from Citrix Online, please read our complete [Citrix Online Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/collaboration/privacy_policy_popup?popup=true&locale=en_US).
