# The Boloto Group Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement sets forth Boloto Groupâ€™s information gathering and dissemination practices regarding personal information collected by Boloto Group directly from individuals ("you") in connection with the websites located at [www.invite5.com](http://www.invite5.com/), [www.joini5.com](http://www.joini5.com/), [www.getthebar.com](http://www.getthebar.com/) and related URLs ("Websites"). 

### What We Collect

Boloto Group may collect various types of personal information from you in connection with the Websites. Such data may include identifying information and contact information (names, titles, addresses, phone and mobile numbers, email addresses, etc.), financial and payment information, information about interests, products ordered or provided, IP addresses, and other interaction and transaction-related data. 

### How We Use Collected Information

**Contact Information**

We may use personal information collected in connection with the Websites to contact you, to send you information about Boloto Group applications or its partners, advertising, to provide services to you, and for other purposes related to our business. 

**Financial Information**

We may ask you to provide your credit card or other financial account information to enable us to charge your account when you purchase services from us. All information you will be asked to provide is protected during transmission using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption to prevent it from being intercepted as it is transferred over the Internet. We provide this information only to third parties involved in processing payments for the services we provide and as otherwise provided in this Privacy Statement. 

**IP Addresses**

We use your unique network address, or IP address, to identify location, help diagnose problems with our servers and to administer the Websites. 

**Cookies**

We use cookies to identify members (session ID cookie). Currently we do not use them to recognize return visitors or to track promotions or campaigns. 

**Disclosure of Collected Information**

Boloto Group may disclose to third parties information we collect from you in connection with the Websites (i) non-personally identifying information (demographics, geography and interests) as requested by advertisers or to provide services requested by you, (ii) as necessary to process payments, (iii) to our subcontractors, service providers and other agents in connection with the provision of services to you (provided such subcontractors and service providers are under a duty to maintain the confidentiality of such information), and (iv) in connection with requests from law enforcement officials, government agencies, or otherwise as required in connection with legal proceedings. In addition, we may transfer information we collect from you to a third party in connection with a merger or sale of our business or assets, in which case the resulting entity or transferee will be permitted to obtain and use such information subject to the terms of this Privacy Statement. 

**Contact**

If you would like to contact Boloto Group regarding the use of your personal information collected in connection with the Websites, the types of third parties to whom we disclose such information, or to access, correct, amend, or delete your personal information collected in connection with the Websites that is stored on our systems, please send an email to [privacy@bolotogroup.com](mailto:privacy@bolotogroup.com) with your request. 

**Linked Sites**

The Websites contains links to external websites and/or advertising to external websites. Boloto Group is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such third parties or their websites. 

**Security**

Boloto Group implements industry-standard security measures designed to prevent the loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, destruction or alteration of the personal information we collect via the Websites. 

**Updates**

Boloto Group may update this Privacy Statement from time to time to reflect changes in its services and customer feedback, and such changes shall become effective promptly after they are posted. Boloto Group encourages you to periodically review this Privacy Statement to be informed of any changes. This Privacy Statement was last updated on September 27, 2009.
