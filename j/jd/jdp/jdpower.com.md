# The J.D. Power Consumer Center - Privacy Policy

  | 

The J.D. Power Consumer Center is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. Therefore, we work hard to make sure our privacy policy reflects the highest Internet standards. That means:

  * The only personal information the J.D. Power Consumer Center obtains about individual visitors to its Website is information supplied voluntarily.
  * In cases where the J.D. Power Consumer Center may need personal information to provide guests and members with customized services, or to inform them about new products or features, we explicitly request such information.
  * All identifiable information provided by guests and members (name, email, address, phone number, credit card number) will not be disclosed to anyone unless guests and members specifically request us to do so.
  * Only statistical information about our members as a group (usage habits, demographics) may be shared with affiliated businesses of the J.D. Power Consumer Center.



The J.D. Power Consumer Center employs strict security measures to safeguard online transactions. Personal information is stored in secured databases and always sent via an encrypted Internet channel. If you have questions regarding the Privacy Policy at the J.D. Power Consumer Center, please send us an email or call 1-800-227-2582.
