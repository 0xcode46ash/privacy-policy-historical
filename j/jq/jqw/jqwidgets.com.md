# Privacy Policy - Javascript, HTML5, jQuery Widgets

#### Collection of Information

When you visit this Web site we collect anonymous user data through the web logs of this web site and analytics software. The collected information is used to analyze and optimize the content of this web site and additional service and products improvements. The type of information collected includes, but is not limited to, user IP address, display type, operating system, browser, time of visit, proxy server address.

#### Services requiring personal information

Some of the services provided on this Web site require user registration and collection of personal information. For example, the access to the jQWidgets online forums and blogs requires registration. We may collect personal information when you purchase software licenses, contact our staff, or register to participate in the forums and blogs. We reserve the right to use this information for marketing purposes, newsletters, promotions, technical support or additional service offers.

#### Opt-out option

You may send us a request to cancel your registration and close your user account. You may also choose to opt-out of receiving promotional and marketing materials by sending an email to [support@jqwidgets.com](mailto:support@jqwidgets.com)
