# Privacy Policy – Jaisiyaram.com

_  
**Privacy Policy**_

This site complies with the following Privacy Policy. By using this site, you indicate your acceptance of Jaisiyaram.com’s Online Privacy Policy.

Shree Bindu Sewa Sansthan (India) and Swami Balendu e.V. (Germany) are non-profit charitable organizations. All donations made are free of tax and thus fully support these charitable organizations.  
The Jaisiyaram Trading Company is our partner company and all profits made are donated to Shree Bindu Sewa Sansthan for the support of their charity projects.

Our sites collect no personally identifying information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals. We do record information such as time of day, browser type, browser language, and IP address when you interact with the site. Jaisiyaram.com uses this information to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our web site. We also use this information to verify the number of discrete times you visit this web site each day.

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who subscribe to our newsletter, request any service and order our products. By filling a form on Jaisiyaram.com the subscriber agrees on receiving emails from Jaisiyaram.com. The subscriber's data is stored in our secured server and protected with up to 256-bit Secure Sockets Layer encryption. Jaisiyaram.com will contact the subscribers with mails regarding the requested service and order and promotional mails for programs and charity projects. The provided information is kept confidential and is not shared with or sold to any other company or organization. All emails contain an [unsubscribe](https://web.archive.org/web/20081223034033id_/http%3A//www.jaisiyaram.com/unsubscribe.htm) link which can be used to opt-out or you [click here to unsubscribe](https://web.archive.org/web/20081223034033id_/http%3A//www.jaisiyaram.com/unsubscribe.htm). It is an automated system and the email-address will be removed from the program and the subscription list within seconds. 

While we will seek to require third parties with whom we have agreements to follow appropriate privacy policies, we do not bear any responsibility for any actions or policies of third parties. Our web site contains links, including e-commerce opportunities with affiliates, partners and associates, to other sites that are not maintained by us. If you visit these sites, please look for their privacy policy.

We can be reached via email at [info@jaisiyaram.com](mailto:info@jaisiyaram.com) .
