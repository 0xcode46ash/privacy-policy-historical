Janpratinidhi.com is an initiative of Srishti, a non Governmental Organisation to provide a multi-lingual umbrella portal which houses almost 25000 web pages of current and ex-Members of Legislative Assemblies and Members of Parliament of the country. The aim of the project is to establish a transparent, neutral and interactive platform for discussion between the leaders and people of the country. JPC and its licensors recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of personal information that may be provided to us while accessing Janpratinidhi.com and its micro-sites (the "Site"). In this pursuit, we have adopted a privacy policy to govern how we treat such information. By using the Site or its services, you consent to collection, storage, and use of the personal information you provided, including any future changes provided/updated by you, for any of the services that JPC offer.  
Not all visitors are required to furnish information while visiting JPC. But to avail certain services on the Site and become a Janpratinidhi account holder, users are required to furnish certain information for the registration process like name, email address, sex, age, and PIN code. This required / mandatory information is service dependent and JPC may use such User information to, maintain, protect, improve and launch new services.  
Such information will not be considered as sensitive if it is freely available and accessible in the public domain or is furnished under the Right to Information Act, 2005 or any other law for the time being in force.  
Cookies are used to remember the information about a user who has visited a website in order to show relevant content in the future. JPC To enhance the user experience, JPC may collect non-personally identifiable information via the use of cookies.  
JPC will not disclose any personally identifiable information about our current or former users to anyone, except as permitted or required by law, or as described herein. JPC will endeavour to maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the confidentiality of the information that we collect online. However, JPC may disclose or share your personally identifiable information:  
1\. Under your permission  
2\. To correct/ address what JPC believe to be false or misleading information or details  
3\. When it is required to be revealed by any court order, decree, or subpoena  
3\. To provide the product or service you have requested  
4\. Under confidentiality restrictions in connection with a sale, merger, transfer, exchange, or other disposition of all or a portion of the business conducted by JPC.  
5\. Where it is reasonably necessary to protect the rights, property or safety of JPC, and its users or the public.  
The Site links to other third party websites. Such sites are governed by their respective privacy policies, which are beyond JPC's control and jurisdiction and and we do not assume any liability related to their collection of information or use.  
From time to time, JPC may email about services, surveys and feedback which can be of interest to you. You can choose not to receive such messages by simply following the 'unsubscribe' steps included in the e-mail.  
If you are visiting the Services from outside India, your data will be transferred to and stored in JPC's servers in India. By using our Services, you permit JPC to collect and use of your data as described in this privacy policy, and acknowledge that data protection laws in India may be different from other countries.  
In our continuous effort to enhance the services you received from us, JPC's Privacy Policy may be amended from to time, in accordance to the new developments. Any such changes will be updated on this page and the registered users will be notified via e-mail.
