# Privacy | JapaneseSportCars.com

We are glad to welcome you to [JapaneseSportCars.com](http://www.japanesesportcars.com/). Please read this privacy statement to ensure that we are committed to keeping secure the privacy of our members’ details.

**What information do we collect?**  
Since Japanese Sport Cars is a Community that’s providing you a place to store your information and to connect with other members, we may require from you some information that may be considered as personally identifiable.

Please provide us with the following information about yourself:

  * nickname
  * real name
  * password
  * e-mail address



Other personal information that we may possibly need: your gender, age, location and a short description about you.
