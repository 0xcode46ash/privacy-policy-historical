# Crisscross Moderation Policy

**Effective** **May 10, 2005**

Your privacy is important to us. This notice explains our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. This notice is available on our homepage.

**Registration information**  
This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the Crisscross websites during registration and when ordering services. The types of personal information we collect includes basic information such as name, address. We also collect credit card and purchase details where necessary.

Currently, we keep registration information for different parts of the site in different databases. To allow users a single login for all Crisscross sites we will unify these databases in the near future.

**Server log information**  
Like all sites, Crisscross keeps detailed logs of site usage. Statistics tracked include country of origin, your browser type, IP address and the pages you request. This information is used to find out which parts of a site are most popular and to provide aggregate marketing information to clients.Â Detail information about your browsing habits is not disclosed to third-parties. 

**Use of information**  
We do not sell your personal information to third-parties, however we may occasionally send mails to users on behalf of third-parties. Users may opt-out of these mailings. 

We only accept third-party mailings that we feel are of genuine interest to our users. We will not send mails for Viagra, casinos, work-at-home programs, pornography or other generic spam. 

We send all registered users regular email updates on the Crisscross system. Users may opt-out of these mailings. 

We send all registered users email when new Crisscross services are launched. Users may opt-out of these mailings. 

We do not keep email addresses entered in the send-article-to-a-friend system. 

**Crisscross email newsletter**  
We use emails from this system to send system messages, to introduce new products, and to send mails on behalf of third-parties. Users may opt out of these mailings. 

**Discussions**  
To register for Crisscross discussions you are required to provide a valid email address. 

We track the IP address of every posting on Crisscross discussions. We remove personally identifiable postings where necessary. 

Please refer to the [Forum Moderation Policy](https://web.archive.org/policy/moderation.htm) for more details.

**Crisscross News Poll**  
No personal information is collected for this poll. 

**Surveys  
**We conduct surveys on behalf of third-parties. Any information collected will be used by third-parties in their marketing efforts. We ask all third-parties to use information gathered in surveys responsibly. However, we have no control over how the third-party uses the information collected. 

**Cookies**   
We write several cookies to your machine to track your progress through the site.

**Data security**   
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. 

All online credit card transactions are performed only on secure servers through a secure SSL connection. Credit Card information for recurring payments is not stored on our public servers. 

We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information 

**Legal disclosure**  
Crisscross reserves the right to reveal registration information pursuant to a valid subpoena or court order. 

We reserve the right to use or share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Crisscross Terms of Use, or as otherwise required by law. 

**Changes to this policy**  
Crisscross may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the email address specified in your Crisscross account or by placing a prominent notice on our site. 

We will transfer information about you if Crisscross is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, we will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy. 

**Accessing or correcting Your information**  
At any time, you may review or update your personal information by logging in to Crisscross or by contacting us at: [ support@crisscross.com](mailto:support@crisscross.com) To protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.

**Contact**  
Please do not hesitate to contact us at [support@crisscross.com](mailto:support@crisscross.com) if you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy.
