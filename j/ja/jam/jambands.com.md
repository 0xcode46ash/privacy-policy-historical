# Jambands.com - Get Your Groove On-Line

| 

[](http://www.jambands.com/cgi-bin/btransfer.cgi?bid=237&posid=6)

Privacy Statement

Jambands.com gives users the opportunity to enter their email address into a form in order to receive updates, news, and information from us. Users who sign up may at any time opt-out of receiving future mailings by emailing [jambands@jambands.com](mailto:jambands@jambands.com); this opt-out information is also included in each mailing we send out. 

Jambands.com contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those Web sites, though we certainly encourage them to respect your privacy. 

This site makes message boards and other forums available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose personal information. 

When you visit our site, information such as the IP address of your computer is automatically conveyed to our server. This information is only used to administer our Web site. 

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with Jambands.com, please contact Dean Budnick ([dean@jambands.com](mailto:dean@jambands.com)).
