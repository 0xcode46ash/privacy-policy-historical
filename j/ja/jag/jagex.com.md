# Jagex Software

**Your Privacy is Important to Us**  
In an effort to support privacy policy disclosures on the Internet, Jagex.com wishes to set an example for other major Web sites by voluntarily making our privacy policy available to the public. We urge all other major Web sites to do the same. 

**Privacy Policy Statement**  
Jagex.com is published by Jagex Software  
We can be reached via e-mail at support@jagex.com  


We use hitbox to collect information on which pages users visit or access. We are only interesting in the average behaviour of visitors, and information about individuals is not used. For more information about this please see http://www.websidestory.com/privacy/ 

We also collect email addresses from users of our multiplayer games, which we store in an internal database. We use this to help us identify the number of unique users we have, and to send an opt-in email newsletter to players who request to receive it. We will not send you any unsolicited email. None of the information we collect in this way will be shared with other organizations for commercial purposes. 

This website receives advertising from the ValueClick network. For more information on what data is collected from Internet consumers through ValueClick ads please see their privacy policy statement at http://www.valueclick.com.
