# Privacy Policy - JPHF Matrix

Your privacy is of utmost value to us at JPHFMatrix and we will take all steps to protect your sensitive information. Please read this policy to understand what information JPHFMatrix.com(hereafter referred to as 'JPHFMatrix') collects, how the information is used, and what choices you have regarding collection and use of your information when using JPHFMatrix. 

This policy covers information you share, both personally identifiable and non-identifiable information, with JPHFMatrix as a visitor of JPHFMatrix web site. If you are a member/user of JPHFMatrix, then you are subject to the JPHFMatrix Terms and Conditions. Please refer to the JPHFMatrix Terms and Conditions for more information if this pertains to you. 

1.1. Your browser must accept cookies so that we can store information and sessions.  
1.2. Cookies will only be used to store your preferences and no identifiable information. 

2.1. We will not share, sell, or trade your personal information.  
2.2. We may use your email to contact you at any time.  
2.3. We will use your payment accounts to pay you.  


3.1. Your password is saved in a secure encyrpted,hashed form in our database. So no one can read it. 

3.1. Your username may be shown by us at anytime, anywhere on our website. Under some circumstances we may mask your username for the specific reason of protecting your privacy.  
3.2. Your username will be displayed in the chat and forum.
