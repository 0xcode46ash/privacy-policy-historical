We at Jeans-Online respect your right to privacy. Jeans-Online does not collect personal information from occasional or unregistered users. Jeans-Online knows that you care how information about you is used and shared.

Information entered during the ordering or registration process will be treated as confidential. Any details submitted are used in strict confidence and only to the extent requested by the user. 

If you opt to subscribe to our newsletter, we will keep you updated on the most current developments, specials, discounts, and new products. 

You may unsubscribe at any time by just sending an email to Jeans-Online with unsubscribe in the subject line. If you decide to unsubscribe we would also like to know why, in order to fix the problem as soon as possible, although you are under no obligation to state your reason(s) in order to unsubscribe. 

Jeans-Online does not sell, rent, exchange nor disseminate user information. We at Jeans-Online appreciate your trust and confidence in our service and security.

Communication between the Client and the Jeans-Online shop is always conducted in a safe way through a 128-bite SSL Thawte protocol.
