# Jenjobs

Â  |  Your privacy on the internet is important to JenJOBS.com. We adopt a strict confidentiality policy towards all personal information submitted by our users and are committed to protecting such information to the best of our ability. This privacy statement applies to the information gathered on JenJOBS.com and discloses how it is used, and how you can correct or change it. 

**Collection of Personal Information**  
Personal information encompasses any information that may be used to identify an individual, including but not limited to, your name, a home or other physical address, email address, telephone number, work experience, qualifications and other such data associated with a resume. 

When you register for our services on [www.jenjobs.com](http://www.jenjobs.com/), you will be asked to provide certain information including but not limited to your username, email address, contact numbers. This information will be used solely for the purpose of contacting you. 

As a jobseeker, you may also voluntary complete a jobseeker profile with information about your personal details, employment details, preferences and academic qualifications and other such data associated with a resume. These include but are not limited to information on your identity card number, date of birth, gender, citizenship, marital status, academic qualifications, employment history and job preference. This will be used for purposes of verification and also for matching services with any job opportunities that become available. In providing the above information, you also agree to share it with all potential employers. 

JenJOBS.com may also track your clicks as you go though our website to understand which of our services and links are popular, in order to better customize our website for effective provision of services relevant to your needs. 

**Use of Personal Information**   
JenJOBS.com is the sole owner of the personal information collected from this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others except as set forth below : 

  1. When you apply for jobs through our website, your resume data and any other pertinent personal information you have provided will be made available to companies you are applying to. Note that these companies may elect to retain a copy of your resume for their records. In this instance, JenJOBS.com cannot be held responsible for the retention, use or privacy of your resume in connection with these companies;
  2. JenJOBS.com may from time to time collaborate with business affiliates and partners to extend exclusive offers to its members. In this instance, JenJOBS.com may utilise your personal information to for purposes of communicating the aforementioned;
  3. JenJOBS.com may release account and other personal information when we believe that it is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce or apply our Terms and Conditions or protect the rights, property, or safety of JenJOBS.com, or our users;
  4. Aggregate statistical information may be used to generate internal reports on user demographics and traffic patterns for our business affiliates and partners.

**Confidentiality of Your Account and Password**  
Upon your acceptance of our Terms and Conditions and upon your registration being accepted by **JenJOBS.com** , an account will be opened for you and a password will be generated to allow you access. You are entirely responsible for the confidentiality of your Password and all use of your Account, whether or not such use was authorised by you. You will immediately notify **JenJOBS.com** of any unauthorised use or other breach in security of your Account. 

**Access to and Modification of Your Personal Information**  
You may review, edit, update or change your personal information at anytime subject to the verification of your identity. To do so, login your account using your login name and password, select the appropriate tab and make the desired changes. 

**Option To Unsubscribe**  
JenJOBS.com offers you the option to removing your e-mail address from our database for a particular service, so you will not receive future communications for the service selected. To do so, click on the unsubscribe link on any subscription mail you receive. 

**Notification of Changes**  
If we decide to change the terms of our Privacy Statement, the said changes will be posted on our website at [www.jenjobs.com](http://www.jenjobs.com/). You are responsible for regularly reviewing this Privacy Statement to apprise yourself of such changes and continued use of our website thereafter shall constitute your consent to such changes. 

If you have additional questions about this privacy statement, the practices of JenJOBS,com, or your dealings with this Web site, please email us at [customerservice@jenjobs.com](mailto:customerservice@jenjobs.com).
