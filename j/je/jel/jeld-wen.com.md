# Privacy Policy | JELD-WEN Doors & Windows

We at **JELD-WEN, inc.** (referred to as " **JELD-WEN, inc.** ," "we," "us," or "our") recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of certain information ("Information") collected about our customers and prospective customers (referred to as "Customers," "you," or "your"). This privacy statement discloses what Information we gather, how we use it, and how to correct or change it. By visiting www.jeld-wen.com (the "Website") you acknowledge that you accept the practices and policies outlined in this Policy.

This privacy ("Policy") discoses what Information we gather, how we use it, and how to correct or change it. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that **JELD-WEN, inc.** does not own or control, or individuals that **JELD-WEN, inc.** does not employ or manage, including those that are linked to the Website.

Please note, if you provide us with your email address or phone number, we or our representative may send you a follow-up email or phone call.

**Effective Date**  
The effective date of this Policy is August 25, 2010.

**Information Collected and How it is Used**  
The information we gather from you enables us to provide you with services, maintain and improve the Website and to process your and others' requests. Information gathered may include the following:

  * _Information You Provide to Us_ : We receive and store any information you enter on the Website or provide to us in any other way. The types of information collected may include your name, address, birth date, preferences, content, credit card information, website usages and other demographic information. You can choose not to provide us with certain information, but then you may not be able to take advantage of certain services or features offered on the Website or off line. The Information you provide is used for responding to your requests for certain information, products and services, customizing the advertising and content you see, processing payments and communicating with you about payments, services and features. We may choose to disclose nonpublic personal information about you, the consumer, to a third party for compensation.



  * _Information that You Disclose or Post, or Allow Others to Disclose or Post_ : Any Information, including photographs, videos or other content, that you voluntarily disclose on or through the Website is public information that will be collected and used by **JELD-WEN, inc**. and may be collected and used by others.



  * _Information Collected Automatically_ : We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with the Website. We automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, cookie information, pages visited and your location on the Website. Generally, our service automatically collects usage information, such as the numbers and frequency of visitors to the Website and its components. We may provide Information to third parties about how our site is used. If you are logged in, then we may associate your information with your Website account. We may also place tags, or web beacons, in HTML-based emails or other communications with you to confirm delivery, to track communications or uses of the Website. Many companies offer programs that help you to visit websites anonymously. While you may not be able to use the Website, or to access all features of the Website if you use such programs, we want you to be aware that these programs are available.



  * _Cookies_ : A cookie is a piece of data generated by a website and stored on your computer. Our Website may use cookies to track unique visits. This simply means that visits to the Website can be uniquely identified and tracked from page to page. Our Website may also use cookies to keep visitors logged into secure areas as they navigate from page to page. This eliminates the need to log in multiple times during your visit. We may also use cookies to help our Website remember preferences or settings you may set. Most browsers have an option for turning off cookies, which will prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, as well as (depending on the sophistication of your browser software) allowing you to decide on acceptance of each new cookie in a variety of ways. We strongly recommend that you leave the cookies activated because cookies enable you to take advantage of some of the Website’s most attractive features.



  * _Information From Other Sources_ : **JELD-WEN, inc.** might receive and store information about you that is sent to us from other sources. This may include updated delivery and address information from vendors with regard to your email address.



  * Email and Phone Communications: You may receive email or phone calls from **JELD-WEN, inc.** from time to time, for instance to request information regarding your account or payment, to notify you of new features, changes to the Website, specials or promotions or to provide you with information that you request, and you hereby consent to receipt of any such email.




**Sharing Information**  
We share information only as described below. You hereby consent to our sharing of information for the purposes listed below.

  * _Businesses and People We Do Not Control_ : We may affiliate with a variety of businesses, including dealers, distributors and advertisers. In certain situations, these businesses assist us in providing you with products or services and we may share your information with them. We also employ other companies and people to perform tasks on our behalf and we may need to share your information with them. Examples include but are not limited to the following: maintaining servers, sending email, removing repetitive information from user lists, analyzing data, providing marketing assistance, processing credit card payments, reviewing content and providing customer service.



  * _Other Users_ : Note that any information that you make public on or through the Website will be accessible by other users and may be used by them.



  * _Business Transfers_ : In some cases, we may choose to buy or sell assets or to pledge assets as collateral. In these types of transactions, user information, including your Information, is typically one of the business assets that is involved and may be transferred. Moreover, if **JELD-WEN, inc.** , or substantially all of its assets were acquired, or in the unlikely event that **JELD-WEN, inc**. goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information, including your Information, would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of **JELD-WEN, inc.** may continue to use your Information as set forth in this Policy.



  * _Protection of **JELD-WEN, inc.** and Others_: We may disclose Information when we believe that disclosure is necessary to comply with the law, to enforce or apply our conditions of use and other agreements, or to protect the rights, property or safety of **JELD-WEN, inc.** our employees, affiliates, users or others. This may include exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction.



  * _With Your Consent_ : We may share Information when you provide us with your consent to do so. For instance, when you request goods, information or services from our affiliates or a third party through us, or when you post information to the Website, you provide your consent for us to share that information with others. Please note that whenever you post public information on or through the Website you are giving us your consent to share that information with third parties.
