At theblog.jessikerbakes.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by theblog.jessikerbakes.com and how it is used.

**Log Files**  
Like many other Web sites, theblog.jessikerbakes.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

**Cookies and Web Beacons**  
theblog.jessikerbakes.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

**DoubleClick DART Cookie**

### **TERMS OF USE**

### PHOTOS

All photographs are the property of Jessiker Bakes, unless otherwise stated.

If you would like to use  **one**  photograph of a recipe, that is fine. if you credit Jessiker Bakes and link back to my website and the applicable recipe.   **Please DO NOT republish my photo and entire recipe at the same time.  **

### RECIPES

Please do not copy and paste text directly from my website, whether in a recipe or otherwise.

If you would like to share a recipe, please republish the instructions or directions in your own words and give credit to Jessiker Bakes (and the original source, if applicable) with a link back to the corresponding post.

Please also do not use my photo for the recipe. Please use your own photo.
