# Privacy Policy | Jesus Loves You

Here at Jesus Loves You, we take your privacy seriously. It is not our intent to send emails, make Facebook posts or share our content with anybody who does not wish to receive it. If you need assistance being removed from any communication channel, please email us @ info@Jesus-Loves-You.org, or read further to see if your question is answered here.

**Privacy Policy**

www.Jesus-Loves-You.org takes your privacy seriously. Our website is completely open to everyone. www.Jesus-Loves-You.org is a blog-format website which posts inspirational articles. Some of the content (videos or images for example), may be embedded from a 3rd-party source like YouTube. Our commentary on these pieces of content is unique. We aim to give full credit for resources used and will always comply with any and all copyright laws. If you feel a piece of embedded content has been used without permission or with incorrect credit, please contact us and let us know. We will correct this matter. If we are using your open-source content and you wish to have it removed from our site, please contact us at info@Jesus-Loves-You.org

**Email List**

If you ever wish to unsubscribe from our email list, please click the “unsubscribe” link at the bottom of any of our emails. Follow that link and you will be given steps to remove your email address from our list. If, after following this action, you continue to receive emails from Jesus Loves You, please contact us at info@Jesus-Loves-You.org, we will gladly help you.

**Facebook Fan Page**

Jesus Loves You maintains a Facebook Fan page @ [www.facebook.com/JesusLovesYou4Ever](http://www.facebook.com/JesusLovesYou4Ever). If you want to remove yourself from the page or wish to quit receiving our inspirational posts, simple visit the page and click the “unlike” link at the bottom of the left column.

**Advertising & Cookies**

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on www.Jesus-Loves-You.com.  
Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to www.Jesus-Loves-You.com and other sites on the Internet.  
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

**More About Google Adsense**

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on www.Jesus-Loves-You.com send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

Jesus Loves You has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

Users may opt out of the use of the Google cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
