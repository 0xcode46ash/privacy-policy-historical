**Privacy Policy**  
This policy covers how LuxeMont treats personal information that we collect and receive, including information related to your past use of LuxeMont products and services. Personal information includes your name, mailing address, phone number, e-mail address, and any other information that is not otherwise publicly available. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that LuxeMont does not own or control or to people that LuxeMont does not employ or manage.

**Information Collection and Use**  
LuxeMont collects personal information when you subscribe to our free regular e-mail service, when you use LuxeMont products or services, when you visit LuxeMont web pages or the web pages of certain LuxeMont partners, when you sign up for the monthly newsletter, and when you enter promotions and giveaways. LuxeMont may combine information that we have about you with information that we obtain from business partners and other companies. LuxeMont may collect information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners, including information about your use of financial products and services that we offer. LuxeMont automatically receives and records information that our server logs from your browser, including your IP address. LuxeMont uses information for the following general purposes: to customize advertising and content you see, to fulfill your requests for products and services, to improve our services, to contact you, to conduct research and to provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

**Opt-Out**  
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communication from us and our partners.

**Delete/Deactivate**  
This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database so as not to receive future correspondences, or to no longer receive our service.  
1\. You can send an email to info@LuxeMont.com  
2\. You can call 619-578-5969

**Information Sharing and Disclosure**  
We provide information to trusted partners who work on the behalf of or with LuxeMont under strict confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help LuxeMont communicate with you about offers from LuxeMont and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information. We respond to subpoenas, court orders, and legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights to defend against legal claims. We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of LuxeMont’s terms and conditions, or as otherwise required by law. If LuxeMont is acquired by or merged with another company, we transfer information about you. In this event, LuxeMont will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

**Cookies**  
LuxeMont may set and access LuxeMont cookies on your computer in order to track you when you’re on our websites and to recognize your visits. LuxeMont may use web beacons to access Luxemont cookies inside and outside of our network of websites and in connection with LuxeMont products and services.

**Confidentiality and Security**  
We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe have a reasonable need to come in contact with that information to or in order to do their jobs. In certain areas, LuxeMont uses industry standard SSL encryption to protect date transmissions. 

**Changes to this Privacy Policy**  
LuxeMont may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your email account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
