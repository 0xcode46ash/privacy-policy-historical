# Privacy Policy

**Introduction**  
Justcontinentalcars.com is owned and operated by Open Design Systems Ltd. The following describes the privacy policy (as amended or otherwise changed from time to time, the "Privacy Policy") applicable to the services available through the Justcontinentalcars.com web site (the "Site") from Open Design Systems Ltd. 

**Our Commitment to Your Privacy**  
At Justcontinentalcars.com we value your trust in us and will work hard to earn your confidence so that you can enthusiastically use our services and recommend us to your friends and colleagues. All details supplied to the Justcontinentalcars.com with your on-line advertisement will be safeguarded both by our privacy policy and also by the 1998 Data Protection Act. 

**Information We Collect**  
We collect personally identifiable information ("Personal Data") about you that you specifically and voluntarily provide to us while using the Site. 

In order to register for full access to the Site we will require you to supply your name, postal address, postcode and e-mail address. 

On-line advertisements placed on the Site will, in addition require payment details, a landline telephone number, with a mobile number and e-mail address supplied as an option. 

Justcontinentalcars.com has created a secure environment for credit card transactions. The Site currently uses Secure Socket Layer technology to encrypt all your personal information including credit card number, name and address to prevent your information from being read during transmission over the Internet. 

**How We Use the Information We Collect**  
Telephone numbers supplied, as part of a vehicle advertisement will be published within the relevant advertisement only for the duration of the booking. 

Credit card information is used solely for the purpose of payment processing. Justcontinentalcars.com retains no record of credit card details. 

**Our Policy Concerning Cookies**  
When you visit our Site, we issue a "cookie" or unique identification code that enables us to recognise your computer. The cookie contains no Personal Data and is merely used to note which sections of our Site you have recently accessed. Based on this information we are able to understand the relative popularity of sections throughout our Site. This in turn enables us to develop relevant content as well as attract advertising from companies that we believe to be of interest to our users. 

**Sharing Your Personal Data**  
Justcontinentalcars.com does not sell or rent your Personal Data to anyone. Justcontinentalcars.com may share your Personal Data with other Open Design Systems Ltd companies. From time to time, Justcontinentalcars.com may contact you to request feedback on your experience using the Site, to assist us in improving it. When you register with our Site, we ask you whether you would like to opt-in to receive future notification on changes to the Site, as well as offers and services from third party companies that we feel might be of interest to you. 

**Protecting Your Personal Data**  
The Personal Data you provide us is saved in a secure environment, stored in a single, secure database, accessible only by Justcontinentalcars.com. 

In accordance with the requirements of the 1998 Data Protection to prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure correct use of information, we will employ reasonable and current Internet security methods and technologies. 

**How You Can Access or Change Your Personal Data**  
Justcontinentalcars.com understands that you may want to change or access your Personal Data. You may do so by accessing your profile. To protect your privacy and security, we will verify your identity before granting access or making changes to your personally-identifying information. 

**Amendments to this Privacy Policy**  
Amendments and Updates to this policy may be made from time to time. Any revisions will be posted on this page so you will always be aware of what information we collect. 

**How to Contact Us**  
Should you have any questions, concerns or comments, [please email us here](https://web.archive.org/contact.php).
