# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy
Overview
JustePourRire.com, Inc and its affiliated entities herein referred to as Just For Laughs consider
the privacy and security of your information as a critical component of the service that we offer.
This Privacy Policy outlines what specific information is gathered and how that information is
used. If you have any specific questions about our Privacy Statement, please contact us via email
at info@hahaha.com
Anti Spam Policy
Just For Laughs enforces a 100% ANTI-SPAM policy for every and all campaigns, emails,
promotions, contacts and any form of email promotion to come out of Just For Laughs' servers.
Just For Laughs will never send out any promotion for any client that utilizes any form of non-
solicited email addresses.
Collection and Use of Information
Just For Laughs operates this Website, http://www.justforlaughs.com. On this Website, Just For
Laughs tracks non-personally identifiable information to help us understand our visitor's needs
related to our web site design.
Just For Laughs also compiles product use information, but in the aggregate only, and may make
that aggregate information publicly available. Under no circumstances will Just For Laughs make
any personal information about an individual user available to anyone.

Unsubscribing
Your email address is used to only send you information you have requested or information
deemed important to communicate by the siteâ€™s editorial team. As part of your use of any Just For
Laughs Product, Just For Laughs allows you to elect to receive, or not receive, certain information
from Just For Laughs and its partners. Just For Laughs adheres strictly to permission-based
email policy. Except as mentioned above, Just For Laughs will not send you unsolicited email
information, commercial offers or advertisements. Just For Laughs does not sell, rent or loan our
contact lists to outside firms. You always have the option to unsubscribe from any of our lists.
Unsubscribe requests are processed within minutes and no further communication will be sent to
users who have stated that they do not wish to receive the specified information.
All Just For Laughs partners who offer you information via email from our Website must adhere to
this unsubscribe policy.


Forwarding
If you make use of the "send to a friend" function to forward emails to others, these recipients will
not be added to any distribution list. However, Just For Laughs reserves the right to send them an
invitation to join the list if they do not sign up themselves.
Log Files
Just For Laughs uses IP addresses and email addresses to analyze trends, administer the site,
track users' movements, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.
Sharing
Just For Laughs shares aggregated demographic profiles with our partners and sponsors. This is
not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person.
Links
Both the mailer and the website contain links to other sites. Please be aware that Just For Laughs
is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. This privacy statement applies
solely to information directly collected by Just For Laughs.
Security
Just For Laughs takes every precaution to protect users' information. All user-related information
is restricted in our offices. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job are
granted access to personally identifiable information. Furthermore, all employees are kept up-to-
date on our security and privacy practices.
Notification of Changes
Just For Laughs reserves the right, at its sole discretion, to change, modify or otherwise alter
these terms and conditions at any time. Such changes and/or modifications shall become
effective immediately upon the posting thereof. Please review the terms and conditions
periodically. Your continued use of the web site and or newsletters following the posting of
changes and/or modifications will constitute your acceptance of the revised terms and conditions.
Legal Disclaimer
Just For Laughs will never provide your personal information to third parties without your express
permission or only in instances where we are obliged to by law or in the good-faith belief that
such action is necessary to (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process
served on un, our partners, sponsors, investors, or affiliates; (b) protect and defend our rights or
property or those of our users, and (c) act as immediately necessary in order to protect the
personal safety of our users or the public.


Reporting Abuse
Just For Laughs will not tolerate any abuse of its system. All detections of any form of abuse
within our site should be reported to: info@hahaha.com
If you have any questions regarding our policy, please contact us at info@hahaha.com and we'll
be more than happy to answer any of your questions.
Thank you.
