## Privacy Policy

At JTplaza.com, we are dedicated towards protecting your privacy. The information which we get lawfully about you, only that information is used by us. There are only two reasons for collecting your information: 

1) To process your order 

2) To provide you with the best possible service 

You will not get any e-mail from us unless you have given any consent to us. 

In the future, we will also give you the option to decline any marketing email from our side. 

We collect only small information about you that include: Your Name, Phone Number, Address and Email Address. 

Without any consent, we are not collecting any personal information about you. 

We are accurately up date your information. By emailing us, you are also able to check out your information which we hold about you. If you are not satisfied and find any mistakenness then we will remove it or correct it quickly. 

We keep your information securely with us with our internal security policy and the law. 

We are also using best and latest technologies to track the patterns of behavior of visitors to our website that comprise ‘cookie’ that would be stored on your browser. To prevent this happening, you can modify your browser. You can contact us if you have any query related privacy.
