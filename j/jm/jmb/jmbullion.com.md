# Privacy Policy | JM Bullion

JM Bullion does not provide any outside parties with your personal information via sale, rent, or loan. We are always working to ensure that your privacy is of the utmost importance and that your personal details are safe.

In signing up at JM Bullion and/or creating an account, you will be added to our mailing list. This list will be used for promotional and information emails from JM Bullion.

All information, including address, phone number, email, credit card information, and any other personally identifiable data is used by JM Bullion only for the completion of orders.

We may use cookies, pixel tags or other means to collect anonymous data related to your use of our website, including, but not limited to, your IP address, pages viewed, sale transactions, and other anonymous shopping data. We retain the right to allow third parties to collect this anonymous data on our behalf and to retain and use this data themselves. It is also allowable for JM Bullion to share this anonymous data with other third parties. This information is used primarily to handle proper delivery of banners and other forms of advertising on the behalf of JM Bullion.

## Opt-Out

If you would like to stop receiving any and all forms of communication from JM Bullion, please contact us by phone or email and we will remove you from our list of acceptable contacts as soon as possible.
