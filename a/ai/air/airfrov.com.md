# Privacy Policy | Airfrov - Get Travellers To Bring Back Overseas Products

We love good bargains, but we hate people who betrayed our trust. That is why we take your information and privacy to the utmost concern. Please feel free to contact us at support@airfrov.com if you require any information.

**We collect personal and activity data, only to make your experience better. Not to sell them.**

We utilize customer relationship management technologies to identify you so that we can deliver a better experience. Our systems also log information like your profile, time spent on Airfrov and your interactions on Airfrov.

We also collect other personally identifiable information that you provide to us, such as your name, address, phone number or email address. With your permission, we may also access other information from services like Facebook connections and friends.

Please feel free to contact us at any time if you ever feel that you have been compromised or you will like to remove your profile or certain data completely from our system. We will make sure that you are comfortable.

By accepting the Terms of Use you also agree that we are allowed to contact you by email or phone or SMS messaging even if you are on the Do Not Call (DNC) registry.

**You can request to see or delete your personal data. Others can see your username and that is all.**

You can check your profile to see any personally identifiable information we have stored, such as your name, address, email or phone number. Currently only your name is visible to other users and we will not disclose other information without your consent. You can also contact us by email to request to see or delete this information.

**We will keep your data until you delete it**

We remove personally identifiable information (such as your name, address, email or phone number) and other preferences associated with your account promptly after you delete your account. We may retain other data indefinitely.

**Nobody likes to be hack - we make sure you stay safe.**

We take appropriate steps to secure your personal information against unauthorized access or disclosure. We encrypt transmission of data on pages where you provide payment information. However, no security or encryption method can be guaranteed to protect information from hackers or human error.

Information we collect may be stored or processed on servers located in any country where we do business.

**When will we disclose your data? Only these.**

Only in certain circumstances as required by law then we will be disclosing your data: (1) with your express consent, (2) to protect our rights or property and other users in a criminal or fraud investigation case or (3) to any successor or purchaser in a merger, acquisition, liquidation, dissolution or sale of assets. Subject to time and legal constraint, we will always attempt to notify you prior to releasing any of your data.

**We will keep you updated on our privacy and regulations**

This privacy policy was last updated on 04/12/2014. Our privacy policy may change from time to time. If we make any material changes to our policies, we will place a prominent notice on our website or application. If the change materially affects registered users, we will send a notice to you by email, push notification or text.
