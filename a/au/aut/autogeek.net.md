 **AutoGeek.net Security Policy**

We understand your concerns about ordering on the internet. We take this issue very seriously.

All information gathered by AutoGeek.net, through your order, is kept strictly confidential and will not be distributed to any company or individual EVER. The information will be used only to make sure that your order gets delivered to you in the timeliest manner possible.

Yahoo!, our host server, does not store your credit card information. All order information sent to AutoGeek.net is kept strictly confidential. We do not trade, barter, rent or sell your name or personal information to anyone for any purpose.

If you would prefer you can place your order by phone, fax, or mail.
