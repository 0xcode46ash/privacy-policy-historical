# AutoResponse Plus: Sequential or follow-up autoresponder software for UNIX / Linux web sites

Gobots Internet Solutions has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. The following details our information gathering and dissemination practices.

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our site. We do not use your IP address in any attempt to determine or verify who you are.

Upon your first visit to this web site, a cookie may be sent to your computer. A cookie is a file that identifies you as a unique user. It stores personal preferences and user data. In no way does this tell us who you are.

Gobots Internet Solutions has one primary use for its cookies: to determine how many times you have previously visited the site.

While this depends on the use of a cookie, visitors to this site always have the option of disabling cookies via their browser preferences. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. You can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, note that some parts of this web site may not function correctly if you refuse cookies.

Traffic Analysis

AutoResponse Plus uses WebTrends Live to analyze traffic to this web site. WebTrends Live does not create individual profiles for visitors. Unlike some tracking services WebTrends Live does not have a database of individual profiles for each visitor. WebTrends Live only collects aggregate data. For more information about WebTrends Live privacy policy, please [click here](http://www.webtrendslive.com/privacy_policy.htm).

This web site collects contact information in various ways, including enquiry forms and order forms. The information collected may be used to send the user information about our company and products, or to get in touch when necessary. Users may opt out of receiving future mailings from us; see the opt out/modify section below.

Our credit card processing facility is provided by [ClickBank](http://www.clickbank.com/) who have their own privacy policy. Gobots Internet Solutions does not receive or store any credit card or financial information whatsoever.

None of the information that is collected by this web site is rented or sold for any purpose whatsoever, under any circumstances.

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information that your provide. We have taken every possible precaution to prevent any unauthorized access to the contents of our servers.

This site contains links to other sites. Gobots Internet Solutions is not responsible for the privacy practices of such sites.

Gobots Internet Solutions provides every user with the opportunity to easily opt out of receiving future communications from us, change and modify information previously provided or remove their information from our databases altogether.

You may do so in the following way by sending an e-mail to:

[support@autoresponseplus.com](mailto:support@autoresponseplus.com)
