### Amirite.net Privacy Policy Statement

You can [contact us here](https://web.archive.org/about#contact) with any queries.

At amirite, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by amirite and how it is used.

We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.

### What information we collect.

  * All the information you enter into the website is saved on our servers - this includes your email address, "about me", gender, etc.
  * We also store your IP address for logging and statistical purposes.
  * If you link your amirite account with your Facebook account (or create an amirite account by connecting with Facebook), your Facebook user ID is stored on amirite.
  * If you link your amirite account with your Twitter account (or create an amirite account by connecting with Twitter), your Twitter user ID is stored on amirite.
  * The city and country your IP address is registered in may be collected and stored automatically. You can opt out of your city being looked up from your [My Settings](https://web.archive.org/myaccount) page. IP location services are provided by [http://ipinfodb.com](http://ipinfodb.com/).



### Cookies.

  * Amirite stores small pieces of information in your web browser called cookies.
  * These cookies contain essential information such as your userID when logged in and what posts you have made.



### How we use your information.

  * Your IP address is used to indentify you as a user, your votes and posts are associated with your IP address.
  * Your Facebook and / or Twitter IDs will be disclosed to other users if you chose to show a link to your Facebook or Twitter profile from your amirite profile.
  * The city or country your IP address is registered in will be disclosed on the map page when you make a post. You can opt out of your city being shown from the My Settings page.
  * The country your IP address is registered in is used to direct your to the correct version of the amirite store when you request it.



### Third parties.

**DoubleClick DART Cookie.**

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on www.amirite.net.  
Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to www.amirite.net and other sites on the Internet.  
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL <http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html>

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners current include Google Adsense.

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on www.amirite.net send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

**Analytics.**

Amirite uses Google Adsense, http://whos.amung.us/ and Statcounter website analytics services. Your IP address may be stored by them and used in accorance with their respective privacy policy.

Amirite has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. www.amirite.net's privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.
