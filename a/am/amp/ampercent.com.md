# Privacy Policy

**1\. Cookies** : The Website uses cookies to remember your information when you make a Comment.This may Include you name,Email address or your Blog,Website Address.These cookies are for Visitor Convenience and is purely optional.When you visit Our site later on these cookies will make your Work easier and save you from typing the fields again.

**2.Comment** : When you make a Comment or contact Us via The contact page your email address is required.Be rest Assured that Your email address will only Be used for Responding to your Comment or email and will never Be made publicly available.

**3.Visitor Statistics** :We collect Visitor statistics using Google analytics.The statistics are collected to identify the Number of Visits and other Metrics which helps us in improving this Blog’s Content.These Statistics is only for our sole use and we never Disclose any Information That may Hurt You.

**4.Ad servers** :  Google as a third party ad Vendor uses cookies to serve advertisements in this site. Google’s use of the [DART cookie](http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/faq.aspx) enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).

We have relationships With other Companies or Websites that allows us placing Advertisements on our Web pages.As a Result of your Visit to our site these ad servers may collect information.Please note that these companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may [contact us](http://www.ampercent.com/assets/contact.php).If you want to Use Our Content in Your Blog or Website See the [instructions here](http://www.ampercent.com/terms).
