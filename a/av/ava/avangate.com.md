Avangate reserves the right to change the Privacy Policy and other guidelines or rules posted on the Site from time to time at its sole discretion, and will provide notice of material changes on the home page of the Site. Your continued use of the Site, or any Materials or Services accessible through it, after such notice has been posted constitutes your acceptance of the changes. Your use of the Site will be subject to the most current version of the Privacy Policy, rules, and guidelines posted on the Site at the time of such use. You should periodically check the "Privacy Policy" link on the Site's home page to view the then-current terms. 

**Area of use**

The Terms of Use are valid and apply to all the Web Sites in the avangate.com domains, sites for which GECAD is the author/owner/administrator. All these Web sites will be further called individually or collectively **Site** or **Sites**. 

  
**Definitions**

  * "personal information" is defined as any information that identifies or can be used to identify, contact, or locate the person to whom such information pertains); 
  * „personal information processing” is defined as any operation or set of operations that is performed against personal information, using automated or manual methods, such as: collecting, registering, organizing, storing, changing or alteration, quering, drilling/mining, using, transmitting – directly or through disemination, combining, blocking, deletion or destruction; 
  * „storage” is defined as the operation of keeping on any type of support of the personal information; 
  * „personal information management system” is defined as any organized structure/system used to manage personal information; 
  * „operator” is defined as any private or corporate legal entity , including public authorithies, that defines the purpose and the objectives of the personal information processing; 
  * „anonymous data” is defined as the data that cannot be linked and/or associated with an identified or identifiable person; 
  * „business contact data” is defined as the data that includes name, position, business address, phones, faxes, e-mail address of an employee of an organization, having this quality. Business contact data do not constitute personal information; 
  * „statistical data” is defined as the data resulting from the processing of personal information using statistical methods and technologies, data that cannot be used in identifying a person and that it is used only for statistical, communication or promotion purposes. 



**Personal data collection**

As a visitor to this Site, you can engage in many activities without providing any personal information. In connection with other activities, Avangate may ask you to provide certain information about yourself by filling out and submitting an online form. It is completely optional for you to engage in these activities. If you elect to engage in these activities, however, Avangate may ask that you provide us personal information, such as your first and last name, mailing address (including ZIP code), e-mail address, employer, job title and department, telephone and facsimile numbers, and other personal information such as IDs. When using Services provided through the Site you may be asked to provide a credit/debit card number, expiry date, other information. Depending upon the activity, some of the information that we ask you to provide is identified as mandatory and some as voluntary. If you do not provide the mandatory data with respect to a particular activity, you will not be able to engage in that activity. 

When you use the Site, Avangate or third parties authorized by Avangate may also collect certain technical and routing information about your computer to facilitate your use of the Site and its Services. For example, we may log environmental variables, such as browser type, operating system, and the Internet Protocol (IP) address of your computer. We use these environmental variables to facilitate and track your use of the Site and its Services. Avangate also uses such environmental variables to measure traffic patterns on the Site. Without expressly informing you in each particular circumstance, we do not match such information with any of your personal information. 

When you submit personal information to Avangate through this Site, you understand and agree that this information may be transferred across national boundaries and may be stored and processed in any of the countries in which Avangate and its affiliates and subsidiaries maintain offices. You also acknowledge that in certain countries or with respect to certain activities, the collection, transfer, storage, and processing of your information may be undertaken by trusted suppliers of Avangate. Such vendors are bound by contract to not use your personal information for their own purposes or provide it to any third parties. 

Avangate has established relationships with various third parties/providers whose services are available to you from this Site or from Web sites that are linked to this Site. In order to use those services, you may be required to provide personal information to the providers. Unless expressly provided to the contrary, personal information that you provide while you are visiting a provider’s Web site will be subject to the privacy policy posted on such site, and personal information that you provide while on this Site in conjunction with a provider’s service will be subject to this Privacy Policy. You should be aware that our agreements with these service providers often provide that they will share with Avangate personal information collected from you. In such instances, Avangate may use this information in a manner consistent with this Privacy Policy. 

In order to reduce errors in our database, authenticate our users, and prevent abuse of our system, we may on occasion supplement the personal information you submit to us with information from third-party sources.

**Protection of your personal information**

The personal information that you provide is stored and resides on a secure server that only selected Avangate personnel and contractors have access to via password. Avangate encrypts your personal information and thereby strives to prevent unauthorized parties from viewing such information when it is transmitted to Avangate. 

Please note that any information you post to a public bulletin board or chat room (the “Forums”), is available to all persons accessing that location. Your e-mail address will not automatically be posted on the Forums unless you modify the default setting to permit that. Your e-mail address would then be available to all persons accessing the Forums. In addition, you should be aware that the Forums offer a service in which subscribers are e-mailed a copy of all messages posted to the Forums on a particular topic. Consequently, if you post a message to the Forums, your e-mail address will be sent to those subscribers that have indicated an interest in the topic of your message. 

**Use of cookies**

When you visit this Site, you can browse the Site and access important information without revealing your identity. In order to improve our Site, we use “cookies” to track your visit. A cookie is small amount of data that is transferred to your browser by a Web server and can only be read by the server that gave it to you. It functions as your identification card and enables Avangate to record your passwords, purchases, and preferences. It cannot be executed as code or deliver viruses. 

Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it. (For some Web pages that require an authorization, cookies are not optional. Users choosing not to accept cookies will probably not be able to access those pages.) 

While Avangate uses cookies to track your visit to the Sites, and our Web servers automatically log the IP/Internet address of your computer, Avangate does not generally use this information to identify you personally. We associate this data to understand how our Site and services are being used so we can understand our customers and offer appropriate products and services.
