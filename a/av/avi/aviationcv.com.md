# Privacy Policy | Aviationcv.com

### Privacy Policy 

**Privacy Policy**

Your privacy is important **to AviationCV.com.** So we've developed a Privacy Policy that covers how we collect, use, disclose, transfer, and store your information. Please take a moment to familiarize yourself with our privacy practices and let us know if you have any questions.

**AviationCV.com** is respecting and protecting the privacy of those who use our website. Information submitted by you will be processed with due care and attention and will only be used for the purposes for which it was intended i.e. to assist the recruitment process.

By using our website and by submitting or registering your details, you consent to us collecting and processing your personal data. **AviationCV.com** will not provide your information to other parties other than as detailed here. We will only provide relevant details to those employers recruiting for the particular posts for which you have applied or, with your prior permission, to employers recruiting for similar posts. When you submit a Curriculum Vitae without applying for a specific position, then relevant details will only be provided to prospective employers after your permission has been received.

Your personal data is held in a secure environment and is accessible only by relevant AviatiovCV.com staff using individual usernames and passwords. It should be noted that the nature of the Internet is such that we cannot guarantee or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us via the Internet. No data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. However we will take all reasonable steps, both technical and organisational, to protect your personal data.

**If you wish to have your personal data removed from our database** , contact us at ****[info@aviationcv.com](mailto:info@aviationcv.com) ** __**  
In the event of a merger, sale or other transfer of all or substantially all of the assets of our company, we reserve the right to transfer information, including personal data, to a third party, provided that it has an appropriate privacy policy and undertakes to use your personal data only for the purposes for which you provided it to us.
