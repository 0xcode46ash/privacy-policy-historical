# AVIRA Antivirus Solutions - AVIRA Privacy

The policies below are applicable to the avira.com website and AVIRA's online product features. 

This privacy statement does not apply to any other online or offline sites, products or services. Please read the complete privacy statement.

GeneralAVIRA respects the privacy of every individual who visits our websites, responds to our interactive advertisements or sends us e-mails. This Privacy Statement outlines the information that AVIRA will collect and the final use of such data. Also, the document is meant to instruct you on what to do if you do not want to have your personal information collected or shared when you visit AVIRA websites, respond to our Web-based advertisements or send us emails.

Security

All order forms on avira.com employ a Secure Socket Layer (SSL) or Secure Electronic Transaction (SET) protocol to provide "secure sessions" for our visitors. Through the "secure session," the information that you input on an avira.com form will be sent and will arrive privately and unaltered at an AVIRA server. This security policy prevents other companies and Web users from being able to access your information. 

Scope and Purpose of This Notice

This Privacy Notice is meant to advise you on the online privacy practices of avira.com and to describe the choices you have regarding the use of the information collected on this site. It also applies to consumer digital registration cards. For information about other offline data collections, use the contact information noted below.

Non-Personally-Identifiable Information Collected Automatically

In some cases, we may collect information about you that is not personally-identifiable. Among this kind of information, we mention: your type of Internet Browser, the computer operating system you are using and the domain name of the website and/or Internet Service Provider from which you linked to our site or advertisement.

Unless you deselect the relevant options when installing your software, the Administrator also collects certain automatic virus reports from the software installed on your computer. This information may include operating system and host names and is employed to provide public statistics regarding computer virus detections, which are also available on the AVIRA website.

As applied to personally identifiable information, we will not sell non-personally-identifiable information to third parties, unless stated at the time of collection. We may share non-personally identifiable information with business affiliates. 

Information Collected through Use of Cookies and Other Technologies

AVIRA automatically receives and records data on our servers from visitor browsers including computer IP (Internet Protocol) addresses and other information using cookies. This information is collected from thousands of site visits and analyzed as a whole. Such analysis looks for trends among many visitors on avira.com or other AVIRA sites, and determines which parts of the site are accessed most frequently and what information visitors find most valuable.

We may also collect and record information about what you viewed on our website. We may use this type of information and combine it with your personal information to help customize our future interactions with you. In doing so, we hope to provide better service to you by tailoring our communications to match your interests - to give you more of what you want and less of what you do not want.

For more information about AVIRA cookies click here. 

Collecting Your Personal Information

This Online Privacy Notice covers the collection and use of personal information. Personal Information is data that can be reasonably used to identify or describe an individual. 

AVIRA collects personal information online when: 

  * you register to become a member of avira.com or another AVIRA service 
  * you use our online services or view our online product features 
  * you make online purchases 
  * you submit questions or comments to us 
  * you request information or materials 
  * you request sales or post-sales service and support for an AVIRA product 
  * you register products online 
  * you participate in online surveys 
  * you participate in online promotions, premiums, sweepstakes or contests 
  * you request we send information to your friends, family and colleagues 



The type of information collected may include name, address, phone number, Social Security or other tax identification number, billing and delivery information, e-mail address, gift recipient information, credit card information and other demographic information. 

Using Your Information

AVIRA uses the personal information you provide to: 

  * register you in programs 
  * create and maintain accounts 
  * process, fulfill, and follow up on orders 
  * answer your e-mail 
  * send information you request 
  * send friends, families and colleagues information on your behalf 
  * send surveys 
  * register products   




We also use this personal information to provide you with information related to your account, the products or services you purchased from us, to better understand your needs and interests, to improve our service and to personalize communications. 

Sharing Your Personal Information

AVIRA will not sell, rent, or trade the personal information you provide online without providing you notice or obtaining your consent. However, AVIRA reserves the right to transfer your personal information in connection with the sale or transfer of all or a portion of our business or assets. We do share your personal information with certain AVIRA business affiliates. For a description of AVIRA's business affiliates, [click here]. 

AVIRA may disclose personal information to third parties without your consent for any of the following reasons: to comply with any law, regulation or court order, to cooperate with Government investigations, to help prevent fraud or to enforce or protect the rights of AVIRA or its subsidiaries. 

AVIRA reserves the right to transfer your personal information in connection with the sale or transfer of all or a portion of our business or assets. If the business is sold or transferred, AVIRA will give you an opportunity to tell us not to transfer your personal information. In some cases, this may mean that the new organization will not be able to continue providing you with the services or products that AVIRA offered. 

Your Choices about Receiving Email

AVIRA may send you e-mails about your orders or your account and in response to your questions. AVIRA and its subsidiaries may also send you informing e-mails about security alerts and important news and/or special offers about products and services that may be of interest to you, unless you indicate you do not want to receive them. 

We will give you the opportunity to let us know your preference regarding the receiving of informing e-mails when you register for a service, when you provide us with your personal information, or when we send you e-mails. If you choose not to receive such e-mails, you may neither receive special information that may be of value to you. This option applies to informing e-mails only, as we may find it necessary to send you e-mails relating to your account, order, product or pricing changes, where applied. 

The informing e-mails that you receive from AVIRA will tell you how to decline receiving future e-mails.

Protecting Your Information

AVIRA implements a variety of administrative, managerial, and technical security measures to help protect your personal information. AVIRA has internal control standards, which relate specifically to the handling of personal information. These include certain controls to help safeguard the information we collect online. 

AVIRA employees are trained to understand and comply with these controls and we communicate our privacy policy, practices and guidelines to our employees. However, while we strive to protect your personal information, you must also take steps to protect your information. We urge you to take every precaution to protect your personal information while you are on the Internet. 

Communities

Some of our sites may enable you to participate in public services such as discussion boards, chats, and live events. Please use discretion when posting personal information about yourself when using these services. Be aware that when you disclose personal information on these sites, such as your name, member name, e-mail address, etc., the information may be collected and used by others to send unsolicited e-mail. The services are open to the public, and what you post there can be seen by anyone and is not protected. AVIRA cannot control the comments that you may receive while you participate in these services. You may find other people's comments to be offensive, harmful or inaccurate. 

Children's Privacy

Protecting the online privacy of children is especially important, and those under the age of 13 are protected by law. For that reason, AVIRA does not knowingly permit children under the age of 13 to become registered members of our sites, or to buy goods and services on our sites, without verifiable parental consent. AVIRA does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information about children under 13, without their parent's express consent. 

If we ever include children under the age of 13 as part of our intended site audience, those specific web pages will be clearly identified and provide an explicit privacy notice, in accordance with the provisions of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We will provide processes to obtain parental approval, provide access to information and allow parents to request removal of their children's personal information. 

AVIRA encourages parents and guardians to spend time online with their children and to participate in their interactive activities and interests. 

Outside Links

Some AVIRA websites contain links to and from other websites and AVIRA is not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. AVIRA encourages you to ascertain the privacy practices of those websites. 

Keeping Your Information Accurate

If you are a registered member of avira.com, you can review and update your member profile using your user name and password. You also have the option of sending an e-mail to the address noted in the "How to Reach Us" section below to request a change to your information or a copy of the personal information we have collected about you online. There may be a nominal charge for information requested. We will make every reasonable effort to make sure your requests are met. To protect your privacy, proof of identity is required. 

How to Reach Us

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy when using an AVIRA website, please [[contact us](https://web.archive.org/en/pages/CONTACT.html)].  
Changes to this Privacy Notice 

AVIRA reserves the right to modify this privacy notice from time to time, by posting a prominent announcement on this page or, in the event of a material change, by notifying by e-mail all customers whose personal information we have retained.

What are "cookies" and how does AVIRA use them?

Cookies are small pieces of information stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive, at a website's request. AVIRA cookies do not contain any personal information and are used for these reasons: (1) we use cookies to keep track of temporary information. For example, cookies allow us to keep track of the files you upload and download; (2) to register you in programs, such as avira.com Membership. Cookies allow us to remember you when you login to the places on our site which require Membership; (3) to remember your country and language preferences; (4) to help us understand the size of our audience and traffic patterns; (5) to manage and present site information and the pictures displayed on your computer, and (6) to deliver information specific to your interests. 

Using our site without cookies

avira.com can be accessed with cookies disabled, however, your visit will be significantly enhanced if cookies are enabled. Some parts of the site will require cookies to be enabled, for example, to become a member of avira.com or another AVIRA Service.

You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it or not. Please click on the "Help" menu of your browser for information on setting cookie preferences.
