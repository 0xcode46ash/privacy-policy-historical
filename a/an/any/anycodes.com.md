# Privacy Policy-AnyCodes

## Privacy Statement

AnyCodes App is committed to safeguarding your privacy online. Please read the following policy to understand how your personal information will be treated as you make full use of our offerings. This policy may change from time to time so please check back periodically. This Privacy Statement only covers the site AnyCodes App.

## Personal Data

Only a user's email address is collected when users provide registration to AnyCodes App. By registering, you agree that AnyCodes App may send you periodical emails promoting coupons and specials from the AnyCodes App merchant base that may be of interest to you. Email addresses are never shared or sold to third-party marketers or third-party agencies.

## Aggregated Information

Aggregated information, such as which page users access or visit, and information volunteered by users is collected through various methods. For example, AnyCodes App collects IP addresses; an IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you are surfing the Web. Web servers (the computers that "serve up" Web pages) automatically identify your computer by its IP address; when you request a page from AnyCodes App, our servers log your IP address. AnyCodes App only uses IP addresses for the purposes of Web traffic analysis and trends.

## Use of Cookies

AnyCodes App uses cookies for this site. A cookie is a small data file that most major websites write to your hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Cookies allow AnyCodes App to measure activity on the site and to improve your user experience, for example by remembering your passwords and viewing preferences.

## Children and Privacy

AnyCodes App encourages parents and guardians to spend time with their children online, and to be fully familiar with the sites visited by their children.

## Individuals under 13

No information should be submitted to or posted on AnyCodes App by users under the age of 13 years without consent of their parent or guardian. AnyCodes App does not provide any personally-identifying information for users under the age of 13, regardless of its source, to any third party for any purpose whatsoever unless disclosed during collection.

## Newsletter & Coupon Alert

AnyCodes App sends newsletter or coupon alerts based on your needs. A newsletter is a periodical email with promoting coupons from AnyCodes App merchants, regularly once a week. Coupon Alerts are aperiodical emails with promoting informaiton of merchants or products you subscribed to. Once you have successfully registered, it means you accept our privacy policy. AnyCodes App may send you coupon alerts and newsletters. If you want to opt out, just click "Unsubscribe Here" at the bottom of our newsletter or coupon alert.

## Links to Other Sites

AnyCodes App contains links to sites other than AnyCodes App, those sites may not follow the same privacy policies as AnyCodes App.

## Effect of Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is not intended to and does not create any contractual or legal rights in or on behalf of any party. We may update this privacy policy to this page, and we encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information about our privacy practices.
