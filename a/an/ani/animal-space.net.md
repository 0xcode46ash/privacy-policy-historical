# Animal Space - a place for animals: Privacy Policy

Our Commitment To Privacy  
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.

Third Party Cookies  
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique “cookie” on your browser.

Personal Information  
This Web site does allow user to enter their email address at their own will for comments on our site. We do not request any personal information or collect any information that personally identifies you or allows you to be personally contacted. Since we do not collect any personal information on this Web site, we do not share any personal information with any third parties nor do we use any personal information for any purposes.

External Links  
This site contains links to other sites. Animal Space is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

Any personal information about our visitors collected on Animal Space(names, email addresses, etc.) is used only for this web site and its services. We guarantee that we will never sell your information or send you any kind of spam. We handle all collected information with great care.

We guarantee that all files hosted on Animal Space are virus checked and do not contain any spyware, dialers, trojans or other malicious programs. However if any damage is caused to your computer we have no responsibility.

 **How To Contact Us:**  
Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send us an email at [info@animal-space.net](http://www.blogger.com/info@animal-space.net).
