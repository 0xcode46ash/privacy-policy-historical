# Privacy Policy | Anki

**Effective February 6, 2015**

THIS PRIVACY POLICY GOVERNS YOUR USE OF THE WEB SITE AND MOBILE SOFTWARE APPLICATION (COLLECTIVELY, THE "SITE") OFFERED BY ANKI, INC. AND/OR ITS SUBSIDIARIES AND AFFILIATES (REFERRED TO AS "ANKI", "WE", "OUR", OR "US").

Anki, Inc. ("Anki," "we," or "us") continually strives to improve the experience of using our products and this is the primary goal in collecting information from our customers and web and mobile application users ("you"). This policy describes how we collect, maintain, protect, use, and disclose information we receive from you when you use our website(s) (the "Website(s)" or "Site(s)"), the Anki DRIVE mobile application (the "App" or the "Anki DRIVE App") and via email to us. This Privacy Policy does not apply to information collected by us offline or through any other means; or by any third party, including through any application or content (including advertising) that may link to or be accessible from or on the Website.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy here or, if the changes are material with regard to information collected from you or your child, by posting a prominent notice on the Website or mobile application or by sending an email to the email address you provide to us prior to the change becoming effective. You should consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes.

## Types of Information We Gather from You And How We Gather Information

**Information Collection**

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Website and App, including:

_Personal Information_

Personal information is information about you that can be used by itself, or combined with other information, to personally identify you. We collect this information directly from you when you provide it to us.

When you purchase an Anki product from Anki.com, you provide us with your name, email, phone number, shipping and billing addresses, credit card information, and other information you affirmatively provide. When you register to receive Anki news we collect age and email address. When you sign up for an Anki account (an "Account" or a "Connected Profile"), we will collect your Driver Name, email address, birthdate, and password. If you choose to have a physical DRV license delivered to you or your child via mail, we will collect your mailing address to deliver a license to you or your child.

If you choose to purchase Anki DRIVE as a gift and send to a third party, we will ask you for friend or family member's name and shipping information. We do not use the shipping information for any secondary purpose.

We may also collect your Internet Protocol ('IP') address. We use your IP address to diagnose problems with our servers, software, to administer our Services and to gather demographic information.

We do not collect more personal information than is reasonably necessary to participate in an activity on the Website or App or enhance your gameplay experience.

**Specific to the Anki DRIVE App**

When you use the Anki DRIVE App we collect your non-unique driver nickname (please do not use your real name), device make and model, Apple vendor ID, vehicle ID, operating system version, and language your device is set to. In addition, we collect app and gameplay data to help improve our products and services. This information may include, but is not limited to, game scores, game achievements, game performance, and your usage of various game features. All information collected by Anki without your explicit permission (such as prior to when you create an Account) is intended to be anonymous information that does not disclose your identity or constitute personal information. It will automatically be transmitted to Anki and used as described herein. The information collected by Anki may be posted in an anonymous and aggregated format by Anki on publicly-accessible web sites such as Facebook, shared with hardware manufacturers, shared with platform hosts, shared with Anki's marketing partners or used by Anki for any other lawful purpose.

We use mobile analytics software to allow us to better understand the functionality of our Application on your mobile device. This software may record information such as how often you use the Anki DRIVE app, the events that occur within the application, aggregated usage, and performance data. We do not link the information we store within the analytics software to any personally identifiable information you submit within the Anki DRIVE app without your explicit permission.

We do obtain non-personally identifiable zip-code level geographic information using devices' IP addresses. You may turn off location services at the device level.

If you choose to use our results sharing feature, the Anki DRIVE App will provide you with options to share your race results via text messaging, email, or social applications you have on your mobile device.

You will have the ability to save images to your device in our sharing of results feature. If you wish to do so, you will receive a notification indicating that the application would like to access your photos which you may allow or deny. We do not access other photos, that you may have saved to your device, we only allow you to save the image you create within our mobile application to our device.

We send push notifications to provide you with alerts and information. You may opt-out of push notifications at the device level.

**Automatically as you navigate through the site: usage Details Cookies, Cookies and Other Technologies**

When you visit our Website, certain information is or can be automatically recorded. Generally, we use this information in an aggregated and anonymized way. If we need to combine this type of information with personal information, we treat it as such. Automatically collected information includes:

  * Statistical or technical information such as, for example, your computer's Internet Protocol address, operating system and browser type, time and date, and how you navigate through and interact with the Website.
  * Information collected via "cookies" or "web beacons," which are small data files that we transfer to your computer for record keeping purposes. We primarily use cookies for login session management. Anki cookies do not collect personal information from you via cookies or web beacons, and we do not combine the information collected through cookies with other personal information you may provide to identify you. You can change the options on your browser to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from a website. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all portions or functionality of our Website.



**Accounts**

You can sign up for a Connected Profile to backup stats, track progress on multiple devices, and reserve a unique Driver Name. If you sign up for an Anki Account via the App or Website, we will collect your Driver Name, email address, birthdate, and password. If you choose to have a physical DRV license delivered to you or your child via mail, we will collect your mailing address to deliver a license to you or your child.

Once you sign up for an Account, your gameplay data is connected to your Driver name and email address in your Account. We may contact you from time to time via email based on your gameplay data.

You may modify your Account information or request deletion of your Account by emailing us at [support@anki.com.](mailto:support@anki.com)

**From Third Parties, such as our business partners: Advertiser Use of Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies.**

When you visit our website, we use third parties to collect anonymized and aggregated demographic and Website use information such as, for example, type of browser and operating system, location, referring website, Internet service provider (or, in the case of mobile devices, mobile carrier, UDID), and type of device. We do not control these third parties' tracking technologies or how they may be used.

We partner with a third party ad networks to manage our advertising on other sites and retarget visitors to our site. Our ad network partners use cookies and Web beacons to collect non-personally identifiable information about your activities on this and other Web sites to provide you targeted advertising based upon your interests. If you wish to not have this information used for the purpose of serving you targeted ads, you may opt-out by [clicking here](http://preferences.truste.com/), or by visiting our list of 3rd Party Operators [here](https://web.archive.org/third-party-operators). Please note this does not opt you out of being served advertising. You will continue to receive generic ads.

As outlined in our [3rd Party Operators](https://web.archive.org/third-party-operators) page, we use Google Analytics with anonymized IP. Google Analytics is a web analysis service provided by Google Inc. (“Google”). Google utilizes the Data collected to track and examine the use of this Application, to prepare reports on its activities and share them with other Google services. Google may use the Data collected to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network. This integration of Google Analytics anonymizes your IP address. It works by shortening all Users' IP addresses, including those within Member States of the European Union or in other Contracting States to the Agreement on the European Economic Area. 

**Emails and Other Communications and Your Choices**

From time to time, we send education, marketing, product support, and research emails. Our marketing emails relate to our product and services offerings: software, hardware products, promotions, news, and events. Our emails may contain "beacons," which are small images that inform us about recipients' receptiveness to our emails. Our third-party mail service provider also provides us with anonymized and aggregated demographic information about the respondents to our emails. In our emails, we may also ask you to voluntarily provide us with additional information about you. Generally, we use information from our marketing and research communications in an aggregated and anonymized way. When our business needs require, we combine it with your personal information, and in such cases we treat it as personal information. You can unsubscribe from our marketing and research emails by following the instructions included in them.

We may also communicate with you about your order or Account, or to address your questions. We may also ask you to participate in surveys. These surveys are purely optional and help us improve our products and provide aggregate information, which we think may interest our registered users and visitors. We may use the information that you voluntarily provide as part of your survey response in the manner outlined in this Privacy Policy. In addition, if you choose to participate in an Anki survey, any information you provide to us, including personal information, will be linked with your Account in our system and to any personal information contained therein. We may also use the information you provide in the aggregate to help us generate statistics regarding use of our Website and App. If you would not like your information to be used in this way, please do not participate in Anki surveys or, if participating, please provide us only with information you consent to our use of in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

**Social Media**

We have a presence on various social media platforms, such as Facebook, Twitter, and YouTube. We receive from them anonymized and aggregated demographic and use information related to our presence on the respective social media platform when you choose to visit us on those sites or to communicate with us.

From time to time, we may conduct contests and sweepstakes on our social media channels or in cooperation with other partners. If you choose to participate in such contests and sweepstakes, we may collect information from you, as described in the terms of the respective contest or sweepstake.

In addition, we may from time to time, offer social sharing features and other integrated tools (such as the Facebook "Like" button), which let you share actions you take on our Website and Anki DRIVE app with other media, and vice versa. The use of such features may enable the sharing of information with your friends or the public, depending on the settings you establish with the third party that provides these features. These features may also collect your IP address, which page you are visiting and may set a cookie to enable the features to function properly. For more information about the purpose and scope of data collection and processing in connection with social sharing features, please visit the privacy policies of the third parties that provide these features.

## How We Use this Information

We use information that we collect about you or that you provide to us, including any personal information to:

  * Provide and deliver products and services you request
  * Allow you to participate in interactive features on our Website
  * Personalize your experience and customize content, marketing, and recommendations
  * Respond to requests that you make, provide you with customer service, send you press releases, general informative announcements, and promotional information about us or our products, and to fulfill any other purpose for which you provide it
  * Assist those who help us in designing and operating our Website, app or help us analyze collected data
  * Analyze, use and learn about the interests of our users in the aggregate and also on a personal level to better understand your interests and needs, or to aid us in serving you better
  * Carry out our obligations and enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered into between you and us, including for billing and collection
  * Protect, investigate, and deter against fraudulent, unauthorized, illegal activity, or violations of our policies
  * Notify you about changes to our Website or any products or services we offer or provide though it
  * In any other way we may describe when you provide the information for any other purpose with your consent



## Sharing Your Information

We may disclose aggregated information about our users, and information that does not identify any individual, without restriction. We will not share personal information with third parties who are not service providers except as described in this privacy policy.

We may share your personal information:

  * With third parties that support our business, such as to process payments, or to store information collected through our site, app, and services, and who are bound by contractual obligations to keep personal information confidential and use it only for the purposes for which we disclose it to them.
  * With a parent company, subsidiaries, joint ventures, or other companies under common control with us.
  * For the purposes of a business deal (or negotiation of a business deal) involving sale or transfer of all or a part of our business or assets. These deals can include any merger, financing, acquisition, or bankruptcy transaction or proceeding.
  * To fulfill the purpose for which you provide it, for any other purpose disclosed by us when you provide the information, or otherwise with your consent.
  * To (a) comply with relevant laws, regulatory requirements and to respond to lawful requests, court orders, and legal process; (b) to protect and defend the rights or property of us or third parties, including enforcing agreements, policies, and terms of use; (c) in an emergency, including to protect the safety of our employees or any person, or (d) in connection with investigating and preventing fraud.

You consent to the transfer of your personal information to the United States. ## Accessing Your Information; Your Opt Out Rights

We offer certain choices about how we communicate with you and what information we obtain about you and your child. You may opt out of receiving any emails from Anki at any time. You may modify your personal information or request deletion of personal information or your Account by emailing us at [support@anki.com](mailto:support@anki.com). We will respond to all access requests within 30 days. We may not accommodate a request to change information if we believe the change would violate any law or legal requirement or cause the information to be incorrect. If you delete your User Contributions from the Website, copies of your User Contributions may remain viewable in cached and archived pages, or might have been copied or stored by other Website users. Proper access and use of information provided on the Website, including User Contributions, is governed by our [terms of use](https://web.archive.org/web/20150321075036id_/https%3A//anki.com/terms-and-conditions).

Please note that although you may opt out of receiving promotional emails, Anki reserves the right to communicate with you via email regarding specific products and services you have ordered, requested, or inquired about.

We will retain your information for as long as your Account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

## Children

The Anki DRIVE App and Anki Website are intended for audiences of all ages including children under 13. When we collect personal information, we will ask users of the Website and the Anki DRIVE App to input their age. For users of the Website under the age of 13, we will not collect personal information.

If a child signs up for an Account, his or her parent will be provided with notice via email and the parent must approve the account before it is activated. If the parent chooses to have a physical DRV license for his or her child’s account delivered to them, we will collect a mailing address from the parent to deliver the license. 

A list of operators who collect or maintain personal information through our Site or App can be found [here](https://web.archive.org/third-party-operators). We will only collect such Personal Information as is reasonably necessary to provide the features and services of the Site and App. Parents have the right to consent to Anki's collection and use of Personal Information from the child without also consenting to its disclosure to third parties.

**If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent or decides to review, change or revoke information provided by his or her child or a prior consent, he or she should contact us at[info@anki.com](mailto:info@anki.com).** We will delete such information from our files within a reasonable time.
