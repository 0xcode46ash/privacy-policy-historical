# Privacy Policy Statement

Privacy Policy Statement

Marketing Extensions Inc., recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of personal information about our customers. As such, this Privacy Policy applies to collection, use, storage and disclosure of information by Marketing Extensions Inc.,

Our postal address is:  
99 Atlantic Avenue, suite #303  
Toronto. Ontario M6K 3J8  
e-mail: privacy@marketingx.com

In General, Marketing Extensions does not collect, store, use or disclose personally identifying information. For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, but not the e-mail address (where possible).

We collect only the domain name, but not the e-mail address of visitors to our web page, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, user-specific information on what pages consumers access or visit and information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations.

The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded, used to improve the content of our Web page and not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

Please be aware that, as part of the Services, Marketing Extensions provides links to Web sites operated by third parties, and is not responsible for the information collection or privacy practices or the content of those third party Web sites or applications.

iBill and Paycom are our credit card processors. Marketing Extensions Inc., does not store or maintain any credit or personal information. All information is stored at iBill's or Paycom's highly secure sites. iBill's Privacy Policy can be viewed by [clicking here](http://www.ibill.com/About/Legal.cfm#Privacy), and Paycom's can be viewed by [clicking here](http://www.paycom.net/).
