# allproduct.com - The World's Best Products Directory for Volume Buyers

About Us

Privacy Policy  
Privacy is important to you and allproducts.com. Please read the following to learn more about our policy on user privacy protection.

Collection and Use of Information

allproducts.com gathers identifiable information on you (the user) when you register with allproducts.com,or when you use certain allproducts.com products or services. allproducts.com may also obtain identifiable information on you from our business partners. 

When you register with allproducts.com, we ask for certain information, such as your name, contact information, as well as business information. Once you register with allproducts.com and sign up for any of our services, we will then know something about you, which will allow us to better serve you.

As is customary on the World Wide Web, allproducts.com tracks your browser including the pages you request, and your IP address; our system automatically keeps track of and records information on our server logs. The primary purpose of this information is to evaluate the performance of our business site, and to inform you of new products and special information, which may be useful and of value to you. 

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our web site. Our third-party advertising company also separately places or recognizes a cookie file on your browser in the course of delivering advertisements to this Site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please [click here](http://www.doubleclick.com/us/corporate/privacy/privacy/default.asp?asp_object_1=&).

Sharing and Disclosure of Information

allproducts.com does not sell or rent information about any of our users to anyone. allproducts.com will only send identifiable information about you to another company or person under one of the following situations:

  1. We have your explicit consent to share the information. 
  2. The information needed is required to provide the product or service you have requested. 
  3. Companies working on behalf of allproducts.com to arrange a product or service you have requested, need your information to provide the product or service requested (Unless explicitly specified, these companies have no right to use any information we provide to them, beyond what is necessary to assist us.) 
  4. We comply with court orders, subpoenas and legal process. 



We find that your actions on our web sites breach the allproducts.com Terms of Service, or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services

Updating and Deleting Account Information

allproducts.com allows you to update your allproducts.com Account Information at any time, including options to receive information alerts pertaining to specials and new products. Your allproducts.com Account Information is password-protected for your privacy and security. You may at any time request deletion of your allproducts.com account by sending an email to  
[service@allproducts.com](mailto:service@allproducts.com). 

Changes to this Privacy Policy

allproducts.com reserves the right to revise this policy when and as we deem it prudent to do so. If any substantial changes are made in the way we use your personal information, a prominent announcement will be posted on our web pages.
