# All The Lyrics Privacy Policy

The following policy is intended to explain how your personal information will be treated as you use of our site. Personal information includes your name, e-mail addresses, click-through activity and any other personal information you may provide here. 

**Traffic data**  
Each time a visitor comes to a AllTheLyrics.com Web site, our server logs some basic technical information such as browser and platform, visitor's domain name, referral data. We are also processing the visitor's activity at our site. When we do present processed information to outside companies, no one will be able to identify you or contact you. 

**Cookies**  
Companies which place advertising on our site have the ability to assign cookies to you. We are using cookies in some parts of our site (in message boards to remember nickname you used last time), but we cannot control that cookies coming with banner ads. Please, check privacy policy of our advertiser: [ Adtegrity.com](http://www.adtegrity.com/privacy.asp). 

**"E-mail to a friend" feature**  
AllTheLyrics.com offers an "e-mail a friend" feature, allowing site visitors to send song lyrics via e-mail to friends. For this feature to function, we need to ask for e-mail addresses of recipient and sender. We do not store or save these addresses and you will not receive e-mail or other communications from AllTheLyrics.com as a result of having used this feature. 

**Other sites**  
AllTheLyrics.com contains many links to other sites on the Web. We cannot control and are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such other Web sites.
