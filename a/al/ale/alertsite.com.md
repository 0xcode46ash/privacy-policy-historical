# AlertSite : The Web Site Monitoring Company

Privacy Policy

The [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/) website is owned and operated by Boca Internet Technologies, Inc., a Florida corporation (“AlertSite”). AlertSite respects your individual privacy. That is why AlertSite adopted this Privacy Policy as evidence of its commitment to the protection of your privacy through adherence to fair electronic information practices. This Privacy Policy puts you in control of how your personal information is processed. You have our promise that AlertSite will not electronically process your personal information in any way that is incompatible with this Privacy Policy.

AlertSite designed its business practices to safeguard your privacy and responsible use of your personal information as part of AlertSite’s attempt to make you a “Customer For Life.”

Applicability of Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is intended to apply to [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/).

This Privacy Policy is a statement of how information about you is collected from [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/), used and potentially shared by AlertSite. In particular, this Privacy Policy will tell you:

  1. what information, including personally identifiable and preferential information is collected from you through [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/);
  2. your choices with regard to providing information;
  3. how the collected information will be used by and with whom it will be shared AlertSite; and
  4. the security of transmissions of any information or communications via [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/);

Personally Identifiable Information

AlertSite does not currently request personally identifiable information from you in order to enable you merely to utilize [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/). However, AlertSite uses a subscription form for you to request services. In connection with this subscription form, AlertSite collects information such as your e-mail address and financial information, such as account or credit card numbers. Information collected from the subscription form is used to send orders, billing materials, information about AlertSite and promotional material from our partners. This information is also used to contact you when necessary in connection with our services. Financial information that is collected is used strictly for internal purposes only. AlertSite will not disclose, sell or give rights to any personally identifiable information from you to any third-party without your consent.

Some of the information AlertSite receives about you or your visit to [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/) may be used for our internal research and to help us to improve the site and the services AlertSite offers to its customers. In general, the Internet addresses of computers, also known as IP addresses, are logged to track user sessions on an anonymous basis. AlertSite analyzes this data for certain trends and statistics, such as which parts of our site users are visiting and how long they spend there. Some of the information AlertSite receives about you or your visit to [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/) may be used for research and to help AlertSite improve the site and the services offered to its customers.

Security of Transmissions

AlertSite utilizes its best efforts to protect the security of any information or communication submitted by you to [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/), including, but not limited to, any personally identifiable information you may include in such submitted information or communication. AlertSite uses [insert type of security technology used] to help ensure that submissions and transmissions by users are kept confidential. All credit card data collected is transmitted and stored in an encrypted format and only authorized individuals of AlertSite are permitted access to such data.

In the unlikely event that an unauthorized third party compromises AlertSite’s security system, AlertSite shall not be responsible for any occurrences or damages that are directly or indirectly caused by an unauthorized third party’s ability to view, use or disseminate your submissions or transmissions.

AlertSite reserves the right to share or disclose any information or communication you may submit to [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/) when AlertSite determines, in its sole discretion, that the disclosure of such information or communication is necessary to identify, contact, or bring legal action against a user who may be:

  1. breaching the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy or the Agreement of Terms of Use of [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/) located at the URL [www.alertsite.com/terms.shtml](http://www.alertsite.com/terms.shtml);
  2. causing intentional or unintentional injury to another;
  3. interfering with another’s rights or property, including AlertSite’s rights or property;
  4. violating any applicable law, rule or regulation; or
  5. such disclosure is required by any applicable law, rule or regulation.

Links

[www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/) may contain links to other websites. This Privacy Policy applies solely to [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/), and AlertSite makes no representations or warranties about, nor is it responsible for, the privacy practices of other websites. When you enter a linked website, AlertSite encourages you to read the privacy policy of that linked website, as its privacy policy may differ substantially from that of [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/).

Cookies

AlertSite utilizes “cookies” to track and store information about [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/) users. “Cookies” are alphanumeric identifiers in the form of text files that are inserted and stored on your hard drive. Cookies track your personal preferences and activities, but in no way contain or refer to any personally identifiable information.

If you do not want cookies to be inserted into your hard drive, you can disable its ability to accept and receive cookies through your web browser. However, keep in mind that if you disable your hard drive’s ability to accept and receive cookies, you may not be able to fully utilize all of the convenience and additional features of [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/).

AlertSite’s use of cookies on [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/) allows AlertSite to aggregate certain information about [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/) users. The aggregated information helps AlertSite to serve you better by compiling user preferences to improve the features of [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/). AlertSite may also share this aggregated information with third parties, but keep in mind that the aggregated information contains absolutely no personally identifiable information.

Changes, revisions or deletions with regard to this Privacy Policy shall be effective immediately upon their inclusion in, or deletion from, this Privacy Policy and the posting of the same in this Privacy Policy on [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/).

You are encouraged to revisit this Privacy Policy from time to time in order to review any changes that have been made. Your continued access and use of [www.AlertSite.com](http://www.alertsite.com/) following the posting of any such changes shall indicate your acceptance of the same.
