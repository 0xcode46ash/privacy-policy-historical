# Aladdin Schools - Privacy

## Privacy Statement

This website, www.aladdin.ie (the “Website”) is operated by Cloudware Limited t/a Aladdin Schools (“Aladdin,” “we,” or “us”), an Irish registered company (registered number – 476869) with registered office at 33 Holmwood, Cabinteely, Dublin 18. This privacy statement outlines what personal data Aladdin collects through the website, how Aladdin uses that information and other information about how Aladdin protects your privacy.

Please read this Privacy Statement carefully. If you do not agree to its terms, then you should not use or access the Website. Your use of the Website signifies your acceptance of this Privacy Statement.

## Information We Collect

If you are simply browsing this website, then Aladdin does not collect any personal data relating to you.

However, please note that we collect a certain amount of statistical information concerning usage of the Website, for example, logs of the number of visitors to the Website, pages visited by those visitors and so on. We also keep records of your web address when you access the Website to enhance your experience on return visits to the Website, assist the design of the Website, identify popular features, and for other managerial purposes. Web logs may also be used if necessary to help identify any person attempting to break into or damage the Website, or use the Website to commit any illegal act.

We use cookies to store your user settings and to enable you to login to the Website. Cookies are small data files that allow websites to recognise repeat visitors and to facilitate their ongoing access to and use of the website. Our cookies involve the assigning of a unique number to visitors. If you wish to disable our cookies, you can do this simply by activating the facility in most web browsers that enables a user to deny or accept cookies. However, if you disable our cookies, you may not be able to access certain aspects or functionality of the Website.

We will respond to requests for contacts and sales made via the Website at www.aladdin.ie using personal information received from you at the time of the request. To request information about our products and services, we may ask you to provide certain information including but not limited to your name, address, telephone number, email address, school name, school address, total enrolment and referral information. We will not share this information with anyone outside of our company and no information will be collected unless you provide it to us voluntarily.

If you are using our web-based student information management technology (“Service”), then you will provide us with your secure username or password in order to use the Service.

Aladdin only processes personal data provided by our school customers through the Service in accordance with their instructions and in this regard acts as our customers’ data processor in providing the Service. Aladdin has and maintains in place technical and organisational measures to ensure that all data processed as part of the Service is kept secure and confidential. In particular, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we manage on behalf of our customers and the information we collect and manage. To protect the privacy and security or users of the Service, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access to information. We also use secure connections to protect sensitive information during its transmission.

## Access to information provided through the service

Student administration Information provided through the Service may be accessed by Aladdin employees and our advisors on a need to know basis. We may also be required to disclose such information to law enforcement authorities where required to do so by law. 

## Links to third party sites

You may be able to link to third party websites from the Website, but we cannot guarantee that those third party sites will respect your privacy in the same way we do. 

## Governing Law

This Privacy Statement is governed by the laws of Ireland and you submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Irish courts for all disputes arising under it.
