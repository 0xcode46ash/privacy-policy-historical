# Privacy Statement - BaldwinHardware.com

**BALDWIN PRIVACY POLICY**

We respect your privacy and want you to know what happens to information we receive when you use this site. Generally, you can visit this site without providing any personal information, such as name, mailing address or email address. We may request personal information in order to provide you with products or services or correspondence, such as brochures, promotional materials and email notifications. We collect and store personal information as appropriate given its nature and your request. 

We share personal information with third parties to fulfill your requests for information, products and services and to improve our services to you. Third parties who have access to personal information agree to treat the information as confidential and not use it for any purpose except to fulfill your requests. We may also share personal information with our parent company, The Black & Decker Corporation and its subsidiaries and affiliates. We may disclose personal information when required by law, governmental or administrative order, or when we deem it necessary to protect the integrity of this site or our interests. We may disclose personal information in the context of the sale or transfer of some or all of our assets or our business and we may retain a copy of the transferred information. 

We track non-personal information about the visits to our site, such as your internet service provider, the web that referred you to us, the pages you request and the date and time of your requests. These aggregated statistics are used for research, promotions, and site development to help us provide you with better services. We may share aggregated demographic information but this is not linked to any personal information that can identify you. We may use cookies to provide you with tailored information. A “cookie” is an element of data that a web site can send to your browser, which may then be stored on your hard drive so we can recognize you when you return. You may set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie. We do not use cookies to retrieve personal information about you from your computer. 

We use commercially reasonable industry standards to protect the security of information that you provide online. Please understand that no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the security of our site or of any information that is transmitted to us over the internet. Other companies may link to our site but we do not endorse such links, companies or theirs privacy policies, products or services. You should review the privacy policies of each website that you visit 

_Notice to California residents about your California privacy rights:_ In addition to the other rights described in this policy, if you are a California resident and our customer, you have the right to request information regarding the manner in which we share certain types of personal information with third parties to the extent used for direct marketing purposes. You have the right to submit a request to us at our designated address and receive the following information within 30 days of our receipt of that request: (1) the types of personal information disclosed to third parties during the immediately preceding calendar year, (2) the names and addresses of third parties that received the personal information, and (3) if the nature of a third party’s business cannot be reasonably determined from the third party’s name, examples of its products or services. You are entitled to receive a copy of this information in a standardized format; any information will not be specific to you individually. All such requests for such information must be in writing and sent to our designated address. 

We will notify you of any material change to our policies that affect your personal information by posting notice of such changes on this site. You may wish to check our site periodically to review our current privacy policy or you may contact us by emailing [webmaster@baldwinhq.com](mailto:webmaster@baldwinhq.com). You may tell us to not use or share your personal information (other than to fulfill your order requests) by contacting us at webmaster@baldwinhq.com and we will process your request in a reasonable period.
