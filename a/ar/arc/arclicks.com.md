# Privacy Policy - ArClicks - Plan | Build | Earn

Your privacy is important to us and we will take all steps to protect your sensitive information. Please read this policy to understand what information ArClicks collects, how the information is used, and what choices you have regarding collection and use of your information when using ArClicks.

This policy covers information you share - both personally identifiable and non-identifiable information - with us as a visitor of ArClicks web site. If you are a member/advertiser of ArClicks, you are subject to the ArClicks [Terms of services](https://web.archive.org/tos/).

The information we collect

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the ArClicks website. On some pages, you can order subscriptions or register to receive email information. 

_The types of personal information collected at these pages are:_

  * Email address
  * Country
  * AlertPay account ID

_The types of non personal information collected at these pages are:_

  * IP address
  * Type of browser
  * Date of visit

_The way ArClicks uses this information:_

  * To personalize your experience
  * To improve our website
  * To process transactions



Our commitment to data security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial 3rd party processors, providers and procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Disclosure to third parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. Though we make every effort to preserve visitor's privacy, we may need to disclose personal information as required by law or if we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding or a court order or legal process served on us, or to assert or defend our legal rights, or to protect us or others from harm to persons or property.

Third party links

You may be able to access third party websites directly from our website. This privacy policy does not apply when you access third party websites. We cannot control how third parties may use personal information you disclose to them, so you should carefully review the privacy policy of any third party website you visit before using it or disclosing your personal information to its provider.

Our commitment to children's privacy

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 18, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 18.
