# Arakne Links Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy Statement

This is the privacy policy statement of the website **Arakne-Links.com**

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes visitor's IP address only.

We collect the domain name and e-mail address of those who submit their sites to our web directory.

The information we collect is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

The information we collect is disclosed:

a) when legally required to do so, at the request of governmental authorities conducting an investigation, to verify or enforce compliance with the policies governing our Web site and applicable laws, or to protect against misuse or unauthorized use of our Web site

b) to a successor entity in connection with a corporate merger, consolidation, sale of assets, or other corporate change respecting the Web site

With respect to cookies: We use cookies to record session information, such as items that consumers add to their shopping cart, and ensure that visitors are not repeatedly sent the same banner ads.

With respect to Ad Servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information.

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our Web site periodically.

Upon request we provide site visitors with access to contact information (e.g., name, email address) that we maintain about them .Consumers can access this information by visiting us at <http://www.arakne-links.com/contact.php>

Upon request we offer visitors the ability to have inaccuracies corrected in contact information. Consumers can have this information corrected by visiting us <http://www.arakne-links.com/contact.php>

With respect to security: We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.

This Policy is effective on November 1, 2005.
