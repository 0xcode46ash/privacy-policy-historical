# Privacy Pledge

At AsianIdeas.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect from you to process orders only. We do not sell, trade, or rent our customers' personal information to others.

When you place an order, we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing address, billing address, credit card number, and expiration date. This allows us to process and fulfill your order promptly, and to notify you of your order status.

We also monitor our customers' traffic patterns within our site to help us develop the design and layout of the store to accommodate your needs.

We will notify you through e-mail about new services and special offers we think you'll find valuable.

AsianIdeas.com welcomes your questions and comments about your privacy. Please send us an e-mail at [Suggestions@asianideas.com](mailto:suggestions@asianideas.com)
