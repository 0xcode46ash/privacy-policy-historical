# Affectv | Privacy Policy

**Affectv is a Social Targeting company. We work with hundreds of websites and social media sites globally, so that we can place display ads and video ads for users based on their internet browsing. We aim to make display and video ads more relevant for users to enhance their web browsing experience.**

Affectv respects your privacy. We will not wilfully collect any personally identifiable information (PII) about you. Furthermore, we do not specifically target children under the age of 13. The IAB (Internet Advertising Bureau), which is a trade association for the Internet marketing industry, defines personally identifiable information as “information that can be traced back to a specific user and provide personal information such as name, address, phone number, etc.”

## What Information Do We Collect?

We use cookies to create ID's so that we can assign collected data to an anonymous profile, an ID will be associated with the following data; URL (website address), the date and time of the visit, and whether the advert was clicked on.

We do not collect any personal information except when an individual specifically and knowingly provides such information, for example, when entering an email address within a display ad. When we request information, we explain how this information may be used and give you an opportunity to request that the information not be used for those purposes. We also give you an opportunity to opt out of future communications from us.

## Social Data

Affectv uses data provided from social platforms to show users relevant adverts. A typical example of this could be users commenting, or sharing, the same media (such as a video or photo). Affectv does not use any PII from social platforms and uses anonymous cookie data to understand connections across social media platforms.

## Cookies

Affectv uses cookies. Cookies are data files stored on your computer that identify your web browser. Cookies enable websites to identify users that have visited them before and therefore customise that users browsing experience. Most browsers permit users to refuse cookies or to be notified before a cookie is accepted. You can visit our site without using cookies.

The Affectv cookie does not harm your computer, and is not spyware. The Affectv cookie is identified as go.affec.tv. To opt out, please visit [opt out](https://web.archive.org/opt_out.php) page.

## Links to Other Sites

Affectv contains links to other sites. These sites may send their own cookies to users, collect data, or solicit personal information. Since we do not control these sites; we cannot be held responsible or liable for the privacy practices or content of such sites.

## With Whom Do We Share Information?

Affectv does not share data with 3rd parties. Unless in circumstances whereby you provide information to an advertiser via an advert in exchange for a service, in which case only the specific advertiser will receive the information.

## Consent and Changes to this Privacy Statement

By using our site, you consent to the collection and use of your information as we have outlined in this privacy statement and to our Terms of Service. Affectv may decide to change this privacy statement from time to time. When we do, we will post the changes on this page so that you are always aware of the information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
