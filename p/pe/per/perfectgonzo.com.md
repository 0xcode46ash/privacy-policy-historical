# Perfectgonzo.com Webmaster Program

  
We take your privacy VERY seriously. All information submitted to Perfectgonzo.com, as well as all email and other correspondence, is treated with the highest confidentiality. We will not sell your information to any commercial entity. We have no obligation and therefore do not disclose individually identifying payout information to any US credit or tax agency. There is of course a possibility, as with anyone, that we could be forced to share information with a legal body but only by means of a court order.

If you chose Epassporte as a payment option and you instructed us to create an account for you, you acknowledge that we must share the information about your name & address to Epassporte in order for your account to be created.

If you have questions or comments please [contact us.](https://web.archive.org/web/20130312144015id_/http%3A//perfectgonzo.com/support.php)
