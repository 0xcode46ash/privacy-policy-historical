# Abilizer HR Advantage

**Your Privacy**

At Abilizer, your privacy and satisfaction are our first priority. It is of the utmost importance to us that you feel confident, safe, and secure in everything you do on this site. 

Our corporate site, www.abilizer.com, uses a service called WebTrends Live to collect site analysis information. WebTrends Live does not collect personal information from visitors but rather, gathers statistical information on traffic patterns, page views, user sessions, new versus returning users, top visitor click-stream paths, browsers, browser version and operating systems, java support, plug-ins and screen resolution. For more details, refer to the WebTrends Live Privacy Policy to learn how they collect and use information. You can find WebTrends Live's privacy policy here:

<http://www.webtrendslive.com/privacy_policy.htm>

In addition, on our corporate site, Abilizer collects your personally identifiable information such as name, corporate email and postal address. We also collect corporate demographic information. However, we only use your contact information to respond to your requests for information and services, and to correspond as appropriate to fulfill our service. If you need to correct, update or remove your information from our corporate site’s database please contact our privacy team at [privacy@abilizer.com](mailto:privacy@abilizer.com)
