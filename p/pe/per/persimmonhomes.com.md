# Persimmon

Privacy statement 

**Data Protection**

Persimmon offers various services from the Site which require you to input selected information about yourself ("the Information"). It is the intention of Persimmon to use the Information to provide you with an improved service and to respond to specific queries and requests. In using the Information Persimmon makes every effort to comply fully with the obligations imposed on it by the data protection legislation in force at that time. 

By opting in (ticking the opt-in box), you consent to the use of the Information by Persimmon, its agents and reasonably selected third parties to provide you with materials and selected marketing that Persimmon believes will be relevant and of interest to you. 

If at any time you wish to remove or amend the Information, please write to the Company Secretary, Persimmon plc, Persimmon House, Fulford, York YO19 4FE, or send an email to the following address: [datachanges@persimmonhomes.com](mailto:datachanges@persimmonhomes.com)
