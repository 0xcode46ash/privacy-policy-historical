# Privacy Policy at Perfumania.com

Why do we gather information?

Perfumania.com gathers personal information to help improve our products and customer service, to communicate with you, to process your orders, to provide an enhanced and more personalized shopping experience and to inform you, and your gift and message recipients, of special offers and discounts from us, our affiliated brands and selected third parties. 

We may also use the information we collect to notify you about important changes to the Web site, new services, and special offers we think you'll be interested in, and to enable you to receive credits for purchases under loyalty or buying programs which you have joined. If you would rather not receive this information, just follow the removal instructions provided. If you suspect any wrong usage of your email address, please email us to [abuse@perfumania.com](mailto:abuse@perfumania.com.). 

What information do we gather?

We collect information through our Web Site, emails, stores and telephone, including when you: place an order, participate in our forums, surveys, contests, sweepstakes, promotions, chats, and membership registrations, and engage in other activities, services, products and resources we make accessible to our customers, members or users. 

Depending on how you are interact with us; we collect some or all of the following types of information: 

your name, address, telephone number, email address, billing information (credit/debit card number, expiration date, alternate or additional billing information and billing address), gender and birth date, if you choose, or are otherwise required to, enter such information, products purchased and occasion type, any promotion code or gift card information, message and gift recipients names, addresses, telephone numbers, and email addresses. 

Information you provide to us may be collected by us even if an order, registration, or other process is started but not completed. Members may also add to their Address Book the name, address and telephone number of friends or other persons to whom they may wish to send gifts or expressions. Only Members have the right to view and edit their Address Book entries. 

How do we protect your personal information?

When you place orders or access your account information, we offer our secure server. Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encrypts all information you enter before it travels over the Internet to us. 

How we use "Cookies" and "Action Tags".

A "cookie" is a small data file sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer. We use cookies to let us know that you are a prior customer, user or Member and to provide certain features to you. Most web browsers allow you to instruct the browser to prevent the use of cookies. However, if you disable this feature, certain aspects of the Perfumania.com Web Site, such as the shopping basket, may not function properly. We have relationships with third parties who may receive non-personal information directly from the cookies placed by our Web Site on your computer to assist in analyzing our business and customers. Our Web Site also uses a common web technology "called "action tags" (also called single pixel gifts or web beacons) to anonymously track purchase, usage and other web activity information. Action tags (including third party tags) may be used to provide data to third parties for tracking purposes. However, our Web Site does not use action tags to collect or transfer personal information. 

Will we distribute your personal information to outside parties?

Perfumania.com will not distribute your personal information to other organizations. 

Links to Other Web Sites 

Perfumania.com wants you to be aware that when you click on links and/or ad banners that take you to affiliated or third-party web sites, you will be subject to the privacy policies and terms of use of those web sites, not ours. We encourage you to read the posted privacy statement and user terms whenever using, and prior to providing any personal information to, any other web site. 

Overall Perfumania.com is committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect to enhance your shopping experience at Perfumania.com. We do not distribute your personal information to outside parties. If you no longer wish to receive any emails or special offers from us, just follow the removal instructions provided in the email.
