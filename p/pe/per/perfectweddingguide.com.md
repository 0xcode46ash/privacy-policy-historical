# Privacy Policy | Perfect Wedding Guide

PWG Holdings, LLC. is committed to your right to privacy. We are constantly making changes as technology advances to ensure that your personal and financial information remains secure. This Privacy Policy serves to inform you, the user, of our practices as they apply to the usage of any collected personal information. We will only use your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. 

YOUR CONSENT

By using our web site and/or providing information, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy (established on June 21, 2002.) Any changes to our Privacy Policy will be reflected on this.   Your continued use of this site following the posting of any amendment, modification or change shall constitute your acceptance thereof.

Children should always get permission from their parents before sending any information about themselves (such as their names, email addresses, and phone numbers) over the Internet, to us or to anyone else. We will not knowingly allow anyone under 13 to register with any of our sites or to provide any other personally identifying information.  The Internet contains information and material that may be inappropriate or offensive to children. We strongly recommend that you supervise and monitor your children's use of the Internet.

THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT

General. PWG Holdings, LLC., as well as its affiliates and advertisers, collect personal information necessary to respond to your requests for products and services and to allow you to use the resources and features available on our web site, including the creation and maintenance of a Free Subscription account and Honeymoon Giveaway contest entry. Personal information includes, but is not limited to, name, physical address, e-mail address, phone numbers, age and any other information that identifies or, when tied to the above information, may identify you as a specific individual. PWG Holdings, LLC., as well as its affiliates and advertisers, also collect information related to your wedding, including dates and places of the rehearsal, wedding and reception, name of your fiancé, annual income and wedding budget, number of wedding guests, and information relating to your gift registry and honeymoon. PWG Holdings, LLC. also uses order forms for you to request certain information, products and services. On such order forms, we collect your contact information, financial information and demographic information.
