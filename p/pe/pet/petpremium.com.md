# Privacy Policy - Petpremium.com

**PetPremium, Inc.** is committed to your right to privacy and to keeping your personal information private. You can visit the PetPremium web site without telling us who you are or revealing any identifying information about yourself. However, because our web site includes areas in which visitors can provide personally identifying information, we have developed this Privacy Policy to inform you of our policies and practices regarding information we collect about you.

This Privacy Policy governs the URL [**www.petpremium.com**](http://www.petpremium.com/ "Go to Homepage") and certain electronic communications sent out by **PetPremium, Inc.** This web site is owned and operated by **PetPremium, Inc**. PetPremium is located at 2530 Meridian Parkway, Suite 2046, Durham, NC 27713. **PetPremium, Inc.** reserves the right to modify or amend this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be preceded by a notice posted to this Privacy Policy at least ten (10) days prior to the effective date of such change.

## **How We Collect Information About You**

## Information You Voluntarily Provide

**PetPremium** may collect and maintains certain personal information about you when you send us an e-mail, complete any of our online forms, complete a survey, provide a testimonial, submit referrals, request information from us, complete a quick quote or enroll for **pet insurance**. Such information may include your name, mailing address, and e-mail address; it may also include the e-mail addresses of those to whom you forward **PetPremium** electronic content, and any other information collected in the forms. You may be asked to provide credit card or checking account information to enroll for **pet insurance**.

## Other Information

**PetPremium** also collects certain other information that cannot identify you personally when you visit our web site. This information includes your IP address and your domain name. **PetPremium** logs these IP addresses and domain names, and aggregates them for system administration and to monitor the use of our site. We use the aggregated information to measure the number of visits to our site, the average time spent on our site, the number of pages viewed, and various other site statistics. This monitoring helps us to provide a more useful and engaging online experience to our website visitors, and helps us to improve the quality of our content.

**PetPremium** uses cookies in order to personalize your experience. Cookies are not used for any other reason on this site. A cookie is a small file that is sent out by a web site, accepted by the user’s browser, and placed on the user’s hard drive. You can always set your browser to warn of cookies being placed, and then choose which cookies you want to accept. **PetPremium** does not match any information gathered by cookies with personal information you may have voluntarily submitted to us. Cookies are required for the online quick quote and enrollment process and are only used while completing the forms. Your personal and financial information are never included in a cookie.

## Ways Information About You Is Used

We may use the information you voluntarily provided to contact you regarding promotions we feel you may be interested in, to keep you updated regarding **pet insurance** programs, and to extend an invitation to you to review our site when updates are made. We may also send such information to the people whose e-mail addresses you provide when forwarding **PetPremium** electronic content or providing referrals. When we do so, our sole intent is to enlarge our family of informed animal lovers and promote the importance of **pet insurance**. Every recipient of our electronic communications can quickly and easily “opt out” of future such communications by following the instructions on any electronic communication we send.

From time to time, we may make such voluntary information, excluding credit card information (which will never be disclosed), available to **PetPremium** affiliate organizations or to partners or sponsors or licensees that provide special programs or services we think you might find interesting or beneficial. **PetPremium** uses very strict standards in determining which reputable companies become partners and licensees, to ensure that such organizations are committed to the protection of visitors’ privacy and committed to the goals of **PetPremium**. If your volunteered personal information will be used in any manner other than as stated above, such use shall be specifically posted. If you wish to update or remove your information, you may unsubscribe at any time in every electronic communication or send a letter to: **PetPremium, Inc.** , Attn: Legal Department, 2530 Meridian Parkway Suite 2046, Durham, NC 27713.

## Other

From time to time, **PetPremium** may link to other web sites not affiliated with PetPremium. **PetPremium** attempts to link only to those web sites which also respect a visitor’s privacy and the goals of PetPremium. However, PetPremium cannot be responsible for the content or policies of third-party web sites. Please check with these third-party organizations or companies for their specific privacy policies. If you find inoperable links or are concerned about any of the sites that PetPremium currently links to, please inform us by contacting us [**here**](http://www.petpremium.com/reach-us/ "Contact Us").

## Security

**PetPremium** uses encryption technology to protect your information. Our web site uses Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology to prevent unauthorized parties from viewing personal information provided to PetPremium during a secure session.

All secure pages on our Web site have been issued a digital certificate by Secure Server Certification Authority. You can view this digital certificate affirming the security of our site by clicking on the image of the closed lock at the bottom of your browser window. A small frame displaying our site’s security information will appear.

We restrict access to your personal information to our employees, our affiliates’ employees, or others who need to know that information to service your account. We maintain strict physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your personal information.

We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Children

We do not sell our products to children. If you are under 18 years of age, you may use this site for informational purposes only. You must be 18 years of age in order to purchase any of our products.

## Choice/Opt-out

Our users are given the opportunity to opt-out of the sharing of their information, as described above, with affiliated third parties by sending a letter to: **PetPremium, Inc.** , Attn: Legal Department, 2530 Meridian Parkway Suite 2046, Durham, NC 27713.
