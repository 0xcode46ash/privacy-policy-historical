INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE  
  
We respect each site visitor's right to personal privacy. To that end, we collect and use information throughout our website only as disclosed in this Privacy Policy. This statement applies solely to information collected on this website.   
**For each visitor to our website, our web server automatically recognizes information regarding the domain and other traffic analysis.**  
**  
The information we collect is:

  * used to improve the content of our Web page. 
  * never shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.
  * not used to collect or track sensitive personally identifiable information of internet users. 

**  
**Registration is not required to enter our site.**  
**We have an order form.**  
**We require information from the user on our order form.   A user must provide contact information (such as name and email) and possibly financial information (such as credit card number, expiration date).  If we have trouble processing an order, we use the information to contact the user.  This information is used: 

  * for billing purposes.
  * to fill the customer orders.

**  
When we transfer and receive certain types of sensitive information such as financial, we redirect visitors to a secure server and use a trusted third party ecommerce service to process credit card and billing information.
