# LGI Distributors - Customer Service

     During normal / basic use of our site(s) we do not gather any information from you or your computer that could be considered in any way private.

We do not require you to submit any personal information to use the basic features of this site. You may stay completely anonymous.

If you would like to use some of the more advanced features of our site or purchase merchandise from us, we will ask for you to submit all required information.

This site may make use of public forums, chat rooms, and/or message boards. Any information you 'post' to these is not covered by this policy.

All Information submitted to us is kept in a safe, secure area.

All personal information collected from you is used only for fulfilling your request / order. The information is not used for any other purpose. We will not sell, trade, or give your personal information to any third party. We will only disclose any personal information when required by law ( i.e. court order or subpoena ).

All non-personally identifiable information collected ( browser type etc ) collected will be used to help improve the quality of service of our site(s). This information may be sold as we see necessary.

We may send email messages to your email address containing special offers. This is not required and you can ALWAYS opt-out. We do send receipt email messages when you purchase merchandise or request specific information from us. These emails are 'one time only' emails.

We use cookies to enhance your experience with our site. These cookies do not and will not contain any personal information. The cookies can only be read by OUR site. Likewise, we cannot read cookies from any other site you may have visited. The cookies cannot connot contain executable code so they cannot contain virii.
