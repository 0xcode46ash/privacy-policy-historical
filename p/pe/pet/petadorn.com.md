# Help zone :: PetAdorn

****

**We respect your privacy!**

Any and all the information collected on this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, disclosed, or loaned!  
Any information you provide will be held with the utmost care and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. If you have any questions, please feel free to call or [email us](https://web.archive.org/web/20061025204137id_/http%3A//www.petadorn.com/store/help.php?section=contactus&mode=update). 

**Security**

The Internet is actually one of the safest methods of using your credit card. After filling out your address information, you will be taken to a SSL (Secure Socket Layer) page with 128-bit encryption provides an extremely high level of security for submitting your payment information. Because of the use of advanced encryption, the task of obtaining stolen credit cards is extremely difficult.   
In comparison, your credit card information is more likely to be stolen by a waiter or waitress at a restaurant, or a desk clerk at a local hardware store. 

Remember, your maximum liability for stolen credit cards under Federal Law is only $50. You can always be sure that your information is secure by spotting the small lock icon in the bottom status bar of your browser.

What to do if you have received a Hoax/Publishing scams or being ripped off online? [ Fraud Resource Center](http://www.auctionbytes.com/cab/pages/fraud/) is a useful resource for you. By educating people about fraud, we can make Cyberspace a safer place to do business. 

**This site uses cookies:**

Cookies are Web browser software stored at the harddrive of user's browser. It does not slow your network connections nor take up significant disk space of your computer.  
The purpose and usage of cookies is to recognize Web site users' browsing activities, it does not collect personal information. This information assists us to design and arrange our Web pages in the most user-friendly manner and enable us continually improve our Web site that better meet the needs of our customers and prospective customers.
