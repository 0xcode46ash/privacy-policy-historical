# Privacy Policy - PENTAX Imaging Company

### Privacy Policy

We collect the following information: 

  * The domain name of visitors to our site 
  * The e-mail addresses of those who complete a "Contact Us" form 
  * The e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail 
  * Site statistics (e.g., the number of visitors to each website page) 



Personal and business information volunteered by users such as survey information, contest forms, product orders, and site registrations. The information we collect is used for providing services and products, improving our website and product offerings, contacting customers and dealers, internal review, and marketing purposes. 

PENTAX RICOH IMAGING AMERICAS does not sell, trade or rent your personal information, including email addresses, to third parties. PENTAX RICOH IMAGING AMERICAS reserves the right to disclose personal information as obligated by law, in response to duly authorized legal processes, governmental requests and as necessary to protect the rights and interests of PENTAX RICOH IMAGING AMERICAS. If you do not want to receive email from us in the future, please contact us at any time and tell us that you do not want to receive email from our company.
