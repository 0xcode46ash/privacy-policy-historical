# privacy

The Peopleclick Authoria companies ("Peopleclick Authoria") provide this Privacy Policy to explain how we collect, use and disclose certain personal information online. 

Peopleclick Authoria provides web-based service software to recruit and manage our client's permanent and contract workforce as well as integrated EEO software and expertise in affirmative action and diversity planning.  In providing our services, Peopleclick Authoria and its agents and subcontractors may have access to certain personally identifiable information.  Personal data that may be maintained by Peopleclick Authoria on behalf of our clients includes, but is not limited to, names, contact information, CV or resume, data of birth, national insurance numbers, social security numbers, salary information and race and ethnic origin.

Peopleclick Authoria respects individual privacy and takes technical, contractual, administrative and physical steps to protect against unauthorized access to and disclosure of individually identifiable information.  Peopleclick Authoria strives to collect and use personal information in a manner consistent with the laws of the countries in which we do business and abides by the Safe Harbor Principles developed by the U.S. Department of Commerce in coordination with European Commission.  Peopleclick Authoria complies with the EU Safe Harbor framework as set forth by the Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of data from the European Union.

Peopleclick Authoriahas been awarded TRUSTe's Privacy Seal signifying that this privacy policy and practices have been reviewed by TRUSTe for compliance with [TRUSTe's program requirements](http://www.truste.com/privacy_seals_and_services/consumer_privacy/privacy-programs-requirements.html) including transparency, accountability and choice regarding the collection and use of your personal information.  TRUSTe's mission, as an independent third party, is to accelerate online trust among consumers and organizations globally through its leading privacy trustmark and innovative trust solutions. If you have questions or complaints regarding our privacy policy or practices, please contact us at [Security@peopleclickauthoria.com](mailto:Security@peopleclickauthoria.com). If you are not satisfied with our response you can contact [TRUSTe here .](http://watchdog.truste.com/pvr.php?page=complaint)

Upon request and within 30 days, Peopleclick Authoria will grant individuals reasonable access to personal information that it holds about them. In addition, Peopleclick Authoria will take reasonable steps to permit individuals to correct, amend, or delete information that is demonstrated to be inaccurate or incomplete. Please contact [Security@peopleclickauthoria.com](mailto:Security@peopleclickauthoria.com)  to change any personal information gathered on the Site.

The types of Personal Information we may collect about you through our sites include contact and identifying information (name, address, email address, phone and fax numbers, institution, title or job position), information about your interest in Peopleclick Authoria products or services, information about your interest in newsletters, seminars, or other services that we provide (solely or jointly with others), information about any other transaction(s) between you or your organization and Peopleclick Authoria and any other Personal Information that you may provide to us in an online form or through an email.  Where we collect Personal Information through online forms, we will notify you of which fields are mandatory for the particular service or functionality you are requesting and which fields are optional. If you choose to provide information that is optional, we may use and disclose such information for the purposes described below.

Peopleclick Authoria takes technical, contractual, administrative and physical steps to protect against unauthorized access to and disclosure of individually identifiable information. If, during their employment, Peopleclick Authoria employees acquire confidential or proprietary information belonging to Peopleclick Authoria or its clients, such information will be handled in confidence and may not be disclosed to unauthorized third parties. Peopleclick Authoria employees are also responsible for the internal security of such information.  Peopleclick Authoria provides information and training to all employees who have access to data maintained on behalf of Peopleclick Authoria customers. Employees who violate Peopleclick Authoria security and safe maintenance of data policies are subject to discipline including termination.  To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of Personal Information, Peopleclick Authoria has adopted appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the Personal Information we process.  We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it.  No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.  Peopleclick Authoria does not share, sell, rent, or trade Personal Information with third parties for their promotional purposes.  If you have any questions about security on our site, you can email us at [Security@peopleclickauthoria.com](mailto:Security@peopleclickauthoria.com).

Data maintained by Peopleclick Authoria is protected by computer security mechanisms including firewalls, encryption, monitoring software, data and network segmentation, and passwords.  Aggregate information of Peopleclick Authoria customer data, not containing any individually identifiable information, may be provided to Peopleclick Authoria clients or prospects or disclosed in Peopleclick Authoria literature.  A visitor to [www.peopleclickauthoria.com](http://www.peopleclickauthoria.com/)  or one of our hosted web sites,who is not seeking employment and not expressing an interest in purchasing products or services, is not required to reveal any individually identifiable information such as name, address or telephone number.

Peopleclick Authoria conforms to Safe Harbor Principles and is committed to cooperating with the EU Data Protection Authorities or their authorized representatives, as an independent third party recourse mechanism for any individual who believes the protection of Personal Information processed by Peopleclick Authoria has been violated. 

Peopleclick Authoria will use Personal Information only in ways compatible with the purpose for which it was collected or authorized by our customers.  We may disclose Personal Information as necessary or appropriate in connection with any of the purposes for which we use Personal Information, including to subcontractors or service providers that help host or support the sites or otherwise provide technical assistance, fulfillment companies that help us to provide requested products or services and business partners that help conduct seminars or otherwise provide requested products, services or information.  We also reserve right to disclose Personal Information in connection with the safe or transfer of all or part of the business.  In these situations where we transfer Personal Information to these types of third parties, we require by contract that such third parties must not use or disclose the information for any purposes other than those specified in this Privacy Policy, and that such third parties must maintain commercially reasonable security measures to protect the Personal Information. In situations involving the sale or transfer of all or part of the business, we will seek to notify users of the ownership change by way of a prominent notice posted on our Web site. We also reserve the right to disclose Personal Information in connection with any judicial or administrative orders, law enforcement requests, or other applicable legal requirements.

We intend to protect the quality and integrity of your Personal Information.  An individual wishing to review or update personal data maintained in the Peopleclick Authoria system should initially contact the third party company to whom they have indicated an interest in employment. If the third party company is unable or unwilling to resolve the discrepancy, the individual should contact Peopleclick Authoria at [Security@peopleclickauthoria.com](mailto:Security@peopleclickauthoria.com). Peopleclick Authoria will respond to your request to delete or change your personal information within thirty (30) days. 

If your personally identifiable information changes, or if you no longer desire our service, you may correct, update, delete or deactivate it by making the change on our member information page or by emailing our Customer Support at [Security@peopleclickauthoria.com](mailto:Security@peopleclickauthoria.com) or by contacting us by telephone or postal mail at the contact information listed below.

Peopleclick Authoria posts customer testimonials on its web site which may contain personally identifiable information such as the customer's name.  Peopleclick Authoria obtains the customer's consent prior to posting the testimonial to post their name along with their testimonial.  If you want to remove your personal information that is being displayed on our website under public pages please send your request to [Security@peopleclickauthoria.com](mailto:Security@peopleclickauthoria.com).

If you wish to subscribe to our newsletter(s), we will use your name and email address to send the newsletter to you.  Out of respect for your privacy, we provide you a way to unsubscribe by providing a functional unsubscribe mechanism in each email communication we send to users.

We reserve the right to disclose your personally identifiable information as required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on our Web site.

We may use other third parties to provide credit card processing on our site. We will share your credit card information as necessary for the third party to provide such processing services.

We may offer products and class registration for you to purchase online. To complete these online financial transactions, our sites may request Personal Information including contact information (such as name, title, email address or shipping address) and credit card billing information. Disclosure of this information is completely voluntary and you have a choice whether or not to disclose this information.

From time-to-time our sites may request Personal Information from you in connection with surveys or contests. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and you have a choice whether or not to disclose this information. Personal Information requested may include contact information (such as name and shipping address), and demographic information (such as zip code). Contact information will be used to notify the winners and award prizes, and may also be used to provide you with additional information about our products or services, or those of our selected business partners.

Peopleclick Authoria may use non-persistent cookies in conjunction with hosting our careers.  A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive.  A cookie can't read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites and is not tied to personal information.  Cookies do not damage your system.  We use these cookies to maintain the context of a candidate's interaction with the company's career site.  You can choose whether or not to accept cookies by changing the settings of your browser.  If you choose not to accept these cookies, your experience at our site and other Web sites may be diminished and some features may not work as intended.

Peopleclick Authoria's Web site may contain links to other Web sites.  Peopleclick Authoria is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites, and this Privacy Policy does not apply to such sites.  We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information.

Peopleclick Authoria may record information that is not individually identifiable about how people access our Web sites. This information includes IP addresses (or the DNS name associated with it), which web site you are visiting Peopleclick Authoria from and what Web software you're using. This information is used in the aggregate to administer our system and make improvements to our site.  We reserve the right to share aggregate and anonymous information with potential partners and other third parties without restriction. 

Peopleclick Authoria reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time, so please review it frequently.  If we make material changes to this Policy, we will notify you here, by email, or by means of a notice on our home page so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it.  If at any point we decide to use Personal Information in a manner that is substantially different from that stated at the time it was collected, we will seek to notify users by way of an email, and provide users with a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner.

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you should first contact Security@peopleclickauthoria.com by email. If you do not receive acknowledgment of your inquiry or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, you should then contact TRUSTe through its Watchdog Dispute Resolution Process at: [http://watchdog.truste.com/pvr.php?page=complaint](http://www.truste.org/consumers/watchdog_complaint.php). TRUSTe will then serve as a liaison with us to resolve your concerns.

Peopleclick Authoria is committed to cooperating with the Data Protection Authorities or their authorized representatives as an Independent third party recourse mechanism for any individual who believes their personal data protection has been violated.  Peopleclick Authoria's Chief Security Officer is responsible for Peopleclick Authoria's compliance with and enforcement of this Privacy Policy; the Chief Security Officer may be reached at [Security@peopleclickauthoria.com](mailto:Security@peopleclickauthoria.com).  The United States Federal Trade Commission is the statutory body that has jurisdiction to hear any claims against the organization regarding possible unfair or deceptive practices and violations of laws or regulations governing privacy.

Peopleclick, Inc. and Authoria Inc., and their respective affiliated companies are the sole owner of the information collected and contained on [www.peopleclickauthoria.com](http://www.peopleclickauthoria.com/).  We may disclose Personal Information when required by law, court order, warrant, subpoena or regulation or in the good faith belief that such action necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law, comply with legal mandates, or to protect the rights, property, personal safety of Peopleclick Authoria's employees, users and the public.
