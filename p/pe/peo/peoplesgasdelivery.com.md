What personally identifiable information is collected from me?
    On some of the Peoples Gas Web pages you can make requests and/or receive materials. The types of personal information collected at these pages include your name, e-mail address, standard contact information, including addresses and phone numbers, employer name, meter number, meter readings and banking information (including account numbers, routing numbers and other account information).

What are cookies and how are they used by Peoples Gas?
    Peoples Gas may place Internet cookies on your hard drive. Internet cookies are small files that may be placed on your hard disk for record-keeping purposes. Cookies may be used to (a) remind us of who you are in order to deliver you better service; (b) estimate our audience size by determining repeat usage of the website to help target advertisements based on user interests and behavior; and (c) measure certain traffic patterns for use as a research tool to understand how our users' habits are similar or different from one another. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it. By not accepting cookies, some Web pages may not display properly or you may not be permitted to access certain information.

  Peoples Gas may also collect IP addresses (the Internet address of a computer) to track a user's session while the user remains anonymous. Peoples Gas analyzes this data for certain trends in statistics, such as which parts of our site users are visiting and how long they spend there. In general, Peoples Gas does not link your IP addresses to anything personally identifiable to you.

How is my information used?
    Peoples Gas will not sell, rent or lease your personally identifiable information to others without your consent. However, by accessing this website, you acknowledge that your information may be shared among our affiliated companies and with our agents, contractors or business partners. When you submit personal information to Peoples Gas, you are agreeing to permit Peoples Gas to access, store and use the information wherever in the world Peoples Gas does business. In particular, Peoples Gas, its agents, contractors and business partners may use your personal information for various business purposes, such as statistical analysis, doing market research, improving services, supporting your customer or other relationship with Peoples Gas and notifying you about services and changes that may affect you, among other business purposes, in an effort to better serve you.

  Accordingly, Peoples Gas may send you direct mailings or contact you by email or telephone about your use of the website, our various products and services or other products and services we feel may be of interest to you.

  From time to time, Peoples Gas may conduct research on our users' demographics, interests and behavior based on the information provided to us during your use of the Peoples Gas website. This research is compiled and analyzed on an aggregated basis. Aggregated data does not include personally identifiable information. Peoples Gas may share this aggregated data with others.

Who is collecting my information?
    When you are on a Peoples Gas website and are asked for personal information, you are sharing that information with only Peoples Gas and its affiliates, unless specifically stated otherwise. If you do not want your data to be shared, you can choose not to allow the transfer by not using that particular service or purchasing those particular goods.

  Please be aware that Peoples Gas advertisers or third party websites (including CheckFree) that have links on our site may collect personally identifiable information about you. The information practices of those websites linked to Peoples Gas, either through advertisements or other hyperlinks, are not covered by this privacy statement. Peoples Gas does not assume responsibility for the actions of other parties.

With whom does Peoples Gas share my information?
    We may share the personal data you provide with other Peoples Gas agents, contractors or our business partners for the purposes of performing services for Peoples Gas. We also believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of the Terms of Use, or as otherwise required by law or requested by any government authority. Except as provided above or in the event of a merger, consolidation, or sale of all or substantially all of our assets, we will not share any of your information with any third party without your permission.

What else should I know about my privacy?
    Third party Internet sites and services accessible through Peoples Gas have separate privacy and data collection practices. Peoples Gas has no responsibility or liability for these independent policies or actions of such third parties. Please be careful and responsible online. If you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return. While we strive to protect your information, Peoples Gas cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information through the transmission process and you do so at your own risk. Our websites are primarily intended for adults and not children under 18 years old. Peoples Gas will not knowingly collect or disclose personal information from minors for any purposes.
