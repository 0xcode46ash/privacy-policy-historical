# Pypestream privacy policy

We collect various kinds of information, some of which is personally identifiable and some of which is non-identifying or aggregated.

**a. Organization information**

When you create a pype on Pypestream, we collect your email address, your organization name, and password. We also collect all information provided by organizations in relation to the distribution of invitations to their users to join Pypestream as also all user information provided to us for the purposes of onboarding users.

**b. User information**

The only information we require to create your Pypestream account is a username, a valid mobile number and a password. Further information you may choose to provide to us in order to personalize and enhance your Pypestream experience is optional and provided at your discretion.

**c. Organization billing information**

We collect billing address and credit card information from organizations which is securely passed to our payment processing partners and is not stored at Pypestream.

**d. Log data**

When you use Pypestream, our servers automatically record information, including information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website or your mobile app sends when you’re using it. This log data may include your IP address, your browser type and settings, the date and time of your request, information about your browser configuration and plug-ins, language preferences and cookie data. Our cookie policy is explained in more detail below.

**e. Use device information**

In addition to log data, we may also collect information about the device you’re using Pypestream on, including what type of device it is, what operating system you’re using, device settings, unique device identifiers, and crash data.

**f. Geo-location information**

Precise GPS from mobile devices may be collected only with your permission. WiFi and IP addresses received from your browser or device may be used to determine approximate location.

**g. Pypestream usage information**

This is information about which organizations, features, content, and links you interact with within Pypestream.

**h. Pypestream communications and content**

Communications that you send and receive within Pypestream and the contents of each Stream. This includes messages, pictures, files and video, the time messages or files were sent and by whom, when or if they were seen by you, and where you received them.

**i. Information from partners or other 3rd parties**

Pypestream may receive information from partners or others that we could use to improve and enhance our product. This might be aggregate level information about which IP addresses go with which zip codes or it might be more specific information about how well an online marketing or email campaign performed.
