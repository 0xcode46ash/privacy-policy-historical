# Planoly: Policies - Visually plan and schedule your Instagram posts

_Planogram, Inc., dba Planoly (“Planoly”), is committed to keeping any and all personal information collected from those individuals that visit our website and mobile app (collectively, the “site”) and make use of our online facilities and services accurate, confidential, secure and private. Our privacy policy has been designed and created to ensure those affiliated with Planoly of our commitment and realization of our obligation not only to meet but to exceed most existing privacy standards._

This Privacy Policy Agreement shall apply to Planoly and all of its affiliated websites and mobile applications, including Stylelink.it, and thus it shall govern any and all data collection and usage thereof. Through the use of Planoly you are herein consenting to the following data procedures expressed within this agreement.

Planoly reserves the right to update and/or change the terms of our privacy policy, and as such we will post those change to our site homepage at planoly.com, so that our users and/or visitors are always aware of the type of information we collect, how it will be used, and under what circumstances, if any, we may disclose such information. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy page periodically for any changes.

**_THIS PRIVACY POLICY IS EFFECTIVE AS OF 08/24/2017._**

**COLLECTION OF INFORMATION**

This site collects various types of personally identifiable information. Specifically, we collect:

  * Voluntarily provided information which may include your name, address, email address, billing and/or credit card information etc., which may be used when you purchase.
  * Products and/or services and to deliver the services you have requested.
  * Information automatically collected when visiting our site, which may include cookies, third party tracking technologies, server logs, and shopping history.
  * Information to the site for provision of the services, including information about your brand (“User Content”).
  * Information you provide when requesting customer or technical assistance.
  * Credit card or other payment information if you make a purchase directly through Planoly or its affiliates.
  * If you are a Brand, as defined in the Stylink.it Terms of Service: 
    * Information about your goods, services, and websites used for Campaigns (as defined in the Stylelink.it Terms of Service).
    * Information about purchases, returns, and customers.
    * Payment information.
  * If you are an Influencer, as defined in the Stylelink.it Terms of Service: 
    * Information about your social media profiles and presence.
    * Payment information.
    * Information necessary to generate and track Affiliate Links (as defined in the Stylelink.it Terms of Service).



Please rest assured that this site shall only collect personal information that you knowingly and willingly provide by way of surveys, completed membership forms, and emails. It is the intent of this site to use personal information only for the purpose for which it was requested and any additional uses specifically provided on this site.

We may also gather information about the type of browser you are using, IP address or type of operating system to assist us in providing and maintaining superior quality service.

It is highly recommended and suggested that you review the privacy policies and statements of any website you choose to use or frequent as a means to better understand the way in which other websites garner, make use of and share information collected.

All photos, videos, and captions uploaded by you belongs to you. We will never use or share your content without your consent.

**USE OF INFORMATION COLLECTED**

Planoly may collect and may make use of personal information to assist in the operation of our site and to ensure delivery of the services you need and request. At times, we may find it necessary to use personally identifiable information as a means to keep you informed of other possible products and/or services that may be available to you from Planoly. Planoly may also be in contact with you with regards to completing surveys and/or research questionnaires related to your opinion of current or potential future services that may be offered.

Planoly does not now, nor will it in the future, sell, rent or lease any of our customer lists and/or names to any third parties. However, Planoly may sell, transfer or otherwise share some or all of its assets, including your personal information, in connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization or sale of assets or bankruptcy event. You may opt out of any such transfer if it materially differs from that set forth in this privacy policy.

Planoly may deem it necessary to follow websites and/or pages that our users may frequent in an effort to show what types of services and/or products may be the most popular to customers or the general public.

If you use Stylelink.it, Planoly may use your personal information to track Qualifying Purchases (as defined in the Stylelink.it Terms of Service) and your use of Affiliate Links. Planoly will also use your personal information to process payments and match Brands and Influencers. 

Planoly may only otherwise disclose your personal information, without prior notice to you, if required to do so in accordance with applicable laws and/or in a good faith belief that such action is deemed necessary or is required in an effort to:

  * Remain in conformance with any decrees, laws and/or statutes or in an effort to comply with any process which may be served upon Planoly and/or our site;
  * Maintain, safeguard and/or preserve all the rights and/or property of Planoly; and
  * Perform under demanding conditions in an effort to safeguard the personal safety of users of Planoly and/or the general public.



You grant Planoly a license to use your User Content as provided for in the Planoly Terms of Service. Please note that we may use your User Content to provide, improve, or promote Planoly’s services and the site.

**CHILDREN UNDER AGE OF 13**

Planoly does not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under the age of thirteen (13) without verifiable parental consent. If it is determined that such information has been inadvertently collected on anyone under the age of thirteen (13), we shall immediately take the necessary steps to ensure that such information is deleted from our system’s database. Anyone under the age of thirteen (13) must seek and obtain parent or guardian permission to use this site.

**UNSUBSCRIBE OR OPT-OUT**

All users and/or visitors to our site have the option to discontinue receiving communication from us and/or reserve the right to discontinue receiving communications by way of email or newsletters. To discontinue or unsubscribe to our site please send an email that you wish to unsubscribe to [support@planoly.com.](mailto:support@planoly.com?Subject=Hello%20Planoly)

**LINKS TO OTHER WEBSITES**

Our site may contain links to affiliate and other websites. Planoly does not claim nor accept responsibility for any privacy policies, practices and/or procedures of other such websites. Therefore, we encourage all users and visitors to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. The aforementioned Privacy Policy Agreement applies only and solely to the information collected by our site.

**SECURITY**

Planoly shall endeavor and shall take every precaution to maintain adequate physical, procedural and technical security with respect to our offices and information storage facilities so as to prevent any loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure or modification of the user’s personal information under our control.

The company also uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) for authentication and private communications in an effort to build users’ trust and confidence in the internet and website use by providing simple and secure access and communication of credit card and personal information.

Through the use of this site, you are hereby accepting the terms and conditions stipulated within the aforementioned Privacy Policy Agreement. If you are not in agreement with our terms and conditions, then you should refrain from further use of our sites. In addition, your continued use of our site following the posting of any updates or changes to our terms and conditions shall mean that you are in agreement and acceptance of such changes.

**REFUND POLICY**

Planoly does not offer any refunds once you have purchased a plan.

If you subscribed on our website and Android app, all plans are automatically renewed according to the billing cycle you’ve subscribed to.

If you subscribed on our iOS app, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple. Please contact Apple directly.

CANCELLATION

Cancellations can be made at any time prior to the end of your billing cycle. You will have access to use the service for the full period you have paid for. No prorated refunds are provided. Please note that neither Planoly nor Apple is able to end your subscription prior to the end of your billing cycle.

If you subscribed on our website and Android app, you can cancel your subscription by going to your billing page at [www.planoly.com/user/billing](http://www.planoly.com/user/billing).

If you subscribed on the iOS app, you can cancel by going to your iTunes subscriptions.
