# Privacy Policy - PlagiarismDetect.com

This page explains our policy regarding the information we collect about our users in the process of their use of PlagiarismDetect website (www.PlagiarismDetect.com). The information presented here applies to the information we collect about the:

  * Visitors of our web site.
  * People who use our services.



## Browsing Information We Collect

Our web server automatically collects standard information. That is your browser type, operating system and access time. The browsing information we collect and the way we collect it do not make it possible for us to identify any of the users.

The browsing information we collect is used to improve the content of our web pages and to customize the architecture of our website. We never sell or share any of the information we collect with any third parties.

We use Google Analytics to analyze the use of our website. This analytical tool uses 'cookies', which are text files placed in your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information in an anonymous form. The data generated by cookies about your use of the website is transmitted to Google. This information is then used to evaluate visitors' use of the website and to compile statistical reports on the website's activity. To find out more about cookies, including how to control and delete them, visit www.aboutcookies.org. If you would like to stop being tracked by Google Analytics, visit http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

## Information We Collect When You Use Our Services

When you start using our website, you will be asked to provide personal information in order to be able to later make payments to us. We have to know our users' personal info (e.g., full name, billing address, etc.) in order to provide the service requested. We use this information for the sole reason of providing the service requested and for other closely related purposes.

If you agree to receive news and updates (done during the registration process) from us, we will use your email address to send you important notifications pertaining to the use of our services. If you wish to receive plagiarism check results to your email, we will also use your email address.

Your email address can be used by our customer care agents only and will not be disclosed to any third party. The information we have is never sold or shared.

We outsource the payment process to 2Checkout.com. We do not have access to our users' credit card numbers. All the payment-related info is stored on www.2checkout.com. If you are concerned with your financial security, please refer to 2Checkout.com's privacy policy.

## Complaints and Requests for More Information

We always try to reach the highest standards in whatever we do. Every complaint we receive is taken very seriously. Please let us know if any of the information coming from us is unfair, misleading or inappropriate. We also welcome suggestions for improving our procedures.

This page does not provide exhaustive information on all the aspects of our collection and use of personal information. We would though be happy to provide you with any additional information that interests you. Send us your requests for additional info via the "Contact us" form.

## Access to Personal Information

*By personal information we mean the information collected during the registration process (we neither store nor need any other info from you)

We try to be as open as possible in terms of letting people know which information exactly we have stored about them. Individuals can find out if we have any of their personal information stored by making a "subject access request" under the Data Protection Act 1998. If we do hold information about you, we will:

  * tell you why we have it
  * tell you who it could be disclosed to
  * let you have a copy of it

To make a personal information request, contact our customer support via the "Contact us" form on our website.




If you do so, we will try to deal with your request without resorting to formalities. We will for instance try to provide you with the information you inquired about over the telephone.

If we do hold your personal information, you can ask us to correct any mistakes by contacting our customer support via the "Contact us" form.

## Links Disclaimer

In order to provide our users with certain information, our website provides links to local, state and federal government agencies, as well as websites of other organizations. We do not endorse or promote any of the content (e.g., viewpoints, opinions, policies, products, services, etc.) presented on the websites we link to. Once you leave our website through a link we have posted, you are subject to terms and conditions and the privacy policy of the website you have been redirected to. This applies to both private and state-owned websites. 

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy may be updated and, if it is, the changes will be implemented on this page as soon as possible. This Privacy Policy was last updated on September 26, 2011.
