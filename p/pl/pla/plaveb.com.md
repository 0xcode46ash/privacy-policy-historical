# Plaveb.com | company privacy policy statement

Plaveb is committed to protecting privacy and personal information that visitors may provide for information purposes. This information is necessary in order to provide you proper service. Member’s and Visitor’s information is only being used for particular project purposes and will not be distributed, sold or rented for any reason to third party. In the unlikely event that we choose to do so in the future with trustworthy external resources, you will be notified by email and given the opportunity to notify us not to share your personal information with any other external party.

Our site’s different registration forms requires user to provide contact and company information. We use this information to contact you back and to serve you better.

You recognize and understand that there is no obligation on you to provide Plaveb with your personal information. Also information provided by you is with your full consent and own decision. You understand that we are under no obligation to verify the accuracy of information source whomever it concerns and we assume that is provided by you with your full consensus unless notified by you regarding fallacy of particular information. You also agree that your quote information will be distributed to multiple vendors and you will be contacted by them for your needs. You also agree to provide correct information to avoid fraudulent activity.

All registered users of the plaveb.com website, by checking “I Agree” checkbox on the on User Registration or any other forms, agree to our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

  * #### Security and Data Protection

Plaveb takes every precaution to protect your personal information. When you submit or view sensitive information via our website, your information is protected, both online and offline. All financial transactions and otherwise sensitive information is protected using 128 bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology. 
  * #### Data Collection and Use

The Plaveb website is an online marketplace that directly connects service buyers with service providers (collectively "registered users"). Plaveb is the sole owner of the information collected on this website. Plaveb will provide a platform for user information exchange among different users, with registered user’s permission, in efforts to facilitating a possible working relationship among registered users. 

Service providers on the Plaveb website agree that some profile information is made accessible to buyers who conduct searches on the Plaveb database. 

Service buyers on the Plaveb Website agree that their personal as well as project information is made accessible to service providers or any other users who conduct searching on the Plaveb database.

  * #### External Links

Plaveb website may have links to other websites and affiliates. Plaveb is not responsible for the content or the privacy of these affiliated websites or any other links that redirect user to external sites.
  * #### Connectivity Data

Plaveb may log IP addresses and or end systems information for better web administration. This information is used for analysis, diagnostics and maintain the integrity of our services. 

For any questions about privacy and similar issues, please contact us at [privacy@plaveb.com](mailto:privacy@plaveb.com).
