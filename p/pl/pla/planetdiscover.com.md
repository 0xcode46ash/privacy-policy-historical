# Planet Discover - Privacy Policy

  
The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices.  
Our site's registration forms require customers and prospective customers (together called "customers") to provide us contact information (like their name and email address). Periodically, we use the information you have provided on the registration form to send you updates about our products and promotional materials. WE DO NOT SELL, TRADE, EXCHANGE OR OTHERWISE MAKE AVAILABLE ANY PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION TO ANY OTHER COMPANY OR ORGANIZATION. 

The customer information is also used to contact the customer periodically about the products and services for which the customer has signed up, as well as general information about Planet Discover. In these mailings, we may include information about product upgrades and enhancements, as well as marketing promotions from Planet Discover. 

This site contains links to other sites. Planet Discover is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. 

As part of the Planet Discover Quest product we will index websites that you provide us the URL or URLs for. We clearly identify the Planet Discover robot when we index these sites and respect the Robots Exclusion Protocol and the Robots metatags. If the owner of a site does not want us to index their site, we will respect their ownership of the site and desist from indexing their website. 

Cookies  
When you log in to access your Planet Discover account, you present your username and password. This login information is encrypted and passed from page to page as you use your Planet Discover account. Your account administrator may configure your account such that your encrypted login information is stored in a session cookie in your browser. This session cookie is stored only for your current browsing session, and is deleted from your computer when you log out or when you close your browser. Use of Planet Discover login cookies is completely at the discretion of your account administrator. 

Security  
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Our site uses firewall and encryption technology to protect your information, and our servers are located in a guarded co-location facility under 24-hour surveillance. 

Canceling Your Customer Login  
By becoming a customer of Planet Discover, you agree that we may contact you regarding your account as described above. To not receive these communications, simply send your request to [terry@planetdiscover.com](mailto:terry@planetdiscover.com). 

Correct/Update  
This site gives customers the ability to change and modify information previously provided. Please log into your account at [http://www.planetdiscover.com](http://www.planetdiscover.com/) to update your customer information. 

Contacting Planet Discover about Privacy  
Questions regarding our privacy policy should be directed to Terry Millard ([terry@planetdiscover.com](mailto:terry@planetdiscover.com)) at Planet Discover. You may also use this address to notify us that you do not wish to receive future mailings from Planet Discover.
