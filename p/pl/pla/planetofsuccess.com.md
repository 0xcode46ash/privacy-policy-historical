# Planet of Success

   
**Your Consent**  
By using our site, you consent to our privacy policy.

**What kind of informations are collected by this site?**  
Information might be collected from you when placing an order as well as filling out an form. 

**For what kind of purpose is this information used for?**  


To create an personalized experience on this homepage; To improve this website and it´s content; To process transactions  
Please note that your information will never be sold, neither exchanged nor transferred to any other company, without your expressed consent. Your information will only be transfered for the express purpose of payment to the service requested.

Will you send periodic emails?

Yes. The email address you have provided in your order, could be used to send you information and updates pertaining to your order. You may also recieve from time to time occasional news, updates and related product information´s, etc. We will always provide you the possibility to unsubscribe from receiving future emails from us. You will just have to follow the unsubscribing instructions at the bottom of the email that was being send to you.

**Are any kinds of cookies used?**  
Yes. There will be also a cookie placed for Google Analytics.  


    **Google Analytics Disclaimer:**
    This website uses Google Analytics to a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. (" Google "). Google Analytics uses "cookies", which are text files placed on your computer and allow an analysis of the use of the Site by you. The information generated by the cookie about your use of this website (including your IP address) is submitted to a Google server in the U.S. and stored there. Google will use this information to evaluate your use of the website, compiling reports on website activity for website operators and providing other use of the website and Internet services. Google will transmit this information to third parties unless required by law or if third parties process this data on Google's behalf. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however, we point out that you can use in this case not all features of this website. By using this site you agree to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner described and for the aforementioned purpose. 

**Will any information be disclosured to outside parties?**  
We do not sell, trade, exchange, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information. Never. Your information will only be transfered for the express purpose of payment of the service you have requested.

**Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance**  
We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.
