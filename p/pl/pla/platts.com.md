# Platts

Privacy 

|   
---  
Contacting us via email or by registering with Platts under our free email alerts, subscribe and checkout areas, reveals your email address and any other contact information (such as phone number or postal address) you provide through this site. Our site uses cookies to customize your experience on this site. Cookies are data placed in the hard drive of your computer to determine whether your computer has accessed this site before and to allow us to provide you with further information in accordance with your stated preferences. These cookies are permanent. We will use your contact information (just the contact information not information collected through cookies), which will be maintained in a secure database in Hightstown, New Jersey in the US, to help us process your order, service your account and respond to your inquiries. 

We may also make your contact information available to other members of the McGraw-Hill family of companies or to reputable business information companies, so that they can inform you about products and services that may interest you. If you do not want your contact information used as set out in this paragraph, simply click the appropriate box(es) below and submit the completed form.
