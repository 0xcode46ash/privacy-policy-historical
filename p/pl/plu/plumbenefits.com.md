# Plum Benefits - Privacy Policy

To put it plainly, we will not sell, rent or share your personal information with non-affiliated third-parties. If you have any other questions or for details of how we may use your information, please read the below.   
________________________________________  
Plum Benefits, LLC ("PLUM BENEFITS") takes your privacy very seriously. Please read the following to learn more about PLUM BENEFITS' Privacy Policy ("Policy"). This Policy details how PLUM BENEFITS receives, collects and uses information in connection with its website ("Site"). 

Collection and Use of Information   
PLUM BENEFITS collects and stores information from you in several ways on different parts of the Site.

Information You Provide:

When you register on the Site, you will be asked to provide personally identifiable information, which may include your name, email address, address, telephone number(s), login name, login password and other self-identifying information (all "Personal Information"), as well as certain other non-personally identifiable information, such as age, gender and areas of interest.  In addition, PLUM BENEFITS may also collect information about the searches you perform, promotional offers you choose and the pages you visit on the Site. PLUM BENEFITS collects Personal Information primarily for the following purposes: (i) to maintain a record of registered users, (ii) to email registered users with news and updates regarding entertainment offers and promotions."  To discontinue receiving any email promotional offers directly from PLUM BENEFITS, just follow the instructions in an email you receive from us. 

PLUM BENEFITS may also make certain message boards and forums available to you (collectively, "Forums"). You are solely responsible for the information and other content you post on and through these Forums and should be aware that when you voluntarily disclose personal information (e.g., name, e-mail address, telephone number) on or through these Forums, such information is generally accessible to, and may be collected and used by, other users.  PLUM BENEFITS does not exercise control over any users or Site visitors, and is in no way responsible, and has not liability whatsoever, for any collection or use of information you may disclose through the Forums. This Privacy Policy does not cover such disclosure of information. You are encouraged to exercise discretion when providing personal information about yourself in and through Forums.

Information We Automatically Collect:

PLUM BENEFITS automatically receives and records certain non-personally identifiable information on its server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookie information, and the webpage that you visit. PLUM BENEFITS collects non-personally identifiable and demographic information for general purposes, including but not limited to increasing the relevance of the Site to our users, and customizing the advertising, promotional messages and content you see, as well as conducting research, and providing anonymous reporting to internal and external clients. Our server logs do not record any Personal Information.  
Cookies and in the Keep Me Logged In Feature:  
Unless you choose to opt-out of our "Keep Me Logged In" feature or use the "log out" button to exit the Site, PLUM BENEFITS will use a cookie to enable the Site to recognize you as a registered user and automatically log you in to the Site when you return.  This cookie, set on your computer hard drive, contains a unique identifier that is used to match your future visits with your account and login on the Site.  However, the cookie itself does not contain any Personal Information.  This cookie has no expiration date.  You may opt-out of this feature by accessing "My Personal Profile" under the "My Profile" tab when you are logged into your account, clicking "Edit My Account Information," changing the "Keep Me Logged In?" setting from "yes" to "no,"  and clicking "submit."  You can also opt-out of this feature every time you leave the Site by clicking on the "log out" button before you close your browser.   This feature can be handy if you are the only person using your computer to access this Site.  We recommend opting out of this feature with respect to a specific computer if you are not the only person who uses that computer.  
If you choose not to accept this cookie from our Site, which you can do by adjusting the settings on your browser, you will still be able to use the Site, but will have to login manually each time you visit. However, if you set your browser not to accept session cookies from our Site, you will be unable to use the Site at all until you allow session cookies.   
Our Use of Your Personal Information:  
We may use your Personal Information to send directly to you email and other communications with PLUM BENEFITS announcements, special offers and other promotional information. Out of respect for your privacy, we provide you with an option to discontinue receiving such promotional emails directly from PLUM BENEFITS by following the instructions in any promotional email you receive from us.  
In addition, we will occasionally need to use your Personal Information to communicate with you by email in response to your inquiries, to provide the services you request, to manage your account and to inform you of service related to our Site operations.  You will continue to receive service announcements and responses to your inquiries even if you opt out of receiving our promotional emails. 

Sharing and Disclosure of Information   
PLUM BENEFITS does not rent, sell, or share Personal Information with other non-affiliated third-parties. PLUM BENEFITS may disclose Personal Information under the following circumstances:   
1. PLUM BENEFITS may provide Personal Information to trusted third-parties who work on behalf of or with PLUM BENEFITS. PLUM BENEFITS attempts to ensure that such companies agree to maintain confidentiality, security and integrity of the Personal Information obtained from PLUM BENEFITS. These companies do not have any independent right to share the Personal Information.  
2. PLUM BENEFITS may disclose, sell or transfer Personal Information in connection with an assignment, sale, joint venture, or other corporate transfer or disposition of some or all of PLUM BENEFITS' assets.  
3. At times PLUM BENEFITS may be required by law or litigation to disclose your Personal Information. We may also disclose information about you if we determine that for national security, law enforcement, or other issues of public importance that disclosure of information is necessary.

  
Children's Privacy   
Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. We operate our website in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). PLUM BENEFITS does not knowingly, directly or indirectly, collect or maintain Personal Information at or on our website from children under the age of 13. No part of the Site is directed at or structured to attract anyone under 13.

Security   
PLUM BENEFITS takes reasonable precaution to protect Personal Information from misuse, loss and unauthorized access. Although PLUM BENEFITS cannot guarantee that Personal Information will not be subject to unauthorized access, PLUM BENEFITS has physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the Personal Information, including 128-bit Verisign Secure Socket Layered Encryption (SSL). Personal Information is protected by secured networks and access is limited to a few authorized employees and personnel.

Changes to Policy  
From time to time, PLUM BENEFITS may revise this Policy. PLUM BENEFITS will post the revised Policy on the Site, so that you can always be aware of what information PLUM BENEFITS collects, how the information is used and under what circumstances such information may be disclosed.  
Other Websites  
PLUM BENEFITS may provide links to various third-party websites from the Site. PLUM BENEFITS has no control over these linked sites, each of which has a separate privacy and data collection practice, independent of PLUM BENEFITS. As such, PLUM BENEFITS is not responsible for, or has no control over, the content or the privacy policies of these third-party websites. PLUM BENEFITS encourages you to read the privacy policies of each and every website that you visit when linking from the PLUM BENEFITS' website. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected by PLUM BENEFITS.

Contact Information  
Please direct all questions in connection with this Policy via email to [contact@plumbenefits.com](mailto:contact@plumbenefits.com) or by writing to Plum Benefits, LLC, Attention: Privacy Policy Administrator, 535 8th Avenue, 9th Floor, New York, NY 10018.
