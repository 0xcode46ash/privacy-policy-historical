# Passion for Football Manager: Privacy Policy

## Your Privacy is important for Passion for Football Manager

Welcome to [Passion for Football Manager](http://www.mypassion4footballmanager.com/), a blog aiming to make you succeed in the world of Football Manager 2014. Passion4FM is committed to respect the privacy rights and concerns for all our visitors. In the modern world of technology your privacy have become more and more important, and this is something we hold in high regard. 

This privacy policy document for Passion4FM, hereby referred to "we", "us", "our" or just "the website", defines what type of information that is collected by us to maintain and provide our unique Football Manager guides and articles. This **Privacy Policy** is effective as of the date set below and is only applicable to the website and not to any other website that you may be able to access from us. These third party companies may have its own data collection, practices and policies. 

In this document we will provide you the answers of these questions: 

\- What kind of information do we collect?   
\- How do we use information about you?   
\- Will personal information be sold/shared with third parties such as advertises and marketing agencies? 

### Cookies - What are they?

A cookie is a small text file which is sent to your computer or mobile device by the webserver so that the website can remember some information about your browsing activity on the website. The cookies will collect information regarding your use of our websites, information about your device such as the device’s technology, your demographic data and, if you arrived at our website from a referring link, the URL of the linking page. You can read more about the different [types of cookies](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTTP_cookie) here. 

#### What kind of information do we collect?

Our website is free to use and can be visited anonymously. You will never need to register to download content, read our Football Manager guides or engage in the vibrant community. We will put much effort to keep it this way, as our main target is to provide you with great guides and tutorials for free. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. We take children’s privacy seriously, and encourage parents to play an active role in their children’s online experience at all times. 

Like many other websites www.myPassion4footballmanager.com do use log files to gather information of your visits. When you visit our website the information gathered within the log files will contain basic information about your behavior, your technology and demographics. In short we will gather information about your; 

\- web browser (Google Chrome, Firefox, Linux or other OS   
\- Internet Service provider (ISP)   
\- country of origin / language   
\- date and time for your visits   
\- visited pages, number of clicks / referring and exit pages to analyze trends 

We do not gather information about your IP (internet protocol) as long as you only visit our pages. We do collect personal identifiable information about you only when you provide it directly to us. We will ask for personal information such as your name and e-mail. Along with the sending in of the form to our services we will get information about your internet protocol (IP). This is done merely to protect us from unprofessional people who wish to spam us. When using the comment field to interact with the fan community on our website, is a matter of third party policies. 

#### How do we use / store information about you? 

The information gathered by us is for our personal use only; maintaining records of the visitors is the sole purpose of acquiring information. The information may, however, be used to bring awareness among our users and providing them with the latest happenings and updates, and provide them with more of their favorite content. 

We do not store any information about your visit to our Football Manager blog other than to analyze and optimize your content and reading experience through the use of cookies. You can turn off the use of cookies at anytime by changing your specific browser settings. You can read more about [how to enable/disable cookies](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/manage-cookies/) in this great tutorial. 

#### Cookies and Third Parties

Some of the third parties we do use, may use cookies and web beacons. The third parties includes, but are not restricted to Google Analytics and Google Adsense. We may occasionally, with the highest discretion include or offer third party products or services. 

We will not disclose your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes when you have not opted into receiving such third party offers and/or newsletters through the website. These third parties will automatically receive your IP address. The advertising networks and ad-servers will use technology on advertises and links on our website to measure effectiveness and personalize the advertising content for better user experience. 

Passion4FM has no access to or control over the use of cookies and storage of personal information created by third parties. If you decide to click on advertises or third party logos displayed on our website, which links to a different website, our privacy policy will no longer apply. You must then read the privacy policy of the advertiser to see how your personal information will be handled on their website. 

**More about Third-Party advertisements**

We also use third party cookies and web beacons in order to support our website through displaying advertisements from our affiliate partners and Google. Third party vendors, including [Google Adsense](), uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website. 

**Doubleclick Cookies**  
Google's use of the [DoubleClick cookie](http://www.google.com/doubleclick/) enables it and its partners to serve ads to you, based on your previously browsing history, and target ads which is of your personal interest, by placing a cookie on your computer. This cookie will not track personal information about you (your name, email address, street address, phone number, passwords or credit card number. 

You can read more about the [DoubleClick cookie used by Google adsense program](https://support.google.com/adsense/answer/2839090) here. 

**How to Disable Ads-serving cookies**  
You may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting Ads Settings. (Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for interest based advertising by visiting aboutads.info.) Deleting the doubleclick cookie is does not mean you are permanently opted out of any advertising program as a new cookie will be added. You can however disallow cookies by settings, the next time you visit a site running the ads. 

"Passion for Football Manager" has the right to amend its privacy policy to suit the needs of the website. This privacy policy is subject to change without notice. Any changes or modifications will be posted on this page. 

Our privacy policy was _last updated and modified October 14th 2013_.
