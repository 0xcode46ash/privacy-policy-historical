# Page-A-Day Calendars || Workman Publishing

WORKMAN PUBLISHING COMPANY, INC. provides this Web site as a service to its customers. By using Web sites owned and operated by Workman Publishing, you are agreeing to comply with the following terms of use. Workman Publishing is committed to respecting the privacy of all visitors to its Web sites: 

**What information does Workman ask for and how is it used?**  
When you register for your digital calendar you will be asked to provide certain information, such as your e-mail address and your ZIP code. We ask for this in connection with registration, future updates, and promotions. Workman and other agents of Workman will use this information to tailor presentations to you, to aid your download, and to provide personalized services. PAGE-A-DAY® DIGITAL has additional terms of use and licensing found in the README at installation. By installing PAGE-A-DAY® DIGITAL CALENDARS, user agrees to the terms as described. 

**Do I get to choose what information I disclose?**  
Yes, you can always choose whether or not to disclose information. Certain information is required for registration, but other requested information is optional and may be given at your discretion. We do not use "cookies" on any of our sites. We keep track of certain statistical information that helps us improve our site and understand our demographics. 

**Who has access to my personally identifiable information?**  
We use information that we collect to make our Web sites more productive. We require certain specific information when you elect to download your Digital Calendar or sign-up to be a Beta Tester. We store that information in secure databases. We may choose to share information, especially aggregate data, with companies who provide content to our Web sites and products. They are not given access to our secure database. 

**Will my information be shared with others?**  
Information provided through Workman Publishing's Web sites is not shared with companies or others who engage in marketing or promotion without your permission. We may however choose to share aggregate information to help define our visitors and thus enhance their future experiences at our Web sites. 

**Who can I contact if I have any questions regarding my privacy?**  
Should you have continued concerns about your privacy or that of your children, please feel free to contact: 

[info@workman.com](mailto:info@workman.com)
