# PayGol

This page contains PayGol's policies regarding collection, use and disclosure of users' personal information as provided to our platform.

By using our services, you agree to this policy.

Collection of information

While using our platform we may ask you to provide personal information such as (but not limited to) your name, email address, telephone number and/or other.

Payouts may require the disclosure of personal information to third party financial entities in charge of issuing them (e.g. account number and account holder name).

We may use your personal information to send you newsletters, marketing or promotional materials. We may also use this information to contact you in order to provide support.

We might use cookies to collect specific information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

Cookies could get stored in your computer. You can refuse the use of cookies; however, this may limit access and functionality of certain parts of our website.

We strive to protect your personal information. We will not share your information, except for very specific cases such as transaction disputes and providers/legal requests.

Our privacy policy may change in the future. The most recent version will normally be available at this webpage. Notable updates might also be published elsewhere on our website or notified through email.
