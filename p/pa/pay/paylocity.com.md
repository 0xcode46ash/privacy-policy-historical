# Paylocity - America's Leading Independent Payroll Processor

We collect personal information on our web product to better serve the client. This can include: e-mail address, street address, telephone numbers, etc. All information is secured using industry standard encryption, firewalls, and site monitoring as well as physical security to protect the user. 

Paylocity clients' privacy is important to us. We guard all private information that is requested. All information is used for Paylocity. We share this information with our business partners if requested by our clients and only for its intended use. We do not sell or rent any private information collected from our web sites to any party. Paylocity may provide statistics on website usage, but will never include private client information. 

Paylocity may disclose information gathered if deemed necessary to: 

  * Conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process;
  * Protect and defend our rights and/or property;
  * Protect the interests of our users.



Paylocity's web sites use session "cookies". A cookie is information stored on your computer browser. Cookies enable the site to lock-on to your session and allow faster, easier, and a safer web site experience. 

All web sites use cookies differently. Paylocity does not record private information in cookies. All information is stored on our protected systems and servers. We only use session cookies which expire when you leave the web site, logoff, or after fifteen minutes of idle time. 

If you have voluntarily provided private information, Paylocity may send you mail or e-mail regarding our products and services. If you do not wish to receive such offers and mailings, you can remove yourself by checking the appropriate box on the submission form. 

Paylocity cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or receive from us, especially from e-mail transactions. We have limited ways of protecting that information until it reaches us. All transactions outgoing are protected using highly effective encryption to the client. 

Paylocity employees are granted access to client's private data only if it is needed to perform their job. All employees go through a background check and are obligated to adhere to our privacy policies. 

While using any Paylocity website or service, never divulge your username or password to anyone. Always use letters and numbers in your passwords. At no time will we ever ask you for your password in an unsolicited phone call or e-mail. Do not ever print your username and password. 

Paylocity.com contains links to our business partners. We only link to websites we trust, but, we are not responsible for the privacy practices employed by other sites, or the content thereof. 

Visit this page regularly as we may change this Privacy Policy as we improve any of our services.
