# Patagonia Customer Service: Privacy/Security

|  We collect your contact information (like email address) and financial information (like credit card numbers). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders and information about our company to you. You may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the _"How to remove your name from our mailing or email list"_ section below. Financial information that is collected is used to bill you for products and services.  
---  
A cookie is a small file that a Web site puts on your hard drive so that it can remember something about you at a later time. Cookies make it easier for you to use our site. 

We use a cookie in order to retain the contents in your shopping cart while you are checking out other areas on our site. When you are done, the gear in your cart is still there for you to buy.   
  
If you register for email or if you provide your email address during the check-out process, we will send you Patagonia emails. These emails will inform you of our launch of new products, of special sales, of environmental issues we think are important and of events taking place in our stores near you. You might receive one or two emails from us in a month. If you currently receive our emails and do not wish to continue receiving them, please see the _"How to remove your name from our mailing or email list"_ section below.  
We use your mailing address to ship your products to you, verify your billing information and to send catalogs to you. We do not share our customer names or addresses with anyone outside of Patagonia. If you do not wish to receive catalogs from us, please see the _"How to remove your name from our mailing or email list"_ section below.

Ordering online at Patagonia.com is safe and easy! Protecting your order information is a priority, so we use VeriSign's Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology. SSL encrypts ALL of your personal information including credit card number name and address to avoid the decoding of that information by anyone other than Patagonia. If you are using a Netscape browser, check the security of your connection by looking at the lower left-hand corner of your browser window after accessing the server. For Internet Explorer, check the little boxes near the lower right-hand corner. If you see an unbroken key or a closed lock (depending upon your browser), then SSL is active. Please refer to the following chart to see which security symbols are used in you browser.

You can also double-check by looking at the URL line of your browser. When accessing a secure server, the first characters of the site address will change from "e;http"e; to "e;https"e;. 

We have implemented the strongest security available for Patagonia.com from VeriSign. VeriSign software enables the use of digital certificates which confirm that your browser in communicating securely with Patagonia.com. You may click on the VeriSign logo to the right to verify that your browser is communicating securely with Patagonia.com. 

Some versions of browsers and some firewalls don't permit communication through secure servers. If you are unable to connect to a secure server or would rather place your order by phone, please call us at 1-800-638-6464.
