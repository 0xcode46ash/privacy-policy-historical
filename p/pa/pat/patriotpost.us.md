# PatriotPost.US Privacy Policy

The Patriot Post, which operates the Internet sites located at [PatriotPost.US](http://patriotpost.us/), [PatriotShop.US](http://patriotshop.us/), [PatriotPetitions.US](http://patriotpetitions.us/) and [Reagan2020.US](http://reagan2020.com/), recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of personally identifiable information collected from visitors to our sites and our subscribers. **The Patriot Post does not release any information on our users or subscriber to any third party under any circumstances.** No identifiable information is automatically collected from visitors to our Web sites. We may collect certain non-personal information to optimize our Web pages for your use. We may use such non-personal information for internal purposes, including but not limited to improving the content of our sites.

The Patriot Post has created these Web sites for your personal information and education. However, you are only authorized to access this Web site or to use the materials contained in the site if you agree to abide by all applicable laws, and our [Subscriber/User Terms and Conditions of Use](https://web.archive.org/info/terms_of_use.php), which constitutes an Agreement between you and The Patriot Post. The Patriot Post reserves the right to modify or amend this Agreement without notice at any time. 

**REPRINT AND FORWARD POLICY:** We endeavor to advance a worldview based on timeless conservative principles. We don't claim to own them! Thus, subscribers may reprint or forward The Patriot Post, in whole or part, with appropriate citation ["The Patriot Post" and/or "(PatriotPost.US)"] in accordance with "fair use" rules and our [Subscriber/User Terms and Conditions of Use](https://web.archive.org/info/terms_of_use.php).
