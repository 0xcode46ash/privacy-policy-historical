# Pat's Papers - Today's Headlines

**PRIVACY POLICY**

We do not sell your personal information to outside parties.

The only identifiable information we collect at this Web site is information you choose to submit, such as when you comment on content or subscribe to a content feed or our update newsletter. We use the information only for the intended purpose. Even when content is ad supported, the advertiser is only privy to broad demographic information.

If you opt-in to receive content by e-mail or other subscription the e-mail will be sent by us. (Note that we may sometimes use a third-party service to transmit a message to you, but your information remains within our control.)
