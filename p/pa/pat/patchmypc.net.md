# Privacy Policy | Patch My PC

### Privacy Policy

Patch My PC is committed to user privacy in all our products and services.

This Privacy Policy informs you how we collect and use the information provided while on our website or using our apps.

### What Information Do We Collect?

  * Website usage when browsing our site
    * We do use Google analytics: [How Google uses data when you use our partners’ sites or apps](http://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/partners/)
  * Forum registration
  * Newsletter registration
  * Patch My PC Home Updater
    * We will make an HTTP web client request on start. This will include the OS and Patch My PC Updater version number.



### Uses Made Of Collected Information

We solely use information to better our products and services. We will never solicit any information you provide us to any external companies.
