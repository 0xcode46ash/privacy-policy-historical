# PanelPlace - Maximize Your Survey Earnings

  
_(Last updated: 7 Aug 2008)_

PanelPlace is operated by YourSay.com (established since 1997) who has paid many of its 400,000+ members globally. We work only with reputable research companies worldwide to bring you surveys that REALLY pay.

**Your privacy is important**

We respect the privacy of every member. This Privacy policy outlines the information we will collect and how we will use that information.

We've developed this Privacy policy from industry guidelines and standards, and local, national, and international laws and requirements. All privacy practices and methods described in this policy apply only insofar as permitted by the applicable standards, laws and requirements. We routinely review our privacy and security practices and continually enhance them to protect your privacy.

**Information Collected**

You may visit our Web site anonymously. Personal information is not collected about you on the Web Site unless you voluntarily submit it.

Anonymously aggregated information about your visit to the Web Site may be collected via a "cookie". A cookie is a piece of computer code that records information such as pages visited and referring Web Sites. You may set the settings on your Internet browser to reject the use of cookies. We caution, however, that by doing so the Web Site may lose some of its functionality.

PanelPlace does not sell your personal information. Personal information is held strictly confidential.

**Use of Information**

The information you provide to us through this Web site is used by PanelPlace to operate this Web site, to provide you with more information and to possibly communicate with you. We may, for example, use the information you provide to us to target ads or send e-mails to you including opt-in reminders and Web site alerts. Your information may also be used to contact you if necessary, such as in the case of assisting you with usage of this Web site or replying your query from our support helpdesk. You may opt-out of receiving our reminders at any time.

We use anonymous data collected from the Web Site to improve the content, layout and usability of the Web Site.

**Third Parties**

As part of the services offered on the Web Site, you may access third-party Web Sites via hyperlinks. The privacy policies of those parties are likely to differ from this ours. PanelPlace is not responsible for the actions or policies of such third parties. We recommend that you review the policies posted on those Web sites before submitting any personal information.

**Security**

We are dedicated to the security of your personally identifiable information, and we take every commercially reasonable precaution to maintain that protection.

**Policy Changes**

We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time.
