# Privacy Statement | ParkVia

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The statement below outlines our practices:

We will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998 (UK). 

All the personal details that ParkVia collects are used only to process your booking with your chosen car park. Your data will therefore be disclosed to your chosen car park (a third party).

The personal information which we hold will be done so securely in accordance with our internal security policy and the law.   
We will not collect excessive information and we will not add you to our e-mail mailing list in unless you have given your consent. 

The type of information we will collect about you includes: 

  * Full name
  * Telephone number
  * E-mail address
  * Vehicle registration number



We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent. The information we hold will be accurate and up to date as far as is possible. We do not store any credit or debit card details. These details are only taken at the time of payment and the process is completed using SSL encryption. We use Google Analytics to collect information about how you use our webiste, helping us to improve it. Google Analyitics uses cookies to do this and all data collected is anonymous. Google's privacy policy can be found at ["http://www.google.com/privacy.html"](http://www.google.com/privacy.html) or should you have any question about your privacy please [Contact](http://www.parkvia.com/en-GB).
