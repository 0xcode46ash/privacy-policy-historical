# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

**Customer Name & Addresses**

Along with many other mail order companies we occasionally make our customer lists (name & address only) available to other carefully selected companies so that they can mail you with information on products or services that may be of interest to you. If you prefer not to receive these mailings please tick the opt-out box on the checkout page or phone or e-mail us at anytime and we will adjust our records accordingly.

Party Pieces like to keep its customers informed of the latest news and offers via its website, catalogues, email newsletters and customer service team.

**Email Addresses**  

**Party Pieces will not pass on your email address to any third party for marketing purposes** *

Party Pieces only sends emails to people with whom they have had contact through one or more of the following routes:

• |    | Items were purchased from Party Pieces via the website, telephone or by post  
---|---|---  
• |    | Party Pieces or First Birthdays catalogues were requested  
• |    | An account was created on the Party Pieces website  
• |    | An email address was entered into the newsletter sign-up box or entry page on the Party Pieces website  
• |    | Party Pieces has rented email addresses from a registered list broker where people have voluntarily submitted their email addresses to receive offers from third parties  
• |    | You have entered a competition via our website  
• |    | *Unless part of a competition entry's terms and conditions which are detailed on each entry form  
  
  
If you have received a newsletter from Party Pieces and believe that none of the above applies to you and would like to stop receiving these newsletters, you can do this in two ways:

Click the link at the bottom of the email you have received and you will be unsubscribed automatically or,

Send an email to emarketing@partypieces.co.uk with unsubscribe in the subject line and we will manually add you to our unsubscribe list.

Privacy Statement

Party Pieces is committed to protecting your privacy and this statement sets out how we store and use information relating to you.

For your protection we are registered under the Data Protection Act 1998 and have given all appropriate notifications to the Information Commissioner.

When you use our site and have not advised us to the contrary you are agreeing that we may use your personal information for the purposes set out in this statement.

Any changes to our privacy policy will be posted on this site so that you may ensure that you are fully informed of how we are using your data and can notify us of any changes to your preferences.

The Information we need and how we use it  
  
When you order with us, we ask for personal information such as your name and address, email address, telephone number and payment card details. This information allows us to process and fulfil your order and to send you confirmation of the details of the order and its despatch.

You also have the opportunity of providing us with your children’s dates of birth and this is used along with your name and address to send you a catalogue, newsletters and an email reminder in good time for the next birthday. Dates of birth are never disclosed to anyone outside Party Pieces

Unless you state otherwise (opt out) we may from time to time share your name and postal address for direct marketing purposes with other companies whose products or services we believe to be of interest to you.

Your email address and children’s dates of birth will never be disclosed to anyone outside Party Pieces. The same applies to your telephone number although with your permission this may be printed on the delivery label of your parcel to help the courier in delivering to you.

Safety and Security of your Information

Our site uses secure server software (SSL) to protect your information whenever you place an order or access your account information. This software encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us.

We also follow a tight security procedure as required under UK Data Protection Legislation (the Data Protection Act 1998) to protect the information that we store about you from unauthorised access.

Use of Cookies

Our cookies do not contain any personal information about you and are used only to determine your browser and user preferences for our site. We use cookies to simplify the shopping process and to enhance the online experience for you. They are also used to analyse data about the way our website is used allowing us to improve our site and tailor it to our customers' needs. To use our site cookies need to be enabled. Please visit our [cookie information](http://www.partypieces.co.uk/cookies/) page to find out more.

Your personal data

If, for any reason, you are unsure about the personal and account information we are holding in your name, please contact our Customer Service team. They will happily review your file and update the records if required. You can contact our customer service team by email, phone or fax.

If you wish to change your existing preferences on receiving offers and other marketing information from Party Pieces please see below:

  * If you do not wish to receive further emails and newsletters from Party Pieces please email [emarketing@partypieces.co.uk](mailto:emarketing@partypieces.co.uk)
  * If you do not want to receive any further catalogue mailings from Party Pieces please email [sales@partypieces.co.uk](mailto:sales@partypieces.co.uk) giving your full name and address



If you do not wish to receive any marketing information and/or offers from carefully selected third parties whose products we think may be of interest to you then please email [sales@partypieces.co.uk](mailto:sales@partypieces.co.uk) giving your full name and address.
