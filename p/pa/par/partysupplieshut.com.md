**Privacy Policy:**

Your personal information is private and secure. Our secure server protects all information shared between us by using advanced encryption from VeriSign and by using high level firewall technology.

We will not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. When you purchase items on our site, we request your name, e-mail address, credit card billing details, shipping addresses, and telephone number so that we can process your order.

You can check the shipping status of your order by logging in to a secure site to review your order history and carrier tracking information. We also provide you information via e-mail with messages for the services you request while on our site.
