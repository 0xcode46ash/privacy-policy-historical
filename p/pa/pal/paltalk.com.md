# PalTalk - Privacy Policy

> **Please be aware that, subject to the terms of the PalTalk Software License Agreement, PalTalk Software, Inc. adheres to the following privacy policies:**
> 
> PalTalk will use its efforts expeditiously to execute a request by a user to terminate its account. 
> 
> PalTalk uses the information you provide us to inform you of new services and offerings from PalTalk and to provide you marketing and promotional offers from advertisers. We will share only aggregate and non-personally identifiable information with advertisers for marketing and promotional purposes. We sometimes provide your information to carefully selected partners for the purpose of sending you email messages. At PalTalk Software, we strive to protect our user's privacy. 
> 
> PalTalk Software, Inc. will never ask for your personal information by voice via the PalTalk program. Any communication from PalTalk Software, Inc. will take the form of an email. 
> 
> All invitations to PalTalk Audio Chat are initiated by our existing users. If one of our users has intentionally sent you an email without knowing you, this is expressly in violation of our acceptable use policy. Please let us know so that we may investigate that user incident and take appropriate measures. 
> 
> PalTalk will always provide you the opportunity to unsubscribe from future communications or to no longer receive our service. An unsubscribe link is provided in each email message we or our selected partners send out. To unsubscribe please click on the link and follow the procedures described. 
> 
> *Please feel free to [contact us](mailto:privacy@paltalk.com) with any problems you may have. We will do our best to solve them! (Please note: this email should not be used for general customer support requests, [click here](https://web.archive.org/web/20021215143804id_/http%3A//www.paltalk.com/paltalk10/Support/titlepage.htm) for customer support)
> 
> **Third Party Advertising**   
>  Some of the ads appearing on this service are delivered to you by DoubleClick, our Web advertising partner. Information about your visit to this site, such as number of times you have viewed an ad (but not your name, address, or other personal information), is used to serve ads to you on this service. For more information about DoubleClick, cookies, and how to "opt-out", please [click here](http://www.doubleclick.net/us/corporate/privacy). 
> 
> **Third Party Cookies**   
>  In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser.
