# PS3Maven Privacy Policy | Ps3 Maven

**Commitment To Privacy**

Your privacy is very important to us! So your privacy protected, we provide the following information to detail our Online practices and how information is collected.

**Information Collected**

This applies to all the information that is collected or provided at PS3Maven.com site. Users, readers, and subscribers may provide comments, tips, ideas and additional feedback which may include their Name, Email, Website and more.

**Information Used**

Information collected voluntarily as mentioned above is only used to display within comments and is not shared with outside parties. We do not share identifying information with advertisers and partners. For example, advertisers may be informed that a certain amount of readers visit a specific post, area, or section of the site, but we do not provide personal information.

Lastly, personal identifiable information that was provided to us is not shared in other means not described above without allowing readers to opt out.

**Information Security**

All information provided at our site is secured from unauthorized access, as to secure personal information provided by our visitors.

**Correction of Data**

If you would like to correct any information submitted by you, or you wish any information provided by you to be removed, you may contact us at [contact@ps3maven.com](mailto:contact@ps3maven.com "Contact PS3Maven"), with your request or inquiry, and we would correct or amend the specific item. In order to reduce errors and retain security, we may follow reasonable steps to in fact verify your identity.

**Contact Us**

If you have any questions or inquries regarding our Privacy Policy, please send us an email at [contact@ps3maven.com](mailto:contact@ps3maven.com "Contact PS3Maven").
