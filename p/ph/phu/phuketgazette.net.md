# Privacy policy

Privacy policy

The Phuket Gazette does not use e-mail to solicit business, nor does it sell (or give away) names, e-mail addresses or other information out of its databases without the express permission of the companies or individuals involved.

Our site's registration form requires users (“Gazetteers”) to give us contact information. This is used internally to determine the identity of parties placing classified ads in the Gazette's online classifieds, entering (or modifying) listings in the Gazette Guide, or placing events on our Phuket Events Calendar.

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, to manage our website, and to gather broad demographic information. We use cookies to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit us.

  
  
**Unsubscribe / Opt Out  
**   
Gazetteers may ‘unsubscribe’ at any time, or simply opt out of e-mailings previously requested (e.g., daily news, bulletins, etc). 

To unsubscribe, see <http://www.phuketgazette.net/users/users_setting>. To remain enrolled as a Gazetteer but opt out of e-mail services, (or to modify a password or other information previously submitted), visit <http://www.phuketgazette.net/users/users_setting>.

 **Contacting the Web Site**

Should you have any questions about our privacy policy or practices, kindly contact us [here](http://www.phuketgazette.net/contact-us).
