# Privacy Policy ~ Phnoy - Pinoy TV Online

[](http://www.phnoy.com/2010/10/privacy.html)

Privacy at PHnoy.com

We, [**PHnoy.com,**](http://www.phnoy.com/) recognize that you, viewers, care about your personal information and how that is used. We work hard to provide transparency when you visit, watch, and view PHnoy.com. If you have concerns and question about PHnoy.com use or about the disclosure of your personal information, please contact us through email at jvadmin@phnoy.com.

PHnoy.com key promises to our guest:

1\. We never sell, transmit or distribute personal information from our guest to third parties or unless required to do so under legislation.

2\. We never provide your email address or other personal information unless you have given us permission.

 **PHnoy.com** uses third-party advertising companies to our site in order to provide and support through serving and displaying advertisements.
