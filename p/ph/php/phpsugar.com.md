# phpSugar - Privacy Policy

You are a visitor on PhpSugar.com web site and for any questions we can be always reached at [](https://web.archive.org/web/20080611091324id_/http%3A//www.phpsugar.com/customer_care.html)

For each visitor to our web site, our web server automatically recognizes **no** information regarding the domain or e-mail address. 

We only collect the e-mail addresses of those who subscribe to our newsletter and purchase our products. Name and e-mail information collected from our orders is used for order notification and for product delivery **only**. We don't collect information regarding your credit card because we don't process any of the payments. For this we use 3rd party payment processors such as: Regnow.com, Emetrix.com, PayPal.com, Moneybookers.com and/or E-gold.com.

The information we have about our newsletter subscribers or customers is **NEVER shared** or sold. This information is stored locally, in a secure place and there's no way for unauthorized persons to access it. 

We do use cookies on our website except only on the forum area. Cookies are used to store certain login details to make future logins quicker and easier for you.

For any questions please [contact us via email](https://web.archive.org/web/20080611091324id_/http%3A//www.phpsugar.com/customer_care.html). 

_Thank you!_
