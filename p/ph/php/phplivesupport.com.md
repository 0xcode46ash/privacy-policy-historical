# PHP Live! Support [ Privacy Policy ]

OSI Codes Inc. Privacy Policy

updated: _June 25 2011 03:24:36._

Collected Information

When signing up for a trial account or when making a purchase, we collect general contact information that will be stored for client lookup. IP addresses are also collected for traffic and stats purposes. We do not store any credit card information for security purposes. 

Gathered Information Will Be Use For

All information collected during trials and purchases are for our internal purposes only. We do not distribute the information to any third-party for any reason. Collected data will be used for: 

  * to better serve you and quickly access the information for support needs 
  * to create tools to improved the client experience 
  * to monitor market trends and target demographics 



Securing Your Information

Collected data is stored within our Enterprise level servers that are monitored 24/7 by techs. Highly sensitive data, such as credit card informations, are not stored. 

Cookies Anyone?

This site uses cookies to track visitors and to improve visitor experiences. When requesting chat support on this site, several cookies are stored to enhance the support experience.
