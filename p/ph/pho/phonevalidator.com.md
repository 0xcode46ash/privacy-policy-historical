# Phone Number Batch Processing | PhoneValidator.com

We do not store the phone numbers run at this website.

We do not use cookies to track visitors to our site. However, we use third party traffic tracking services that use cookies to identify system usage. Information returned from the tracking service may include publicly available information about the I.P. address but it does not include personally identifiable information. 

We may store the I.P. address of website users to identify system abuse.

We do not collect personal information of users of this website. The only exception to this are customer service inquiries where the customer service email request is stored indefinitely. This information is not reviewable or editable once submitted and cannot be deleted by the sender. In this case, the consumers name and email address are stored as part of a received email. We do not spam, trade or sell email addresses used in correspondence to us.

Any changes to our privacy policy will be seen on this page -- please check back often. The effective date of the notice can be seen below.

Any questions or notices related to privacy must be directed via email using the contact page [here](http://www.crimetime.com/contact.aspx).
