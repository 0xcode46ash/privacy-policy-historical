# Philippine Business Directory - Advertise With Us

We at [PhilippineCompanies.com](http://www.philippinecompanies.com/ "Business Directory Philippines") respect the privacy of our site visitors and are committed in protecting any and all information we may require of our visitors as they peruse our website. Below are some of our commitments in the use and distribution of any private information that our visitors might divulge during his/her visit to our site. 

This site utilizes third party advertising networks that utilizes cookies that enables them and their partners to serve ads to visitors of this website based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Information given by users to this website is strictly voluntary. We do however require certain information from users who wish to utilize certain services of this website or wish to contact the owners of this site. These contact information will be kept strictly confidential and accessible only to qualified personnel.

This website allows visitors to contact members or posters of this website directly via an online form. A copy of the correspondence between visitors and members/posters are stored to ensure the security of our members and the website as a whole. We will not divulge user information to any third party without the prior consent of the users other than as requested or required by the Government of the Republic of the Philippines of this website for any legal proceedings or other purpose.

Information obtained from users of this site is used mainly to contact users willing to avail of our services and to answer questions, comments and suggestions from our visitors. We may, however, request for additional information that we believe can help us serve our visitors better. Divulging of this additional information is strictly voluntary.
