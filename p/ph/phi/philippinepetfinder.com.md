|  Privacy Policy and User Agreement  
•   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   •   • 

Philippine Pet Finder uses its best efforts to respect the privacy of its online visitors. Philippine Pet Finder may use "cookie" technology (a tiny element of data that is sent to your browser, which may then be stored on your hard drive so we can recognize you when you return) only to obtain non-personal information from our online visitors in order to improve their online experience. For example, we track the total number of visitors and their return visits to our site in the aggregate.

Philippine Pet Finder only gathers personally identifiable data, such as names, addresses, email addresses, etc. when voluntarily submitted by a visitor. For example, personally identifiable information may be collected in order to place a pet ad or submit a website to the pet directory. This information is used by Philippine Pet Finder or our partners who are involved in the operation of the Philippine Pet Finder network of sites for internal purposes only.

Philippine Pet Finder does not rent, sell or share e-mail addresses or any other user information! E-mail addresses are used for routine personal or business communications between Philippine Pet Finder and our customers. This may include, but not limited to, system maintenance notices, posting confirmations, and newsletters to those customers who have requested to receive them.

Philippine Pet Finder must provide information to local, state, and federal authorities who subpoena information for investigatory purposes.

Philippine Pet Finder reserves the right to delete users and delete ads for any reason without any prior notice.Philippine Pet Finder also reserves the right to change this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for any changes.
