# PickJam Privacy Policy

#### Privacy Policy

PickJam LLC ("PickJam"), the operate of PCHQuiz4Cash takes your privacy very seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

What this Privacy Policy Covers   
This Privacy Policy covers PickJam's treatment of personally identifiable information that PickJam collects when you are on the PickJam site, and when you use PickJam services. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that PickJam does not own or control, or to people that PickJam does not employ or manage. 

**Anonymous information** refers to data that cannot be tied back to a specific individual. For example, PickJam collects some information each time a visitor comes to a PickJam site so we can improve the overall quality of the visitor's online experience. PickJam collects the visitor's IP address, referral data, and browser and platform type. PickJam collects this user information for purposes of product monitoring, product improvement, and to aid future development decisions. This information may also be shared with advertisers and our business partners on an aggregate non-personal basis.   
**Personally identifiable information** refers to data that tells us specifically who you are, such as your name and postal address, which may be collected in addition to the anonymous data described above. 

PickJam collects personal information in the following ways from different parts of its Web site:  
When you submit answers to a PickJam quiz:  PickJam collects your email address so that we may notify you if you win and alert you to special offers and promotions at PickJam.  
When you win and respond to our winners notification email:  PickJam collects your physical mailing address so we can send a check via postal mail.  
When you register for PickJam news: PickJam collects your email address.  
Other Registration: PickJam may collect personal information when you register for other PickJam services. 

When you use our Tell a Friend feature: The email address information you provide to us when you use the 'Tell a Friend feature' is used to send an email to your friend on your behalf, inviting that friend to visit PickJam.  We will only send one email to your friend and they will not receive further emails from PickJam unless they visit the site or explicitly opt-in to PickJam communications.

Information Sharing and Disclosure.  PickJam will not disclose your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:  We may disclose personally identifiable information in response to legal process, for example, in response to a court order or a subpoena. We also may disclose such information in response to a law enforcement agency's request, or where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of our terms of use, or as otherwise required by law;  We may transfer information about you if we are acquired by or merged with another company or in the unlikely event that PickJam is dissolved; We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested; We need to send the information to companies who work on behalf of PickJam to provide a product or service to you. (Unless we tell you differently, these companies do not have any right to use the personally identifiable information we provide to them beyond what is necessary to assist us.). 

Cookies.  PickJam may set and access PickJam cookies on your computer. PickJam does not allow other companies to access the cookies we set on your computer. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to PickJam's cookies.  Certain areas of our site use outside advertising companies to display advertisements.  These ads may also contain cookies and PickJam does not have access to this information.

**Security.   **We will only collect and use personal information in accordance with this privacy policy to the extent deemed reasonably necessary to serve our legitimate business purposes, and we will maintain appropriate safeguards to ensure the security, integrity, accuracy and privacy of the information you have provided. In addition, we will take reasonable steps to assure that third parties to whom we transfer any personal information will provide sufficient protection of that information.

**Other Web Sites; Links.   **Our web site contains links to other web sites. PickJam is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. 

Review, Correction of Your Information and Requesting Removal from Mailing Lists and Deactivating Your Account.  If for any reason you are concerned that the personal information maintained by PickJam is not correct, or should you wish to be removed from our mailing list, please email us at [support@pchquiz4cash](mailto:support@pchquiz4cash).  We will be happy to review, update or remove information as appropriate. We may still retain your information in our files however, to resolve disputes, enforce our user agreement, and due to technical and legal requirements and constraints related to the security, integrity and operation of our websites.

Conclusion.  By using this site, you signify your assent to the PickJam Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use the PickJam site. We reserve the right to make changes in this policy at any time. Please check the policy each time you use our web site to ensure you are aware of any changes in our privacy practices. Our privacy policy will indicate the date it was last updated. Your continued use of our site will signify your acceptance of the changes to our Online Privacy Policy.
