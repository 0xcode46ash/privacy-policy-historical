# PC Express Online | Privacy Policies

  
**PC Express Online Privacy Policy**  
Updated: Friday, Sept. 20, 2002  


* * *

PC Express Online completely respects and protects the privacy of the individuals who visit our website. Individually identifiable information about you will not be willfully disclosed to any third party or connected with you personally.

In general, when you visit PC Express Online and access information you remain anonymous. If you make a purchase order from PCX, the information you are required to provide us, such as your name and contact information, are only used to improve our services to you. 

WE WILL NOT PROVIDE ANY OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION TO OTHER COMPANIES OR INDIVIDUALS WITHOUT YOUR PERMISSION.

**Public Support Forum**  
PC Express Online provides a Support Forum available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in this section become public information. Exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. PC Express will not be held responsible for these users' personal comments; we only provide this section for public access in order to improve on our service and communication with our valued customers.  


**Unsolicited Mail**  
To keep you informed about our latest offers, we may notify you of current promotions, specials and new additions to the PCX website. You may unsubscribe from our distribution list for this service by following the unsubscribe instructions in any email you receive from us.   


**Privacy Policy Update**  
By visiting the PC Express Online, you consent to the collection and use of your information as we have outlined in this policy. PC Express may decide to change this Privacy Policy without Prior notice.  


**Inquiries**  
If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy please feel free to contact us any time at [webmaster@pcx.com.ph](mailto:webmaster@pcx.com.ph) and we will answer any additional queries you may have.
