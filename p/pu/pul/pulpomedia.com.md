# Pulpo, an Entravision Company » Privacy Policy

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Sites, which may include information defined as personal data or personally identifiable information under an applicable law (“Personal Data”). For example, we may collect information such as your name, birth date, gender, county, zip code, e-mail address, telephone number, address, job title, company/organization name, current supplier status, social media credentials (including profiles when you subscribe to specific websites), and information related to inquiries and requests. We also may collect information about the internet connection and equipment you use to access our Sites. As you navigate through the Sites, we may automatically collect information such as your usage details, IP addresses, and information collected through cookies.

Information You Give to Us. The information we collect on or through our Sites may include:

Information you voluntarily provide when submitting an order form or other request to receive information, products, or Services on our Sites or a third party website or platform. The order form will ask you to give us contact information (like name or email address), financial information (like account or credit card numbers), and demographic information (like zip code or age).

Information you submit to us when/if you get in touch with our customer service, technical, or legal team, or any other employee at Pulpo, via contact form, email, chat or through other means.

Information you provide when you download any software or application made available to view and/or download in connection with Services that is owned or controlled by Puplo and/or its licensors, affiliates and suppliers.

Information you provide us for registering for or using any of our Services.

Information that you provide by filling in forms and applications on our Sites, including your username, password, your name, birth date, gender, county, zip code, e-mail address, telephone number, address, job title, company/organization name, current supplier status, social media credentials (including profiles when you subscribe to specific websites), and information related to inquiries and requests.

Information you provide when interacting with one of our Services, including participating or communicating in any public chat rooms, blogs, forums, message boards, news groups, or any form of communication in connection with the Sites. Please note that submitting an online comment is public and generally accessible to, and may be collected and used by, third parties. Pulpo is not liable for the distribution of your Personal Data that is put on a public forum.

Information given to us when you apply for career opportunities through the Sites. If you choose to apply online with Pulpo, Pulpo will collect any Personal Data provided in your application materials, for the purposes of reviewing and responding to your application, if appropriate.

Information you provide when signing up for newsletters and other types of informational material when you inquire about products, technical support, and customer service; and when you provide feedback about our Sites.

Records and copies of your correspondence with us (including e-mail addresses).

Information that you provide us by voluntarily conducting an online survey, contest, sweepstakes, or other online promotional activity administered by us. These activities may ask you for contact information (like name or email address) and demographic information (like zip code or age).

Information you provide us when you request information about an offer advertised through our network.

Information you provide us when you enter into a transaction through an offer delivered through our network or Sites.

Information you provide us when you request services from advertisers on whose behalf we provide fulfillment services or place online advertisements.

If you give us the Personal Data of a third party, you represent that you have provided notice to, and received authorization to provide such information from, that third party. Please note that in some cases, if you choose not to provide Personal Data you may not be able to engage in these services.

Information We Collect About You. As with many other websites, as you navigate through and interact with our Sites, we may use automatic data collection technologies to collect certain information about your equipment, browsing actions, and patterns, including:

Information about your computer and internet connection, including your IP address, operating system, host domain, and browser type. This may include data considered to be Personal Data in some jurisdictions, such as IP address or Device ID.

Details of your visits to our Sites, including the resources that you access and use on our Sites, traffic data, location data, logs, language, date and time of access, frequency, and other communication data.

Details of referring websites (URL).

The information we collect automatically is statistical data that helps us improve our Sites and deliver a better and more personalized service, including by enabling us to:

Determine website traffic patterns.

Count web visits.

Manage and improve the Services.

Track usage.

Conduct security and monitoring practices.

Determine traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our Sites.

Help us to know which pages and content are the most and least popular.

Observe website search patterns to provide more intuitive navigation cues.

Determine the frequency of user visits and last date of visit.

We may share this information, and other non-Personal Data, with advertisers or other third parties that are not part of the Pulpo family on an aggregate or other basis that does not disclose your identity or contact information. We may also obtain Personal Data and make it Non-Personal Data by, for example, removing personally identifiable elements (such as your name) or by aggregating Personal Data with information about other individuals in order to “anonymize” or “de-identify” the information. We may also augment the Personal Data and Non-Personal Data we collect with information from other sources, including, for example, zip code, age, gender, language preference or income range in order to generate aggregate information that reflects activities, habits, preferences, or interests about our users as a group. We do not knowingly collect, use or share any financial account numbers or social security numbers for purposes of online behavior advertising without your specific consent.

### How we use your information

Pulpo will only use your Personal Data you provide in a manner that is consistent with this Privacy Policy. If Pulpo obtains Personal Data from a third party, such as a business partner, our use of that information is also governed by this Privacy Policy. To enhance Pulpo’s ability to tailor the Sites to your preferences, we may combine information that you provide us on registration and other online sign-up forms with other information about you that we may obtain. Except where it is required by law, we may use non-Personal Data without restriction.

Below please find information about how we process information collected about you (including Personal Data). We may process information about you, including Personal Data, where the processing is:

Necessary to personalize your experience. We may use the Personal Data you give us to better tailor your Sites experience to your interests, and to send you information about Pulpo and its Services as well as promotional material on behalf of some of our partners. We use other information, including Personal Data, that you provide to us to show you content in which you may be interested and to display the content according to your preferences. We will also share this information within the Pulpo family in order to enhance your experience on the Sites and other Pulpo websites.

Necessary to advertise and market products and services of third-parties outside Pulpo with whom we do business (“Business Partners”), including to present content that is tailored to your interests, including online behavioral advertising and to analyze the effectiveness of advertising, promotions, and offers.

Necessary to conduct research and analysis, product development and reporting, including to better understand our audience; produce data analytics, statistical research, and reports; and to review and change our products and services.

Required by law.

Necessary to provide access to certain features on the Sites and receive information suited to their interests.

Necessary to contact you with information about the Pulpo family and promotional material on behalf of some of our partners, and to contact you when necessary.

Necessary to administer our Sites, help diagnose problems with our servers, to analyze trends, to track users’ webpage movements, to help identify you and your shopping cart, and to gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.

Necessary for us to comply with a legal obligation.

Necessary for entering into or the performance of a contract with you. For example:

To carry out our obligations arising from any contracts entered into between you and us.

To provide you with the information, products, and Services that you request from us.

To process your supplier application and registration form, and to create online accounts for your organization and its authorized agents.

To communicate with you about our contracts or your account.

Necessary for the purposes of the legitimate interests pursued by a third party or us (to the extent it does not infringe on any data subject’s fundamental rights and freedoms). For example:

To make sure that content from our Sites is presented in the most effective manner for you and for your computer.

To allow you to participate in interactive features of our Sites and Services (when you so choose).

To keep our Sites safe and secure (for example, security protection and fraud prevention).

To provide you with technical support and to improve our Sites and Services.

To provide you with information that you have requested and respond to your inquiries.

To notify you about changes to our Services or our Sites.

To manage our everyday business needs, such as auditing, administration of our Services, analytics, and the enforcement of any corporate reporting obligations, or other policies.

Necessary to investigate and enforce, if necessary, a user’s legal requests, including, but not limited to, potential copyright or intellectual property infringements.

Pursuant to valid consent provided by you, in order to provide you with information about goods or Services we feel may interest you. If you do not want us to use your Personal Data in this way, simply do not opt-in when we ask for your consent on the relevant forms where we collect your data.

Allowed for any other purpose where you have provided valid consent where it is legally required.

disclosure of your information

We may disclose aggregated information about our users, and information that does not identify any individual, without restriction. Anonymous click stream, number of page views calculated by web beacons, and aggregated demographic information may also be shared with Pulopo’s advertisers and business partners. Subject to applicable law and regulations, we may disclose Personal Data that we collect or you provide as described in this Privacy Policy:

When you provide any Personal Data to any of our “special relationships” that are not owned by Pulpo. These special relationships may include “powered by” partners, business partners, sponsors, and co-branded sites (referred to here as “co-branded pages”). These might include, for example, pages that share our name and that of another entity, such as TicketMaster. You should look for a specific Privacy Policy on any such co-branded page. Any Personal Data that you provide when signing up at one of those co-branded pages may be shared with our third party partner. Check our partners’ websites for information regarding their privacy policies.

When you apply on the Sites for a job, we may, in our sole discretion, forward the information you submit to our parent, subsidiaries and affiliates for legitimate business purposes.

To companies that are affiliated with Pulpo, that is, are controlled by, or under common control with, Pulpo.

Subject to applicable law and regulations, if the Pulpo Sites is sold to, or merges with, another company not owned by Pulpo, you should expect that some or all of your Personal Data collected from the Sites may be transferred, sold to, or assigned to the buyer/surviving company. If so, Pulpo will seek to obligate the acquiring company to use any Personal Data transferred by the Pulpo Sites in a manner consistent with this Privacy Policy, but cannot guarantee that it will be able to impose that requirement or that the acquiring company will comply.

To companies that are affiliated with us, that is, are part of Pulpo.

To our Business Partners so that they may market and advertise their products and services, administrate and manage transactions and relationships with audiences, and personalize user experiences for audiences.

To advertisers and business partners that are not affiliated with Pulpo, but would like to send you information about their products and services. For example, some of the companies who supply prizes for Pulpo’s contests may have special offers or promotions that might be of interest to users of the Pulpo Sites. We will not share your Personal Data with such third parties without your permission.

To perform tasks (such as order fulfillment and payment processing) required to complete purchases you make through Pulpo. Pulpo must disclose your credit card information with such third-parties to complete any payment transaction on our Sites.

To third parties who assist us in operating our Sites, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

For any other purpose disclosed by us when you provide your information or with your consent when required by applicable law and regulations.

To enforce or apply our Terms of Use and/or other agreements, including for billing and payment collection purposes, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Pulpo or our customers, or others. This may include an exchange of information, including your Personal Data, to other companies and organizations for the purposes of fraud protection and credit risk reduction.

With outside contractors, vendors, or agents who help us manage our information activities (i.e., message boards, sweepstake administration, order fulfillment, sweepstakes and prizes, storage, market research, reporting, hosting, technical support, statistical analyses, data processing, and other purposes), or with outside contractors, agents, vendors, or sponsors who help us with administration, judging and prize fulfillment aspects of contest, promotions, and sweepstakes. These outside contractors, agents, vendors, or sponsors may only use your Personal Data to provide Pulpo with a specific service and not for any other purpose without your consent. Pulpo may only provide your Personal Data to a third party in those instances where you have chosen to receive certain information and have been notified that the fulfillment of such a request requires the sharing of your Personal Data.

With third parties where you have chosen to receive certain information and have been notified that the fulfillment of such a request requires the sharing of your Personal Data.

With third parties who help us customize our advertising and marketing messages you receive on our Sites, or may work with outside companies to do so. Your non-Personal Data may be shared with these companies so this customization can be accomplished. Pulpo prohibits these companies from sharing your information with any third party or from using it for any purpose other than targeting advertising.

To protect the safety or security of individuals, such as to: (1) comply with the law or legal process or cooperate with law enforcement; (2) protect the safety and security of users or members of the public; (3) protect or defend the legal rights of the Company, its employees, agents, advertisers, publishers or other contractors (including enforcement of agreements); (4) protect against fraud or for risk management purposes; (5) protect or enforce our Sites policies; or (6) protect the rights, property or safety of the Company or the public. Except where required by law, we may share non- Personal Data in other ways without restriction.

With third-parties regarding specific registration modules, like survey, contests or sweepstakes. We will post a conspicuous statement on the registration module to the effect that we will be sharing with third parties the Personal Data and information collected.

When necessary to support certain functions and features of our Sites. For example, this may occur at a feature that enables you, via our Sites, to ask questions of persons or entities that are not part of the Pulpo family. By responding to those features of the Sites, whether by email or direct entry of information on our Sites, you are consenting to our transferring that information to such persons or entities.

To comply with the law or a legal process, such as a court order or any regulatory proceeding.
