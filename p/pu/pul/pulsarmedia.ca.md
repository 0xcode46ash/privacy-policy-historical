# Pulsar Media :: Privacy Policy

Pulsar Media has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site:

**IP ADDRESS:**

In order to increase security we log your ip address during a login attempt. IP address are removed from our database after 24 hours and are not used for any other purposes other than login attempts. We do not share your IP address with third parties, affiliates or any other organizations or corporations.

**FORM SUBMISSIONS:**

All inquiries made through our contact form, are kept strictly confidential and are not shared with any third parties or affiliates. Information collected from our contact form is used for internal purposes only.
