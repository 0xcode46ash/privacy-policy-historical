# Pun of the Day: Privacy Policy

## Pun of the Day's Privacy Policy

Pun of the Day, and its advertisers and affiliates, are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users.

What information do we collect? At Pun of the Day, the only information we normally receive is your e-mail address, whether you join our mailing list, or give your comments or feedback about our site. Pun of the Day occasionally asks you to complete surveys or enter contests where we collect information that we use for research. In these cases, aggregate information only is saved beyond the length of the survey / contest. Personal information (your e-mail address) is not permanently saved.

How do we use this information? Pun of the Day stores E-mail addresses only for use on our mailing lists, or during a contest in order to notify the winner. If you unsubscribe from our mailing lists, your e-mail address is not kept. If you give us your e-mail address for any other reason, it will not be saved. Survey or contest information is stored only in the aggregate in order to identify demographic information.

Pun of the Day will not sell, trade, rent or otherwise distribute your e-mail address or other personal information to outside parties, should you join our mailing list or send us submissions or feedback.

The Pun of the Day mailing list is hosted by [Egroups.com](http://www.egroups.com/). They also have a strict [privacy policy](http://www.egroups.com/info/privacy.html).

Cookies are small pieces of information that some web sites use to store data using your browser on your computer's hard drive. Pun of the Day does not use cookies. Some Pun of the Day sponsor sites may use cookies to assist in tracking click-thrus. No personal information is collected.

Tell us what you think. Pun of the Day welcomes your questions and comments about privacy. Please click [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20010616144011id_/http%3A//www.punoftheday.com/pun_07.htm) or send e-mail to [ webmaster@punoftheday.com](mailto:webmaster@punoftheday.com).
