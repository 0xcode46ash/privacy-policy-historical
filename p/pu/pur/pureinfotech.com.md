# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Statement for Pureinfotech**

We can be reached via e-mail through this contact form [here](http://pureinfotech.com/contact-us).

Pureinfotech has created this privacy statement to demonstrate our commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: Pureinfotech.com

We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, administer our Web site, analyze trends, track user’s movements, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. The IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

We may share aggregated demographic information with our partners and advertisers. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person. We use third parties for services such as our advertisement delivery. We may also use third parties to provide other services. When the user signs up for these services, we will share names, or other contact information such as email address for the third party to provide these services as requested.

This website contains links to other websites. Pureinfotech is not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or anything else regarding such websites.

We use outside advertisement companies to display ads on our website. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our website, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our third party advertisement company and we do not have access to this information.

We do our best to ensure that this website has security measures in place to protect the loss and misuse of the information under our control. All accounts have regularly changed passwords. All data at this website is backed-up regularly. Pureinfotech is not responsible for any damages in the result of a situation where there is a loss/manipulation of data.
