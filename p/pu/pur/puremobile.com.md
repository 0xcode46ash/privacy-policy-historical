# Help Center for Frequently Asked Questions - PureMobile

PureMobile is hosted using a private server where it hosts the PureMobile.com and PureMobile.ca Online Store pages, the online ordering system, and the order data.

PureMobile automatically collects order information but may only use this information in order to process orders authorized and initiated by the customer. All information provided is kept secure and confidential.

PureMobile has taken a number of steps to safeguard the integrity of customer data and prevent unauthorized access to information that is stored in its various systems. These measures are designed and intended to prevent corruption of data, block unknown or unauthorized access to our systems and data, ensure the integrity of information that is transmitted between PureMobile and its customers, and to provide protection of confidential information that may be in our possession.
