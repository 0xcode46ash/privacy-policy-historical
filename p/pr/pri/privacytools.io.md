# Cyphertite | Privacy Statement

Cyphertite was built from the ground up to privately and securely store your personal data. We make your privacy and the security of your data our absolute priorities. In order to do this we maintain strict controls over every process involved in the storage of your data. We never have access to your unencrypted data or to the keys necessary to decrypt the data that you store with us. You and only you have the information necessary to access your data. Your encrypted data is safeguarded in our custom-built storage appliances which are maintained in our high-security data centers. Safeguarding your data and insuring your privacy is what we do best.

In order to provide you with our secure backup service we necessarily collect some basic personal information such as your email and your payment information. We do not disclose your information to third parties except to process credit card transactions. The data used to process credit card transactions adheres to a strict privacy policy supported by TrustE and BBBOnline and is available via: [PCI Standards Documents Library](https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/security_standards/documents.php).

Our appliances do generate certain information which will be available to us such as the frequency of use and amount of encrypted data stored on our servers. This data is used solely to enable us to provide and improve our secure storage service or in order to help support you.

You may remove your personal information as well as your stored data at any time by signing in to [https://www.cyphertite.com](https://www.cyphertite.com/) or contacting [Cyphertite support](https://www.cyphertite.com/support.php). To remove your information, please visit [your account](https://web.archive.org/web/20150629022332id_/https%3A//www.cyphertite.com/account.php) and click "Cancel Account." If you cancel your account, we will purge your stored encrypted data from our systems, however, some of your billing and usage data may still be retained in our archives for a short period of time.

We may update or change this privacy statement from time to time. We will notify you of any significant or material changes to our privacy statement by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your account or by placing a prominent notice on our Site. Significant changes will go into effect 30 days after notification.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. If you have any questions or issues with regards to your privacy, please contact us directly by visiting [Cyphertite support](https://www.cyphertite.com/support.php).
