# Privacy

  |   |   |    
At PrintPlace.com we value your confidence in us and will take all the necessary steps to ensure your confidence is not misplaced. As an expression of our commitment to this core PrintPlace.com value, we respect your privacy and will not in any way jeopardize the information you share with us. This means that any and all personal information you share with us will not be shown to any third party entities or individuals.

We at PrintPlace.com are always looking for ways to provide you with better service and information and product selections second to none in the commercial print world. To help us achieve these goals, we collect information from our Web site about the areas our customers enjoy visiting and the topics that interest them. We may ask for your feedback and collect this information to share in an anonymous fashion so we may provide you with better services and products. At no time will we ever rent, sell, or distribute any of your personal information for any purpose! However, from time to time, we may send you announcements of new and exciting products or services we offer. If at any time you do not wish to receive these announcements, please contact us at [support@printplace.com](mailto:support@printplace.com) and we will immediately take you off our routing list. 

Although we are committed to your privacy and will make every effort to ensure that all our partners do the same, we cannot guarantee that our partners' privacy policies are as comprehensive and strict as ours. Therefore, we advise you to review our partners' privacy policies prior to using their products or services to confirm that they address your privacy concerns. 

We are constantly working to add new features to our Web site, and may change, modify, or update this privacy policy to ensure it addresses all the privacy issues raised by these additions or changes. This policy is in full compliance with all applicable laws and regulations. If the law requires us to disclose any of your information, we will make every attempt to stop such a disclosure, but may, nevertheless, be legally forced to comply. If this occurs, we will make every attempt to notify you that a legal disclosure has become necessary. We will post any changes or modifications of this privacy policy on this page so you are always aware of how we protect your privacy. 

As always, we welcome your feedback and hope you will let us know if anything concerns you about this policy or our site. Please e-mail us at [support@printplace.com](mailto:support@printplace.com).
