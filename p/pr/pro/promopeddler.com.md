# Privacy Notice Promopeddler.com, Online On Time, Promotional Products

> Dear Valued Customer,
> 
> We understand, respect, and share your concerns about privacy. We want to assure you that Promopeddler.com respects your privacy. We will not sell, rent, or loan any personal information regarding our customers to any third parties.
> 
> When an order is placed, we collect basic information including your name, home address, phone, and e-mail address. We use this information to confirm your order and to respond to any questions you may have. Where required, we also share this information with certain intermediaries (UPS, Authorize.net, Product Manufacturers) strictly to process and ship your order only. We fully trust these agencies, and have had trouble free transactions with our customers privacy kept confidential.
> 
> We do not provide this information to any other parties. We do not acquire any more information about consumers than is required by law or is otherwise necessary to provide a high level of service efficiently and securely. We train all of our employees about the importance of privacy. We give access to information about customers only to those employees who require it to perform their jobs.
> 
> Promopeddler.com has multiple security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information that we collect. Due to constantly changing Internet secure transaction handling measures, we contract with Squaretrade® and Authorize.net to safely handle your 128 Bit SSL encrypted web transactions. All orders are processed with Secure Sockets Layering 128 bit strong encryption technology using Squaretrade® Secure Certificates.
> 
> Your security is our top priority, we have contracted with Squaretrade® so that we are using the highest level of secure technology available, our secure server is monitored 24 hours a day by firewalls and a human security specialist. Authorize.net, Inc., is the world's leading provider of internet payment services and secure electronic payment software. Authorize.net's Internet payment service enables merchants to accept payments securely & conveniently over the Internet, using their patented Transact-Secure.
> 
> Encryption Technology, along with an Internet fraud detection service is powered by a patented neural network solution and proven profiling technology. This enables us provide you with the highest possible security. They will focus on what they do best handle your information safely, so we can focus on what we do best; providing you with the best possible buying experience! We have completed over 790,000 successful transactions using the methods listed above to safely handle the approval process for us, with no complaints or problems. We believe 110% in the ability of the companies we contract with to handle your information even more securely than we could, and with compliance with all of our privacy policies stated here.
> 
> If requested, we will provide any information, only if required by law. We will make sure to notify you by e-mail if there ever is a compromise of these policies, or if we have been forced to share any information by law.
> 
> If you have further questions about our privacy policy or have other comments on how we can improve your shopping experience, please do not hesitate to e-mail us using our contact page. Or call us Toll Free 1-800-455-1350 We always enjoy hearing from you!
> 
> We want to assure you that your business is very important to us, we would never do anything to jeopardize the trust we strive very hard to earn. Your business is truly appreciated; thank you and we wish you the best success with your Promotional Product advertising campaigns!
> 
> Sincerely,  
> Monte Baumgartner  
>  President and Co-founder
