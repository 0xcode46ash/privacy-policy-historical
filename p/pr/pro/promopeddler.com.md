>> Dear Valued Customer,   
> **W** e understand, respect, and share your concern about privacy. We want to assure you that Bagpeddler.com respects your privacy. We will absolutely not sell, rent, or loan any personally identifiable information regarding our valued customers to any third party. When an order is placed, we collect basic information including your name, home address, phone, and e-mail address. We use this information to confirm your order and to respond to any questions you may have. Where required, we also share this information with certain intermediaries (UPS, Authorize.net, Product Manufacturers) strictly to process and ship your order only. We fully trust these agencies, and have had several thousand trouble free transactions with our customers privacy kept confidential. We do not provide this information to any other parties. We do not acquire any more information about consumers than is required by law or is otherwise necessary to provide a high level of service efficiently and securely. We train all of our employees about the importance of privacy. We give access to information about customers only to those employees who require it to perform their jobs. 
>> 
>> **P** romopeddler.com has multiple security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information that we collect. Due to constantly changing Internet secure transaction handling measures, we contract with OLM LLC. and Authorize.net to safely handle your 128 Bit SSL encrypted web transactions. All orders are processed with Secure Sockets Layering 128 bit strong encryption technology on our OLM Safe-Order.net Secure Server using Thawte Secure Certificates. Your security is our top priority, we have contracted with OLM so that we are using the _Most_ 100% Secure Technology available, our secure server is monitored 24 hours a day by firewalls and a human security specialist. Authorize.net, Inc., is the world's leading provider of Internet payment services and Secure electronic payment software. Authorize.net's Internet payment service enables merchants to accept payments Securely & Conveniently and securely over the Internet, using their patented Transact-Secure. Encryption Technology, along with an Internet fraud detection service is powered by a patented neural network solution and proven profiling technology. This enables us provide you with the highest possible security. They will focus on what they do best handle your information safely, so we can focus on what we do best; providing you with the best possible bags buying experience! We have completed over 790,000 successful transactions using the methods listed above to safely handle the approval process for us, with no complaints or problems. We believe 110% in the ability of these companies we contract with to handle your information even more securely than we could, and with compliance with all of our privacy policies stated here. 
>> 
>>   **I** f requested, we will provide any information Only if required by law. We will make sure to notify you by e-mail if there ever is a compromise of these policies, or if we have been forced to share any information by law. 
>> 
>>   **I** f you have further questions about our privacy policy or have other comments on how we can improve your shopping experience, please do not hesitate to e-mail us using our contact page. Or call us Toll Free 888-466-7329. We always enjoy hearing from you!

>>   **W** e want to assure you that your business is very important to us, we would never do anything to jeopardize your trust we strive very hard to earn. Your business is truly appreciated; thank you and we wish you the best success with your Promotional Product advertising campaigns!
