# Proto Labs - Privacy Policy

We welcome you to the Proto Labs Website, including protolabs.com, protomold.com, firstcut.com and the other websites that display this Privacy Policy (collectively, the “Website”). The Website is operated by Proto Labs, Inc. ("Proto Labs", "we", or "us"), located at 5540 Pioneer Creek Drive, Maple Plain, Minnesota 55359. Proto Labs takes privacy matters seriously. Therefore, we are providing this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. By submitting information to us and/or by using our Website, you give your consent that all information that you submit may be processed by Proto Labs as described below. This Privacy Policy was last revised on May 20, 2008.  


**CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY**

We may amend our Privacy Policy at any time, without notice to you, by posting such revised Privacy Policy on this page. Please consult this page regularly so you remain aware of what information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we may disclose it. Any changes will only apply to information collected after the change is posted.

 **WHAT TYPE OF INFORMATION DOES PROTO LABS COLLECT?**

 ** _Information You Provide_** At various places on the Website, we may request personally identifiable information ("PII") such as your name, phone number, email, billing address, credit card number, shipping address and other information. You may choose to provide PII in order to obtain a quote, request a product or service, or register on the Website. You can always choose not to provide such information, but unless such information is provided, you may not be able to use a particular feature on the Website. **_Information Sent to Us by Your Web Browser_** The Website may automatically track certain automatic anonymous information ("AAI") from you, such as the URL of the website you visited prior to accessing the Website, what browser you are using, your IP address, or other information. Such information does not by itself identify you personally. However, if you have registered or made a purchase on the Website, it may be possible for us to link the information provided by your browser to information that identifies you personally. **_Cookies_** "Cookies" are small pieces of information that a website will send to and store on your computer's hard drive. Proto Labs may use cookies to make our services available or easier for you to use. We may also store PII in cookies to improve our services and facilitate your use of the Website. Acceptance of cookies is not a requirement to browse our Website, however certain features and services may require the use of cookies to work properly.

 **OUR USE AND DISCLOSURE OF YOUR INFORMATION**

We use information that we collect from you to fulfill orders that you place through the Website, to customize your experience on the Website, to improve our products and services, for marketing, and to develop other features of the Website and as we may disclose from time to time in privacy notices or “click through” agreements relating to certain sections of our Website or special offerings or promotions. If you provide us with your email address as part of a transaction or promotion through our Website, or if you opt-in to receive communications from Proto Labs, we may periodically send you information about products and promotions and invite you to participate in surveys or to provide feedback. From time to time, we may also send you information on products or services of third parties we believe would be of interest to you. In addition, we may contact you regarding a quote you have requested or an order you have placed, or to respond to your request for assistance. If you enter a promotion through our Website, we may ask you for certain PII such as your name, address and/or email address in order to administer the promotion and send you emails related to the promotion. Entering such promotions and providing the required PII is solely your choice. Proto Labs may share your PII: (i) with Proto Labs' affiliates or subsidiaries, (ii) if Proto Labs is required by law to do so in connection with inquiries or investigations into potentially illegal activities, (iii) in the event of a transfer of ownership of Proto Labs, merger or other similar transaction, or (iv) as disclosed in any privacy notice or “click through” agreement pertaining to certain sections of the Website or special offerings or promotions. In addition, we may share your email address with trusted third parties for sharing information with you about products or services we believe may be of interest to you.

 **LINKS TO THIRD-PARTY WEBSITES**

Our Website may contain links to third-party websites that we do not control. This Privacy Policy does not apply to personal information collected on third-party website. We are not responsible or liable for, and do not endorse, the data privacy practices or the content of any third-party website.

 **SECURITY**

While we endeavor to maintain appropriate measures to protect the security, confidentiality and integrity of your personal information, we cannot guarantee the security of PII transmitted over the Internet and urge users to take every precaution to protect personal information while using the Internet. Do not send Proto Labs any confidential or proprietary information other than as necessary to process your quote or register on the Website. We have installed encryption software conforming to the Secure Socket Layers (SSL) protocol to safeguard all of the commerce related (i.e. credit card and billing address) information you send to us. It is important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your password and to your computer. If your password is compromised, notify Customer Service at once at [customerservice@protolabs.com](mailto:customerservice@protolabs.com), or call us at 877.479.7501 between the hours of 7:30 a.m. and 5:30 p.m. Central Standard Time.

 **ACCESSING AND UPDATING YOUR INFORMATION**

You may update your information at any time or request that your information be removed from our lists by sending an email to [marketing@protolabs.com](mailto:marketing@protolabs.com).
