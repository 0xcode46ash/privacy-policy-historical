# Pro Sports Daily's Privacy Policy

Pro Sports Daily automatically saves information collected from visitors into our log files. This information includes the users' IP address, web agent, referral link, and pages visited. 

To use our forums, you are required to submit your email address during registration. The forum software also places a cookie file on your computer to help facilitate logging in. 

None of this information will ever be sold or shared. 

Our ad affiliates also collect user info.
