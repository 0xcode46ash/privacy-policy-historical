# Pro Sports Daily's Privacy Policy

Pro Sports Daily automatically saves information collected from visitors into our log files. This information includes the users' IP address, web agent, referral link, and pages visited. None of this information will ever be sold or shared. 

Our mailing lists are 100% spam free. We will never sell or distribute any of your email addresses.

Our ad affiliates also collect user info. [Click here](http://www.fastclick.com/about/privacy2.html) and [here](http://www.valueclick.com/privacy.html) to read their privacy policies. 

If you have any questions, [Email Us](mailto:comments@prosportsdaily.com)
