# Property118.com: Privacy Policy | Property118.com

**Property118.com is a partner trading style of Innovative Landlord Solutions LLP.**

The term ‘Property118.com’ or ‘us’ or ‘we’ or ‘our’ refers to the owner of the website which is  **Innovative Landlord Solutions LLP** \- registered office; 1st Floor Woburn House, 84 St Benedict’s Street, Norwich, NR2 4AB – Limited Liability Partnership registration number OC351654.

The following statement discloses consumer privacy practices for the websites www.property118.com and services portals associated with the online services offered to licensed users by Innovative Landlord Solutions LLP.

Property118.com may update its policy from time to time without notice, so please revisit periodically.  
**  
Members Recommended Suppliers**  
Property118.com allows its members to list their recommended suppliers of professional trades and services via the Property118.com website. On submitting personal details to us or by accessing links on the Property118.com website and user services portals, you may interact with a number of our members recommended suppliers.

Property118.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of our members recommended suppliers. On submission of personal and/or business information  to Property118.com or as you visit their websites, contact the members recommended suppliers directly and /or use their services, we encourage you to read their privacy statements, as they may differ from ours in some particulars. Property118.com is not responsible for any advice or service received from a members recommended supplier.

**Personal Information**  
Property118.com understands its customers’ need to protect personal and/or business information. In providing Property118.com’s services and our members recommended suppliers services to you, and to suggest ways in which you can enhance the use of Property118.com  and our members recommended suppliers services, personal and/or business information may be gathered, tracked or used.

In most cases, you will be asked to provide personal, property and/or business information as appropriate for Property118.com or our members recommended suppliers to provide the required service in a secure environment to you. This may include name, address, business, email, telephone, facsimile or other information necessary to provide effective services and support to you.

**Requested Information**  
We request and use personal or business information when you register to become a Property118.com member, request more information or contact from us and/or our members recommended suppliers, correspond with us or engage in other activities on our websites. For example, if you request to book in to an event online or via telephone, we will ask you for information necessary to authenticate you as a potential customer or existing client.

**How We Use the Information We Gather and Track**  
We may contact you by post, phone, electronic mail, electronic mediums and sms, and we may use personal, business and account information in the following ways:

  * To provide you with information about Property118.com  websites, newsletters, informative emails, market information, invitations to useful events, and research on future product ideas or improvements;
  * To assist us in ensuring your contact details are correct for contacting you;
  * To provide you access to members recommended suppliers services, offers and information that you have requested;
  * To assist us in creating better products and services to meet your future needs;
  * To provide you with access to services or otherwise engage in activities;
  * To request optional participation with relevant market research or online surveys;
  * Internal record keeping.



Property118.com will not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to any other third party except where disclosure is made at your request or are required by law to do so. They will be retained on Property118.com’s database for which only Innovative Landlords Solutions LLP have access to.

You may request details of personal information which we hold about you under the Data Protection Act 1998. A small fee will be payable. If you would like a copy of the information held on you please write to Property118.com, Unit K – Aspiration House, Iceni Court, Norwich International Business Park, NR6 6BB.

**Legal Disclosures**  
Please be advised that in certain instances, it may be necessary for Property118.com  to disclose your personal and/or business information to government officials or otherwise as required by our legal obligations. This could include a court proceeding that requires us to disclose such information.

**Limiting the Use of Personal Information**  
On submission of personal and/or business information to Property118.com we request that you read, understand and agree to our [Terms and Conditions](http://www.property118.com/index.php/terms-conditions), which includes our Privacy Policy (You are here), [Email Policy](http://www.property118.com/index.php/email-policy), and [Forum and Blog Terms of Use](http://www.property118.com/index.php/terms-of-use).

If after submitting your personal and/or business information you wish to change your contact details or preferences you may contact us by using one of the communication channels at the bottom of this statement and we will promptly update our records with your request.

We also provide ‘opt out’ options on the footer of all marketing emails. Please see our [Email Policy](http://www.property118.com/index.php/email-policy) for more information.

**Security and Quality of Personal Information**  
We have undertaken appropriate security measures to protect your personal and/or business information, including technical and procedural steps to protect your data from misuse, unauthorised access or disclosure, loss, alteration or destruction.

We make a sincere effort to respond to your requests to update or correct your personal and/or business information. If you believe that Property118.com does not have your current personal information, you can update it by using one of the communication channels at the bottom of this statement.

**Cookies and Website Traffic**  
Property118.com may use cookies from time to time. A cookie is a small amount of data stored on the hard drive of the individual’s computer that allows us to identify the individual with his or her corresponding data that resides in our databases. You may read more about cookies at [http://www.cookiecentral.com](http://www.cookiecentral.com/). Property118.com may use cookies to personalise your experience on our online services or portal pages, or to track end user usage of Property118.com services for customers. If you do not want Property118.com to use cookies in your browser, you can set your browser to reject cookies or to notify you when a website tries to put a cookie in your browser software. Rejecting cookies may affect your ability to use some of the products and/or services at Property118.com’s website(s).

Property118.com may also use cookies, originating IP addresses, time of visit, etc. to track your visit to our web sites; however such basic Internet traffic statistics will not be tied with other personal information held by Property118.com.

**Other Privacy Management Practices**  
Please remember that information you submit in a discussion group, blog or other forum will be deemed published, public information and is therefore no longer confidential. The communication of personal, business and property information in such venues means that information can be collected and used by others and you should assess that risk before doing so. This includes your disclosure of information concerning your account profile, personal data, or other data obtained by use of Property118.com websites. For more information please visit our [Forum and Blog Terms of Use](http://www.property118.com/index.php/terms-of-use).
