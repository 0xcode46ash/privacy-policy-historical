PropertyGuys.com Privacy Policy  
Updated January 2004  
  


Our customer's privacy is important to PropertyGuys.com. This policy is our guaranteed commitment to the protection of any personal information collected and how it is used and disclosed. 

  
  


PropertyGuys.com collects personal information such as names, addresses, contact information, *date of birth, *social insurance number, *financial information, and specific homeowner and property details. Such information is provided to us by the customer and is used for either the marketing of a property, the identification of a property for a potential buyer, for the assessment of creditworthiness, or the promotion of our home marketing services. 

* Please note that date of birth, social insurance number, and financial information is only collected at the option of the client when selecting our "List Now, Pay Later - Flex Plan" financing option. 

  
  


PropertyGuys.com does not sell any information collected about our customers. PropertyGuys.com only discloses personal information on a strictly confidential basis: 

  1. To our corresponding Head Office and or industry associates affiliated with our system to complete transactions initiated by you the customer. 
  2. To credit rating organizations. 
  3. If you request or consent to the disclosure. 



  
  


Each PropertyGuys.com franchisee maintains his or her own client information and is not an employee of PropertyGuys.com Inc. All PropertyGuys.com client files are kept physically at the office of each franchise location, at PropertyGuys.com Inc. Head Office, and electronically on PropertyGuys.com computer systems. We insure that access or alteration to personal information is limited to those individuals who have a valid business reason to access the information. 

  
  


PropertyGuys.com is committed to its customers. We welcome and encourage your feedback. If you have any questions or comments regarding this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by sending an e-mail request to [privacy@propertyguys.com](mailto:privacy@propertyguys.com) or in writing to: _Privacy Officer, PropertyGuys.com, 128 Highfield Street, Suite 210, Moncton, NB, E1C 5N7_.
