# Private Policy | ProducerSpot

Technologies such as: cookies, beacons, tags and scripts are used by ProducerSpot and our marketing partners, affiliates, or analytics or service providers.

These technologies are used in analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole.

 **ProducerSpot respects your privacy!**

###  _How does ProducerSpot  use cookies?_

We collect information from you when you subscribe to our newsletter or enter a giveaway or a promotion via our website, and we guarantee to not give your e-mail address to other parties.

We only provide articles and information, we never ask for personal information like Credit Card Numbers, IP addresses, Home Addresses, Digital Identity, Telephone Number, National identification number, etc..

We use these cookies to manage online advertising and revenue share arrangements. Our approved, advertising partners, such as Google Adsense, use cookies to provide advertising to you and to enable us to manage our relationship with those advertisers by, for example, tracking how many unique users have seen a particular advertisement or followed a link in an advertisement.

If you have any questions regarding ProducerSpot’s privacy policy & cookie policy you may contact us through our [contact form](http://www.producerspot.com/contact).
