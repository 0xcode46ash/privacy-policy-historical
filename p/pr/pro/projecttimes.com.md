# Project Management Times and Requirements - Privacy Policy

[](http://tellafriend.socialtwist.com/)

**Project Times and Your Privacy  
**  
In order to ensure that we at ProjectTimes.com Inc. are in compliance with the _Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act_ (PIPEDA), we have established the _Project Times Privacy Commitment_ , and have designated a Privacy Officer to oversee its implementation.   
**  
Types of Information We Collect and Store**

  * Information we have received from you through your subscription to Project Times or through your requests for information about ProjectWorld. This is limited to your name, business title, address, telephone number and email address. 


  * Information you may provide to us on a voluntary basis when we conduct a survey of your needs, wishes and opinions regarding Project Times, ProjectWorld or the Project Management profession in general. 



  
**Why We Collect this Information**

  * Information is collected to let us e-mail you quarterly to inform you that a new Project Times issue has been posted online, and to keep you aware of upcoming ProjectWorld activities. 


  * To occasionally allow carefully selected companies to provide information about products and services of specific interest to Project Management practitioners through independent third party mailing houses. 

  
**Project Times Privacy Commitment**

We maintain a file of each of our readers under carefully controlled conditions to ensure it cannot be accessed by unauthorized persons. We limit the information we collect to the absolute minimum necessary to us to contact you as outlined above. If you no longer wish to receive notification of new Project Times online postings, information about ProjectWorld or other project management products and services, or to file a privacy complaint, please contact:
