# Privacy Statement - Progress Energy

|  |  | 

Progress Energy values the privacy of each visitor to our site. To better protect your privacy, Progress Energy is providing this notice to explain our online information practices, and to explain the choices you can make concerning the way your information is collected and used.

**Why We Collect Information**

Why we collect information  
In general, you can visit the Progress Energy Web site without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. However, there are times when we may need information from you. If you register to access various services related to your residential account or other applications involving your business account on this Web site, you may be asked to submit personal information such as your account number, name, address, phone number and e-mail address. Similar information may be required to participate in Progress Energy subsidiaries' Online Account Access and e-bill payment program.  You may also be asked to submit additional personal information or financial information in order to use certain specific services offered in these customer account transaction areas. We will protect all information that you provide us on the Web site, as set forth below.  
  
---  
  
**Three Ways We Use Information:**

  1. _Personal Information:  
_The personal information you provide us not only enables you to use various customer services offered on the Web site, but also enables us to provide you with better service generally. We may use this information to contact you to respond to an inquiry that you send via our Web site or to keep you informed about Progress Energy and its services. You may "opt out" of receiving such communications at any time by informing Progress Energy that you wish to have your e-mail address deleted from Progress Energy's files by calling 919-546-6111 or filling out a feedback form online. If you choose to communicate with Progress Energy or its subsidiaries by an online feedback form, then e-mail from Progress Energy or its subsidiaries to you will be considered to be sent with your consent and will be accessible to anyone who has access to your e-mail.

You should protect the confidentiality of your personal account number and personal identification or password. You are responsible for all activity on the Web site accessed by your personal identification number or password. The information provided through Online Account Access is intended solely for the private use of the customer. Any unauthorized entry of Online Account Access or unauthorized use including copying or other reproduction of the information is prohibited.

Access by Progress Energy and subsidiary employees or consultants to any personal information you provide is limited to only that necessary to provide the business service offered and to otherwise allow such employees or consultants to perform their duties. Progress Energy may use the personal information you supply to notify you about important news concerning our company, including new products and services, product updates and technical support issues, and special offers we think you may be interested in.

  2. _Shared Information:  
_Progress Energy and its subsidiaries will not sell, rent or lease to others your personally identifiable information. Progress Energy will share the personal data you provide only with other Progress Energy subsidiaries and affiliates and/or business partners who are acting on our behalf. In order to provide you with services offered on the Web site, or to complete transactions or requests initiated by you on the Web site, we may transfer you to Web sites operated by third parties that help us provide services to you. Progress Energy does not assume responsibility for any of these sites, their content, or their privacy policies. Progress Energy does not endorse or make any representations about them or any information, software or other products or materials found there, or any results that may be obtained from using them. If you decide to access any of the third-party sites linked to the Progress Energy site, you do so at your own risk.
  3. _Anonymous Audience Traffic Analysis:  
_In the process of administering our site, we maintain and track usage through Web server logs. Web server logs provide such information as what types of browsers are accessing our site, what pages receive high traffic, and the times of day our servers experience significant load. We use this information to improve the content and navigation features of our site. It is not used to personally identify you.

  
  
**Consent**

Progress Energy and its subsidiaries will use and share the personally identifiable information you provided to us only for purposes described above. We reserve the right to disclose any and all pertinent customer information to law enforcement or other government officials as we, in our sole discretion, believe necessary or appropriate.

**Our Commitment to Data Security**

Progress Energy is concerned about the security of your information. As an added level of security, several online forms on this site utilize Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) when transmitting and receiving data. SSL helps prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of information. SSL works by using a private key to encrypt data before it is transmitted over an Internet connection. Both Netscape Navigator and Internet Explorer support SSL. However, we cannot guarantee the security of any information transmitted over the Internet.

**External Links**

Links to third-party Web sites from the Progress Energy Web site are provided solely as a convenience to you. If you use these links, you will leave the Progress Energy site. We have not reviewed these third-party sites and do not control them. Progress Energy does not assume responsibility for any of these sites, their content, or their privacy policies. Progress Energy does not endorse or make any representations about them or any information, software or other products or materials found there, or any results that may be obtained from using them. If you decide to access any of the third-party sites linked to the Progress Energy site, you do so at your own risk.

**Energy Audits/Surveys**

Information regarding your account with any of our subsidiaries is always confidential. However, we would like for you to understand that we will use your account information and any other information you provide to us to perform any requested energy audit or survey. We may also use this information to better understand how you use energy, and to improve our service to you. This may include providing you with information regarding various products and services that might be of interest to you.

Any energy survey or audit that is performed using this Web site and any recommendations that you may receive using this Web site will be based on methods and algorithms that are commonly used in the energy industry. However, the quality of the output from any energy audit or survey and any advice or recommendations that may be provided will depend on quality of information that you supply. You must also understand that every situation is unique, and we cannot guarantee that all relevant factors have been considered in providing you with any recommendations. Other factors may effect your energy costs such as your lifestyle, weather, orientation of your building, etc.

We are not recommending one product or company over another, and cannot guarantee the performance of nor shall we be responsible in any way for any product or service building improvements that you may decide to purchase or install. While we may give you one or more options or recommendations from which to choose, the ultimate decision to purchase or install products and building improvements will be yours. We do not guarantee that you will realize any energy or other cost savings even if all recommendations are implemented.

Your continued use of Progress Energy's Web site following the posting of changes to this Privacy Statement will mean you accept those changes. We reserve the right to change this privacy policy statement at any time.
