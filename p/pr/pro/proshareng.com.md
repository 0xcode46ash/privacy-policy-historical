# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

At Proshare, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy statement outlines the types of personal information we receive and collect, and how it is used.

**Log Files**  
Like many other web sites, Proshare makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses and other such information gathered are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

**Cookies and Web Beacons**  
"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored on your computer's hard drive by your browser. These allow service providers store your details and provide features to make your use of the service easier. Most browsers allow the cookie function to turn off. You can delete cookies from your hard drive at any time. However, this will mean that any settings such as your stored username and password will have to be reset.

Proshare does not use cookies to store information about visitor’s preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser. If you however notice any and you wish to disable such cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.

Further, we advice that you set your browser to allow mails from @proshareng  to drop in your inbox; otherwise important mails and updates may drop into your bulk folder or be deleted completely.

For those using company addresses, we advice that you contact your IT department to obtain necessary permissions to receive this updates in compliance with best practice e-mail download/access requirements

**Questions Regarding this Privacy Policy**  
If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us by sending an e-mail to [info@proshareng.com](mailto:info@proshareng.com). Please include "Privacy Policy" in the subject line of your inquiry.

**Revision to this Privacy Policy**  
Proshare reserves the right to change or modify this Privacy Policy at any time by posting revisions on this site. Such changes will be effective immediately after posting, whether dated or not.
