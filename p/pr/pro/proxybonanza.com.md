# Proxybonanza.com

[](http://proxybonanza.com/)

#### Privacy policy

  * When signing up for Proxybonanza.com service, users are required to give us contact information. Certain information, such as your email address are collected in order to verify your identity and for use in our records. Information collected at this website is kept in our records only as long as is necessary to provide requested services to our clients.
  * We keep logs containing information about access made to and through our service. These proxy logs are not revealed to anyone outside of Proxybonanza.com except portions that may be needed to confirm abuse, and are deleted on a regular basis. We DO NOT analyze these logs, we DO NOT share these logs with any third party, we only keep them in case of abuse complain and to keep track of bandwidth usage.
  * We do not share the email address of people who contact us with any third party.
  * All use of our site is confidential. We only reveal information if legally required to do so, or if there is clear evidence of abuse our system.
