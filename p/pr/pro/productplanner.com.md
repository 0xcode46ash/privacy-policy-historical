# Terms of Service - Product Planner

At Product Planner, we recognize that your privacy is important. We value your trust and confidence and are firmly committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and visitors to our website. Product Planner is not in the business of selling or renting individuals’ personal data to other companies for marketing purposes.

The following documents describe the privacy practices of Product Planner. Below you can find detailed information on when and why we collect personal information, how we use it and the limited conditions under which we may disclose it to others.

Product Planner encourages its users to abide by all applicable laws, rules and regulations, as we promise our clients that we will do ourselves. It is Product Planner’s policy to use cookies (a small text file Product Planner stores locally on your computer for record-keeping purposes) only for allowing you to login to Product Planner without entering your login information. The cookie information does not include personal data such as name, age, phone number, e-mail address or mailing address nor does Product Planner link cookies to personal data such as name, age, phone number, e-mail address or mailing address. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, however, you may set your web browser to reject cookies. For more information on how to reject cookies, see your browser’s instructions on changing your privacy settings.

If you have any questions or comments about this policy, our services, or your experience with Product Planner, please contact us at [productplanner@gmail.com](mailto:productplanner@gmail.com)
