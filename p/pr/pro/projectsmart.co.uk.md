# Privacy Statement for Project Smart

  


### Privacy Statement for Project Smart

Project Smart has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: Project Smart.

We use cookies to measure the number of times people return. Cookies, by themselves, cannot be used to find out the identity of any user. Project Smart will only store information provided voluntarily and this information will never be kept on-line or made available to anyone else.

Our newsletter and feedback forms require users to give us contact information in the form of an e-mail address and optional telephone number. We use customer contact information from the forms to send the information requested and nothing else. Users may opt-out of receiving future newsletters; see the unsubscribe feature available on this site and at the bottom of each newsletter.

This site contains links to other sites. Project Smart is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these web sites.

Our site uses a third party to securely process payments. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for the products and services requested. Project Smart does not have access to any customers personal financial details. Information about purchases will not be disclosed.

We occasionally display banner ads on our site. These may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by other companies and we do not have access to this information.

If you have any questions about this statement please feel free to [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20030416191406id_/http%3A//www.projectsmart.co.uk/contact_us_feedback.html).
