# Privacy Policy | Project Smart

Project Smart has created this privacy policy to show our firm commitment to your privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.

Our website uses cookies. A cookie is a small text file that we put on your computer. These cookies allow us to anonymously distinguish you from other users of the website, which helps us to provide you with a good experience and also allows us to improve our site. Cookies, by themselves, cannot be used to find out the identity of any visitor. Project Smart will only store information provided voluntarily and this information will never be kept on-line or made available to anyone else.

We occasionally display advertisements on our site. These may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our website, cookies received with advertisements are collected by third parties and we do not have access to this information. For detailed information about how we use cookies see our [Cookie Policy](https://web.archive.org/web/20130510204926id_/http%3A//www.projectsmart.co.uk/cookie-policy.html).

Our feedback form requires visitors to give us contact information in the form of an email address. We use visitor contact information from the form to send the information requested and nothing else. Your personal information will never be sold or given to anyone else.

This site contains links to other sites. Project Smart is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these websites.

Our site uses a third-party to process payments securely. Financial information collected is used to bill the customer for the products and services requested. Project Smart does not have access to any customer's personal financial details. Information about purchases will not be disclosed.

If you have any questions about this policy please feel free to [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20130510204926id_/http%3A//www.projectsmart.co.uk/contact-us.html "Contact Us").

## Article Categories

[**Project Planning A Step by Step Guide**](https://web.archive.org/web/20130510204926id_/http%3A//www.projectsmart.co.uk/project-planning-step-by-step.html "Project Planning A Step by Step Guide")  
The key to a successful project is in the planning. Creating a project plan is the first thing you should do when undertaking any kind of project. Often project planning is ignored in favour of getting on with the work. However, many people fail to realise the value of a project plan in saving time, money and many problems.

[**BOSCARD (Terms of Reference)**](https://web.archive.org/web/20130510204926id_/http%3A//www.projectsmart.co.uk/boscard.html "BOSCARD \(Terms of Reference\)")  
When looking to gain support and approval for your next project, it might be worth thinking BOSCARD. The acronym stands for background, objectives, scope, constraints, assumptions, risks and deliverables. These headings are commonly found in terms-of-reference and project initiation documents.

[**10 Golden Rules of Project Risk Management**](https://web.archive.org/web/20130510204926id_/http%3A//www.projectsmart.co.uk/10-golden-rules-of-project-risk-management.html "10 Golden Rules of Project Risk Management")  
The benefits of risk management in projects are huge. You can gain a lot of money if you deal with uncertain project events in a proactive manner. Experience shows that 10 golden rules exist for implementing risk management successfully in your project.
