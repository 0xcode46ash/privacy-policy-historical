# Privacy Policy - Watch Online TV - Live TV - Free Online TV

Our site use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit.  
Some of our advertising partners like Google Adsense may use cookies on our site.  
We do not receive any of information from our third party advertisers. We have no control over such third party advertisers or the use of any such information that is collected.  
The data gathered from these cookies will be used to help AdSense publishers better serve and manage the ads on their site(s) and across the web. Summary:

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.  
Google use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site(s) and other site(s) on the Internet.  
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
