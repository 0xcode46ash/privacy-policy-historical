# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy:**

We respect the privacy of all visitors to our website.

We do not sell, disclose, lease, rent, or share any personal information including e-mail addresses, and we do not use cookies on this website.

In the event that we should ever utilize an Advertising Network that sets cookies, we would not receive or collect any of the information.

This website provides external links to other websites. We are not responsible and cannot control the content and security of other sites you visit once you leave our website.

Questions regarding this privacy policy should be directed to:   
info @ programurl.com
