# Privacy Policy - ProofHub

We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, aggregate information on what pages consumers’ access or visit, and information provided voluntarily by the consumer (such as survey information and/or site registrations). The information we collect is used to improve the content of our web pages and the quality of our service, and is not shared with or sold to other organizations for commercial purposes, except to provide products or services you’ve requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

  1. It is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, group, place, region, nation,etc., violations of [Terms of Service](https://web.archive.org/web/20150912021333id_/https%3A//www.proofhub.com/terms), or as otherwise required by law.
  2. We’d transfer information about you if ProofHub, LLC is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, ProofHub, LLC will notify you before regarding information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.



##### Information gathering and usage

  1. When you register for ProofHub we ask for information such as your name, company name, email address, billing address.
  2. ProofHub, LLC uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provision, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research.



##### Cookies

  1. A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from one of the website’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive.
  2. Cookies are required to use the ProofHub service.
  3. We use cookies to record current session information, but do not use permanent cookies. You are required to log-in to your ProofHub project site after a certain period of time has elapsed. It is necessary to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents.



##### Data storage

  1. ProofHub, LLC uses third party vendors and hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run ProofHub. Although ProofHub, LLC owns the code, databases, and all rights to the ProofHub application, you retain all rights to your data.



##### Encryption

All data is encrypted via SSL/TLS when they’re transmitted from our data centers to your browser and vice-versa. The backups taken for your data is also encrypted. You can read more about it on our [security](https://web.archive.org/web/20150912021333id_/https%3A//www.proofhub.com/security) page.

##### Disclosure

ProofHub, LLC may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with subpoenas or when your actions violate the [Terms of Service](https://web.archive.org/web/20150912021333id_/https%3A//www.proofhub.com/terms).

##### Changes

ProofHub, LLC may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your ProofHub primary account holder account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.

##### Questions

Any questions about this privacy policy should be addressed to [support@ProofHub.com](mailto:support@ProofHub.com)
