# Privacy Policy | Digital Electronics Corporation

Digital recognizes the importance of the personal information of our customers. In order to protect the personal information of our customers and maintain mutual trust with them, we make the following commitments:

  * We appropriately collect, use, and manage personal information only to the extent necessary for our fair and legitimate business activities.
  * In order to prevent unauthorized access to personal information and the loss, destruction, alteration and leakage, etc., of personal information, we implement appropriate information security measures and continue to improve such measures all of the time.
  * We observe the laws and regulations and other standards applicable to the protection of personal information.
  * We continuously review our activities for the protection of personal information and make efforts to improve them.
