# Privacy Policy - Pro Rank Tracker

## Privacy Policy

Pro Rank Tracker having a mailing address at P.O Box 22602, Tel Aviv 61226, Israel (hereinafter, referred to as “we”, “our, “us”, “Company”) collects from the individual user (hereinafter, referred to as “you” or “your”) certain information through the Website http://proranktracker.com/ (hereinafter, referred to as “Website”). This Privacy Policy contains our policies and procedures governing the collection and handling of information that identifies an individual user or that could be used to contact or locate him or her (hereinafter, referred to as “Personal Information”). This Privacy Policy applies only to the Website. It does not apply to any other websites.

#### 1\. Personal Information collected by us

1.1 The Website may contain online forms that ask users to provide their: (1) first and second name; (2) company name; (3) email address; (4) Physical address; (5) phone number; and (6) PayPal account.

#### 2\. **The purpose of collection of Personal Information**

 **

2.1 We use your Personal Information to provide you with the services you request and to communicate with you. We also use that information to the extent necessary to enforce this Privacy Policy and to prevent imminent harm to persons or property. Moreover, the Company may use aggregated Personal Information for industry research and academic research.

#### 3\. Merger, acquisition or sale of all or a portion of its assets

3.1 If the Company is involved in a merger, acquisition or sale of all or a portion of its assets, the Company reserves the right to transfer personal information or non-personal information to a party involved in the merger, acquisition or sale, to the extent such information is necessary to carry out the merger, acquisition or sale.

#### 4\. Protection of Personal Information

4.1 We employ information security tools to protect Personal Information collected by us. However, even with the use of such tools, we cannot guarantee the security of Personal Information. By using the Website, you acknowledge and agree that we make no such guarantee, and that your use of the Website is at your own risk.

#### 5\. Third party access to Personal Information

5.1 We give certain independent contractors access to your Personal Information. Those contractors assist us with the maintenance of the Website. All those contractors are required to sign contracts in which they promise to protect Personal Information using procedures reasonably equivalent to ours. We also may disclose Personal Information to attorneys, collection agencies, or law enforcement authorities to address potential privacy violations, or illegal behavior. And we disclose any information demanded in a court order or otherwise required by law or to prevent imminent harm to persons or property.

#### 6\. Accessing and correcting your Personal Information

6.1 If you would like to access or modify your Personal Information, please log into your account on the Website and click on the “Edit Account Details” page.

6.2 If you would like to delete your Personal Information stored on our servers, please contact us at the address mentioned in Section 11 of this Privacy Policy. We will respond within seven days from receiving your request for deleting your Personal Information.

#### 7\. Cookies

7.1 We use cookies to make use of the Website as convenient as possible. Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to a computing device for record keeping purposes. The majority of web browsers are set to accept cookies. The cookies do not themselves personally identify users. However, they identify users' browsers. The cookies allow the Company to remember your preferences and improve your experience of the Website.

7.2 It is possible to set your browser to refuse cookies. Please note that this may limit the services provided by us through the Website. If you would like to stop receiving cookies, check your browser's help information or visit the following links for further information:

  * [Internet Explorer](http://windows.microsoft.com/en-GB/windows7/How-to-manage-cookies-in-Internet-Explorer-9)
  * [Firefox](http://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/Cookies)
  * [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/bin/answer.py?hl=en-GB&answer=95647&p=cpn_cookies)
  * [Opera](http://www.opera.com/browser/tutorials/security/privacy/)



#### 8\. Amendment of this Privacy Policy

8.1 We may change this Privacy Policy at any time by posting a new version on the Website. The new version will become effective on the date it is posted.

#### 9\. Retention period

9.1 We will retain your Personal Information for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

#### 10\. Your consent

10.1 By using the Website, you are consenting to our processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us. The term “Processing” includes, but is not limited to, collecting, storing, deleting, using, combining and disclosing information.

#### 11\. Contacting us

11.1 If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please send us a message by using the online form available on [Contact us page](http://proranktracker.com/members/submitticket.php?step=2&deptid=1) , or please write to the following mailing address:

P.O Box 22602, Tel Aviv 61226, Israel
