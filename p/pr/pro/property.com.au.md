# Property.com.au - real estate australia property for sale lease and rent

This Privacy Statement is made on behalf of Property.com.au Pty Ltd and all references to "PCA", "we", "us", or "our" in this statement are references to Property.com.au Pty Ltd (ACN 095 798 888) and its related bodies corporate. 

**Protecting your information is important**  
At PCA, we understand the importance you attach to information that identifies you (your 'personal information'). This Privacy Statement tells you how we collect information when you visit this site and what we do with it. 

What information we collect about you and what we do with it  
PCA collects information about you each time you come to any part of the PCA websites. We collect information about the time, date, duration and usage of the PCA websites. 

PCA uses non-personalised statistics about the use of our website to continually improve it and to improve our customer service. 

If you submit information to PCA either by using an electronic form or by sending an email we collect that information and use it for the purposes that you have provided it. 

In addition, we may use your information to provide you with special offers, information about new products and special promotions that we think may interest you. If you do not want to receive this information, you can let us know by sending PCA an email at privacy@property.com.au, by mail Locked Bag 5, Eagle Farm, QLD 4053 or call (07) 3114 9999. Please provide us with your customer details including name, address and contact number. In addition, indicate if you do not wish to receive any more promotional material. 

**What is a Cookie and what are they used for?**  
A cookie is a text file placed into the memory of your computer by our computers. Our computer can retrieve a copy of this text file. 

Cookies are used for a number of purposes. PCA uses cookies to identify you for the purposes of aiding security in your use of the website. 

If you think the information we hold about you is incorrect or you want to update it  
We rely on the accuracy of the information you provide to us. If you think that we hold information about you that is incorrect in any way, or out of date, or you simply want to notify us of something new, please send PCA an email at privacy@property.com.au indicating that your information is incorrect and the details. 

**Our security procedures**  
In designing this web site, we have incorporated security procedures and practices that we consider are consistent with Australian industry practice. We review our security procedures from time to time and update them when relevant. 

Personal information about you and your business with PCA is only accessible by you and by those who are authorised by us to access it. They also must use a password, and are bound to safeguard the information and access it only for approved business purposes. 

**Links to other sites**  
You may be able to access various other sites by clicking on links that we have provided. You should be aware that other sites are not subject to our privacy standards and procedures. 

**Changes to our Privacy Statement**  
This information relates to our current privacy standards. We may vary our privacy standards from time to time. We will notify you of these changes by publishing them on this website. 

**Feedback**  
If you have any comments or suggestions regarding our website or this privacy statement please email us at [privacy@property.com.au](mailto:privacy@property.com.au)
