# Privacy Policy - Project Wedding

The [Project Wedding Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/info/privacy_policy) describes how we treat personal information when you use the Project Wedding website. 

  


#### Personal Information

  


  * You do not need to register an account to view vendor reviews, discussions, and photos.
  * You will need to register an account in order to add content to the site.
  * When you register an account, we store your email address, location, password and name.
  * To improve the site's usability, we record usage statistics.
  * We use your ip address to automatically figure out your closest vendors.

  


#### Uses

  


  * We may send you information related to your Project Wedding account.
  * If you consent, we may send you news about important updates or events concerning the Project Wedding service.

  


#### Information Sharing

  


  * We will not sell, rent or share your personal information with any third parties except when required by law.
  * We reserve the right to provide advertisers with aggregated non-personal information, such as the number of times an ad was clicked.

  


#### Your choices

  


  * You may change your personal information by going to [Account Settings](https://web.archive.org/account/show).
