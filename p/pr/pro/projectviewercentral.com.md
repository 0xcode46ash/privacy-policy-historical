We do use cookies to enhance the users experience while visiting this site. 

## Advertisers

## Personal Information

All personal information gathered by Housatonic Software/www.projectviewercentral.com is used solely by us and no third parties. 

## E-mail Addresses

If you contact us via an e-mail contact point on this site we will reply by e-mail to the address you give us. 

Unless explicitly stated, we do not collect e-mail addresses in order to build mailing lists either for our own use or to sell, rent or give away to others. We may, from time to time, offer the opportunity to subscribe to newsletter or other services. Any information we collect for those purposes will only be used as explicitly stated at that time. We do not share any personal information that you choose to give us with any third parties.
