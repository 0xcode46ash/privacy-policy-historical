Collection of Information
We collect “automatic information” and “personally identifiable information.”
“Automatic information” is information collected by our web server (which may be hosted by a third-
party) that your web browser makes available whenever you visit our Site, such as IP address of your
computer, your operating system version and browser type, the duration of your visit to the Site, and
other standard tracking data we use to evaluate site traffic and usage patterns.
The Site also uses “cookies” which are bits of data sent by a website through the browser to the
computer of the user visiting the site that enables our Site to return the results the browser expects.
Most website browsers automatically accept cookies, but you may be able to turn off the cookies
acceptance option in your browser’s settings. However, disabling the cookies support function of the
browser may prevent some portions of our Site from functioning properly, and you may not be able to
fully utilize our Site. We may from time to time use advertising companies to develop banner ads for


our Site. These ads may contain cookies that may be sent to your browser through banner ads. We do
not have control over cookies placed by our advertisers, nor do we have access to any information their
cookies may collect.
“Personally identifiable information” is information that tells us who you are, such as your name, job
title, company, physical mailing address, e-mail address, telephone and fax numbers and , if applicable,
financial information such as your credit card information. We may collect this information when you:
    I.   Register to attend Createasphere trade shows, conferences and events;
   II.   Subscribe to our publications, including e-newsletters’
  III.   Register with our Site;
  IV.    Communicate with us by e-mail’
   V.    Post comments, ratings or messages to any of our chat groups, bulletin boards or forums;
         and/or complete online forms, surveys or contest entries.
You always have the option not to provide the personal information we request. If you choose not to
provide the personal information we request, you can still visit our Site, but you may be unable to
access or use certain options and/or services.

identifiable information to send you marketing, advertising and/or product/service offerings
(‘Commercial Communications”).
We may share personally identifiable information with our affiliates and with our partners, including our
exhibitors, sponsors and advertisers, for marketing, advertising, and/or product/service offering
purposes. While we will seek to require all such third parties to follow appropriate privacy policies

actions or policies of third parties.
If you which to limit the Commercial Communications your receive from us and/or our affiliates and
partners, and/or do not wish to have your personally identifiable information shared with third parties,
you may opt (i) not to have your personally identifiable information shared with third parties, (ii) not to
receive Commercial Communications from third parties, and/or (iii) not to receive Commercial
Communications from us except for Commercial Communications related to the product or service for
which you provided your personally identifiable information, as described below under ”Opt-Out
Option”.
We may also disclose personally identifiable information in special cases when we have reason to
believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact, or bring legal action against
someone who may have breached our Terms of Use, or who may be causing injury to or interference
with (either intentionally or unintentionally) our rights or property, other users of our Site, or anyone else
who could be harmed by such activities. We may also disclose personally identifiable information when
we reasonable believe that the law requires it.
In addition, as our business changes, we may buy or sell various assets. In the event all or a portion of
assets owned or controlled by Createasphere, or any subsidiary or affiliated entity, are sold, assigned,


transferred or acquired by another company, personally identifiable information any be among the

If you submit financial information to us, we may use that information to verify your credit and collect

The Site is a general audience web site. We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable
information from users of this Site who are under the age of thirteen.
If we become aware that a user of our web site is under the age of thirteen and has provided any
personally identifiable information to use without verification of parental consent, we will remove that
child’s personal information from our files.
