# Privacy Policy - NO.1 CLASSIFIEDS - FREE ADVERTISING - PRABHANJAM .COM

## Privacy Policy

We may collect non-personal informations such as internet protocal address, operating system, browsing software used, and internet service provider. Those information does not identify you personally. When you respond to advertisements or post contents on the Website with or without registering, we may collect personal information that you provide such as your name, mailing address, email address, home country, and zip code ("Registration Information"). You may have also asked to provide us your phone, fax numbers, gender, age, and/or company name in certain categories. If you provide your phone number and / or e-mail id, it will be displayed in your posting.

We may also collect additional information that our users provide, such as new or deleted postings, new or deleted comments, keyword searches, scam reports and new contact sellers. We use a third-party payment gateway system to process payments for purchases, and other third party systems to monitor site traffic, which may, in some instances, store your personal and / or non-personal information which is described earlier in this privacy policy.

If you have provided your phone number and/or e-mail id in any of the advertisement you post in our website, you agree to receive communications on the provided phone number and / or e-mail id from our side which may include but not limited to automated voice calls and/or text messages. The communication will be used to validate any information you provided on the Website.

We use the collected personal and / or non-personal information to provide our services, customize users' experience, measure interest in our services, inform users about services and updates, communicate marketing and promotional offers to you, resolve disputes, collect fees, troubleshoot problems, encourage safe trading, enforce our policies, and as described already.

The users are important to us and hence all information contained in www.prabhanjam.com is treated as strictly confidential and they will not be disclosed or shared or sold to any external person or organisation without users’ explicit consent. We use proper systems to protect the security and integrity of any personal information against unauthorized access and disclosure. But unfortunately data transmission over the internet cannot be guaranteed to be completely secure. So we cannot ensure or give warrant to the security of any information you transmit to us and you do so at your own risk.

But we may have to disclose users’ personal information to Governmental or law enforcing agencies or our regulators for any investigation or judicial process from time to time, and the same we will be done under proper authority. We may also disclose information to administer our policies, respond to claims if a content or listing violates other’s rights, or protects anyone’s rights, property or safety.

We may use cookies to manage our users’ sessions and to store preferences, tracking information, and language selection. Cookies may be used whether you register with us or not. "Cookies" are small text files transferred by a web server to your hard drive and thereafter stored on your computer. The types of information a cookie collects include the date and time you visited, your browsing history, your preferences, and your username. In some instances, our third-party service providers may use cookies on the Website. We cannot control or access cookies used by third- party service providers. This Privacy Policy covers only Cookies used by us, and not any cookies used by third parties. You have the ability to either accept or decline the use of cookies on your computer, whether you are registered with us or not. Typically, you can configure your browser to not accept cookies. However, declining the use of cookies may limit your access to certain features of the Website. For example, you may have difficulty logging in or using certain interactive features of the Website, such as the OLX Forum or Comments feature.

By using this Website or the Service, you consent to collect, store, and use of the personal and non-personal information you provide for any of the Services that we offer, and you consent to our collection of any changes or updates that you may provide to any information you provide that is collected by us.

This page may be updated and / or upgraded and / or modified (partially and / or fully) time to time without any notice and users should visit this page frequently to know the alterations.
