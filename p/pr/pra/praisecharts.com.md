# Privacy Policy - PraiseCharts Christian Sheet Music

| 

For your protection, all personal information that we gather from your account is safely stored on our secure server. PraiseCharts does not sell or lend its contact list to any other business. All information is volunteered, and gathered for the purpose of serving you more effectively and efficiently. We do not collect credit card information. All financial transactions are handled through authorized, third-party merchant providers -- namely, [PayPal](http://www.paypal.com/) and [Internet Secure](http://www.internetsecure.com/). Any personal information that is gathered by PraiseCharts may be viewed and edited by going to [www.praisecharts.com/account](http://www.praisecharts.com/account) and [www.praisecharts.com/billingdetails](http://www.praisecharts.com/billingdetails). Furthermore, on the Billing Details page, you can opt in or out of our email update.
