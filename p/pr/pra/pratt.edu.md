# Pratt Institute | About Pratt | Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting Pratt.edu, your privacy is very important to us. 

Our policy at Pratt Institute is to collect no personal information about you when you visit our website, unless you explicitly choose to make such information available to us.

When you visit Pratt.edu, our servers only read the Internet domain and IP address from which you accessed our website. This does not result in any gathering of your personal email address or other personal information.

We do collect generic data on web pages accessed by visitors to our website. This is done so we can improve our website content, and such information is not shared with other third-party schools or companies. If you choose to share personal information with us we will use the information only for the purpose you intended. We will not disclose the information to third parties, unless required by law. By entering your email address on a form on this website, it is possible for your email address to be released in response to an official public records request. We use email addresses only for their intended purpose and do not give them out unless required by law.

If you have any questions about our privacy policies please feel free to contact us at [website@pratt.edu](mailto:website@pratt.edu).
