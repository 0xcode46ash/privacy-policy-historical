# Pramerica Systems Ireland Limited

|   
| 

Privacy and Business Integrity  
  
---  
Pramerica respects your privacy, and we are committed to protecting it. We provide this privacy statement to tell you how we do that in relation to your interaction with this Website.   


Sites Covered by This Privacy Statement

This privacy statement applies to the www.pramerica.ie website. It does not apply to any linked websites. Pramerica has no control over those other websites. To learn about the privacy policy of a linked website, please refer to the privacy statement for that website. Pramerica has no responsibility for the privacy practices of other websites. 

[Back to top](https://web.archive.org/web/20071118182852id_/http%3A//www.pramerica.ie/privacy.htm#top)

You can browse our website without telling us any personal information. There are times where you may choose to give us personal information about you. We need certain information from you so we can respond to your request. For example, you may ask that a Pramerica representative call you regarding employment opportunities. We need certain information from you so we can respond to your request. 

[Back to top](https://web.archive.org/web/20071118182852id_/http%3A//www.pramerica.ie/privacy.htm#top)

Kinds of Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily give us on this website,for example, in applying for a job. This information commonly includes your name, address, email address, telephone number, education and professional background. Our website has security safeguards that are designed to protect the security of any personal information that you decide to give us via the Internet. 

[Back to top](https://web.archive.org/web/20071118182852id_/http%3A//www.pramerica.ie/privacy.htm#top)

We use personal information collected on this website in several ways: · to respond to your request for a call or information regarding employment opportunities at Pramerica; and · to process requests or submissions regarding employment opportunities. Any information that is provided by you will be treated in accordance with the terms of the Data Protection Acts, 1988 and 2003, and any amending legislation as may be adopted in Ireland from time to time. By providing information to us on this website, you explicitly consent to our transferring this data to our head office in the U.S.A., understanding that Privacy laws in the U.S. are different than in Ireland. 

[Back to top](https://web.archive.org/web/20071118182852id_/http%3A//www.pramerica.ie/privacy.htm#top)

Disclosure of Information

We will not give Personal Information to non-Pramerica affiliated companies to allow them to offer their products to you. We will disclose information collected on this site to Pramerica or appropriate affiliated company to review or process your employment application materials. We may disclose it to third parties as required or permitted by law. For example, we may disclose information: · to comply with any legal or regulatory requirements; and· to companies that may acquire part or all of a Pramerica business or that of our parent. In any other circumstances, we will only share information with third parties with your written consent. You may at any time request a copy of any personal information we hold relating to you. 

[Back to top](https://web.archive.org/web/20071118182852id_/http%3A//www.pramerica.ie/privacy.htm#top)

Pramerica, its directors, employees, agents, affiliates or other representative and its and their parents and associated companies, shall not be liable in respect of any claims, emergencies, demands, causes of action, damages, losses, expenses, including without limitation, reasonable legal fees and costs of proceedings arising out of or in connection with the use and/or dissemination of personal information relating to you in accordance with this privacy policy and your consents. 

[Back to top](https://web.archive.org/web/20071118182852id_/http%3A//www.pramerica.ie/privacy.htm#top)

This privacy statement is effective as of August 5, 2004. We reserve the right in our sole discretion to change this statement. If we change it, we will post the current privacy statement on this page, and you should regularly check this privacy statement for any amendments. Any changes to this statement will be effective as of the day they are posted.
