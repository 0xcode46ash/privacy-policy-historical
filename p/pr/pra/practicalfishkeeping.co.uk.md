# Practical Fishkeeping magazine | Privacy Statement

[home](https://web.archive.org/web/20060502074220id_/http%3A//www.practicalfishkeeping.co.uk/pfk/pages/home.php) Â» privacy statement 

The Practical Fishkeeping website and its publisher, Emap Active Ltd., is committed to protecting your privacy and security. We only use the information we collect about you with your consent to process your enquiry or order and to tell you about our services. We will never wilfully disclose personal information about you to any third party, other than to process an order or enquiry, or to send you a competition prize, without first receiving your permission or unless we are under a legal obligation to do so. 

### Who else will we tell about you?

We will not pass on your personal details to any third party, including site sponsors or advertisers, without your permission (except for the sole purpose of sending any prizes you may have won to your address). We may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, sales, traffic patterns and related site information to our third-party partners, but these statistics will not contain any information about you from which you could be identified. 

### Your consent

By making an enquiry with practicalfishkeeping.co.uk, you consent to the collection and use of the information you provide solely for the purposes of processing and fulfilling your request. All changes to this document will be on this page, keeping you up to date with our privacy policy whenever it may change. 

### In summary

We're committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect only for the purposes for which you have consented. We won't pass on your personal information to others without your permission. You can always enquire about or check the information that you have given us by contacting the Website Editor Matt Clarke.
