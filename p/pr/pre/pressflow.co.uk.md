# PressFlow.co.uk | Privacy Policy

  
This privacy policy sets out how PressFlow.co.uk uses and protects any information that you give PressFlow.co.uk when using this website.

PressFlow.co.uk will not collect any personal information about you unless you provide it to us voluntarily. If you do not want your personal information collected, please do not submit it.

If personal information is collected, such data will only be used for the specific purpose for which it was sought and to which you consented. Your personal information will not be used for any other purpose.

We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to other companies.

PressFlow.co.uk may contain links to enable you to visit other websites of interest easily. However, once you have used these links to leave our site, you should note that we do not have any control over those websites. Therefore, we cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide whilst visiting such sites and such sites are not governed by this privacy statement. You should exercise caution and look at the privacy statement applicable to the website in question.

PressFlow.co.uk reserves the right to add or alter privacy policy at any time without notice.
