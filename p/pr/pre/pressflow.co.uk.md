# PressFlow.co.uk | Free Online Press Release Distribution Service

This privacy policy sets out how PressFlow.co.uk uses and protects any information that you give PressFlow.co.uk when using this website.

PressFlow.co.uk will not collect any personal information about you unless you provide it to us voluntarily. If you do not want your personal information collected, please do not submit it.

If personal information is collected, such data will only be used for the specific purpose for which it was sought and to which you consented. Your personal information will not be used for any other purpose.

We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to other companies.
