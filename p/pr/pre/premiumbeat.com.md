# Privacy Policy

We, at [premiumbeat.com](http://www.premiumbeat.com/), fully understand and respect your online privacy and have taken the necessary steps to protect your personal information.

To this end please be assured that: 

  1. We never sell, share or rent our e-mail list. Your e-mail address is used exclusively by us to communicate with you when necessary (if we experience a problem with your order for example) or if you have requested information .
  2. The credit card information provided when you purchase a product from us is processed through a separate website that features state-of-the-art protection and encryption processing system. premiumbeat.com does not even have access to your credit card number, and none of your credit information is stored on our server. 
  3. For statistical purpose, we do gather general information when you visit our website. For example we like to know from which country you are visiting, what browser you use, which of our pages are visited the most, which kinds of music are most popular, etc. This sort of information allows us to serve our customers better. However, we do not gather personal information from visitors.
  4. When you purchase a product from us, you fill in a form with your address, phone number and e-mail in order to complete the transaction. We keep this information securely on file in case we need to communicate with you, and to expedite future transactions. But we do not share this information with anyone else.
  5. We will sometimes provide you with links to external websites. [Premiumbeat.com](http://www.premiumbeat.com/) is not responsible for the validity of these links, but we will correct any error as soon as they are brought to our attention. Furthermore, we cannot assume any responsibility for the privacy policy of external websites. 



For any questions or additional information please contact us at [info@premiumbeat.com](mailto:info@premiumbeat.com)
