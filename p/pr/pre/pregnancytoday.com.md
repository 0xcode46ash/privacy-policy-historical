# iParenting: Privacy Policy Statement

Hi everyone,  
iParenting is dedicated to making its family of award-winning Web sites the best destination for parents on the Web. To that end, we realize that many visitors are concerned about how the information they provide on our network is treated by iParenting Media. We have created the following policy to address those concerns. We may change or update the policy from time to time, so be sure to check back. If you have questions or concerns, or just want to chat, please contact us at [info@iparenting.com](mailto:info@iparenting.com). It is your feedback that makes iParenting such a special place! 

Privacy Policy Statement

iParenting  
P.O. Box 1780  
Evanston, IL 60204  
We can be reached via e-mail at [info@iparenting.com](mailto:info@iparenting.com), or you can reach us by telephone at 847-556-2300. 

We do not collect personally identifiable information from you unless you voluntarily or knowingly provide that information to us. You may provide that information to us by signing up for one of our weekly newsletters, answering a survey, entering a contest or asking our experts a question, among other things. If your choose to provide that information to us, we will use it for the purpose in which it was intended (i.e. to notify you if you are a winner, etc) or to understand our audience better in and effort to meet changing demands. If you submit content to our site via a contest, sweepstakes, or other promotions, or through birthstories.com, iparentingstories.com, or expert questions, we own all rights to that information and can use it in any form, online, print or other. 

This information is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes. E-mail list membership is only shared with companies for marketing if the subscriber has given iParenting explicit permission by opting in to our promotional lists. WE DO NOT SELL E-MAIL NAMES. If you feel your name has been subscribed to a list in error, please contact us at [webmaster@iparenting.com](mailto:webmaster@iparenting.com), providing the e-mail address that was incorrectly subscribed and the name of the list it was subscribed to so we can remove your address from our list. 

Our Web server recognizes IP addresses of visitors to our network and domain names from which you access the Internet (ex: earthlink.com if you use Earthlink to access the Internet), the date and time of your visit to our site, the operating system and browser you use and other technical information. This allows us to aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit and how they are viewed. The information then helps us provide new and innovative tools and content that respond to our community’s needs. 

**Cookies**  
Occasionally we use cookies on our Web site. Cookies are stored on your computer and send information back to us, letting us know whether you have visited us before or are a new visitor. The cookie is sent back to our site only and doesn’t contain any personal information about you or provide us with any means to contact you. In addition to visit information, a cookie may allow you to log in automatically to features on our network such as the [Pregnancy Daily](http://pregnancydaily.com/) or the [Baby Daily](http://babydaily.com/). It also allows you to store your birth plan at [Birthplan.com](http://birthplan.com/). In general, cookies allow for a more personalized user experience. 

If you wish to delete cookies from your computer, you can consult the operating instructions of your browser to do so. In some browsers, you can turn off cookies, preventing Web sites from setting them on your computer. No user will be refused access to our network based on allowance of cookies. 

**Children**  
iParenting Media is a network aimed at parents. We believe that it is essential that parents are involved in their children’s Internet experiences. Therefore, in compliance with federal law, we do not knowingly collect information of any kind from anyone under the age of 13. Any information that we received that is identified as being submitted by someone under 13 will be purged completely from our database and all accounts associated with that information will be terminated immediately. 

**Third Party Advertising**  
The ads appearing on this Web site are delivered to you by DoubleClick, our Web advertising partner. Information about your visit to their site, such as number of times you have viewed an ad (but not your name, address, or other personal information), is used to serve ads to you on this site. For more information about DoubleClick, cookies, and how to "opt-out", please [click here](http://www.doubleclick.com/us/corporate/privacy/privacy/default.asp?asp_object_1=&). 

**Third Party Cookies**  
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser to be sent back only to that advertiser. 

Thank you for being a part of our community.
