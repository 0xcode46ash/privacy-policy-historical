# Privacy Policy | Press Ganey

**Privacy Policy**  
Press Ganey Associates, Inc. and all affiliates (“Press Ganey”) respect the privacy of all patrons who use our web sites. Our Privacy Policy reflects our intent to protect the private information of all users of our web sites. We urge you to please read this privacy policy as, together with the terms and conditions, it will form the basis for your use of Press Ganey web sites.

Except to the extent required by law, we will not sell, lease, or release your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address or other personal data without your consent. Press Ganey does not contribute to or participate in cooperative databases, which give other companies access to such personal information. 

By accessing and using this web site you will be deemed to have read and accepted the terms and conditions, together with the practices set out in this privacy statement.

The information we collect from you may include personal data. We will only collect and process such information as is appropriate and necessary according to the type of services you wish to establish with us. To the extent that the information you provide to us includes personal data, you agree to Press Ganey processing all such personal data for the purposes of providing requested services.

Press Ganey's corporate web site ([http://www.pressganey.com](https://web.archive.org/)) collects and stores only personal information that clients and visitors knowingly provide. 

**Press Ganey Forums**  
Our Client Forums web sites (<https://www.pressganey.com/forum>) collects and stores only personal information that clients and visitors knowingly provide. Any information you choose to share when using any of these forums or functions becomes public information.

Do not post any information or other material protected by copyright without the permission of the copyright owner. By uploading information or other material onto the Forum web site, the uploading party warrants and represents that it owns the copyrighted information or other material, or has received permission from the person or entity who owns the copyrighted information or other material. By uploading information or other material onto the Forum web site, the uploading party grants Press Ganey the non-exclusive right and license to display, copy, publish, distribute, transmit, print and use such information or other material. Subject to the grant of this license, the uploading party retains any other rights that the party may have in such information or other material.

**Information Integrity/Security**  
To provide a secure environment that is protected from unauthorized third-party access, alteration, theft, or misuse Press Ganey uses industry-standard technology including encryption and firewalls to protect personal information gathered online. To enable us to provide a secure environment, Press Ganey may use personal information internally to diagnose problems or to contact you if necessary.

**Third-Party Links**  
This web site and _Press Ganey Briefings_ contain links to other web sites. Press Ganey is not responsible for the content of such web sites including any changes or updates that are made to the linked web site or the accuracy or completeness of the information provided on those web sites. Press Ganey encourages its web site users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this web site.

**_Press Ganey Briefings_**  
_Press Ganey Briefings_ is a biweekly e-mail Press Ganey sends containing useful information. If you no longer wish to receive Press Ganey Briefings, you may opt out by clicking the “Unsubscribe” button at the bottom of the Briefings e-mail.

**Corrections or Updates**  
If at any time you wish to correct or update information that you have already provided, the information may be changed or modified using the same system that was used to enter the information.
