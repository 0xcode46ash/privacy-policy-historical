**_Thank you for visiting PressTV.com._**

**The Information We Need**   
You can vote in polls, express an opinion, or subscribe to our online newsletter. The types of information we inquire include: name, address, e-mail address, telephone number, and fax number, and information about your interests in our programs, and services. 

**How We Use the Information**   
We may use the information to communicate with you, such as to notify you when we make changes to our subscriber agreements, to fulfill a request by you for an online newsletter, or to contact you about your account with us. 

**Our Commitment to Security**   
We have installed physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and help prevent unauthorized access, and maintain data security. 

**About Cookies**   
This site contains cookie. Cookie is a small text file of information attached to a user's hard drive while the user is browsing the Web site. A Cookie can contain information such as user ID, and user preferences. 

**How to Contact Us**   
If you have any questions you may contact us at [webmaster@presstv.com](mailto:webmaster@presstv.com)
