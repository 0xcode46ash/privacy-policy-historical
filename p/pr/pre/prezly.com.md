# Privacy Policy - Prezly

### Protecting Your Privacy

We believe in protecting your privacy. We've put together this privacy policy to explain how your information is protected, stored, and used. By using the service, you consent to the terms and conditions of this privacy policy and are aware that our policies may evolve in the future.

### Data Collection and Storage

We collect and store all information provided during your use of Prely (f.e. personal information, contactlists, press releases, ...). This information will not be disclosed to any third-party without your explicit consent.

However Prezly uses third party vendors and hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run the website. However, you retain all rights to your data.

Prezly uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provisioning, identification and authentication, services improvements, contact, and research.

### Disclosure

Prezly may disclose personal information under special circumstances, such as to comply with subpoenas.
