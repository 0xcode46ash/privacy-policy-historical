## About PreOwnedWeddingDresses.com

We collect aggregate information website traffic; for example, what pages consumers access or visit. This statistical information we collect is used for internal review and to improve the content of our website. It is not shared with any other organizations for commercial purposes.Â  We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies.

We serve google interest based advertising on our site. Google interest based advertising serves ads based on a google assigned user interest category, which google determines by your visits to pages throughout the google content network.

If you supply us with your postal address or email address, you will receive the information for which you provided us your postal address or email address as well as periodic news and information from PreOwnedWeddingDresses.com.

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. Such Personal Information is collected for the sole purpose of adding your name and contact information to PreOwnedWeddingDresses' mailing list in order to provide you with publications, promotions, updates and invitations to upcoming PreOwnedWeddingDreses events that may be of interest to you. By providing PreOwnedWeddingDresses.com with Personal Information on these portions of the Site, you consent to the collection and use of such information in accordance with this privacy statement.

PreOwnedWeddingDresses.com does not disclose any Personal Information to any third parties, except as required by law. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check our website periodically.

Please let us know of any questions or concerns via our [contact us](https://web.archive.org/contact-us.html) form.
