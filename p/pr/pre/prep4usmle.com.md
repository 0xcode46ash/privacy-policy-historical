# Prepare for USMLE - Privacy statement

| Privacy statement   
---  
  


The core message is that "Prepare for USMLE" will never sell or distribute your personal information.

"Prepare for USMLE" makes every effort to assure your confidentiality and keep your trust and do not sell, give or otherwise distribute your private information to any third party.

"Prepare for USMLE" collects personal information from you during the registration process you complete to add resourses or to become a member of our Online Medical Community.

Although very rare we may use this information to contact our users announcing major updates on this site you will have the opportunity to decline any mailings from "Prepare for USMLE".

"Prepare for USMLE" uses reasonable precautions to keep secure the information disclosed, however we can not be responsible for any breach of security by any actions of any third parties.
