# Prepare for USMLE - Privacy statement

      **Privacy statement**

The core message is that "Prepare for USMLE" will never sell or distribute your personal information.

"Prepare for USMLE" makes every effort to assure your confidentiality and keep your trust and do not sell, give or otherwise distribute your private information to any third party unless you have given permission through your subscriptions.  
"Prepare for USMLE" collects personal information from you (name, email, or other information) during registration you complete to add resourses.  
The information you provide allows us to inform you about major updates of our page. You also have the opportunity to decline to receive any mailings from "Prepare for USMLE".  
"Prepare for USMLE" uses reasonable precautions to keep secure the information disclosed. However "Prepare for USMLE" is not responsible for any breach of security or for any actions of any third parties which receive the information
