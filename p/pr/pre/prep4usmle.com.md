# Prep4usmle.com: Privacy Policy

Prep4USMLE.com - Privacy Policy

Information collected from our members, such as email addresses, is never sold or otherwise redistributed to any third parties. Tracking of user activity is soley for internal purposes in order to provide you with the best possible experience.

Optional information such as location, interests, occupation etc is displayed in member profiles for all to see. If you don't wish such information to be public, do not provide it.

Your IP address may be used to help diagnose problems with our server, to administer our Web site or to gather broad demographic information.

Prep4USMLE.com contains links to other web sites and it is not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

We may use outside ad companies to display advertisements which may contain cookies to which we do not have access.

In addition, you may wish to review Prep4USMLE.com - [Terms & Conditions](http://www.prep4usmle.com/terms/).
