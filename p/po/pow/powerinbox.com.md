# Privacy Policy - PowerInbox

#### PowerInbox Privacy Policy

We (PowerInbox Inc. and/or PowerInbox Ltd. and/or ActivePath Ltd. or “POWERINBOX”) know that you care about how your personal information is used and shared, and we take your privacy seriously. By visiting the powerinbox.com website or other websites owned by us (“site”), or by using any other services or products on or through the site, you acknowledge that you accept the practices and policies outlined in this PowerInbox Privacy Policy.

This PowerInbox Privacy Policy covers our treatment of personally identifiable information (“Personal Information”) and other information we gather when you are accessing our site or when you use our services or products. This policy does not apply to (and we are not responsible for) the practices of companies that we do not own or control or to individuals we do not employ or manage.

##### The Privacy Philosophy of POWERINBOX and Security

Your privacy is important to POWERINBOX. However, due to the nature of Internet communications and evolving technologies, unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure, and other factors, the security of user information may be compromised at any time. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. We welcome any comments and suggestions for improvement at: [support@powerinbox.com](mailto:support@powerinbox.com).

  1. ##### **THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND WHY**

**Website Visitors.** As a visitor to the site, we collect the following types of information:

    * **Cookies.** When you visit our site we will send your browser a cookie which is stored in your browser and allows us to uniquely identify it. We use cookies to help us better understand our users and our product which allows us to improve our service. This data may include storing user preferences and product utilization information such as number of visitors, average time spent on pages, features used, and other information which helps us determine which features are most important to you and working effectively.
    * **Logs.** When you visit our site or use our services or products we capture and store the data that your browser sends us. This data may include your browser type, language, Internet Protocol address, the date and time of your request and the cookie that helps us identify your unique browser.
    * **Usage Information.** Generally, our service automatically collects usage information, such as the numbers and frequency of visitors to our site and its components. We only use this data in aggregate form, that is, as a statistical measure, and not in a manner that would identify you personally. This type of aggregate data enables us to figure out how often customers use parts of our site or services or products so that we can improve our services and products performance.
    * **Communication.** Anytime you send us a communication we may keep this communication so that we can respond to your requests or inquiries and help improve our services to you.
    * **Information You Provide.** We also receive and store any information you enter on our site or provide to us in any other way. The types of Personal Information collected may include your name, user name and email address. The Personal Information you provide is used for such purposes as improving the content of our site, customizing and improving our services, and communicating with you about specials and new features.
    * **Other Sites.** This PowerInbox Privacy Policy only applies to the powerinbox.com site and other sites owned by PowerInbox. We do not exercise control over any third party sites that our sites may link to. These other sites may place their own cookies or other files on your computer, collect data or solicit personal information from you.
  2. ##### **HOW WE SHARE INFORMATION**

    * **Protection of POWERINBOX and Others.** We may share your Personal Information and other information if we have a good faith belief that it is required to: (1) comply with law, regulation, subpoena or court order; (2) detect, prevent or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues; (3) enforce the provisions of this PowerInbox Privacy Policy and/or any other agreements between you and POWERINBOX, including investigation of potential violations thereof; or (4) protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of POWERINBOX, its visitors, users and/or the public.
    * **Business Transfers.** If POWERINBOX or substantially all of its assets were acquired, or in the unlikely event that POWERINBOX goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. Accordingly, we may transfer or assign all the information we have collected as part of a merger, acquisition, sale or other change of control. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer may continue to use your Personal Information and other information as set forth in this policy.
    * **Non-Personal Information.** We may share your non-personally identifiable information with partners and other third parties for the purpose of helping us improve the POWERINBOX platform and the overall performance of our service, or allow us to provide recommendations. For example, utilizing data to determine the probability that a consumer will enjoy a specific POWERINBOX service or product.
    * **Data Providers.** We may also engage one or more data providers who may collect web log data from you (including IP address and information about your browser or operating system), or place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser to enable you to receive customized ads or content. These cookies contain no personally identifiable information. The cookies may reflect de-identified demographic or other data linked to data you voluntarily have submitted to us (e.g., your email address) that we may share with a data provider solely in hashed, non-human readable form. You can opt-out of these data provider cookies by going to [http://www.aboutads.info/choices](http://www.aboutads.info/choices/).
    * **With Your Consent.** Except as set forth above, we will only share your Personal Information with third parties with your prior consent.
  3. ##### **INFORMATION SECURITY**

We endeavor to take security measures to guard against unauthorized access to the systems where we store your data. This includes internal reviews of our data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures and physical security measures. However, despite the measures we take we cannot warrant the security of any information provided to us. Unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure, and other factors, may compromise the security of user information at any time.

  4. ##### **UPDATING YOUR INFORMATION**

Through the process provided on our site, we allow you to access certain information about you for the purpose of viewing, and in certain situations, updating that information. The types of information that you can access and update will change as our site changes. You may request deletion of your account by sending an e-mail to [support@powerinbox.com](mailto:support@powerinbox.com) and responding affirmatively to any confirmation e-mails that we may send. Please note that some information may remain in our records after deletion of your account.

  5. ##### **POLICY CHANGES**

We may make changes to this policy from time to time. If we make changes in the way we use Personal Information, we will notify you by posting an announcement on our site or sending you an email. Users are bound by any changes to the PowerInbox Privacy Policy when he or she uses our site or services after such changes have been first posted.

If you have feedback, questions or concerns contact us at [support@powerinbox.com](mailto:support@powerinbox.com)
