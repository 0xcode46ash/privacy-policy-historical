# Site Policy

**_Note: The link to privacy policies at the bottom of each page on this site takes you to the privacy policies of Ezoic, which supplies the advertising for this site. Many of these policies do not pertain to this site. The policies that do pertain to this site are reproduced here._**

**_Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on poemsforfree.com. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to poemsforfree.com and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network site policy at the following URL:<http://google.com/privacy_ads.html>_**

**_Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include Google Adsense, Commission Junction, Widget Bucks, Adbrite, Clickbank, Epic / Azoogle, Chitika, Linkshare, Amazon, Kontera, Advertising.com, CPAStorm, Burst, Valueclick, InfoLinks, Vibrant Media, 24/7 Real Media, Tribal Fusion, Casale, Tremor Media, Adify._**

**_These third-party ad servers or ad networks apply technology to the advertisements and links that appear on poemsforfree.com that send them directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see._**

**_These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, go to the following URL:[ http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp)_**

**_Poemsforfree.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers._**

**_You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. Poemsforfree.com's site policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites._**

**_If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites._**

**_We ourselves do not collect any information about you whatever. If you click on the links to follow us on YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, or Google Plus, that information goes directly to the sites on which you follow us. If you click on the link to subscribe to the Poem of the Day, that information goes directly to Feedburner, a service of Google._**

_**If you e-mail me, your e-mail address will not be released to third parties for any purpose whatsoever. No information you give me will be released to any organization, commercial or otherwise, for any purpose whatsoever.**_

_**However, transmissions to and from this web site are not encrypted, and therefore total confidentiality of the information you supply cannot be guaranteed.**_

**_By using our site, you consent to our web site site policy._**
