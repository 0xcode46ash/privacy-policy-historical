PokemonDeluge's services are provided on an 'as is' basis without warranties of any kind. PokemonDeluge makes no guarantee of availability of service and reserves the right to change or remove any functionality or feature of PokemonDeluge's services. In no event shall PokemonDeluge be liable for any damages arising out of the use of or inability to use PokemonDeluge's services or any content there on.

When you visit our website, PokemonDeluge DOES NOT collect any personally identifiable information about you such as name, address, or phone number. However, We do obtain IP addresses and some types of non-personally identifiable information such as the ISP, browser etc. This information collected by us is mostly used for the purpose of targeted advertising (so that the ads displayed to you are relevant to you and the region you live in) and tracking user trends.

PokemonDeluge, like many other sites, uses 'cookies' to store session data to allow you to stay logged in ,save your preferences and collect statistics of the site (helps to see what features are popular among users).

Your emails are only used for password recovery,in case you forget your password. They will never be sold, or given to a third-party. Also, emails will never be displayed in your profile when others view it.

To catch scammers and phishers, our systems scan messages containing certain phrases to warn the receiver of the message. We are not responsible if your account gets stolen. [Learn how you can keep your account safe](http://pokemondeluge.info/Protecting_your_Account).

The chat is not run by PokemonDeluge and is not responsible for what goes on in chatroom.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
