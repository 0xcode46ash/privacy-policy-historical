# Privacy - Support - Pokemon World Online

Pokemon World Online and its employees will never ask you for your password or account information.

Your email address used upon registration will not be shared with the public, nor will it be given to third party companies. eMail security is very important, and as such it will remain strictly within our records.

You are not required to provide specific details about yourself, including your name, address, or other personal aspects of your life.

Pokemon World Online refuses to sell or otherwise give personal information about its players to third parties.

Pokemon World Online will not gather or store personal information about its players (unless the information is provided willingly, such as PayPal information for memberships).
