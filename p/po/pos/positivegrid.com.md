# Positive Grid – privacy

### Positive Grid Respects Your Privacy

Use of Personal Information

Positive Grid may use the information it collects to provide the services and information you select, to better understand its customers, to provide improved products, define effective customer services, and to assist with site administration. An “Unsubscribe” link is included in the footer of most correspondence.

With your permission, we may also use this information to send you information about Positive Grid products, the effective use of Positive Grid products, tutorials for audio recording or information Positive Grid feels will be of interest to you, such as product updates or introductions.

Positive Grid will not share or sell or otherwise distribute your information or its collected information with any third party partner.

Aggregation and Use of Anonymous Data

Positive Grid may from time to time aggregate information provided by you to create “anonymous” statistical and demographic data. This anonymous data helps Positive Grid to understand and know its customers better.

As you browse Positive Grid, advertising cookies will be placed on your computer so that we can understand what you are interested in. Our display advertising partner, AdRoll, then enables us to present you with retargeting advertising on other sites based on your previous interaction with Positive Grid. The techniques our partners employ do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address or telephone number. You can visit this page to opt out of AdRoll and their partners’ targeted advertising.
