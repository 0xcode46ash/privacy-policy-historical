# Privacy Statement

Privacy Statement                                                                                            Effective 3/26/14
Dear Customer,
The Portillo Restaurant Group* is concerned about protecting your privacy and the safety of all our Web site
visitors. Before you use The Portillo Restaurant Group Web site, submit any personal information or place an
order through Portillo’s E-store, please read this message.
A Special Note for Parents
The Portillo Restaurant Group believes that parents and guardians should supervise their children's online
activities and consider using parental control tools available from online services and software
manufacturers that help provide a child-friendly online environment. These tools can also prevent children
from disclosing online their name, address, and other personal information without your permission.
What This Site Contains
Our Web site contains information about the various restaurant concepts within The Portillo Restaurant
Group, including Portillo's Hot Dogs, Honey-Jam Cafe, Luigi's House, Julian’s Bar and Portillo's Home
Kitchen. Check out the locations nearest you, or the entertainment available at Julian’s Bar. You can also
obtain information about working for Portillo's! When visiting our Web site, don’t forget to browse in our E-
Store and order some favorite items to ship to your friends. Be sure to visit us in person to discover the
great food and quality service you've been waiting for!
Information Collected
The Portillo Restaurant Group is very sensitive to the privacy concerns of the visitors to its Web site. As a
general policy, no personal information is collected automatically from visitors to our Web site. However,
certain non-personal information of visitors is recorded through the standard operation of The Portillo
Restaurant Group's Internet servers. This information is primarily used to provide an enhanced online
experience for the visitor. Information tracked includes the type of operating system in use (e.g., Macintosh,
Windows) and the type of browser being used by the visitor (e.g., Netscape, Internet Explorer).
Other uses of this information include internal review of the number of visitors to the site, but only in an
aggregate and non-personally identifiable form. E-mail addresses and other personally identifiable
information (such as first and last name, home or other physical address, telephone number) about visitors
to this site are known only when voluntarily submitted. Such voluntary submissions include emails sent to
us, registration for a mailing list, placement of an order through Portillo’s E-store and creation of an account
on our Web site. A visitor who creates an account on our Web site may login to his or her account to edit
the information provided.
Unless otherwise disclosed during the collection or required by law, personally identifiable information that
may be collected in connection with a visit to this site is retained by The Portillo Restaurant Group and is not
sold, transferred or otherwise disclosed outside the company. All information gathered online will be used
only by The Portillo Restaurant Group for internal marketing analysis and to improve our products, services
and Web site. Terms for information collection within the E-store are governed by the E-store Provisions.
Please read these terms before shopping at Portillo's E-store.
To the extent that any of the sites accessible through links from The Portillo Restaurant Group's Web site
have different practices, the different practices will be applicable at those sites.
Use of Cookies
When you visit our Web site, we may store some information on your computer. This information will be in
the form of a "Cookie," and will help us in several ways. For example, Cookies allow us to tailor our Web site
to better match your interests and preferences. With most Internet browsers, you can erase Cookies from
your computer hard drive, block all Cookies, or receive a warning before a Cookie is stored. Please refer to
your browser instructions or help screen to learn more about these functions.
E-Store Provisions:
Ordering Information We Collect Online for Portillo's E-Store
When a customer places an order online, we collect information associated with the transaction. This
information includes the customer's name, mailing address, products ordered, shipping addresses, and
credit card information. We ask for this information so that we may fulfill the order. The information is
stored on our customer file in a secure environment.
*Refers collectively to Portillo’s Hot Dogs, Inc. and its subsidiaries, Portillo’s of Arizona, LLC and Portillo’s Hot Dogs of Indiana, LLC,
Portillo’s Food Service, Inc., Honey-Jam Cafe, LLC, Portillo’s Restaurant Systems, Inc. d/b/a Luigi’s House and Golden Dogs, Inc.


When a customer places an online order, we will send an e-mail confirmation of the order. No e-mail
acknowledgement is sent for orders placed by telephone. We also may occasionally send an e-mail featuring
a special promotion or product that may be of interest to our customers. In addition, when a customer
places an order online, we add the customer's name and address to our mailing list. If a customer does not
wish to receive our mailings, he or she can send the mailing label to the following address:
         The Portillo Restaurant Group
         Attn: Guest Services
         2001 Spring Road, Suite 500
         Oak Brook, IL 60523
Use of Information We Collect
The Portillo Restaurant Group does not sell or share information gathered on our website with third parties.
Information gathered online will be used by The Portillo Restaurant Group for internal marketing analysis
and to improve our products, services, and Web site. In addition, name and address information collected in
the E-store will be used for future mailings and promotions. If you do not wish to receive these mailings or
promotional materials, please send us an e-mail at guestservices@portillos.com requesting to be removed
from our mailing list.
