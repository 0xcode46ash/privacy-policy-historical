# The Portillo Restaurant Group - Privacy Statement

#### Privacy Statement

**Dear Customer,**   
The Portillo Restaurant Group is very concerned about protecting your privacy and the safety of all our Web site visitors. Before you use The Portillo Restaurant Group Web site, submit any personal information or place an order through the Portillo E-store, please read this message. 

**A Special Note for Parents**   
The Portillo Restaurant Group believes that parents and guardians should supervise their children's online activities and consider using parental control tools available from online services and software manufacturers that help provide a kid-friendly online environment. These tools can also prevent children from disclosing online their name, address, and other personal information without your permission. 

**What This Site Contains**   
The Portillo Restaurant Group's Web site contains information about the various Portillo Restaurant entities, including Portillo's Hot Dogs, Barney's, Barnelli's, Key Wester, Hemingway's Bar, Luigi's House and Portillo's Home Kitchen. Check out the locations nearest you, or the entertainment available at Hemingway's Bar. You can also obtain information about working for Portillo's! Before you leave our site, be sure to check out our E-Store and ship your favorite item to your friends. When you click off, jump in your car and visit us in person to discover the great food and quality service you've been waiting for! 

**Information Collected**   
The Portillo Restaurant Group is very sensitive to the privacy concerns of the visitors to its site on the Internet. As a general policy, no personal information is automatically collected from visitors to the site. However, certain non-personal information of visitors is recorded by the standard operation of The Portillo Restaurant Group's Internet servers. This information is primarily used to provide an enhanced online experience for the visitor. Information tracked includes the type of operating system in use (e.g., Macintosh, Windows) and the type of browser being used by the visitor (e.g., Netscape, Internet Explorer). 

Other uses of this information include internal review of the number of visitors to the site but only in an aggregate and non-personally identifiable form. E-mail addresses and other personally identifiable information (such as a first and last name, home or other physical address, telephone number) about visitors to this site are known only when voluntarily submitted. This includes such things as submittal of an e-mail, registration for a mailing list, or placing an order with Portillo's E-store. 

Unless otherwise disclosed during the collection or required by law, personally identifiable information that may be collected in connection with visitors to this site, is retained by The Portillo Restaurant Group and is not sold, transferred or otherwise disclosed outside the company. All information gathered online will be used only by The Portillo Restaurant Group for internal marketing analysis and to improve our products, services and Web site. Terms for information collection within the e-store are governed by the E-store Provisions. Please read these before shopping at the Portillo's E-store. 

To the extent that any of the sites accessible through The Portillo Restaurant Group's Web site have different practices, those different practices will be applicable at those sites. 

**Use of Cookies**   
When you view our Web site, we may store some information on your computer. This information will be in the form of a "Cookie" and will help us in many ways. For example, Cookies allow us to tailor the site to better match your interests and preferences. With most Internet Browsers, you can erase Cookies from your computer hard drive, block all Cookies, or receive a warning before a Cookie is stored. Please refer to your Browser instructions or help screen to learn more about these functions. 

**E-Store Provisions:**   
Ordering Information We Collect Online for Portillo's E-Store   
When a customer places an order online, we collect information associated with the transaction. This information includes the customer's name, mailing address, products ordered, shipping addresses, and credit card information. We ask for this information so that we may fulfill the order. The information is stored on our customer file in a completely secure environment. 

When a customer places an online order, we will send a confirming e-mail message. No e-mail acknowledgement is sent for orders placed by telephone. We may also send an occasional e-mail featuring a special promotion or product that may be of interest to our customers. In addition, when a customer places an order online, we add the customer's name and address to our mailing list. If a customer does not wish to receive our mailings, they can send the mailing label to the following address: 

The Portillo Restaurant Group   
2001 Spring Road, Suite 500   
Oak Brook, IL 60523-2032   
Attn: Catering Department 

**Use of Information We Collect**   
The Portillo Restaurant Group does not sell or release any information gathered on our Web site to any third parties. Information gathered online will be used by The Portillo Restaurant Group for internal marketing analysis and to improve our products, services, and Web site. In addition, name and address information collected in the E-store will be used for future mailings and promotions. If you don't wish to receive these mailings or promotions, please send us an E-mail confirming your request to be removed from our list.
