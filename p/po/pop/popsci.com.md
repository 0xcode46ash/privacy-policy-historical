# Popular Science | Privacy Policy

The site you are viewing follows the PopSci.com/Popular Science Privacy Policy. The PopSci.com/Popular Science web site is maintained by Time4 Media, 2 Park Avenue, New York, NY, 10016. This policy covers all web site services offered by PopSci.com/Popular Science and its partners, including bulletin boards, registration, contests, promotions, E-mail, e-commerce, and the like. 

PopSci.com/Popular Science collects no personally identifying information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals. PopSci.com/Popular Science may place a "cookie" in the browser files of a PopSci.com/Popular Science user's computer. The cookie itself does not contain any personally identifying information although it will enable PopSci.com/Popular Science to relate a user's use of PopSci.com/Popular Science to information that the user has specifically and knowingly provided to PopSci.com/Popular Science. 

A user's personally identifying information may be used by PopSci.com/Popular Science for editorial purposes. PopSci.com/Popular Science may also use such information for marketing and promotional purposes and may share the information with companies that it has pre-screened. Individuals always have the ability to stop their information from being used for such purposes. Users' information may be shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis. 

A user's personally identifying information will not be transferred to any other party other than PopSci.com/Popular Science's e-commerce and community services partners unless otherwise stated at the time of collection. While PopSci.com/Popular Science will seek to require other third parties with whom it has agreements to follow appropriate privacy policies, PopSci.com/Popular Science does not bear any responsibility for any actions or policies of such third parties. In addition, this site contains links to other sites. PopSci.com/Popular Science is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

PopSci.com/Popular Science users should be aware that when they voluntarily disclose personal information (e.g., user name, E-mail address) on the bulletin boards that information can be collected and used by others and may result in unsolicited messages from other posters or parties. 

KIDS: Be sure to ask your parents for permission before you send any information about yourself to us or anyone else over the Internet. 

PARENTS: Any such information collected by PopSci.com/Popular Science from children is used for editorial participation and feedback purposes, together with any other purposes that will be described where the information is collected, and will not be transferred to any third party. 

Your E-mail address is used in in the following manner: 

We often add new products and features to our services and we want you to be fully informed of the changes that affect your community. We will occasionally send you updates about our service. PopSci.com/Popular Science does not sell or trade the E-mail addresses of its users. 

We may use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in PopSci.com/Popular Science in accordance with our Privacy Policy, cookies received with banner ads are collected by outside ad companies and are bound by their respective privacy policies. 

Security:   
PopSci.com/Popular Science has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. 

Choice/Opt-Out: 

PopSci.com/Popular Science provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at our Web site. 

PopSci.com/Popular Science gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service.
