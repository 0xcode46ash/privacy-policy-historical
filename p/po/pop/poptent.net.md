# Privacy Policy: Poptent Media

Your privacy is important to you, the user ("You") and to Poptent Inc. ("Poptent"). Poptent will operate its web site, www.poptentmedia.com ("Site") by the following principles. The principles apply to information that personally identifies You ("Personal Information"). BY CONSENTING TO THE TERMS OF USE FOR THE SITE, OR BY ACCESSING THE SITE, YOU CONSENT TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY (the "Policy"). 

Poptent is also committed to complying fully with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998. Our information collection practices are described below. We urge kids to check with their parents before entering information on any website and we urge parents to discuss with their kids restrictions regarding the online release of personal information to anyone they don’t know. This is a commercial web site intended for people over the age of 13. Our email address and contact forms are not for use by those under the age of 13. In order to comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) we have elected to prohibit the use of our contact forms by pre-teens. By utilizing our contact form(s) you certify that you are at least 13 years of age or older.

Please do not contact or email us if you are under the age of 13 unless you have uploaded the [Parental Notice and Consent Form](https://web.archive.org/resources/poptent-parental-consent-form.pdf), completed and signed by your parents.

* ### Personal Information

The portion of the Site accessible to the general public will not expressly ask for information that personally identifies You or that allows the Site to contact You. However, the Site will allow You to register and establish a profile containing information selected by You to showcase Yourself to Your peers and to the brand personnel that will be visiting the Site to see ads created for their brands ("Creator Profile"). You acknowledge and agree that any information that you place in a Creator Profile will be accessible by all users of the Site. Information contained in the Creator Profile is not considered Personal Information under this Agreement. 

Portions of the Site will expressly ask you to register and generate a user ID ("Your Site Unique ID") and password ("Site Password") in order to access and use such portions. Certain other portions may require additional codes. The information that You provide to Poptent during the Poptent member registration or renewal process, including without limitation the information listed below, together with any additional information You provide to Poptent and information regarding the manner in which You use Your Poptent membership is Personal Information, will not be processed or disclosed by Poptent except as permitted by this Policy or as required or allowed by applicable law or legal process.

* ### Accuracy of Personal Information

When possible, the Site will provide You with the means to make sure that Your Member information is correct and current. You may view Your member information by entering the restricted portions of the Site using Your Site Unique ID and Site Password and certain other codes as necessary. To correct any factual inaccuracies, You must edit Your Member Information and hit submit. 

* ### Protection of Personal Information

Poptent endeavors to protect the security of Personal Information collected on the Site from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. The Site uses an encrypted connection when transferring sensitive Personal Information. Poptent also has put in place physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to help safeguard and secure the information Poptent collects online in accordance with industry standards. When Poptent contracts with third parties to perform any services supporting the Site, Poptent will require that the third parties protect the security of the Personal Information collected with comparable security standards. 

Neither passwords nor security systems are foolproof or necessarily work as intended, including encryption, and people can commit intentional crimes, make mistakes or fail to follow policies. Although Poptent takes what we believe are commercially reasonable security precautions, Poptent does not guaranty security. You agree to do what You reasonably can to help promote security (e.g., never reveal your password or make it accessible to others). 

If You or any other person believes they are a victim of identity theft entitled by law to request information from Poptent, write Poptent at Poptent' contact address in principle 4 below and also provide all required information. Poptent will supply without charge the information legally required to be disclosed that Poptent then has, subject to applicable law and reserving all of our rights and defenses. 

* ### Use of Personal Information & Opt-Out Policy

Poptent uses Your Personal Information to operate the Site, to contact You (including without limitation regular e-mail alerts); to effect, enforce, investigate, collect on and account for transactions related to the Site; to adjust Poptent' offerings of products and services; to provide information to sponsors; and to comply with applicable law. Poptent may provide Your Personal Information, or You may provide Your information directly, to sponsors and third parties who are responsible for implementing some of Poptent' services (e.g., third party hosting). Unique identifiers are used to verify Your identity. 

Poptent uses Your Internet Protocol (IP) address to analyze trends, track a user's movement through the Site, help diagnose problems with Poptent' server, and to administer the Site. IP addresses are not linked to Personal Information. 

Poptent uses "cookies" to save Your Site Unique ID and Site Password so that You have the option of choosing not to have to re-enter them each time You visit the Site. A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to Your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to You and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to You. 

The Site contains links to other sites, including to sponsor's sites. Poptent is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites or of sponsors. 

Poptent also uses outside companies to host portions of the Site and the ads. Poptent will require that these third parties do not further use or disclose Your Personal Information for any purpose other than providing the services. 

Please be aware that this Policy and any choices You make on the Site will not necessarily apply to Personal Information you may have provided to Poptent in the context of other, separately operated, Poptent products or services.
