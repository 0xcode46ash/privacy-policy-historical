> **_Contests & Giveaways_**
>
>> For contests, we may ask visitors for contact information (name, address, phone number) and/or demographic information (postal code, occupation, or interests). The customer's contact information is used to contact him or her when e-mail does not work. The demographic information is used to find industry targetted sponsors. We only analyze group numbers -- never singling out an individual's preferences or information.
> 
> **_Website Logs_**
>
>> pocketnow.com's web server compiles a log file of visitors to our site. This collects the same standard information that most web server software does. This includes the type of browser you are using, your IP addresses, incoming domain, time, date, and pages viewed.
> 
> **_NAI Compliance_**
>
>> We use third-party advertising companies to serve our ads on our Web site. These third-party advertising companies employ cookie and 1 x 1 pixel GIF images or web beacons to measure and improve the effectiveness of ads for their clients. To do so, these companies may use anonymous information about your visits to our website and other websites. This information can include: date/time of banner ad shown, the banner ad shown, utilized cookie, and current IP address. This information can also be used for online preference marketing purposes.
>> 
>> If you want to prevent a third-party advertiser from collecting data, currently you may either visit each ad network's web site individually and opt out or visit the NAI gateway opt-out site to opt-out of all network advertising cookies. [**Click here**](http://www.networkadvertising.org/optout_nonppii.asp) for the NAI gateway opt-out site. This site will also allow you to review the third-party advertising companies’ privacy policies.
>
