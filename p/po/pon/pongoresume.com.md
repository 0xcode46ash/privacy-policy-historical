# Privacy - Pongo Resume

## Privacy

**Privacy Statement, Security, Copyright, and Terms of Use**

**I. Privacy Statement**

Pongo, LLC, is committed to protecting the privacy of each user of our applications. We take the security of information seriously and have established security standards and procedures to guard against unauthorized access to application user information. Pongo believes your personal information should be protected. We do not disclose or sell personal information such as your name, address, email address, or telephone number to third parties for any purpose, as part of a mailing list, or otherwise, without your permission. However, we do retain the right, in our sole discretion, to offer you products/services of third party providers in which we think you may have an interest. Additionally, we will take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, and alteration. Pongo reserves the right to compile aggregate (not individual) demographic information provided by Users of the site to continually improve the usefulness of the site, and for our own statistical and marketing purposes.Â Users receive email newsletters and account updates from Pongo Resume and may opt out of these at any time by following instructions included in the email or by contacting our Customer Service department at 866-486-4660 or online at <http://www.pongoresume.com/content/3/support.cfm>.

**II. Security Statement**

Our security system protects communications between your browser and our web server using industry-standard 128-bit SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption. You can tell when data is being transmitted in a secure manner by looking for the secure key or lock icons at the bottom or your browser window.

**III. Cookies  
**

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. We use cookies on this site.

**IV. Log Files  
**

Like most standard web site servers we use log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.

If the user has questions or concerns about the information that is presented here, he or she can forward them to Pongo at support@pongoresume.com, or contact Support Services, Pongo, LLC, 44 Bearfoot Road, Northborough, MA 01532 USA.
