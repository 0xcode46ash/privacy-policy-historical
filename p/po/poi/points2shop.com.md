# Points2shop - Earn Points for Free Rewards

## Privacy Policy Points2Shop

**3\. Anonymous Access**

3.1 Visitors can access the web site home pages and browse the sites without disclosing personal data.

**4\. Services and Links**

4.1 Points2Shop LLC web sites allow communication with other visitors and posts information that can be accessed by other visitors. Communication via these posts is voluntary. All information communicated is the responsibility and discretion of the individual and may be collected by other visitors.

4.2 Points2Shop LLC sites may include links to third-party Web service providers. Points2Shop LLC is not responsible for the content, accuracy, or privacy policies of third-party web services.

**5\. Automatic Data Collection**

5.1 Points2Shop LLC may automatically log personal information by means such as programming or link information automatically logged by such means with personal data about specific individuals for purposes of technical and customer administration.

5.2 Points2Shop LLC employs the use of cookies to store personal data or links information stored in cookies with personal data about specific individuals for purposes of technical and customer administration.

**6\. Data Collection and Purpose**

6.1 Points2Shop LLC collects data volunteers while using our services.

6.2 Points2Shop LLC does not collect information about visitors from other sources, such as public records or bodies, or private organizations.

6.3 Access to collected personal data is available upon request.

6.4 Postal address information is only used to receive information for which the address is provided. This includes periodic contact from Points2Shop LLC to enquire about customer satisfaction and the status of customer accounts.

6.5 Telephone numbers collected by Points2Shop LLC are only used to promote and support affairs internal to Points2Shop LLC and are not shared or distributed to third parties for any reason. 

**7\. Children's Privacy**

7.1 Points2Shop LLC does not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 13. Minors 13 to 18 are permitted to access the services of the site only with parental permission. It is the responsibility of the individual and the parent to ensure such permissions are obtained.

7.2 Points2Shop LLC does not take specific steps to protect the privacy of children who disclose personal data.

7.3 Points2Shop LLC does not provide information about personal data practices on the home pages, or at those sites where personal data is collected.

**8\. Disclosure**

8.1 Points2Shop LLC does not disclose personal data to subsidiaries or other organizations without your permission.

**9\. Confidentiality and Security**

9.1 Points2Shop LLC does not employ the use of secure transmission methods to send personal data.

9.2 Points2Shop LLC has implemented security policies, rules and technical measures to protect the personal data from unauthorized access, improper use or disclosure, unauthorized modification and unlawful destruction or accidental loss.

9.3 Points2Shop LLC employees and data processors with access or association to the processing of personal data are obliged to respect the confidentiality of visitor personal data.

9.4 Points2Shop LLC does not disclose personal data to State institutions and authorities unless required by law.

**10\. Access to Personal Data**

10.1 Visitors may request access to their own specific personal information collected by Points2Shop LLC by e-mailing a request to [info@points2shop.com](mailto:info@points2shop.com) or visiting the member account information section of the web sites: [http://www.points2shop.com/members/account/](https://web.archive.org/account/index) or, [http://www.cashle.com/members/account/](https://web.archive.org/account/index)

10.2 Access to personal information may require proof of identity

10.3 Access to personal data is provided without charge.

10.4 Personal data may be challenged or updated and ,where appropriate, data will be completed, erased, rectified or amended.

10.5 Points2Shop LLC reserves the right to refuse access to personal data. In such a case reasons will be given for refusal.

10.6 You can also close your account through our website. If you close your account, we will mark your account in our database as "Closed," but will keep your account information in our database. This is necessary to deter fraud or abuse of the site, by ensuring that persons who try to commit fraud or abuse of the site will not be able to avoid detection simply by closing their account and opening a new account. However, if you close your account, your personally-identifiable information will not be used by us for any further purposes, nor sold to or shared with third parties, except as necessary to prevent fraud and assist law enforcement, or as required by law.

**11\. Privacy Compliance**

11.1 The Points2Shop LLC privacy policy is compliant with the following international and national regulations:

11.1.1 Global: OECD Privacy Guidelines on the Protection of Privacy and Transborder Flows of Personal Data Guidelines

11.1.2 Global: United Nations Guidelines for the Regulation of Personal Data Files Adopted by General Assembly Resolution 45/95 of 14 December 1990

11.1.3 United States: Privacy Act of 1974

11.1.4 European Union: European Convention on Human Rights

11.1.5 Canada: Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act

11.2 Privacy compliance is ensured by periodic self-assessment.
