# Polk Audio privacy policy at polkaudio.com

Privacy Policy

This Website is maintained by Polk Audio, 5601 Metro Drive, Baltimore, MD 21215. 

Polk Audio uses the information furnished in response to its product registration forms and surveys to better understand the lifestyles and tastes of our customers, and to produce a better site for you to visit. We may also use the information to notify you of news that may interest you, but only at your request. 

**None of the information is sold or shared with other parties. You will receive no unwanted "spam" mail as a result of leaving information on this site.**

If you do not wish to receive future communications from Polk Audio, please contact us by phone at (800) 377-7655 or by email at [polkinfo@polkaudio.com](mailto:polkinfo@polkaudio.com).
