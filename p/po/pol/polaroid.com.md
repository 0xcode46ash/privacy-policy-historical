# Privacy Policy Statement

The purpose of this site is to provide you with information about our products and services, corporate information, and customer services. 

We collect:  


  0. the email addresses of those who communicate with us via email;  

  1. aggregate and user-specific information on what pages consumers access or visit;  

  2. information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations. 



The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web pages and is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes. 

We reserve the right to change this statement at any time. 

[Click here](https://web.archive.org/service/servfone.html) to find the Polaroid Customer Service contact number for your region of the world. 

Thank You.
