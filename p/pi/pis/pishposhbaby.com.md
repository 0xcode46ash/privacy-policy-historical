 **PishPoshBaby Privacy & Security Statement**

The information we request from each customer is their name, billing and/or shipping address, phone number, email address and payment information. We will use your information to respond to you, regarding the reason you contacted us. We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request, e.g. to ship an order. If you need to make any corrections to the information we have on file, you can contact us via email or phone.
