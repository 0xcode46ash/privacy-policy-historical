# PixTeller Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy

**PixTeller is founded on the principle of helping people create new images. We know that you care about how your personal information is used and shared, and we take your privacy very seriously. By visiting the PixTeller website, you are accepting the practices outlined in this policy.**

This Privacy Policy covers PixTeller's treatment of personal information that PixTeller gathers when you are on the PixTeller website and when you use the "PixTeller services". This policy does not apply to the practices of third parties that PixTeller does not own or control, or to individuals that PixTeller does not employ or manage. 

## Information collected by PixTeller

We only collect personal information that is relevant to the purpose of our website, which is to enable users to discover and share information with one another. This information allows us to provide you with a customized and efficient experience. We do not process this information in a way that is incompatible with this objective. We collect the following types of information from our PixTeller users: 

**Information you provide:** We receive and store any information you enter on our website or provide to us in any other way. You can choose not to provide us with certain information, but then you may not be able to take advantage of many of our special features. 

**Registration:** In order for you to use PixTeller services, such as creating new posters , you must complete a registration form. As part of this registration form, we require select personal information (including your full name and e-mail address). 

**User Profile:** To allow you to express yourself beyond just the information collected during registration, we enable you to provide additional information, such as favorite URLs. In addition, you may choose to include photos of yourself in your profile. 

**Automatic Information:** We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with us. PixTeller and its authorized agents automatically receive and record certain "traffic data" on their server logs from your browser including your IP address, PixTeller cookie information, and the page you requested. PixTeller uses this traffic data to help diagnose problems with its servers, analyze trends and administer the website. 

**Links:** PixTeller may keep track of how you interact with links in posts across our Services including third party services and clients by redirecting clicks or through other means. We do this to help improve our Services, including advertising, and to be able to share aggregate click statistics such as how many times a particular link was clicked on. 

## PixTeller and Cookies

PixTeller uses third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. 

Advertisements served by Google, as a third party vendor, use DART cookies. These cookies are used to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy. 

## Email Communications

PixTeller is very concerned about your privacy and we will never provide your email address to a third party without your explicit permission. You may receive notification e-mails from PixTeller, which inform you of actions that have been performed on the site or system e-mails (e.g. those regarding forgotten user passwords) and legal notices (e.g. updates to our policies) from us. 

## Keeping Information Secure

Your PixTeller account information is protected by a password for your privacy and security. Protect against unauthorized access to your password and to your computer by logging off once you have finished using a shared computer. 

Only employees who need personal information to perform a specific job (for example, a customer service representative) are granted access to it. All of our employees are kept up to date on our privacy and security practices. 

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have personal information on a child under the age of 13, we will delete that information from our systems. 

## Changes to this Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version of the policy will govern our use of your information and will always be at PixTeller.com/privacy_policy. If we make a change to this policy that, in our sole discretion, is material, we will notify you via e-mail to the email associated with your account. By continuing to access or use the Services after those changes become effective, you agree to be bound by the revised Privacy Policy. 

## Your consent and contact information

By using the PixTeller Services and our web site, you consent to the collection and use of your information as we have outlined in this policy and to our Terms of Service Policy. PixTeller may decide to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of the information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. 

Feel free to contact us any time and we'll answer any additional questions you may have. If you have additional questions, feel free to [Contact us](http://www.pixteller.com/contact).
