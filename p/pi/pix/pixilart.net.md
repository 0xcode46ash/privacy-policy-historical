# Pixilart - About This Pixel Drawing Tool

##### Information We Collect

We collect cookies that are used to track and store user information throughout the website.

##### E-mail Addresses

We do not share or sell your e-mail address. Your email address is only used so you can login.

##### Social Login

We do not sell or share your information. Social logins use basic information to prevent spam and create secure logins.
