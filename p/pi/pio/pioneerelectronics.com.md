# Pioneer Electronics Privacy Policy

Pioneer Electronics Privacy Policy

> Pioneer has created this Privacy Statement to demonstrate our commitment to Visitor privacy. 
> 
> This Privacy Statement discloses Web site privacy practices for Pioneer. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Pioneer Web site.

**Visitor Information Collection and Use**

> Visitor IP address helps us diagnose problems and administer the site.  Visitor's computer provides IP address and Domain combination when logging on (unless blocking has been setup via Visitor's web browser).
> 
> A technology known as 'cookies' may be used to record Visitor activity while visiting the site. Cookies are used to save your logon password and remember items you purchase and place in your shopping cart.
> 
> Data collected during your visit is used to help tailor the visit by providing content to your preference. The information provided may be shared with other Pioneer companies, and aggregated (consolidated) demographic information may be shared with advertising third parties. 
> 
> At the time Visitor's personal information is being collected, consent will be requested. A statement of the purpose of collection, intended use, and user identification will be provided. Visitors choosing to  opt-out will have the following options: 
> 
>   * Email: [administrator@pioneerservice.com](mailto:administrator@pioneerservice.com)
> 
>   * Pioneer Contact Center: 1-800-421-1404 
> 
> 

> 
> Visitor's request for changes or deletion of personal information should be directed to the email address noted below.

**Links & Forums**

> To better serve Visitors, Pioneer will provide external links to other sites. Pioneer is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 
> 
> Public forums such as chat rooms, message boards, and/or news groups may be made available to Visitors. Pioneer encourages Visitors to remember that  information disclosed in public forums is public information, and caution should be exercised when disclosing personal information. 

**Security **

> Pioneer takes reasonable precautions to protect Visitor information. Sensitive information (Social Security Number, Credit Card Number) submitted online is protected via industry SSL (Secure Socket Layer). Offline, the same care is taken to protect all Visitor information.
> 
> As Pioneer continues to upgrade and enhance its Web sites, our commitment to Visitor Privacy remains paramount.
> 
> Questions regarding this Privacy Statement can be directed to:  
>  [administrator@pioneerservice.com](mailto:administrator@pioneerservice.com)
