# Privacy Policy | Pink is the New Blog | Everybody's Business Is My Business

## Privacy Policy

When you visit _Pink Is The New Blog_ , standard information such as your IP address and referral data is collected. _Pink_ also collections information you might choose to voluntarily submit, such as names, email addresses, mailing addresses, phone numbers, interests and preferences about our services, and other personal data. Generally this information is collected when visitors sign up for a user account, comment on the sites, initiative communication with _Pink_ , or enter a contest or promotion; none are required to visit or use a _Pink_ site.

_Pink_ does not rent or sell your personal information.

The site does place cookies on your computer. Our cookies come may from our publishing system and our advertisers. Cookies help store your name or email address when commenting or logging in to _Pink_. Our advertisers and our ad serving technology may place cookies placed on your computer for tracking purposes. _Pink_ does not monitor or control these cookies.
