# Privacy Policy - Dailypo

We recognize that your privacy is very important and take it seriously, which is why we developed this privacy policy to explain how we collect, use, communicate, disclose and make use of personal information.

## What is Pintaram?

Pintaram is an advanced Instagram marketing platform, aiming at helping brands, advertisers, and publishers find and explore the latest trends of hashtags, users, photos and videos on Instagram.

## Information Collection and Use

By logging in on Pintaram with Instagram account, you are authorizing us to collect the following info:

  * Access your basic information: Your media & profile info
  * Access your friend lists: Your follower & following lists
  * Access public content: Media & profile info of public users
  * Like photos: Post and delete likes on your behalf
  * Comment on photos: Post and delete comments on your behalf
  * Follow accounts: Follow and unfollow accounts on your behalf



## Links

Our site may contain links to third party websites which may or may not have affiliation with. These third party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. We do not share your personal information with those third parties except as discussed in this document.

## Cookies

Cookies are small files that Pintaram transfers to your computer's hard drive through your web browser (if you allow this) that enables Pintaram to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. Our cookies are linked to your login information so that you can use Pintaram from your computer throughout the day without logging in each time you visit us.
