## Collection of information from the Website

When using the Website you may voluntarily disclose personal information to us. Our server may automatically record details such as your internet address, domain name and the date and time of your visit to the Website. We use this information for statistical purposes.

We also use this information in order to provide you with a personalised experience when you visit the Website. Through the use of a cookie (a small data file that a website may write to your hard drive when you visit it), we can record the pages you visit and the information you require. Through analysis of these records, we provide you with an improved service when you revisit the Website. 

Many internet browsers are set up so that they accept cookies. You may be able to change these settings so that cookies are not accepted or you are provided with options to accept or reject them as they are sent to your browser.

## Why we collect personal information and consequences of not providing it to us

We collect personal information in order to notify you when the Website will become fully operational and to send you the PINCHme newsletter and other updates by email.

## Security of personal information

Irrespective of whether personal information is stored electronically or in hard copy form, we take reasonable steps to protect the personal information we hold from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification or disclosure.

## Access and correction

You have a right to access personal information we hold about you, subject to some exceptions allowed by law. We will comply with any such request except where the Privacy Act 1988 or the National Privacy Principles allow us to refuse to do so. There is no fee for making such a request, but we may charge for providing access. 

We take all reasonable precautions to ensure that the personal information we collect, use and disclose is accurate, complete and up-to-date. However, the accuracy of that information depends to a large extent upon the information you provide. If any personal information you provide to us changes, please let us know. Contact details are set out below.

## Privacy Complaints

If you wish to complain about any breach or potential breach of this Privacy Policy or the National Privacy Principles, please contact us. Contact details are set out below. Complaints will be considered and responded to within 21 days. We will always endeavour to resolve any complaint to your satisfaction. However, if you are unhappy with our response, you are entitled to contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner, which may investigate your complaint further.
