# Privacy Policy | Pin Payments

This Privacy Policy details the commitment from Southern Payment Systems to protect you and your customer's personal information. Complete Terms and Conditions are available within this website and are the authority in the case of inconsistency.

## Policy applicability

The Policy applies to this website (pin.net.au) and the Pin Payments platform (API). It does not apply to services or companies that are linked to from this website.

By using this website or Pin Payments, you are accepting the practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree to the Policy, please discontinue use of the website and Pin Payments . Continued use of Pin Payments is considered implicit agreement to this Policy.

## Information collection

We require information related to your business and your customers to facilitate your registration and use of Pin Payments.

In addition to the information you provide to us, Southern Payment Systems may collect information about your business from third parties for the purposes of identification and verification.

## Information sharing

Southern Payment Systems does not sell or rent you or your customer's personal information to marketers or third parties.

Your information may be used for purposes relating to the application and operation of Pin Payments which may involve interaction with financial and risk management organisations.

## Notification of changes to this policy

Southern Payment Systems reserves the right to alter this policy and you are encouraged to check it periodically for updates.
