# Welcome to Pin IMG , a free image upload solution. Simply browse, select, and upload!

Pin IMG policy is to respect and protect the privacy of our users. Pin IMG will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent.

Pin IMG will not sell or give your email address to anyone and you will never be placed on any lists. However, Pin IMG may disclose your information when Pin IMG believes in good faith that the law requires it.

Pin IMG may use your email address to inform you about Pin IMG major service changes, important updates, or special promotions.

Pin IMG website record your IP address but uses it only for internal purposes. It will never be given to any other person/company/website for any reason.

Pin IMG website uses cookies only to maintain your information as you move throughout this site. A cookie is a small piece of information used to identify an individual when the person is accessing a site. At no time is any personal information stored in a cookie, nor is any tracking information made available to other sites for any reason.

Pin IMG reserve the right to change this Policy anytime without prior notification. In this case the new Policy will be published at http://img.pinimg.com/info.php?act=privacy_policy

If you have any question, please do not hesitate to [Contact us](http://img.pinimg.com/contact.php?act=contact_us).
