# 

* * *

At PitchWeekly, we take your right to privacy very seriously. 

Our privacy policy is as follows: Unless stated otherwise, as in the case of prize drawings, giveaways, and special promotions, we never sell, give, lend, show, or otherwise share any information about our clients or visitors to our web site.

PitchWeekly does share general demographic information about our clients as a group with our partners. We decide when and with whom to share this information on a case-by-case basis. PitchWeekly DOES NOT share or sell your registration information to anyone outside of the company. All registration information is kept private. If you have any concerns whatsoever about our privacy policy, please send your comments to [pitch@pitch.com](mailto:pitch@pitch.com).
