# CSS CHOPPER'S Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy (“Privacy Statement”) owned by CSS Chopper reflects its commitment and transparency towards the privacy sustained for its customers and visitors, who interact with csschopper.com anyhow. Our privacy section highlights that we might use the IP address of our site visitors in order to administer and assist them in analyzing the problems using our web servers. For this reason, we require the personal and/ or contact information about the site users in order to provide effective information to them about the company. 

**Note:** csschopper.com may have shared the links and content of the varied other websites here, for which CSS Chopper is not lawfully accountable for any such content as well privacy practice, applicable to their websites. 

#### Gathering your personal Information voluntarily 

From the means you connect with us such as through order form, request form etc., we collect your personal information to identify you using your first name, last name, contact address, email address, Date of Birth, gender, phone number etc. Such type of information is voluntarily submitted by the user that helps them to communicate better the company. 

However, CSS Chopper pays stringent attention on how your sensitive information is used and shared. Additionally, we assure that your confidential information is perfectly safeguard with us and it will not be sold, disclosed, updated or shared with anybody in any case other than our reliable resources & partners that provide assistance and support to your project. 

#### Information collected through technology

Generally when you visit our website, our web servers gather the information about your IP address/ domain names in order to analyze the statistics such as number of visits made on the website, pages viewed, time spent on the site by the user etc. The purpose of evaluating the statistics is to take measurable steps in boosting the website services to enhance the customer’s experience and serving them far better than before. We collect information using cookies and navigational data (such as URL) to find the date & time of the visit, browser type, operating system etc. 

#### Use of gathered information

The gathered information that is, submitted by you or collected by us is helpful in making our services better for enhanced and personalized customer experience like quickly responding to the request against the queries, facilitating the quote, process transactions and so on. The gathered information will be shared with the third party in cases, if:

  * We feel that it is necessary in order to fulfill your ordered services
  * Providing better consultation as per your service request



However, we may also use your personal information in providing promotional emails, RSS feed, electronic newsletters and latest offers related to your business’ interests but only after your consent to “SUBSCRIBE” and at anytime it can be unsubscribe as per the request. 

#### Your Rights

You have complete rights to stop receiving marketing emails, newsletter, RSS Feed and other sort of electronic communication through “unsubscribe” feature selected as per your rights.

Each time you use CSS Chopper, you agree with the entire practices and policies demonstrated in the privacy statements. Subsequent use of our site and its services shall indicate that you are acquainted with the amendment made to the policies and agree the terms and conditions cordially.
