# Privacy Policy from Central Restaurant Products

At Central Products, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Central Products, Inc. takes precautions to ensure the security of your Company information and strives to keep it accurate. Central Products, Inc. carefully protects your Company information from loss, destruction, falsification , manipulation and unauthorized access or unauthorized disclosure. Central Products, Inc. may use your Company information to contact you about a Central Products, Inc. offer in support of your business needs or to conduct online surveys to understand better our customers' needs and profiles. If you choose not to have your Company information used to support our customer relationship, Central Products, Inc. will respect your choice. Central Products, Inc. does not now and does not intend to sell, rent or otherwise market your Company information to third parties. We use the information we collect on the site to make shopping possible and to enhance your overall shopping experience.
