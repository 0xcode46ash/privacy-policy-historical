# Bangkok serviced apartments Thailand service apartment rentals/for rent/rental - Centre Point

The Centre Point Serviced Apartments would like to express our gratitude to everybody who is interested in using the services provided in our www.centrepoint.com. 

In order to better serve and satisfy your needs, we will use your personal information that you give us through this website as well as in other ways to enhance the benefits of using our services. We have the policy to keep the privacy of your personal information strictly confidential as we understand your concern for the security of your information. However, by visiting our website, you are accepting the following practices. 

**Allow us to collect your personal information**

In using the services from this website, you will be requested to fill in some certain personal information, provide user ID, and password and/or other necessary information. This information will be stored in our database system and will provide you with the following benefits:

  * Make an online booking through our website.
  * You may receive an automatic e-mail confirmation when making a reservation on the Internet, and occasionally from our reservation sales agents for inquiries related to a booking you have made for any of our services and products.
  * Get free PR news and information i.e. make public announcement regarding products or services and inform you about special promotions. The services will be provided to you via e-mail communication but if you don't want to receive the information, we will provide you with the option not to receive the mails. 



**Accept our Security System in protecting your information**

  * The information that you provide us will be kept confidential in form of documents and electronic data as well as in every process of information transfer. It will be stored in the database server to protect from any losses, damages, changes and prevent other person from taking advantages from personal information without permission.
  * The Centre Point has no policy to provide or disclose users' personal information to the third party; not even name, address, e-mail address, statistics of website usage, or other personal information, or use the information for commercial benefits. It is the exception in case that the third person is involved in the transaction or as a service provider for any particular service on the website. Or, if the disclosure of information is demanded as a result of the enforcement of the law. For example, it will be disclosed in order to halt the argumentation with the users; or for verifying the credit card information.
  * The system operator or the customer service providers under the Centre Point's contract are not allowed to acquire users' information for personal benefits; and are required to keep the information as confidential as stated in the contract, except that the disclosure of information is requested by the Centre Point.
  * The Centre Point permits only the Centre Point's officers and Siam Commercial Bank's authorized officer to access users' information. Anyhow, that officers have to be well-trained and have to totally understand the privacy policy.
  * In case the Centre Point have to disclose users' personal information, with the reason out of that mentioned above, the Centre Point will contact the users for permission. However, it depends on the personal consideration of users whether or not he/she want to disclose the information. 



* If the users discover the inaccuracy of personal information, please contact the officer urgently.

**Links to Other Websites**

The privacy and security policy of this website does not cover the usage of content in website that is linked from Centre Point's website. Therefore, you should strictly follow the rules and regulation of that website because the Centre Point will not resume any responsibilities for any losses and damages which might occur from using services of other website.

**Changes of Policy**

The Centre Point reserves the right to change the policy in keeping the information of users without prior notice. However, if there are any changes regarding the privacy policy, the Centre Point will inform you via e-mail or other suitable methods to ensure that you are well acknowledged about the changes of the policy.
