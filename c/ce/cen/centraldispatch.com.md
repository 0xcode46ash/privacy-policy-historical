# Privacy Policy for CentralDispatch | The Auto Industry's Vehicle Shipping Marketplace

PLEASE READ THE FOLLOWING. THE FOLLOWING POLICIES CONCERN YOUR PRIVACY WHEN YOU ACCESS OR OTHERWISE USE THIS WEB SITE AND ITS SERVICES.

###### Non-Personal Information We Collect

When you enter this Web site, information is gathered from your Internet browser. This information may include your IP address (which allows us to determine the identity of your Internet Service Provider), your operating system and the type of browser software that you are using to access this Web site. This information allows us to upgrade this Web site as necessary to optimize its efficiency. All information obtained from your browser is anonymous, and no personal information is ever transmitted to us in this manner.

While we do not at this time use cookies to collect information about how you use this Web site, we may do so in the future. A "cookie" is a small text file that a Web site can place on your computer's hard drive in order, for example, to retain your preferences and/or registration information for your next visit to that Web site. The cookie transmits this information back to that Web site's operator. You can choose not to receive a cookie file by enabling your Web browser to refuse cookies or prompt you before accepting a cookie. Note that refusing to accept cookies may impact the performance of a Web site. 

###### Personal Information We Collect

Most portions of this Web site are "registration-required" pages that require you to submit personal information before we grant you access. Such information includes your name, company name, address, email address, phone numbers and other identifying information about you and your company, including, but not limited to, Employer Identification Number (EIN), Motor Carrier number, insurance and license information and business references. We may share the personal information you disclose to us on this Web site for any reason, including, but not limited to, within our company, with third parties who subscribe to this Web site, with third parties who work with us on marketing, product development, service enhancement and other similar matters, with third parties in connection with a sale of our company or with third parties in response to legal requests or requirements.

###### Security Precautions

Where appropriate, we may employ firewalls, encryption technology, user authentication systems and access control mechanisms to control access to our systems and data. However, you should exercise discretion with respect to the submission of any personal or financial information to this Web site. Transmitting information via the Internet is never completely secure and, therefore, any information submitted may be intercepted, collected, used or disclosed by others.

###### Links to Other Web Sites

All links to Web sites not part of our company are made without any representations or warranties relating to the integrity, security or legitimacy of such Web sites. This Privacy Policy cannot and does not apply to external Web sites and, once you leave this Web site, you are subject to that Web site's privacy policies and procedures. You should be certain to consult each external Web site's own privacy policy before providing personally identifiable information. If you have concerns about any linked Web site, we invite you to contact us.

###### Intended for Adults Only

This Web site is for use by adults only. We will not solicit, collect or maintain personal information from children under the age of 18.

###### Terms of Use

By accessing and browsing this Web site, you also accept the [Terms of Service](https://web.archive.org/memagree.htm) of this Web site. Nothing in this Privacy Policy limits or qualifies the Terms of Service, and the Terms of Service should be reviewed by you in their entirety.

###### Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of March 22, 2006. We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time by posting a modified version on this Web site.

###### Contact Us

If you have any questions about our company, this Web site or this Privacy Policy, please contact us by clicking on the [Contact Us](https://web.archive.org/contactus.htm) link on this Web site.
