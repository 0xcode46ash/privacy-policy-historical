# CertaPay [ Terms of Use ]______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

|  |   


Protecting your privacy is important to CertaPay (referred to as **"CertaPay"** ). This _Policy_ explains how CertaPay collects, uses, and discloses the personal information you may provide while using the CertaPay Web site and the services provided on or through the Web site (collectively, the **"Web Site"** ). 

  
YOUR CONSENT

1\. By using the Web Site, you signify your consent to CertaPay's collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information in accordance with this _Policy_. If you do not agree with this _Privacy _Policy__ , you may not use the Web Site.

  
INFORMATION AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED

2\. _Non-Identifiable Information_ \- Like many other Web sites, the Web Site automatically collects certain non-identifiable information regarding Web Site users, such as the Internet Protocol (IP) address of your computer, the IP address of your Internet Service Provider, the date and time you access the Web Site, the Internet address of the Web site from which you linked directly to the Web Site, the operating system you are using, the sections of the Web Site you visit, the Web Site pages read and images viewed, and the content you download from the Web Site. CertaPay uses this non-identifiable information for Web Site and system administration purposes and to improve the Web Site. CertaPay may disclose the non-identifiable information to others and permanently archive it for future use.

3\. _Cookies_ \- The Web Site uses "cookies", a technology that installs information on a Web Site user's computer to permit the Web Site to recognize future visits using that computer. Cookies enhance the convenience and use of the Web Site. For example, CertaPay uses the information provided through cookies to recognize you as a previous user of the Web Site (so you do not have to enter your personal information every time), offer personalized Web page content and information for your use, track your activity at the Web Site to respond to your needs, and otherwise facilitate your Web Site experience. You may choose to decline cookies if your browser permits, but doing so may affect your ability to use certain features of the Web Site. 

  
PERSONAL INFORMATION COLLECTED BY CERTAPAY

4\. _Personal Information Provided By You_ \- During your use of the Web Site, you may be asked to voluntarily provide certain personal information such as your name, , email address, postal address, and telephone number. It is always your choice whether or not to provide personal information. If you choose not to provide requested personal information, however, you may not be able to use certain features. CertaPay may also keep a record of all correspondence with you.

5\. _Personal Information Collected From Third Parties_ \- CertaPay may collect certain personal information about you from external sources, including personal references, to the extent permitted by law. CertaPay will use and disclose your personal information collected from such external sources in accordance with this _Policy_ , as amended from time to time.

6\. _CertaPay's Use of Your Personal Information_ \- CertaPay may use your personal information to contact and correspond with you, to respond to your inquiries, to determine whether to grant access to certain special features of the Web Site, to comply with legal requirements. CertaPay may develop a member profile for you. CertaPay may also send to you, from time to time, email or other communications containing information about CertaPay, and other matters (including third-party products or services) CertaPay believes will interest you. At any time you may ask CertaPay to stop sending you email and other information by contacting [privacymanager@certapay.com](mailto:privacymanager@certapay.com). Also, each unsolicited email CertaPay sends to you will tell you how to decline further unsolicited email. From time to time CertaPay may also send you email containing important information regarding the Web Site. You may not opt out of receiving such email.

7\. _Disclosure of Your Personal Information_ \- CertaPay may disclose your personal information in the following circumstances: 

(i) _Disclosure to Service Providers_ \- In order to provide the Web Site, CertaPay must disclose some of your personal information to certain service providers including technical service providers. Before personal information is disclosed to a service provider, the service provider will agree with CertaPay to protect the privacy of your personal information in a manner that is consistent with this _Policy_. A list of CertaPay's service providers to whom personal information may be disclosed, as amended from time to time, may be found by clicking here. If you do not wish CertaPay to provide your personal information to those service providers, you may not use the Web Site. 

(ii) _Disclosure in Business Transfers_ \- CertaPay may be involved in the sale, transfer or reorganization of some or all of its business at some time in the future. As part of that sale, transfer or reorganization, CertaPay may disclose your personal information to the acquiring organization, but will require the acquiring organization to agree to protect the privacy of your personal information in a manner that is consistent with this _Policy_. If at any time you do not wish CertaPay to disclose your personal information as part of the sale or transfer of some or all of CertaPay's business, please notify CertaPay at privacymanager@certapay.com.

(iii) _Law Enforcement and Legal Disclosure_ \- CertaPay may disclose your personal information to a government institution that has asserted its lawful authority to obtain the information or where CertaPay has reasonable grounds to believe the information could be useful in the investigation of unlawful activity, or to comply with a subpoena or warrant or an order made by a court, person or body with jurisdiction to compel the production of information. CertaPay may also disclose your personal information in order to comply with court rules regarding the production of records and information, to CertaPay's legal counsel, or as otherwise permitted or required by law.




8\. _Location of Information_ \- CertaPay will store and process your personal information in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.

9\. _Securing Your Information_ \- To help protect the confidentiality of your personal information, CertaPay employs security safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the information. Nevertheless, due to the nature of the Internet and Internet technologies, security and privacy risks cannot be eliminated and CertaPay cannot guarantee that your personal information will not be disclosed in ways not otherwise described in this _Policy_.

10\. _Accessing Your Personal Information_ \- You may request access to your personal information and information about CertaPay's collection, use and disclosure of that information by contacting CertaPay at [privacymanager@certapay.com](mailto:privacymanager@certapay.com). Subject to certain exceptions prescribed by law, you will be given reasonable access to your personal information, and will be entitled to challenge the accuracy and completeness of the information and to have it amended as appropriate. You can help CertaPay maintain the accuracy of your information by notifying CertaPay of any changes to your personal information.

OTHER MATTERS

11\. _Other Web sites_ \- The Web Site may contain links to other Web sites or Internet resources. When you click on one of those links you are contacting another Web site or Internet resource. CertaPay has no responsibility or liability for or control over those other Web sites or Internet resources or their collection, use and disclosure of your personal information. 

12\. _Web Site Use Agreement_ \- The _Web Site Use Agreement_ governing your use of the Web Site (which may be viewed by clicking here), contain important provisions, including provisions disclaiming, limiting or excluding CertaPay's liability for your use of the Web Site and provisions determining the applicable law and exclusive jurisdiction for the resolution of any disputes regarding your use of the Web Site. Each of those provisions applies to any disputes that may arise in relation to this _Policy_ and CertaPay's collection, use and disclosure of your personal information, and are of the same force and effect as if they had been reproduced directly in this _Policy_

13\. _Former Users_ \- If you cease using the Web Site or your permission to use the Web Site is terminated by CertaPay, CertaPay will continue to use and disclose your personal information in accordance with this _Policy_ as amended from time to time. 

14\. __Privacy Policy_ Changes_ \- This _Policy_ may be changed from time to time in CertaPay's sole discretion and without any prior notice or liability to you or any other person. CertaPay's collection, use and disclosure of your personal information will be governed by the version of this _Policy_ in effect at that time. New versions of this _Policy_ will be posted here. Your continued use of the Web Site subsequent to any changes to this _Policy_ will signify that you consent to CertaPay's collection, use and disclosure of your personal information in accordance with the changed _Policy_. Accordingly, when you use the Web Site you should check the date of this _Policy_ and review any changes since the last version. CertaPay also suggests that you bookmark this page and periodically review this _Policy_ to ensure that you are familiar with the most current version. 

15\. _Your Comments_ \- If you have any comments or questions about this _Policy_ or your personal information, please contact CertaPay at [privacymanager@certapay.com](mailto:privacymanager@certapay.com)
