# Celebrity Pro

### Privacy Policy

CelebrityPro.com automatically collects and stores the following information about you: the name of the domain from which you access the internet, the date and time you accessed our site, and the Internet address of the website or connection from which you came. We use this information to measure the number of visitors to the site and to its different sections to better develop CelebrityPro.com in order to address our users' preferences and to attract new users. 

You waive your privacy rights when you knowingly post personal information on public services such as our online forums. Any consequences and/or results of your information being used in any manner due to its public availability is not the responsibility of CelebrityPro.com whatsoever. 

Users of CelebrityPro.com can enter contests, use the message forums, and voluntarily participate in other promotions. To provide these services to our users, CelebrityPro.com generally request that the user provide no more than a name and an email address. We do not require any of our users to disclose more information than is reasonably necessary to participate in an activity as a condition of participation. We use the information collected to distribute the requested content, to notify contest winners, to enable password protected access to certain services and sections within the site, and to provide whatever other service the user has authorized in providing the contact information. 

CelebrityPro.com does not consider your IP as private personal information and therefore does not warrant its protection in this regard. 

Occasionally, CelebrityPro.com may poll the users of its site and request background information such as address, age range, gender, occupation, interests, hobbies, etc. The provision of any such information is always voluntary and never unnecessarily a condition of access or use. The information collected in user polls is solely for the internal purpose of letting CelebrityPro.com know who its users are and what they like in order to be better able to serve them. User poll information is only maintained and released by CelebrityPro.com, if at all, in aggregate statistical fashion and never in any manner that would allow for personal identification of a particular user. 

CelebrityPro.com does not sell, trade, lease or distribute any personal information of its registered users. However, CelebrityPro.com may release this information if required by law, to enforce its terms of service, or to protect the rights, property or safety of CelebrityPro.com, its registered users, or others. Please note that CelebrityPro.com's privacy policy does not cover the web sites or web pages that link to, are affiliated with, or are hosted by CelebrityPro.com.
