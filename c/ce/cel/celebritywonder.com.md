# CelebrityWonder.com Privacy Policy

**CelebrityWonder.com Privacy Policy**

Your privacy on the Internet is important to us. As a part of the operation of CelebrityWonder.com, we gather certain types of information. The information will be used to send you a notification whenever specific services become available or to send you a reply whenever you ask a question to us. We will also send a notification whenever we launch any new services/products that suit your needs.

As part of the registration process in survey, newsletter, and contact-us forms, you will be asked to provide an email address and your personal information such as: your age, job, salary, education and address. CelebrityWonder.com may also collect information about you from third party sources. Furthermore, CelebrityWonder.com monitors incoming and outgoing site traffic data to help diagnose problems with its servers and to administer its Web site. This information will also be used to display banners advertisement that meets your interest, age, job, and education.

When someone visits CelebrityWonder.com, cookies are placed on the browsers. Cookies are a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from web server and that stored on your computer hard drive. Cookies are used as an important and necessary part of providing customizable and personalized service to our loyal visitors. We also use cookies to collect information on which advertising banners/pop-ups are seen by our visitors. Cookies are used to store and sometimes track information about you. 

We undertake substantial efforts to protect the confidentiality of the identity, preferences and other information we have collected and will never knowingly allow access to this information to anyone outside CelebrityWonder.com. 

You may at anytime request a removal of information stored on CelebrityWonder.com server by contacting [us](http://www.celebritywonder.com/contact.html). 

The foregoing policy is effective as of April 2003, and CelebrityWonder.com reserves the right to change this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for changes. This statement and policies outlined herein are not intended to and do not create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party. 

**Third Party Advertising**

We use MaxWorldWide, FastClick, UGO, Adtegrity, GorillaNation, AskJeeves, and other third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/optout_nonppii.asp)

**Third Party Cookies**  
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.

If you have any question about this privacy, please [contact us](http://www.celebritywonder.com/contact.html).
