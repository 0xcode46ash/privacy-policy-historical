# Celsias - Privacy Policy

    At Celsias, we are committed to maintaining your privacy.

    This privacy policy explains how we will use any information that you may provide us:

  * You always have the right to add, delete or change any personal data you have provided us. 


  * We will never sell or rent your personal information to any third party.   



  * Our registration input form to become a member of Celsias asks you to provide an email address.  Unless otherwise stated, we will only use that address to send you information about Celsias such as new developments, special announcements, newsletters, RSS feeds, reminders, etc. and you may opt out of any and all such communications at any time.


  * We will invite you to refer friends to Celsias, but we do not permanently store, sell, or rent any such email addresses that you provide us.


  * If you post a comment to our blog or use our “Contact Us” form, we will collect your contact information in order to respond to you.   



  * Our advertisers may request information from you but we have no control over their privacy policies.


  * We use ‘session cookies’ which are used by your Internet browser to improve your online experience with Celsias.


  * We also use ‘persistent cookies’ to personalize your experience on Celsias and to help protect you from account abuse.


  * To better understand our traffic patterns, we will occasionally capture the URL from which you came to Celsias and the URL to which you go next, as well what browser you are using, and your IP address. Again, this information is never disclosed to any third party, and any personal identifying information is removed from the information.


  * You are responsible for not disclosing your user name or password to anyone.


  * We recommend that you logout at the end of each session on Celsias.



    If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or your privacy at Celsias, please [contact us.](https://web.archive.org/web/20080619074708id_/http%3A//www.celsias.com/beta/index.php?option=com_contact&Itemid=3)
