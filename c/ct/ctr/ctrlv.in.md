# CtrlV.in Privacy Policy and Conditions of Use - CtrlV.in Image Hosting

## What information we store.

  * In common with other websites, log files are stored on the web server saving details such as the visitor's IP address, browser type, referring page and time of visit. 
  * Cookies are saved in your browser for the purposes of remembering what images you have uploaded and when you log in. 
  * When you create an account the information you provide is stored on our server.
  * Images, annotations and captions you upload are stored on our server.



## How your information is used.

  * The information is used to enhance the visitor's experience when using the website and to display personalised content. 
  * Your personal information will never be sold, rented, leased or given to third parties.
  * You may receive emails from us to inform you of news of our services or offers by us or our affiliates. 
  * If you choose to make an image passworded, we will never reveal the password to anybody (we don't even know it ourselves). 
  * Our staff do not have the ability to view passworded images. The only exception is in cases where the image has been reported. 
  * Our staff cannot see the name of a person who uploaded an anonymous/passworded image or album, just like everyone else. 
  * Our staff will never reveal any personal information and will never reveal private/passworded/anonymous images/albums. The only exception to this is if we are required to do so under the instruction of law enforcement or court order. 



## Your options.

  * If you would like any of your information removed at any time you can do so by contacting us via the Help link at the bottom of this page. 
  * You may be able to block cookies via your browser settings but this may prevent you from access to certain features of the website. 



## Third parties.

  * We use advertising and analytics code from [Google Adsense](http://adsense.google.com/) and [Statcounter](http://statcounter.com/). These third parties may gather information about you and use it in accordance with their own respective privacy policies.
