# Current Results Privacy Policy

Personal information is collected with permission only, and used to service and manage Current Results and its email alerts. Current Results Nexus respects privacy and does not collect or use personal information for any other purpose.

Personal information is never sold, rented or shared in any way with another party.

Anyone who has signed up for email alerts can stop receiving email from Current Results by going to [this page](https://web.archive.org/Alert/removeemail.php) and entering their email address.

Google would also like you to know that when it displays ads on this website third parties may be placing and reading cookies on your browser, or using web beacons to collect information while serving ads. Current Results Nexus does not have access to nor control of cookies or web beacons placed by third parties.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies by modifying the settings in your browser. Turning off the browser's cookies will also prevent web beacons from tracking your activity. Find out how to [disable and enable cookies](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/manage-cookies/).
