# Current Results Privacy Policy

Personal information is collected with permission only, and used to service and manage Current Results. Current Results Nexus respects privacy and does not collect or use personal information for any other purpose. Personal information is never sold, rented or shared in any way with another party.

Google would also like you to know that when it displays ads on this website third parties may be placing and reading cookies on your browser, or using web beacons to collect information while serving ads. The cookies include the DART or DoubleClick cookie used by Google to show ads based on your visits to this and other websites. Current Results Nexus does not have access to nor control of cookies or web beacons placed by third parties.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies by modifying the settings in your browser. Turning off the browser's cookies will also prevent web beacons from tracking your activity. Find out how to [disable and enable cookies](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/manage-cookies/). You can opt out of using the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
