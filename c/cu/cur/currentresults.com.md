# Current Results Privacy Policy

Personal information is collected with permission only, and used to service and manage Current Results and its email alerts. Current Results Nexus respects privacy and does not collect or use personal information for any other purpose.

Personal information is never sold, rented or shared in any way with another party.

Anyone who has signed up for email alerts can stop receiving email from Current Results by going to [this page](https://web.archive.org/Alert/removeemail.php) and entering their email address.
