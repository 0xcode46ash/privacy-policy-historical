# Privacy Policy | Cutie Gadget

.

>  **Cutie Gadget Privacy Statement**
> 
> What written under this is the Privacy Statement for all Cutie Gadget blog including all the websites run under the www.cutiegadget.com domain.
> 
> Please read this statement regarding our blogs. If you have questions please ask us via our contact form.
> 
> **Email Addresses**
> 
> You may choose to add your email address to our contact list via the forms on our websites. We promise that we will never share you email with any third party and that we will remove your email at your request. We won’t send advertising via email. If you have any problem removing your email address please contact us via our contact form.
> 
> **Ownership of Information**
> 
> NamaSitusAnda is the sole owner of any information collected on our websites.
> 
> **Commenting**
> 
> Most Cutie Gadget blog contain comment sections (a.k.a. message boards). We do not actively monitor these comments and the information on them is for entertainment purposes only.We remove the nofollow to increase comment  in this blog. If we are alerted to something we deem inappropriate in any way, we may delete it at our discretion.  We really HATE **spam and will delete the spam comments when we found it!** I also encourage you write the related comments and not too short comments.
> 
> **Cookies**
> 
> Currently we assign cookies to our readers in order to save their preferences. This data is not shared with any third party, but will only used for ad preference purpose. Accessing our websites is not dependent on accepting cookies and all major browsers allow you to disable cookies if you wish.
> 
> **Third Party Cookies**
> 
> Many of our advertisers use cookies in order to determine the number of times you have seen an advertisement. This is done to limit the number times you are shown the same advertisement. Cutie Gadget does not have access to this data.
> 
> **Traffic Reports**
> 
> Our industry-standard traffic reporting records IP addresses, Internet service provider information, referrer strings, browser types and the date and time page
