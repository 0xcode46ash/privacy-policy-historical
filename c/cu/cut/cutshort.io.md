# Privacy | CutShort

TL;DR;

We will handle your personal information carefully and respectfully. We will never post or contact your friends without your permission. We are proud that you are trusting us with your social profiles and will always act to uphold that trust.

This privacy policy describes the personal information collected by AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd., a Pune, India based company, through its websites and apps that together constitute the "CutShort" service (the Service), and how and for what purposes it is collected and used.

Information Collected

AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd. collects and stores information when you sign up for an account on the Service. You enter, among other things, your name, email address, date of birth, and gender. AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd. uses your email address to provide services in connection with the Service, such as updates, notifications, and reminders, and to contact you on behalf of other users of the Service who send you communication, among other things. The information collected by AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd. is sent to servers located in the United States and/or other countries around the world.  
In addition, we may display advertisements on our service from third parties (e.g. partners, advertising companies, and ad networks). These third parties use tracking technologies, such as cookies, to measure the effectiveness of their advertisement. AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd. does not provide any personal information to these third parties.

Messages and Notifications

At times, we will notify you by email about updates, changes, and other service related information. We will never ask you for your password or other personal information in those notifications and communication, or in any other communication from AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd..

Activity on the Service

AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd. stores information about user activity and use of the Service (e.g. among other things, page views, IP address, requested URLs, referring URLs, visit date and length, and file type requests). AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd. uses this information to administer the Service and perform statistical analyses of user behavior and characteristics in order to measure interest in and use of the various areas and features of the Service. We also send cookies to your computer in order to uniquely identify your browser and improve the quality of our service. We may use persistent cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them). Persistent cookies can be removed by following your browser help file directions. If you choose to disable cookies, some areas of the Service may not work properly or at all. Our cookies are linked to your information, such as your name, email address and password.

Disclosure of Information

AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd. may disclose non-personal information in the aggregate to third parties (e.g. Advertisers) relating to user activities and behavior on the Service.

Links to third parties

The Service may contain links to third party websites and / or services with which we may or may not have affiliation. We do not share your personal information with those third parties, and are not responsible for their privacy practices.

CutShort and Children

The Service is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information without parental consent, we take steps to remove such information and terminate the child's account.

Security

AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd. follows generally accepted industry standards to protect personal information submitted to the Service. Transmission of data over the Internet, or electronic storage, is not 100% secure. Therefore, while commercially acceptable means to protect personal information are used, AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd. cannot guarantee its absolute security.  
AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd. or any AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd. representative will never ask you for your password or other personal information, including financial information. Please treat requests for your password or other personal information as suspicious and promptly contact AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd..  
Please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in India or anywhere our servers and central database are located and operated. Please be assured that we seek to take reasonable steps to ensure that your privacy is protected. By using our Service, you understand that your information may be transferred to our facilities and those third parties with whom we share it as described in this privacy policy.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

AppyHappy Software Pvt. Ltd. may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, that in our sole discretion, are material, we will notify you either by posting a notice of such changes prior to implementing the change on the Service and/or via the email associated with your Service account.
