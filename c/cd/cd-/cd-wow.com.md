We collect information about our customers via server logs, cookies, order forms, and contests or competitions. Information about you or any other individual customer will not be sold or rented to any third party, including advertisers. The only time we may share information such as name, email address or phone number, is if you have linked into us from a partner's site. The information may then be shared with that particular partner. General information concerning site usage and demographics may be made available to advertisers and partners, but provides an overall picture and does not include any individual details.

CD WOW! operates an 'opt in' mail system in conjunction with Select Your Mail. Replying to the 'unsubscribe' link contained in the mail will ensure that your name will be removed from the mailing list.

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your interactions with CD WOW! you are welcome to contact our Customer Service representatives.
