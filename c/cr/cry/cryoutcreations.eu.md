# Privacy Policy • Cryout Creations

We’re privacy freaks. Cryout Creations does not track you as an individual and we do our best to collect and request as little information from you as legally required to.

However, for both your and our convenience we use third party services that can be considered trackers or which can collect visitor data:

  * Google Analytics for measurement, collection and analysis of web data to help us understand our traffic and optimize our website
  * Tawk.to for providing live chat on our site – this is active only on selected site sections
  * [Akismet](https://akismet.com/privacy/) for screening comments and forum posts for spam.



You can opt to block this functionality on our website (and any other website) at any time by blocking third party scripts. You can do so with plenty of browser extensions: [Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/extensions/privacy-security/), [Opera](https://addons.opera.com/en/extensions/category/privacy-security/), [Chrome](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/search/privacy), [Safari](https://safari-extensions.apple.com/?q=privacy).

We rely on external services for the processing of payments, handling of emails or live chat support:

  * When making a purchase the personal information you provide will be processed and stored by our payment processors [2Checkout](https://www.2checkout.com/policies/privacy-policy) or [PayPal](https://www.paypal.com/uk/webapps/mpp/ua/privacy-full) according to their privacy policies.
  * If you subscribe to our newsletter your name and e-mail address will be processed by our emailing services provider, [Mailerlite](https://www.mailerlite.com/privacy-policy/).
  * If you use our live chat system, any information you enter in the live chat window is accessible and can be processed by our live chat provider, [Tawk.to](https://www.tawk.to/privacy-policy/).



Our website uses SSL encryption to ensure privacy and security between your browser and our server at any time. We do not receive nor store any payment information and we use password hashing and salting for your account security. We also use account locking policies to protect against brute force attacks or password guessing.

We store the information you enter upon registration, in the forum, through our support system or when making an order only in connection to providing you the purchased products or services or for support. You can [request](https://www.cryoutcreations.eu/contact) we completely delete this information from our system at any time after you are no longer using our services in accordance with the [EU GDRP](https://ec.europa.eu/info/law/law-topic/data-protection_en).  


#### Cookies

We both love and hate cookies

This website does make use of cookies. These are used to ensure proper running of some of the website functionality (comments, account management). You can always completely disable cookies in your browser. Your browser also provides the option to delete all previously stored cookies.

None of the information gathered via cookies identifies you as a person but for functionality purposes it can identify your device.
