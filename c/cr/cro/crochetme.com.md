# Crochet me Magazine Privacy Policy

Crochet me may ask you for personal information for various reasons. At each request, a clear statement of the purpose of collecting such information will be available. All information collected will be kept confidential and will be used only for the purposes described. Your information will never be shared with any third party without your permission.
