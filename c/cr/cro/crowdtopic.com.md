# Privacy Policy

> **Your privacy is important to us. Please read this document and take time to understand it. By registering with CrowdTopic.com and using the site you have accepted these terms. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use this site. This privacy policy may change from time to time. You will be notified of such changes via your registered email with this site.**
> 
> **Information Collected and Stored**  
>  To post on CrowdTopic.com we require your current email address. This information, together with your IP address when you register and post, is collected and retained along with your topic posts. You may choose to make available and save other personal information such as name, age, location, email address, photograph, on line contact details, interest etc via your Control Panel. 
> 
> Private Messages and emails transmitted through CrowdTopic.com may be stored. 
> 
> Other information that is collected is from website logs (such as browser and operating system information, Internet service provider, etc.) but these are generally non-personal. 
> 
> **Usage and Disclosure of Information**  
>  CrowdTopic.com does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at this site, including email addresses, demographics, etc. 
> 
> We do reserve the right to disclose user information to third parties when we have reason to believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be causing (or planning) injury to or interference with (either intentionally or unintentionally) our rights or property, other website users, or anyone else that could be harmed by such activities. This includes disrupting the community or website. We may disclose personal information without notice to you when we believe in good faith that the law requires it or **to respond to an emergency situation**. We will NOT disclose any personally identifying information for non-emergency situations such as those that do not threaten life, limb, or major property. 
> 
> You may elect, via your Control Panel to allow other members to email you. This is done without disclosing your email address to the sender. Only you as a user of this website will modify that feature unless you request it to be changed by an administrator. 
> 
> To assist in the maintenance of your account with CrowdTopic.com (lost passwords for example) it may be necessary for the CrowdTopic.com server, an Administrator or Moderator to contact you via your email address. CrowdTopic.com reserves the right to send occasional test messages, major announcements, and account maintenance messages to your email address to establish the validity of that email address. 
> 
> Usage statistics from website logs may be posted on the CrowdTopic.com website and may be provided to other interested parties. If they are, there will be no individually identifying stats posted on this or any other internet website or shared with any third parties.
> 
> We will use all reasonable efforts to safeguard the confidentiality of any personal information but will have no liability for disclosure of any information obtained due to errors in transmission or the unauthorized acts of third parties. 
> 
> **Information Collection by other Companies**  
>  There may be links to other sites from CrowdTopic.com pages. It is important to check the Privacy Policies of these sites as you view them and provide information on them. There is a strong possibility they do not maintain the same policies as CrowdTopic.com. 
> 
> **Cookies**   
>  Cookies are used to store information and make your visit more convenient and time efficient. Cookies are text files that are placed on your hard drive by our site to save your preferences. For example if you allow the cookie from CrowdTopic.com you may visit the forums without logging in each time. This cookie collects NO personal information and does not forward your information or browsing habits to any third party. 
> 
> **Newsletters**  
>  CrowdTopic.com occasionally contacts members via email to keep them up to date with information relevant to this website or their accounts. You may elect whether you receive these emails or not via the email options setting in your Control Panel. 
> 
> **Children under 13**  
>  This website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly allow children to register with us, post information, communicate with us, or to use any of our online services and do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information for any children under 13. 
> 
> **Transfer of Business Assets**  
>  If CrowdTopic.com, or in turn its business assets change hands, personal information acquired through the site will be included in the relocated assets. 
> 
> **Contact**  
>  Feel free to email the main **[site administrator](http://www.crowdtopic.com/sendmessage.php)** if you have any questions, concerns or comments regarding our privacy policy.
