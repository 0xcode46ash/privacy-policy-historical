Privacy

Blimey Holdings Pty Ltd (ABN 98 101 558 847), publishes Crikey, a daily email newsletter, and its associated website, www.crikey.com.au. Blimey Holdings’ administration is managed by Private Media Partners (ABN 36 268 936 932), which employs staff and manages subscriber databases and accounting services for the Blimey Holdings businesses. This privacy policy applies to both Blimey Holdings and Private Media Partners.

**Privacy Policy Purpose  
** The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to advise customers, staff members and those applying for positions with Blimey Holdings and Private Media Partners what kind of information the organisations may collect, how that information may be used, to whom it may be disclosed, how that information is stored (including security of the information), the processes for correcting information held and the disposal of such information by Blimey Holdings and Private Media Partners.

**Use and Disclosure of Personal Information  
** Blimey Holdings will use personal information provided to it only for the purpose for which it was provided. Personal information will not be used for any other purpose without the consent of its provider unless Blimey Holdings is required by law to disclose that information. Blimey Holdings may disclose personal information to Private Media Partners and may be required to disclose personal information to third parties in order to provide a service requested. For example, newsletter subscriptions may require disclosure of personal information to third parties for delivery and billing fulfilment, disclosure necessary to fulfil the purpose for which the personal information was provided. Other than in circumstances such as these, Blimey Holdings will not disclose personal information provided, such as name, address, email address or telephone number, to any organisation or person outside Blimey Holdings and Private Media Partners unless the provider has authorised Blimey Holdings to do so. No data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be totally secure. Remember to close your browser when you have finished a user session. This is to ensure that others cannot access your personal information and correspondence if you share a computer with someone else or are using a computer in a public place like a library or Internet café. You as an individual are responsible for the security of and access to your own computer.

**No Sale of Personal Information  
** Blimey Holdings or Private Media Partners will not sell or receive payment for licensing or disclosing personal information. From time to time Crikey subscribers may be invited to respond to advertisers’ market surveys in which information is collected for both Crikey and other organisations. In such cases no personal information will be disclosed by Blimey Holdings or Private Media Partners to the advertiser.

**Access and Correction  
** Blimey Holdings and Private Media Partners will provide, where possible, access for providers of personal information to their file. Providers may correct any information that is incorrect and instruct Blimey Holdings to delete the information from its files. Those who have reason to believe information held is inaccurate should contact Blimey Holdings in writing to:

Office Manager  
Private Media Partners  
PO Box 1380  
Collingwood VIC 3066

Reasonable steps will be taken to correct the information so that it is accurate, complete and up to date.

**Security  
** Blimey Holdings and Private Media Partners use reasonable administrative, technical, personnel, and physical measures to safeguard personally identifiable information in its possession against loss, theft, and unauthorized use, disclosure or modification.

**Complaints About Privacy  
** Queries regarding privacy complaints and disputes should be addressed to:

Office Manager  
Private Media Partners  
PO Box 1380  
Collingwood VIC 3066

Blimey Holdings or Private Media Partners will investigate all complaints.

**Changes to Blimey Holdings Privacy Policy  
** Blimey Holdings is continually monitoring privacy developments and accordingly, from time to time may modify this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the law, self-regulatory initiatives and/or technology. Amendments to the Blimey Holdings' Privacy Policy will be posted on www.crikey.com.au.
