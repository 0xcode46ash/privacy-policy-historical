# Cree Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy tells you what kinds of information we collect about our customers, users and visitors and how we use and share that information. By using our website or other Cree services, you accept the policies and practices that we have described in this Privacy Policy. We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time, by posting updates to our website. An update will be effective for any website use after the date of the update. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy from time to time for possible changes. Your use of our website or services after an update constitutes your agreement to the update. In this Privacy Policy, the words Cree, we, us, and our refer to Cree, Inc. and its subsidiaries.

Our websites are controlled and operated within the United States of America, and are designed for use by businesses worldwide and by consumers within the United States. CREE MAKES NO REPRESENTATION THAT OUR WEBSITE IS APPROPRIATE FOR USE BY CONSUMERS OUTSIDE THE UNITED STATES.

**What Does This Privacy Policy Apply To?**

This Privacy Policy applies to our websites that reference it, and to our mobile websites, smartphone applications, social media sites, postings and email and other communications you may have with us. This Privacy Policy does not apply to information collected by our subsidiary E-conolight through its websites or otherwise. This Privacy Policy also does not apply to any website(s) owned and operated by suppliers, manufacturers, distributors, advertisers or other third parties. This website contains links to other sites that are not owned, maintained, operated or endorsed by or for Cree and may not be subject to the same privacy practices or security protocols. Please read the other sites’ privacy policies to understand how your personal information is collected, used and protected when you use those sites.

**Third-Party Services**

The website may offer features and services that are administered or provided by third parties hired by Cree. To receive these services, you may be required to provide these third parties with personal information. Those services are subject to separate terms and conditions established by the third-party providers, including separate privacy policies, which may be different from this Privacy Policy.

**What Information Do We Collect?**

Our information collection includes information obtained through our website, from our mobile websites, smart phone applications, social media, postings, email and offline. 

Cree collects information about you in three different ways. The first is when you voluntarily provide information to us. The second is through automatic collection of traffic and other information about visitors to our website. The third is when we receive information from third parties that collect and provide information to businesses like Cree to help us provide better products and services. 

_Information that you voluntarily provide to us_

You can read about Cree and our products on our website without sharing any personal information. Cree may ask you for personal information when you sign up to use a website feature. 

In certain places on this website, you may be asked to provide your name and/or email address to communicate with us. Opening an account may require information, for example your name, company, email address, telephone number, mailing address, and credit card numbers. It is your option to provide the information requested. To place an order, we may ask you for financial information such as a credit card number, billing and shipping addresses. 

Cree may retain records of any messages that you may send to us through this website or other websites owned by Cree, by email, by communication with Cree through social media sites, or by posting.

We may collect information when you provide product reviews, post online questions or submit information to us. We may choose to share your comments through our marketing campaigns and communications. Please see our [Terms of Use](https://web.archive.org/Legal/Terms-of-Use) for more information regarding submitting information to Cree.

_Information collected automatically_

When you visit our website, our information technology systems automatically collect information about the equipment you are using and about your use of our website and services. Examples of the information we collect and analyze include the Internet Protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer to the Internet, the login name and password for the website if you sign in for access, the email address that you use to communicate with us, your computer and connection information (for example, browser type, version, and time zone setting, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system, and platform), and records of your purchases with us. We sometimes aggregate information like this from multiple customers and visitors to create data that can be analyzed to determine general customer and visitor levels of interest in particular products or services. We also may collect and analyze URL click stream information to, through, and from our websites, which shows your website navigation history. This information may include the date and time of your visit, cookie number, if any, and products you viewed or searched for. We may also use browser data such as cookies for fraud prevention and other purposes. We may use software tools to measure and collect session information, including page response times, download errors, length of visits to certain pages, page interaction information (for example scrolling, clicks, and mouse-overs), and methods used to browse away from the page. We may also collect technical information to help us identify your device for fraud prevention and for dealing with diagnostic purposes relating to our website.

_Other information we might collect_

We may also receive personal information about you from third parties, including contact information, user information, address and shipping information and credit information. 

**How We Use Information We Collect**

Cree does not disclose personally identifiable information collected from you during visits to this website except as provided in this Privacy Policy. Information may be shared among Cree, Inc. and its subsidiaries, agents, distributors and resellers and with third parties Cree has engaged for purposes of informing you about Cree products or services or providing products or services to you. We may use your personal information to improve our services, to customize the content you see, and for research, marketing and other business purposes. We may also contact you from time to time to inform you of our products and services and to ask for comments. We may work with online advertising companies to provide you with advertising that is tailored to your interests. These ads may be seen on our website, other Cree websites or on other third party sites that you visit. These ads may be based on information gathered as you browsed our website or based on your transactions with us. Cree may share your information with third party service providers for processing payments made by credit or debit card in connection with fulfilling and delivering ordered products or other material. Cree also may disclose your information if Cree has a good faith belief that the disclosure is required by law, is required in order to enforce the [Terms of Use](https://web.archive.org/Legal/Terms-of-Use) for this website, or is necessary to detect, prevent or address fraud or security issues or to prevent harm to Cree or others. Cree may transfer your information to companies that purchase lines of business from Cree that have been displayed our websites.

**Third-Party Collection and Use of Information**

We do not allow third parties to use our website to collect information about a user's online activities over time and across different sites. Our website may offer features and services that are administered or provided by third parties, and to use those features or receive those services you may be required to provide those third parties with personal information. That personal information may be subject to separate privacy policies of those third parties that may be different from this Privacy Policy. Otherwise, Cree does not allow third parties to collect and use personally identifiable information from or through our website except for the purpose of providing services to or on behalf of Cree, and the personally identifiable information may not be used for any other purposes.

**How We Use Cookies**

Depending upon the settings of your browser, Cree may make use of cookies or other technology to store information about you and your visits to this website on your equipment so that Cree’s systems can recognize you and provide you information more efficiently the next time you visit our website. 

Cookies enhance our ability to provide you information and services. Cookies are pieces of text that a website stores on your computer that can be accessed by the website when you return to the site. Cookies help us improve your experience since they recall your use of the Internet and our website and services, your preferences, and your login and password to our website.

We are aware that not everyone wants to permit the use of cookies. If you do not want to enable cookies, the help feature on most browsers will tell you how to change your browser settings to manage cookies.

**Do Not Track Requests**

Our website honors do not track requests that are submitted to us by your browser when you visit our website. Your browser will submit these requests if you have enabled the “do not track” feature of your browser. 

**How We Protect Information**

Our site uses industry- standard encryption technology to provide for the security of your credit card numbers, security codes and expiration dates. We also have reasonable procedural and technical standards to protect the security of our site and the information we maintain. We cannot guarantee, however, that any electronic commerce or communication is totally secure.

**How We Protect Social Security Numbers**

We may collect Social Security numbers (SSNs) from you in the course of business – for instance, for the purpose of running a credit report. We strive to protect the security of SSNs in our possession, custody or control. We maintain confidentiality through reasonable safeguards designed to protect against the loss, misuse or unlawful disclosure of SSNs, including, but not limited to, limiting access to SSNs.

**What If You Don’t Want to Give us Personal Information**

Personal information is not required to simply visit the areas of our website available to the general public. Personal information is required for you to purchase online, request information, or take advantage of certain services and features that we might offer.

**Removing or Updating Personal Information**

If you believe your personal information is not accurate, or if you would like us to delete personal information collected by Cree, please contact us at [webmaster@cree.com](mailto:webmaster@cree.com).

**Email**

Recipients of our email marketing messages can unsubscribe from receiving promotional email by using the unsubscribe instructions located in the bottom of the message.

**Children’s Information**

Cree does not seek information from persons less than 13 years of age, and no information should be submitted to this website by visitors less than 13 years of age without the consent of their parent or guardian.

**Users Outside the United States**

The website is managed from and operated in the United States. If you are located outside of the United States, please be aware that any information you provide to us will be transferred to the United States, and by using the website or providing your information, you consent to this transfer, and understand that the United States may not provide the same level of protection for your personal information as the laws of your country.
