# Creating Keepsakes

**We do not rent or sell your e-mail address.**

_Creating Keepsakes_ has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. The following discloses our information-gathering and dissemination practices for this web site: [http://www.creatingkeepsakes.com](http://www.creatingkeepsakes.com/). 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our web site. Your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information. 

Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart. We use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and for other purposes. 

Our site uses an order form through which you can request information, products and services. We request that you provide contact information (such as your e-mail address), financial information, and demographic information (such as your zip code, age, or income level). We collect contact information through the order form so we can process your order and so we can send you information about our company. You may opt out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt out section below. We require contact information with product orders so we can contact you about your order if necessary. Financial information that is collected is used solely to bill you for products and services that you request. 

Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. We use this data to tailor your experience at our site, providing you with content in which we believe you would be interested and displaying the content according to your preferences. 

Our online surveys ask for your contact information (such as your e-mail address) and demographic information (such as your zip code, age, or income level). We use contact data from our surveys to send you information about our company and to contact you if necessary. You may opt out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt out section below. 

We run contests on our site in which we ask for your contact information (such as your e-mail address) and demographic information (such as your zip code, age, or income level). We use contact data from our contests to send you information about our company and to contact you if necessary. You may opt out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt out section below. 

**Choice/Opt-Out**

Our site provides you the opportunity to opt out of receiving communications from us when we request information about you. To remove your information from our database so that you will not receive future communications, visit the following URL: <http://www.creatingkeepsakes.com/newsletter/subscribe/?forward=unsubscribe>.
