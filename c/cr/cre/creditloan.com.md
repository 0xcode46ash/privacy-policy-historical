**Personally Identifiable Information**

Your personally identifiable information may be collected during the registration process, in order for you to receive Loan Products and Information from Third Party Providers.   **Your information may be collected when you otherwise agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy, but do not complete the applicable registration process in full.**.  The types of personally identifiable information that may be collected include, without limitation: (a) your full name; (b) mailing address; (c) e-mail address; (d) home, work and mobile telephone numbers and the best time to call you; (e) date of birth; (f) whether or not you are a US citizen; (g) whether or not you are over eighteen (18) years of age; (h) whether or not you or a spouse are a regular or reserve member of the military; (i) your time at your current residence; (j) whether or not you rent or own your home; (k) your occupation, employer name and time spent at your current job; (l) the loan amount requested; (m) your annual, pre-tax income; (n) how you are paid; (o) how frequently you are paid; (p) the anticipated dates of your next two (2) paychecks; (q) the names, telephone numbers and source of relationship of two (2) personal references; and (r) any and all other information requested on the applicable registration form.  For purposes of this Privacy Policy, items (j) through (q) in the preceding sentence shall be considered Sensitive Information.
