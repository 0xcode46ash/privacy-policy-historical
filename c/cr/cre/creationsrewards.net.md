# CreationsRewards.Net - Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

|  | 

**Terms of Service**

When you signup for the CreationsRewards.Net Online Rewards Program, you hereby agree to all terms and conditions listed below. You must be at least 13 years of age to use the CreationsRewards website. You will provide us with complete and valid personal information, such as your name, home address, and Email. You cannot sign up for more than one account. You are allowed to have one CreationsRewards account per person, per computer. Everyone in a household could join CreationsRewards, however they must have their own computer to access the Internet. You are responsible for keeping your CreationsRewards password private. Any fraudulent activity on your account will cause CreationsRewards.Net to cancel your account. You agree to receive periodic advertising Emails from CreationsRewards.Net that are not paid emails. Emails will be sent on a weekly or biweekly basis and will provide you with information on the CreationsRewards site as well as new features and offers that can earn you additional points. You will be rewarded for your participation in CreationsRewards by receiving points. Points are redeemable for a PayPal redemption or a Web Certificate. You must meet the minimum requirements of redeeming before you will receive your reward. Rewards will be sent within one to four weeks after the redemption request has been received. You cannot spam the CreationsRewards website in any way. You cannot send out your referral information in unsolicited Emails, Usenet newsgroup posts, or mass mails. You will have your account terminated if you are found to be spamming. Multiple accounts from the same computer also constitutes termination of all accounts under the particular household. We cannot be held responsible for tracking errors on signups and offers. We cannot be held responsible for missing point credit on actions not credited to your account. Reasons for lost credit include, but are not limited to: firewalls, ad bars, web browser features, cookies, and images. If we do not show your transactions in our master reports, we were not paid by the merchant and therefore we cannot pay you for actions not credited to us. This agreement may be updated at any time. You will be notified if we update the Terms of Service agreement. If you disagree to these Terms and Conditions, you will have to cancel your account and forfeit all points earned if you did not redeem before canceling your account with CreationsRewards. By using CreationsRewards.Net, you hereby agree to all Terms of Service set forth in this agreement.

**Privacy Policy**

CreationsRewards.Net is serious about protecting our members privacy. The CreationsRewards.Net web server automatically logs your IP address every time you visit our site. This is used to help us with any problems in accessing our website as well as getting a general view of demographic information on our visitors. By joining CreationsRewards, you provide us with personal identifying information, such as your name, home address, and Email address. This information is never sold to third parties. It is solely used by CreationsRewards to send member update emails which keep members up-to-date on the CreationsRewards website. We also use your personal information to send out your rewards. You can opt-out of such Emails at any given time. All personal information is kept in a secured database and is protected from being stolen and altering of members data. CreationsRewards links to other sites on the Internet. We are not responsible for these site's privacy policies and content. You can opt-out of CreationsRewards.Net advertising Emails by contacting us. you can also decide to cancel your membership with CreationsRewards. By canceling your membership, all personal information is deleted from our database, including point information. We will have no record of your information after you cancel your account. If you ever need to update your personal information, you can do so inside of the Member's Home Page.
