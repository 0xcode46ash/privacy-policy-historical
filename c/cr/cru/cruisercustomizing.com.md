# Self-Service Support

At CruiserCustomizing.com we recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of you as a user of this Website. This Website does not collect Personal Information about you except for information that you voluntarily provide. Personal Information is kept confidentially for CruiserCustomizing business use only to assist you in your current and future purchases or in analyzing sales trends. Address information from online orders are used to fulfill your orders and might be forwarded to our vendors for shipping purposes. We also use your contact information to get in touch with you if necessary. 

Use of Cookies:  
We use cookies to enhance your experience on the site, for example we use a cookie to store your userid when you login as a member and to keep your shopping cart information. 

You can reject cookies and still use the site but won't be able to use certain features that require a login or use the shopping cart. 

Customer information is considered to be an asset of CruiserCustomizing.com and as such may be transfered to another company as part of an acquisition of CruiserCustomizing.com.
