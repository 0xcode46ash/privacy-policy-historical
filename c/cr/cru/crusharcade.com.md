# Play Games at CrushArcade.com

This privacy policy explains the nature of how we use personal information collected at this website. By using this website, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy. 

**Personal Information**

CrushArcade.com collects personal information volunteered by customers. Such information may include name, email, address, phone number, fax number, payment information, and other pertinent information. This information is used to for marketing and business purposes. Your personal information will never be shared with third-parties unless we are legally required to do so.

Personal information is used to facilitate business transactions and marketing initiatives. From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our website to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt-out of these new uses.

**Cookies**

Cookies are used to store shopping cart data, visitor preferences, session information, or past activity on CrushArcade.com in order to facilitate business transactions and provide more relevant services when visitors return to our site.

**External Links**

CrushArcade.com links to third-party websites. Content and privacy policies are the responsibility of their respective owners.

**Security**

CrushArcade.com has security measures in place to protect against loss, misuse, or alteration of information under our control. Credit card transactions are always processed through an SSL encryption.

**Contact Information**

We can be reached via e-mail at [**support@CrushArcade.com**](mailto:support@CrushArcade.com)
