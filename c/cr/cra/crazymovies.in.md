# Privacy Policy

CrazyMovies("CrazyMovies") takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.  


|   
  
---|---  
  
What this Privacy Policy Covers

  * This Privacy Policy covers CrazyMovies’s treatment of personally identifiable information that CrazyMovies collects when you are on the CrazyMovies site, and when you use CrazyMovies’s services. This policy also covers CrazyMovies's treatment of any personally identifiable information that CrazyMovies's business partners share with CrazyMovies. 

  * This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that CrazyMovies does not own or control, or to people that CrazyMovies does not employ or manage. 




Information Collection and Use

  * CrazyMovies collects personally identifiable information when you register for a CrazyMovies account, when you use certain CrazyMovies products or services, when you visit CrazyMovies pages, and when you enter **promotions or sweepstakes**. CrazyMovies may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners. 

  * When you register with CrazyMovies, we ask for your name, email address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. Once you register with CrazyMovies and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us. 

  * CrazyMovies also automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, CrazyMovies **cookie** information and the page you requested. 

  * In addition, when you use CrazyMovies's services, you may make some of your personal information public. If you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return and CrazyMovies will not be responsible for the use or misuse of such information. 

  * CrazyMovies uses information for three general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, to fulfill your requests for certain products and services promoted by CrazyMovies or other companies, and to contact you about specials and new products. CrazyMovies is not responsible for the accuracy, completeness, veracity and reliability of any advertising and content, products and services which may be accessed or advertised to you and CrazyMovies will not be liable for any loss or damage arising out of the same. 




Information Sharing and Disclosure

  * CrazyMovies will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone. 

  * CrazyMovies will send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when: 

    * We have your consent to share the information; 

    * We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested; 

    * We need to share the information with certain service partners in order to respond to your comments or to resolve service issues. (Unless we tell you differently, these partners have no independent right to use this information except to respond to your comments or to resolve the service issues.); 

    * We respond to requests from any Indian or foreign government, security, defence, revenue, regulatory or other authority, agency or officer; 

    * We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; or 

    * We find that your actions on our web sites violate the **CrazyMovies's[Terms of Service](http://crazymovies.in/terms.html)** or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services. 




Cookies

  * CrazyMovies may set and access CrazyMovies **cookies** on your computer and you may not be able to disable these cookies. CrazyMovies ensures that all information collected and accessed by CrazyMovies through the access of these cookies will be maintained in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

  * CrazyMovies allows **other companies** that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to CrazyMovies's cookies. 

  * CrazyMovies uses **web beacons** to access our cookies within our network of web sites and in connection with CrazyMovies products and services. 




Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences

  * CrazyMovies gives you the ability to edit your CrazyMovies Account Information and preferences at any time, including whether you want CrazyMovies to contact you about specials and new products. CrazyMovies is not responsible and will not be liable for the accuracy, completeness, veracity and reliability of any information including any customized information which may be accessed by or provided to you, which could vary depending on your CrazyMovies Account Information and preferences including your browser preferences. 




Security

  * Your CrazyMovies Account Information is password-protected for your privacy and security. 




Changes to this Privacy Policy

  * CrazyMovies may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.
