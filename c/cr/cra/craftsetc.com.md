# Crafts Etc! Privacy Policy

  |  |   
---  
   
CraftsEtc.com is committed to protecting your privacy and providing the most secure on-line shopping experience possible.   
   
CraftsEtc.com collects information such as your e-mail address, full name, billing & shipping address, phone number, and billing information. This information is used for the sole purpose of processing your order. This information is not sold to anyone and is only available to those responsible for processing your order.   
   
If you are registered in our monthly drawing and/or for our newsletter Craftabilities, your e-mail address will be stored in our secure database. This database will not be sold, shared, rented, or made available in anyway to third parties. The database is only available to those personnel who conduct the monthly drawing and those who upkeep and maintain the database. You will from time to time receive special notices from us regarding sales or special promotions but it will only be mailed to those that have signed up for the drawing and/or our newsletter.  
   
Cookies are small text files that your web browser stores on your computer's hard drive. The cookies are used to keep track of items you put in your shopping cart and to help us keep track of what ad campaigns you may have found us from. Cookies also enable us to look at the customers who visit and design our site to fit you. CraftsEtc.com cookies are not available to other companies and will not retrieve any personal information such as name, address, and credit card information from your computer.  
   
**Security**  
We use SSL (secure socket layering) 128-bit encryption to protect your information as it goes through the Internet. This secure server software encrypts all of your personal information such as your name, address, phone number, and billing information.   
   
We also have a digital certificate through Verisign. This guarantees that data exchanged online is protected from theft and/or tampering. Their seal verifies that CraftsEtc.com is a secure site.   
   
If you have any questions that are not answered here, please feel free to contact us at 1-800-888-0321 Ext. 1275 or e-mail us at [crafts@craftsetc.com](mailto:crafts@craftsetc.com) .  
   
We may change our Privacy Policy. All changes will be posted to the site and be effective immediately.
