# CrazyFreelancer - Privacy Policy

## The information we collect and how we use:

## 

When you order a logo or website design, we need to know your name, your company name, your e-mail address and mailing address. This allows us to process and fulfill your order and notify you of your order status. We also ask for information about your company to have a creative direction for the design of your business needs or web design logo. All rights to the logo artwork and logo design we created at the request of a client belong to the client.

CrazyFreelancer Is committed to protecting your privacy. To provide accurate information and design your logo or website design, CrazyFreelancer, a fast response request the information necessary to complete your order with precision. All information is strictly for use by CrazyFreelancer PrintingForLess.com and your partner will not be shared with third parties. CrazyFreelancer Uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) to protect your privacy when you place an order online.
