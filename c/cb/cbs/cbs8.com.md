| **PRIVACY POLICY**  
---  
  
CBS8.com takes your privacy very seriously. We are committed to protecting your privacy online. We are also committed to providing you with the very best experience we can on our web site. For this reason we may gather certain personal information about you in order to help us customize our content to your tastes and preferences. The following policy lists how your personal information will be treated and used as you make full use of our web site. This policy may change from time to time so please check back periodically.

POLICY OVERVIEW

The security of personal information is a high priority at CBS8.com. Our web site and all associated data are implemented with safeguards to protect against loss, unauthorized access, destruction, misuse, modification and improper disclosure. No computer system or information can ever be fully protected against every possible hazard. But CBS8.com is committed to providing reasonable and appropriate security controls to protect our web site and their information against foreseeable hazards.

The information you provide helps us make the CBS8.com site more fun and useful for you. It also enables us to give you updates about our services. When you become a registered member of our site, you may receive updates about our services. Users should not receive any email or postal mail from any other parties as a result of signing up to become a member of CBS8.com unless the user has expressly "opted-in" to receiving such email.

Please keep in mind that whenever you voluntarily disclose personal information in public areas of CBS8.com, such as message boards, online classifieds, or personals, your information can be viewed and possibly used by others. We advise users not to disclose information that can be used to contact the user ("Personally Identifiable information") that they do not wish for others to obtain, such as full name, home address, or phone number.

INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE

Below are more details about the information that CBS8.com gathers from you and how we use it.

  * Email Newsletters. CBS8.com offers email newsletters. Only registered users will be able to receive newsletters. We will use your email address only to send you the newsletter(s) __ that you signed up for. As a policy, we do not rent, sell, or in any way share our newsletter subscriber lists to third parties but we may send notices to the lists on behalf of third parties and our newsletters may __ contain advertisements from CBS8.com sponsors or advertisers. If you want to stop receiving our newsletter, follow the procedures to unsubscribe at the bottom of any newsletter we send you. 

  * Email Updates to Members. CBS8.com may periodically send service email updates announcing special offers and new features to registered users. If you want to stop receiving our email service announcements, please follow the procedures to unsubscribe at the bottom of any newsletter we send you.

  * Promotions. We may ask you for Personally Identifiable Information at other times, including, but not limited to, when you enter sweepstakes, contests or promotions sponsored by a third party. If you choose to enter sweepstakes, contests or promotions of this nature, we may share your Personally Identifiable Information with the third party so they can contact you. The third party may use that information to send you advertisements or other notices, and it is possible that your information may be shared with others.

  * Surveys and User Research. CBS8.com conducts email and web-delivered surveys from time to time, as well as instant polls, to gather information about our audience. It is important to remember that taking these surveys and polls is entirely optional. You have no obligation to respond to them. We share only the aggregate results of these surveys, not individual data, with our advertisers and partners to help them better understand our services and monitor the reach of their advertising on our service. We may also store some of the information that our users provide in these surveys to help us understand your interests and to make the site more useful for you.

  * Use of Cookies. On the Internet, a "cookie" is a piece of information (a file) that a web site transfers to a user's computer for record-keeping purposes. The use of cookies is common on the Internet. The process we use to serve advertisements and to track use of our site involves cookies. Cookies help us and our advertising track the frequency and placement of advertisements. Cookies also enable us to measure aggregate, or total usage and traffic to our site and to specific offerings. This aids us in producing content that best meets our users’ preferences. __ In order to implement new services, we may use other types of cookies as well. We also may use cookies to understand better what parts of our site you like best. We may use that information to display advertisements that we think will be of particular interest to you. You may configure your browser to reject cookies, but this may interfere with some functionality of CBS8.com. 

  * Other Third Parties. CBS8.com may share non-personal, aggregate, or summary, information regarding our users with other third parties not mentioned in this policy. This kind of information does not identify you individually.

  * Legal Authorities. CBS8.com may also disclose Personally Identifiable Information in cases when we have reason to believe that it is necessary to identify someone who may be causing injury to CBS8.com’s rights or property, other users, or anyone else. CBS8.com may disclose Personally Identifiable Information of our users to government authorities at our discretion. 




YOUR CONSENT

By using our web site, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information by CBS8.com.

WEBSITES MENTIONED

The products and services contained on this website are not necessarily endorsed or recommended by KFMB, its staff or management. KFMB cannot be held liable for the accuracy or integrity of these products and services.

CHANGES TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY

From time to time, we may change our Privacy Policy. We will post those changes on this page so that our visitors will always be aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we share the information with others.
