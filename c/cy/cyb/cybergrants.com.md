# CyberGrants.com - About Us - Privacy Statement

|  |  |  |  |    


**CyberGrants understands and respects the privacy of your data.**

We collect certain information about our users. This information assists us in maintaining your membership. The following are the guidelines for what we do with your data. You can rest easier knowing that it is _not sold to anyone for any reason_.

  * We have put measures in place to maintain your on-line privacy.
  * We only use your personal information to help us service your account, to improve our services to you, to provide you with products you have requested, and to inform you about additional products or services that may be of interest to you.
  * We only use your information for customer contact, account maintenance, billing, and to inform you of new features, improvements and any other customer-affecting reason.
  * CyberGrants never sells your personal information to anyone or any organization. We do, however, maintain on-line data transmissions with the IRS for Tax Exempt verification purposes. This is simply for verification of valid tax IDs when we are informed of possible misuse of the CyberGrants.com site. Please review the Terms of Use for more information.
  * Your membership is protected via SSL (Secure Sockets Layer). This measure of protection is used by many electronic commerce sites and on-line banks. Your membership is accessible by inputting your username and personal password. These are unique in our database and your password should never be shared with anyone.
  * The CyberGrants.com website collects limited information about your computer. Such information includes your TCP/IP address, your browser type and your domain. The information we collect is used only by CyberGrants staff to assist us in the monitoring of system performance and up-time.
  * The CyberGrants.com website contains links to other sites on the web and is not responsible for the content, security, privacy, capabilities or availability of such sites.
