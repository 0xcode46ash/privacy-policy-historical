# CycleFish.com - Privacy Policy

CycleFish.com - Privacy Policy

We can be contacted through our [Contact Us](https://web.archive.org/contact.php) or by email at; [info@cyclefish.com](mailto:info@cyclefish.com), page should you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy.

We assign a unique identifier to each user that signs up on our site. We do not share personal information with any participating merchants or other third parties. We use this unique identifier in order to provide you with the most relevant content on our site based on the information you have supplied.

We may also from time to time send email, directly from us and never from a third party, letting you know of new features and content on CycleFish.com in which you may have an interest. You may at any time request to not receive these emails and continue to use all of the other services available only to registered members on our site.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail to [opt-out@cyclefish.com](mailto:opt-out@cyclefish.com).

With respect to cookies: We use cookies ONLY to store temporary, non-private information during your session on the site. Absolutely NO information you provided to us during your registration will ever be stored in a cookie. All CycleFish.com cookies are temporary and destroyed immediately upon leaving our website.

CycleFish.com will never ask you for in any form, your home address, phone number, social security number or any banking or financial data.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses, state or local chapters of the Better Business Bureau, state or local consumer protection office, The Federal Trade Commission by phone at 202.FTC-HELP (202.382.4357) or electronically at http://www.ftc.gov/ftc/complaint.htm.
