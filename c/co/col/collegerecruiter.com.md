# Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to CollegeRecruiter.com will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to let us know. 

**Resale or Disclosure of Information to Third Parties:**

CollegeRecruiter.com does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site to third parties other than our authorized staff, contractors, customers and vendors. Employers and other such organizations may access our resume bank directly, but users may opt to not include their resumes in our resume bank even if they apply to jobs advertised on our site. 

**Browser Information Collected on Our Website:**

We analyze our website logs to constantly increase the traffic to and revenues of our website and to improve the value of the materials available on website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that use our site. 

**Use of Cookies:**

CollegeRecruiter.com uses cookies to more effectively serve banner advertising and to track the website from which our visitors were referred. Our cookies are not personally identifiable and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually use our site. 

**Privacy of Our Email Lists:**

We maintain several email lists to keep our users informed about new features on our website, changes to our affiliate program, and to provide a meeting place for our users. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our lists to anyone. In addition, our list server software will not divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone but authorized staff, contractors and vendors. 

The terms of service of our website, including pricing and privacy policies, are subject to change without notice.
