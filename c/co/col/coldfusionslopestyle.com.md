# ColdFusion SlopeStyle - Privacy Policy

**ColdFusion SlopeStyle**  
1629 Clarkson St. #14  
Denver, Colorado 80218  
303.829.1437  
Email: [info@coldfusionslopestyle.com](mailto:info@coldfusionslopestyle.com)

For each visitor to our website, our web server automatically recognizes only the domain name, but not the e-mail address.

We collect: (1) the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us by e-mail, (2) aggregate statistics about which web pages visitors access and (3) information volunteered by visitors such as survey or contest information and/or website registrations. We set a cookie if you enter the website via one of our special-purpose response doorway pages.

The information we collect is used by us to improve the content of our website, it may also be used to contact you for product marketing purposes. The cookie is only used to measure the effectiveness of various promotional campaigns.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us, please tell us by sending email, or calling, or writing, to ColdFusion SlopeStyle, contact information listed above.

If you supply us with your postal address on-line you may receive periodic mailings from us with information about new services, or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive mailings, please tell us.

If you supply us with your telephone number on-line you may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding new services, or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive telephone calls, please tell us.
