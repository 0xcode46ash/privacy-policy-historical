# Privacy Policy | Content - Information About CozyCot |

**Your information**

**What will you do with my email address?**

Not too much. You'll receive our daily CozySpree offering in your inbox, and occasional alerts relating to your purchases. We take your personal information very seriously – our focus is to use your e-mail to inform you of our great sprees every day. Our privacy policy gives the full story – please take a look.

**What will you do with my credit card and billing information?**

This info is recorded via a Secure Socket Layer connection (SSL) and is fully encrypted. This and your other personal information is safe and secure and will only be used for your intended purpose.
