# Consumer's Advocate - Educated Decisions Make Informed Consumers.

ConsumersAdvocate Privacy Policy 1. ConsumersAdvocate’s Commitment to Privacy. This privacy policy (“Policy”) is designed to advise you about how ConsumersAdvocate collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide. Your use of the ConsumersAdvocate website has been made conditional upon your acceptance of the terms of this Policy. ConsumersAdvocate provides reviews and information (the “Services”) at no cost to you . ConsumersAdvocate does not provide any legal services. Your use of the Services requires that you provide certain personally identifying information (“Information”). ConsumersAdvocate will also share this Information with certain third parties (“Advertising Partners”) if you have consented to such use. 2. Information Collected. a. Information You Provide to ConsumersAdvocate: All information you provide to ConsumersAdvocate, you provide voluntarily. ConsumersAdvocate may collect any information you enter on our site, or that you give us in any other way. You can choose not to provide certain information, but you may not be able to take advantage of the Services. Without limitation, ConsumersAdvocate collects the following information: your first and last name, your birthdate, your address; including street, number, city, state, and zip code, your email address, your phone number, your vehicle make, model, and year; the ownership or lease status of your vehicle, your vehicle’s primary use, your estimated average mileage, the status of your current insurance coverage, your insurance coverage type, your insurance coverage level, your insurance coverage deductible, the date of your insurance expiration, the length of time you have been insured, your gender, your marital status, your occupation, and your education level. All personally identifying information types collected will be clearly labeled at the time you provide it. b. Automatic Information: When you visit a website, you disclose certain information, such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, operating system, referring page, exit page, and the time of your visit. This site, like many other sites, records this basic information about visits to our site. This type of information is not visually labeled or seen, but is automatically collected by the ConsumersAdvocate website. c. “Cookies”: Cookies are pieces of information that are transferred to your computer from a web server. Most browsers are set up to accept cookies, but you can change your settings to have your browser notify you when you receive a new cookie or to refuse to accept cookies. 3. How and When the Information is Used. The Information ConsumersAdvocate collects is used for providing you the Services. ConsumersAdvocate may also use the Information to notify you about changes to our website, new services, or special offers. ConsumersAdvocate will also share this Information with its Advertising Partners if you have consented to such use. The Advertising Partners with which ConsumersAdvocate shares your information will use the Information to offer you their products and services. Your consent to this Policy also authorizes ConsumersAdvocate to provide your Information to law enforcement or governmental officials pursuant to a legal duty, as ConsumersAdvocate believes or understands it. 4. Protection of Your Information. The privacy and protection of your information is important to ConsumersAdvocate. ConsumersAdvocate does not make any personal information available to third parties without your consent. 5. Who Has Access to the Information. ConsumersAdvocate will not disclose any Information to Advertising Partners without first receiving your consent. However, once you have provided consent to the use of your Information by Advertising Partners and such Information has already been obtained by our Advertising Partners, the subsequent use of such Information is controlled by the practices of those Advertising Partners, which is beyond the control of ConsumersAdvocate. ConsumersAdvocate does not make any representation or warranty as to the collection, privacy, security, or further use of your Information once received by its Advertising Partners. 6. Methods Used to Protect Your Information. ConsumersAdvocate uses security software to protect the confidentiality of your Information. In addition, ConsumersAdvocate’s business practices are reviewed periodically for compliance with policies and procedures governing the security and confidentiality of its information. ConsumersAdvocate’s business practices limit employee access to confidential information, and limit the use and disclosure of such information to authorized persons. Despite the foregoing security features employed by ConsumersAdvocate, no amount of security measures can completely guarantee the safety and security of your Information or its online transmission. Accordingly, you waive all claims relating to or arising out of, and ConsumersAdvocate will not be liable for, any breach of security in the case your Information is compromised. 7. Children. This website is not intended to provide services or sell products to children under the age of 18 without the consent and involvement of a parent or guardian. If you are under 18, you may use this website only with the involvement of a parent or guardian. By using the website, you represent and warrant that you are either 18 years old, or are using the services offered by this website with the supervision and involvement of a parent or guardian 18 years of age or older. This website is not intended to provide any Services to children under the age of 13. You may not use this website if you are under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect Information from children under the age of 13, nor do we knowingly distribute such Information to Advertising Partners. If we become aware that we have inadvertently received Information from someone under the age of 13, we will delete such information from our records and prohibit that person’s use of the Services. 8. How You Can Access Your Information. You can request access to all your Information currently maintained by ConsumersAdvocate by sending an e-mail to [e-mail address]. Additionally, you may contact ConsumersAdvocate by calling [phone number] or writing to [physical address]. 9. Consent. By using the Services, you consent to this Policy, including the collection, use, and transmission of your Information as described herein . You can also opt-out. By opting out, ConsumersAdvocate will cease using your Information for its Services, and will not share your Information with any Advertising Partners until you consent to such use again in the future. To opt-out, send an email to [e-mail address] or click here: Opt-Out. Opt-out requests will be handled within seven (7) days. 10. Changes to Privacy Policy. If ConsumersAdvocate’s Policy or procedures change, ConsumersAdvocate will immediately post those changes to the website . Any such changes will be effective immediately upon being posted, unless otherwise stated in the change. Please review the Policy regularly so you can stay informed of ConsumersAdvocate’s practices, as they may change from time to time. 11. Effective Date. This Privacy Policy is effective as of 2/4/13.
