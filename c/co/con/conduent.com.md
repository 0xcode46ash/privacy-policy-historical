# About Us : Privacy Policy

Conduant will only collect, process and store information about you that we have openly collected from you or with your consent. The personal details you supply on Conduant.com, including any sensitive data, will be held for processing, marketing purposes, and for mailing you with information only about Conduant. Your details will be used only by Conduant and will not be made available to any Third Party. You consent to your details being held for the above purpose.

In addition to this we do log your IP address which is automatically recognised by our Web Server. We will only collect information that is relevant to providing the service. 

If you use standard e-mail for communication with Conduant, this will not be protected and you should be aware that Conduant cannot guarantee that it remains confidential while in transit. All e-mail sent to Conduant will be monitored and checked to ensure our systems operate effectively. 

Cookies are used on the Conduant site for storing session information only.
