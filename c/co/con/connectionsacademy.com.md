Connections Academy respects and understands the importance of privacy and security for our online customers. Upon request, all Connections Academy customers have the ability to access personal information maintained by Connections Academy, including contact information, unique identifier information, transaction information and financial information. This information is used only by Connections Academy and its affiliate Sylvan Learning Systems, Inc. to contact consumers for marketing and operational purposes. Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers or e-mail addresses on-line may receive telephone or e-mail contact from us with information regarding special promotions, new products and services or upcoming events. 

If at any point you no longer want to receive information from Connections Academy you can e-mail us at [customersupport@connectionsacademy.org](mailto:customersupport@connectionsacademy.org) and ask to be removed from our contact lists. 

Connections Academy and its affiliate Sylvan Learning Systems, Inc. will not release, sell, rent, or trade the personal information of any of our customers to any third party.
