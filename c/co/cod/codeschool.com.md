# Privacy Policy - Code School

This Privacy Policy applies to all products and services offered through Code School. 

## What We Collect and How It's Used

  * **Information you provide:** When you sign up for or use our products, we may request personal information from you. We will not sell this information to others, but may use it to offer you additional products, promotions, or for future development ideas. 
  * **Communications:** When you communicate with Code School, through email or other means, we may retain copies, recordings, or transcripts. These will be used for such things as internal training, responses to your inquiries, suggestions, or ways to improve the quality of our product. 
  * **Cookies:** Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help make websites easier to use. We use cookies to remember things like your preferences and settings. Third parties may place cookies on your browser after you visit our site for targeted ads that could show up on other sites you visit. ([You can opt-out of receiving targeted ads](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/).) If you reject cookies, you will not be able to access our site. 



## Sharing Your Information

Unless we have your permission or applicable laws are being enforced, we will not share your information. 

Code School is owned by Pluralsight, LLC, a Nevada limited liability company ([www.pluralsight.com](http://www.pluralsight.com/)). As such, by visiting and interacting with our site, you agree that we may share your information with Pluralsight and you understand and acknowledge that your use of the Code School site is also subject to [Pluralsight's Privacy Policy](https://www.pluralsight.com/privacy). 

## Security of Your Information

Since you provide us with personally identifiable information, we take commercially reasonable steps to protect your information against unauthorized access or disclosure. Access to your information is restricted to employees, contractors, and affiliates of Code School who are bound by confidentiality obligations. 

## Changes

We may update or change our Privacy Policy as needed. We will notify you of any significant changes.
