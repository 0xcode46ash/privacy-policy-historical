# Privacy Policy | CrossOver Support | CodeWeavers

  1. [__Home](https://web.archive.org/support/wiki/)

  3. [Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/support/wiki/website/privacy)



  * CodeWeavers is dedicated to the privacy of its customers.  

  * Whatever specific information you give to us will be used only to enable user specific features of the CodeWeavers Store. We will not give your information to third parties, nor will we send you email you did not request. We may use your information in the aggregate to better understand our market.  

  * The CodeWeavers Store uses cookies only to preserve login information in your web browser. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to these cookies.  

  * We may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.
