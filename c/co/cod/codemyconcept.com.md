# Code My Concept

Non-disclosure agreement (NDA) for CODEMYCONCEPT.com clients

CODEMYCONCEPT fully respects your professional artwork and will only use it to assist you with the successful completion of your project. Your copyrights will remain yours at all times.

CODEMYCONCEPT will not take your artwork, files or images or use them in any other website that is not yours; we will not resell it, or give it to anyone under any circumstance.

CODEMYCONCEPT.com guarantees that all information shared with us, including artwork, files or images, communication and contact information, will remain confidential at all times.

CODEMYCONCEPT.com will not contact your clients or the clients of your clients under any circumstance.

### Data Security

### Information Collected

This is an example of the type of information we will require from you and the type of information will be collected or submitted to CODEMYCONCEPT through our order form, product request, and other pages. You will most likely be asked to share the following information:

* Email address

* Client information requests

* Order-specific information, including source files and project notes

### How we use your information

The personal information is only used to complete your order. We do not and will not share this information with outside parties.

Your email address is used only to for notifications about the status of your order. It is not used for any other purpose, and it is not shared it with outside parties.

Finally, we never use or share your project or any information related to your project with any other parties. All the information provided to us online will remain safe and secure at all times.

### How you can access or correct your information

You can certainly correct factual errors in your personal information by sending us an email and providing us with prove that shows an error.

To protect your privacy and security, we take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections to your project.
