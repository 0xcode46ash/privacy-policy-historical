## Privacy Policy Notice

  


### Non-Disclosure Agreement

CodeMyConcept respects your professional artwork. We claim no rights over design mockups our clients provide. We will not take your artwork for use on any other site, or resell it. CodeMyConcept guarantees that all information that you share with us, will remain confidential. We respect your business and We will never contact your clients or the clients of your clients.
