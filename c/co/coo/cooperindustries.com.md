# Cooper Industries | Privacy Policy

Cooper respects the privacy of individuals who visit this site. Although Cooper collects the domain names of visitors, it does not collect e-mail addresses. Cooper may request the visitor's name and contact information in order to respond to certain inquiries and requests. However, Cooper does not provide to third parties any information collected from visitors.
