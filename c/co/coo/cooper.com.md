# Cooper | Privacy

* * *

Cooper respects your privacy. We will never sell your contact information or share it with another company. We also promise not to fill your inbox with useless junk. 

If you have questions or concerns about how your information is used, email [webmaster@cooper.com](mailto:webmaster@cooper.com).
