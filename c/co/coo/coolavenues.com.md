**_cool_ avenues.com Privacy Policy** 

The policies below are applicable to www.coolavenues.com. The Cool Avenues site is maintained by Zygote Technologies Pvt. Ltd., Mumbai, India. We can be reached via e-mail at [webmaster@coolavenues.com](mailto:webmaster@coolavenues.com) 

Cool Avenues respects each individual user's right to privacy. Cool Avenues may place a "cookie" on the visitor's hard drive in order to facilitate a user friendly experience. Cookies are small pieces of information that a Web site places on your computer's hard drive for tracking purposes. Cool Avenues uses cookie information it receives by placing the cookie on user's hard drive to improve the efficiency of customer service on our online subscription based sites, allowing subscribers to easily change addresses, and add or cancel subscriptions by automatically verifying that you are a subscriber. 

Cool Avenues also uses cookies to enhance our site content by compiling aggregate information about what specific sections users visit and which pages. Cookies may also be placed by our advertisers or independent third parties who actually operate the servers on which the ads reside. Although, Cool Avenues encourages third parties to adhere to appropriate privacy standards and policies, Cool Avenues is not responsible for the actions or policies of such parties. 

Cool Avenues collects personal identifying information, when such information is specifically and knowingly provided, from users during (i) contest, survey, sweepstakes or site registration promotions, and (ii) subscription registration for sites or services requiring such registration information. Cool Avenues may use this information for marketing and promotional purposes. Cool Avenues, occasionally likes to inform our users of special deals, new products, and other third party product and service offers that we believe our users will find interesting. Individual users can request, at any time, that their information not be used for such purposes by sending an e-mail request to Cool Avenues at the address given above. ]

If a Cool Avenues user knowingly provides personal information such as a user name or e-mail address in a chat or bulletin board area, that user should be aware that the information provided can be collected and used by others in that area. No information should be submitted to Cool Avenues by children 16 years of age or under without their parent's or guardian's consent. Cool Avenues does not monitor the age of its user audience.
