# Privacy Policy - CompareTV

## What information do we collect?

We only collect solicited information you provide including personal information when you:

\- Contact us – via our online contact form  
\- Provide comments – via our online comment function

You can also visit our site anonymously.

## What do we use your information for?

We don not keep you data for marketing or other purposes. Your personal data is only used to reply to commets of contact form submissions.

## How do we protect your information?

Your data is protected by the secure server services of crazy domains. www.crazydomains.com.au

## Do we use cookies?

We do use cookies for the purpose of remarketing:

\- We display online advertising to some visitors to our site as they browse other websites  
\- We use third-party vendors, including Google, to show you ads on sites across the Internet.  
\- Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your past visits to our website.  
\- Anyone can always opt out of Google’s use of cookies by visiting Google’s Ads Settings. http://www.google.com/settings/ads  
\- In order to opt out of a third-party vendor’s use of cookies you can visit the Network Advertising Initiative opt out page. http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/

## Use of DoubleClick’s remarketing pixels

We use DoubleClick’s display remarketing pixels and click tracking, you may opt out of DoubleClick’s use of cookies by visiting the DoubleClick opt-out page https://www.google.com/settings/ads/onweb#display_optout or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page: http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/

## Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety.

## Third party links

Occasionally, we may include or offer up third party products or services to users of our website. These third party sites will have separate and or independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.

## Your Consent

By using our site, you consent to our privacy policy

## Changes to our Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page.
