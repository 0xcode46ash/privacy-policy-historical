# Privacy Policy - UBM Americas

### PRIVACY STATEMENT

_Effective August 22, 2015_

Welcome to our website. We are UBM Americas, a group of aligned UBM commercial entities and brands serving business and professional communities across North and South America. We created this website as a convenient, central place for you to find more about all of our events, publications and offerings.

**Scope**

This Privacy Policy only covers this website and any similar informational site where it is published.

Our divisions and brands publish their own privacy statements to describe their respective practices. We encourage you to consult those statements as you look to discover the events and offerings that may be of interest to you.

**Information We Collect About You**

We don’t collect any information about you as an individual from the site unless you choose to submit your name and contact details to us. If you do, we, or one of our business entities, may use the information you provide to answer your request.

We use analytics cookies to collect information about how the site is used. We do not connect that information about you as an individual and only review the results on an aggregated basis. Most browsers offer tools to block or opt out of most cookies.

**Other Important Information**

The UBM America companies are subsidiaries of UBM plc. We collect and process information on servers around the world.

We will post and date any changes to this Privacy Policy. Please check here periodically. Your visit to this website constitutes your consent to the practices in place at the time of your use.

If you have any questions about UBM’s privacy practices, please address them to [privacyrequests@ubm.com](mailto:privacyrequests@ubm.com) or
