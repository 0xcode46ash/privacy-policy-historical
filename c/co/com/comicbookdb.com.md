# Comic Book DB - The Comic Book Database

## Privacy Policy

When you sign up for a ComicBookDB.com account, we ask you for personal information (such as your name, email address and a password).

This information is only used for the purposes of providing functionality for ComicBookDB.com to our users. The only outside organizations that may have access to a limited amount of your information are our contest sponsors if you enter one of the contests that we run. Otherwise, **it is not shared with anyone.**
