# ComicSpace - Privacy Policy

### [Home](https://web.archive.org/) / Privacy Policy

### Visitor Information:

ComicSpace.com does NOT collect your personal information. However, each time you access a page on ComicSpace.com, the following aggregate data is recorded...

  * Your IP address
  * The Web page that referred you
  * The date and time of your visit
  * The name and version of your Web browser



The information collected by ComicSpace.com is used to...

  * Determine the popularity of individual pages and sections
  * Improve site quality and services



The information collected by ComicSpace.com is NOT shared with other organizations.

###  ComicSpace Account 

When you sign up for a free ComicSpace account, your email address will be recorded and stored in the ComicSpace.com database. Sign up is completely optional, and does not happen automatically. Your email address will be recorded ONLY if you fill out and submit the [Sign Up](https://web.archive.org/register.php) form.

After creating a ComicSpace account, you can easily delete your account by logging in and going to _Manage Account_ > _Delete Account_.

Your email address will NOT be shared with other organizations.

### Cookies:

ComicSpace.com uses cookies to...

  * Identify return visitors



### Ad Servers:

ComicSpace.com allows other reputable companies to place ads on its pages. As a result of your visit to ComicSpace.com, ad server companies may collect information about you such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information. For further information, consult the privacy policies of...

  * [Google](http://www.google.com/ads/)
  * [ValueClick](http://www.valueclick.com/)
  * [Amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com/)
  * [GameFly](http://www.gamefly.com/)
