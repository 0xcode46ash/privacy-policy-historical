# Privacy Policy - CompareCards.com

**  
PRIVACY POLICY**

Comparecards.com is committed to protecting the privacy of its users.  The following overview has been prepared to inform you of our privacy policy. 

Comparecards.com does not collect personally identifiable information from you or your computer when you visit or browse the website. Comparecards.com is a marketplace that simply connects you with the various credit card companies and/or credit reporting agencies that you voluntarily select.  You will be asked to provide personally identifiable information to the various credit card companies and/or credit reporting agencies which you select or to other third-party websites to which you are linked from Comparecards.com.  Comparecards.com does not have access to such information unless you affirmatively provide us with it.  Comparecards.com is not responsible for the transmission or handling of any personally identifiable information that you provide to the various credit card companies, credit reporting agencies or other external websites to which you are linked from Comparecards.com or for their privacy practices.  Please review such third parties’ respective privacy policies available on their individual websites.

To the extent that you affirmatively provide personally identifiable information to Comparecards.com (e.g., in an e-mail to Customer Service), Comparecards.com will not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

In order to provide you with the most relevant credit cards that Comparecards.com has access to, Comparecards.com logs the Internet Protocol (IP) address that is automatically assigned to the computer which you use to visit our website.  Comparecards.com does not make any connections between such IP address and any private personally identifiable information.  Comparecards.com may use IP addresses to compile and provide aggregate statistics about our visitors’ traffic patterns in order to administer the services provided by Comparecards.com.

Comparecards.com may utilize cookies to enable you to save voluntarily lists of types of credit cards or for other purposes to enhance useability of the website.  However, Comparecards.com does not utilize cookies on our website to obtain any personally identifiable information about our users. Comparecards.com cannot represent the same for external or third party websites to which you are linked from Comparecards.com.  Please review such third parties’ respective privacy policies to determine whether they utilize cookies. 

Comparecards.com reserves the right to change its privacy policy and statement in the future to comply with applicable law or otherwise.  Any changes will be posted under our “Privacy Policy” webpage.

We encourage you to take adequate precautions to protect your personally identifiable information, both on and off the Internet.  To learn more, visit: <http://onguardonline.gov/index.html>.

This privacy statement applies solely to information presented and obtained at Comparecards.com. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us by [clicking here](https://web.archive.org/home/contactus).
