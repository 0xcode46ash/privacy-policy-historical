# Comp-U-Plus Computer Peripheral Reseller

## Privacy Statement for www.compuplus.com

Comp-U-Plus Direct has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to consumer privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for www.compuplus.com. 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Additionally, your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart.

Our site's checkout process requires you to provide us with your contact, shipping, and financial information, information. This information is used to for order fulfillment, and to keep you updated on the status of your order. We do not maintain a mailing or email list, and definitely do not sell our customer information.

## Security

All of your personal information is entered on our secure SSL server for your protection. 

## Correct/Update

If you would like to change any submitted customer information, you can send an email to [webmaster@compuplus.com](mailto:webmaster@compuplus.com) , or call Customer Service at 845-352-8100.
