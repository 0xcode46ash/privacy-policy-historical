# Privacy and Personal Data

This privacy policy describes the ways we collect, store, use and protect your personal information. By visiting this website and/or using any related data published on, by or through Country IP Blocks, Remote Technology Management, it's subsidiaries, affiliates or related entities, you accept this policy. We may amend this policy at any time and without notice by posting a revised version on our website. The revised policy will be effective immediately upon posting it to our website. We last modified this policy on Februaryr 26, 2008.

### How we collect information about you.

When you visit our website, we collect your IP address and related information, such as timestamp, browser type, pages visited, data requested, data received, form input, etc.

### How we protect and store personal information

Personal information is used to describe information that can or may be associated with a specific person or IP address and can be used to identify a specific person or IP address. Your personal information is stored on our systems within the United States.

### How we use the personal information we collect

Your personal information is used to improve your Country Block IP website experience, to improve our security and to maintain controls over the use of our bandwidth & published website data, and to maintain copyright controls on our data.

### How we share personal information with other parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except by court order. We do not use your personal information to create mailing lists for use or sale. We do not use your personal information to generate SPAM or other unwanted contact.

### How long do you keep my personal data?

We may retain your data for any length of time.
