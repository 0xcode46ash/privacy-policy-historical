# CouponCraze.com - Coupons, Online Coupon Codes and Discounts

FlashVIC, Inc. ("CouponCraze.com") has created this privacy policy in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. 

This site contains links to other sites. CouponCraze.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

This site uses cookies for internal site usage for the custom coupon category. Also javascript and cookies are used for web tracking. 

We run contests on our site where contact information (like their email address) are used as contact data for our contests to send users prize information or for weekly newsletter mailings. All mailings are completely opt-in. 

Any extensions, gadgets, or applications for CouponCraze.com do not collect any personal information. The only transmission of data are API requests for data from our database of offers.
