For each visitor to our web site, our Web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address of the visitor.

We collect the e-mail address (where possible) of visitors to our web site who elect to provide it. Other information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations are sometimes requested on an elective, voluntary basis.

The information we collect is used for internal review, and usage statistics generation, with the ultimate goal of improvimg the content of our web site. This data helps us decide how and when customize the content and/or layout of our pages for best user functionality.

With respect to cookies: We use cookies so that we can identify the unique number of visitors to our web site, no personal information is collected or stored in a cookie.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by writing to us at the above address, or visiting us at [http://www.couponconnector.com/lists/?p=unsubscribe&id=2](http://www.couponconnector.com/lists/?p=unsubscribe&id=2).

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses or phone number.
