# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

* * *

This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for Remember Coupons and its affiliates. This privacy notice applies solely to information collected by this web site and others connected with Remember Coupons Inc. We will, at your request, notify you of the type of personal information that has been collected about you and how any inaccuracies may be corrected.

**Information Collection, Use, and Sharing  
**  
We are the only owners of the information collected on this site and we only have access to information that you voluntarily give us. We will not sell this information to anyone. Your information will not be shared with any third party outside of our organization, other than that which is necessary to, for example, ship an order. Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about specials, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy. 

**Security  
**  
We take extensive measures to protect our customers’ information, and therefore, when you submit sensitive information via our website your information is protected both online and offline. All credit card information transmitted over our sites or related sites is sent via encryption and is therefore perfectly safe. All information submitted to us including but not limited to credit card information is kept in a perfectly secure environment, both on and off line. In the nearly 20 years of the parent company of Remember Coupons engaging in e-commerce, we have never once suffered a breach of confidence or security.
