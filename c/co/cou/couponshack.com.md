# Privacy | Coupon Shack

At Coupon Shack, we take pride in our quality of service to you. In doing so, we assure you that the information we collect kept safe and secure using the latest online security features. We do not collect any individual information without your consent. Any information that is collected is only intended to help provide you with a better shopping experience. We will not share any information about our visitors/members with third party, unless specifically requested in writing from any federal or state authority. Use of this site acknowledges that you have read and understand our privacy policy. Thank you for visiting Coupon Shack and allowing us to provide this valuable free service to you!

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
