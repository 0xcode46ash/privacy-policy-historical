# Coupon Shack: Privacy Policy

We do not collect any information other than from what site you came from (referral site) at CouponShack. The coupons offered are a free service that is made possible with advertising from participating merchants. We do not share or disclose any personal information of any visitors to CouponShack. If required, we reserve the right to report any information collected to any legal authority. Use of the CouponShack.com site indicates your knowledge and acceptance of this privacy policy.
