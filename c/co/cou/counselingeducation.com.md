# Privacy Policy

We value privacy, and therefore, promise to always respect yours. As a website visitor, there are many opportunities for you to share your private information. Please read the following CounselingEducation Privacy Policy, in order to keep up to date with our privacy practices.  


CounselingEducation will collect personally identifiable information at times. Our Web server will automatically recognize consumer domain names, but not your e-mail addresses. We may acquire your e-mail address when you communicate with us via e-mail. Other data that may be collected includes visitor access information, and voluntary information including survey and site registration information. Certain information is collected by CounselingEducation in order to improve our content, customize the layout of our web pages, notify consumers of website updates, or to help our partners deliver relevant advertisements for your benefit.

This site uses cookies in order to store website user preferences, track session information, monitor access, provide notifications on areas of interest, record visitor past activity in order to provide a satisfactory experience, and to generate relevant website content and advertisements.  


You may opt-out of CounselingEducation emails at any time. Simply email us at Support@CounselingEducation.com. We may share your e-mail address with our business partners in order to supply you with relevant ads, and opportunities of interest to you. If you wish to keep your e-mail address private, please let us know at support@CounselingEducation.com.

Note, that if you have provided CounselingEducation with your mailing address, you may periodically receive emails from us. This can include information, updates, or special offerings on products, services, and events. If you do not wish to receive any such information, you may notify us via email at Support@CounselingEducation.com. Please provide us with your legal name and mailing address, so that we may effectively remove you from our mailing list, and those companies we work with.

Any visitor who supplies CounselingEducation with their phone number, can expect to receive telephone reminders of special events, promotions, offerings, and so on. If you do not wish to receive such calls, email us at Support@CounselingEducation.com and we will remove you from our list. Please include your legal name and telephone number in the email. Your name and number will be removed from our list, as well as the lists of those for which we work for and with.  
In order to provide our visitor�s with exceptional service, and to secure a satisfying experience, we work with high quality Ad Servers. These companies generate highly relevant, targeted advertisements. Your information is used to provide you with products and services that are of interest to you. The information used includes your IP address, domain type, and click stream data. 

In certain cases, consumer information may be used in a new way. In the event of these unanticipated changes, CounselingEducation will post the new policy information on this page. Please check this policy periodically to be aware of our current privacy practices.  
At a visitor�s request, they will be able to access the information submitted by them, and collected and stored by us.  
In the event that our data includes factual inaccuracies, a visitor has the ability to request that the data be corrected.

Although we may not be held accountable for the loss, damage, or misuse of information collected through this website, we do take all appropriate measures to secure and protect your personally identifiable information.

If at any time you feel as though CounselingEducation is in violation of this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email address listed below.

Email us at Support@CounselingEducation.com
