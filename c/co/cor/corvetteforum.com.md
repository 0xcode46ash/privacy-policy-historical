**Corvette Forum's Privacy Policy**  
Corvette Forum has a privacy policy that guarantees your privacy on our site. Any information that we collect from you for purposes of site registration, event registration, product reviews, or for other purposes will remain private and confidential unless you specifically choose to allow us to make this information public. 

We will not sell or borrow for use any information about you or your browsing habits to any third-party or representative. You will also be given the ability to opt-out at any time from any information that your provided us, including your name and email address.

Corvetteforum.com works with a third party that serves ads to this site. To find out more about how Flycast manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to: [http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html](http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html).
