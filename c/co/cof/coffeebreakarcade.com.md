# Coffee Break Arcade / Privacy Policy

  
Thank you for visiting CoffeeBreakArcade.com and reviewing our privacy policy. Our privacy policy is very simple: We collect **no** personal information about you when you visit our website unless you choose to provide that information to us. 

This site, Coffee Break Arcade, uses the services of Tribal Fusion for the serving and/or targeting of ads, promotions and other marketing messages. To do this, Tribal Fusion collects anonymous data through the use of cookies. To find out more about how Tribal Fusion manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to [**http://www.tribalfusion.com**](http://www.tribalfusion.com/)
