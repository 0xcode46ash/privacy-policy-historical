# Privacy

* * *

### PRIVACY STATEMENT FOR COSTUME IDEA ZONE

##### AmazingFX has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to privacy. This statement discloses our information-gathering and dissemination practices for the Costume Idea Zone web site.

##### Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information.

##### This site contains links to other sites. AmazingFX is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

##### We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. Ads may contain cookies, and appear to be coming from our site, but in reality they are coming from our partners who are serving ads on our site. Our site does not use cookies. We do not have access to any information about you from cookies you might receive while visiting our site.

#### CHILDREN'S GUIDELINES

##### The Costume Idea Zone web site does NOT collect personally identifiable online contact information without prior parental consent or parental notification. 

##### The Costume Idea Zone web site does NOT give the ability to publicly post or otherwise distribute personally identifiable contact information without prior parental consent.

#### USER INFORMATION DISPLAYED ON THE WEB SITE

##### Users of the Costume Idea Zone web site can submit costume ideas by going to the [Submissions Page](http://costumeideazone.com/CIZ/Submit.aspx). Submissions are added to the site at the discretion of AmazingFX, and will be edited as deemed necessary by the AmazingFX staff. Submissions that are listed on the site will include the first and/or last name of the submitter, if name(s) are provided by submitter. If a name is not provided, the idea will be posted without attribution. No email addresses or other personal information will be displayed. Users who do not wish to receive attribution for costume ideas should say so in their email message.

##### Users who have a currently listed costume suggestion, and wish to change or remove the attribution information, should send email to: [ customerservice@costumeideazone.com](mailto:customerservice@costumeideazone.com).

#### CONTACTING THE WEB SITE

##### If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please contact the Webmaster at: [ customerservice@costumeideazone.com](mailto:customerservice@costumeideazone.com).
