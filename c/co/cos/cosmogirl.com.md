**Privacy Policy and Terms of Use**

This privacy policy describes how Hearst Communications, Inc., the publisher of CosmoGIRL! magazine ("CosmoGIRL!"), treats the personally identifiable information that is collected about you when you visit the web sites to which this policy is linked, and the information collected on CosmoGIRL!'s behalf to the extent CosmoGIRL uses third-party service providers to host the web site or collect information about site users. 

_What is Personally Identifiable Information:_

"Personally identifiable information" is information that we can use to identify you as an individual. Personally identifiable information includes your name, address, telephone number, and any other information that is connected with you personally.

This policy also does not apply to information you may provide to us offline. 

Cosmogirl.com collects no personally identifying information about our users unless they knowingly provide us with some. We may place a "cookie" (a small file that contains no personal information) in your computer's browser file, which helps us gather information about your use of the site to better serve all our visitors, and which enables us to provide you some of the games and features on our site. 

We may use any personal information you send, such as name, age, location, and personal preferences, for content or for marketing and promotional purposes or to send you our newsletter, and we may share your personal information with third parties who may choose to contact you to promote their own goods and services.
