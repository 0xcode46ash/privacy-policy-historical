# Privacy Policy - CosmoBC.com

Aside from the data and information you upload to your CosmoBC.com account (if you are a member), we only collect anonymous statistical information about the use of the portal, as permitted by law. All this is for the sole purpose of enhancing the quality of our services. This information, although anonymous, will not be sold, traded or exchangend with any third party.

When you contact us by email through our contact form, your email address and any personal information disclosed will remain confident and will not be sold, traded or exchangend with any third party.
