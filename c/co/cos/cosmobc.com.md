# Privacy Policy

Aside from the data and information you upload to The CosmoBC Community (if you are a member), we only collect anonymous statistical information about the use of the portal, as permitted by law. All this is for the sole purpose of enhancing the quality of our services. This information, although anonymous, will not be sold, traded or exchangend with any third party. 

All personal information and data uploaded to The CosmoBC Community belongs to their owners and can be removed from the site at any time. The privacy settings can be set to restrict access to personal content by other members if the owner choses so. For all public content you upload you must agree that it will be accessible to all members and guests of The CosmoBC Community. 

When you contact us by email, your email address and any personal information disclosed will remain confident and will not be sold, traded or exchangend with any third party.
