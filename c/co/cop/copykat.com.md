# CopyKat.com

Privacy Policy 

CopyKat Creations, is deeply commited to protecting the privacy of Internet users. Below is information outlining our continued support for the responsible management of consumer information. 

Consumer Information 

CopyKat Creations does not collect any personally identifiable information about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address. However, CopyKat Creations does obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information such as your computer's Internet address, operating system and browser software. We analyze this data in order to provide higher quality services according to the visitor's resources. In some circumstances, we will ask you to voluntarily provide personal information, such as when subscribing to a mailing list, or an E-Announcement List we will notify you that such information is being collected, and identify which informational items are required and which are optional. 

CopyKat Creations. does not transfer or sell this information to third parties. 

Cookie Information CopyKat.com, as well as most Internet sites, uses "cookies" to enhance your experience on the web. A cookie is a unique number that is provided to your computer when you access the website. Our cookies enable you the opportunity to subscribe to our free mailing list, and our E-Announcment service. A cookie will not permit a website operator to learn any personal information about you unless you have previously provided that information to Copykat.com. 

You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the opportunity to decide whether to accept it. 

Third Party Advertising 

CopyKat Creations. works with a third party that serves advertisements to this site. To find out more about how Flycast manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving advertisements on this site, please visit : [ http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html. ](http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html.)

We use DoubleClick's Network, Sonar, and other third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here [http://www.sonar.com/naiprivacy](http://www.sonar.com/naiprivacy/).

Third Party Cookies 

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertisers may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser. You can immediately opt-out of Doubleclick's unique cookies and have it switched to a non-unique cookie by clicking here [ http://www.doubleclick.net/cgi-bin3/optout/check2.pl](http://www.doubleclick.net/cgi-bin3/optout/check2.pl). You will have to repeat this process if your cookies get deleted or you move to a new computer. More information on opting out of Doubleclick's cookies can be found at [ http://www.doubleclick.net/privacy_policy/privacy.htm](http://www.doubleclick.net/privacy_policy/privacy.htm).
