# Privacy Statement | Coast Digital

You are here: [Home](http://www.coastdigital.co.uk/) > Privacy policy

Coast Digital does not capture and store any personal information about individuals who access this website, except where you voluntarily choose to give us your personal details via email, or do so by enquiring about or ordering any of our services.

In the latter instances, the personal information you give us is used exclusively by Coast Digital to provide you with current and future information about our products and services and any other services described on this website. You have a right to know about the personal information Coast Digital hold about you. You also have the right to have your data corrected or deleted. Please address all your requests and/or queries about our data-protection policy to Coast Digital at our UK office address.
