Online Privacy

In order to provide you with a high level of service, we need to know who you are and understand your financial needs. To do this, we may require you to provide certain personal information beyond your name and address. We understand your concerns with providing this information. Your privacy and the security of your information are important to us. 

Any information you provide will be kept confidential and will be used only in conjunction with your member relationship with Coast Capital Savings. Information regarding your account status and any products or services you purchase will not be disclosed or sold to any outside organization.

E-mail

We cannot guarantee confidentiality of the content of any e-mail transmission to or from this site. Our web site allows you the opportunity to contact us by e-mail. If you choose to do so, please keep in mind that your e-mail address will be revealed to us in the e-mail. Your e-mail information will only be used for the specific purpose of responding to your request. It will not be sold or shared with others outside the Credit Union unless we are compelled to do so by law.

Site Tracking

We collect and store information regarding the domain from which you access the Internet, the date and time you access our site and the Internet address of the web site from which you linked to us. Other than that, we collect information on visitors only in the aggregated sense and use it for internal purposes only. This information enables us to tell which areas of our site are of most interest to our visitors, which promotions are most effective and to identify traffic patterns.
