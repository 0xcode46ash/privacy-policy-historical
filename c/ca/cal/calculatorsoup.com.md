# Calculator Soup Privacy

|  | 

We do not collect any information entered into, or results calculated from, any of our calculators. Once you close your browser window, any script information that was created as a result of your use of any calculator, is lost.

If you provide us with your email address when contacting us we will only use it for the purposes of contacting you regarding your comments or suggestions. We do not sell or share your information with any other parties.

## Privacy Policy Statement

This is the web site of CalculatorSoup.com, The Company, owned and operated by Southborough Website Design at <http://southboroughwebsitedesign.com/>

In general, we receive two types of information from users:

  * Personal Information 
    * In the form of return email addresses when a user sends electronic correspondence to us requesting specific information or to make a comment or request a calculator. We will only use this information to communicate regarding the request. 
  * Aggregated Information 
    * Information about users as a whole. The Company receives certain types of information about general activity at CalculatorSoup.com. This does not include personal identifying information, but does include how many times CalculatorSoup.com is visited, what pages or search terms are requested, what domain names (e.g. "aol.com") visitors are coming from and the date and time of each visit. The Company gathers and compiles this information into an aggregate form for statistical analysis and reporting. These reports track website activity overall rather than on an individual level and do not include personally identifiable information.
    * Because aggregated information does not include personal identifying information, it cannot be used to identify you. We may disclose aggregated data to third parties, such as advertisers, or include it in public records.



The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web pages and used to notify users, rarely, about updates, corrections or major changes to our Website. We do not share personal information with outside parties.

With respect to cookies: We use cookies to track set user preferences however, all calculators will still work if you disable your cookies.  


With respect to Ad Servers: When and if ads are present, we allow other companies to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information. For further information, consult the privacy policies of: <http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html>.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above form, address or phone number.
