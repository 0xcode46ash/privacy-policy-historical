# CUSD Public Website

> **_Information Collection & Use_**
> 
> CUSD is the sole owner of any information collected from this site. We will not sell, share, or rent any information to others.
> 
> **_Cookies_**
> 
> A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Our site does NOT use cookies to keep track of our visitors 
> 
> **_Log Files_**
> 
> Our server records log files of visitors, but no individual user info is logged. This information is used purely for purpose of analyzing visitor traffic. We do not log IP addresses, nor is anything linked to personally identifiable information. 
> 
> **_Links_**
> 
> This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this web site. 
> 
> **_Training Class Registration_**
> 
> If a user wishes to register for a class online, we ask for personal information such as social security number, date of birth and email address. All information provided by the user for this purpose is optional although necessary to complete the registration process. CUSD is the sole owner of any information collected from this site in this manner. We will not sell, share, or rent any information to others.
> 
> **_Notification of Changes_**
> 
> If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on our homepage so our users are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under circumstances, if any, we disclose it. We will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected. If you feel that CUSD is not abiding by its posted privacy policy, or have any additional questions, you should contact: [webmaster@capousd.org](mailto:webmaster@capousd.org)
