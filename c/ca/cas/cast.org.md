# Privacy Statement: My CAST: CAST

CAST is a licensee of the TRUSTe Privacy Program. This statement discloses the privacy practices for [http://www.cast.org](http://www.cast.org/). TRUSTe’s certification covers the gathering and dissemination of information on the website not any services, products or software. When you visit a Web site displaying the TRUSTe trustmark, you can expect to be notified of:

  1. What information of yours is collected, 
  2. The organization collecting the information, 
  3. How the information is used, 
  4. With whom the information may be shared, 
  5. What choices are available to you regarding collection, use and distribution of the information, 
  6. The kind of security procedures that are in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of information under CAST's control , 
  7. How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information. 



If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact Julie Wyman ([jwyman@cast.org](mailto:jwyman@cast.org)). If you do not receive acknowledgment of your inquiry or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, you should then contact [TRUSTe](http://www.truste.org/users/users_watchdog.html) will then serve as a liaison with the Web site to resolve your concerns.

### _Information Collection and Use_

If you register to become a My Cast Member we collect your email address and ask you to create a screen name and password. Your e-mail address serves as your username when you log-in to My CAST. Plus, if you opt to receive e-mail updates from CAST (periodic newsletters, event announcements, etc.), these notices will be sent to your e-mail address. If you no longer wish to receive email updates from us, you can opt-out. See the **Choice/Opt-Out and Correct/Update** section below. 

### _External Sites_

This site contains links to other sites. **The CAST Web site** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

### _Public Forums_

Areas of this site make use of on-line message boards. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.

### _Cookies_

Cookies are used in this site to maintain a user's preferences with regards to the site's interface. Cookies are not tied to any personally identifiable information. Users can still enjoy the CAST site without cookies, however preferences for fonts, colors and images will not be preserved without cookies.

### _Server Access Log Files_

IP address, browser type and Web pages visited are logged by our Web server. This information is used for internal site maintenance purposes only.

### _Security_

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Regular back-ups are performed on the Web server and all database information is stored in secured directories.

### _Choice/Opt-Out and Correct/Update_

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and to modify or remove their information.

**My CAST or National Consortium Registration:** Registered users will receive at least one newsletter e-mail after they register. To opt-out of subsequent mailings they can reply to the e-mail with "unsubscribe" in the subject or can e-mail [cast@cast.org](mailto:cast@cast.org) directly. 

To update their MyCAST account information, users can also login to their [MyCAST](https://web.archive.org/web/20021012090108id_/http%3A//www.cast.org/mycast) account. When a user has logged-in, a link labelled "Update My CAST account settings" provides the user with the opportunity to select changes they wish to make, including opting to not receive My CAST e-mail.

### _Notification of Privacy Policy Changes_

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on our Homepage so our users are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under circumstances, if any, we disclose it. If at any point we decide to use personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time it was collected, we will notify our registered users by way of an email. Users will have a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner. We will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected. 

### _Sharing Information_

#### What Organization Is Collecting the Information

CAST is solely repsonsible for the information collected on the CAST Web site. No other organization is involved with this collection.

#### Sharing of Information

All information collected on the CAST Web site is solely used for internal CAST purposes. No information is shared with third parties. 

#### Third Party Intermediaries

CAST uses an organization called ViaTech Technologies, Inc. to allow users to make online purchases of it's eReader product. Transaction made with ViaTech are covered under their privacy policy at <http://www.elicense.com/privacy.asp>. 

#### Supplementation of Information

CAST does not supplement the information it receives from site users with information from any third party.

### _Our Feedback Form_

The CAST online [feedback form](http://www.cast.org/aboutsite/index.cfm?i=306) provides our visitors with an opportunity to provide the organization with useful comments and suggestions. Visitors are encouraged to provide an email address if they would like their feedback answered, however feedback can be made anonymously and will not be tied to any personally identifiable information. Information collected via the feedback form is covered by this privacy policy. 

### _Online Surveys_

From time to time the CAST Web site published [online surveys](http://www.cast.org/udl/index.cfm?i=1781) that visitors are encouraged to participate in. Participation in these surveys are optional. All data collected in these surveys is covered by this privacy policy.

### _Co-Branded Site_

The [Universal Learning Center](http://ulc.cast.org/) is a co-branded Web service of CAST's and is compliant with the privacy policy stated here.

### _Legal Disclaimer_

Though we make every effort to preserve your privacy we may need to disclose personal information when required by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law or comply with a legal process served on our Web site.

### _Contacting the Web Site_

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact Julie Wyman CAST 39 Cross Street Peabody, MA 01960 [cast@cast.org](mailto:cast@cast.org)
