# c4m Privacy Policy

                                  c4m Privacy Policy
Colbette II Ltd., Athalassas, 178, 3rd Floor, 2025 Nicosia, Cyprus (hereinafter "c4m",
“Colbette” “we", "our" or "us") is always committed to protecting and respecting your
privacy.
This policy (hereinafter “c4m Privacy Policy”), together with our Terms of Use of the
cash4members affiliate program (http://cash4members.com/en/pdf/c4m/agb.pdf) and
any other documents referred to in it set out the basis on which any personal
information we collect from the Webmasters of the website cash4members.com, or
that a Webmaster provides to us, will be processed by us. Please read the following
carefully to understand our views and practices regarding the Webmaster’s personal
information and how we will use them.
DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION
Compensation: the compensation payable in respect of the Content as set out in
clause 4 of the Terms of Use of the cash4members affiliate program.
Content: all materials/works including text, information, data, images, photos,
drawings, typographical arrangements, audio or video material in any medium or
form created/produced and provided by the Publisher to us and then published by the
Publisher in order to sell them to the Users.
Group Company: means each party and any undertaking which is a subsidiary
undertaking of Colbette, a parent undertaking of Colbette, a subsidiary undertaking of
a parent undertaking of Colbette or a subsidiary undertaking of a subsidiary
undertaking of Colbette, or a subsidiary or parent undertaking of any of them.
Interaction: the interaction between the Publisher and the Users, including, but not
limited to, responding to paid text messages from Users and private chats via
webcam between Users and the Publisher via our Site.
Marketing Materials: any marketing or creative materials, reseller layouts and
membership areas offered to the Webmasters by c4m on the website
cash4members.com or in a different way to promote the Sites.
Personal Information: means any information relating to a Webmaster including,
but not limited to, the individual's name, gender, date of birth/age, mailing address,
telephone number, email address, personal password and banking account details.
Publisher: a self-employed person or entity producing Content, submitting such
Content to and publishing it on our Site in order to offer such Content for sale and/or
a self-employed person offering Interaction via our Site.
Sites:      means       the     Internet    platform    http://www.MyDirtyHobby.com,
http://www.MyDirtyhobby.de, http://www.privatamateure.com and, if applicable,
websites of selected business partners.
User: the customers          of  the  Sites  for,  and   the   ultimate user   of,  the
Content/Interaction.
User Account: an account registered by a User at the Sites.

Webmaster: a self-employed person or entity being registered on the website
cash4members.com and being the beneficiary of the Compensation for the
promotion of the Sites.
Webmaster Account: an account of a Webmaster registered on the website
cash4members.com.
INFORMATION WE MAY COLLECT, PROCESS AND USE FROM THE WEBMASTERS
A person can visit the website cash4members.com without providing any Personal
Information.
Only information without any personal references, like for example the name of the
person’s Internet Service Provider, the domain name of the website that the person
came from, the names of the data requested by the person as well as the date and/or
time of the requests will be collected by us.
Personal Information will be collected only in the event the Webmaster is voluntarily
providing such information, for example, but not limited to the registration process of
a Webmaster Account or if the Webmaster is contacting us. We will use and store
this information only for the intended purpose it has been submitted to us, in
particular to ensure the performance of the contractual relationship between you and
us.
In addition and to the extent permitted by law, you give your consent that we
temporarily store information about the technical device you are using in order to
access the website cash4members.com, including where available your IP address,
operating system and browser type and version for system administration –
irrespective of whether the person is in possession of a Webmaster Account.
In the following events, we may collect and process the following (Personal)
Information:
   (Personal) Information a Webmaster provides by filling in forms on the website
    cash4members.com. This includes information provided at the time of registering
    a Webmaster Account to use the website cash4members.com and to promote
    the Sites.
   If a Webmaster contacts us or any Group Company or third party that has been
    instructed by us to provide Webmaster support services (irrespective of how the
    Webmaster gets in contact with us, the Group Company or third party), we may
    keep a record of that correspondence and all related information (for example,
    email address, Skype-username, telephone no. etc.).
   Details of transactions a Webmaster carries out through our website
    cash4members.com.
   Details of the visits of the Webmaster to the website cash4members.com
    including, but not limited to, traffic data, location data, weblogs and other
    communication data, whether this is required for our own billing purposes or
    otherwise and the resources that the Webmaster is accessing.
   Personal information the Webmaster is obliged to submit to c4m in order to
    identify the Webmaster.

We use information held about you in the following ways:
   To assign the Users, Publishers and other Webmasters referred by the
    Webmaster and their actions and bookings to the Webmaster.
   To carry out our obligations arising from any contracts entered into between you
    and us, including, but not limited to, billing purposes regarding Users, Publishers
    and other Webmasters referred by the Webmaster and their actions.
   To provide the Webmaster with Compensation.
   To prevent potential breaches of the Terms of Use of the cash4members affiliate
    program and any applicable law.
   To notify you about changes to our service.
Furthermore, unless you have elected not to receive information by us, we may use
your Personal Information to provide you with information about our services,
Marketing Materials, promotions and news as well as providing you with support
services. If you no longer wish to receive such information, you may cancel the
receipt in your Webmaster Account settings on our website cash4members.com.
In the course of the registration of a Webmaster Account to user our services, a
Webmaster needs to confirm his acceptance of the c4m Privacy Policy and that he is
bound by the Privacy Policy as well.
COOKIE POLICY
Our website cash4members.com uses cookies to distinguish you from other
Webmasters on the website. This helps us to provide you with a very good
experience when you browse our website and also allows us to improve our website.
We are currently using the following types of cookies:
1. Cookies that are strictly necessary: These are cookies that are required for the
    operation of our website cash4members.com. This includes, for example, cookies
    that enable a Webmaster to log into the members area at cash4members.com
    and to check if a Webmaster is allowed access to a particular service.
2. Analytical cookies: Such cookies allow us to recognize and count the number of
    Webmasters and to see how Webmasters use and explore the website
    cash4members.com. This helps us to improve the website.
3. Functionality cookies: This type of cookies allows us to recognize you when
    you return to the website cash4members.com and to remember, for example,
    your choice of language etc.
4. Targeting cookies: These cookies record the visit of a Webmaster at the
    website cash4members.com and the pages a Webmaster has visited. Also, these
    cookies are used to identify the websites and the Marketing Materials you are
    accessing the website cash4members.com from. For this purpose, the
    information provided by your internet browser will be used to in order to
    purposefully customize the content of the website.

Rejection of our cookies
You are entitled to refuse the storage of cookies on your computer by activating the
settings of your internet browser software that allows you to block cookies. However,
we advise you to reconsider such decision as you may, unfortunately, not be able to
use all of the provided services and/or to access all or part of the website
cash4members.com.
GOOGLE ANALYTICS
This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc.
(“Google”). Google Analytics uses “cookies”, which are text files placed on your
computer, to help the website analyze how users use the site. The information
generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address)
will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google
will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the website,
compiling reports on website activity for website operators and providing other
services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this
information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third
parties process the information on Google's behalf. Google will not associate your IP
address with any other data held by Google. You may refuse the use of cookies by
selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however please note that if you
do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of this website. By using this
website, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner
and for the purposes set out above.
WHERE WE STORE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION
In addition to the services provided by Google Analytics, the Personal Information we
collect from you may be transferred to, and stored at, a destination outside the
European Economic Area (hereinafter “EEA"). It may also be processed by staff
operating outside the EEA who works for us or for one of our suppliers. Such staff
may be engaged in, among other things, the fulfilment of your order, the processing
of your payment details and the provision of support services. By submitting your
Personal Information, you agree to this transfer, storing or processing. We will take
all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in
accordance with this Privacy Policy.
SECURITY
Any payment transactions will be encrypted using SSL technology. Where we have
given you (or where you have chosen) a password which enables you to access
certain parts of the website cash4members.com, you are responsible for keeping this
password confidential. We ask you not to share your password with anyone.
Unfortunately, the transmission of information via the internet is not completely
secure. Although we will do our best to protect your Personal Information, we cannot
guarantee the security of your Personal Information transmitted to our website; any
transmission is at your own risk. Once we have received your information, we will use
strict procedures and security features to try to prevent unauthorised access.

DISCLOSURE/SHARE OF YOUR INFORMATION
Selling information about our Webmasters is not part of our business. Sharing such
information or disclosing it is therefore limited to the following extent:
In connection to the website cash4members.com, we may disclose your Personal
Information to any employee of c4m and/or to a Group Company.
Also, we may disclose or share your Personal Information with third parties in the
following events:
    With a Group Company that is obliged to process the personal information in
     accordance with this c4m Privacy Policy and as permitted by applicable data
     protection laws.
    If we share Personal Information of a Webmaster with Group Companies or third
     parties that are employed by us to perform functions on our behalf in terms of
     Webmaster support. Those parties have access to personal information needed
     to perform their functions, but may not use it for other purposes. Also, they are
     obliged to process the personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy
     and as permitted by applicable data protection laws.
    If we have to share the Webmaster’s Personal Information with third parties
     employed by c4m in order to process the payment of the Compensation to
     Webmasters. Those parties have access to Personal Information needed to
     perform their functions, but may not use it for other purposes. Also, they are
     obliged to process the Personal Information in accordance with this Privacy
     Policy and as permitted by applicable data protection laws.
    If c4m or substantially all of its assets are acquired by a third party, in which case
     Personal Information held by it about its Webmasters will be one of the
     transferred assets to the third party.
    If we are under a duty to disclose or share your Personal Information in order to
     comply with any legal obligation; or to protect the rights, property, or safety of
     c4m or a Group company, our Webmasters or others. This includes exchanging
     information with other companies and organisations for the purposes of fraud
     protection and credit risk reduction.
NEWSLETTER
You have the right to ask us not to process your Personal Information for sending
you our newsletter. If you no longer wish to receive such information, you may cancel
the receipt of such information in your Webmaster Account settings on our website
cash4members.com.
EXTERNAL LINKS
Our website cash4members.com contains links to and from the websites of our
payment service providers. If you follow a link to any of these websites, please note
that these websites have their own privacy policies and that we do not accept any
responsibility or liability for these policies. Please check these policies before you
submit any Personal Information to these websites.

ACCESS TO INFORMATION
You have the legal right to access Personal Information held about you. In order to
protect the privacy of your Personal Information, please send all inquiries by mail to
Colbette II Ltd., Athalassas, 178, Irene Tower, 3rd Floor, 2025, Nicosia, Cyprus.
Unfortunately, such information cannot be requested by email.
RIGHT OF WITHDRAWAL
You have the right to withdraw consent to this policy. However, in this event, we are
entitled to terminate the contractual relationship between you and us with immediate
effect.
DATA RETENTION
We will hold your Personal Information on our systems for as long as it is necessary
for the provision of services to you based on the contractual relationship. If a
Webmaster asks for the deletion of the Webmaster Account, we will no longer use
the Personal Information except for administration purposes and/or for fraud
prevention measures.
CHANGES TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY
Any amendment or re-enactment of a statutory provision or subordinate legislation or
any change of our internal processes may make it necessary to amend the current
version of the Privacy Policy. In such events, the Webmasters will be informed about
any changes to the Privacy Policy at least 30 days in advance before changes will
come into effect. If you do not use your right to withdrawal (as stated above), the
current version of the Privacy Policy is applicable.
CONTACT
Any questions and comments regarding our Privacy Policy are always welcomed and
should be addressed to privacy@colbette2.com.
