Commission Advance Company LLC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.

I. Introduction Commission Advance Company LLC recognizes the importance of respecting the privacy of our clients and applicants, and we want to assure you that any information Commission Advance Company LLC gathers will be used for its own business needs. Commission Advance Company LLC will not sell or share any information that we   
learn about you from your visit to our website, and Commission Advance Company LLC will gather only information it needs to serve you better.

Commission Advance Company LLC also recognizes the need for security on the Internet, which is why we have Internet security standards and procedures in place to protect against unauthorized disclosure of information or access to information about you from our website. We use the latest in 128-bit encryption technology to protect all of your information. In addition, we limit employee access to personally identifiable information to those employees who have a business purpose for having access to such information.

II. Does Commission Advance Company LLC collect Personal Information? Yes, Commission Advance Company LLC obtains personal information in the following ways:

1\. Your Application and/or Account Opening  
Commission Advance Company LLC's principal business is to offer financial services to licensed real estate professionals. If you are not a licensed real estate professional you will not be able to use our services. In order to provide your service, we may ask you to provide us with some or all of the following information:

• Name   
• Mailing addresses   
• E-mail address   
• Social Security number   
• Birth date  
• Real estate license number  
• Income verification (1099's)   
• Home and business phone numbers   
• Broker and escrow/title details   
• Previous and year to date commission earnings

We may request additional financial information in order to provide you financial services. In addition, we may request information that helps us keep your account secure. We use the information you give us to optimize the financial services we offer, evaluate your creditworthiness and prevent fraud. We will not be able to process your application if you do not provide us with the information requested on the application. We do not share this information with third parties.

We recognize that you may have concerns about the security of your information in making an application on-line. If you are not comfortable opening an account on-line you can call us at (888)-654-2274 or print the application screen and fax it to us at (888)-965-2274.

2\. Your Statement and Communications with Commission Advance Company LLC  
Upon opening of an account, we track your transactions including where and how you access and use the account in order to protect against fraudulent use of the account. In addition, we keep track of when and why you write or speak with us regarding your account. This allows us to provide consistent quality service. We will communicate with you by fax or telephone to obtain the information necessary to service your account.

3\. Visiting Commission Advance Company LLC on the Web  
We obtain information on-line. We track the flow of traffic on our website. We obtain personal information from visitors to our site at the time a visitor applies for one of our products or services. In addition, our computer and your computer may communicate during your visit to our website which allows us to customize screens and identify which part(s) of the site has been accessed.

III. How does Commission Advance Company LLC use Personal Information? Commission Advance Company LLC uses personal information in a few ways:  
As noted above, Commission Advance Company LLC uses the information to evaluate your request to open an Advance Account and to establish your account limits. Thereafter, we use the information to provide our clients with the highest level of service and protect our clients from fraudulent use of the account.

Commission Advance Company LLC is always looking for opportunities to provide value to our clients by making available offers of goods and services that are suited to the needs and interests of our clients.

We will disclose your personal information in response to a lawful request issued by a court, government agency, or regulatory authority with jurisdiction to make the request.

You may also select how you want to receive the offers of goods and services. You may choose to opt out of receipt of offers through the mail, by telephone, fax or by e-mail. You may opt out of one, two or all of these kinds of delivery. You may choose not to receive the offers of goods and services that we make available to our Clients by writing to us at:

V. Do I Have Access to My Account Information? Yes, you have the right to obtain information about you including your application and details of your account use. If you believe there is an error or you have a question about your account, call us toll free at (888)-654-2274. Call us as soon as possible after you become aware of the possible problem and give us the following information:  
• Your name, company name and account number.   
• Describe the error and explain, if you can, why you believe there is an error.   
If you need more information, describe the item you are not sure about. We will respond to you as soon as possible.

  
VI. What If I Have a Question or Problem? Commission Advance Company LLC is committed to preserving the privacy of our Clients. We have described the manner in which we collect and use personal information. In the event you have concerns, questions or a problem arising out of our performance pursuant to this policy, it should be directed to us via toll free telephone at (888)-654-2274 or toll free fax at (888)-965-2274.

VII. Can I E-mail Commission Advance Company LLC? Yes. In order to maximize the security of e-mail to Commission Advance Company LLC, we have established an easy and secure method to send us e-mail. Please e-mail to privacy@commissionadvance.com.

VIII. How Will I Be Notified of Changes To The Privacy Policy?If there is a change in our privacy practices we will notify you by fax and/or email. Additionally, we will obtain written or active email consent from you if we intend on using the information we have already collected in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection.

IX. Do You Share Information With The Advance Rewards Partners?No, we do not share information with our Advance Rewards Partners including Advance Rewards for referrals. We only receive the information necessary from our Advance Rewards Partners to credit you with the cash rewards you have earned.

X. How Does Your Site Use Security and Encryption Technology?Commission Advance Company LLC recognizes the need for security on the Internet, which is why we have the latest in Internet security standards and procedures in place to protect against unauthorized disclosure of information or access to information about you from our website. We use the latest in 128-bit encryption technology to protect all of your information. In addition, we limit employee access to personally identifiable information to those employees who have a business purpose for having access to such information. All employees are fully aware of our security policies and practices and are required to use password protection to gain access to our systems. Our security policies are reviewed quarterly.

XI. What Are The Privacy Policies of Links That Appear On Your Site?Commission Advance LLC is not responsible for any privacy issues regarding links that appear on our site. You should review any such privacy policies directly.
