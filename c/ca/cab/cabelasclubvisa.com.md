Definitions
Affiliates                                   Companies related by common ownership or control. They can be

                                               Incorporated.
Nonaffiliates                                Companies not related by common ownership or control. They can be

                                               independently offer their products or services to you.
Joint marketing                              A formal agreement between nonaffiliated financial companies that
                                             together market financial products or services to you.
                                             • World’s Foremost Bank does not jointly market.
Other important information
California Residents: We do not share personal information about customers with California billing addresses except
as permitted by Cal. Fin. Code Secs. 4050 et seq. and other applicable law.
Vermont Residents: We do no share personal information about customers with Vermont billing addresses except
as permitted by 8 Vt. Stat. Ann. § 10201 et seq. and other applicable law.
