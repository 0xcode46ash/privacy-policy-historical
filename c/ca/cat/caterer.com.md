# caterer.com | Privacy Policy

  


**Privacy Policy**

**E-mail communication from Caterer.com and related businesses**   
Caterer.com sends regular e-mail updates on industry news as well as careers, recruitment and training features. Caterer.com may also use the contact information you provide to communicate via e-mail, on behalf of other web sites and publications that are part of the Caterer & Hotelkeeper Group. We may also use this information to communicate with you by e-mail about other related web sites, magazines and events that may be of interest to you. Please contact us with your e-mail address(es) at webadmin@caterer.com should you wish to unsubscribe from any of the following: 

  * News Alert
  * Careers Bulletin
  * Learning Update
  * Recruiter Focus
  * Product Update
  * All e-mails from Caterer.com and related businesses



Alternatively you can click on the ‘unsubscribe’ link on any of the e-mail communications, which will automatically delete the e-mail address you give, from our systems.

**Other communication**

Your information may also be disclosed to other companies who wish to communicate with you by e-mail, direct mail (post) or telephone about their products and services including future job and career opportunities. This could involve transfer to countries outside the European Economic Area, where data protection laws are less stringent. 

If you do not wish to receive communication from other companies, please visit our data protection page at [www.reedbusiness.co.uk/dataprotection](http://www.reedbusiness.co.uk/dataprotection) or write to us at Caterer.com, Quadrant House, The Quadrant, Sutton, England, SM2 5AS.

**Online applications and CVs**  
We understand the importance of maintaining the confidentiality of your CV, job preferences and other information you may give us while applying to jobs through Caterer.com.

If you choose to attach your Word CV as part of your application this will firstly be checked for any viruses by Caterer.com and then deleted from our systems within 7 days.

If you choose to use the Caterer.com template to create a CV, this information will be stored on our database, and will be made available for searching and viewing by potential recruiters who may contact you to discuss employment opportunities. These may be direct recruiters, employment agencies or other organisations.

After six months we will contact you by e-mail and ask you to update your CV. If you do not reply or do not update it we will delete your CV from our database.

**Please note:** Your current employer may be a customer of Caterer.com, or a client of one of our customers. Caterer.com cannot restrict the customers who access CVs on our database and cannot be held responsible for any eventuality. As part of the CV registration process Caterer.com keeps your personal details including your e-mail address private. Potential recruiters may contact you by e-mail but your e-mail address will not be revealed to them.

**'Cookies'**  
We make use of cookies to gather valuable information about how users move around the site. This enables us to understand how the site is used and to make improvements. The information we gather by use of cookies does not include personally identifying information. 

In addition, the third party companies who sell and provide advertisements to the site make use of cookies for the purpose of serving and targeting advertisements more effectively. For more information about these cookies, how you can opt out of receiving them and how this will affect your use of web sites, please go to: 

[http://www.reedbusiness.co.uk/dataprotection ](http://www.reedbusiness.co.uk/dataprotection)  
<http://www.adtech.info/privacy_en.php>

**Statistical information**  
Aggregate statistical information provided by us to our advertisers or others regarding sales or web site usage will not include personally identifying information. 

**Access to Data**  
You have the right to be told what personal information we hold about you on our databases. You should write to us at Caterer.com, Quadrant House, The Quadrant, Sutton, Surrey SM2 5AS stating your full name and address, and all e-mail updates, publications or services from Caterer.com to which you know you subscribe. A small administrative charge will be made for fulfilling this request.
