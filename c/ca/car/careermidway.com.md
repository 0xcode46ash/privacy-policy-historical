# careermidway.com | disclaimer and privacy policy

Now a day incidents like theft of one's identity are observed quite frequently on the Internet. We all need to be careful and brainy about the usage of our personal data. We endeavour to ensure that the information you have submitted to us will remain private and protected and will not be disclosed or used in any way without your proper authority. We are committed to provide all our users a safe and secure experience.

All the material in this website is given for information purpose only. Our major purpose is to facilitate needy job seekers with regard to avail them latest and number of job listings at one place in free.  We are not making money of any thing taken from newspapers of Pakistan and Middel East and other job posting sources.   We are really greatful to Pakistan / Middle East newspapers particularly Dawn, The News, Daily Jang, Khaleej Times, Gulf News as well as other job posting sources in this regard.

We will not be responsible for any ad misleading the people. We are only providing these ads on website to facilitate the users freely, so we are not responsible for false information, if any.

We are not obliged to put all the ads of the newspaper(s) on the website. So if some harm occur due to this then we won't be responsible.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. 

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
