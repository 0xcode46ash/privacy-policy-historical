# Privacy Policy

  
Carsforsale.com respects the privacy of its users on this site. This statement explains how we use some of the information that you may supply to us through the carsforsale.com Web site. If any modifications are made to this policy, they will be posted on our Web site. 

**Personal Information:**  
When you sign up to sell your vehicle(s), use our car locator service, or reply to an ad on our site, we may ask for some personally identifiable information about you. We will only share this personally identifiable information with third parties in order for them to provide information or perform services specifically requested by you. Carsforsale.com may use some of this information to perform statistical analyses in order to inform others about the profile and demographics of our users. We will only disclose the results of these analyses in aggregate form to third parties. 

**E-Mail Addresses:**  
Email addresses will not be distributed or sold to third parties. The carsforsale.com site uses e-mail addresses to uniquely identify and communicate between the various users of our service. If you provide your e-mail address in connection with a request for information, third parties may use that information to contact you. Carsforsale.com may also use your e-mail address to contact you regarding: ad status, new features, confirmation of registration, or other service related notices. 

**Links to Other Sites:**  
This Web site contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or policies of those sites. Any use of personal information provided to third parties will be governed by their privacy statements and policies. 

**IP Addresses:**  
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. 

**Security:**  
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. 

**Children's Privacy:**  
We do not knowingly acquire personal information from children under the age of 13. 

**Correct/Update Personal Information:**  
If you wish to modify any information that you previously provided go to 

[http://www.carsforsale.com/my_profile](http://www.carsforsale.com/My_Profile) to edit.
