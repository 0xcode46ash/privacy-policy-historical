# Carbonfund.org - Privacy Policy

The following statement details the [www.carbonfund.org](http://www.carbonfund.org/) privacy policy. If you have any questions, please contact us at [info@carbonfund.org](mailto:info@carbonfund.org).

_What personal information is collected and how is it used?_

No personally identifiable data is collected from individuals through this website, unless this information is given by you in regards to online donations.

Online donations are secure. When you make a donation we will collect the following information in order to process the order: name, address, phone number, email address and credit card information. If you are making a donation on behalf of someone else, we will collect their name, address, and email address if provided. We use your information to process your donation and to verify credit card data. We use your mailing address to send your certificate, if ordered, and to let you know about our projects through our publications. Your phone number is used only to contact you if we need to verify order information and occasionally to thank you for your contribution. We do not share your phone number with telemarketing centers. Your email address may be used to contact you regarding your donation or to let you know about our projects. Carbonfund.org respects your privacy and will discontinue contacting you at your request, either written or oral.

We do not share your personal information with any other organization, except for a third-party financial institution to process credit cards. The information sent to them is only to verify credit card numbers and process the order in a secure environment. We do not rent or share our mailing list. If you give a donation via one our partnerships, then your personal information is shared with the sponsoring partner and the third-party financial institution to process the credit card. 

_Is this information secure?_

When persons visit our site, data collected by our computers will not be publicly displayed. It is used for our internal data collection purposes only, and is held by our server in a secure location. Our donation and shopping area uses secure web pages when your credit card information is collected. Our online credit card processing system is handled by a third party. Information is relayed to them through secure channels using SSL (Secure Sockets Layer).

_What other types of information are collected, and how are they used?_

Cookies:

We currently do not employ software technology referred to as "cookies." 

Aggregate information:

When guests enter our site, our server picks up information used to help us provide our users with better services and for internal record keeping. This information is picked up automatically from your Internet Service Provider and web browser, and includes your type of computer operating system and the browser that you are using, IP addresses, and demographic data. Also collected is the number of people viewing our web site. This information is collected in non-identifiable form.

_What happens when our privacy policy is updated?_

We may update our web site or make changes in our procedure, which may require an update to our privacy policy. This change would appear in our posted policy and your continued usage of our site would indicate agreement and compliance with this new policy.
