# CareerCast, Inc.

CareerCast has gone to great lengths to respect the privacy of the individuals, job seekers, employers and site administrators that visit our site and the sites that we power. The "Powered By CareerCast" sticker provides you with the knowledge that the information you entrusted with us is confidential. This privacy statement discloses how we use the information you choose to provide in your navigation through our site. For specific question about the privacy policies of the sites that we power, you should visit the sites and review their privacy policy statements. 

**Information Collection**

CareerCast has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. Our data collection policies allow you to choose how much information you provide and how we use the information you have shared. It is our intent to use this information to help further your experience in a robust environment in which your confidentiality is of the utmost importance. 

**Usage Tracking**

Generally available information about your machine capabilities are used to allow your experience to be a rewarding one. This includes the use of a user's domain name, browser type, MIME capabilities, and IP address provided by every user's browser. CareerCast tracks user traffic patterns via the Web server but does not associate this information with an individual user. This says that a user session may be tailored or tracked by browser or origin, but the user will be confidential.

**Cookies**

In addition to information provided by your browser, small data files called cookies are created to help tailor your site visit. This information is stored on your hard disk for later retrieval by us the next time you visit. Cookies, by themselves, cannot be used to identify the individual user. This data includes only the information that you volunteer to provide us; email address, name, address, phone number, company information, and site preferences. On your return visit to our site, we can streamline your login to the site and provide you with the relevant updates for information you have chosen to be informed about.

**Personal Information**

Job seekers are provided with Confdentiality based features that allow them to protect or hide their identity form certain sources. If and when you choose to apply for a job at a specific company, only then will your information be visible to a hiring manager or recruiter at the specific company you applied to. You may also post a resume, cover letter, profile or a job application that may be accessible to employers and certain recruiters that are provided access to the site(s). Only selected individuals (HR Personnel, hiring managers or recruiters of the company, or our support staff) have the authority to access the information you provide. We do not use the resumes or other personal information for any other purpose and never share them with outside parties. If you would like to review your information previously submitted or would like us to delete it, you may do so on the site(s) you are using or go to our [Feedback Form](http://careercast.careercast.com/texis/feedback?lookid=careercast) and reference the site you are using.

While CareerCast has taken steps to protect your privacy, this site contains links to other sites. CareerCast is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

**The "Refer A Friend or Email Job" service**

You can choose to electronically forward a job description to someone else by clicking on the "Refer a Friend" or "Email Job" link or button on a job page. The user must provide the recipient's email address for which to send the job. This information is used only in the case of transmitting the job information and is not saved in our system. A brief text message provided by the sender is included with the job referral. This information is not used for any other purpose.

 **Security**

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

**Contacting the Web Site**

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this web site, you can contact us through the [ Feedback Form](http://careercast.careercast.com/texis/feedback?lookid=careercast).
