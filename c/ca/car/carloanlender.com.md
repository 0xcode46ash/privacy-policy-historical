# CARLOANLENDER

In connection with your transaction, CarloanLender.com and its subsidiaries may obtain in information about you as described in this notice. The Privacy Notice applies to all consumers who complete the credit application ("Credit App") in this website. 

You are providing information so that we may collect and use your Information (described in the next section) about you. This does not apply to information obtained in a non-financial transaction. The intent is to provide privacy policies and practices, as we are required to do by law. 

1\. We collect non-public personal Information such as Customer Information we receive from you on your Application (in this website or through email). Such Customer Information includes your name, address, social security number and income, as well as any other information you provide on your Application.   
2\. We disclose your Information by submitting your Application to financial service providers such as GMAC, World Omni Financial, Southeast Toyota Finance, SunTrust Bank, etc.   
3\. We disclose your Information by submitting your Application to non-financial service providers such as General Motors, Toyota Motor Sales, Mazda Motors of America, Mitsubishi Motors of America, Saturn Corporation, American Suzuki, Fidelity Warranty, JMIC Life Insurance Company, GMPP, etc.   
4.When you visit our website(s), we collect anonymous information to help us provide better customer service. 

We may also disclose all Customer Information we collect as described above to nonaffiliated third parties as permitted by law.

If you prefer that we not disclose nonpublic personal information about you to nonaffiliated third parties, please contact us at [stopdisclosing@carloanlender.com](mailto:stopdisclosing@carloanlender.com).

How We Protect Customer Information: We restrict access to Customer Information about you to our employees and third parties who need to know that information in order to manage our systems and your account, and provide you with the products and services you request. Additionally, we maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to protect your Customer Information in a manner consistent with industry standards for similar companies. We will review our safeguards from time to time, and strive to maintain them in a manner consistent with federal regulations that may be in effect from time to time. 

This is not intended to be a contract.
