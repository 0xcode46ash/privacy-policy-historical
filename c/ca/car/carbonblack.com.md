#### Carbon Black (the Product) 

  * Does not alter, transmit or interact with any user-generated personal or proprietary information.
  * Keeps your data entirely within your enterprise unless you expressly allow external access (e.g. MSSP).



  


#### 

Carbon Black, Inc.

  * Will keep your use of Carbon Black confidential if you ask us to.
  * Will gather the minimum amount of information we need from you to exchange information about the product and to process orders.
  * Will retain the minimum amount of information we need to provide support and upgrades.
  * Will not sell your personal information to others.
  * Will not collect or retain any payment (credit card, bank account) information online. Payments are handled via a PCI-compliant third-party.
  * Will use the contact information we obtain from you to let you know about product updates, new offerings or product- or company-related events.
