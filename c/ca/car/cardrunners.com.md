# CardRunners: Poker instruction, poker videos, and poker training from the best online poker players in the world

CardRunners, LLC and its affiliates and related entities (collectively, the “Company”) are firmly committed to safeguarding your privacy online. Please read the following Privacy Policy to understand how your personal information will be treated as you make full use of our offerings. The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to inform you what the Company does, what kinds of information we may gather about you and how we protect it, and the limits of our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, we ask that you refrain from using our products and services. Please note that this site <http://www.cardrunners.com/> is subject to development and changes. The full range of our products and services, and therefore potential uses of information, is variable. This site and the Privacy Policy will be updated from time to time, so please review this page when you next visit. This version of our Privacy Policy is effective November 25, 2008.  


**What we do:** We are primarily in the business of providing poker training services and other educational material related to poker.

**Information we collect:** When you open an account with the Company, we will ask you to provide us with certain personal information. We will use your personal information to complete various transactions, to communicate with you, ensure compliance with our User Agreement, and to provide you with customer support services.

**Disclosures of your personal information to third parties:** We do not disclose your information to third parties, except as described in this policy. Non-affiliated companies that assist the Company in providing services to you are required to maintain the confidentiality of such information and only use it in the course of providing such services as the Company dictates. In the event our Company is acquired or merges with another business, the information we collect will be transferred as one of the acquired or merged assets of our business. Additionally, we may disclose your information to others information if we believe, in our sole discretion, it is relevant to (a) fraud or other unlawful activity, (b) other activities that could expose us to legal liability, or (c) to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process.  


**Information Security:** To the extent that we collect personal information about you, we will use our best efforts to safeguard this information. We limit access to your personal information to authorized employees of the Company, its agents and, as described above, to service providers who adhere to stringent standards of privacy. We also mainain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to help protect the information against loss, misuse, and unauthorized disclosure. Please do your part in protecting your personal information by never sharing your username/password with others, logging off when you are not using our website, and avoid accessing our website from a public computer.

**What are the limits of our Privacy Policy?** Please note that this policy applies only to our Company and not to other companies or organizations to which we link, or businesses that use their own privacy policies. Nothing in this Privacy Policy should be construed to limit or prevent our compliance with lawfully issued orders, warrants, or subpoenas requiring us to produce any information provided by you.   


**Do you have questions about this Privacy Policy?** If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Company by accessing the “Contact Us" menu on your dashboard at www.cardrunners.com.
