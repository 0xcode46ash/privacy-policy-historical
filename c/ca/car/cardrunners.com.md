# CardRunners - Privacy Policy

CardRunners, LLC (the “Company”) is firmly committed to safeguarding your privacy online. Please read the following Privacy Policy to understand how your personal information will be treated as you make full use of our offerings. The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to inform you what the Company does, what kinds of information we may gather about you and how we protect it, and the limits of our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, we ask that you refrain from using our products and services. Please note that this site <http://www.cardrunners.com/> is subject to development and changes. The full range of our products and services, and therefore potential uses of information, is variable. This site and the Privacy Policy will be updated from time to time, so please check back to this page when you next visit.

**What do we do?** We are primarily in the business of providing poker training tools and other educational material related to poker.

**What information do we collect and how do we protect it?** In general, we collect information that is necessary to provide services to our registered users, promote our services, and establish our market presence. **We do not sell, trade, or rent the personal information that registered users provide**. When you open an account with the Company, we will ask you to provide us with certain personal information. We will use your personal information to complete various transactions, to communicate with you, ensure compliance with our User Agreement, and to provide you with customer support services. To the extent that we collect personal information about our registered users, we will use our best efforts to safeguard this information. Please do your part in protecting your personal information by never sharing your username/password with others, logging off when you are not using our website, and avoid accessing our website from a public computer.

**What are the limits of our Privacy Policy?** Please note that this policy applies only to our Company and not to other companies or organizations to which we link, or businesses that use their own privacy policies. Nothing in this Privacy Policy should be construed to limit or prevent our compliance with lawfully issued orders, warrants, or subpoenas requiring us to produce any information provided by any registered user. Additionally, we may disclose to others information about registered users which we believe, in our sole discretion, is relevant to (a) fraud or other unlawful activity, and (b) other activities that could expose us to legal liability. We may collect aggregate data about our registered users (for example, demographic information regarding the number of registered users who visit casinos in Las Vegas) for advertising and marketing purposes, to test the receptivity to new products and services, and to better target special offers.

**Do you have questions about this Privacy Policy?** If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Company by accessing the “Contact Us” menu.
