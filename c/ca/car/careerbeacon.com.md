# CareerBeacon.com Home

**Our Commitment**

At CareerBeacon.com, we understand the importance of maintaining the privacy of our visitors. We are committed to providing a safe and secure experience, every time you visit our website. We have worked and will continue to work to provide the highest level of privacy protection available on any employment site. This Privacy Policy is our commitment to you. We encourage you to read it.

**Information Collection and Use**

CareerBeacon.com will not sell, share or rent your information to others in ways different from what is noted in this Privacy Policy. Career Beacon.com collects personal information from users on a voluntary basis only in order to deliver our services to you. We do not sell or disclose your personal information to anyone, for any reason other than as required or permitted by law. For the purposes of this policy, personal information means information about an identifiable individual.

We do not sell our e-mail lists and will not correspond with you unless you indicate you wish to receive correspondence from us. It is also important to note that our subscription-based website feature has an easy means to unsubscribe from the service.

CareerBeacon.com collects information from you at several different points on our website. They are:

> **Anonymous Browsing**  
>  You are able to visit CareerBeacon.com without revealing any personal information about yourself. We do track your IP address to assist us in administering our website. We use this to gather broad demographic information about you, such as your location, the domain name you used to access our site, the type and version of browser you are using, and your Internet Service provider. We also collect aggregate information about how you are using our site. This is used by us to measure the number of visits, average time spent, page views and other statistics about the site in general. This information is used to improve our site and does not include any information which could personally identify any of our users.
> 
> **Notifier™**  
>  If you register for our Notifier **™** service, we will ask you for your e-mail address and specify certain career preferences. Based on these preferences, we will send you e-mail notifications about opportunities that match your preferences.
> 
> **Send to a Friend**  
>  All of our career postings have a Send to a Friend option. This allows you to send an e-mail with a link to particular career posting to people you think might be interested. In order to use this service, you must enter the e-mail address you want to send the posting to, your name and your e-mail address. We do not gather, track or record this information.
> 
> **Newsletter**  
>  You must enter your e-mail address to subscribe to our newsletter.
> 
> **Survey**  
>  Our site has an on-line survey feature. Your participation is completely voluntary. The information that is requested may include contact information and demographic information. Survey information is used to improve the site and may also be shared in aggregate form with our clients.

**Links**

This website contains links to other sites. Please be aware that Career Beacon.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our site and to read the privacy statements of all sites that collect personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies only to information gathered by this website.

**Sharing**

We share aggregated demographic information with our clients. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify you in particular.

**Security**

We take every precaution to protect your information. When you submit personal or sensitive information via our website, your information is protected both on and off-line. Simply put, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to secure and safeguard the information we collect on-line.

Our employees do not access user information, except as a requirement to maintain the site. All of our employees are aware of the sensitivity of your personal information and the importance we place on privacy. They are committed to doing whatever they can to ensure that your information remains protected.

We review web security policies every three months. Whenever any change or modification is made, employees are made aware of the change and the importance we place on privacy.

**Opt In/Opt Out/Deleting Information**

Our site offers you the opportunity to opt-out, or discontinue receiving communications from us. This applies to our Notifier **™** service. On the main Notifier **™** registration page there is an option to remove yourself from our mailing list.

We will ensure that all personal information is as accurate, complete and up-to-date as is necessary for the purposes identified in this policy and to minimize the possibility that inappropriate information is used to make a decision about the individual concerned. We will retain personal information for only as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes identified in this policy.

**Notification of Changes**

If we change or add to the purposes for which we collect, use or disclose your personal information, your consent will be obtained before we implement such new purposes in respect of your personal information.

If we choose to change our privacy policy, we will post the changes in this section of our homepage.
