# privacy-policy - CamelBak.com

### PRIVACY POLICY

### CamelBak respects and safeguards your online privacy.

Please read on for more details regarding our commitment to your privacy.

### What information do we collect? How is this information used?

CamelBak collects user information by registration and warranty return process. Generally, this information is internally applied to continually develop and improve out site. We only contact individuals who specifically request email updates or for return merchandise authorization. Individuals may opt-out of receiving future online communications.

### Will CamelBak disclose information it collects to third parties?

CamelBak does not sell, trade, or rent or release your personal information (name, email, address, postal address, telephone, etc.) to others without your consent. Also, CamelBak may provide related site information to reputable third parties but these aggregate statistics include no personally identifying details. This site contains links to other sites. CamelBak is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

### Choice/Opt-Out

CamelBak provides the opportunity to opt-out of receiving online communications. Select the opt-out option from the newsletter.

### In Summary

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Information collected is internally applied to continually improving out site. Individuals may opt-out of receiving future online communications.
