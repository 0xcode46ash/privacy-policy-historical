# What we do

Cactus considers any and all information related to its clients to be strictly confidential. All our employees have signed confidentiality agreements and will not make such information available to anyone outside Cactus , unless authorized in writing by the client. We respect the privacy of each of our clients; therefore, their personal information (name, address, e-mail address, contact number, etc.) will never be made available to any outside party.
