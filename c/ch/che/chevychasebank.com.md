# Chevy Chase Bank - Privacy Statement

At Chevy Chase Bank and its subsidiaries, we understand that privacy and security are important concerns in your banking relationship with us. We understand and are sensitive to the concerns about the impact that Internet technology may have on your privacy. We think that electronic delivery of services can be of great benefit to you, and we want you to be assured that we are treating your personal information in a professional, confidential manner. 

**How we collect and use information:**   
Ordinarily, we do not collect any personal information, including your e-mail address, if you are only browsing our Site. However, if we ask you to supply us with personal information, and you do so, we may retain that information for certain purposes. For example, we may retain personal information if you: 

> * send us an e-mail -- to enable us to respond to you   
> 
> * respond to online questionnaires -- so we may use your feedback to improve our services   
> 
> * enter into a promotion or sweepstakes from our Site   
> 
> * request a call, e-mail, or information about our financial products and services 

Chevy Chase Bank and its subsidiaries will limit the collection of information about you to that which we feel is important to administer our business, provide superior service, and offer opportunities that we think may interest you. We will not disclose personal information to outside parties unless there is an appropriate business or legal reason. 

**If you send us an e-mail:**   
We request that you **do not** send us confidential information, including your bank and credit card account numbers, social security number, or other information you wish to protect against possible interception over the Internet.
