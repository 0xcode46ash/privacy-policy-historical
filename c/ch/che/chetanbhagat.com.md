# Privacy Policy | Chetan Bhagat | Official Website

**External Site Links**  


ChetanBhagat.com links to other websites on the internet. Content and Privacy Policies are the responsibility of their respective owners

**P Addresses and Logging**  


ChetanBhagat.com server logs information about visitors, including IP addresses, date/time visited, referring website, length of stay, etc. This information is used for site statistics only.

**Visitor Information**

Information collected like Name, Email, Address, etc. is kept secured and is not sold or distributed to third parties. This information is collected solely for the purpose of reaching back to you.
