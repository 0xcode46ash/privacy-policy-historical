# Chemical Engineering Magazine Privacy Statement

* * *

### Your Privacy Matters To Us

Chemical Engineering Magainze collects personal information about our users only during registration for the Chemical Engineering Magainze Online service and from our use of "cookietechnology as you use the service. 

Chemical Engineering Magainze respects your right to privacy. 

The data we collect is used in the aggregate to give us demographic information about the entire Chemical Engineering Magainze audience in order to improve the Chemical Engineering Magainze service. The personal information you provide allows us to inform you about updates to the service and to notify you of products and services of Chemical Engineering Magainze, its affiliates and third parties that we believe may be of interest to you. We also share aggregate information -- not individual data -- with our advertisers to help them better understand our services and to monitor the reach of their advertising on the service. 

Chemical Engineering Magainze uses reasonable precautions to keep the personal information disclosed to us secure and to disclose such information only to third parties we believe to be responsible. However, we are not responsible for any breach of security or for any actions of any third parties which receive the information.
