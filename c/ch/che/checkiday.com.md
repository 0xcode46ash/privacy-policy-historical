# Privacy Policy | Checkiday.com

We take privacy seriously. Any information sent between you and our servers is encrypted.

## What information do we collect?

We do not collect any information from our users. However, services that we use including Google Analytics, Google Adsense, and Quantcast may collect user information.

## How do we use that information?

We use data from Google Analytics and Quantcast to better understand how our site is being used so we may improve the user experience. We use Google Adsense to serve ads so that we can keep this site free.

## What information do we share?

We do not and will not sell or share your information. We cannot sell what we do not have.
