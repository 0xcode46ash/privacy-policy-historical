**ChessHere Privacy Policy**

ChessHere takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy. 

This policy covers how ChessHere treats personal information that ChessHere collects and receives.  


**Personal information and other data we collect**

\- Your email address is required to finish registration process.

\- Whenever you access the site, your IP address will be recoreded.

\- You have the choice to provide personal information(name, age, about me) in your profile page, but this is optional.

**How do we use these information:**

\- We never share your personal information with any third party.

\- Your email is only used to send you some game notifications which can be disabled by you, and no one can see it.

\- We save your IP address to make sure you don't create multiple accounts.

\- Your personal information that you provide in your profile will be visible to the public.
