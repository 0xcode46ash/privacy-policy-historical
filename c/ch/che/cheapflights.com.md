# Privacy policy

**About Us**  
This is a privacy policy for the owners of Cheapflights.com, Cheapflights (USA) Inc. 

We invite you to contact us if you have questions about this policy.

or [contact us](https://web.archive.org/contact-us/) online. 

**Validity**  
Cheapflights reserves the right to amend this policy from time to time. 

Our electronic privacy policy (P3P) states that our policy is valid for 52 weeks from the time it is loaded by a client. 

**Email addresses**  
We do not want you to receive unwanted email from us. We try to make it easy to opt-out of any service you have asked to receive. We would not provide your email address to a third party unless you asked us to. For example, sweepstakes entries require you to provide an email address - in such cases you often have the option to receive information from the prize provider. 

**Log Files**  
Cheapflights.com records details of individual accesses to its web pages. We use this information to provide a more relevant site for you. This information may include details of internet IP addresses and browser types. Access, by third parties, to full log files may be allowed under controlled circumstances for the purposes of auditing. Abstracted log information not showing individual's actual internet addresses is provided to third parties. 

**Cookies**  
Cheapflights.com uses cookies which tells us about how users navigate the site. We use this information to provide a more relevant site for you. Your web browser should allow you to choose whether or not to allow cookies - you can still use our site if you have them disabled. For more information on cookies click below: [http://www.cookiecentral.com](http://www.cookiecentral.com/)

**Third Party Advertising**  
The banners appearing on this website are sometimes delivered to you, on our behalf, by a third party. Information about your visit to this site, such as the number of times you have viewed an ad (but not your name, address, or any other personal information) is used to server ads to you on this site. 

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, the third party may place or recognise a unique cookie on your browser, but it will not collect any personally identifiable information about you. 

**Purchases**  
Cheapflights.com does not sell you travel products, but directs you to sites that do. These sites may store information such as your name and address and credit card details. They may operate their own privacy policies. 

**Third Party Booking Tools**  
Cheapflights.com displays third party booking tools. When a users utilises these tools they are operating under the terms, conditions and privacy policy of those third parties. Please read the relevant part of their websites.
