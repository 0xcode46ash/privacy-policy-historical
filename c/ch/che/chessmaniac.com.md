# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

**The ChessManiac.com Privacy Policy**

ChessManiac.com is committed to preserving your privacy online. When you visit our site, we collect two types of information from you: 1) specific information you voluntarily submit and 2) general information our site gathers automatically. Specific Information You Submit If your email ChessManiac.com for any reason, the information in your email will only be used by us to contact you directly. We will keep your information on file only for the purpose of providing service. We may also share some of your information with third parties that are directly involved with support issues, such as product technical support. We will never willfully disclose any individually identifiable information to any other third parties without a user's specific permission to do so. We do not sell users' names or email addresses to other companies. We will only provide individually identifiable information to third parties only where it is required by law. 3) We have contracted with ValueClick to monitor certain pages of our website for the purpose of reporting website traffic, statistics, advertisement 'click-throughs', and/or other activities on our website. Where authorized by us, ValueClick may use cookies, web beacons, and/or other monitoring technologies to compile anonymous statistics about our website visitors. No personally identifiable information is collected by or transferred to any party other than the Advertiser. For more information about how the information is collected and used by ValueClick, please see ValueClick's privacy policy at <http://www.valueclickmedia.com/member_privacy.shtml>   
To opt-out of receiving Cookies from third party advertising networks click [ here.](http://networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp)

**Email move notification**

If you opt in to our email move notification you will receive an email with the chess move that has been made by your opponent.

**How to opt out of the email notification system**

If you wish to no longer get messages from our system you will need to log in to your account and navigate to your setting page. The url of the settings page is http://www.chessmaniac.com/webchess/configure.php From this page you can opt out of the email move notification system by selecting the correct option. 

**General Information We Gather**

As you browse our Web site, we collect standard tracking data, which we use to evaluate site traffic and usage patterns. Furthermore, cookies and/or web beacons collect data in the ad serving process. This information is aggregated with tracking data from all site visitors. We use such aggregated information to help us evaluate our site and to improve our services to clients. 

**Third Party Links**

This site contains external links to other web sites, such as (but not limited to) other chess related resources. ChessManiac.com has no control over the content of third party sites and is not responsible for any information or content contained on these sites, nor is ChessManiac.com responsible for the privacy policies of those sites and the information they may gather expressly or automatically
