# : Privacy Statement

### Privacy Statement  


* * *

At Cheaper Than Dirt! we respect and protect your privacy. We are all worried about Internet transactions and Big Brother. We don't trust the government either. We will not allow your name, what you buy or your email address to be shared with anyone.

Our Internet Site will not rent your name or mailing address to outside parties. We will not give out your email address.We will not supply Big Brother with any info on you unless we are forced to by Subpoena. You will automatically receive the Cheaper Than Dirt Mail Order catalog when you register at this Internet Site. To be removed from this mailing, fax your registration information to 817-740-7999 and request to be removed from the catalog mailing list.

We do need to get information on you to handle your order. Here is what we need and what we do with it:

  * First and last name 
  * Billing address 
  * Shipping address(es) 
  * Email address 
  * Phone numbers 
  * Credit card information 



We collect all or some of this information on the pages where you create your account. We use this information as follows: 

  1. We fulfill your order based on this information.

  2. We provide you with information about your order (such as, whether your order has been received, processed or shipped).

  3. We ask for your phone number in the event that our customer service representatives need to contact you and cannot reach you by email. We save the information about products you purchased to provide you with the best possible customer service and the most up-to-date information about your order.

  4. We use your mailing address to ship your orders, and if you have requested a catalog, to mail catalogs to you.

  5. We use your email address as your unique identifier for tracking your order and for identifying you for future orders. Also, if you have selected to be notified by email about special offers, products or events available to Cheaper Than Dirt! customers, we will from time to time email you information we think will interest you. When you create your account with Cheaper Than Dirt!, you are given the option of unchecking the email updates box. If you do this, we will communicate with you only in relation to your orders. You can change your preferences at any time by going to the Your Account page and selecting Account Profile.

  6. Credit card information is used to bill you for products you have ordered and to track sales.

  7. We use your product category preferences to notify you about new releases when you sign up for email announcements.




When you visit the Cheaper Than Dirt! site, our system creates a cookie to facilitate navigation through the site and keep track of your purchases. This cookie is also used to remember information you gave us so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site.

Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you'd prefer, you can set yours to refuse cookies. You will still be able to order from Cheaper Than Dirt! using the Shopping Cart, but our system will not remember you and each time you shop with us, you will have to re-enter your name and address information.

**Credit Card Processing and Security**

Shopping with Cheaper Than Dirt! is absolutely safe--you never have to worry about credit-card safety when you are shopping at our site. We guarantee that each purchase you make is protected and safe. If fraudulent charges are ever made, you will not have to pay for them.

We use the latest encryption technology to keep your personal information safe. All your ordering information -- including your name, address and credit card number -- is encrypted using a secure server for maximum security. Your credit card and billing information cannot be read as it travels to our ordering system. To ensure that your information is even more secure, once we receive your credit card information, we store it on a server that isn't accessible from the Internet. 

Credit card transactions are handled by a third-party financial institution, which receives the credit card number and other personal identifying information only to verify the credit card numbers and process transactions.

**How do I change, update, or correct personally identifiable information about myself?**

If at any time you would like to change any information in your account, just go to the Your Account link (for security reasons, you must be logged on for this link to appear) in the left column of any page. This will take you to the Your Account page. Click on Account Profile. Here you can change or update your email address, billing address and subscription preferences.

To unsubscribe from any email announcement from Cheaper Than Dirt!, simply click Reply to any message you receive and type the word "remove" in the subject field.

**Other**

By using the Cheaper Than Dirt! site, you consent to the collection and use of your information by Cheaper Than Dirt! as described in this policy.

Cheaper Than Dirt! fully cooperates with law enforcement agencies in identifying those who use our services for illegal activities. We reserve the right to release information about members who we believe are in violation of our content guidelines. We will not release your information unless subpoenaed to do so. 

Cheaper Than Dirt! may update this policy from time to time; please check this page periodically for changes. By using this site, you signify your agreement to the Cheaper Than Dirt! Privacy Policy.
