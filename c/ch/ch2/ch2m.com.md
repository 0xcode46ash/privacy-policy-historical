# CH2M HILL - Privacy Policy

[Home](https://web.archive.org/corporate)

STATEMENT: CH2M HILL acknowledges and honors the privacy of users of this CH2M HILL Internet Web site. You can visit this CH2M HILL Internet Web site without telling CH2M HILL who you are. CH2M HILL does not require your personal information to obtain access to this Internet Web site. However, you may choose to give CH2M HILL personal information about yourself, such as your name, address, phone number, and email address. When you provide CH2M HILL with such data or other information about yourself, CH2M HILL will act in a responsible manner. CH2M HILL will make a sincere effort to respond in a timely manner to your requests to correct inaccuracies in the personal information that you provide to CH2M HILL. CH2M HILL may store, use, and disclose personal information as authorized or required by applicable law, including making disclosures that are necessary or advisable to: (1) protect the rights, health, safety, or property of CH2M HILL or others; (2) allow for lawful security related investigations or law enforcement investigations; and (3) comply with applicable laws, regulations, or other lawful requirements. Except as stated above, CH2M HILL will not transfer, exchange, license, sell, lease, or otherwise disclose your personal information without your approval; however, CH2M HILL may collect and/or provide aggregate statistics about CH2M HILL's Internet Web site and Internet Web site users to other parties. CH2M HILL is committed to comply with the United States' Children Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). In viewing this Internet Web site, CH2M HILL may collect certain anonymous information from you such as: the domain name of the site providing you with Internet access; your Internet protocol address used to connect your computer to the internet; your browser type; your type of operating system; the time you spend surfing the CH2M HILL Internet Web site; the specific pages that you view at this Web site; and other items that assist CH2M HILL in administering this Web site. When you view certain parts of this CH2M HILL Internet Web site, information may be stored on the hard drive of your computer. This information may be in the form of a "cookie" or other technologies that will permit the adaptation of the CH2M HILL Internet Web site to better reflect your particular interests, priorities, and preferences. A "cookie" is a piece of data that an Internet Web site transfers, via your browser, back to your computer's hard drive or other parts of your computer system to assist CH2M HILL with its general Web site recordkeeping activities. Please note that this CH2M Hill Internet Web site contains hyperlinks to other non-CH2M HILL Internet Web sites and CH2M HILL does not control or monitor the privacy policies of those sites. This CH2M HILL Privacy Statement only applies to information collected and maintained by CH2M HILL. CH2M HILL reserves the right to modify this Privacy Statement at any time by updating this Privacy Statement Web page. This Privacy Statement was last updated on February 25, 2003. Questions regarding this policy statement should be directed to: [michelle.girts@ch2m.com](mailto:michelle.girts@ch2m.com).
