# Privacy Policy and Copyright : Chronicle Books

Chronicle Books recognizes that it is essential to protect the privacy of users on the Web. For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, but not the email address. The information is used for internal review as a way to track our visitation and make improvements to the site, then discarded. No other information is collected without the consent of the visitor.

To enable users to purchase books via chroniclebooks.com, a user's personally identifying information will be shared with a third-party service provider solely for the purpose of order fulfillment and credit card verification. A user's personally identifying information will not be sold or rented to any other party. Site visitors have the option of saving shipping and billing information on the site to expedite future orders. If you choose not to register this information on the site, once we have processed your order, we will safely discard your credit card information. All credit card information is stored on a secure server. We may hold on to your billing and shipping information for future mailings promoting books similar to the one you ordered, but we will not share or sell this information at any time.

We collect the email addresses of registered users, but this information is used solely for the purpose of our opt-in email newsletters and gift reminders, and for no other mailings. To remove your email address from the newsletter subscriber list, [click here.](http://www.chroniclebooks.com/site/catalog/index.php?main_page=pubs_unsub)

Occasionally, we conduct contests or promotions through our Web site, and ask visitors to submit their name and address. Aside from opt-in only email newsletter registrations, this information is used only for the purpose of the contest or promotion and then discarded.

KIDS: Make sure you have the permission of your parent or guardian before sending any information about yourself (name, address, email address, etc.) to us or anyone else over the Internet, and before making purchases online.
