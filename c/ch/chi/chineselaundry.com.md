# Chinese Laundry - Privacy Policy

Chinese Laundry Shoes is concerned about your right to privacy. When you visit the Chinese Laundry web site, you can be assured that any information you share with us will be treated confidentially and will not be disclosed to anyone outside of Chinese Laundry. 

**E-MAIL ADDRESSES**  
The e-mail addresses provided to subscribe to Chinese Laundry e-mail updates _will not_ be shared with any third parties.  If you wish to subscribe or unsubscribe from this mailing list or need more information about the mailing list, please go to the [Customer Tools](https://web.archive.org/web/20030210114954id_/http%3A//chineselaundry.com/tools.asp) page. 

**BILLING INFORMATION**  
Information provided to purchase online with us (including billing name, billing address, telephone number, and fax number) will not be shared with or sold to anyone outside of Chinese Laundry nor to any third party.  Payment information (credit card numbers and expiration dates) is treated confidentially and _will not_ be shared with anyone outside of Chinese Laundry or the financial institutions used to process payments. 

**SECURITY**  
We use Thawte Consulting certificates and Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) to encrypt information and keep data private and confidential between your machine and Chinese Laundry. This technology makes it safe to transmit your credit card number over the Internet.

Look for two security icons, the "s" after "http" in the address line and the lock in the top menu bar and bottom status bar of your browser in Netscape (or in the bottom status bar only in Internet Explorer). 

**POLICY CHANGES**  
Chinese Laundry reserves the right to modify its Privacy Policy at any time. In the event that we make a material change to our policy we will post a notice of the change on this page. 

**CONTACT INFORMATION**  
If you still have questions about our privacy policy please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20030210114954id_/http%3A//chineselaundry.com/contact.asp).
