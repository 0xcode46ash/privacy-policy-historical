# ChirpMe Privacy Policy

**ChirpMe takes your privacy very seriously:**

**Nothing you do on ChirpMe will be seen on Facebook or your other social networks unless you explicitly ask us to publish specific information.**  
You'll never be surprised about what appears in your Facebook News Feed or Wall. 

**INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE BY ChirpMe**  
When you register a profile with ChirpMe, your profile including any information (name, address, gender) will be registered with ChirpMe. You may identify yourself through a social network account in which ChirpMe may receive additional information about your profile that may include personal interests, gender, age, and geographical location.

You should also be aware that much content delivered through ChirpMe originates with other content providers. Such other content providers may gather information about you, including by asking you to provide information through the site itself. 

Finally, in order to provide the best possible service and to allow us to make certain internal reports and make recommendations, we collect aggregate information about the use of the Service, including information about users accessing the ChirpMe site such as internet protocol addresses, browser type, browser language, referring / exit pages and URLs, other browser history, platform type, number of clicks, domain names, landing pages, pages viewed and the order of those pages, the amount of time spent on particular pages, and the date and time ("Activity Information"). 

ChirpMe may use and publicly display to other users on the ChirpMe site users' social network profile photographs, social network profile information, social network News Feed Posts and any other content the user has granted ChirpMe access to through identifying themselves through their social network.

**PERSONS UNDER THE AGE OF 18**  
ChirpMe does not knowingly collect or maintain personally identifiable information or non-personally-identifiable information on the Site from persons under 18 years of age, and no part of the Site is directed to persons under 18. If you are under 18 years of age, then please do not use or access the Site or the Services at any time or in any manner. If ChirpMe learns that personally identifiable information of persons under 18 years of age has been collected on the Site, then ChirpMe will take the appropriate steps to delete this information. 

**CHANGES IN OUR PRIVACY POLICY**  
This Privacy Policy is not intended to and does not create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party. ChirpMe reserves the right, at any time and without notice, to add to, modify, change or update this Privacy Policy, simply by posting such change, update or modification on the Site. Any such change, update or modification will be effective immediately upon posting on the Site.

**CONTACTING THE SITE**  
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, the practices of the Site, or your dealings with the Site please email [support@chirpme.com](mailto:support@chirpme.com)
