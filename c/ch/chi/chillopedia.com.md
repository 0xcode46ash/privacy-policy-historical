# Privacy Policy

23 Jul 2010 by NK, Comments Off

At **chillopedia.com** , we recognize that privacy of your personal information is important. We will **never** sell your personal information to third parties and will always safeguard your information.

As with most other websites, we collect the data you provide to us when you visit or post a comment to our site. This information may include your name, your email address, your city,your IP address, your ISP, the browser you used to visit our site (such as Internet Explorer, Chrome, Safari or Firefox etc), the times you visited our site, the time you spent on our site and which pages you visited throughout our site. Your personal information will **never** be released, sold, or rented to any entities or individuals outside **chillopedia.com** under any circumstances.
