# ChurchStaffing.com - Church Jobs Employment Ministry Resumes

**Privacy Policy**  
**ChurchStaffing.com** has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: [ChurchStaffing.com ](http://www.churchstaffing.com/)

#### Information Automatically Logged 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. 

#### Cookies 

Our Site uses cookies to control the displaying of items such as pop-up windows on our site. 

#### Advertisers 

Advertisers on this site write their own ad copy. We cannot be responsible for inappropriate ad copy. If there is an advertisement that may be inappropriate we ask that it be brought to our attention. Advertisements on this site may link to an external link. See the section on "External Links" below. 

#### Registration Forms 

Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name, email, and postal address), financial information (like their bank account or credit card numbers), and demographic information (like their zip code, age, or income level).   
Contact information from the registration forms is used to ship purchases, and on user profiles if designated. The customer's contact information is also used to get in touch with the customer when necessary. In addition, your contact information only may be shared with other divisions of our company, who may in turn contact you.   
Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.   
Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services.   
Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site.  This information is shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis. 

#### External Links 

This site contains links to other sites. **ChurchStaffing.com** is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

#### Security 

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. 

#### Choice/Opt-Out 

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving promotional/marketing information from us. 

  1. Update your account information by logging into your account. 



#### Data Quality/Access 

This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided: 

  1. Update your account information by logging into your account.

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact:  [churchstaffing@churchstaffing.com ](mailto:churchstaffing@churchstaffing.com)
