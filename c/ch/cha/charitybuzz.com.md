# Charitybuzz | Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting this Charitybuzz website (the "Site") and reviewing this Security and Privacy Policy (the "Privacy Policy"). This Site is owned, operated and controlled by Charity Buzz, Inc. d/b/a Charitybuzz ("Charitybuzz"). Your privacy is extremely important to Charitybuzz. We have developed this Privacy Policy to cover the information we collect from you, and how we collect, use, disclose, transfer, and store your information. Your use of our Site and services signifies your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, you may not use our Site or our services. Throughout this Privacy Policy Charitybuzz is sometimes referred to herein as "we," "us," and "our."

## What is Personally Identifiable Information.

"Personally Identifiable Information" is information that can be used to uniquely identify a single person. This may include your name or associated company name, email address, postal address, and home or work telephone number. You may be asked to provide your personal information when you register on the Site and any time you are in contact with Charitybuzz. Charitybuzz may combine your personal information with other information to provide and improve our products, services, Site content, promotional materials, business practices, or customer service.

## What Personally Identifiable Information We Collect.

You are voluntarily and knowingly supplying us with information when you create a Charitybuzz user account, register your credit card, bid on or win Property and/or an Experience through an online auction, donate property and/or an experience for use in connection an auction or other program or register to receive information from Charitybuzz. An "Experience" may include, but is not limited to, a celebrity meet and greet, dinner with a celebrity or other such experience. "Property" is tangible property, including but not limited to artwork, apparel, food, wine, sports and music memorabilia. In order to participate in an auction or other program or to donate Property and/or an Experience you must complete the registration process and some of this information will identify you personally or enable you to be identified personally, either alone or in combination with other information available to us. Such information may include your name, mailing address, phone number, email address, contact preferences, and credit card information.

When you share your content with family and friends, invite others to join you on Charitybuzz, or bid on, purchase, or win Property and/or an Experience for another person or which you will share with another person, Charitybuzz may collect the information you provide about those people such as name, mailing address, email address, and phone number. Email addresses may be collected via forms on the Site and from time to time other information may be collected via this Site or through an offline manner.

## How We Use Your Personally Identifiable Information.

The personal information we collect allows us to keep you posted on Charitybuzz's latest auctions and other programs. It helps us to improve our services, content, and advertising. We may use personal information for internal purposes such as auditing, data analysis, and research to improve Charitybuzz's products, services, and customer communications or share with affiliates, partnering organizations, charities, or other third parties. For more information on disclosure to third parties see the section below entitled "Disclosure to Third Parties." In addition, we may aggregate Personal Information in such a way that it is no longer identifiable and we may use this aggregated information for any purpose. At your request and where the law requires us to do so, we will make good faith efforts to confirm what personal information we hold about you, update your information, remove your information, fulfill specific opt-out requests, and/or correct any inaccuracies in such personal information if you contact us at unsubscribe@charitybuzz.com to submit such requests. We may decline to process a request that is unreasonable, jeopardizes the rights or privacy of others, is impractical or for which access is not otherwise required.

## Use of Non-Personally Identifiable Information/Cookies and Other Technologies.

We also collect non-personally identifiable information in a form that does not permit direct association with any specific individual. We may collect, use, transfer, and disclose non-personally identifiable information for any purpose. Like many websites, our Site may use "cookies" and other similar tracking devices or technologies to collect information that is sent to us automatically by your web browser which will help us understand user behavior, tell us which parts of our Site people have visited, and facilitate and measure the effectiveness of advertisements, web searches and other content. This information typically includes your IP address, the address of the web page you were visiting when you accessed this Site, the name of your operating system (such as Windows or Macintosh), the name and version of your browser (such as Internet Explorer or Safari), and the date and time of your visit, among other things. The information we receive depends on the settings on your web browser. Although most web browsers automatically accept cookies, most computers allow you to change your browser setting to prevent acceptance of cookies. You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie or you may choose to block cookies with your browser. Please note that if you choose to erase or block your cookies certain features or parts of the Site may not work properly.

We treat information collected by cookies and other technologies as non-personally identifiable information. However, to the extent that Internet Protocol (IP) addresses or similar identifiers are considered personal information by local law, we will treat these identifiers as personal information. Similarly, to the extent that non-personally identifiable information is combined with personal information, we will treat the combined information as personal information for the purposes of this Privacy Policy.

Our third party partners may also employ clear gifs (a.k.a. Web Beacons/Web Bugs), images, and scripts that help them better manage content on our site. We do not tie the information gathered to our Customers' or Users' personal information.

## Disclosure to Third Parties.

Charitybuzz may share personal information with affiliates, partnering organizations, charities, donors, or other third parties that work with Charitybuzz. Without limiting the foregoing, Charitybuzz will share personal information about successful bidders, winners or buyers with the donor and charitable organization associated with the auction or other program relevant to such bidder, winner or buyer. Charitybuzz may also share personal information with companies who provide services such as information processing, shipping, consignment, fulfilling customer orders, delivering products to you, managing and enhancing customer data, providing customer service, assessing your interest in our products and services, marketing purposes, and conducting customer research or satisfaction surveys. These companies are not authorized by us to use your personal information in any other way. You will receive notice when your Personally Identifiable Information might be provided to any third party for any reason other than as set forth in this Privacy Policy, and you will have an opportunity to request that we not share such information.

It may be necessary for you to supply your social security number or other government issued identification number for use in connection with conducting background checks and security screenings for certain Experiences (such as, by way of example only, those that involve high profile celebrities). We do not store this information in our system but we will share this information with our affiliates, partnering organizations, charities or other third parties who will be involved in completing the background checks and security screenings.

It may also be necessary by law, legal process, litigation, and/or requests from public and governmental authorities within or outside your country of residence for Charitybuzz to disclose your personal information and in the event of such necessity we will have the right to make such a disclosure. We may also disclose personal information, without notifying you, if we determine that for purposes of national security, law enforcement, or other issues of public importance, disclosure is necessary or appropriate. We may also disclose personal information about you if we determine that disclosure is reasonably necessary to enforce our Terms of Use or protect our operations, users, charities or donors. If we suspect criminal activity, we may also share our server logs, which contain visitors' IP addresses, with the appropriate investigative authorities who could use that information to trace and identify individuals.

In addition, we may share specific personal information as disclosed in a specific Privacy Notice posted on the page or area of the Site where you provided the information. You will receive notice when your Personally Identifiable Information will be provided to any third party for any reason other than as set forth in this Privacy Policy, and you will have an opportunity to request that we do not share such information.

Additionally, in the event of a reorganization, merger, or sale of all or a portion of our business we may transfer, sell, or assign any and all of your personal information as part of such reorganization, merger or sale.

## Protection of Personal Information.

Charitybuzz has been awarded the TRUSTe Certified Privacy Seal which signifies that Charitybuzz has met the comprehensive privacy certification requirements established by TRUSTe and it complies with the highest standards for protecting personal information.

Charitybuzz takes precautions including administrative, technical, and physical measures to safeguard your personal information against loss, theft, and misuse, as well as against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have industry standard measures in place to safeguard and secure information and data we collect through the Site. It is important that you understand, however, that no website or database is completely secure or "hacker proof" and we cannot guarantee complete security of the information you submit to us. You are responsible for taking personal steps to protect your personal information against unauthorized disclosure or misuse, for example, by protecting your password from unauthorized use or disclosure.

## Retention of Personal Information.

We will retain your personal information only as long as we need it for the purpose for which it was originally collected (or to which you have subsequently consented) or for other legitimate purposes (such as regulatory compliance and use consistent with this Privacy Policy), or as required or permitted by law.

## Privacy of Children Online and Parental Controls.

We do not use our Site to knowingly solicit personal information from or market to anyone under the age of majority in his or her state or country. If you have not reached the age of majority in your state or country you may not use this Site and should not provide any information about yourself through this Site. Our goal is to comply with applicable laws and regulations relating to the collection and use of information from children, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of the United States and other applicable laws or regulations. Please be aware that there are parental control tools available online that can be used to prevent children from submitting information online without parental permission or from accessing material that is harmful to minors. If you believe that we received information from a child or other person protected under such laws, please notify us immediately by postal mail addressed to Charity Buzz, Inc., 11th Floor, 437 Fifth Avenue, New York, NY 10016 or by email at privacy@charitybuzz.com and we will take reasonable steps to remove that information from our database.

## Third-Party Sites and Services.

Our Site may contain links to unrelated third-party websites, products, and services. Information collected by third parties is governed by their privacy practices and we have no control over such practices. We encourage you to learn about the privacy practices of those third parties. This Privacy Policy applies only to the personal information collected through this Site and you agree that Charitybuzz has no liability with respect to such third party sites or their privacy policies.

## Testimonials/Comments/Reviews.

We may post customer testimonials, comments, and reviews (whether in text, audio, video, or other format) on our Site which may contain your Personally Identifiable Information. By providing Charitybuzz with testimonials, comments, and reviews you consent to such posting and consent to Charitybuzz's use in connection with advertisements, promotional pieces, marketing materials, or any purpose consistent with this Privacy Policy.

## Postings.

When you use the Charitybuzz services, or applications or post on a Charitybuzz blog, forum, chat room, or social networking site, the personal information you share is visible to other users and can be read, collected, or used by them. You are responsible for the personal information you choose to submit in these instances.

## Notification of Privacy Statement Changes.

We may at any time, and without notice to you, update, modify, or change this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our information practices. If we make any material changes we will notify you by means of a notice on this Site prior to the change becoming effective. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices. Your continued use of the Site following any such change, including the submission of any personal information, constitutes your agreement that all personal information collected from or about you from this Site after the revised Privacy Policy is posted will be subject to the terms of the revised Privacy Policy.
