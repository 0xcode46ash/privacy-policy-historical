# Chat Hour - Privacy Policy

• Chat Hour (formerly WeirdTown Chat) does not rent, sell, or share any personal information about you with any third party unless you expressly tell us to.

• Chat Hour automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, Chat Hour cookie information, and the page you request.

• Chat Hour may set and access Chat Hour cookies on your computer.

• Chat Hour reveals your IP address when you send messages in our chat rooms without logging in. If you are logged in, your IP address is private.

• We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs. We may reveal your personal information to your internet service provider or the police authority if they request the information for investigations regarding your illegal activities or terms of use violations.

• Chat Hour may update this policy from time to time. You should review this policy periodically. If there are any significant changes to this policy, we will put the latest updated date next to the Chat Hour's Privacy Policy link
