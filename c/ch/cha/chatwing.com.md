# ChatWing | Privacy policy

  
Web Services LLC owns and operates Chatwing below is information about how information is used when using and operating Chatwing. 

  
**INFORMATION COLLECTION**   
Chatwing uses information you provide to allow you the user to do various features using Chatwing. By providing your email address Chatwing has the ability to create an account for your personal chat experience. Our primary goals in collecting information are to provide and improve Chatwing services, features and content and to enable users to simply use Chatwing to the fullest. Chatwing stores users IP addresses for various reasons to help make Chatwing service better for webmaster, websites and users. When a user send a message the IP address of the user is displayed to the website admin and moderator so they can ban, delete or blacklist that users message. 

  
**PAYMENT**   
Chatwing does not store any payment information from users. 

  
**ADVERTISING**   
Chatwing DOES NOT serve any sort of advertising to users of any kind. 

  
**INTERNATIONAL TRANSFER**   
Your information may be transferred to - and maintained on - computers located outside of your state, province, country or other governmental jurisdiction where the privacy laws may not be as protective as those in your jurisdiction. If you are located outside the United States and choose to provide information to us, Chatwing transfers Personal Information to the United States and processes it there. Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by your submission of such information represents your agreement to that transfer. 

  
**CONTACTING US**   
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/site/contact)
