# Privacy Policy on Chamber of Commerce.com

ChamberOfCommerce.com respects the privacy of individuals who visit this site and other sites operated by ChamberOfCommerce.com. This statement discloses the privacy practices for this web site. ChamberOfCommerce.com wants you to understand the terms and conditions surrounding our collection and use of your personally identifiable information through this site.

**INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE.**

This site only collects personally identifiable information when you voluntarily provide information to the ChamberOfCommerce.com through the site. 

While the site automatically logs information about you from your browser when you visit the site, such as your IP address, the pages you visit and similar information, it does not collect any personally identifiable information about you during that process. This site may place and access cookies when you visit the site. A cookie is a small data file that web sites commonly write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file contains information that can identify your computer each time you visit the site. This site uses cookies as a way to measure activity and traffic patterns on the site. You may choose to set your computer to reject cookies, if you wish.

From time to time, you may be asked to voluntarily provide us with information about you at various places on the site. Personal information which we may ask you to provide includes your name, location, e-mail address, company affiliation, telephone number, and other similar information. 

ChamberOfCommerce.com may use the information which you voluntarily furnish through this site to send you information and promotional material, to process your requests for information or services, or to customize the site for your future visits. 

**SHARING AND DISCLOSURE.**

ChamberOfCommerce.com is not in the business of selling or renting your personally identifiable information. ChamberOfCommerce.com may share information about you with others under the following circumstances: 

  1. when necessary to third parties who work for ChamberOfCommerce.com in order to process your requests; 
  2. when you consent to disclosure; 
  3. by operation of law, when such information is transferred as a part of sale of assets including the assets represented by this site; 
  4. to comply with law, court orders, subpoena or legal process; or 
  5. to enforce or apply ChamberOfCommerce.com agreements or protect the ChamberOfCommerce.com rights.



**THIRD PARTY ADVERTISING**

We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you.

**THIRD PARTY COOKIES**

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.

**CORRECTING AND DELETING INFORMATION.**

If the information you have given ChamberOfCommerce.com changes and you would like to modify it or if you would like to view the information ChamberOfCommerce.com has about you, send your request to the following location. In addition, if you wish to remove information about yourself from ChamberOfCommerce.com files, also send your request to the following location.

1\. Email your request to: [Chambers@ICINetwork.com](mailto:Chambers@ICINetwork.com)

With any request, provide ChamberOfCommerce.com with a means to identify you and contact you if necessary. ChamberOfCommerce.com will respond to appropriate requests within a reasonable time.

**DISPUTES AND CHANGES TO POLICY.**

ChamberOfCommerce.com may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time. If ChamberOfCommerce.com decides to change its privacy practices for this site, it will post those changes to the Privacy Policy on the site in advance of the changes taking effect. Periodically check the site to review changes. Any privacy-related disputes arising out of use of this site is subject to the site's [terms and conditions of use](https://web.archive.org/index.cfm?objectid=569943F9-1422-74FF-325980FBAB24AE8B).
