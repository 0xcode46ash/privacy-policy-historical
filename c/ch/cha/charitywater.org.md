# privacy policy.

disclosure statement for online gifts.

When you donate to charity: water in the name of someone else or for an online promotion, charity: reserves the right to disclose your first and last name, email and amount donated to the person in whose name the donation is made.  This allows participants to know how much they have raised towards their goal, and who to thank. 

_privacy policy._

Any personal information collected on charitywater.org (i.e. email address, name, home address) is for the sole purpose of the charity: organization and may be used to communicate project updates.  charity: water does not transmit, transfer or sell private information to any third parties. 

for further inquiries, contact info (@) charitywater.org
