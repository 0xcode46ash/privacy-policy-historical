# Privacy Policy | CIC

CIC uses reasonable efforts to protect the privacy of its Website visitors, users and registrants (for purposes of the Privacy Policy, “users”) and their respective information pursuant to the terms of this Privacy Policy, which applies solely to information collected by this Website. If you feel that we are not abiding by our posted privacy policy, please contact [legal@cic.com](mailto:info@cic.com)

### Information Collection and Use

CIC is the sole owner of the information collected on its Website. CIC will not sell or use this information in any way other than for CIC business without your prior approval. CIC does not collect or require any personal information for all publicly accessible web pages. CIC may track certain user information solely to administer our Website, track users’ respective logins and movements, including the domains from which people visit the Website, and analyze this data for trends and statistics on an aggregated basis only. This aggregated information is not linked in any way to personally-identifiable information. CIC may share aggregated information solely with its service providers that have agreed to be bound by this Privacy Policy. CIC will also disclose data to the extent required by law and reserves the right to transfer users’ data to a third party in the course of a sale or reorganization of CIC’s business or assets.

### Usage of Cookies

We may use data collection devices such as "cookies" on certain pages of the Website to help analyze our web page flow, measure effectiveness, and promote trust and safety. Cookies are small files placed on your hard drive that are tied to your IP address and assist us in providing our services. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our Website.

### Personally-Identifiable Information

CIC collects information from our users who voluntarily elect to provide such information. CIC is the sole owner of the information collected on our Website and CIC does not sell, share or rent this information to others except as disclosed herein. If, at any point, CIC decides to use any of its users’ personally-identifiable information in a manner other than that stated at the time of collection, CIC will use all commercially reasonable efforts to provide each user with the opportunity to opt out of CIC’s modified use of his/her information at that time. Children under the age of eighteen should not provide any personal information on this Website. Under applicable law, which provides additional privacy protections for children who use the Internet, we will not retain any information provided by a user whom we know to be under 18. If a user’s personally-identifiable information changes or if a user desires to terminate his/her registration, he/she can correct, update or remove all data provided to us via the registration information page or by emailing [legal@cic.com](mailto:legal@cic.com).
