# Cision Wire | Privacy Statement

**Cision’s Privacy Statement**  
At Cision we care about providing you with the tools and information to manage and protect your online privacy. We have therefore developed a policy to inform you about the types of information this website collects, who may receive that information, and what we may do with the information.   
By using this website/subscribing to our services, you consent to the use of your personal information in accordance with this Statement. If we change the Statement we will post updated Statement on this webpage so that you will be aware at all times of the information we collect and how we use it.  
While we have made every attempt to make this Statement as clear as possible, if you have any questions about this Statement, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/web/20080914023246id_/http://www.cisionwire.com/en/contact/)

 **Cookies**  
Cookies are small files that contain information a Web site uses for tracking purposes. This web site uses cookies to facilitate the visitor experience and/or to enhance site content by compiling aggregate information about which specific sections and pagers visitors use. You may set your web browser settings to block cookies.

 **Information collection**  
Cision collects the following types of information:  
Traditional contact information such as mailing addresses, phone and fax numbers.

Internet contact information such as email addresses.

Information about the visitor’s computer system, such as the web browser type, IP address and operating system.

The number of web pages visited, click stream data, and other navigation information.

Non-financial identification information, such as user name and password combinations.

This is statistical data about our users' browsing actions and patterns, and does not identify any individual.

Compiling this information helps us to better understand site usage patterns and which types of content are of greatest interest to the visitors of our site. In addition, this information allows us to notify visitors of updates to our site, and to contact them with information about Cision's portfolio of services. 

**Information sharing**  
The information we collect is not shared with email advertisers or other parties. Only Cision employees who need access to the information in order to do their jobs have access to it. These employees are fully committed to our privacy and security policies. Please therefore be aware that information may be transferred and processed internationally within the Cision group, to Cision companies in for instance the US, Canada and Norway.

If you believe we are not handling your information in accordance with our Privacy Statement, or if you want us to correct any incorrect information, please contact our [Client Services](https://web.archive.org/web/20080914023246id_/http://www.cisionwire.com/en/contact/). If for some reason we have violated our Privacy Statement, we will correct the error as soon as you notify us. You also have the right to be informed once a year about how we are using your information.
