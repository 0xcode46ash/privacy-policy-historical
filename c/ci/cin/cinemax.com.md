# CINEMAX PRIVACY NOTICE

**CINEMAX PRIVACY NOTICE** PRIVACY PRACTICES AND POLICIES

CINEMAX automatically collects Web site activity data that is used by us for internal review in aggregate form. E-mail addresses and other personally identifying data about visitors to this site are known to CINEMAX when voluntarily submitted or posted to us. 

CINEMAX uses personally identifying data provided by visitors to contact visitors who communicate with us. Personally identifying data is collected primarily to enable CINEMAX to mail additional information about CINEMAX to Web site visitors. For example, we direct e-mail to visitors who provide us with their e-mail addresses for specific purposes such as responding to comments or being notified if they have won one of our contests. If you at any point no longer wish to receive communications from CINEMAX, please forward such requests to us at [Webmaster@hbo.com.](mailto:webmaster@hbo.com)

KIDS -- About Your Privacy

Kids should be sure to ask mom or dad for permission before sending any personal information over the Internet. 

A SPECIAL NOTICE FOR PARENTS

While CINEMAX encourages children to consult with their parents before furnishing data, parents should supervise their children's online activities and consider using parental control tools available from online services and software manufactures that help provide a child-friendly online environment. These tools can also prevent children from disclosing online their name, address, and other personal information without parental permission. For additional information about parental control tools, please consult the "Consumer Assistance" page of direct Marketing Association home page (www.the-dma.org).
