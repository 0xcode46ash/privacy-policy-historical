# Privacy Policy | City Sightseeing Toronto

### **Privacy Policy**

  * Personal information will only be used or disclosed for the purposes for which it was collected, except with your consent or as required or permitted by law. Personal information will be retained only as long as necessary for these purposes or as required by law.
