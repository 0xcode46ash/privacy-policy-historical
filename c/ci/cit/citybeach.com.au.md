# Privacy Policy

Consumer Data

1\. We do not collect personal information when you visit our site unless you place an order or contact us via our web-based email form.

2\. When you place an order you will be asked for the following personal information: your name, email address, mailing address, delivery address and phone number, and credit card data.

If you use 3D secure our banking partner could provide a pop up window requesting you to answer a specific challenge question you have previously organised with Visa/Mastercard/AMEX. This information is not stored by Fewstone Pty Ltd a Trustee of City Beach Trust trading as City Beach.

We may share this information, and only that information which is absolutely necessary, with those third parties that are involved in the processing of your order. For example, the financial institution that issued your credit card and shipping carrier for delivery of your order.

3\. We do not share, sell, rent or barter any identifiable personal information to any third party without your permission.

4\. When you submit feedback or questions via email, we will request your name and email address. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiries.

5\. If customers share their product knowledge and recommendations with other users via the product review functionality, this data will be the sole property of City Beach and could be used in any manner deemed appropriate by City Beach. This disclaimer relates to product information only.

Security Capabilities

1\. Information supplied by e-commerce gateway provider. i.e. Westpac Banking Corporation ABN 33 007 457 141.

2\. Cybersource is used by this website as a secure payment gateway. Cybersource is a Payment Cars Industry (PCI) Data Security Standard (DSS) compliant institution.

3\. For credit card transactions, your information is secured by using 128 bit Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology, which encrypts information you input.

Newsletter

1\. We offer a number of free email newsletters to share information about our site and/or what's happening in-stores with consenting users. Users may cancel their free email subscription at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of each email newsletter.

Website Data

Your website data is collected for analysis and evaluation in order to assist us to improve the City Beach website and the services and products we provide. Website data is also used so we can enhance and personalise your shopping experience on the City Beach website. This data will not be used in association with any other personal information.

When you visit the City Beach website, our servers will automatically collect information that your browser sends when you visit our website. City Beach uses web analytics tools and other third party technologies, such as Google Analytics and DoubleClick Cookies, to collect non-personal information in the form of various IP anonymised user metrics when you visit the City Beach website.

These tools and technologies collect and analyse certain types of information, such as: browser type, time on site, mobile device type, referring and exit URLs, onsite behaviour and usage information, site usage metrics and other relevant information. We may also permit certain third party companies to help us tailor advertising that we think may be of interest to you based on your use of City Beach website and/or Services and to otherwise collect and use data about your use of City Beach website and/or Services. 

Google has additional information available about their [Remarketing Privacy Guidelines](http://adwords.google.com/support/aw/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=100746), [Policies](https://support.google.com/adwordspolicy/answer/143465?hl=en&rd=1), and [Restrictions](http://adwords.google.com/support/aw/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=178363).

You may choose to opt out of the DoubleClick cookie by visiting the [Google advertising opt-out page](http://www.google.com/policies/technologies/ads/) or you may opt out of Google Analytics by visiting the [Google Analytics opt-out page](http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

If you believe that your privacy has been breached by City Beach please contact us at [customerservice@citybeach.com.au](mailto:customerservice@citybeach.com.au) and we will resolve the issue.
