# Privacy Policy

Copyright Â© 2004-2008. All Rights Reserved. Moving Traffic, Inc., operator of CityTownInfo.com, is not a broker or a lender, and all offers for loans or mortgages found on this site are made solely by the advertiser.

Moving Traffic, Inc., the publisher of CityTownInfo.com (CTI), is committed to the privacy of the users of this site. As such, the company has established this simple, straightforward privacy policy: 

  * CTI does **not** store or retain any personal information about its users.
  * CTI maintains standard web site log files. This information is for understanding traffic patterns to the web site and contains no personally identifiable information.
  * Any information provided by users in a form is sent directly to our partner's web site for processing for the purpose of matching users with appropriate service providers (such as licensed mortgage loan specialists).
  * CTI's partners maintain their own privacy policies that users may review before continuing to provide information on their sites.
  * CTI and its partners will **not** ask users for their social security numbers or credit card details as a requirement for receiving quotes from participating lenders.
  * CTI uses industry popular analytical packages to help gather statistical information about traffic to the site. These third-party tools set cookies to perform session tracking to determine when a user moves from one page to another. No personally identifiable information is recorded.
  * CTI uses third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit the website. These companies may use information ( **not** including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services that may be of interest to you. They may set and retrieve cookies to achieve this. [DoubleClick](http://www.doubleclick.com/) and [Mediaplex](http://www.mediaplex.com/) are two of the larger ad servers that may deliver ads through CTI. A full list of companies that may be involved in displaying ads on CTI can be found [here](https://www.google.com/adsense/support/bin/answer.py?answer=94149). Contact these companies for further details.
