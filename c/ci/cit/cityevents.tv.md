# CityEvents : Venues, Events, Nightlife, Club listings, Events, Night clubs, Club Videos, Promoters, Job offers, Talents, New York, Miami, California, Guestlists, DJs, Models, Club Photos, Dancers, Photographers, Musicians ...

In CITYEVENTS.TV INC. we take your Privacy seriously. We created this Privacy statement to demonstrate our firm commitment to Privacy. The following outlines our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website. 

**Information Automatically Captured from Visitors**

We may use your IP address (the series of numbers associated with your individual computer) to help us maintain our website and identify problems with our server. Your IP address does not disclose any personally revealing information about you. 

We may use cookies to identify traffic that comes through advertising and to facilitate a more user friendly experience, to understand site usage and to improve the content and offerings on our sites. This helps us to create a personalized experience for you when you visit our site. The use of cookies is a very common practice on the Internet. Cookies are text files we place in your computer's browser to store your preferences. Cookies, by themselves, do not tell us your e-mail address or other personally identifiable information unless you choose to provide this information to us by, for example, registering at one of our sites. However, once you choose to furnish the site with personally identifiable information, this information may be linked to the data stored in the cookie. 

**The information you have provided**

By signing up and providing personal information, you agree and acknowledge that we may use such information (including but not limited to personally identifiable information that may be collected such as: name, address, e-mail address, telephone numbers). 

We also may collect certain non-personally identifiable information when you visit many of our web pages such as the type of browser you are using (e.g., Netscape, Internet Explorer), the type of operating system you are using, (e.g., Windows '95 or Mac OS) and the domain name of your Internet service provider (e.g., America Online, Earthlink). 

**How we use this information**

We may use the information you provide about yourself to fulfill our service. 

We may use this information to communicate with you. 

We may use your contact information to send you valuable updates, status reports and newsletters from CITYEVENTS.TV or out partners and friends. The demographic information that you gave at registration (e.g. your gender and age) may be used to tailor these emails to you. We may also customize these emails to you based on the location (area code, city, etc.) that you entered. 

When participating in CITYEVENTS.TV, you are giving permission to CITYEVENTS.TV to forward your email address to our mailing system only. CITYEVENTS.TV will not forward your email address or contact information to any 3rd party unless you choose to do so by joining their mailing list or their buddy list. 

The personally identifiable information we collect from you may be used to improve the content and functionality of our website, customize the content or layout of our pages or to notify you about updates to our website. We also may use this information in the aggregate to analyze site usage, as well as to offer you products, programs, or services. 

We may disclose personally identifiable information in response to legal process, for example, in response to a court order or a subpoena. We also may disclose such information in response to a law enforcement agency's request. 

Agents and contractors of CITYEVENTS.TV who have access to personally identifiable information are required to protect this information in a manner that is consistent with our Privacy Policy by, for example, not using the information for any purpose other than to carry out the services they are performing for CITYEVENTS.TV . Although we take appropriate measures to safeguard against unauthorized disclosures of information, we cannot assure you that personally identifiable information that we collect will never be disclosed in a manner that is inconsistent with our Privacy Policy. 

**Collection of information by third-party sites and sponsors**

Our website may contain links to other sites whose information practices may be different than ours. Visitors should consult the other sites' Privacy notices as we have no control over information that is submitted to, or collected by, these third parties. 

Our website may offer content (e.g., contests, sweepstakes, or promotions) that is sponsored by or co-branded with identified third parties. By virtue of these relationships, the third parties may obtain personally identifiable information that visitors voluntarily submit to participate in the site activity. CITYEVENTS.TV has no control over these third-parties' use of this information. 

Our website may use a reputable third party to present or serve the advertisements that you may see at its Web pages. Our Privacy Policy does not cover any use of information that a third-party ad server may have collected from you. 

CITYEVENTS.TV does not consider the acquisition of, and/or sale of the assets of CITYEVENTS.TV violation of this Privacy Policy or any of the items mentioned within it.
