# citypaper.net Privacy Policy

We don't appreciate it when web sites we visit abuse our privacy rights, so we pledge never to abuse yours.

City Paper’s goal is to provide readers with the information they want. The minimal information gathered is used for that purpose only. Other personal information given voluntarily — in filling out surveys, entering contests, etc. — will not be used for advertising purposes. This information is used only for City Paper’s own marketing, web site improvement, special incentives and report statistics. City Paper will not share this information with any other organizations, nor use it on others’ behalf. Access to this information is limited to City Paper employees responsible for web site marketing and production.

For updating, deleting or opting out of any lists generated on our web site, please contact:
