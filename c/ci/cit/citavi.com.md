What We Collect

We require personally identifiable information to issue licenses, to provide support to users of Citavi Pro and Team, and to send any newsletters you request. We will not use your personal information for any other purposes, such as advertising.

You Decide

We don't share any information with third parties. Any information you share stays here, except as is required by law. We also don't use any surreptitious methods to collect data.

Your Data is Safe

Addresses are sensitive personal information, and we treat them as such. All of our systems are protected by comprehensive intrustion protection and antivirus software.

Erase All Traces

If you want us to delete the personal information we do have, just email a request to [info@citavi.com](mailto:info@citavi.com?subject=Please%20delete%20my%20personal%20information&body=Please%20remove%20my%20entry%20from%20your%20customer%20database.%20I%20acknowledge%20that%20this%20makes%20it%20impossible%20for%20you%20to%20retrieve%20my%20license%20key%20if%20I%20need%20it%20again,%20for%20example%20to%20install%20on%20a%20new%20computer.).
