#### Citation Machine is committed to providing the utmost in privacy security.

Citation Machine does not collect personally identifiable information from anyone without his or her request. All e-mail correspondence is kept in strict privacy and will not be disclosed to parties other than Citation Machine.

When advertisers request, Citation Machine will provide aggregate information on the general makeup of the site's viewers. Such aggregate information includes the number of pageviews, times of pageviews, continental locations (if applicable), most hit URLs, and browser types.

Citation Machine's advertising partners may serve third party cookies that do their own tracking. These companies may use non-personally identifiable information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements on our sites, other websites, and other forms of media about goods and services that may be of interest to you. You can opt out of third-party cookies either by blocking them from your browser or by visiting Network Advertising Initiative and opting out from the networks directly.

These policies are subject to change, so please make sure to check back periodically.

Although Citation Machine has a strict privacy policy, all interactions with the Internet pose some inherent dangers. Please be aware of these and conduct all your transactions with caution. Please be careful and responsible when you are surfing online.
