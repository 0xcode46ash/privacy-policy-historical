This website is administered by Cigniti Technologies Ltd. We are committed to maintain your privacy and ensure best security protocols are always in place. ### Website Registration Forms

Our site is designed to require you to complete a registration form if you are interested in obtaining additional information or products from us. Therefore, registration forms may require you to give us contact information (like your name, email, and postal address).

Cigniti Technologies Ltd does not engage in the practice of selling or distributing contact information to other companies. Contact information from our registration forms is used to send requested product information and promotional material to prospects. Sometimes the mailings are handled by a third party fulfillment house that requires access to the information on registration forms in order to send you requested materials. In addition, contact information is sometimes shared with authorized Cigniti Technologies Ltd value added resellers who are designated by Cigniti Technologies Ltd to follow up with the prospective customer.

### Supplementation of Information

This site sometimes supplements the information that you provide with information that is received from third parties. For instance, if inaccurate address information is received, we may use third party software to fix such erroneous items.

### Submissions

Any information provided to Cigniti Technologies Ltd by way of this website is considered open information. While your information will never be shared with any other company without permission, it is not considered confidential and can be used for research or marketing Cigniti Technologies Ltd or any divisions within. If you do not wish to receive any marketing from us, reply to the email address found on the bottom of all direct marketing campaigns. If you opt out of any direct marketing campaign and subsequently resubmit your information, you will be added to our marketing address book once more and resume receiving direct marketing from us. 

### Cookies

Our Site may use cookies to track the length of time you spend on the site, or to store frequently used user information to personalize your online experience.

### External Links

This site contains links to other sites. Cigniti Technologies Ltd is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Websites.

### Changes to Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our privacy and/or security policies, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
