# Cipinet Privacy Policy

Cipinet takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

**Privacy Policy Overview**

This Privacy Policy covers how Cipinet treats personal information Cipinet collects and records when you submit your website for inclusion in Cipinet Web Directory. 

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies or individuals that Cipinet campaigns for, listed on Cipinet web directory. This policy also does not apply to people Cipinet does not employ or manage. Please verify their Privacy Policy by visiting their website.

**Information Collection and Use**

For web site submission, visitors must create an account with us. Account activation code is emailed to verify email address. We ask for website and some personal information. Every information submitted is saved for future reference. Email address is only used for sending email when your website is either approved or disapproved. Website submission in Cipinet web directory is paid inclusion service effective Mid of April 2007. Yearly, Basic or Sponsored link submission is required to get listed. Sponsored listing will be ranked higher in particular category or sub-category and web directory search pages. At the time of new website submission, if the website is not found suitable, it will be rejected and total amount paid will be refunded. No money will be refunded at any time if the website is approved. Website will be disapproved if Terms and Conditions are not followed and an editor does not find it suitable for inclusion. Discounted offer, if any, is valid from the day it appears on Login Page and is valid till the date mentioned.

**Information Sharing and Disclosure**

Cipinet does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people and/or affiliated and nonaffiliated companies.

**Cookies**

Cookies are used on our website for the purpose of logging into your account.

The [DoubleClick DART cookie](http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/faq.aspx) is used by Google in the ads served on Cipinet websites displaying AdSense for ads. Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy.](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)

**Security**

Cipinet stores your personal information inside our premises under safe custody. Access is limited to those employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information.

**Changes to this Privacy Policy**

Cipinet may amend or update this policy from time to time. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by placing a prominent notice on our Home Page and Privacy Policy page.
