# Privacy policy

  


**1\. Security and confidentiality**

Personal information and payment data are protected by means of encryption (SSL mode). Payment information is never stored and is sent securely to our payment partner Cybersource.

**2\. Collection and use of information**

Personal data   
When you navigate around the site Clarins may collect the data that you provide. The customer information collected by Clarins when an order is placed are also required by Clarins and its commercial partners to process the order. It will only be used internally by Clarins in order to make a delivery or to improve our service to you. In accordance with the data protection and civil liberties law no. 78-17 of January 6 1978, the customer has the right to access, correct, contest and delete any personal data held by Clarins by contacting consumer relations.

Newsletters   
The information that you provide may also permit us to send you newsletters if you have opted in. These newsletters always indicate our name and the nature of the newsletter. If you do not wish to receive this type of correspondence, please unsubscribe by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of each newsletter that you receive.

**3\. Cookies**

Clarins reserves the right to install cookies on your computer if you visit the website. In general, these store information concerning your computer's navigation around our site (the pages that you have visited, the date and time of the visit, etc.) which we can then read when you revisit the site. The use of cookies serves to improve our services and to personalize your next visit to the site. 

You can choose to disable the use of cookies or modify the acceptance parameters by configuring the Internet options for your browser.
