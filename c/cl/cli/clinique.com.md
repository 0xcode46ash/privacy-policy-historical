# Clinique Privacy Policy

Clinique Privacy Policy 

When you register at the Clinique Web site, we respect your privacy. Clinique will never give your user information to anyone unless you specifically tell us you want us to. The information you provide us is used to help personalize product information and offers for you. 

Your name and address information is used only for mailing you free sample offers, and will not be given to any third party without your consent. Your e-mail address is used only for sending you information and updates about Clinique products, tips and the Web site. 

On our [registration form](https://web.archive.org/countrify/templates/user/register.tmpl/privacy.html) you can select whether or not you want to receive e-mail from us. If at any time you wish to be taken off the Clinique e-mail list, please [click here to unsubscribe](mailto:unsubscribe.clinique@mail.0mm.com) with the word "remove" as the only word on the first line of the body of the message. If you need to contact a human to be taken off the mailing list, please send mail to [tech@clinique.com](mailto:tech@clinique.com). 

If you want to send [feedback](https://web.archive.org/customerservice/feedback.tmpl) to us about a privacy issue or anything else, [click here](https://web.archive.org/customerservice/feedback.tmpl). 

Thank you.
