# Privacy Policy | Help.VideoClips.com

## [Privacy Policy](http://help.videoclips.com/privacy-policy/ "Permalink to Privacy Policy")

  1. We do not spam – EVER! Your email address is used as your login name to our site, not for unsolicited marketing.
  2. There is a weekly mailing list that you can opt in to; you are not automatically added to that list.
  3. We do not sell our email lists to anyone. Customers come first.
  4. We will never have pop ups advertising other companies products or services. We hate pop ups.
  5. We only bill for what you buy. There are no hidden fees or cross sells.
  6. Videoclips.com is owned by **LaHoma II**. We have been providing low cost adult entertainment for over fifteen years. We are reputable, honest and we want your business!
  7. People love what we offer, our inexpensive pricing and our customer service.



**LaHoma II**  has created this privacy policy in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to our user’s privacy. The following discloses our information gathering practices.

Our site’s registration form requires users to give us contact information (such as an email address). We use your contact information from the registration form to send you special offers and updates from  **LaHoma II** , if you opt to receive them. While you can browse the site, you will need to register in order to view videos and take advantage of certain features. You will be asked to subscribe to our mailing list and if you opt to receive them, you will receive occasional emails with video announcements, special deals, and discounts for our products – never for third party products. You can choose to stop receiving this email at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link on the bottom of your email. The only email messages you will receive from  **LaHoma II**  when you elect not to receive informational email will be to confirm any purchases you make on our site.  **LaHoma II**  will never sell your email address to any other company or agency.

**LaHoma II**  uses cookies to make browsing our site easier. You have the option of turning off cookies within your browser. Many features of our site require cookies to be enabled.

Our sites uses a secure online ordering system which enables clients to purchase minutes for viewing movies online, as well as rent movies. **LaHoma II**  collects users financial information; such as account and credit card numbers for verification purposes only. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services and never for any other reason.  **LaHoma II**  has a strict billing policy, and you will only be billed for the products you select. There are no monthly fees or memberships on any of the  **LaHoma II**  websites.

**LaHoma II**  websites may contain links to other sites.  **LaHoma II**  is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
