# ClipArt.com

Privacy Statement

This is the website of ClipArt.com. When a consumer visits our web pages, our web server automatically recognizes only the user's domain name, and not his or her email address (where possible). We collect only the domain names, and not the email addresses of visitors to our web pages. 

The email addresses of those who communicate with us via email are gathered only if they are volunteered by the consumer, such as via survey and/or site registration. The information we collect is used to improve the content of our website, customize the content and/or layout of our web pages, notify consumers about updates to our website, or contact consumers for marketing purposes. We will not share your email address with other organizations for commercial purposes without your consent. 

If you supply us with your postal address online you may receive mailings from other reputable companies. We will not share your phone number. You can, however, have your name and address put on our do-not-share list by sending email to us or simply unchecking the box in your "Account" area. If you provide us with your exact name and mailing address, we will make sure your name is removed from the list. 

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers online may receive telephone contact from us regarding orders they have placed online. They also may receive telephone contact from us regarding new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such telephone calls, please let us know by sending email to us or by unchecking the box giving us permission under "Account." Please provide us with your exact name, mailing address, and phone number. 

ClipArt.com will periodically collect demographic and interest category information from users via surveys. ClipArt.com members may voluntarily provide information, including that regarding personal preferences, interests, geography, and demographics. The purpose of this data collection is to ensure that ClipArt.com members receive only the most relevant, targeted offers and information.
