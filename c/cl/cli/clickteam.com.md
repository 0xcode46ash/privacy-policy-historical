# Clickteam.com - Creative Tools for the Creative Age

**Clickteam  is committed to respecting the privacy of its customers and the privacy of our website visitors, customers, vendors, and mailing list subscribers.

The following company wide policy has been established: 

**

Clickteam will not share, sell, rent or otherwise disclose any personal information of the customers without their advance permission, unless otherwise ordered by a court of law.   
All information is used solely to deliver the requested service from the website visitor, customer of Clickteam, vendor or mailing list subscriber.
