# Clinch - Privacy policy

**Clinch Dynamic Video Ads**

**Note:** **** The following Privacy Policy refers only to Clinch Labs LTD’s ‘Dynamic Video Ads’ product. The Privacy Policy for Clinch’s automatic video editing mobile applications (iOS, Android) is available in the mobile apps (in the Settings section).   ** **

**_Privacy Policy_** ****

At Clinch Labs we take your privacy very seriously. The purpose of this privacy policy is to explain the principles of our product ‘Clinch Dynamic Videos’ videos and ads solution and the data that it collects from our dynamic online video viewers ("you").

Clinch Labs reserves the right, at our discretion, to change this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be effective 10 days following posting of the revised privacy policy on our website. Your continued use of our website 10 days following such posting means you are accepting those changes.  

**About Clinch Dynamic Video Ads**

Clinch Labs specializes in smart automated video editing. Our product ‘Clinch Dynamic Videos Ads’ is transforming clients online shopping experience and improving communication between a brand and its audience through innovative, engaging videos. These videos result higher conversion rates and ROI to our clients. Our objective is to summarize and personalized video ads to customers of our clients' websites across the web. 

Clinch Labs strongly believes that in a fast paced world, people like to get the short, summarized version of things that highlights the important content. Interact with it and explore more exciting content. Therefore by turning online catalogs and structured webpages into dynamic product video ads, our clients’ audiences are, as we’ve seen, significantly more likely to engage with them and search for additional information or to take advantage of a specific offer. 

**What information does Clinch Dynamic Video Ads collect?**

Clinch Labs, with its product Clinch Dynamic Videos, does not collect any personal information that can be used to identify you. Personal information refers to any information that may be used, either as-is or combined with other information, to identify an individual, such as, but not limited to, a first and last name, personal profile, physical address, an email address or other contact information. In cases when data is collected, it is completely anonymous and includes information, which does not enable identification of an individual user, such as the Internet Browser you are using to access websites, whether you have visited our clients’ websites, and random collected data about how our clients’ websites and services are being used. The information Clinch Labs might be aware of is that a certain user (No personal information included - name, address, email, etc.) visited several products pages recently and this information is used to present videos and ads that are relevant to those products.

**How is this anonymous information collected?**

Clinch Labs may choose to collect the anonymous information through the use of browser cookies. Browser cookies record, for example, the pages you browsed on our clients' websites. When you visit our clients’ websites that Clinch Dynamic Videos is integrated with, your browser sends the anonymous cookie and based on the information stored there, you should receive better-targeted and relevant video ads than the standard you would receive without using Clinch Dynamic Videos. Clinch Labs has no way, need or desire to identify you throughout this process.

You can remove our cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browser's "help".

**Display of Clinch Dynamic Video Ads.**

Clinch Labs dynamic video ads run on many Internet websites as part of their promotion, advertising and video programming. The video ads could appear in relevant product pages, as stand-alone ads, social media posts or ads, banners and before or during the playback of a video that you chose to watch. Many of these video publishers access Clinch Labs through third-party advertising networks or video ad exchanges. Those networks may also place cookies on your browser upon visiting a Clinch customer’s website to facilitate ad serving as described above. You may opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting the [Google advertising opt-out page](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html). In addition, you can opt out of any other third party vendor's use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt out page.

**Links & Third-Party Sites**

Clinch may choose to implement and incorporate, as part of the assets in its video creation, content from Google Maps / Earth / Places APIs. Therefore by using Clinch application, clients and users acknowledge and agree to be bound by [_Google Privacy Policy_](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy) _._
