# Privacy Policy - ClickBank

The following privacy statements address our information collection practices with respect to certain types of information that we receive: 

  * [The Client Privacy Policy](http://http//www.clickbank.com/client_privacy.html) addresses the types of information gathered through this website, [http://www.clickbank.com](http://http//www.clickbank.com), from vendors and affiliates. It also addresses how we collect, use, and disclose personally identifiable information. 
  * [The Customer Privacy Policy](http://http//www.clickbank.com/customer_privacy.html) addresses the types of information gathered through this website, [http://www.clickbank.com](http://http//www.clickbank.com) and www.clickbank.net (where the order form resides), from customers when a purchase is made. It also addresses how we collect, use, and disclose personally identifiable information.
