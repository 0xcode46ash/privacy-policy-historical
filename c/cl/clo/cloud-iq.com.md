# Cloud.IQ

## Privacy

cloud.IQ endeavours to safeguard your privacy.

  * We will only contact you when you contact us. 
  * Your information will only be shared with the brands you have requested information from. 
  * We will not send unsolicited text messages to you. 



Using cloud.IQ's services will not authorise us or our client to send any unsolicited marketing communications to you. Our client, from whom you have requested information, is required to ask your permission before delivering any unsolicited communications to you. Our client is required to act in accordance with the Data Protection Act and Spam Act 2003 (Aus) in relation to any personal information we pass on to them. 

Please find details of the information we keep below: Information collected as part of cloud.IQ's services is collected by cloud.IQ Limited, a marketing services company based in London. We are committed to protecting your privacy. We will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully (in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998 and Spam Act 2003). 

We collect information about you to: 

  * process your request; and 
  * to register you for the service, to enable you to request the information you want more easily in the future. 
  * The type of information we will collect about you includes: 
  * your name (directly from you only) 
  * address (from publicly available sources) 
  * phone number (direct from you only) 
  * email address (directly from you only) 
  * details of information requests you have made from cloud.IQ in the past 



We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent. 

We will pass on the information we collect about you to our clients to enable them to process and respond to your request. We will also keep your personal information on our database to enable you to send requests more easily in the future. We will only use your personal information to process such requests and will not send you any unrelated communications in the future unless you have given us your prior consent.
