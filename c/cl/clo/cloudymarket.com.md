# SAP Techno Functional

## Privacy Policy

You can use our services to download our products listed in the website for FREE or by paying the amount specified for the products. We collect information for newsletters, promotion offers and for staying connected with our website with updated information and much more that is not listed here.  
Your privacy matters to both of us and please spend time to read and get familiar with our practices.  
We do not collect any information via cookies, IP addresses, location information, browser informations or credit card details in any way.  
We are not responsible for any information collected by our partners, third parties like Google AdWords, DoubleClick, Payment Gateway or any ad networks. Please refer the respective websites for their privacy policies.  
We may collect email address, name, phone number, age, gender, country etc while you subscribe with us.  
We do not sell your personal details with our partners or third parties.  
We may use your personal details to give offers, promote our products, send email notifications etc.  
The personal information provided to us is non-indexable by search engines and it is stored privately in our database.  
We hold the rights to share your personal information with companies or organizations for legal reasons that is governed by government laws.  
We do not alter your information provided to us.  
Our privacy policies changes from time to time. Please visit this web page reguraly to be updated about our privacy policy.
