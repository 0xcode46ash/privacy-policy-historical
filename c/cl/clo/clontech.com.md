We at CLONTECH are committed to maintaining the privacy of our customers. From time to time, we collect the following information from visitors to our web site.

  * Visitor's domain name
  * Information provided to us voluntarily by visitors (for example: phone number, e-mail address, technologies of interest, survey responses)
  * Collective information on pages visited or downloaded by all visitors within a specified timeframe



Any information that is collected by CLONTECH is kept strictly confidential and is only collected and used for purposes of supporting our customers and providing them with improved services. It is not our policy to share this information with any third parties.
