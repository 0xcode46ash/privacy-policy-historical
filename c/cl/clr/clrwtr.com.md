# CTI Industrial Factory Automation Parts Distributors USA ABB AC Tech Xycom SEW Turck Sola Sunx Leeson Saftronics IDEC Hammond Lenze

[**www.clrwtr.com**](http://www.clrwtr.com/) wants to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy by providing you this privacy policy statement.

**Cookies**

Clearwater Technologies, Inc.'s site uses cookies for the sole purpose of session identification. Information about you is not stored in the cookie. For added protection, [**www.clrwtr.com**](http://www.clrwtr.com/) cookie session will time out after an hour of detected site inactivity.

**Information Collected**

Clearwater Technologies, Inc. collects information for statistical purposes and improving the site's overall operational quality. [**www.clrwtr.com**](http://www.clrwtr.com/) will not disclose your personal data to potential advertisers, research firms, or business partners. You have the option to request that we not send any information to you via e-mail or regular mail.

**Security**

Clearwater Technologies, Inc. makes commercially reasonable efforts to protect the confidentiality of your information and to protect against unauthorized access. Our [**www.clrwtr.com**](http://www.clrwtr.com/) website has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of sensitive information under our control.

**Contact Webmaster**

For any questions pertaining to our privacy policy, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this website, e-mail our [**Webmaster**](mailto:donm@clrwtr.com), or call us at (800) 894-0412.
