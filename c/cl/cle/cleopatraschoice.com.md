# Cleopatra's Choice Privacy Policy

Cleopatra's Choice values your privacy greately and will never give out, rent or sell any of your email or address information. When you place an order with we need to collect certain information from you including your address, phone number, name and credit card information. Because our website and database use 128 bit SSL encryption, your information is kept safe from hackers or email spammers. You can be absolutely sure that Cleopatra's Choice will never divulge your email address or any other information you provide to us.

We have email spam just as much as you do and we value your privacy!
