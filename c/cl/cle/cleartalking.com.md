# About Cleartalking.com

Cleartalking.com takes your privacy very seriously. We know that you care how information about you is used and shared, and we appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly. Except as otherwise discussed in this Privacy Policy, this document only addresses the use and disclosure of information we collect from you online when using our services at cleartalking.com. 

However cleartalking.com has links to other third party web sites and we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. Please read the fine print of privacy policy of those web sites to know how your information is handled by them.

We maintain a directory of web sites related to telecom industry. If you submit your web site to consider for inclusion in our directory, the information we collect in connection with our web directory is used only to interact with you, in case needed. We collect your personal information only with your informed consent in other cases such as completing a form on our site, e-mailing us to contact or when you register in our forum. 

By registering to cleartalking.com forum you submit a chosen name and an e-mail address. No sensitive personal information, such as Social Security or Credit Card numbers are collected and stored by us. Few information provided by you when you register with our forum goes into a "public profile", all information held in that profile is held there at your own risk. You can edit the information provided by you via your User Control Panel. If you need any assistance with this, then contact us.

When you access our forum, we store and read cookies to and from your computer. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser to enable our forums and general website features to recognize your browser for when you return to this web site. By using cleartalking.com, you agree that both the web server, and any trusted 3rd party we chose, may place a tracking cookie on your machine and access it at any time during your session. In general, cookies are safe and are not a security issue when being used from 'trusted' internet sites.

Cleartalking.com is intended for individuals over the age of 13. Any information collected is intended to be of individuals over the age of 13. According to COPPA, our site does NOT cater to children. "Operators of commercial websites or online services directed to children under 13 that collect personal information from children" require a COPPA agreement and privacy policy. Since we do not cater to this age group, no COPPA exists on cleartalking.com. 

Cleartalking.com will not sell, transmit or distribute any of your personnel information held without your prior permission or consent, unless required by law. We will take all reasonable effort to ensure that all personal information about you submitted is maintained secure on our server. However, we make no warranty to this.

We may modify this Privacy Policy from time to time without giving any notice to you. It is your responsibility to check this page for updates or changes. By visiting cleartalking.com, you accept our privacy policy practices described in this notice. If you have any questions, please contact us.
