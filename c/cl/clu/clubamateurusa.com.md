# ClubAmateurUSA.com Privacy Policy

**General Browsing**

ClubAmateurUSA.com gathers navigational information about where you go on our web site. This information allows us to see which areas of our web site are most visited and helps us improve the quality of your online experience by recognizing and delivering more of the most visited features and services. Additional non-personally identifiable information (e.g., domain type, browser version, service provider, IP address) may be collected which will provide information regarding your use of our web site (such as the time of your last visit to a page on our site). ClubAmateurUSA.com is not geared toward anyone under the age of 18 (21 where applicable). 

**Disclosure of Personal Information**

  
The information you provide to us when you join ClubAmateurUSA.com (e.g., name, postal address, e-mail address, telephone number and age) is used to enable you to receive e-mail from us. Additionally, this information will be used to expedite your shopping experience at ClubAmateurUSA.com's online store during the check out process.

ClubAmateurUSA.com does not sell, rent, or trade your email or mailing addresses to third parties. We may, however, use third parties to help us provide services to you, such as fulfilling orders, processing payments, monitoring site activity, organizing information, conducting surveys, and administering emails, drawings, or contests. If you no longer wish to receive marketing email from ClubAmateurUSA.com please send us [EMAIL](mailto:marketing@clubamateurusa.com).

You should be aware that we may disclose specific information about you if required to do so by law or based on our good faith belief that it is necessary to conform or comply with the law or is necessary to protect the users of our web site, the site, or the public.   
Non-US Residents

If you are visiting ClubAmateurUSA.com from a location outside of the U.S., your connection will be through and to servers located in the U.S., your orders placed through ClubAmateurUSA.com will be accepted and fully processed in and shipped from the U.S. and all information you provide will be processed and securely maintained in our web servers and internal systems located within the U.S.

**Use of Cookies**

ClubAmateurUSA.com uses a browser feature known as a cookie, which assigns a unique identification to your computer. The cookies are typically stored on your computer's hard drive and used by ClubAmateurUSA.com to help track your clicks as you navigate through the pages within ClubAmateurUSA.com web site. In addition, ClubAmateurUSA.com uses cookies to help keep track of items you put into your shopping cart and to tell us whether you have visited ClubAmateurUSA.com. This allows frequent visitors to maintain shopping carts between visits. Cookies also allow ClubAmateurUSA.com to make our site more responsive to your needs, while delivering a better and more personalized experience to you.

**Links**

For your convenience, our web site may contain links to other sites. ClubAmateurUSA.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites.

**Questions / Changes In Policy**

If you have questions or concerns with respect to our privacy policy, please contact us. Should there be a material change to our policy regarding use of your personal information, we will update the privacy policy statement on this web site.

**Security**

ClubAmateurUSA.com believes in providing a safe and secure shopping experience for all of our customers. We provide stringent and effective security measures on our web site. It is our policy to never send private information, such as your credit card number, via email.
