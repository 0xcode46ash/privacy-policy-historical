# CZ-USA -> Privacy Policy

We collect and use information we believe is necessary to administer our business and to provide you with customer service.

We value you as a customer and take your personal privacy very seriously. We respect your right to privacy and we do not sell customer information. Your information will be used solely for purposes of CZ-USA and won't be disclosed to third parties.
