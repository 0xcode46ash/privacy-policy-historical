# 411.com - Online Directory Assistance

WhitePages.com is one of the internet's largest sources of people information. Our goal is to bring you the most comprehensive, relevant people search experience possible. We believe that privacy is a key component of providing you with a great service. As a result, we created a variety of resources to share our perspectives regarding privacy. We also provide various tools and services that you may find useful in protecting the privacy of your personal information. These resources and information include:

**Privacy Policy**. Our Privacy Policy (below) is provided to help you understand how we collect and use the different types of information you may choose to provide to us when using our Sites and Services.

[Privacy Central](https://web.archive.org/10001/privacy_central?pagetype=). Privacy Central provides detailed information about how you can control the information that may be published about you on different websites. It also includes details on how you can request that information be removed or edited on our Sites.
