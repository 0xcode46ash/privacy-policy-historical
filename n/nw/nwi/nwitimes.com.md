# Northwest Indiana News: nwitimes.com

  
[ **PRIVACY POLICY** ]  
  
nwitimes.com takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

**What this Privacy Policy Covers**

This Privacy Policy covers nwitimes.com's treatment of personally identifiable information that nwitimes.com collects when you are on the nwitimes.com site, and when you use nwitimes.com's services. This policy also covers nwitimes.com's treatment of any personally identifiable information that nwitimes.com's business partners share with nwitimes.com.   
This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that nwitimes.com does not own or control, or to people that nwitimes.com does not employ or manage.

**Information Collection and Use**

nwitimes.com collects personally identifiable information when you use certain nwitimes.com products or services, when you visit nwitimes.com pages, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. nwitimes.com may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners.

nwitimes.com automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, nwitimes.com cookie information and the page you requested.

nwitimes.com uses information for three general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, to fulfill your requests for certain products and services, and to contact you about specials and new products. 

**Information Sharing and Disclosure**

nwitimes.com will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.

nwitimes.com will send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when: 

  * We have your consent to share the information; 
  * We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested; 
  * We need to send the information to companies who work on behalf of nwitimes.com to provide a product or service to you. (Unless we tell you differently, these companies do not have any right to use the personally identifiable information we provide to them beyond what is necessary to assist us.); 
  * We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; or 
  * We find that your actions on our web sites violate the nwitimes.com Terms of Service, the nwitimes.com Terms of Service, or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services. 

**Cookies**

nwitimes.com may set and access nwitimes.com cookies on your computer. nwitimes.com allows other companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to nwitimes.com's cookies. 

nwitimes.com uses web beacons to access our cookies within our network of web sites and in connection with nwitimes.com products and services. 

**Security**

In certain areas nwitimes.com uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions. 

**Changes to this Privacy Policy**

nwitimes.com may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages. 

**Questions or Suggestions**

If you have questions or suggestions, email us at [comments@nwitimes.com](mailto:comments@nwitimes.com).
