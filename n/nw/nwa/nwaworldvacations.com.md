# WorldVacations - Privacy Statement and Terms of Use

**Northwest Airlines WorldVacations WorldWeb Reservations and CyberDeals Privacy Policy**

_**To view our Usage Agreement[click here](https://web.archive.org/servlet/nw_hmNavigation?aid=wvcstatc&pageCode=terms).**_

As a User of WorldVacations WorldWeb, nwaworldvacations.com, you are in complete control of your travel planning needs. This includes controlling the use of information you provide to WorldVacations and its affiliates. 

When you reserve or purchase travel services through WorldVacations WorldWeb, we provide only the relevant information required by the car rental agency, hotel, or other involved third party to ensure the successful fulfillment of your travel arrangements. We also use information you provide during User registration or as part of the reservation process to customize the content of our site to meet your specific needs and to make product improvements to WorldVacations WorldWeb. 

We do not **_sell_** individual customer names or other private profile information to third parties and have no intention of doing so in the future. We do share User information with our partners only for specific and pertinent promotional use **_but only_** if our customers have opted to receive such information. As a User of WorldVacations WorldWeb you have the option to receive updates from WorldVacations, Northwest Airlines, and Northwest WorldPerks Partners about sales in your area, special offers, new WorldVacations and Northwest Airlines services and noteworthy news. To receive this information you must register through [E-mail Sign-Up](https://web.archive.org/servlet/nw_hmNavigation?aid=emlemlrq). If you decide you would rather not receive these emails, you can always [unregister](https://web.archive.org/servlet/nw_hmNavigation?aid=emlemlrq) or update your information in [E-mail Sign-Up](https://web.archive.org/servlet/nw_hmNavigation?aid=emlemlrq). 

We respect and will continue to respect the privacy of our customers who use WorldVacations WorldWeb. For more information about protecting your privacy, please see the [Frequently Asked Questions](https://web.archive.org/servlet/nw_hmNavigation?aid=wvcstatc&pageCode=questions).
