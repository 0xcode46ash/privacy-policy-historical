# Privacy policy

Nolf.info has implemented following principles in privacy policy, which describes how we collect, use and share personally identifiable information ("PII") (that is, information such as your full name, email address, mailing address or telephone number) we obtain from and about individuals visiting our site.  
We do not collect information online via our web site from children under 13. In addition, we will not obtain PII of children under 13 from third parties unless those third parties have complied with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act ("COPPA").  
From time to time Nolf.info may revise this privacy policy to reflect changes in the law, technology or industry initiatives. Therefore, you should review it periodically so that you are up to date on our most current policies and practices.

**COLLECTION:  
Collection of personally identifiable information ("PII") by Nolf.info  
**_General._ Nolf.info does not collect PII through internet site located at www.nolf.info. We may, however, collect PII from you manually in order to create or enhance our relationship with you. We may also obtain PII about you from a third party, whether in connection with some form of registration, contest, poll or otherwise. When we collect PII from you or obtain PII about you it is because you are voluntarily submitting the information to us or a third party in order to participate in activities like contests, games, surveys, forums or some form of registration, or because you want us to furnish you with products, services or information. On those occasions when we obtain PII from a third party, we use only reputable sources of such information.  
_Cookies._ Cookies are small bits of information that our internet site could place (but there are no cookies present on our site at this time...) on the hard drive of your computer. Cookies remember information about your activities on our site and enable Nolf.info to make your visits to our site more enjoyable. For example, cookies can store your password for easy logon to a site you have previously visited, can save your preferences for a personalized home page or can track the items you have placed in your "shopping cart" during a visit to online store. Since Nolf.info does not collect PII online via web site at this time, Nolf.info does not combine cookies with PII. However, some affiliated sites are (or could be) run by third party(s), such third party(s) may combine cookies and other similar tools with your PII.  
You can also program your computer to warn you each time a cookie is being sent or to refuse cookies completely. However, without cookies you won't (or couldn´t) have access to certain features available only through this technology (but there are no such features on our site at this time).

**NOTICE:  
Nolf.info will provide you with notice about its PII collection practices  
**When Nolf.info collects PII from you, we will make sure you are informed about how and why the information is being collected and the types of uses Nolf.info will make of the information.  
This policy also describes the types of other companies that may want to send you information about their products and services and therefore want to share your PII (See "Use" below). However, at the time your PII is collected, Nolf.info will notify you of your options regarding our use of your PII, including whether we can share it with other companies (See "Choice" below).  
When we collect PII from consumers in manual format, such as a post card or subscription form, providing detailed notice sometimes proves impractical. So, instead, consumers will be provided with a short notice that describes how to obtain the full text of this policy and other relevant information from us. Even when PII is collected manually, consumers will always be informed up front how and why the information is being collected and the types of uses Nolf.info will make of the information. They will also be given the ability to choose whether such information may be used for purposes other than the one for which it was submitted or may be shared with third parties.  
Nolf.info web site (www.nolf.info) may be linked to Internet sites operated by other companies. Some of these third party sites may have look like our one even though they are not operated or maintained by us. Although we choose our business partners carefully, Nolf.info is not responsible for the privacy practices of web sites operated by third parties that are linked to our site(s) or for the privacy practices of third party(s). Once you've left our Nolf.info site via such a link, you should check the applicable privacy policy of the third party to determine how they will handle any PII they collect from you.  
Nolf.info web site may also be linked to sites operated by companies affiliated with our site. Visitors to those affiliated sites should still refer to their separate privacy policies and practices, which may differ in some respects from this policy.

**CHOICE:  
Nolf.info will provide you with choices about the use of your PII  
**Nolf.info will provide you with the opportunity to choose whether your PII may be used for purposes different from the purpose for which it was submitted. You will also be able to choose whether your information is shared with companies that are not affiliated with Nolf.info.

**USE:  
Nolf.info´s use of PII  
**Nolf.info will use your PII in a manner that is consistent with this policy and only for the purpose for which it is submitted, unless other uses are disclosed at the time of collection and you allow us to use your information in these other ways.  
Nolf.info may share your PII with companies that are affiliated with us. Nolf.info may also share your PII with companies that are not affiliated with us, but would like to send you information about their products and services. However, you will always have the opportunity up front to choose whether we may share your information with such unaffiliated third parties.  
Our employees, agents and contractors must have a legitimate business reason to obtain access to your PII. Outside contractors or agents who help us manage our information activities may only use PII to provide Nolf.info with a specific service and not for any other purpose.  
If Nolf.info obtains PII from a third party, such as a business partner, our use of this information is governed by this policy.  
Nolf.info may be required to disclose PII to comply with legal requirements, such as a court order, and to protect the safety and security of visitors to our web site.

**SECURITY:  
Nolf.info protects the security of PII  
**Nolf.info uses reasonable administrative, technical, personnel and physical measures to safeguard PII in its possession against loss, theft and unauthorized use, disclosure or modification. In addition, Nolf.info uses reasonable methods to make sure that PII is accurate, up-to-date and appropriately complete.

**ACCESS:  
How to access, correct or delete your PII  
**You may review or delete your PII from our records at any time, except, of course, in those circumstances when access would violate the privacy of another individual, when access is prohibited by law, or when the information requested is related to litigation involving the entities that comprise Nolf.info.  
If you would like to review your PII in our records, or change any of your PII previously submitted, please e-mail us at webmaster@nolf.info.  
If you would like your PII deleted, please contact us at webmaster@nolf.info. We will immediately delete your information from our database. However, there may be residual information that will remain within our databases that may or may not contain your PII. This residual information will not be given to any other third party or used for any commercial purpose.

**REMEDIES AND COMPLIANCE:  
How to contact Nolf.info about privacy concerns  
**Should you have any inquiries about this privacy policy or complaints about our privacy policy or your PII that we may have collected and/or used, please contact webmaster of this site (webmaster@nolf.info).
