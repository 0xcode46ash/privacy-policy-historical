**Commitment To Privacy**  
 | Your privacy is very important to us. To assist in protecting your privacy this notice explains our information practices and the choices you have regarding the way your information is collected and used. Please refer to this notice when personally identifiable information is requested by North American Motoring.   
   
**Information That We Collect**  
 | 

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the North American Motoring website(s). In specific areas of this site, you can make information requests, register to receive materials, and order products. The types of personal information collected in these areas are: 

  * Name 
  * Address 
  * Email address 
  * Phone number 
  * Credit/debit card information 



In other areas, you may submit information regarding other people. For example, if you were to send a electronic postcard to a friend from this site, you would be required to submit the recipient's email address. In such circumstances, types of personal information collected may include: 

  * Name 
  * Address 
  * Phone Number 
  * Email address 

  
   
**Information Use**  
 | 

When placing a request for information or to order products, we use your personal information only to complete that request. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete the request.

When placing a request for information or to order products that are to be sent to another individual, we use the information about that person only to complete that request. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that request. 

Ownership verfication requires Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) and other serial number information which is not used for any other purpose. This information is not shared with outside parties.

We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties. 

You may register as a member of this website. The information provided during this registration is used to notify members of site news, industry events, new functionality offered, etc. via email. Information submitted using our website will not be shared with outside parties. 

We use non-identifying and aggregate information to support feature development that benefits the majority of the community. For example, we may inform an advertiser that certain number of people visited a specific area of our website. We do not disclose anything that could be used to identify individuals. 

We will never use your information for a purpose other than those described above without first providing you the ability to opt-out or prohibit its unrelated use.   
  
   
**Commitment To Data Security**  
 | 

To ensure data accuracy, proper use of information, and prevent unauthorized access, we have implemented physical, electronic, and management procedures to secure and safeguard the information we collect.   
  
   
**Commitment To Children's Privacy**  
 | 

To protect the privacy of children under age 13, we do not collect or maintain information for those stating that are under age 13. Additionally, our website is not structured to attract those under age 13.  
  
   
**Accessing Or Correcting Your Information**  
 | 

You have the ability to access and correct your personally identifiable information submitted to this site.

To more fully protect your privacy and security, reasonable steps will be taken to authenticate your identity before being allowed to add, delete, or modify information.   
  
   
**Contacting North American Motoring**  
 | 

If you have any questions or concerns regarding these polices, please email us at [info@northamericanmotoring.com](mailto:info@northamericanmotoring.com).
