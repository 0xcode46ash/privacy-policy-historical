# Privacy Policy - NCS - NoCopyrightSounds : NCS – NoCopyrightSounds

Privacy Policy 

## **SPECIFIC TERMS & CONDITIONS**

You may be asked to submit personal information about yourself in order to use the services on the Site. For registration this is your name and email address and if you complete your profile this includes first name, last name, city and mobile number (all optional). By connecting via Facebook, you provide access to your basic information by default, which includes name, picture, gender and locale, plus your email address. When you connect via Facebook you can choose in the permissions to allow us to post to your behalf and manage your events. We will post one message on your timeline to tell your friends you have connected to NoCopyrightSounds. To place an order on NoCopyrightSounds you will be asked to submit credit card information, billing address and, if you are buying merchandise, delivery address in order for us to process payment. How do we use your data?

By entering your details on the Site you hereby authorise NoCopyrightSounds (NCS) and its service and content providers to provide you with the services you have selected. All of the information you provide is essential to provide or personalise the service you have requested from NoCopyrightSounds.

When you enter the Site your computer will automatically be issued with a cookie. Cookies used by NoCopyrightSounds are text files that identify your computer to our Server to allow personalised log in. By using the Site you consent to this use of cookies.

You can opt in to receive emails from NCS. From NCS you will receive newsletters and occasional offers and promotional emails. From NoCopyrightSounds you will automatically receive transactional emails such as e-tickets, order receipts and password resets. We will not authorise any third party to send you any promotional information unless you specifically opt into any information mailing list we may create.

## **INFORMATION WE COLLECT**

This is the information the we typically collect to help us provide you with our services, and to keep improving them;  
1\. Name  
2\. Address  
3\. Email  
4\. Telephone numbers  
5\. Demographic information such as age, sex, location, preferences and interests  
6\. Other information relevant to customer surveys and/or offers

Information on how you use our website to help us provide you with an ever developing service, this includes details of your domain name and internet protocol address, the operating system, geographical location, cookie details, how long you viewed pages for, the route through which you navigated the website, and website you visited prior to ours.

We use and retain this information for many reasons including internal record keeping, improving our products and services, sending promotional emails about products and special offers that we think you may find interesting. From time to time, we may use your information to contact you via email, phone, or email, for market research purposes and questionnaires. We may use the information to customise the website according to your interests.

## **USING YOUR INFORMATION**

Collecting information helps us to get a better idea and understanding of your needs. We use the data to help intelligently offer you products, services and offers that we think will suit you. We are constantly looking to improve your shopping experience. The main uses of your data are: – To provide access to member’s areas of the site.  
• To administer privileges and rewards.  
• To personalise and tailor our service to meet your needs  
• To carry out market research  
• To improve the website, its function and our service  
• To send you an occasional newsletter, detailing special offers and rewards that we think will interest you.

## **COOKIES**

Cookies are small pieces of information stored by your internet browser on to your computer’s hard drive. Like most website providers, NoCopyrightSounds uses cookies to enable us to make a link between you and the information you have provided to our website and therefore provide you with personalised content so that we can give you a better experience when you return. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, although you can choose not to.

## **HOW TO UNSUBSCRIBE:**

If, at any time, you prefer not to receive email marketing information from us, please follow the unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email. Alternatively you can contact us at hello [at] nocopyrightsounds.co.uk and instruct us to remove your email address from our marketing database. For us to be able to do so, please include your email address in the body of your email and write “Unsubscribe” in the subject line. You can also contact us on 0044 (0) 207 741 0050 on weekdays between 9am and 5pm (GMT) or send a request to Privacy Matters, AEI Media Ltd, Unit B, 4 Tyssen Street, E8 2FJ, London. Customer service email address

For general enquiries please contact us at support [at] nocopyrightsounds.co.uk or use the Help tab on the side of the site.

NoCopyrightSounds will take all reasonable measures to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control and to ensure that the Site has complied with the provisions of the Data Protection Act 1998 or any other relevant United Kingdom legislation relating to the handling of personal information. If any of the information that you have provided to us changes you must notify us by editing your billing information in Your Account.

You have the right to request a copy of the personal information that NoCopyrightSounds holds about you as a result of your use of the Site. A fee of £15.00 is payable for each such request. Please address requests to The Data Protection Officer at NCS, hello [at] nocopyrightsounds.co.uk
