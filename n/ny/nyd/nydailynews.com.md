Welcome to www.nydailynews.com, the website of Daily News, L.P. (the "Daily News") and the New York Daily News. As a visitor to this website, you agree to the Privacy Policy that appears below. The Daily News reserves the right, at its discretion, to modify the Privacy Policy in any way at any time.  
  
  
Privacy Policy  
  
The Daily News does not automatically collect any personally identifiable information, such as a name, address, e-mail address, or telephone number, from visitors to our website. Occasionally, as a visitor to our website, you may be asked to voluntarily supply the Daily News with certain personally identifying information. You may not supply such information unless you are thirteen (13) years of age or older. If you choose to provide the Daily News with this information, unless such information is provided in the course of subscribing to the New York Daily News, or unless other uses are specified at the time of collection, such information will be used only for: (a) the purpose for which it was submitted; and (b) to create aggregate statistics about activity on and visitors to our website. When you submit such information to the Daily News, it will not be encrypted.

Our website sometimes includes surveys, contests, sweepstakes and promotions, which each visitor can participate in only once. In order to ensure that no visitor participates more than once, the Daily News may use a "cookie" to track user activity. This cookie does not contain any personally identifiable information about the visitor, but only a unique number with no meaning outside of the Daily News's website. 

Our website contains links to websites that are not operated by the Daily News. The Daily News makes no representations about the privacy practices of these third party sites. 

The advertisements appearing on our website are delivered to you by a third party, DoubleClick. Doubleclick uses cookies to track user activity. The Daily News does not have access to or control these cookies. To find out more about [Doubleclick's privacy policy](http://www.doubleclick.net:8080/privacy_policy) and Doubleclick's use of cookies or to learn how to "opt out" from cookies, visit [Doubleclick](http://www.doubleclick.net/) for additional information.

If you submit information in the course of subscribing to the New York Daily News, the Daily News may use this information for other purposes and may provide this information to third parties.
