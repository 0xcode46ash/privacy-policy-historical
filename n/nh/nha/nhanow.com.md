# Privacy Statement | NHA

Privacy Policy

## **NHA Privacy Policy**

NHA is dedicated to protecting the privacy of all personal information our customers provide.

### **Data Collection**

NHA collects personal information during the login process used to access the testing portion of our website. The demographic information we request is used in aggregate in various analyses of our assessments to ensure that it does not discriminate against any group represented by the demographic information we collect.

NHA does not store credit-card information. NHA is PCI-compliant and regularly scanned by a security company that has been approved by major credit-card companies.

If appropriate, NHA will use your contact information to send relevant messages via email or postal mail. 

### **Information Sharing**

NHA will not sell, rent, trade or lease your personal information to others unless we have requested and have received your permission or are required by law to disclose it.

### **Data Protection**

NHA is committed to ensuring the security of your information. We use industry-standard processes such as encryption to ensure that sensitive data is protected while transmitted to the ATI website.

### **Consent**

By use of the NHA website, you are consenting to the collection and use of personal information as outlined in the policy above. Any changes to the policy will be posted on this page.
