# NIC Corporate Web Site - Contact Us

NIC respects the privacy interests of Web users and has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to preserving the privacy of visitors to this site.

**Information Collection and Use**  
Our site’s information request form requires users to provide contact information (such as name, mailing address, and e-mail address) so we can fulfill requests for information about NIC. This information is used by NIC Inc. for its own internal purposes only and is not sold or otherwise provided to any outside party for any reason.

**Choice/Opt-Out**    
Visitors who join NIC’s e-mail news lists may opt out by sending an e-mail to [invest@nicusa.com](mailto:invest@nicusa.com).

**Security**  
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alternation of information under our control.

**External Sites**  
This site contains links to other Web sites. NIC is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these sites, and we encourage visitors to review the privacy policies of such sites.
