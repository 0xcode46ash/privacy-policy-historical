# Nimble Technology: Privacy Policy - provides XML data integration software for real-time unified views of database, data warehouse, and unstructured sources. Create enterprise information portals, business intelligence and other applications.

PRIVACY POLICY

Effective April 1, 2000 

Overview of Nimble Technology™, Inc. Privacy Practices

At Nimble Technology™, we are committed to maintaining your confidence and trust in us, our products, and our Web site. To that end, we are providing this notice, which explains our practices regarding the information that we collect about you. Unless different terms are specified in a form or contract provided to you by Nimble Technology, the terms of this Privacy Policy apply to Nimble Technology's collection and use of personally-identifying information collected from our Web site. 

Acceptance of this Privacy Policy

By using this Web site you agree to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy. Without limitation of the foregoing, you understand and agree that (1) information about you collected by Nimble Technology may be stored indefinitely, (2) Nimble Technology may store, access and use the information in any jurisdiction, including jurisdictions in which the privacy laws may be different and less protective than those in your home jurisdiction, and (3) Nimble Technology may collect, process and use this information internally for a variety of purposes. Please do not use this website if you do not agree to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy. 

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Nimble Technology reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. If we do so, we will post any changes on our Web site. 

What information does Nimble Technology collect about you and how is it used? When you visit our Web site, we automatically collect some basic information about your computer as part of the software operation of our Web site. The type of information we collect includes: 

  * User name, date and time; 
  * IP address (your computer's unique signature); 
  * Operating system (e.g., Windows 98); 
  * Browser software (e.g., Netscape, Microsoft Internet Explorer); 
  * An Internet Service Provider (e.g., AOL). 



We may use this information to compile statistics for the purpose of determining the number of daily visitors and how those visitors are using the Web site. These statistics may be used to help us understand the needs of our users and develop and continually improve our products and services to better serve those needs. We do not intend to sell or provide personally identifying information about our users to third parties. 

Links to Other Web sites.

Links from our Web site are provided for your convenience only. Nimble Technology is not responsible for the content of any third party Web site or the privacy practices of such third parties. In addition, any link from our Web site to another website does not indicate that Nimble Technology endorses the services or policies of such third party Web site. 

Questions

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your personal information is used, please e-mail us at [privacy@nimble.com](mailto:privacy@nimble.com). You may also write to us at Nimble Technology, Inc., 1938 Fairview Ave. East, Suite 100, Seattle, WA 98102.
