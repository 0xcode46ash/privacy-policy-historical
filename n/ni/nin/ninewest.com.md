# NINE WEST :: SECURITY AND PRIVACY

Nine West respects your privacy and has instituted policies intended to ensure that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. 

All information input by you and transmitted to our site is protected by an encryption using Secure Sockets Layer Technology (SSL). SSL encrypts your order information before it is transmitted to us to avoid the decoding of your information by anyone other than Nine West. 

We collect customer information to communicate with you for marketing and promotional purposes and to enhance your shopping experience. The data includes customer information we gather through processing your purchase orders and sales transactions on our web site and from information you provide to sales associates in our Stores. 

Nine West does not sell, rent or trade to third parties any personal information you provide to us online or at our stores, nor do we give such information to third parties except to third parties with whom we contract to assist us in maintaining and organizing our customer information in order that we may fulfill promotional offers and communicate with you. We do not authorize those third parties to make any other use of your information. However, Nine West may use and/or disclose information about your demographics and use of our web site in a manner that does not reveal your identity. 

For more information on what information we collect, how it is secured, used or shared with third parties, and how you may access, remove or change your information, click on the frequently asked questions below. 

By using our web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Nine West. If our privacy policy and practices change in the future, we will let you know about those changes on this page so that you will always be aware of what information we collect and how we are using and/or disclosing it.

**What information does Nine West collect through its web site?**  
Nine West collects customer information to communicate with you for marketing and promotional purposes and to enhance your shopping experience. When you join our online membership club, Club 9, order an item from our web site, or participate in an online contest or promotion, we collect personal information such as your name, mailing address, e-mail address, phone number and, in the case of purchase orders, your credit card number and expiration date as well. If you register as a member of Club 9, we also collect your user name and password, and certain demographic information, such as your age group. If you create a “Wish List” in connection with your Club 9 membership, we collect information from you on your selection of favorite products featured on our web site, including the style number and the color and size in which you would prefer these items. In addition, we may collect information about your product purchases from our web site and from our stores as well. From time to time, we may gather information from you when you choose to participate in a customer survey and provide information to us in responding to the survey. 

**How does Nine West use this information?**  
Nine West uses the information gathered from a purchase order by you to confirm and process the sales transaction or to contact you with questions or communications about your order, if necessary . We may also use the information collected from your purchase order, or from your response to a customer survey, participation in a contest or promotion, or from your registration as a Club 9 member, to enhance and personalize your shopping experience with Nine West, evaluate our web site design and services to better meet your needs, and to communicate with you by e-mail or postal mail from time to time regarding our products, services, promotions and contests which may be of interest to you in light of the information we have collected from you. If you join Club 9, we use the information you provide to identify you when you visit our web site, and to provide you with e-mail, promotional offers and access to your Wish List if you have created one. If you e-mail your Wish List to someone of your choosing, that person can access the information you have provided on the Wish List to place an order according to your specified preferences to be sent to you. 

**Does Nine West disclose your information to others?**  
Nine West does not sell, rent or trade to others any personal information you provide to us online, nor do we give such information to third parties, except to those third parties with whom we contract to assist us in maintaining and organizing your customer information so that we may fulfill orders, promotional offers and communicate with you. We do not authorize those third parties to make any other use of your information. From time to time, when we offer sweepstakes, contests or promotions, or when another company joins in a promotion or contest with us, participants may be asked to provide certain personal information in order to participate. Information provided by customers who choose to participate may be used by us and by any company offering the contest or promotion to notify winners or to fulfill the promotional offers. You may choose not to participate in these contests and promotions if you do not want to have your information shared with us or the other companies that may be sponsoring those contests or promotions. When you choose to participate in any sweepstakes, contests or promotions, or request to receive e-mail, promotional offers or product updates, you give Nine West permission to use your personal information for marketing and promotional purposes. Nine West may also use and share with other companies aggregated information about your demographics and your use of our web site in a manner that does not reveal your personal identity. At times, your personal demographic and user information may be combined with data from third parties and disclosed in an aggregated form which does not reveal your identity. 

**Does Nine West use “cookies”?**  
“Cookies” are small data files or small bits of information that are stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive when you visit our web site so that we can recognize you. Cookies do not alter your hard drive in any way and cannot be used to access any personal information from your computer. They merely enable us to personalize your shopping experience by helping us keep track of your order as you shop on our web site, maintain information about your use of our web site, and help us recognize you when you visit our web site again. We also use cookies to identify the web site you visited immediately before visiting our web site and we maintain this information through the use of cookies to enable us to evaluate and improve our web site design. Cookies generally can be turned off by changing your browser so that it will not automatically accept cookies. However, if you change your browser to refuse cookies, we cannot recognize you or track your order so as to enable you to make a purchase from our web site or to access your personal information. 

**How can you access, update or delete or change your information?**  
Information about you such as your name, password, e-mail address, mailing address, telephone number, and credit card information is maintained by us when you register as a member of our online club, Club 9. To access this information, click on the Club 9 logo on our web site, sign in with you user name and password, and the click onto “Profile” to update, edit or delete any personal information. Whenever you want to communicate with us to change, update or delete any personal information or to remove you from our e-mail or postal mailing lists, you may send us an e-mail to the e-mail address indicated under our Customer Service feature on our web site. 

**How does Nine West protect a child’s privacy?**  
In keeping with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act, we will never knowingly request personal identification information from any person under the age of 13, without first seeking prior verifiable parental consent. If we become aware that a user of our web site is under the age of 13 and has registered as a member of Club 9 without prior verifiable parental consent, we will remove that child’s personal information from our files. 

**Does Nine West have links in its web site to the web sites of others?**  
There may be links on our web site to the web sites of others. These other web sites are not under our control and may collect personal information from you. Our Privacy and Security Policy does not address the information, privacy and security practices or policies of any other web site. 

**How does Nine West secure my personal information?**  
All information input by you and transmitted to our site when you place an order or provide personal information is protected by an encryption using Secure Sockets Layer Technology (“SSL”). SSL encrypts your order information and personal information before it is transmitted to us to avoid the decoding of your information by anyone other than Nine West.
