# Privacy Statement

NintendoLife.com respects each individuals right to personal privacy. We will collect and use information through our Web site only in the ways disclosed in this statement. This statement applies solely to information collected at NintendoLife.com.

 **Part I. Information Collection**  
NintendoLife.com collects information through our Web site at several points.

We will on occasion use your IP address to help determine your geographic location, this enables us to show more relevant products on our pages. For example, a UK user would see PAL/EU games and a US user would see NTSC/USA games.

We may also collect user profile information, including a users email address, which our users divulge after registering to the website. Your email address will never be shown publicly on the website.

We collect this information through registration forms, your control panel and automatic logging files. The information is collected voluntarily, you are not required to register any information with the website.

We do employ cookies. A cookie is a small text file that our Web server places on a userâ€™s computer hard drive to be a unique identifier. Cookies enable NintendoLife.com to know which user is accessing the website, these are called "Sessions". Our cookies never collect personally identifiable information.

Any personal information collected by NintendoLife.com will not be sold, licenced or shown to any third parties.

 **Part II. Information Usage.**  
The information collected by NintendoLife.com will only be used for NintendoLife.com activities, no third parties will see your data.

Registered users may occasionally receive additional announcements from us about products, services, special deals, and a newsletter. Out of respect for the privacy of our users we present the option to not receive these types of communications.

The information we collect may be used to create customer profiles based on browsing or purchasing history. Once again, this personal information will never be passed on to a third party.

We offer links to other Web sites. Please note: When you click on links to other Web sites, we encourage you to read their privacy policies. Their standards may differ from ours.

Our Web site offers chats, forums and/or message boards. Please be advised that information posted in these venues becomes public knowledge.

If our policy on information collection or uses changes, will advise you by updating this privacy policy at http://www.nintendolife.com/information/privacy.

 **Part III. Access to Information**  
NintendoLife.com maintains the accuracy of our information by periodically cleaning our user profiles and/or contacting users requesting the update of their account. Users may access their own personal information and contact us about inaccuracies they may find. Also users may request the deletion of their information from our database by [email request](http://www.nintendolife.com/help/contact).

 **Part IV. Problem Resolution**  
If problems arise, users may contact NintendoLife.com by email and telephone. We are committed to resolving disputes as soon as possible.

 **Part V. Data Storage and Security**  
NintendoLife.com protects user information with the following security measures: ssh and server firewalls.
