# ncalculators.com's Privacy Policy

  | 

## **Privacy Policy**

We take our user's privacy very seriously. If you have any questions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us.    
By using ncalculators.com’s content, you accept our Terms and Conditions which includes this Privacy Policy. If you do not want to be bound by ncalculators.com’s Privacy Policy, or Terms and Conditions, Please do not use, browse or access our website or any service we provide  


## **Gathering User Information & Sharing **

Ncalculators.com does not share the information it collects, whether general or personal, in any specific way with third parties without visitor consent or as otherwise required by law. 

  
Ncalculators.com may gather personal or general user related information:   


**1\. Personal Information**

Ncalculators.com will never sell, lease or share personal information, including names and email addresses, with any third party. Users or Visitors will not be required to provide personal information for ordinary use of the site. Users or Visitors may be required to provide Scraps to our website need to provide their personal information, such as names and email addresses, in response to specific services or tasks

**  2\. General Information**

Ncalculators.com tracks general information, like many other websites, tied to our visitors to enhance our visitors' experiences by analyzing trends, tracking user movement around the site, administering the site and gathering demographic information. This information that is tracked, also referred to as log files, includes but is not limited to, IP (internet protocol) addresses, browser types, ISPs (Internet Service Providers), exit pages, access times, referring web sites and click activity. This information that is tracked does not identify a visitor by name personally

One way ncalculators.com collects this general information is through cookies, a small text file with a unique identifying string of characters. Cookies help ncalculators.com store information about visitors' preferences, record user specific information about the pages users access and customize web content based on a visitor's browser type or other information that the visitor sends through their browser.

You can disable cookies in your web browser so that cookies are not set without your permission. Note that disabling cookies may limit the features and services available to you. The ncalculators.com cookie sets are not tied to any personal information. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective support websites.
