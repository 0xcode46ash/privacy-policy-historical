# e-recruitment software - e-recruitment software specialists

This policy applies to nga.net pty ltd. Nga.net pty ltd is bound by the National Privacy Principles contained in the Commonwealth Privacy Act. This policy explains the type of information that is collected by nga.net and how such information is used.

What kind of personal information nga.net collects and how it is collected.

In general the personal information nga.net collects includes (but is not limited to) name, address, contact details, title, occupation and where relevant membership of professional associations and preferred income information.

_Automatic collection_ : Cookies: We may use a feature of your browser called a cookie to assign your computer a 'User ID'. Cookies do not contain information by which nga.net can identify you. They identify your computer to our servers. You may configure your browser so that you are notified before a cookie is downloaded or so that your browser does not accept cookies. Nga.net may use information contained in cookies to make assumptions about the user of the computer and to provide users of that computer with focused advertising which nga.net believes may be of interest, based on that information. To disable your cookies, please refer to the help menu within your Internet browser.

How nga.net may use the information you provide.

Nga.net may use the information it collects from you for the primary purpose for which it is collected and for such other secondary purposes that are related to the primary purpose of collection.

Nga.net generally uses personal information to:

1.  | Provide you with products or services you have requested  
---|---  
2.  | Personalise and customise your experiences on the site  
3.  | Help nga.net manage and enhance its services  
4.  | Communicate with you  
5.  | Provide you with ongoing information about opportunities stemming from nga.net in which nga.net believes you may be interested  
6.  | Provide you with the opportunity to receive emails from nga.net  
  
In most cases where nga.net asks you to provide information about yourself, if you do not provide nga.net with that information, nga.net will not be able to provide you with the requested product or service.

Seeking access to the personal information nga.net holds about you.

You have the right to seek access the personal information held about you and to advise nga.net of any inaccuracy. There are some exceptions to this right set out in the Privacy Act.

If you make an access request, nga.net will ask you to verify your identity and specify what information you require. Nga.net may charge a fee to cover the costs of meeting your request.

Need further information?

If you have any privacy concerns please contact us. You can also obtain further information from the Privacy Commissioners web site, [www.privacy.gov.au](http://www.privacy.gov.au/) or by telephone on 1300 363 992.
