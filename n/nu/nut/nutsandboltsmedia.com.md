# Privacy Policy | Nuts and Bolts Media

Your privacy is important to Nuts and Bolts Media. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Nuts and Bolts Media collects, and the ways in which Nuts and Bolts Media uses that personal information.

## Personal information collection

Nuts and Bolts Media may collect and use the following kinds of personal information:

  * information about your use of this website (including anonymous data regarding content viewed, entry and exit links);
  * information that you provide using for the purpose of registering with the website (including your name, email address, IP address, and website URL);
  * information about transactions carried out over this website (including your Paypal email address, name, and IP address);
  * any other information that you send to Nuts and Bolts Media.



## Using personal information

Nuts and Bolts Media may use your personal information to:

  * administer this website;
  * enable your access to and use of the website services;
  * publish information about you (such as a testimonial on the website with your prior consent;
  * send to you products that you purchase;
  * supply to you services that you purchase;
  * send you statements and invoices;
  * collect payments from you; and
  * send you periodic marketing communications.



Where Nuts and Bolts Media discloses your personal information to its agents or sub-contractors for these purposes, the agent or sub-contractor in question will be obligated to use that personal information in accordance with the terms of this privacy statement.

In addition to the disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified elsewhere above, Nuts and Bolts Media may disclose your personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.

## Securing your data

Nuts and Bolts Media will take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information.

Nuts and Bolts Media will store the personal information you provide.

Information relating to electronic transactions entered into via this website will be protected by encryption technology.

## Updating this statement

Nuts and Bolts Media may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this website.

You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.

## Other websites

This website contains links to other websites.

Nuts and Bolts Media is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third party.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or Nuts and Bolts Media’s treatment of your personal information, please write:
