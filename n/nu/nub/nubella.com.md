**Nubella, LLC. Privacy Statement**

Nubella, LLC. ('Nubella', 'So Dish magazine, 'us', 'we' or 'our') is committed to protecting your privacy. This Nubella Privacy Statement applies to all of Nubella's offerings, ranging from our Internet destination to our offline magazine. This Privacy Statement is effective upon acceptance in registration for new registering users, and is otherwise effective on March 30, 2004 for all users. If you have additional questions, you may send email to info@Nubella.com or call (972) 735-0828. 

**Tracking and Use of Cookies**

As you surf around Nubella's website, it is possible that several cookies* will be set on your computer. We use the information from cookies to deliver personalized content, remember browser preferences, ensure accurate survey feedback, and improve our visitors' experiences on the site. No personal information is ever stored in out cookies, even if you have entered your name or email address on other parts of our site. Nubella does not sell information collected by cookies nor use the information for commerce-related purposes. In addition, we will not filter content based on your preferences without permission. Nubella also employs an invisible gif image to count the number of visitors to our site. The intent here is to purely track the number of page loads we get to optimize our server capacity. We do not track any individual data and behavior for any purpose than to provide you a better and easier-to-use website. 

* What is a cookie? A cookie is information that a web site puts on your computer's hard drive so the site can remember your preferences or which pages you visited on that site.

 **Third-Party Advertising**  
We use MaxOnline and other third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements for goods and services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices regarding not having this information used by these companies, please [click here](http://www.maxonline.com/privacy_policy/index.php).

 **Third-Party Cookies**  
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser.

 ** **Acceptance of Nubella's Privacy Policy Terms and Conditions**

By using this site, you signify your agreement to the terms and conditions of this Nubella Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to these terms and conditions, please do not use the site. We reserve the right, at our sole discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for any changes. Your continued use of Nubella's web sites following the posting of any changes to these terms shall mean that you have accepted those changes. If you have any questions or concerns, please e-mail us at [info@nubella.net](mailto:info@nubella.net).
