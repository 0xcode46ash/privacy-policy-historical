# 10 Downing Street - Privacy Policy

|   
---  
|  **PRIVACY STATEMENT** The No.10 website, with the exception of the registration system, does not store or capture personal information, but merely logs the user's IP address (Internet Protocol: standard allowing data to be transmitted between two devices) which is automatically recognised by the webserver. The No.10 site uses cookies for collecting user information from the site and the No.10 site does not collect any information about you except that required for system administration of the website. If you register to receive updates your information will be held on a secure server and the data will not be shared with any organisations outside Government. It will be used only to provide you with e-mail updates on the topics you have requested or postal information if you request that service. This privacy statement only covers the No.10 website at [ http://www.pm.gov.uk ](http://www.pm.gov.uk/) . Other links within this site to other websites are not covered by this privacy statement.
