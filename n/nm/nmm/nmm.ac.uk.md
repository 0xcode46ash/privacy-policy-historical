# Privacy statement : National Maritime Museum

  
This Website contains some personal information. It is our prime concern to ensure that we operate within the confines of the Data Protection Act 1998 and the Freedom of Information Act 2000. 

We have software on the Website that collects statistics from IP data to determine what times of day people access our site, which country they access the Website from, how long they visit the site for, what browser they are using etc. In some areas of our site, a cookie may be placed on your computer. 

The personal information we collect is that from the feedback form e.g. a users name, address, hobbies etc that users have given voluntarily. We also collect personal information when individuals subscribe to a site (e.g. the Journal for Maritime Research), make a request for information or buy products from the online shop. We use this information to supply the services that have been requested and to monitor and control the quality of our Website. 

For a copy of the information we hold on you or if you feel any of the data on this site has caused or is likely to cause damage or distress please contact us at the address below.

It is our policy not to pass this information on to any third party.
