# PRIVACY NOTICE

## Privacy Notice to Wx Plotter / NerdTests Users

Wx Plotter and NerdTests follows these simple privacy guidelines:

**Personal information provided to this website will never be intentionally sold or revealed to third parties.**

All software is free of adware, spyware, trojans, and similar such programs.

I.P. address will be tracked for purpose of security and internal tracking of unique visitors. This practice of logging I.P. addresses is common among many web sites (i.e. Microsoft, Yahoo, AOL, etc...). 

Wx Plotter and The Tester may, from time to time, send out important information regarding this site to you if you have submitted your email address to us. If, upon receiving an email from us, you choose to no longer receive emails from us, an opt out link will be provided which you will need to click in order to remove yourself from our mailing list.

Any further questions regarding this policy should be directed to: [support@nerdtests.com](mailto:support@nerdtests.com?subject=Privacy)

Last Update: April 5, 2005.
