# Nexonia expense reports, timesheets, customer service - Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us. It affects all aspects of our business from how we communicate with you to how we handle your personal information. The following policy is our commitment to your privacy. If you have any questions about our privacy commitment, you can contact us by email at [help@nexonia.com](mailto:help@nexonia.com) .

**Scope**

This policy applies to all activities related to personal information, namely, the collection, use, communication or disclosure, and correction of files containing personal information. 

**Identifying Purposes**

The purposes, or object of the file, for which personal information is collected shall be identified by Nexonia Inc. before or at the time the information is collected and the subsequent use limited to the fulfillment of those purposes or such others as are not incompatible with those purposes and as are specified on each occasion of change of purpose. 

**Limiting Use, Disclosure and Retention**

Personal information shall not be used or disclosed for purposes other than those for which it was collected, except with the consent of the individual concerned or as required by law. 

**Collection Limitation**

The collection of personal information shall be limited to that which is necessary for the purposes identified by Nexonia Inc. and any such data shall be obtained by lawful and fair means and, where appropriate, with the knowledge or consent of the individual concerned. 

**Data Quality**

Personal data should be relevant to the purposes for which it is to be used, and, to the extent necessary for those purposes, should be accurate, complete, and up-to-date. 

**Consent**

The knowledge and consent of the individual is required for the collection, use or disclosure of personal information, except where inappropriate. 

**Safeguards**

Personal information shall be protected by reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification. 

**Customer Access**

Upon appropriate identification and written request, a customer shall be informed of the existence, use and disclosure of his or her personal information and shall be given access to that information. 

**Accuracy of Information**

A customer shall be able to challenge the accuracy and completeness of the information contained in his or her file and have it amended as appropriate.

Nexonia Inc. has committed itself to conducting business in accordance with these Principles in order to ensure that the confidentiality of personal information is protected and maintained. Therefore, all employees of Nexonia Inc. and all agents, consultants and other persons or organizations acting for or on behalf of Nexonia Inc. are required to comply with these principles.
