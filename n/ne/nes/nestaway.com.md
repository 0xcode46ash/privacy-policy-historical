# Privacy Policy | Nestaway

NESTAWAY Technologies Private Limited (hereinafter referred to as “ **We** ”, “ **Us** ”, “ **NESTAWAY** ” or “ **Our** ”) is concerned about the privacy of the users (hereinafter referred to as “ **You** ”, “ **Your** ” or “ **User** ”) of its website (“ **Site** ”) located at [https://www.nestaway.com](https://www.nestaway.com/) and has provided this privacy policy statement (the “ **Privacy Policy** ”) to familiarize You with: the manner in which NESTAWAY collects, uses, stores, discloses and transfers Your Personal Information (as defined below) collected through the Site. 

**1.** **Purpose** __

1.1. NESTAWAY’s Privacy Policy explains: 

(a) What information is collected and why such information is collected from You; 

(b) How such information is used and stored; 

(c) How such information can be accessed and updated by You; 

(d) Why such information is disclosed and transferred to certain third parties. 

1.2. NESTAWAY has tried to keep the Privacy Policy as simple as possible. We value the trust You place in Us.. 

Please read the following statement to learn about Our Personal Information gathering and dissemination practices. 

**2.** **Information We Collect**

2.1. Based on You accessing and / or availing any of the services provided on the Site, we may collect certain Personal Information from You. 

2.2. The “ **Personal Information** ” that We collect from You includes, but is not limited to, Your name, account password, postal address, phone number, email address, contact preferences, ID proof (PAN, Passport, Voter ID etc.), bank account/credit card/debit card details, proof of employment, proof of educational background, and other information that would assist Us in verifying Your identity. __

Personal Information also includes, among others, any information that does not reveal Your specific identity, such as, browser information, information collected through Cookies (defined below), pixel tags and other technologies, demographic information, _etc._

2.3. As is the case with most other websites of similar nature as Our Site, Our Site gathers some information via various methods such as 

2.3.1. automatically when You visit the Site; 

2.3.2. when you provide your Personal Information on the Site, and stores it in log files., 

2.3.3. through the use of cookies 

For example, We may store environmental variables, such as browser type, operating system, speed of the central processing unit (CPU), referring or exit web pages, click patterns and the internet protocol (IP) address of Your computer. 

2.4. Cookies are pieces of information that are stored on a User’s computer when the User accesses a website. The Site may use temporary cookies to help you access some of the special functions within the database driven areas of NESTAWAY’s site. Once you leave Our Site, these cookies expire. Cookies deployed by NESTAWAY do not collect Your Personal Information. 

**3.** **Access to Personal Information**

3.1. You are required to ensure that Your Personal Information is accurate, complete and up-to-date by logging into Your Account. We shall also make reasonable efforts to provide You with the opportunity to request correction of Your Personal Information provided to us if the same is inaccurate; or even to delete the Personal Information. Further, We may decline to process requests which We find to be contrary to the terms laid down under this Privacy Policy, Our Site terms and conditions or any applicable laws, require disproportionate technical effort, jeopardize the privacy of others or are extremely impractical. 

**4.** **Security**

4.1. We work hard to protect Your Personal Information from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction. Pursuant to the same, we: 

(a) Encrypt our services using secure server software, which is the industry standard and among the best software available today for secure transactions. 

**5.** **Information We Share**

5.1. NESTAWAY may disclose or transfer any of Your Personal Information to any group entity, contractor, service provider, government agency within or outside your jurisdiction as may be deemed to be necessary or appropriate including: (a) under applicable law, including laws outside Your country of residence; (b) to comply with legal process; (c) to Auditors (d) to respond to requests from public and government authorities including public and government authorities outside Your country of residence; (e) to enforce Our Terms of Use; (f) to protect Our operations or those of any of Our affiliates; (g) to protect Our rights, privacy, safety or property, and/or that of Our affiliates, You or others; and (h) to allow Us to pursue available remedies or limit the damages that We may sustain. 

5.2. In addition, we may disclose or transfer Your Personal Information with other corporate entities and affiliates within or outside your jurisdiction to: 

(a) Help detect and prevent identity theft, fraud and other potentially illegal acts; 

(b) Correlate related or multiple accounts to prevent abuse of Our services available on the Site; and 

(c) To facilitate joint or co-branded services that You request for where such services are provided by more than one corporate entity. 

5.3. We may disclose or transfer Your Personal Information of Yours within or outside your jurisdiction to: 

(a) To NESTAWAY’s third party providers who provide goods / services such as contact information verification, payment processing, order fulfillment, customer service, website hosting, data analysis, infrastructure provision, IT services, auditing services and other similar services to enable them to provide the relevant services; . 

5.4. You acknowledge that some entities and /or countries where we may transfer your Personal Information may not comply with data protection policies which are as stringent as the laws of your own country or the policies followed by NESTAWAY. You acknowledge that it is adequate that when NESTAWAY transfers Your Personal Information to any other entity within or outside Your country of residence, NESTAWAY will place contractual obligations on the transferee which will oblige the transferee to adhere to the provisions of this Privacy Policy 

**6.** **Posting Your Requirement**

6.1. Posting certain requirements on the Site for certain properties, whether for sale or rent, may require you to provide us with Personal Information such as Your valid ID proof and proof of employment, prior to posting such requirements. You hereby acknowledge and permit NESTAWAY (directly or through its contractors) to contact you to at the contact number as provided by You, for intimating You on events, potential buyers, tenants or properties that other Site users might be interested in. You further consent to Us maintaining in our records your contact details that would be used for customer inquiry purposes. 

**7.** **Change in Privacy Policy**

NESTAWAY may update this Privacy Policy at any time, with or without advance notice. NESTAWAY will revise the "Updated" date at the bottom of this Privacy Policy, upon updating the Privacy Policy. NESTAWAY suggests that the User regularly check this portal to apprise themselves of any updates. In the event there are significant changes in the way NESTAWAY treats Users’ Personal Information, NESTAWAY will display a notice on the Site or send Users an email, as provided for above.. If a User uses the Services after notice of changes have been sent to such User or published on the Site, such User shall be deemed to have provided his/her/its consent to the changed practices.

The information practices of these third parties are not covered by this Privacy Policy. NESTAWAY would not be liable for any loss or damage caused to You as a result of such third parties collecting such Personal Information, or any other types of information from You.

It may also happen that you include links and hyperlinks of third party websites. The listing of such third party external sites (by You or by Us) does not imply endorsement of such site by NESTAWAY. Such third party sites are governed by their own terms and conditions and when you access such third party sites, you will be governed by the terms of such third party sites. We do not make any representations regarding the availability and performance of any of the external sites. We are not responsible for the content, terms of use, privacy policies and practices of such third party websites.
