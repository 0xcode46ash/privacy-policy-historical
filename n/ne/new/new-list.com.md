We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information. We use cookies to personalize this Web site to individual users and to save you time when logging into New-List.com.

This site contains links to other sites. New-List.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. New-List.com recommends you refer to each Network Member's Privacy Statements.

New-List.com uses subscription forms for individuals to request information by subscribing to Email Newsletters based on their categories of interest. We collect visitor's contact information (like your email address) to provide you with the information that you request. We will periodically send you email referring to news and updates to New-List.com.  
  
**Sharing Information**  
New-List.com does not share, sell or rent your email addresses to outside third parties. Confidential information will only be disclosed due to law or court demands. ****  
**Supplementation of Information**  
We do not supplement your information with any outside third parties.  
**Security**  
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. New-List.com employees are made aware of all security policies and are maintaining and protecting your personally identifiable information.  
**Choice / Opt-In**  
We provide opt-in opportunities for all email lists that you choose to receive. We use your email addresses to confirm your subscription and validity of the provided email address. Your reply to this email is necessary for completing your subscription to any email list.  
**Correct / Update**  
Change the email address that you have subscribed with by first unsubscribing with your current email address. Then, resubscribe with your new email address. All email list welcome messages contain the information necessary for subscription and removal from their appropriate list.  
**Unsubscribe**  
We provide unsubscribe / opt-out opportunities for all email lists that you choose to receive.  All email list welcome messages contain the information necessary for removal from the list. Removal from all lists is instant and automated by our servers.
