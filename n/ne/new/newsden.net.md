# Privacy | NewsDen

**We respect your privacy**  
At NewsDen, we are committed to providing a quality online service. It is in our best interest that our users feel safe while they visit our site in order to read news and share their comments.

**US and EU safety regulations**  
NewsDen complies with all the regulations set forth by the European Union and United States Department of Commerce, regarding collection, use and retention of data.

**Information we collect**  
The information we collect from our users are vital stats that we need in order to make our services better. This type of information is typically non-personally identifiable and does not impose any risk/harm to our site visitors.

**Data we share  
**Viewers have the option to register on the site if they wish to become a user and post comments or questions to any of the articles posted. The e-mail data collected from users is strictly compliant to the above mentioned standards and we do not send spam e-mails or share our user data with any other company.  We also do not publish our user’s e-mail IDs on our site aside from the user name/nickname IDs that are displayed when comments are posted.

_If you have any questions regarding NewsDen’s Privacy Policy please contact:_ [enquiries@newsden.net](mailto:enquiries@newsden.net).
