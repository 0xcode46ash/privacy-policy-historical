# KOTV.com - The News On 6

**SAYING IT SIMPLY...**

  * If you send us a comment about news stories or events, your name, if you provide it, may be used in the context of your comment during a newscast.
  * Personally identifiable information submitted in connection with a third party promotion, such as a contest or sweepstakes, may become the property of the third party.
  * KOTV.com will never gather personally identifiable information about a visitor without making an obvious request for it and disclosing our intention to do so. An opportunity to request that your information not be shared with any third parties will always be provided.
  * All personally identifiable information submitted by anyone who identifies himself or herself as under 18 years of age will not be shared or provided to any third party. 



**THE LEGALLY CORRECT VERSION...**

Griffin Television, LLC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the kotv.com website. 

This site contains links to other sites. kotv.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. We request that third parties with whom we have agreements or relationships follow similar privacy policies. We do not, however, have any responsibility for their actions or policies. 

Certain information, such as the domain name of the Internet access provider used by visitors which is anonymous as to individuals is used for our internal purposes to better develop our site and to quantify traffic to the site for our advertisers. 

**Contests**

We run contests on our site in which we may ask visitors for personally identifiable information. Information submitted in connection with a contest or other third party promotion may be shared with the promotion sponsor. kotv.com will never gather personally identifiable information about a visitor without making an obvious request for it and disclosing our intention to do so. An opportunity to request that your information not be shared with any third parties will always be provided. All personally identifiable information submitted by anyone who identifies himself or herself as under 18 years of age will not be shared or provided to any third party. 

**Public forums**

We make chat rooms, forums, and message boards available to our users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. 

Comments sent to kotv.com, and KOTV as e-mail or using a message board or feedback form may be used, along with your name, if you provide it, on our newscasts.

**Subscription Services**

News and marketing information is sent to you from this site by subscription only. Your e-mail address is the only information we require to fulfill the subscription. You can opt out of receiving the information by contacting the web master at the address listed below. You may unsubscribe at any time using the automated "unsubscribe" feature or by contacting the web master at the address listed below. 

**Financial Transactions**

The online store operated by kotv.com uses software, hardware, banking and merchandise fulfillment services contracted through third parties. Information provided with each transaction is used solely for the purpose of completing that transaction.

**Cookies**

We use cookies with our online polls to provide a unique designation for each user and limit poll respones to a single vote for each user. Cookies are also used in our moderated chat rooms to establish a unique identifier for each visitor and facilitate an automated re-entry to the chat room. We do not collect any information about you using cookies.

We use an outside ad company to display some ads on our site. The ads may contain cookies. Cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company and we do not have access to this information. 

**Children's Guidelines**

Children are encouraged not to publicly post or distribute identifiable contact information on this site without prior parental consent. We do not entice children by the prospect of a special game, prize or other activity to divulge more information than is needed to participate in the activity. All personally identifiable information submitted by anyone who identifies himself or herself as under 18 years of age will not be shared or provided to any third party.
