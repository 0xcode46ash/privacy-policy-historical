# netmums in your area?

### **Netmums Privacy Policy**

> **Our Commitment To Privacy**  
>  Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our main homepage, our registration page and on each local site home.  
>  The Information We Collect: This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the netmums website. In order to view the netmums site you are asked to register as a member. The personal information collected at registration is: 

  * **Name**
  * **Postcode**
  * **Email address**



> **Use and storage of your personal information **  
>  When you supply any personal information to netmums (e.g. for registrations, comments, community services or etc) we have legal obligations towards you in the way we deal with that data. We must collect the information fairly, that is, we must explain how we will use it and tell you if we want to pass the information on to anyone else. In general, any information you provide to netmums will only be used within netmums. It will never be supplied to anyone outside netmums without first obtaining your consent, unless we are obliged or permitted by law to disclose it. **We publish a newsletter and occasionally operate on line surveys. We will never sell or share our members e-mail addresses.**
> 
> **Our Commitment To Data Security**  
>  To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. 
> 
> **Our Commitment To Children's Privacy**  
>  Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 16, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 16.
>
