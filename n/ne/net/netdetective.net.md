# Harris

  
We believe you should control your personal information and that only you should decide how, when and with whom that information is shared. Our commitment to putting you in control of your personal information and protecting your privacy is the reason we do not sell, rent or share an consumer's personal information without permission. It's why we provide a simple and easy-to-use service on our Web site that allows customers to control the information they want us to have. It's also why we play a leading industry role in online privacy. We work closely with technology groups and policy organizations to make sure you and our other customers receive the highest levels of privacy assurance and compliance from our products and business operations.
