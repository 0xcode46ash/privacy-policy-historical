# Security information | NetFlorist.co.za

We are committed to providing a secure shopping environment and in order to ensure you can shop at NetFlorist with complete peace of mind, we have outlined our security measures for your information.

We respect the privacy of visitors to our website and outline hereunder what visitor and member information is collected, what the information may be used for and how you can restrict or change the information we have and how we use it.

**Why do we require certain information?**  


  * It allows us to learn about your shopping preferences, thereby tailoring our products and services to suit you.
  * We are able to identify special offers or promotions that would be of value to you.
  * To ensure that we are able to provide our services quickly, efficiently and to the high standards of quality and excellence that we aim for.
  * To introduce new services on the website which would improve your shopping experience or provide additional value to you.
