# Network Advertising Initiative- Privacy Policy

**Our Privacy Policy**  
Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. 

**Browser information collected on the website:**

> Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

**Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:**

> NAI does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site. 

**Use of cookies:**

> We do not use cookies on this site.
