# Netsize - Information_LegalPrivacy

Privacy Policy General:  


Netsize Privacy Policy covers the collection and use of personal information that is collected by Netsize when you visit our website and register to receive our guide. Your privacy is a priority for Netsize, and we go to great lengths to protect it.

Why Netsize collects your personal information:  
Netsize registers your personal information in order to provide you with the guide and newsletters you have explicitly requested and in order to provide you with a complete and personalized range of products and services and to keep you updated of our latest news and offers.

You can manage the type of information you receive from Netsize by:  
\- updating your profile,  
\- unsubscribing from the message you receive,  
\- e-mailing us (please see Contact paragraph).

Who Netsize discloses your information to:  


In order to better come up to your expectations, Netsize may share your personal information with all Netsize entities anywhere in the world.  
Netsize will also disclose your personal data to the distribution partners of the Netsize guide.  
Netsize won't disclose your personal data to any other third party without your consent, unless:  
\- It is required by law,  
\- It is necessary in order to provide you with a service which you have requested,  
\- We suspect that your use of Netsize services is in breach of their terms of use,  
\- To protect the Netsize rights or property, or any Netsize customer,  
\- To lessen a serious threat to a person's health or safety.  
The use of your data by the distribution partners is ruled by their own privacy policy rules. We invite you to contact them directly.
