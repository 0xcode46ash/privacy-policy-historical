## Cookies

"Cookies" are small files that may be placed on your hard disk when you visit a Web page. A Cookie helps us keep track of return visitors and can make your subsequent visits to our Web site simpler by tracking the pages you visit on our site. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a Cookie, which gives you the opportunity not to accept it. However, when you do not accept Cookies, certain pages on some websites may not function properly.
