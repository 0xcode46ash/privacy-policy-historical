# Nanigan's Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

**Information Sharing and Disclosure

Nanigans.com

** does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or companies.

Nanigans.com will respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights ordefend against legal claims; We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Nanigans.com terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.

Nanigans.com does not provide any personal information to the advertiser when you interact with or view a targeted ad. However, by interacting with or viewing an ad you are consenting to the possibility that the advertiser will make the assumption that you meet the targeting criteria used to display the ad.

Nanigans.com will not contact children under age 18, their parents must provide contact information.

Nanigans.com does not ask a child under age 18 for more personal information, as a condition of participation, than is reasonably necessary to participate in a given activities.
