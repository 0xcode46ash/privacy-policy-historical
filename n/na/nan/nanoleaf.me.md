# Nanoleaf Privacy Policy : Nanoleaf.me

Nanoleaf Ltd. and all its subsidiaries and related companies including Doodle Labs Inc. (collectively “we”) take privacy seriously. We believe the personal data you share with us should be used for the purpose of working to your benefit. Our ultimate goal when collecting any data is to improve the lives of our customers and help better the environment through technology. This policy is applicable to our website and our connected lighting products (“Smarter Series”).

## Why share your data?

Sharing your personal data with us allows us to serve you better in the following ways:

  * Using our connected lighting services including our Smarter Series;
  * Using other connected home services that connect with our Smarter Series;
  * Buying products on [shop.nanoleaf.me/](https://nanoleaf.me/shop/);
  * Providing you with sales support;
  * Letting you know about the latest and greatest software updates for our products;
  * Improving our website/app through usage statistics;
  * Knowing what products and services are truly important to you.



## What data do we collect?

Our website collects information to help us understand our customers and create the best experience possible. This data includes things like your IP address, cookie ID, web browser, location, and pages you visit on our website. Our website uses Google Analytics to help us learn about who visits our site and what pages are being looked at.

When purchasing items, we may collect your name, email address, payment information, billing and shipping addresses, and telephone number. We use this information for processing your order.

## Where is your data kept?

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal data. Payment data for any purchases made through our store is encrypted using SSL and stored with AES-256 encryption. Our store is hosted by Shopify. For more insight, you may also want to read Shopify’s Terms of Service  
(<https://www.shopify.com/legal/terms>) or Privacy Statement (<https://www.shopify.com/legal/privacy>).

Our Smarter Series also uses the latest in encryption technology to communicate using HomeKit and ZigBee.

Unless required by law, we will not disclose your personal information to third parties.

## Parental information

While we design our products to be simple enough for use by all family members, we do not specifically target our products toward children under the age of 13. We do not intend to collect personal information on minors under the age of 13 and we encourage parents and guardians to supervise how children are sharing information online.

## Amazon Alexa Voice Service

Personally identifiable information may be collected and stored by Nanoleaf and Amazon when you use Amazon Alexa to operate Nanoleaf products via the Alexa Voice Service. Such information when collected will never be provided by Nanoleaf to third parties without your express written consent, except to Amazon for the purpose of operating the Alexa Voice Service, or where required by law. Using this feature means that you agree to both this policy and to the [Amazon Alexa Terms of Use](https://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=201809740).

## Questions? Contact us.

If you have provided us with your personal data, we will give you reasonable access to it so you can change or delete it. We comply with the requirements of local laws such as those in California and elsewhere. For contact information, check out our website at  
[//nanoleaf.me/contact-us/](https://nanoleaf.me/en/contact-us/) or feel free to email us at [hello@nanoleaf.me](mailto:hello@nanoleaf.me). If you are less inclined to digital communications, you can always mail us at the addresses listed on  
[//nanoleaf.me/contact-us/](https://nanoleaf.me/en/contact-us/).

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

We are always looking to serve our customers better and as a result we anticipate that this policy will need to be updated as we evolve. In serving you better, we reserve the right to change this policy occasionally. We will make reasonable efforts to update you on items relevant to you.

Updated: October 2015.
