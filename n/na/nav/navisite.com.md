# NaviSite, Inc. | About Us | Corporate Brochure

**Privacy Statement for NaviSite, Inc.**

NaviSite has a solid commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices of our corporate website (http://www.navisite.com). 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. A user completing one of our site's forms may be required to provide contact information (like their name and email address). We may use the contact information from a form to send the user information about our company. 

Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the subsequent sections below. Our web site contains links to other sites. http://www.navisite.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

**Forms and Surveys**

Our site uses forms for customers to request information about our products and services. We collect visitor's contact information. We do not disclose any information we receive from our Web site users to third parties, except as it may apply to our partners (see Partners section below). 

Our online forms also asks visitors for contact information. The customer's contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. 

Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings by checking the appropriate box on the form. 

**Partners**

NaviSite does not distribute information acquired via our corporate web site to outside parties.

In some cases where we have an agreement with a Partner, NaviSite may share information submitted by users on our forms, to promote, market, and make referrals for a partner's respective services or product. Such partners have agreements with NaviSite that all lead information forwarded to them from NaviSite will be used for contacting only the user/customer.

Some partners may also have their own privacy policy. Users should become familiar with such a policy before submitting any of their personally identifiable information to NaviSite that may be associated with that partner. 

NaviSite retains no responsibility or liability for privacy policies of those entities.

**Forums, Chat, Message Boards and News Groups**

Information posted publicly at Navisite or anywhere on the Internet is not secure. Please be aware that posting personal information on a message board, chat room or news group becomes public information. You should exercise extreme caution when posting personal information to such an area. NaviSite cannot ensure security or protection in any of these Internet areas. 

**Cookies, Transaction IDs, etc.**

Cookies and Transaction ID's are some examples of the ways to track traffic and other statistics on web sites. NaviSite may use one or more of these methods only to benchmark our web site's performance. 

You may refuse cookies by turning off that feature on your browser. 

**Security**

Information received from online users is sent to a secure location on our server, where it is then stored on a password accessible database. Information received, is only disclosed to select individuals with a "need to know" at NaviSite. 

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about the visitor.
