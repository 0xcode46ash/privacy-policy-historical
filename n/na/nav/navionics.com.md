# Privacy Policy | Navionics

If you are looking for privacy policy concerning our mobile applications, [please click here](http://www.navionics.com/en/privacy-policy-mobile)

This privacy policy explains how we use personal information collected at navionics.com or the Navionics® web store (collectively the “Site). Please read this privacy policy before using the Site or submitting any personal information. By using the Site, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy. These practices may be changed, but any changes will be posted and changes will only apply to activities and information on a going forward, not a retroactive basis. We encourage you to review the privacy policy whenever you visit the Site to make sure that you understand how any personal information you provide will be used.  
Note: the privacy practices set forth in this privacy policy are for the Site only. If you encounter links to external websites from within our Site, please review the relevant privacy policies posted at those websites for their specific uses.

### DATA YOU PROVIDE

#### Personal Data

You provide certain personal information in order to take advantage of the offerings on the Site, such as joining our mailing list or registering and making purchases on our on-line store. We use and store such information in order to manage your store account and your subscriptions to mailing lists, and to fulfill your orders and customer-service requests. You can choose not to provide such information, but this may prevent you from using the Site as desired.

#### Automatic Information

Certain technical data is also automatically collected whenever you visit or log on to the Site. Although some such information could be used to identify you when combined with information held by third parties, we use such information only internally, to assemble and analyze anonymous statistical information on the use of the Site and to ensure that the Site works correctly. Except to prevent or investigate fraudulent or criminal activity, this information is deleted promptly after we use it. Examples of information automatically collected include: the internet protocol (IP) address you use to connect to the Internet; Uniform Resource Identifiers (URI) for resources requested; dates and times of requests; the method used in submitting the request to the server; the size of the file obtained in response; the numeric code indicating the status of the response given by the server (successful, error, etc.); and other parameters relating to the operating system and the user's computer environment.

#### E-Mail Address

When you provide us with your e-mail address, we will email you as necessary to process your orders or respond your request. If you join our mailing list, we may also send you e-mail to inform you about Navionics® cartographic products, including possible updates and new releases. If at any time you no longer want to receive informative e-mail from Navionics, please send an empty e-mail to the following e-mail address [unsubscribe@navionics.it](mailto:unsubscribe@navionics.it).

### COOKIES

A cookie is a small string of characters that is sent to your device when you visit the Site. Cookies enable the Site to recognize your browser and your preferences during and between your visits to the Site. Cookies may store certain information, such as date and time of access, from whichever site or search engine you came from, how many times you have visited the Site, and where you are located. We do not acquire any of your personal data in this way. Cookies are not used to identify you, but only to enhance your experience of the Site and to enable us, or third parties on our behalf, to analyze and enhance all users’ use of the Site. You can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However some of the Site’s functions may not work properly without cookies.

### SECURITY

It is our intent to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you. We use a variety of current technologies and processes for protection of our customer data.

### SHARING OF INFORMATION

We do not share or sell any personally identifying information gathered from our visitors with any agency or company for marketing purposes. We may share such information within the Navionics group of companies (or third-party providers of accounting or administrative services); with governmental agencies or other companies assisting us in prevention or investigation of fraud or other crime; or when required by law or court order.
