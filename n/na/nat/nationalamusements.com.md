# PRIVACY STATEMENT FOR NATIONALAMUSEMENTS.COM

  
National Amusements has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for these websites: nationalamusements.com., showcasecinemas.com and multiplexcinemas.com.

We do not require registration of personal information (i.e., names, address or telephone number) in order to view our site. All personal information is submitted voluntarily to enter contests, make purchases or subscribe to National Amusements Updates. IP addresses and e-mail addresses are used to gather broad demographic information and is not released to third parties, sponsors or advertisers.

Users may voluntarily register to receive periodic updates from National Amusements. Those subscribers agree to receive promotional materials from National Amusements and other entertainment companies. We provide an “opt-out” area for users. 

Contest and order forms require users to voluntarily give us contact information (name, email, and postal address, telephone number) and demographic information (zip code, age or favorite theatre). This information is used to ship prizes, purchases and to inform users of special events such as theatre openings.

In compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act we encourage users to be 13 years of age or older before subscribing to our e-mail service, entering contests or making purchases online.

This site contains links to other sites. Visitors to other sites within National Amusements should refer to the specific privacy policies posted on those sites. National Amusements is not responsible for content of linked sites or their data collection practices.

National Amusements strives to keep information on the Site up to date but assumes no liability or responsibility for errors or omissions in the content of the Site. National Amusements assumes no responsibility and shall not be liable for damages or viruses that may infect your computer equipment or other property due to access, browsing, e-mails, or downloading materials from the Site.

All content and information (including, but not limited to, showtimes, guides, promotional contests, press releases, reviews, photos, images, trademarks) displayed on this site is protected by copyright and other intellectual property laws. You may not modify, publish, transmit, transfer, sell, reproduce, repost or alter any such content.

By using this site, users agree to the conditions described in this policy. National Amusements reserves the right to modify, alter or otherwise update this policy at any time. Visitors are encouraged to review this policy from time to time.
