NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture does not read any of your private online communications.

NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. Use of your Personal Information

NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture collects and uses your personal information to operate the NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture Web site and deliver the services you have requested. NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture and its affiliates. NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.

Use of Cookies

The NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture Web site use "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture pages, or register with NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture site or services, a cookie helps NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture services or Web sites you visit.

NATA - National Aptitude Test in Architecture secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure.
