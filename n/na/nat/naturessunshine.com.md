### © Privacy Statement of Natures Sunshine Products, Inc.  
June 12, 2001

Keeping customer information secure is a top priority for all of us at Nature’s Sunshine Products. We are providing you this privacy notice to help you understand how we handle the personal information about you that we collect. The provisions of this notice will apply to former customers as well as current customers unless we indicate otherwise. _

Visitors Anonymous Access

_ Visitors may access our Web site home page and browse our site without disclosing personal data. _

Web Site Services

_ Our Web site does not enable visitors to communicate with other visitors or to post information to be accessed by others. _

Automatic Collection of Information

_ We do not automatically log personal data nor do we link information automatically logged by other means with personal data about specific individuals. We do not use cookies to store personal data nor do we link non-personal information stored in cookies with personal data about specific individuals. _

Data Collection and Purpose Specification

_ We collect the personal data that visitors may volunteer while using our services. We do not collect information about our visitors from other sources, such as public records or bodies or private organizations. To access the table of personal data collected and purposed for which it is used, please click here, or scroll down to the bottom of this page. We do not collect or use personal data for any purpose other than that indicated in the table. If we wish to use personal data for a new purpose, we offer the means to consent to this new purpose by indicating in a box at the point on the site where personal data is collected. _

Children’s Privacy

_ We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. _

Disclosure

_ We do not disclose personal data to other organizations. _

Confidentiality/Security

_ We give the option of using a secure transmission method to send us the following types of personal data: 

*primary personal data (such as name and contact details)   
*identifiers (such as credit card details and Web site password) 

All our employees and data processors who have access to and are associated with the processing of personal data are obliged to respect confidentiality of our visitors’ personal data. __

_Access to Personal Data_

You can ask us whether we are keeping personal data about you by sending an e-mail to [webmaster@natr.com](mailto:webmaster@natr.com). Upon request and within a reasonable time, we will provide you with a readable copy of the personal data that we keep about you. We may require proof of your identity before providing such data. We allow you to challenge the data that we hold about you and, where appropriate, you may have the data: 

*erased,  
*rectified or amended,  
*completed.

We allow you to challenge our decision to refuse to provide you with a copy of your personal data if such is withheld. __

_Privacy Compliance_

We maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards that comply with federal standards to guard your non-public personal information. We seek to comply with all applicable national laws and global or regional regulatory schemes. We also self-regulate the collection and use of personal data. _
