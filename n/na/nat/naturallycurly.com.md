# Our Privacy Policy on NaturallyCurly.com

NaturallyCurly.com and its advertisers and affiliates are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. NaturallyCurly.com only collects personally identifiable information about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address, when you register to become a member of the site. This information is used only to allow our users to receive information and reviews from NaturallyCurly.com that are relevant to you.
