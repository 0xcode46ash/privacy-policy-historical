# Privacy Policy

**Natural Fertility Info.com  
(KMF Publishing Inc. or “KMFP”)  
Privacy Policy **

**What information does KMFP collect from our users and how do we collect it?**  
KMFP only contacts individuals who specifically request that we do so or in the event that they have signed up to receive our free newsletters or have purchased one of our products. KMFP collects personally identifying information from our users during online registration and online purchasing. Generally, this information includes name and e-mail address for registration to receive our free newsletters and name, postal address, and credit card information when purchasing our products. All of this information is provided to us by you. 

**How do we use this information?**  
This information is used for internal marketing purposes only. KMFP does not sell, exchange, or release your personal information (name, e-mail address, mailing address, credit data, etc.) Your information is not shared with any other third party or company outside of billing and shipping . 

**How do we store your information?**  
Your information is stored at the list server that delivers KMFP newsletters. Your information can only be accessed by those who help manage those lists in order to deliver e-mail to those who would like to receive KMFP’s newsletters. 

All of the newsletters that are sent to you by KMFP include an unsubscribe link in them. You can remove yourself at any time from our newsletters by clicking on the unsubscribe link. You can also contact us at customersupport@natural-fertility-info.com to change your information at any time.

Private Customer information (credit cards, S.S. Numbers, financials, etc.) will not be kept on file for more than 60 days.

**Disclaimer**  
This policy may be changed at any time at KMFP’s discretion. If we should update this policy, we will post the updates to this page on our Website. This policy was revised March 5, 2008. 

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy please direct them to customerservice@natural-fertility-info.com. You may also send a letter to:
