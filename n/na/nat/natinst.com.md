# National Instruments Online Privacy Statement

  
Legal Information

National Instruments Online Privacy Statement

Your right to privacy is a priority to us at National Instruments. We recognize your need for reasonable control of personally identifiable information that you share with National Instruments. Personally identifiable information is any information that is associated with your name. National Instruments uses this information to better understand your needs and provide you with better service. Below are the guidelines we use for protecting the information you provide during a visit to our Web site, [www.ni.com](https://web.archive.org/). 

**Subscription and Registration**   
In a few areas on our Web site, we ask you to provide information that will enable us to enhance your site visit or to follow up with you after your visit. For example, we may request information on our Web site to: 

  * Mail you white papers, application notes, or other literature that you request 
  * Send you evaluation versions of our software on CD format that you request 
  * Place your order electronically for hardware and software products 
  * Verify the shipping status of your order 
  * Answer technical support questions regarding our hardware and software products 
  * E-mail to you our electronic newsletter, _NI News_



Submitting your information is completely optional. 

In each of the instances above, we may ask for your name, e-mail address, and/or other appropriate personal information that we would need to complete your request. In the case of newsletters or mailing lists, you can "unsubscribe" to these mailings at any time. 

The information you provide is kept confidential and will only be used to support your customer relationship with National Instruments. Currently National Instruments may share your information with National Instruments entities located in other countries and/or business partners in order to satisfy your business needs. **Information regarding you or your order and the products you purchase will not be sold to any outside organization.**

**Internet Commerce**   
The National Instruments Online Store is registered with Web site identification authorities so your browser can confirm that you are communicating with the authentic National Instruments Online Store. We also secure our Web site with Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology. We use SSL technology to encrypt all sensitive information transferred between you and our site and render it unreadable to anyone trying to intercept the information. Credit card numbers are only used for processing payment and are not used for other purposes. 

If you do not feel comfortable giving us your payment information online, we do offer a [print and fax](https://web.archive.org/store/payment.htm#printandfax) option in the Online Store that enables you to print the contents of your shopping cart and fax your order to us. **Public Forums**   
Our Web site makes chat rooms, forums, and/or message boards available as a service to you. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.
