# 3 Wishes Lingerie - Privacy Statement & Security Polic

**PRIVACY POLICY  
** Our Pledge To You, Our Customer.

3 Wishes Lingerie respects your Privacy. We do not sell, rent or loan any identifiable information (including e-mail address, phone number, etc.) regarding our customers to anybody. Any information you give us is held with the utmost care and security, and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented.      ****Use of Information

3 Wishes Lingerie will not collect any personal information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals. In order to process your order, 3 Wishes Lingerie must obtain some personal information from each customer. Examples of information you may provide us with: name, postal address, e-mail address and phone number, which will be used to process your order.

**SECURITY POLICY  
**Using your credit card at 3 Wishes Lingerie is 100% safe.

Third Party verification by industry security leader THAWTE. Test it - click the THAWTE Authentic Site seal at the bottom right of this page to verity the security of this site. (Valid Certificate will open in a new window)

When you use your credit card to shop at 3 Wishes Lingerie, rest assured that the transaction is secure. Our servers use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL), an advanced encryption technology that works with Netscape Navigator (versions 2.0 and above), Microsoft Internet Explorer (version 3.0 and above), and AOL (AOL 3.0 and above). 

What all this means is, that this technology safeguards your personal information and guarantees your privacy because you'll be unable to inadvertently place an order through an unsecured connection.

In fact, shopping online at 3 Wishes Lingerie is safer than using your credit card at a restaurant or department store. Give it a try, you'll soon find yourself appreciating the fun, convenience and security of shopping at home or work.

For your information, most websites you visit will put a cookie on your machine. 3 Wishes Lingerie uses this feature of your Internet Web browser called a "cookie" to assign a unique identification to your computer. Cookies are files that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive. 3 Wishes Lingerie creates and then uses these cookie files to track your clicks as you go through the 3 Wishes Lingerie Web site. 

3 Wishes Lingerie only uses cookies to keep track of items you put into your shopping cart and to help us determine whether you came to 3 Wishes Lingerie from a particular Internet link or banner advertisement.

Cookies can also be used to help save and retrieve information used on the 3 Wishes Lingerie site. That way, you won't have to re enter information upon every new visit to the 3 Wishes Lingerie Web site. Cookies cannot be used by 3 Wishes Lingerie to retrieve any personal information about you unless you have previously purchased from 3 Wishes Lingerie. 3 Wishes Lingerie does not and cannot use cookies to retrieve personal information about you from your computer unless such information was knowingly and willingly provided by you.
