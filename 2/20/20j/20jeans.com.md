# 20JEANS™ for Men. Privacy Policy

Your privacy is one of our top priorities. At 20jeans.com, your personal information is only collected in order for us to process orders. 

We will not rent,sell or share any of your information with any other companies or third parties. 

We process all credit card payment transactions through Paypal Website Payments, a highly secured payment processor. Paypal hosts and handles all the payment information and we do not handle any sensitive financial data on our server. Thus, your financial information will never be saved in our system. All paypal transactions are encryted  to ensure privacy of your credit card informtaion. Please visit paypal.com for more details.

After registration, information such as name, address, contact information can be edited at any time, and an account can be closed permanently by contacting our support team - [**customerservice@20jeans.com**](mailto:customerservice@20jeans.com).
