**_PRIVACY POLICY_**

24Wrestling.com takes pride in protecting the privacy of guests that visit our site.

**Third Party Advertising**

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you.

**Third Party Cookies**

In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.

**Information Collection And Use**

At 24Wrestling.com, it is possible to enter contests, vote in polls etc. In these areas, the user often must provide personally identifiable information, including but not limited to: name, e-mail address and information about your interests in and use of various products, programs, and services.

In addition to direct submission of personal information, 24Wrestling.com can collect certain non-personally identifiable information when you visit many of our web pages such as the type of browser you are using (Internet Explorer Netscape, etc) or the name of your Internet service provider (America Online, Comcast, etc).

**How We Use This Information**

The information (such as web browsers, internet service providers, etc) is used to tally our website's hits and traffic. Be assured that the information gathered by 24Wrestling is not shared with any other website, or persons.

**Contact**

If you have any additional questions regarding our privacy document, don't hesitate to contact us. You can e-mail us [here](mailto:webmaster@24wrestling.com).
