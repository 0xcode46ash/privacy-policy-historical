# Optus - About Optus - Privacy

Optus understands that privacy is important to you. Optus is also required to comply with the requirements of the Telecommunications Act 1997, the Privacy Act 1988 and the Privacy Amendment (Private Sector) Act 2000.

Please refer to the following privacy information:

  * Read the [Optus Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/aboutoptus/About+Optus/Legal+%26+Regulatory/Privacy/Privacy+Policy/Privacy+Policy) for information about how Optus handles Personal Information consistent with our privacy obligations. The Policy includes information on why we collect information, how we use it, who we disclose it to and how we take steps to update and secure it.
  * Refer to our [Privacy Statement](https://web.archive.org/aboutoptus/About+Optus/Legal+%26+Regulatory/Privacy/Privacy+Statement/Privacy+Statement) for information on privacy safeguards on our web sites. This includes information as to how we collect, use and disclose information gathered on our web sites, including the use of cookies.
  * Read the [Privacy Collection Statement](https://web.archive.org/aboutoptus/About+Optus/Legal+%26+Regulatory/Privacy/Privacy+Collection+Statement/Privacy+Collection+Statement) for specific information about why we may be collecting Personal Information from you and how we will be using it and disclosing it. This will provide you with the information you may need prior to providing us with your Personal Information. The collection statement also includes information as to how you can Opt Out of marketing contact from Optus and how you can seek access to your Personal Information.
