# Privacy Statement for OperaMail

Opera Software has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. This statement discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for [OperaMail](http://www.operamail.com/). This privacy statement does not include privacy information for the websites [MyOpera](http://www.myopera.com/), [Opera.com](http://www.opera.com/), [Opera.no](http://www.opera.no/), or [OperaSoftware.com](http://www.operasoftware.com/). Please consult the privacy statements of [MyOpera](http://my.opera.com/privacy.html) or [Opera.com, Opera.no, and OperaSoftware.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20020402080803id_/http://www.opera.com/privacy.html) for information regarding their privacy policies.

### Collection of IP Addresses

Opera Software will on occasion collect visitors' IP addresses to help diagnose problems with servers and to administer Web sites. IP addresses will not be used for any purposes other than these.

### Information Collected from Registration Forms

OperaMail's registration form requires visitors to provide contact information (e.g. e-mail addresses or telephone numbers) and demographic information (e.g. age, occupation). Visitors' contact information is used to get in touch with them when necessary.  Demographic data is used to tailor visitors' experience at the site. 

### Links to Other Sites

OperaMail contains links to other sites. Opera Software is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

### Advertisement

Opera Software uses an outside ad company to display ads on OperaMail. These ads may contain cookies that appear to be coming from OperaMail, but in reality the cookies are coming from partner sites. OperaMail does not use cookies. Opera Software does not have access to any information about visitors from cookies received while visiting OperaMail.

### Security

OperaMail has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information provided to Opera Software by its visitors.
