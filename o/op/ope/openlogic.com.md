# OpenLogic | Privacy Policy

OpenLogic is committed to our users’ privacy and this privacy statement covers all information provided to us including information provided on the site [www.openlogic.com](https://web.archive.org/page.php?PHPSESSID=e9ed5a3ceb6977ab96bcf4d770a07c85), a web property of OpenLogic, Inc.

OpenLogic is the sole owner of the information collected including registration information, contact information and billing information. This information is for the sole use of OpenLogic and will not be given away, sold or displayed to others for any reason with the exception of information provided for shipping and billing purposes as discussed below. Though we make every effort to preserve user privacy, we need to disclose personal information required by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on our Web Site.

We use an outside shipping company to ship orders and a credit card processing company to bill users. These companies do not retain, share, store or use personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes. Sensitive information, such as credit card numbers, is encrypted and is protected with the best encryption software in the industry – SSL.

In addition, the Web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that OpenLogic is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information.

As part of our on-going customer education and service, you may receive email updates related to our products and services. You may choose to be removed from any or all mailing lists by sending a request to [support@openlogic.com](mailto:support@openlogic.com) at any time.
