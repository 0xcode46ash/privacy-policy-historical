# Privacy Policy - Open Objects

* * *

Open Objects Software respects and protects the privacy of the individuals that visit Open Objects Software's website and customers of Open Objects Software. Privacy is of great concern to most users of the Internet, and especially email. Open Objects Software are acutely aware of and sensitive to the privacy concerns of visitors to our Web site. Whether you are a customer of our various products or a visitor to our site, we assure you that we do not collect personal information from you unless you provide it to us.

What Information Will Open Objects Software Gather? 

We do not collect any personal information from a visitor to our site unless that visitor explicitly and intentionally provides it. Any voluntary, explicitly collected information will be from forms on this website or through email correspondance with Open Objects Software. 

## What Will Open Objects Software Use My Information For?

Open Objects Software will only use your contact details in correspondance with you directly about Open Objects Software's products. If you are enrolling for a trial of Open Objects Software software, you may be asked to provide certain personal information. Please note, however, that we are asking for this information for the limited purposes of creating your licence, and authenticating your identity in order to issue you with the licence. This information is stored solely for the purpose of tracking these licenses and their expiry. Sales information is stored as required by the tax law of Open Objects Software's operating country, the United Kingdom. Some examples of how we may use your information:

  * **Sending you responses and updates**  
We generally respond to any e-mail questions, requests for product or service information, and other inquiries that we receive. We may also retain this correspondence to improve our products, services, and Web site, and for other disclosed purposes. Frequently we retain contact information so that we can send individuals updates or other important information about our products. 
  * **Facilitating the support, renewal, and purchase of our products**  
We may use the information you submit to contact you to discuss the support, renewal, and purchase of our products and services. 
  * **Validating your identity**  
Some information collected is to verify you are who you say you are for licence generation 
  * **Forming the encrypted contents of a Licence**  
Your name, company name, address and contact information is encrypted into generated licence keys. 
  * **If we are required to disclose by law**  
If we are required by law to disclose certain information to local, state, federal, national or international law enforcement authorities, we will do so (for example, we may disclose the identity of purchasers of certain software products to the U.K Customs and Excise, as required under the terms of our taxation laws).
