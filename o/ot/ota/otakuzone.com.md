# Home - OtakuZone.com

* The following webpage outlines OtakuZone's collection and use of personal information from it's users. OtakuZone values the privacy of our members and users. We will never share any personal information of anybody who logs on to OtakuZone with anyone which includes your e-mail address, name, and location. Upon logging on to OtakuZone such things as your IP address and hostname are logged for statistical and security reasons.

* #### 1\.  Cookies

A cookie is a very small text file placed on your system upon logging on to OtakuZone (and most other websites). This file serves as your identification card and is uniquely yours, and can only be read by the server that gave it to you. Cookies tell us that you have returned to a specific web page on OtakuZone and help us track your preferences and transactional habits. The basic function of cookies is to help our server remember who you are.

* #### 2\.  Children's Privacy

Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important. For that reason, OtakuZone does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13 or knowingly allow such persons to register. If you are under 13, please do not send any information about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, or email address. No one under age 13 is allowed to provide any personal information to or on OtakuZone. In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you find that we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us at Email address.

* #### 3\.  Information We Collect

User-Provided Information:  You provide us information about yourself, such as your name and e-mail address, when you register with the Service.  If you correspond with us by email, we may retain the content of your email messages, your email address and our responses.

* #### 4\.  How We Protect Your Information

OtakuZone uses commercially reasonable physical, managerial, and technical safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of your personal information. We cannot, however, ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to others and you do so at your own risk. Once we receive your transmission of information, OtakuZone makes commercially reasonable efforts to ensure the security of our systems. However, please note that this is not a guarantee that such information may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed by breach of any of our physical, technical, or managerial safeguards.

To protect your privacy and security, we take reasonable steps (such as requesting a unique password) to verify your identity before granting you access to your account. You are responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your unique password and account information, and for controlling access to your email communications, at all times.

* #### 5\.  Third Parties

Third Party websites may collect information from users of OtakuZone. This information will include your IP address, hostname, and information about your system to help us serve you better. These are purely used for statistical reasons, and will not be used in any way other then that. Some programs that may collect this information include: eXTReMe Tracking, Google, Nedstat, and Webalizer.

* #### 6\.  Compromise of Personal Information

In the event that personal information is compromised as a result of a breach of security, OtakuZone will promptly notify those persons whose personal information has been compromised, in accordance with the notification procedures set forth in this Privacy Policy, or as otherwise required by applicable law.

* #### 7\.  Website Policy Changes 

OtakuZone reserves the right to change this, and any other policy located on our website at anytime without notifying our users. If you have any comments, questions, or concerns at [OtakuZone.com](http://www.otakuzone.com/).
