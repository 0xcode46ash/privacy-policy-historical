# Start - Privacy_policy, on Official.fm

### Information gathering and usage

The contents of your Official.fm page is stored and maintained on official.fm servers in order to provide the service. When you sign in to access your personal page from anywhere we ask for information such as your email address. Official.fm will never sell, rent or share your personal information, especially your e-mail addresses, with any third parties for marketing purposes without your express permission.

### Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. Cookies are required to use the Official.fm service. We use permanent cookies to record your session information.

### Client data storage

Although Official.fm owns the data storage, databases and all rights to the Official.fm application, you retain all rights to your data.

### Changes

Official.fm may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified when you signed in or by placing a prominent notice on our site.

### Questions

Any questions about this Privacy Policy should be addressed to [support@official.fm](mailto:support@official.fm).
