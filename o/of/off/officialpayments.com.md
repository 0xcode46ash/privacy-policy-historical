# Official Payments Corp.

Official Payments Corp.® is a licensee of the TRUSTe Privacy Program.  


TRUSTe is an independent, non-profit initiative whose mission is to build users' trust and confidence in the Internet by promoting the principles of disclosure and informed consent. Because this site wants to demonstrate its commitment to your privacy, it has agreed to disclose its information practices and have its privacy practices reviewed and audited for compliance by TRUSTe. When you visit a Web site displaying the TRUSTe mark, you can expect to be notified of what information is gathered/tracked; (2) how the information is used; and (3) with whom the information is shared.  


This privacy statement discloses the privacy practices for http://www.officialpayments.com and is effective March 1, 2001. If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should contact [privacy@officialpayments.com](mailto:privacy@officialpayments.com). If you do not receive acknowledgment of your inquiry or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, you should then contact [TRUSTe](https://web.archive.org/web/20061018234203id_/https%3A//www.officialpayments.com/pc_exit.jsp?JSESSIONID=F27ZZYTZ8RyV1Wk!-553836191!-579660039&url=http://www.truste.org/consumers/watchdog_complaint.php). TRUSTe will then serve as a liaison with the Web site to resolve your concerns.  


Official Payments Corporation is strongly committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who use our Web site. In order to provide our government payment services accurately, securely and efficiently, we must obtain certain personal information about you. This information typically includes some or all of the following: name, address, social security or other personal identification number for a particular jurisdiction, e-mail address, credit or debit card number, expiration date and cardholder name, and jurisdictional payment information (e.g., amount of tax obligation to be paid). In addition, we may request certain optional information about you in order to enhance our services further, to help you use our services more effectively and/or to inform you about additional services that may be of interest to you.  


Disclosure of Information to Third Parties | Except as described in the next sentence, Official Payments will not willfully sell or disclose your customer identifiable information with third parties (without your consent), except as required by law or to respond to a subpoena or other valid governmental order. However, by the very nature of our service, we share selected information about you with the government entity or entities whose obligations you are using our service to pay and with the relevant card companies for payment authorization and processing purposes. We may share aggregated demographic information with our government clients and others for marketing purposes, but this information is not linked to any personal information that can identify you.  


In addition, Official Payments may use customer identifiable information to investigate and help prevent and prosecute unlawful activity or activity which threatens the Web site or our users’ privacy or otherwise violates the terms and conditions for this Web site.  


Security | Official Payments is committed to ensuring the security of your personally identifiable information. We will take reasonable precautions to protect this information from loss, misuse, unauthorized disclosure or alteration, including, putting in place appropriate physical, electronic and administrative procedures to safeguard and secure such information. We use encryption when collecting or transferring sensitive data (e.g., card information). For further information on browser security, check out our [Security Tips](https://web.archive.org/web/20061018234203id_/https%3A//www.officialpayments.com/gb_ht_secu.jsp?JSESSIONID=F27ZZYTZ8RyV1Wk!-553836191!-579660039) in the How To section.  


Use of E-Mail Addresses and Other Contact Information | In the ordinary course of our business, we will use the contact information you provide us to confirm a payment transaction and/or to alert you regarding any difficulty we may experience in attempting to process your payment. With your permission, we may also contact you to remind you of an upcoming payment obligation (e.g., a quarterly tax payment which is due) or to inform you of new or additional services offered by us from which you may benefit. Users are required to contact Official Payments (via email at [privacy@officialpayments.com](mailto:privacy@officialpayments.com)) to opt out of receiving notifications about new or additional services.  


Cookies | A cookie is a piece of data stored on a user’s hard drive which contains information about that user. Web sites often use cookies to help them analyze web traffic, to let them know when a user visits a particular site or to respond to you as an individual when you use a particular web application. The Official Payments Web site does not utilize cookies.  


Log Files | We use IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the Web site, track our users’ movements and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.  


Links to Other Web Sites | Our Web site contains links to other Web sites operated by our government clients and business partners. Please be aware that Official Payments is not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites. We encourage you to be aware as to when you leave our Web site and to read the privacy policy of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information. In addition, the entities operating these third-party sites may use cookies. However, we have no access to or control over these cookies.  


Correcting Personally Identifiable Information | If you have submitted incorrect personally identifiable information through this Web site and would like to revise this information, you may make appropriate revisions within the registration section of the site (if you have previously registered on the site) or may contact our customer service department via e-mail at [customerservice@officialpayments.com](mailto:customerservice@officialpayments.com) or via telephone at 1-800-487-4567 (option #4).  


User Acceptance of Privacy Policy and Changes to Privacy Policy | By using this site, you signify your assent to this privacy policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use our site. If we make any substantial changes in this privacy policy (including with respect to how we use customer identifiable information) we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our Web site thirty (30) days prior to implementing the change (to the extent commercially possible). Users may opt out of the changes in how we use the customer identifiable information that you previously provided to us by e-mailing us at [customerservice@officialpayments.com](mailto:customerservice@officialpayments.com). Official Payments encourages you to periodically review the Privacy Policy. Your continued use of the Official Payments Web site following the posting of changes to these terms will mean that you accept those changes.
