# Pure Chat Privacy Policy

### Use of Information

We collect and use the following information to provide and improve our application and service for you:

#### Account information:

Your name, email address, phone number, payment info, and physical address you provide during sign-up.

#### Chat data:

When you use our application, we store and process chat data and information related to them. This provides access to features like chat transcripts and integration with other applications.

#### Usage data:

We collect information about the devices you use to access our website and services. This includes things like IP addresses, the type of browser and device you use, and identifiers of your devices. Your devices may also transmit location information to us depending on its individual settings.

#### Cookies and other technologies:

We use technologies like cookies to keep track of your preferences and profile information. For example, cookies help us with things like remembering your username for your next visit, understanding how you are interacting with our website, and improving them based on that information. Cookies are also used for statistics that do not use your personal information.

  


### Information Sharing

We may share information as mentioned below but we do not sell it to third-parties.

#### The PureChat Team:

Information may be shared internally to help provide, improve, protect, and promote our service.

#### 3rd Party Applications:

If you choose to integrate PureChat with 3rd party applications, remember that their use of your information will be governed by their privacy policies and terms.

#### Legal:

We may disclose your information to third parties if necessary to comply with the law.

  


### Security

Security is important to us and we work hard to follow generally accepted standards to protect and manage your personal information.

#### Security:

Our team is dedicated to keeping your information secure and testing for vulnerabilities. Chat data is encrypted during transmission using secure socket layer (SSL) technology.

#### Retention:

We retain information you store for as long as we need it to continue providing service.

  


### Changes

We may revise this Privacy Policy at any time and will post the most current version on our website. If we feel any change is significant, we will notify users of our services.

  


### Contact

From time to time, we may contact you with important announcements, updates to our products or promotions related to our services. You may choose to opt out of promotional type of announcements.

If you have questions or concerns about PureChat, our service and your privacy, contact [help@purechat.com](mailto:help@purechat.com)
