# Offeo

**1.1: Introduction  
1.2: Information Use  
1.3: Information Disclosure **

**1.1:** Introduction Offeo treats the information of its user with great confidentiality. Respecting the right that users have to keep their information confidential, only information that is pertinent to and required for our operation is collected and stored. This information is never disclosed to other parties, internally or externally, unless proper legal authority is given or required by the parties involved. 

**1.2:** Information Use Offeo only collects information from its users that is relevant to and required for its operation. As such, this information is only used within Offeo, never disclosed publicly, and only disclosed to parties that have a legal requirement for such information.

**1.3:** Information Disclosure Offeo only discloses its users information upon the requirement of law enforcement from pertinent regions. This information is only disclosed upon proof of such authority, and a distinct court order to access such information.
