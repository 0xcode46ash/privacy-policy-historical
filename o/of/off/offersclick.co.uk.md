# Offers Click - Privacy Policy

The sites listed below are direct marketing trade and domain names of Acxiom Limited, a UK company, which integrates data, services and technology to create and deliver customer and information management solutions for many of the largest, most respected companies in the world.

Included sites:-  
[www.emailinform.co.uk](http://www.emailinform.co.uk/)  
[www.offersclick.co.uk](http://www.offersclick.co.uk/)  
[www.researchopinionpoll.co.uk](http://www.researchopinionpoll.co.uk/)  


We are absolutely committed to protecting your privacy. We will not use or share the information you give without your prior consent, which is given by completing the on-line registration forms on our site so you can enter prize draws and receive special offers. We will use every means at our disposal to ensure that data given to us by you is securely stored.

## Use of your data

Your answers will be protected by the Data Protection Act 1998 and controlled by Acxiom Ltd. By submitting this form you consent to your information being used by Acxiom for developing products & services, marketing, research, & updating and correcting databases. Acxiom may pass this information to other reputable organisations, who may use it for those purposes too. Acxiom and these reputable organizations may contact you by mail, email, telephone, SMS and in other ways, with details of their products and services and for other legitimate business reasons. [Please click here if you would like unsubscribe](http://www.offersclick.co.uk/ereg/SiteRender.aspx?SiteID=530&ThemeID=2).

## Provision of your data to third parties

To protect your privacy regarding the use of your data we work with reputable delivery companies to send these offers and prize draws to you. Acxiom Ltd ensures that all delivery companies we use comply with the Data Protection Act 1998.

## Acxiom Limited Complaints Procedure

A complaint may be registered using any of the contact points listed in this privacy statement.   
We will acknowledge your complaint within 5 working days and respond in full within 20 working days.  
If you are unhappy with our response to your complaint you have the right to escalate the matter to our industry self -regulator the Direct Marketing Commission.

## Cookies

When you visit any of our websites a small text file (known as a 'cookie') will be placed on your computer; the only thing this cookie is used for is to enable us to recognise you when you return to one of our websites and saves you having to fill out the registration form each time you visit. The cookie will not allow a website to learn any personally identifiable information (such as your name and address) that you have not otherwise given to us. The cookie our websites easier and more rewarding for you.

## Security Policy

The importance of security for your personally identifiable information is of utmost concern to us. We have systems in place to assist us in providing secure transmission of your information from your PC to our servers. Unfortunately, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your personal information, Acxiom Limited cannot and does not ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or from our online products or services, and you do so at your own risk. The policy of Acxiom Limited does not extend to anything that is inherent in the operation of the internet, and therefore beyond the control of Acxiom Limited, and is not to be applied in any manner contrary to applicable law or governmental regulation.
