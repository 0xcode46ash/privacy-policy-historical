**Children Under 13**  
You can look at our site, but you cannot make a purchase. You also cannot become a registered user, or use any service that asks for personal information. Our site is not designed for children. We do not wish to collect personal information from children under 13.This policy is designed to protect children. If you register with us and we discover that you are under 13, we will delete your registration. We will send you a message if we do this. If you are under 13 and want to make a purchase, please ask a parent to place the order for you.

**Anti-Diversion Policy**

Product diversion is when someone who is not authorized to sell a product obtains the product illegally and sells it. Or even worse, tampers with the product and then sells it as the original. Professional hair care products are meant to be sold by Cosmetologists licensed by their State Board of Barbering and Cosmetology or licenced by the company to be able to offer their hair care line. Overallbeauty.com has been licenced to be able to offer all the haircare lines we offer.
