# Overton's - Customer Service : Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy Statement**

This is the web site of Overton's.

Our postal address is:  
111 Red Banks Road, Greenville, NC 27835  
We can be reached via e-mail at [service@overtons.com](mailto:service@overtons.com)  
Or you can reach us by telephone at 1-800-334-6541

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, but not the e-mail address (where possible). 

We collect only the domain name, but not the e-mail address of visitors to our web page, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit and information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations.

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web page and not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending email to us at the above address or calling us at the above telephone number or writing to us at the above address and telling us that you do not want to receive e-mail from our company.

If you supply us with your postal address on-line you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending email to us at the above address or calling us at the above telephone number or writing to us at the above address. Also you may receive mailings from other reputable companies. You can, however, have your name put on our do-not-share list by sending email to us at the above address or calling us at the above telephone number or writing to us at the above address.

Please provide us with your exact name and address. We will be sure your name is removed from the list we share with other organizations.

Simply put, a cookie is a small piece of information that web sites use to remember information about users. Overton's only uses transient (and not persistent cookies) on our web site. Transient cookies provide continuity from page to page and within a single visit, these cookies store information such as your shopping cart items.  They do not contain any personally identifying information.  If you are having a problem keeping items in your cart please "turn on cookies" in your browser's preferences.
