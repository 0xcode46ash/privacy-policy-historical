**_Privacy Statement_** Overclockers Club is dedicated to keep your privacy safe and secure. We will never sell or give any of your information to advertisers, sponsors, or anyone else. We log your IP address for security of our systems in the event of an attack. We use cookies on our site for statistics only. Our site contains links to other sites. Overclockers Club nor I am responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.  
We have Anti Spam scripts that help prevent your email from being gotten from the forums by spam bots.  
If you have any questions reguarding this Privacy Statement please contact us.
