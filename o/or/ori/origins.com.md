# Origins | Privacy Policy

When you register at the Origins Website, in accordance with the policy in this statement, we will respect your privacy. Origins will not give your user information to unrelated third parties unless you authorize us. The information you provide us is used to help personalize product information and offers for you. 

Your name and address information is used only for mailing you offers, and will not be given to any unrelated third party without your consent. Your e-mail address is used for sending you information and updates about Origins products, tips and the Website. 

On our **[registration form](https://www.origins.com/reg_options.tmpl)** you can either select whether or not you want to receive e-mail from us. If at any time you wish to be taken off the Origins e-mail list, please send a message to [**origins@mail.0mm.com**](mailto:origins@mail.0mm.com) and type "remove" in the Subject line. If you need to contact a person to be taken off the mailing list, please send mail to [**origins-tech@origins.com**](mailto:origins-tech@origins.com). 

[If you want to send **feedback**](https://web.archive.org/contact/index.html) to us about a privacy issue or anything else, **[click here.](https://web.archive.org/contact/index.html)**
