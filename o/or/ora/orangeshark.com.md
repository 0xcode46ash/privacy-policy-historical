# OrangeShark Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains which information we collect, how we use that information and what choices you have concerning this information. This site may update or modify these Privacy Policies at any time without prior notice to users.  If at any point portions of this Privacy Policy is deemed void or unenforceable, then that portion will be considered removed from this Privacy Policy and shall not affect the validity and enforceability of the rest of the Privacy Policy.    
It is the user’s responsibility to periodically review this Privacy Policy for modifications and or updates.  By continued usage of this site, the user agrees to the most up to Privacy Policy which will be listed on this web page.  If the user is in disagreement with any part  or whole of this site’s Privacy Policy, the user is legally required to stop all usage of this site’s account activities, participating in any activities or services offered by this site.

 **Your information is protected:** **  
** This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and/or alteration of your personal information. If you provide us with information (e.g., credit card numbers, address), we encrypt your transmissions using SSL ("Secure Sockets Layer") technology. In addition, we keep personal information you provide on servers that are protected by firewalls and located in a physically secure facility, accessible only by permitted OrangeShark employees.

 **Information we collect:** **  
** During usage of this site and its software, this site collects several pieces of information from the users.  Please read the instructions listed below for better understanding.  
   
This site uses a unique id per user called “Username”.  This site requires each user to create a username to participate in activities on this site.  This site may: 

_Email:_

This site requires each user to register their primary email address with this site.  The user’s email address will be used to contact the user in regards to activities conducted on the site (such as challenge participation, challenge results, advertiser feedback, deposits, withdrawals, password look up, customer service etc), internal marketing done by this site and regarding third party products.  In addition, if the user interacts with our advertiser/sponsors they may receive marketing and other materials from the advertiser.  
When you participate in our Money tournaments, OrangeShark needs your real name, e-mail address, credit card number, expiration date and billing address. This allows OrangeShark to process tournaments, reward cash prizes and track funds that you withdraw from your OrangeShark account. 

_Username:_

 _  
_

Users are required to create a username to participate in challenges and other games or functions in the site.  This site may use a user’s username for displaying challenge results, internal winner announcements, promotional and marketing uses without any notice to the user to whom the username belongs.

_Demographic Info:_

 _  
_

To play in cash tournaments, or to deposit funds into a user’s account, the user must enter their demographic information.  This site may use the user’s country and state/province during this sites internal promotions and other marketing.

_Surveys:_

 _  
_

This site likes to hear back from its users.  We conduct surveys from time to time to get feedback from users.   Information returned by the user during surveys may be used for our internal promotional and other marketing needs.

_Winnings:_

 _  
_

USA tax players will be required to submit their social security number to file Form 1099.  This is required when a user reaches earnings of over $600 in a calendar year.  Failure to submit this information will result in this site freezing that user’s account.

_Other information:_

 _  
_

To better, user experience we gather additional information.  We collect and record user IP address, internet service provider details, geographical information, login and out time sessions, player activity on the site and softwares used, cash winnings, performance on games and game statistics. 

_Cookies:_

 _  
_

Many of the above activities are conducted by cookies dropped into the user’s temporary internet files.  Cookies are small data files that let this site identify users and their activities on the site.  It can also be able to store login and out sessions of a user.  ****User participating on advertiser sponsored sites or advertisements may get a cookie dropped by the site they are visiting.   Please review that site’s Privacy Policy while on their site. We are not responsible if the user ignores to read third party privacy policy and terms and conditions before accepting an offer from third parties. **  
**  
**Sharing of Information** **  
** Customer privacy and their trust in us is very important.  We never sell or send advertiser/sponsors or 3rd party affiliates any user information unless the user specifically requests us to do so.  Please read all information below.

_Pre-fill:_

 _  
_

When a user is on an advertiser/sponsor site, the site may pre-fill some of the information fields when the user registered.  This is done, so that the user can go through the advertiser process at a much faster rate and avoid any information mistakes.

_Employee Use:_

 _  
_

Our staff (which includes customer support, withdrawal services, deposit services, marketing among others), need at times access to user information.  The sharing of this information is done in a professional and secure manner.  The staff has access to personal information needed to perform their functions, but may not use it for other purposes.

_Protection of this site and others:_

 _  
_

We release account and other personal information when we believe release is appropriate to enforce or apply our Terms and Conditions and other agreements; or protect the rights, property, or safety of this site, our users, or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction. 

_Corporate Transfers:_

 _  
_

As we continue to develop our business, we might sell or buy subsidiaries, or business units, or we may share some or all of your personal information with another business entity should we plan to, merge with, or be acquired by that business entity. In such transactions, customer information generally is one of the transferred business assets but remains subject to the promises made in any pre-existing Privacy Policy (unless, the customer consents otherwise). Also, in the unlikely event that this site or substantially all of its assets are acquired, customer information will of course be one of the transferred assets. You will be notified of such a transfer through a notice posted on our Web site. 

_Legal Compliance:_

 _  
_

We release account and other personal information when we are required to do so in order to comply with the law.
