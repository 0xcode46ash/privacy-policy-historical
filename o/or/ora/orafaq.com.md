# Oracle FAQ: Privacy Policy Statement

[Index](https://web.archive.org/web/20050405031002id_/http%3A//www.orafaq.com/faq2.htm) > [About this FAQ](https://web.archive.org/web/20050405031002id_/http%3A//www.orafaq.com/navfaq.htm) > Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy Statement:

The Oracle FAQ respects the privacy of its visitors and participants. This statement explains our privacy and data-protection practices: 

  * Wherever we collect personal information, we will do so transparently and only with your informed consent

  * We do not sell, trade or rent personal information.

  * We do not take part in spam advertising, nor do we support or help people doing so.

  * We cannot protect private information (like E-mail addresses) left by users on message boards and chat forums. We will, however, remove such information when requested to do so.

  * We assume your consent to this policy if you continue to visit our site.


If you have any additional questions about our privacy policy, please contact our [webmaster](https://web.archive.org/web/20050405031002id_/http%3A//www.orafaq.com/supemail.htm).
