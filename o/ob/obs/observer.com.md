# The New York Observer

**_

Privacy Policy

_**

The New York Observer ("Observer") has adopted the following privacy policies. These policies may be revised from time to time. You should review our privacy policy periodically.

**

Personal Information

**

When you visit our website or register for Observer.com, we may collect personal information from you that may include your name, age, address, telephone number, e-mail address, IP address, credit card information, site usage, survey information and other information. 

We may use information we collect from you individually or aggregated with other userâ€™s information as follows: (1) to process, validate and verify subscriptions and renewals; (2) to send you e-mail notifications about our existing products and services, special offers, or to otherwise contact you; (3) to improve our publications or develop new features, products and services; (4) to provide advertisers and third parties with aggregate information about our users and usage patterns; (5) to customize our publications; and (6) to allow reputable third-parties to email or mail you information about products or services we think would be appropriate or interesting to you (unless you opt out from receiving such information, as set forth below). Persons who supply us with their telephone or fax numbers may receive telephone or fax contact from us. We will not share telephone or fax numbers with other organizations. In addition, Observer employees, agents and contractors must have a legitimate business reason to obtain access to your information.

We may also disclose personal information if we in good faith believe that such disclosure is necessary to (1) comply with the law or legal process; (2) protect and defend our rights and property; (3) protect against misuse or unauthorized use of observer.com; or (4)protect the personal safety, property or rights of our users or the public.

**

Opt-Out

**

When we collect personal information from you, you may elect to "opt out" of having your individually identifiable information shared with other companies or organizations who may send you information about products or services. Opt out requests for mail or email must be submitted in writing to our website administrator at the address set forth below.
