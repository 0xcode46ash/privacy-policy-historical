# Privacy & security | Oxfam India

Oxfam India is committed to protecting your privacy. Any information you supply us with will be held by Oxfam India and used to communicate with you about Oxfam Indiaâ€™s work.

  


##### Resale or disclosure of information

Oxfam India does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected by our website, including email lists, to other organisations.

  


##### Information we collect

Personal Information. "Personal information" is information that is uniquely identifiable to you, such as your name, address, email address, phone number, credit card number and other personal information that is not otherwise publicly available.

Using certain features of our website may require that you provide us with your personal information. For example our website may include the following features:

Contests/Sweepstakes

Surveys

Donation form

Newsletter Sign-Up

Mobile Alert Sign-Up (SMS) 

Contact Us (email)

Public forums (e.g. message boards)

To use these features, we may require you to provide your email address, mobile number, name and/or address and certain demographic information (e.g. gender, age, income level, etc.). When you give us personal information to use the features above, you expressly consent to our collection, storage, use and disclosure of this information as described in this Privacy Policy.

We may use your personal information to communicate with you through website, email, sms and text about:

New campaigns on our website;

New contests; 

News that we think may interest you about us; 

Website layout and analysis

Responses to your questions/comments to us. 

We may use your demographic information for in-house statistics, advertising, promotions and marketing target information. 

If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from marketing and/or service email communications from Oxfam India please email us at friendsofoxfam@oxfamindia.org with your request.

  


##### Privacy of our email lists

Individuals who join our mailing lists via our website or through our campaigning engagements are added to our email database. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our lists to anyone. 

  


##### Google Analytics Advertiser Features

The Google Analytics features you've implemented based on Display Advertising (e.g., Remarketing, Google Display Network Impression Reporting, the DoubleClick Campaign Manager integration, or Google Analytics Demographics and Interest Reporting). Visitors can opt-out of Google Analytics for Display Advertising and customize Google Display Network ads using the [Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)
