# Online School Admissions Privacy Policy

**Our policy is to respect and protect the privacy of any user of Online School Admissions.**

**  
****# 1     Outline  
**Thank you for visiting Online School Admissions and giving a look to our Privacy Policy. Your privacy is imperative to us, and considering the fact our policy simply states that: we will not collect your personally identifiable information while you visit the Online School Admissions unless you choose to provide that information. This Privacy Policy does not portray information collection practices of other sites, including those linked to or from this site.

**  
# 2     How We Use Our Collected Data?  
**Primarily, we use details of a school or individual for enabling purchase transactions online at Online School Admissions or simply to promote a school through a public display of its information and facilities on a school profile page. Otherwise, we use all your information whether its your personal, demographic and profile data to augment your experience at Online School Admissions, enabling us to present content we think you might be interested in.  We may also use this data to improve our site, for promotional purposes, for statistical analysis, and for editorial or feedback purposes for our advertisers. Information collected by us may be added to our databases and used for future e-mails or postal mailings regarding site updates, new products and services, upcoming events, and/or status of orders placed online.

**  
# 3     Children Stay Away!  
**Online School Admissions is for all school going children but it is requested that its membership is not for children under thirteen (13) years of age, and children under such age must not use the web site or its services by themselves and do not submit any personal information about themselves.

**  
# 4      Your Rights..  
**You are provided with several options due to which you can bar receiving communications from us and or our partners when we ask for your information. We also enable you to eliminate your information from our database. And if you do so, it will no longer be used by us for any purpose. Although, enquiry about your purchase or any other technical difficulty is your sole right that will be entertained by us by any means. ****

**  
# 5     Disclosure   
**We will disclose your personal information to third parties only and only with your prior clear permission other than the content displayed on the school profile page or in relation to it.. We keep the right to disclose aggregate demographic and profile data to third parties. Online School Admissions contains links to other sites and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. We may use third-party advertising companies to dish up ads on our behalf. Any information that these third parties gather through action tags and cookies is completely anonymous.

**  
# 6     We Can Contact You  
**We keep the right to contact you regarding your account status and changes to subscriber agreements, privacy policy, or any other policies or agreements relevant to you.

**  
# 7     Indemnification  
**You agree to assure, preserve and hold harmless Online School Admissions.com and its employees, team, agents and any person officially engaged with it, from and against any and loss, expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees and expenses), claims, damages and liabilities linked to or associated with your use of the website and any breach of this Privacy Policy by you.

**  
# 8     Note:  
**We reserve the right to change our privacy policy at any time but by informing you of the existence and location of the new or revised privacy policy or by posting the changes online at Online School Admissions. **
