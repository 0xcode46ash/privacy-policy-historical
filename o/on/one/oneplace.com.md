# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**

* * *

At OnePlace.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about you to send you information about the latest products and services at our site and to learn from you about new products and services that you would like us to provide. Please read on for more details about our privacy policy.

**What information do we collect? How do we use it?**

  * When you sign up to OnePlace.comâ€™s newsletter, we need to know your e-mail address. This allows us to notify you about changes and upgrades to our site. 
  * When you sign up, we may ask for your name although you can choose not to provide it to us. We will use your name in order to personalize communications we have with you. 
  * When you sign up, we may ask for your zip code although you can choose not to provide it to us. We use your zip code in order to better understand who our customers are by matching it with other, commercially-available, information. 
  * We may also use the information we collect to occasionally notify you about important functionality changes to the Web site, new OnePlace.com services, and special offers we think you'll find valuable. If you would rather not receive this information, please send an email to [ notify@OnePlace.com](mailto:notify@oneplace.com). Make sure to list each account or e-mail address you have registered with us.



**How does OnePlace.com protect customer information?**   
All of the customer data we collect is protected against unauthorized access.

**Will OnePlace.com disclose the information it collects to outside parties?**   
OnePlace.com does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. However, OnePlace.com may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, traffic patterns, and related site information to reputable third-party vendors, but these statistics will include no personally identifying information.

**In summary**   
We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. If you never want to receive any announcements or special offers from us, send us an email at notify@OnePlace.com. Remember to list each of the e-mail accounts you have given us.

**Your consent**   
By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by OnePlace.com. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.

**Tell us what you think**   
OnePlace.com welcomes your questions and comments about privacy. Please send e-mail to [ feedback@OnePlace.com](mailto:feedback@oneplace.com).
