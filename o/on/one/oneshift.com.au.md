# Privacy policy - OneShift

This privacy policy describes how the OneShift website, tools, applications, and services handle your personal information.

OneShift collects, uses, and shares personal information to help OneShift work and to keep OneShift safe (details below)

Information posted on OneShift is obviously publicly available.

Our servers may be located in various places both in Australia and overseas. Therefore, if you choose to provide OneShift with personal information, you are consenting to OneShift's collection, use, disclosure of your personal information and to the transfer and storage of that information on our servers.

OneShift collects and stores email addresses, phone numbers, physical contact information, computer sign-on data, statistics on page views, traffic to and from OneShift and ad data (all through cookies ) and other information, including users IP address and standard web log information. 

## Use

OneShift uses personal information to provide, our services resolve disputes, collect fees, and remedy problems.

The personal information is also used to encourage safe trading and enforce our policies, to customize, measure, and improve user experience and our services, content, and advertising and to deliver targeted marketing, service updates and promotional offers to you.

The use of your personal information as described allows OneShift to do other things for users when OneShift collects the information.

## Disclosure

OneShift does not sell or rent the personal information of users to third parties for their marketing purposes without the explicit consent of the user

OneShift may disclose personal information to respond to legal requirements, enforce our policies, respond to claims that a posting or other content offends the rights of others, or protect the rights, property, or safety of others.

OneShift may also share personal information with service providers who help with our business operations and third-party websites and publishers so ads (or the content of the ads ) published on OneShift can also be published on a broader array of venues or information vehicles. 

## Registration and sharing through third-party services

OneShift may offer single sign-on services that allow you to use third party login credentials to sign into OneShift, With your permission, OneShift may also transfer profile information contained in your third-party profile. OneShift may also, for your use, enable you to import information about who you are connected, as well as enable you to share information with those third-party sites (other than listings), at your discretion. If you wish to discontinue you such sharing, you will be able to do so.

## Communications and email tools

You agree to receive marketing communications from OneShift unless you tell OneShift that you prefer not to receive such communications. If you do not wish to receive marketing communications from OneShift, simply indicate your preference by following directions provided with the communication.

If you use the tools in OneShift to send content to a friend, OneShift does not permanently store your friends; addresses or use or disclose them for marketing purposes.

## Access, Modification, and Deletion

You can see, modify or erase most of your personal information by reviewing and modifying your posting or account status page, by signing on to OneShift or by contacting us through the contact Us page.

OneShift deletes personal information when OneShift no longer needs it for the purposes OneShift described earlier.

If you have further questions, you may contact us through the Contact Us Page.

## Security

OneShift uses a variety of tools (encryption, passwords, physical security) to protect your personal information against unauthorized access and disclosure.  OneShift cannot however guarantee unauthorized access and disclosure of your personal information.

## Cookies

OneShift or our service providers may use “cookies” (small files placed on your hard drive ) on certain of our pages to help analyze our web page flow; customize our services, content and advertising; measure promotional effectiveness, and promote trust and safety.

Please note that OneShift offers certain features that are available only through the use of cookies and uses cookies to help identify you and maintain your signed-in status.

You are always free to decline cookies if your browser permits, although doing so may interfere with your use of some of our sites or services.

OneShift sometimes work with service providers who may place cookies on your computer to serve and measure the effectiveness of ads on our or our advertising partners behalf and collect anonymous information about how you use websites owned by OneShift and its affiliates. This information allows them to help OneShift customize our content and advertising. OneShift does not allow these companies to collect any personal information about you using these cookies. For more information about why OneShift use cookies, please contact OneShift through our Contact Us page.
