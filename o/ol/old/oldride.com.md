# Help & Support

### Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us. OldRide will never sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. The following is the manner in which OldRide handles information gathered about your from your visit to this website through our web pages, newsletters, or forms you complete within this site.

By using OldRide, you acknowledge you have read, understood and agree to be bound by the policy provided. If you do not agree to this policy, do not use this site and exit immediately.

OldRide reserves the right to revise this policy at any time without prior notice. If, at any time you do not agree with the Privacy Policy of this site, immediately discontinue using and accessing this site. By using OldRide, you agree to be bound by an such revisions and it is your responsibility to remain up-to-date on the policies of this site. 

**1.) Type of Information Collected  
** You may browse OldRide without entering any personal information and thereby remain anonymous during your visit. However, there are cases where we may ask you for your personal information. When this information is collected, you will know because it will require you to fill out a form requiring your name, address, email address and the such to provide services including subscribing to newsletters, entering contests, and buying or selling in the marketplace. If you do not wish to give your personal information, you can still visit most of the site, buy you may not be able to access certain options, contests, offers and services. If you change your contact information, you may update or delete your account details by signing into your MyOldRide Account at any given time. 

_Personal Information:_ We require personal contact information when you sign up for an OldRide Account and place vehicle and parts listings. The required information is used to effectively and accurately create, maintain, and display OldRide's classified services. Upon signing up, you will be required to provide OldRide with a Username, your name, email address, state and country. Additional personal information such as, primary address and phone number, is required and collected if you choose to become a buyer or seller on OldRide and complete the verification process. Optional demographic information is also collected, but not required.

In addition, OldRide also collects certain information upon each use of the site including: your bidding, buying and selling activities, your IP address, your posts or replies on OldRide Answers, Rusty Rides, and messages sent through OldRide and correspondence you send to OldRide.

**2.) How Information is Collected  
** Members are requested to submit personal information and listing item information using online forms to Register, Place an Ad, and MyOldRide services. Users who are reluctant to pass personal information online can choose to work with a customer service representative or sales representative to utilize member services.

Every computer connected to the Internet is given a domain name and a set of numbers that serve as that computer's "Internet Protocol address" also known as the "IP address." When a visitor requests a page from our Site, our web servers automatically receive that visitor's domain name and IP address. The domain name and IP address reveal nothing personal about you other than the IP address from which you have accessed our Site. We use this information to examine our Site traffic, but do not collect and evaluate this information for individuals. Our web servers do not record the email addresses of the visitors to our Site unless you voluntarily provide that information to us.

**3.) How Information is Used  
** OldRide collects personal information to verify authenticity and ensure safe marketplace commerce, resolve disputes, enforce our policies and identify problems on OldRide.

Information is also collected and recorded to maintain email lists as well as allow users full use of their MyOldRide account. We utilize the emails to keep members informed of the site services, site performance, promotions, and offer information regarding industry related services and products. At NO TIME will your personal information ever be sold to an outside vendor!

**4.) How Information is Disclosed  
** Your OldRide username is displayed throughout the site and is associated with your activity. People visiting OldRide will be able to see your items for sale, bids you have made, feedback and reviews you have received and your community activity. OldRide will never disclose your real name to other users. Notices to other OldRide members about suspicious activity or violation of policy agreements will refer to Usernames only.

If needed, OldRide will share your personal information with other OldRide members to help smooth the progress of a transaction and allow members to contact you. This information (name, address, and phone number) will only be disclosed as a result of a transaction that has taken place on OldRide.com and you are either the buyer or seller.

While OldRide uses all reasonable efforts to safeguard the confidentiality of your information, we have no liability for any breach of security, or disclosure of any information obtained due to errors in transmission, or for any actions of any third parties that otherwise obtain your information.

**5.) The Use of User Information  
** When engaging in a transaction on OldRide, you have the ability to exchange and share personal & financial information with the opposite party. We encourage you to make your own privacy policy known as well as respect the privacy of others. Once you have engaged in a transaction, OldRide cannot guarantee the privacy or security of your information and we strongly encourage you to evaluate the policies and behaviors of the other party before choosing to reveal your information. As a member of OldRide.com, you agree to only use user information for unsolicited and transactions only offered through OldRide.

**6.) Disclosure of Information for Legal Purposes  
** We may disclose personal information to respond to legal requirements, enforce our policies, respond to claims that a listing or other content violates the rights of others, or protect anyone's rights, property, or safety. Such information will be disclosed in accordance with applicable laws and regulationsOldRide will disclose information to Law Enforcement when such information is appropriate to comply with the law or to protect property and others rights, property or safety of OldRide and its users. By signing up for a membership account you authorize OldRide to disclose any information to authorities about you to assist with investigations of fraud, property violations, privacy or other illegal activity or any activity that might expose OldRide to legal liability.

OldRide will not disclose information to outside third parties except under the following circumstances:

  * When required by law or in good-faith belief that such action is necessary.

  * To co-operate with the investigation of purported unlawful activities

  * To comply with a subpoena or other legal process 

  * To protect and defend the rights or property of our customers and visitors to OldRide.

  * To protect and defend the rights or property of OldRide LLC.

  * To identify persons, who may be violating the law or rights of third parties. 




In the event that OldRide LLC, its subsidiaries or affiliates or their assets are sold, merged or otherwise involved in a corporate transaction, your personal information will likely be transferred as part of that transaction. We reserve to right to transfer your information without your consent in such a situation; provided that we will make reasonable efforts to see that your privacy preferences are honored by the transferee. You may Opt Out from allowing us to share your personal information by sending a written request to OldRide LLC. __

**7.) Security of Information**  
To protect the member's account information, OldRide.com assigns each member with a unique username and password of the users choosing. These unique identifiers are disclosed to the user via email immediately upon registering with OldRide.com. Only members have the ability to modify their personal information and modify and/or delete ads as needed. OldRide will terminate any member's account upon their request, however personal information is not removed from our database due to technical limitations.

**8.) Up-to-Date Personal Information  
** With an MyOldRide Account, you are able to update your personal information, including changing your email address, password, phone number, etc. You are responsible for keeping your account information up-to-date and accurate. OldRide will check and store your personal information to prevent fraud and assist with any disputes or investigations.

**9.) Password  
** When you sign up for an OldRide Membership, you become responsible for all actions taken with your username, email address and password. We do not recommend sharing your OldRide account information with any third parties. Once an account is created, you are responsible for all actions taken with this information. If you feel your account has been compromised, please contact OldRide support immediately at 1-360-746-6630 or support@oldride.com.

**10.) Cookies  
** A cookie is a small file of information that is stored on your computer's hard drive. Certain sites in the OldRide.com network utilize cookies in order to provide members and merchants with a more personalized experience by remembering who you are when you revisit the site. Cookies also track visitor activity and traffic patterns among the sites so that we can identify users' interests and behaviors. We use cookies to identify which areas of our site you have visited or used. Cookies can tell us how and when pages in our site are visited and by how many people. With this information, we can learn about visitor's browsing habits when using OldRide to better improve existing user services and develop new products appropriate for our audience.

When visiting a site, you can choose whether to accept cookies or not by changing the settings of your browser. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies, or allow your browser to show you when a cookie is being sent. If you choose not to accept cookies from this site or others, you might notice that some of the features may not work as intended and you may be prevented from making a purchase online.

**11.) Credit Card Transactions  
** OldRide.com uses thawte and secure servers to transmit all online credit card transactions in an encrypted format. Verify you are submitting information over a secure server by locating https:// in your browser's web address bar. Credit card information is stored on a trusted secure off-site server.

**12.) Purchasing and/or Registering**   
If you are purchasing items from OldRide or advertising on the site, you will be required to enter your personal information including name, email address, mailing address, shipping address, phone number, etc. This allows us to process your transaction and to notify you of your purchase status. Your information is transmitted through our secure servers and will never be shared or sold to any third parties except as otherwise provided in this Privacy Policy. For all credit card transmissions, we use the industry standard SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encrypted servers. SSL codes the information transferred between you and the server rendering it unreadable to anyone trying to intercept your information. You will know when you are on a secure web site when the front of the web address has [https://www.oldride](https://web.archive.org/%22https://www.oldride//%22) instead of the normal http://www.oldride.

**13.) Third Party Links and Advertising  
** To add to the member's experience, OldRide provides links to a variety of Classic Car Community websites. OldRide has no control over its advertisers' privacy policies, and encourages users to review their policies before submitting personal information to these sites.

**14.) Links to Other Sites on OldRide  
** As a resource to our visitors, OldRide provides links to other web sites. We try to carefully choose web sites that meet our standards, are useful and relevant to the Classic Car Community. However, the presence on links to non-OldRide web sites does not mean that OldRide endorsed or accepts any responsibility for the content or use of such sites. OldRide has no control over such sites and will not be responsible for the privacy practices of such web sites. OldRide does not share your personal information with the sites which OldRide contains links for. 

**15.) Children and Minors Online Privacy  
** OldRide complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) which stipulates that Web site operators cannot knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age without verifiable parental permission. Children under the age of 18 are not eligible to use any of our products or services contained in this site. Therefore, no information should be submitted to this site by visitors under 18 years of age. Visitors under 18 are not allowed to register for contests, newsletters, clubs or activities. You must be 18 years or older to purchase or sell products on this site. If you are a minor, you can use our products and services only in conjunction with your parent or guardian.

**The information provided is subject to change** Memberships are subject to termination if they do not comply with OldRide's Privacy Policy. By using this site, you agree to the collection and use of information provided by OldRide LLC as specified in this Privacy Policy and you agreed to be bound by the provisions set forth in this policy. If changes are made to the Privacy Policy, they will be displayed on this page so you are aware of the information we collect. Other areas on this site may include additional or different provisions relating to collection and disclosure of personal information. 

For assistance or support please call 1-360-746-6630 or email [support@oldride.com](mailto:support@oldride.com)

**SPECIAL NOTE:** Please keep in mind that whenever you voluntarily disclose personal information online in a forum accessible by others, for example through classified listings, discussion lists, newsgroup, or elsewhere, that information can be collected and used by others. If you post your personal information online that is accessible to the public (and not a form provided by OldRide) you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties. Please be careful whenever you choose to post your information online.
