# Privacy Policy | Outrageous Mastery

> We are committed to the protection of the rights of visitors to this site. Any data which is collected from you throughout this site will be used solely for the purpose intended, and will not be given, lent, or sold to any third party. 
> 
> None of the information gathered about our users will be published on this site or anywhere else. 
> 
> ## The secure payment system
> 
> We use the services of Authorize.net to handle secure ordering on this website. Authorize.net has extensive anti-fraud measures in place to protect your personal and credit-card details.
> 
> The information collected by Authorize.net is for the purposes of completing the transaction and will not be used by them for any other purpose. For further information on the Authorize.com privacy policy please visit them at [www.Authorize.net](http://authorize.net/)
> 
> ## Your rights regarding your personal data
> 
> We are based in the USA and our website is bound by American law.
> 
> ## Any Questions?
> 
> If you have any further concerns regarding your privacy please contact our [customer support team](http://outrageousmasters.com/support/)
> 
> "We will never Sell, Barter, or Rent your email address to any unauthorized third party."
