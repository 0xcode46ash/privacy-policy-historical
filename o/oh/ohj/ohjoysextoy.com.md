# Bucko – A Comical Murder-Mystery - Privacy Policy

[Bucko.com](http://www.buckocomic.com/), [Darcomic.com](http://www.darcomic.com/) and [Ohjoysextoy.com](http://www.ohjoysextoy.com/) and all its related content, does not collect personal information without your knowledge or consent. Nor do we sell, rent or give personal information onto other party’s.

These websites both utilize advertisement, which in turn use advertising cookies. We do not control these common place cookies. These cookies contain information relevant to the access of our sites for advertisement purposes. You may opt out of this third-party vendor’s use of cookies by visiting the [Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp). Currently Bucko.com only utilizes [Google Adsence](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html), while Darcomic.org uses [Project Wonderful](http://www.projectwonderful.com/), [Google Adsence](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html) and [ContextWeb’s ADSDAQ](http://www.contextweb.com/). You may also be able to opt out of these cookies at those respective links.

We also count and follow the visitors activity while surfing our sites. This is for analytical purposes only, used only to see which comics where the most popular and as such create better comics for you. This data is of a superficial nature, and we will never be able to identify or contact you with it.

All these websites are intended for mature audiences.
