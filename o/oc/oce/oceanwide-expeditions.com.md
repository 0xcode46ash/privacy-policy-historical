# Privacy Policy

### Privacy Policy

We make great efforts to protect the information you give us. Any information, including personal information is stored and used carefully and will not be shared with unauthorised third parties. 

### Personal Information

We collect personal details, such as your name, address and contact details such as your email address only when you use our website to send us an enquiry, order a news letter or other documentations or any other related promotions. We will use that information to respond to your message or to send you additional information concerning your request and future travel programs / offers. Please bear in mind that email correspondence is not necessarily secure.

In order to maintain and improve our internet (security) management, including (customer service oriented) improvements, we automatically collect data from you, including your internet domain, the internet pages (or – if applicable – the website links you use) visit, your browser type and operating hardware system, date and time and length of your visit. We may also use “cookies”, information collected shall never be shared with third parties. In case you so not wish to have cookies stored on your computer, you can switch them off in your browser.
