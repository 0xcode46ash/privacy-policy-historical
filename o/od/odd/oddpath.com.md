# Oddpath - Privacy policy

The Oddpath Privacy Policy statement discloses the privacy practices employed by Oddpath. 

## Collection and use of user information

Oddpath collects personal information (name and e-mail) from its users. Except as required by (a) federal, state or international law, (b) subpoena or court order, (c) the user's written consent or other legit,genuine and ethical emergencies. Oddpath will not sell, share, or rent the user information to any thirdparty. 

## Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of information stored on the user's computer. The cookie allows the Oddpath system to identify a user as a registered member and save some preferences that has nothing to do with a user's personal information. A small piece of encrypted data is stored in the cookie when a user logs in and this helps Oddpath identify the user later and automatically sign him/her in without the need of any manual action from the user. This cookie, in no way exposes any sensitive information.   
Oddpath's business partners make use of cookies on the website for various purposes such as serving/tracking advertisements, logging and measuring the effectiveness of advertisements etc. Oddpath, has control over the use of these cookies and is not responsible for the data collected through these cookies and how/what for the data collected by these cookies are used. 

## Correspondences

Oddpath may contact its users from time to time to notify of important changes on the system, technical updates etc. Oddpath may also store correspondences from its users for tracking purposes and these won't be shared/sold or rented to any thirdparty except as requred by (a) federal, state or international law, (b) subpoena or court order, (c) the user's written consent or other legit,genuine and ethical emergencies. 

## Security

Oddpath employs the best methods to protect its user information and data. Sensitive user information is enrypted and securely stored in a database. None of this information is stored on shared servers, but dedicated servers in a secure environment. 

## Choice/Opt-out

Oddpath allows its users to opt-out of receiving communication(emails) or the service at any time. If the user decides to opt-out of the service, his/her personal infromation and files/data will be removed from Oddpath unless required by law or court order. 

## Changes

Oddpath reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any time. The notice of changes will be either put up on the website or sent via email to the users, unless emergency requires otherwise. The user is asked to check periodically for updates and changes. If you have any questions or feedback regarding this policy, please contact support@Oddpath. 

## No guarantees or warranties

Due to technical, legal and practical limitations, Oddpath cannot warrant and does not guarantee, the non-disclosure of the user's private/personal information and data in ways not described in this privacy policy. Oddpath may disclose information to government authorities or thirdparties under certain circumstances, or third parties may unlawfully intercept or access private data or communications. As the user, you authorize Oddpath to disclose any information about you to law enforcement or other government officials/bodies as Oddpath, in its sole discretion, believes necessary, prudent or appropriate, in connection with an investigation of fraud, intellectual property infringement, or other activity that is illegal or may expose Oddpath to legal liability.
