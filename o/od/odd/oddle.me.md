# Oddle | Privacy

Last modified: 13 Sep 2013

## Information we collect

We will only collect your details for setting up an account with Oddle. You are free to modify any information listed on your Oddle dashboard. 

## How we use your information

Your information helps us to create an account for you in order to use our service. This can beis done manually by our staff or yourself. 

## Information we share

We will not reveal to anyone who are not an employee of Oddle. We will only share your information to the privillaged employee(s) who served you directly or indirectly   
However, we will not sell your information to any organizations. 

## Changes

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice (including, for certain services, email notification of privacy policy changes).
