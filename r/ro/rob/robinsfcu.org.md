# Robins Federal Credit Union Website Privacy Policy

Robins Federal Credit Union Website Privacy Policy
Our Online Privacy Practices
Keeping financial and personal information secure is one of our most important responsibilities. Robins
Federal Credit Union is committed to protecting the information of members and visitors who use our
website. As part of this commitment to our members, we have state-of-the-art security measures in
place to ensure online account access is safe and secure. We value your trust and handle all personal
information with care.
As with information we collect through other means, we use the information you provide online to
respond to your needs, service your accounts, and provide you information about other services.
Secure Transmissions
To ensure information remains confidential, Robins Federal Credit Union uses encryption technology
such as Secure Socket Layer (SSL) to protect information you enter and submit from our website.
When using Robins Federal Credit Union's online banking, you are accessing your account information
using a secured connection and the transfer of your account data from our host system to your
personal computer is in encrypted.
Email Security
Regular email is not secure. You should never provide information that is sensitive or confidential such
as your social security number, account number, or PIN through unsecured email.
Requests to change your personal information including online banking password, email address, and
mail address must be made by contacting our call center or visiting your local branch.
Cookies
A cookie is a small piece of information that a website stores directly on the computer you are using.
Cookies may contain a variety of information from a simple count of how often you visit a website to
information which allows customization of a website for your use. Robins Federal Credit Union uses
cookies to gather data about the usage of our website and to ensure that you have access to your
account information. Anytime a cookie is used, personal information is encrypted for our use only and
is protected from third party access (a cookie cannot be read by a website other than the one that
sets the cookie).
Third Party Links
Robins Federal Credit Union provides links to other organizations’ websites. These links are provided
as a service to our members. Robins Federal Credit Union is not responsible for the content of these
other organizations’ websites. We do not guarantee or endorse any of the content in these links.
Robins Federal Credit Union does not represent either the third party or the member if the two enter
into a transaction.
The privacy and security policies on these links may differ from those published and practiced by Robins Federal Credit Union.
We encourage you to read the privacy policies of all websites you visit, especially before providing any personal non-public
information.
