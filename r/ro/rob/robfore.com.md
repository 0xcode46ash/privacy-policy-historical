# Listech Inc Privacy Statement

**Privacy Statement**

Listech, Inc. has created this statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.

The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for Low-Intrest-Rate.com:

  * User information is automatically logged.
  * Low-Intrest-Rate uses its users’ IP address to help diagnose problems with its server and to administer its Web site.
  * User IP address is also used for broad demographic information gathering.



**Advertisers**  
Low-Intrest-Rate uses an outside ad company to display ads on its site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information. Some customer data is shared with the advertising companies.

 **Registration Forms**  
Our site's registration form requires users to provide their contact information (i.e. - name, postal address), unique identifiers (i.e. \- social security number), and demographic information (i.e. - zip code, age, income level). Contact information from the registration forms are used to send our customers information about our company. The customer contact information may also be used in the event Low-Intrest-Rate needs to get in touch with the customer.

 **Demographic and Profile Data**  
This information is shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis. We use this data to enhance our users’ visits to our site by providing content that will most likely suit their needs. We attempt to display content according to our users’ preferences.

 **Surveys**  
Our online surveys ask visitors for contact information (i.e. - email address). Low-Intrest-Rate reserves the right to use all contact information obtained from the surveys to get in touch with the customer when necessary.

 **Contests**  
We run contests on our site in which we ask visitors for contact information (i.e. - email address). Contact information gathered from the contests is used to send our customers information about our company and other promotional material provided by our partners. The contact information may also be used to get in touch with the customer when necessary. This contact information may be shared with other companies who may want to contact our customers.

 **External Links**  
This site contains links to other sites. Low-Intrest-Rate.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

 **Special Relationships**  
Low-Intrest-Rate.com has special relationships with a number of companies.

 **Contacting the Web Site**  
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, feel free to email us at admin @low-intrest-rate.com.
