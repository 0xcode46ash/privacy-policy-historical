Rooja is committed to protecting all the information you share with us at this site (the "Site"). At Rooja, it is necessary for us to collect certain personal information for you to have an enjoyable shopping experience. We respect and protect the information you share with us as set forth in this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy applies to the Site. This Privacy Policy does not apply to other web sites to which we link. In this Privacy Policy, “we”, “us”, “our” and other similar references mean Rooja and “you” and “your” and other similar references mean a specific user of the site.  You agree that your use of the Site signifies your assent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Site.

## Users Information and Privacy

At Rooja, We follow strict procedures in order to protect the confidentiality, security, and integrity of data stored on our systems. The website uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to encrypt and securely transmit your credit card number and other personal information.  Only those employees who need access to your information in order to perform their duties are allowed such access. Any employee who violates our privacy and/or security policies is subject to disciplinary action, including possible termination and civil and/or criminal prosecution. 

Rooja may be required to disclose information in order to comply with a subpoena, court order, administrative or governmental order, or any other requirement of law, or when Rooja, in its sole discretion, deems it necessary in order to protect our and our affiliates’ rights or the rights of others, to prevent harm to persons or property, to fight fraud and credit risk, or to enforce or apply our Website terms of use. Personally identifiable information may be transferred as an asset in connection with a merger or sale (including any transfers made as part of an insolvency or bankruptcy proceeding) involving all or part of our business or as part of a corporate reorganization, stock sale, or other change in control.

## Providing your information

You register (Log in) with your email address and a password. This gives you access to your account every time you visit the website (The ‘Site’). In order to make a purchase you provide us with your name, phone number, email, billing address, shipping address and credit card number. This information is shared with select personnel and third parties in order to complete transactions, respond to your requests, delivery of services and products and to notify you about special promotions, offers and updates that might be of interest to you. You may opt-out of receiving e-mails from Rooja at anytime by clicking unsubscribe in any of Rooja’s e-mails.

## Cookies

This website will assign cookies (pieces of data) to your web browser in order to track your navigational patterns as you browse the website. These cookies are essential in order to use the website’s important functions. Cookies also help us personalize the website experience and tailor it according to your needs. Please not that the cookies we use do not store personally identifiable information about you or your finances. Viewers of the website may choose to disable cookies at any time through their browsers. If you block or refuse to accept cookies, or delete cookies between visits to the site, you may not be able to access or use certain features or functions of the Rooja website.

## Information Analysis

 We may use your information to track consumer trends through server statistics. This information allows Rooja to pinpoint high traffic areas to ensure we have enough capacity to properly serve all of our members. This collected information includes: the IP address of the user accessing our Website (i.e. the unique I.D. number of the user's computer), the type of browser (Internet Explorer, Firefox, etc.) and the operating system (Windows, Mac OS, etc.), the Website the user last visited before linking to our Website, how long the user accessed our Website in any given session, and the date and time of access. The information that is collected in this manner does NOT provide us with any personally identifiable information about our users. It helps us track “where” our users are coming from. This information may be shared with advertisers, sponsors, investors, strategic partners, and others in order to help grow our business. 

## Site Affiliates

Rooja’s subsidiaries, other affiliated companies and carefully selected vendors and business partners are collectively called “Site Affiliates”. We may share information with our site affiliates in order for them to perform services like Affinity, frequent-user, and reward programs, marketing services, website hosting and designing and other business operations (including providing goods and services to our users, as necessary to complete transactions you request). Some of these third parties may require access to your information in order to make the services provided through our Website work. All these companies are contract bound and obligated to use the Information disclosed to them only for the purpose of providing the particular services in connection with our Website. 

External Links: There might be links to other websites from our website. We are not responsible for how these companies/websites collect, protect, use and disclose the personal information that you provide them. If you choose to access a third party Site linked to our Site, you do so at your own risk and subject to any terms of service or privacy policy (if any) associated with such third party Sites.

## Privacy Policy Modifications

You acknowledge that this Privacy Policy is part of the Terms of Use. Your website visit and any dispute over privacy is subject to this policy and our Terms of Use, including limitations on damages. Rooja may edit the privacy policy and the terms of use at any time. Such changes will be posted through e-mail and website notifications so that you are always aware of our policy. Unless stated otherwise, our current Privacy Policy applies to all information that we have about you and your account. However, we will never materially change our policies and practices to make them less protective of customer information collected in the past without the consent of affected customers.
