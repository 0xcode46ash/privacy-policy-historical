# Royalty Yachts Mediterranean Charters Exclusive Mega Yachts

Information provided to us, including client and passenger names, is held in the highest regard and in strictest confidentiality.

We will secure your booking with the dates and your yacht of choice and then the yacht owner or managing agent will establish the contract between them and the client.

We do not use, nor do we condone the use of, unsolicited bulk email. 

Our site uses forms for information requests. Contact information from all forms (including name, address, and email address) is used to process and deliver yacht charter reservation orders and mail information about our company to our customers ONLY. We respect your privacy, and we value your business. At no time will we share any of this information with any other person or company. 

Royalty Yachts does not, and will never, sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including contact form information, brochure requests, or email details. 

Royalty Yachts is committed to taking also reasonable measures to protect your personal information from theft, misuse, and alteration.
