From time to time this website may also include links to other websites. Such links are provided to give access to further information and do not indicate that we endorse the website to which we are linking and neither do we have any responsibility for the content of the linked website.

_Royal Society Publishing_ receives personal information through this website. You may, for example, be asked for personal information if you want to take advantage of web-based activities such as online ordering, subscription to our journals or registering for specific services, eg TOC alerting.

We do not store financial details such as credit or debit card numbers on this website.

In any case, where you provide personal information, we will only use it to deliver the services you have requested. We may need to pass your personal information to anyone to whom we transfer our rights and duties and we may also disclose your information to any applicable regulatory body or if the law requires or allows us to.

_Royal Society Publishing_ will occasionally inform you of new services and products related to those you have ordered or registered for. You have the right to opt out of receiving this information and if you choose to do so, please contact [Debbie Vaughan.](mailto:debbie.vaughan@royalsociety.org)

We may use cookies to collect personal information from people who register for updates on our website. This personal information is used for this purpose and no other. Cookies are used in the publishing transactions to keep track of the contents of your shopping cart once you have selected an item, to store delivery addresses if the address book is used and to store your details if you select the 'Remember me' option.

The website may contain links that make it easy for users to visit other websites. If the user uses the links to leave the website and visit a website operated by a third party, we do not have any control over that website. Accordingly, we cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which users have provided whilst visiting such websites. Users should exercise caution and look at the privacy statement applicable to the website in question.

By using the website and not advising us to the contrary, you agree to us using the data in the way set out in these terms and conditions.

Under the Data Protection Act 1998, you may request details of personal information that we hold about you. A fee will be payable. If you want to make a request, you should write to [Human Resources](mailto:humanresources@royalsociety.org?subject=Personal%20information%20request).

You should also write to [Human Resources](mailto:humanresources@royalsociety.org?subject=Information%20you%20hold%20is%20incorrect%2Fincomplete)Â if you believe that any information we hold about you is incorrect or incomplete. Any information that is found to be incorrect or incomplete will be corrected as soon as possible.

We strive to protect the security of information which you provide to us, and will use up-to-date measures to provide such security.

####  Summary of terms

  
**Browser**

Used to locate and display web pages via a software application. This site is optimized for use with all [main browsers.](http://highwire.stanford.edu/help/browser.dtl)

**IP address**

If you are connected to the internet you will have an IP address. It may look something like this: 198.184.98.9

**Web server**

Delivers (serves up) web pages to your computer.

**Cookies**

A cookie is a small amount of data which is stored on your computer and which is revealed to us when you use this website. We use cookies only to make it easier to identify you when you are using the website. We do not use cookies to collect additional information about you. You can configure your web browser not to accept cookies, although this may hinder the functionality of the website.
