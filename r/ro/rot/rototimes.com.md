Privacy Statement for www.rototimes.com website (the "Website"): 

**General Information About the Website**  
The Roto Times and RG Ventures, LLC d/b/a CDM Sports (collectively referred to herein as the "Company") have created this privacy statement to make you aware of our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: www.rototimes.com. The Roto Times is a division of CDM Sports. We encourage you to carefully review and familiarize yourself with all of the terms of this privacy statement. In addition, you should check the Company's privacy statement from time-to-time for changes. The Company reserves the right to make changes from time-to-time for its own business purposes and as may be necessary to comply with applicable laws. 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Website. We use cookies to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our Website and for other purposes. 

User information is collected at the Website for basic site registration; game, contest or league participation; and the purchase of premium content or services. 

Our Website's registration forms (used to obtain a user ID or to sign up for premium services) require users to give us contact information (eg. name, email address and zip code), and optional demographic information (eg. age). We also track products and/or services that users may purchase. We use customer contact information from the registration form(s) to send the user information about our company and promotional material from some of our sponsors, marketing partners and advertisers. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the user, when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings, either from the Company or its sponsors, marketing partners or advertisers. Please see the choice/opt-out section below. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our Website and shared with third parties. This information is shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis. 

**Security**  
Our Website has reasonable security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Specially hardened operating systems that have been "patched" against known security risks are provided. Internal security staff monitor the operating systems for protection against threats. All hardware systems are further protected by state-of-the-art firewall technology. No technology is 100% secure, but the Company makes reasonable efforts to maintain the security of its user database. 

**Public Forums, Chat Rooms, Message Boards**  
Our Website makes chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users from time-to-time. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding whether to disclose your personal information. Please keep in mind also that the Company reserves the right to remove any posts or information which the Company, in its sole discretion, deems is profane, offensive, slanderous, libelous or may violate applicable laws. The Company reserves the right to discontinue user access to chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups at any time in Company's sole discretion. Please keep the messages clean! 

**Other Websites**  
Our Website may, from time-to-time, contain links to websites owned or operated by third parties. If you access those third party websites, please be advised that those third parties may have their own privacy policies which may differ substantially from the Company's privacy policies. The Company is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of those third parties.
