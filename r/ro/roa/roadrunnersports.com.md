# Security & Privacy Information

**Your Credit Card is Safe**  


Your credit card number **cannot** be read by a third party while in transit and will remain **private and completely protected.** We promise.

Road Runner Sports uses the most advanced and highest level of online security available today (128-bit encyption). Our Commerce Server uses Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) which encrypts or codes all the data you enter so you can relax and feel completely secure with your order. 

**Still Not Comfortable Ordering Online?**  


No problem. We accept orders by phone, fax and mail as well as online. Just choose the method that's right for you. Click here to view [alternate ordering methods](https://web.archive.org/web/19981203105658id_/http%3A//www.roadrunnersports.com/roadrunner/question.asp#phsales).

**Road Runner Sports' Privacy Policy?**  


We know how important it is to protect the information provided by visitors to our site or obtained through cookies.

Our Web server recognizes the domain name and e-mail address (when provided) of each visitor to our site.

We collect the domain name and e-mail address (when provided), aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, user-specific information on what pages consumers access or visit, and information volunteered by the consumer.

This information is used to improve the content of our Web page, to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual consumer, to notify consumers about updates to our Web site, and to contact consumers for marketing purposes.

If you do not wish to receive e-mail regarding new products, special sales, or other marketing promotions, please e-mail us at [ listening@roadrunnersports.com](mailto:listening@roadrunnersports.com)

**Disclosure Statement-Use of Cookies**

The Road Runner Sports Web site utilizes cookies to provide our site visitors the most enjoyable experience possible. We do not collect personal information without visitor knowledge, nor do we re-sell or distribute any site visitor data.

If you have any questions about our policy, please send us an e-mail at:  [listening @roadrunnersports.com](mailto:listening@roadrunnersports.com)
