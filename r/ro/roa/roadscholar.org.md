# Elderhostel : Privacy Web Statement

This is the website of Elderhostel, Inc. It is our goal to provide the best online experience possible for our visitors. At the same time we are very sensitive to the privacy concerns of our visitors and participants alike. 

We save email addresses of those who send them to us but will only contact individuals directly with their permission. We also collect volunteered information (such as survey information and/or registration applications). We believe that collecting this information allows us to improve the content of our website. 

We use cookies to help with our program registration process, storing information from screen to screen. When you are conducting an account transaction or registering to receive the Elderhostel ENews Bulletin or paper catalogs, we always use industry-standard encryption technologies to protect your privacy. 

Elderhostel does not use any information collected from visitors for anything other than Elderhostel business and Elderhostel does not give, sell or trade any information we collect on our site with any non-Elderhostel entity. If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the following addresses or phone number.
