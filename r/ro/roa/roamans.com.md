# Plus Size Clothing - Fashion for Plus Size women at Roaman's

## Last updated on: 03-01-08

[REDCATS USA’s](http://www.roamans.com/) Family of Brands offers customer-friendly policies designed to afford you a most secure and safe shopping experience. Click PRIVACY POLICY, YOUR CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS, PRIVACY STATEMENT and [TERMS OF USE](http://www.roamans.com/roamans/help/security-privacy.asp?nav=2&static_link_id=5) for the details or simply scroll down.

Redcats USA is committed to protecting your privacy.  The purpose of this privacy policy is to assist you in understanding the type of personal information we may collect from you, how such information may be used and the choices that are available to you concerning the use of your information and your ability to correct or modify the information you have provided us.  This policy is only applicable to customers in the United States.  

We reserve the right to modify or update this policy from time to time.  The effective date of this policy, as noted above, is the date that this policy was last updated.  We encourage you to periodically review this policy for any changes.  By visiting this web site you agree to be bound to the terms and conditions of our Privacy Policy.  If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use or access our web site.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, you may email us at [PrivacyOfficer@RedcatsUSA.com](mailto:privacyofficer@redcatsusa.com).  We welcome your feedback and suggestions to help us improve our policy and practices.

## Your California Privacy Rights>

The following only applies to California residents. 

Redcats USA may from time to time share your personal information with third parties, including our affiliates, licensees and marketing partners, for their direct marketing purposes. If you do not want us to share your personal information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes, please contact each of the appropriate brand or brands by phone or email and request that your information not be shared. 

For more information on how your personal information is collected and used, please read our PRIVACY STATEMENT below.
