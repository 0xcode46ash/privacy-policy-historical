# Privacy and Fair Lending | RoadLoans.com

**How does Santander Consumer USA collect my personal information?** | We collect your personal information, for example, when you. 

  * Apply for financing or pay your bills
  * Give us your contact information or provide employment information
  * Give us your income information



We also collect your personal information from others, such as credit bureaus, affiliates, or other companies. For online services, we or our service providers may use cookies, which are pieces of data that help web sites recognize return visitors and to personalize and enrich your online visits. For more details, please refer to our [Online Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/About%20Us/How%20We%20Protect%20Your%20Privacy%20Online).
