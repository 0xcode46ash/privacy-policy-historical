**Information Collected**

The website collects two forms of information: (1) Information you choose to submit, such as your email address when you fill out a form, and (2) information your browser sends automatically when you visit the website.

Third Party services include Google Analytics and Siteimprove. The data collected by these services is anonymous and is not connected to any personal information. We use these services to serve ads to our audiences, understand how people use the website, and how to make the user experience better. The services do not share information with each other.

This website contains links to other websites. Please be aware that we do not control or claim any responsibility for the privacy practices or content of other websites.
