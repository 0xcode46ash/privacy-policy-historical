# Privacy Policy - Rose Publishing

At Rose-Publishing.com, we realize that our customers are concerned about their personal privacy. Will my address be rented out? Will my email be given to spammers? The following paragraphs address these and most other issues regarding our company's Privacy Policy. However, if you do have a question that is not dealt with on this page, please feel free to email, write, or call us and we will be happy to assist you in finding the information you need.

**Rose Publishing will not rent or lend your personally identifiable information such as email address, phone number, or physical address to anyone outside of Rose Publishing or any entity owning Rose Publishing.** In the event that Rose Publishing is sold or transferred, personal information may remain the property of Rose Publishing or be assigned to or accessed by the purchasing entity. 

We collect the domain name of each visitor to Rose-Publishing.com and the email addresses of those who communicate with us via email. We keep track of which of our Web pages consumersâ€™ access or visit while on our site, and then use that information for internal review to improve the content of the site and therefore improve the individual customer's experience at Rose-Publishing.com. This information is collected for internal purposes and is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

If Rose-Publishing.com has your email address on file, you may occasionally receive emails with special offers or notices about our site that might interest you. We hope you enjoy receiving these emails. However, if you would prefer not to receive any e-mail of this nature from us, you can unsubscribe from our email list in any mailing. You may also let us know by sending an e-mail to us at inforose-publishing.com with the word "unsubscribe" included in the subject field. You can also be removed from our e-mail list by calling us toll free at 1-800-358-3111, or writing us at Hendrickson Rose Publishers, P.O. Box 3473, Peabody, MA 01961-3473. (Please specify in your correspondence that you do not want to receive future email notices from Rose Publishing, Inc.)

If you supply us with your postal address online, you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending an email to "inforose-publishing.com", by calling us at 1-800-358-3111, or by writing to us at Hendrickson Rose Publishers, P.O. Box 3473, Peabody, MA 01961-3473. Please specify in your correspondence that you do not wish to receive mailings from Rose.
