# R O C K . c o m : Privacy Policy

|  Rock.com assures your privacy in order to make your online experience with us as safe and pleasant as possible. Our policy is not to divulge or sell your name, address, email address, credit card information or personal information to any third party without your written consent. The only exception is in the unlikely event that we are required by law or legal process to provide information about a customer. In the future it is possible that we may decide to share such information with a trustworthy third party or partner. You may inform us that you do not wish to have information pertaining to your purchases with us to be shared by clicking [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20000510215837id_/http%3A//www.rock.com/scripts/privacy2.cfm). 

When you create an account with Rock.com you are given the option to receive information via email pertaining to special opportunities or promotions. If at any time you would prefer not receive such information, you can contact us and we will remove your address from our mailing lists. 

To further market research, we occasionally may share non-personal, non-individual statistics or demographic information in aggregate form with our marketing partners, advertisers or other third parties. This is limited to general information such as total traffic, total sales, etc. and in no way includes any individual or specific purchases. 

Rock.com uses several sophisticated encryption technologies and other methods to insure that your online transaction is secure and that your personal information is safe. You may choose whether or not you would like us to keep your credit card information in our database, and all of your account information is encrypted and password protected. If you have chosen to store your credit card information in your account with us, we have a number of safeguards in place to insure that your credit card and purchasing information cannot be accessed or obtained from the outside by anyone but you. If you do not feel comfortable making a purchase online, you can also submit your information via telephone, fax or e-mail. 

If you have any questions please feel free to contact us.
