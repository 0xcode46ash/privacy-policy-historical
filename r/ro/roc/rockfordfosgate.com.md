# PrivacyPolicy

### Privacy Policy

Welcome to Rockford Fosgate's website. You may visit this site anonymously, without revealing any personal information. At times, Rockford Fosgate will request personal information to provide you with a specific product, service, or information. This information may include your name, shipping address, billing address, telephone numbers, credit card information and e-mail address. Personal information is only collected when necessary to provide a product, service, or information. By providing such information, you are agreeing to allow Rockford Fosgate to use the information provided to complete the transaction. Rockford Fosgate will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent.

Rockford Fosgate collects personal information to provide you with a personalized on-line experience. Your email address will be used ONLY when you consent to use it to receive scheduled email newsletters, special discounted offers, and other similar email information. Rockford Fosgate values its customers' privacy and will not disclose any customer-specific information to third parties without their consent. Any additional demographic information collected from customers will be collected on an optional and voluntary basis.

As discussed below, Rockford Fosgate cannot be held responsible for personal information voluntarily disclosed by you to third parties. Please safeguard your personal information.

(Rockford Fosgate reserves the right to update this policy at any time by posting a notice on its website regarding the existence and location of the new or revised privacy policy.)
