# Rocketmiles - Privacy Policy

The policy was last modified on December 12, 2012

## What This Privacy Policy Covers

Rocket Travel knows that you care about how information about you is used and shared, and wants you to feel safe online. For that reason, we want to let you know how we treat personal information that we collect from users of both the full and mobile versions of our web site (together, the “Rocket Travel Site”). By providing personal information through the Rocket Travel Site you explicitly agree to our collection and use of all such information as described in this privacy policy.

## What information we gather and use

When you use the Rocket Travel Site or the booking or other services available through the Rocket Travel Site, you may provide certain personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, telephone number, mailing address, credit card number or similar information. We may also collect personally identifiable information automatically when you use the Rocket Travel Site. Finally, we might receive information about your from third party sources. Some of the personally identifiable information we receive from you is needed to set up your account, submit content, and transmit your reservation information to a travel partner. If you access the Rocket Travel Site from a mobile device, we may also gather geographic location information from you, to better customize your experience using the Rocket Travel Site.

When you use the Rocket Travel Site, we also receive and store certain non-personally identifiable information, such as your operating system type, browser type, IP address, mobile device type, etc. We may also anonymize your personally identifiable information.

## How we use and share information

We will not sell your personally identifiable information to anyone without your permission. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our business, processing credit card transactions or otherwise conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce Rocket Travel Site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses. We share and use the information we collect in the following ways:

  * _To personalize your experience:_ Your information helps us to better respond to your individual needs.
  * _To improve our website:_ We continually strive to improve our offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you.
  * _To improve customer service:_ Your information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs.
  * _To process transactions:_ Specifically, your information will be shared with hotels and other travel partners to process transactions made on the Rocket Travel Site, and to third party providers of credit card transaction processing .
  * _To send periodic emails:_ The email address you provide for order processing will be used to send you information and updates pertaining to your order. We may also email you about specials and promotions offered through our Site.



If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future promotional emails from us, you may do so by following the detailed unsubscribe instructions included at the bottom of each promotional email that you receive from us.

## How we protect your information

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or enter, submit, or access your personal information. We offer the use of a secure server. All supplied sensitive/credit information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our payment gateway providers database only to be accessible by those authorized with special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential. After a transaction, your credit card number(s) will not be stored on our servers, unless your reservation type requires us to forward such information to the hotel provider. Any credit card numbers are processed and stored in a PCI compliant manner.

## Use of Cookies

Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computers hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the sites or service providers systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. We use cookies to help us remember and process the items in your shopping cart and understand and save your preferences for future visits. If you prefer, you can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies via your browser settings. Like most websites, if you turn your cookies off, some of our services may not function properly.

## Updating and Correcting Information

We encourage you to promptly update your personally identifiable information if it changes. You may contact us to have the information associated with your account updated, deleted or removed. However, because we keep track of past transactions, you cannot delete information associated with your past transactions on the Rocket Travel Site, nor can we delete information included in our back-up files.

## California Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

Because we value your privacy we have taken the necessary precautions to be in compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. We therefore will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent. As part of the California Online Privacy Protection Act, all users of the Rocket Travel Site may make any changes to their information at anytime by contacting us at concierge@rocketmiles.com or Rocket Travel, Inc., 22 Fells Road, Winchester, MA 01890. Please be sure to include your full name, address, email address and specifically what type of information you do not want to receive. Please allow up to 6 to 8 weeks to process your request from the date of receipt.

## Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We do not collect any information from anyone under 18 years of age. The Rocket Travel Site, and all products and services offered on the Rocket Travel Site are all directed to people who are at least 18 years old or older. If you under the age of 18, you should not become a registered user or attempt to purchase any products or services on the Rocket Travel Site.

## Links to Other Sites

The Rocket Travel Site may contain links to other sites (including those embedded in advertisements) over which we have no control. Privacy policies on such linked sites may be different from our privacy policy. We encourage you to review the privacy policy at any site you visit.

## Terms and Conditions

## Changes to our Privacy Policy

The use of your information is subject to the privacy policy in effect at the time of use. We may amend this privacy policy from time to time. We post the current effective date of this policy at the top of the policy. Changes to the policy will be posted in the revised privacy policy with a new effective date on the Rocket Travel Site. We encourage you to review our privacy policy periodically, because by visiting the Rocket Travel Site, you agree to accept any such changes.
