**PBC** respects the privacy of every individual who visits our site, and we recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of personal information collected. Our Privacy Policy has been carefully crafted to take your privacy concerns into consideration. We may modify these policies from time to time to better address your concerns or to make other additions, deletions or changes that **PBC** determines to be appropriate.   
   
** What do we collect?**  
At **PBC** , we don't collect personal information about you (name, address, e-mail, and so on) unless you provide it to us voluntarily and knowingly. Whenever we do collect personal information from you, we will explicitly notify you that we are doing so and will provide you an opportunity to restrict how this information is to be used by indicating your preferences on the form we use to collect information. If you do not want your personal information collected, please do not submit it to us. The types of information we collect are: first and last name, company name, title, email address, company URL, mailing address, telephone number and other relevant information about why you are contacting **PBC**. Please note, however, that all users are able to visit our website without telling us who they are or revealing any personal information about themselves.  
   
**How do we use it?**  
**PBC** use the information to provide you with news about services and offerings that we believe may be of interest to you, based on the information provided to us. If you sign up for an electronic newsletter or e-mail list, each message will include instructions on how to "unsubscribe" from that list.  
** Questions or Concerns?**  
If you have any questions, comments or concerns regarding **PBC** 's privacy policy and/or practices, please contact us
