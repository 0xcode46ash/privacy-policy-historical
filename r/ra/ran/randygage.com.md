# Privacy Policy of Gage Direct, Inc.

**Gage Direct Privacy Policies** Gage Direct, Inc. respects your privacy and recognizes your right to protection of all personal information you share with us, including your name, address, e-mail address, telephone number, etc.You are welcome to visit gagedirect.com and randygage.com anonymously. Personal information is not collected. Your Internet address (IP address: the address of your computer) is logged to help us learn which parts of the web site you visit, and how long you spend on the individual pages. Gage Direct does not link your IP address to any personally identifiable information. Your browser automatically informs us of its name and version number; again, however, no personal information is gathered. **Limited Information Gathering**  
Like most commercial web sites, Gage Direct may use cookies so we can better serve you with tailored information when you return. Cookies are small text identifiers sent to your computer to facilitate subsequent visits. Most browsers let you define preferences to notify you when you are sent cookies and let you decide whether to accept or reject them. Virtually all browsers let you remove cookies from your computer. Cookies cannot retrieve information from your computer, spread viruses, or capture your e-mail address. Gage Direct uses the information collected with cookies to track site use and improve our service to you and other visitors. Gage Direct does not use cookies to gather personal information about you.

"Gage Direct, Inc. uses WebTrends Live to analyze traffic to this web site. WebTrends Live does not create individual profiles for visitors. Unlike some tracking services WebTrends Live does not have a database of individual profiles for each visitor. WebTrends Live only collects aggregate data. For more information about WebTrends Live privacy policy, [_please click here_."](http://www.webtrendslive.com/privacy_policy.htm) **Choice**  
Gage Direct may collect personal information voluntarily supplied by you when you order products, enter contests, complete surveys, and the like. Gage Direct clearly informs you in advance, and explains how this personal information is to be used. You have a right not to provide this information. If you choose not to provide such information, you are still welcome to visit NetworkMarketingTimes.com, but may be unable to participate in certain options, offers and services.  
  
**Uses of personal information**  
If you supply Gage Direct your postal address, you may receive periodic mailings to inform you of new products, services, events, etc. If you prefer not to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending an e-mail message to [_wecare@gagedirect.com_](mailto:wecare@gagedirect.com)  
  
**Security**   
Gage Direct takes all appropriate and reasonable steps to protect all information you share with us from unauthorized access or disclosure. We understand that you might be worried about ordering online. This site uses the latest methods of security to protect your sensitive customer information. All credit card numbers and other sensitive information will be transfered using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption. We offer many different methods of payment for online orders. You can choose to pay during checkout by using any of the following methods: 

  * Credit Card Online
  * Online Check using our PaybyCheck system
  * Gift Credit Card Online (seperate shipping and billing addresses)
  * Credit Card using FAX
  * Check/MO through Postal Mail
  * Credit Card through Postal Mail



If you still feel uneasy about ordering online, [_please check out our other ordering methods_](https://web.archive.org/web/20010330204811id_/http%3A//www.randygage.com/html/howtoorder.html)[.](https://web.archive.org/web/20010330204811id_/http%3A//www.randygage.com/html/howtoorder.html)
