# Ranking Today Privacy

For each visitor to our web site, our web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address. We collect the e-mail addresses of those who subscribe to our directory and our online optimization tools.

Information: Information collected from members is used for subscription notification and to send service update information only. It is not shared with other organizations for any purposes. 

Cookies: We do use cookies on our web site to store login details to make future visits quicker and easier for you.

Security: We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site. If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us via our online contact form.
