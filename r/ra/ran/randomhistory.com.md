# Privacy Policy - RandomHistory.com

At RandomHistory.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Therefore, all personally identifiable information we collect from you will be used solely to improve your experience with the site. We will never sell or rent any of your personal information to a third-party company. By using this site, you signify your agreement to the terms and conditions of the following privacy policy. For your protection, we promise to strictly adhere to the following terms.

 **Information We Collect**

All personally identifiable information we collect from you is supplied on a voluntary basis. When you choose to contact us, we will keep your email address and other personally identifiable information contained within the email completely private. We will use your email address only to reply to your questions, comments, or concerns.

 **Use of Cookies**

As you browse through RandomHistory.com, it is possible that cookies will be set on your computer. A cookie is a small text file that does not personally identify users, although it does identify a user's computer. A cookie will not harm your computer and does not contain viruses. We will use information gathered from these cookies only to monitor site traffic.

 **Links to Third-Party Websites**

Throughout RandomHistory.com, you may come across links to relevant, third-party websites. When you click on these links, you will be subject to the privacy policies of these sites. While we support the protection of privacy on the web, we cannot be responsible for the content, actions, or policies of any third-party websites. We encourage you to carefully examine the privacy policy of each site you visit and be aware of where you are at all times.

 **Changes to the Privacy Policy**

We reserve the right to amend our privacy policy at any time. Any changes we make to the privacy policy will be posted on this page.

 **Questions or Comments**

If you have any questions or comments regarding this privacy policy, please email them to: [contact@randomhistory.com](mailto:contact@randomhistory.com).
