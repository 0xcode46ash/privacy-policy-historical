# 

Rank My Tattos.com believes that electronic privacy is crucial for the ongoing success of the Internet as a service, commercial, and entertainment medium. 

Personal Information  
On our web site, we will not collect any personally-identifiable information about you unless you provide it to us voluntarily. If you do not want your personal information collected, please do not submit it. 

Cookies  
The use of advertising cookies sent by third-party servers is standard in the Internet industry. You may occasionally get cookies from our advertisers. 

Advertising  
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, e-mail address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. 

Traffic Data  
Each time a visitor comes to our site, information is collected to improve the overall quality of Rank My Tattos.com sites. Rank My Tattos.com collects the visitor's domain name/IP Address (e.g., whether the user is logged on from netscape.com or yale.edu or 255.255.255.0), referral data (e.g., we record the address of the last URL a user visited prior to clicking through to a Rank My Tattos.com site), and browser/platform type (e.g., a Netscape browser on a Macintosh platform). In addition to collecting these types of information, Rank My Tattos.com's advertisement delivery system collects aggregate search terms for internal reporting and targeted advertising. Rank My Tattos.com's advertisement delivery system collects visitor information for purposes of product monitoring, product improvement, and targeted advertising. 

Rank My Tattos.com also counts, tracks, and aggregates the visitor's activity into our analysis of general traffic flows at Rank My Tattos.com sites (e.g., tracking where traffic comes from and how traffic flows within the Rank My Tattos.com sites). Rank My Tattos.com may merge this information into group data, which may then be shared on an aggregated basis with potential advertisers, research firms, and business partners. Rank My Tattos.com will not disclose your individual identity or personal data. When Rank My Tattos.com does present aggregated information to potential advertisers, research firms and business partners, no one will be able to identify you or contact you. 

Account Information  
Certain information about your account may be made available to the public for contact and abuse tracking purposes.

Resale  
Rank My Tattos.com does not resell any personally-identifiable information collected at our site to third parties. 

Contact Us  
Any and all questions regarding this statement should be directed to privacy@RankMyTattos.com.
