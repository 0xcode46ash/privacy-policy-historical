# Ramco Systems

Website Privacy

**Overview**  
  
Thank you for visiting our website and reviewing our Privacy Policy. Your use of our website signifies that you agree with all terms of this Policy. Ramco Systems is not responsible for the information practices employed by Web sites linked to or from our website, including our affiliate partners. Please remember that when you use a link to go from our website to another website, Ramco Systems' Privacy Policy is no longer in effect. Your browsing and interaction with any other website, including websites which have a link on our website, is subject to that website's own rules and policies.  
**  
What information do we collect and why?**

We do not require you to provide any information just for visiting and reading our website. Some sections of our website may collect personal information like your name, telephone number and email address. We may use this personal information for informing you about our new services and updates to the website.

**How do we protect your personal information?**

We value your privacy and we will not provide your personal or company information to any third parties to use for direct advertising or promotional purposes. We may disclose your personal information to the appropriate legal authorities where we believe disclosure is necessary.

**Changes to our Privacy Policy**

Ramco Systems may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. You may check the last update section to keep track of the changes to this policy.

**Children's Issues**

The website is not directed to children under thirteen years of age.
