# Privacy Policy & Non Disclosure for PSD to HTML and Wordpress | Convert Designs into Handcoded, W3C valid HTML & CSS | RapidxHTML

#### Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. We guarantees that any information submitted via order-now form, email or instant messenger will be used solely for the purpose of completing the order, delivering the markup and addressing any customer service issues. Such information is not used for any other purpose and is not shared with outside parties.

#### Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

We also respect your artwork and your business. Your copyrights will always remain yours. We will never use your artwork on any other site, sell it, or give it to anyone under any circumstance.

We guarantees that any information shared with us will remain confidential. We also guarantee that all communications between the company and client are confidential and will remain so.

[Contact us](http://rapidxhtml.com/contact-us/) if you have any query or need more information.
