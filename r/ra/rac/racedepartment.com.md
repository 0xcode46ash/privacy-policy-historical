# RaceDepartment - RaceDepartment.com Privacy Policy

This privacy policy sets out how RaceDepartment.com uses and protects any information that you give RaceDepartment.com when you use this website.

RaceDepartment.com is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, then you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.

RaceDepartment.com may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page from time to time to ensure that you are happy with any changes. Policy changes will be communitcated via the Anouncements forum. This policy is effective from Friday 1st July 2011.  
**  
What We Collect And How It's Used**

_Registered Users_

As part of the membership signup process we collect personal information including email addresses, dates of birth, and IP addresses. We use this information to identify you and to contact you. Your email address is used buy the forum system to deliver optional automated notifications of thread updates and private messages. We may also send newsletters from time to time, you can elect not to receive these mailings in your user settings page. RaceDepartment.com will never forward on your personal information to third parties for any purpose unless we are legal enforced to do so.

RaceDepartment.com uses cookies when a user is logged in to track session information and identify the user during their stay in order to deliver personalised content such as private messaging. Cookies can be disabled in modern browsers from the client/user side. Please refer to your browsers documentation if you wish to do so.

_Licensed Members  
_  
In addition to the above, members who choose to apply for Licensed Membership, to take part in RaceDepartment.com's online sim racing activities, are required to provide their full name. This will be publicly visible. While member profiles and member lists are not submitted to search engines for indexing, a persons posts will be and as a result Licensed Members' names will appear in web searches.

_All Users_

RaceDepartment.com collects non-identifiable statistical information from all users. This is used for planning hardware and software requirements, improving our services, and securing goods/services for prizes and reviews etc.

**  
Donations**

RaceDepartment.com is funding with donations from it's members. All donations are processed through PayPal. As part of the transaction the only information available to RaceDepartment.com is the users registered email address from PayPal. PayPal insulates donators payment information from us. If you would like more information regarding the information stored by PayPal about your donation transaction please see the PayPal Privacy Policy   
and Terms of Use.  
**  
Security  
**  
Weare committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In orderto prevent unauthorised access or disclosure,we have put in place suitablephysical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard andsecure the information we collect online.

**Links to External Sites  
**  
RaceDepartment.com contains links to other sites. RaceDepartment.com cannot accept responsibility for the content or privacy practises of these sites. We encourage users to be aware when they leave our site and review the privacy policy of linked sites on their arrival.

**Surveys and Contests** From time-to-time our site requests information via surveys or contests. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and you may choose whether or not to participate and therefore disclose this information. Information requested may include contact information (such as name and shipping address), and demographic information (such as zip code, age level). Contact information will be used to notify the winners and award prizes. In cases where a competition involves a thrid party organisation who will also collect data the terms of this will be made available with the competition/survey entry information. Survey information will be used for purposes of monitoring or improving the use and satisfaction of this site.
