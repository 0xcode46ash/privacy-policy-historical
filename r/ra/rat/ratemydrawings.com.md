# RateMyDrawings Privacy Policy

> RateMyDrawings strongly believes in their members right to privacy.
> 
>   
>  **Information Collection and Use  
>  **RateMyDrawings records information about the interaction between a web user's browser and our servers. Those interactions include a users IP address and their interaction with drawings such as their ratings. This non-personally identifiable information is collected from web users to assist in the proper function of our service. RateMyDrawings does not collect, use, sell, or purchase web user information that is personally identifiable.
> 
>   
>  **Email Address Confidentiality  
>  **RateMyDrawings takes all precautions to ensure your email address is never used or stolen. We will not sell or rent your email address to third parties and will only use your email address for registratin on our websites and activation of your drawings and RateMyDrawings announcements.
> 
>   
>  **Information Sharing  
>  **Web user information such as account and profile information is stored securely at our web hosting providers facilities with the highest security implementations. We have taken care in choosing hosting facilities that have secure measures, however we are not held accountable for issues outside of our control by these hosting providers. This information along with users email addresses is not shared and will not be sold or rented to any third parties.
> 
>   
>  **Third Party Advertising  
>  **We use various third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use non-personal information such as your computer IP address to track your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you.
> 
>   
>  **Privacy of information  
>  **RateMyDrawings respects members rights to privacy. It is our policy not to monitor the contents of accounts or private messages other than if we are given reason to suspect activities that purposely cause disruption of services, or illegal or unethical activity. While we do make every effort to maintain the integrity of account data and private messages, we cannot be liable for any data that is lost or shared outside of our control. 
> 
>   
>  **Ownership of Information  
>  **RateMyDrawings is the sole owner of all data collected by RateMyDrawings on this site. Ownership of drawings is explained in detail in our website Terms and Conditions. 
> 
>   
>  **Security**  
>  RateMyDrawings takes reasonable precaution to protect the information collected through its websites against loss, misuse or alteration. All information is password protected and only employees of RateMyDrawings that require access to this information to conduct normal business are given permissions to use it.
> 
>   
>  **Web Links  
>  **When you leave this site though links found on this site to other websites (whether knowingly or not) RateMyDrawings can no longer be responsible in any way for any material that you encounter and RateMyDrawings excludes (to the fullest extent permitted by law) any liability that may arise with respect to or as a result of such material causing any damage, costs, injury or financial loss of any kind.
