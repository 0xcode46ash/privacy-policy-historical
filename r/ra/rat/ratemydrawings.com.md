# Rate My Drawing - draw, and get rated! - - - mixarcade.com - - -

> RateMyDrawings strongly believes in their members right to privacy.
> 
>   
>  **Information Collection and Use  
>  **RateMyDrawings records information about the interaction between a web user's browser and our servers. Those interactions include a users IP address and their interaction with drawings such as their ratings. This non-personally identifiable information is collected from web users to assist in the proper function of our service. RateMyDrawings does not collect, use, sell, or purchase web user information that is personally identifiable.
> 
>   
>  **Email Address Confidentiality  
>  **RateMyDrawings takes all precautions to ensure your email address is never used or stolen. We will not sell or rent your email address to third parties and will only use your email address for activation of your drawings and RateMyDrawings announcements.
> 
>   
>  **Information Sharing  
>  **Web user information such as account and profile information is stored securely at our web hosting providers facilities with the highest security implementations. This information along with users email addresses is not share and will not be sold or rented to any third parties.
> 
>   
>  **Third Party Advertising  
>  **We use various third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use non-personal information such as your computer IP address to track your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you.
> 
>   
>  **Ownership of Information  
>  **RateMyDrawings is the sole owner of all data collected by RateMyDrawings on this site.
> 
>   
>  **Security**  
>  RateMyDrawings takes reasonable precaution to protect the information collected through its websites against loss, misuse or alteration. All information is password protected and only employees of RateMyDrawings that require access to this information to conduct normal business are give permissions to use it.
> 
>   
>  **Web Links  
>  **When you leave this site though links found on this site to other websites (whether knowingly or not) RateMyDrawings can no longer be responsible in any way for any material that you encounter and RateMyDrawings excludes (to the fullest extent permitted by law) any liability that may arise with respect to or as a result of such material causing any damage, costs, injury or financial loss of any kind.
