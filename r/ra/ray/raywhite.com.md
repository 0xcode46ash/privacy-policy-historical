# Property Australia, New Zealand & Indonesia - Ray White Real Estate

|    
|  In order to improve our on-line service to you, The Ray White Web Site uses "cookies" to track certain information about your visit to this site. A cookie is a small amount of information that is transferred to your browser by a Web server. It can only be read again by the server that gave it to you. It serves as a memory for you and saves your favourite search criteria so you do not have to enter the same information every time you come to The Ray White Web Site. It cannot be executed as code or deliver viruses. Saving this information via a cookie is totally optional and no personal information is acquired through the cookie unless you specifically provide it. 

You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it. 

This information does not identify you personally and you remain anonymous unless you have otherwise provided The Ray White Web Site with personal information.
