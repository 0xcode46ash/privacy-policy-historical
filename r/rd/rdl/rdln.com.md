# Red Lion Hotels - Terms and Conditions

<< Back Privacy Policy

In conjunction with current web practices aimed at protecting the privacy of our users, the following statement discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the websites www.westcoasthotelpartners.com, www.redlion.com, www.ticketswest.com, and www.fastixx.com. 

## Data Collected 

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Our site may use cookies to keep track of your shopping cart, and to save your password and the information described in the next paragraph so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site. We do not use cookies for any other purposes. Your use of this site is your consent to this process.

Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (such as their name and email address) and optionally, demographic information (such as their marital status, education, or income level). Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section. Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. We use this data to tailor the visitor's experience at our site, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences. We may provide this information to others providing content to this site.

Our site uses forms for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect visitor's contact information (such as their email address) and financial information (such as their account or credit card numbers). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders to our customers. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services. Credit card information is not stored on-line. We do not provide this credit card information to others.

These sites (www.westcoasthotelpartners.com, www.redlion.com, www.ticketswest.com and www.fastixx.com) contain links to other sites and are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

## Choice/Opt-Out 

Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor.

This site gives users the option of removing their information from our database. They will no longer receive communications from us or receive our service.
