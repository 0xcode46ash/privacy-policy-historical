# Review Centre Privacy Policy

>   
> 
> 
> ### Review Centre Privacy Policy
> 
> When you write a review, you may optionally include extra information about yourself (sex, age, occupation and income). This information is not published with your review. The information may be used for research and analysis of market trends. 
> 
> Your email address is collected so we can advise you when your review has been approved. If you have chosen to receive the Review Centre newsletter then we will use this email address to send it to you. 
> 
> Your email will not be passed to third parties unless you have opted to allow us to do so.
