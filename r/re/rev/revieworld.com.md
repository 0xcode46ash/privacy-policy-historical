# Privacy Policy - Revieworld

This policy explains what information we collect from you, how we use it and your associated rights and responsibilities. 

This statement forms part of our [Terms and Conditions](https://web.archive.org/web/20051112045317id_/http%3A//www.revieworld.com/terms.html) including our [Terms and Conditions of Posting](https://web.archive.org/web/20051112045317id_/http%3A//www.revieworld.com/posting_policy.html). This statement provides information only about the data collection practices of **www.Revieworld.com** and **www.Reevoo.com** (websites owned by Revieworld Ltd). 

#### How we use your customer information

When you give us information or comments (‘Content’), it may be used for a number of different purposes, which are listed below: 

  1. **Administration and reviews** Some of the basic information you give us is necessary for us to operate our service. We use your Content to provide feedback to retailers but we do not disclose your personal information when we do so.
  2. **Customer services** Where we ask for your contact details, such as your name, address, e-mail address and/or telephone number, we do so to enable us to resolve any queries you may have about using the Website.
  3. **Offers** You can opt in to receive information by email from time to time about updates, special offers or promotions we are offering. This service is entirely optional and you can unsubscribe at any time.
  4. **Third Parties** We may disclose your personal data and any relevant Content to members of our group, third parties who provide services on our behalf, trusted partners, our professional advisors and/or otherwise as required by law.



#### Sharing information

Apart from your first name and location, which are automatically displayed with reviews and comments (unless you change the default configuration), other than as specified above we will only disclose your personal information to third parties with your explicit consent. 

We may share aggregate statistics about products, traffic patterns and purchasing trends and your Content with our respective suppliers and partners, but this information does not include any information which could identify you. 

#### How long will we keep your information?

We will retain your personal data for up to two (2) years or so long as Revieworld’s relationship continues with you unless you contact us and request we delete your personal data. 

#### Sale of business

If our business is sold or integrated with another business your details may be disclosed to our advisers and any prospective purchasers and their advisers and will be passed on to the new owners of the business so that the new owners can continue to offer the services currently offered by us. 

#### Your consent

In posting comments, you consent to the collection, processing and use of this information by both ourselves and, where relevant, permitted third parties in the ways described above. 

An up-to-date privacy policy will always be maintained on this site so you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it and in what circumstances we disclose it. Our privacy policy may change from time to time. If you have any questions about this privacy statement or would like more information about us, or you believe content held by Revieworld infringes your privacy, please email us at [privacy@revieworld.com](mailto:privacy@revieworld.com). 

#### Security

We have made every reasonable effort to ensure we protect your personal information and employ appropriate security methods and protections. 

#### Cookies

Cookies are small files in your computer’s web browser that enable website providers to recognise your computer when you visit their websites. We use this facility to identify you, so as to make using the website easier for you. The facility also assists in the collection of information, such as how many people visit and return to our website, and to make certain areas of the website function properly. 

#### Data access

Revieworld is registered as a data controller under the Data Protection Act. In accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998, you are entitled to request details of the personal information we hold about you. We may charge a fee for complying with your request. If you become aware that the personal information we hold about you is inaccurate, you may request that we amend it. Any requests should be made in writing to us at [privacy@revieworld.com](mailto:privacy@revieworld.com). 

#### Government relations and lawsuits

We may disclose data about you to the government if we are required to do so or if we think the disclosure is appropriate, and you authorize these disclosures. We may also disclose data about you in connection with actual or potential lawsuits (a) if we are required to do so, (b) if such disclosures may potentially mitigate our liability, or (c) to enforce our rights. 

#### Children

If you are 16 or under, please do not use our site or provide any data to us.
