# About Revolution Health - Revolution Health

## Privacy policy highlights

**Effective Date: April 17, 2007**

At Revolution Health, we understand that your health is a very personal matter, and we appreciate the trust you place in us to treat your information carefully and sensibly. This document outlines the types of personal information we collect and use when you use our services, as well as some of the steps we take to safeguard information. We hope this will help you make an informed decision about sharing personal information with us.

This notice provides highlights of the full [Revolution Health Privacy Policy,](http://www.revolutionhealth.com/about/privacy-policy) which describes in detail the privacy practices that apply to the Revolution Health website and [Revolution Health Premium ](http://www.revolutionhealth.com/about/premium-services)[Membership.](http://www.revolutionhealth.com/about/premium-services)

**Personal information and other data we collect**  


  * We collect [personal information](https://web.archive.org/about/privacy-policy-glossary#personal) when you voluntarily provide it to us or authorize us to collect it on your behalf. 
  * We collect [non-personal information](https://web.archive.org/about/privacy-policy-glossary#non) such as browser type, IP address, and Web pages visited to help manage our site.
  * We use [cookies](https://web.archive.org/about/privacy-policy-glossary#cookies), [beacons](https://web.archive.org/about/privacy-policy-glossary#web) and other technologies to offer a personalized experience of our site and to manage our email programs. 
  * When you make a purchase from us, we may use your personal information to obtain additional information from third parties. We will only obtain and use information from third parties if you give us your permission to do so. [Read more](https://web.archive.org/about/privacy-policy#disclosure) in the full Privacy Policy. 



**Uses of information**  


  * We use the information we collect to provide the services you request, including personalized content and advertising. ****
  * We only send marketing communications to you with your consent.
  * We do not share your personal information with marketers unless we have obtained your consent. 
  * We do not sell, rent or lease your personal information to insurance companies or pharmaceutical companies. To provide you with products or services you request, we will share your information with other companies that work on our behalf. 
  * We may share [aggregated, non-personal information](https://web.archive.org/about/privacy-policy#aggregate) about you with third parties.
  * We may share information with third parties in limited circumstances, including complying with legal process, preventing fraud or imminent harm, and ensuring the security of our system or network. [Read more](https://web.archive.org/about/privacy-policy#link) in the full Privacy Policy.



**Your choices**

  * We offer you choices when we ask for your personal information whenever reasonably possible.
  * You can refuse cookies in your browser, although some of our features or services may not function properly as a result. 
  * You can control the [privacy levels](https://web.archive.org/about/privacy-policy#public) for different community features on our site, with settings that range from private to viewable by everyone. 
  * You can control the level to which we [personalize](https://web.archive.org/about/privacy-policy#choice) your experience.

**Safeguarding your information**  


  * We have physical, technical and procedural safeguards to help protect your personal information.
  * We limit access to your personal information to employees having a reasonable need to access this information.
  * Parts of the site that contain more sensitive information, such as your personal health portfolio and account information are protected with encryption. 



**How to contact us**  
If you have any questions or concerns about our policy or its implementation, you may contact us at this email address: [privacyhelp@revolutionhealth.com](mailto:privacyhelp@revolutionhealth.com).
