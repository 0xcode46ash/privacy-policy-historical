# Privacy Policy Retailmart | Uses of Information | Commitment to Data Security | Online Shopping Site

**Use of the Information**  
Your information is used when requesting for more information, or buying a product or service from Retailmart's partners, affiliates or third party sites, only to complete the order. Your information is not shared with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete the order.

Retailmart.com uses return email address to answer the email and the same is done for any other purpose. Retailmart.com uses non-identifying information to design its website and shares the same with advertisers. Also, Retailmart does not disclose anything that could be used to identify any individual. It does not sell or rent any of your personal information to anyone.

**Commitment towards Data Security**   
To prevent unauthorised access, maintain data accuracy, and to ensure the correct use of information, Retailmart has put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information which is collected online. The internal security and privacy policies are periodically reviewed and enhanced as necessary and only authorised individuals hold access to the information provided by the users of Retailmart.com. 

**Corrections to your information**  
You are provided access to update all your personally identifiable information that Retailmart.com collects, only by logging in to your account by providing your used ID and secure Password. This procedure is practiced to safeguard your information, as Retailmart takes reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.

Retailmart.com offers great time to shopping enthusiasts.

For more information contact us at [**support@retailmart.com**](mailto:support@retailmart.com)
