# Rewards1.com - Your premier source for freebies!

## Personal Information

Rewards1.com ("r1") respects your privacy and does not share, sell, trade or otherwise disseminate your collected personal information ("Personal Information") without your express consent. 

Personal Information is information which can be used to specifically identify, contact, or locate you, examples include your first and last name together, mailing address, email address, and phone number. 

## How We Use It

r1 will contact you for necessary reasons including, without limitation support, reward claims, promotions, earning opportunities, announcements and other administrative reasons.  Full privacy controls including opt-out options can be found under Account controls. 

r1 may share non-personally identifiable information with 3rd Party Sponsors ("Sponsors") to identify and evaluate potential earning opportunities. At no time does r1 disclose your Personal Information without your express consent. 

Non-personally identifiable information is information such as gender, age, and country of origin. 

For custom reward requests which require delivery from 3rd party vendors, r1 may share Personal Information such as your name and mailing address for shipping purposes.  Vendors are required to use the information only for fulfilling the goods and services as required by r1. 

Full access to and edit controls of your Personal Information, including Account closure, can be found through the Account Personal Info settings and controls. 

## How They Use It

To earn points members may be required to submit Personal Information to Sponsors, of which r1 assumes no control or responsibility of.  Please read each Sponsors User Agreement and/or Terms of Service carefully.

## Cookies & Trackers

r1 uses cookies, sessions and other temporary data elements to track member activity.  These tracking elements are an essential part of the r1 service as they identify members, ensure accurate credit, and help provide a seamless member experience. 

## Children under 13

In accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 ("COPPA"), r1 does not collect, store, or use personally identifiable information from users under 13 years of age.
