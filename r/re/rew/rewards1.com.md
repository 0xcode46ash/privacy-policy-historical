# Rewards1.com - Your premier source for freebies!

## Personal Information

Rewards1.com ("R1") respects your privacy and does not share, sell, trade or otherwise disseminate your collected personal information ("Personal Information") without your express consent. 

Personal Information is information which can be used to specifically identify, contact, or locate you, examples include your first and last name together, mailing address, email address, and phone number. 

## How We Use It

R1 will contact you for necessary reasons including, but not limited to: support, reward claims, promotions, earning opportunities, announcements and other administrative reasons.  Full privacy controls including opt-out options can be found under Account controls. 

R1 may share non-personally identifiable information with 3rd Party Sponsors ("Sponsors") to identify and evaluate potential earning opportunities. At no time does R1 disclose your Personal Information without your express consent. 

Non-personally identifiable information is information such as gender, age, and general geographic location. 

For custom reward requests which require delivery from 3rd party vendors, R1 may share Personal Information such as your name and mailing address for shipping purposes.  Vendors are required to use your information only for the purposes of fulfilling the goods or services as requested by R1. 

Full access and edit controls to Personal Information on file, including functions such as Account closure, can be found through the Account Personal Info settings and controls. 

## How They Use It

To earn Points members may be required to submit Personal Information to Sponsors, of which R1 assumes no control or responsibility for.  Please read each Sponsor's offer documentation, User Agreement and/or Terms of Service carefully before engaging with any sponsored Incentive.

## Cookies & Trackers

R1 uses cookies, sessions, identifiers and other temporary data elements to identify, track, ensure accurate credit and provide other essential site services necessary for a seamless member experience. 

## Children under 13

R1 complies with the US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 ("COPPA") governing the collection, storage, and use of Personal Information from children under 13, and does not knowingly accept members under 13 years of age.
