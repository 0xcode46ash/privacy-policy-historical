# Privacy Policy refdesk.com

_Refdesk's Privacy Policy_

Refdesk is committed to protecting the privacy of site visitors. 

At Refdesk we do not purposefully gather any personal information about our visitors unless they are requesting information, providing feedback, or subscribing to services. Refdesk does not collect cookies. A [ 'cookie' ](http://www.cookiecentral.com/) is a small data file that certain Web sites write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file can contain information such as a user ID that the site uses to track the pages you've visited. Refdesk contains links to other sites, which may or may not collect cookies. You should review the privacy policy of such sites, if this is a concern. Refdesk is not responsible for the privacy practices of linked sites. 

Refdesk will never wilfully disclose personally identifiable information about our customers to any third party without the permission of the involved customer except in situations required by applicable law. 

Please note: This site contains links to other sites. Refdesk is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these sites.

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with Refdesk, please feel free to [ contact us ](mailto:rbdrudge@refdesk.com) at your convenience. 

Thank you.
