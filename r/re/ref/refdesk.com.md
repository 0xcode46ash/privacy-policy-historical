# Privacy Policy refdesk.com

Refdesk is committed to protecting the privacy of site visitors and will _never_ share your E-mail address with any other person or company.

At Refdesk we do not gather any personal information about our visitors. Refdesk does not collect cookies. A [ 'cookie' ](http://www.howstuffworks.com/cookie.htm) is a small data file that certain Web sites write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file can contain information such as a user ID that the site uses to track the pages you've visited. There are also 'Third Party' cookies. These cookies are placed by ad networks, not by Refdesk. 

Refdesk uses one ad network for advertising services: 24/7 Media, and their privacy policy is available at [24/7 Media ](http://www.247media.com/privacy.html).

__Please note: Refdesk contains links to other sites. Refdesk is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these sites.__

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with Refdesk, please feel free to [ contact us. ](mailto:webmaster@refdesk.com)

Thank you for using Refdesk.
