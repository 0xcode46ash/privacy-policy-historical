# Referral Frenzy - Privacy

[Home](http://referralfrenzy.com/ "Home") [Members](http://referralfrenzy.com/members/ "Members") [Join](http://referralfrenzy.com/join/ "Join") [Help](http://referralfrenzy.com/help/ "Help")

Referral Frenzy respects your right to privacy and we understand that visitors to our website need to be in control of their personal information. We have created this statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to internet privacy. The following explains our use of information collected on this website. 

Referral Frenzy does not and will not sell mailing lists to other companies, or divulge any of your personal details to other companies or individuals. 

Referral Frenzy uses your I.P. (Internet Protocol) address to gather broad demographic information. We use cookies to retain information on who referred you to our site and to allow you to access our member only pages. 

To join Referral Frenzy requires users to submit their name and email address. This is used to contact our members by email on a daily basis. Members can opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time by logging in and deleting their account. 

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this website, please contact us.
