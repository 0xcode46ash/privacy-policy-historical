# Privacy Policy

 

This is the web site of **Rent Digs, LLC**.

We can be reached via e-mail at: [support@RentDigs.com](mailto:support@RentDigs.com)

We collect the e-mail addresses only of those who register on our site.  Rentdigs.com will not sell these email addresses or display them on the web.  Users wishing to make contact via email with registered users, may only do so by filling out the online contact form.

We do not set any cookies.

If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail at the above address.

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact if users of the site  use the number to contact you with questions or to set up a showing of the Apartment, House on Condo you have listed for rent.

We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies.

If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and we will use for these new purposes only data collected from the time of the policy change forward. 

We never provide site visitors with access to information that we have collected and that we maintain about them with the exception of data related to the marketing of the Apartment, House or Condo listed for rent.

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name or IP address.

Consumers can have this information corrected by or logging into the management account for the Apartment, House or Condos listed for rent.

If a registered user would like their account and all information deleted from our site and database, please contact us at the above email.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above email.
