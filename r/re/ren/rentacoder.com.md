# Privacy Statement

_**For those who view Rent A Coder**_ Internet user privacy and confidentiality is of the utmost importance to Rent A Coder and it's owning company, [Exhedra Solutions, Inc](http://www.exhedra.com/). It's reputation and integrity is directly related to how it serves its web customers. 

As a result,  Rent A Coder openly volunteers its practices of collecting information, its targeting capabilities, and its use of cookies. If you have any questions pertaining to this privacy policy and its contents, please direct your correspondence to [privacy@exhedra.com](mailto:privacy@exhedra.com).

_**Normal Viewing Information**_

As you visit the site, Rent A Coder collects information which is specific to how you use the site. Rent A Coder servers note details such as the server you are logged onto, the geographic location of the ISP you use, the type of browser you use, and your IP address. This information is obtained solely for the purpose of targeting ads and measuring a given ads effectiveness on behalf of Rent A Coder clients and customers.

_**The Cookie Principle**_

Rent A Coder uses cookies to improve your Web browsing experience. When you first come to the site, Rent A Coder assigns a unique random number to your computer browser - commonly known as a "cookie."  This cookie is used to maintain your personal settings (...so you only have to choose it once at the beginning of the site, and don't have to choose it over and over). 

_**Email addresses and Demographic Information given to Rent A Coder**_

Users who sign up for and use advanced features of Rent A Coder, such as making a bid request, or bidding on work may be asked for additional information including email address and possibly demographic information. BECAUSE WE PROTECT OUR USERS FROM SPAMMERS, Rent A Coder DOES NOT SELL THIS INFORMATION TO EXTERNAL COMPANIES. Email addresses are used purely to verify the user's true identity and prevent fraud, while demographic information is used purely for the purpose of targeting ads and measuring their effectiveness on behalf of Rent A Coder clients and customers.

_**3rd Party Measurement Services**_  
Rent A Coder works with several third parties that serve ads to this site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services of interest to you.

Often, they will employ a third-party measurement service other than Rent A Coder for tracking the performance of an ad program. In these instances, Rent A Coder has no control over the information collected by the third party, and may not be held responsible for the actions of the third party measurement service where collected information is concerned.

To find out more about how Double-click manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site and to know your choices about not having this information used by DoubleClick, please go to 

<http://www.sonar.com/naiprivacy>.To find out more about how Engage manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to <http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html>.

 If you have questions regarding other third party measurement services and their respective methods of information collection, please contact [privacy@exhedra.com](mailto:privacy@exhedra.com).

**Third Party Cookies**

  
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.
