Privacy Policy  
  
---  
   
At Rent.com.au, we will not provide any information that individually identifies a user to a third party. We fully respect and will uphold your privacy.  
   
This means any information such as names, contact details, e-mail addresses, etc will never be disclosed, other than as part of your property ad.  
   
Rent.com.au will collect aggregate information of a general nature, such as number of impressions, what portal users came from, average time spent on this site etc, simply for marketing and business strategy purposes, but none of this information will specifically identify individual users.  
   
To enhance your Web experience, this site uses anonymous identifiers ("cookies") to collect anonymous preference and interest visitor information. This information is provided to the Ad-Server and is used by the Ad-Server to create anonymous interest profiles. This information does not include and is never merged with personally identifiable information (such as name, address or email address) or any information that is considered sensitive or controversial or that would compromise your privacy. The Ad-Server(s') profiles may be accessed by the website and marketers to tailor site content, promotions and advertising, and to perform marketing research. Consumers can opt-out from these profiles at any time. For more information on the privacy policies of the Ad-Servers that serve advertisements on this site, including your option not to have this information used for this purpose, refer to: <http://us.www.sabela.com/information/privacy.html> or <http://www.bmcmedia.com/privacy>
