Privacy Policy  
  
RezLive.com takes your privacy concerns seriously and will not share your personal information without your consent.

If you choose to use one of the features below, your information will be used as follows: RezLive.com will never disclose or sell any of your personal information, including your name, address, shopping, or transaction history to any third party without your permission.  
If you are a member, we will use your e-mail address to send correspondence about RezLive.com, including: 

* Reservation confirmations 
* Important updates about your travel plans 
* Newsletters 
* Notifications of travel specials If you have additional questions or concerns regarding the RezLive.com site, please e-mail us at Feedback@RezLive.com.com. We read every customer e-mail and will respond as soon as possible.  
  
   
  
We Welcome your Suggestions to Serve you Better, write back to us on [Feedback@RezLive.com](mailto:Feedback@RezLive.com)
