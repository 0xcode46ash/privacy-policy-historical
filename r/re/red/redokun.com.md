# Privacy - Redokun

Redokun respects your right to privacy. All information collected by Redokun is used to improve the experience and quality of the Service. 

##### User Information

When you register for Redokun site, we collect information such e-mail address and billing information. All accounts come with a 14-day trial period, during which you are not required to enter billing information. When you register for a Redokun user account, we collect only the e-mail address and any user profile fields which are filled out, such as the user name. This information is used solely for identification inside the Service and email communication to and from the Service between you and the support staff of the site you are using. 

##### Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data which often includes an anonymous unique identifier that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and is stored on your computers hard drive. Cookies are required to use Redokun in order to uniquely identify your browser and user preferences while logged in. We use non-permanent cookies that last for up to two months, after which you will be required to log in to the Service again. 

##### Data Storage

Redokun uses third party vendors and hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run Redokun. Although Redokun owns the code, databases, and all rights to the Redokun application, you retain all rights to your data. 

##### Security

Redokun is hosted at Linode (https://www.linode.com), a world-class VPS hosting provider. For more information on their security, please see Linode’s website (https://www.linode.com/security). All of your data is transmitted over HTTPS; however, we only encrypt sensitive information, i.e. login/passwords, when storing. 

##### Private Discussion

Redokun features the ability to make content private or public. These settings fall under the responsibility of the Redokun site in which they are published within. Redokun is not liable for user content on other sites. 

##### Disclosure

Redokun may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with subpoenas when a users actions may violate the Terms of Service. 

##### Changes to this Privacy Policy

Redokun reserves the right to update this policy at any time without prior notice. Any significant changes to this policy will be sent out to the account owners registered email address. 

##### Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through email at contact@redokun.com.
