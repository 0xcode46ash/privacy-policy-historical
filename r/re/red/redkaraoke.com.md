# RedKaraoke - Help

Even though [REDKARAOKE](http://www.redkaraoke.com/) is an entertainment website designed for fun, it is necessary for us to keep a record in order to store recordings, maintain an index, etc.; to do all of this, we need to have official legal coverage to guarantee the proper handling of our users’ data. Thus, here is the legal text that our attorneys created: 

**PRIVACY AND PERSONAL DATA PROTECTION POLICY**

1.1. Through this notice, Planeta Red S.L. (hereafter the “SERVICE PROVIDER”), with registered office at Parque de Santa Juliana 7, suite 5, 26004, Logroño (La Rioja), hereby informs all users of the portal ([www.redkaraoke.com](http://www.redkaraoke.com/)) and all apps owned by the SERVICE PROVIDER (all hereafter referred to as the “Portal”) of its Privacy and Personal Data Protection Policy regarding the use and browsing of the Portal, in accordance with the current legislation on this matter.

1.2. In order to register with the Portal, the user must provide certain personal data (hereafter “Personal Data”) through the applicable forms. Accordingly, the USER declares that his or her data is accurate and current.

1.3. In compliance with the current regulations on data protection, the SERVICE PROVIDER hereby informs the user that all personal data (including but not limited to: name, username, images, voice, and audio) will be stored in a file property of the SERVICE PROVIDER and registered with the Spanish Data Protection Agency, the purpose of which is to maintain and manage the contractual relationship with it, as well as the submission of commercial communications regarding the products and services of the SERVICE PROVIDER.

1.4 Listed below by the PROVIDER are the purposes of the collection of user personal data:

• Management of possible incidents.

• Checking and verification of the quality of services provided.

• Execution of prize competitions.

• Submission of general information about the development and performance of the service or product requested or procured by the User. In regards to promos or contests: Submission of information regarding said promo or contest (contest winners, handling and delivery of prizes, and/or any other type of information related to these).

• Compliance with the applicable accounting and fiscal duties.

• Development of market research and statistics, marketing, and preferences regarding the services provided.

1.5 Furthermore, the SERVICE PROVIDER can send you commercial communications through any means, including electronically, regarding services related to your activity.

1.6. By consenting to the Terms of use for the Service, through the completion and submission of the corresponding data collection form, the User accepts and authorizes the use of his or her Personal Data by the SERVICE PROVIDER for the purposes and conditions established in this Policy.

1.7 The User declares that all personal data included in recordings or other content or projects created by the user has been collected with the consent of everyone involved. Failing that, all responsibility would fall on the user.

The user can exercise, according to content of these regulations, the rights of access, rectification, cancellation, and opposition by sending a letter to Planeta Red S.L. to the address indicated above, or by sending an email to  with an attached copy of the user’s DNI or a different document verifying his or her identity.

**3\. SECURITY MEASURES**

3.1. The SERVICE PROVIDER undertakes to comply with its obligation of secrecy in regards to personal data and its duty to keep this data safe, even after the contractual relationship has concluded, by adopting the security measures imposed by the current legislation and in accordance with the state of technology, in order to prevent the alteration, loss, treatment, or unauthorized access to this data. 

3.2 The responsible entity and any other entities that intervene during any phase of processing (based on the authorization of the user) are subject to the strictest professional secrecy, and expressly undertake to adopt the levels of protection and measures necessary to ensure the security of personal data and to prevent any alteration, misuse, loss, theft, treatment, or unauthorized access thereof. This notwithstanding, the user should be aware and is expressly warned that security measures on the Internet are not impregnable. 

3.3. Planeta Red S.L. ensures that it has taken the appropriate security measures in its facilities, systems, and files, as well as in the confidential treatment of Personal Data in compliance with the applicable current legislation.

**4\. COOKIES AND OTHER STORAGE DEVICES**

**Concept**

In order to use the Portal, the use of cookies and other similar storage devices (both ours and third parties’) is necessary. Both are used with the purpose of providing a better service for different browsers.

Cookies used on the Portal’s sites and webpages are only associated with the browser of a specific computer (an anonymous User) and do not provide a User’s first or last name. Users can configure their browsers to provide an on-screen alert when they receive cookies and to prevent cookies from being installed on their hard drives. 

**Consent**

By using the Portal you are consenting and agreeing to the storage of these cookies in your computer or device for the purposes herein stated.

If you decide not to accept our cookies, you may continue to use our website in the usual manner, but your user experience may be slightly impaired.

Analytic cookies: analyze and measure users’ browsing habits.

Google Analytics: [www.google.com/analytics](http://www.google.com/analytics)

Advertising cookies: These are designed to provide Users with third party advertising information related to the web content being viewed at the time, and to notify third parties’ campaign control systems when a campaign has been registered.

<http://www.google.com/adsense/start/>

Cookies for sharing social media content: These are used to identify content that has been shared on other websites.

<https://www.facebook.com/>

<http://www.google.com/intl/es/+/learnmore/>

Cookies for measuring and tracking different marketing campaigns developed by the SERVICE PROVIDER or third parties hired by it.

**Deleting Cookies**

Your browser may occasionally generate a pop-up message offering to prevent the installation of cookies. If your browser does not provide this option automatically, you can prevent the installation of cookies by following your browser’s instructions. 

You can allow, view, block, or delete cookies installed on your computer by configuring the options of the browser installed on your computer.

You can find information on how to do this by going to the following links:

Firefox: <http://support.mozilla.org/en/products/firefox/cookies>

Chrome: [http://support.google.com/chrome/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=95647](http://support.google.com/chrome/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=95647)
