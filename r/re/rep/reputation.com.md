# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy 

We are committed to protecting our visitors' privacy and encourage you to thoroughly read this privacy policy. This document contains the complete privacy policy for Reputation, LLC's websites ReputationLLC.com and SFpolitics.com. 

**1\. Collection of Information**  
No personal information will be collected from you unless you choose to share such information with us. You may submit your email and other personal information to receive our Email Updates. You will be notified per email when parts of our website(s) have been updated and/or there is information that we think is important to share with you. At any time you may email us at [Webmaster@ReputationLLC.com](mailto:Webmaster@ReputationLLC.com) to be removed from our database.

**2\. Sharing of Personal Information**  
We do not share or otherwise transfer personal information to a third party with the exception of the following circumstances: A) For purposes of sharing information ReputationLLC.com and SFpolitics.com are one and the same. Information shared with one is deemed to be information shared with the other. B) As we continue to develop our business, we may sell, buy, merge or partner with other companies or businesses. In such transactions, user information may be among the transferred assets. Notice to you of the transfer of your information is not required. C) Third party vendors may have access to personal information to provide technical support for the operation of our websites. D) To protect against misuse of our websites. E) To protect the personal safety of our users. F) When required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process. G) To comply with the investigation of any criminal matter, conducted by proper and verified law enforcement authorities.

**3\. Surveys and Polls**  
On our sites we may requests information from users via surveys or polls. Participation in these surveys or polls is completely voluntary and the user therefore has a choice whether or not to disclose personal information. The required information typically includes name, email address and zip code. Personal information will be automatically entered to receive our Email Updates and also used for purposes of monitoring, or improving the use and satisfaction of our websites. Polling information will be used for informational purposes and will be posted on our websites. A cookie will be placed on your computer to verify that you have voted. We use the cookie to restrict users from voting more than once. Users' personally identifiable information is not shared with third parties unless we give and receive prior notice and consent. Though we may use an intermediary to conduct these surveys or polls, they may not use users' personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes. 

**4\. Log Files**  
Like most standard web site servers we use log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user=s movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. We may track key words entered into our search engine to gage users' interest in various parts of our websites. We use this information to improve our websites.

**5\. Cookies**  
At present time do not use any cookies unless we run a survey or poll (see section 3. Surveys and Polls). This policy may change in the future if we decide to accept advertising or promotional partners or for any other reason.

**6\. Protection of Our Websites**  
We monitor traffic to our websites, and you expressly understand that any unauthorized attempt to upload or download information, or any attempts to cause any damage to our websites will be prosecuted to the fullest extent of the law. Anyone using our websites consents to such monitoring

**7\. Access to Password Protected and Secure Areas**  
To access and use areas that are secured by username and password you must first be authorized in writing. We may prosecute unauthorized attempts to access these secured areas.

**8\. Links to Third Party Websites**  
These Sites contains links to third party websites. Please be aware that Reputation, LLC is not responsible for the privacy practices of such third party websites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy policies of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy policy applies solely to information collected by Reputation, LLC and our websites.

**9\. Correcting/Updating/Deleting/Deactivating Personal Information**  
If a User's personally identifiable information changes (such as phone, email, phone or postal address), or if a user no longer desires to be contacted by us, user may correct, update or delete/deactivate User's personally identifiable information by emailing us at [Webmaster@ReputationLLC.com](mailto:Webmaster@ReputationLLC.com) or by contacting us by postal mail at the contact information listed below.

**10\. Notification of Changes**  
We reserve the right to change this policy at any time. Notice to you of the change of the policy is not required. Please check periodically for changes. Information collected prior to the time any change is posted will be used according to the rules and laws that applied at the time information was collected. Your continued use of our websites following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes.
