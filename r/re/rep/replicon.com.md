# Replicon - About Replicon

Privacy Policy Statement

Replicon Inc. is committed to honoring the privacy of our customers and visitors. As a result we adhere to strict privacy protection guidelines ensuring your personal information is kept confidential. We do not share or solicit the information captured in any way. We respect your identity and keep it private. You will not be subject to intrusive marketing. 

As someone who has an interest in Web TimeSheet, we would very much like to learn more about you and your timesheet requirements and therefore, we collect this data to better meet your needs in the future. The information we receive from you is strictly used to further our relationship with you and help you along the evaluation process of Web TimeSheet, if you require it. The better we know you, the more we can tailor our product materials and sample databases to provide you with a higher level of support and customer satisfaction. 

We respect the privacy of our newsletter subscribers, those who answer our surveys, and those who fill out our Suggestion Boxes or Download forms. It is our policy: 

  * To respect the purpose for which our site visitors gave us information. 
  * Not to sell or rent our lists to third parties for e-mail marketing or any other purpose. 
  * If you request to be removed from our mailing list, the forms available on our website can be used to do that.
