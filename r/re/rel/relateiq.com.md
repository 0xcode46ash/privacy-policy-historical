# SalesforceIQ

## Overview: Privacy Policy

Salesforce.com, inc. and its affiliates (“Salesforce” or the “Company”) are committed to responsible information-handling practices in relation to their SalesforceIQ business. 

The following privacy statements describe the different types of personal information the Company handles relating to the SalesforceIQ Web sites and the SalesforceIQ Service (as defined in the Web Site Privacy Statement) and the Company's privacy practices with respect to such personal information: 

The [Web Site Privacy Statement](https://www.salesforce.com/company/privacy/) addresses the Company's handling of personal information collected from visitors to the SalesforceIQ Web sites and users of the SalesforceIQ Service.

The [Indexed Content Privacy Statement](https://www.salesforceiq.com/privacy-policy/index-content-privacy-statement) addresses the Company's handling of information, including personal information, which has been collected from publicly available content on the Internet or obtained from third party data sources, and made available through the SalesforceIQ Service for the benefit of and at the direction of the Company’s Customers.

Questions about the Company's information-handling practices as they relate to the SalesforceIQ Web sites and SalesforceIQ Service should be directed to the Salesforce Privacy Office:
