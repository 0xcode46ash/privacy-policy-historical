# All-in-one CRM, business email, contact management, and sales force automation: Relenta

The Relenta Privacy Policy describes how we treat personal information when you use our Services. This privacy policy should be read in conjunction with our [Terms of Service](http://relenta.com/tos.html) and other policies.

Please note that we may change our policies at any time, and pursuant to our [Terms of Service](http://relenta.com/tos.html), it is your responsibility to keep up-to-date with and adhere to the policies posted here. You can review the most current version of our Privacy Policy at any time at: http://www.relenta.com/privacy.

Relenta asks that you provide personal information when you create your Relenta account, including current and accurate contact, identification, and other information that may be required as part of the registration process and/or continued use of the Service. 

Relenta transmits, stores, processes and maintains all information, data, text, software, graphics, messages, files, account settings, or other data materials relating to your account ("Content"), in order to provide the Service to you.

When you use Relenta or visit [www.relenta.com](http://www.relenta.com/) website, our servers automatically record certain information about your use of Relenta or www.relenta.com website. Similar to other web services and websites, Relenta records information such as account activity (including use of disk space, number of emails sent and received, login history), and other information commonly found in webserver log files, including address of the web page viewed, your browser type, IP address, date and time of access, cookie ID, and referring URL. 

Relenta maintains and processes your account and its Contents to provide and improve the Service to you.

Relenta's computers process the information in your messages for various purposes, including formatting and displaying the information to you, sorting and organizing email messages for you, preventing unsolicited bulk email (spam), creating backups of your email messages and other account Content, and other purposes relating to offering you the Service.

Relenta may send you information related to your use of the Service.

When you send email, Relenta includes information such as your email address and the email itself as part of that email.

We do not sell, rent or otherwise share your personal or account information with any third parties except in the limited circumstances described in the [Terms of Service](http://relenta.com/tos.html), such as when we believe we are required to do so by law.

You may change your Relenta account settings through your account management interface.

You may organize, sort, or delete your messages or terminate your account through your account management interface. Such deletions or terminations will take immediate effect in your account view. Residual copies of deleted messages and accounts may take up to 90 days to be deleted from our servers and may remain in our backup systems.
