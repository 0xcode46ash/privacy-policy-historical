# Recruiter.com - Privacy Policy

  
Recruiter.com respects the privacy of its visitors. Recruiter.com does not collect any personal information through the web site without the knowledge of the visitor. 

Our Recruiter.com web servers collect the IP address, operating system, browser software and geographic location of our visitors. We also collect information about which pages our visitors visit, the order in which the pages were visited, and which hyperlinks were clicked. This information helps us evaluate the effectiveness of various parts of our web site. 

Personal Information:  
When you subscribe to the Recruiter.com newsletter(s), you are required to provide certain information about yourself such as your name, address, email address, phone number, as well as your company details such as the company name, your job function and your skills set. This subscription information is captured in our database. Recruiter.com will take reasonable precautions to ensure the security of your information. 

This personal information will be used to contact you regarding Recruiter.com announcements, special offers and upcoming events. We do not provide your full contact information to third-party vendors. All promotions are sent out through www.recruiter.com. We depend on the revenue generated by these promotions to subsidize the operation and continued development effort of the Recruiter.com website, and to provide you with the critical market knowledge and insider information that you seek.
