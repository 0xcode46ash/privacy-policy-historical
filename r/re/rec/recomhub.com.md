# Privacy - Recomhub

We believe that customized, relevant advertising enhances your internet experience. Any collection of user information on Recomhub.com adheres to the following privacy policy

Recomhub collects non-identifiable user information like domain names, IP addresses, web browser, operating system, time of access, search patterns, and browsing behavior. This anonymous information is collected via cookies, log files, and web beacons to be shared with our affiliates, channel partners, collaborating merchants, and advertising companies.

[Opt Out](http://optout.networkadvertising.org/) of these automated tools.

We do not use automated tools to collect personally identifiable information. Personal information like your name, email, business information, and age must be submitted manually (like when you contact us or register for the newsletter). Any information of this nature submitted by you to Recomhub will be collected and stored.

Recomhub is not responsible for the gathering, storage, and use of your personal details by third-parties featured or linked to from our website. Every website and service has it’s own privacy policies, and we encourage users to learn more about how websites gather information.

We are proud to comply with the policies of industry organizations like the [ **Network Advertising Initiative** **(NAI)**](https://www.networkadvertising.org/), the [**Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA)**](http://digitaladvertisingalliance.org/), and the[ **Interactive Advertising Bureau (IAB)**](https://www.iab.com/). These organizations offer best practices and policy review in the field of online behavioral advertising.
