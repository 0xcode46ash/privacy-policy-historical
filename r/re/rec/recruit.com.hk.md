Services provided by Recruitonline.com are operated by Recruit Holdings Limited. Recruitonline.com is committed to protecting the privacy of users of its services and products. It is our policy to pledge to meet internationally recognized standard of personal data privacy protection and in particular to comply with the requirements of the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap.486) of the Laws of Hong Kong Special Administrative Region. In doing so, we will ensure compliance by our staff to the strictest standards of security and confidentiality. This policy applies to all registered and unregistered users of Recruitonline.com and may from time to time be updated, revised, reposted and changed where Recruitonline.com deems necessary. 

Please read the following carefully to understand our policy and practices regarding how your personal information will be treated. 

Recruitonline.com will collect your personal data (including your name, e-mail address, date of birth, country and occupation) when you register as a member of Recruitonline.com or when a member of Recruitonline.com furnishes us with your personal data or when you use services of Recruitonline.com 

The purposes for which your personal data are used or are to be used included: 

1\. to provide us with a better understanding on the demographics of the registered users;   
2\. to enable us to provide other services to better tailor your needs;   
3\. for direct marketing of our products and services;   
4\. to enable the provision and downloading of games; and   
5\. for such purposes as would enable us or our affiliates or the transferees of such data to provide website or other electronic services, which we believe, are suitable for or of interest to you. 

Recruitonline.com will use reasonable precautions to keep personal data disclosed to us secure and will not disclose your personal data including selection and/or downloading of games unless we are required to do so by law or we reasonably believe that such action is necessary to :- 

1\. comply with the law or applicable regulations;   
2\. protect and defend our rights and property;   
3\. protect against misuse or unauthorized use of Recruitonline.com; or   
4\. protect the personal safety or property of our users or the public.   
Recruitonline.com also links to a wide variety of other sites. We are not responsible for their contents or their privacy policies or how they treat information about their users. 

Your personal data will be kept until such times as you make a written request to terminate your membership of Recruitonline.com. 

You may choose not to receive any direct marketing e-mail or updates about special benefits, promotion or offers from us by writing or emailing to us at the address specified below. 

You may make data access and/or correction request to us at the following address. If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at **customer@recruit.com.hk.**
