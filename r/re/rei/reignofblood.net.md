# PP

ROB Privacy Policy

Contact: To contact us please email webmaster@imperial-empires.net

Third Party Adverts:  
ROB will contain adverts around the various pages, many of these being 3rd party adverts. We take no responsibility for what these adverts may contain, but we do try and restrict the type of adverts shown.

Underage:  
If you are under 13 years of age, you must ask permission from your parent/guardian to signup to ROB. If we get contacted informing us that you have not recived permission you will get your account deleted.

Personal Information:  
ROB registration requires you to input your personal information which includes a valid e-mail address and age to authenticate you. Your E-Mail is used to verify your account and to get in touch with you. Your IP address is logged and monitored on this site. This information is only available to staff and used for administration and monitoring purposes.Your information will not be sold or used on any other site but ROB. 

Public:  
This site contains areas where members voice their opinions, where ever this may be. We cannot take responsibility for this, all though we do moderate content on ROB, if you find anything which you find offense contact a member of staff. Also in the public areas there may be links to a external site, we do not moderate any of these external sites so we cannot be blamed for what it may contain. 

Passwords:  
Your password is created and used by you to login to your account. We try to protect our users passwords with care using encryption in db to secure them. But we cannot guarentee their safety, if in the unlikely event of unauthorized access to the db, we cannot be blamed for the loss of accounts/passwords. 

Sharing:  
We will not sell your passwords,emails,personal information to another company.
