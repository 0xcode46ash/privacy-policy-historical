# Privacy policy for our free reseller hosting program

### PRIVACY POLICY

  * **1\. Automatically Logged Information** \- We use your IP address to help identify visitors and to improve the website for different browser/operating system configurations, ultimately to provide the shared hosting reseller with better experience.
  * **2\. Email Communication** \- We will not intentionally monitor or disclose any of your e-mail messages unless we believe it's necessary or we are required to do so:

\- To enforce these Terms and Conditions,  
\- By law,  
\- To defend our corporation in any action,  
\- To protect our property, dignity and reputation.

ResellerCluster will not send to its customers any unsolicited information, including e-mails, except as mentioned below. New customers will receive a ResellerCluster welcome e-mail providing the user credentials and explaining the features provided by the service. ResellerCluster may also send periodic member letters to announce important service changes, new features, technical issues updates and news about the free reseller hosting program and services. 

  * **3\. Cookies** \- Our site uses cookies to identify you each time you visit our site. We also use cookies for website statistics. These statistics consist of the browser and operating system types, search engine keywords, referrer IDs, how long a visitor stays at the site, and pages viewed at our site. In no way do these statistics track users after they leave ResellerCluster's website.
  * **4\. Advertisers** \- Your personal information will never be sold or traded for any reason. You agree to respect the privacy and confidentiality of others. Since privacy is a function of your password, we encourage you to change your password frequently and to not share it with anyone.
  * **5\. Registration Forms** \- Contact information from the registration forms is used ONLY to contact the customer when necessary. ResellerCluster keeps your personally identifiable information private and does not share it with any unauthorized third parties.
  * **6\. External Links** \- This site contains links to other sites. ResellerCluster's website is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.
  * **7\. Security** \- This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.
  * **8\. Changes to our privacy policy** \- ResellerCluster reserves the rights to change the privacy policy without notice and the reseller [YOU] is solely responsible to check for such changes, follow up and comply with them.
