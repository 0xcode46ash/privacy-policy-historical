# Resume Companion | Welcome

I. Privacy Statement  
Resume Companion, LLC, is committed to protecting the privacy of each user of our applications. We take the security of information seriously and have established security standards and procedures to guard against unauthorized access to application user information. Resume Companion, LLC believes your personal information should be protected. We do not disclose or sell personal information such as your name, address, e-mail address, or telephone number to third parties for any purpose, as part of a mailing list, or otherwise, without your permission. However, we do retain the right, in our sole discretion, to offer you products/services of third party providers in which we think you may have an interest. Additionally, we will take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, and alteration. Resume Companion, LLC reserves the right to compile aggregate (not individual) demographic information provided by Users of the site to continually improve the usefulness of the site, and for our own statistical and marketing purposes. Users receive e-mail newsletters and account updates from Resume Companion, LLC and may opt out of these at any time by following instructions included in the e-mail or by contacting our Customer Service department at support@resumecompanion.com. 

II. Security Statement  
Our security system protects communications between your browser and our web server using industry-standard SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption. You can tell when data is being transmitted in a secure manner by looking for the secure key or lock icons at the bottom or your browser window. 

III. Copyright and Limitations on Use  
The resume builder applications on this URL are powered by Resume Companion, LLC. The information presented in these applications is copyrighted and may not be distributed or copied, including all text, images, and graphics, without prior written permission from Resume Companion, LLC. According to the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act of 1986, any unauthorized attempts by users to edit, update, or misrepresent any data on this site are prohibited and may be punishable by law. If the user has questions or concerns about the information that is presented here, he or she can forward them to Resume Companion, LLC at support@resumecompanion.com.
