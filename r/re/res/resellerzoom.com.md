# Reseller Zoom - Privacy Policy

By submitting an online order form, or by using Reseller Zoom’s service, Customer hereby agrees to the following Privacy Policy. Customer agrees that it shall comply with and require its end users to comply with this Privacy Policy, Reseller Zoom’s Terms of Service (TOS), Reseller Zoom’s Acceptable Use Policy (AUP), and Reseller Zoom’s No-Spam Policy (NSP). In this document, the word “Agreement,” with a capital “A,” refers to the TOS, the AUP, the NSP, and the Privacy Policy collectively.

**1\. Introduction.**

Hosting Zoom, Inc. respects the privacy of every individual who visits its Web sites and uses its services. This privacy statement provides notice of the standards and terms under which Hosting Zoom, Inc. protects the privacy of information supplied by visitors to sites on the World Wide Web that are owned and operated by Hosting Zoom, Inc., including www.resellerzoom.com. This privacy statement provides notice of our information collection practices and of the ways in which your information may be used. This policy may change from time to time, so please check back periodically to review this information.

On those sites where you voluntarily offer any feedback, data, answers, questions, comments, suggestions, ideas or the like, Hosting Zoom, Inc. will treat that portion of the information as non-confidential and non-proprietary and, except as otherwise expressed in this privacy statement, Hosting Zoom, Inc. assumes no obligation to protect such information from disclosure.

**3\. Non-Personally Identifiable (Generic) Information.**

In general, Hosting Zoom, Inc. gathers some generic information automatically. Generic information does not reveal the identity of the visitor. It usually includes information about the Internet address assigned to your computer, the number and frequency of visitors, and the Hosting Zoom, Inc. sites visited. Hosting Zoom, Inc. gathers this information for the limited purpose of determining customer service and Web site needs. We accomplish this by using certain technologies, including “cookies” (a technology that can be used to provide the visitor with tailored information about Hosting Zoom, Inc. services). Hosting Zoom, Inc. does not combine information collected in this way with any personally identifiable information. You can set your Web browser to notify you when you receive a cookie and you can refuse it. However, refusing some cookies may make it impossible to use or navigate the Hosting Zoom, Inc. Web site properly. 

**4\. Your Hosting Zoom, Inc.-Hosted Web Site, Bulletin Boards, Chat Rooms and Third Party Sites.**

Information that you disclose in a public space, including on any bulletin board, chat room, or Web site that Hosting Zoom, Inc. may host for you as part of your Hosting Zoom, Inc. services, is available to anyone else who visits that space. Hosting Zoom, Inc. cannot safeguard any information you disclose in these locations. Additionally, Hosting Zoom, Inc. Web sites may contain links to sites that belong to third parties unrelated to Hosting Zoom, Inc.. Hosting Zoom, Inc. cannot protect any information you may disclose at these sites and recommends that you review the privacy policy statements of those sites you visit.

Notwithstanding the foregoing and in compliance with applicable laws, Hosting Zoom, Inc. (i) cooperates fully with state, local, and federal officials in any investigation relating to any content (including personal or private electronic communications transmitted to Hosting Zoom, Inc.) or purported unlawful activities of any user of the Hosting Zoom, Inc. Service, and (ii) takes reasonable measures to protect its proprietary rights. For the limited purposes of accomplishing such cooperation and measures and in compliance with applicable laws, Hosting Zoom, Inc. may be required to disclose personally identifiable information.

In addition, Hosting Zoom, Inc. may elect to monitor the areas of communication of any kind (i) to satisfy any law, regulation, or government request; (ii) if such disclosure is necessary or appropriate to operate Hosting Zoom, Inc.’s business; or (iii) to protect the rights or property of Hosting Zoom, Inc. or others. 

**6\. Sale or Transfer of Hosting Zoom, Inc.’s Stock or Assets.**

In connection with the potential or actual sale or transfer of any of its interest in www.hostingzoom.com, and other sites owned by Hosting Zoom, Inc., and any of the assets or stock of the company, Hosting Zoom, Inc. reserves the right to sell or transfer your information (including, but not limited to, name, address information, and other information you provided to Hosting Zoom, Inc.) to a third party that (i) concentrates its business in Web hosting or communication products or services; (ii) agrees to be Hosting Zoom, Inc.’s successor in interest with regard to the maintenance and protection of information collected and maintained by Hosting Zoom, Inc.; and (iii) agrees to the obligations of this privacy statement. To the extent it is practicable to do so, this paragraph shall also apply in the event of any bankruptcy proceedings involving Hosting Zoom, Inc..

In the event that Hosting Zoom, Inc. plans to transfer your information to a third party incident to a potential or actual sale of any of Hosting Zoom, Inc.’s assets or stock, or incident to any bankruptcy proceedings involving Hosting Zoom, Inc., Hosting Zoom, Inc. will:

• Notify you by e-mail, at the e-mail address then on file for your account, at least thirty (30) days prior to the transfer taking place; and  
• Give you the opportunity to decide whether you wish for your information to be transferred to the third party. Please note, however, that if you choose not to allow your information to be transferred to the third party, you may have to terminate or cancel your service with Hosting Zoom, Inc.. At the time of providing notice to you of the upcoming transfer of information to a third party, Hosting Zoom, Inc. will tell you whether you will have to terminate or cancel your service, should you decide not to allow Hosting Zoom, Inc. to transfer your information to the third party.
