**Privacy Policy**

Restaurant.com is committed to safeguarding your online privacy. Because we respect your privacy, Restaurant.com operates its Web site(s) in accordance with the following guidelines: 

  * Restaurant.com will explicitly ask when it needs information that personally identifies you or allows Restaurant.com to contact you ("Personal Information"). Generally, we request this information when registering, when requesting a particular service, or when entering a sweepstakes or contest. When possible, Restaurant.com will provide you with the means to make sure that your Personal Information is correct and current. We encourage you, our user, to contact us if any of your information is ever incorrect. 
  * Restaurant.com will not collect personally identifiable information from any person under the age of 13 years old. If a child under the age of 13 years of age attempts to register with Restaurant.com, we will not consider the request unless a parent or guardian establishes the account. Restaurant.com is committed to the protection and safety of children using the World Wide Web and recommends that children under the age of 13 years old seek permission from a parent or legal guardian before sending personal information to anyone online. 
  * Restaurant.com and its necessary service partners will use your Personal Information to operate the site and services and to inform you of new features, services, and products from Restaurant.com. 
  * You may stop the delivery of informational or promotional mail from Restaurant.com by following the instructions on the mail received. 
  * Restaurant.com may disclose Personal Information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Restaurant.com; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Restaurant.com or the users of Restaurant.com, and (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Restaurant.com, Restaurant.com itself, or the public. 
  * If at any time you believe that Restaurant.com has not adhered to these principles, please notify Restaurant.com by e-mail at [info@Restaurant.com](mailto:info@Restaurant.com) and we will use all commercially reasonable efforts to promptly determine and correct the problem. 
  * To read more about privacy policies and procedures on the Internet we suggest that you visit sites such as [www.ftc.gov](http://www.ftc.gov/), [www.epic.org](http://www.epic.org/), [www.aclu.org](http://www.aclu.org/), [www.privacyalliance.org](http://www.privacyalliance.org/) and [www.eff.org](http://www.eff.org/).
