# Privacy Policy | dataSpring

Effective Date: November 1, 2016

## Introduction

**dataSpring (“dataSpring” or “our” or “us” or “we” or “dS”) is committed to maintaining the privacy of users to our website (“Clients” “you” “Suppliers” “Contacts”) and to individuals who provide us personal information and data. This privacy policy applies to all websites offered by dataSpring. We refer to those products, services and websites collectively as “Services” in this policy. Although this Policy is not a contract and does not create any legal rights, it is a statement of dataSpring’s commitment to safeguarding private information.**

**You may always submit concerns regarding our Privacy Policy or our privacy practices to[contact@d8aspring.com](mailto:contact@d8aspring.com).**

## Information We Collect

dataSpring conducts online market research studies in numerous countries around the world using online surveys through our Services. We are interested in developing and keeping strong, deep and lasting relationships with all parties that have an interest in our products and services.

In order to operate our website in the most efficient manner, we collect certain data and information through automated means including, IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, information on actions or activities taken or engaged in on our website, and dates and times of website visits. Please refer to our “Cookie Policy” for more information.

Additionally, forms and applications on our website and within our web applications collect personally identifiable information, such as first name, last name, email address, physical address, job title, company name and phone number. By providing such personally identifiable information, you consent to its collection.

dataSpring’s services are not intended for children under the age of 13. If you are under 13 years old, please do not provide personally identifiable information of any kind whatsoever on the dataSpring. If you are the parent of a child under 13 and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at [contact@d8aspring.com](mailto:contact@d8aspring.com) and we will remove that information.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information that you provide us via this website and mobile app in the following ways:

  * To process any inquiries raised by you and other communications initiated by you in relation to your dealings with dataSpring and any services provided by the Company;
  * To keep you informed of dataSpring’s products, services and activities;
  * To produce and deliver requested products, services and reports; and
  * To provide requested quotes, panel books and brochures, we require that you provide personally identifiable information (PII) to ensure delivery and execution of your request.



dataSpring does not share or collect personally identifiable information with other third-party organizations for their marketing, advertising or promotional use without your consent or except as part of a specific program or feature for which you will have the ability to opt-in or opt-out.

We reserve the right to disclose any information that is gathered when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on our website.

Any information collected via this website will be treated in the strictest confidence and in accordance with all the pertinent laws and regulations in the countries in which we do business.

## How We Protect Your Personal Information

dataSpring uses reasonable security measures in an effort to prevent loss, misuse and alteration of information under our control; these include on-line and off-line physical, electronic and managerial safeguards.

dataSpring cannot, however, guarantee protection of all information in all cases. For example, we cannot guarantee protection against interception, misappropriation, misuse, or alteration, or that your information will not be disclosed or accessed by accidental circumstances or by the unauthorized acts of others. Consequently, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us and you do so at your own risk.

If you have any questions about the security of our website or web applications, you can send an e-mail to [contact@d8aspring.com](mailto:contact@d8aspring.com).
