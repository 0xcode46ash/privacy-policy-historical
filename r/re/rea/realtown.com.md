# InternetCrusade.com

Our Privacy Policy is posted in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. The following statements disclose general information related to the gathering and use of any personal information through any web site maintained directly by the InternetCrusade®. This privacy policy may be updated from time to time, so please check back frequently. 

1\. Information volunteered by visitors is collected by InternetCrusade® to assist us in better serving you. This could include any personal information and/or Web site registrations (name, eMail, URL). This information may be used to send occasional updates to you regarding our services. 

2\. Within the scope of our web site and services, you may encounter web sites or affiliate content that solicit your eMail address or other personal information. It is up to you to evaluate the privacy policy for each of these entities. In no event will InternetCrusade® be held liable. 

At any time, you may give notice that you no longer wish to receive future information from InternetCrusade® by sending an eMail to [Privacy@InternetCrusade.com](mailto:Privacy@InternetCrusade.com) with the word "REMOVE" in the subject line. You may also modify any information previously sent to us by sending an eMail to [Privacy@InternetCrusade.com](mailto:Privacy@InternetCrusade.com)

3\. Your IP address may be logged for identification purposes, to help diagnose the functionality of our server, or to help enforce certain restrictions regarding the use of free services that we may offer. This information is not shared or sold to any third parties, and would only be made available to law enforcement agencies with a valid court order. Your IP address is not collected for advertising purposes and is never shared with advertisers. 

4\. This site contains links to other sites. InternetCrusade® is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

5\. Our site statistical measurement tools reveal to us your browser type and the referring page from which you are visiting us; this helps us maintain our site design for the browser most visitors are using as well as helps us identify where visitors are coming from or learning about us online. 

6\. Credit information that you and credit authorizers provide when you make payments by credit card or electronic check for products or other services will only be used to process the transactions you request. This information will be provided to and maintained by reputable credit reporting databases, but will never be sold, shared or provided to other third parties. 

7\. This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. We use secure servers and maintain security procedures and standards which we believe are as safe as today's technology permits. We test these procedures and modify them regularly as new technologies become feasible. 

8\. Basically, we collect information that is voluntarily provided to us and we use it only for the purpose it was provided for. If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, the practices of this web site, or your dealings with this web site, please do not hesitate to contact us by sending an eMail to [Privacy@InternetCrusade.com](mailto:Privacy@InternetCrusade.com) If at any time you think these policies have not been followed by InternetCrusade®, please let us know by sending an eMail to [Privacy@InternetCrusade.com](mailto:Privacy@InternetCrusade.com) and we will make every reasonable effort to correct the problem.
