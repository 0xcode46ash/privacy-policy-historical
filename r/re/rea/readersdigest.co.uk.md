# Privacy Policy| Magazines, Books, DVDs and CDs | Reader's Digest UK

**PRIVACY POLICY  
  
READER'S DIGEST ONLINE PRIVACY PLEDGE**

In over 80 years in the Direct Marketing business, Reader's Digest has adhered to the highest standards in assuring our customers' privacy. All information - including customer accounts, transactions, and all correspondence, is handled with the utmost care. 

**YOUR EMAIL INFORMATION  
** Once you enter your email address in a Reader's Digest web site, including rdplus.net (i.e. submitting a form, entering a competition, etc.), you may receive email correspondence from Reader's Digest.Â  You will always be able to choose not to receive emails from us, either at the time of completing a form, or by following the [unsubscribe instructions](https://myrd.readersdigest.co.uk/myrd/forms.nsf/webforms/unsubscribe) in each email that we send out.

 **YOUR DATA PROTECTION RIGHTS**

Individuals may obtain details of their own data from Reader's Digest. For details of how to inspect your own data, for which a charge will be made, please **[click here](http://www.readersdigest.co.uk/contact-us-i-6.html)** to contact us. 

The Reader's Digest Association Ltd (RD) may pass your customer information to other RD companies and tell you about other RD products and services, by post, telephone or email. We may also pass your information to other carefully selected companies whose offers may be of interest to you. If you do not wish to receive offers from either RD or other companies, please [click here](http://www.readersdigest.co.uk/contact-us-i-6.html) to contact us.

 **THIRD PARTY ADVERTISEMENTS AND COOKIES**

www.readersdigest.co.uk contains third party advertisements that may employ the use of cookies (small text files stored on your computer) - such cookies will not be used to collect any personally identifiable information about you.
