# Privacy Policy

ExoScience does not collect personal information during your visit to this site. Non-personal information that is collected (eg. IP address, browser, computer OS etc.) is used internally, and is not sold. 

> This site, ExoScience - Science of Space, uses the services of Engage for the serving and/or targeting of ads, promotions and other marketing messages. To do this, Engage collects anonymous data through the use of cookies. To learn more about Engage, including your ability to opt out of the Engage system, go to <http://www.engage.com/privacy>.
