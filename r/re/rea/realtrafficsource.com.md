# Real Traffic Source

#### Real Traffic Source - Privacy

We at Traffic Source respect and are committed to protecting our clients' privacy. With this intention we have adopted our Privacy Policy.

Under no circumstances will we sell your personal information with any person or organization. We will not sell or otherwise disclose, except as required by law, your email address or any other information we collect as a result of your visit to our site or your becoming a customer/reseller. Traffic Source does not engage in sending unsolicited email, and we will not knowingly participate in business with anyone who does. We may send out marketing materials sent out directly by Traffic Source about special advertising offers.
