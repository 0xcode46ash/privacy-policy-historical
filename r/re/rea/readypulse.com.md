# ReadyPulse - Social Marketing Software, Social Media Analytics

ReadyPulse is a service by Social Yantra Inc. 

Social Yantra respects your rights to privacy and is committed to treating information about you responsibly. 

Social Yantra will collect and use information through our websites including SocialYantra.com in the ways disclosed in this statement. This statement applies only to information collected at ReadyPulse.com. 

Social Yantra collects email addresses and contact information only when our website visitors elect to provide us with such details. The information we collect is used for internal review, used to improve the content of our website, to notify consumers of product and content updates to our website, and/or used to contact consumers for marketing communication. 

We answer emails or respond to email comments directly. We do not sell or otherwise disclose email addresses to third parties. Our websites contain links to other sites. 

Social Yantra is not responsible for the content or privacy policies of these sites. Changes or updates to our privacy policy will be posted on this page.
