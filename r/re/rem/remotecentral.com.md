##### Our Privacy Policy:

Remote Central recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of you as a user of this website. Remote Central does not collect Personal Information about you except for the information that you voluntarily provide (such as data submitted in contest entries, forum posts, emails or file submissions where you do not specifically indicate that the information contained within is to be considered confidential). All Personal Information is treated confidentially and is not released to any third-party entity. Ever. And we mean it.
