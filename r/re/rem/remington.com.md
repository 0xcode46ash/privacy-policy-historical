# Privacy Policy - On Line Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy – August 2010**

This policy covers The Freedom Group Family of Companies websites and discusses the use of information collected at these sites. Below is a summary of Freedom Group Family of Companies’ online privacy policy. 

**Information We Collect**  
We may use cookies or other programs to collect basic, non-identifiable information from you as you explore the Freedom Group Family of Companies sites. This data is used to enhance and personalize your web experience with us, and to provide us with basic information about how users access and benefit from our sites. 

You would only be asked for personally identifiable information to serve one or more specific purposes, including purchasing products and receiving newsletters or other company information, like product announcements. We will not provide any of your personal information to other companies or individuals without your permission, but we may need to provide your name and delivery address to third parties that The Freedom Group’s Family of Companies uses for the purposes of delivering specific services to you (e.g., a shipping company or fulfillment house if you have asked us to send something to you). Please note that Freedom Group Family of Companies will fully cooperate with any court order or other legal process properly requesting information that we hold. 

Personal financial information will only be used for the reason it was provided, for example to complete a purchase with one of the Freedom Group Family of Companies. 

If you sign up for an electronic newsletter or e-mail list, each message will include instructions on how to “unsubscribe” from that list. We give you the opportunity to elect not to receive future materials from us. 

**Protecting Your Privacy**   
The Freedom Group Family of Companies will take reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. All of our websites that collect information will process and store that information in databases located in the United States. 

Our websites do provide links to third party sites. Since we do not control those websites, we encourage you to review the privacy policies posted on these third party sites and any other sites that you visit. Additionally, the existence of any hyperlink does not imply that Freedom Group Family of Companies endorses the linked company, product or service. 

**Children’s Privacy  
** None of the websites covered by this policy knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under age 13. If we discover that a child under the age of 13 has provided us with any personally identifiable information, we will delete that information from our systems. 

The Freedom Group Family of Companies encourages parents to go online with their children. Parents should understand the sites their kids are visiting and which sites are appropriate. There are parental control tools available such as browsers and filtering software that prevent children from accessing inappropriate sites. 

**Updating Information**  
You can help The Freedom Group Family of Companies maintain the accuracy of your information by notifying us of any changes to your address, phone number or e-mail address. If you have previously given your personal information to us and no longer want to be contacted via e-mail, please contact us. If at any time you wish to stop receiving an electronic newsletter you can unsubscribe as explained in the newsletter. 

If you have previously given your personal information to us through our online magazine and no longer want to be contacted via e-mail, please unsubscribe using the link at the bottom of the e-mail. 

If you need assistance in updating your information, please contact us. To aid our processing of your request, please include your contact information, the name of the service to which you register or to which you need the changes applied, and the details of the change (e.g., update email address, unsubscribe from a newsletter). 

**Ownership**  
One or more of The Freedom Group Family of Companies owns all the information presented on its websites and has copyrights on such information. All opinions, ideas, suggestions, and other feedback submitted through our websites may be used by Freedom Group Family of Companies and its affiliates, without any restriction and free of charge, and nothing contained in this policy shall limit or otherwise affect this. 

The Freedom Group Family of Companies include Remington, Marlin Firearms, Bushmaster Firearms International, DPMS/Panther Arms, Harrington & Richardson, Barnes Bullets, Advanced Armament Corp., Mountain Khakis, EOTAC, Dakota Arms, L.C. Smith, The Parker Gun Companies, and affiliates such as Remington Outdoor Foundation, a 501 (C)(3) organization founded by Remington Arms Company, Inc. 

**Questions?**  
The Freedom Group Family of Companies welcomes comments and questions on this policy. We are dedicated to protecting your personal information, and will make reasonable efforts to keep that information secure. We may occasionally update this policy. All revisions will be posted to this site and will become effective on the date that the modified policy is posted on our site. 

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please notify us. This policy was last updated on August 20, 2010.
