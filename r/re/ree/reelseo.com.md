# ReelSEO.com Privacy Policy

At ReelSEO, we have created this privacy statement in order to show our commitment to your privacy. This page discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for the website reelseo.com.

## Information Gathering

As with most other websites, we automatically collect and utilize the data contained in our site log files. The information in the log files will include your IP (internet protocol) address, your ISP, browser used (IE, Firefox, Safari, etc...), the date and time you visited our site and which URLs you visited throughout our site.

We track your activity with Google Analytics and WordPress Analytics.

## Advertising

Cookies received with banner ads are collected by their respective owners, and we do not have access to this information. Cookies are used on ReelSEO only for login, social sharing, commenting, and other related community purposes.

## External Links

This site contains links to other sites including in some rare cases, affiliate links. ReelSEO.com and it's authors are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.  Please refer to the destination website policies for such information.

## Personal Information

When you comment or subscribe to an email list, we will ask for your name, email address and additional information (optional). When you register on the site or when you sign up for special events, webcasts, or whitepapers, we may request additional info. Your email and other information may be used for contacting you about updates to ReelSEO and if requested, for our newsletters. This site complies with all rules and requirements of the [CAN-SPAM Act](http://transition.fcc.gov/cgb/consumerfacts/canspam.html).

## Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.

## Contacting the Web Site

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact us at the following page: [About us.](http://www.reelseo.com/about/)
