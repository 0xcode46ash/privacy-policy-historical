# reQall: Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to QTech, Inc. (“QTech”). This Web Site Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") tells our visitors ("You") about:

  1. the information QTech, Inc. ("QTech") collects through our website, www.qtechinc.com (“Site”) and through the use of QTech services such as reQall and speaQ (“Services”); and 
  2. how we use that information.

**Consent**

By accessing our Site or using the Services, You are consenting to the information collection and use practices described in this Privacy Policy, as modified from time to time by us. If we decide to change our Privacy Policy, we will post a new statement on our Site and change the date at the top of the statement. Therefore, we encourage You to check the date of our Privacy Policy whenever You visit this Site for any updates or changes.

**Types of Information**

_Contact Information  
_ When You register with us, Your personal information, including Your name and email address, is stored with us (“Contact Information”). We collect this data so we may communicate with You individually or as part of our mailing list. This information is not shared with third parties except in the circumstances described below.  
_Audio Recordings  
_ During Your use of the reQall or speaQ service, You may transmit certain audio recordings, communcations or information to QTech (“Audio Commnunications”). 

**How We Use Your Information:**

  * QTech’s primary purpose of collecting and using the Contact Information is to provide You with a smooth, efficient and customized experience when using the QTech Services. We may use the Contact Information to provide customer support and troubleshoot problems. We may also use the Contact Information to send you information related to your account or other QTech Service offerings and other promotional offerings.
  * QTech’s primary purpose of using and transmitting any Audio Communications is in order to provide You with the QTech Services.
  * In addition, the Services may include relevant advertising and related links based on Your location, Your call history and other information related to Your use of the Services.
  * We may also use Your Contact Information and Audio Communications for the purposes of: 
    * Providing our products and services to other users, including the display of customized content and advertising;
    * Auditing, research and analysis in order to maintain, protect and improve our services;
    * Ensuring the technical functioning of our network; and
    * Developing new services.

**Keeping Your Information**

QTech will retain Your Contact Information and Audio Communications for as long as it is necessary to perform the Services, carry out marketing activities or comply with applicable legislation.

**Sharing Your Information**

  * We do not sell, rent or otherwise share Your Contact Information or Audio Communications with any third parties except in the limited circumstance of when we are compelled to do so by a valid, binding court order or subpoena, and in such cases we will only provide such information as minimally required to comply with the request. 
  * When You send email (by forwarding or replying to a voicemail), QTech includes information such as Your email address and the email itself as part of that email.
  * Although we don't currently work with advertisers, if we did in the future, we would only provide them with aggregated non-personal information such as the number of times one of their ads was clicked.
