# The Right Start - Babies to Kids

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about you to process your orders, provide you with a personalized shopping experience and bring selected partners and opportunities to your attention. Please feel free to contact us with any specific questions you may have. 

This site contains links to other sites as a convenience and for informational purposes only. Right Start and RightStart.com are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

Cookie Technology  


RightStart.com uses a feature of your Internet Web browser called a "cookie" to assign a unique identification to your computer. Cookies are files that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive. Cookies enable RightStart.com to enhance your site experience by providing certain information about your computer and your visits to the web site. Such information may also be used for internal review in aggregate and non-personally identifiable form. Cookies can also be used to help save and retrieve Customer usernames used on the RightStart.com site, to tell us whether you have visited RightStart.com previously, to retrieve information on past purchases and other preferences. This is done for your convenience and means that you do not have to reenter this information on every visit to RightStart.com. 

While most browsers are configured to accept cookies, you can set your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent to your computer. Some portions of this web site, however, may not function properly if cookies are refused. 

Cookies cannot be used by RightStart.com to retrieve any personal information about you unless you have registered with or purchased from RightStart.com. RightStart.com does not and cannot use cookies to retrieve personal information about you or your family (e.g., names, ages, addresses, email address, etc.) from your computer. We can only use information specifically provided by you. 

Collection of Personally Identifying Information  


Our web site collects no personally identifying information about you or your family (e.g., names, ages, addresses, email address, etc.) except when you specifically submit this information. 

Minors are also advised to obtain parental permission before filling out any on-line forms. Please see our section on Parental Supervision for further details. 

Use of Information  


We do not sell your personally identifiable information to others. We may provide you information about products and services from partners we select that we think may be of interest to you. 

Your Consent  


By using our web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by RightStart.com. We assure you that if we make any changes to our privacy policy, we will post all changes - so you will know exactly what information we are collecting, how we're using it, and any circumstances where we are disclosing it. 

We encourage you to visit this page periodically for any changes.
