### Privacy Policy

I respect your privacy, so the following notices are given in order to be as transparent as possible:

  * Google AdSense — Google uses cookies and/or Web beacons in order to collect various information during the ad serving process. For more information, visit [Google’s Privacy Center](http://www.google.com/privacypolicy.html).

Adsense also may utilize third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit this website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, a good place to start is [Google’s list of possible third-party advertisers](https://www.google.com/adsense/support/bin/answer.py?answer=94149).

  * Mint Statistics — The Mint statistics package uses cookies to track your usage of this site. The majority of this information is anonymous (i.e., is not linked to you), but if you have left a comment on this site, Mint links your name with which sites you’re visiting, how often, and for how long. This information is not, however, publicly available.
