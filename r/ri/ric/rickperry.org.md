# Rick Perry for President 2012 | Privacy Policy

RickPerry.org, Inc. is responsible for the content produced on RickPerry.org. We link to other websites and blogs, but we are not responsible for the content or the privacy policies of these websites or blogs. Because we have no control over web sites that we provide links for, you agree and acknowledge that RickPerry.org, Inc. is not responsible for the availability of resources or external sites and does not endorse and is not responsible or liable for any content provided therein. Accordingly, you also agree and acknowledge that Texans for Rick Perry will not be responsible or liable, directly or indirectly, for any damage or loss caused or alleged to be caused by or in connection with use of or reliance of any such content, services on or through any such site or resource or goods. We encourage you to read the privacy statements on each web site you visit.

While using this website, you do not have to identify yourself or divulge personal information. If you should choose to give us your personal information, you decide the amount of information you wish to provide.

To comply with federal election law and Federal Election Commission regulations, we are required to make our best efforts to collect and report the name, address, employer, and occupation of individuals whose contributions aggregate in excess of $200 per election cycle. Online contributions are non-refundable.

Online contributions to RickPerry.org, Inc. are transmitted using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption. SSL is a coding system that lets your browser automatically encrypt data before you send it. We also protect account data by placing it on a secure portion of our Website. No data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, however, so we cannot ensure or warrant the security of such data. In the event that insufficient information is obtained to complete a contribution transaction or the contribution transaction transpired unsuccessfully, RickPerry.org, Inc. may attempt to follow-up with you via information you provided to allow you to complete the contribution transaction.

We collect the IP addresses of our visitors and use that data to gather broad demographic information about our visitors. We use cookies to enhance your experience at RickPerry.org, Inc. A cookie is a small file that is placed on your hard drive. Cookies do not contain any personal information about you. You can opt-out of our use of cookies by disabling cookies on your browser.

All materials on this website are protected by copyrights, trademarks, and/or other intellectual property rights. These materials are owned by RickPerry.org, Inc. or are used with permission of their owners or as otherwise authorized by law. Use of this website does not give any user a license or right under any patent, copyright, trademark or other intellectual property or personal right of Campaign or any third party. You also agree to observe the privacy of all other users and not to use the personal information of any other user for any commercial, promotional or other purpose. If you believe that any material on this website infringes your copyright, please notify us pursuant to the Digital Millennium Copyright Act.

### We reserve the right to store any information about the people you contact through our website.

We have established and maintain profiles and pages on several third-party social-networking and user-generated content websites. RickPerry.org, Inc. is not responsible for the content of individual users or the privacy policies of these websites. We may contact you via these website using any contact methods that exist on the website and are within the website’s terms of service agreement. RickPerry.org, Inc. reserves the right to republish any content that you publicly post or share on our social-networking and user-generated pages without compensation for use on our website or in any other communications or advertisements and for use with other individuals or entities, including other internet user, as well as print or broadcast news media to use such materials in their communications.

If you choose to upload photos on Rick Perry’s Facebook Page or RickPerry.org, we reserve the right to use those photos for any purpose. By uploading, you certify that you own any and all copyrights in the materials, and the photographs do not contain inappropriate content.

We may use your email address to provide you news and information about RickPerry.org, Inc. We may also use your zip code and state to send you email messages about upcoming events or activities in your area. By registering with this website and submitting personally identifiable information to RickPerry.org, Inc., you agree that RickPerry.org, Inc. may share your name, email address, physical address and other demographic or response information with other persons or entities that share common goals or beliefs with RickPerry.org, Inc. You may receive communications from these other persons or entities. By submitting materials to RickPerry.org, Inc., you consent to our sharing of your personally identifiable information.

When you sign one of our petitions, we treat your name, city, state, and any comments as public information. We may provide compilations of petitions, with your comments, to national leaders, without disclosing email addresses. We may also make comments along with your city and state available to the press and public online.

This website complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998. We do not knowingly contact or collect personal information from children under 13. This website is not intended to solicit information of any kind from children under 13. If we are informed that we have unintentionally received personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information.

### Text Messaging – Terms and Conditions

Opt-out and Support: Text “STOP” to 95613 from your mobile phone and we will unsubscribe you from our SMS text messaging service immediately. You will receive an OPT-OUT confirmation message and will not receive any additional messages until you re-register on our website OR you initiate additional text requests to the 95613 short code.

Billing/Pricing: RickPerry.org, Inc. does not charge for texting keywords to 95613. Standard text messaging rates apply. Check your wireless service plan as your carrier may charge you for text messages. Other charges (such as Msg & Data Rates) may also apply.

Parents Consent: If you are under 13 years old please get your parents’ permission before you subscribe.
