**Rio Grande's Privacy Policy**

Rio Grande is committed to ensuring the privacy of its members. We comply with guidelines set by various privacy organizations so we can offer peace of mind to all visitors of our website.

For each visitor to our website, our Web server automatically recognizes the consumer's domain name and e-mail address (where possible). We collect this information, as well as the e-mail addresses of those who post messages on our feedback forms, communicate with us via e-mail, and make postings to our chat room. We also review which pages consumers access and information volunteered by the consumer, such as surveys and/or site registrations.

We use the information we collect for our own marketing purposes; **we do not share any of this information with other organizations.**

We collect your personal information with your consent. If you give your postal address on-line, you may receive periodic mailings from Rio Grande with information about new products and services or upcoming events. If you supply us with your telephone number, we may contact you with information about orders you have placed on-line. If you do not want to receive mailings, phone calls, or e-mail from us in the future, please let us know. Contact us and we'll honor your request.
