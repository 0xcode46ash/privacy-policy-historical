# Privacy

**OUR PRIVACY POLICY**

This Website is committed to protecting the privacy of our online visitors as well as our members. We collect information from online visitors and members who

create an account with our membership site and/or ask to be on our newsletter mailing list and/or receive periodic email announcements . Anyone who wishes can choose to be removed from our mailing list or our membership site at anytime. 

All online visitor and member data collected by http://RipTideArmy.com is protected against unauthorized access. We will not sell, trade, or give your personal information to other companies or organizations.
