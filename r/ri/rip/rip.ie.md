# Privacy: RIP.ie - Death Notices Ireland

## Privacy

This statement relates only to the RIP.ie website. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of other websites that may be linked to from RIP.ie

**Collection and use of personal information**

RIP.ie does not collect any personal data about you on this website, apart from information which you provide to us voluntarily (for example by e-mailing us or by using any of our online forms). We do not make any information that you provide to us available to any third parties, and such information is used by RIP.ie only for the purposes for which you provided it. 

**Collection and use of technical information**

The RIP.ie does not use cookies, apart from temporary "session" cookies which enable your web browser to remember which pages on the site you have already viewed. You can use this website even if you have disabled the cookies function in your web browser.

All matters and disputes concerning privacy issues involving the RIP.ie website are subject to the laws and jurisdiction of the Republic of Ireland.
