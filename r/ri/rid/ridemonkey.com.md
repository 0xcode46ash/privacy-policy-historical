# Ridemonkey privacy policy

**Personal Information**  
At Ridemonkey.com, we require you to register before using many of our interactive features. This helps us to maintain the highest integrity for user generated content. During registration we ask you for personal information. We will never give out your individual personal information to anyone unless required by law. 

**Cookies**  
Like most other web sites requiring registration, Ridemonkey.com uses "cookies". This allows you to conveniently save your password without having to re-enter it every time you visit the site. This is the only thing that cookies are used for. Ridemonkey.com does not use cookies to track patterns of activity within the site. To learn more about cookies, click on your browser's help menu. 

**Public Forums**  
Ridemonkey.com's discussion forums are public forums. Please be cautious of any personal information you decide to disclose. Ridemonkey.com is not responsible for any actions resulting from the information you post. 

**Changes to this Privacy Policy**  
Ridemonkey.com may change how we collect, use or treat your personal information. Please check back periodically to review any changes that we have made to the Privacy Policy.
