# RINGSIDE COLLECTIBLES WWE Toys, Wrestling Action Figures, Jakks Pacific, Classic Superstars Action F: Privacy Policy

**PRIVACY POLICY**

**Commitment to Privacy**

We know you're concerned about protecting your privacy online. So are we. At Ringside Collectibles, we treat your personal information (especially your credit card number) with the highest standards of safety, security and confidentiality. Your privacy is very important to us. Ringside Collectibles respects your concerns about maintaining strict privacy. Any information that you divulge to us is kept in confidence.

**What Information Do You Gather About Me?**

Ringside Collectibles use the customer information collected at ringsidecollectibles.com. We don't collect personal information without your knowledge. We don't sell, rent or give your personal information to unrelated parties for them to use for themselves or anyone other than www.ringsidecollectibles.com and its affiliates without your prior permission. Examples of personal information include: your full name, home address, billing address, e-mail address and phone number.

This policy explains how we collect information about you and how we use that information. We also outline the choices you have. By shopping at ringsidecollectibles.com, you accept the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, contact us via e-mail. 

**What we gather:** We need your name, billing address, shipping address, e-mail address, billing phone number, shipping phone number and credit card number to process and ship your order. If we have a question about your order, we may contact you by e-mail or by phone. For example, we may call you to verify your credit card information on a large purchase. We do this for your protection.

**Who we share it with:** We do not share any of your information that we collect. Ringsidecollectibles.com doesn't sell, rent or give information about you to unrelated parties for them to use for himself or herself or anyone other than ringsidecollectibles.com. 

**Information about kids:** Our site is designed for adults and adults who make purchases for kids. Ringsidecollectibles.com doesn't knowingly solicit or collect information from children who are 12 or younger. We post a prominent warning that tells children 12 and younger to seek assistance from a parent or guardian before making a purchase at ringsidecollectibles.com

**Contacting Ringsidecollectibles.com Customer Service:** If you contact ringsidecollectibles.com customer service, we will use your name, e-mail address, phone number and order history to answer your question or solve your problem quickly. 

**Legal requests:** We may release customer account or other information if we're advised that release is necessary to comply with the law, or to protect the rights, property and safety of ringsidecollectibles.com, our customers or others. This may include the exchange of information with companies and organizations or law enforcement for loss prevention and credit risk reduction.

**Protecting your password:** We encrypt your password to ensure no one else has access to your account. If you forget your password, you can create a new one. You can do your part to protect your personal information by keeping your password a secret and making sure you log off completely if you share a computer.

**What about My Account?**

**Creating an account:** Creating an account is required to purchase from ringsidecollectibles.com. It also makes it even faster and easier to shop at ringsidecollectibles.com.

**Storing your credit card number:** Your credit card information is not kept on file as part of Your Account. You don't need to store your credit card number at ringsidecollectibles.com to use Checkout. You re-enter your credit card number each time you checkout.

**Contact Ringsidecollectibles.com**

If you have more questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site or your dealings with ringsidecollectibles.com, e-mail mail to: cs@ringsidecollectibles.net.
