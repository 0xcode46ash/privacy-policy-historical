# RPGamer > Privacy & User Policies

**Terms Of Use**

By visiting or using any document or resouce contained on this _website_ \- hereafter to be read as "[http://www.rpgamer.com/](/)" - you agree to be bound by the provisions of these _Privacy & User Policies_. If at any time you have questions or concerns regarding the information provided by this document, please forward all such discourse to [webmaster@rpgamer.com](mailto:webmaster@rpgamer.com?subject=Privacy and User Policies) where the appropriate administrator will respond to you as quickly as possible. _RPGamer_ \- hereafter to be read as "RPGamer and/or its affiliates" - reserves the right to modify, add, or remove portions of these policies at any time; it is therefore in your best interest to review this document periodically.

**Collection & Dissemination of Information**

In order to continue to provide the best possible environment and content for our visitors, RPGamer collects demographic information of an impersonal nature from everyone that accesses our website. This information includes but is not limited to the following:

  * Browser Software & Version
  * Operating System Software & Version
  * Native Language Information
  * World Geo Location Information
  * Referrer & Bounce Information
  * Advertising & Marketing Information
  * Pageview Information
  * Keyword & Search Information
  * General System Information



RPGamer reserves the right to periodically collect other kinds of impersonal demographic information by selecting a random portion of our readership to take part in a demographic survey. None of the impersonal information collected for our internal demography will ever be shared with any person or company with the exception of our business affiliates.

Information of a personally identifying nature will never be collected without a visitor's prior knowledge. RPGamer may collect personally identifying information as part of a contest, for membership to our forum, for access to our IRC chat channels, for utilization of our newsletter, for the purposes of tracking unique user patterns, or to collect information for the purposes of moderating one of our public outlets. This information includes but is not limited to the following:

  * First & Last Name
  * Snail Mail Address
  * Email Address
  * Age
  * Sex
  * IP Address



RPGamer adheres explicitly to the provisions of _COPPA_ \- hereafter to be read as "Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998"; you can access further information about COPPA and protecting the rights of children at <http://www.ftc.gov/ogc/coppa.htm>. None of the personally identifiable information collected for our internal demography will ever be shared with any person or company with the exception of our contest sponsors.

**Cookies**

Cookies are small text files that can be used to store and pass information from page to page on the internet. RPGamer uses cookies in a number of functions across our website. These functions include but are not limited to the following:

  * Message Forum
  * Weekly Poll
  * Advertising & Marketing



**Advertising & Marketing**

RPGamer uses the resources of a third party company in order to serve targeted advertising. Our advertising company - [Gorilla Nation Media](http://www.gorillanation.com/) \- uses cookies in order to more successfully target advertising to the needs of our viewers; should you decide to opt out of this process you can find more information at <http://www.networkadvertising.org/consumer/opt_out.asp>.

RPGamer is affiliated with a number of third party companies in order to provide information about and access to products that relate to our content. Any sales provided to these companies through our affiliate network provide a monetary return to RPGamer that will be used to help maintain our website. Our affiliates include but are not limited to the following:

  * Anime Books
  * Anime Nation
  * Game Music Online
  * Play Asia
