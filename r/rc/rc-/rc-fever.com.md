**What This Privacy Policy Covers**  


  * This policy covers how Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store treats personal information that Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store collects and receives, including information related to your past use of Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available. 
  * This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store does not own or control, or to people that Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store does not employ or manage. In addition, certain Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store associated companies such as [SanDisk](http://www.sandisk.com/) and [Sony](http://www.sony.com/) have their own privacy statements which can be viewed by clicking on the links. 

**Information Collection and Use**

**General**  


  * Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store collects personal information when you register with Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store, when you use Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store products or services, when you visit Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store pages or the pages of certain Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store partners, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies. 
  * When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. For some financial products and services we may also ask for your address, Social Security number, and information about your assets. Once you register with Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us. 
  * Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners, including information about your use of financial products and services that we offer. 
  * Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store cookie information, and the page you request. 
  * Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients. 

**Children**  


  * When a child under age 13 attempts to register with Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store, we ask that he or she have a parent or guardian create a Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store Account to obtain parental permission. 
  * Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store will not contact children under age 13 about special offers or for marketing purposes without a parent's permission. 
  * Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store does not ask a child under age 13 for more personal information, as a condition of participation, than is reasonably necessary to participate in a given activity or promotion. 

**Information Sharing and Disclosure**  


  * Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances: 
    * We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store communicate with you about offers from Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information. 
    * We have a parent's permission to share the information if the user is a child under age 13. Parents have the option of allowing Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store to collect and use their child's information without consenting to Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store sharing of this information with people and companies who may use this information for their own purposes; 
    * We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; 
    * We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law. 
    * We transfer information about you if Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy. 
  * Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store displays targeted advertisements based on personal information. Advertisers (including ad serving companies) may assume that people who interact with, view, or click on targeted ads meet the targeting criteria - for example, women ages 18-24 from a particular geographic area. 
    * Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store does not provide any personal information to the advertiser when you interact with or view a targeted ad. However, by interacting with or viewing an ad you are consenting to the possibility that the advertiser will make the assumption that you meet the targeting criteria used to display the ad. 
    * Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store advertisers include financial service providers (such as banks, insurance agents, stock brokers and mortgage lenders) and non-financial companies (such as stores, airlines, and software companies). 

**Cookies**  


  * Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store may set and access Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store cookies on your computer. 
  * Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store lets other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to Align ESky Walkera RC Helicopters Store's cookies.
