# Runbox Mail Manager

When registering for runbox Mail Manager you are asked to provide certain information, such as your name and contact information. Some information is required for registration, but other information is optional and may be given at your descretion. The information will be used to customize the presentation of the service, configure certain parts of the service, and communicate directly with you should it be necessary. You may update this information at any time by clicking the “Account” button on the navigation bar.

Your Account information is private and will not be shared with any third parties. runbox.com AS will not access or disclose your Account information except with your permission or unless acting under a good faith belief that such action is necessary to: 

  * conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process
  * protect and defend the rigts or property of runbox.com AS
  * enforce the runbox Mail Manager Terms of Service
  * act to protect the interests of its members or others
  * administer, maintain, and improve the service

runbox.com AS may, from time to time, email you regarding our service. We work continuously to improve the service, and we are convinced that you will benefit from updates about new features.

During the registration process you are given the choice as to whether you want to be listed in the “runbox white pages”. If you select “yes”, your registered name and location will be listed, though you are free to reconsider at any time. 

| Your information is stored in secure databases and is password protected so that only you have access to your Account information. We recommend that you do not divulge your password to anyone. runbox.com AS will never ask you for your password in an unsolicited phone call or email.

Remember to log out of your runbox account and close all browser windows when you have finished using the service. This ensures that others cannot access your personal information and correspondence at the computer you use.

runbox.com AS is a global company and has users all over the world. Information you submit while using our service may be sent electronically and used, stored, and processed outside of the country you accessed our web site from. While runbox.com AS strives to ensure that your information is treated securely, no data transmission over the Internet or any wireless network can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. runbox.com AS cannot warrant the security of any information you transmit to us, and you do so at your own risk.

For more information about how runbox.com AS protects your online privacy, please contact us at info@runbox.com.
