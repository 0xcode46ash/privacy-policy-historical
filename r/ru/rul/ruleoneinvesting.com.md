# Privacy Policy - PHIL TOWN

This Privacy Policy describes the use of personal information you may supply to this Site.  The Policy may change from time to time without notification, so please visit this Policy whenever you use this Site.

This is how we will handle information we learn about you from your visit to our web site.

We may ask you for your name, age, street address, city, state, zip code, e-mail address, comments, suggestions or other information. Also, your domain name will be automatically recognized when you visit our site.

We will use this information only for our own internal purposes.  However, from time to time we may offer through this Site products or services from third parties vendors and payment companies (such as credit card transaction companies like Visa and American Express).   These third parties may have privacy policies that differ from BTA, Inc.’s and it is your responsibility to review their respective policies.  You acknowledge that BTA, Inc. will not have any responsibility or liability in connection with such third party products, services or use of your information.

We may also use your information to send you emails containing updates, information, announcements about Phil Town appearances and the like.  You will be given an opportunity to “opt-out” of receiving such emails.

By using this site, you signify your assent to our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to our Privacy Policy, please do not use this site. We reserve the right, at our complete discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time. Please check back periodically for any changes we may make to the policy. Your continued use of this Site following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes.

We want to be very clear: We do not obtain personal information about you other than that which you choose to provide, and we will not sell, rent, trade or otherwise disclose the personal information you provide to anyone outside of BTA, Inc. and its advertising and promotional agencies and consultants.

Thank you for visiting the [Phil Town](http://www.philtown.typepad.com/) site.
