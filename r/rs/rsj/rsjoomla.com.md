# Privacy Policy

All the information collected by RSJoomla.com from its customers include: 

  * usernames
  * real names
  * passwords
  * email addresses
  * access credentials to the website



All the above information is confidential and will not be disclosed to any of our partners or to any third parties. RS Joomla.com reserves the right to use the above information for statistics and marketing purposes only. 

All payment information (i.e. credit card number, holder's name, etc.) is not stored by RSJoomla.com. Payments and refunds are handled by 2checkout.com, which is the only authority capable of processing financial information. 2checkout.com is also to be contacted in the matters of invoices and all accounting information 

At times, in order to solve our customer's issues more rapidly and professionally, our support staff will ask for login information (Joomla Super Administrator password, ftp, phpmyadmin, etc). This information will not be forwarded to 3rd parties nor stored by RSJoomla.com
