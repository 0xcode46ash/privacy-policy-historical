# Genting Malaysia | Resorts World Genting - City of Entertainment | Privacy Policy

** Privacy Policy**

> * * *
> 
> ** Personal information**
> 
> We are committed to protecting the privacy of our online customers. We guarantee that our customer database will not be sold or marketed for any reason whatsoever to third parties. Any discretionary information submitted is handled with utmost care and urgency, and will be treated with due respect even in administrative representation.
> 
> We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their job.
