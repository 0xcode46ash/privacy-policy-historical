# Privacy Policy

_This site is hosted by Yahoo! Store. Yahoo! hosts the store pages, ordering system, and order data. Yahoo! automatically collects order information but may only use this information in the aggregate. If you accessed this store through Yahoo! Shopping, Yahoo! also automatically collects information about your shopping experience. Please see the[Yahoo! Privacy Policy](http://privacy.yahoo.com/privacy/us/) for more information about how Yahoo! uses this information._

**Privacy**  
Mr. Beer takes your privacy seriously. Mr. Beer will not share or sell any personal information about its customers to anyone. Your credit card information, address and email are held completely private.

 **Security**  
Mr. Beer is committed to ensuring the security of your information. We are confident that your credit card number and personal information are secure. Credit card numbers are only used for processing payment and are not used for other purposes. For online transactions, Mr. Beer as a Yahoo Store uses the Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) security system, which is widely accepted as the Internet security standard. When you submit your credit card number it is encrypted so your information is secure as it travels across the Internet.

 **Email Communications**  
Mr. Beer will use the email you provided when ordering to communicate with you the status of your order and any other information regarding your order. We will also use your email address to notify you of special promotions and offers. You may choose to remove yourself from our email list by emailing removefromlist@mrbeer.com.
