# Mrs Clean's House Cleaning and Maid Service Privacy Policy FAQ

## House Cleaning Services and Maid Service Frequently Asked Questions

You'll find the answers to our most frequently asked questions on house cleaning services and maid services. Use your browser's back button or click here to return to the main page [House Cleaning Service FAQ](http://www.mrscleanusa.com/frequently-asked-questions.html "House Cleaning Services FAQ").

### Privacy Policy **http://www.mrscleanusa.com/**

Your privacy is protected with us. We do not collect and we will not sell or rent your personal information.

We will never release, or otherwise disseminate, your personal information without your permission. You may visit mrscleanusa.com without revealing any personal information about yourself.

Our visitors are able to read all pages on mrscleanusa.com including all information on our cleaning services, postings in the cleaning forums, and our house cleaning tips and stain removal help pages.

Personal information (name, address, email, phone, home specs) is required if you request an online house cleaning estimate. Other than using this information to verify the service area and provide an accurate cleaning estimate to you, this online data is not used by us further in any way.

Personal information is required via registration (name, email) to post information in the cleaning forum sections of mrscleanusa.com. Registration is not required to read any post, or read the house cleaning tips or stain removal pages.

mrscleanusa.com uses statistical tracking software to provide metrics for analysis of site volume. This software may use cookies to track user navigation for summary reporting only.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. 

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. 
  * Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet. 
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
