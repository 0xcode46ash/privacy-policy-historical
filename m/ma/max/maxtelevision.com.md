# MAXTelevision.com

PLEASE READ THIS PRIVACY STATEMENT CAREFULLY BEFORE USING THIS WEB SITE.

This Privacy Statement is applicable to all Multi Screen Media Pvt Ltd (MSM) Web sites and other sites on the Internet and World Wide Web owned and operated by MSM (the "MSM Sites"), and all services provided thereon, including all MSM Forums (collectively, unless noted otherwise, the "Services").

MSM respects the privacy concerns of all users of the Services. MSM also recognizes the valuable potential of technology to help people communicate with the companies whose Web sites they visit, particularly through customer databases. Responsible use of the information you share with MSM is paramount. In order to help you understand how the information you provide MSM will be used, the following guidelines have been created.

Personally Identifiable Information:

In order to use certain portions of the Services, you may be required to provide certain personal information about yourself, such as your real name, age, address, telephone number, occupation and e-mail address. If you do provide MSM with personally identifiable information, we will take all reasonable and appropriate steps to protect this information. MSM will not disclose, share, sell or rent personally identifiable information to any third party without your prior consent except to authorise third party/agencies to collect, maintain and share data on our behalf.

Other than registration information, you may be asked to provide certain personal information in connection with your use of or participation in certain promotions such as entering sweepstakes, contests, surveys, newsletters, etc, ("Special Promotions"). In these instances, you will be informed of the intended use(s) of the requested information.

**Non-Personally Identifiable Information:**

To participate in a Special Promotion, you may be asked to provide certain information about yourself, which does not personally identify you. For example, you may be asked to provide information concerning your music or other entertainment preferences, purchasing habits, and the like. MSM requests this information in order to enhance your online experience by understanding you better and to also make you aware of new services, programs or offers in which you may be interested.

In general, you can use the Services (except MSM Contests) while remaining anonymous. However, in some cases, MSM may still be able to collect certain non-personally identifiable information about your use of the Services. For example, MSM might collect information concerning the type of Internet browser you are using, the type of computer operating system you are using, the domain name of your Internet service provider and/or the Web site or advertisement from which you linked to the MSM site.

When you visit one of the MSM Sites or advertisements appearing thereon, or use one or more of the Services, we may use an industry-wide technology called "cookies" which stores certain information on your computer and which will allow us to customize your experience to better match your interests and preferences or simply facilitate your signing in to use the Services. Most browsers will allow you to erase cookies from your computer hard drive, block acceptance of cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. Please refer to your browser instructions or help screen to learn more about these functions.

In general, MSM may use non-personally identifiable information to improve and enhance the Services. However, from time to time, MSM may share this information with other entities in aggregate, non-personal form to show general demographic and preference information among users of the MSM Sites.

**Other sensitive information:**

Whenever you give MSM sensitive information (e.g., credit card numbers), MSM will take all commercially reasonable steps to protect such information by establishing a secure connection with your Web browser. MSM employs an industry-wide technology known as a secure-socket-layer ("SSL") to protect the transmission of payment information to the Site. Unless otherwise specified herein, credit card numbers are used only for payment processing and are not retained for marketing or any other purposes  
.  


**Postings to Forums:**

All postings, whether in a chat room, on message boards or other method of communication within the Forums will generally be available to and freely accessible by other users of the MSM Forums. Therefore, users should not expect that any information they post will be kept private or confidential and are encouraged to exercise discretion when using the MSM Forums. Additionally, your member name may also be posted in conjunction with your post in the MSM Forums.

**Hyperlinks:**

The MSM Sites may contain links to or advertisements concerning other Web sites. Other sites may also reference, advertise, or link to MSM Sites. MSM is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such other sites and expressly disclaims all liability associated with your use of and the content on such other sites.

**Consent to Processing:**

By providing any personal information to the MSM Sites, all users, fully understand and unambiguously consent to the collection and processing of such information in India. You also consent to allow MSM to use the personal information to use for any other new use, authorise third party/agencies to collect, maintain and share data on MSM's behalf.  


MSM is dedicated to protecting your personal information and welcomes comments and questions on this policy.

MSM reserves the right to change this policy at any time by notifying guests of the existence of a new privacy policy.

For those who are under 13 years old, please submit your personal information after taking your guardian's consent.
