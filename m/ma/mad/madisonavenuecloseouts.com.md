# Madison Avenue Privacy Policy

In this agreement, "we/our/us" means Madison Avenue Closeouts, or Madison Ave Closeouts, you/user means site visitors and/or members, as the context requires. 

We may amend this Privacy Policy at any time by posting the updated Privacy Policy on this page. The revised terms will take an immediate effect when they are posted unless specified. By registering with us, you are agreeing to all the Privacy Policy mentioned below. 

**Our Solid Commitment**   


We are committed to respecting your privacy. We will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998. We collect your information to provide you with the best possible service. We will not e-mail you for marketing purposes in the future unless you have given us your consent. If you have any questions/comments about privacy, please e-mail us at [info@madisonavenuecloseouts.com](mailto:info@madisonavenuecloseouts.com)

**Links on our site**

This site contains links to third party sites. When using a third-party site via a link on our site, please review the Privacy Policy posted on that site. We are not responsible for any privacy issues arising from the use of linked third-party sites.

**Disclosure of information**

We will not disclose information about your individual visits to our Web site or any of the personal information that you provide such as your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address or credit/debit card details to any outside parties PERIOD. The only exception to this rule, is when required by law to do so. 

**Cookies**

We must use technology to track the visitors to our site. This is mainly due to the e-sommerce application needs. This can include using a "cookie" which would be stored on your browser. You can usually modify your browser to prevent this happening. The information collected in this way can be used to identify you on return visits unless you modify your browser settings.
