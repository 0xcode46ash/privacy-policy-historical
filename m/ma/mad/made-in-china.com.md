# Made-in-China->Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy **

To protect your privacy online is one of the firm commitments of Made-in-China.com. Hence the following policy is prepared to let you know how your personal information is treated as you use the various services in Made-in-China.com. This policy may change from time to time because more and more customized services will be provided for you in the coming future. So please check back periodically.

 **Why Made-in-China.com collects your personal information?**   
We mainly collect your personal information as you register as a member of Made-in-China.com. We use the registration information for gathering statistics on our membership. We will use these statistics to classify our members by, for example, industry and country, to target our registered members for new services and promotions. We may use your email address to contact you regarding such new services and products in which you might be interested. 

In conclusion, by knowing a little about you, Made-in-China.com is able to provide more relevant content, and hence better service, to you.

 **What personal information is collected?**   
We collect your name & e-mail address. As to your company details, you have to supply us it's name, location, phone & fax number, web address, description, type, turnover and number of employees. 

You may edit your above information (Made-in-China.com Member Profile Information) at any time by using your Made-in-China.com ID and password. 

Top

 **Does Made-in-China.com collect other information about its users?**   
In addition to the use of Profile Information to operate the site, Made-in-China.com also gathers anonymous information, which may be used by Made-in-China.com or shared with its advertisers. This is information which does not personally identify you, but may be helpful for marketing purposes or for improving the services we offer. Generally, this information is collected through "traffic data" and may entail the use of "Cookies", "IP addresses" or other numeric codes used to identify a computer. All sites on Made-in-China.com where you are prompted to log-in or customizable require that you accept cookies. 

Top

 **How your personal information may be treated?**   
Made-in-China.com keeps all of your Profile Information private and does not give it to any third parties. Made-in-China.com will not disclose your Profile Information unless acting under a good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (1) conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process; (2) protect and defend the rights or property of Made-in-China.com; (3) enforce the Made-in-China.com Terms & Conditions; or (4) act to protect the interests of its members or others. Profile Information collected by Made-in-China.com is stored in secure operating environments that are not available to the public. 

Made-in-China.com does research on our users' demographics, industry and geographic based on the information provided to us upon registration from our server log files. We do this to better understand and serve our users. This research is compiled and analyzed on an aggregated basis. Made-in-China.com may share this aggregated data with advertisers or business partners.

 **With whom your information may be shared?**   
As a general rule, Made-in-China.com will not disclose any of your personally identifiable information except when we have your permission or under special circumstances. Made-in-China.com may disclose your personal information to our business partners or sponsors only for marketing purposes. Also, Made-in-China.com may disclose or access account information when we believe in good faith that the law requires it and for administrative and other purposes that we deem necessary to maintain, service, and improve our services. 

Top

 **What is our responsibility to ensure your privacy?**   
Once we receive your personal information, we make our best effort to ensure its security on our systems and will not share it with any third parties. Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet or any wireless network can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your personal information, Made-in-China.com cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or from our online products or services, and you do so at your own risk. 

Top

 **What you can do to protect your personal information?**   
Your Made-in-China.com Account Information and Made-in-China.com Member Profile are password-protected so that you and only you have access to this personal information. You may edit your Account and Profile Information at any time by using your Made-in-China.com ID and password. Hence we recommend that you do not divulge your password to anyone. 

The information that you provide to post an item for trade or to make an offer is posted in a public area, available to all users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.
