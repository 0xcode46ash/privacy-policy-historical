# majortests.com : Privacy information

At majortests.com we care about providing you with tools and information to manage and protect your online privacy. We have developed this privacy policy to inform you about what information this web site collects, who may receive that information, what we may do with the information, and how long we keep it. While we have made every attempt to make this policy as clear as possible, if you have any further questions about it, please contact us: 

  * website: http://www.majortests.com/
  * feedback: http://www.majortests.com/contact.php



## Privacy Questions

###### What types of information does this website collect?

As with most webservers ours collects the following information: 

  * IP address, pages visited, date and time of visit, browser used, OS used and referring webpage



###### Does this website use cookies to keep track of my information?

No, this website does not use cookies. However, advertisers linked to from this website may use cookies, in which case their use is governed by their respective privacy policies.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.google.co.uk/privacy_ads.html).

###### Why is this website collecting the information?

We collect the information for the following purposes: 

  * To complete the current activity of the visitor.
  * For website administration purposes.
  * To evaluate, review, or improve the website.



###### Who will receive the information?

The information will be shared with the following parties: 

  * Our internal staff and our direct agents agents, such as a printing bureau.



###### How long will the information be kept?

The information is only kept as required and will be destroyed as quickly as possible.

###### Can I review my information?

We cannot uniquely identify you by the information we collect.

###### Exceptions

All data sumbitted by you to us via the contact page is excluded from this privacy policy. Rest assured that the information submitted will only be used to respond to your queries and will not be used in any other way withour your prior consent.

Data submitted to the tutor directory will be visible on the publicly accessible parts of majortests.com. Hence, only submit information that you are comfortable with being displayed in such a way.
