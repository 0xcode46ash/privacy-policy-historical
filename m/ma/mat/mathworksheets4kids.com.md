# Privacy Policy

The privacy policy gives a brief overview on what kind of information is collected by mathworksheets4kids.com, how it is being used, and what measures are taken to safeguard it.

 **What information do we collect and why?**

Mathworksheets4kids takes matters with regard to the privacy of users very seriously. We are responsible for the Personally Identifiable Information (PII), which a user discloses to us when information is shared.

This personal information is collected only when you are a registered as a user on our website. Once you register with us, we collect your user name and password, (created by you) to provide you an authentic access to our website. If you choose to subscribe to our website, the transactions will be via Pay Pal, a trusted vendor of ours. Your credit card details will never be saved to the server. We also collect the details of the state and the country from which you log in. We use log files to identify a visitor's IP address, ISP (Internet Service Provider), demographic information, date and time of visit, and the number of hits on our website.

We also retain your feedback and suggestions received over e-mail to improve upon our products and our website, thereby providing you high-quality worksheets.

All the above information collected is used primarily to render the best possible service and to simplify the transaction processes.

 **Third-party disclosure**

We do not sell, rent or share the details of our transactions or any other personal information to third-party vendors. However, we may share these details with our trusted third-party vendors. These are essentially vendors who provide substantial services to upgrade our website. As we are in the process of expanding our services, we may require you to provide additional personal information in the future.

**Google, the third-party vendor**

Google, a third party vendor, uses the DART cookie to serve ads to our users based on their visits to our website, www.mathworksheets4kids.com .Users may control the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy, details of which are available in the following URL- [http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html.](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html)

Google AdSense and Google certified third-party vendors or ad networks may use cookies, JavaScript or web beacons on our site to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see. Google AdSense also displays certified third-party vendors and networks ads. [List of third-party vendors and ad networks](https://www.google.com/support/adsense/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=94149)

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use various technologies to deliver advertisements. Links that appear on [www.mathworksheets4kids.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20170118120841id_/https%3A//www.mathworksheets4kids.com/index.php) are sent directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs.

**How do we protect your information?**

We scan our website on a regular basis to make sure your visits to our website are made secure. Your personal information is contained behind secured networks, and we use the SSL (Secure Socket Layer) technology where all your credit information is encrypted. Various security measures are taken to safeguard your personal information. All transactions are processed through a secured payment gateway.

 **Do we use cookies?**

Yes, we utilize cookies to retrieve user details for visits made to our website. This storage of information is temporary. The cookies are used in a few areas of our website to facilitate easy use for visitors to our website. This information is primarily used to check if a user is logged in, review the settings of the page, and for advertising and marketing purposes. All these steps are taken to serve you better.

 **Protection of children's privacy**

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), 1998, applies to the collection of personal information from children below the age of 12. The legislation allows parents or guardians to review personal information collected from their children. If any information is furnished by a child below the age of 12, please write to us to retrieve the information.

 **Other Information**

This privacy policy is subject to change from time to time. If you have any queries regarding our privacy policy, drop in an email to our technical support team at [webmistress@mathworksheets4kids.com](mailto:webmistress@mathworksheets4kids.com).
