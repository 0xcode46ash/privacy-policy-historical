MATE4ALL.COM, THE ONLINE DATING'S PRIVACY POLICY 

Mate4all.Com, The Online Dating's Privacy policy is to respect and protect the privacy of our visitors. Mate4all.Com, The the Online Dating Service will not willfully disclose individually identifiable information about its visitors to any third party without first receiving that visitor's permission. This policy statement tells you how we collect information from you and how we use it. Using the Internet should be an informative and enjoyable experience and we want to help you make it so. 

Mate4all.Com, The Online Dating Service does NOT shares information about our visitors with advertisers, business partners, sponsors and other third parties. However, in most cases, we talk about our visitors in aggregate, not as individuals. For example, we will say Mate4all.Com, The Online Dating Service's audience is A percent females and B percent males. This data is used to customize Mate4all.Com, The Online Dating Service content and advertising for our visitors. 

There are cases where Mate4all.Com, The Online Dating Service may ask you for personal information such as your name or address. We do this when you register for contests/promotions or services that require registration or subscription (for example, mailing list). Mate4all.Com, The Online Dating Service needs to collect this information for fulfilling prizes, tracking/verifying compliance with Mate4all.Com, The Mate4all dating policies as well as local and national laws operating within the territorial jurisdiction of Delhi and/or for editorial and feedback solicitation purposes. When we intend to use your personal information, we tell you up front. This way you can decide whether you want to give us the information or not. For example, we may want to post the names of contest winners on our site or send you materials via e-mail or regular mail at your request. In case you change your mind or some personal information may change (such as your marital status), we will endeavor to provide a way to correct, update or remove the personal data you give us. 

Mate4all.Com,The Online Dating Service warns parents and guardians to spend time online with their children and do not allow to participate them in any of the online dating activity. 

Guests under 18 years of age are NOT allowed to participate in contests or any kind of activity on Mate4all.com.

PARENTS: You should always supervise your children's use of the Internet. For information about commercially available products to assist you in filtering material that you may consider harmful to minors, please see http://worldvillage.com/wv/school/html/control.htm or other similar sites. 

Please keep in mind that whenever you give out personal information online --- for example, via message boards, Online dating profiles or chat --- that information can be collected and used by people you don't know. While Mate4all.Com, The Online Dating Service strives to protect its user's personal information and privacy, we cannot guarantee the security of any information you disclose online and you do so at your own risk. 

Mate4all.Com, The Online Dating Service's policy does not extend to anything that is inherent in the operation of the Internet, and therefore beyond Mate4all.Com, The Online Dating Service's control, and is not to be applied in any manner contrary to applicable law or governmental regulation. We reserve the right at our discretion to make changes to this policy at any time. Please review this page periodically for changes.
