# Mattress Firm - Privacy Terms

**Privacy Terms**

Mattress Firm is committed to making your search for the perfect mattress and a great night sleep as pleasurable and easy as possible. Maintaining your privacy is part of that commitment. 

**_Rest Easy: What Do We Collect?_** Most of the data that we collect is voluntarily provided to us when you enter our site. Your Internet ‘IP’ address, browser type, domain name, access times & referring website addresses are collected. This helps us assist you by allowing us to understand how visitors use our services. This data also becomes part of your unique profile and allows us to make your shopping experience beneficial. 

**_Rest Easy: How We Use It?_** We take seriously the trust you’ve place in us, therefore you have our commitment that we will not sell, rent or lease your personally identifiable information to others. We may, at times, produce internal reports about our user demographic, age and online usage information. This allows us to work more closely with advertisers to provide content and offers that are more relevant to our users. We will never include any personal information of our users in these reports. We reserve the right to use or disclose any information without notice or consent as needed to satisfy Federal or State law or regulations (including our Terms of Use), to satisfy legal requests from any state court or government department or agency; to conduct investigations of consumer complaints; possible breaches of law; to protect the integrity of our site and our property; to protect the safety of visitors or others; to fulfill your requests; or to cooperate in any legal investigations.   
**_  
Rest Easy: How Secure is your Site?_** Mattress Firm, Inc. has taken your security seriously. We have limited access to private and confidential data to those employees with a specific need to retrieve this information. To maintain this highest level of security, we do not store private information on our website. Your financial applications are directly routed to Wells Fargo’s secured site.
