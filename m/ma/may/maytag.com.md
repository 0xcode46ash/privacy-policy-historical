# Maytag - Customer Service - Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**  
Maytag Appliances recognizes its obligation to its customers, community and online visitors to adhere to the highest standards of decency, fairness, and integrity in all its operations. Likewise, we're dedicated to the fundamentals of protecting consumer privacy on the Internet, especially for kids. We've developed our Privacy Practices from guidelines established by the American Association of Advertising Agencies, Association of National Advertisers, and the Children's Advertising Review Unit (CARU).

We do not sell or rent information about our customers. Maytag Appliances only collects personal information from our visitors on a voluntary basis. We don't require this information to obtain access to any of our sites. Personal information received from any visitor will only be used for internal Maytag Appliances or Maytag Corporation purposes and will not be sold or provided to third parties.

Personal information may include name, address, email address, age, etc., and may be collected for contest entry forms, requests for information from Maytag Appliances, to join our updates list, etc. We may also occasionally ask, on a voluntary basis, if appropriate, for personal information for our internal use, such as marketing and research. We'll maintain your privacy and will not sell or provide this information to third parties.

Maytag may use "cookie" technology to obtain non-personal information from its online visitors. The cookie tracks the total number of visitors to our sites in an aggregate form (you're one of a group and your information is not singled out). We don't extract personal information in this process nor do we provide this information to third parties. We also do not contact you. Cookies allow us to update and improve our sites.
