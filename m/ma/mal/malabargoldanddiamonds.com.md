# Celebrate the Beauty of Life

Malabargoldanddiamonds.com should not be held responsible for errors and omissions in the site. We do not honor any erroneous prices quoted on the site.  The prices are subject to change. Malabargoldanddiamonds.com may in its sole discretion add, delete, or change some or all of its products and services at any time. Malabargoldanddiamonds.com has attempted to accurately depict the products offered on the Site. However, the products also may appear larger or smaller than their actual size depending on your monitor.

#####  Applicable Law

Malabargoldanddiamonds.com reserves the right to make changes to the site as well as to the terms, conditions and disclaimers.  
Malabargoldanddiamonds.com understands your concern on security of personal information on our website. We appreciate the trust that you have shown in our services and we shall not misuse the given information in any way.

#####  Information Collected

In order to process the order that you placed with us, we need to have some basic information like:  
Your First Name, Your Last Name, Your Address, City, Zip code, State, Country, Phone Number and Contact E-mail address.  
Apart from the basic information, the system gathers information on your computer’s internet connection like IP address etc. This information is used by us to deliver the web pages to you, calculate the visitors to our site and to know the geographical location of our visitors. However, we do not allow any unauthorized person or organization to use the information collected from you.

#####  Information Modification

If you wish to modify the information given, you can do that through the ‘Edit Profile’ option.

#####  Information Sharing

We do not rent, sell, barter, or give away your information to anyone. However, to some extent, information is passed on to the courier companies, credit card processing companies, vendors, etc. to enable them to perform their functions related in fulfillment of your order. Apart from this, information may also be needed to be shared with local authorities for fraud detection, and for the safety of our site, employees, management, users, members and other affiliates associated with us.

#####  Information Usage

One of the most important information collected from you is the e-mail address, which is used to communicate to you about the progress of your order. Besides the e-mail, all other information is held highly confidential.

#####  Cookies Usage

Cookies store data on the pages you visited while on our site. This lets us know the products that you are interested in, which in turn helps in making site better. The cookie can be turned off from most browsers and it is also possible to delete the cookies from the computer. However, this may affect the functioning of our site.
