# Security & Privacy Policy

MALL DIRECTORY

| 

Security & Privacy Policy

When you register to buy items on the site we ask for information such as your name, address, email address, phone number, cell number, fax number, user name, credit card information and notification preferences. 

Malleries uses cookies to provide continuity from page to page.  Within a single visit, these cookies store information such as your shopping list to simplify checkout.  They do not contain any personally identifiable information. To access any secure areas such as your account information, you must still enter your password. The cookie itself does not tell us who you are, your email address or anything else personal.  We use cookies to provide our visitors with a better experience on our site.

**Information Sharing and Disclosure**  
Malleries does not sell, share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except under the following circumstances: 

  1. We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to any person, violations of Malleries’ terms of use, or as otherwise required by law. 
  2. We transfer information about you if Malleries is acquired by or merged with another company.



**Data Security and Storage**  
In order to protect and preserve the security of the data we collect, we have established procedures to safeguard the information collected throughout this website. To protect your security, we will also take commercially reasonable steps to verify your identify before granting access or making corrections. Notwithstanding these procedures, we do not accept any liability for misuse of the site by others or from viruses, or links to other sites or to objectionable material.

We do not guarantee the preservation of data, records or other information that may expire or be removed or deleted, all without notice or liability. Seller grants Malleries non-exclusive publishing rights in print or electronically of uploaded images and data.   
Records Retention, Usage

Malleries shall have non-exclusive publishing rights to all information including, but not limited to, pictures, descriptions, input and sales data pertaining to your listings on all Malleries sites.  This information may be used for certain purposes including, but not limited to, providing price guide information to paying subscribers. Malleries will make a good faith effort and use commercially reasonable efforts to keep all transactional information anonymous by a number of means, including removing references to company names and account numbers associated with such data.

**Confidentiality and Security**  
1) We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information in order to do their jobs.   
2) We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you.   
3) Your Malleries Account Information is password-protected.   
4) In certain areas Malleries uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.
