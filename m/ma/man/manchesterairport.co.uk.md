# Manchester Airport : Privacy Policy

At Manchester Airport PLC (MAPLC) we are committed to protecting your privacy. We have therefore created this privacy statement in order to explain our practices for this web site and to demonstrate our commitment to you.  
Travel Partner Websites  
MAPLC's web site contains links to other web sites where you may buy products and services. Please note that MAPLC is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such other web sites.

**Data Protection Notice - Please Read  
**From this website MAPLC would like to gather certain information to use for marketing, promotional and statistical purposes in line with the provisions of the Data Protection Act 1998. However, we do not collect personal information about you when you are browsing through our site.

**What we collect  
**We will only collect personal information when you give it to us.

If you visit our feedback area and give us feedback or feedback through the aircraft noise complaints section, your e-mail address will be used to respond to you, where you have requested a response.

Your comments may be used to enable us to improve services.

We may collect additional information in connection with your participation in any promotions or competitions offered by information and us you provide when giving us feedback. We also monitor customer traffic patterns and site use, which enables us to improve the service we provide.

**Car Parking Bookings  
**When you make a car park booking with us we collect information about each transaction but we do not store your full card details on our server in any way. We do, though, over a secure encrypted link pass your full card details to our 3rd party payment processor [insert name], which processes your payment.

**Data Collection and Usage  
**By registering on our site or by using our car park booking services available through this site you agree that MAPLC will retain copies of the personal information which you provide when registering and that this information will be held by MAPLC

**How we use Cookies  
**Manchester Airport's website router will be stored as a cookie for one week on your computer to facilitate download speed. There are several other cookies on this website, which are all session based and will therefore become inactive when you leave Manchester Airport's web site. You have the option not to use the cookies by changing your browser settings, or by deleting them from your temporary Internet files.

MAPLC and, on our behalf, Nedstat use cookies for the following purposes: session management. If you would like us not to use cookies please turn cookies off in your browser or please click the 'cookies off' button. If you accept our cookies, please note that they will remain in your computer for five years, unless you delete them. Please note that turning off cookies may limit your use of our website and services.
