# MANEXA, Inc.

This web site is owned and controlled by Manexa, Inc., 5700 South Mo Pac, Suite C310, Austin, TX 78749. 

  This site recognizes the home server of visitors, but not their e-mail addresses. Data on visitors' home servers are aggregated for internal review and then discarded. This site also uses "cookie" technology to help personalize your experience within the site. 

  The names and contact information of persons who request information about Manexa, or its products, or utilizes its online services are added to our database in Austin, Texas, USA. Users may be contacted in the future regarding by Manexa itself or by organizations identified as affiliates on its web site regarding products, services, or future opportunities. Names of users viewing sensitive broadcasts may be identified if required by courts of record in legal proceedings broadcast over Manexa’s network. We do not sell or rent any information about our visitors, and we have no plans to do so in the future. 

  If you give Manexa your contact information over this Web site, and you wish to have your name removed from Manexa’s database, please send email to [information@manexa.com](mailto:%20information@manexa.com)[.](mailto:%20information@manexa.com)

  If you want to find out what information Manexa has in its database about you, please write to us at the address above, or email [information@manexa.com](mailto:%20information@manexa.com). Please include your name and other information in the same format that you supplied to Manexa so we can be more certain of matching your request to the correct information.
