# Maurices - customer service -

Maurices is committed to protecting the privacy of our web page users. We want to provide a safe, secure user experience. We will use our best efforts to ensure that the information you submit to us remains private, and is used only for the purposes of assisting your search activities. We do not disclose information about your personal information that you provide to any outside parties.

_What personal information do you collect?_

When you are placing an order on our website, we need your name, email address, phone number, billing address, and shipping address and payment information. This information is only used to process your order and ensure that your items arrive in a timely manner.

When you sign up on our website for Maurices information, we need only your name, mailing address, and e-mail address. When you enter a contest or other promotional feature, we will ask only for the information needed to administer the contest and notify the winner.

_Do you share my personal information? If so, with whom?_

We will not provide, sell, or trade your mailing address, e-mail address, or any personal information to a third party with these exceptions:

  * When we believe in good faith that the law requires it (i.e. when someone is suspected of being involved in illegal online activities)
  * To protect the rights or property of Maurices or its affiliates
  * Under limited circumstances specifically described to you when we collect the information, as in the rules of sweepstakes or other promotions



_How else might Maurices use my personal information?_

To serve you better, we may combine information you give us online and in our stores about your activities and product interests. We may use this information to enhance and personalize your shopping experience with us and to provide you with service and products tailored to your specific needs and interests.

_Will Maurices send me unsolicited e-mails?_

No. Unless you sign up to receive information on our web site, or in one of our stores, you will only receive an e-mail from us when you have e-mailed a specific question or comment to Maurices, or to notify you of the progress of any orders you may have placed with us. If at any time you do not want to continue to receive information, please contact us.

_What about sales flyers and other mail from Maurices?_

Unless you have signed up in our stores or on our web site to receive our promotional material through the mail, we will not send you unsolicited mailings. If you have signed up, and decide that you do not want to continue to receive these mailings, [click here](https://www.maurices.com/coreg/index.jsp?step=register) to unsubscribe.
