# Privacy Policy

At MakeHitsongs.com, we respect your privacy on the internet. As such, we would advise you to take some time to go through our privacy policy. We collect only the information necessary for us to complete your order or to contact to you regarding the status of your order. The information we collect includes your name, email address, shipping address, and billing address. The information we gathered from you will be used to improve your online shopping experience and to keep you in the loop of our latest happenings, promotions, updates, etc.

However please do take note that MakeHitsongs.com does not rent or sell your name or personal information without your permission.

MakeHitsongs.com does utilize "cookies". This is to ensure that we recognize you as a recurring visitor and also for us to track traffic patterns on our website. Be rest assured that the information we have gathered is kept completely anonymous and will be used to improvise the user-friendliness and functionality of MakeHitsongs.com.

If you use this website, you are accepting the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy, and we will have the right to use your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to have your information used in any of the ways described in this Privacy Policy, you must discontinue use of this website.

MakeHitsongs.com reserves the right to update this privacy policy at anytime. Updates to our privacy policy will be sent to the email address that you have provided us.
