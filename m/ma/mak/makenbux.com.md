# MakenBux : Privacy

  | 

## Privacy

  

* **Account security**  
Your private user information such as your e-mail and email addresses will never will not be shown, given or sold.  
This information is used for contact purposes and to send you a new password in case you request it.  
Your user password will be stored in an encripted format.  
Your user password will never be shown, sold or given.
*  **Information we collect**  
Upon registration with Us, we accumulate and store all user inputted information, as well as your IP address, browser information, date and time of the record conception.
*  **Cookies**  
Each time you login to your account, a basic ID cookie is installed on your computer that is used as a virtual identification card throughout all the member pages. The cookie is uninstalled each time you logout of the members area.  
Your browser must accept cookies.
*  **Legality**  
We will however, always cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies and authorities in any investigations concerning fraud or any type of Internet crime.
