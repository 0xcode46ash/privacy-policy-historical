# Makeup.com

We value our customers and endeavor to create an enjoyable and valuable experience on our website. The following Privacy Policy applies to all visitors of Makeup.com:

At Makeup.com we promise that your personal information is secure. Every transaction is processed by using the latest industry standards for secure online credit card transactions. Secure servers protect your information using advanced encryption and firewall technology throughout the ordering process. For security reasons credit card information should never be sent via email.

What information do we collect?

When you place an order at Makeup.com, we may collect the following information in order to promptly and securely process your order: your name, telephone number, email address, shipping information (credit card number, expiration date and billing address), products purchased and promotional codes.

We use information we collect from you to process your orders and to email you special promotional offers and discounts from Makeup.com. We never share your personal information with any third party. Makeup.com may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, sales, traffic patterns, and related site information to reputable third parties, but these statistics will not include any personally identifying information.

What are cookies?

"Cookies" are defined as a collection of information, usually including a username and the current date and time, stored on the local computer of a person using the World Wide Web used primarily by websites to identify users who have previously visited the site.

Your consent

By using our website and submitting your information to the Makeup.com network, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Makeup.com, Inc. If we decide to change our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. If you are not comfortable with any of the changes to our Privacy Policy, you will have the opportunity to remove your information from our network at any time.

Links

Some sites within our network contain links to other sites. Makeup.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites. If you have a question about the privacy policy of those websites, please visit them directly.

Contacting Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, our site practices, or your personal experience dealing with this website, please feel free to [contact our office](http://www.makeup.com/contact.jsp).
