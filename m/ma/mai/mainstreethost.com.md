Privacy Policy 

Information Collected

  * We collect only the information provided by when you open an account on our website, place an order or communicate with us through an e-mail or phone. 




How we use your information

  * To process your information. 

  * To provide better service when responding to your requests or questions. 

  * To administer promotions, new services, contests or events. 




Disclosure to third parties

  * To process credit or debit card transactions. 

  * To process email or direct mail campaigns using other services that is controlled by us. If you do not wish to receive e-mails from us, you can unsubscribe. 

  * We do not sell your e-mail address to an outside party. 




Security

  * Securing your personal information is very important to us. When entering sensitive information, the information is encrypted using secure socket layer technology (SSL). We follow the generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it.
