# Privacy Policy - Mailplane

### Customer Information

Limited information is supplied to mailplaneapp.com in the event of a sale or when subscribing to the newsletter. This includes your name, IP Address and website domain name. **This information is not sold or distributed to third parties.** Credit card payments are processed through [FastSpring.com](http://fastspring.com/). Payment information is collected solely by [FastSpring.com](http://fastspring.com/) to securely process the order, and is not accessible to mailplaneapp.com.

### Cookies

mailplaneapp.com uses cookies to store session information.

### IP Addresses and Logging

mailplaneapp.com server logs information about visitors, including IP addresses, date/time visited, referring website, length of stay, etc. This information is used for site statistics only.

### External Site Links

mailplaneapp.com links to other websites on the internet. Content and Privacy Policies are the responsibility of their respective owners.
