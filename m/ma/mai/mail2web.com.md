# mail2web.com Information Center

Version 1.1 – January 1, 2004

**Purpose.** This Privacy Policy (the “ **Policy** ”) governs all aspects of how mail2web® (“ **We** ”, “ **Us** ” and “ **Our** ”) collects, uses, maintains and discloses personal information from all users (“ **Users** ”, “ **You** ” and “ **Your** ”) of this website (“ **Site** ”) and service. The privacy of Users is very important to Us and We are committed to protecting the confidentiality of all information entrusted to Us by You. mail2web.com complies with the requirements of the Canadian Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (“PIPEDA”).

**mail2web.com is not an e-mail service, but an e-mail application.** Registration is not required, but is available to Users interested in customizing the application making it easier for regular Users. We may store e-mail messages, attachments, or passwords temporarily while you are using the mail2web.com application. After You have logged off from the application or the session has timed out, your email address and your password is removed from our system. Any remaining email messages and/or attachments that may reside on the server will be removed automatically within one hour. All activities that You conduct through the Site are considered private and are not recorded in a personally identifiable way. The data collected for the use of the mail2web.com application is anonymous and is used for statistical purposes only. [Click here to learn how mail2web.com works](https://web.archive.org/web/20041204090510id_/http://info.mail2web.com/Help/Article.aspx?URL=basics/how%20does%20mail2web%20work.html) or [click here for general help](https://web.archive.org/web/20041204090510id_/http://info.mail2web.com/Help/index.aspx).

**Personal Information that mail2web.com collects.** mail2web.com does not collect any personal information unless you choose to use the customized version. The customized version of mail2web.com requires storing your email address (“ **Personal Information** ”) to function, should You wish to provide it. We may also collect demographic data about Your income level, occupation, gender and information about the industry in which You work (the “ **Ancillary Personal Information** ”) if you choose to use the customized version of mail2web. mail2web may also collect Your name and email address should you attempt to contact us by way of Our web-based feedback and/or support form hosted on the mail2web.com Site, or if you contact us by email for any other reason.

**Your consent.** Your provision of Personal Information to mail2web.com means that You agree and consent that mail2web.com may collect, use and disclose your Personal Information (and Ancillary Personal Information should You wish to provide it) in accordance with this Policy. If You do not agree with these terms, You are requested not to provide any Personal Information (or Ancillary Personal Information) to mail.2web.com. The customized mail2web.com service can only be offered if You provide Personal Information to mail2web.com. Consequently if You choose not to provide us with any required Personal Information, mail2web.com will not be able to offer You such services.

**How mail2web.com uses and discloses Personal Information.** We use Your Personal Information to identify You when You use the Service. We never use Your Personal Information for any other purpose, and we do not share or sell Your Personal Information. If You choose to provide Us with Ancillary Personal Information, that aggregated data is _**only**_ used by Us as part of Our advertising engine and for statistical purposes. 

**Disclosure of Personal Information.** We _**never**_ disclose your Personal Information, except pursuant to the requirements of Section 7(3) – Disclosure without knowledge or consent – of PIPEDA and Division 1 – Protection of Personal Information – of PIPEDA, in which circumstances mail2web.com may be obligated to disclose information without consent.

Personal Information may also be subject to transfer to another company in the event of a change of ownership of all or part of mail2web.com. This will occur only if the parties have entered into an agreement under which the collection, use and disclosure of such information is restricted to those purposes that relate to the business transaction, including a determination whether or not to proceed with the business transaction, and is to be used by the parties to carry out and complete the business transaction.

**Maintenance and Security of Personal Information.** mail2web.com retains Personal Information about Users as long as mail2web.com believes it is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. Currently, mail2web.com holds the Personal Information in the City of Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 

Personal Information about Users that is maintained on our systems is protected using industry standard security measures and mail2web.com complies with the security safeguards principal of Schedule 1, Clause 4.7 of PIPEDA. However, we cannot guarantee that the information submitted to, maintained on, or transmitted from our systems will be completely secure and transmission of information over the Internet is susceptible to possible loss, misrouting, interception and misuse. 

**Access to Personal Information.** mail2web.com provides individuals with access to Personal Information held about them and complies with the individual access principal of Schedule 1, Clause 4.9 of PIPEDA.

Pursuant to Schedule 1, Clause 4.9.5 of PIPEDA, if You believe that Your Personal Information on file is not correct, You may request an update of that information by sending a request to the person indicated below. Alternatively, Registered Users update and/or remove the Ancillary Personal Information through the web-based mail2web.com Settings page located after logging into their Customized mail2web.com page. mail2web.com reserves the right not to change any Personal Information but will append any alternative text You believe appropriate. 

Pursuant to Schedule 1, Clause 4.9.5 of PIPEDA, You may also request that mail2web.com delete Your Personal Information from mail2web.com’s system and records. However, due to technical constraints and the fact that mail2web.com backs up its systems, Personal Information may continue to reside in mail2web.com’s systems after deletion for some time. Individuals, therefore, should not expect that their Personal Information would be completely removed from mail2web.com’s systems in response to an accepted request for deletion. Any Personal Information that You provide in connection with the customized mail2web.com service will remain on mail2web.com systems until it is deleted by You. mail2web.com reserves the right to remove and delete inactive accounts.

As per Division 1, Clause 9 of PIPEDA, mail2web.com reserves the right to decline access to Personal Information where disclosure of the information requested is not required .

Pursuant to the principal in Schedule 1, Clause 4.9.4 of PIPEDA, where information will not or cannot be disclosed, the individual making the request will be provided with the reasons for non-disclosure . Where information will be disclosed, mail2web.com will endeavour to provide the information in question within a reasonable time and no later than thirty (30) days following the request. 

mail2web.com will not respond to repetitious or vexatious requests for access. In determining whether a request is repetitious or vexatious, mail2web.com will consider such factors as the frequency with which information is updated, the purpose for which the information is used, and the nature of the information.

To guard against fraudulent requests for access, mail2web.com will require sufficient information to allow it to confirm the identity of the person making the request before granting access or making corrections.

**Amendment of mail2web.com practices and this Policy.** This statement is in effect as of January 1, 2004\. mail2web.com will from time to time review and revise its privacy practices and this Policy. In the event of any amendment, an appropriate notice will be posted on this website. Policy changes will apply to the information collected from the date of posting of the revised Policy to mail2web.com’s website as well as to existing information held by mail2web.com.

If You have any comments or suggestions to improve the service, please write us at [suggestions@mail2web.com](mailto:%20suggestions@mail2web.com).
