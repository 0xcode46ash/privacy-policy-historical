# Main Page Site

## Privacy Policy

Last update: December 2013

The Site is committed to securing your personal information. By using the Site, you agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. The Site reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The Site recognizes that your privacy is very important and therefore strongly encourages you to regularly review this Privacy Policy and be aware of any such updates. Please read the information below to learn more about our Privacy Policy. The use of the Site is also subject to our terms and conditions, which together with this Privacy Policy also governs your visit to the Site. 

Data Collection

Traffic data is being generated when your computer connects to the internet and to the Site and is not sufficient independently to identify you. The Site may collect the following information: origin of the connection (ISP), IP address, type and version of your internet browser, length of connection, etc. This data may be used for statistical purposes to analyze the Site’s traffic activities such as most and least-viewed pages, activity per day and hour, etc. Traffic data is always processed anonymously. 

Cookies

The Site uses cookies to store information such as your personal preferences when you visit the Site. The Site does not use cookies to collect users' personally identifiable information. The Site may place “cookies” on your computer hard drive to record your visit and to restore your personalized visual homepage. 

A cookie is a simple text file that is stored on your computer. It will contain some anonymous information such as a unique identifier and the site name and some digits and numbers. It allows the Site to remember information such as your preferences and to collect anonymous traffic data. Cookies make the interaction between our users and the Site faster and easier. 

Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies. You can choose to disable or selectively turn off the Site’s cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings, or manage your preferences in programs such as Norton Internet Security. However, this can affect how you are able to interact with the Site as well as other websites. 

You may suppress cookies after your visit or configure your internet browser to prevent them. However, you should be aware that in this event your preferences will be lost. 

Advertisement

The Site uses third party advertisements to support the Site. Some of these advertisers may use web technologies such as cookies and web beacons on the Site. These technological tools send participating advertisers information such as your IP address, your ISP, the browser you used to visit the Site, and in some cases, information regarding your installed Flash software. This is generally used for geo-targeting purposes or for showing certain ads based on specific sites visited. 

Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to the Site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of third-party vendor's use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page:http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp

Personal Information

You do not need to open an account and provide us with personally identifiable information to use the Site. If we collect and process any personal information (like newsletter registration or email correspondence) we use it in a fair and lawful manner and in respect of your rights. We will never share your personal information with third parties. We may use your contact information to send you updates and promotions of current and future features of the Site. Please be aware that we may provide your personal information if we are required to do so by law or compelled by a court of law. 

Analytics

The Site uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous traffic data. 

Please contact us at: admin@mainpagesite.com
