# Privacy Statement

**Privacy Statement**

Masterworks Fine Art respects your privacy. In a world filled with information that moves as fast as the internet, information that pertains to you should be controlled by each individual. You should be the person that decides what information others should know about. Furthermore, you should know what information other people have and use that concerns your business. It's as simple as that. 

**What information we collect and how we use it**

There are times when we may need information from you. At those times you are requested to fill out a form which is submitted to us. We collect your email address, telephone number, and other contact information that you provide to us when filling out our order forms. Masterworks Fine Art use email addresses and personal data provided by customers only in connection with Masterworks Fine Art. This information along with other information that you provide is used so that we may contact you about any art that you may reserve or inquire to know about. Furthermore, we may contact you about any special promotions, contests, or art related news that pertains to your interests. You can always unsubscribe to any information that we send you (if we do) by simply telling us. 

**We do not distribute personal information about our customers to parties outside of Masterworks Fine Art.**
