# Privacy Statement We Respect Your Privacy

**  The Masti4india.Com Privacy Statement** **Member Information:**  
The Masti4india.com does not keep any credit card or physical address information from its members.  All member passwords are encrypted and the webmaster has no idea what any of the member's passwords are.  For information regarding the privacy of your credit card information please visit [PayPal](http://www.paypal.com/).  
   
---  
**Cookies:**  
This web site does not use cookies to obtain information from its visitors.  The only way cookies are used is for the purpose of validating logins or sessions to access the members area of the site, and to handle the fair voting of images by visitors.  
   
**External Sites:**  
There are links to other web sites on this site.  If you visit any of these sites The Masti4india.com cannot guarantee the privacy policy used by these sites and cannot be held liable for any information collected at these sites.  
   
**Banner Ad Campaigns:**  
To help me keep this site running and pay for some simple overhead costs We have have implemented some banner ad campaigns.  You might see some pop up windows while you are visiting my site, along with other types of banners.  I have tried to choose companies that provides relevant ads and content in their banners. You can get more information regarding these ads by visiting the ad sponsor's site at [Burst Media.](http://www.burstmedia.com/) [Adhearus](http://www.adhearus.com/) [Clicksor](http://www.clicksor.com/)  
**Use of Email:**  
Any email sent to The Masti4india.com via the [contact](https://web.archive.org/web/20050205232757id_/http%3A//www.masti4india.com/Contact_us.htm) link is not archived in any way.  No email addresses are kept or sold to any third parties. This is also true of anyone who signs up for the [ Mailing List.](https://web.archive.org/web/20050205232757id_/http%3A//www.masti4india.com/mailing_list.htm)
