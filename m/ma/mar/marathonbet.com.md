# Marathon Online Sportsbook - Privacy Policy

## Use of Information

Registration with marathonbet.com is mandatory if you wish to use this website.

During the registration stage we will ask you for personal information. This will include your name, address, date of birth and contact details, such as your email and telephone number.

You may update your personal information at any time in the My Account/Personal Details section of the website or by telephoning the Customer Support team. We will also ask you to enter a unique identification ID and a password which you will use for any subsequent logins on this website.

As the internet is a global commodity, its use to collect and process information data may involve the transmission of this data on an international basis Therefore, by registering and using our website, you acknowledge and agree to our processing of your personal data in this way.

We may occasionally wish to contact you with information on promotional offers (such as Free bets), technical updates and new site features. If you wish to unsubscribe, please correct the correspondence check boxes on the My Preferences - Other Settings page accordingly. Alternatively, this can be done by contacting Customer Support.

## Cookies

When using the website a cookie will be stored. A cookie is a small piece of data used by your browser which is stored on your computer for a period of time. Every time you log into your account, a cookie will be created and stored. Once you log out, the cookie will be destroyed.

If you would like to find out more about cookies, please visit: <http://www.cookiecentral.com/faq/>

## Security

This website has been designed to provide the best levels of security to the customer. All critical communications between you and the website are encrypted using 256 bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.

When you log in, we recognize your IP address. This is then recorded on our system. When you log in again, you can see your last log-in date and the IP address used. This is done to assist you in preventing unauthorised access to your account.

There are also a number of additional security measures in place to protect the confidentiality and integrity of your information. Our servers have installed the latest firewall technology to act as a solid barrier to prevent unauthorised access.

## Communications

All telephone calls to and from the Customer Support team will be recorded, and may be used for training and security purposes.

## Other Sites

Our web site contains links to other sites. The company have no influence over other sites privacy policies, and as such have no responsibility for any information you may enter when accessing one of these links.

The company reserves the right to amend this privacy policy at any time.

## Queries

Should you have any queries about the use of information under this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [support@marathonbet.com](mailto:support@marathonbet.com)
