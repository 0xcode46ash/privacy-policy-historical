**Information Provided by You**  
In order to serve you better, we will ask personal information if you wish to register for a contest, apply for an Roundy’s Rewards Card, request information from Roundy's, apply for a job, or otherwise participate in a program offered by a Roundy's Site. Roundy's will only collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide through a Roundy's Site or e-mail. 

When you participate in a contest or promotion, or apply for or change information relating to an Roundy’s Rewards Card, we may request that you voluntarily submit personal information such as your name, address, e-mail address and phone number to provide you with the information or service you requested and to perform analyses of our services. If you would prefer that Roundy's not collect any personally identifiable information from you, please do not provide us with any information. We do not share this information with outside parties, however, Roundy's may disclose personally identifiable and aggregate anonymous information to companies and individuals employed to perform functions on our behalf (such as contest or promotion administration) or with our affiliates, partners or parent company. These companies and individuals may have access to your personal information to perform their business functions on our behalf as well as their own business purposes such as assisting us in administering various promotions, and providing specific promotional offers to you. Roundy's will provide those other companies with the information they need to perform the services and, except in the case of contests and promotions administered by outside companies, we prohibit them from using the information for other purposes. We do not control how other companies use the information you may provide directly to them (such as pursuant to a contest registration) and recommend that you check the privacy statement of the company requesting information for a description of their data collection practices. Please be aware that subsidiaries and affiliates of Roundy's are not outside parties or outside companies and that we may periodically share personal information or aggregated information with Roundy's subsidiaries and affiliates. 

In addition, Roundy's Sites collect personal information when you apply for a job or email consumer relations. We have no obligation of any kind with respect to such information except as provided in this Statement. We use such information internally for the purposes collected and we treat any comments, suggestions and the like as public information. 

**Email**  
When an email address is given by a visitor indicating inclusion in a Roundy's subscription list, we will send notices to the visitor in the form of an email. You may opt-out of this subscription at any time by updating your profile. [Sign In](https://web.archive.org/Profile/SignIn.aspx), click on 'My Account' and uncheck Weekly Email.

**Third Party Websites**  
The Roundy's Sites may contain links to other web sites over which we have no control. Roundy's is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of other web sites to which you may link from a Roundy's Site. We encourage you to review the privacy policies and terms and conditions of such web sites so that you can understand how those companies collect, use and share your information. This Statement applies solely to the information that Roundy's collects on the Roundy's Sites and does not apply to information Roundy's may collect by any alternative means or media such as at stores owned by Roundy's.   
  
**How to Change Your Information**  
If you have submitted personally identifiable information through a Roundy’s Site and would like to change that information, please [Sign In](https://web.archive.org/Profile/SignIn.aspx) and click on 'My Account'.

**Security Measures**  
Roundy's has instituted various procedures to prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, ensure the correct use of information and safeguard and secure the information we collect about you. However, Roundy's cannot guarantee that unauthorized access or disclosure of information will never occur. 

**Children's Privacy**  
Roundy's websites do not collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13. From time to time we may display information that is specifically aimed at children, for example coloring pages. However, registration is never required to access these pages and we do not collect personal information in connection with these activities.
