# Market Intellisearch: Financial news and insight into market trends and investment sentiment.

  
NON-REGISTERED USERS  
Currently certain content and tools on the MARKETINTELLISEARCH.com web site, such as delayed stock analysis, commentary, Power Rank ratings and options analytics are available to all users without registration. Market Intellisearch does not collect any personal information from you when you visit these areas of the Market Intellisearch web site. 

We collect non-identifiable information to measure how our users use the Market Intellisearch Web site. This information may include statistics about which pages are visited by users numbers and timing of unique visits. We use IP addresses to analyze trends and track user's movement. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Third Party Advertising  
The ads appearing on this web site are delivered to the users by our web advertising partners. Information about the users' visit to this site, such as number of times they have viewed an ad is used to serve ads to users on this site. Personal user information is not shared with our advertising partners.

CHANGES TO PRIVACY POLICY  
Market Intellisearch may change this policy from time to time without notice. Any changes to this policy will be promptly posted on this web site so we encourage you to refer back to this policy each time that you visit. Your use of Market Intellisearch's web site after any changes have been posted will constitute your agreement to the modified Privacy Policy.
