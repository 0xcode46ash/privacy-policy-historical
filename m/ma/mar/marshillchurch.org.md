**Information Usage**  
MarsHill.fm collects aggregate information on what pages users access or visit, information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations.

Like most web sites we use log files which track information such as

·         internet protocol (IP) addresses

·         browser type

·         internet service provider

·         referring/exit pages

·         platform type

·         date/time stamp

·         number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.

The information we collect is used for internal review only. It is used to improve the content of our web page and is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.

**Cookies**

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s computer tied to information about the user.  We do not use cookies. 

**Personal Information Usage**  
If you supply us with your postal address on-line you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on Mars Hill Fellowship, including but not limited to:

·         sunday church service information

·         upcoming events

·         information about our web site

·         volunteer opportunities

If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by emailing us at the above address.

Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information including, but not limited to:

·         sunday church service information

·         upcoming events

·         volunteer opportunities

If you do not wish to receive such telephone calls, please let us know emailing us at the above address.

**Third Party Advertisers**  
With respect to Ad Servers: We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies. 

**Security**  
With respect to security: We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses or phone number.
