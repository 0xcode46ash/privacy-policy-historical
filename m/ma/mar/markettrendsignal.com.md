# Privacy Policy

**PRIVACY POLICY**

_Market Trend Signal™ Privacy Policy_

The Market Trend Signal™ Privacy Policy is designed to assist you in understanding how we collect and use personal information you provide to us and to assist you in making informed decisions when using our site, our products, and our services.

**What Information Do We Collect?**

When you visit our Web site you may provide us with two types of information: 1) personal information you knowingly choose to disclose that is collected on an individual basis; and 2) Web site use information collected on an aggregate basis as you and others browse our Web site. All information collection pages are under a secure setting.

Personal Information You Choose to Provide

**Personal Information**

Market Trend Signal™ only collects personal information when you request our services and only uses the information collected to provide those services for you. If you choose to make a purchase, you will be asked to provide contact information such as your name and shipping address, and financial information such as your credit card number and expiration date. Your personal information is used for billing purposes and to process your order. Market Trend Signal™ handles all of your data with utmost confidentiality.

**Third Parties**

Any personal information provided by you will not be traded, rented, sold or otherwise shared with anyone outside of Market Trend Signal™, or used for reasons beyond those specifically explained in this policy. From time to time, we may engage third parties to process your information on our behalf; however, no Market Trend Signal™ affiliate (such as our credit card company) has permission to retain, share, store, or use your personal information for any reason other than to provide any service you request.

**Security**

To prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or improper use of your information, we have established appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial safeguards to protect the information we collect in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

**Registration Information**

You will provide us information about yourself, your firm or company, and your practices when you register to be a member of Markettrendsignal.com, register for certain services, or register for email newsletters and alerts. You may also provide additional comments on how you see Markettrendsignal.com so that we may attend to your needs and interests.

Credit Card information is collected when you subscribe to any markettrendsignal.com service, including any membership trial service. Your personal and financial information is stored on a secure server with infusionsoft.com and is removed completely upon termination of your subscription service.

**Email Information**

If you choose to correspond with us through email, we may retain the content of your email messages together with your email address and our responses.

**Web Site Use Information**

Similar to other commercial Web sites, our Web site utilizes a standard technology called “cookies” (see explanation What are Cookies? below) and Web server logs to collect information about how our Web site is used. Information gathered through cookies and Web server logs may include the date and time of visits, the pages viewed, time spent at our Web site, and the Web sites visited just before and just after our Web site. We, our advertisers and ad serving companies may also use small pieces of code called “web beacons” or “clear gifs” to determine which advertisements and promotions visitors and members have seen and how visitors and members respond to these advertisements and promotions.

**What are cookies?**

Cookies are messages that Web servers pass to your Web browser when you visit Internet sites. Your browser stores each message in a small text file that is temporarily and explicitly used by Market Trend Signal™. When you request another page from the server, your browser sends the cookie back to the server. These files typically contain information about your visit to the Web page, as well as any information you’ve volunteered, such as your name and interests.
