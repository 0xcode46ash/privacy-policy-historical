# Privacy & Security - MarketLive, Inc.

This is the website of MarketLive, Inc.

Our postal address is:  
625 Second St., Suite 100  
Petaluma, CA 94952  
We can be reached via email at [info@marketlive.com  
](mailto:info@marketlive.com)You can reach us by telephone at 877-341-5729

MarketLive, Inc. collects the email addresses, names, mailing addresses, and other contact information of those who voluntarily provide it when they request information or publications from MarketLive. All such information is used primarily to provide the information requested. From time to time we may send other information in addition to this if we feel it will be of interest, such as material about new products, services, or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such additional information, please let us know by sending an email to _[info@marketlive.com](mailto:info@marketlive.com)_ or by phoning us at 877-341-5729.

Our Web servers do not collect any information about our visitors, their Web domain, or their email address. We do not set cookies. We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies.

The information we collect is not sold, lent, rented, or otherwise shared with other organizations for any purpose.

If you wish to correct any inaccuracies in our record of your contact information or if you do not want to receive email from us in the future, please let us know through email, by phone, or in writing at the above address.

We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses or phone number.
