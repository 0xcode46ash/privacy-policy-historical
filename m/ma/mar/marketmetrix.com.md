# Market Metrix - The Global Leader of Satisfaction Solutions for the Hospitality Industry

**Market Metrix Privacy Statement**  
Effective Date: 1/31/2006 

Market Metrix, LLC, a leading provider of Customer Satisfaction and Human Resource services, is committed to protecting the privacy of the information in our care. In providing services to our clients, we act as a Data Processor; collecting and analyzing survey data from our clients' customers and presenting the results back to our clients. We do not collect personally identifiable information of our client's customers, we return aggregate reporting data to our clients.

**Collection of Personal Information**  
Market Metrix collects personal information in several different ways:

_Contact Us_ \- The Contact Us portion of this website collects personal information, such as name,company and email address, for purposes of facilitating business communication between Market Metrix and individuals or companies. It is not shared with any third party or used in ways not disclosed by this privacy agreement.

_Our Clients_ \- Personal information used within our services for our customers is initially provided off-line and entered into our system by our Professional Services team. Updates, deletions and additions to this data may be made online by authenticated users through our client websites or by making a request to our Professional Services staff via mail, phone, email, or through the contact us link at the top and bottom of this page.

_Client Customers (Surveys)_ \- Market Metrix clients provide us with personal information for their customers, such as name, phone number, email address, company, city, state, etc, which is then used by Market Metrix to invite these customers to take surveys concerning the products or services offered by our client. All personal information for our client customers that is used for the generation of these surveys and associated reports are provided to us via secured digital transmission. This information is used to facilitate these surveys and will only be used in ways disclosed by this privacy agreement.

Anyone may request to have their email addresses added to our "do not contact" list by submitting their email address through the opt-out form ([click here](https://web.archive.org/web/20070423120311id_/http%3A//www.marketmetrix.com/CSOptout.asp)), by following the "opt-out" link provided in the survey invitation, by mail, or by email to [webmaster@marketmetrix.com](mailto:webmaster@marketmetrix.com). Opt-outs through the form are instantaneous, all other methods may require up to 10 days from receipt of request. Our 'do-not-contact' list applies to all of our clients.

_Aside from our surveys, below are the email communications sent by our site:_

_MMHI Newsletter_ \- Personal information, such as name and email address, collected through the sign up form for the MMHI newsletter is used to create the distribution list for the newsletter and possible sales purposes. Additionally, individuals with whom Market Metrix has a prior business relationship may be included in the distribution list and those names and email addresses may be collected offline. This list is not shared with any third party and is not used in ways not disclosed by this privacy agreement.. To be removed from this newsletter, please email [webmaster@marketmetrix.com](mailto:webmaster@marketmetrix.com) or follow the unsubscribe instructions within the newsletter email. 

_Client Notification_ \- Frequently, Market Metrix will send automated emails informing our clients of account maintenance, data processing status, or other related information. These emails do not provide an opt out link, however the notify list is managed directly by the client using tools on our web site. Alternatively, clients may contact their client service representative or send email to [webmaster@marketmetrix.com](mailto:webmaster@marketmetrix.com) to request removal. 

_General Notification_ \- On occasion Market Metrix will send email informing our clients and affiliates of service outages, maintenance, or other important changes. We do not offer an opt-out mechanism for these types of communications.   


**Transmission and Use of Personal Information**  
Market Metrix utilizes industry standard data security measures to transmit and store personally identifiable information and to ensure that all personally and non-personally identifiable information that we receive is secure against unauthorized access. We will accept only the necessary data for our services from our clients, utilize encryption technologies in their transfer, and require standard authorization techniques in their utilization. For surveys in languages other than English, third party translators may be given access to personally identifiable information in order to perform the translation service. All translators are required to sign agreements restricting them from sharing data with any other party.

_Cookies_  
A cookie is a small text file placed on your browser by a web server. Marketmetrix.com utilizes cookies to identify individuals to assist in navigation and security and to display the proper survey pages. Cookies on marketmetrix.com are not used to identify individual users for any other non-technical purpose. Marketmetrix.com does not use third party cookies.

_IP Addresses_  
Market Metrix tracks IP addresses of visitors to our site in order to track site usage statistics. This data is collected for aggregate usage reporting and is not used to identify individual visitors to our site.

_Tracking_  
Market Metrix uses unique identifiers on survey invitation emails to ensure proper survey delivery and to track the response rates of invitations.

_Law Enforcement and Legal_  
We reserve the right to disclose personally identifiable information as required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on our Web site.  


**Changes to This Privacy Policy**  
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this privacy statement, the homepage, and other places we deem appropriate so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it. We reserve the right to modify this privacy statement at any time, so please review it frequently. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you here, by email, or by means of a notice on our homepage.
