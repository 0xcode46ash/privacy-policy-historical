# Privacy Policy – Mapme

Thank you for visiting the Internet web site located at the URL http://Mapme.com (the “Site”) of Map Labs Ltd.  (“MapMe”). This Privacy Policy details certain policies implemented throughout MapJam governing the use of personally identifiable information about users of our Site and users of our mapping services (the “Services”).

## Privacy Policy Updates

Due to the Internet’s rapidly evolving nature, Mapme may need to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If so, Mapme will post its updated Privacy Policy on our Site located at http://Mapme.com so you are always aware of what personally identifiable information we may collect and how we may use this information. Mapme encourages you to review this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes. Your continued use of this Site and/or continued provision of personally identifiable information to us will be subject to the terms of the then-current Privacy Policy.

## Information Collection and Use

You can generally visit our Site without revealing any personally identifiable information about yourself. However, in certain sections of this Site and when you use our Services, we may invite you to participate in surveys, questionnaires or contests, contact us with questions or comments or request information, provide us with feedback, or complete a registration form (“Registration Data”). Due to the nature of some of these activities, we may collect personally identifiable information as part of your Registration Data, such as your name, birthday, contact data such as your address, email address, phone number, username, password, credit card number, and other contact information that you voluntarily transmit with your communication to us. We also may collect information about you and your use of our Site and Services through your use of our Site and Services as set forth below in the “Aggregate Information” paragraph.

We may use your personally identifiable information to verify that you are an individual and not a “bot,” register you to use our Services, contact you to deliver certain services, news or information you have requested (including advertising information about Mapme or its partners), verify your authority to enter our Site, and improve the content and general administration of the Site and our Services and for targeted advertising.

## Children’s Privacy

Mapme recognizes the privacy interests of children and minors under the age of 18 and we encourage parents and guardians to take an active role in their children’s online activities and interests. This Site is not intended for children and minors under the age of 18. Mapme does not target its Services or this Site to children and minors under the age of 18. Mapme does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children and minors under the age of 18.

## Cookies

We may use small text files called cookies to improve overall Site experience. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s hard drive containing information about the user. Cookies generally do not permit us to personally identify you.

## Aggregate Information

Our Site may track the total number of visitors to the Site, the number of visitors to each page of our Site, browser type, and IP addresses, and we may analyze this data for trends and statistics in the aggregate, but such information will be maintained, used and disclosed in aggregate form only and it will not contain personally identifiable information. We may use such aggregate information to analyze trends, administer the Site, track users’ movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.

## Disclosure

We may provide any personally identifiable information supplied by you and the data generated by cookies and the aggregate information to the vendors and service agencies that we may engage to assist us in providing our Services to you. For example, we may provide your personally identifiable information to a credit card processing company to process any payment that you may make or to a marketing, research and/or advertising agency to send advertising to you on our behalf. Such third party entities will be restricted from further distributing your personally identifiable information and from using your personally identifiable information for commercial purposes and may be obligated to a written confidentiality agreement that is at least as protective of your personally identifiable information as the terms of this Privacy Policy. We will disclose your personally identifiable information if we reasonably believe we are required to do so by law, regulation or other government authority. We will not sell your personally identifiable information to any company or organization except we may transfer your personally identifiable information to a successor entity upon a merger, consolidation or other corporate reorganization in which Mapme participates or to a purchaser of all or substantially all assets of Mapme, Inc. to which this Site relates.

## Links to Third Party Sites

The Site may provide links to other Web sites or resources over which Mapme does not have control (“External Web Sites”). Such links do not constitute an endorsement by Mapme of those External Web Sites. You acknowledge that Mapme is providing these links to you only as a convenience, and further agree that Mapme is not responsible for the content of such External Web Sites. Your use of External Web Sites is subject to the terms of use and privacy policies located on the linked to External Web Sites.

## Security

We may employ procedural and technological security measures that are reasonably designed to help protect your personally identifiable information from loss, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or destruction. Mapme may use encryption, password protection, and other security measures to help prevent unauthorized access to your personally identifiable information. The data that you transmit as part of your use of the Services (“Storage Data”) is in encrypted form and Mapme does not have access to your Storage Data in its unencrypted form.

## Notice to Non-United States Users

The information you enter on this Site or otherwise provide to Mapme may be transferred outside of your country to the United States of America which does not necessarily offer an equivalent level of protection to that is required in other countries or the European Union. In particular, you are advised that the United States of America uses a sectoral model of privacy protection that relies on a mix of legislation, governmental regulation, and self-regulation. By agreeing to the terms of use and this privacy policy, you consent to the transfer of all such information to the United States of America which may not offer an equivalent level of protection to that required in your country and to the processing of that information by Mapme on its servers located in the United States of America as described in this Privacy Policy.

## Correction/Updating Personally Identifiable Information

Mapme provides you with the ability to review and correct any of the personally identifiable information that you have provided to us. If you wish to review or correct any information you have provided to us, you may access your account online. If you would like us to delete your data entirely, please send a request using [our contact form ](http://mapme.com/contact/).

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy please contact us via email at support@Mapme.com
