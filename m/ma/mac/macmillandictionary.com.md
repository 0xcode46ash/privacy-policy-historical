# Privacy policy for Macmillan Dictionary: the Free Online English Dictionary and Thesaurus

### Privacy Statement

In accordance with the new Data Protection Act, macmillandictionary.com is committed to observing standards ensuring that all personal information obtained from this site is used fairly and held securely.

### Personal information

We will not display your email address on this or any other website, nor will we give it to anyone else. Please contact us if you would like further information on Macmillan Dictionaries and other language related services.
