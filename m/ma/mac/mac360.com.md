# Privacy Policy | Mac360

The writers and publisher of Mac360 are committed to maintaining the privacy of personal information that you may provide. This Privacy Policy describes how we treat personal information received about you when you visit the Site.

## Links To Other Sites

We may provide content, services, or links from other sites and make them available to you from links located on Mac360. Those other sites are not subject to this Privacy Policy. Please review the privacy policy at each site to determine how that site protects your privacy.

## Information We Collect

 **General Information** : When you use certain features of Mac360, including Comments and Contact, we may collect personally identifiable information from you, such as, but not limited to, your name, location, and email address. We do not, however, knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Mac360 does not use tracking cookies, tracking scripts, analytics trackers (including Google Analytics), or other information which can be used or shared with other entities. Your privacy is important so we do not track our readers.

 **Mac360 Usage Information** : Our servers automatically collect IP addresses and standard usage information when your browser visits the site. This information helps us evaluate how visitors read and and navigate the site on an aggregate basis, including the number and frequency of visitors to each page on the site, when they visit, and the length of their visits, but no other information is collected, and no personally identifiable information is gathered or analyzed.

 **Surveys** : We may conduct occasional surveys and other customer research. These are entirely voluntary and you may decline to participate.

 **Registration** : We may require that anyone who wants to comment about articles featured on Mac360 or about other comments posted on Mac360 will be required to register, even though reading articles on the site is free. We request that every comment be signed with the commenter’s full registered name and email address. Names may be posted on article Comments, but email addresses are not shared with third parties and not made visible on the Comment section.

 **Comments** : All communication, including comments and email, become the property of Mac360. We may collect and use information from each commenter, or from email messages provided by you to Mac360.

## How We Use Information We Collect

Mac360 may, at our discretion, use information you provide to Mac360 this way:

For the purposes for which you specifically provided the information including, for example, to send notifications that you have requested, and to post your responses to articles.

To send you email notifications about Mac360, new services, products, offers, or advertising, or to otherwise contact you.

To enhance or develop features, products, and services on Mac360.

To provide Maca360’s advertisers and other third parties with aggregate information about our readers and their usage patterns.

To allow us to personalize the content and advertising that you and others may view based on personal characteristics or preferences.

We may combine the information that we collect from you on Mac360 with information that you provide to us in connection with your use of our other products, services, and other sites.

## Third Parties

We do not use third party network advertisers, such as Google AdSense, to deliver advertisements to you. Our advertisers are selected based upon the quality of their product or service, and each advertiser undergoes scrutiny to determine both viability and ‘fit’ with Mac360’s readers. Our advertisers do not have access to personally identifiable information.

Except as specifically set forth in this Privacy Policy, we will not share your personally identifiable information with any entity outside of the site, unless you opt in to having your personally identifiable information shared with a company that is not affiliated with us, or as may be required by law.

## Disclosing Information

We may disclose and use personally identifiable information to resolve disputes, troubleshoot problems, enforce our Terms of Service (for example, when necessary to protect our or a third party’s intellectual property or proprietary rights), or to alert you to changes in our Policy or agreements that may affect your use of the Site. We may also disclose personally identifiable information when we, in our sole discretion, believe it is necessary or appropriate, in connection with a sale or transfer of some or all of our assets in the Site, or when we, in good faith, believe that the law requires us to do so.

## Your Ability To Choose

You may inform us that you do not wish to receive information from Mac360 should you elect to register to use a portion of the site. We may or may not, at our sole discretion, honor your request.

## Cookies And Tracking

Mac360 does not place “cookies” on your computer’s browser, either directly or through third parties. Cookies are small text files that can tell advertisers and tracking agent what you are doing online, even though they usually don’t record your name or other personally identifiable information. Cookies are common on the internet but we have chosen not to use cookies.

Not all cookies are tracking cookies. Most sites may place cookies on your computer in addition to cookies placed by advertisers. The only cookies used on Mac360 would be utilized to save your registration information, if you choose to register, but they do not tell us what you do or where you go online.

## Security

What information Mac360 collects is stored in server log data on our limited access servers. We will maintain safeguards to protect the security of these servers, however, such data is not personally identifiable information.

## Policy Modifications

Mac360 may make material change to this Privacy Policy at any time and we cannot provide notification to readers regarding such changes. You are required to read the Policy for updates each time you visit Mac360 or be bound by the policy details.
