## MACPROVIDEO.COM - Privacy Policy

MACPROVIDEO.COM recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of all information that you provide to us. Therefore, we created the following Privacy Policy to guide our relationship with our friends and users.

**Your Personal Information**

We may request that you voluntarily supply us with personal information, including your e-mail address, postal address, home or work telephone number, and credit card number (but only if you choose to), for such purposes as correspondence, making a purchase. If you choose to give us personal information via the Internet, then it is our intent to let you know how we will use this information. We will not share specific information about you to advertisers or third parties without prior notice or your consent.

**Use of Your Personal Information**

We do not use personal identifying information for any reason that is not disclosed either in this policy or at the time the information is requested. There may be instances where you will have the opportunity to subscribe to an e-mail list that will send information about a specific topic to your e-mail address. You will always have the option of unsubscribing to these lists.

**Third Parties Disclosures**

We do not share personal identifying information with any third party without your permission. However, PLEASE BE AWARE that when you voluntarily disclose personal information such as your name or e-mail address at third-party web sites, the information collected by them is outside of our control and may result in unsolicited messages from others.

**Accuracy of User Information**

Questions regarding our Privacy Policy, the accuracy of your personal information, or the use of the information collected should be directed via e-mail to [mark@macprovideo.com](mailto:mark@macprovideo.com)

These privacy policies are effective as of May 1, 2004. We reserve the right to change this policy at any time and will post any such changes as soon as they go into effect.

**Your Acceptance of These Terms**

By using the MACPROVIDEO.COM web site, you signify your agreement to both our "Privacy Policy" and Terms and Conditions". If you do not agree with either or both of these policies, please do not use this web site or purchase any of our learning products, which appear on this web site. Your continued use of the MACPROVIDEO.COM web site following the posting of any changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes as well.
