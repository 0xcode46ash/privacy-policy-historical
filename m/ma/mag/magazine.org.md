# MPA Website Privacy Policy

**[](https://web.archive.org/web/20010606143222id_/http%3A//magazine.org/index.asp)**

**MPA PRIVACY POLICY**

This is the Website of the **Magazine Publishers of America** , the industry association for the consumer magazine industry. Our postal address is 919 Third Avenue, New York, New York, 10022-3901. Our general phone number is 212-872-3700. Our general facsimile number is 212-888-4217. 

The main purpose of our Website is to provide information to our domestic, international, and associate members. We can provide requested information via e-mail or hard-copy transmission. If you request information, please specify which method of response you desire.

Our Website does not automatically collect any individually identifiable information from visitors. E-mail addresses are not captured or stored for future use, unless you provide your address and request information.

If you provide us with your postal or e-mail address, you may receive periodic mailings, containing information we believe you may find useful, including notices of new services offered by MPA, upcoming events, published newsletters and press releases. If you do not wish to receive this information, we will be happy to remove your name from our database. You may notify us by mail or facsimile at the above contact address and fax number. Please specify if you do not wish to receive any future electronic contacts and/or First-class mailings. 

We do not share our mailing lists with any other organizations and any personal information you provide MPA will not be disseminated to third parties. We will not contact visitors to our Website by telephone. Information we provide is compiled from a variety of public data sources. 

We do our best to ensure accurate, timely, and complete information. We cannot, however, guarantee the accuracy of information obtained from external sources. If you have any questions about MPA's privacy policy, please contact us at the above phone number.
