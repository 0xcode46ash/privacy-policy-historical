# The Magazine Group

Privacy Policy

The Magazine Group Privacy Policy - August 2003

www.magazine-group.co.uk is published by Three PM Ltd (trading as The Magazine Group) .   
During your visit to our site, we may collect and identify information about you, and this policy seeks to provide you with specific information about how this is done. You should review this policy from time to time in order to be aware of any changes. Continued use of the site signifies your acceptance of this privacy policy. 

Personal Information - this refers to information that tells us specifically who you are, e.g. name, telephone number or postal address. We generally require this information to provide services you have requested, such as processing your order. You choose to disclose as much personal information as you wish, but you should be aware that we may not be able to provide the service without all of the information requested. 

We also collect information from you using technology, which may not be apparent to you, e.g. your IP address, which is not personally identifiable. We use this information for internal review, to improve the site and its services. 

Cookies are used on the shopping pages to keep track of the contents of your shopping cart once you have selected an item.

**Email**  
If you provide us with your email address, we may send you information about promotions by The Magazine Group. Should you want to opt out of receiving this information, please email [privacy@magazine-group.co.uk](mailto:privacy@magazine-group.co.uk) or write to The Magazine Group, Building 960, Cornforth Drive, Sittingbourne research Centre, Sittingbourne, ME9 8BR, UK. We do not pass email addresses on to third party advertisers. Please indicate when ordering whether you wish to receive direct mail from other companies. 

**Security**   
Once you have selected the magazines you wish to order, proceed to our secure server where delivery and payment details are taken. The secure area of this site is a BT Trust Services secure site, certified by Verisign. Please click on the Verisign logo on our secure pages for further details.

**Your Consent**   
By using www.magazine-group.co.uk, you consent to the gathering of information as described in this policy. If you have further questions, please email [privacy@magazine-group.co.uk](mailto:privacy@magazine-group.co.uk) or write to The Magazine Group Building 960, Cornforth Drive, Sittingbourne research Centre, Sittingbourne, ME9 8BR, UK.
