# Privacy Policy | Magic Freebies

Magic Freebies is committed to protecting your privacy. Please read on for our privacy policy and if you have any more questions please don't hesitate to contact us.

#### 1\. Your Information and Personal Data

We may collect information from you at times for the purposes of customising your visitor experience whilst using our site.

#### 2\. Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure and we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

#### 3\. Cookies

A cookie is a small file which asks permission to be placed onto your computer’s hard drive, which once you agree, helps analyse web traffic or lets you know when you visit a particular site.

Cookies help us to provide you a better website, enabling us to see which pages you find useful and which ones you don’t. The cookie in no way gives access to your computer or information about you, other than the data that you choose to share with MagicFreebiesUK.

When you visit a domain, the web pages on the domain may contain a third-party domain. This allows them to follow you from one site to the other. These are called Third-party cookies.

You can choose to accept or decline cookies if you prefer by adjusting the setting within your web browser.

When you visit a domain, the web pages on the domain may contain a third-party domain. This allows them to follow you from one site to the other. These are called Third-party cookies.

If you want to stop cookies being stored on your computer in future, please refer to your browser manufacturer's instructions by clicking “Help” in your browser menu.

We use Google Analytics remarketing codes to log when users view specific pages or take specific actions on a website. This allows us to provide targeted advertising in the future. If you do not wish to receive this type of advertising from us in the future you can opt out using the [DoubleClick opt-out page](https://www.google.com/settings/ads/onweb#display_optout) or the [Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp).

#### 4\. Disclosure

By signing up to our mailing list you will receive daily email newsletters, freebie alerts, site updates and competitions from Magic Freebies

  2. Click the 'unsubscribe' link which is situated at the bottom of every e-mail we send.
