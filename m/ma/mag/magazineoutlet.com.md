**Magazineoutlet.com Privacy Statement**

****

Magazineoutlet.com values your privacy and is dedicated to protecting your personal information. Our privacy statement is a summary of our privacy policy. You can read our complete privacy policy by clicking here. 

Magazineoutlet.com logs IP addresses (a unique number assigned to every computer on the Internet) in order to improve security, analyze trends and administer the site. We track your movement within the site, the pages from which you were referred, access times and browser types. We use this information in aggregate and do not link it to you personally. We promise to take measures to protect all the personal information you provide at all times. We do not rent, sell or lease your personal information, and you may opt-out of the use of your information for secondary purposes at any time - click [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20031223091754id_/http%3A//www.magazineoutlet.com/magoutlet/email_form_optout.asp) for details. You also have the opportunity to correct or change the information you have provided to us.

**A Special Note About Children**

Persons under the age of 18 are not eligible to use any services on this site. 

**Order**

We request information from you on our order form. This information is used for order fulfillment, billing and customer service. You must provide delivery information and your credit card number and type in order for us to fulfill your order. If you visit Magazineoutlet.com by linking through one of our partners or affiliates, they may forward us your delivery and credit card information, saving you from reentering the information on our site. 

**Tracking Technology**

A cookie is a piece of data we store on your hard drive when you visit certain web pages. It allows us to track and target your interests. Even if you set your Browser to reject cookies, you may still use our site. If you have been offered our services through one of our marketing partners or affiliates (see "Use of Information" section below), we may share the information you enter with that partner or affiliate. To do so, we use a technology called clear GIFs, which automatically and securely transfer the information you enter. In the future, we may use other similar technologies on the site for the same purpose. If we do so, we will modify our privacy policy accordingly. See the "Notification of Changes" section below. 

**Log Files**

We log IP addresses (a unique number assigned to every computer on the Internet) in order to improve security, analyze trends and administer the site. We track your movement within the site, the pages from which you were referred, access times and browser types. We use this information in aggregate and do not link it to you personally. 

**Use of Information**

_Synapse Group, Inc. Companies_

SynapseConnect, Inc. is a wholly owned subsidiary of Synapse Group, Inc. From time to time, we may share your information with other subsidiaries of Synapse Group, Inc. in order to provide all the services necessary to fulfill your subscription(s) as well as provide you with all of the opportunities Synapse Group has to offer. 

_Contractual Relationships_

We provide your information to our third party service vendors with whom we have contractual relationships for the purpose of servicing our processing of your magazine subscriptions. These vendors provide various services on our behalf such as customer service, web hosting, billing and fulfillment. These vendors are not allowed to sell, rent or lease your personally identifiable information and cannot use your information for any secondary purpose. 

_Third Party Marketing Affiliates and Publishers_

When you order a magazine, we forward your contact information to the applicable magazine publisher for fulfillment. If you visit Magazineoutlet.com by linking through one of our partners or affiliates, your information also may be shared with the partner or affiliate which originated the link to our site. In those instances, your information will be governed by the privacy policy of the magazine publishers for the titles you have chosen to receive, or said partners and affiliates. 

_Exceptional Circumstances_

Please note that if Magazineoutlet.com, SynapseConnect, Inc. or it's parent, Synapse Group, Inc., or any of their lines of business is sold or disposed of as a going concern, whether by merger, sale of assets or otherwise, the relevant customer database of Magazineoutlet.com could be sold as part of that transaction. We also may have to release personally-identifying information when it is necessary to comply with judicial or other government subpoenas, warrants or orders. 

**Links**

This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that SynapseConnect is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. When you leave our site, we encourage you to read the privacy statements of each web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this web site. 

**Security**

This web site takes measures to protect your personal information by using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption, which is recognized as an industry standard in encryption technology. 

We also offer our exclusive credit card guarantee. If your credit card number is ever used without your permission as a direct result of you having made a purchase on Magazineoutlet.com, we'll reimburse you for the liability not covered by your credit card company, up to a maximum of $50. 

**Special Offers and Choice/Opt-Out**

From time to time, we may present you with specially selected offers from SynapseConnect or one of our partners. Out of respect for your privacy, you can forfeit these opportunities, by clicking [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20031223091754id_/http%3A//www.magazineoutlet.com/magoutlet/email_form_optout.asp). 

**Site and Service Updates**

We communicate with you to provide requested services and on issues relating to your account. We also send site and subscription service updates. You are not able to unsubscribe from these important announcements, which contain important information about your subscriptions and Magazineoutlet.com. 

**Correcting/Updating Personal Information**

If you want to change your personal information (such as your address or credit card number) you can do so on the Magazineoutlet.com site. Upon verification, we will provide you with the information we have for all your active orders placed with us. To change your address, click [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20031223091754id_/http%3A//www.magazineoutlet.com/magoutlet/email_form_change_address.asp). To request your record or for any other changes, use our customer service form by clicking [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20031223091754id_/http%3A//www.magazineoutlet.com/magoutlet/email_form_customer_service.asp). 

**Notification of Changes**

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on our site by identifying them as changes to our previous privacy policy. We will identify those changes for at least 60 days so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it.
