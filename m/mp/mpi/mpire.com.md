**Mpire Corporation - Privacy Policy**

Mpire Corporation (" **Mpire** ") takes your privacy concerns seriously and has created the following Privacy Policy to explain to you Mpire's information collection and use practices. The Privacy Policy describes what information Mpire collects through its services and website, and how Mpire uses this information. By using Mpire's services and website, you agree that you have read and understand this Privacy Policy and you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

**Changes to the Privacy Policy**  
Mpire may periodically update this Privacy Policy. Mpire will notify you of significant changes to the Privacy Policy by placing a prominent notice on the Mpire website. It is your responsibility to review the most recent version of the Privacy Policy and remain informed about any changes to it.

**What Information Is Collected?**  
Mpire may collect both personally identifiable information (" **Personal Information** ") and information about your use of Mpire's services and website that is not identifiable to you personally (" **Non-personal Information** "). Non-personal Information may include technical information such as the type of Internet browser you use, the date and time you access the website, your Internet protocol address, and which pages you view on the website. Personal Information may include your name, address, email address, telephone number, and place of business. You will have the opportunity to provide Mpire with Personal Information through registration for Mpire's services.

**Use of Non-personal Information**  
Mpire uses Non-personal Information to improve its services and this website. Mpire may share Non-personal Information with select third parties on an anonymous and aggregate basis, but never in a way that directly connects you to the Non-personal Information.

**Use of Personal Information**  
Mpire uses Personal Information to provide you with access to Mpire's services. In addition, Mpire uses your Personal Information to respond to your requests, personalize your access to Mpire's website and services, and contact you with information that might be of interest to you. Mpire will not share your Personal Information with any third party except as set forth in this Privacy Policy or with your permission.

Mpire may disclose your Personal Information to third parties if Mpire believes that the disclosure is necessary to enforce this Privacy Policy or the terms governing your use of Mpire's services, or protect the rights or property of Mpire or another party. Mpire may also create anonymous aggregate information from your Personal Information and share this anonymous aggregate information with select third parties. This anonymous aggregate information will not identify you or your Personal Information. Mpire reserves the right to disclose your Personal Information when Mpire believes that an applicable law, regulation, or legal process requires it.

**Product Information**  
Periodically, Mpire may contact you regarding Mpire's services, but only if you have indicated a preference to receive this information. To unsubscribe from these communications, you must log into your account and change your communication preferences.

**Contractors**  
Mpire may retain the services of certain outside contractors. At times, Mpire must disclose relevant Personal Information to these contractors so they may provide services to Mpire. Mpire requires its contractors to keep this information confidential and to only use the information on Mpire's behalf.

**Security of Information**  
Mpire uses security measures to protect the privacy of your Personal Information. Mpire limits the employees and contractors of Mpire that can access your Personal Information and requires these employees and contractors to adhere to a confidentiality policy. Mpire also uses technological security measures to protect your Personal Information. However, transmissions over the Internet are not completely secure, and any information you send to or through Mpire's services and website may be accessible by others. Mpire does not guarantee the success of its security measures.

**Cookies**  
Cookies are small text files stored by your Internet browser on your computer when you visit a website. Mpire uses cookies to improve its services and website and make them easier to use. Cookies permit Mpire to recognize you and avoid repetitive requests for the same information. You may change your Internet browser's settings to refuse these cookies. However, if you do so, you may not be able to access certain parts of the Mpire website or Mpire services.

**Children**  
Children under the age of 13 are prohibited from registering for Mpire's services. Mpire does not intentionally collect or use any Personal Information from children who identify themselves as being under the age of 13. By using Mpire's services you represent to Mpire that you are at least 13 years old.

**Use of Third Party Services**  
This website may contain links to third party websites and the Mpire services may provide access to third party websites and services. Mpire is not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please visit the privacy policies of these third parties in order to understand their privacy and information collection and use practices.

**Mergers and Acquisitions**  
In the event that Mpire or a portion of Mpire's assets are acquired by another company, the information collected by Mpire through this website and Mpire's services, including Personal Information, may be one of the transferred assets.

**No Error-Free Performance**  
Mpire does not guarantee error-free performance under this Privacy Policy. Mpire may not always catch an unintended privacy issue, despite Mpire's efforts to do so. Mpire will use reasonable efforts to comply with this Privacy Policy and will take prompt corrective action when Mpire learns of any failure to comply with it. Accordingly, Mpire welcomes your feedback regarding any privacy concerns that you may have.
