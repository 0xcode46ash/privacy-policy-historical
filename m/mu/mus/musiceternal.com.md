**Privacy Policy**

 **PURCHASE INFORMATION**

All information you provide is entered on a secure page to transmitting to Internet Secure for transaction of membership.

The receipt is sent to both you (the member) and also to an e-mail within Musiceternal.com and/or DJ-Draven.com. 

The copy of the receipt is used only to:

  * -Verify purchase of membership

  * -Internal records

  * -Contact information for processing membership




Under **_NO_** circumstances is this information given or sold to any third party.

**OTHER INFORMATION**

All additional information that is obtained by member after purchase may be posted on Musiceternal.com and/or DJ-Draven, but only after approval by that member. Otherwise, it is not presented, unless it is a review, song Of The Week, or some other means that does not show material or information provided by you, the member.
