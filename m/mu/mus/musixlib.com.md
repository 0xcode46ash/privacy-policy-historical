# MusixLib - Albums And More

Privacy Notice 

1\. Purpose: This document intended to set out musixlib's commitment to your privacy and to the protection of your personal data (hereinafter "Data"), under the terms and conditions set out in the Terms of Use. 

2\. Collection of Data: As some activities on musixlib's site require registration, we collect and process data that you provide during the registration. We also collect your IP address and information provided by your web browser and computer. When you use musixlib's site, we may record information about your usage of the site. The collection of data occur both for registered users and occasional users, the Website may implement an automatic tracking process (such as cookies), which you may block by changing your browser settings. 

3\. Data recipients: The data requested and collected is required in order to use the site's features. We may use a range of information as set forth above to provide you with advertising. Your Data may be transmitted to third parties. musixlib may further release data if the law requires it to do so or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with the law or respond to a court order, subpoena, or search warrant or to protect musixlib's rights and interests. However, we do not use your email address to send commercial or marketing messages without your consent. We may use your email address without further consent for non-marketing or administrative purposes. Please note that any personal information or video content that you voluntarily disclose online (such as videos, comments, your profile page etc) may be collected and used by others. Share responsively. 

4\. Data security and retention: musixlib sees great importance to the security of your data, and takes all appropriate steps to keep it secured. musixlib keep the data for as long as necessary for its purposes. musixlib may keep the data for longer periods of time for any reason. 

5\. Linked Content

Certain sections of the site may involve sites which belongs to third parties.  
musixlib is not and shall not be responsible for that,  
and any use by the user of the sites belonging to third parties is in his sole responsibility.

Therefore, musixlib will not be responsible for actions that occurred at sites belonging to third parties. Moreover,  
musixlib warns from sites belonging to third parties and offers the user to read carefully the terms of use of those sites. 

6\. Notification of Privacy Statement Changes

We may update this privacy statement to reflect changes to our information practices.  
If we make any material changes we will notify you by email (sent to the e-mail address specified in your account) or by means of a notice on this Site prior to the change becoming effective.  
We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices 

7\. Use of Information

musixlib may use information  
(whether personal identifying information or not) that we collect about you to:

· Manage your relationship with us and provide you with customer support.

· Perform research and analysis about your use of, or interest in, our products, services or content, or products, services or content offered by others.

· Communicate with you by e-mail, postal mail, telephone and/or mobile devices about products or services that may be of interest to you either from us or third parties.

· Cooperate with legal, criminal or regulatory investigations or proceedings.

· Manage our business.

· We may use an outside ad company to display ads on our site.  
These ads may contain cookies.  
While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site,  
cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company,  
and we do not have access to this information. 

8\. Your rights: You are entitled to access and correct your data by requesting to do so via the Contact us section, or by sending an email letter to the following email: support@musixlib.com
