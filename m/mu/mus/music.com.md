# Music.com - The Search is Over!

This is the web site of Music.com, Inc. Music.com can be reached via e-mail at [info@staff.music.com](mailto:info1@staff.music.com).

User Information

For each visitor to the Music.com site, our Web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address. Music.com does not directly collect information on consumers who browse the site.

Cookies

With respect to cookies: Music.com does not use cookies, however, some of its ad partners may use cookies to ensure that visitors are not repeatedly sent the same banner ads. For information on our ad partners privacy policies please read the Ad Server section of this policy.

Ad Servers

With respect to Ad Servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our site. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information. For further information, consult the privacy policies of: <http://www.valueclick.com/> & <http://www.bigmailbox.com/>.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above email address.
