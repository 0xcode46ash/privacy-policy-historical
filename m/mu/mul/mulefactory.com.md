# Privacy and data protection | Mulefactory the item and currency shop

  * Mulefactory respects your privacy rights and protects all of your personal information provided by you. This document describes you how our company uses and stores your personal information. 

By using our site you agree that you have read this Privacy Policy. We reserve the right to edit this document any time in the future so we encourge you to visit this site often. If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy feel free to contact us any time via Live Help or e-mail. 

We use your personal data only for the transaction. All data of the customers will be saved and processed considering the prescriptions of the European Union. 

There are several pages in our website that may link you to other websites that do not operate under this Privacy Policy. When you are gone to any of these website this Privacy Policy no longer applies. 

  * Your personal information means any information about you such as your e-mail address, name, phone number and other official documents you may share with us through our conversation. We don't always need you to send us official documents but when we do it is for security reasons. Our company collects personal information to verify the accuracy of your name to screen for fraud. We only use your e-mail address to contact you about your order status. We do not share your personal data nor your e-mail address or ingame contact with any third parties without your explicit consent. We use technical and organisational safety precautions to protect your personal data from manipulation, loss, destruction or access of unauthorized persons. 

Our company stores all of your information on computers located in and controlled secure facility protected from physical or electronic unauthorized access or use. We protect your privacy and integrity of the information collected by our employees for administrative protocols, technical safeguards and physical security controls designed to limit access, prevent or decent unauthorized access, loss or manipulation of the information under its control. Our method of electronic storage is 100% secure. 

  * Mulefactory has **ZERO TOLERANCE** for any frauds about payments and access or acquire customer informations on their websites. We keep the rights to forward your verification such as telephone number or personal data to PayPal or Moneybookers (Skrill) for further review if any troubles with your payment occurs (charge back attempt, uncleared payment). Mulefactory aggressively prosecutes to the fullest extent of the law against those perpetrators apprehended conducting fraudulent activities on its web site. We work with local, nations and international fraud investigation agencies and employs a variety of electronic and other means to discourage, detect and intercept fraudulent activites.
