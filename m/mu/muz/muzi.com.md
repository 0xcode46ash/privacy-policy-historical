# Muzi Privacy Policy

  
Muzi is deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users, and more specifically, its own members. Below is information outlining our continued support for the responsible management of user information. 

During the process of delivering content to you, Muzi does not collect any personally identifiable information about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address. However, Muzi does obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information such as the Internet Service Provider you use to connect to the Internet, your browser type, etc. The information collected by Muzi is used for the purpose of analyzing traffic pattern and readership "hotspot", so that Muzi can deliver better content that suits your need. 

Muzi, as well as most sites on the Internet, use "cookies" to enhance your experience as a pre-joined member. What are cookies and why do we use them? A cookie is a unique number that is provided to you the first time you log into Muzi as a member. This unique number, which is stored in a cookie file on your computer, helps Muzi target content to you. This cookie can help Muzi ensure that only authorized user can get to his/her content of interest. 

If you do not want the benefit of cookies, there is a simple procedure to manually delete your cookies. Please consult your web browser's Help documentation for more information. 

If you have any additional questions about Muzi's dedication to consumer privacy, please contact us at [support@muzi.net](mailto:support@muzi.net)
