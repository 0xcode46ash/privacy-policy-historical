Much Games Privacy Policy

* * *

MuchGames has made this statement to demonstrate firm commitment to privacy. It is our outmost intent to ensure that all of our visitors are happy and that all user information is disclosed to the public. All our information is kept secure from the public and the user has full control on how much information is revealed.   
---  
**Automatically Logged Information**  
We may log certain information such as your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. It may also be used to prevent voters from cheating and reviewers from spamming.   
**Cookies**  
We use various cookies to remember certain user preferences. Cookies are stored for login purposes and statistics monitoring.   
**Advertisers**  
We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information.   
**Online Forms**  
Our site`s profile form requires users to give us contact information (like their name, email address), unique identifiers (like their email address), and demographic information (like their zip code or age). Contact information from the registration forms is used to get in touch with the customer when necessary. Information within the submission and send to friend forms is not given away to anyone except the one to whom it concerns. All our data in our forms is kept safe and private. 

Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. 

Unique identifiers (such as email address) are collected from Web site visitors to verify the user`s identity, and for use as account numbers in our record system. 

Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site. This information is shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis.   
  
**Game Submission**  
We grant all our game authors full privileges and control to their material. It gives game developers distinct business opportunities to promote their material and in return receive massive traffic response from millions of gamers all over the world. If an author is not satisfied with having their game posted on MuchGames, they may request a removal at any time.
