# MHSchool: McGraw-Hill

Macmillan/McGraw-Hill Privacy Notice

When you submit an online form to request a catalog, to respond to a survey, or to communicate with us for any other reason, the information we collect from you (e.g. your name, mailing address, email address) is added to our electronic mailing list. Occasionally, Macmillan/McGraw-Hill may use that information to notify you, our customer, of updates to our Web site or of products and services that may be of interest to you. Please be assured that Macmillan/McGraw-Hill will never share information we collect from you with companies outside the family of The McGraw-Hill Companines.

If you would like to review, edit, or delete any information we have collected from you, or if you have questions or concerns about this privacy notice, please email us at [mmh_privacy@mcgraw-hill.com](mailto:mmh_privacy@mcgraw-hill.com?subject=Privacy%20Policy%20Inquiry) or write to us at the following address:
