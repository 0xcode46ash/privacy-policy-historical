# MyZA - Privacy

**Privacy Statement**

We are committed to protecting the privacy of personal data collected on our websites. This statement summarizes the policies of these websites regarding the collection, use, and transfer of data.

**Address**

This website is operated by MyZA.  
PO Box 3955

6056

Facsimile 086 505 4114  
We can be [contacted by email HERE](http://myza.co.za/contact/)

**The Information We Collect**

We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this Privacy Statement.

**User ID and Password**

MyZA reserves the right to change User Ids and Passwords at any time to protect the security of the site.

**Registration**

As a visitor to our website you may be required to give us contact information such as your name and e-mail address. We may use this information to send you additional information about products and services or to contact you if necessary.

**Links**

This website contains links to other sites. MyZA is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such sites.

**Security**

Your data is not disclosed to third parties on the internet and your profile and password remain confidential at all times.

**Log Files**

We use IP addresses to analyse trends, administer the site and track user movement. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

**Newsletters**

Established members will occasionally receive e-newsletters. Out of respect for the privacy of our users we present the option to not receive these types of communications. Please see opt-out below.

**Correction/Updating Personal Information**

If your personal information changes, or if you no longer desire our service, you may correct, update or remove your personal data provided to us. This can be done by e-mailing through the ‘contact us link’ or by facsimile.

**Opt Out Clause**

From time to time the information that is volunteered may be used by MyZA for communication or marketing purposes to members. However, MyZA supports the Promotion of Access to Information Bill and will not make your details available to any other company or organisation. If you do not want to receive communication about particular MyZA products and services that may be of interest to you, please advise us when providing your information, or subsequently by sending an e-mail to us at the above address. We will ensure your name is removed from that particular MyZA communication.

**Notification of Changes**

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post these changes on this page so you are always aware of what information we collect and how we use it.
