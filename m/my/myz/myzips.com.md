**Information Collection & Usage**  
Our site will not share, sell, or otherwise divulge any information gathered on this site with unauthorized parties. 

**Third Party Cookies**  
A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. Once the user closes their browser, the cookie simply terminates. Some of our business partners use cookies on our site (for example, advertisers). However, we have no access to or control over these cookies. Please refer to the privacy policies of the network for details. In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser. 

**Log Files**  
Our site uses log files to review visitor trends, improve visitor experience and gather standard demographic information. Any IP addresses used in these studies are not personally identifiable. 

**Sharing**  
We do not share, sell, or rent any information with any unauthorized third parties. 

**Links**  
This website contains links to other websites. Myzips.com is not responsible for the content of any third party website. Please review the privacy policies of each and every website you choose to visit. Our privacy policy applies to our site only. 

**Third Party Cookies**  
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" or "web beacon" on your browser to collect information. 

**Managing Cookies**  
You can adjust your own personal cookie settings in your browser. Please access your browsers "Help" section and search for "cookie" to manage how your browser handles cookies. You should be able to do everything from allowing all cookies to blocking all cookies.
