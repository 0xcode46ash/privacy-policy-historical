# Privacy

We wish to ensure your privacy and that of your personal electronic communication.

  * We will not share your e-mail address or other specific personal information without your consent.
  * We may use "cookies" to generate profile information that may be used to create personalized content, services and advertising on or related to the Site or any other sites owned or operated (either wholly or in part) by us.
  * We may also use your profile information to create aggregate reports. Such profile information is anonymous and does not contain any personally identifiable information.
  * We may also use personal information supplied by you when you complete questionnaires or other information requests on the Site to create mailing lists and other databases.
  * We will use such mailing lists and other databases only for our own purposes, and we will not sell or share them with other parties. However, we reserve the right to cooperate fully with local, provincial or state and federal officials in any investigation relating to any content provided by you (including personal or private electronic communication transmitted on the Site) or purported unlawful activities that may involve your participation.
