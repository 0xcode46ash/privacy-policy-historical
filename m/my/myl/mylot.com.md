# myLot - Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy**   
You have the right to control your personal information. 

We also want to present information to you that is tailored your needs. For this reason, we ask you to register or to provide personal information when you visit certain areas of the site, or subscribe to a mailing list or search feed. We use the personal information you share with us to ensure the content and services that we provide are tailored to your needs. No information about your subscriptions, activities or any personally identifiable information we collect is shared outside the company. 

If you register with the site, or opt in to receiving notifications when providing personal information, myLot may use your email address to alert you to product upgrades, special offers, updated information, and new services. If you request it, we will cease to contact you using one or more of these methods. 

**Email Addresses, Publishers and Subscriptions**   
myLot is the sole owner of the information collected on this web site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. Your email address will never be shared by myLot with third parties. 

**Server Logs**   
The myLot web server and parts of the myLot application log activity to file. We use logs to provide site-wide reporting, to ensure smooth operation of the services myLot delivers, and for general web site and application maintenance. Like almost all Internet servers, logged information may include timestamps, your client IP address (as it appears to the server), and the page(s) you visit. Our detailed server logs will not be shared with any third party. We may however elect to publish aggregate statistics on visitor activity from time to time based on log information (e.g. "we had 1,234 visitors yesterday"). We will never publish any personally identifiable information in these analyses. 

If you have any questions about myLot privacy policies please contact us.
