    Privacy Statement

|  |  |   
---|---|---  
| MyPlayCity.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site. You can insert contact information (e.g, your name and e-mail address) to our registration forms. We use customer contact information from the registration form to send the user information related to MyPlayCity.com only when requested by the user. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary. Your e-mail address will never be given or sold to a third party and will only be viewed by MyPlayCity.com. Your pager e-mail address will not be disclosed and will only be used for sending our updates upon request. This site contains links to other sites. MyPlayCity.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. If you have any questions about this privacy statement, you can contact us at [privacy@MyPlayCity.com](mailto:privacy@MyPlayCity.com). For other questions regarding the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, contact [webmaster@MyPlayCity.com](mailto:webmaster@MyPlayCity.com)
