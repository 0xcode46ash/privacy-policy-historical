# Banner Maker - Privacy Policy

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html). 

We use cookies to track your changes to your banner in order to provide services such as storing banner settings across multiple sessions. We also use cookies to identify logged in users and maintain sessions. None of these cookies are used for advertising purposes. We do not share the information from our visitors or VIP members with any third-party with the exception of where they collect such information for the purpose of billing or in conjunction with advertising as specified in the above paragraph. We do not send unsolicited emails to our users or visitors.
