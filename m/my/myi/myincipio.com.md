### Privacy Policy

Incipio Technologies, Inc. respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") outlines the personally identifiable information that Incipio collects through its website, the purposes for which Incipio uses such information, and our commitment to maintaining your privacy by not unnecessarily disclosing such information to third parties.

  


  * **Information Collection and Use**
Categories of personally identifiable information collected by Incipio's website include: first and last name, billing address, shipping address, e-mail address, telephone number and billing information. The personally identifiable information collected by Incipio is used to process and fulfill customer orders. 

Incipio may use your email address for promotional communications, including newsletters, special offers and promotions, press releases and special event announcements. If you do not wish to receive promotional communications from Incipio, please email Incipio at support@incipiotech.com.

Excepts as required to fulfill your order or to comply with applicable laws, Incipio will not sell or disclose your personally identifiable information to third parties.

Because Incipio is concerned about the privacy and safety of children, we will not knowingly request personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.
