# Total Card, Inc.

**TOTAL CARD, INC.**

**PRIVACY POLICY NOTICE  **

Total Card, Inc. collects nonpublic personal information about you from the following sources: 

  * Information we receive from you on applications or other forms:
  * Information about your transactions with us or others; and
  * Information we receive from a consumer reporting agency.



 We do not disclose any nonpublic personal information about our customers or former customers to anyone, except as permitted by law.

Total Card, Inc. restricts access to nonpublic personal information about you to those employees who need to know that information to provide products or services to you.  Total Card, Inc. maintains physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal standards to guard your nonpublic personal information.
