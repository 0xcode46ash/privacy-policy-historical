# Myfit Privacy Policy

Your personal information is our number one concern at http://www.myfit.ca. Myfit Fitness Inc. values your trust and will not allow any third parties access to your information. Myfit Fitness Inc. makes it our policy that any information that you enter with us is kept strictly confidential. We also only collect your email address and name to send updates about our own company updates. 

If you have any concerns about the information we collect please do not hesitate to contact us with the link in the footer.
