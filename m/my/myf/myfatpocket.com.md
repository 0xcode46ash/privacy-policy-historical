# Privacy Policy - MyFatPocket - Because You're Smart &amp; Beautiful. From Top To Toe.

| My-Fat-Pocket.com (MFP) takes the private nature of your personal information seriously. In order to send members with the MFP newsletter, the member’s e-mail address is needed. Member’s name, geography, and telephone number may also be requested. My-Fat-Pocket.com does not collect any other personal information that readers and / or members do not expressly provide.   
---  
   
My-Fat-Pocket.com will never sell, rent or share readers’ or members’ personal information—including e-mail address—with any third parties for marketing purposes without permission. My-Fat-Pocket.com may share readers’ and / or members’ personal information for the purpose of delivering MFP communications as required by law.   
   
Non-Personal Demographic Information  
   
MFP may also request non-personally identifiable demographic information, such as annual income, buying preferences, or age. This information is optional. This data may be used to improve our site and/or tailor the experience, showing you content that we think you might be interested in, and displaying content according to your preferences. We may also share this information with others, such as advertisers interested in advertising on My-Fat-Pocket.com, only in aggregate, anonymous form. In no case will we link aggregate user data with personally identifiable information.   
   
Usage Information  
   
In addition to collecting personal and non-personal information, My-Fat-Pocket.com may also collect non-personal, aggregated information about members’ use of the newsletter. This information is not personally identifiable. For example, this information will tell us how much time users spend on the MFP site, from which other sites subscribers came, and to what other sites subscribers go. The collection of this information allows us to, among other things, prepare for traffic load demands and to efficiently deliver your newsletter.   
   
Choice/Opt-Out  
   
My-Fat-Pocket.com complies with the relevant laws under the Republic of Singapore. If readers / members receive communication materials concerning new products or services that was not expressly requested, MFP will provide you with an e-mail address to opt-out.  
   
Tracking   
   
Like most sites, My-Fat-Pocket.com makes use of browser "cookies." My-Fat-Pocket.com will never use cookies to retrieve information from a computer that is unrelated to our site or services. My-Fat-Pocket.com also reserves the right to use transparent GIF images to track the e-mails that we send out. In the course of serving advertisements to this site, MFP’s third-party advertisers may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser.   
   
Links to Other Sites  
   
As part of the service, My-Fat-Pocket.com may create links allowing readers and members to access third-party sites. My-Fat-Pocket.com is not responsible for the content that appears on those sites and does not endorse these sites. Please consult the individual privacy policies of those sites in order to determine how they treat user information.   
   
Security  
   
All information described above is stored on restricted database servers. If material changes are made to this policy and / or how your personal information is handled, MFP will post an updated version and change the updated date of the policy. If you have any questions about this policy or the My-Fat-Pocket.com site in general, please contact us at [marketing@My-Fat-Pocket.com](mailto:marketing@My-Fat-Pocket.com) .
