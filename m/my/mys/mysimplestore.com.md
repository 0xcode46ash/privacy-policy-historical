**Privacy Policy**

**My Simple Store's Privacy Policy**  
  
My Simple Store respects your right to privacy. My Simple Store will never sell any of the personally identifiable information we collect--your name, e-mail address, preferences, My Simple Store mall purchase information, site activity, or other personally identifiable information--to any third party, ever, without your consent or court order.   
  
Though we make every effort to preserve your privacy we may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on our Web site.  
  
My Simple Store is an online retailer. My Simple Store's privacy statement, below, pertains to these services and the management of data related to providing these services.  
**  
MY SIMPLE STORE PRIVACY STATEMENT**

**Q: How does My Simple Store use the information collected?**  
A: We utilize your e-mail address to take care of all logistical details involved in fulfilling your e-commerce transaction. We will disclose information that we gather only in the aggregate in order to analyze our usage and attract advertisers, corporate sponsors or merchant affiliates. We will take great care and caution to keep whatever information we gather safe.   
  
**Q: What security measures are taken to protect member information?**  
A: Non-authorized personnel do not have physical access to our computers or database. Employees review security guidelines on a quarterly basis, as well as any time new policies are added. The servers that we use to store personally identifiable information are kept in a secure facility.

**Q: Are cookies used by My Simple Store?**  
A: We use ‘cookies' to make visiting our site and visiting My Simple Store merchants a better, easier experience. These cookies allow us to recognize you when you visit My Simple Store. We do not share this information with others.

**Q: How will I be notified of changes to these privacy statements?**  
A: If My Simple Store materially changes our practices in handling personally identifiable information, as described in this policy, we will notify you by posting the changes on this privacy statement and on the My Simple Store's Web site at least 30 days prior to their implementation. You will have a choice as to whether or not we use your information in this different manner. In addition, if we make any material changes in our privacy practices that do not affect user information already stored in our database, we will post a prominent notice on our web site notifying users of the change.

Please [**Contact us**](http://mysimplecity.com/comersus/store/comersus_contactUs.asp) with any questions or comments that you might have.
