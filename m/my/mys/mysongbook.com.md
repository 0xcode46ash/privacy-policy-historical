# MySongBook - The Ultimate Guitar Pro Tablature Archive

Personal Information

This Web site requests non-obligatory personal information from our members because they do not have to transmit their right names in the form during the member registration or when a tab is submited. We do not share any personal information with any third parties or for selling.

External Links

This site include links to different sites but MSB is not responsible for the contents inside these sites.

Cookies

Cookie is only temporary to permit the navigation on the site. It is automatically deleted when the user leave the site.  
A permanent cookie is also used to remember your site language preference.
