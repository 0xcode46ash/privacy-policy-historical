# MyAdMonster.com Privacy Policy | Post Free Ads

### MyAdMonster.com Privacy Policy

MyAdMonster.com stores any and/or all information you provide, on our servers with the exception of any financial or account information you provide to PayPal. We do not receive your financial information from PayPal.Your information will never be sold, disclosed or provided in any manner to third parties for any reason whatsoever. We also take all measures we can to ensure the security of your information while is resides on our servers. On occasion, user information is purged from our servers when it is no longer needed for your advertising. We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [ click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
