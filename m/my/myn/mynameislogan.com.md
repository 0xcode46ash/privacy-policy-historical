# Privacy Policy | Bookmarking

AmazingMark respect and protect the privacy of every individual who uses AmazingMark. Individually identifiable information about you is not handed over to third parties without your permission first, as described in this privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) is.

**AmazingMark and Cookies**  
In your visit to AmazingMark, AmazingMark sends a “cookie” to your computer. A cookie is a collection of data that identifies you as an individual user. AmazingMark use cookies to improve the quality of our service and to better understand our user base. AmazingMark does this by storing the user’s choice in a cookie and monitor user trends and search patterns. AmazingMark will not disclose its cookies to third parties except when it is except if required in legal proceedings such as search warrant, subpoena, statute, or court order.

Most browsers have settings to accept cookies. You can change your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is sent. Please note, some parts of the Service Locator AmazingMark will not work properly if you refuse cookies.

AmazingMark not collect specific information on yourself (such as your name, e-mail address, etc..) Unless you, the knowledge and to your satisfaction, providing such information.
