# Privacy Policy

MyHaircuts.com, its advertising partners and network affiliates, are committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. The following statement details our ongoing support for the responsible collection and management of Internet user information. 

**Internet User Information**

When we deliver an ad to you, we do not collect any personal identifiable information about you, such as your name, address, telephone number or email address. MyHaircuts.com does, like other Internet advertisers, collect certain information which can not personally identify you. This latter category of information is generally referred to as non-personally-identifiable information includes information like the server or internet service provider you use, type of browser used. 

Furthermore, MyHaircuts.com does not divulge any information regarding the users who use email for correspondence with the webmaster or any representative of MyHaircuts.com. 

**MyHaircuts.com never sells any information, such as email address, to any third parties.**
