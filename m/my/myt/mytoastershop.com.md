# Privacy - My Toaster Shop

We will only use the information you provide to better serve you and to process your request.  Everything stays confidential.  [_mytoastershop.com_](http://mytoastershop.com/index.html) may use your information to keep you informed of future sales and new product that my benefit you.  Your information will not be shared with third parties.

We have put the appropriate physical and electronic safeguards into place to protect the information we receive.  You can order with confidence that your information is safe.
