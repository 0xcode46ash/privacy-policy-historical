# Privacy Policy | MyTravelGuide.com

MYTravelGuide has been assisting travel customers since the year 2000. We are committed to ensuring the privacy of each of our customers, partners, and affiliates. We value your trust in us and will work hard to earn your confidence so that you can enthusiastically use our services and recommend us to your friends and family.

## Information We Collect

We collect identifiable information (“Personal Data”) submitted voluntarily from customers and travel services alike. This information is used to make MYTravelGuide easier to use. This includes information submitted as part of attraction and hotel reviews and user profiles. These profiles are used to make the services available on the Site easier to use. This includes favorite places, recently viewed places and visitor information needed to make helpful travel recommendations.

MYTravelGuide may also contact you via email or email newsletter to request feedback on your experience using MYTravelGuide. This is done to assist us in improving our site, or to offer special savings or promotions to you later via email. If you would prefer not to receive special savings or promotions via email, you may simply unsubscribe or “opt-out” at any time. To unsubscribe, simply reply to MYTravelGuide using the hyperlink provided in these notices.

## Links to Other Sites from MYTravelGuide

We provide links to various sites on the Internet, not owned or operated by MYTravelGuide, to better help you research travel destinations like hotels, airports, attractions, and restaurants. We are not responsible however, for the privacy policies operations of other outside web sites, even though links to them may be provided.

## Use of Cookies on Our Site

To provide you with personalized services on our site, we use “cookies” technology. Cookies allow you to access previously viewed areas of MYTravelGuide faster and with better quality so that you can easily find your favorite place. Cookies do this by storing images and text on to your hard-drive. The cookies we us in no way however, store or read any personal or specific information about our users.

For advertising purposes, we also use cookies to understand what web sites bring customers to our site. To do this, we may use a third-party advertising company to serve ads when you visit our site. The company may use information (not including your name, address, e-mail address or telephone number) about your visits to our and other web sites, in combination with non-personally identifiable information about your purchases and interests from other online and offline sources, in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. In addition, we share web site usage information about visitors to our sites with this company for the purpose of managing and targeting advertisements and for market research analysis on our site and other sites. For these purposes, our third-party advertising company and we may note some of the pages you visit.

## Sharing Your Personal Data

MYTravelGuide does not sell or rent your Personal Data to anyone. MYTravelGuide may share your Personal Data with its subsidiaries, and as described below:

**Certain Services:** In order to provide you with certain services, we may share your Personal Data with one of our third party partners, or require that you transact directly with a third party partner. Below is a list of such services:

**MYTravelGuide’s E-mail Alerts:** Many of the operations we perform require us to hire other companies to help us with these tasks. For example, MYTravelGuide hires companies to help us handle mail and e-mail (including sending and delivering packages, postal mail and e-mail) analyze the data we collect, market our products and services, handle credit card transactions and provide customer service. The companies we employ to help us with these tasks have access to Personal Data to perform their functions, but may not use it for other purposes. Also, MYTravelGuide requires all agents to whom Personal Data is disclosed to enter into confidentiality agreements to protect your Personal Data.

**Future Business Transfers:** Our business is constantly changing. As part of that process, MYTravelGuide may sell or buy other companies, and Personal Data may be transferred as part of these transactions. It is also possible that MYTravelGuide, or substantially all of its assets, may be acquired by another company, whether by merger, sale of assets or otherwise, and Personal Data may be transferred as part of such a transaction.

**Compliance with Law and Protection of MYTravelGuide:** MYTravelGuide reserves the right to release Personal Data in order to comply with the law. We may also release Personal Data to enforce or apply the terms and conditions applicable to our products and services, or protect the rights, property or safety of MYTravelGuide, our users, or others.

**Consent:** If none of the categories described above apply, we will notify you to obtain your consent if we believe it is necessary and appropriate to share your Personal Data with someone else. You will have the right to tell us not to share your Personal Data.

## Accuracy of Pricing Information

MYTravelGuide.com is not a booking agent and does not charge any service fees to users of our site. The consumer should be aware that agents who list travel product with MYTravelGuide.com may not have included all fees and surcharges in their listed prices. Airfares, in particular, are frequently subject to additional fees (many of which are required by law). Examples include the Federal September 11th Security Fee, international departure and arrival taxes and fees, federal excise tax, and other service, handling and miscellaneous fees and surcharges.

Furthermore, MYTravelGuide.com. makes no guarantees for availability of prices advertised on the site. Listed prices may require a stay of a particular length or have blackout dates, qualifications or restrictions.
