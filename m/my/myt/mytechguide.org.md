# Privacy Policy « My Technology Guide

We respect your privacy and we are committed to safeguarding your privacy while online at our site My Technology Guide (www.mytechguide.org). The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site.

( _This Privacy Policy was last revised on September 20, 2009._ )

Internet user privacy is of high importance to mytechguide.org. The success of our site depends upon our ability to maintain the trust of our users. If for any reason you have concerns about our website’s privacy policies, please [contact us](http://www.mytechguide.org/contact/). We are committed to ensuring that every user’s needs are addressed.

**Collection of Personal Information**

In the course of delivering this site to you, mytechguide.org does NOT collect any personally-identifiable information about you, such as your name, address, phone number or email address from users of this web site without your knowledge, and are taking measures to protect your privacy.

You can send us email requesting help, technical support, feedback, etc. and your email will NOT be shared in any way with any third party(ies), and will only be used to send replies to you.

Ads used on this website can use cookies and/or web beacons to collect data in the ad serving process. You can remove them at any time using your browser tools.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this matter, please feel free to [contact us](http://www.mytechguide.org/contact/).

**Newsletter**

If a user wishes to subscribe to our newsletter (powered by Feedburner), we ask for contact information such as name and email address. Out of respect for our users privacy we provide a way to opt-out of these communications.

**Links**

This Web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site.

**Advertisers**

We use outside ad companies to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies and are collected by our ad companies, and we do not have access to this information. We work with the ad companies: [Chitika](http://www.google.com/search?q=http://www.chitika.com/) and [Kontera](http://www.google.com/search?q=http://www.kontera.com/). Please check their websites for respective privacy policies.

**Contact Information**

If users have any questions or suggestions regarding our privacy policy, please [contact us](http://www.mytechguide.org/contact/).
