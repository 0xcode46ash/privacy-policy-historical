# My Republica

MyRepublica.com recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of information you provide us.

When you provide personally identifiable information to us, we ensure that those you intent to keep private are not disclosed.

We may use your personal information for our own purpose like conducting researches about use of our website and types of users but will not distribute it to third party under any circumstance other than required by laws of Government of Nepal.

We may use your email addresses to send you promotional emails. We reserve the right to send you offer and information about MyRepublica.com and its affiliates.

However, you will have the rights to unsubscribe any such mails from us. We do not monitor cookies.
