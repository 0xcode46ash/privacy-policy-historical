# Privacy Policy | My Wellness Club

### Privacy Policy

Agate Technocom Ltd realises you are sensitive about how information about you is used and shared. We also appreciate your faith that we will handle this information sensibly. This statement outlines Agate’s privacy policy. We request all visitors to our website to be aware of this policy.

**Personal Information**

AGATE Technocom does not wittingly allow other organisations to track individual visitors to our website. Our information about such visits is limited, and we use this only to analyse general traffic patterns (like ascertaining page popularity), and to undertake routine system maintenance.

AGATE Technocom does not sell, rent or otherwise commercially share any personal information from emails to [info@agatetechnocom.com](mailto:info@agatetechnocom.com). This information is only to help us respond to emails.

**Links**

Our website may contain links to other sites. Please understand we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Our privacy statement pertains only to information collected by this website.

**Conditions of Use and Revisions**

If you have any question about Agate Technocom’s privacy practices, we request you to email to [info@agatetechnocom.com](mailto:info@agatetechnocom.com), and we will address it. You may also contact us by phone or postal mail. We may amend this privacy policy from time to time, and this website will reflect such changes.
