# My Own Business, Inc. - Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. 

**Registration:**  
You do not need to register nor disclose any personal information in order to take this course. 

**Personal Business Plans:**  
A blank form for your business plan can be downloaded to your computer in either Microsoft Word, or WP formats. You can utilize this format to prepare your personal business plan in complete privacy. 

**Feedback Comments and Suggestions:**  
Feedback is voluntary and will be used solely to improve the course and/or to provide statistical data which can be used to obtain funding from non-profit sources. By using our web site and submitting your feedback, you consent to the collection and use of this information by My Own Business, Inc. 

**Information Sharing:**  
No personal information is furnished to any third-parties. 

**Changes:**  
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
