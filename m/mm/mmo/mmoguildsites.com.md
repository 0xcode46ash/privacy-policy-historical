# Privacy Policy for Shivtr

Shivtr is committed to protecting the privacy of those who visit the Shivtr website and use the Shivtr Service. Amendments to this statement will be posted to this URL and will be effective when posted. Your continued use of this site following the posting of any amendment, modification, or change shall constitute your acceptance of the amendment. We will notify Shivtr users by email when significant changes are made to the privacy policy or by placing a prominent notice on our site.

We collect the email addresses of those who communicate with us via email, information about the pages consumers access on the Shivtr website, and information voluntarily submitted by the consumer (for example, filling out a survey or service registration form). The types of information that may be requested include your name, email address and date of birth.

The information we collect is used to improve the quality of the Shivtr website and Service, to communicate information about the Shivtr Service, to bill your account for services provided, for research, and to contact you about your account when necessary.

Your information will not be sold to or shared with other companies or organizations for commercial purposes.

## Cookies

When you use the Shivtr Service we may store some information on your computer. This information will be in the form of a "cookie" or similar file. Cookies are small pieces of information stored on your hard drive, not on the Shivtr website. We do not use cookies to spy on you or otherwise invade your privacy. They cannot invade your hard drive and steal information.

We use cookies to help you navigate the Shivtr website and Service as easily as possible, and to remember information about your current session. You must enable cookies on your web browser to use the Shivtr Service.

## Advertising

Our Ad-Supported version of Shivtr uses third-party advertising companies to serve ads. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on Shivtr.
  * Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to Shivtr and other sites on the Internet.
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).



## Security And Storage

Shivtr and the Shivtr Service has industry standard security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. While there is no such thing as "perfect security" on the Internet, we will take all reasonable steps to insure the safety of your information.

## Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy statement or your dealings with the Shivtr Service, you can contact us at [support@shivtr.com](mailto:support@shivtr.com).
