# Privacy Policy - MMA Warehouse.com

|  | 

_This site is hosted by Yahoo! Store. Yahoo! hosts the store pages, ordering system, and order data. Yahoo! automatically collects order information but may only use this information in the aggregate. If you accessed this store through Yahoo! Shopping, Yahoo! also automatically collects information about your shopping experience. Please see the[Yahoo! Privacy Policy](http://privacy.yahoo.com/privacy/us/) for more information about how Yahoo! uses this information._

The above is standard Yahoo! Store policy. As for us, our privacy policy is as follows:

MMAWarehouse.com absolutely does not sell, share or trade your name, address, email address, buying information, or anything else about you with any other stores, catalogs, or individuals.

We will never send you unsolicited email. Any e-mails we do send with news or product information are strictly opt-in, and you may opt-out at any time for any reason. Your name will not be added to any of these lists at any time without your permission. We do not purchase email lists for our use.

Please contact us if you have any questions concerning this policy. Thank you!
