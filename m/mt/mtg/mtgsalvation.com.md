# Privacy Policy

Last modified: July 1, 2008

If you have any questions about this Policy, please feel free to [contact us](http://forums.mtgsalvation.com/sendmessage.php) through our website.

### What information do we collect and how do we use it?

**Cookies** are used to store several options on MTG Salvation between your visits, including your account identification.

To sign up for an **account** we ask you for personal information (such as your nickname, email address and a password). We will not use this information for any other purpose for which it was collected.

When you visit MTG Salvation, information that your **browser** sends whenever you visit a website is automatically recorded.

### How can I start or stop receiving e-mail from you?

We only send emails you explicitely requested. This includes registration codes and thread/private message notifications/digests. If you have an account on MTG Salvation you can edit your notification settings [here](http://forums.mtgsalvation.com/profile.php?do=editoptions) ("Default Thread Subscription Mode" and "Private Messaging"). We will never share your email address or send you newsletters you didn't explicitly request.

### Do you collect information from children?

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13 or knowingly allow such persons to register. No one under age 13 may submit any personal information to MTG Salvation. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without parental consent, we wil delete that information as quickly as possible. Please [contact us](http://forums.mtgsalvation.com/sendmessage.php) if you believe we have collected information from a child under 13.
