# Privacy Policy For MTHerald.com

### Privacy Policy for MTHerald.com

At MTHerald.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy-policy document outlines the types of personal information that are received and collected by MTHerald.com, and how it is used.

### Log Files

Like many other web sites, MTHerald.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes Internet protocol (IP) address of a visitor, screen resolution and operating system of the visitor, type of browser used to access this site, Internet Service Provider (ISP) of the visitor, date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks. These information may be used to analyze the trends, to administer the site, to track the visitor’s movement around the site, and to gather demographic information. IP addresses and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

### Cookies and Web Beacons

MTHerald.com does use cookies to store information about a visitor’s preferences, to record user-specific information on which pages the user accesses or visits, and to customize the web page content based on the visitor’s browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

Furthermore, some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include but not limited to:

  * Google Adsense
  * Clickbank
  * Chitika
  * Amazon



These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements, and links that appear on MTHerald.com are served directly to your browsers from their servers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

MTHerald.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt out of certain practices. MTHerald.com’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

### DoubleClick DART Cookie

  * Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on MTHerald.com.
  * Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to the users based on their visits to MTHerald.com and other sites on the Internet.
  * Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL: http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html



Moreover, if you wish to disable cookies at your end itself, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to [contact us](http://mtherald.com/contact/ "contact the author of MT Herald").
