# Privacy policy

### _**www.military-quotes.com Privacy Policy**  
_

All information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to [mail me](mailto:privacy@military-quotes.com).

_**What information is collected and how is it used?**_

**_Military Quotes Forum:_** Only username, password and an e-mail address is required for registering in the forum. Each registered user can chose to hide their e-mail address for the public.  
All other information entered will be visible in your profile, and is therefore entered at your own risk.

_**Military History Topsites:**_ I collect information such as email address, site URL, description of site, title of site, password, and banner URL. This information is used to review a site, add it to the Top list, and allow users to visit your site.
