# Privacy Policy – Mideast Time

Your privacy is very important for Mideast Time news service. Here we are going to define some rules and guidelines about the privacy policies applied by Mid East Time.

From time to time we may show some advertisements in the sidebar or in navbar. These ads will be served through third parties such as Google or Double Click companies. The mentioned platforms use your cookies in order to show you targeted advertisements. The ads will be served base on your history in surfing Internet and visiting other websites. If you are not relax with this form of targeting you can easily opt-out from the campaigns or you may turn off your cookies. Please Mideast Time news service note that has no access or control over these cookies that may be used by third party advertisers.

When visiting Mideast Time, the IP address used to access the site will be logged along with the dates and times of access. This information is purely used to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement and gather broad demographic information for internal use. Most importantly, any recorded IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

In some news stories and reports we may put external links to other reliable sources. We are not responsible for the privacy policies on these websites. You should be aware that the privacy policies of these sites may differ from our own.

The contents of this statement may be altered at any time, at our discretion. You can check back this page in order to get notified about the latest changes.

If you have any questions regarding the privacy policy of Mideast Time then you may [contact us](http://www.mideasttime.com/contact-us/ "Contact Us") using this page .

_We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads and collect information when users visit our site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements on our site, other websites and other forms of media about goods and services of interest to you.
