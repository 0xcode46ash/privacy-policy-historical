# Mitsubishi Motors

|  PRIVACY AND CONSUMER INFORMATION 

Mitsubishi Motor Sales of America, Inc. 

Thank you for visiting the official website of Mitsubishi Motor Sales of America, Inc. 

Mitsubishi Motor Sales of America, Inc. is sensitive to the privacy concerns of visitors to our site, so we've set a few ground rules to keep the area fun, secure, and useful for everyone. 

Personal information usage 

Our site is made available to you to provide information. Upon entering the official Mitsubishi Motor Sales of America, Inc. site, certain visitor information is recorded by our Internet servers. This information is used to provide an enhanced online experience for you, the visitor. The only information we collect during normal website usage is the name of your Internet service provider, the website that referred you to us, the pages you request, and the date and time of those requests. This information is used to generate statistics and to measure site activity to improve the usefulness for visitors. To track this information, you may be issued a "cookie," which helps track your "session" while online. During normal website usage, we do not collect or store any personally identifiable information such as name, mailing address, email address, phone number, or social security number. In some situations, you may voluntarily provide personally identifiable information in connection with a promotion, brochure request, inquiry to a Mitsubishi Retailer or inquiry to the webmaster. Your information is never provided to any company outside of the Mitsubishi Motors family and/or our Marketing Partners. 

Miscellaneous 

This site can be viewed anywhere in the world, but is intended for use in the United States of America. Likewise, Mitsubishi Motors vehicles are available in many parts of the world; however, Mitsubishi Motor Sales of America, Inc. only distributes vehicles in the United States of America. 

Links

Mitsubishi Motor Sales of America, Inc. makes an effort to include links to sites that are in good taste and hopefully of interest to you. Our site may be linked from another website. We do not control, nor are we responsible for, the content of these sites, and a link to or from our website does not imply endorsement of the site.
