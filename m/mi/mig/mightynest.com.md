# Privacy Policy | MightyNest

**Privacy Policy**

MightyNest is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect certain information during the order process to fulfill your orders and requests. MightyNest does not store any credit card information. 

MightyNest.com was built with features designed to protect information that we obtain from you and utilizes relevant encryption software and other safeguards to ensure our e-commerce site is safe. We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information. 

You may receive periodic emails from us with information on new products, specials, or upcoming events. If wish to be removed from our mailing list, please let us know by emailing [customerservice@mightynest.com](mailto:customerservice@mightynest.com) with your full name and email address.
