# Privacy Policy | Ministry-To-Children.com

We will never share your email address with a third party. We value your privacy. Our comments require valid email for follow-up and to prevent spam comments. Our e-mail newsletter requires a “double opt-in” process and is easy to unsubscribe. 

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website, like Google Adsense. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [click here](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).
