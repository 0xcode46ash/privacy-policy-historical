# Mind Movies

**MindMovies takes your privacy seriously.**

Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

**What this Privacy Policy Covers**

This Privacy Policy covers MindMovies treatment of personally identifiable information that MindMovies collects when you are on the MindMovies site, and when you use MindMovies services. This policy also covers MindMovies treatment of any personally identifiable information that MindMovies business partners share with MindMovies or that MindMovies may collect on a partner's site.

This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that MindMovies does not own or control, or to people that MindMovies does not employ or manage.

**Information Collection and Use**

MindMovies collects personally identifiable information when you register for a MindMovies account, when you use certain MindMovies products or services, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. MindMovies may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners.

When you register with MindMovies, we may ask for your name, email address, birth date, gender, post code, phone numbers, occupation, industry, and personal interests. Once you register with MindMovies and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.

MindMovies also automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, MindMovies cookie information and the page you requested.

MindMovies uses information for three general purposes: to customise the advertising and content you see on our pages, to fulfill your requests for certain products and services, and to contact you about specials and new products.

**Information Sharing and Disclosure**

MindMovies will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.

MindMovies will only send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when:

We have your consent to share the information;

We need to share your information in order to provide the product or service you have requested;

We need to send the information to companies that work on behalf of MindMovies to provide a product or service to you. (Unless we tell you differently, these companies do not have any right to use the personally identifiable information we provide to them beyond what is necessary to assist us.);

We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; or

We find that your actions on our web sites violate the [MindMovies Terms of Service](http://www.mindmovies.com/footer/terms.html) or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services.

**Cookies**

MindMovies may set and access MindMovies cookies on your computer.

MindMovies allows other companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. The way other companies use their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to MindMovies cookies.

**Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences**

MindMovies gives you the ability to edit your Mind Movies Account Information and preferences at any time.

All newsletter (bulk) emailings contain an unsubscribe link which will allow you to manage which emails you receive.

If at anytime you wish to contact us to update your account or email preferences you may do so through our support email address support@mindmovies.com or with the contact details in the 'Points of Contact' section below.

**Security**

Your MindMovies Account Information is password-protected for your privacy and security.

In certain areas MindMovies uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.

**Changes to this Privacy Policy**

MindMovies may edit this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.

**Questions or Suggestions**

If you have questions or suggestions complete a [ feedback form ](http://mindmovies.com/footer/support.php).

**Points of Contact**

At any time you can contact us by mail at 113 West G Street #122 San Diego, CA 92101, by email at support@mindmovies.com, or by telephone at 213-674-8663.
