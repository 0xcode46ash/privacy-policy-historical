# Privacy policy | MindArc

MindArc IT Consulting Group recognises that your personal information is important to you and share your concerns about how information is collected, used and shared. We are committed to safeguarding your personal information and will always handle your personal information securely and carefully.

We are committed to complying with all applicable Australian Privacy Laws and ensure that our staff are aware of their obligations. The following should give you a greater understanding on how we collect and use customer information.

### Why we collect information

We collect information for a number of reasons including:  
1\. To deliver services to you  
2\. To complete transactions with you or on your behalf  
3\. To better understand your requirements and preferences  
4\. To Improve our service

### How we collect this information

We collect information in a number of ways:  
1\. When you order services online.  
2\. If you complete one of our surveys or respond to requests for information as part of a promotion.  
3\. If you submit a request for information via our website.

### What we collect

The personal information we may collect is in most cases supplied by you when you place an enquiry or provide feedback to us. Name, address, company, phone number, e-mail address, current website and transaction information.

### Who we may share your information with

We will often need to pass your personal details such as your name and contact details to contractors who perform part of our service delivery. We may also disclose personal information to government authorities as required by law. In these circumstances, we will ensure that these third parties undertake to protect your privacy.

If you would like to access or update your personal information, write to us via email, post or via telephone. We prefer to receive written requests as in many cases this assists us to verify your identity.

### Direct marketing

If you receive marketing offers from us and do not wish to receive them in the future, please let us know.
