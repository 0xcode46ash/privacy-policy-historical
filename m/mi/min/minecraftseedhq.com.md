# Privacy Policy - Minecraft Seed HQ

MinecraftSeedHQ.com (Minecraft Seed HQ) is committed to protecting your privacy online. Certain facets of the use of MinecraftSeedHQ.com require the gathering of limited amounts of personal information. Please read the following to understand how this information is used.

## What information does MinecraftSeedHQ.com collect?

MinecraftSeedHQ.com asks for a name and an email address when a visitor submits information for the purpose of sharing it on MinecraftSeedHQ.com, when contacting site personnel and when subscribing to our email lists. Email address information is not, and WILL NOT, be sold to other companies for any purposes. We keep all visitor and subscriber email information private.

Only visitors who expressly sign up for an email list will receive emails from our mailing lists. If at any time a subscriber to one of MinecraftSeedHQ.com’s email lists decides they no longer with to receive email from MinecraftSeedHQ.com they can easily unsubscribe by following the instructions at the bottom of our emails.

## What information do parties other than MinecraftSeedHQ.com collect?

Some advertisers on MinecraftSeedHQ.com may ask or obtain information from our visitors via banners. We do not manage this data collection and this practice is not covered in this privacy policy. If you are worried by this, don’t click on any banners displayed on MinecraftSeedHQ.com

## What are “cookies” and how do we use them?

Cookies are small bits of data that are sent to your browser and stored on your hard drive. We require that cookies have been turned on (in your browser) in order to use this site.

We also collect IP addresses and browser info in order to report on demographics and technology used by our visitors.

## Online behavioral advertising

This site works with third parties, including advertising companies and website analysis firms, who use cookies to collect Non-Personally Identifiable Information (“Non-PII”) when you visit our Site and third party sites about your surfing activities. This Non-PII, collected through cookies, is typically used by these third party advertising networks to serve you with advertisements while on third party sites tailored to meet your preferences and likely interests. If you want to prevent a third-party advertiser from collecting data, currently you may either visit each ad network’s web site individually and opt out, or visit the NAI gateway opt-out site to opt-out of all network advertising cookies. Click [here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp) for the NAI gateway opt-out site.

## What do we do with the information we collect?

If you’ve requested correspondence via email, we do that and do it in a thoughtful manner. That is the only thing that MinecraftSeedHQ.com does with the information collected.

By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information by MinecraftSeedHQ.com as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

## Changes to our Privacy Policy

From time to time, we may change our Privacy Policy. We will post those changes on this page so that our customers will always be aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we share the information with others.
