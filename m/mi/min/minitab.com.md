# Privacy Policy - Minitab

### Global Privacy Policy (excluding Australia and all countries in the European Union)

Minitab Inc. and its subsidiary, Minitab, Ltd., are committed to the online privacy of all of our visitors and users and have instituted appropriate Privacy Policies. This page describes Minitab's Global Privacy Policy for all countries not in the European Union. All questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy should be addressed to Minitab Inc. by sendingÂ an e-mail to [legal@minitab.com](mailto:legal@minitab.com) or calling +1-814-238-3280.

Minitab is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. All of the information you provide is kept confidential on secured servers and will not be shared, sold or rented to third parties; however, we may share some information with our authorized international representatives, partners and authorized agents, but only for purposes related to Minitab business and to provide you with information and updates concerning our products and services.

We collect personal information from you when you request a trial of our products and/or when you purchase or request further information about our products and services. We obtain non-personal system information from you when you download and/or launch a trial of our products. We may contact you using personal data you have voluntarily provided in order to notify you of changes in information and updates concerning our products and services. Occasionally, we may contact you to participate in a business-related survey and may request information from you; however, participation is entirely voluntary and you have the choice of whether or not to disclose any information that may be requested.

We track general, non-personalized information in order to know how many people visit specific pages of our web site so that we may improve those pages. We may use your IP address to gather broad demographic information. We use cookies in a very limited sense in order to deliver information specific to your interests.

www.minitab.com contains some links to other sites. Minitab Inc. and its subsidiary are not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites.

Unless you have specifically asked us not to, we may contact you via e-mail, postal mail or telephone about Minitab products and services that may be of interest to you, including our newsletter.

E-mail communications you receive from Minitab will generally provide a link allowing you to opt-out of future e-mail or to change your contact preferences. In addition, at any time you may opt-out of receiving future communications from us and change your contact preferences as follows:

  1. You may change your account's contact preferences through the [Manage Account section](https://web.archive.org/my-account/default.aspx "go to manage your account").
