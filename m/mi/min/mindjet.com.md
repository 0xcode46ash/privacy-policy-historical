# Mindjet: Privacy Policy

Mindjet, LLC ("Mindjet", "we", or "us") is a licensee of the TRUSTe Privacy Program. TRUSTe is an independent, non-profit organization whose mission is to build users’ trust and confidence in the Internet by promoting the use of fair information practices. This privacy statement covers the web site [www.mindjet.com](http://www.mindjet.com/) (the "Web Site"). Because Mindjet wants to demonstrate its commitment to your privacy, it has agreed to disclose its information practices and have its privacy practices reviewed for compliance by TRUSTe. 

If you have questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy (the "Privacy Policy"), you should first contact Mindjet’s Privacy Officer by emailing [privacy@mindjet.com](mailto:privacy@mindjet.com). If you do not receive acknowledgement of your inquiry, or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, you should then contact TRUSTe at the following web site address: <http://www.truste.org/consumers/watchdog_complaint.php> TRUSTe will then serve as liaison with Mindjet to resolve your concerns. 

The TRUSTe program covers only information that is collected through the Web Site, and does not cover information that may be collected through software downloaded from the Web Site. 

The Privacy Policy governs information that is collected through the Web Site, through the use of the Mindjet software ("Software"), and other services offered on or through the Web Site (collectively, the "Services"). 

Please read the Privacy Policy carefully. It will serve to explain what Personal Information we will gather from you, and when and how this information is used. 

"Personal Information" is information about a person that is personally identifiable to such person like the person’s name, address, e-mail address, or phone number, as well as other non-public information that is associated with the foregoing. 

"Anonymous Information" means information that is not associated with or linked to Personal Information; Anonymous Information does not permit the identification of individual persons. 

The Web Site is not directed at anyone under the age of 18. Please check back periodically for changes in our Privacy Policy. 

* **Your Privacy.**

We understand that your privacy is very important to you. At Mindjet we believe that our online visitors have a right to know our practices regarding the information we may collect when you access or use our Service. Other web sites that are linked to/from the Mindjet Web Site and entities that may be co-branded with Mindjet may have different policies which apply to their collection and management of information; therefore, please review the privacy policy notices on those web sites and of those entities carefully for details.

* **Notice to California Residents – Your California Privacy Rights (As Provided by California Civil Code section 1798.83).** A California resident who has provided personal information to a business with whom he/she has established a business relationship for personal, family, or household purposes (a "California Customer") may request information about whether the business has disclosed personal information to any third parties for the third parties’ direct marketing purposes. In general, if the business has made such a disclosure of personal information, upon receipt of a request by a California Customer, the business is required to provide a list of all third parties to whom personal information was disclosed in the preceding calendar year, as well as a list of the categories of personal information that were disclosed. California Customers may request further information about our compliance with this law by e-mailing [privacy@mindjet.com](mailto:privacy@mindjet.com). Please note that we are required to respond to one request per California Customer each year, and we are not required to respond to requests made by means other than through this e-mail address. 
* **The Information that We Collect.**

From time to time, we may ask for certain Personal Information from you so that you may participate in activities or enjoy the Services. Generally, we collect information in the following ways: information we receive from entries to Mindjet contests; information we receive through our Services and when you register for our Services or when you register the Software; information about your transactions with us; and information provided to us, with your permission, through third parties, such as our resellers. 

More specifically, when you complete a contact form, sign up to receive a newsletter, or register a product with us, we will collect your name, your company’s name, your primary job function, department, your email and physical addresses, your phone and fax numbers. When you sign up as a partner, we collect information about the type of partner your company may be, as well as your company’s name, your name, your primary job function, email and physical addresses, phone and fax numbers, the url, the number of employees and sales people, annual revenue, markets, years in business, and your customers. When you sign up as a partner for a download, we collect your name, your company’s name, its size, your primary job function, your email and physical addresses, your phone number, and the number of people for whom you are evaluating the software and if you sign up for a trial download, we collect the foregoing information as well as your job title and your industry. When you sign up for our "Accelerator" download, we collect your name, your company’s name, your email and physical addresses, your primary job function and how you heard about the Accelerator. When you sign up to participate as a part of an "early look" release of a product, we will collect your name, your company’s name, your email and physical addresses, your organizational site, the type of software and computer platforms utilized on your systems. If you sign up for a service center account, we collect your name, your primary job function, your company’s name, the industry in which it operates, the company’s size, the address, phone and fax numbers, and details about whether or not you have purchased products from Mindjet before. When you contact us for support, we collect your name, your company’s name, your department, your primary job function, your physical and email address, your phone and fax numbers, the version of the product that you are using, the build number and user type, as well as information about your system (i.e., MS Office version, operating system type, html browser type, and your printer model). 

If you provide feedback to us, we may use and disclose such feedback for any purpose, provided we do not associate such feedback with your Personal Information, unless we are authorized by you to do so. We will collect information contained in such feedback and will treat any Personal Information contained in it in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

* **Use of Cookies, GIF Files, and other Technology.**

**Cookies.** Cookies are pieces of information that some web sites transfer to the computer that is browsing that web site ("Cookies"), and are used for record-keeping purposes at many web sites. The use of Cookies makes web surfing easier by performing certain functions such as saving your passwords, background information, and your personal preferences regarding your use of the particular web site. Many consider the use of Cookies to be an industry standard. Your browser is probably set to accept Cookies; however, it may be set to block acceptance of cookies, erase Cookies from your computer hard drive, or receive a warning before a Cookie is stored. If you would prefer not to receive Cookies, you can alter the configuration of your browser to refuse, erase, or to warn you of the use of Cookies. If you choose to have your browser refuse Cookies, it is possible that some areas of web sites that use Cookies will not function properly when you view them and your online experience will be limited. 

Mindjet uses Cookies to administer the Web Site and not to track surfing habits. We collect personal identifying information (the "Information") under the following situations: (1) to the extent that you provide Information to us in an e-mail message or in your profile; (2) to the extent that your web browser provides Information to our web server, like the site you linked from, the identity of your internet service provider, or the type of browser you are using; and (3) to the extent that you provide Personal Information to us in a CGI form (for example, when you complete a search request). 

**GIF Files.** Mindjet uses pixels, or transparent GIF files, to help manage online advertising. These GIF files may be provided by our ad management partner and enable it to recognize a unique Cookie on your web browser, which in turn enables us to learn which advertisements bring users to our Web Site. The Cookie was placed by us, or by another advertiser who works with our ad management partner. With both Cookies and spotlight technology, the information that we collect and share is Anonymous Information, which may be shared in aggregate form, and it is not personally identifiable and therefore does not contain your name, address, telephone number, or email address. 

**Monitoring Scripts.** Mindjet uses certain third party web analytics software in order to monitor the use of its Web Site. Mindjet may also employ a monitoring script to enables it to proactively request chats with you. If a user accepts a chat, the monitoring script will capture pre- and post-chat surveys that are submitted by such users, as well as the user’s IP address and browser version; however, the third party web analytics software provider does not use any of this data. 

If you have concerns about cookies or GIF Files, or need assistance on how to enable the cookies on your system, please contact us at [support@mindjet.com](mailto:support@mindjet.com).
