# Privacy Policy

Information provided to Mindjet is for internal use by Mindjet.

Mindjet will maintain your privacy and will not offer your information to third parties.

When you order products, request feedback, download software, or enter contests from the Mindjet Web site, Mindjet may ask you to provide contact and preference information by submitting an online form during the order process.

We collect this information for a variety of reasons, including Mindjet's effort to inform you of upgrades, changes in service, special offers, and other products and services that you may be interested in.

When you submit personal information to Mindjet, you understand and agree that Mindjet may access, store, and use your customer profile in any of the countries that Mindjet has offices.

Mindjet is not responsible for content or privacy practices of Web sites that are not affiliated with Mindjet's product Web sites or any other site that may or may not be linked to Mindjet's product pages.
