# Miche Bag - Privacy Policy

## Privacy

This web site is owned and operated by Starling Marketing, LLC. Starling Marketing created and operates this web site to service its client, Miche Bag, Inc.. Starling Marketing is committed to respecting online privacy and recognizes your need for appropriate and reasonable protection and management of the personal information you share with us on this web site. As a reflection of that commitment, this Privacy Policy describes how Starling Marketing collects and uses any personally identifiable information that you submit via this web site, and other information.

##  Information collected

When you visit our web site, we collect certain identifying information about you, which may include your name, street address, telephone number, e-mail address, or other identifying information. This information is stored on our servers and used to fulfill your order or information request. Wherever your information is maintained at Starling Marketing, we intend to take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect the information you provide us from unauthorized access or disclosure.

We also provide this identifying information to Miche Bag as part of their effort to provide you with information about its services. Other than Miche Bag, Starling Marketing does not intend to transfer your personal information without your consent to third parties who are not bound to act on Starling Marketing’s behalf, unless such transfer is legally required.

## Choice/Opt-Out 

You may choose whether to supply us with personal information. Our site also provides users the opportunity to opt out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about the visitor. Likewise, we will provide users with the opportunity to change any personally identifiable information previously provided, or to opt out of our further use of such information. To review or request changes to any of your personally identifiable information, or to opt out of its use, please contact us via email at info@michebag.com.

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our web site uses cookies (small files on your computer), to collect information about web site usage and to understand where visitors learn about our web site. We also may use cookies to simplify the re-entering of your data. Many browsers allow you to control cookies, including whether or not to accept them and how to remove them.

Tracking technologies may record information such as Internet domain names and host names, Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser software and operating system types and dates and times our web site is accessed. We keep this information for an indefinite amount of time to improve the operation of our site and to provide better services to our users. Our use of Cookies and tracking technologies helps us to improve our web site and your use of the web site. 

## Contacting You 

Miche Bag and Starling Marketing utilize different methods of responding to information requests, such as mailing brochures or replying via email or telephone. By using our service to request information from Miche Bag, you consent to Miche Bag and Starling Marketing (or our partners and vendors) to contact you using the methods of their choice, to fulfill your information request, even if you may be listed on the National Do-Not-Call Registry. Starling Marketing and Miche Bag reserve the right to contact you in the future to help improve our operations or to introduce new services, such as surveys, to ensure that we have followed up with your information requests in a timely manner. 

## Children's Privacy

Starling Marketing’s web sites are not directed at individuals less than 13 years of age. We do not intend to collect personal information from such individuals.

## Questions or Comments

We invite you to contact us if you have questions or comments about our Privacy Policy. You may contact us: Send a letter to: Starling Marketing, 1545 South 4800 West Salt Lake City, UT 84104 Call us at (801) 918-1038 Email us at: info@michebag.com If you are a California resident and would like to request certain information regarding the disclosure of your personal information to third parties for direct marketing purposes, please submit your request in writing to the address above.

## Privacy Policy Changes

Starling Marketing may change this Privacy Policy at any time. Should we revise this Policy in the future, we will immediately publish the amended Policy on our web site. We recommend that you check our web site frequently to view recent changes or updates.

## Your Consent

By using this web site, you consent to the terms of our Privacy Policy and to Starling Marketing processing information as described above. Should our privacy policy change, we intend to take reasonable steps to ensure that these changes are posted on this web site.
