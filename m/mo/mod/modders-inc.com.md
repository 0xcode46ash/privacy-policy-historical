# Modders-Inc Privacy Page

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at webmaster@modders-inc.com.

At Modders-Inc, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by Modders-Inc and how it is used.

 **Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information**  
Certain visitors to Modders-Inc.com’s website that choose to interact with Modders-Inc.com in ways that require Modders-Inc.com to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Modders-Inc.com gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who use our forums to provide a username and email address. In each case, Modders-Inc.com collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with Modders-Inc.com. Modders-Inc.com does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

 **Log Files**  
Like many other Web sites, Modders-Inc makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protcol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

 **Cookies**  
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Modders-Inc.com uses cookies to help Modders-Inc.com identify and track visitors, their usage of Modders-Inc.com website, and their website access preferences. Modders-Inc.com visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Modders-Inc.com’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Modders-Inc.com’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

 **Privacy Policy Changes**  
Although most changes are likely to be minor, Modders-Inc.com may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in Modders-Inc.com’s sole discretion. Modders-Inc.com encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.

 **DoubleClick DART Cookie**  
– Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on Modders-Inc.  
– Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to Modders-Inc and other sites on the internet.  
– Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – <http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html>

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include …. Google Adsense, Google DoubleClick, JustRelevant, Pricegrabber, Vibrant.

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on Modders-Inc send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

Modders-Inc has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. Modders-Inc’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or websites.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.

Modders-Inc does use code from sources such as facebook, AddThis, Kontera and others for the sole purpose of allowing visitors and users to share or like content from Modders-Inc. Modders-Inc does not have any control on how this information is collected or used by any of these sites or companies.

Any information collected during any contest or events is solely used by Modders-Inc for the duration of the contest or event and is not sold or used for any marketing purposes by either Modders-Inc or venders unless it is agreed upon at the time you sign up for a contest or event.

Opt-Out Policy Modders-Inc.com gives users options to opt out wherever practical; such choices include: – Opting not to receive our electronic newsletters when registering for a site – Opting not to receive review site notifications

Unsubscribe Policy Users may unsubscribe from any of our electronic newsletters or site notifications at any time by following the instructions contained at the end of each newsletter or notification.

Use of Collected Information Collecting the information described above enables us to tailor our content to our readers’ needs.
