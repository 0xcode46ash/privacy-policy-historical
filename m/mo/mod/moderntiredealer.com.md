# Modern Tire Dealer

  


_Modern Tire Dealer_ Magazine strives to protect the privacy of visitors to the _Modern Tire Dealer_ Website. Any information collected through Cookies and server logs is used to gauge how the site is being used and how _Modern Tire Dealer_ Magazine can improve its visitors' online experiences. Any and all information collected is kept strictly confidential and no personal information is passed on to any third party 

Use of the _Modern Tire Dealer_ Magazine Website does not require any form of registration or subscription to the magazine. Subscribers to the magazine may receive access to additional pages within the site and may receive e-mail detailing new features and news on the _Modern Tire Dealer_ Magazine Website. Visitors who visit the _Modern Tire Dealer_ Magazine Website can also sign up to receive _Modern Tire Dealer_ Magazine's e-mail newsletter without subscribing to _Modern Tire Dealer_. This e-mail may be sponsored or contain advertising in addition to the information provided by _Modern Tire Dealer_ Magazine in the e-mail message. 

When you request a subscription/renewal to our magazine or e-newsletters, we may, from time to time, send you carefully-screened messages on behalf of selected advertisers that might be of direct interest to you. Any e- mail or fax message of this type will be labeled as coming from _Modern Tire Dealer_ Direct. To stop receiving these messages, please follow the instructions at the bottom of each correspondence.
