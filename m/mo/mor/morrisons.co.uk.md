# Morrisons Supermarket - Privacy Policy

## 1\. PRIVACY POLICY

  
Wm Morrison Supermarkets PLC and Safeway Stores Ltd are committed to the protection of your privacy. Please read the following privacy policy to understand how we use and protect the information you provide to us. 

**By registering or entering this site you consent to the collection, use and transfer of your information under the terms of this policy.**  


## 2\. INFORMATION THAT WE COLLECT FROM YOU

  
2.1 All personal information sensitive or otherwise given to us through this website will only be held and used with care and in accordance with this policy and the Data Protection Act 1998.

2.2 When you visit and/or register the website at certain areas of the website (for example the employment section) you may be asked to provide relevant personal information and occasionally sensitive ersonal information about yourself including your name, contact details, availability for work, salary details, previous employment and experience, education, ethnic origin, disabilities, criminal record and other relevant information relating to your application for employment.

2.3 We may also collect personal information (but not sensitive personal information) about your usage of the website as well as information about you from messages you post to the website and e-mails or letters you send to us. 

2.4 We also monitor customer traffic patterns and website use, which enables us to further develop this website to be useful to you. 

2.5 We will not collect any personal information about you, except any such information as is relevant and where it is specifically and knowingly provided by you.

2.6 We will not collect any sensitive personal information about you, except through our employment section and then only such sensitive personal information that is relevant and where you have given explicit consent to the processing of such information. 

## 3\. USE OF INFORMATION

  
3.1 We will use and analyse the personal information we collect so that we can administer, support, improve and develop our business. 

3.2 We will use and analyse the sensitive personal information we collect solely for the purposes of considering your suitability for future employment with Wm Morrison Supermarkets Plc and/or Safeway Stores Limited and for related recruitment and human resources purposes relevant to the recruitment process or for future employment purposes.

3.3 No information collected by us on this website will (save as set out in this policy) be shared with, sold, licensed rented or loaned to third parties without your consent. 

## 4\. DISCLOSURE OF YOUR INFORMATION

  
4.1 The information you provide to us will be held on our computers in the United Kingdom and accessed by or given to our staff working within the United Kingdom.

4.2 We may share non-personal aggregate statistics (group) data about visitors to this website and traffic patterns with connected group companies or other parties within the United Kingdom. However, we will not (save as set out in this policy) sell, transfer or share any personal information about you as an individual user of this website outside Wm Morrison Supermarkets PLC or Safeway Stores Ltd.

4.3 Where you have explicitly consented by way of “opt-in” when providing us with your personal details, we may also allow carefully selected third parties (for example job centres), including other companies in our group, to contact you occasionally about other vacancies which may be of interest to you.  They may contact you by post, telephone or fax, as well as by e-mail.  If you change your mind about being contacted by these companies in the future, please let us know.  

4.4 If our business enters into a joint venture with or is sold to or merged with another business entity, your information may be disclosed to our new business partners or owners.

4.5 We will co-operate with law enforcement agencies as well as related third parties to enforce laws such as fraud and other rights.  You authorise us to disclose any information about you to such law enforcement or other government officials as we, in our sole discretion, believe necessary in connection with the investigation of fraud or other activity that is illegal or may expose us or you to legal liability.  You further authorise us to process your information including sensitive personal information where the processing is necessary for the purpose of legal proceedings, obtaining legal advice, establishing or defending legal rights or for the administration of justice or the exercise of functions of a public nature. 

4.6 By accepting this Privacy Policy you authorise us as part of any application for employment to process any sensitive data that relates specifically only to your racial or ethnic origin for the sole purpose of equal opportunity monitoring.  

4.7 You further authorise us to process your sensitive personal information where such processing is necessary for performance of our obligations under employment law.

## 5\. COOKIES

  
Cookies are small amounts of information which we may from time to time store on your computer. Cookies make it easier for you to log on to and use the site during future visits.  They also allow us to monitor website traffic and to personalise the content of the site for you.  You may set up your computer to reject cookies by changing your browser settings although, in that case, you may not be able to use certain features on our site.  

## 6\. SECURITY AND DATA RETENTION

  
6.1 In addition to these policies your personal data is protected in the UK by the Data Protection Act 1998. Under this Act we will only process any personal data we hold about you in a lawful and fair manner. We will keep your personal data secure to prevent access by unauthorised third parties and against unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction and damage. Information provided will be stored on computer for a reasonable period or for as long as the law requires and may be used for analysis purposes as provided in this policy.  In relation to any sensitive personal data provided as part of an application for employment this shall be stored no longer than 6 months or if your application is successful it will be retained during your employment and for 3 years after you leave. 

6.2 All information once processed and no longer required will be destroyed in a confidential manner.

## 7\. ACCESSING AND UPDATING

  
You are entitled to see the information held about you and you may ask us to make any necessary changes to ensure that it is accurate and kept up to date.  If you wish to do this, please contact us.  We are entitled by law to charge a fee of £10 to meet our costs in providing you with details of the information we hold about you. 

## 8\. POLICY CHANGES

  
Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this website and where appropriate, through email notification and will take effect immediately. This policy was last updated on 1 October 2004.

## 9\. EMAILS

  
We will not email you in the future unless you have given your consent.  However, we will email you in reply to your emails to us or in response to “opt-in” boxes etc.  We will not pass on your email address except as mentioned above under “Disclosure of your Information".

## 10\. CONTACT

  
All comments, queries and requests relating to our use of your information are welcomed and should be addressed to Customer Service Department, Wm Morrison Supermarkets PLC, Parry Lane, Bradford  BD4 8TD.
