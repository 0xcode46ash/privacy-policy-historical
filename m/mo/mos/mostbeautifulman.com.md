# Most Beautiful Man -- MostBeautifulMan.com -- Privacy Policy

**MostBeautifulMan.com Privacy Policy**  


Your privacy on the Internet is important to us. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy. 

MostBeautifulMan.com gather certain types of information. The information will be used to send you a notification whenever we launch new features of the site, to send you notification of the latest updates or to send you a reply whenever you ask a question to us. 

As part of the registration process in entry, newsletter, and contact-us forms, you will be asked to provide an email address and your personal information such as: your name, country and sex. Furthermore, MostBeautifulMan.com monitors incoming and outgoing site traffic data to help diagnose problems with its servers and to administer its Web site. 

Cookies are used as an important and necessary part of providing customizable and personalized service to our loyal visitors. Cookies are used to store and sometimes track information about you. Cookies are a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from web server and that stored on your computer hard drive.

MostBeautifulMan.com does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies. We undertake substantial efforts to protect the confidentiality of the identity, preferences and other information we have collected and will never knowingly allow access to this information to anyone outside MostBeautifulMan.com. 

You may at anytime request a removal of information stored on MostBeautifulMan.com server by contacting [privacy@mostbeautifulman.com](mailto:privacy@mostbeautifulman.com). 

The foregoing policy is effective as of November 2001, and MostBeautifulMan.com reserves the right to change this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for changes. This statement and policies outlined herein are not intended to and do not create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party.
