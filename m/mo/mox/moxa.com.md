# Moxa Privacy Policy

## Safeguarding Your Privacy

  
Moxa Inc. ("Moxa" or "we", "us", or "our"), is strongly committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information collected at or in connection with a Moxa website. This privacy policy document outlines the types of information we collect and how it is used.

**Anonymity**  
You may visit this web site without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. We may gather general information, such as your IP address, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and so forth, from which people visit us and analyze this data for trends and statistics. This is general information that does not intrinsically contain personal information.

**Choices  
** There are times, such as when you request information, that it is to your benefit for us to collect personal data from you. We always inform you before we collect any data, explain clearly what we intend to do with the data, and give you the option not to supply any data if that is your wish. Furthermore, you may [inform us](http://www.moxa.com/member/feedback.aspx) at any time that you wish to "opt out" of certain uses of data that has already been supplied.

**Cookies and Beacons**  
On our website, we sometimes use cookies to make it easier for you to fill out electronic or provide you with better browsing experience. When filling out a form on our website, you may be given the option to "remember me on this computer." If you select this option, a cookie will be saved to your computer's hard drive for future reference. Note that you have the option to configure your browser to reject all cookies.

**Disclosure of Information to Third Parties**  
Moxa Inc. does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected in membership forms our customer email subscription lists at our official website, promotion pages, or any other online venue.

**Accuracy**  
We do our best to maintain the accuracy of personal information you supply to us. To assist us in this task, we have implemented tools that will allow you to [review and update](http://www.moxa.com/member/mymoxa.aspx) personal information whenever you wish. Or, submit your request in writing [here](http://www.moxa.com/member/feedback.aspx). 

**Security**  
We use the most up to date methods to protect the information you share with us from unauthorized access or disclosure.

**Marketing**  
We use the Google AdWords remarketing service to advertise on third party websites (including Google) to previous visitors of our site. This may mean that previous visitors to our site will see advertising relevant to activities they have undertaken on our site. This advertising could be in the form of a link on the Google search results page or in the Google Display Network. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on someone’s past visits to the Moxa website. Of course, any data collected will be used in accordance with our own privacy policy and Google’s privacy policy. 

You can set preferences for how Google advertises to you using the [Google Ad Preferences page](http://www.google.com/settings/ads/onweb/), and if you want to you can [opt out of interest-based advertising entirely by cookie settings](http://www.google.com/settings/ads/onweb/) or [permanently using a browser plugin](https://www.google.com/settings/ads/plugin).

We reserve the right to change this privacy statement without notice. However, even when we update this privacy statement, we will use any information collected previous to the update in accordance with the privacy statement that was in effect at the time that information was collected.
