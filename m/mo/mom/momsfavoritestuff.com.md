# Privacy Policy • Mom's Favorite Stuff

Your privacy is important to us. Here is the type of information that we collect and share.

**Giveaways:**

By entering a contest, you agree that if you win a prize, we may share your email and mailing address with our contest sponsors, for the express purpose of contacting you regarding the prize. For submitted comments, all information you submit will be published if your comment is approved. We often use Rafflecopter for giveaways and if you leave your information with our third-party giveaway widget, Rafflecopter, you are bound by their privacy policy and not ours.

**Advertisements:**

We use third-party advertising companies, such as Google Adsense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click [here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp).

**Cookies:**

We do use cookies to store information, including your personal preferences when you visit our site. Our advertisers may also use cookies about your visits to this and other websites. You can click [here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp) to learn more how how you can opt out.

**Log Files:**

Like most other site, we collect and use the data contained in log files, including your IP (internet protocol) address, your ISP (internet service provider), the browser you used to visit our site (such as Chrome or Firefox), when you visited our site, and which pages you’ve visited.

**Email Addresses:**

If you provide your email address in our double-opt in system, or in a comment, we will only use your email address for the purpose for which it was collected. Any comments left on the site are visible to all readers.
