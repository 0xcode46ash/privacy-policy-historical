# Privacy

  


## Privacy Policy

Here is our Privacy Policy on Mom’s Favorite Stuff. For submitted comments, all information you submit will be published if your comment is approved. We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits on Mom’s Favorite Stuff.

This information may be used in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click [here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp)
