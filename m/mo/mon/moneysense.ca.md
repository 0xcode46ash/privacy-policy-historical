## Privacy on Moneysense.ca and Quicken.ca 

#### **Privacy Policy**

While visiting the Moneysense.ca Web site (www.moneysense.ca) you may find yourself on pages co-branded with Quicken.ca (www.quicken.ca), which may, through hyperlinks, eventually lead you to the Quicken.ca site. The following policy applies to both sites. 

Rogers Media Publishing Inc. develops and operates the Moneysense.ca Web site . Rogers New Media develops and operates the Quicken.ca Web site through an exclusive alliance between Rogers Media Inc., a subsidiary of Rogers Communications Inc., and Intuit Canada Limited.

#### **What is personal information?**

Personal information may include such information as your name, email address, mailing addresses, financial information, birth dates and information collected electronically via cookies. 

#### **Does Rogers Media Inc. ask for personal information?**

Yes. Rogers Media Inc. will ask for your name, address, email address, and other personal information when you sign up for personalized services, such as portfolio tracker, online transactions and email newsletters (listservs). You will also be asked for personal information when you enter contests or promotions sponsored by us or sponsored by our partners. 

#### **How does Rogers Media Inc. use information it gathers about me?**

Rogers Media Inc. collects and uses your name, email address and other personal information, for example, to provide you with a product or service that you requested. This information also enables Rogers Media Inc. to develop and customize products to better meet your needs and preferences and to offer you products and services from Rogers Media Inc., any of its affiliated companies within the Rogers group of companies and other sources that may be of interest to you. 

Rogers Media Inc. aims to provide you with the most relevant content and advertising possible. We also aim to help our advertisers reach their target audience. 

Rogers Media Inc.'s Web site provides the opportunity to combine these goals. For example, if advertisers give us an ad and say they want to reach investors 30 to 50 years old, we display the ad to users who have told us they fit that description. Our advertisers never have access to personal information about you. Only Rogers Media Inc. or affiliated companies within the Rogers group of companies have access to information about you account. 

#### **Does Rogers Media Inc. share my personal information? If so, with whom?**

Rogers Media Inc. will not release your personal information with these exceptions: 

  * When you give us permission to do so; 
  * When we believe in good faith that the law requires it; 
  * To protect the rights or property of Rogers Media Inc. or its affiliates; 
  * Under limited circumstances specifically described to you when we collect the information, such as in a Terms of Service or Use agreement, or in the rules of sweepstakes or other promotions; 
  * To affiliated companies within the Rogers group of companies. 



#### **When else might Rogers Media Inc. use my personal information?**

Subject to confidentiality agreements, Rogers Media Inc. may match aggregated, statistical user information to parameters established by third parties, without releasing user information. This aggregated information does not contain any personally identifiable information. For example, we may disclose aggregated user statistics (for example, 31% of our users are professionals) in order to describe our site or services to trading partners, advertisers, other third parties and for other lawful purposes. 

#### **Will Rogers Media Inc. use my information for direct mailings?**

Rogers Media Inc. or Rogers affiliated companies may send you information about our products or services that may be of interest to you. If you do not want to receive direct mailings, all you have to do is say so when you give us your personal information. If you have given permission and change your mind, you can ask to be taken out of future direct mailings. 

#### **How does Rogers Media Inc. protect the privacy of children?**

The privacy of children is important to us. Your children should always ask you for permission before sending personal information to Rogers Media Inc., or to anyone else. Any personal information we gather from children under the age of 16 would be used solely for demographic purposes. We will not knowingly send direct mailings, including email or other correspondence to anyone who indicates that they are younger than 16 years. 

#### **Does Rogers Media Inc. use cookies?**

Yes, Rogers Media Inc. uses cookies in these ways: 

  * We use cookies to remind us who you are and to find your account information in our database when you access a service so you do not need to log in at every visit. This helps us to provide you with service tailored to your specific needs and interests. A cookie is set when you register for a service. 
  * We use cookies to estimate our audience size. Your browser is given a unique cookie that helps us determine whether yours is a repeat visit or a first visit. 
  * We use cookies to determine the browser the visitor uses so the site can be designed to work properly with the most common versions of different browsers. 
  * Advertisers that place ads with Rogers Media Inc. may use cookies. 



#### **Does Rogers Media Inc. use IP addresses?**

Rogers Media Inc. collects IP addresses for the purposes of system administration and to create aggregate statistical reports for advertisers. 

#### **What is Rogers Media Inc.'s policy on deleting or deactivating my name or account from our database?**

Rogers Media Inc. will delete your name from its database upon your written request sent to us via email. When you register for a service and your account is created, we will confirm your registration by sending an email to the address that you supplied to us. This confirmation will also contain information on how to delete your name from our database. Please keep this email confirmation and follow the instructions exactly when requesting that your name be deleted from our database. 

#### **What else should I know about protecting my privacy?**

Generally speaking, whenever you disclose personal information online, that information may be intercepted, collected and used. That goes for information disclosed on message boards, in chat areas - or even in email. If you post personal information online, that information may be intercepted and may be accessible to the public and may expose you to unsolicited messages. Rogers Media Inc. is not responsible if that information is intercepted. As a result, you transmit information to anyone at your own risk. 

Rogers Media Inc.'s partners, co-branded sites and third party Internet sites and services accessible through our site, or third parties collecting information for their own contests or promotions may have their own privacy and data collection practices. Rogers Media Inc. is not responsible for and bears no liability for these policies or actions. 

You alone are responsible for keeping passwords and information safe. Be careful and responsible whenever you're online.

Rogers Media Inc. co-branded sites and content partner sites may have privacy policies independent of Rogers Media Inc.. This Privacy Policy extends only to data collected on pages hosted on the Rogers Media Inc. servers. #### **Public Forums**

This site makes chat rooms, forums, message boards and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. 

#### **Security**

This site has reasonable security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Any information transmitted via the Internet may be intercepted by third parties. Rogers Media Inc. or any of its affiliated companies within the Rogers group of companies assumes no liability for interception, alteration or misuse of information transmitted over the Internet. 

#### **Children's Guidelines**

Rogers Media Inc. is not marketed or directed towards children. Nevertheless, it is possible for children to publicly post or distribute personally identifiable contact information without prior parental consent within chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups. No communication, however, will be sent to anyone or information collected from anyone who indicates that they are younger than 16 years old without their parents' permission. 

#### **Choice/Opt-Out**

At the point on our site where we request information about the visitor, our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving email communications from us, our affiliated companies and our trading partners. In addition, this site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future email communications. 

To unsubscribe from any Quicken.ca newsletter, go to: 

<http://www.quicken.ca/eng/myoptions/index.phtml?body=a2>

* If you are already logged into Quicken.ca, you will be taken directly to the newsletter subscription center. Simply deselect the newsletter(s) you wish to unsubscribe from and click "Submit Settings." 

* If you are not currently logged into Quicken.ca, you will first be prompted to enter your username and password. Once your account is verified, you will be taken directly to the newsletter subscription center. Then deselect the newsletter(s) you wish to unsubscribe from and click "Submit Settings." 

We do not rent or give e-mail addresses provided by customers to outside companies or organizations. We follow the Canadian Marketing Association’s guidelines on e-mail usage and assume that anyone who has supplied an e-mail address to us as part of their subscription has established a relationship with CB Media and this gives us the option to correspond with those subscribers via email. If you wish your emailed account removed from our system please contact us by [email](mailto:service@moneysense.ca) at service@moneysense.ca.
