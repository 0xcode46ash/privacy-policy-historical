# Monitis Privacy Policy

Monitis recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of all information provided by users of our services. We have adopted this privacy policy to assure our users that their personal information will be kept confidential.

The registration form requires users to provide their e-mail address and other information. This information is kept confidential and will only be used for account verification and notifications to the email address listed on your account. We do not disclose, sell or give this information to any other companies or organizations without your consent.

### COOKIES

  * A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive.
  * Cookies are required to use the Monitis service.
  * We use cookies to record current session information.



### DISCLOSURE

  * Monitis may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with subpoenas or when your actions violate the Terms of Service.



### CHANGES

Monitis may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to your sign up email address or by placing a prominent notice on our site.

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact [info@monitis.com](mailto:info@monitis.com).
