## Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Monsterleech.biz collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Monsterleech.biz purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Monsterleech.biz visitors use its website. From time to time, Monsterleech.biz may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website. 

Monsterleech.biz also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. Monsterleech.biz does not use such information to identify its visitors, however, and does not disclose such information, other than under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information, as described below. 

  


## Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to Monsterleech.biz websites choose to interact with Monsterleech.biz in ways that require Monsterleech.biz to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Monsterleech.biz gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who use our website and chooses to register, provide a username and email address. In each case, Monsterleech.biz collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with Monsterleech.biz. Monsterleech.biz does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities. 

  


## Aggregated Statistics

Monsterleech.biz may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. For instance, Monsterleech.biz may reveal how many downloads a particular file got, or say which plugins are most popular based on checks from polls. However, Monsterleech.biz does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. 

  


## Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

Monsterleech.biz discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors, and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Monsterleech.biz’s behalf or to provide services available at Monsterleech.biz websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using Monsterleech.biz websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. Monsterleech.biz will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors, and affiliated organizations, as described above, Monsterleech.biz discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only when required to do so by law, or when Monsterleech.biz believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of Monsterleech.biz, third parties, or the public at large. If you are a registered user of a Monsterleech.biz website and have supplied your email address, Monsterleech.biz may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to date with what’s going on with Monsterleech.biz and our products. We try to keep this type of email to a minimum. If you send us a request (for example via a support email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. Monsterleech.biz takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information. 

  


## Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Monsterleech.biz uses cookies to help Monsterleech.biz identify and track visitors, their usage of Monsterleech.biz website, and their website access preferences. Monsterleech.biz visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Monsterleech.biz websites, with the drawback that certain features of Monsterleech.biz websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
