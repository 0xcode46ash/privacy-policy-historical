# Privacy Policy | Money Crashers

Here at MoneyCrashers.com, we take online privacy very seriously and actively work to protect the privacy of our visitors and mailing list subscribers.

 **Mailing List Email Addresses**  
We only collect the names and email addresses of subscribers who have voluntarily opted in to receive periodic messages from us. We hold this information confidentially and do not release or disclose it under any circumstances unless demanded by law. General information about the size of the list (i.e. total number of subscribers) may be disclosed.

 **Cookies**  
We use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on MoneyCrashers.com. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visits to various sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the [Google ad and content network privacy policy](http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html).

 **Log Files & Web Analytics**  
Like many other Web sites, MoneyCrashers.com makes use of web analytics programs and log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

 **Email**  
Email sent to the site is considered property of MoneyCrashers.com. However, we will usually reach out to receive consent before publishing on the site.

 **Comments**  
Comments left on the site are the responsibility of the commentators. We reserve the right to remove comments or edit them due to any errors. All email addresses submitted with comments are held confidential and are never disclosed to third parties.

 **External Links**  
MoneyCrashers.com contains many links to other related sites. We make no promises as to the reliability of those sites and their privacy policies.
