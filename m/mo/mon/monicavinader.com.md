## Privacy Policy

All the information collected on our site will remain private and will not be disclosed to any third parties. This information will only be used to fulfill your order, for billing purposes or to get in touch with you in response to your order or enquiry. All your information will be restricted to our offices.

We take all precautions to protect your privacy. If you have any queries, please contact us:
