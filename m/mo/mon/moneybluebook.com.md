# Â» Privacy Policy

[**Money Blue Book**](http://www.moneybluebook.com/) is committed to the privacy of its readers and respects the wishes of all visitors to keep their personal identifying information confidential.

#### Collection of Personal Information

This web site does not request any personal information other than your name, email, and website address for blog comment participation purposes. However, the providing of this information is at your own discretion should you choose to share it. For blog comment purposes, your provided name and website address may be displayed for other readers to see but your e-mail will remain hidden from the general public. Your email address will only be used to contact you about any comment or site updates. Your name, e-mail address, and website information will never be distributed, rented, or sold to any third party for any purpose without your consent.

However, this site reserves the right to release information if legally compelled to do so by proper judicial or governmental authorities. Disclosure shall be strictly limited to only those that the site is compelled to disclose.

#### Statistical Tracking

This site does not collect personally identifiable information other than visitor tracking data for the purpose of improving the quality and performance of the site. However, this website and its affiliates may use tracking scripts such as Stat Counter, Site Meter, Google Analytics, and Crazy Egg to collect non-personally identifiable information, such as browser type, operating system, domain name, time spent on the website, or referring links. Statistical data such as visitor IP address may also be collected and stored for security and performance analysis purposes. The information and data collected will never be shared or disseminated to any third party for any other purpose.

#### Advertising

This website employs direct as well as third party advertisement banners and affiliate links. Some of these ads may contain and utilize cookies, but this site does not have access to that information.

**Spam**

This site does not intentionally send unsolicited e-mail messages that could be legitimately construed as Spam.

**Security**

This site implements reasonable security measures to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.

**Policy Changes**

This website reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time without notice.
