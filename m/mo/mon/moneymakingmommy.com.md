### **PRIVACY POLICY**

This site complies with the following Privacy Policy. We can be reached by e-mail at [momsworkathome@yahoo.com](mailto:momsworkathome@yahoo.com). This site collects no personally identifying information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals. Moneymakin' Mommies will not sell or give away personal information to third parties.

Moneymakin' Mommies works with a third party that serves ads to this site. To find out more about how Flycast manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to [http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html.](http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html)

While we will seek to require third parties with whom we have agreements to follow appropriate privacy policies, we do not bear any responsibility for any actions or policies of third parties.

Users should be aware that when they voluntarily disclose personal information (e.g., user name, e-mail address) on the bulletin boards or in the chat areas, that information can be collected and used by others and may result in unsolicited messages from other posters or parties. 

Children should ask their parents for permission before sending any information about themselves to us or anyone else over the Internet. 

Users can send e-mail to us at [momsworkathome@yahoo.com](mailto:momsworkathome@yahoo.com) with any further questions.
