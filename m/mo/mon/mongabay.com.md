# Mongabay.com privacy policy

Privacy statement for mongabay.com 

Mongabay.com does not ever share any personal information you may provide to any other parties. Mongabay.com does not sell, lease, or distribute e-mail addresses to others. 

Personal information is collected when users sign up for the mongabay.com email list. When you unsubscribe, your email address is discarded. 

Mongabay.com does use cookies -- mostly from Google Analytics.
