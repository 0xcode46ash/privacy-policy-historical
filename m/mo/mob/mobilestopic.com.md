# Privacy Policy, Latest Mobile Phone News & Reviews by MobilesTopic.com

  


### MobilesTopic.com is committed to protecting your privacy.

At MobilesTopic.com we work hard to personally review and test all hosting companies listed on our site. We are also constantly updating the information we provide to ensure that it is current. However, we are not responsible for any mistakes, omissions or misinformation that might occur, including personal experiences with those companies.

### What information do we collect?

We do not collect personally identifiable information from our website users unless it is voluntarily supplied to us. When users visit our websites, they may choose to join our newsletters. Only those who provide us with personally identifiable information will be able to receive newsletters. The information we collect include a name and an e-mail address.

### How do we use the information?

We use the information you provide us to provide our users with updates and information regarding the various products that we review, new products and technologies that enter the marketplace, and notifications of new review sites that we create. **We will never sell, trade, or otherwise give away any personal information that we collect.**

Thank you for visiting our site. 

Sincerely, 

## MobilesTopic.com
