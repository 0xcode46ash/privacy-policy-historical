# Privacy Policy

### General Policy

Mobile Phones respects your privacy. The information that we collect helps us and our clients monitor our service and provide a better service for you in the future. 

### Cookies

Mobile Phones may use cookies to track advertising information and website usage. On our clients' sites we may use cookies for the purpose of tracking visitor numbers, advertising or sale information. Please be aware that this activity does not collect any personal information that can be used to identify you in any way. 

### Third Party Sites

We operate in correlation with third party sites like online shops. We may at times share non-personal information with these third parties. Please be aware that we do not collect any personal information that can be used to identify you in any way. 

### Usage Of Information

If requested we may submit your information to local or government authorities. 

### Policy Changes

By using our site you agree to accept this privacy policy and are aware that our policy may change with time. Any changes to this policy will appear on this web page.
