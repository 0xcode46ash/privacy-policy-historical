# Motor Trader - Privacy Policy

PROTO Malaysia Sdn Bhd (co. no. 429449-W) takes your privacy seriously. We are committed to safeguard your privacy while providing you with valuable online services. This policy describe how we collect and how we use and protect your personal information.

## COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

PROTO Malaysia Sdn Bhd (co. no. 429449-W) takes your privacy seriously. We are committed to safeguard your privacy while providing you with valuable online services. This policy describe how we collect and how we use and protect your personal information.

  2. Get a quote for insurance or finance
  3. Value your car
  4. Subscribe our magazines
  5. Participate in our forum
  6. Participate in our market survey



In order to perform the above services, you must register as a user and the information which you volunteer to submit and supply such as your full name, I/C number, date of birth, postal address, email address, mobile number, home or office number. You are required to supply your car registration number, make and model, year of manufacture, insurance renewal dates when you request for valuation of your car, insurance and finance quote. When you make any payment online, bank and credit card information are collected over a secured connection approved by Bank Negara Malaysia only and registering for this service you consent to this transfer of information.

If you choose not to register your personal information, you may still use most of the site available on this Motor Trader web site.

We collect information about users during the registration process and through participation in our promotional activities, forums and polls. In addition, our server automatically log information about your preferences according to your preferences as provided by you, including your IP address and your browsing behavior through the site. This logged information is only in an aggregate form, and no individual user is identified therein. The aggregate data allows us to analyze site traffic and better understanding how we can improve efficiency and value of our services. Our web servers do not automatically identify individual users or record information regarding a user's e-mail address unless the user supplies them. 

## USE OF INFORMATION

**General**

PROTO Malaysia Sdn Bhd is a holding company and information supplied in this site may be shared with our subsidiaries or related companies or other group companies available at the web site. We will take reasonable measures to ensure that information supplied to us is held securely. All our employees and data processors, who have access to, and are associated with the processing of personal details herein are obliged to respect the confidentiality of our visitors' details except if required by law or regulations. Our sites contains links to partners or other third party websites, we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the contents of such websites Please take note that we can accept no responsibility for the contents of these sites. 

Amendments and updates to this policy may be made from time to time. Any revisions will be posted on this page so you will always be informed of any changes.

**User contact details**

User telephone numbers and email addresses supplied as part of an advertisement will be published during the relevant duration of the booking period, this is to enable potential buyer to contact the seller. All details supplied to us with your on-line advertisement will be safeguarded except for cases under fraud and criminal offences. Where advertising packages include additional benefits your agreement to these terms and conditions includes consent for your data to be used by others to provide those benefits, subject always to your being offered the opportunity to opt out of those benefits upon receipt.

User contact details and other information supplied during registration process are used for purposes mentioned as below:-

  1. Send administrative notice from time to time, including user password reset and subscription renewal notice
  2. Send notification of winning bids. Buyer and seller contact details will be made available to both parties to conclude auction transaction. 
  3. To generate overall audience profile of users visiting our website.
  4. For billing and debt collection
  5. Send user emails or postal newsletter or promotional offers from our selected partners that we believe may be of interest to you provided that the user has opted to receive such notification.



**Bank account and credit card information**

Bank account and credit card information is used solely for the purpose of payment processing. Motor Trader retains no record of user bank account and credit card details.

## USE OF COOKIES

Our server issues "cookie", standard cookies technology to every visitor to our website to store your preference. These contain nothing more than a server generated code or unique identification code which is used to distinguish visitors from each other and ensure that your visit is private and separate from all other visitors to the site. Cookie store settings to help facilitate your use of our websites; no personal identifiable information is recorded by the cookies which are stored in your own computer. We may also use cookies to help improve the relevance of the types of products we show you when you visit our web site.

We advise you to only select the "Remember me" option if the computer you use is not shared with others.

## PUBLIC FORUMS

This site makes chat rooms, forums, and/or message boards available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas become public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. We respect freedom of speech and request that users respect one another in exercising said freedom.

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this site, you may email us at [info@motortrader.com.my](mailto:info@motortrader.com.my)
