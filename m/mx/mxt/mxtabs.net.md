# Mxtabs - Privacy Policy

**Advertisers**

We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. Please read the ad company's privacy policy [**here**](http://www.burstmedia.com/release/privacy.asp)

**Personal Information**

This Web site requests non-obligatory personal information from our members. The only required information is your name and e-mail adress. We do not share any personal information with any third parties nor do we use any personal information for any purposes, including selling. You are under no obligation to give your real name when submitting a tab or when becoming a member.

**External Links**

This site contains links to other sites. Mxtabs is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites, or their content. 

**Public Forums**

This site uses forums. Mxtabs is not responsible for any information you disclose while partaking in the forums .

**Cookies**

Cookies are used for logins, and for logins only. These cookies do not receive any information about you or your system, and are used only for authorization purposes

**Ip Adresses**

Mxtabs uses your IP Adress for tracking purposes only (measuring site traffic).

 **Computer Information**

Mxtabs gathers your computer resolution and operating system. This information is only used to make our site better accessible to the viewers.

**Contact Information**

You can contact the webmaster at [mxtabs@mxtabs.net](mailto:mxtabs@mxtabs.net), with any problems and concerns that you might have.
