# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

### PLEASE READ THIS PRIVACY POLICY CAREFULLY TO UNDERSTAND OUR VIEWS AND PRACTICES REGARDING YOUR PERSONAL DATA AND HOW WE WILL TREAT IT. TAKE A MOMENT TO FAMILIARIZE YOURSELF WITH OUR PRIVACY PRACTICES AND LET US KNOW IF YOU HAVE ANY CONCERNS.

This Privacy Policy describes Microvirt policies for collecting, using and disclosing your personal data. By using any of our product, you consent to the collection, transfer, processing, storage, disclosure and other uses described in this Privacy Policy.

Microvirt is committed to respecting your privacy and maintaining accuracy, confidentiality and security of your personal information. We does not sell, share or distribute customer information to any third party organization.

Microvirt collect contact information of those who communicate with us via e-mail or SNS and feedback information volunteered by the consumer via survey or post. The information we collect is used to improve the user experience of our product, and is not shared with or sold to any third organization for commercial purposes.

### WHAT WE DO

Microvirt only collect basic, non-personal technical information. 

By default, we collects statistics about your usage of our product, like software version, downloaded apps, apps usage time, which are anonymized before being sent.

### WHAT WE DON'T DO

Microvirt never sell your personal information.

We don't automatically collect personal information without your permission, although we do automatically collect technical information about how users interact with our product, We don't track your activity across the internet, although we do receive and keep information about how you got our product.
