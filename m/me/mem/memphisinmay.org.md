# Memphis in May - Privacy Policy

[](https://web.archive.org/web/20060625020616id_/http%3A//memphisinmay.org/print.asp?cid=70&title=Privacy%20Policy)

Memphis in May respects each web site visitor’s right to personal privacy.  Therefore, all information collected on this site is:

  * Used to notify visitors regarding special updates to Memphis in May events, 
  * Used to improve our Web page, 
  * Never shared with other organizations for commercial or non-commercial purposes.



With respect to Visitor’s Survey:  Memphis in May uses the survey solely to chart demographics of our visitors.

If you feel that this site is not following stated information policy, you may contact state or local chapters of the Better Business Bureau.
