# Privacy Policy ~ Meow Wolf

### Use of Information

As a general policy no personally identifiable information, such as your name, address, or email address, is automatically collected from your visit to Meow Wolf. However, certain non-personal information is recorded by the standard operation of Meow Wolf Internet servers. Information such as the type of browser being used, its operating system, and your IP address is gathered in order to enhance your online experience.

Our site’s various interactive forms and/or surveys may request that you give us contact information (such as your name, mailing and/or email address). Information submitted at the time of registration or submission may be used for promotional purposes by Meow Wolf. Personally identifiable information is never sold or leased to any third parties.

### Links to External Sites

Meow Wolf is not responsible for the content or practices of third party web sites that may be linked to from our site. Visitors to these web sites should refer to each web site’s respective privacy policies and practices. Links with the symbol will take you to another website.

### Communications

Our site may provide you the opportunity to opt-in to receive communications from us. You always have the option of removing your name from any email list and not receive any future communications. In order to ensure immediate removal from any list, follow the specific instructions outlined within the communications you receive from Meow Wolf. However, if you are unable to complete the process indicated in the aforementioned communication, then just send us an email with a copy of the undesired email attached to the request and state you wish to be removed from the mailing list.

### Acceptance of These Terms

By using our site, you signify your acceptance of the Meow Wolf Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use this site. Meow Wolf reserves the right to modify, alter or otherwise update this policy at any time, so visitors are encouraged to review this policy from time to time. Your continued use of our site following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes.
