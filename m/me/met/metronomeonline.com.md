# Privacy Policy | METRONOME ONLINE - free online metronome

**PLEASE READ THIS PRIVACY POLICY CAREFULLY BEFORE USING THIS WEBSITE.**

MetronomeOnline.com is committed to protecting your privacy. We only use the information we collect about you to provide the services you have requested. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for MetronomeOnline.com Web site: 

MetronomeOnline.com does not sell, market, rent, trade, or otherwise share personally identifiable information with any third party. MetronomeOnline.com may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, sales, traffic patterns, and related site information to reputable third-party vendors, but these statistics will include no personally identifying information. MetronomeOnline.com may release account information when we believe, in good faith, that such release is reasonably necessary to (i) comply with law, (ii) enforce or apply the terms of any of our user and project agreements or (iii) protect the rights, property or safety of MetronomeOnline.com, our users, or others. 

MetronomeOnline.com is committed to fully complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 and does not accept registration or any type of personally identifiable information from children younger than 13 years of age. We urge children to check with their parents before entering information on any Web site and we encourage parents to discuss with their kids the restrictions regarding the online release of personal information to anyone they don't know. 

MetronomeOnline.com Web site might contain links to other Web Sites. MetronomeOnline.com makes no representations whatsoever about any other Web site accessed through MetronomeOnline.com Site. MetronomeOnline.com has no control over the content of non-MetronomeOnline.com Web sites, even if they contain the MetronomeOnline.com' logo. In addition, a link to a non-MetronomeOnline.com Web site does not mean that MetronomeOnline.com endorses or accepts any responsibility for the content, or the safety of use, of such a Web site. 

##  Registration 

Our registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name) and unique identifiers (like their email address). We use customer contact information from the registration form to provide services on the Web site and to send the user information about our company and promotional material from some of our partners. Users have a choice to opt-in to receive such newsletters. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the Opt-In/Out section below. Your email address is necessary as a unique identifier when you create an account. All information you provide, including your email address, is uniquely identifying and is not shared with anyone outside MetronomeOnline.com. 

##  Changing Personal Information 

If you wish to change any of the personal information that you completed when you registered on MetronomeOnline.com, click the "Profile" link once you sign in. You then get to a page that allows you to update personal information including email address, password, mailing address, and all other options. 

This site has security measures in place to ensure that personally identifiable information collected in the future is secure, including limiting the number of people who have physical access to our database servers as well as electronic security systems. 

By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by MetronomeOnline.com. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. 

##  Cookies 

Our website uses cookies, information files that your Web browser places on your computer for the duration of your visit to a Web site. MetronomeOnline.com uses Cookies for keeping your session active and for personalization purposes. 

MetronomeOnline.com also uses cookies to track our affiliates as part of our affiliate program and our advertising efforts. These cookies are placed on your computer and serve to track the commissions and generated sales. 

##  Opt-In/Out of Requests for Information 

We also give users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communication, such as email, from us by sending such a request to [info@metronomeonline.com](mailto:info@metronomeonline.com). This site also gives users the option to deactivate their account if the user no longer wants to use our services by sending such a request to [info@metronomeonline.com](mailto:info@metronomeonline.com) or by logging into their account and making such action under the Profile section. 

##  Contacting the Web Site 

If you have any questions, comments or concerns about MetronomeOnline.com privacy policy, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please contact us at [info@metronomeonline.com](mailto:info@metronomeonline.com).
