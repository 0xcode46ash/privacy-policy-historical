# Privacy Policy

BoldtMedia LLC operates www.mergemedia.net, which is an Internet portal site and has links to external sites. BoldtMedia is not responsible for the contents of these sites, and does not operate any of the offers. The primary reason MergeMedia lists these offers, is so visitors may post feedback in the form of comments. Additionally, we provide descriptions on our site for the convenience of our visitors and these descriptions are written to the best of our knowledge and editorial guidelines. We cannot be held responsible for any of these descriptions.

**Collecting Data**  
BoldtMedia only collects your name and email address with your consent to join our newsletter. We will also log your IP,and the time/date in which you signed up in order to prevent abuse. We do not collect any other personal information at this point of time.

**Sharing Data**  
BoldtMedia does not disclose your personal information to any third parties.

**Statistical Analysis**  
BoldtMedia does keep track of the pages you surf to and your user preferences, but we only view the data as a form of statistical analysis. We do not view individual surfing styles of users.

**Copyrights and Trademarks**  
All the trademarks and copyrights on this site are the property of the respective owners, and have to the best of our knowledge, been used with permission. Most of the promotional offers listed on www.mergemedia.net are from various sites around the Internet, and we do our best to ensure trademark and copyright compliance. If there are any discrepancies, please contact our legal department, whom will immediately work with your legal department to reach a rapid and amicable solution.

**Visitor Comments**  
Visitors are permitted to post comments at www.mergemedia.net on each page and offer, and these comments obviously reflect the opinions of the visitor who posted them, and are not endorsed by BoldtMedia, and may not reflect the views of BoldtMedia. Your mileage may vary.

**Is it REALLY Free?!**  
Some of the offers listed on our site are not 100% free. You may have to pay Shipping or handling fees or be subjected to other conditions to get some free items. When you are giving out your personal information, please **read their privacy policy and all details first.**

**State and Local Laws**  
If any of the web-sites which are listed on the BoldtMedia property, mergemedia.net, contain offers which are prohibited by a state or local law which affects you, We insist that you consider the offers null and void, and you should not participate. It is your responsibility to know your state and local laws.

BoldtMedia has created this privacy policy to demonstrate our business in the highest ethical standards and to uphold our promise to the public. However, our policy might change periodically, due to the changes within the environment.

For questions regarding this privacy statement or the practices of this site, please [contact us](http://www.mergemedia.net/contact.html) anytime.
