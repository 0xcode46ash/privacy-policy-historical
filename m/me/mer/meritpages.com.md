# None

meritpages.com is committed to providing its online profile users with services and information in an efficient and secure manner. This privacy policy explains what personal information is collected on meritpages.com and from users of meritpages.com’s various online services (collectively, the “Profile”), how that information is used and what measures meritpages.com takes to safeguard its users’ privacy. 

_**What personal information is collected through the Profile?**_

When you claim or create a meritpages.com account, you provide some or all of the following information: name, user-name, password, and email address. Some of this information, including your name and user-name, may be listed publicly on the Profile, such as on your profile page and in the search results. Some services of the Profile, including general browsing, do not require registration. You may also provide additional information that you would like to be made public on your profile page, such as biographical information, your location, and photos. Your profile page will also include public directory information such as your hometown, college, major and year of graduation, if applicable, which is provided by third-parties, such as colleges, universities and public agencies. 

_**Please note**_ that by voluntarily providing us with this personal information, you are consenting to our use of it in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you provide personal information in connection with the Profile, you acknowledge and agree that such personal information may be transferred from your current location to the offices and services of meritpages.com and the authorized third parties referred to herein located in the United States. 

_**How does meritpages.com use the personal information collected through the Profile?**_

Information about individual users collected through the Profile is used for internal business purposes, including, without limitation, customizing services, contacting members regarding use of the services, and responding to requests for information. For example, if you contact us by e-mail, we will use the personal information you provide to answer your question or resolve your problem. On occasion, meritpages.com or one of its members or affiliates may also use this information to inform you of news, service upgrades or new meritpages.com services or products that may be of particular interest to you. 

_**What other information is collected through the Profile and how is this information used by meritpages.com?**_

As you know, the Profile is primarily designed to help you share information with the world. Most of the information that you provide to us is information you are asking us to make public through the Profile and is not considered personal information. 

When you interact with meritpages.com through the Profile, we receive and store non-identifiable information, which is collected passively using various techniques, but cannot presently be used to specifically identify you. This information may be aggregated to better understand the demographics and behaviors of our profile users. For example, meritpages.com tracks aggregate anonymous statistics regarding usage of the Profile and may provide this anonymous information to members, affiliates, advertisers and for other lawful purposes. 

_**Does meritpages.com provide personal information collected through the Profile to third parties?**_

meritpages.com does not sell, trade or rent personal information collected online and will not knowingly disclose any individual personal information to third parties except as follows: 

  * Non-private or Non-Personal Information. We may share or disclose your non-private, aggregated or otherwise non-personal information, such as your public bio and the information displayed in your profile.
  * With your Consent. We may share or disclose your information with your consent, such as when you use a third party web client to access your meritpages.com account.
  * Related Third Parties. In order to provide you with requested services, meritpages.com may provide contact or other necessary information to affiliated sites, such as readMedia.com, that partner with meritpages.com for syndication, broadcast, distribution or publication of your information.
  * Legal Action. When disclosure is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be causing injury to or interference with the rights (including property rights) or safety of other users or third parties or of meritpages.com.
  * Compliance with Law. When meritpages.com reasonably believes that disclosure is required by law or to comply with legal process served on meritpages.com.
  * Terms of Service. In order to enforce our Terms of Service.
  * Business Transfers. As we develop our business, we might sell or buy businesses or assets. In the event of a corporate sale, merger, reorganization, dissolution or similar event, personal information may be part of the transferred assets.



**_How can I change or update the information I provide through the Profile?_**

For Profile services and products, where user profile information is collected and stored in order to provide you with requested services, meritpages.com offers you the opportunity to change or update your profile information at any time by visiting the “My Account” section of the Profile. 

**_Does the Profile use cookies (information that the computer that hosts our Profile gives to your browser when you access the Profile)?_**

The Profile may use cookies, however meritpages.com will only use cookies to facilitate functions of the Profile and to analyze Profile usage and will not record or store any information about your movements on the Internet outside of your use of the Profile. For example, the Profile may set a cookie on your browser that allows you to access the Profile without needing to remember and enter a password more than once during a visit to the Profile. Please note that if you click on a link to a third-party website from the Profile, the linked-to site may have different policies and practices with regard to cookies and meritpages.com has no control over and is not responsible for such policies or practices. You should always consult the privacy policy governing any website you visit on the Internet for complete information about their policies and practices. 

**_What measures does meritpages.com take to safeguard its online users’ privacy?_**

Information you submit to meritpages.com upon registration for specific services is password-protected so that the only parties who have access to the information besides meritpages.com and its agents and affiliates are you and those you provide that information to. In order to protect the integrity of the password system, members and profile users should refrain from disclosing passwords and immediately change any passwords that may have been compromised. meritpages.com will never ask you for your password by e-mail, so members and their employees should not respond to unsolicited e-mail requests asking for password information even if such requests appear to be from meritpages.com. 

Please be advised that no data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. meritpages.com is committed to protecting users’ personal information, but cannot guarantee or warrant the security of this information when it is sent to or through the Profile. Transmission of such information is therefore at the risk of the user. 

**General.**

  * a. **_Rights Reserved / Change in Terms._** meritpages.com reserves the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason by posting a notice on the Profile. Users are responsible for regularly reviewing the Privacy Policy. The most current version of the Privacy Policy can be reviewed by clicking on the “Privacy Policy” hypertext link located on the Profile. If you do not agree to the Privacy Policy or any changes or updates, your only remedy is not to use the applicable Profile and to cancel any accounts or subscriptions that you may have established to access the Profile. Your continued use of the applicable Profile after a change or update has been made to the Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change or update.
  * b. **_User Choices / Opt-out Rights._** You can use the meritpages.com website without providing personal information. If you choose not to provide any personal information, you may not be able to use certain meritpages.com services. meritpages.com may use the personal information that you provide through the Profile for marketing communications with you relating to news, upgrades, feedback, services or products. These such marketing communications will contain instructions permitting you to “opt-out” of receiving future communications. You will not be able to “opt-out” of communications with meritpages.com relating to your use of the Profile, including your account, set-up and support services, as we need to be able to contact you in order to provide you with the requested service.
  * c. **_Exclusions._** This Privacy Policy does not apply to any personal information collected by meritpages.com other than personal information collected through the Profile. This Privacy Policy shall not apply to any unsolicited information you provide to meritpages.com through this Profile or through any other means. Any unsolicited information shall be deemed to be non-confidential and meritpages.com shall be free to reproduce, use, disclose, and distribute such unsolicited information to others without limitation or attribution.
  * d. **_Children._** meritpages.com does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are under the age of 13, please do not submit any personal information through the Profile. If you have reason to believe that a child under the age of 13 has provided personal information to meritpages.com through this Profile, please contact us, and we will endeavor to delete that information from our databases.
  * e. **_Terms of Service._** Please review meritpages.com’s [Terms of Service](http://meritpages.com/terms).
