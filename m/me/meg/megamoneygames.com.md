# Untitled Page

At MegaMoneyGames our primary goal is to earn your trust so you can fully enjoy your online experience. We are continually reviewing and updating our online gaming software and technology to ensure that we have done everything we can to make MegaMoneyGames the most secure, safe and trustworthy gaming site online. 

In an online environment, the prevention of collusion and hackers are key areas of concern in respect to maintaining integrity. It is not always easy to ask someone to provide their personal information and to transfer it across the internet, especially when there is money involved. At MegaMoneyGames one of our highest priorities is your trust. We ensure that we keep your information protected; that the games are fair; and that our customer support team is available to you 24/7. 

### What Types of Information does MegaMoneyGames Collect from its Users?

MMG (MegaMoneyGames) only collects personal information from our users only for individual identification purposes such as User’s name, mailing address, phone number, credit card number and financial information. MMG may also collect non-personal information for identification purposes such as types of internet browsers, ISP and operating system, IP addresses and URL’s of previously visited websites. MMG will also collect any information provided during any and all types of communication with the website (MegaMoneyGames.com). 

### How is My Personal Information Collected?

MMG collects player’s information during the player sign-up process. During this process MMG will collect personal and non-personal information such as name, email address, financial details and any other information needed for the registration process 

MMG also collects information from 3rd party analytic and reporting technologies where non-personal information such as IP addresses, browser types, operating system types, internet domain names and host names, click stream patterns, dates and times that the user accesses the MegaMoneyGames website. 

### How will my Personal Information be used?

All information collected is to be kept 100% strictly confidential for MMG’s internal purposes only. The information collected will be used to maintain, evaluate and improve MMG’s services and the MegaMoneyGames website. Any personal information collected will not be shared with any other organizations for commercial and non-commercial purposes and will only be used for internal purposes only. These internal purposes can be classified as sending out cash-out checks, accepting deposits, communicating promotional and informational materials, and providing customer support.

The only instance where MMG does disclose personal information to any third party is for the purposes of processing your payments, confirming your identity, your deposit and/or cash out methods, as well as by notice under law, statute, regulation, or governmental authority.

MegaMoneyGames will keep your Winning and cash-out information strictly private and confidential and does not provide this information to any third party unless acting under a good faith belief that such information is required to be disclosed under law, regulation or any governmental or competent regulatory authority. Notwithstanding, MegaMoneyGames may publicize certain details regarding Winnings for promotional purposes (The Prize and relevant Game Name, date of winnings, first letter of the first Name of the Winner, country, state and city). 

In regards to the issue of how MegaMoneyGames uses your non-personal information, we use it to track your use of the MMG website and for other internal, in particular to provide for, maintain, evaluate and improve the MegaMoneyGames services and the website.

### Can I "Opt Out" From Receiving Communications From MegaMoneyGames?

You may "opt out" from receiving any type of communication from MegaMoneyGames anytime by simply notifying us at remove@megamoneygames.com 

### How does MegaMoneyGames use Cookies?

In order to provide you with a more personalized user experience MegaMoneyGames uses cookies to track your use of the MegaMoneyGames website. The use of cookies is also used to allow you to sign into your MegaMoneyGames account more easily.

### Can I Review and Change my Personal Information?

You can review and change your personal information at any time you wish by simply entering your MegaMoneyGames account, and clicking update profile. You may also change your personal information by contacting customer support and providing them with the proper account details.

For your protection, you will be required to provide proof of your identity. If you wish, we will delete all of your Personal Information (except for your transaction history) from our records upon your request. However, if you choose to delete your Personal Information, you may not be able to access some areas of the Website or use some of the MegaMoneyGames Services.

### How will MegaMoneyGames Protect my Personal Information?

Personal Information - we use only the best, up to date, methods to protect your Personal Information from unauthorized access or disclosure. All Personal Information is transferred from and to our Website using the latest encryption technologies (SSL). Whenever we obtain Personal Information we use the most suitable physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to secure such Personal Information. MegaMoneyGame maintains a highly secure server possessing the latest Firewall technologies available in today's marketplace, and we are committed to constantly updating our security systems with the latest products concerning online security.

### How Do I Agree to the Privacy Policy?

You choose to agree with MegaMoneyGames Privacy Policy when creating a new account in the MegaMoneyGames website by clicking the “I Accept” button in the sign up page. When you click the “I Accept” button you automatically agree to collection, use and disclosure of your confidential information by MegaMoneyGames within this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the MegaMoneyGames privacy policy you may not use the MegaMoneyGames website nor any of MegaMoneyGames services.
