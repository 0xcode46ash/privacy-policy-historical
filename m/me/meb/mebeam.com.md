# privacy - Mebeam, free video chat rooms and conferencing

Privacy

Mebeam.net does not record any video or audio  
and never publishes or shares any information,  
about you, your friends, or your conversations.

Mebeam.net does not collect any personal information  
that is used to identify you.  
If any information is collected,  
it is used internally to improve the service.
