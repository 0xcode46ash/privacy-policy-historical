# MeasuringU: Privacy

Protecting your private information is our priority. This Statement of Privacy applies to MeasuringU and governs data collection and usage (including CXsurveys.com). For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, by agreeing to take part in the survey, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of your Personal Information  
MeasuringU may collect anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your age, gender, household income, race or religion.

Information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by MeasuringU. This information can include your operating system, screen resolution, browser type and browser resolution.

MeasuringU encourages you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from the survey so that you can understand how those websites collect, use and share your information. MeasuringU is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of MeasuringU.

Use of your Personal Information  
MeasuringU does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. MeasuringU will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edict of the law or comply with legal process served on MeasuringU; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of MeasuringU; and (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of respondents to MeasuringU surveys, or the public.

Security of your Personal Information  
MeasuringU secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure.
