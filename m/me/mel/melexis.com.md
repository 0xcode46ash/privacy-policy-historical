# Privacy Policy | Melexis

## Commitment to data privacy protection

Protecting the security and privacy of personal data is very important to Melexis and to the way we conduct our business in compliance with laws on privacy, data protection and data security.   
Please read our Privacy Policy carefully so that you can understand how we collect and use the personal data that you provide to us. 

## Data collected and stored automatically

When you access our website, your web browser automatically provides data to our web server for technical reasons. This data will include items such as time and date of access, URL of the referring web page, file accessed, volume of data sent, name and version of your browser, your operating system and your IP address. This data is stored separately from other data that you enter while making use of our website. It is impossible for us to associate this data with a particular individual. This data may be evaluated for statistical purposes.

## Personal information you accept to provide

By submitting your personal data to us, you accept and give your permission to us for the processing of your personal data as set out in this policy.

The respective provider or operator of the pages linked has sole responsibility for the content of these pages.

## Security of personal information

Melexis makes reasonable efforts to ensure the security of your Personal Information. Only a limited number of persons within our IT Department or the operational team administrating the website as well as specific persons of Melexis or its affiliates, who are in the appropriate position to answer your requests, are authorize, upon your request, to access, modify or delete your Personal Information.

You have the right to protest against processing of your personal data by written request and you also have the right to demand access to this information and if needed, make corrections. If you wish to do so, or in case you should have other questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal data not answered by this Privacy Policy, please contact the Webmaster at pgy@melexis.com.
