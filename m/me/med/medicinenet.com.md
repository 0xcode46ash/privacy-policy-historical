# MedicineNet.com Medical References

  **Friday, June 23, 2000**    
**MedicineNet.com Privacy and Security Policy**

At MedicineNet.com your privacy is important to us. We will never willfully disclose personally identifiable information about our customers to any third party without prior consent.

**TRUSTe Certification**[](https://www.truste.org/validate/3900)

The overall Privacy Policy verifies that MedicineNet.com is a member of the TRUSTe program and is in compliance with TRUSTe privacy principles. This policy discloses the privacy practices for MedicineNet.com's entire web site, including sites powered by MedicineNet.com; MedTerms.com and AboutArthritis.com.

TRUSTe is an independent, non-profit initiative whose mission is to build users' trust and confidence in the Internet by promoting the principles of disclosure and informed consent. Because this site wants to demonstrate its commitment to your privacy, it has agreed to disclose its information practices and have its privacy practices reviewed and audited for compliance by TRUSTe. 

If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact the MedicineNet.com Smart Service Team by e-mail at [ smartservice@medicinenet.com](mailto:smartservice@medicinenet.com). If you do not receive acknowledgment of your inquiry or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, you should then contact TRUSTe ([ http://www.truste.org/users/users_watchdog.html](http://www.truste.org/users/users_watchdog.html)). TRUSTe will then serve as a liaison with the Web site to resolve your concerns.

**Our Philosophy**     Back to Top

MedicineNet.com has developed this privacy policy to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy and security. We understand as a provider of medical information the sensitive nature of each transaction. As such, we give you the power to decide how much information you provide combined with the ability to view or update your personally identifiable information at any time. In addition, you control what information you receive from MedicineNet.com--we will never contact you without prior consent. All transactions with MedicineNet.com and sites powered by MedicineNet.com are designed to empower your healthcare decisions.

**Information We Collect**     Back to Top

We need your e-mail address and a password of your choosing, to set-up and login to your account. If you choose to participate in a discussion (message board or chat), we need your name, e-mail address and a password of your choosing, to establish an account to allow access to discussions. Any personally identifiable information you disclose through the use of discussions (message boards or chats) is voluntary. MedicineNet.com is not liable for any personally identifiable information you choose to disclose as part of a discussion (message boards or chats).

Periodically, we may also ask for other demographic information as part of a survey or poll. It is not mandatory for you to answer these questions. If you decide to answer these questions, your individual answers will remain confidential. We will only use survey information in the aggregate to study statistical results. Examples of such information are as follows; age, gender, occupation, marital status, family size, areas of interest, or medical related questions. 

We collect traffic patterns and site usage information throughout our site. As part of this we collect the URL you came from in entering our site, what browser you are using, your IP address, and the URL you go to from our site if it is a link or advertiser on our site. In addition, we use Cookies to assign a unique, random number to identify your computer. Cookies, by themselves, cannot identify you or any other individual using your computer. Cookies allow us to keep track of shopping carts, login information (for increased security), and site traffic patterns and usage.

**How We Use Your Information**     Back to Top

At MedicineNet.com we continuously strive to improve your experience. To do so, we internally use personally identifiable information to personalize the customer experience and to improve our content and product offering, marketing and promotional efforts, and site design and layout. 

Personally identifiable information will be utilized to offer you customized benefits through optional Newsletters or e-mail Update Alerts. You may at any time opt-out of receiving special information through the Newsletter and Forum Update Registration area of My Account. Any personally identifiable information you disclose through the use of discussions (message boards or chats) is voluntary and will be used in accordance with MedicineNet.com's [Discussion Rules](https://web.archive.org/web/20000623081928id_/http%3A//www.medicinenet.com/Script/Main/art.asp?li=MNI&d=236&articlekey=13353).

We develop summary-not individual-reports about our customers. These reports may be shared with our partners or advertisers, however we will never disclose personally identifiable statistics. An example of this information might be "10,000 people clicked on advertisement 'A' today and of those people, 40% previously indicated an interest in arthritis."

**Disclosure of your Information**     Back to Top

We do not sell or rent any personally identifiable information about you to any third party. We may share non-personal, aggregate information regarding customer demographics, traffic patterns, sales, and site usage with our partners, advertisers or sponsors. We will never willfully disclose personally identifiable information about our customers to any third party without prior consent. We are required to share certain information with our partners, in accordance with our Security Policy. Certain information will never be disclosed, including your password. 

In special cases MedicineNet.com may believe it is necessary to disclose personally identifiable information in order to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be violating or causing injury to or interference with MedicineNet.com's rights or property, other users, or any other party that could be harmed by such activities. We may also disclose or access personally identifiable information when we believe in good faith that the law requires it.

Other companies that have links or do advertising on our site may collect information about you when you click on their advertisement or link. We do not control the collection of this information and suggest you read the privacy statements of each of these companies.

**Opt-out Features**     Back to Top

MedicineNet.com believes that you should control your healthcare decisions, including what information you receive from us. The choices are yours to make. You can tell us if you don't want to receive the Newsletters and e-mail Update Alerts, through My Account, or by e-mail, phone, or mail.

**Changing Your Personal Information**     Back to Top

You may correct and update your personal information at any time. All you have to do is go to My Account and choose e-mail address and password to edit the information. If you participate in discussions (message boards or chats), you can update your personal information at any time by choosing edit profile once in the discussion environment.

**Deactivating Your Name**     Back to Top

You can at any time deactivate your name from our customer database by e-mail at [ smartservice@medicinenet.com](mailto:smartservice@medicinenet.com). Deactivation of your name will not remove your previous transaction history. However, this information will be stored by your unique number associated with your Cookie, not by any individually identifiable information. This information will not be able to be reactivated at this time.

For accounting, legal, and customer service reasons you cannot completely delete personal information entered to establish your first and subsequent purchases.

**Your Consent**     Back to Top

By using the MedicineNet.com web site, those sites powered by MedicineNet.com, and the services available to you on our sites, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described above. If we change our privacy policy, we will immediately post the changes on this page so that you are always aware of our policies.

**Links on our Site**     Back to Top

MedicineNet.com has provided links to various sites that we believe provide valuable information to you, our viewer. However, we are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or actions of such web sites.

**Security Policy**     Back to Top

How We Protect Your Personal Information 

[](https://digitalid.verisign.com/as2/232f76c7497c5c84af2ad1b86a76b%20fc6)  
We utilize all appropriate security and encryption methods provided by VeriSign™ and CyberSource®.  MedicineNet.com uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology, a security standard supported by Microsoft Internet Explorer (3.0 and higher) and Netscape Navigator (2.0 and higher), whenever sensitive information is transmitted between your browser and our network and when we transmit between our network and CyberSource®. Furthermore, your credit card and other personal information will be (a) encrypted (using 128-bit encryption) when transmitted through any part of our computer network, (b) secured behind a computer firewall when retained in our electronic files and (c) protected by password thereby accessible only by entering your user name and password.
