# Privacy Statement

Medtronic understands that privacy is very important to you. This policy tells you how we protect and use information that we obtain through our Web site. 

The information we receive, and how we use it, depends on what you do when visiting our Web site.

**IF YOU VISIT THE SITE ONLY TO READ OR DOWNLOAD INFORMATION:**

If you visit our site to read or download information, such as information about a health condition or about one of our products, we will collect and store only the following information: 

We will not sell, exchange or publish this information. We use this information to help us make our site more useful to visitors and to help us in our business. We may share this information with consultants that we hire to help us.

**IF YOU COMPLETE A FORM ON OUR WEB SITE:**

You may also decide to send us information over the Internet using a form on our Web site. By submitting such information, you agree that Medtronic companies (including those based in the U.S.A.) may keep a record of it and may use it to respond to your request(s) and update your implant record in compliance with FDA and any other applicable requirements. You also agree that Medtronic may disclose the information to third parties, including consultants, as necessary to respond appropriately to your request(s) and to comply with FDA and any other applicable requirements.

We will not sell, exchange or publish the information you send us on one of our forms.

Your message to us over the Internet will be encrypted if your browser allows. Encryption protects the privacy of your message as it is transmitted over the Internet. However, encryption cannot guarantee your message is protected against unlawful interception.
