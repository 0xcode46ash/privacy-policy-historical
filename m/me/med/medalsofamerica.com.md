# Medals of America Online - Privacy Policy

The information that you provide and information about your order may be combined with other personally identifiable information (such as demographic information and past purchase history) available from our records and other sources. This information will be used to make our future communications with you more efficient. This information may also be shared with other marketers to bring you offers of interest. If you prefer that we do not share your name and address with other marketers or have questions about this policy, please email us at [optout@usmedals.com](mailto:optout@usmedals.com?subject=Privacy%20Policy).
