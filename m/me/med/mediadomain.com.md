# MediaDomain.com: Privacy statement

MediaDomain.com, has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this site, MediaDomain.com. 

MediaDomain.com's registration form requires users to give us their email address. The email address is used to contact the poster, _only when necessary_ , concerning matters pertaining to the MediaDomain.com message boards or chat. Email addresses are not used in mass mailings from MediaDomain.com, nor are they sold to or shared with third parties. 

When posting to message boards, visitors may optionally include their email addresses. These email addresses are not used by MediaDomain.com for any purpose. Users may use the words "none" or "n/a" when posting to avoid publishing their email address

We track IP addresses to help administer our Web site. In some instances IP addresses may be used to assist in deterring and/or preventing abusive or criminal activity on message boards.

Our site uses cookies to count and track site visits anonymously. A cookie is a small piece of data that many Web sites write to a file on your hard disk. A cookie can contain data like an ID number used to track what pages are visited. A cookie cannot by itself be used by a Web site to obtain personal information about the user or to read information from your computer other than that which has been voluntarily provided by you or is contained in cookies given by the same Web site.

We use outside ad companies to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies that may be used for purposes such as limiting the number of times an ad is seen by the same person, monitoring how many people have viewed certain ads, or other advertising-related purposes. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are controlled by our ad companies, and we do not have access to information regarding these cookies. 

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here <http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp>

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please contact us at [privacy@MediaDomain.com](mailto:privacy@MediaDomain.com)
