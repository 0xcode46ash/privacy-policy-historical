# MediLexicon - Privacy Policy

MediLexicon will respect the confidentiality of the information relating to its individual users and visitors and the laws governing the privacy of medical information that apply in the country and state where the Web site is located. Information identifying a user will not be disclosed by MediLexicon staff to anyone unless specifically authorized by that individual. The information provided to MediLexicon will be used only for customer support and market research.
