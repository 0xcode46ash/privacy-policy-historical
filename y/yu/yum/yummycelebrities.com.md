# Privacy l Yummy! Celebrities and Entertainment

**Privacy** (updated)

Yummycelebrities.com respect your privacy.

Yummycelebrities.com collect info such as email address and site url. The information is used to provide you the information you requested (either by posting a comment or contact us by email).

Yummycelebrities.com logs IP addresses and browser types for systems administration purposes. These logs may be analyzed to improve the surfing experience of the users and to improve the contents of the website.

Yummycelebrities.com will not disclose your information to any 3rd parties with exception of summoned by law enforcement agencies.

Yummycelebrities.com will not attempt to obtain any other private info without the approval of the users and readers, other than what is mentioned above.
