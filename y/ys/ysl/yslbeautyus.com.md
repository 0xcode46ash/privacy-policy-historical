# YSL Beauty US Official Online Store

**PRIVACY POLICY**  
Thank you for visiting [www.yslbeautyus.com](http://www.yslbeautyus.com/), the US online store of YSL Beauté (the “Site“). YSL Beauté (“we/us/our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. Among other things, this Privacy Policy describes the personal information collected about you through the Site, why YSL Beauté collects it, how we use it and when we share it with third parties..

**WHAT DOES THIS PRIVACY POLICY COVER?**  
This Privacy Policy applies to any personal information collected through the Site, in circumstances when you knowingly provide such information to us. 

  YSL Beauté reserves the right to make changes to the Site and its disclaimers, policies, and terms and conditions at any time without notice. If YSL Beauté makes a material modification to these Terms and Conditions, including the Privacy Policy, it will notify you by sending an e-mail to the e-mail address associated with your account or by displaying a prominent announcement above the text of this Terms and Conditions or the Privacy Policy, as appropriate, for thirty (30) days. YSL Beauté is not responsible if you do not receive such notification e-mail due to the actions of your ISP or any e-mail filtering service.

**PERSONAL INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USAGE**  
We do not collect personally identifiable information about you, except when you provide it to us. Our goal is to record information to process your orders and if you request so, to inform you about our special offers, events and promotions. 

**a. e-mail address for newsletter**  
You may choose to opt-in to receive our newsletters or any other e-mail communications from YSL Beauté and/or its affiliates during the registration process or by signing up on the Site specifically to receive such communications. ****

If you prefer not to receive such opt -in information e-mails, please do not opt-in to receive this information. . If you do opt-in and decide later that you would no longer like to receive these emails, you may opt-out in the My Account section or on any e-mail sent by YSL Beauté. ****

**b. names, addresses, phones**  
If you register to receive newsletters and/or when you purchase product(s) through the Site, we collect and store the following personally identifiable information: first and last name, billing and shipping address(es), phone, and e-mail addresses to process your order. 

If you register with the Site, we will also collect and store other personal information such as your product interest and your previous purchases on the Site. 

YSL Beauté ships orders through trusted warehouse and shipping carriers such as UPS, FEDEX, and USPS which are not permitted to use personal information for purposes other than actual YSL Beauté shipping. ****

**c. Billing information**  
When you purchase product(s) through the Site we will collect your billing (e.g., credit card) information to process your payment. As an ultimate protection of your privacy, we do not store, at any time, your credit card information on the Site. (We shall request your credit card information at each purchase.) YSL Beauté processes payments through certified processors and banks compliant with this Privacy Policy. ** **

If you would prefer that we not collect any personally identifiable information from you, please do not provide us with any such information. ** **

**USE AND DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION WITH THIRD PARTIES**  
When you submit your personally identifiable information on the Site you are giving your consent to the collection, use and disclosure of your personal information as set forth in this Privacy Policy. YSL Beauté may use the personal information you provided us to keep you informed of our special events or promotions (as provided above), to administer the Site, and/or to process any orders you may place at the Site. Otherwise we may use the personally identifiable information you provided us only to review our performance, improve our service to you, or to communicate with you about your order if you placed your order on the Site.

In the normal course of serving its clients, YSL Beauté may disclose the personal information you provided us to our affiliates and agents. YSL Beauté contracts with third parties that perform services for YSL Beauté, such as assistance in maintaining and managing the Site and our customer database, or creating and distributing YSL Beauté communications via e-mail. YSL Beauté may disclose information such to third party service providers if such disclosure is necessary for the provision of the services. YSL Beauté does not authorize any of its service providers to make any other use of the personal information you provided us. Any party to whom YSL Beauté discloses the personal information you provided us will be required to maintain the confidentiality of such information and otherwise comply with this Privacy Policy.

YSL Beauté reserves the right to transfer personal information to a third party in the event of a sale of stock, merger or consolidation or other transfer of all or substantially all of the assets of YSL Beauté, whether voluntary or by operation of law, to such third party, provided that such party agrees to protect the confidentiality of your personal information and otherwise complies with our Privacy Policy.

YSL Beauté may also disclose any of your personal information in its possession, to law enforcement or other governmental officials or agencies in response to a subpoena, search warrant, or court order; or in connection with a legal claim or proceeding; or as otherwise required by law.

**SECURITY AND COMMUNICATION OVER INTERNET**  
Beyond the measures we take to protect your personal information on the Site, YSL Beauté also cares about how we exchange information with you. We use industry-standard Secure Sockets Layer ("SSL") authentication to guarantee the confidentiality of online transactions made on our site. SSL authentication and encryption of the information that you send to us over the Internet help protect your online transaction information from third party interception.

We urge you to protect your own privacy. We recommend that you do not share your password with anyone or share your password in an unsolicited phone call or e-mail.   
Unfortunately, despite all of our efforts, you should be aware that no technology is perfect and there is always some risk involved in the transferring of data through the Internet, particularly if you send YSL Beauté information via unsecured means, such as e-mails.

**CHILDREN'S PRIVACY**  
Children under the age of 13 are allowed to visit www.yslbeautyus.com, but cannot make a purchase. You may not provide personal information to us. Our site is not designed for children, YSL Beauté does not knowingly collect personal information on any person under the age of 13. ** **

**COOKIES**  
We collect some **anonymous** information about how you use the site by setting and accessing cookies on your computer. These cookies track information such as how often you visit our web site, what pages you view, and where you go after you leave the site. The cookies track your computer, not you. They are not connected to any personal information about you. ** **

**ACCURACY AND RETENTION**  
We are committed to keeping our records as accurate as possible in updating the personal information you provide us. You may access, update or correct all the personal information you provided us; change your e-mail communication preferences; and/or remove your personal data from our systems entirely, by contacting our Customer Service as 866-554-4YSL (866-554-4975). ****Please be sure to include your email address, full name and, if you are register with the Site, your password. Please note that it may take up to 15 business days to update your personal information or to remove you from our list. ****
