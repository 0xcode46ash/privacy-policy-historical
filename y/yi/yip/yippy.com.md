# Capita Business Travel | About Us | Legal Information

  


## Privacy

We take care to protect the privacy of customers and users of the Capita Business Travel website. Set out below is an explanation of how we process information about visitors to the site. 

Collection of personal information  
Information which we collect and store during normal use of the site is used to monitor and analyse how parts of the site are used. Such use does not result in any personally identifiable date being collected or stored.

You have the option on certain pages within this site to submit personal information to Capita Business Travel in order that we might send you further information or email alerts. These pages provide explanations as to how this information is to be used.

We will not disclose without your consent any personal information we collect about you when you visit the site to a third party outside The Capita Group Plc. 

In connection with any application, request or enquiry you make, your information will be passed directly to the relevant business unit within the Group. 

By submitting your personal information through this website, you shall be consenting to it being processed in the manner described above by The Capita Group Plc.
