**We Collect Through Automatic Data Collection Technologies.** As you navigate through and interact with this Website, we or our service providers may collect your website usage information using automatic data collection technologies. The website usage information we or our service providers automatically collect is typically statistical data that does not individually identify you, but we may maintain it or associate it with personal information we collect in other ways or receive from third parties. This type of information helps us improve this Website and deliver a better and more personalized service, including by enabling us to: estimate our audience size and usage patterns; gain information about your preferences or that would be relevant to you, allowing us to customize this Website or some of its features accordingly; and recognize you when you return to this Website.

We also may allow third parties to use automatic data collection technologies on this Website to collect information about your online activities over time and across other websites or online services (behavioral tracking). Click here for information on how you can opt-out of behavioral tracking on this Website and how we respond to web browser "do not track" signals and other mechanisms that enable consumers to exercise choice about behavioral tracking. The technologies we use for this automatic data collection may include:

  * **Cookies (or browser cookies).** A cookie is a small file placed on the hard drive of your computer. You may refuse to accept browser cookies by activating the appropriate setting on your browser. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it refuses cookies, our system will issue cookies when you direct your browser to this Website and grant us permission to access your location information. If you refuse cookies or delete them, you may be unable to access certain parts or features of this Website.
  * **Flash cookies.** Certain features of this Website may use local stored objects (or Flash cookies) to collect and store information about your preferences and navigation to, from and on this Website. Flash cookies are not managed by the same browser settings that manage browser cookies.
  * **Web Beacons.** Pages of our the Website may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs. pixel tags and single-pixel gifs) that permit Yakima to count users who have visited those pages and for other related website statistics such as recording the popularity of certain website content and verifying system and server integrity.



We do not control these third parties' automatic data collection technologies or practices or how they may be used. If you have any questions about an advertisement or other targeted content, you should contact the responsible provider directly. For information about how you can opt-out of receiving targeted advertising from many providers, see Choices About How We Use and Disclose Your Information.
