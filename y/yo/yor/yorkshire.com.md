# Privacy Policy - Welcome to Yorkshire.com

**Privacy Statement**

Welcome to Yorkshire will not do anything with your personal details without your permission. You're in control. Any information you supply us with will be retained on a database and in addition to being used to contact you with information about your particular travel interests, may be used for statistical purposes. If so used, the statistical reports generated will not identify individuals in any way. Welcome to Yorkshire will not send you information. Your data will be retained on a database and used for statistical purposes only.

From time to time we may also wish to contact you in association with carefully selected travel partners whose products and services are relevant to you. If you do not wish to receive information from such companies, simply indicate this during any forms that you fill in. Welcome to Yorkshire will not sell, trade or rent your information to others unless you have given us your permission. With your permission we will only share the information with carefully chosen, reputable and trustworthy third parties.
