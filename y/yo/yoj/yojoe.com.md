# YOJOE.COM | Privacy Policy

**Yo Joe is committed to protecting your privacy**.

Yo Joe's server logs collect certain limited information about you - your IP address, what page you're visiting, and what browser you're using. Only summarized information may be released - your personal browsing habits will not be examined or shown to anyone.

If you send us email, we will not sell your email address to spammers.

**Banner Advertising**

Banner Advertising is run through two ad affiliate programs - Commission Junction [[http://www.cj.com](http://www.cj.com/)] and On Response [[http://www.onresponse.com](http://www.onresponse.com/)]. Their privacy policies are available on their web sites.

**Web Polls**

Web Polls are hosted through Sparklit.com. Sparklit's privacy policy is available at <http://www.sparklit.com/privacy>.

**Yo Joe Depot**

The Yo Joe Depot will not provide customers' personal information to any third party for any type of marketing, solicitation, or survey purposes.

The [Yo Joe Depot](http://www.yojoe-depot.com/) is hosted by Yahoo! Store. Yahoo! hosts the store pages, ordering system, and order data. Yahoo! automatically collects order information but may only use this information in the aggregate. If you accessed this store through Yahoo! Shopping, Yahoo! also automatically collects information about your shopping experience. Please see the Yahoo! Privacy Policy for more information about how Yahoo! uses this information.

* * *

If you have questions about our policy, please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/contact.shtml).
