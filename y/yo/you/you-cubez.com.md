# Privacy Policy | You-Cubez.com

This Privacy Policy is applicable to any personal information, which is submitted via the web site located at  
www.you-cubez.com. We hope the following statement will help you understand how You-Cubez.com collects, uses and safeguards the personal information you provide to us on our site. 

##### Data Collection

Whilst using our service at www.you-cubez.com, you may encounter areas in which you are asked to submit User Information. This User Information will only be used for the purposes for which it was collected and in accordance with this Privacy Policy. 

During both registration and logging in, your IP address is stored. 

You may request for your account to be terminated and User Information removed at any time, by contacing us at info@you-cubez.com. 

We will never sell, lease or otherwise transfer any personal information obtained from customers to third parties unless required to do so by the law.

##### How your data is used

By submitting your User Information, you allow us to hold this information in a secure manner. 

Your User Information (e-mail address) may be used to send e-mails regarding new features, website updates and special offers. Such e-mails may include advertising for third party companies/organisations. 

You may unsubscribe from e-mails at any time, by logging in to your account and unsubscribing ("My Info" area) or by sending an e-mail to info@you-cubez.com with the subject 'UNSUBSCRIBE'.

##### Cookies

Cookies may automatically be used to collect information/data. "Cookies" are small text files that store basic information; for example, display your name/personal details if this has been previously submitted.

##### Updates

You may update, amend or correct your personal information which can be found in your "My Info" area in your account (You must be logged in) or you may notify us via the form on the Contact page.

##### Contact/Other

If you have questions or comments with regards to this privacy policy, please use the form on the contact page or e-mail us at: info@you-cubez.com.

Any website that you may connect to from You-Cubez is not covered by this Privacy Policy.

We may change this privacy policy from time to time and so therefore, you should check this page regularly.
