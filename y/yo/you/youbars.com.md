# You Bar - Fresh, Handmade Nutrition Bars

---  
  
_You bar_

collects personal information from visitors to our website on a voluntary basis; however, visitors are not required to give such information to access our website. Personal information may include, without limitation, name, address, phone number and e-mail address. _You bar_ uses visitors' personal information to confirm your order. We do not give out any of your information to any other company or individual.

All information gathered about each visitor to The _You bar_ website is subject to and protected by the Electronic Communications Privacy Act.

Although we will use all reasonable efforts to safeguard the confidentiality of any visitor information collected, _You bar_ will have no liability for disclosure of any visitor information obtained due to errors in transmission or the unauthorized acts of third parties.

_You bar_ reserves the right to change or update this privacy policy, or any other policy or practice, at anytime with reasonable notice to users of its website. Any changes or updates will be effective immediately upon posting to www.youbars.com.
