# yourminis

PLEASE READ THE FOLLOWING.  THIS PRIVACY POLICY CONCERNS YOUR PRIVACY WHEN YOU USE THIS WEB SITE.

**Introduction.**   This Web site (this “Site”) contains information related to the products and services of Goowy Media Inc., a California corporation (“we”, “us” or “our”).  This privacy policy concerns your privacy when you use this Site. 

**Browser Information.**   When you enter this Site, information is gathered from your Internet browser. This information may include your IP address (which allows us to determine the identity of your Internet service provider), your operating system and the type of browser software that you are using to access this Site.  This information allows us to upgrade this Site as necessary to optimize its efficiency.  All information obtained from your browser is anonymous, and no personal information is ever transmitted to us in this manner.

**Cookies.**   We may use cookies to collect information about how you use this Site and the services, products and information on this Site.  A “cookie” is a small text file that a Web site can place on your computer’s hard drive in order, for example, to retain your preferences or registration information for your next visit to such Web site.  The cookie transmits this information back to such Web site’s operator.  You can choose not to receive a cookie file by enabling your browser to refuse cookies or prompt you before accepting a cookie.  Note that refusing to accept cookies may impact the performance of this Site. 

**Personal Information.**   Certain portions of this Site or the services, products and information on this Site are “registration-required” pages that require you to submit personal information before we grant you access.  For example, uploading widgets to this Site requires you to provide personal information.  Such information may include your name, address, e-mail address and phone number. 

**Sharing of Personal Information.**   We may share the personal information you disclose to us on this Site.  Within our company, we limit access to such information to authorized individuals.  However, we may also disclose such information to affiliated and unaffiliated third parties who work with us on marketing, product development, service enhancement and other similar matters.

**Unsolicited E-Mail.**   Should you voluntarily provide your personal information to us, you may receive periodic e-mail messages from us.  Such messages may include announcements regarding this Site and the services, products and information on this Site.

**Security Precautions.**   Where appropriate, we may employ firewalls, encryption technology, user authentication systems and access control mechanisms to control access to our systems and data.  However, you should exercise discretion with respect to the submission of any personal information to this Site. Transmitting information via the Internet is never completely secure and, therefore, any information submitted may be intercepted, collected, used or disclosed by others.

**Links to Other Web Sites.**   This privacy policy cannot and does not apply to external Web sites.  Once you leave this Site, you are subject to the privacy policies and procedures of such external Web sites.

**Not Intended for Children.**   This Site is for use by persons age 13 or older.  We will not solicit, collect or maintain personal information from children under the age of 13.  We will terminate your use of this Site or the services, products and information on this Site if you are under the age of 13.

**Terms of Use.**   By using this Site, you accept our [Terms of Use](http://blog.yourminis.com/2006/09/terms_of_servic.html).  Nothing in this privacy policy limits or qualifies our [Terms of Use](http://blog.yourminis.com/2006/09/terms_of_servic.html), our Terms of Uploading (with respect to your uploading a widget to this Site) or any “click-wrap” agreement on this Site, all of which should be reviewed by you in their entirety.

**Effective Date.**   This privacy policy is effective as of April 30, 2007.  We reserve the right, at our sole discretion, to change, modify, add or remove any portion of this privacy policy, in whole or in part, at any time, which shall be effective when notice of such is posted on this Site.

**Contact Us.**   If you have any questions about our company, this Site or this privacy policy, please [contact us](http://blog.yourminis.com/contact_us/index.html).
