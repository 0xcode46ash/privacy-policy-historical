**General Privacy Statement**

As is our company policy with all information gathering, any information sent to Young America through this site will be kept confidential. We collect personal information from our visitors on a voluntary basis only. Personal information may include name, title, company, address, phone number, e-mail address, etc. The individual information we collect is used only for marketing our products and services and to create Web site content which is helpful for you. We do not sell, rent or transmit personal information to other organizations.  **Consumer Privacy Statement**

For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes the consumer's domain name and e-mail address (where possible). We collect the e-mail addresses of those consumer who communicate with us via e-mail. The return address and or phone number information and promotion/offer inquiry information, as volunteered by the consumer, is used by us to contact the consumer about their inquiry and may, as a part of routine activity reporting, be forwarded only to the Young America client sponsoring the promotion/offer. If you supply us with your postal address on-line you will only receive the information from Young America for which you provided us your address. Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding inquiries placed on-line.
