# Privacy Policy -

Should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, you can be assured it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.

We will not sell, distribute or lease any personal information you share with us to third parties unless we have your permission or are required by law to do so.

We are committed to ensuring that any private information you have shared with us is secure. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure any information we collect.

We may periodically contact you by any means you have shared with us, in any manner you have given us permission to use, to promote our or third party products, special offers or other information which we think you may find interesting, or perhaps for market research purposes, each only if and in accordance to your prior consent. We may use the information to customize the website according to your interests.

If you change your mind at any time about what information you wish us to keep or how you would like us to use it, please contact us.

We are not responsible for republished content from this blog on other blogs or websites without our permission.

Our website contains links to other websites which may be of interest. We do not have any control over other websites you visit. You should exercise caution and understand any privacy statement/policy at these other websites.

This website does not store any information about your visit other than to analyze and optimize your content and reading experience through the use of browser-volunteered information, such as type and computing platform, and “cookies”. A cookie in no way gives us access to your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us. You can turn off the use of cookies at any time by changing your specific browser settings. This may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website, however.

Some of our advertisers occasionally serve you cookies as well.  We do not have control over cookies placed by advertisers, and we disclaim all liability in connection therewith.  You may opt out of cookies using the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page at <http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/> or via the services offered by individual advertisers.

We may use advertising service vendors to help present advertisements on this website. These vendors may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to serve you advertisements tailored to interests you have shown by browsing on this and other sites you have visited, to determine whether you have seen a particular advertisement before and to avoid sending you duplicate advertisements. In doing so, these vendors may collect non-personal data such as your browser type, your operating system, Web pages visited, time of visits, content viewed, ads viewed, and other clickstream data. The use of cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies by these advertising service vendors is subject to their own privacy policies, not ours, and we disclaim all liability in connection therewith.

This privacy policy is subject to change without notice and was last updated on December 1, 2015.   If you have any questions feel free to contact me directly: khale@teamhalemedia.com
