# Privacy Policy | YourListen

Your privacy means allot to us. Our Privacy Policy is here to inform you how we gather, place, use and disclose your information when you use YourListen.com, and all related services.

By using and registering with YourListen.com, you are consenting to the use of your personal information in the manner set out in this Privacy Policy. 

## Data Protection and Privacy

All information is collected, stored and used in accordance with our Privacy Policy. Read below to ensure you understand how our privacy policy works.

By accepting our Terms of Use and using YourListen.com you also accept the terms of the Privacy Policy.When you register with YourListen.com you will need a user name, password and email address. It is your responsibility to keep all of your registration data up to date. All registration information provided falls under the terms set forth in the YourListen.com Privacy Policy.If your username or password is lost or stolen or think your account has been compromised first change your username and password then contact us at: support@yourlisten.com

## Information collected about you

You do not need to provide us any information when visiting the site. The only time we collect information from you is when you register for an account. When you register for an account we will only collect your user name, password and email that you register with. All other information you provide on your profile is optional. What information you provide publicly on our site is also controlled by you. You have full control over your data.

There is some information we collect upon visiting YourListen.com. This data is collected via cookies and Google analytics.

**We collect the ip address from the device you visit the site on:**

  1. The site you visited before coming to YourListen.com
  2. Audio files you upload
  3. Which other users you follow and which users follow you
  4. Connection of your facebook account
  5. The time, frequency and duration of your visits to the Website
  6. Your browser type and operating system



We use traffic log cookies to identify which pages are being used. This enables us to view data about web page traffic and improve our site for you. You can refuse to accept cookies by enabling the setting on your computer. If you decide on to do so you may be unable to access bound components of our web site. Our advertisers may use cookies over that we have no management.

Your email address will be used to send you newsletters and email notifications for uploads and follows. To unsubscribe from newsletters and email notifications email us at support@yourlisten.com

## Your Information

We will not share your personal information with any third party, unless you have opted to connect your account with a third party website. We will not permit any third parties to contact you directly on an unsolicited basis in relation to their own products or services

You can control access to other services and applications to which you have connected your account to YourListen.com.

Information in your profiles accept for your email will be accessible by other users on YourListen.com. We do however provide you with an option to make particular information in your profile private.

If required by law we will disclose your YourListen.com information. 

## Third-Party Ad Servers

We use third party advertising companies to target and serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use cookies and other tracking technologies to serve as relevant of ads to you as possible.

Our website might have links to third party websites. When you click a link and leave to a third party website we are not responsible for the protection of your privacy and our privacy policy does not govern. 

If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please see: [http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp]()

## Facebook Registrations

YourListen.com allows you to sign up and log in using your Facebook account. If you register with Facebook, Facebook will confirm that you give permission to share particular information from your Facebook account with YourListen.com.

This information is collected by Facebook and is provided to us under the terms of Facebook's privacy policy. You are in control of what we receive from Facebook using the privacy settings in your Facebook account.

You can also control the activities you perform on YourListen.com that we send to Facebook. 

## Children

YourListen.com is not intended for use by children. Anyone under the age of 13 (thirteen) is not permitted to use the site or register for an account. If it comes to our attention that we have collected personal data from a person under the age of 13 (thirteen), we will terminate your account.

## Privacy Statement

If you are concerned about how your information is collected or used you need to occasionally check on our Privacy Policy. If you have any other concerns not mentioned in the Policy, please contact us using our support email which is support@yourlisten.com

If you choose to not provide your consent, or if you withdraw your authorization, you should not use the website as a registered user, since many of the website's features are based on users consent. 

## Alterations and Updates to our Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. Modifications to our Privacy Policy will be available here on our Privacy Policy page.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to suggest changes to this Privacy Policy email us at support@yourlisten.com
