# youhot.com

### Privacy Policy

Effective as of October 8, 2007

YouHot.com respects online privacy.  This Privacy Policy applies to all services offered by YouHot.com, including its website (the “Website”).  If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us at privacy@YouHot.com.

**Information Collection and Use**

YouHot.com is an online service that allows its users to communicate and share videos, pictures, and other information.  The personally identifiable information you choose to provide on this Website is used to operate, maintain, and provide you the features and functionality of the Website, as well as provide personalized features to you.  Please be aware that any personal information or video content that you voluntarily disclose online (on your profile page, in publicly accessible areas of the Website, in discussion groups, messages, etc.) becomes publicly available and can be viewed, read, collected, or used by others.  YouHot.com is not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit in these online areas.

_Registration_ :  In order to use the YouHot.com Website, you must first complete the registration form and create a user name and password, which is used to protect your account from unauthorized access.  During registration you are required to give your email address, which we may use to send updates, a newsletter, or news regarding the service.  YouHot.com will also communicate with you in response to your inquiries, to provide the services you request, and to manage your account.  YouHot.com will send you strictly service-related announcements on rare occasions when it is necessary to do so.

_User Profile_ :  YouHot.com also collects other user profile date, including without limitation, personal interests, members’ pictures, member’s first names, and gender in order to allow users to find and communicate with each other.

_Log Files_ :  We gather certain non-personally identifiable information automatically and store it in log files.  This aggregate information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data.  YouHot.com uses this aggregate information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends, to administer the Website, to track users’ movements around the Website and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole.

_Cookies_ :  A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user’s computer for record-keeping purposes.  YouHot.com uses cookies on this Website.  YouHot.com does not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our Website.  If you reject cookies, you may still use our Website, but your ability to use some areas of our Website, such as **[need a few examples]** , will be limited.  Some of our advertisers use cookies on our Website.  YouHot.com has no access to or control over these cookies. 

_Clear Gifs (Web Beacons/Web Bugs)_ :  YouHot.com employs [or our third party advertising partner employs] a software technology called clear gifs (a.k.a. Web Beacons/Web Bugs), that help us better manage content on our Website by informing us what content is effective.  Clear gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of web users.  In contrast to cookies, which are stored on a user’s computer hard drive, clear gifs are embedded invisibly on web pages and are about the size of the period at the end of this sentence.  YouHot.com tie [do not tie] the information gathered by clear gifs to our customers’ personally identifiable information. 

YouHot.com uses clear gifs in our HTML-based emails to let us know which emails have been opened by recipients.  This allows us to gauge the effectiveness of certain communications and the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.  If you would like to opt-out of these emails, please see “Choice and Opt-out.”

_Surveys, Contests, or Marketing Campaigns:_   From time-to-time, YouHot.com may provide you the opportunity to participate in contests, marketing campaigns, or surveys on our Website.  If you participate, we will request certain personally identifiable information from you.  Participation in these surveys, marketing campaigns, or contests is completely voluntary and you therefore have a choice whether or not to disclose this information.  The requested information typically includes contact information (such as name and mailing address), and demographic information (such as a zip code). 

YouHot.com will use this information to notify contest winners and award prizes, to monitor Website traffic or personalize the Website (in the case of anonymous information collected in surveys), to send participants an email newsletter, or to contact users to participate in upcoming marketing campaigns.  YouHot.com may use a third party service provider to conduct these surveys, marketing campaigns, or contests, however, that company is prohibited from using our users’ personally identifiable information for any other purpose.  YouHot.com will not share the personally identifiable information you provide through a contest, marketing campaign, or survey with other third parties unless we give you prior notice and choice.

_Invitations or Other Communications to Non-members_ :  If you choose to use our invitation service to tell a friend about our Website, we will ask you for your friend’s name and email address.  YouHot.com will automatically send your friend a one-time email inviting him or her to visit the Website.  YouHot.com stores this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email and may include the tracking the success of our invitation program.  Your friend may contact us at privacy@YouHot.com to request that we remove this information from our database.

**Choice/Opt-out**  
YouHot.com provides you the opportunity to ‘opt-out’ of having your personally identifiable information used for certain purposes, when we ask for this information.  If you no longer wish to receive our newsletter and promotional communications, you may opt-out of receiving them by following the instructions included in each newsletter or communication or by emailing us at privacy@YouHot.com.  You will be notified when your personal information is collected by any third party that is not our agent/service provider, so you can make an informed choice as to whether or not to share your information with that party. 

**Third Party Advertisers**  
YouHot.com or by one of our advertising partners may deliver the ads appearing on this Website.  Our advertising partners may use cookies on this Website, which allow it to recognize a user's cookie when a user visits this Website in order to deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of interest to you.  YouHot.com has no access to or control over these cookies.

**Links to Other Sites**  
This Website contains links to other sites that are not owned or controlled by YouHot.com. Please be aware that YouHot.com is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other websites.  YouHot.com encourages you to be aware when you leave our Website and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information.  This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected by this Website. 

**Correcting, Updating, or Removing Information**  
If your personally identifiable information changes, or if you no longer desire to use our Service, you may update your information through your membership account or deactivate it by emailing our Customer Support at support@YouHot.com.

**Security**  
The security of your personal information is important to us.  YouHot.com follows generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it.  No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however.  Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

**Changes in this Privacy Statement**  
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this Privacy Policy, the homepage, and other places we deem appropriate so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it.  YouHot.com reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time, so please review it frequently at www.YouHot.com.

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@YouHot.com
