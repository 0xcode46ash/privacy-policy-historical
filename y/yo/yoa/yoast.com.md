# Privacy Policy - Yoast - Tweaking Websites

We at Yoast have created this privacy statement in order to show my commitment to my and your privacy. This page discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for the website yoast.com.

## Information Automatically Logged

As with most other websites, we collect and use the data contained in log files. The information in the log files include your IP (internet protocol) address, your ISP (internet service provider, such as AOL or Shaw Cable), the browser you used to visit our site (such as Internet Explorer or Firefox), the time you visited our site and which pages you visited throughout our site. 

We track your activity with [Google Analytics](http://www.google.com/analytics/), [Yahoo! Analytics](http://web.analytics.yahoo.com/), [Kampyle](http://www.kampyle.com/), [Mint](http://www.haveamint.com/) and sometimes [Crazy Egg](http://crazyegg.com/), [SiteSpect](http://www.sitespect.com/) and [Google Website Optimizer](http://www.google.com/websiteoptimizer).

## Advertising

We run our own advertising system, which does not use cookies. Some of my advertisers choose to host their own banner, and could thus use it set a cookie.Cookies received with banner ads are collected by their respective owners, and we do not have access to this information. Cookies are used on Yoast only for login and commenting purposes.

## External Links

This site contains (affiliate) links to other sites. Yoast.com and it's authors are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

## Personal Information

When you comment or subscribe to an email list, we ask for your name, email address and optionally website. When you register on the site we allow you to give us a bit more info. Your email and other information might be used for contacting you about updates to Yoast, and, when you've subscribed to the newsletter, for regular newsletters. Your **personal information will not be distributed** to anyone for any purpose.

## Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.

## Contacting the Web Site

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact us at the following page: [contact us](https://web.archive.org/contact/).
