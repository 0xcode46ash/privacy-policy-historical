# Yorkshire Building Society -- Your Society -- Introduction

You may have concerns about the security of your information when using our web site. This section of the site explains the measures we have taken to protect and make secure your personal information. 

We also take the privacy of your personal information very seriously and have established our [Privacy Policy](https://web.archive.org/web/20040409040342id_/http%3A//www.ybs.co.uk/security_privacy/privacy/index.jsp) to explain this to you. 

In building this web site we have used highly recognised, reliable and industry standard technologies to ensure your details are secure and kept confidential. See our [Security](https://web.archive.org/web/20040409040342id_/http%3A//www.ybs.co.uk/security_privacy/security/index.jsp) section for more information.
