# Yesmail - Permission-Based E-mail Marketing: Company

Yesmail is a licensee of the TRUSTe Privacy Program. TRUSTe is an independent, non-profit organization whose mission is to build users' trust and confidence in the Internet by promoting the use of fair information practices. Because we want to demonstrate our commitment to your privacy, we have agreed to disclose our information practices and have our privacy practices reviewed for compliance by TRUSTe. By displaying the TRUSTe trustmark, we agree to notify you of:

  1. What personally identifiable information of yours or third party personally identification information is collected from you through the Web site. 
  2. The organization collecting the information. 
  3. How the information is used. 
  4. With whom the information may be shared. 
  5. What choices are available to you regarding collection, use and distribution of the information. 
  6. The kind of security procedures that are in place to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of information under Yesmail's control. 
  7. How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information. 



If you have any questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact [privacy@yesmail.com](mailto:privacy@yesmail.com) by e-mail. If you do not receive acknowledgement of your inquiry or your inquiry has not been satisfactorily addressed, you should then contact TRUSTe, [www.truste.org/consumers/watchdog_complaint.php](http://www.truste.org/consumers/watchdog_complaint.php) TRUSTe will then serve as a liaison with the Web site to resolve your concerns.

Consumer privacy is a top priority to Yesmail and to our clients and partners. We support the protection of privacy rights as a fundamental element of our business.

As companies and individuals look to Yesmail for e-mail marketing solutions, they can be assured that we will respect their needs for privacy. Our relationships are our most valuable asset and the very basis for our reputation, so we will only disclose information that companies and individuals have given us explicit permission to disclose.

This privacy policy discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for the Yesmail Enterprise and Yesmail Professional solutions and the Yesmail Web site, www.yesmail.com. TRUSTe can only cover the activities on this Web site, www.yesmail.com.

### Information Collection and Its Use

We use e-mail links throughout the site to allow you to contact us directly with any questions or comments you may have. We read every message sent in and try to reply promptly to every one when appropriate. The information we receive from you is used to respond directly to your questions or comments. We may file your comments to improve the site, or review and discard the information. Your personal information is not shared with third parties.

Yesmail automatically logs your IP address, the log files are deleted after 30 days. This information is not linked to any personally identifiable information. The Yesmail Web site does not use cookies.

Our site's information forms request your name, e-mail address and telephone number as well as additional information to help us determine your needs related to our solution. We use this information to send you updates about our company or to contact you if requested or necessary. You may change your personal profile or opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time, please see our Choice/Opt-Out section below.

If you need to correct or update your information, please e-mail [privacy@yesmail.com](mailto:privacy@yesmail.com). 

### Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. E-mail is sent to those employees that need to see it in order to respond to your inquiries. All of our employees are aware of the importance of keeping any information that is disclosed within the e-mail confidential and only will disclose to others within our organization.

### How We Notify You of Changes to our Privacy Policy 

Whenever we change our privacy policy, we will post a notice on our Web site indicating that a new version is available. If Yesmail changes how it uses a user's personally identifiable information, the user will be notified by e-mail and they may opt-out at that time.

### Links

The Yesmail site contains links to other sites. Yesmail is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of these linked sites as their privacy policy may differ from ours.

We may disclose personal information when required by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law or comply with a legal process served on our Web site.

For any questions concerning the information collection practices of this Web site please contact: [privacy@yesmail.com](mailto:privacy@yesmail.com). 

### Permission Marketing is Our Philosophy

Yesmail facilitates the most consumer-oriented marketing practices in the e-mail marketing industry. By supporting permission e-mail, we allow individuals to identify personal preferences and needs, and then only communicate with them regarding those specific areas for which they have identified an interest. Using our unique opt-in survey methodology, we truly can understand the individuals' personal needs and tailor communication accordingly.

### Prospecting Services Deliver Only Targeted Information 

The prospecting services provided by Yesmail are targeting services that allow companies to find individuals who would be most interested in using their products and services. These services give companies the ability to send very targeted communication to a very targeted audience. At any time, if an individual wishes to be removed from these databases, he or she may choose to opt out and be eliminated from the database.

### Choice/Opt-Out

Yesmail makes it easy for individuals to change their information requests or opt-out of future communications from Yesmail altogether. Whether they sign up for Yesmail-powered e-mail communications from a client or register a Yesmail product and request future product news, they'll be informed of easy opt-out instructions with each communication should they decide to remove their name from our databases.

Our Yesmail privacy policies contain additional opt-out information specific to the service or product. 

### Children's Online Privacy Protection

The safety of children is important to us. Yesmail's Web site is a general audience site and is not directed to children under the age of 13. Similarly, Yesmail's services are not directed to nor intended for anyone under the age of 13. Yesmail will not knowingly accept, collect or maintain personally identifiable information from or about anyone under the age of 13 years. Yesmail relies on the truthfulness of individual registrant certifications when accepting data from Network Partners. In the event that Yesmail receives information that establishes that a registrant is under the age of 13 years, Yesmail reserves the right to eliminate that person's information from its database. If you believe Yesmail has any information from or about anyone under 13, please contact us at [privacy@yesmail.com](mailto:privacy@yesmail.com).
