# Leftronic » Privacy

Last updated on October 9th, 2012.

This Privacy Policy describes how Leftronic, Inc. protects your privacy when you use our website and application. Please read it carefully – by using the site you agree to all of the terms and conditions below.

We understand how important your data is, and we take security very seriously. We are dedicated to making sure that your privacy is respected and that your data is always kept secure. You may add delete or modify your personal information at any time by contacting us at [support@leftronic.com](mailto:support@leftronic.com). We will not sell, exchange or otherwise share your personal information with third parties.

### Collection of Personal Information

Our application can access data from third parties on your behalf, and data provided to us by you through our API. These features are only activated with your explicit permission and can be deactivated by you at any time. Once data collection is deactivated, the application will no longer aggregate any data.

Other personal information may be collected on the site, such as your name, e-mail address, and credit card information when you set up an account.

### Use of Personal Information

The information we collect on the site and the data that is collected by our application is used to communicate with you, to process your transactions, to improve our website, and to customize the products and services we provide you.

Personal information may be shared with companies and organizations that perform services on our behalf, such as companies that provide data management, data storage, or other support services. Our affiliates process this information strictly in compliance with our privacy policy and appropriate confidentiality and security measures. We may disclose personal information if required to comply with applicable law, investigate a possible crime such as fraud or identity theft, or to protect your vital interests.

### Opt-Out of Promotional Materials

You may opt-out of receiving promotional communications by using the “Unsubscribe” link at the bottom of emails or by contacting us at [support@leftronic.com](https://www.leftronic.com/privacy/support@leftronic.com).

### Security

We work hard to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction through effective security precautions. All data transmitted to and from our dashboard web application is encrypted and transmitted in a secure way. We also encrypt and transmit any other sensitive information (such as credit card information and passwords) collected on our website.

### Cookies

A cookie is a small file which asks permission to be placed on your computer’s hard drive. We use cookies for analytics (see below) as well as to store your information, such as log-in credentials. A cookie in no way gives us access to your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.

### Analytics

We gather data through web analytics services in order to better understand how people use and interact with our website and application. Under no circumstances is your information shared with a third party.

### Minors

You must be at least 13 years old to use this website and our products and services. We do not knowingly collect or use personal information about visitors under 13 years of age.

### Changes

You will be informed when this privacy policy changes.
