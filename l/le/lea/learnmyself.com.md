# Security and Privacy Policy

Learnmyself is a not for profit organization which exists to further general knowledge in the field of psychometrics as well as serve as a portal for entertainment. We do not and will never sell information provided to this site to any third parties. We do not contact users unless we have requested permission to do so via email. 

We do collect visitors statistics such as referral data and content usage in order to optimize our content. This data is anonymous meaning no personal data is stored. Once again we will never sell this data to third parties. 

## How to contact us

Please use the contact form if you wish to contact us to discuss any of the issues outlined in our security and privacy policy. 

### Security

Learnmyself is dedicated to the security of information gathered on our website. We conduct regular security reviews and ensure our software is running the most secure and stable versions of any security updates.
