# Article submission site

Privacy Policy

### PRIVACY POLICY.

##### THIS STATEMENT SPELLS OUT THE PRIVACY POLICY OF LEADARTICLES.COM

Authors are be rest assured that information such as name and email address are held in strict confidence. 

We do not share personal information with any other person. All information is for the sole purpose of providing valuable information to enhance authors' contributionto leadarticles.com 

Authors can at anytime asked to be removed from authors' list. Just send a blank email to **webmaster@leadarticles.com** with "remove from authors list" in the subject line.
