# Privacy Policy | Learn Interest Rates

[Learn Interest Rates](http://learninterestrates.com/) (LIR) respects your privacy and is committed to safeguarding your privacy while online at this site. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.

Like most standard website servers, LIR uses log files. This includes Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.

**E-mail Subscription**

If a user wants to subscribe to this blog via e-mail, LIR requires for contact information such as name and email address. Out of respect for the users’ privacy, a way to opt-out of these feature is provided.

We do not spam and we hate it as much as you do. LIR guarantees the security of your email that you had given us.

**Cookies**

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s computer tied to information about the user. This site use cookies for tracking visitors’ activity. Some of LIR’s business partners use cookies on our site such as our advertisers. However, LIR has no access to or control over these cookies.

**Links**

This website contains links to other sites. Please be aware that LIR is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. This site encourages  users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personal information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this site.

**Advertisers**

LIR uses advertisement companies to display ads on this site. These ads may contain cookies and are collected by the ad companies, and LIR does not have access to these information.

LIR works with the following ad companies: Google Adsense and Infolinks. Please check these websites for their respective privacy policies.
