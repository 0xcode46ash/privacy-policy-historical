# Learn Photo Editing

**PRIVACY POLICY**

This policy covers how we use your personal information. We respect your privacy and will take all measures to protect your personal information. 

We keep your information confidential, any personal information received will only be used to fill your order. We will not sell or redistribute your information to anyone.
