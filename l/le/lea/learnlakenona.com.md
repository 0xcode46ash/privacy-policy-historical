# Privacy Policy of Lake Nona | Lake Nona

  * [Home](http://www.lakenona.com/)
  * [Privacy Policy](http://www.lakenona.com/privacy-policy/)



The website (the “Site”) is published and maintained by Lake Nona.  Lake Nona will not collect personal data (name, address, phone number, email address) through this website unless you provide it voluntarily.  If you provide personal data, we may use it to better understand your needs and improve our products and services.  We may use that data to provide you with information you requested from us and to contact you.  Our users are given the opportunity to “opt out” if they do not wish to receive promotional materials from us by replying to these communications with “unsubscribe” in the subject line of the e-mail or by e-mailing us at [info@lakenona.com](mailto:info@lakenona.com).  We may provide third parties with aggregate (not individual) information about visitors to our Site.  We will not sell, rent or market personal data to third parties.

In some cases, we may automatically collect technical information when you connect to our website.  Such information (typically the internet browser and computer operating system you are using) is not personally identifiable.  We may store some information in your computer in the form of a “cookie.”  Cookies let us tailor our website to your interests and preferences.  Refer to your internet browser for information about how to erase or block cookies.
