From time to time, we may ask you to complete questionnaires or surveys, which we use to obtain additional demographic information about our users, to measure and improve the quality of our service and to better assess your interests and needs. We do collect end user computer information like, technical information related to your computer and the manner in which you are accessing our website which includes information such as the internet protocol (IP) address of your computer, what operating system your computer is using and what browser software (e.g., Explorer, Netscape or other).
