# Leadfusion Privacy Policy

We respect and protect the privacy of those who visit or use our Web site. We want you to know how your information is used.   The following principles govern our information practices and other privacy aspects of our Web site:

  * Collection of information
  * Use of information
  * Sharing of information
  * Choice to Opt-out
  * Security of Information
  * Updating and Correcting Personal Information
  * Notification of Changes
  * Use by Children
  * Third Party Privacy Practices
  * Contacting Leadfusion



## Collection of Information

Leadfusion is the sole owner of all information collected on [www.leadfusion.com](http://www.leadfusion.com/) (the "Web Site").   Leadfusion may collect information from our Web Site visitors at different locations on our Web site; including but not limited to the following:

_Forms_  
There are various forms contained throughout the Web Site including but not limited to Request for Product and Pricing Information, Technical Support Request, and Feedback Forms which are designed to collect certain information. These forms may collect the visitors' information, including but not limited to name, address, phone, title, email address, visitor's Leadfusion's name, visitor's Leadfusion's URL, and comments entered into the forms.

_Product Demonstrations_  
Leadfusion product demonstrations will perform calculations and return results based on financial, demographic, age identifiers, and other data input by the visitor. The input data is collected by Leadfusion. 

_Customer Login_  
Authorized Leadfusion clients can log into Leadfusion's administrative web site by entering their account name and password to verify their identity and establish a secure connection when logging into the administrative area. Activity performed on the administrative web site is captured by Leadfusion for auditing and tracking purposes and to provide services to client. 

_Log Files_  
Like most standard web site servers, Leadfusion uses log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service providers (ISP), referring and exit pages, platform type, date and time stamp, number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track visitor movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information.  This data is automatically collected when you use our Products and pages relating to such Products.  Data collected in log files are not linked to personally identifiable information, such as name, address, email, etc.  However, personally identifiable information may be collected when you provide such information to us.

_Cookies_  
Leadfusion's Web site and Products may use "cookies" which are placed on visitor’s personal computers.  For example, cookies used in Products enable data persistence so that visitors do not have to re-enter their information when utilizing more then one Product or when returning to use Product at a later time.

## Use of Information

Information collected by Leadfusion on the Web Site will be used to contact the visitor in direct response to their inquiry or for marketing purposes. Visitors who do not choose to 'opt-out' of future communications may receive future notification about Leadfusion products. Contact from Leadfusion includes: 

  * Visitor Requests and Feedback
  
Leadfusion will contact visitors upon request when they submit a form. Visitors who do not choose to opt-out may receive future notification about Leadfusion products and services.

  * Client Service Messages
  
We communicate with clients on a regular basis to provide requested services or service notifications. With regard to issues relating to their account we reply via e-mail or phone.

## Sharing of Information

_Legal Disclaimer_  
Though we make every effort to preserve visitor privacy, we may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on our Web site. 

_Third Parties_  
We DO NOT Share Personal Information with third parties (i) unless such parties are providing a service for Leadfusion; (ii) in the event of a business transition as set forth below; or (iii) if required to do so by law.

_Business Transitions_  
In the event that Leadfusion goes through a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition, a sale of all or a portion of its assets, visitors' personal information will, in most instances, be part of the assets transferred.

## Security of Information

At Leadfusion we take precautions in accordance with industry standards to protect our visitors' information. When a visitor submits information via our Web site, their information is protected both online and off-line.

When our clients are logged into the administrative area managing their licensed products and their activities are protected by a 128-bit SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption technology. While we use SSL encryption to protect information online, we also protect visitor and client information off-line in accordance with industry standards. Nevertheless, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure and while Leadfusion strives to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee or warrant that it will remain secure. You agree that Leadfusion shall not be held responsible for harm that you or any person may suffer as a result of a breach of confidentiality in respect to your use of the Leadfusion's Web site or Products or any information that is collected or that you transmit to us.

## Notification of Changes

Leadfusion reserves the right to change this privacy policy without notice. If, however, we are going to use personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection we will notify visitors and clients via e-mail. Visitors and clients will have a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner. However, if a visitor or client has opted out of all communication with Leadfusion, they will not be contacted, nor will their personal information be used in this different manner.
