# LeadFusion Privacy Policy

LeadFusion is offered by FinanCenter, Inc. At FinanCenter, we dedicate ourselves to helping our clients capture and cultivate online leads. In that effort, we respect and protect the privacy of those who visit or use our Web site. We want you to know how your information is used. LeadFusion/FinanCenter, Inc. shall hereinafter be referred to as "Company." 

Because of the financial nature of our business, the LeadFusion Web site ("Web Site") is not designed to appeal to children under the age of 13. Therefore, we do not knowingly attempt to solicit or receive any information from children.

If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, please email [privacy@leadfusion.com](mailto:privacy@leadfusion.com), call toll-free at 1-877-205-9825, or write to FinanCenter, Attn: Privacy Advocate, 1860 East River Road, Suite 200, Tucson, Arizona, 85718.

## Information We Collect

Company may collect information from our Web Site visitors at different locations on our Web site; including but not limited to the following:

Forms  
There are various forms contained throughout the Web Site including but not limited to Request for Product and Pricing Information, Technical Support Request, and Feedback Forms which are designed to collect certain information. These forms may collect the visitors' information, including but not limited to name, address, phone, title, email address, visitor's company's name, visitor's company's URL, and comments entered into the forms. 

Product Demonstrations  
Company product demonstrations will perform calculations and return results based on financial, demographic, age identifiers, and other data input by the visitor. The input data is collected by Company. 

Customer Login  
Authorized Company clients can log into Company's administrative web site by entering their account name and password to verify their identity and establish a secure connection when logging into the administrative area. Activity performed on the administrative web site is captured by Company for auditing and tracking purposes and to provide services to client.

## Using Information

Information collected by Company on the Web Site will be used to contact the visitor in direct response to their inquiry or for marketing purposes. Visitors who do not choose to 'opt-out' of future communications may receive future notification about Company products. Visitors may opt-out or opt-in by sending an email request to [privacy@leadfusion.com](mailto:privacy@leadfusion.com). 

Log Files  
Like most standard web site servers, we use log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service providers (ISP), referring and exit pages, platform type, date and time stamp, number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track visitor movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information. Data collected in log files are not linked to personally identifiable information, such as name, address, email, etc. 

Cookies  
Company's products use "cookies" which are placed on the visitor's machine for data persistence so visitors do not have to re-enter their information when utilizing more then one product or when returning to the products. 

Communications from FinanCenter  


  * Visitor Requests and Feedback  
Company will contact visitors upon request when they submit a form. Visitors who do not choose to opt-out may receive future notification about Company products and services. 

  * Service Announcements  
On rare occasions it is necessary to send out a strictly service related announcement. For instance, if our service is temporarily suspended for maintenance we might send an e-mail to notify our clients. 

  * Client Services  
We communicate with clients on a regular basis to provide requested services. With regard to issues relating to their account we reply via e-mail or phone.

## Sharing Information

Legal Disclaimer  
Though we make every effort to preserve visitor privacy, we may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on our Web site. 

Third Parties  
We DO NOT Share Personal Information with third parties (i) unless such parties are providing a service for Company; (ii) in the event of a business transition as set forth below; or (iii) if required to do so by law.

Business Transitions  
In the event that Company goes through a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition, a sale of all or a portion of its assets, visitors' personal information will, in most instances, be part of the assets transferred.

## Information Security

At Company we take precautions in accordance with industry standards to protect our visitors' information. When a visitor submits information via our Web site, their information is protected both online and off-line.

When our clients are logged into the administrative area managing their licensed products and their activities are protected by a 128-bit SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption technology.

While we use SSL encryption to protect information online, we also protect visitor and client information off-line in accordance with industry standards. All Company employees are kept up-to-date on our security and privacy practices. 

Any questions about the security of our Web site and the information collected should be directed to [privacy@leadfusion.com](mailto:privacy@leadfusion.com).

## Notification of Changes

Company reserves the right to change this privacy policy without notice. If, however, we are going to use personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection we will notify visitors and clients via e-mail. Visitors and clients will have a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner. However, if a visitor or client has opted out of all communication with Company, they will not be contacted, nor will their personal information be used in this new manner.
