# Your Privacy Rights

**Scope** This statement applies to the Web sites operated by Lexus, a division of Toyota Motor Sales, U.S.A., Inc. (Lexus).

[](https://web.archive.org/web/20110620230405id_/http://www.lexus.com/privacy/online_statement.html?s_ocid=complete_privacy_statement)

[](https://web.archive.org/web/20110620230405id_/http://www.lexus.com/privacy/online_statement.html?s_ocid=personal_info_sharing_practices#personal%20Info%20Practices)

**Scope** During 2010, Lexus shared personal information about our customers with specific third parties where the information was used for the recipient's own marketing.

## Personal Information We Collect

[](https://web.archive.org/web/20110620230405id_/http://www.lexus.com/privacy/online_statement.html?s_ocid=personal_info_we_collect#jInformation%20We%20Collect;%20How%20We%20Use%20and%20Share%20it)

  * We collect personally identifiable information you choose to provide, which may include your name, mailing address, e-mail address and phone number.
  * We collect and store the name of your Internet service provider, the browser and type of machine you are using, the IP address and the Web site that referred you to us, and the pages you visit to help manage our Web site.
  * We use cookies and Web beacons to manage our Web site and e-mail programs. We do not use these to collect or store personal information.
  * We may also collect certain demographic information from you in connection with surveys or from commercially available services.



## Uses and Sharing

[](https://web.archive.org/web/20110620230405id_/http://www.lexus.com/privacy/online_statement.html?s_ocid=uses_sharing#jInformation%20We%20Collect;%20How%20We%20Use%20and%20Share%20it)

  * We use personal information to provide you with services or information you request, such as new vehicle information alerts, promotion and mailed brochures.
  * We also use personal information when you request to participate in promotional activities such as sweepstakes, contests, games, special events, blogs and chats.
  * We share personal information with our business partners to assist us in fulfilling your requests. These companies cannot use your personal information for any other purpose than the reason you provided it to Lexus.
  * We also share personal information with companies affiliated with Lexus and our authorized dealers and dealer advertising associations for their own use.
  * We do not share personal information with any other marketers for their own use unless you have requested that we do so.



## Your Choices and Access

[](https://web.archive.org/web/20110620230405id_/http://www.lexus.com/privacy/online_statement.html?s_ocid=your_choices_access#jYour%20Choices%20and%20Access%20to%20Your%20Information)

  * You have the right to have a copy of the personal information you provide.
  * You have the right to correct or update your personal information.
  * You may instruct Lexus to remove you from our programs you have subscribed to by using the unsubscribe link provided in our e-mail programs.



## Important Information

  * **[Children's Privacy](https://web.archive.org/web/20110620230405id_/http://www.lexus.com/privacy/online_statement.html?s_ocid=childrens_privacy#jChildren's%20Privacy)**. Lexus does not seek information from children under the age of 13.
  * **[Phishing Scams](https://web.archive.org/web/20110620230405id_/http://www.lexus.com/privacy/online_statement.html?s_ocid=phishing_scams#jInformation%20About%20Phishing%20Scams). Lexus will never ask you to update your personal information through e-mail.**
  * **[Acceptance](https://web.archive.org/web/20110620230405id_/http://www.lexus.com/privacy/online_statement.html?s_ocid=acceptance_privacy_statement#jYour%20Acceptance%20of%20Our%20Privacy%20Statement)**. By using this Web site, you signify acceptance of this Privacy Statement.
  * Lexus is committed to working with consumers to obtain a fair resolution of any complaint or concern about privacy.
