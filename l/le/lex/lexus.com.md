Normal Web Site Usage

You can visit Lexus' Web site to read vehicle product information or use our online tools without telling us who you are and without revealing any personal information. The only information we collect and store during normal Web site usage is the name of your Internet service provider, the browser (including available plug-ins and browser functionality) and type of machine you are using, the IP address, the Web site that referred you to us, the pages you request and the date and time of those requests.

We use this information to generate statistics and measure site activity to improve the usefulness of customer visits. During normal Web site usage, we do not collect or store personally identifiable information such as name, mailing address, e-mail address, phone number or Social Security number.

Collection Of Personally Identifiable Information

There are instances when Lexus requests personally identifiable information to provide the Web site visitor with a service or correspondence (promotions and mailed brochures). This information, such as name, mailing address, e-mail address, type of request and possibly additional information, is collected and stored in a manner appropriate to the nature of the data by Lexus and is used to fulfill your request. Lexus makes every effort to ensure the secure collection and transmission of sensitive user information using industry-accepted data collection and encryption methodologies. We do not treat all personal information that you provide to us as sensitive user information. For example, sensitive user information does not include your zip code when it is transmitted without other personal information. The information you provide is used by Lexus and its business partners to improve the services we render. It is never provided to any other company for that company's independent use. Lexus will never sell your personal information to any other company. Owners who subscribe to the Lexus e-mail list can access the Preferences page and change their user profile [here](http://www.lexus.com/owner/). For users who are not currently registered on our site, please call Customer Satisfaction at 1-800-25-LEXUS to have your name removed. Also, be sure to note other comments regarding use of information on specific pages within our site.  


Use Of Cookies

Cookies are pieces of information that a Web site transfers to an individual's hard drive for record-keeping purposes so they may track site and user activity. The use of cookies on lexus.com is in accordance with industry standards and you will find them at most major Web sites. By showing how and when visitors use the site, cookies help Lexus to better track those areas of our site that are popular and those that are not. Many improvements and updates to the site are based on data such as total number of visitors and pages viewed.

Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you'd prefer, you can set yours to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, it is possible that some parts of the site will not function properly if you do so. Also, we will not be able to recognize you as a visitor over time.
