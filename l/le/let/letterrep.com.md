# LetterRep.com - Our Privacy Policy!

We here at LetterRep.com want to provide as wide a variety of letters as possible. If you have a letter saved somewhere on your hard drive and don't mind publishing it, please click on the link below and send it to us. We'll remove all personal information and post it on the site. Thank you for your support! [**  
Please send us your letters!**](https://web.archive.org/web/20020609212109id_/http%3A//www.letterrep.com/form.html)

Or, now you can fax us your letters. Our fax number is: 1-954-343-4639

> **_Notice:_**  
>  "Personal information" includes information that can identify you as a specific individual, such as your name or email address. While you can browse our site without providing any personal information, you will need to provide some personal information in order to take advantage of certain features.
> 
> **_Browsing:_**  
>  We also use a standard web counter and standard web server log files to help us count visitors and evaluate our site's technical capacity. We use this information to find out how many people visit LetterRep.com, help us arrange the pages in the most user-friendly way, keep the site browser-friendly, and to make our pages more useful to our visitors.
> 
> **_Email, Mailing Lists, and Other Services:_**  
>  For some of our features you will be asked to provide your e-mail address as a return address, for example, if you've requested a letter we don't have through our form mail program. We'll use your address to reply to you, letting you know a letter you've requested has been uploaded. If at some time we create a newsletter and you sign up for it, your e-mail address will be used for sending you newsletter editions. Beyond that, we will never use your email address to send you any unsolicited message or information, nor will we share it with or sell it to anyone else for such use. We will send you our monthly newsletter only if you submit your e-mail address and request it through a form provided online.
> 
> **_Linking To Other Sites:_**  
>  Please remember that when you use a link to go from LetterRep.com to another web site, the LetterRep.com Privacy Policy is no longer in effect. Your browsing and interaction on any other web site, including any site that has a link on LetterRep.com, is subject to the rules and policies of that site. We encourage you to read the rules and policies of the sites you visit to further understand their procedures for collecting, using, and disclosing personal information.
> 
> **_Our policy regarding sharing of information:_**  
>  Our policy is simple: we never sell nor rent your personal information to any third parties under any circumstances.
> 
> **_Changes to the LetterRep.com Privacy Policy:_**  
>  This Privacy Policy was posted in October 2001. LetterRep.com reserves the right to change, modify or amend this policy at any time. LetterRep.com takes privacy matters very seriously, and intends to stay at the forefront of privacy policy and protection matters. LetterRep.com will occasionally update our privacy policy and we will post those updates on this page. You are responsible for revisiting this page to review our privacy policy updates.
> 
> **_How to contact LetterRep.com:_**  
>  Please note that whenever you submit any information to us in connection with any LetterRep.com related services, you consent to LetterRep's processing of this information in the United States as necessary to provide the services in which you are participating. If you have any questions, comments, criticisms, or complaints about LetterRep.com's privacy policy and/or the way it affects you, please feel free to [_e-mail us_](https://web.archive.org/web/20020609212109id_/http%3A//www.letterrep.com/form.html).
