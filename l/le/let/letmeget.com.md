# Privacy Policy | letmeget.com

**Information collections:**  
It is not mandatory to give all the personal information at time of user registration. We need just a valid e-mail address for further communication. All the information in Profile is not mandatory in any way. We don't verify such personal information or photographs. We check the url of the link submitted by automatic scripts.  
**Cookie** :  
We are continuously working on the experience with our website. It requires us to use cookie and such tools that stores users data in browser.

It is widely used on internet and generally doesn't make any harm to userâ€™s information.

Please visit <http://www.allaboutcookies.org/cookies/>.

**Third Party Advertisers:**  
We work with third-party companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to our website in order to provide advertisements on this website, other websites and other forms of media about goods and services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please [click here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/consumer/opt_out.asp).

**Log Files:**  
Like most Web site servers we also use log files. Log file can provide information like, Ipaddress, browser name, referring link, internet service provider, date time and other information. We Use such information for our sole purpose, we don't share or sell such information.

**Privacy policy Limitation** :  
Our Privacy policy is only limited within our website. When you click on story link and leave our website, it will not include you in our policy.

**Change in Privacy policy:**  
We can change our privacy policy at any time without any notice. However we will announce here the last date of update.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of August 1, 2008 and last updated June 18, 2009.

**Advertisement:**  
B4tea.com is ad supported website. We place third party advertisement in most of our pages. We are not responsible for any privacy policies of their websites. Such advertisement may use cookies or log, but it is harmless.

If you have any questions of suggestion regarding our Privacy policies, Please [contact us](https://web.archive.org/contact-us).
