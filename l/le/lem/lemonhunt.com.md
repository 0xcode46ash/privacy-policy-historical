# Welcome to Datorsam Complete IT Solutions

## Datorsam is committed to honoring the privacy of our customers and visitors.  


As a result we adhere to strict privacy protection guidelines ensuring your personal information is kept confidential. We do not share or solicit the information captured in any way. We respect your identity and keep it private. You will not be subject to intrusive marketing.

As someone who has an interest in our product(s), we would very much like to learn more about you and your requirements and therefore, we collect this data to better meet your needs in the future. 

The information we receive from you is strictly used to further our relationship with you and help you along the evaluation process of our product(s), if you require it.

The better we know you, the more we can tailor our product materials and sample databases to provide you with a higher level of support and customer satisfaction.
