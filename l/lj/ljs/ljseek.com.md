# LJSEEK.COM: Privacy Policy

| 

LjSEEK.COM takes privacy very seriously. We collect very limited amount of personal information and store it securely.

During your web site navigation we collect IP address, date and time of the visit, visited page, referer, cookies and other HTTP request properties.

Third party advertisers may set their own cookies on your machine to track user interaction. These cookies are subject to privacy policy of appropriate companies. These companies to not have access to cookies set by LjSEEK.COM

In feedback form you're asked to provide your name and Email address. These are only used to send email to our stuff and is not shared with third parties. We only will use this email to contact you regarding your request. We will not send SPAM news or offerings to provided email address.

As our service is being developed this privacy policy may change at any time.
