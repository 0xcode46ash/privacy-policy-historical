Privacy Policy

As an Herbalife Independent Distributor I value every customer and want your online experience to be the best. To insure integrity I will only use any customer information collected by for identification and contact purposes and or for demographic information to be used by our staff.

This information will be used to help ensure that its services are best designed to meet its customers' changing needs. Regarding e-mail, I do not send unsolicited or "spam" e-mail. I use e-mail addresses collected for a number of e-mail alerts and newsletters which are used to communicate new information, special offers and new products to our customers. All customers are given the opportunity to unsubscribe at any time. I do not sell or rent e-mail lists to third parties. It is against my policy to provide any customer information to any third party without the customer's prior permission.

If you need any help or have a question please contact us at [**ronnie@loseit.com**](mailto:info@loseit.com)
