# Privacy

Passware Inc. is committed to protecting your privacy. We will not sell, distribute, rent, or share information about you, your purchases, or data you submitted with any company not directly involved in processing an order.

## Newsletter

We allow you to register to receive periodic email correspondence from us through our Newsletter. The information we collect for this purpose is your email address. Unsubscribe link is provided at the bottom of the newsletter.

## Cookies

A "cookie" is a small data file stored by your web browser on your computer's hard drive. Passware Inc. collects cookies on our Website to capture information about page visits. This information is anonymous and we use this information only internally to gauge page popularity, analyze traffic patterns on our site, and guide development of other improvements to our site.

## Accessing Your Information

Customers may access their contact information through e-mail or a customer service center in order to correct discrepancies and update records with new contact information.

## Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be modified from time to time. Any modifications to our privacy policy will be reflected first on this page.
