# Privacy Policy - Love and Lemons

Love and Lemons uses Google Analytics to collect demographic and advertising information. None of this information is personally identifiable â€“ it is completely anonymous. Love and Lemons may share this information with third parties, primarily for sponsorship purposes.

If you would like to opt-out of providing this information, please use the [Google Analytics Opt-Out Tool](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/).

Email addresses collected for comments are never used for anything besides comment notification, and occasionally for private responses to comments. These email addresses will not be sold or distributed to third parties, except for the winners of contests, or if a post calls out a specific exclusion to this policy.
