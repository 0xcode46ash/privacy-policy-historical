# LoveMyFlash.com Privacy Policy

LoveMyFlash collects no personal details from its users. The only information we store is a users MySpace URL and which layout they have chosen. This information is kept stricly confidential, and its sole purpose is to collect statistical information. No profile information is stored by LoveMyFlash.

## Newsletter

Users may opt-in to our newsletter by providing their e-mail address. This e-mail address will be solely used to send updates relating to the LoveMyFlash website and service or one of it's partners website or services. LoveMyFlash will not disclose personal information to any third party.

If you would like to contact us regarding our privacy policy [click here](http://www.lovemyflash.com/contact/).
