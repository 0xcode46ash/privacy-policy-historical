**Your privacy is guaranteed when you shop with LoveAndChic.Com We will NEVER release any of your personal information to outside parties and you will never receive unwanted e-mails.  
When you sign up with us, you will receive information on new styles and upcoming promotions, but you may request to stop receiving this information at any time. To stop receiving information, simply send an e-mail request to sales@loveandchic.com   
All transactions submitted to us are encrypted with 48 bit SSL security and cannot be intercepted by outside parties, guaranteed! **

  


**What Types of Internet-Related Information am I also sharing with you? 1\. Cookies "Cookies" are small pieces of information that can be stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. When you choose to register with our eShop and sign in, we send a small bit of code (a cookie) back to your hard drive via your browser where it is stored temporarily until you close your browser. 2\. IP Address We can use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our eShop. Your IP address is used to help identify you and to gather broad demographic information.**
