We have developed this Privacy Policy to demonstrate our commitment to protecting your privacy. We encourage you to read this Privacy Policy carefully when using our website or services or transacting business with us. By using our website, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. 

Please contact site administrator ("Contact Option") for any further questions that you might have regarding our privacy practices.

**Information Collected by us**  
LoveVivah.Com is an online social networking and matrimonial service that provides online networking to its members to interact with each other. To make it work, we provide members ways to search others and also do system matches using the information provided by you. To make this happen you may be required to enter personal, health and other information that is displayed on the site. The information provide by you may be sent to servers located in the different countries. 

  1. We will collect information that you directly enter into our site such as personal data, financial information, health data, email, password, interests, partner preferences etc. We may also store the personal data of others entered or provided by you on the site. This include data entered, messages, interests, comments, email, SMS, chat etc.
  2. We will use cookies and other technologies to collect some information about you and your activities on website. This includes, but not limited to your IP, browser version, access time, cookies data, referring web sites etc. We may use some internet technologies to track use of our sites. This helps us in customizing the experience of user to our site.
  3. Cookies: While you access our website, we may assign your computer one or more cookies, to facilitate access, manage session and personalize your experience. By using these cookies we will collect information about your online activities. Information collected may be used by us to show you some information or advertisement as per the collected data. In some case we may use third parties to collect data. Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to sign in or use some of the features of our website.
  4. We may allow third-parties, including our authorized service providers, advertising companies and ad networks, to display advertisements on our site. These companies may use tracking technologies to collect information. We do not directly provide any information directly entered by you on our site to them. They can use cookies and other technologies to track information.
  5. We may share and collect information from our partners' service providers. We may use some external vendor, affiliate or third party company to collect some information about our members and store them. This sometime helps us to understand your needs in a better way to help you in finding the right partner for you. At the same time it can sometime help us in identifying the people involved in illegal activities. In addition, we do conduct surveys and contests and collect/request information from users and store it.

  2. Affiliates and Partners: We may share the relevant information to our partner company so that you can get benefited by the services provided by our partner companies or in case you click a link on our site to get a specific service from our partner company.

In response to a subpoena or similar investigative demand, from a law enforcement or other government agency; to establish or exercise our legal rights; to defend against legal claims; or as otherwise required by law. In such cases, we may raise or waive any legal objection or right available to us. 
  4. In connection with corporate transaction, such as the sale of our business, merger, acquisition, consolidation, a divestiture, asset sale, or in the unlikely event of bankruptcy.

**How you access your information AND Choice of Collection & use of Information**  


  1. You as a registered user can log into your account to review and update your personal information. You can do so by modifying your profile, updating your contact and partner information. If you have online account with us you can also contact site administrator to deactivate your account. Once your account is deactivated, you will not be able to sign in to our website or access any of your personal information. If you close your account, we may still retain certain information associated with your account for research & analysis. In addition, if certain information has already been provided to third parties as described in this Privacy Policy, retention of that information will be subject to those third parties' policies. 
  2. You may decide not to provide with certain non-required information. Not providing information may result in your being unable or not properly use certain features of our website. 
  3. Once we start sending commercial communication and sharing your information for commercial purpose, we will provide you option to enable or disable for special offers. Once you disable the option we will stop sharing your information with partners & affiliate moving forward and stop sending you commercial emails. Please note, if we have already shared your information with someone than you need to contact them directly to stop receiving communication from them. 
  4. You also control the cookies information that we write on your computer. You can refer to your respective browser help to find out how you can delete the cookies from your computer after visiting our website.

Therefore, although we take steps to secure your information, we do not promise, and you should not expect, that your personal information, searches, or other communications will always remain secure. Users should also take care with how they handle and disclose their personal information and should avoid sending personal information through insecure email.
