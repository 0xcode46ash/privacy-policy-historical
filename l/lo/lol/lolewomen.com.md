# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is meant to inform you of our work ethics toward the personal information we gather on the visitors of the Lolë Website. 

Feel confident that when visiting the Lolë website, contacting any Lolë point of contact, participating in Lolë contests or subscribing for Lolë newsletters, you do not need to worry about privacy. We assure all our visitors and consumers that the personal information we gather on this website will not be disclosed or sold to any other company. 

Lolë will not use the personal information requested by its visitors to communicate with them unless the visitor gave his prior consent to receive such information. 

If you have any concerns regarding the privacy of your information please don’t hesitate to write to us at Canada@coalision.com or home@coalision.com
