# Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy for www.lollu.net**

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at admin@lollu.net. 

At www.lollu.net, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by www.lollu.net and how it is used. 

**DoubleClick DART Cookie**   
.:: Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on www.lollu.net.  
.:: Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to www.lollu.net and other sites on the Internet.   
.:: Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL - <http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html>

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.
