# Privacy Policy - League of Legends Statistics - lolking.net

## Privacy Policy

Don't worry, we're keeping you safe!

* * *

This LolKing privacy policy (the "Privacy Policy") is intended to inform you of our policies and practices regarding the collection, use and disclosure of any information you submit to us through LolKing. This includes "Personal Information," which is information about you that is personally identifiable such as your name, e-mail address, user ID number, etc.

### USER CONSENT

By accessing or otherwise using LolKing, you agree to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy, which is incorporated into and forms a part of the LolKing terms of service (set forth on Terms and Policies). Your Personal Information will not be used or disclosed for purposes for which you have not given consent or which are not permitted under applicable law.

* * *

### COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

#### Personal Information That You Provide

In general, we collect Personal Information that you submit to us voluntarily through LolKing. We also collect information that you submit in the process of creating or editing your account and user profile on LolKing. For example, our registration and login process requires you to provide us with your name, valid email address and password of your choice. When you personalize your profile and use the features of LolKing, we will collect any information you voluntarily provide, and we may also request optional information to support your use of LolKing, such as your gender and other demographic information. We collect information in the form of the content that you submit during your use of LolKing, such as photos, reviews, ratings and other information you choose to submit. We may also collect information about you and your friends who use LolKing, from any social network you may have connected from, in order to provide you with a more personalized experience.

#### E-Mail And E-Mail Addresses

If you send an e-mail to us, or fill out our "Feedback" form through LolKing, we will collect your e-mail address and the full content of your e-mail, including attached files, and other information you provide. We may use this e-mail address to contact you, for things such as notifications of deals and other related information. You may indicate your preference to stop receiving further promotional communications as further detailed below.

#### Information Collected Via Technology

As you use LolKing, certain information may also be passively collected and stored on our or our service providers' server logs, including your Internet protocol address, browser type, and operating system. We also use Cookies to gather information regarding the date and time of your visit. This type of information is collected to make LolKing more useful to you and to tailor the experience with LolKing to meet your special interests and needs.

We may use both session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent Cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience with LolKing. Persistent Cookies can be removed by following your Internet browser help file directions. In order to use our services offered through LolKing, your web browser must accept Cookies. If you choose to disable Cookies, some aspects of LolKing may not work properly, and you may not be able to receive our services.

### USE AND DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION

Except as otherwise stated in this Privacy Policy, we do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share for marketing purposes the Personal Information that we collect with third parties, unless you ask or authorize us to do so.

In general, Personal Information you submit to us is used by us to provide you access to LolKing, to improve LolKing, to better tailor the features, performance, and support of LolKing and to offer you additional information, opportunities, promotions and functionality from us, our partners or our advertisers at your request. Additionally, we do share your content preferences and other information with the social network with which you have connected to LolKing, along with those companies and persons you have asked us to share your information with.

In the event we go through a business transition such as a merger, acquisition by another company, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your Personal Information may be among the assets transferred. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur and are permitted by this Privacy Policy, and that any acquirer of ours or that acquirer's assets may continue to process your Personal Information as set forth in this Privacy Policy.

We may disclose your Personal Information if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to (a) comply with relevant laws or to respond to subpoenas or warrants served on us; or (b) to protect and defend our rights or property, you, or third parties. You hereby consent to us sharing your Personal Information under the circumstances described herein.

The ability to access information of third-parties from LolKing, or links to other websites or locations, is for your convenience and does not signify our endorsement of such third-parties, their products, their services, other websites, locations or their content.

## Feedback

If you provide feedback to us, we may use and disclose such feedback for any purpose, provided we do not associate such feedback with your Personal Information. We will collect any information contained in such feedback and will treat the Personal Information in it in accordance with this Privacy Policy. You agree that any such comments and any email we receive becomes our property. We may use feedback for marketing purposes or to add to or modify our services without paying any royalties or other compensation to you.

## Security

We are committed to protecting the security of your Personal Information. We use a variety of industry-standard security technologies and procedures to help protect your Personal Information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Even though we have taken significant steps to protect your Personal Information, no company, including us, can fully eliminate security risks associated with Personal Information.
