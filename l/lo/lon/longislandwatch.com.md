# privacy

**Privacy and Security**  
All credit card data is processed on secure servers, using the most current industry standard encryption technology. Credit card data is never processed or stored on our site. To verify security, check that the address in the browser address bar begins with "https", and look for the SSL padlock symbol in the browser status bar.

Island Watch respects your privacy and is committed to safeguarding your personal information. Any information you submit to us is used strictly to process your order. We will never disclose any personal information to any third parties, unless it is ordered by a court of law.

|   | 

"Cookies" are small files that are stored on your computer and used by web sites to enhance browsing when you visit. On this site, cookies are used only to allow the shopping cart to function correctly. For this purpose only, please ensure "cookies" are enabled.

If you have any questions regarding the above policies, please email us.
