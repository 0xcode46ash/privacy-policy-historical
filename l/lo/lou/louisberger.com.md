# The Louis Berger Group - Privacy Policy

We welcome you The Louis Berger Group, Inc.'s website. We ask that you review this Privacy Policy, which is intended to constitute a binding license agreement that conditions your use of this site ("Agreement"). Our goal is to protect our rights in order to provide you with access to the free content available at this site. If you do not wish to be bound by this Agreement after you have read it, please leave the site. If you remain on this site, or return thereafter, you agree to be bound by this Agreement. 

**Information Collected and Stored Automatically**   
When you visit this page we automatically collect and store only the following information:   
1\. Your Internet address.   
2\. The date and time you access our site.   
3\. The pages you visit.   
4\. The location from which you access the site.   
5\. The type of operating system you use. 

We use this information to help us make our site more useful to visitors, to learn about the number of visitors to our site, to ensure that this service remains available to all users, and to identify unauthorized attempts to change information or otherwise cause damage. 

**Personal Information**  
In general, you can visit The Louis Berger Group, Inc. website without providing any personal information. However, we offer the opportunity to submit a resume to us and, from time to time, we may solicit other information. In order to register online, we may request personal information from you for the sole purpose of tracking and evaluating registrants. Any information you elect to submit through an online registration process will not be sold to any other agency or corporation. 

**Website Security**   
For website security purposes and to ensure that this service remains available to all users, The Louis Berger Group Inc. utilizes a computer system that employs software to monitor network traffic to identify unauthorized attempts to upload or change information, or otherwise cause damage. Any attempts to cause damage or unauthorized access to confidential data could be subject to legal action.
