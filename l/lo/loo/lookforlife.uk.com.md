# Look For Life

Protecting your privacy is of the utmost importance to us and we are fully committed to providing a service that safeguards your privacy. This page outlines how we will use the information that you provide. It also outlines what we will not do with your information. 

We may collect information about you when you contact us via phone, internet of by any other means of communications. This may be though out website or in response to a communication from us.

Your confidentiality is important to us. We will use your personal information for purposes including: 

i) The provision of quotations 

ii) To pass on to our chosen third parties so that they can determine suitable policies

iii) So that we can perform market research in order to ensure that we are targeting the market in the most efficient way

Communication from Look For Life and its partners will generally be over email, by telephone and post. If you have preferences with regards to contact please contact us and let us know.

Information you supply may be used by insurance companies to whom we provide services for the purpose of administering and processing your application 

We reserve the right to amend or modify this Privacy Statement at any time and in response to any changes in law that may apply to our operations.
