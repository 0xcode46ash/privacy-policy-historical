# Welcome to Local.pk

**Our Principles**

At Local.pk the privacy of our users is a matter of serious concern for us. Our aim is to provide secure, reliable and trustworthy user experiences on our website. For this purpose we provide our users full control over their profiles. 

**Collection and Usage of Information**

Upon creating your profile on our website, we do request certain information like your name, email, profile image etc. This information is sloley collected for providing enhanced user experience on the website.

**Information Disclosure**

\- We do not share our users information with third parties for advertisement and marketing purposes.

\- We can disclose your information to legal government entities under law, if we believe such action is neccessary for the sake of  a) Judicial Procedure b) Improving national security c) Preventing Crimes or online frauds  d) Copy right issues.
