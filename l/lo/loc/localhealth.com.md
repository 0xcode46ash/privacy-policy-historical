# Directory - LocalHealth.com Healthcare Yellow Pages

LocalHealth.com and LocalHealth.com commit to protecting the privacy and security of our users of LocalHealth.com Healthcare Yellow Pages product.

This Privacy Statement describes how we handle information we collect through the online LocalHealth.com Healthcare Yellow Pages.

LocalHealth.com and LocalHealth.com have a contractual agreement to provide the LocalHealth.com Healthcare Yellow Pages as a service to consumers who are interested in finding a new physician. The LocalHealth.com Healthcare Yellow Pages helps these consumers locate physician contact information quickly and easily. LocalHealth.com is the technology provider who develops, hosts and maintains this application under contractual agreement with LocalHealth.com. 

**Information Sharing and Disclosure**

**What information is collected and why?**  


LocalHealth.com collects information from its users in two ways: (a) Web server logs/IP addresses, and (b) cookies,

(a) Web Server Logs / IP addresses   
An IP address is a number assigned to your computer whenever you access the Internet. All computer identification on the Internet is conducted with IP addresses, which allows computers and servers to recognize and communicate with each other. LocalHealth.com collects IP addresses to conduct system administration, report aggregate information to affiliates, sponsors, or partners, including LocalHealth.com, to conduct site analysis and Web site performance review. LocalHealth.com will also use IP addresses to identify users who threaten LocalHealth.com service, sites, users, customers, or others. 

(b) Cookies   
A cookie is a piece of information that the computer that hosts our site gives to your computer (your browser) when you access a Web site. The cookie uniquely identifies your browser to the server so the server can maintain a session. Sessions allow us to temporarily store information on the server to help make the Web experience better. For example, the system will remember you are looking for a doctor in the 78729 zip code area so you will not have to retype the information on every screen. Also, In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertisers may place or recognize a unique �cookie� on your browser.

_Personally identifiable information on users (name, e-mail, or home address, etc.) is NOT collected from users of the LocalHealth.com Healthcare Yellow Pages._

**Will this information be shared with others?**

(a) LocalHealth.com may disclose nonpublic information about your visit to nonaffiliated third parties as permitted by law, or required by law, such as when: 

  1. We need to share the information to provide service you have requested,
  2. Statistical information about users as a group (usage habits, demographics) is gathered and might be shared with an affiliate, subsidiary, sponsor, or partner of LocalHealth.com and LocalHealth.com,
  3. We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process.



(b) Third Party Advertising  
We use [ Third Party Advertising Companies ](https://web.archive.org/thirdparty.html) to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, [please click here](https://web.archive.org/thirdparty.html).

**Confidentiality / Security**

LocalHealth.com restricts access to your nonpublic personal information to those employees who reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide services to you. This information will not be used in any method for personal or professional gain and will be kept confidential.

LocalHealth.com maintains physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to guard your nonpublic personal information. More specifically, Hi-Privacy Networks has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under Hi-Privacy Network's control. LocalHealth.com employs strict security measures to safeguard online transactions. All personal information is stored in a secured database. Monitoring and logging of users' activities is also enabled to track potential misuse.

**Safe Harbor Disclaimer Statement**

The referral service is sponsored by LocalHealth.com. Only physicians who can produce a valid state license to practice medicine may participate. There is no charge or fee to you or the participating by each physician is voluntary. 

The information in the directory is compiled from multiple sources including White and Yellow Page Phone Book information and information entered directly by Healthcare Providers. Healthcare Providers are welcome to enter their professional and practice information so consumers can find their listing.

The doctors in this Healthcare Yellow Pages are independent practitioners or physician employees. The independent practitioners are not employees, agents or representatives of LocalHealth.com, or LocalHealth.com and are solely responsible for the provision of medical services to you.
