# Privacy | Lax.com

**Lax.com Privacy Policy:**

At LAX.com, your privacy is our top priority. Our only goal in collecting personal information from you is to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience while using our services. When you shop on our site, we may collect personal information such as your name, e-mail address, billing address, shipping address, telephone number, product selections, credit card or other payment types. We do not and will not sell or rent your information to others. We use customer information we collect only for the purposes of filling orders, contacting purchasers to inform them of their order status, sending promotional information (only if requested), enhancing the operation of our site, for marketing, for statistical purposes, and to administer our systems. We only want to improve your experience. We may use third parties to help us fulfill orders. Certain items such as helmets and goals need to ship separately and therefore those third parties will be provided with only your shipping information. We also use third parties to help process payments, provide support, serve site content, help administer promotional emails, and to administer drawings or contests. We only give them access to the information needed to fulfill their requirements. Our computer system protects personal information using the most up to date and advanced firewall technology.

We take our customers security and privacy very seriously and will not provide, rent, or make available in any way, customer information to any party outside of LAX.com. Customer order information, credit card information and phone numbers are used to process the orders only.

**Credit Card Security:**

We guarantee that you will be able to use your credit card securely while shopping at LAX.com. All important and private financial and personal information is encrypted before it is transmitted to us from our secure cart page. Encrypted personal information can not be read or recorded as it is being transmitted over the internet. We process all of our transactions through  **CyberSource** , the largest and most trusted card processor in the world.
