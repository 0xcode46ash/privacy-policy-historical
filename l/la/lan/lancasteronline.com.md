# Lancaster Online.com: Your Lancaster County News Source

**Privacy**  
At Lancaster Newspapers, Inc. we want our customers to know their personal information, such as name, phone number, address, email address, etc. is safe when browsing our sites, therefore we do not divulge personal information to any third party at any time for any reason. We may use personal information to contact you for surveys and aggregate data collected to advise our advertisers in decisions and to display ads targeted to a specific audience, i.e. women over age 25. At no time will the advertiser have access to personal information.  
Personal information posted in public areas, such as TalkBack may be collected by a third party. Please be aware of this when posting in public forums.  
LancasterOnline.com may also disclose account information in special cases when we have reason to believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be violating our User Agreement or may be causing injury to or interference with (either intentionally or unintentionally) LancasterOnline.com's rights or property, other LancasterOnline.com users, or anyone else that could be harmed by such activities.  
This policy may be amended at any time.

Please direct questions to [privacy@lancasteronline.com](mailto:privacy@lancasteronline.com).  
LancasterOnline, c/o Lancaster Newspapers, Inc. P.O. Box 1328, Lancaster, PA 17608-1328.
