# Privacy Policy

At laneige, we value your privacy and want to make you feel as comfortable as possible about using our website. The privacy policy governs information you provide us on us.laneige.com, as well as all affiliated laneige related online communication such as the review.

  


## Personal Information

Personal information that is collected by us.laneige.com may include account, email promotions, Facebook, sweepstakes, contests and promotions, as well as feedback and comments. Whether or not you provide the personal information to us is in your discretion, but if you choose not to provide the information, you may not be able to use some of the services offered on our site. When creating an account, you may be asked for your full name and email address. The password that you create will be your responsibility.

  


## Sweepstakes Contests and Promotions

There are times when you may be asked to give your personal information and/or other demographic information if you enter in any of our sweepstakes, contests, or promotions. We may use third party applications on Facebook, or other third party websites to host sweepstakes, contests and/or promotions. In those cases, the information you provide will be governed by the third party's privacy policy.

  


## Feedback (Comments and Reviews)

Feedback such as questions, comments, concerns and reviews may be stored so that we can effectively respond to you. This is collected to better serve and provide you with the correct information. 

There may be times when we use your feedback and other demographic information for future marketing initiatives and to help improve our site. We do not sell, or rent your email address with other organizations for any marketing purposes.

  


## Protecting Your Information

us.laneige.com has very high standards to protect the security of your personal information both online and offline. We use available technology and techniques to ensure the best security for our customers. 

Any personal information that we collect is encrypted with Secure Socket Layer technology (SSL). This is an industry standard that is used to protect online businesses for security purposes. We take as many special measures as possible to protect your personal information. We recommend that you keep your password and private information safe.
