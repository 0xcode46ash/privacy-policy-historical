# Privacy Policy :: Lanka Link

We are highly concerned of our users' privacy.  **   _La nka Link_** collect only aggregate information pertaining to visitors to its network of sites, including domain name, visited pages, referring URLs, and other publicly available information.  **   _La nka Link_** uses this information to help improve its web site and services, and to customize the content of its pages for each individual user. 

We  **_ Lanka Link_** do not sell, trade or rent the personal information of our users to third parties.  Therefore your privacy is protected by us all times. 

Happy Surfing!
