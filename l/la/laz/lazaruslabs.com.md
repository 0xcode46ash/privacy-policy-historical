# Lazarus Labs - Privacy Policy

Lazarus Labs recognizes your need for protection of your privacy and for responsible management of personally identifiable information that you share with us, including information by which you can be identified. We have established this Privacy Policy in order to demonstrate our respect for, and commitment to, protecting your privacy.

When you register with Lazarus Labs we obtain certain information about you, including your internet address. Our company tracks internet addresses of domains from which users visit us and utilizes this information to help identify customers and their purchases as well as to help administer our web site.

Some of our web site pages utilize 'cookies' in order to help us serve you better and more personally when you re-visit our site. We use these cookies to save your password so that you do not have to re-enter it each time you visit our site as well as to provide information and content according to your interests. Please note that you can set your browser to notify you when you are sent a cookie, thereby giving you the opportunity to choose whether or not to accept it.

The order form on our web site requires you to furnish your contact information, such as name, email address, zip code and password. We use this contact information to send information about Lazarus Labs and promotional material and to contact you if necessary. Lazarus Labs gives users the opportunity to choose not to receive email updates and other information pertaining to the web site. If you do not wish to receive such communications, please indicate when you are ordering or contact our offices.

Portions of the web site may prompt you to provide certain personal information in your user profile. You have the option to choose not to provide such information, but if you choose not to you may be unable to access certain offers and services of the web site.

In addition, Lazarus Labs may, from time to time, ask personal preference and/or demographic questions. Such information may be used for analyzing user preferences and tastes, overall site effectiveness, and other marketing purposes. Again, you have the option to choose not to answer such questions. Please note that Lazarus Labs does not currently sell, rent or trade any information that identifies particular individuals to any third party or parties.

Lazarus Labs has taken security measures to protect the loss, misuse and/or alteration of information we receive. Though no security measure can be considered foolproof, we feel we have in place a system that is as secure as current technology permits. Our network firewall combines hardware and software designed to keep unauthorized users from getting access to our network.

Lazarus Labs uses an online order form for ordering products and/or services through our web site. We collect financial information, such as credit card numbers, through this form. Such information is used to bill for the products and/or services that customers order.

Lazarus Labs may make message boards, forums and/or chat rooms available for your use. We want to inform you that information disclosed via these areas becomes available to other members and suggest you use caution in disclosing your personal information. The web site may contain links to other sites. Lazarus Labs is not responsible for the privacy measures and practices or the content of such web sites.

**Right to Change Policy**  
Lazarus Labs is constantly innovating and enhancing the products and services we provide. As such, we reserve the right to revise our Privacy Policy to match the products and services that we provide. To ensure that you are aware of any updates, we encourage you to check our Privacy Policy during every visit to the site or when you use our products and services.

Regardless of how this policy may change and evolve over time, Lazarus Labs will not collect or maintain your private and personal identifiable information without your consent. Should you submit personal information to Lazarus Labs, we will hold it in the strictest of confidence and will not sell, license or disclose said information to any third party without your prior consent.

Lazarus Labs, however, does reserve the right to disclose your personal information, if, required to do so by law, or in the good faith belief that such action is reasonably necessary to comply with legal process, respond to claims, or to protect the rights, property or safety of our company, it's employees, customers, or the public.

**Children's privacy**  
We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. This Site is not intended or designed to attract children. Lazarus Labs does not knowingly or willingly collect personal identifiable information from children under the age of 18.

This Privacy Policy forms a legal agreement between you and Lazarus Labs. Your use of this website indicates your agreement to the terms of this Privacy Policy. The Electronic Communications Privacy Act (ECPA) shall not apply to any communications with Lazarus Labs. Lazarus Labs reserves the right to make revisions to this agreement at any time without notice and will post changes and updates to the Privacy Policy by posting to this website.
