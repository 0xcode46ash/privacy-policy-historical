# LatinBoyz.Com Privacy Policy

|  | **PRIVACY POLICY**  
_LatinBoyz.Com is committed to safeguarding your privacy online. Please read the following policy to understand how your personal information will be treated as you make use of our service. This policy may change from time to time so please check back periodically._

  * We do not sell or trade our customer lists (EMail addresses, e.t.c...) to other websites or companies.
  * We do not be disclose information collected to State institutions and authorities except if required by law or other regulation.
  * Some services on this website are operated by third party party companies. This policy applies only to information given directly to KLKLA Services Company (LatinBoyz.Com) only.  All information given to third party companies are subject to their policies.
  * Information collected during the member sign-up process is used to verify credit card billing only, as a fraud protection measure. You will not receive phone calls or postal mail for promotional reasons from us at any time. Information collected during sign-up process and usage of membership may be used to dispute credit card chargebacks and/or fraudulent transactions.



_This listing is not intended to cover every possibility. If you have any other questions that are not addressed above, please feel free to_ _[contact us](mailto:webmaster@klkla.com?subject=Privacy%20Policy)_ _._
