# Privacy Policy - Lashkaraa.com

_Last Updated: October 01, 2013_

 

 ** _Our Commitment to Your Privacy_**  
At LASHKARAA, your privacy is very important to us.  We strive to ensure that your experience with LASHKARAA is one that you will want to repeat and share with your friends. Part of our commitment to you is to respect and protect the privacy of the personal information you provide to us. We will never disclose customer information to any third party. The information below is designed to inform you of what information we collect, how it is used, how it is safeguarded, and how you can contact us if you have any questions or concerns.

 ** _Collection and Use of Information_**  
You may browse our website without giving us any personal information about yourself to us. However, LASHKARAA may at times ask for personal information such as your name and address. This information is required at the time of purchase and/or subscription to our e-mails for promotional offers. We will only send promotional offers by e-mail to those who subscribe to our mailing list or have previously placed an order. If for any reason you decide not to continue receiving promotional e-mails from LASHKARAA, you may simply unsubscribe from our mailing list. LASHKARAA ccasionally uses basic information for marketing purposes. This information includes demographics and items purchased by customers. However, this information is not and will never be shared with any third party companies. All marketing research and implementations are done within LASHKARAA.

**_Security_**  
We understand that security is a critical issue to our customers at LASHKARAA. We are committed to the protection of the personally identifiable information that you share with us. Therefore, use a Level 1 PCI DDS Compliant technology to encrypt your credit card information as it travels over the Internet. At LASHKARAA, we strive to safeguard your personal information.

 _ **Contact Us**  
_ When you submit your personal information to us, you are giving us your consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy. We are always available to discuss your questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy and our privacy practices. For any questions/concerns, please e-mail us at info@lashkaraa.com.

LASHKARAA will occasionally update this Privacy Policy. When a revision is made we will revise the "last updated" date on this page. We encourage you to check the date of our Privacy Policy each time you visit our Website for any updates or changes.
