  * Your privacy is important to us

|    
---|---  
  
> We want you to know that your personal information will not be sold or given to third parties. The following paragraphs describe how we use you information and what we do to protect it.
> 
> We, at LastMinuteTravel.com™, Inc. ("LastMinuteTravel.com"), have created this Privacy Policy (this "Statement") in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to the protection of your privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site (this "Site").  
  
  * Contact Information

| Back To Top  
---|---  
  
> LastMinuteTravel.com requires minimal contact information (like name, email address, ZIP code and country of residence) to be provided by you when you sign-up for this Site's opt-in email services. Your email address is also required so we can send reservation confirmation emails to you when you make a reservation. This contact information is used to provide you with email notifications of important information regarding the services provided by this Site and time sensitive, third party offers based upon criteria specified by you. The consumer may optionally supply additional contact information, such as regular street address. When provided, this information is used in identifying the consumer uniquely in case of lost User ID or profile information.   
  
  * Cookies

|    
---|---  
  
> "Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive or memory. This Site places cookies on your system as part of your use of this Site and of the opt-in email services. This Site uses two types of cookies - ones that goes away when you close down your browser ("Session cookies"), and others that persist from one visit to our site to another ("Persistent long-term cookies").
> 
> Session cookies are used to enable you to 1) log onto this Site as a registered user or travel provider, and 2) use all permitted pages of this Site without repeatedly logging on at every page. Persistent long-term cookies are used to allow this Site to automatically recognize who you are when you return to this Site, so that you are not required to log in again.
> 
> Persistent long-term cookies are only set on your computer, if you choose the "Remember who I am and log me in automatically next time" option when you log in. You can choose to not exercise this option, in which case you will have to log in manually the next time you visit the Site. You must decline this option when using a computer other than your usual computer. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but usually allow you to change their setting to prevent that. Even without a cookie, you can still use the basic functionality of the Site, i.e. search for travel offers and click through to providers' sites. However, without cookies you will not able to log in as a registered user or as a travel provider. The data in security-sensitive cookies is stored in an encrypted fashion to provider greater security.

|    
---|---  
  
> LastMinuteTravel.com occasionally offers sweepstakes and contests to its users. The rules of any sweepstake or contest offered on this Site will be posted on this Site while that sweepstake or contest is running. As part of a sweepstake or contest, individually identifying information about you may be shared with a third party solely for the purposes of allowing the third party to conduct winner selection, notification, and prize delivery.

> LastMinuteTravel.com does not share this information for any other reason without receiving your permission as specified above in the Section titled "Disclosure of Contact Information".  
  
  * Usage Tracking

| Back To Top  
---|---  
  
> This Site's Web server automatically collects some information during your visit to this Site, including your "IP address". Your IP address is an identification number used by computer networks to identify your individual computer so that data (such as the Web pages you request) can be sent to you.
> 
> LastMinuteTravel.com uses your IP address to help diagnose problems with this Site's Web server and to administer this Site. LastMinuteTravel.com also tracks usage patterns on this Site so that it knows how people are using this Site. LastMinuteTravel.com does not share your individual usage pattern with third parties. Any information LastMinuteTravel.com shares with third parties regarding usage patterns is aggregated, so that the usage pattern of any individual user cannot be identified.

  * Security

| Back To Top  
---|---  
  
> This Site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All user profiles require the input of a correct password in order to view or modify the contact information specified in the user profile. In addition, access to this Site's Web servers is limited by security measures including, without limitation, firewall software and password protection. Further, any pages requiring credit card information are protected using Secure Sockets Layer ("SSL") encryption.  
  
  * Change/Delete Policy

|    
---|---  
  
> You may terminate your opt-in email notifications at any time by setting the notifications per day to zero (0) under your user profile settings. In addition, you may request that we delete your user profile by sending an email request to [Remove@lastminutetravel.com](mailto:remove@lastminutetravel.com) with the word "remove" in the subject. All email communications regarding your user profile should specify your User ID, Name and the email address specified in your user profile. Please do not include your user profile password in any email messages you send to us or otherwise disclose your password to anyone. For security reasons, we will never ask you for your password by email or over the phone.
