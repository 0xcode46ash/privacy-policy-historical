# Privacy Policy

Effective October 2012

  1. **Your consent**  
By submitting your personal data to [www.lsi.edu](http://www.lsi.edu/) you automatically accept that Language Studies International Ltd. registers and stores said personal data for the specific purposes outlined below in this Privacy Policy. You also agree to that your personal data may be transferred to and stored in a country that is not a member of the European Union or part of the European Economic Area. 

  2. **Information about you**  
When you book a course or request a brochure through our website, we collect certain information about you such as: name, address, email, sex, phone number and date of birth. We never share personal information about you with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete your booking. The personal information you provide when making a booking or requesting brochures is only used to complete that booking or brochure request. We do not use such information to send updates, new brochures or other marketing material to you, unless you have explicitly indicated in the request form you have completed that you would like us to. When answering emails, we will use your return email address. We will not use your email address for any other purpose and we will not share it with outside parties. 

  3. **Children's privacy**  
For the protection of children's privacy, we require parental consent from users under the age of 16 who wish to book a course with us. 

  4. **Use of cookies**  
[www.lsi.edu](http://www.lsi.edu/) uses cookies. Cookies are messages that websites transfer to your computer, which stores them as text files. They tell us how and when pages on our site are visited and by how many people. Cookies do not collect personal information by which people can be identified, although they do identify a user's computer. We do not combine information collected through cookies with other personal information in order to detect your identity, screen name or email address. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to stop this. If you do so, your experience of our website may be affected. 

  5. **Accuracy**  
LSI will at your request or on our own initiative update, correct or delete any incomplete, inaccurate or outdated personal data on you. 

  6. **Data security**  
Personal information collected by [www.lsi.edu](http://www.lsi.edu/) is stored in secure environments that are not accessible by the public. 

  7. **Areas not covered by this Privacy Policy**  
Personal information that you provide during email exchanges or any kind of chat session are not covered by this Privacy Policy. We will protect your privacy to the best of our ability but please keep in mind that you control the information you disclose when using email or any kind of chat software. You should always be careful when distributing your personal information in this manner. Please be aware that this policy applies to [www.lsi.edu](http://www.lsi.edu/) and other LSI websites, [www.lsi-paris.fr](http://www.lsi-paris.fr/), [www.lsizh.ch](http://www.lsizh.ch/) and [www.lsi.de](http://www.lsi.de/). This policy does not apply to other companies' or organizations' web sites to which we may link. 

  8. **Amendments**  
Any changes to our Privacy Policy will be posted on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we may disclose it to other companies. 

  9. **Your opinion**  
[www.lsi.edu](http://www.lsi.edu/) welcomes your questions and comments about our privacy policy via our email address [info@lsi.edu](mailto:info@lsi.edu)
