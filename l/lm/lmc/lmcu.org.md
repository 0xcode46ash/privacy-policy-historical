# Lake Michigan Credit Union - Privacy Policy

**LMCU Privacy Policy**

Lake Michigan Credit Union's mission is to provide safe and sound lifetime financial service to meet member needs. 

One way we do this is by utilizing Internet convenience to provide members with yet another option for unsurpassed service. We understand members expect us to use the information they give us with confidentiality. 

The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices on our website www.lmcu.org. 

  * Any information collected via feedback, loan application, or survey is entered voluntarily by our web site visitors. We do NOT share this information with anyone outside our organization. 
  * All information is sent directly to e-mail, which is checked daily by appropriate personnel. 
  * Telephone, address and e-mail information is only used should we need to contact you regarding a request you have made of us. 
  * IP Addresses: LMCU collects IP addresses for purposes of system administration. You are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of passwords or account information when using our on-line banking system. We encourage you to change your password periodically for exclusive security control.
