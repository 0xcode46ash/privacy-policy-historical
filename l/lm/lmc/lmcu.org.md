# Privacy Policy

                                     Privacy Policy
                                        Effective January 01, 2003
  Where you and your money belong.
Lake Michigan Credit Union (LMCU) is required by law to give you this
privacy notice to explain how we collect, use and safeguard your personal
information.
LMCU pledges to provide you with competitive products and services that
meet your financial needs and to help you reach your financial goals, which
necessitates that we share your information with our affiliated third party
financial service providers, who provide financial and leasing services.
In order to assist you in meeting your financial needs and goals we have
entered into agreements with other companies that provide either services to
us or additional financial products for you to consider. Under these
arrangements, we may disclose all of the information we collect, as described
below, to other companies that perform marketing or other services on our
behalf or to other financial instiutions with whom we have joint marketing
agreements. To protect our members' privacy, we only work with companies
that agree to maintain stong confidentiality protections and limit the use of
information we provide. We do not permit these companies to sell your
information to third parties.
    INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND DISCLOSE ABOUT YOU
We collect the following non-public personal information about you from a
variety of sources and may disclose all the information we collect to our
affiliated companies.
~ From membership and loan applications and other forms,we obtain
    information such as name, address, Social Security number and income.
~ From your transactions with us and our affiliates, we obtain information
    such as your account balances, payment history, purchase transactions,
    and credit card usage.
~ From consumer reporting agencies, we obtain information such as your
    credit worthiness and credit history.
~ From verification of information you provide on LMCU applications and
    other forms, we obtain information from current or past employers, other
    financial institutions and other sources.
These disclosures typically include information to process transactions on
your behalf, conduct the operations of the credit union, follow the instructions
that you authorize, and to protect the security of your financial records.
   DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION ABOUT FORMER MEMBERS
If you terminate your membership with LMCU, we will not share any
information we have collected about you, except as permitted or required by
law.
                  HOW YOUR INFORMATION IS PROTECTED
We restrict access to your personal information to only those employees who
have a specific business purpose in utilizing your data. Our employees are
trained in the importance on maintaining confidentiality and member privacy.
We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with
federal regulations to safeguard your nonpublic personal information
