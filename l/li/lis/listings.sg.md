### Privacy Policy

Professionals, consumers and other persons using the Sites are individually and collectively referred to as “you”, and LISTINGS SG and its subsidiaries and affiliates are referred to as, "we", "us" and "our". We use this website to provide you with information, resources, tools, products and services specific to the real estate industry and related markets, and to provide you with information about us and our advertisers, and our respective products and services.

We are committed to implementing policies and procedures designed to protect your privacy and security. We want you to understand what kinds of information we gather about you, how this information is used and safeguarded, and how you can control its use.

In conjunction with this Privacy Policy, you should also review our Terms of Use for a complete understanding of the rules governing our Sites.

#### What information is collected from me?  


#### Information You Give Us

Information collected from you will vary, depending on how you use the website and what information you may choose to provide to us. It is possible to browse certain sections of the website without submitting any personally identifiable information. Personally identifiable information (“Personal Information”) means individually identifiable information about you that we collect online, including but not limited to, your full name, a user name and password, email address, phone number, for certain products or services, or financial information, and for certain e-commerce transactions your credit card information. If you choose to take advantage of some of our products and/or services, or to access certain of our tools or functionality, you may be asked to provide Personal Information. Whenever we ask for Personal Information, we strive to provide a link to this Privacy Policy to help you understand how your Personal Information will be used before you decide whether to disclose it. You can choose not to provide us with Personal Information, but then you may not be able to fully take advantage of certain features of our website and we may not be able to provide you with certain requested information, products and/or services.

#### Automatic Collection of Information

We automatically track certain information about you as you visit and use our website so as to to help us to better understand how we can enhance your overall experience on this website. This information may include your computer’s Internet Protocol address, the URLs and website pages that you have, the number of times you visit each page, the search queries you have made, and your browser type. This automatically gathered data includes information provided through the use of "cookies".

#### Information from Cookies

Cookies are small files that we send to and store on your computer so that we can recognize it as a unique machine the next time you visit the website. You are always free to decline any cookies we use if your browser permits. Some of our advertisers and third-party service providers may also utilize their own cookies.

#### Personal Information  


We may share the Personal Information we collect from you only with your consent or under the following circumstances:

User-Requested Services and Information: Certain products or services on our Sites may seek Personal Information from you, which may be used in the following ways:

  * For products and services provided by the website, the information you submit is used by us to provide the information, product or service you have requested. We may also share this information with others, but only to the extent necessary to fulfill your request or to facilitate your use of their services.   

  * We may use other companies and individuals to perform supporting functions for the various tools, functionality, products and services offered through our website on our behalf. These service providers will not be permitted to use the information collected from you for any other purpose other than performing the support functions for LISTINGS SG.
  * For products and services offered to you jointly by us and a third party, your Personal Information may be shared with or independently gathered by the third party. In these situations, LISTINGS SG will only use your Personal Information as outlined in this Privacy Policy, but the third party may use the information in different ways.  




#### Protection of rights 

We will release Personal Information if we believe that such action is appropriate to: (1) comply with legal process, (2) enforce the Terms of Use Agreement for the applicable Site, or (3) identify, contact or bring legal action against persons or entities who are or we believe will cause injury to us or a third party, or (4) otherwise protect the rights, property or personal safety of LISTINGS SG, its employees, website users or others.

#### Registration Information

The Personal Information you provide when you become a registered user is used primarily to help us customise your online experience, increase the convenience of accessing new tools, products and services, and, if applicable, provide the products and/or services you purchase or request. Your email address serves as a unique identifier in our system, and together with your password is designed to prevent unauthorised access to any information you choose to store on the website. The data you give us about your personal preferences and demographics may be used to help us offer content, advertisements, products, and services that we believe will be of interest to you. In addition, we may share this information, in aggregate form, with our current and potential advertisers so that they can present advertisements more effectively. If you forget your password for any registered area of the website, we have an automated password recovery system, which can be accessed by clicking on the "Forgot Password?" link on the sign-in page.

#### Non-personal Information  


Automatically Gathered Information: Information that is automatically gathered, including through the use of cookies, may be shared, in aggregate form with other third parties, including listing providers, and advertisers, so that we may better understand how to enhance your overall experience on the website. When such data is aggregated, all Personal Information is eliminated.

#### How can I access and change my information?  


To view, edit or delete the Personal Information you submitted on the website during registration, click on Edit My Profile on your profile page. 

#### How is my information protected?  


Whenever Personal Information about you is stored on our computers, that information is password-protected from unauthorised access or use. In addition, this information is kept on servers that are located behind a firewall. We appreciate your help in safeguarding the integrity of your own and others' privacy. We encourage you to let us know immediately if you suspect that the information you share with us is being used in any way contrary to this Privacy Policy. No data transmission over the Internet or the storage of information on servers can be guaranteed to be 100% secure and while we strive to protect your personal information, as described herein, we cannot guarantee or warrant its complete security. To contact us, please send an email to: [privacy@listings.sg](mailto:privacy@listings.sg)

#### Scope of This Privacy Policy: Outside Links  


Please be aware that our website links to other Web sites that may collect personally identifiable information about you. This Privacy Policy only applies to the website, and we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content for any linked Web sites. You should refer to the privacy policies and statements of other Web sites or contact the respective Web Administrators of those Web sites to obtain information regarding their information collection, use and disclosure policies.
