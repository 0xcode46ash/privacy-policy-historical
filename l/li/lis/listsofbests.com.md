# Lists of Bests : Privacy Policy

## Lists Of Bests Privacy Policy

Lists Of Bests collects personal information when a user registers for the site. This information is not used for anything other than to enhance your experience here at the site. This privacy statement covers the site listsofbests.com.

**Collecting personal information:**

When registering for Lists Of Bests, you're asked to provide some personal information, such as your name and email address. Once logged in, a user can edit their profile information and provide even more personal information if they wish to do so. This additional information includes instant messaging usernames, a website url, and a way to provide a brief description of yourself.

**Using your personal information:**

This personal information is used only for your experience at Lists Of Bests. Your personal information **will never be shared** with any third party. Once registered, you have several options as to how much - or how little - information you wish to be displayed on your profile page.

**Updating this privacy policy:**

This privacy policy is pretty bare at the moment and will most likely change over time. However, one thing that will **not** change is this: Lists Of Bests will never share your personal information with any third party. Please return to this page occasionally to view updates.
