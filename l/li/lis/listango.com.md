# Listango - Privacy Policy

Listango values the privacy of our users. By visiting Listango, or using any of its services, you are accepting the practices and policies outlined in this Privacy Policy.

#### What this Privacy Policy covers

This Privacy Policy covers the treament of personal information that Listango gathers when you access the Listango web site and/or Listango services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to the practices of third parties that Listango does not own or control, or to individuals that Listango does not employ or manage.

#### Information Collection and Use

We only collect personal information that is relevant to the purpose of our web site. We will never share your personal information with third parties without your express consent.

#### Registration

In order to use Listango services, such as posting new lists to the web site, you must complete a registration form. As part of this registration form, we require your email address.

#### Cookies

We use cookies so that we can recognize registered members.

#### IP Address

We collect IP addresses to gather and analyze broad demographic information about our users.

#### Links to other sites

Our site contains links to other sites. Listango is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites.

#### Mailings

We occasionally may use the information we collect to send you updates regarding our site, new services, and special offers. You will always be able to opt-out of receiving any mailings.

#### Business Transfer

User information is considered a business asset. If Listango, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, user information would be one of the transferred assets.

* * *

#### Corrections/Updates

If you need to update any of your personal details or contact information, visit [your user profile](https://web.archive.org/account) to change your preferences.

#### Changes to this policy

Listango may update this policy at any time. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your Listango account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
