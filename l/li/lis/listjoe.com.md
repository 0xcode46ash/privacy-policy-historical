# ListJoe

**Stored Information**

Your member information, including name, email, website, address, ip address, is kept on file to help faciliate the use of this service. Advertisers absolutely do not have access to your information. ListJoe.com will never rent, share, or otherwise disclose your information to any third parties for any reason.
