# Lightningcast - Streaming Media Advertising

Lightningcast Inc. ("we," "us" or "Lightningcast") has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for our sites, our affiliates sites, including, www.lightningcast.com, (collectively "Our Sites"). 

**IP Address:** We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is also used to gather broad demographic information. 

**Cookies:** Our sites may use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site. Cookies are also used to make sure you don't hear or see the same ad repeatedly. Most Internet browsers allow you to control the kind of cookies you automatically accept or whether you accept cookies at all. Please refer to your Internet browser’s instructions for instructions on disabling cookies. 

**Registration and Personalization Forms:** Our Sites’ registration and personalization forms may ask users to give us contact information (such as their email address, zip code and age). We also ask users to voluntarily give us certain demographic information (such as education level, favorite musical styles and favorite artists) in order to bring each user information they will find interesting. We may use customer contact information from the registration form to send the user information about our company or promotional material from some of our partners. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the delete/deactivate section below. Demographic and interests profile data is used to tailor the visitor's experience at our site, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences. This information is shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis. Information about specific visitors is not made available to advertisers. 

**Site Participation:** User participation on Our Sites is voluntary and information is collected in order to create a more personalized user experience. We may also use this information to determine user interest in receiving information about specific products and services from Lightningcast and some of our partners. Users may opt-out of receiving any such informational mailings; see the delete/deactivate section below. 

**Profiles:** Public user profiles are never displayed to other users with any personal identifying information such as street addresses or real names. 

**Surveys:** We may from time to time encourage visitors to participate in online surveys that ask participants for contact information, demographic and entertainment interests information. We may use contact data from our surveys to send the user information about our company or promotional material from some of our partners. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the delist/deactivate section below. Under no circumstances may anyone under the age of 13 participate in any survey. Users 13 and over, but under the age of 18, should ask their parent’s permission before participating in any survey. 

**Advertisers:** We use an outside ad company to stream some of the ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with streamed ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information. Most Internet browsers allow you to control the kind of cookies you automatically accept or whether you accept cookies at all. See your Internet browser’s instructions for information on disabling cookies. 

**We reserve the right to:** Release current or past member information in the event that we believe that a user is in violation of the terms of service or content guidelines, used to commit unlawful acts, or the information is subpoenaed, or when we deem it necessary or appropriate; Deny users access to our sites to repeated offenders of the Lightningcast.com guidelines or terms of service; Amend or change these guidelines at any time and without notice. 

**Children Under 13:** All general information collected from children under 13 is used only for statistical analysis, editorial participation, feedback and those purposes described in the registration process. We do not give children's personal information to third parties, regardless of whether children choose to allow us to disseminate their personal information for promotional opportunities. 

**Opt-Out:** Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us or our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor. This site contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 

**Delete/Deactivate:** We give users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service. You can send email to [remove@Lightningcast.com](mailto:remove@Lightningcast.com). 

**Contacting the Web Site:** If for some reason you believe Lightningcast has not adhered to these principles, please notify us by email at [info@Lightningcast.com](mailto:info@Lightningcast.com) or notify us in writing at the address below, and we’ll fix the problem promptly.
