# Privacy Policy

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to [contact us by email](http://www.livingrichwithcoupons.com/about-2/contact).

At Living Rich With Coupons & Living Rich Media LLC, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by Living Rich With Coupons & Living Rich Media LLC and how it is used.

**Log Files**  
Like many other Web sites, Living Rich With Coupons & Living Rich Media LLC makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

**Cookies and Web Beacons  
**Living Rich With Coupons & Living Rich Media LLC does not use cookies.

**DoubleClick DART Cookie  
**.:: Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on Living Rich With Coupons & Living Rich Media LLC  
.:: Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to www.livingrichwithcoupons.com and other sites on the Internet.  
.:: Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include ….  
Google Adsense  
Commission Junction  
Amazon  
Burst Network  
Glam Network  
Media.net

**We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here<http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp>**

Living Rich With Coupons & Living Rich Media LLC privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
