# Privacy Policy – Live in Style

[ ](http://www.liveinstyle.com/)

Privacy Policy

  * LiveinStyle.com takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following information to learn more about our privacy policy. 
  * What this Privacy Policy Covers

    * This Privacy Policy covers LiveinStyle.com's treatment of personally identifiable information that LiveinStyle.com collects when you are on the LiveinStyle.com site, and when you use LiveinStyle.com's services. 
    * This policy also covers LiveinStyle.com's treatment of any personally identifiable information that LiveinStyle.com business partners share with LiveinStyle.com. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Experience Commerce does not own or control, or to people that Experience Commerce does not employ or manage.
  * Information Collection and Use

    * LiveinStyle.com collects personally identifiable information when you register for a LiveinStyle.com account, when you use certain LiveinStyle.com services, when you visit LiveinStyle.com pages, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. LiveinStyle.com may also receive personally identifiable information from our business partners. 
    * When you register with LiveinStyle.com we ask for your name, email address, mobile number, date of birth and city. Once you register with LiveinStyle.com and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us. 
    * LiveinStyle.com also automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, LiveinStyle.com cookie information and the page you requested. 
    * In addition, when you use LiveinStyle.com's services, you may make some of your personal information public. If you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return.
    * LiveinStyle.com uses information for three general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, to fulfill your requests for certain products and services, and to contact you about specials and new products.
  * Information Sharing and Disclosure

    * LiveinStyle.com will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone. 
    * LiveinStyle.com will send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when: 
      * We have your consent to share the information;
      * We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested; 
      * We need to share the information with certain service partners in order to respond to your comments or to resolve service issues. (Unless we tell you differently, these partners have no independent right to use this information except to respond to your comments or to resolve the service issues.);
      * We respond to requests from any Indian or foreign government, security, defence, revenue, regulatory or other authority, agency or officer;
      * We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; or 
      * We find that your actions on our web sites violate the LiveinStyle.com: Terms of use or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services.
  * Cookies

    * LiveinStyle.com may set and access LiveinStyle.com cookies on your computer. 
    * LiveinStyle.com allows companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to LiveinStyle.com’s cookies. 
    * LiveinStyle.com uses web beacons to access our cookies within our network of web sites and in connection with LiveinStyle.com products and services.
  * Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences

    * LiveinStyle.com gives you the ability to edit your LiveinStyle.com Account Information and preferences at any time, including whether you want LiveinStyle.com to contact you about specials and new products. 
    * You may request deletion of your LiveinStyle.com account by visiting our Account Deletion page, verifying your password once more and confirming your choice to terminate the account. Soon, you can read here about what information may possibly remain in our archived records after your account has been deleted.
  * Security

    * Your LiveinStyle.com Account Information is password-protected for your privacy and security. 
    * In certain areas LiveinStyle.com uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions. 
  * Changes to this Privacy Policy

    * LiveinStyle.com may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.
  * **Questions or Suggestions** If you have questions or suggestions, please mail us at [info@liveinstyle.com](mailto:info@liveinstyle.com) .
