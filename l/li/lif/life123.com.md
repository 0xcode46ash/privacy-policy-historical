Cutting Edge Fitness shall not be liable for damages of any kind arising out of your access or inability to access an Cutting Edge Fitness maintained Web site or your reliance on the information contained within the Web site.

Clicking on certain links within Cutting Edge Fitness will take you to other Web sites for which Cutting Edge Fitness takes no responsibility.

Cutting Edge Fitness collects information that can identify you only when it is voluntarily offered. Cutting Edge Fitness uses this information in accordance with its privacy policy, which is described below.

Cutting Edge Fitness requests personal information when you sign up for our services, including your name, email address, and other information in order to process your order. When you purchase products from us or register for any of our newsletters, contests or services, we automatically place you on our list to receive e-mail regarding updates about special offers, new products, and new services. If you do not wish to receive e-mail updates, please send us an [e-mail](mailto:remove@cuttingedgefitness.com?subject=Remove) with "remove" in the subject line. **

**Privacy Policy**  


Recognized by the industry for our commitment to safeguarding consumer privacy on our Web site, we operate under a strict set of privacy principles. Cutting Edge Fitness takes care to respect the privacy of its online "visitors / shoppers". Information that can identify any online visitor is collected only when voluntarily offered by the visitor. In cases where Cutting Edge Fitness may need personal information to provide visitors with services/products specifically for them, or to inform then about new features or services, visitors are asked explicitly for that information.

Our Internet Web sites collect nonpersonal information from online visitors in order to track the total of number of visitors to the site in aggregate form and identify the type of Internet browser (e.g., Netscape®;, Internet Explorer®) and operating system (e.g., Windows®, Macintosh®, etc.) used by the visitor. This information allows us to continuously improve our Web site.

Information that can identify a visitor, such as a name or address, is not collected in this process, nor does Cutting Edge Fitness require disclosure of such information. Information that can identify a visitor is collected only when the visitor provides it voluntarily. 

Any changes in this privacy policy will be promptly disclosed in these pages.

**Information We Collect** **  
  
** This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on this website. On some pages, you can order products, make requests, and register to receive materials or information. The information collected at these pages are:  Name, Billing Address, Shipping Address, Email address, Phone number, Credit/Debit Card Information. We use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order only to complete that order. This information is confidential, known only to us. We do not share this information with any outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order and maintain a customer database. __We do not retain credit card numbers or other payment information for purposes other than processing your secure online orders. After your order is placed, your credit card and other payment information records are securely stored on an independent computer with no access to or from the internet. Any hardcopy information that is printed out locally and is not sent along with your order (internal copies of credit card transaction receipts, for example) is crosscut shredded before being disposed of. Cutting Edge Fitness uses cookies to make visiting our Web sites easier. **Cookies are required for shopping cart use.**
