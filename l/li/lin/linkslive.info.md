# Link Directory Share Ravenue With Google Adsense

**Privacy Policy Overview  
**This Privacy Policy covers the LinksLive.info treatment of personal information that is recorded when you submit a link or contact us. Other than the information requested in our online forms, no other information is collected from visitors to our web site.  
Our privacy policy does not apply to the practices of other companies advertised, or otherwise listed, on the LinksLive.info web site, nor does it extend to people that LinksLive.info does not employ or manage.

**Information Collection and Use  
**LinksLive.info collects minimal personal information when you submit a link. The only information collected in these instances is the information needed for Your listing, IP address and the date of your or submission.  
LinksLive.info has not to date used email address information for any purpose, but reserves the right to deliver notices to link publishers regarding changes or updates to the LinksLive.info site and service. Also You may receive some important notices regarding your link.

**Information Sharing and Disclosure  
**LinksLive.info maintains your email address and other pertinent information on the premises. Your contact information is never released or sold to any vendor, partner or outside agency. 

**Security**  
Your email address information is stored in a protected directory. Access is restricted to LinksLive.info staff.

**Changes to this Privacy Policy  
**LinksLive.info may amend this policy from time to time. If significant changes are made in the way your personal information is used, we will post a prominent announcement on our site notifying you of the change.
