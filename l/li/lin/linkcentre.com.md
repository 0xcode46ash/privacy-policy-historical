# Linkcentre Privacy Policy.

Linkcentre Privacy Policy 

This policy applies to the web pages held within the [www.linkcentre.com](http://www.linkcentre.com/) domain. 

Linkcentre is committed to maintaining the privacy of it's users. When a user performs a search across either the Search Engine or Directory, Linkcentre does not store any information which could identify the user. Linkcentre does collect statistical information about the number of keyword and directory searches but Linkcentre does not store any information which could identify who performed the search. 

The Linkcentre database holds information about web pages. The following information is voluntarily entered into the database by Web Publishers: 

Web Page Title  
Web Page Address (URL)  
Description of Web Page  
Name of person entering the information  
Email address of person entering the information  
Comments through a feedback form 

The name and email address of the person entering the information is stored in order to send a confirmation email when the information has been added to the database. Linkcentre does not disclose the name and email address to any third party or make it accessible through the web site. 

This site, Linkcentre, uses the services of Engage for the serving and/or targeting of ads, promotions and other marketing messages. To do this, Engage collects anonymous data through the use of cookies. To learn more about Engage, including your ability to opt out of the Engage system, go to [http://www.engage.com/privacy.](http://www.engage.com/privacy)

Linkcentres email and discussion services are provided by Everyone.net. To find out how Everyone.net manages the privacy of information please visit <http://www.everyone.net/main/html/p_policy.html>

Linkcentre uses WebTrends Live to analyze traffic to this web site. WebTrends Live does not create individual profiles for visitors. Unlike some tracking services WebTrends Live does not have a database of individual profiles for each visitor. WebTrends Live only collects aggregate data. For more information about WebTrends Live privacy policy, please click [here.](http://www.webtrendslive.com/privacy_policy.htm)

If you have any questions of concerns about this policy, please contact [Nick Cookson by email](mailto:nick.cookson@linkcentre.com?subject=Linkcentre%20Privacy%20Policy.) or by writing to Interlink Advertising, 26 Stronsay Place, Bispham, Blackpool, Lancashire, UK, FY2 0HE. 

Linkcentre reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. All changes will be posted on this page. Last updated 22nd February 2001.
