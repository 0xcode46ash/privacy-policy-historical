# Privacy - LinkExtend - The Ultimate Browsing Tool

We do not collect any information from users of our toolbar. Our web server does not recognize the domain or e-mail address of any user.

From time to time we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future, we will post the policy changes on our web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out. If you have any concerns about how your information is used, you should check back at our web site periodically. Upon request visitors can have inaccuracies corrected in their contact information.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you can contact us. We can be reached via e-mail at [info@linkextend.com](mailto:info@linkextend.com). If you joined our mailing list and you no longer wish to receive e-mail from us, please let us know by sending an e-mail to the above address.

LinkExtend is a freeware project. Please [donate](https://web.archive.org/web/20091224205433id_/http://www.linkextend.com/donate) to help us grow.
