The Linkury privacy policy is designed to allow the users of its web sites and software applications (the “Linkury Products”) with information allowing them to make informed choices with respect to their use of the Linkury Products and their provision of personal information.

Your use of Linkury, and any disputes arising from it, is subject to this Privacy Policy as well as the Terms of Use of particular Linkury Products and all of its dispute resolution provisions including arbitration, limitation on damages and choice of law. Linkury reserves the right to change our Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use at any time. Non-material changes and clarifications will take effect immediately, and material changes will take effect within 30 days of their posting on this site. We encourage you to regularly review the Linkury Product web sites to familiarize yourself with changes which may occur from time-to-time. If we make changes, we will post them and will indicate at the top of this page the policy’s new effective date. In case a material change is made to this Privacy Policy, a prominent notice will be displayed via the installed toolbar. Once you confirm you’ve read the new Privacy Policy and accept it, the notice on the toolbar will disappear.

Linkury is working in cooperation with third party companies, these companies will present you with different information in form of ads, shopping comparison services and special offers. Using Linkury’s products do not require you to register for any of our products because we simply do not need you to. We do not require the submission of any information that identifies you personally, such as your name, address, email address and other (“Personal Information”). While we would never collect any personal information, in order to improve and personalize our products, surfing habits may be used for statistical analysis.

#### Information obtained by virtue of your visiting Linkury web sites

###### **When you use Linkury Products, we will collect the following information:**

1\. The Internet domain and IP address from which you access the Linkury Products.  
2\. Screen resolution of your monitor.  
3\. The date and time you access the Linkury Products.  
4\. The page you are visiting with the Linkury Products.  
5\. If you linked to a Linkury web site from another referring web site, the address of that web site.

Linkury does not share this information with third parties and use this information for optimization purposes only. By using the Linkury Products, you are consenting to have this above-mentioned data transferred to and processed both within and without the United States of America. The information collected by Linkury uses only for statistical analysis, remains confidential and is not transferred or provided to any third party. By using the Linkury website, you agree to the preceding uses of your information in this way by Linkury. In case you decide to use the “contact us” option in the website, please note that your name and email address will be sent to Linkury’s support team. This information will only be used in order to reply to your request by email. Neither of this information is kept in our database, nor is it shared with any third party.
