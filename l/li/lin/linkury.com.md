# Linkury - Monetize And Distribute

### Privacy

We at Linkury, Inc. and its wholly owned subsidiaries ("Linkury") are committed to providing web sites and software products that respect the privacy of its visitors and users respectively. This page summarizes the privacy policy and practices for all Linkury Web Sites, as well as for the Linkury software products. 

The Linkury privacy policy is designed to allow the users of its web sites and software applications (the "Linkury Products") with information allowing them to make informed choices with respect to their use of the Linkury Products and their provision of personal information. 

Your use of Linkury, and any disputes arising from it, is subject to this Privacy Policy as well as the Terms of Use of particular Linkury Products and all of its dispute resolution provisions including arbitration, limitation on damages and choice of law. Linkury reserves the right to change our Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use at any time. Non-material changes and clarifications will take effect immediately, and material changes will take effect within 30 days of their posting on this site. We encourage you to regularly review the Linkury Product web sites to familiarize yourself with changes which may occur from time-to-time. If we make changes, we will post them and will indicate at the top of this page the policy's new effective date. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you through notice on our home page. We encourage you to refer to this policy on an ongoing basis so that you understand our current privacy policy. 

Linkury is working in cooperation with third party companies, these companies will present you with different information in form of ads, shopping comparison services and special offers. Using Linkury's products do not require you to register for any of our products because we simply do not need you to. We do not require the submission of any information that identifies you personally, such as your name, address, email address and other ("Personal Information"). While we would never collect any personal information, in order to improve and personalize our products, surfing habits may be used for statistical analysis. 

####  Information obtained by virtue of your visiting Linkury web sites 

**When you use Linkury Products, we will collect the following information:**

1\. The Internet domain and IP address from which you access the Linkury Products;  
2\. Screen resolution of your monitor;  
3\. The date and time you access the Linkury Products;  
4\. The page you are visiting with the Linkury Products;  
5\. If you linked to a Linkury web site from another referring web site, the address of that web site  


By using the Linkury Products, you are consenting to have this above-mentioned data transferred to and processed both within and without the United States of America. The information collected by Linkury uses only for statistical analysis, remains confidential and is not transferred or provided to any third party. By using the Linkury website, you agree to the preceding uses of your information in this way by Linkury.
