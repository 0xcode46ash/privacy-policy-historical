**Privacy Policy**

The ERP Consulting Exchange recognizes the importance of protecting the information collected from our online users. We created this privacy statement to explain our online information practices and to demonstrate our commitment to the online users' privacy. Please note that this policy applies only to sites maintained by The ERP Consulting Exchange or its subsidiaries, and not to web sites maintained by other companies or organizations to which we link. 

**The Information We Collect**  
The information The ERP Consulting Exchange collects from our online users is used for internal purposes only to fulfill requests for products, programs, and services, to respond to inquiries, and to offer other products, programs, or services that we believe may be of interest to you. We do not sell any information to others for marketing purposes. 

**Member Service**  
Some online users may choose to register with us online. This online service is specifically used for internal purposes and our business partners to find possible business opportunities. This service requires users to fill out an online form that contains contact information. This information is also used to contact you with upcoming opportunities, news, and promotions. You may choose to not receive further information at any time; for details, please go to the Remove from Lists Section.

**IP Addresses**  
We may use your IP address and not just your email address to help diagnose problems with our server, to administer our Web Site, or to gather broad demographic information. 

**Online Surveys**  
From time to time, we may conduct online surveys. Our surveys may ask you for demographic information, such as ZIP code or your industry sector. If the online survey collects contact data, we may use that data to send you additional information only if we specifically asked for your permission in the survey. 

**Demographic and User Profile Data**  
We may use demographic or user profile data we collect to tailor your Web experience, showcase content that we think may interest you, and display information that matches your preferences. This data also gives us an insight into your needs and interests.

**Remove from Lists**  
At any time you can opt-out of receiving information from The ERP Consulting Exchange. You can opt-out of portions of communications. 

The services that The ERP Consulting Exchange provides have an option that you can control. Simply visit [www.erpcx.com](http://www.erpcx.com/) and log in using your username and password. At this point you may choose to remove yourself from the respective registrations. 

If this does not satisfactory remove you from the lists that you are receiving or if you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, feel free to contact:
