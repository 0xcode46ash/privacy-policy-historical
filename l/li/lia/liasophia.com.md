# Lia Sophia Fashion Jewelry - Share the Love of Jewelry

Privacy Policy

**lia sophia** is dedicated to protecting your privacy. We do not require any personal information to enter our Web site. All information gathered on this Web site is on a voluntary basis.

If you choose to process orders or request information from us, you will be required to provide personal data. **lia sophia** will use this for our business purposes only and will not share, sell or barter it. We may provide collective data and statistics about sales, Customers, trends and related site information to third-party vendors, but this will not include personal information unless you have requested us to do so. If deemed necessary, **lia sophia** will release your information to protect our rights and comply with state and federal laws.

Consent

By entering this Web site, you agree to **lia sophia's** collection and use of this information in accordance of this policy.

Collected Information and It's Uses

When placing an order online, we need your name, mailing address, phone number, e-mail address and credit card information with expiration date for processing.

When you ask us to locate an Advisor in our "Advisor Locator," we need your name, mailing address and e-mail address in order to create your profile.

This information will be used for our business purposes only.

Security

All personal data collected on this Web site is secure. **lia sophia** has state-of-the-art technology in place to guarantee any personal information you provide is secure and protected from transfer, storage and distribution by using secure sockets layer (SSL) technology. We use digital secure site service from Verisign to provide you with the best protection technology on the Internet.
