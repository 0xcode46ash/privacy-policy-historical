# lia sophia - Privacy Policy

At lia sophia®, a leading high quality fashion jewelry company, we have systems in place that involve managing your personal information to ensure the fast and most efficient delivery of jewelry to you. We take seriously our responsibility to protect this information and have created this Policy in order to make you aware of the type of information we collect and your rights to and our responsibilities in safeguarding it.

Security  
To protect your nonpublic personal information on the Internet, we use industry-leading secure socket layer (SSL) technology from Comodo Secure to ensure protection of any personal identifiable information you voluntarily submit to us. 

Payment Card Industry Compliance  
Identity and credit card theft continues to be a growing concern among consumers today. That’s why lia sophia is proud to be a participating member of the Payment Card Industry (PCI) Council, and undergoes an annual audit of compliance with the Council’s PCI Data Security Standard. By maintaining this association and adhering to this comprehensive standard, lia sophia demonstrates its commitment to proactively protecting Customer account data.

Consent  
By entering this website, you are providing information on a voluntary basis and agree to lia sophia’s collection and use of this information in accordance with this Policy. 

Information Covered By This Statement  
Our Policy applies to all nonpublic personally identifiable information and all information provided to or obtained by lia sophia resulting from jewelry orders, requests for assistance in locating a lia sophia Advisor and requests and activities relating to the lia sophia opportunity ("nonpublic information"). Examples of nonpublic information are social security numbers, addresses, account numbers and balances, phone numbers and e-mail addresses. This information will be used for our business purposes only and our Policy will continue to cover information we may collect about you during the course of our relationship as well as after the relationship has ended.

Cookies and the Use of Information  
We may collect non-personally identifiable information through "cookies," which are text files used to store information about your interests and preferences, as well as non-personally identifiable technical and navigational information to assist us in providing you better service. 

We share nonpublic personally identifiable and non-personally identifiable information with affiliated and non-affiliated companies and other third parties that perform services on our behalf for your benefit. These companies and third parties must abide by this Policy keep nonpublic information confidential and use it only for its intended purposes. Periodically, we are required to disclose nonpublic information to other third parties in order to protect our rights and to comply with state and federal laws.

Non-Affiliated Companies  
You may limit our ability to share nonpublic information with non-affiliated companies. This is called "opting out." If you do not want your nonpublic information shared, please contact us at [privacy@liasophia.com](mailto:privacy@liasophia.com) to opt out. Please know, however, especially if you are a lia sophia Advisor, opting out could adversely affect our ability to interact and service you as a lia sophia advisor.

Children  
lia sophia’s website is not designed for or intentionally targeted at individuals thirteen (13) years of age or younger. It is not our policy to intentionally collect or maintain information about anyone in this age group.

lia sophia Advisors  
This Policy does not apply to the collection, use or disclosure of information by lia sophia Advisors, who are independent third party contractors of lia sophia and are not otherwise affiliated with lia sophia. While we try to ensure that our Advisors properly handle nonpublic personally identifiable information in accordance with applicable law, we are not responsible or liable for the statements, acts or omissions of lia sophia Advisors. 

Safe Harbor  
lia sophia complies with the U.S.-EU Safe Harbor Framework and the U.S.-Swiss Safe Harbor Framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of personal information from European Union member countries and Switzerland. lia sophia has certified that it adheres to the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles of notice, choice, onward transfer, security, data integrity, access, and enforcement. To learn more about the Safe Harbor program, and to view the lia sophia certification, please visit [www.export.gov/safeharbor](http://www.export.gov/safeharbor).

This Policy may be amended, from time to time. If lia sophia decides to change its Policy, we will post those changes and revise the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of the Policy.

lia sophia reserves the right to transfer any and all nonpublic personally identifiable and non-personally identifiable information we collect to a third party in the event of any disposition of all or any portion of lia sophia’s business.

If you have questions about this Policy or wish to update or correct your nonpublic personal information, please contact us at [privacy@liasophia.com](mailto:privacy@liasophia.com) or lia sophia, Attn: Privacy Administrator, 1235 N. Mittel Blvd, Wood Dale, IL 60191.

Privacy Compliants By European Union Citizens  
In compliance with the US-EU and US-Swiss Safe Harbor Principles, lia sophia commits to resolve complaints about your privacy and our collection or use of your personal information. European Union or Swiss citizens with inquiries or complaints regarding this privacy policy should first contact lia sophia at [privacy@liasophia.com](mailto:privacy@liasophia.com).

lia sophia has further committed to refer unresolved privacy complaints under the US-EU and US-Swiss Safe Harbor Principles to an independent dispute resolution mechanism, the BBB EU SAFE HARBOR, operated by the Council of Better Business Bureaus. If you do not receive timely acknowledgment of your complaint, or if your complaint is not satisfactorily addressed by lia sophia, please visit the BBB EU SAFE HARBOR website at [www.bbb.org/us/safe-harbor-complaints](http://www.bbb.org/us/safe-harbor-complaints) for more information and to file a complaint.

Effective June 2004 to Present
