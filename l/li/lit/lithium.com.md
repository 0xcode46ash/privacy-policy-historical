# Lithium Technologies Social CRM and Community Solutions - Privacy Policy - Lithium

  
Last Updated 10 March 2011

### Policy Scope

This policy covers key areas about the personal information Lithium collects on our Web site, how we use your personal information, and your choices about its usage.

### Personally Identifying Information

We collect your name and contact information such as phone number, company, title, and email address.

We also use cookies and may use other connection related information such as your IP address and browser type to track our site usage to provide you a personalized experience. 

### Uses

We use your personal information responsibly and utilize it to communicate with you and to help us improve our products and services.

### Security

We use industry standard security measures to protect your personal information such as firewalls and SSL encryption.

### Your Privacy Choices

You have the option to opt-out of receiving promotional material from us such as promotional emails or newsletters.

You may contact us to review, update, or delete your information from our mailing list.

### Certifications

We self-certify compliance with US-EU Safe Harbor framework.

Lithium's privacy policy and practices are certified by Truste in the Truste Privacy Seal program.
