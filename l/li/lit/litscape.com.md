# Privacy and Cookie Information

* * *

The knowledge and consent of the customer are required for the collection, use or disclosure of customer information, except where required by law.

Litscape.com absolutely and unconditionally will not sell, exchange or otherwise distribute personal information to a third party for any reason.

Currently, we do not use or read cookies for any purpose. In the future, cookies may be necessary for transactional session tracking. These cookies will contain only randomized session identification numbers and will expire at the completion of a transaction. See our cookie policy below.

Server log files are maintained as necessary for web site operations. Web and mail server log data are collected and may be provided to third party authorities to assist in criminal or Internet abuse investigations. Using standard log formats, these files contain such information as Internet IP addresses, connection times, referring URLs, and may contain email addresses and search queries. Every Internet user should be aware that such information routinely is collected, stored, analyzed, and frequently distributed for all Internet transactions, not just ours!

Depending on the nature and locale of a transaction, Litscape.com is subject to compliance with the following relevant Privacy Legislation. Specifically, inter-provincial and international transactions are covered under the Canadian legislation, while intra-provincial transactions (within Alberta) are covered under the Alberta legislation.

[Canada: Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (S.C. 2000, c. 5)](http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/P-8.6/)

[ Alberta: The Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA)](http://www.psp.gov.ab.ca/index.cfm?page=legislation/act/)

* * *

This site does not use cookies for general content browsing. For our interactive tools, we may use session cookies and authorization cookies to protect our systems from abuse. No personal information is collected at any time.

## Adsense

Our site is supported by advertising revenues. For this purpose, we use Google Adsense which may result in cookies being set through their services. For details about how Google Adsense uses cookies, please follow their link:

[How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.](http://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/partners/)

## AddThis

We allow third-party companies, including AddThis, to collect certain anonymous information when you visit our website. These companies may use non-personally identifiable information during your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. These companies typically use a cookie or a third party web beacon to collect this information. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice, you can visit [Your Choices Online: A Guide to Online Behavioral Advertising](http://www.youronlinechoices.eu/) and [Netword Advertising Initiative](https://web.archive.org/web/20161119022324id_/http%3A//www.litscape.com/www.networkadvertising.org)

[Privacy Practices for End Users who use the AddThis Sharing Button on Publisher Websites](http://www.addthis.com/privacy/privacy-policy#publisher-visitors)

## European Commission Requirements

The European Commission has legislated that all websites available to European Union (EU) member countries (i.e. all websites) obtain consent from their visitors to use cookies. **If you are viewing this site from an EU member country your continued use of this site is your agreement with our cookie policy as stated above!**

For more information about cookies and this policy, please visit [Cookies - European Commission.](http://ec.europa.eu/cookies/index_en.htm) ("The European Commission is the executive of the European Union and promotes its general interest.")
