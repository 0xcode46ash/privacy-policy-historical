At Linensnthings.com, your right to privacy is a main concern. That's why we help you maintain control over your personal data on the Internet. We do not sell or loan any personal information to any third party, and we adhere to strict guidelines for protecting the information you provide us with during a visit to our site.

We are concerned with the privacy of your credit card information. We offer you the industry standard security measures available through your browser called SSL encryption. Encrypted information cannot be read or recorded when it travels over the Internet. Access to your personal information is only provided to our employees for the purpose of fulfilling your Internet order or in assisting you with a service issue.

  
**Gift Registry**  
The Gift Registry is handled differently, in that cookies are used to save your registry number so you won't have to reenter all of your information every time you return to the site. Users of the Gift Registry will receive follow up contact after the event.

  
**Acceptance of these Terms** **  
** When you use our Web site you are agreeing to accept the privacy policy of Linens ‘n Things. We reserve the right to make changes or edit this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes we will post it on our site.
