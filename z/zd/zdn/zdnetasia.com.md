# ZDNetAsia: Privacy Statement

ZDNetAsia collects personal information about our users only during registration for the ZDNetAsia service, from our use of "cookie" technology as you use the service and during entry into a ZDNetAsia contest or sweepstakes. 

ZDNetAsia respects your right to privacy. As part of the registration process, we give you the opportunity to decline to receive any mailings from either ZDNetAsia or other parties to whom ZDNetAsia may provide such information. 

The data we collect from cookies and other places is used in the aggregate to give us demographic information about the entire ZDNetAsia audience in order to improve the ZDNetAsia service. The personal information you provide - name, address, etc. -- allows us to inform you about updates to the service and to notify you of products and services of ZDNetAsia, its affiliates and third parties that we believe may be of interest to you. We also share aggregate information -- not individual data --with our advertisers to help them better understand our services and to monitor the reach of their advertising on the service. 

Personal information is also collected when you choose to personalize information to meet your specific needs or interests. Such data is used to better serve you, but you are free to choose not to personalize ZDNetAsia sites. 

ZDNetAsia uses reasonable precautions to keep the personal information disclosed to us secure and to disclose such information only to third parties we believe to be responsible. However, we are not responsible for any breach of security or for any actions of any third parties which receive the information. ZDNetAsia also links to a wide variety of other sites. We are not responsible for their privacy policies or how they treat information about their users.
