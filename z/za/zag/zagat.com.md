# Zagat: Privacy Policy

We always have and always will respect your privacy. Any information that you give us is held with the utmost care and will not be used other than as described in this Privacy Policy.

Zagat Survey, LLC ("we" or "us") has created this Privacy Policy to demonstrate its commitment to your privacy on zagat.com. The "Zagat" name and reputation have been built on the trust of its customers, and we hope that our commitment to online privacy will inspire the same trust in you while you are visiting zagat.com. Please read this Privacy Policy to understand how your personal information will be treated as you use zagat.com. Please see our [Terms of Use](https://web.archive.org/terms_of_use.asp) for more information about our online policies in general. 

**The Information We Collect**  
We collect personal information from you on zagat.com only when it is specifically and knowingly provided by you.

**User Registration**. Registration on zagat.com is strictly voluntary. To register on zagat.com, you must provide your name, address, and e-mail address. You may also provide additional demographic information, such as your year of birth and occupation.

**Product Ordering**. To simplify shopping in our Shop, you may choose to keep your credit card number and its expiration date on file. We also request your billing and shipping address. We transmit this information safely over the Internet in encrypted form and store it on a secure server. This information is used only to bill you for and ship to you the zagat.com products that you order.

**How We Use Your Information**  
We use your e-mail address periodically to e-mail you with updates and newsletters about new features, special offers, and targeted promotional items available on zagat.com. You may choose not to receive these e-mail communications by so indicating in response to one of our e-mails, and each e-mail will clearly explain how to opt- out. Any demographic information that you voluntarily share with us is used to tailor zagat.com to your interests. We also may share this demographic information with other companies with whom we do business, but only on an aggregated basis so that none of your personally identifiable information is disclosed.

**With Whom We Share Information**  
We share your personally identifiable information with other companies only in connection with processing payments for online purchases that you make in our Shop and with delivery of e-mail to our registered users. These other companies have agreed not to disclose your personally identifiable information to anyone else. We do not sell any of your personally identifiable information or do anything that makes it possible for other parties to contact you directly without your consent. We may, however, disclose personal information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to conform with the law or legal process or to protect or defend our rights or property.

**Security**  
Zagat.com has security measures in place to help protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. All information is stored in a secure operating environment.

**IP Addresses**  
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer zagat.com.

**Cookies**  
We use cookies for limited purposes. Cookies are small pieces of text stored on your computer that help us know which browser you are using and where you've been on zagat.com and on certain sites to which you may link in order to use some of our features. Cookies also tell us which pages are being viewed and which are not. This helps us make zagat.com more user- friendly. We do not use cookies to retrieve personally identifiable information about you.

**Links**  
We may provide links to third-party web sites as a service to our users. Please be aware that we cannot control and are not responsible for the information collection practices of such web sites, which may differ from those of zagat.com. We encourage you to review and understand the privacy policies on these web sites before providing any information to them.

**How You Can Control Your Information On Our Site**  
You can make changes to your personal information by e-mailing us at [ZagatPrivacyCenter@zagat.com](mailto:ZagatPrivacyCenter@zagat.com).

**Questions**  
Protecting your privacy is one our highest priorities. If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, you may e-mail us at [ZagatPrivacyCenter@zagat.com](mailto:ZagatPrivacyCenter@zagat.com). 

**Consent**  
By using zagat.com, you consent to the collection, use, and storage of your information by us in the manner described in this Privacy Policy. We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will alert you of any such changes by updating this Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review our Privacy Policy whenever you use this Web site to make sure you understand how your information will be used.

Thank you for reading this Privacy Policy and for using zagat.com!
