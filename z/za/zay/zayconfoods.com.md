# PRIVACY POLICY

PRIVACY POLICY
Zaycon Privacy Statement
Zaycon Foods, LLC is committed to protect your online privacy and believes it is important
for you to know how we handle the information we receive from you via the internet.
Our Privacy Policy describes what information we collect online, how we protect it and what choices you have about how that
information is used. We encourage you to read this Policy carefully so that you will understand the privacy standards used to
ensure the security and conﬁdentiality of your information. Should you have any questions about this policy or our privacy
practices, please contact us in the following manner:
          1. Send an e-mail to Melissa Perez at melissa@zayconfoods.com
          2. Write to us at: Zaycon Foods attn: Privacy Ofﬁcer, 16201 E. Indiana Ste 2150, Spokane Valley, WA 99216
What Personal Information Zaycon Collects
Zaycon will collect information that identiﬁes you online only when you voluntarily provide it to us through our site.
The Zaycon bill payment link which allows you to make electronic payments, requests that you provide personal ﬁnancial data
for the payment of invoices. Information from the bill payment link does not display or transmit any personal health care
information about you or from your account.
How Zaycon Protects the Security of Personal Information
It is Zaycon’s policy to protect your personally identiﬁable information against unauthorized access or release. All the personal
information you provide to us is handled through a Secure Socket Layer which is a leading Web technology that encrypts your
account information. Additionally, Zaycon maintains all personal patient data within the Zaycon information system ﬁrewalls that
operate on separate Zaycon servers. The general public may not access these servers.
Collection and Use of Non-Personal Information/Cookies
Zaycon tracks visitors to our web site by collecting data elements called “cookies.” Cookies collected from the Zaycon website
are used to identify which computer you are using, assist in the navigation of the web site, and remember computer settings.
Cookies are not permanently maintained within our tracking system. You may prevent your computer from accepting cookies
by modifying the properties on your web browser, however, stopping your computer from accepting cookies may limit your
web browser’s functionality on the Zaycon website.
Internet Links to Other Sites
As a convenience to our site visitors, Zaycon’s website may contain links to a number of sites that may provide useful information.
Links contained on the Zaycon website which transfer you to a non-Zaycon site are not maintained by Zaycon and may contain
different information and/or different privacy policies from those of Zaycon. You may wish to review the privacy policies of
these web sites and/or receive speciﬁc information regarding that site’s privacy policies and procedures after leaving Zaycon’s
site. Zaycon is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of a non-Zaycon website. Zaycon does not control, or have any
input into, whether internet-linked sites use or accept cookies on their sites.
Children’s Access
Zaycon is committed to the protection of children’s privacy, security, and safety. Our websites are created for an adult
audience and are not designed or intended to attract children in any way. We do not knowingly solicit, collect or accept any
information from children on this website.
Changes to the Zaycon Privacy Policy
Zaycon may alter or make changes to this privacy policy from time to time when developments occur and/or technology
improvements are implemented. When changes are made, we will update our privacy policy and notices. Please review this
page periodically to ensure that you are aware of any such updates.
Your Consent
By visiting and using Zaycon’s website and the contents therein, you consent to the collection, use and disclosure of information
as described in this policy.
How to Contact Zaycon Foods
Zaycon welcomes feedback and questions regarding our privacy policy. If you need further information or have questions
regarding this privacy policy, please send an e-mail to: melissa@zayconfoods.com.
