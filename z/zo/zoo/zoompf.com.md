# Privacy Policy | Zoompf

Zoompf Inc was founded by experts from the security and privacy fields and we understand the importance of protecting your privacy and the personal information that you disclose to us. We know that providing personal information is an act of trust and we take that trust seriously. For this reason, we strive to make our privacy practices as clear and easy to understand as possible. 

## Personal Data We Collect

When using our website, such as when downloading certain web content or when you to request to be contacted by a Zoompf Inc representative, you will provide us with certain information including: 

  * Your full name
  * Your email address
  * Your telephone number
  * Your employer
  * Additional information about your inquiry



Zoompf Inc classifies all of this collected data as personal information. With only 2 exceptions, Zoompf Inc will never sell, rent or otherwise disclose any of your personal information to any third party without your prior and explicit consent. 

The first exception is we will share this information with law enforcement only if we are required to do so for legal reasons. 

The second exception is if a 3rd party ever acquires Zoompf Inc, our assets, included any collected data, would be owned by the 3rd party. Also, if Zoompf Inc buys or sells subsidiaries or business units then in such transactions personal information could be one of the transferred assets. In either of these instances Zoompf Inc and this privacy policy would no longer govern the collected data. 

## Other Data We Collect

Zoompf Inc utilizes web analytics software to monitor how people navigate and interact with our website. No personal information is associated this data and it is used exclusively to improve the quality of our website. This collection of data involves the use of cookies and your can opt-out of this collection by disabling cookies in your web browser. 

Zoompf Inc provides a free web performance scanning service which collects information about the websites that are assessed using the service and their performance characteristics. This information includes the website, its technologies, its content, and its performance issues. We may use information about the websites and their performance characteristics as examples in content we publish, such as blog posts, whitepapers, and other marketing material. 

## Links To Third Party Sites

Our site may contain links to other sites. Zoompf Inc is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content, of those web sites 

## Changes To This Privacy Policy 

From time to time, Zoompf Inc will review and may edit this policy in accordance with potential changes to our business and as required by law. The most current version will always be located at <http://zoompf.com/privacy-policy>
