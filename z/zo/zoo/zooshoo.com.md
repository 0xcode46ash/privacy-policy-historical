# Zooshoo | Policy

Privacy Protection: 

_ZooShoo.com_ collects your name, billing & shipping address, credit card information, and email address for the sole purpose of processing your orders only. 

Your email address will be used to send your order and shipping confirmations, and for relevant correspondence regarding your order. We may send you occasional email notices relating to our products or promotions. If you do not want to receive email from us in the future, please let us know by sending an email to: [customerservice@zooshoo.com](mailto:customerservice@zooshoo.com). 

_ZooShoo_ respects your privacy. We will never sell, rent, or give away ANY customer information to any other party. 

Security while you Shop: 

Ordering from _ZooShoo.com_ is safe and secure. From the moment you enter our site, you are on a secure server, using what is called SSL (secure socket layer) technology. Your credit card and personal information is encrypted when transmitted to us and are stored in a secure environment. This encryption keeps your personal information, including your credit card, secure from theft. Our site is actively monitored 24/7 to make sure that we are always open, safe and ready for your shopping experience.
