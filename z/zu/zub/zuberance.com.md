#### Types of Information

We may collect the following types of information when you visit the Site or use the Services: End User Information: It is possible to use the Services without providing any personal information. However, there are instances where you must provide us with certain personal information in order to take full advantage of the Services ("End User Information"). End User Information may include but is not limited to name, screen name, and valid email address.

Content: By using the Services, you acknowledge that your reviews, photos, videos, opinions, messages, screen name and other such content you post to publicly facing sites ("Content") is intended to be posted for public consumption. We may display such Content on the Site and facilitate distribution to a wider audience through Third Party Services. Depending on what you elect to post, Content may or may not personally identify you. We provide our customers with your Content so that they might improve the products and services that you are evaluating through your use of the Services.

Log Information: When you access the Services, we record information about your visit, whether or not you formally submit End User Information. This may include but is not limited to your Internet Protocol address, browser type, internet service provider, referring and exit pages, operating system, date-time stamp, central processing unit on your computer, and clickstream data ("Log Information"). Log Information is not linked to any personally identifiable information and it is used to administer, track and improve the Services and gather general demographic information about our user base as a whole.

Cookies: We store certain information from your browser using unique identifiers known as "cookies". "Cookies" are small text files that are stored on your computer when you visit a website and transmit information about you and your use of the Site. They identify your computer, but not you personally. You may disable cookies; however doing so may limit the functionality of the Services.

Third Party Information: We may receive certain non-personally identifiable demographic information (such as zip code) from our partners ("Third Party Information"), and we may combine this information with what we have collected from your Log Information, Cookies, and/or your use of the Services. We may also track information publicly available on other websites providing Third Party Services.

"End User Information" may identify you personally and is therefore considered "Personal Information" for the purposes of this Privacy Policy. "Log Information", "Cookies", and "Third Party Information" do not identify you personally and are expressly not considered Personal Information" for the purposes of this Privacy Policy. "Content" is not considered "Personal Information" for the purposes of this Privacy Policy; however, depending on what you choose to publicly share, Content may identify you personally. Unless otherwise defined, references to "information" generally include any and all types of information described herein. In the event that we directly combine Personal Information with the other types of information described herein, the combined information is treated as Personal Information under this Privacy Policy.

  


#### Disclosure of Information

Personal Information is never sold to a third party, however we may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances: (a) we share your information with the company providing the product or service that you are evaluating through your use of the Services, which use will be subject to each respective company's own privacy policy; (b) we may share your Log Information and/or Cookies with third parties generally for direct marketing purposes in order to provide targeted advertising for other products and services that may be of interest to you; (c) we may share your information with our subsidiaries, affiliates or service partners in order to process information on our behalf or to assist us with providing the Services, including certain marketing efforts. Prior to sharing your Personal Information, we require that any such subsidiary, affiliate or service partner agree to process your information in compliance with this Privacy Policy, and honor strict confidentiality and security requirements as dictated by Zuberance; (d) we have a good faith belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of such information is reasonably necessary to: (i) satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process or governmental request; (ii) detect, prevent, or otherwise address potential wrongdoing, fraud, security or technical issues; (iii) protect against imminent harm to the rights, property or safety of Zuberance, its users or the public as required or permitted by law; or (iv) enforce applicable Terms of Service, including investigation of potential violations thereof; (e) we become involved in a merger, acquisition, or any form of sale of some or all of our assets; or (f) where we have your consent.

Third parties with whom we have shared your information may independently obtain other demographic data about you in the event they choose to use their own cookies, pixel tags, or other such mechanisms. We are not responsible for the information gathered by these third parties. You expressly authorize us to collect, use, disclose and retain any information that we, in our sole discretion, deem necessary for the purposes mentioned above.
