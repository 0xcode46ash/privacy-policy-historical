# Privacy Policy :: Zumvu

Lets begin with a note of thanks to you for using zumvu.com. As an user at zumvu.com you must know how the information that we collect from you is safe with us. This privacy policy is there to help you know what information we collect from you, what we do about it and how and why is it safe with us. In case you feel that something thats going in your mind is still not explained here , please feel free to get back to us at info@zumvu.com. We assure to get back to every mail personally asap.

**What information we collect**  
By signing an account at zumvu.com, you voluntarily give us few information like your name, date of birth, profile photos, email addresses etc. These personal informations is used for the sole purpose of serving you better. It can be used to notify you about the new features, to reply back to your posted queries and questions and for various other issues. We however assure you to not disclose your identity and personal information on any third party sites. 

**Collecting The technical Data**  
While you are using zumvu.com, the server on which it runs might collect your log data including few information which the browser sends when you browse a website. The technical team at zumvu.com uses cookies to improve the use of our site. This allows us to remember your screen name, tell us that you have visited our website before, and may contain information that will make your next visit to our website more enjoyable. This however is never used for storing your personal information such as your name or to retrieve information from your computer that was not originally sent in a cookie. 

**How is your information used at zumvu.com**  
The information that we collect here at zumvu.com is used for the sole purpose of making us serve you better. The information provided by you is used to provide our products to you and indeed make them better. We suggest you blogs you might like. For example, if you’ve followed a blog that you’re interested as in tourism or quotes websites, we may suggest similar blogs to you. 

The information collected is also used to notify you about the new features introduced in the site, send you updates, newsletters and other information of your interest. 

**Third Party Advertising**  
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. Some of the advertisements you see on our Site are delivered by third parties, such as ad networks, advertising agencies, advertisers etc. These third parties may collect information about you and your online activities, either on the Site or on other websites, through cookies and other technologies in order to understand your interests and deliver to you advertisements that are tailored to your interests. Zumvu.com does not have an access or control over the information so collected. 

**Our policy for children**  
[Zumvu.com](http://zumvu.com/) is not meant for children below 13 years of age. So in case you find out that your child has registered an account with us pls contact us and we will get back to you. 

**Changes to ths policy**  
From time to time, zumvu.com may choose to make amendments to this policy. We request users to log in here and keep themselves updated with the information. The right know how will always help us to serve you better. 

To review, modify or remove the information you have provided to us, or to contact us with any questions or complaints, please e-mail us at [info@zumvu.com](mailto:info@zumvu.com)

By registering an account at zumvu.com and using it, you accept to abide by this policy.
