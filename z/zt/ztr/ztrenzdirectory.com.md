# Privacy Policy :: zTrenz Directory.com

We welcome your valuable feedback please feel free to contact **Support Center**

 **Collection of Information:**

zTrenzdirectory collect information from our users at several different points and ztrenzdirectory.com is the owner of the information collected on our website. 

**Submissions**

While submitting your website for review, a user must first complete the submission form. During submission process a user is required to give contact information including name and email address. This information may be used to contact you if there is a need for a revision to your site reviews. ztrenzdirectory.com notes and saves information such as time of day, browser type, browser language, and IP address with each query. That information is used to verify our records and to provide more relevant services to users.

 **Use of Information:**

**USER'S PROFILE**

We store information that we collect through log files, and the information provided during the zTrenzDirectory submission service, to create a profile of our users. A profile is stored information that we keep on individual users that detail their characteristics. Consequently, collected information is tied to the users personal information to provide offers and improve the user experience. We DO NOT share your profile with other third parties.

 **LOG FILES (USER'S)**

Like most standard Web site servers we use log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses, etc. are not linked to personal information.

 **Communications with zTrenzDirectory.com**

**SERVICE ANNOUNCEMENTS**

On few occasions it is necessary to send out a strictly service related announcement. For instance, if our service is temporarily unavailable due to maintenance we might send users an email. Generally, users may not opt-out of these communications because these communications are not promotional in nature.

 **CUSTOMER SERVICE**

Please feel free to contact us if you have any suggestions to improve our services at info.AT.ztrenzdirectory.com (Replace .AT. with @)

 **Sharing of Information**

Instances in which ztrenzdirectory.com will share users' Personal Identification Information:

 **LEGAL DISCLAIMER**

Though we make every effort to preserve user privacy, we may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on our website.

**AGGREGATE INFORMATION (no-personal identifications)**

We share aggregated demographic information with our partners and advertisers. This information includes: page views, unique visitors, browsers, and additional broad demographical data. This is not linked to any personal identification information.

 **THIRD PARTY ADVERTISERS**

zTrenzDirectory.com shares website usage information about users with reputable third party advertisers for the purpose of targeting our advertisements. Be advised that tough we make every effort to accept only reputable advertisers, that we cannot held responsible for the Privacy Policies of any advertiser, promotional link, sponsor, or other third party. Please make sure to check the individual Privacy Policy of any third party site that you visit.

 **THIRD PARTY INTERMEDIARIES**

We use PayPal to bill users for services. These companies do not retain share, store or use personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes.

 **BUSINESS TRANSITIONS**

In the event ztrenzdirectory.com which is one of the projects of zTrenz Web Solutions Pvt. Ltd. goes through a business transition, such as a merger, being acquired by another company, or selling a portion of its assets, users’ personal information will, in most instances, be part of the assets transferred. Users will be notified via prominent notice on our website for 21 days prior to a change of ownership or control of their personal information. If as a result of the business transition, the users’ personally identifiable information will be used in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection they will be given choice consistent with our notification of changes section.

 **CHOICE**

Users who no longer wish to receive communications may opt-out of receiving these communications by replying to info.AT.ztrenzdirectory.com (Replace .AT. with @) with "not interested" in the subject line in the email.

 **Links / Listings**

This website contains links / listings to other websites. Please be aware that we, ztrenzdirectory.com, are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other websites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our website and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this website.

 **Security**

ztrenzdirectory.com takes every precaution to protect our users’ personal identification information. When users submit sensitive information via the website, their information is protected both online and off-line.

We protect sensitive information online; we also do everything in our power to protect user-information off-line. All of our users’ personal information, not just the sensitive information mentioned above, is restricted in our offices. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job are granted access to personal identification information. Our employees are encouraged to use password-protected screen-savers when they leave their desk. Furthermore, ALL employees are kept up-to-date on our security and privacy practices. Our employees are reminded about the importance we place on privacy, and what they can do to ensure our users’ information is protected. 

If users have any questions about the security on our website, Please contact our **Support Center**.

**Updating/ Modifying Personal Information**

If a user’s personal identification information changes or wants to update his/her personal information (such as zip code, phone, email or postal address), This can be done by contacting our **Support Center**. 

**Notice of Change**

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this privacy statement, the homepage, and other places we deem appropriate so our users are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it. We will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected. 

**Contact Information**

Please contact us at our **Support Center** info.AT.ztrenzdirectory.com (Replace .AT. with @) **.**
