# ZTE - Privacy Statement

  
By accessing and using this Site, you hereby agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.  
What information is collected?  
When you want to log in the website, make subscription, order some products or service, or submit content to this web site, your personal information are required, such as your name, email address, company, country, etc. ZTE is committed to the protection of the privacy of its web site visitors, unless compelled by law or competent jurisdiction.   
Personal Information  
In order to respond to your questions, fulfill your requests or assist you in login the site, it may be necessary to ask for personal information such as your name, address, e-mail address and telephone number. We may use this information to respond to your requests, or to contact you via mail, e-mail or phone to inform you of new products, services or promotions we may offer. If you place an order for a product or magazine, request a service or submit content to this site, we may need to contact you for additional information required to process or fulfill your order and/or request. However, unless compelled by applicable legislation, we will not provide this information to a third party without your permission, except as necessary to process your order, fulfill your requests. You also hereby grant to ZTE the right to exchange any information provided by you between ZTE's affiliates for the purposes mentioned before.  
Cookies  
Some of our Web Pages use "cookies" so that we can better serve you with more tailored information when you return to our Web site. Cookies are pieces of information that are transferred to your computer's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. The information stored will make your usage of our Web site easier and faster   
ZTE does not use cookies to retrieve information from your computer that was not originally sent in the cookie, and we do not use information transferred through cookies for any promotional or marketing purposes. Some ZTE Web Pages use cookies to track usage patterns in order to improve the usefulness and effectiveness of our Web site. This information is not shared or sold to any third parties whatsoever.   
Changes  
This privacy policy is subject to changes without notice. Therefore, you need review the policy when you re-visit the website.
