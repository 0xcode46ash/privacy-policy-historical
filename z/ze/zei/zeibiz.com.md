# Privacy Policy | Zeibiz

**Your Privacy**

The privacy of our visitors to our site is most important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. We will never share your personal information on other company.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here <http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp>

**Longtail Video and SpotXchange Video Ads**

SpotXchange and Longtail Video Ads is a video advertising platform that can be seen in some of our website ‘s video content. Videos under JW player has a pre-roll, in-roll and post-roll advertisements.

**ValueClick Media, Technorati Media  
**

These are the sponsored advertisements visible at any pages on this website.

**Apture**

Apture is a Social Networking Sharing tool that appears on the top page when readers scroll down to the bottom of the page. This toolbar allows users to share article to their Facebook, Twitter and Email account. Apture also enable user to search by **highlighting** a specific **Name/Word** within the page or typing on search box. It gives search results from Google, Bing and Amazon including Photos, Videos and Merchant Items for sale.

**Media  Artifact Disclaimer**

**Photos and Videos** in this website belongs to their respective owners. **Photos** are taken from available sources on the internet and mostly tagged with credentials. **Videos** on this website are mostly embedded from YouTube.
