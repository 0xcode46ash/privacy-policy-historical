# Zebra Technologies Corporation Privacy Statement

  
Privacy Statement--Zebra Technologies  


This Website Privacy Statement (“Privacy Statement”) describes how Zebra collects, uses, and discloses personally identifiable information on this website (“Website”). By using this Website or submitting any personally identifiable information, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Statement and the Zebra Website Terms of Use.

**Information Collection**  
User information and other site activity data, including domains, browsers, and pages accessed, is collected throughout the Website. This information is used for standard reporting purposes and to improve the Website content and display. Personally identifiable information may be collected through the Website, including name, physical address, telephone number, email address, and other contact information, and other information that is combined with your name or identifiers, such as the products or services ordered, financial and payment information, name and nature of your business, site activity data, and delivery and fulfillment information (“Personal Information”). This Personal Information is collected where required or appropriate in connection with the purchase and delivery of Zebra® products and services, to allow Zebra to contact customers when necessary, to tailor the user experience, and to protect Zebra from fraud may be collected throughout the Website. 

**Use and Disclosure of Information  
**Zebra may disclose Personal Information to IT providers and other subcontractors that assist with the operation of the Website and/or the associated business transactions. Personal Information may also be used and disclosed to law enforcement personnel and other persons in situations where we believe that it is necessary to identify, contact, or bring legal proceedings against someone who may be misusing the Website, using the Website without proper authorization, interfering with the rights of Zebra, or when Zebra reasonably believes that the law requires such disclosure. We may also disclose Personal Information where necessary or appropriate in connection with the sale or transfer of all or part of the business. In addition, the information may be used and disclosed to verify your compliance with the Zebra Website Terms of Use 

Persons who opt in by checking the appropriate box on various forms on the Website will be added to our internal mailing list and may be mailed product information from Zebra Technologies. If you do not wish to receive such information, please [ e-mail us](mailto:listmaster@zebra.com) with the word "unsubscribe" in the subject line. We will remove your name from our subscriber list within ten business days. Except as otherwise provided in this Privacy Statement, Zebra does not sell or rent internal lists to other companies or vendors. 

**Links to Other Internet Sites  
**The Website may contain links to Internet sites and services that are maintained by third parties. Those sites may have privacy and security policies that are different from those followed by Zebra Technologies. Zebra has no control over those sites and is not responsible or liable for the policies and practices followed by such third parties. 

**Revisions to this Privacy Statement  
**Zebra reserves the right to change this Privacy Statement from time to time in our discretion. Please check this statement frequently and particularly before you submit additional Personal Information via the Website. All revisions to this Privacy Statement will be posted on the Website via a link from the home page. In the event that we change a significant or material term in this Privacy Statement, we will notify affected visitors by revising the link on the home page to read “ **Revised Privacy Policy.** ”
