# Zebra Technologies Corporation Privacy Policy Statement

**Privacy Policy Statement**

**Zebra Technologies Corporation  
333 Corporate Woods Parkway  
Vernon Hills, IL  60061**

We can be reached via e-mail at [inquiry4@zebra.com](mailto:inquiry4@zebra.com), or you can reach us by telephone at 847-634-6700.

User information and site activity data, including domains, browsers, and pages accessed, is collected throughout the web site. This information is used for standard reporting purposes and to improve the site content and display. Information required for the purchase and delivery of Zebra® products and services, to allow Zebra to contact customers when necessary, to tailor the user experience, and to protect Zebra from fraud, may be collected throughout the site. 

Zebra may disclose information in situations where we reasonably believe that it is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal proceedings against someone who is misusing the site, using the site without proper authorization, is interfering with the rights of Zebra or when Zebra believes that the law requires such disclosure. In addition, the information may be used to verify your compliance with the Zebra website Terms of Use.

Persons who opt in by checking the appropriate box on various forms on our site will be added to our internal mailing list and may be mailed product information from Zebra Technologies. If you do not wish to receive such information, please[ email us](mailto:listmaster@zebra.com) with the word "unsubscribe" in the subject line. Please provide us with your name and phone number (for verification purposes). We will remove your name from our subscriber list within five business days. Zebra Technologies Corporation does not sell or otherwise distribute internal lists to other companies or vendors.

Zebra's web site may contain links to Internet sites and services that are maintained by third parties. Those sites may have privacy and security policies that are different from those followed by Zebra Technologies. Zebra has no control over those sites and is not responsible or liable for the policies and practices followed by such third parties. 

By using or visiting this site, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Policy and the Zebra Terms of Use, both now and in the future.
