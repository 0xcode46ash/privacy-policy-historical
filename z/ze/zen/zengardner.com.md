# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy For Natural Medicine

At NaturalMedicine.com, herein also known as Natural Medicine, your privacy is of paramount importance to us. We do NOT publish or sell names or any information collected for any services, forums or memberships on this site. We value your privacy and promise to take every reasonable precaution not to make available your contact information in any way.  
Natural Medicine collects information from people who have joined, simply so that we can contact them again. We may use email lists to correspond with people who have joined. While we at Natural Medicine will take every reasonable precaution to safeguard the privacy of your information, we can not absolutely guarantee the privacy of submitted information or the security of our site because of unexpected events or events beyond our control.  
Thank for visiting Natural Medicine.
