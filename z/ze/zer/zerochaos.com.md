# ZeroChaos - Company Info - Our Commitment To Your Privacy

ZeroChaos.com ("ZC") is committed to maintaining the privacy and integrity of all information gathered on its web site. The following document discloses our information gathering and dissemination processes for ZC.

ZC gathers information such as your IP address and time of visit for use in gathering broad demographic data. We use a technology called "cookies" on our site to maintain session information while you are logged in. 

ZC requires that you provide personal information to gain access to our site and take advantage of the products we offer. To register with ZC you will provide certain information including your name, social security number, address, date of birth, phone number, fax number, e-mail address, emergency contact information, bank account information and tax information. ZC will share this personal information with those entities necessary to provide you the benefits offered by ZC. These entities include, but may not be limited to, your insurance company, 401k provider, Internal Revenue Service, state and local regulatory agencies, banks necessary for direct deposit of paychecks, and the companies for which you work. ZC may also share this information with its legal counsel and accounting firm if necessary. 

At present, if you choose to have health insurance through ZC your personal information will be provided to Great-West Insurance Company, address, phone, fax, and e-mail. Also, if you choose to participate in ZC's 401k plan, your personal information will be provided to Reliance Trust address, phone, fax, and e-mail and Daily Access Concepts address, phone, fax, and e-mail. ZC reserves the right to provide your personal information to other insurance and 401k providers in the event that the providers listed above change. In the course of their business with ZeroChaos.com, technology and security consultant firms may have access to data in the possession of ZC.

ZC also requires that you provide information about third parties. In particular this will include the names, addresses, phone numbers, fax numbers, and e-mail addresses of billing contacts and supervisors at the companies for which you provide services through ZC. You will also provide the names, addresses, phone numbers, fax numbers, and e-mail addresses of emergency contacts. If necessary, this emergency contact information may be shared with companies for which you provide services through ZC.

ZC will not release personal information to nonessential third parties.

ZC reserves the right, however, to collect data in aggregate form and distribute such data to advertisers. This aggregate information will not reveal your personal identity. 

ZC also reserves the right to provide any of your information as required by law, court order, or other valid legal process. 

Your information is stored in a secure database that has several levels of access control, including a firewall. ZC has undertaken appropriate, physical, electronic and managerial procedures to protect information we collect online. 

At present you cannot change your personal information via the ZC website. You can call (407) 447-3000 to correct or modify any personal information provided to ZC. ZC is required by law to maintain certain personal information even after you terminate your relationship with ZC. You may have all other personal information deleted by calling (407) 447-3000. 

ZC reserves the right to change this privacy statement at anytime. You should review the Privacy Statement each time you log onto ZC or, at minimum, each month.
