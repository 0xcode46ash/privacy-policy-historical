This is our contract with you regarding your personal information that is submitted during the registration process. Please read through the following and, if you have any questions, feel free to [**Contact Us**](https://web.archive.org/web/20040402132906id_/http%3A//zerogamers.com/contactus.php "Contact Us").

**Our Approach:** Zerogamers respects your privacy. Our approach is to ensure you that any information that you submit to us is solely used only in subjection between you and . This privacy will always remain viewable to you. 

**Your Information:** Personal information that you submit to us when signing up for our community is used by Zerogamers for the sole purpose of serving you. Such information is not sold, shared or distributed with any outside third parties and is held solely between said and yourself. Information that you have provided is as follows: 

  * **Email:** Our site asks you to submit your email when registering as away of verifying your legitimate status with us. Your email address is used on behalf of Zerogamers as a way of sending updates and newsletters via the forum administration system. This information is always kept private and is not shared, sold or distributed to third parties.
  * **IP:** Your Internet Protocol, otherwise known as your exact connection to the Internet from your PC (consider if your home address, but for the Internet) is available for viewing by administration. Because of the sensitivity in such material, only accesses this when in an alert state or for investigative purposes as a result of abuse reports.

**Changes:** This policy will remain for your viewing at all times. Zerogamers has the ability to change, alter or cancel this agreement at any time. However, any changes made will be sent to all active members and will also be updated via this page immediately upon change. 

**Disqualification:** Our contract with you - this privacy statement - may be cancelled and your privileges deemed null and void in the event of a broken rules statement act. Disqualification will come with a message from our administration and, given the situation, a warning or immediate banning may result.
